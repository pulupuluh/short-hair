How To Get Curly Hair Without A Perm For Guys

Written by Bardi Jun 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to switch things up? Getting curly hair without a perm may seem like a difficult task, but it's definitely achievable. In this article, we'll be discussing the steps and tips to achieve curly hair without a perm for guys.

Description

Curly hair has always been a popular trend, but not everyone is born with it. Perms can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to know how to achieve curly hair without a perm. This article will guide you through the process and provide helpful tips to maintain your curly hair.

How to

The first step to getting curly hair without a perm for guys is to start with clean hair. Shampoo and condition your hair, making sure to fully rinse out all the product. Towel dry your hair until it's damp and apply a curl-enhancing product. You can find these products in the hair care aisle at your local drugstore.

Next, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls without causing frizz. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair while drying to enhance your curls.

If you have longer hair, you can use a curling iron or wand to create curls. Make sure to use a heat protectant before using any hot tools on your hair. Wrap sections of your hair around the curling iron or wand, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair have been curled.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean hair
2. Apply a curl-enhancing product
3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair
4. Scrunch your hair while drying to enhance your curls
5. Use a curling iron or wand to create curls (optional)
6. Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools on your hair
7. Wrap sections of your hair around the curling iron or wand
8. Hold for a few seconds before releasing
9. Repeat until all sections of your hair have been curled
10. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling
- Don't overuse hot tools on your hair, as this can cause damage
- Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized
- Experiment with different curl-enhancing products to find what works best for your hair
- Use a satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and keep your curls intact while sleeping
- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends, which can hinder the curling process.

FAQ

Can I get curly hair without a perm?

Yes, it's possible to get curly hair without a perm. You can use curl-enhancing products, a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer, or hot tools like a curling iron or wand to create curls.

Will using hot tools damage my hair?

Using hot tools on your hair can cause damage if not used properly. Make sure to use a heat protectant before using any hot tools and don't overuse them. It's important to take care of your hair and keep it moisturized to prevent damage.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's recommended to wash curly hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle. You can also use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain your curls. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing to prevent frizz and keep your curls intact. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and steps, you can achieve curly hair without a perm for guys. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain your curls and prevent damage. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair. Have fun with your new curly hair!
