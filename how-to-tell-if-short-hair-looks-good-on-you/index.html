How To Tell If Short Hair Looks Good On You

Written by Aprilia Jul 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short hair is a bold and daring style that can suit many different face shapes and hair types. However, before taking the plunge and chopping off your locks, it's important to determine whether short hair will look good on you. In this article, we'll discuss how to tell if short hair is right for you and provide tips on how to rock the look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Tell if Short Hair Looks Good on You
  2. Step by Step

How to Tell if Short Hair Looks Good on You

There are a few factors to consider when determining whether short hair will suit you:

Face Shape

Short hair tends to look good on those with oval, heart-shaped, or square faces. If you have a round face, short hair may not be the best choice as it can make your face look wider. A good rule of thumb is to choose a style that contrasts with your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, choose a style with volume on top and less volume on the sides.

Hair Texture

Short hair looks best on those with straight or wavy hair. If you have curly or kinky hair, short hair may be more difficult to manage and style. Consider your hair's natural texture before choosing a short hairstyle.

Personal Style

Short hair is a bold and daring choice that may not suit everyone's personal style. Consider your wardrobe and makeup preferences before making the cut. If you prefer a more feminine or glamorous look, short hair may not be the best choice.

Step by Step

If you've considered the factors above and have decided that short hair is right for you, here are some steps to take before making the cut:

Consult with a Hairstylist

A hairstylist can help you determine the best short hairstyle for your face shape and hair texture. They can also provide tips on how to style and maintain your new look.

Try a Virtual Makeover

Many online tools allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles virtually. This can give you an idea of how you'll look with short hair before making the cut.

Tips

If you've decided to go for the chop, here are some tips for rocking short hair:

Experiment with Styling

Short hair can be versatile and fun to style. Experiment with different products, tools, and techniques to find a look that suits you.

Accessorize

Add some flair to your short hair with accessories such as headbands, hair clips, and scarves.

Maintain Regular Trims

Short hair requires regular trims to maintain its shape and style. Schedule appointments with your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ

Does short hair look good on everyone?

Short hair can suit many different face shapes and hair types, but it may not be the best choice for everyone's personal style.

What short hairstyles are best for round faces?

Short hairstyles with volume on top and less volume on the sides can be flattering for those with round faces.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular washing and conditioning to keep it healthy and shiny. Use products specifically designed for short hair and avoid over-styling with heat tools.

In conclusion, determining whether short hair looks good on you involves considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Consult with a hairstylist, try a virtual makeover, and experiment with styling and accessories to rock your new look.
