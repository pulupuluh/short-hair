Is Straight Hair Coming Back In Style

Written by Daniel Mar 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

In recent years, curly and wavy hair has been all the rage. But with the constant evolution of fashion and beauty trends, many people are wondering if straight hair is making a comeback. In this article, we will explore the answer to the question, "is straight hair coming back in style?"

Description

Straight hair has been around for centuries and has been a popular hairstyle for both men and women. However, in the last few years, curly hair has been the go-to look for many people. This is due to the rise of natural haircare, which promotes embracing your natural hair texture. But in 2023, it seems that straight hair is making a comeback.

How to Achieve Straight Hair

There are several ways to achieve straight hair. One of the most popular methods is by using a straightening iron or flat iron. This tool uses heat to straighten the hair and can be used on all hair types. Another method is to use a chemical relaxer. This process involves applying a chemical solution to the hair, which breaks down the protein bonds and relaxes the hair strands. However, this method can be damaging to the hair and should only be done by a professional.

Step by Step Guide

Using a Straightening Iron

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray
  3. Divide your hair into sections
  4. Starting at the roots, place the straightening iron on a section of hair and glide it down to the ends
  5. Repeat on all sections of hair
  6. Finish with a hairspray or serum to keep the hair in place

Using a Chemical Relaxer

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine if this is the right method for your hair type
  2. Wash and condition your hair
  3. Apply the relaxer to your hair, following the instructions provided by the stylist
  4. Rinse out the relaxer and apply a neutralizing shampoo
  5. Apply a deep conditioner to the hair and let it sit for 5-10 minutes
  6. Rinse out the conditioner and style as desired

Tips for Maintaining Straight Hair

Maintaining straight hair requires a bit of effort, but the results are worth it. Here are some tips to keep your straight hair looking its best:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat tools
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down the hair
  • Invest in a good quality straightening iron or flat iron
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep the hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using hot water when washing the hair, as it can strip the natural oils

FAQ

Is straight hair easier to maintain than curly hair?

It depends on the person and their hair type. Some people find that straight hair is easier to maintain, while others prefer curly hair. It all comes down to personal preference.

Can I straighten my hair if it is naturally curly?

Yes, you can straighten curly hair using a straightening iron or chemical relaxer. However, it is important to use a heat protectant spray and to avoid overusing heat tools, as this can damage the hair.

How often should I straighten my hair?

It is best to limit the use of heat tools to once or twice a week. Overusing heat tools can damage the hair and cause split ends.

Hair Care

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it is important to take care of your hair. This includes regular washing, conditioning, and deep conditioning treatments. It is also important to avoid overusing heat tools and to use a heat protectant spray when using them. With proper care, you can achieve any hairstyle you desire.

So, is straight hair coming back in style? It seems that way. But, regardless of what is trending, it is important to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
