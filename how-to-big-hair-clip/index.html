How To Big Hair Clip A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to add some volume and style to your hair, big hair clips are a great option. Not only do they add volume and texture, but they can also help keep your hair out of your face. In this article, we'll show you how to big hair clip in a few easy steps.

How to Big Hair Clip

Before we get started, here's what you'll need:

  • A big hair clip
  • Hairspray
  • A comb

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply some hairspray to help hold your style in place. Use a comb to detangle your hair and create a part.

Step 2: Tease Your Hair

Using a comb, tease the crown of your head to create volume. Gently backcomb your hair in small sections, starting at the roots and working your way down.

Step 3: Clip Your Hair

Once you've teased your hair, gather it at the crown of your head. Take your big hair clip and clip it in place. Make sure the clip is secure and tight.

Step 4: Finish Your Style

Use hairspray to hold your style in place. You can also use a curling iron or straightener to add more texture and volume.

Tips for Big Hair Clips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your big hair clip:

  • Choose a clip that matches your hair color
  • Use hairspray to help hold your style in place
  • Tease your hair to create volume and texture
  • Experiment with different clips and styles

FAQ

Q: Can I use a big hair clip on short hair?

A: Yes, you can use a big hair clip on short hair. However, you may need to tease your hair more to create volume.

Q: How do I remove a big hair clip?

A: To remove a big hair clip, simply unclip it and gently comb out your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are a few tips to help keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to big hair clip like a pro. Whether you're looking for a casual or formal style, big hair clips are a versatile accessory that can help you achieve the look you want.
