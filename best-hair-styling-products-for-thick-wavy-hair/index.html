Best Hair Styling Products For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Robby Apr 02, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly, frizzy hair? Do you struggle to find the right hair styling products that work for your thick wavy hair? Look no further, as we have rounded up the best hair styling products for thick wavy hair that will help you achieve gorgeous, manageable locks.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description:

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks voluminous and adds texture to your hair, it can be challenging to manage and style. The right hair styling products can help you tame your mane, define your curls, and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

How To:

The key to achieving the perfect hairstyle for thick wavy hair is to use the right hair styling products. Here are the best hair styling products for thick wavy hair:

1. Hair Oil:

One of the best ways to keep your thick wavy hair healthy and shiny is by using a hair oil. Hair oils can help nourish and moisturize your hair, making it more manageable and easy to style. Some of the best hair oils for thick wavy hair include argan oil, coconut oil, and jojoba oil.

2. Mousse:

Mousse is a great hair styling product for thick wavy hair as it helps define your curls and adds volume to your hair. Apply a small amount of mousse to your damp hair and scrunch your hair to create defined curls. Some of the best mousses for thick wavy hair include OGX Locking + Coconut Curls Styling Mousse and TRESemmé Flawless Curls Defining Mousse.

3. Curl Cream:

Curl cream is another great hair styling product for thick wavy hair. It helps define your curls, reduces frizz, and adds shine to your hair. Apply a small amount of curl cream to your damp hair and scrunch your hair to create defined curls. Some of the best curl creams for thick wavy hair include Ouidad Advanced Climate Control Featherlight Styling Cream and DevaCurl SuperCream Coconut Curl Styler.

4. Hairspray:

Once you have styled your hair, use a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place. Hairspray can help tame flyaways and keep your hair looking smooth and polished. Some of the best hairsprays for thick wavy hair include L'Oreal Paris Elnett Satin Hairspray and Kenra Professional Volume Spray 25.

Step By Step:

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style thick wavy hair:

Step 1:

Start by washing your hair with a nourishing shampoo and conditioner that is designed for thick wavy hair.

Step 2:

Apply a hair oil to your damp hair and comb it through to distribute it evenly.

Step 3:

Apply a small amount of mousse or curl cream to your damp hair and scrunch your hair to create defined curls.

Step 4:

Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat to enhance your curls.

Step 5:

Once your hair is dry, use a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry as it can cause frizz and breakage.

FAQ:

1. Can I use the same hair styling products for thick wavy hair as I do for straight hair?

No, you should use hair styling products that are specifically designed for thick wavy hair as it requires different products to keep it looking healthy and manageable.

2. Can I use hair oil on my hair every day?

Yes, you can use hair oil on your hair every day as long as you use a small amount and avoid applying it to your scalp.

Hair Care:

In addition to using the right hair styling products, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips for taking care of thick wavy hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dyes.

By using the best hair styling products for thick wavy hair and taking care of your hair, you can achieve gorgeous, manageable locks that will turn heads wherever you go.
