Best Hairstyles For Square Face Female

Written by Robby Aug 31, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a square-faced female looking for the perfect hairstyle? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for square face females, along with tips on how to style them and how to take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A square face has a strong jawline and forehead, with straight sides. The best hairstyles for square face females are those that soften the strong angles of the face and draw attention away from the jawline.

How To

The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for a square face is to create balance. This can be achieved by adding volume to the top of the head, and softening the jawline with layers or waves.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some of the best hairstyles for square face females:

Long Layers

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume to the top of your head.
  3. Use a curling iron to add soft waves to the ends of your hair.
  4. Apply a small amount of serum to the ends of your hair to add shine.

Side Swept Bangs

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume to the top of your head.
  3. Create a deep side part.
  4. Sweep your bangs to the side, using a flat iron to add a slight bend.
  5. Apply a small amount of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Textured Bob

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume to the top of your head.
  3. Cut your hair into a bob, with layers around the face.
  4. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a square face:

  • Avoid straight, blunt bangs, as they can accentuate the jawline.
  • Soft waves and curls are great for adding volume and softening the face.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and movement in the hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for square face females:

What hairstyles should square face females avoid?

Square face females should avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can accentuate the jawline. They should also avoid blunt bangs and straight, flat hairstyles.

Do layers work well on square face females?

Yes, layers work well on square face females, as they can soften the strong angles of the face.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it is important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant when styling with heat tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle for your square face, you can create a look that is both flattering and stylish. So go ahead and try out some of these hairstyles – you might just find your new go-to look!
