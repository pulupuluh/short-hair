What Glasses Suit A Narrow Face

Are you someone who has a narrow face and is struggling to find the perfect pair of glasses that suit your face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you on what glasses suit a narrow face and give you some tips on how to choose the perfect glasses for your face shape.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Choose Glasses for a Narrow Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing Glasses for a Narrow Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Glasses Wearers

Description

A narrow face is characterized by a slim forehead, narrow cheekbones, and a pointed chin. If you have a narrow face, it is important to choose glasses that will help balance out your features and make your face appear more proportionate. The right glasses will not only enhance your face shape but also add a touch of style to your overall look.

How to Choose Glasses for a Narrow Face

When choosing glasses for a narrow face, the key is to look for frames that are wider than your cheekbones. This will help balance out your face and make it appear wider. You should also avoid glasses that are too small or too oversized as they can overwhelm your face and make it look even narrower.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to choose glasses for a narrow face:

  1. Measure the width of your face at the widest part, which is typically your cheekbones.
  2. Look for glasses that are wider than your cheekbones to create balance and proportion.
  3. Avoid glasses that are too small or too oversized.
  4. Choose glasses with a wider bridge to help create the illusion of width.
  5. Opt for glasses with rectangular or square frames to add structure to your face.
  6. Avoid glasses with round or oval frames as they can make your face appear even narrower.
  7. Choose glasses with bold, thick frames to add definition to your face.
  8. Avoid glasses with thin frames as they can make your face appear even narrower.
  9. Consider glasses with decorative accents such as studs or embellishments to add a touch of style.
  10. Try on different styles of glasses to see which ones suit your face shape best.

Tips for Choosing Glasses for a Narrow Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing glasses for a narrow face:

  • Choose glasses with a wider bridge to help create the illusion of width.
  • Opt for glasses with rectangular or square frames to add structure to your face.
  • Avoid glasses with round or oval frames as they can make your face appear even narrower.
  • Choose glasses with bold, thick frames to add definition to your face.
  • Avoid glasses with thin frames as they can make your face appear even narrower.
  • Consider glasses with decorative accents such as studs or embellishments to add a touch of style.
  • Try on different styles of glasses to see which ones suit your face shape best.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear oversized glasses if I have a narrow face?

A: While oversized glasses can be trendy, they can overwhelm a narrow face and make it look even narrower. It is best to stick to glasses that are proportionate to your face.

Q: What frame colors should I choose for glasses if I have a narrow face?

A: Neutral colors such as black, brown, and tortoiseshell are great options for glasses if you have a narrow face. These colors are versatile and can be worn with a variety of outfits.

Q: Can I wear glasses with round frames if I have a narrow face?

A: Round frames can make a narrow face look even narrower. It is best to stick to glasses with rectangular or square frames to add structure to your face.

Hair Care Tips for Glasses Wearers

Wearing glasses can affect the way your hair looks, which is why it is important to take care of your hair when wearing glasses. Here are some hair care tips for glasses wearers:

  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it stick to your glasses.
  • Opt for hairstyles that keep your hair away from your face such as a high ponytail or bun.
  • Choose glasses that complement your hairstyle and face shape.
  • Use a headband or hair clips to keep your hair in place.

Now that you know what glasses suit a narrow face, it's time to go out and find the perfect pair of glasses for your face shape. Remember to keep these tips in mind when choosing glasses to help you find the best pair for your features.
