Does Dark Hair Make You Look Younger

Written by Aprilia May 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, we start to look for ways to turn back the clock and look younger. One question that many people ask is, "Does dark hair make you look younger?" In this article, we will explore whether or not dark hair can help you look younger and provide tips on how to care for your dark locks.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Dark hair has been known to make people look younger. This is because as we age, our hair naturally starts to lose pigment, and lighter hair can often make us look older. By contrast, dark hair can make us look more youthful and vibrant.

Additionally, dark hair can also help to create contrast with our skin, making our features stand out more. This can give us a more striking and youthful appearance.

How to

If you are considering going dark to look younger, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a shade that complements your skin tone
  • Consider your natural hair color and how drastic of a change you want to make
  • Be prepared for the maintenance that comes with dark hair (more on that later!)

Step by step

If you decide to go dark, here are the steps you should follow:

  1. Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best shade for you
  2. Prepare your hair by deep conditioning it to ensure that it is healthy and strong
  3. Have your hair dyed by a professional to achieve the desired shade
  4. Maintain your dark hair by using color-safe shampoo and conditioner and avoiding excessive heat styling

Tips

To make your dark hair look its best and help you look younger, here are a few tips:

  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage and split ends
  • Consider using a hair oil to add shine and protect against heat damage

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about whether or not dark hair makes you look younger:

Is it true that dark hair makes you look younger?

Yes, dark hair can make you look younger because it creates contrast with your skin and can help to make your features stand out more.

What is the best shade of dark hair to make you look younger?

The best shade of dark hair to make you look younger depends on your skin tone and natural hair color. A professional hairstylist can help you determine the best shade for you.

What are some tips for maintaining dark hair?

To maintain dark hair, use color-safe shampoo and conditioner, avoid excessive heat styling, and use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Hair Care

If you decide to go dark, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, which can cause damage and breakage
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage and split ends
  • Consider using a hair oil to add shine and protect against heat damage

By following these tips, you can ensure that your dark hair looks its best and helps you look younger and more vibrant.

Conclusion

So, does dark hair make you look younger? The answer is yes, it can. By creating contrast with your skin and making your features stand out more, dark hair can help you look more youthful and vibrant. If you decide to go dark, be sure to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
