What Can I Use To Slick My Hair Back

Written by Luffy Aug 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a sleek and stylish hairstyle that will turn heads? Slicking your hair back is a timeless look that is perfect for any occasion. But what can you use to achieve this look? In this blog post, we will explore the different products and techniques that you can use to slick your hair back.

Description

Slicking your hair back is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. It involves smoothing your hair back from your forehead and temples, creating a sleek and sophisticated look. This hairstyle is perfect for both men and women and can be worn for any occasion.

How to Slick Your Hair Back

If you want to achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo. This will remove any product buildup and leave your hair clean and shiny.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to gently dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp. This will make it easier to apply the styling products.

Step 3: Apply Product

There are a variety of products that you can use to slick your hair back, including pomade, gel, wax, and hairspray. Choose the product that works best for your hair type and desired look. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to distribute it from roots to ends.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair

Use a fine-tooth comb to slick your hair back, starting from the front and working your way to the back. Make sure to create a smooth and even finish.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To ensure that your hair stays in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will also add shine and hold to your hairstyle.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Use a small amount of product to avoid weighing down your hair
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type
  • Don't be afraid to add volume to your hair by teasing it at the roots before slicking it back
  • Use a comb to create a neat and polished finish

FAQ

What products should I use to slick my hair back?

There are a variety of products that you can use to slick your hair back, including pomade, gel, wax, and hairspray. Choose the product that works best for your hair type and desired look.

Can I slick my hair back if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can slick your hair back if you have curly hair. However, you may need to use more product to keep your curls in place.

How often should I wash my hair if I slick it back?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days if you slick it back regularly. This will prevent product buildup and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Hair Care for Slicked-Back Hair

To keep your slicked-back hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo to remove product buildup
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and hydrated
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools

Slicking your hair back is a great way to achieve a classic and sophisticated look. By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve a sleek and polished hairstyle that will turn heads.
