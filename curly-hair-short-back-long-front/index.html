Curly Hair Short Back Long Front A Trendy Hairstyle For 2023

Written by Luffy May 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curls have always been a symbol of beauty and uniqueness. Women with curly hair often struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that suits their hair type. However, in recent years, the curly hair short back long front has become a popular trend.

Description

The curly hair short back long front is a hairstyle in which the back of the hair is cut short while the front is kept long. This style can be achieved on all hair types, but it's particularly flattering on curly hair. The length of the front hair can vary from shoulder-length to waist-length, depending on your preference.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're considering trying out the curly hair short back long front style, here are the steps to achieve it:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair to make sure it's clean and well-moisturized. This will help to prevent tangling and breakage during the styling process.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots to avoid pulling and breaking your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts: the back and the front. Clip the back section out of the way, so you can work on the front.

Step 4: Cut the Back

Using scissors, cut the back section of your hair to your desired length. You can go as short as a pixie cut or leave it slightly longer.

Step 5: Cut the Front

Unclip the front section of your hair and start cutting it in layers. The length of the layers should gradually increase as you move towards the front of your hair. This will create the long front effect.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

You can style your curly hair short back long front in various ways. You can leave it natural, use a diffuser to enhance your curls, or straighten the front for a sleek look.

Tips to Maintain the Look

Here are some tips to maintain your curly hair short back long front:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid dryness and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Is the curly hair short back long front suitable for all face shapes?

    2. Yes, this style can be customized to suit all face shapes. You can adjust the length of the front hair to accentuate your features.

  2. Can I achieve this style on relaxed hair?

    3. Yes, you can achieve this style on relaxed hair. However, it's important to note that chemically-treated hair is more prone to breakage, so you need to take extra care of your hair.

  3. What styling products should I use for this style?

    4. You can use a variety of styling products, such as mousse, gel, or curl cream to enhance your curls. You can also use a heat protectant if you're using heat styling tools.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to maintain its health and beauty. Here are some tips to take care of your curly hair:

  • Wash your hair less frequently to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, the curly hair short back long front is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your preferences. With proper care and styling, you can rock this look with confidence and style.
