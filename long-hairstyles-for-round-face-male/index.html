Long Hairstyles For Round Face Male Tips And Ideas

Written by Bardi Mar 02, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a round face, you know how challenging it can be to find the right hairstyle that flatters your features. Long hair can be a great option for round-faced men to create an elongated appearance. In this article, we’ll cover some tips and ideas for long hairstyles for round face male to help you look your best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose a Long Hairstyle for Round Face Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Round Face Male
  • Tips for Maintaining Long Hair for Round Face Male
  • FAQs about Long Hairstyles for Round Face Male
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair for Round Face Male

How to Choose a Long Hairstyle for Round Face Male

When choosing a long hairstyle for round face male, the goal is to create an illusion of length while avoiding anything that adds width to the face. Here are some things to keep in mind:

Consider Layers

Layered hairstyles can add volume and texture to your hair, making it appear longer and more structured. The layers should be long and should always start below the chin to avoid adding width to the face.

Avoid Center Parting

A center parting can create symmetry but can also make the face appear rounder. Instead, opt for a side parting or a messy side-swept look to create a more angular appearance.

Choose Wisely

When selecting a long hairstyle, choose a style that complements your face shape. A great way to do this is by looking at celebrities with similar features and hairstyles. Consider the length, texture, and whether it complements your facial features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Round Face Male

Once you’ve chosen the perfect hairstyle, it’s time to style it. Here’s a step-by-step guide to achieve a simple yet stylish look:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Towel-dry your hair and comb it to remove tangles.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as mousse or hair gel. This will help add volume and texture to your hair, making it appear longer and more structured.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a hairdryer to blow-dry your hair. Use a round brush to create volume and texture, focusing on the crown of your head. Make sure to dry your hair completely.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair to your desired look. Add a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair for Round Face Male

Maintaining long hair requires a bit of effort but is worth it in the long run. Here are some tips to help you maintain your long hair:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Visit a stylist every six to eight weeks for a trim.

Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Invest in a quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, or use a heat protectant spray if you must use them.

FAQs about Long Hairstyles for Round Face Male

Q: Can a round face pull off long hair?

A: Yes, absolutely! Long hair can be a great option for round-faced men, as long as the hairstyle is chosen wisely and styled correctly.

Q: What type of hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or a center parting. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add length and structure to the hair.

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair for Round Face Male

Invest in a Quality Brush

Invest in a quality brush that suits your hair type. A boar bristle brush can help distribute natural oils throughout your hair, keeping it healthy and shiny.

Avoid Over-washing

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days.

Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet can help keep your hair healthy and strong. Make sure to eat plenty of protein, fruits, and vegetables.

With these tips and ideas for long hairstyles for round face male, you can create a look that flatters your features and makes you feel confident. Remember to choose a style that complements your face shape and to maintain your hair with regular trims and a healthy hair care routine.
