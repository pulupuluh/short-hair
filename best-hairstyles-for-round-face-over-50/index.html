Best Hairstyles For Round Face Over 50 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're over 50 and have a round face, you may be wondering what hairstyles would flatter your features. Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll go over the best hairstyles for round faces over 50, including how to style them, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for round faces over 50, it's essential to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Line up a ruler against your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Measure the width of each section to determine which parts are the widest.
  4. Compare your measurements to the descriptions of different face shapes to determine which one you have.

Now that you know your face shape let's take a look at the best hairstyles for round faces over 50.

Best Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 50

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is an excellent option for those with round faces as it helps elongate the face. A short pixie cut also draws attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

2. Bob Cut

A classic bob cut is another great option for those with round faces. Try a chin-length bob that grazes your collarbone to create the illusion of length.

3. Layered Cut

A layered cut with long layers is another excellent option for those with round faces. Layers add volume and texture to your hair, making it more flattering.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for those with round faces as they help create the illusion of length. Try a long, wispy bang that grazes your eyebrows.

5. Soft Waves

Soft waves are another great option for those with round faces. Waves create the illusion of texture and volume, making your hair look more flattering.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Now that you know the best hairstyles for round faces over 50 let's take a look at some tips for styling your hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy products that weigh down your hair.
  • Try blow-drying your hair with a round brush to create volume and texture.
  • Use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear long hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear long hair if you have a round face. Just make sure to add layers and texture to your hair to create the illusion of length.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

A: No, you don't have to avoid bangs if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose wispy, side-swept bangs to create the illusion of length.

Hair Care Advice

Finally, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair as it can damage your hair.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for round faces over 50 and how to style them, it's time to experiment with different looks and find what works best for you. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
