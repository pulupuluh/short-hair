What Is A Good Hair Cut For A Round Face

Written by Christine Aug 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, you might be wondering what kind of haircut would suit you best. The good news is, there are plenty of options out there! In this article, we'll go over some of the best haircuts for round faces, as well as tips for caring for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine the Shape of Your Face
  • Haircuts for Round Faces
  • Tips for Caring for Your Hair
  • FAQ

How to Determine the Shape of Your Face

Before we get into haircuts, it's important to determine the shape of your face. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Look at the shape of your face and determine if it's round, oval, square, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped. If you're not sure, you can always ask your hairstylist for help.

Haircuts for Round Faces

Now that you know you have a round face, let's talk about the best haircuts for you. The goal with a round face is to add some angles and length to your face, so you want to avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your head. Here are some options:

Long Layers

Long layers can add some dimension to your hair and create the illusion of length in your face. Avoid blunt cuts and instead opt for layers that start at your chin or below.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to elongate your face and make it look slimmer. Just make sure the bangs are long enough to sweep to the side and not too short.

A-Line Bob

An A-line bob can help to create angles in your face and add length. Make sure the longest part of the bob is at your chin or below.

Long Pixie Cut

A long pixie cut can be a great option for a round face. The longer layers can help to create the illusion of length in your face, while the shorter layers add some texture and dimension.

Tips for Caring for Your Hair

Now that you have a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and looking its best. For example, if you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot tools. Also, try to limit the amount of heat you use on your hair.

Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. How often you need a trim will depend on your hair type and how quickly it grows, but aim for every 6-8 weeks.

FAQ

What other haircuts are good for a round face?

In addition to the haircuts mentioned above, you might also consider a lob (long bob), a shag haircut, or a layered bob.

Can I pull off short hair with a round face?

Yes! A short haircut can be a great option for a round face, as long as it's done in the right way. Talk to your hairstylist about what kind of short haircut would work best for your face shape.

Do I need to style my hair every day?

Not necessarily. The haircuts mentioned above are all low-maintenance and can be styled in a variety of ways. Talk to your hairstylist about what kind of styling routine would work best for you.

In conclusion, there are plenty of great haircuts for round faces. The key is to add some angles and length to your face, and avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your head. With the right haircut and some good hair care habits, you can have healthy, beautiful hair that flatters your face shape.
