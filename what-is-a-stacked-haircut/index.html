What Is A Stacked Haircut

Written by Robby Jul 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut that can give you a fresh look? If yes, then a stacked haircut might be the perfect choice for you. It is a versatile haircut that can suit all hair types and face shapes. In this article, we will discuss what is a stacked haircut, how to get it, and some tips and tricks to maintain it.

What is a Stacked Haircut?

A stacked haircut is a popular haircut style that features graduated layers at the back. The layers are cut in such a way that they appear to be stacked on top of each other, creating a voluminous and layered effect. The length of the layers can vary, but they are usually shorter at the back and longer at the front. This haircut is also known as a graduated bob or an inverted bob.

Who can get a Stacked Haircut?

A stacked haircut can flatter all face shapes and hair types. It is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your preferences. However, it is best suited for people with medium to thick hair as it can add volume and texture to the hair. If you have thin hair, you can still get a stacked haircut, but you might have to add some layers to create the desired effect.

How to Get a Stacked Haircut?

If you want to get a stacked haircut, it is best to visit a professional hairstylist. They can help you choose the right length and style that will suit your face shape and hair type. Here are the steps involved in getting a stacked haircut:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up.
  3. Start cutting the back section of your hair, gradually cutting shorter layers as you move towards the nape of your neck.
  4. Once you have finished cutting the back section, move on to the sides and front of your hair.
  5. Create a smooth and seamless transition between the different layers.
  6. Style your hair using a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume and texture.

Tips for Maintaining a Stacked Haircut

Once you have got a stacked haircut, it is essential to maintain it regularly to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips to help you maintain your stacked haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage the hair and make it look dull and lifeless.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and smoothness to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles and accessories to create a unique and personalized look.

FAQs

1. How long does a stacked haircut last?

A stacked haircut can last for about six to eight weeks. However, it depends on your hair growth and how well you maintain the haircut.

2. Can I get a stacked haircut with short hair?

Yes, you can get a stacked haircut with short hair, but the layers will be shorter and less noticeable.

3. Can I style my stacked haircut in different ways?

Yes, you can style your stacked haircut in different ways, such as straight, wavy, curly, or messy. You can also add accessories like headbands, clips, or scarves to create a unique look.

Hair Care for Stacked Haircut

A stacked haircut requires regular maintenance and care to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips specifically for a stacked haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage the cuticles and make your hair look frizzy and dull.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Apply a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair as it can cause breakage and split ends.

In conclusion, a stacked haircut is a trendy and versatile haircut that can give you a fresh and stylish look. It is best suited for people with medium to thick hair, and it can flatter all face shapes. If you are looking for a new haircut, why not try a stacked haircut and experiment with different styles and accessories to create a personalized look.
