How To Know Your Hair Texture Male A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Apr 07, 2023 · 6 min read

Knowing your hair texture is essential to determine the right products and styling techniques that work best for your hair. Many men struggle to identify their hair texture, which can lead to problems like dryness, breakage, and hair loss. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to know your hair texture male.

How to Know Your Hair Texture Male

Hair texture refers to the thickness and diameter of individual hair strands. There are three types of hair texture:

  • Straight – hair strands that are smooth and straight.
  • Wavy – hair strands that have a slight wave or curl.
  • Curly – hair strands that are tightly curled or coiled.

Knowing your hair texture can help you choose the right products and styling techniques suitable for your hair. Here’s how you can determine your hair texture:

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to determine your hair texture:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Avoid using any hair styling products before the test.
  3. Allow your hair to air dry or use a towel to dry it gently.
  4. Using a comb or your fingers, separate a small section of hair from the crown of your head.
  5. Hold the hair strand between your thumb and index finger.
  6. Run your fingers down the hair strand from the root to the tip.
  7. Observe the hair strand to determine its texture:
  • Straight hair – hair strands that are smooth and straight with no visible bends or waves.
  • Wavy hair – hair strands that have a slight wave or curl that forms an S-shape.
  • Curly hair – hair strands that have a tight curl or coil that forms a spiral or ringlet shape.

Tips for Identifying Your Hair Texture

Here are some tips to help you identify your hair texture:

  • Look at old pictures of yourself with your natural hair.
  • Ask your hairstylist about your hair texture during your next visit.
  • Pay attention to how your hair reacts to humidity and weather changes.
  • Check the size of your hair follicles – larger follicles indicate thicker hair strands.
  • Take a closer look at your hair strands under a magnifying glass.

FAQs

1. Does hair texture change with age?

Yes, hair texture can change with age due to hormonal changes, stress, and other factors.

2. Can hair texture be genetic?

Yes, hair texture is mostly determined by genetics.

3. Can hair texture be changed?

While you cannot change your hair texture permanently, you can use different styling techniques and products to alter its appearance temporarily.

Hair Care Based on Hair Texture

Once you have determined your hair texture, you can choose the right products and styling techniques that work best for your hair. Here are some tips for hair care based on your hair texture:

  • Straight hair – use lightweight products that won’t weigh your hair down, and avoid heavy oils and butters.
  • Wavy hair – use a leave-in conditioner to define your waves, and avoid using a brush to prevent frizz.
  • Curly hair – use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a curl-defining cream or gel to enhance your curls.

Knowing your hair texture is crucial for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. By following the tips and steps outlined in this article, you can determine your hair texture and choose the right products and styling techniques that work best for you.
