What Kind Of Hair Attracts Guys

Written by Luffy May 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to attracting guys, many women wonder what kind of hair is most appealing. While there is no one right answer, there are certain hair types and styles that tend to catch a guy's eye. In this article, we'll explore what kind of hair attracts guys and offer some tips for achieving the look you desire.

Natural Hair

Many guys are attracted to women who embrace their natural hair texture, whether it's curly, wavy, or straight. Natural hair can be low-maintenance and exudes confidence and authenticity. If you're looking to enhance your natural hair, consider using products that cater to your hair type, such as a curl cream for curly hair or a sea salt spray for wavy hair.

Long Hair

Long hair has been a symbol of femininity for centuries, and many guys find it alluring. If you're looking to grow out your hair, be patient and take good care of it by regular trims and deep conditioning treatments.

Wavy Hair

Wavy hair has a relaxed, beachy vibe that many guys find attractive. To enhance your waves, try using a diffuser when blow-drying or scrunching your hair with a curl-enhancing product.

Blonde Hair

Blonde hair has long been associated with youthfulness and beauty, and many guys gravitate towards women with blonde locks. If you're thinking of going blonde, make sure to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and keep your hair healthy and hydrated with regular deep conditioning treatments.

Red Hair

Red hair is a rare and striking hair color that can turn heads. If you're thinking of going red, consider choosing a shade that flatters your skin tone and use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your vibrant color.

Curly Hair

Curly hair can be playful and flirtatious, and many guys find it captivating. To enhance your curls, try using a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream and avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.

Updos

While many men love long, flowing locks, updos can be just as attractive. A sleek chignon or messy bun can be both elegant and alluring, and can show off your facial features.

Straight Hair

Straight hair can be sleek and sophisticated, and many guys are drawn to its simplicity. To achieve straight hair, use a flat iron or blow-dry your hair with a round brush, and use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Highlights

Adding highlights to your hair can add dimension and depth, and can be an attractive feature for many guys. Consider adding highlights that complement your skin tone and hair color, and avoid over-processing your hair by spacing out your salon appointments.

Hair Care Tips

Whether you're looking to enhance your natural hair or try a new style, taking good care of your hair is essential. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, attractive hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too frequently to prevent heat damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

Q: What kind of hair do guys like?

A: There is no one right answer to this question, as everyone has different preferences. However, many guys are attracted to natural hair, long hair, and hair that is well-maintained and healthy.

Q: Does hair color matter to guys?

A: Again, this can vary depending on the person. However, many guys find blonde hair and red hair especially attractive.

Conclusion

While there is no one "right" kind of hair that attracts guys, there are certain styles and textures that tend to be more appealing. Whether you're looking to enhance your natural hair or try a new color or style, remember to take good care of your locks to keep them healthy and attractive.
