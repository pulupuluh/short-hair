What Hair Suits A Heart Shaped Face

Written by Robby Aug 14, 2023 · 5 min read

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones, and a narrow chin. If you have this face shape, you can enhance your natural beauty by choosing the right hairstyle that complements your features.

There are several haircuts and styles that suit a heart shaped face. The aim is to balance the wide forehead and cheekbones with a narrower chin. In this article, we’ll discuss some of the best hairstyles for a heart shaped face.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a heart shaped face, the key is to create balance. You want to add volume to the lower part of your face and draw attention away from your forehead. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds width to your chin area
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your forehead
  • Consider side-swept bangs to draw attention to your eyes
  • Opt for layers to add volume to the lower part of your face

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of the best hairstyles for a heart shaped face:

Long Layers

Step 1: Get your hair cut into long layers

Step 2: Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the lower part of your face

Step 3: Use a curling iron to create loose waves

Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

Step 1: Get a bob haircut that hits just below your chin

Step 2: Add side-swept bangs that hit just above your eyebrows

Step 3: Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing a hairstyle for a heart shaped face:

  • Avoid blunt bangs
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your forehead
  • Consider a deep side part to create balance
  • Try a messy updo for a special occasion

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a heart shaped face?

Avoid blunt bangs and hairstyles that add volume to your forehead, such as a high ponytail or slicked back hair.

What kind of bangs suit a heart shaped face?

Side-swept bangs that hit just above your eyebrows can draw attention to your eyes and balance your features.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Don’t wash your hair every day, as it can strip your hair of natural oils
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident with your heart shaped face.
