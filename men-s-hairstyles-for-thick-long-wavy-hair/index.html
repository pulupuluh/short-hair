Men s Hairstyles For Thick Long Wavy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Aug 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you a man with thick, long, wavy hair? Do you struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your hair type? Look no further! In this blog post, we will explore the best men's hairstyles for thick long wavy hair.

Description

Thick, long, wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it can create a unique and attractive look. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. Men with this hair type often struggle to find hairstyles that suit them. This guide aims to provide a range of options for men with thick long wavy hair.

How To

When it comes to styling thick long wavy hair, it's important to consider the natural texture and volume of the hair. Here are some steps to achieve some of the best hairstyles for this hair type:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition

The first step in any hair styling routine is to shampoo and condition your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for thick hair to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess moisture. Avoid using a blow dryer as this can cause damage to your hair.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product such as a hair wax or pomade to your hair. This will help to define your waves and hold your hairstyle in place.

Step 4: Style

Style your hair according to the hairstyle you have chosen. Use a comb or your fingers to create the desired shape and texture.

Men's Hairstyles for Thick Long Wavy Hair

Now that you know how to style your hair, it's time to explore some of the best men's hairstyles for thick long wavy hair:

1. The Messy Quiff

The messy quiff is a popular and versatile hairstyle for men with thick long wavy hair. This hairstyle involves a messy, textured top with shorter sides and back. Use a hair wax or pomade to create the messy texture.

2. The Surfer Hair

The surfer hair is a classic hairstyle for men with thick long wavy hair. This hairstyle involves a longer top with shorter sides and back. Use a sea salt spray to add texture and create the wavy look.

3. The Long Wavy Hair

If you have long thick wavy hair, embrace it! Keep your hair long and natural. Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain and style your thick long wavy hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid tugging and damaging your hair.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons as these can cause damage to your hair. Instead, embrace your natural waves.

3. Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Look for a mask that is specifically designed for thick hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's hairstyles for thick long wavy hair:

1. Can I straighten my thick long wavy hair?

Yes, you can straighten your hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

2. What is the best styling product for thick long wavy hair?

The best styling product for thick long wavy hair depends on the hairstyle you want to achieve. Hair wax, pomade, and sea salt spray are all great options.

3. How often should I wash my thick long wavy hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your thick long wavy hair to keep it healthy and voluminous. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Hair Oil

Use a hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz. Look for an oil that is specifically designed for thick hair.

2. Trim Regularly

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Use a hat or hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

Now that you have all the information you need, go ahead and try out some of these men's hairstyles for thick long wavy hair!
