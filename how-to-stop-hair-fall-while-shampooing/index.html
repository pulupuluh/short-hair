How To Stop Hair Fall While Shampooing

Written by Petter Feb 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Do you often notice hair strands in your shower drain after shampooing? Hair fall is a common problem faced by both men and women. While hair fall can occur due to various reasons, shampooing can aggravate it. But don't worry, there are ways to stop hair fall while shampooing. In this article, we will discuss effective tips and tricks to prevent hair fall while shampooing.

How to Stop Hair Fall While Shampooing

Before we dive into the tips, let's first understand why hair fall occurs while shampooing. Shampooing can strip off natural oils and moisture from your hair, making it dry and prone to breakage. Moreover, the harsh chemicals present in shampoos can damage your hair and scalp, leading to hair fall. Here's how you can prevent it:

1. Choose the Right Shampoo

The first step to prevent hair fall while shampooing is to choose the right shampoo. Look for shampoos that are gentle and free from sulfates, parabens, and other harsh chemicals. Sulfates can strip off natural oils from your hair, making it dry and brittle, while parabens can damage your hair and scalp. Choose shampoos that are enriched with natural ingredients like aloe vera, tea tree oil, and coconut oil that nourish your hair and promote hair growth.

2. Don't Over-Shampoo

Over-shampooing can also lead to hair fall. Shampooing your hair every day can strip off natural oils and make your hair dry and brittle. Instead, shampoo your hair two to three times a week. If you have oily hair, you can use a dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil and keep your hair fresh.

3. Massage Your Scalp

While shampooing, massage your scalp gently with your fingertips for a few minutes. Massaging your scalp can stimulate blood circulation and promote hair growth. It also helps to distribute the shampoo evenly and prevents tangles and breakage.

4. Rinse Your Hair Properly

Rinsing your hair properly after shampooing is crucial to prevent hair fall. Make sure to rinse your hair with lukewarm water to remove all the shampoo residue. Leftover shampoo can clog your hair follicles and cause hair fall. Also, avoid rubbing your hair with a towel to dry it. Instead, gently pat your hair with a soft towel or an old t-shirt to remove excess water.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to prevent hair fall while shampooing:

  1. Wet your hair with lukewarm water.
  2. Take a small amount of shampoo and apply it to your scalp.
  3. Massage your scalp gently with your fingertips for a few minutes.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water.
  5. Apply conditioner to the ends of your hair.
  6. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes and then rinse it off with cool water.
  7. Gently pat your hair with a soft towel to remove excess water.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall While Shampooing

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall while shampooing:

  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Brush your hair before shampooing to prevent tangles and knots.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair after shampooing.
  • Avoid using hair styling tools like hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

FAQs

1. Can shampooing too much cause hair loss?

Yes, over-shampooing can strip off natural oils from your hair, making it dry and brittle. This can lead to hair breakage and hair fall.

2. Which shampoo is best for preventing hair fall?

Choose shampoos that are gentle and free from sulfates, parabens, and other harsh chemicals. Look for shampoos that are enriched with natural ingredients like aloe vera, tea tree oil, and coconut oil that nourish your hair and promote hair growth.

3. Can massaging your scalp prevent hair fall?

Yes, massaging your scalp can stimulate blood circulation and promote hair growth. It also helps to distribute the shampoo evenly and prevents tangles and breakage.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some additional hair care tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Avoid using hair styling tools like hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles like braids, ponytails, and buns that can pull your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution by wearing a hat or a scarf.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent hair fall.

By following these tips and tricks, you can prevent hair fall while shampooing and promote healthy hair growth. Remember, a little care goes a long way in maintaining healthy and beautiful hair!
