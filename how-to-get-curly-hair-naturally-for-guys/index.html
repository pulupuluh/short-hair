How To Get Curly Hair Naturally For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curly hair is a unique feature that can make guys stand out from the crowd. However, not all men are born with natural curls. Luckily, there are a few ways to get curly hair naturally without using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools. In this guide, we'll explore how to get curly hair naturally for guys, including step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Curly Hair Naturally for Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Instructions
  3. Tips for Getting Curly Hair Naturally
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Get Curly Hair Naturally for Guys

If you're looking to get curly hair naturally, there are a few methods you can try:

  • Using a hair perm kit
  • Using hair rollers or rods
  • Using a twist-out method
  • Using a braid-out method
  • Using a flexi-rod method

Each method has its pros and cons, and the results may vary depending on your hair texture and length. It's essential to choose a method that works best for your hair type and fits your lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get curly hair naturally using the twist-out method:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner and a styling product, such as a curl defining cream or gel, to your hair. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  4. Divide your hair into small sections and twist each section tightly from the roots to the ends.
  5. Let your hair air-dry or sit under a hooded dryer until your hair is completely dry.
  6. Once your hair is dry, unravel each twist and separate the curls gently with your fingers.
  7. Apply a hair oil or serum to add shine and definition to your curls.

Remember to be gentle with your hair when twisting and separating the curls to avoid causing damage or frizz.

Tips for Getting Curly Hair Naturally

Here are some tips to help you get curly hair naturally:

  • Choose a method that works best for your hair type and length.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner and a styling product to your hair to define your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and blow dryers, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add shine and moisture to your curls.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping on a satin or silk pillowcase and using a hair bonnet or scarf.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting curly hair naturally for guys:

Q: Can I get curly hair naturally if I have straight hair?

A: Yes, you can get curly hair naturally even if you have straight hair. You can use the methods mentioned earlier, such as twist-outs or flexi-rod sets, to create curls in your hair.

Q: Will getting curly hair naturally damage my hair?

A: Getting curly hair naturally using gentle methods, such as twist-outs or braid-outs, should not damage your hair. However, using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools can cause damage and breakage.

Q: How long will my natural curls last?

A: The duration of your natural curls depends on your hair texture, the method used, and how well you maintain your curls. On average, natural curls can last up to a week or more with proper care.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for guys with curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner and a styling product to define your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Detangle your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to avoid causing breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and blow dryers, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping on a satin or silk pillowcase and using a hair bonnet or scarf.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and methods, you can get curly hair naturally and maintain healthy, defined curls without using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools. Experiment with different methods and products to find what works best for your hair type and lifestyle. Remember, healthy hair is happy hair!
