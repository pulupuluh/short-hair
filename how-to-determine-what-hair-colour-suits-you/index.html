How To Determine What Hair Colour Suits You

Written by William Mar 28, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour and looking to switch things up? Choosing a new hair colour can be a daunting task, but it doesn’t have to be. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to determine what hair colour suits you best.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine What Hair Colour Suits You
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Determine What Hair Colour Suits You

The first step to determining what hair colour suits you is to identify your skin tone. There are three main skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones, cool skin tones have pink or bluish undertones, and neutral skin tones have a mix of both.

Once you have identified your skin tone, consider your natural hair colour. If you have warm undertones, you may look best with shades of red, copper, or golden blonde. If you have cool undertones, ash blonde, platinum blonde, or cool brown shades may be the most flattering. For neutral undertones, you can pull off a variety of colours, but it’s best to stick with a natural shade that complements your skin tone.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide to determining what hair colour suits you:

  1. Identify your skin tone: warm, cool, or neutral
  2. Consider your natural hair colour
  3. Choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and natural hair colour
  4. Consult with a professional stylist before making any drastic changes

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hair colour:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much upkeep the new colour will require
  • Think about the colours that complement your wardrobe
  • Look at pictures of celebrities or people with similar skin tones to see what colours look good on them
  • Try a temporary hair colour before committing to a permanent change

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about determining what hair colour suits you:

What hair colour looks good on warm skin tones?

Shades of red, copper, and golden blonde are the most flattering for warm skin tones.

What hair colour looks good on cool skin tones?

Ash blonde, platinum blonde, and cool brown shades are the most flattering for cool skin tones.

Can neutral skin tones pull off any hair colour?

Neutral skin tones can pull off a variety of colours, but it’s best to stick with a natural shade that complements your skin tone.

Hair Care Tips

After choosing a new hair colour, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain the colour and keep it healthy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Avoid washing your hair too often to prevent stripping the colour

By following these tips and tricks, you can determine what hair colour suits you and make a confident decision on your next hair colour change. Remember to consult with a professional stylist before making any drastic changes and take care of your hair to maintain the colour and keep it healthy.
