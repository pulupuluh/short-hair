How To Keep Hair Slicked Back All Day A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 25, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking to achieve a sleek and polished look, there's nothing quite like a slicked back hairstyle. But keeping your hair in place all day can be a challenge, especially if you have thick or unruly hair. In this blog post, we'll show you how to keep hair slicked back all day, so you can look and feel your best from morning to night.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Keeping Hair Slicked Back

  5. Hair Care Tips for Slicked Back Hair

How to Keep Hair Slicked Back All Day

To keep your hair slicked back all day, you'll need the right products and techniques. Here are some tips to help you achieve that polished look:

1. Use a Strong Hold Product

The key to keeping your hair in place is using a strong hold product. Look for a gel, wax, or pomade that is designed for slicked back hairstyles. These products will provide the hold you need without making your hair look greasy or weighed down.

2. Apply Product to Damp Hair

For best results, apply your product to damp hair. This will help the product distribute evenly and ensure that your hair stays in place all day. Avoid applying too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy or crunchy.

3. Use a Fine-Tooth Comb

To create a sleek and polished look, use a fine-tooth comb to brush your hair back. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down, smoothing your hair as you go. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction you want it to stay.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Once you've combed your hair back, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help set your style and keep your hair in place all day. Look for a hairspray that is designed for strong hold, but won't leave your hair feeling crunchy or stiff.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to try slicked back hair for yourself? Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type, and make sure to rinse thoroughly. Towel dry your hair until it is damp, but not soaking wet.

Step 2: Apply Your Product

Next, apply your product to your hair. Start with a small amount and work your way up as needed. Rub the product between your palms to warm it up, then apply it evenly to your hair.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Back

Use a fine-tooth comb to comb your hair back. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down, smoothing your hair as you go. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction you want it to stay.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is combed back, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Hold the can about 12 inches away from your head and spray evenly over your hair. Allow the hairspray to dry before touching your hair.

Tips for Keeping Hair Slicked Back

Here are some additional tips to help you keep your hair slicked back all day:

1. Don't Touch Your Hair

Once you've styled your hair, avoid touching it as much as possible. The oils from your fingers can make your hair look greasy and cause it to lose its hold.

2. Use a Satin Pillowcase

If you're trying to keep your hair slicked back overnight, consider using a satin pillowcase. Satin is gentler on your hair than cotton, which can cause friction and frizz.

3. Avoid Windy Weather

Windy weather can wreak havoc on a slicked back hairstyle. If you're going to be outside on a windy day, consider wearing a hat or using a scarf to protect your hair.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently than someone with dry hair. As a general rule, aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I use hairspray on wet hair?

A: No, you should always apply hairspray to dry hair. Applying hairspray to wet hair can cause it to become crunchy and weighed down.

Hair Care Tips for Slicked Back Hair

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

1. Use a Clarifying Shampoo

If you use a lot of styling products in your hair, consider using a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup. This will help keep your hair looking and feeling clean.

2. Deep Condition Regularly

To keep your hair moisturized and healthy, deep condition your hair once a week. Look for a conditioner that is designed for your hair type and leave it on for 5-10 minutes before rinsing.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

If you use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. You should also avoid using high heat settings and limit your use of heat styling tools as much as possible.

With these tips and techniques, you can keep your hair slicked back all day and achieve a polished and sophisticated look. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
