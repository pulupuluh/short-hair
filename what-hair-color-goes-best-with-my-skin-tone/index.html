What Hair Color Goes Best With My Skin Tone A Guide For 2023

Written by Eveline Sep 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hair color or try something new, it's important to consider what will look best with your skin tone. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to figure out where to start. This guide will walk you through how to determine your skin tone and what hair colors will complement it best.

  • Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones
  • Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones
  • Hair Colors for Neutral Skin Tones

  • FAQ

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in figuring out what hair color will look best on you is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone is the natural color of your skin, which is affected by genetics, sun exposure, and other factors. There are three main categories of skin tone: warm, cool, and neutral.

To determine your skin tone, you can look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear greenish, you likely have warm undertones. If they appear bluish, you likely have cool undertones. If you can't tell if they're green or blue, you likely have neutral undertones.

Another way to determine your skin tone is to consider how you look in different colors. If you look best in warm colors like red, orange, and yellow, you likely have warm undertones. If you look best in cool colors like blue, green, and purple, you likely have cool undertones. If you look good in both, you likely have neutral undertones.

Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones

If you have warm undertones, you'll look best in hair colors that have warm undertones as well. Some good options include:

  • Rich browns
  • Caramel
  • Honey
  • Golden blonde
  • Copper
  • Auburn

These colors will complement your skin tone and bring out the warmth in your complexion.

Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones

If you have cool undertones, you'll look best in hair colors with cool undertones. Some good options include:

  • Platinum blonde
  • Ash blonde
  • Ash brown
  • Jet black
  • Blue-black
  • Burgundy

These colors will complement your skin tone and bring out the coolness in your complexion.

Hair Colors for Neutral Skin Tones

If you have neutral undertones, you're lucky because you can pull off both warm and cool hair colors. Some good options include:

  • Golden blonde
  • Caramel
  • Ash brown
  • Burgundy
  • Chocolate brown
  • Jet black

These colors will complement your skin tone without overpowering it.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

When choosing a hair color, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your natural hair color. If you're looking to make a drastic change, you may need to bleach your hair first, which can be damaging.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you don't have a lot of time or money to spend on touch-ups, choose a color that will grow out well.
  • Consult with a professional. A hairstylist can help you determine the best color for your skin tone and give you advice on maintenance.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Once you've chosen a hair color, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve the color.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the color. Aim for every other day or every few days.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can also damage the color. If you do use them, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair a color that doesn't complement my skin tone?

A: Of course! Ultimately, it's up to you to decide what hair color you want. If you love a certain color, go for it!

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: This depends on the color and how fast your hair grows. Some colors will need touch-ups every 4-6 weeks, while others can go longer.

Q: Will coloring my hair damage it?

A: Coloring your hair can be damaging, especially if you bleach it. However, there are ways to minimize damage, like using a deep conditioner and avoiding heat styling tools.

Choosing the right hair color for your skin tone can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look. By following these tips and taking care of your colored hair, you can enjoy your new color for weeks to come.
