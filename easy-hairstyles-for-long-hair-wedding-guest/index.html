Easy Hairstyles For Long Hair Wedding Guest

Written by Aprilia Jul 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you attending a wedding as a guest and have long hair? You're in luck! Long hair offers endless styling options that you can try out. However, with so many options, it can be overwhelming to choose a hairstyle that complements your dress and the occasion. In this post, we'll share some easy hairstyles for long hair wedding guest that you can do yourself without the need for a hairstylist.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's talk about the basics. When styling your hair for a wedding, it's essential to consider the dress code and the theme of the wedding. You don't want to show up with an edgy hairstyle when the wedding has a traditional theme.

Another crucial factor to consider is the weather. If the wedding is in the summer, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that keeps your hair off your neck to avoid sweating. On the other hand, if the wedding is in the winter, you can opt for a hairstyle that keeps your ears warm.

Step by Step

Now, let's get to the hairstyles. Here are some easy hairstyles for long hair wedding guest:

Braided Updo

Step 1: Start by parting your hair in the middle.

Step 2: Braid both sides of your hair and secure the ends with an elastic band.

Step 3: Take one braid and wrap it around the back of your head, securing it with bobby pins.

Step 4: Repeat step 3 with the other braid, tucking the ends under the first braid.

Step 5: Finish the look with hairspray.

Half-Up Half-Down Curls

Step 1: Curl your hair using a curling iron.

Step 2: Take a section of hair from each side of your head and bring them to the back.

Step 3: Secure the sections with a clear elastic band.

Step 4: Use bobby pins to hide the elastic band.

Step 5: Finish the look with hairspray.

Tips

  • Try out the hairstyles before the wedding day to avoid any surprises.
  • Don't use too much hairspray as it can make your hair look stiff.
  • Accessorize your hairstyle with hairpins or flowers for an added touch of elegance.

FAQ

What if I have short hair?

If you have short hair, you can still try out some of these hairstyles by using hair extensions or wigs.

Can I wear my hair down?

Yes, you can wear your hair down, but make sure it's styled in a way that complements your dress and the occasion.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your hair before the wedding day is essential. Make sure to wash your hair a day before the wedding to give it enough time to air dry. Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it. Also, consider using a hair mask a week before the wedding to give your hair extra shine and hydration.

With these easy hairstyles for long hair wedding guest, you'll look stunning and effortless at the same time. Don't forget to take lots of pictures!
