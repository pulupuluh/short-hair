Best Hair Style For Square Face Older Woman

Written by Frank May 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are an older woman with a square face shape, you might be wondering what the best hair style is for you. Well, look no further, as we have compiled some of the most stylish and flattering haircuts that are perfect for your face shape. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve these hair styles, as well as tips on how to care for your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve Best Hair Style for Square Face Older Woman
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and an angular chin. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, which can create a boxy or masculine appearance. The best hair style for a square face older woman is one that softens the angles of the face and adds volume to the hair. Layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and shoulder-length hair are all great options for this face shape.

How to Achieve Best Hair Style for Square Face Older Woman

The first step in achieving the best hair style for a square face older woman is to consult with a professional stylist. They will be able to recommend cuts and styles that will flatter your face shape and hair type. It is important to bring in pictures of styles that you like, as well as pictures of styles that you don't like, so that the stylist can get a better understanding of your preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve one of the best hair styles for a square face older woman:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair to one side.
  3. Take a 2-inch section of hair from the front and twist it away from your face.
  4. Secure the twist with bobby pins.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
  6. Take the remaining hair at the back and create loose waves using a curling iron.
  7. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair and keeping it looking healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear short hair if I have a square face shape?

A: Yes, you can. However, it is best to avoid very short styles that accentuate the angles of your face. A chin-length bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can be flattering on a square face shape.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth, such as biotin, vitamin C, and iron.

In conclusion, the best hair style for a square face older woman is one that softens the angles of the face and adds volume to the hair. Layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and shoulder-length hair are all great options for this face shape. Consult with a professional stylist to find the perfect style for you, and follow our tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair.
