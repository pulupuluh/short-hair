Is Short Hair In Style For 2021

Written by Aprilia Jun 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short hair has always been in style, but it seems to be making a comeback in 2021. Many celebrities and influencers have been spotted rocking short, stylish haircuts. If you're considering chopping off your locks and going for a shorter 'do, here's everything you need to know about the trend.

Description

Short hair is defined as any haircut that sits above the shoulders. It can range from a pixie cut to a lob (long bob) and everything in between. Short hair has been popular for years, but it's making a resurgence in 2021.

How to

If you're considering getting a short haircut, the first step is to find a style that you love. You can browse magazines or online galleries for inspiration. Once you've found a style, it's important to find a hairstylist who is experienced with short haircuts. They can give you advice on what will work best for your face shape and hair type.

Step by Step

1. Find inspiration for your short haircut.
2. Find a hairstylist who is experienced with short haircuts.
3. Discuss your desired style with your hairstylist.
4. Get your haircut!
5. Maintain your short haircut with regular trims.

Tips

Short hair requires more maintenance than longer hair. You'll need to get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh. You may also need to style your hair more often to keep it looking its best.

When styling your short hair, use products that are designed for your hair type. For example, if you have fine hair, use a volumizing mousse to add body to your locks. If you have curly hair, use a curl-enhancing cream to define your curls.

FAQ

Q: Will short hair look good on me?

Short hair can look good on anyone! The key is finding a style that works for your face shape and hair type. Your hairstylist can give you advice on what will work best for you.

Q: Is short hair low maintenance?

Short hair can be low maintenance, but it depends on the style. A pixie cut may require more styling than a lob, for example. You'll also need to get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Q: Can I still style my short hair?

Yes! Short hair can be styled in many different ways. You can use a straightener to create sleek locks, or add curls for a more voluminous look. Experiment with different styles to find what works for you.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. You'll need to get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh. You may also need to use styling products to keep your hair in place.

When washing your short hair, use a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your locks. If you have dry or damaged hair, use a hydrating hair mask once a week to nourish your tresses.

Conclusion

Short hair is definitely in style for 2021. If you're considering getting a short haircut, find a style that you love and a hairstylist who is experienced with short haircuts. With the right maintenance and styling, your short haircut will look amazing all year round!
