Mens Haircut Long On Top Fade On Sides A Guide To Modern Styling

Written by William Apr 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut that's versatile enough to suit any occasion, then the mens haircut long on top fade on sides might just be the perfect choice for you. This popular hairstyle features longer hair on top that gradually fades into shorter sides, creating a clean and sharp look that's both trendy and timeless.

Description

The mens haircut long on top fade on sides is a classic style that has been around for decades. It's a versatile look that can be worn in a variety of different ways, depending on your personal style and preferences. The longer hair on top can be styled in a variety of different ways, from slicked back to messy and textured, while the shorter sides give the hairstyle a clean and sharp look.

How to

If you're thinking about getting a mens haircut long on top fade on sides, then here's how to achieve the look:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step is to decide how long you want your hair to be on top. This will depend on your personal style and preferences, as well as your hair type and texture. If you have thick hair, you might want to go for a shorter length, while those with finer hair might prefer a longer style.

Step 2: Decide on the Fade

The next step is to decide on the length of the fade. This will be determined by how short you want the sides to be. You can choose from a low, medium or high fade, depending on your personal style and preferences.

Step 3: Choose Your Styling Products

Once you've got your haircut, you'll need to choose the right styling products to achieve the look you want. Depending on your hair type and texture, you might need to use a pomade, wax or gel to style your hair on top.

Tips

If you're thinking about getting a mens haircut long on top fade on sides, then here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Choose a length that suits your face shape and personal style.
  • Make sure you choose the right fade length for your hair type and texture.
  • Invest in high-quality styling products to help you achieve the look you want.
  • Take care of your hair by using a good quality shampoo and conditioner.

FAQ

What kind of face shape is best suited for a mens haircut long on top fade on sides?

A mens haircut long on top fade on sides can suit a variety of different face shapes, including oval, square and round. It's a versatile style that can be adapted to suit your individual features.

How often do I need to get my haircut to maintain a mens haircut long on top fade on sides?

It's recommended that you get your haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your mens haircut long on top fade on sides.

What kind of styling products do I need to use to achieve the look?

The type of styling products you need will depend on your hair type and texture. If you have thick hair, you might need a strong hold pomade or wax, while those with finer hair might prefer a lighter gel or mousse.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut long on top fade on sides looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. This means using a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy, as well as using styling products that won't damage your hair.

It's also important to avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. Instead, try to let your hair dry naturally as much as possible, and use a hair dryer on a low heat setting if you need to.

By following these simple hair care tips, you can keep your mens haircut long on top fade on sides looking sharp and stylish for weeks to come!
