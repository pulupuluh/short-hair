Short Hairstyles For Oval Face Female A Guide To The Best Cuts

Written by Petter Jul 15, 2023 Â· 11 min read

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off a wide range of hairstyles. However, finding the perfect short hairstyle for oval face female can be overwhelming. That's why we've put together a comprehensive guide to help you choose the best cut for your face shape.

Description

An oval face is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin. This face shape is also longer than it is wide, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face. If you have an oval face, you can experiment with a variety of short hairstyles that flatter your face shape.

How to Choose the Best Short Hairstyle for Oval Face Female

When choosing a short hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the best cut for your oval face:

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture plays a big role in determining which short hairstyle will work best for you. If you have fine hair, a bob or pixie cut can add volume and texture to your hair. If you have thick hair, a layered haircut can help thin out your hair and create movement.

Think About Your Personal Style

When choosing a short hairstyle, it's important to think about your personal style. Do you prefer a classic, elegant look or a more edgy, modern style? Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and make you feel confident and comfortable.

Consider Your Face Shape

While oval faces can pull off a variety of short hairstyles, some cuts are more flattering than others. For example, a bob that hits at the chin can accentuate your jawline, while a pixie cut can show off your cheekbones.

Step-by-Step Guide to the Best Short Hairstyles for Oval Face Female

Here are some of the best short hairstyles for oval face female:

1. Bob

A bob that hits at the chin is a classic and flattering short hairstyle for oval face female. You can opt for a straight, sleek bob or add some waves or curls for a more textured look. This cut works well for all hair textures and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and edgy short hairstyle that can look amazing on oval faces. This cut can accentuate your cheekbones and show off your neck and jawline. You can opt for a classic pixie cut or add some layers and texture for a more modern look.

3. Lob

A lob, or long bob, is a versatile and stylish short hairstyle that can work well for oval face female. This cut hits just above the shoulders and can be worn straight, wavy, or curly. You can also add some layers or bangs to customize the look to your face shape and hair texture.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle

Short hairstyles require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

1. Schedule Regular Haircuts

To keep your short hairstyle looking fresh and polished, you should schedule regular haircuts every 4-6 weeks. This will help keep your hair in shape and prevent split ends and breakage.

2. Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can make a big difference in how your short hairstyle looks and feels. Choose products that are specifically designed for your hair texture and style, such as volumizing mousse for fine hair or smoothing serum for thick hair.

3. Experiment with Styling

Short hairstyles are versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Experiment with different styling tools, such as a curling iron or flat iron, to create different looks. You can also try different hair accessories, such as clips or headbands, to add some flair to your hairstyle.

FAQ

What short hairstyles flatter oval faces?

Oval faces can pull off a variety of short hairstyles, including bobs, pixie cuts, and lobs. These cuts can accentuate your best features, such as your cheekbones and jawline, and work well for all hair textures.

How often should I get my short hair cut?

Can I style my short hair in different ways?

Short hairstyles are versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. You can use styling tools, such as a curling iron or flat iron, to create different looks. You can also try different hair accessories, such as clips or headbands, to add some flair to your hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different hair care routine than long hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Short hair can be more prone to oiliness and buildup, so it's important to use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for a shampoo that is sulfate-free and designed for your hair texture.

2. Condition Regularly

Conditioner is important for keeping short hair soft and manageable. Use a conditioner that is designed for your hair texture and apply it from mid-length to the ends of your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools can damage short hair, so it's important to protect your hair from heat damage. Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using any styling tools, and avoid using high heat settings.

4. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are important for keeping short hair looking its best. Schedule haircuts every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair in shape and prevent split ends and breakage.

5. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's UV rays can damage short hair, so it's important to protect your hair when you're outside. Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your hair from sun damage.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you can find the perfect short hairstyle for oval face female and keep your hair looking its best. Don't be afraid to experiment with different cuts and styles to find the look that works best for you.
