Best Haircut For Oval Face Female Over 50 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Jul 21, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you an oval-faced female over 50 looking for a new haircut? You're in luck because we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing the best haircut styles for oval-faced women over 50, how to achieve them, tips for maintaining your new haircut, and more. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

As an oval-faced woman over 50, you have a lot of options when it comes to haircuts. The oval face shape is considered the ideal face shape because it is well-proportioned and balanced. However, as we age, our hair may thin out and lose its luster. That's why it's important to choose a haircut that not only flatters your face shape but also makes your hair look healthy and voluminous.

How to

Before choosing a haircut, it's important to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. If you have thin hair, a layered haircut can add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a bob or pixie cut can help you manage your hair better. If you have a busy lifestyle, a low-maintenance haircut like a lob or shoulder-length cut may be more suitable.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a new haircut:

  1. Research different haircut styles and find inspiration.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.
  3. Bring pictures of the haircut you want to show your hairstylist.
  4. Communicate clearly with your hairstylist about your expectations and preferences.
  5. Maintain your new haircut with regular trims and hair care.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Consider using hair products like mousse or hairspray to add volume and hold to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for oval-faced women over 50:

What is the best haircut for oval-faced women over 50?

The best haircut for oval-faced women over 50 is one that flatters your face shape and complements your personal style. Some popular options include layered haircuts, bobs, pixie cuts, and shoulder-length cuts.

What are some hairstyles to avoid if you have an oval face?

Hairstyles to avoid if you have an oval face include blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, and hairstyles that are too voluminous at the crown of your head.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair and a flattering haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know the best haircut for oval-faced women over 50, it's time to book your appointment with your hairstylist. Remember to communicate clearly about your preferences and maintain your new haircut with proper hair care. With the right haircut and hair care, you can look and feel your best at any age.
