How to Make Big Fluffy Curls - Step by Step Guide

If you're looking for a way to add volume and bounce to your hair, big fluffy curls are the way to go! In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to create big, beautiful curls that will turn heads.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Big Fluffy Curls
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Creating Big Fluffy Curls
  • FAQs about Big Fluffy Curls

How to Make Big Fluffy Curls

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the tools and products you'll need to create big fluffy curls:

  • Curling iron
  • Hairspray
  • Heat protectant
  • Round brush
  • Sectioning clips

Now that you have your tools and products ready, let's get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into 2-3 inch sections using sectioning clips. Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Start at the root of your hair and wrap the section of hair around the curling iron barrel. Hold for 10-15 seconds and release the hair. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Brush Out Your Curls

Use a round brush to brush out your curls and create big fluffy curls. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your big fluffy curls with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Creating Big Fluffy Curls

  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel size to create bigger curls.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly - this will create small, tight curls instead of big fluffy ones.
  • Brush out your curls gently to avoid creating frizz.
  • Try using a volumizing mousse or spray before blow drying your hair for extra volume.

FAQs about Big Fluffy Curls

How long will big fluffy curls last?

Your big fluffy curls should last all day if you use hairspray to hold the style in place.

Can I create big fluffy curls with naturally straight hair?

Yes! Follow the same steps as above, but add a volumizing product before blow drying your hair to create more volume.

Do I need to use a specific type of hairspray?

No, any light hold hairspray will work well for big fluffy curls.

Hair Care Tips for Big Fluffy Curls

  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create big fluffy curls that will make you feel like a Hollywood starlet. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best!
