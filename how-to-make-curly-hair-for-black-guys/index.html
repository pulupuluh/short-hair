How To Make Curly Hair For Black Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Curly hair is a popular hairstyle among black men. It provides a unique texture and adds volume to your hair. If you have straight hair and want to make it curly, you can achieve it with the help of some products and techniques. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make curly hair for black guys.

How to

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step is to wash your hair with a shampoo that suits your hair type. It will help to remove dirt and oils from your hair and scalp. Rinse your hair thoroughly with water and pat it dry with a towel.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to make it soft and easy to manage. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the conditioner evenly throughout your hair. This will help to prevent tangles and knots.

Step 3: Apply a Curl Enhancing Cream

Apply a curl enhancing cream to your hair to create curls. Use your fingers to apply the cream from the roots to the ends of your hair. Scrunch your hair while applying the cream to create more defined curls.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to create curls and prevent frizz. Dry your hair on a low heat setting and use your hands to scrunch your hair while drying it.

Step 5: Apply a Hair Oil

Apply a hair oil to your hair to add shine and reduce frizz. Use a small amount of oil and apply it to the ends of your hair. Avoid applying too much oil as it can make your hair look greasy.

Tips

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curly hair as it can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent your hair from getting tangled while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my hair curly without using products?

A: Yes, you can make your hair curly without using products. You can use braids, twists, or rollers to create curls. However, using products will help to enhance and define your curls.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: You should wash your curly hair once or twice a week. Washing your hair too often can cause it to become dry and brittle.

Q: How can I prevent my curly hair from becoming frizzy?

A: You can prevent your curly hair from becoming frizzy by using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, using a hair oil, and avoiding brushing your hair.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol as it can dry out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often as it can cause damage and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make your hair curly and keep it healthy and looking its best. Remember to use products that are suitable for your hair type and avoid using too much heat on your hair. With patience and practice, you can achieve the perfect curly hairstyle for black guys.
