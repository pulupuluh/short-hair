Best Volumizing Hair Products In South Africa

Written by Christine May 09, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking for the best volumizing hair products in South Africa, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll be sharing some of the best products available on the market to give your hair that extra lift and bounce. Whether you have fine, thin hair or just want to add some volume to your locks, these products will help you achieve your desired look.

Description

Having voluminous hair is something that many people desire. It can make your hair look fuller, bouncier, and overall healthier. However, not everyone is blessed with naturally voluminous hair. That's where volumizing hair products come in. These products are designed to give your hair that extra boost, without weighing it down or making it look greasy.

How to

If you're not sure how to use volumizing hair products, don't worry. It's actually quite simple. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step is to wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo. This will help to remove any buildup or debris that could weigh your hair down. Rinse thoroughly and follow up with a volumizing conditioner.

Step 2: Apply Volumizing Product

Once you've towel-dried your hair, it's time to apply your volumizing product. This could be a mousse, a foam, or a spray. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the roots and the mid-lengths. Don't apply too much, as this could weigh your hair down.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Now it's time to blow-dry your hair. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume. Blow-dry in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Once your hair is dry, you can finish it off with a blast of cool air to set the style.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your volumizing hair products:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to prepare your hair for styling.
  • Apply your volumizing product to damp hair, not wet or dry hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair in sections, using a round brush to lift the roots.
  • Finish your style with a volumizing hairspray to lock in the volume.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about volumizing hair products:

What are the best volumizing hair products in South Africa?

Some of the best volumizing hair products in South Africa include:

  • TRESemmé Beauty-Full Volume Mousse
  • John Frieda Luxurious Volume Perfectly Full Mousse
  • L'Oreal Paris Elvive Fibrology Air Volume Dry Shampoo
  • OGX Biotin & Collagen Shampoo

Can volumizing hair products damage my hair?

No, if you use volumizing hair products correctly, they shouldn't damage your hair. However, if you use too much product or apply it incorrectly, it could weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Do I need to blow-dry my hair after using volumizing hair products?

Yes, blow-drying your hair after using volumizing hair products can help to lift your roots and create volume. However, if you don't have time to blow-dry your hair, you can let it air-dry and then use your fingers to lift your roots.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you're using volumizing hair products. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot tools.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid over-drying.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this could weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

With these tips and product recommendations, you can achieve voluminous hair that looks healthy and full of life. Give them a try and see the difference for yourself.
