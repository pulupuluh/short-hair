How To Do Space Buns With Short Thick Hair

Written by Luffy Feb 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a fun and easy hairstyle that can be done with short thick hair? Look no further than space buns! This trendy style is perfect for any occasion, from a casual day out to a formal event. In this article, we will guide you through the steps to achieving the perfect space buns with short thick hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we get started, let's go over the basic steps to creating space buns with short thick hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Section your hair into two parts, one on each side of your head
  3. Create a ponytail on each side and secure with an elastic band
  4. Twist each ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun
  5. Secure the buns with bobby pins and hairspray to keep them in place

Now that you have the basic idea, let's dive into the step by step process.

Step by Step

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. If you have thick hair, it's important to use a lightweight shampoo and conditioner to avoid weighing down your hair. Once your hair is dry, brush it out to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Part your hair

Part your hair down the middle or on the side, whichever you prefer. Use a comb or your fingers to create a clean part line. Make sure the part line is straight and even.

Step 3: Create the buns

Take one section of your hair and twist it tightly until it starts to coil on itself. Wrap the twisted hair around itself to create a bun, and secure it with bobby pins. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Secure the buns

Use bobby pins to secure the buns in place. Make sure to tuck any loose ends under the bun and secure them with bobby pins. Use hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect space buns with short thick hair:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look
  • Add texture to your hair before creating the buns by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo
  • Add accessories like bows or clips to dress up the buns

FAQ

Can space buns be done with short thick hair?

Absolutely! Space buns can be done with any hair length or texture. Just make sure to secure the buns tightly with bobby pins.

How long do space buns last?

With the right amount of hairspray and bobby pins, space buns can last all day and night!

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you're styling it frequently. Here are a few tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners
  • Avoid using too much hairspray or other styling products that can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized

With these tips and tricks, you can create perfect space buns with your short thick hair. Whether you're heading to a music festival or just running errands, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads!
