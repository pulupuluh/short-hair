How Do I Know A Hairstyle Will Suit Me

Written by Frank Sep 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's hard to know which one will suit you best. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll discuss how to know if a hairstyle will suit you, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the details, let's talk about what makes a hairstyle suit you. Your face shape, hair texture, and personal style all play a role in determining which hairstyles will work for you.

For example, if you have a round face, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this will only make your face look rounder. Instead, you'll want to opt for hairstyles that elongate your face, such as long layers or a deep side part.

Similarly, if you have thick, curly hair, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that works with your natural texture, rather than against it.

How to

So, how do you know if a hairstyle will suit you? Here are some things to consider:

1. Face Shape

As we mentioned earlier, your face shape is an important factor to consider when choosing a new hairstyle. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round face: choose hairstyles that elongate your face, such as long layers or a deep side part.
  • Square face: opt for soft, rounded hairstyles that soften your features.
  • Oval face: lucky you! Most hairstyles will suit you, so feel free to experiment.
  • Heart-shaped face: choose hairstyles that balance out your face shape, such as a bob or shoulder-length cut.

2. Hair Texture

Your hair texture also plays a role in determining which hairstyles will suit you. Here are some tips based on different hair types:

  • Straight hair: try a blunt bob or a sleek, straight style.
  • Curly hair: embrace your curls with a layered cut or a curly bob.
  • Wavy hair: a beachy, tousled look or a long, layered cut will work well.
  • Thick hair: opt for a layered cut to add movement and texture.
  • Thin hair: try a short, layered cut or a pixie cut to add volume.

3. Personal Style

Lastly, your personal style should also be taken into account when choosing a new hairstyle. Do you prefer a classic, timeless look? Or do you like to experiment with bold, edgy styles? Make sure the hairstyle you choose reflects your personal style and makes you feel confident.

Step by step

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a new hairstyle, here are the steps to follow:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair texture.
  3. Think about your personal style.
  4. Research hairstyles that suit your face shape and hair texture.
  5. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hairdresser.
  6. Ask your hairdresser for their professional opinion on which styles will suit you best.
  7. Try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to get a better idea of how the hairstyle will look on you.
  8. Make sure the hairstyle you choose fits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain.
  9. Book an appointment with your hairdresser and enjoy your new look!

Tips

  • Don't be afraid to try something new, but make sure it suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hairdresser to help them understand what you're looking for.
  • Use virtual hairstyle apps to see how different hairstyles will look on you.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairdresser for their professional opinion.

FAQ

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It's not uncommon to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, try to find hairstyles that balance out your features and work with your hair texture.

What if I don't like the hairstyle I chose?

Don't worry, hair grows back! If you're not happy with your new look, talk to your hairdresser about making changes or growing out your hair.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a new hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when you do use heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using hair masks or treatments to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know how to choose a hairstyle that suits you, it's time to book an appointment with your hairdresser and try something new!
