Best Hairstyle For Round Face Short Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline May 20, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have a round face and short hair, you may be wondering what hairstyle would suit you best. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one for you. In this guide, we will show you the best hairstyle for round face short hair and provide you with tips and tricks to keep your hair looking great.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by a wider forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you want to choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face and create the illusion of more defined cheekbones. Short hair can be a great option for round faces because it can help to create a more sculpted look.

How To

The best hairstyle for round face short hair is a layered bob. This style works well because the layers help to create volume at the top of your head, which elongates your face. The length of the bob should fall just below your chin, which will help to create a more defined jawline.

Step By Step

Here’s how to get the perfect layered bob:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: the top, the sides, and the back.
  3. Starting at the back, use a razor to create layers throughout your hair.
  4. Once you have created layers throughout your hair, use a flat iron to straighten your hair.
  5. Apply a small amount of product to your hair to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling your hair:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will make your face look wider.
  • Use a flat iron to create a sleek, straight look that will elongate your face.
  • Choose a hairstyle that frames your face, such as a layered bob or a side-swept fringe.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face look rounder.

FAQ

What other hairstyles work well for round faces?

In addition to the layered bob, other hairstyles that work well for round faces include long layers, a side-swept fringe, and a pixie cut.

How can I make my hair look thicker?

To make your hair look thicker, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a root-lifting spray before blow-drying your hair.

How often should I get my hair cut?

You should get your hair cut every six to eight weeks to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking great, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best hairstyle for round face short hair, you can confidently walk into your next hair appointment and leave with a look that flatters your face shape. Remember to choose a style that elongates your face and avoid hairstyles that add width to your face. With the right cut and styling, you can create a look that is both chic and flattering.
