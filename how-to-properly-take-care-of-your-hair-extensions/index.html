How To Properly Take Care Of Your Hair Extensions

Description

Having hair extensions can be a great way to add length and volume to your hair. However, it's important to take proper care of them in order to keep them looking their best. This article will provide you with tips on how to properly take care of your hair extensions.

How to

One of the most important things you can do to take care of your hair extensions is to brush them regularly. Use a brush with soft bristles and start at the ends of your hair, gradually working your way up to the roots. Avoid brushing your hair extensions when they are wet, as this can cause damage.

Step by step

Step 1: Brush your hair extensions regularly with a soft bristle brush.
Step 2: Avoid brushing your hair extensions when they are wet.
Step 3: Wash your hair extensions gently using a sulfate-free shampoo.
Step 4: Condition your hair extensions with a lightweight conditioner.
Step 5: Use a detangling spray to help prevent tangling.
Step 6: Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair extensions.
Step 7: Store your hair extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use.

Tips

When washing your hair extensions, be gentle and avoid scrubbing too hard. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a lightweight conditioner to keep your hair extensions looking their best. You can also use a detangling spray to help prevent tangling.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair extensions?

A: It's recommended that you wash your hair extensions every 6-8 weeks, or as needed.

Q: Can I use heat styling tools on my hair extensions?

A: It's best to avoid using heat styling tools on your hair extensions, as this can cause damage. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Q: How do I store my hair extensions?

A: Store your hair extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use. You can also use a hair extension hanger or storage bag to keep them organized.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your hair extensions looking their best. Be sure to brush your hair regularly, avoid heat styling tools, and use gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. With proper care, your hair extensions can last for months, giving you beautiful, voluminous hair.
