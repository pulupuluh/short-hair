It is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways, making it a popular choice for men of all ages. Description

The short on sides long on top hairstyle is a classic look that has been popular for decades. This style is characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head with longer hair on the top. This haircut can be styled in many different ways, making it versatile for any occasion. It is a low maintenance cut that looks great on men of all ages.

How to Cut a Man's Hair Short on Sides Long on Top

To cut a man's hair short on sides long on top, you will need the following tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape

Before you start cutting the hair, it is important to wash and dry the hair thoroughly. This will make it easier to cut and style the hair. Once the hair is dry, put the haircutting cape around the neck of the person getting the haircut.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut a man's hair short on sides long on top:

  1. Start by using the clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head. Use a number 2 or 3 guard to leave about 1/4 inch of hair on the sides and back.
  2. Use scissors to blend the hair on the top of the head with the hair on the sides and back.
  3. Cut the hair on the top of the head to the desired length. Use scissors to cut the hair in a straight line, from the front to the back of the head.
  4. Blend the hair on the top of the head with the hair on the sides and back.
  5. Use the clippers to trim the hair around the ears and the back of the neck.
  6. Use scissors to add texture to the hair on the top of the head.
  7. Style the hair on the top of the head using your fingers or a comb.

It is important to take your time when cutting hair to avoid making mistakes. If you are unsure about how to cut the hair, it is recommended to seek the advice of a professional hairstylist.

Tips for Cutting Hair

Here are some tips to help you cut hair like a pro:

  • Use sharp scissors to make clean cuts
  • Start with a longer guard on the clippers and work your way down to a shorter guard if needed
  • Take breaks if you get tired to avoid making mistakes
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from different angles
  • Be patient and take your time

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the haircut.

2. Can I style my hair differently with this haircut?

Yes, the short on sides long on top haircut can be styled in many different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or spiky.

3. Is this haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this haircut is versatile and can be adapted to suit any face shape.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain the style and health of the hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and style of the haircut

Following these hair care tips will help to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Conclusion

The short on sides long on top haircut is a classic and versatile style that can be adapted to suit any occasion. By following the step-by-step guide and tips in this article, you can cut a man's hair short on sides long on top like a pro. Remember to take your time and be patient when cutting hair to avoid making mistakes. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and low maintenance haircut that looks great on any man.
