What Hair Styles Suit A Round Face

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on what hair styles suit a round face. Whether you have long or short hair, there's a hairstyle that will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. So, let's get started!

  • Step by Step Guide to Choosing a Hairstyle for a Round Face

Description

A round face is characterized by its soft, curved lines and equal width and length. The cheekbones are usually the widest part of the face, and the jawline is round and soft. When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape to find a style that will balance your features and create a more flattering look.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for round faces, let's first determine if you have a round face. Here are some tips to help you determine your face shape:

  • Measure your face from the hairline to the chin and from cheekbone to cheekbone. If these measurements are roughly the same, you have a round face.
  • Look in the mirror and draw an imaginary line from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin. If your face is widest at the cheeks and tapers slightly at the forehead and jawline, you have a round face.
  • Take a photo of yourself straight on and trace the outline of your face. If the resulting shape is circular, you have a round face.

If you're still unsure, consult with a hairstylist who can help you determine your face shape and recommend a style that will flatter your features.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing a Hairstyle for a Round Face

Now that you've determined your face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle that will enhance your natural beauty. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or a bob that ends at the chin.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, such as a voluminous updo or a high ponytail.
  • Choose hairstyles that create angles and elongate the face, such as a side-swept bang or a layered cut that falls below the chin.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make the face appear even rounder.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and create the illusion of a more angular face shape.

Remember, the key is to find a style that balances your features and enhances your natural beauty. Consult with a hairstylist who can recommend a style that is tailored to your face shape and personal style.

Tips for Styling Hair for a Round Face

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for your round face, it's important to know how to style it to create a more flattering look. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add height to the crown of the head.
  • Try a side-swept bang to create angles and elongate the face.
  • Consider adding layers to create the illusion of a more angular face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat, as they can make the face appear even rounder.
  • Experiment with different partings to create angles and enhance your features.

Remember, the key is to find a style that works for your face shape and personal style. Play around with different hairstyles and styling techniques to find a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or a bob that ends at the chin. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make the face appear even rounder.

What hairstyles are best for a round face?

Hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, create angles, and elongate the face are best for a round face. Consider a voluminous updo, a side-swept bang, or a layered cut that falls below the chin.

Should I add highlights or lowlights to my hair?

Adding highlights or lowlights can add dimension to the hair and create the illusion of a more angular face shape. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best color and placement for your face shape.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Now that you've found the perfect hairstyle for your round face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair and make it look dull.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to deeply nourish and condition the hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of the hair.
  • Protect the hair from the sun and environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective hair spray.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful, no matter what style you choose.

Conclusion

Choosing a hairstyle for a round face can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can find a style that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Remember to consider your face shape, consult with a hairstylist, and experiment with different styles and techniques to find a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
