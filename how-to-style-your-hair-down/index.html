How To Style Your Hair Down A Relaxed Guide

Written by Robby Mar 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a simple yet stylish hairdo, you can never go wrong with wearing your hair down. Whether you have long, medium, or short hair, this look is perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll teach you how to style your hair down in a relaxed and easy-to-follow manner.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Your Hair Down

Styling your hair down may seem simple, but there are a few things you need to consider to achieve the perfect look. Here are some tips:

  • Choose the right hair products for your hair type.
  • Decide on the texture you want for your hair.
  • Consider your face shape and choose a style that flatters it.
  • Accessorize your hair with hair clips, headbands, or scarves if desired.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have an idea of what you need to consider before styling your hair down, here's a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair until it's about 80% dry.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling.
  4. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Once your hair is completely dry, use a flat iron to straighten it if desired.
  6. Add volume to your hair by teasing the roots with a comb.
  7. Finish off by applying a light hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips and Tricks

To make your hair down look even more stylish, here are some additional tips:

  • If you have wavy or curly hair, add some texture by using a sea salt spray.
  • If you have straight hair, add some curls or waves using a curling iron or hot rollers.
  • Add some shine to your hair by applying a small amount of hair oil.
  • If you're going for a more formal look, consider adding some braids to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling your hair down:

  • Can I style my hair down if I have short hair? Absolutely! Just follow the same steps, but use a smaller round brush to blow-dry your hair.
  • How do I prevent my hair from getting frizzy? Use a smoothing serum or cream before blow-drying your hair, and avoid using too much heat.
  • How often should I wash my hair? It depends on your hair type, but generally, washing it every other day or every two days is enough to keep it clean and healthy.

Hair Care Tips

Styling your hair down is a great way to show off your natural beauty, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your hair down like a pro in no time. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, this hairstyle is versatile and easy to maintain. So go ahead and rock your natural beauty!
