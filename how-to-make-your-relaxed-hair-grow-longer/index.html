How To Make Your Relaxed Hair Grow Longer

Introduction

Long, luscious hair is a dream of many women. However, if you have relaxed hair, you may find it difficult to grow your hair past a certain length. Relaxers can weaken the hair, making it more prone to breakage and damage. Fortunately, there are things you can do to promote healthy hair growth and achieve the length you desire. In this article, we will discuss how to make your relaxed hair grow longer.

How to Make Your Relaxed Hair Grow Longer

The key to growing longer hair is to promote healthy hair growth and prevent damage. Here are some things you can do to make your relaxed hair grow longer:

  • Eat a healthy diet: Your hair needs nutrients to grow, so make sure you are eating a balanced diet with plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Moisturize regularly: Relaxed hair can be dry and prone to breakage, so it is important to moisturize regularly. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair daily.
  • Avoid heat: Heat styling tools can damage your hair and cause breakage. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.
  • Protect your hair at night: Sleeping with a satin or silk scarf or pillowcase can help prevent breakage and keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim regularly: Trimming your hair every 8-12 weeks can help prevent split ends and breakage.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make your relaxed hair grow longer:

  1. Start with a healthy scalp: Your scalp is the foundation of healthy hair growth, so make sure to keep it clean and moisturized. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-washing.
  2. Moisturize regularly: As mentioned earlier, relaxed hair can be dry and prone to breakage, so it is important to moisturize regularly. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair daily.
  3. Avoid heat: Heat styling tools can damage your hair and cause breakage. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.
  4. Protect your hair at night: Sleeping with a satin or silk scarf or pillowcase can help prevent breakage and keep your hair moisturized.
  5. Trim regularly: Trimming your hair every 8-12 weeks can help prevent split ends and breakage.
  6. Use a deep conditioner: Once a week, use a deep conditioner to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  7. Avoid tight hairstyles: Tight hairstyles can put tension on your hair, causing breakage and damage. Opt for looser styles, and avoid pulling your hair too tight.
  8. Be gentle when combing: Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and be gentle to avoid breakage.
  9. Avoid chemical treatments: Chemical treatments, such as coloring and relaxing, can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. If possible, avoid these treatments.
  10. Be patient: Hair growth takes time, so be patient and consistent with your hair care routine.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when growing out relaxed hair:

  • Protect your hair from the sun: UV rays can damage your hair, so wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray when spending time in the sun.
  • Stay hydrated: Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair and scalp healthy.
  • Consider supplements: Supplements, such as biotin and vitamin E, may help promote healthy hair growth.
  • See a professional: If you are struggling with hair growth, consider seeing a professional stylist or dermatologist for advice.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I wash my relaxed hair?

You should wash your relaxed hair every 7-10 days, or as needed. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, leading to dryness and breakage.

Can I still use heat on my hair?

Yes, you can still use heat on your hair, but it is important to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low to prevent damage.

How long will it take for my hair to grow?

Hair growth varies from person to person, but on average, hair grows about half an inch per month. With proper care, you can expect to see noticeable growth within a few months.

Hair Care for Longer Relaxed Hair

Once you have achieved the length you desire, it is important to continue taking care of your hair to maintain its health and length. Here are some tips for caring for longer relaxed hair:

  • Continue to moisturize regularly: Even if your hair is longer, it is still important to keep it moisturized to prevent breakage.
  • Protect your ends: Your ends are the oldest and most fragile part of your hair, so it is important to protect them from damage. Trim them regularly, and avoid heat and tight hairstyles.
  • Avoid over-manipulation: Handling your hair too much can cause breakage, so avoid over-styling and over-brushing.
  • Get regular trims: Trimming your hair every 8-12 weeks can help prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and taking proper care of your hair, you can make your relaxed hair grow longer and achieve the length you desire. Remember to be patient and consistent, and don't be afraid to seek professional advice if needed.
