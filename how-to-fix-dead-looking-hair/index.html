How To Fix Dead Looking Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Apr 13, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Having dead looking hair is one of the most frustrating hair problems that many people face. It can be caused by a variety of factors, including overuse of heat styling tools, chemical treatments, lack of proper hair care, and more. But don't despair, with the right approach, you can fix your dead looking hair and bring it back to life. Here's a comprehensive guide on how to do it.

  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to Fix Dead Looking Hair

Fixing dead looking hair is not an overnight process. It requires patience, effort, and the right approach. Here are some steps you can take to bring your hair back to life:

1. Get a Trim

One of the first things you should do is to get a trim. Dead ends are one of the main causes of dead looking hair, so getting rid of them can make a big difference. Make sure to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends from forming.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Deep conditioning treatments are a great way to nourish your hair and give it the moisture it needs. Look for a treatment that contains ingredients like keratin, argan oil, or coconut oil, which can help to repair and strengthen your hair. Use the treatment once a week for best results.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons can be damaging to your hair, especially if you use them regularly. Try to avoid using them as much as possible, or at least use them on a low heat setting. If you must use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

4. Be Gentle When Brushing

Brushing your hair too roughly can cause breakage and damage, so be gentle when brushing. Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush with soft bristles, and start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

5. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can have a big impact on the health of your hair. Make sure to eat plenty of protein, which is essential for healthy hair growth, as well as fruits and vegetables, which contain vitamins and minerals that can help to nourish your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to fix your dead looking hair:

Step 1: Get a trim

Visit a salon and get a trim to get rid of dead ends and breakage.

Step 2: Use a deep conditioning treatment

Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and give it the moisture it needs.

Step 3: Avoid heat styling tools

Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, or at least use them on a low heat setting. Use a heat protectant spray before using them.

Step 4: Be gentle when brushing

Use a wide-toothed comb or soft-bristled brush to avoid causing breakage and damage.

Step 5: Eat a healthy diet

Eat a healthy diet that is rich in protein, fruits, and vegetables to promote healthy hair growth.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you fix your dead looking hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can dry it out and make it look dull.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Avoid using hair ties that are too tight, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Consider taking hair supplements like biotin or collagen to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fixing dead looking hair:

Q: Can I fix my dead looking hair without cutting it?

A: While getting a trim is the best way to get rid of dead ends, you can also try using a deep conditioning treatment and avoiding heat styling tools to improve the health of your hair.

Q: How long does it take to fix dead looking hair?

A: It depends on the severity of the damage, but it can take several weeks or even months to see a noticeable improvement in the health of your hair.

Q: Can I use home remedies to fix my dead looking hair?

A: Yes, there are several home remedies that can help to improve the health of your hair, such as using coconut oil, honey, or avocado as a hair mask.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo every 2-3 days.
  • Condition your hair after every wash.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.
  • Get a trim every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends from forming.

Fixing dead looking hair can be a challenge, but with the right approach, it is possible to bring your hair back to life. Follow these tips and steps, and you'll be on your way to healthy, shiny, and beautiful hair.
