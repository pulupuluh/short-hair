Can I Wear Long Hair At 60

Written by Robby May 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair changes along with our bodies. But, can women over 60 wear long hair? The answer is yes! Long hair can be a great option for women over 60, and it's all about finding the right style and taking care of your hair properly. In this article, we will go over everything you need to know about wearing long hair in your 60s.

Description

Long hair has always been associated with youth and beauty, but as women age, they tend to opt for shorter hairstyles. However, just because you're over 60 doesn't mean you have to cut your hair short. Long hair can be a great option for women of any age, as long as it's styled properly and taken care of. In fact, long hair can be very flattering on older women, as it can soften features and create a youthful look.

How to Wear Long Hair at 60

If you're thinking of wearing long hair in your 60s, there are a few things you should consider. First, you should choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair type. Second, you should take care of your hair properly to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for wearing long hair in your 60s:

Choose the Right Style

When choosing a long hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape and hair type. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to opt for layers to create more dimension. If you have thin hair, you may want to avoid heavy layers that can weigh down your hair. A good stylist can help you choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair type.

Take Care of Your Hair

Long hair requires more maintenance than short hair, so it's important to take care of it properly. This includes using a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends. You may also want to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're new to wearing long hair, here is a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Choose a Style

Before you can start growing out your hair, you need to choose a style that you like and that flatters your face shape and hair type. Look online for inspiration and bring pictures to your stylist to help you communicate what you want.

Step 2: Start Growing Your Hair

Once you've chosen a style, it's time to start growing your hair out. This can take some time, so be patient. You may want to use hair supplements or vitamins to help your hair grow faster.

Step 3: Get Regular Trims

Even though you're growing your hair out, it's important to get regular trims to prevent split ends. This will also help your hair grow faster and stay healthy.

Step 4: Take Care of Your Hair

As mentioned earlier, taking care of your hair is crucial when wearing long hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling tools, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is long enough, it's time to start styling it. You can wear it straight, curly, or wavy, depending on your preference. You may also want to experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, to add some variety to your look.

Tips for Wearing Long Hair at 60

Here are some additional tips for wearing long hair in your 60s:

  • Choose a low-maintenance style that you can easily manage
  • Avoid heavy bangs that can make your face look heavier
  • Use hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add some dimension to your hair

FAQs

Q: Is it safe to wear long hair at 60?

A: Yes, as long as you take care of your hair properly.

Q: What are some good long hairstyles for women over 60?

A: Some good long hairstyles for women over 60 include layered cuts, beachy waves, and blunt cuts.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: You should get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair

Conclusion

Long hair can be a great option for women over 60, as long as it's styled properly and taken care of. By following the tips in this article, you can wear long hair with confidence and look great at any age.
