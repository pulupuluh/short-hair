How To Make Curtain Bangs Look Good In A Ponytail

Written by Frank Aug 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle right now, and they look great when worn down. But what about when you want to wear your hair up in a ponytail? Don't worry, you can still rock the curtain bangs look with a few simple tricks. In this post, we'll show you how to make curtain bangs look good in a ponytail.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Curtain Bangs Look Good in a Ponytail

How to Make Curtain Bangs Look Good in a Ponytail

The key to making curtain bangs look good in a ponytail is to make sure they blend in with the rest of your hair. You don't want them to stick out or look like a separate piece of hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Texturizing Spray

Before you put your hair up in a ponytail, use a texturizing spray on your curtain bangs. This will help give them some volume and make them blend in better with the rest of your hair.

2. Pull Your Bangs Back

When you put your hair up in a ponytail, pull your curtain bangs back with the rest of your hair. Don't leave them hanging down in front of your face. This will help them blend in better with the rest of your hair.

3. Use Bobby Pins

If your curtain bangs are still not blending in with the rest of your hair, use bobby pins to secure them in place. This will help keep them in line with the rest of your hair and prevent them from sticking out.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to making curtain bangs look good in a ponytail:

  1. Start by using a texturizing spray on your curtain bangs.
  2. Pull your hair back into a ponytail, making sure to include your curtain bangs.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure any pieces of hair that are sticking out.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs in a Ponytail

Here are some additional tips to help you style your curtain bangs in a ponytail:

  • Try different ponytail heights to see what looks best with your curtain bangs.
  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • Don't be afraid to use bobby pins to keep everything in place.

FAQs

Can I wear my curtain bangs in a ponytail if they're too short?

If your curtain bangs are too short to pull back into a ponytail, try using bobby pins to secure them to the rest of your hair. This will help them blend in better.

Will a high ponytail or low ponytail look better with curtain bangs?

It really depends on your face shape and personal preference. Try both and see which one you like better.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Here are some hair care tips to keep your curtain bangs looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup and keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your bangs.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at the length you want.

By following these tips, you can make your curtain bangs look great in a ponytail. Don't be afraid to experiment with different ponytail styles and accessories to find what works best for you.
