How To Cut A Short Bob On Thick Hair A Guide For Beginners

Written by William May 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to change up your hairstyle with a short bob on your thick hair? It can be intimidating to cut your own hair, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a chic and stylish look. In this article, we will guide you through the steps of how to cut a short bob on thick hair.

Description

A short bob is a haircut that is typically chin-length or shorter. It is a versatile and trendy style that can flatter any face shape. Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse when it comes to hairstyles. While it can be voluminous and full of body, it can also be challenging to manage and style.

How to Cut a Short Bob on Thick Hair

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into four sections: the top, left side, right side, and back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start with the back section. Comb the hair straight down and cut it to the desired length. It's best to cut small sections at a time to ensure evenness.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Move on to the side sections. Comb the hair straight down and cut it to the same length as the back section. Make sure the sides are even with each other.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, move on to the top section. Comb the hair straight up and cut it to the desired length. Use the back section as a guide for length and make sure the top is even.

Tips

- Take your time and cut small sections at a time.
- Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
- Wet your hair before cutting to make it easier to manage.
- Start with a longer length and cut more if needed.
- If you're unsure, it's better to cut less than more.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my own hair?
A: Yes, you can cut your own hair. Just make sure you have the right tools and techniques.

Q: What if I make a mistake?
A: Don't worry, hair grows back. If you make a mistake, you can always go to a professional hairdresser to fix it.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Regular trims can also help to keep your short bob looking fresh.

Conclusion

Cutting a short bob on thick hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and trendy look. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if needed. Happy cutting!
