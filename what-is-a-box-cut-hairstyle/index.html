What Is A Box Cut Hairstyle

Written by Christine Jun 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Box cut hairstyle, also known as the buzz cut, is a popular men's haircut that has been around for several decades. It is a low-maintenance, short hairstyle that is perfect for those who want a clean and neat look without having to spend a lot of time on their hair. This hairstyle is characterized by its sharp and defined edges, which give it a box-like appearance. In this article, we will go over everything you need to know about the box cut hairstyle, including how to get it, its history, and how to take care of it.

How to Get a Box Cut Hairstyle

Getting a box cut hairstyle is relatively easy, and you can do it yourself at home with the right tools. Here's how:

Step 1: Get the Right Tools

Before you start, you will need a good quality clipper, a pair of scissors, and a comb. Make sure your clipper has different guard sizes so you can adjust the length of your hair.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard Size

Decide on the length of your hair and choose the guard size accordingly. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down until you reach the desired length.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Start cutting your hair, starting from the sides and working your way up to the top. Use scissors to cut the hair around your ears and at the back of your neck.

Step 4: Define the Edges

Use a trimmer or clippers without a guard to define the edges of your hairline, sideburns, and neckline. This will give your hairstyle a sharp and defined look.

Tips for Maintaining a Box Cut Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your box cut hairstyle:

- Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and clean. - Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp and hair healthy. - Avoid using too much product on your hair as it can weigh it down and make it look greasy. - If you want to change up your hairstyle, consider adding a fade or taper to the sides.

FAQs

What is the History of the Box Cut Hairstyle?

The box cut hairstyle has been around since the 1950s and was popularized by actors like James Dean and Steve McQueen. It became a popular hairstyle for men in the military and has since become a classic look that never goes out of style.

Can Anyone Get a Box Cut Hairstyle?

Yes, anyone can get a box cut hairstyle, regardless of their hair type or texture. However, it is best suited for those with straight or wavy hair.

What is the Difference Between a Box Cut and a Buzz Cut?

The main difference between a box cut and a buzz cut is the length of the hair. A box cut is slightly longer than a buzz cut and has more defined edges.

Hair Care for Box Cut Hairstyle

To keep your box cut hairstyle looking its best, you should follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair every two to three days with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. You should also avoid using hot styling tools as they can damage your hair. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a blow dryer on a low setting. Finally, be sure to get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and clean.

Conclusion

A box cut hairstyle is a classic and timeless look that is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance and neat hairstyle. With the right tools and a little bit of practice, you can easily achieve this look at home. Just remember to maintain your hairstyle regularly and follow a proper hair care routine to keep it looking its best.
