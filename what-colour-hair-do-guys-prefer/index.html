What Colour Hair Do Guys Prefer A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 13, 2023 · 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on the most popular hair colors that guys prefer on women. In this article, we’ll go over the most popular hair colors for women and provide some tips on how to maintain your hair color. If you’re looking to change your hair color or if you’re curious about what guys like, keep reading!

Table of Contents

  • Popular Hair Colors
  • How to Maintain Your Hair Color
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Popular Hair Colors

Blonde, brunette, and redhead are the most popular hair colors for women. Of these three, guys prefer blonde hair the most. It’s not surprising, as blonde hair is associated with youthfulness and femininity. That being said, not all guys prefer blonde hair. Some guys prefer brunettes or redheads, so it’s important to choose a hair color that suits you.

There are also some less common hair colors that guys find attractive. For example, guys find black hair very attractive, especially when it’s shiny and healthy-looking. Silver and platinum hair colors have also become popular in recent years, and many guys find these hair colors very attractive as well. Ultimately, the most important thing is to choose a hair color that suits your skin tone and personality.

How to Maintain Your Hair Color

Once you’ve chosen a hair color, it’s important to maintain it properly so that it looks its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair color:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair color from fading
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause your hair color to fade more quickly
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as these can damage your hair and cause your hair color to fade more quickly
  • Use a color-safe hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep your hair color looking vibrant

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

When choosing a hair color, it’s important to consider your skin tone and natural hair color. Here are some tips for choosing the right hair color:

  • If you have fair skin, choose a hair color that is lighter than your natural hair color
  • If you have medium skin, choose a hair color that is close to your natural hair color
  • If you have dark skin, choose a hair color that is darker than your natural hair color
  • Consider your eye color as well – if you have blue or green eyes, blonde hair will look great on you. If you have brown eyes, consider a warmer hair color like auburn or chestnut
  • Try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see how different hair colors will look on you before committing to a new hair color

FAQ

What hair color do guys find most attractive?

Guys generally find blonde hair the most attractive, but this varies from person to person.

Do guys prefer natural or dyed hair?

This varies from person to person, but many guys prefer natural hair.

Can I dye my hair multiple colors?

Yes, you can dye your hair multiple colors, but it’s important to choose colors that complement each other and to have a professional stylist do the dyeing.

Hair Care

Finally, it’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as these can damage your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy

With these tips in mind, you’re sure to find a hair color that suits you and that guys find attractive. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hair color that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
