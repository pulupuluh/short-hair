Women s Short Haircut Styles 2021 The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle this year, consider going short! Short haircuts have been gaining popularity among women in recent years, and 2021 is no exception. From pixie cuts to bobs, there are a variety of short haircut styles to choose from. In this article, we'll discuss the most popular women's short haircut styles of 2021, along with tips on how to style and care for your new 'do.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut style that has been popular among women for decades. It's a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling, making it a great option for busy women. The pixie cut is characterized by short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on top.

To style a pixie cut, simply apply a small amount of styling product to your hair and use your fingers to create texture and volume. You can also use a flat iron or curling wand to add waves or curls to your hair.

Pros of the Pixie Cut

  • Low-maintenance
  • Easy to style
  • Great for showcasing facial features

Cons of the Pixie Cut

  • Not suitable for all face shapes
  • May require frequent trims to maintain shape

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another classic short haircut style that has been popular among women for decades. It's a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit any face shape and hair type. The bob cut is characterized by hair that is cut straight around the head at chin length or shorter.

To style a bob cut, blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and curl at the ends. You can also add waves or curls to your hair using a flat iron or curling wand.

Pros of the Bob Cut

  • Versatile
  • Easy to style
  • Great for all face shapes

Cons of the Bob Cut

  • May require frequent trims to maintain shape

Lob Cut

The lob cut, also known as the long bob, is a popular short haircut style that falls just above the shoulders. It's a great option for women who want to try a shorter haircut without going too short. The lob cut is characterized by hair that is cut straight around the head at shoulder length or shorter.

To style a lob cut, blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and curl at the ends. You can also add waves or curls to your hair using a flat iron or curling wand.

Pros of the Lob Cut

  • Gives the appearance of a shorter haircut without going too short
  • Great for all face shapes
  • Easy to style

Cons of the Lob Cut

  • May require frequent trims to maintain shape

Shag Cut

The shag cut is a trendy short haircut style that has been gaining popularity among women in recent years. It's a great option for women who want a more textured and layered haircut. The shag cut is characterized by hair that is cut into layers of varying lengths, with the shortest layers framing the face.

To style a shag cut, apply a small amount of styling product to your hair and use your fingers to create texture and volume. You can also add waves or curls to your hair using a flat iron or curling wand.

Pros of the Shag Cut

  • Trendy and modern
  • Great for adding texture and volume to hair

Cons of the Shag Cut

  • May require more styling time than other short haircuts

Fade Cut

The fade cut is a short haircut style that is popular among women who want a more edgy and masculine look. It's characterized by hair that is cut shorter on the sides and back, with longer hair on top. The fade cut can be customized to suit any face shape and hair type, and can be styled in a variety of ways.

To style a fade cut, apply a small amount of styling product to your hair and use your fingers to create texture and volume. You can also add waves or curls to your hair using a flat iron or curling wand.

Pros of the Fade Cut

  • Edgy and modern
  • Great for showcasing facial features

Cons of the Fade Cut

  • May require frequent trims to maintain shape
  • Not suitable for all face shapes

How to Style Your Short Haircut

Styling your short haircut is easy and can be done in just a few minutes. Here are some tips for styling your short hair:

  • Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair
  • Use your fingers to create texture and volume
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and curl
  • Add waves or curls to your hair using a flat iron or curling wand

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Own Hair

If you're feeling brave and want to cut your own hair, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry
  2. Section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back
  3. Starting with the back sections, use a pair of scissors to cut your hair to the desired length
  4. Move on to the front sections, using a mirror to help guide you
  5. Trim any uneven pieces until your hair is even all around
  6. Style your hair as desired

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Maintaining your short haircut is easy with these tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain shape
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage

FAQs About Women's Short Haircut Styles

1. What is the most popular women's short haircut style of 2021?

The most popular women's short haircut style of 2021 is the bob cut.

2. What is the easiest short haircut style to maintain?

The easiest short haircut style to maintain is the pixie cut.

3. Will a short haircut look good on me?

A short haircut can look good on anyone, as long as it's customized to suit your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some hair care tips specifically for short hair:

  • Get regular trims to maintain shape
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage
