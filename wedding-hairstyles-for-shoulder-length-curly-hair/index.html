Wedding Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Curly Hair A Guide

Written by Bardi May 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you a bride-to-be with curly hair and wondering what hairstyle would be perfect for your big day? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the best wedding hairstyles for shoulder length curly hair that will make you look stunning on your wedding day. Read on to find out more.

Description

Shoulder length curly hair can be tricky to style, especially for a big occasion like a wedding. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can create a beautiful and elegant hairstyle that complements your natural curls. There are a variety of styles to choose from, whether you prefer an updo or leaving your hair down. The key is to find a style that you feel comfortable with and that suits your overall bridal look.

How To

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and keep them in place. Next, use a comb or brush to detangle your hair and section it off into manageable pieces. You can then start styling your hair according to the hairstyle you have chosen.

Updo

An updo is a classic wedding hairstyle that works well for shoulder length curly hair. A low bun or chignon can look elegant and sophisticated, while a high bun or ponytail can give you a more playful and youthful look. You can also add braids or twists to your updo for added texture and detail.

Half Up, Half Down

If you prefer to leave some of your hair down, a half up, half down style can be a great option. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit your preferences. You can opt for a simple half up, half down style with a few curls left loose, or you can add braids or twists to create a more intricate look.

Side Swept

A side swept style is perfect for showcasing your curls while keeping them under control. You can sweep your hair to one side and pin it in place, leaving some curls to frame your face. This style works well with an off-the-shoulder dress or a dress with a low back.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve a simple updo for shoulder length curly hair:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band.
  3. Twist your ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Take small sections of hair from the front and sides of your head and twist them towards the back of your head.
  5. Secure the twisted sections with bobby pins, tucking them under the bun.
  6. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your shoulder length curly hair for your wedding:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use products that are specifically designed for curly hair to enhance your natural texture.
  • Consider adding accessories like flowers or a tiara to your hairstyle for added detail.
  • Do a trial run of your hairstyle before your wedding day to ensure that it looks the way you want it to.

FAQ

What should I do if it's raining on my wedding day?

If it's raining on your wedding day, you may need to adjust your hairstyle to accommodate the weather. Consider opting for a hairstyle that can be easily touched up, such as a low bun or a half up, half down style. You can also use products like hairspray and anti-frizz serum to help keep your hair in place.

Can I wear my hair down for my wedding?

Yes, you can definitely wear your hair down for your wedding. Just make sure that it is styled in a way that complements your dress and overall bridal look. You may also want to consider adding some accessories like a veil or hairpiece to your hairstyle.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your shoulder length curly hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and ideas, you can create a stunning wedding hairstyle for your shoulder length curly hair. Remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful on your special day!
