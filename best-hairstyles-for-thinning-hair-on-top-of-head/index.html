Best Hairstyles For Thinning Hair On Top Of Head

Written by Oliver Apr 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thinning hair on the top of your head, it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your needs. You want a style that not only looks good but also helps to hide your thinning hair. In this article, we will guide you through the best hairstyles for thinning hair on the top of your head.

Description

Thinning hair on top of the head is a common problem that affects both men and women. It can be caused by various factors such as genetics, hormonal changes, age, and stress. If you are suffering from thinning hair, you need not worry as there are many hairstyles that can help you to hide the thinning areas and give you a fuller look.

How to

Here are some tips on how to choose the best hairstyle for thinning hair on top of the head:

1. Short Hair

Short hair is an excellent option for those with thinning hair on top of the head. A short haircut can make your hair look thicker and fuller. You can opt for a buzz cut or a crew cut, which is a classic style that looks great on many face shapes.

2. Layered Hair

Layered hair is another great option for those with thinning hair. A layered cut can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller. You can also add highlights to your hair to create a more dimensional look.

3. Side Part

A side part is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for those with thinning hair on top of the head. By parting your hair on the side, you can create the illusion of fuller hair. You can also use a hair product to add volume to your hair and create a more textured look.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to create the best hairstyles for thinning hair on top of the head:

1. Short Hair

To create a short haircut, you will need to visit a professional stylist. They will be able to give you a buzz cut or a crew cut, which is a classic style that looks great on many face shapes.

2. Layered Hair

To create a layered haircut, you will need to visit a professional stylist. They will be able to add layers to your hair and create a more textured look. You can also add highlights to your hair to create a more dimensional look.

3. Side Part

To create a side part, you will need to use a comb to part your hair on the side. You can then use a hair product to add volume to your hair and create a more textured look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

1. Use a Quality Shampoo

Use a high-quality shampoo that is specifically designed for thinning hair. This will help to nourish your hair and make it look fuller.

2. Avoid Heat

Avoid using hot styling tools such as straighteners and curling irons. Heat can damage your hair and make it look thinner.

3. Use Hair Products

Use hair products such as volumizing mousse and hairspray to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have thinning hair on the top of my head?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair if you have thinning hair on the top of your head. However, you should avoid hairstyles that are too heavy or bulky, as they can make your hair look thinner.

Q: Is it necessary to visit a professional stylist to create these hairstyles?

A: It is recommended that you visit a professional stylist to create these hairstyles. They will be able to give you the best advice on what style will suit your face shape and hair type.

Q: Can I use hair extensions to hide my thinning hair?

A: Yes, you can use hair extensions to hide your thinning hair. However, it is important to choose extensions that match your hair color and texture.

Hair Care

To maintain your hair's health, you should follow a proper hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo, conditioning your hair, and using hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type. You should also avoid using hot styling tools too often and protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

In conclusion, there are many hairstyles that can help you to hide thinning hair on the top of your head. By choosing the right style and following a proper hair care routine, you can have healthy, beautiful hair that looks fuller and thicker.
