Is Grey Hair Trendy In 2020 A Complete Guide

Description

Grey hair has been a trend for a few years now and it seems that it's here to stay. Whether you're in your 20s or 50s, grey hair can be a stylish and glamorous choice. But is grey hair trendy in 2020? The answer is yes! More and more people are choosing to embrace their natural grey hair or dye their hair grey to keep up with the trend. In this article, we will guide you on how to achieve grey hair, tips on maintaining it, and other frequently asked questions.

How to Get Grey Hair

If you're looking to get grey hair, there are a few different options. You can either embrace your natural grey hair or dye your hair grey. Embracing your natural grey hair is the easiest option, but if you don't have grey hair yet, you can achieve it by dyeing your hair.

Embracing Your Natural Grey Hair

If you already have grey hair, then you're one step ahead! You can simply embrace your natural grey hair by keeping it healthy and well-maintained. This means using products that are specifically formulated for grey hair, such as shampoos and conditioners that help to enhance the silver tones and prevent yellowing.

Dyeing Your Hair Grey

If you don't have grey hair yet, you can achieve it by dyeing your hair. There are a few different ways to dye your hair grey, including:

  • Semi-permanent hair color
  • Permanent hair color
  • Hair toner

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're considering dyeing your hair grey, here is a step-by-step guide to achieve the look:

  1. Bleach your hair to a pale blonde color
  2. Apply the grey hair dye, making sure to follow the instructions on the packaging
  3. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time
  4. Rinse the dye out with cool water
  5. Apply a silver or purple toner to enhance the grey color
  6. Rinse the toner out with cool water

Tips for Maintaining Grey Hair

Maintaining grey hair can be a bit of a challenge, but with the right tips, it can be easy. Here are a few tips for maintaining grey hair:

  • Use a purple shampoo to prevent yellowing
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons to prevent damage to your hair
  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized

FAQ about Grey Hair

Q: Is grey hair suitable for all skin tones?

A: Yes, grey hair can look great on all skin tones. It's all about finding the right shade of grey that complements your skin tone.

Q: Will dyeing my hair grey damage my hair?

A: Dyeing your hair any color can cause damage, but if you take proper care of your hair and use high-quality products, you can minimize the damage.

Q: Can I dye my hair grey at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair grey at home, but it's important to follow the instructions on the packaging and take necessary precautions to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Grey Hair

If you have grey hair, it's important to use products that are specifically formulated for grey hair. Look for shampoos and conditioners that help to enhance the silver tones and prevent yellowing. You should also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can cause damage to your hair. Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, grey hair is trendy in 2020 and beyond, and it's a great choice for anyone who wants to embrace their natural hair color or try something new. Whether you're dyeing your hair grey or embracing your natural grey hair, make sure to take proper care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
