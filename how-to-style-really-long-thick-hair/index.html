How To Style Really Long Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have really long and thick hair, you may find it challenging to style your hair on a regular basis. The sheer volume and length of your hair can make it difficult to manage, leaving you frustrated and overwhelmed. But with the right tools and techniques, you can style your hair in a variety of ways that showcase its beauty and uniqueness. In this article, we'll walk you through some simple steps and tips on how to style really long thick hair.

Table of Contents:

  • Hair Care Tips
  • How to Style Really Long Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Long Thick Hair
  • FAQs

Hair Care Tips

Before we dive into styling, it's important to talk about hair care. Long and thick hair requires a lot of maintenance to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as it can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair while it's wet to prevent breakage.

How to Style Really Long Thick Hair

Now that you know how to take care of your hair, let's talk about how to style it. Here are some easy and stylish ways to style long thick hair:

1. Braids

Braids are a great option for long thick hair as they can help to keep your hair neat and tidy. You can try a simple braid, a fishtail braid, or even a French braid. Braids work well for both casual and formal occasions.

2. Ponytails

Ponytails are another classic hairstyle that works well for long thick hair. You can keep it simple with a low ponytail or add some volume with a high ponytail. You can also try a braided ponytail for a more intricate look.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a great way to show off your long thick hair while keeping it out of your face. You can add some curls or waves to the ends for a more romantic look.

4. Updos

Updos are a great option for formal occasions or when you want to add some elegance to your look. You can try a sleek bun, a messy bun, or a braided updo.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to do a simple braid:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the top of your head and divide it into three equal parts.
  3. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  4. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  5. Repeat steps 3-4 until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Tie the end of the braid with an elastic band.

Tips for Styling Long Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling long thick hair:

  • Use a volumizing spray to add volume to your hair.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add texture to your hair.
  • Invest in good quality hair accessories like hair clips, headbands, and scrunchies.
  • Experiment with different hair partings to change up your look.
  • Try different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

1. How often should I wash my long thick hair?

You should aim to wash your long thick hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from getting too oily or dry.

2. What hot tools should I use on my long thick hair?

You should use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools like a flat iron or curling iron. Aim to use these tools no more than once a week to prevent damage to your hair.

3. How can I prevent my long thick hair from getting tangled?

You can prevent tangling by brushing your hair regularly, using a detangling spray, and tying your hair up before going to bed.

4. How do I keep my long thick hair looking healthy?

Keeping your hair healthy involves regular hair trims, deep conditioning treatments, and avoiding excessive heat and chemicals. Be sure to use high-quality hair products that are gentle on your hair.

Styling long thick hair may seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, you'll be able to create a variety of beautiful looks. Be patient and experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you. And most importantly, don't forget to take care of your hair so that it stays healthy and shiny!
