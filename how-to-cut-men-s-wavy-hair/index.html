How To Cut Men s Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting men's hair can be a daunting task, especially when dealing with wavy or curly hair. However, with the right technique and tools, it is possible to achieve a great haircut. In this article, we will walk you through the steps on how to cut men's wavy hair, including tips and frequently asked questions.

How to Cut Men's Wavy Hair

Before starting, it is important to make sure you have the right tools, such as sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clippers. It is also recommended to work on dry hair, as wet hair can appear longer and cause uneven cuts.

Step 1: Preparation

Begin by washing and drying the hair, making sure it is clean and free of any product. Comb the hair to remove any tangles and create a part if desired.

Step 2: Clipper Work

Use hair clippers to trim the sides and back of the head, starting with a longer guard and gradually working your way down. Be sure to blend the hair at the top and bottom for a natural look.

Step 3: Scissor Work

Use scissors to trim the top of the hair, starting with the longer sections and gradually moving to the shorter ones. Use a comb to lift the hair and trim it to the desired length, taking care to follow the natural wave pattern.

Step 4: Detailing

Use scissors to trim any stray hairs and create texture as desired. Pay close attention to the hair around the ears and neckline, as these areas can be difficult to reach.

Step 5: Finishing

Finish the haircut by using scissors to trim the sideburns and any remaining facial hair. Run your fingers through the hair to check for any uneven sections or areas that need further trimming.

Tips for Cutting Men's Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting men's wavy hair:

  • Work on dry hair to achieve a more accurate cut.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and trim it to the desired length.
  • Cut the hair in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Be patient and take your time, especially with the detailing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should men with wavy hair get a haircut?

It is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the hair.

Can men with wavy hair get a buzz cut?

Yes, men with wavy hair can get a buzz cut, but it may not be as noticeable due to the texture of the hair.

How do I style men's wavy hair after a haircut?

Use a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to enhance the natural waves and add texture to the hair.

Hair Care for Men's Wavy Hair

In order to maintain healthy and vibrant wavy hair, it is important to follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle the hair, especially when wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to hydrate and protect the hair from damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, or use them sparingly with a heat protectant spray.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut men's wavy hair and achieve a great haircut. Remember to take your time and pay attention to detail, and your client will be thrilled with the results.
