How To Get Soft Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 09, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of having straight and boring hair? Do you want to spice up your look with some soft wavy hair? Well, you’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll show you how to get soft wavy hair in simple and easy steps. From hair care to styling, we’ve got you covered. So, let’s get started!

Table of Content

  • Hair Care
  • How to Get Soft Wavy Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Hair Care

Before we dive into how to get soft wavy hair, let’s talk about hair care. Proper hair care is essential to achieve healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

Following these simple tips can make a huge difference in the health and appearance of your hair. Now, let’s move on to how to get soft wavy hair.

How to Get Soft Wavy Hair

To get soft wavy hair, you’ll need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Instead, use lukewarm water to wash your hair.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant

If you’re going to use hot tools to create soft waves, make sure to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Use a hair dryer to dry your hair. Make sure to use a diffuser attachment to create soft waves. To do this, flip your head upside down and dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will create volume and texture in your hair.

Step 4: Use a curling iron

Use a curling iron to create soft waves in your hair. Start by sectioning your hair into small sections. Wrap each section around the curling iron and hold it for a few seconds. Release the curling iron and let the hair cool down. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 5: Brush out your waves

Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to gently brush out your waves. This will create soft and natural-looking waves in your hair.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to get soft wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  2. Apply a heat protectant
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment
  4. Section your hair into small sections
  5. Use a curling iron to create soft waves in each section
  6. Brush out your waves using a wide-toothed comb or your fingers

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use a curling iron with a barrel size that suits your hair length
  • Don’t hold the curling iron on your hair for too long, as it can damage your hair
  • Apply a hair spray or texturizing spray to hold your waves in place
  • Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for you

FAQ

Q: Can I get soft wavy hair without using hot tools?

A: Yes, you can use other techniques such as braiding or twisting your hair to create soft waves.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week.

Q: Can I use a hair mask instead of a conditioner?

A: Yes, you can use a hair mask instead of a conditioner once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.

Conclusion

Getting soft wavy hair is easy if you follow these simple steps. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right tools and products to achieve healthy and beautiful hair. With a little patience and practice, you can rock soft wavy hair like a pro!
