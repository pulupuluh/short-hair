How To Change Your Hairstyle In Animal Crossing

Written by Christine May 06, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’re an avid player of Animal Crossing, you know just how important it is to have a unique and stylish look in the game. One great way to change up your appearance is by changing your hairstyle. In this tutorial, we’ll go over how to change your hairstyle in Animal Crossing step by step.

How To

To change your hairstyle in Animal Crossing, you’ll need to visit the in-game salon called Shampoodle. This salon is run by a poodle named Harriet who will help you change your hairstyle. Here’s how to do it:

Step 1: Unlock Shampoodle

Before you can change your hairstyle, you’ll need to unlock Shampoodle. To do this, you’ll need to spend at least 10,000 Bells at the Able Sisters’ shop. Once you’ve done this, a new building will appear in your town called the Dream Suite. Visit the Dream Suite at least once, and the next time you play, Shampoodle will be unlocked.

Step 2: Visit Shampoodle

Once you’ve unlocked Shampoodle, you can visit the salon by going to the second floor of the Able Sisters’ shop. Harriet will be waiting for you.

Step 3: Choose Your Style

Harriet will ask you a series of questions about your current hairstyle and what you’d like to change. Answer her questions honestly and choose the hairstyle you want. You can choose from a variety of styles, including short, medium, and long hair, as well as curly, straight, and wavy hair.

Step 4: Pay for Your New Hairstyle

Once you’ve chosen your new hairstyle, Harriet will ask you to pay 3,000 Bells. Pay the fee and your new hairstyle will be complete!

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing:

  • Make sure you have enough Bells to pay for your new hairstyle before visiting Shampoodle.
  • Be honest with Harriet about what you want. She’ll be able to give you the best hairstyle if you’re specific.
  • Make sure you’re happy with your new hairstyle before leaving the salon. You won’t be able to change it again for at least a week.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing:

How often can I change my hairstyle?

You can change your hairstyle once a day. However, you’ll need to wait at least a week before changing it again.

Can I choose any hairstyle I want?

No. The hairstyles available at Shampoodle are based on your character’s gender and the length of their current hair.

How much does it cost to change my hairstyle?

It costs 3,000 Bells to change your hairstyle.

Hair Care

Just like in real life, taking care of your hair in Animal Crossing is important. Make sure to visit Shampoodle regularly to keep your hair looking its best. You can also use hair products like hair spray and hair gel to keep your hair in place.

Changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is a fun and easy way to switch up your look. Follow these steps to get a new hairstyle and make sure to take care of your hair to keep it looking great.
