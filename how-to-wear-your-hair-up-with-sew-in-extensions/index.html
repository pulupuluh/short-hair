How To Wear Your Hair Up With Sew In Extensions

Written by Petter Feb 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In this article, we will discuss how to wear your hair up with sew-in extensions. Sew-in extensions are a great way to add length and volume to your hair, and they can be styled in many different ways. However, wearing your hair up with sew-in extensions can be a bit tricky, but with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a beautiful and secure updo.

How to Wear Your Hair Up with Sew-In Extensions

If you have sew-in extensions, you might be wondering how to wear your hair up without revealing the wefts or damaging your natural hair. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Before starting, make sure your hair is brushed and tangle-free. Use a wide-tooth comb or a paddle brush to remove any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small parts, leaving the top part of your hair out. Secure each section with a hair clip or elastic band.

Step 3: Create a High Ponytail

Gather the top part of your hair into a high ponytail using an elastic band. Make sure the ponytail is tight and secure.

Step 4: Create a Braid

Braid the ponytail and secure the end with another elastic band. You can create a simple three-strand braid or a more intricate braid, depending on your preference.

Step 5: Wrap the Braid Around the Ponytail

Wrap the braid around the base of the ponytail, covering the elastic band. Use bobby pins to secure the braid in place.

Step 6: Add Texture

If you want to add texture to your updo, you can use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls. Just be careful not to damage the extensions or your natural hair.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your updo with a light hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing your hair up with sew-in extensions:

  • Use quality hair extensions that match your natural hair color and texture.
  • Avoid using too much tension when creating the ponytail or braids to prevent damage to your natural hair.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to hide them in the updo.
  • Experiment with different updo styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Can I wear my hair up with sew-in extensions?

Yes, you can wear your hair up with sew-in extensions, but you need to be careful not to reveal the wefts or damage your natural hair.

How do I hide the wefts when wearing my hair up?

You can hide the wefts by wearing a wig cap or using a hairnet, or by securing them with bobby pins or hair glue.

Can I use heat tools on my sew-in extensions?

Yes, you can use heat tools on your sew-in extensions, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid applying too much heat.

Hair Care

To keep your sew-in extensions looking their best, you need to take care of them properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products or oils that can weigh down your hair.
  • Don't sleep with wet hair or in a rough pillowcase that can cause tangling.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your sew-in extensions.

By following these tips and steps, you can wear your hair up with sew-in extensions confidently and stylishly.
