How To Maintain Soft Dread Curls A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver May 11, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you are someone who loves to experiment with your hair, you might have tried different hairstyles over the years. In recent times, soft dread curls have become increasingly popular among women of all ages. But maintaining these curls can be a bit of a challenge, especially if you have relaxed hair. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to maintain soft dread curls in relaxed hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Maintain Soft Dread Curls
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Soft Dread Curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Soft Dread Curls

Description

Soft dread curls are a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. These curls are created using a special technique that involves twisting the hair into tight coils. The result is a beautiful and bouncy hairstyle that looks great on all hair types. However, maintaining these curls can be a challenge, especially if you have relaxed hair. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to maintain soft dread curls in relaxed hair.

How to Maintain Soft Dread Curls

Maintaining soft dread curls in relaxed hair can be a bit challenging. But with the right techniques and products, you can keep your curls looking great for weeks. Here are some tips on how to maintain soft dread curls:

1. Moisturize your hair regularly

Moisture is key when it comes to maintaining soft dread curls. Relaxed hair tends to be dry and brittle, which can cause your curls to lose their shape. To avoid this, make sure to moisturize your hair regularly. Use a leave-in conditioner or moisturizer to keep your hair hydrated.

2. Avoid using heat

Heat can damage your hair and cause your curls to lose their shape. Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons. Instead, opt for heatless styling methods like bantu knots or perm rods to maintain your curls.

3. Use a satin pillowcase

Sleeping on a cotton pillowcase can cause your hair to lose moisture and create frizz. To avoid this, use a satin pillowcase. Satin is a smooth and soft material that reduces friction and helps to keep your curls intact.

4. Don't over-wash your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip away its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. To maintain soft dread curls, it is important to avoid over-washing your hair. Instead, wash your hair once a week with a sulfate-free shampoo.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to maintain soft dread curls:

Step 1: Moisturize your hair

Apply a leave-in conditioner or moisturizer to your hair to keep it hydrated.

Step 2: Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently. Avoid using a brush as it can cause your curls to lose their shape.

Step 3: Style your hair

Use a styling cream or gel to define your curls. Twist your hair into coils and let it air dry or use a hooded dryer.

Step 4: Protect your hair at night

Use a satin scarf or bonnet to protect your curls while you sleep.

Tips for Maintaining Soft Dread Curls

Here are some additional tips for maintaining soft dread curls:

1. Avoid using heavy products

Heavy products can weigh down your curls and make them lose their shape. Use lightweight products that won't weigh down your hair.

2. Don't touch your hair too much

Touching your hair too much can cause your curls to become frizzy. Avoid touching your hair too often.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

The sun can dry out your hair and cause your curls to become brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to protect your hair from the sun.

FAQs

Q. Can I maintain soft dread curls in relaxed hair?

A. Yes, you can maintain soft dread curls in relaxed hair by following the tips mentioned in this article.

Q. How often should I moisturize my hair?

A. You should moisturize your hair regularly, at least once a day.

Q. Can I use a brush to detangle my hair?

A. No, you should avoid using a brush to detangle your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb instead.

Hair Care Tips for Soft Dread Curls

Here are some additional hair care tips for soft dread curls:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Trimming your hair regularly will help to prevent split ends and breakage, which can cause your curls to lose their shape.

2. Avoid using harsh chemicals

Harsh chemicals like relaxers and dyes can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Avoid using these chemicals if possible.

3. Eat a healthy diet

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals will help to keep your hair healthy and strong.

By following the tips mentioned in this article, you can maintain soft dread curls in relaxed hair and keep your hair looking great for weeks. Remember to moisturize your hair regularly, avoid using heat, and protect your hair from the sun. With the right techniques and products, you can rock soft dread curls with confidence!
