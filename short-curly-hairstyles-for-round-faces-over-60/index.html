Short Curly Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 60

Written by Oliver May 28, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you over 60 with a round face looking for a new hairstyle? Look no further! Short curly hairstyles are perfect for round faces as they add volume and texture to your hair, making your face appear longer and slimmer. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect short curly hairstyle for round faces over 60, along with some tips and frequently asked questions.

Description

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and lose its natural volume. This is where short curly hairstyles come in handy. They add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker. Short curly hairstyles are perfect for round faces as they add height to your hair, making your face appear longer and slimmer.

How to

To achieve the perfect short curly hairstyle for round faces over 60, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a nourishing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt and buildup from your hair, leaving it clean and soft.

Step 2: Apply Mousse

Apply a dollop of mousse to your damp hair, starting from the roots to the tips. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 3: Blow-Dry

Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on a low heat setting. This will help to enhance your natural curls and add volume to your hair.

Step 4: Style

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define your curls. Take small sections of your hair and wrap them around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Continue until all your hair is curled.

Step 5: Finish

Finish off your look by using a hairspray to hold your curls in place. You can also add some shine serum to add some extra shine to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short curly hairstyle for round faces over 60:

  • Use a lightweight mousse to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural curls.
  • Use a curling iron to define your curls and add texture to your hair.
  • Finish off your look with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.
  • Use a shine serum to add some extra shine to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short curly hairstyles for round faces over 60:

What are the best short curly hairstyles for round faces over 60?

The best short curly hairstyles for round faces over 60 are those that add volume and texture to your hair. Some great options include a bob, a pixie cut, or a layered haircut.

How do I style short curly hair?

To style short curly hair, start by applying a lightweight mousse to your damp hair. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment, then use a curling iron to define your curls. Finish off your look with a hairspray and some shine serum.

What hair care products should I use for short curly hair?

You should use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, a lightweight mousse, a curl-defining cream, and a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Hair Care

To keep your short curly hairstyle looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a nourishing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dyes or relaxers.

Now that you know how to achieve the perfect short curly hairstyle for round faces over 60, it's time to try it out for yourself! Remember to follow our tips and hair care advice to keep your hair looking its best.
