Which Short Hairstyle Is Good For Round Face

Written by Bardi Apr 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a short hairstyle that will flatter your round face? You've come to the right place! In this article, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for round faces, including tips on how to style and care for your hair. So, let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose a Short Hairstyle for Round Face
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling a Short Hairstyle for Round Face
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle for Round Face
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Short Hairstyle for Round Face

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a soft jawline. The goal of a short hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles, which will help balance out the fullness of the cheeks and chin. The ideal short hairstyle for a round face should be layered, with volume at the crown and texture throughout the hair.

How to Choose a Short Hairstyle for Round Face

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, consider the following:

  • Length: Short hairstyles that fall above the chin are ideal for round faces, as they help elongate the face.
  • Layers: Layers add volume and texture to the hair, creating the illusion of length and angles.
  • Side-swept bangs: Side-swept bangs help elongate the face and create the illusion of angles.
  • Avoid blunt cuts: Blunt cuts can make a round face look even fuller.

Step by Step Guide to Styling a Short Hairstyle for Round Face

Follow these steps to style your short hair for a round face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting at the crown to add volume.
  4. Create texture in your hair using a texturizing spray or wax.
  5. If you have side-swept bangs, blow-dry them to the side using a round brush.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle for Round Face

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hairstyle for a round face:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can damage it.
  • If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, use a diffuser when blow-drying to enhance your natural texture.
  • If you have straight hair, use a flat iron to create sleek, angled lines.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for round faces:

  • Can I wear a bob if I have a round face? Yes, a bob can be a great short hairstyle for a round face, as long as it is layered and textured.
  • What is the best haircut for a round face with thick hair? A layered pixie cut can be a great option for a round face with thick hair, as it adds volume and texture while keeping the hair short and manageable.
  • Can I wear bangs with a short hairstyle for a round face? Yes, side-swept bangs can be a great addition to a short hairstyle for a round face, as they help elongate the face and create the illusion of angles.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyle for Round Face

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • If you color your hair, use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid using too many styling products on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

Now that you know which short hairstyle is good for round face, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new look!
