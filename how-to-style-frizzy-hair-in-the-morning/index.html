How To Style Frizzy Hair In The Morning A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 07, 2023 · 9 min read

Frizzy hair can be a nightmare to deal with, especially in the morning. But with the right styling techniques and products, you can tame your frizz and have beautiful, manageable hair all day long. In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style frizzy hair in the morning, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Frizzy Hair in the Morning
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Frizzy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Frizzy Hair

How to Style Frizzy Hair in the Morning

Styling frizzy hair in the morning can be a time-consuming process, but with the right products and techniques, you can make it a breeze. Here are some tips to help you style your frizzy hair:

1. Start with a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

To keep your frizzy hair under control, start with a good shampoo and conditioner that are designed to hydrate and nourish your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, or shea butter, which are known for their moisturizing properties.

2. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

After you’ve washed your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized throughout the day. This will also help to keep your frizz under control and make your hair easier to style.

3. Apply a Frizz-Control Serum

A frizz-control serum can be a lifesaver when it comes to styling frizzy hair in the morning. Apply a small amount of serum to your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that tend to get particularly frizzy.

4. Blow-Dry Your Hair on Low Heat

Blow-drying your hair on high heat can actually make your frizz worse. Instead, use a low-heat setting and a diffuser attachment to dry your hair. This will help to keep your hair smooth and frizz-free.

5. Use a Flat Iron or Curling Iron

If you want to add some extra style to your hair, use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools to protect your hair from damage.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you style your frizzy hair in the morning:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After you’ve washed your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized.

Step 3: Apply a Frizz-Control Serum

Apply a small amount of frizz-control serum to your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that tend to get particularly frizzy.

Step 4: Blow-Dry Your Hair on Low Heat

Blow-dry your hair on low heat using a diffuser attachment to keep your hair smooth and frizz-free.

Step 5: Use a Flat Iron or Curling Iron

If desired, use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls. Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools.

Tips for Styling Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your frizzy hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling and damaging your hair.

2. Don’t Over-Wash Your Hair

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, leading to more frizz. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

3. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and keep your hair looking smooth and shiny.

4. Try a Hair Mask

Using a hair mask once a week can help to nourish and hydrate your hair, reducing frizz and making your hair easier to style.

FAQs

Q: How do I prevent my hair from getting frizzy?

A: To prevent frizzy hair, make sure to use a good shampoo and conditioner that are designed to hydrate your hair. Use a leave-in conditioner and frizz-control serum, and avoid washing your hair too frequently.

Q: Can I use oil to control frizz?

A: Yes, you can use oil to control frizz. Apply a small amount of argan oil, coconut oil, or another type of hair oil to your hair to help keep it moisturized and frizz-free.

Q: How do I style my hair if I don’t have time to blow-dry it?

A: If you don’t have time to blow-dry your hair, try using a hair diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help to keep your hair smooth and frizz-free without taking as much time as a full blow-dry.

Hair Care Tips for Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you manage your frizzy hair:

1. Use a Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Sulfates can be harsh on your hair and strip it of its natural oils, leading to more frizz. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to help keep your hair moisturized.

2. Avoid Brushing Your Hair When Wet

Brushing your hair when it’s wet can cause it to break and lead to more frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

A deep conditioning treatment can help to nourish and hydrate your hair, reducing frizz and making your hair easier to style.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and frizz-free.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily style your frizzy hair in the morning and enjoy beautiful, manageable hair all day long.
