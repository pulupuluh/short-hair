Cutting Men s Hair Bald On Top A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy May 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

In recent years, cutting men's hair bald on top has become a popular hairstyle. This trend has been adopted by many men who want to achieve an edgy and modern look. However, it is important to note that cutting hair bald on top requires skill and precision. In this guide, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to achieve this hairstyle, as well as tips and information on hair care.

Description

The bald on top hairstyle is achieved by cutting the hair on the top of the head very short or completely shaved off, while leaving the sides and back longer. This creates a contrast between the bald top and longer sides and back. It is a popular hairstyle for men who want a low-maintenance look that is both stylish and modern.

How to Cut Men's Hair Bald on Top

Step 1: Preparation

Before you start cutting, make sure that the hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that the cut is even. You will need a pair of clippers, a comb, and a pair of scissors.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Decide on the length you want for the top of the head. Use the clippers to cut the hair as short as possible. If you want a completely bald look, use a razor to shave off the hair completely. Be careful when using a razor and make sure to use a shaving cream to avoid irritation.

Step 3: Blend the Sides and Back

Use the clippers to blend the sides and back of the head with the top. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to make sure that the transition is smooth. Make sure to check for any unevenness and make adjustments as necessary.

Step 4: Clean Up

Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs and make sure that the cut is even. Use the clippers to trim the hair around the neck and ears. Clean up any loose hairs and wash the hair to remove any clippings.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair Bald on Top

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting men's hair bald on top:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to ensure that the cut is even.
  • Use a mirror to check for any unevenness and make adjustments as necessary.
  • Be careful when using a razor and make sure to use a shaving cream to avoid irritation.
  • Use a moisturizing lotion or oil to keep the scalp moisturized and prevent dryness.

FAQs

Is it easy to cut men's hair bald on top?

While cutting men's hair bald on top may seem simple, it requires skill and precision. It is best to have a professional do the cut if you are not experienced.

Can men with any face shape pull off the bald on top hairstyle?

Yes, men with any face shape can pull off the bald on top hairstyle. However, it is important to work with a professional stylist to determine the best cut for your face shape.

How often should I get a haircut if I have a bald on top hairstyle?

You should get a haircut every 2-4 weeks to maintain the bald on top hairstyle. This will ensure that the cut remains even and the contrast between the bald top and longer sides and back is maintained.

Hair Care for Men's Bald on Top Hairstyle

It is important to take care of the scalp and hair after cutting men's hair bald on top. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo to wash the hair and scalp.
  • Apply a moisturizing lotion or oil to the scalp to prevent dryness.
  • Wear a hat or use sunscreen to protect the scalp from sunburn.
  • Use a soft-bristled brush to stimulate blood flow to the scalp.

Cutting men's hair bald on top is a stylish and modern hairstyle that requires skill and precision. By following the steps and tips outlined in this guide, you can achieve a great look that is both edgy and low-maintenance.
