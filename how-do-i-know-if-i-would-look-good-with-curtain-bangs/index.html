How Do I Know If I Would Look Good With Curtain Bangs

Written by Daniel Jun 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're thinking about getting a new hairstyle, you may be considering curtain bangs. These bangs are a popular trend that has been seen on many celebrities and influencers. But how do you know if curtain bangs will suit you? In this article, we will discuss how to determine if curtain bangs will complement your face shape and hair type.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Considering Your Hair Type
  • Pros and Cons of Curtain Bangs
  • Styling Tips for Curtain Bangs
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Determining Your Face Shape

Before deciding on whether or not to get curtain bangs, it's important to determine your face shape. Certain hairstyles can enhance your best features while others can accentuate your flaws. To determine your face shape, you can use a mirror and measuring tape or an online face shape calculator.

The most common face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Oval
  • Diamond

Curtain bangs can work well with oval and heart-shaped faces. If you have a round or square face, you may want to consider a different style of bangs as curtain bangs may make your face appear wider. If you have a diamond-shaped face, you may want to avoid curtain bangs as they may emphasize your sharp features.

Considering Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider is your hair type. Curtain bangs work best with medium to long hair that is straight or wavy. If you have curly hair, curtain bangs may not sit well and may require more styling effort. Additionally, if you have thin hair, curtain bangs may make your hair appear even thinner.

Pros and Cons of Curtain Bangs

Like any hairstyle, curtain bangs have their pros and cons. Some of the pros include:

  • They can frame your face and enhance your features
  • They can add dimension to your hair
  • They can be styled in various ways

Some of the cons include:

  • They require regular maintenance and trimming
  • They may not work well with certain face shapes and hair types
  • They may require more styling effort than other types of bangs

Styling Tips for Curtain Bangs

If you decide to get curtain bangs, here are some styling tips:

  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume
  • Use a straightener to create a sleek and polished look
  • Add texture with a sea salt spray or texturizing spray
  • Part your bangs in the middle to create the signature curtain effect

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

Q: Will curtain bangs suit my face shape?

A: Curtain bangs work best with oval and heart-shaped faces. If you have a round or square face, you may want to consider a different style of bangs.

Q: How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

A: You should trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.

Q: Can I style my curtain bangs in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your curtain bangs in various ways, such as parting them in the middle or to the side, or sweeping them to one side.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair and bangs regularly to avoid buildup and oiliness
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your bangs between washes
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your bangs
  • Protect your hair and bangs from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray

If you're still unsure if curtain bangs will suit you, consult with a hairstylist who can give you personalized advice based on your face shape and hair type. With the right cut and styling, curtain bangs can be a chic and fashionable addition to your look!
