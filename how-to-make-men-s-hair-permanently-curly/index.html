How to Make Men's Hair Permanently Curly - A Complete Guide

If you are tired of having straight hair and want to have curly locks permanently, then you are in the right place! In this guide, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to make men's hair permanently curly. Read on to find out more.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Make Men's Hair Permanently Curly
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Making Men's Hair Permanently Curly
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Curly Hair

Description

Permanently curly hair is a great way to change your look and add some texture to your hair. However, it is important to note that making men's hair permanently curly is a long-term commitment and requires a lot of effort and maintenance. There are several methods to make men's hair permanently curly, including chemical treatments, heat styling, and natural methods. In this guide, we will focus on chemical treatments as they provide long-lasting results.

How to Make Men's Hair Permanently Curly

Before we begin, it is essential to prepare your hair for the chemical treatment. This includes washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and oils. It is also recommended to deep condition your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy. Once your hair is ready, you can choose from the following chemical treatments:

Brazilian Keratin Treatment

Brazilian Keratin Treatment, also known as BKT, is a popular method to make men's hair permanently curly. This treatment involves applying a keratin-based solution to your hair, which is then sealed with a flat iron. The keratin smooths out the hair cuticle, making it straight, but also adds a slight curl to the ends. The results can last up to 6 months, depending on your hair type and maintenance.

Perms

Perms are a classic method to make men's hair permanently curly. This method involves applying a chemical solution to your hair, which breaks down the protein structure and reforms it into a curly pattern. The hair is then wrapped around rods, and a neutralizer is applied to set the curls. The results can last up to 3 months, depending on your hair type and maintenance.

Japanese Hair Straightening

Japanese Hair Straightening, also known as thermal reconditioning, is a method that can make men's hair permanently curly or permanently straight. This treatment involves applying a chemical solution to your hair, which breaks down the protein structure and reforms it into a straight pattern. The hair is then flat ironed to set the straight pattern. The results can last up to 6 months, depending on your hair type and maintenance.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make men's hair permanently curly using the Brazilian Keratin Treatment:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and oils.
  2. Blow-dry your hair until it is completely dry.
  3. Divide your hair into sections.
  4. Apply the keratin-based solution to each section of your hair, making sure to saturate it completely.
  5. Comb through your hair to ensure the solution is evenly distributed.
  6. Allow the solution to sit for the recommended time, usually around 30 minutes.
  7. Rinse your hair thoroughly with water.
  8. Blow-dry your hair until it is completely dry.
  9. Flat iron your hair to seal the keratin and create the desired curl pattern.

Tips for Making Men's Hair Permanently Curly

Here are some tips for making men's hair permanently curly:

  • Choose a method that suits your hair type and lifestyle.
  • Follow the instructions carefully and do not leave the chemical solution on for longer than recommended.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to maintain the health of your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your curly hair to prevent damage and frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.

FAQs

How long does the Brazilian Keratin Treatment last?

The Brazilian Keratin Treatment can last up to 6 months, depending on your hair type and maintenance.

Can I color my hair after getting a perm?

It is recommended to wait at least 2 weeks before coloring your hair after getting a perm to prevent damage.

Can I swim with permanently curly hair?

It is recommended to wear a swim cap to protect your curly hair from the chlorine and saltwater.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Maintaining curly hair requires some effort and care. Here are some tips to keep your curly hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your curly hair when it is dry to prevent frizz.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and blow dryers, on your curly hair.

With these tips and methods, you can make men's hair permanently curly and enjoy a new, stylish look. Remember to take care of your curly hair to maintain its health and longevity. Happy styling!
