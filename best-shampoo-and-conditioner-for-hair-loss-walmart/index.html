How to use

Using the OGX Biotin and Collagen Shampoo and Conditioner is easy. Here's how:

Shampoo

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly.
  2. Apply a generous amount of shampoo to your scalp and massage it in with your fingertips.
  3. Rinse thoroughly with warm water.
  4. Repeat if necessary.

Conditioner

  1. After shampooing, apply a generous amount of conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  2. Leave it on for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Rinse thoroughly with cool water.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use the OGX Biotin and Collagen Shampoo and Conditioner:

  1. Wet your hair thoroughly.
  2. Apply a generous amount of shampoo to your scalp and massage it in with your fingertips.
  3. Rinse thoroughly with warm water.
  4. Apply a generous amount of conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  5. Leave it on for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Rinse thoroughly with cool water.
  7. Gently towel dry your hair.
  8. Apply a hair serum or oil to the ends of your hair.
  9. Style as desired.

Tips for Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as blow dryers and curling irons, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly into a ponytail or bun, as this can cause tension and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth, such as biotin, iron, and vitamin C.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
