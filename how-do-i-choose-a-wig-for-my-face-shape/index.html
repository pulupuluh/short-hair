How Do I Choose A Wig For My Face Shape

Written by William Mar 20, 2023 Â· 11 min read

Choosing the right wig for your face shape can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. With a few simple tips and tricks, you can find the perfect wig that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose a wig for your face shape, step by step.

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a wig for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. Take note of the widest part of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the length of your face.

If your face is widest at the cheekbones and tapers down to a narrow chin, you have a heart-shaped face. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have a square-shaped face. If your face is longer than it is wide and has a pointy chin, you have an oblong face. If your face is widest at the temples and narrows at the jawline and forehead, you have a diamond-shaped face. If your face is longer than it is wide and has a rounded chin, you have an oval-shaped face. And finally, if your face is as wide as it is long, you have a round-shaped face.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can move on to choosing the right wig type.

Choosing the Right Wig Type

There are several different types of wigs to choose from, including synthetic wigs, human hair wigs, lace front wigs, and full lace wigs. The type of wig you choose will depend on your budget, lifestyle, and personal preferences.

If you're on a budget, a synthetic wig may be a good option for you. Synthetic wigs are made from synthetic fibers and are often less expensive than human hair wigs. They also require less maintenance and can be worn right out of the box.

If you're looking for a more natural look, a human hair wig may be a better option. Human hair wigs are made from real human hair and can be styled just like your natural hair. They are also more durable than synthetic wigs and can last for several years with proper care.

If you're looking for a wig that blends in seamlessly with your natural hairline, a lace front wig or full lace wig may be the way to go. Lace front wigs have a piece of lace at the front of the wig that mimics a natural hairline, while full lace wigs have a piece of lace that covers the entire head, allowing for more styling options.

Deciding on Hair Length

The length of your wig should also be taken into consideration when choosing a wig for your face shape. If you have a round face, a longer wig can help elongate your face and create a more balanced look. If you have a square face, a shorter wig can soften your features and create a more feminine look.

For heart-shaped faces, a chin-length bob or layered wig can help balance out a wider forehead. For oblong faces, a wig with volume at the sides can help create the illusion of width. For diamond-shaped faces, a wig with volume at the crown can help balance out a narrow chin.

Picking the Right Wig Color

When it comes to choosing the right wig color for your face shape, consider your skin tone and natural hair color. If you have a warm skin tone, opt for wig colors with warm undertones, such as golden browns and honey blondes. If you have a cool skin tone, go for wig colors with cool undertones, such as ash blondes and cool browns.

If you're not sure what wig color to choose, consider trying on a few different shades to see which one complements your skin tone and features the best.

Styling Your Wig

Once you've chosen the right wig type, length, and color for your face shape, it's time to style your wig. Keep in mind that not all wig styles will work for all face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, avoid wigs with too much volume at the sides, as this can make your face appear wider.

If you're unsure about how to style your wig, consider going to a professional stylist who can help you create a look that complements your face shape and features.

Caring for Your Wig

Proper care is essential for keeping your wig looking its best. Be sure to follow the manufacturer's instructions for washing and styling your wig. Use only products that are specifically designed for wigs, as regular hair care products can damage the fibers.

When you're not wearing your wig, store it on a wig stand or in a wig box to help maintain its shape. Avoid exposing your wig to excessive heat or sunlight, as this can cause the fibers to become brittle and break.

FAQs

Q: How do I know if a wig will fit my head?

A: Most wigs come in standard sizes that will fit the average head. If you're unsure about the size, measure your head and compare it to the manufacturer's size chart.

Q: Can I wear a wig if I have a sensitive scalp?

A: Yes, there are wigs available that are specifically designed for people with sensitive scalps. Look for wigs that have a soft, breathable cap and that are made from hypoallergenic materials.

Q: Can I style my wig with heat tools?

A: It depends on the type of wig you have. Synthetic wigs cannot be styled with heat tools, as the fibers will melt. Human hair wigs can be styled with heat tools, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray before applying heat.

Choosing the right wig for your face shape can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following these tips and tricks, you can find a wig that enhances your natural beauty and boosts your confidence.
