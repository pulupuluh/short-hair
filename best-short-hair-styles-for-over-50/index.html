Best Short Hair Styles For Over 50

Written by Daniel Mar 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, our hair goes through many changes. We may notice that our hair is thinning, losing its natural color, or becoming more brittle. However, that doesn't mean we can't still rock a stylish haircut. In fact, short haircuts are a great option for women over 50. They're easy to manage, can help take years off your face, and can be incredibly versatile.

  • How to Choose the Best Short Hair Style for Over 50
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair
  • FAQ about Short Hair Styles for Over 50

How to Choose the Best Short Hair Style for Over 50

Choosing the right short haircut can be a daunting task, but with a few tips, it can be a breeze. First, consider your face shape. A short haircut can highlight your best features and minimize any flaws. If you have a round face, consider a bob or pixie cut. If you have a square face, go for a layered cut with soft edges. If you have a heart-shaped face, try a short crop or a chin-length bob. And if you have an oval face, you're in luck! You can pretty much rock any short haircut.

Next, consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, a layered cut can add volume and dimension. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or a pixie cut can help tame your hair and make it more manageable.

Finally, consider your personal style. Do you want something edgy and bold or something classic and timeless? Do you want to add some color or keep it natural? The possibilities are endless!

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Styling short hair can be quick and easy. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or a styling cream, to add volume and hold.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and dimension.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Short haircuts require less maintenance than longer styles, but there are still a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

FAQ about Short Hair Styles for Over 50

Q: Can women over 50 still rock a short haircut?

A: Absolutely! Short haircuts can be incredibly stylish and flattering on women of all ages.

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. In fact, a well-styled short haircut can take years off your face.

Q: What if I don't like my short haircut?

A: Hair grows back! If you're not happy with your short haircut, you can always grow it out or try a different style.

Hair Care Tips for Women Over 50

As we age, our hair needs change. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often.
  • Consider using a hair growth serum to help promote thicker, fuller hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

With these tips and the right short haircut, you can look and feel your best at any age. So why not take the plunge and try a new short hairstyle?
