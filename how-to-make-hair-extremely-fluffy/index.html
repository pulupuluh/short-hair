How To Make Hair Extremely Fluffy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to add some serious volume to your hair, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll take you through the steps to create hair that's so fluffy, it'll be the envy of everyone you meet. From products to techniques, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • Section 1: Prep Your Hair
  • Section 2: Blow-Dry Your Hair
  • Section 3: Add Volume
  • Section 4: Finish Your Look
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Section 1: Prep Your Hair

The first step to achieving fluffy hair is to start with clean, freshly washed hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some extra oomph to your locks. Towel dry your hair, being careful not to rub too vigorously as this can cause breakage and frizz.

Next, apply a lightweight, volumizing mousse or foam to your hair. This will help to add body and hold to your style. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Section 2: Blow-Dry Your Hair

To create maximum volume, it's important to blow-dry your hair upside down. Start by flipping your head over and using your fingers to lift and separate your hair. Use a large, round brush to help smooth your hair and create volume at the roots.

As you blow-dry, focus on the roots of your hair, using a high heat setting to help lift and create volume. Once your roots are dry, flip your hair back over and continue to blow-dry, using a medium heat setting to smooth and shape your hair.

Section 3: Add Volume

Once your hair is dry, it's time to add even more volume. Use a teasing brush or comb to backcomb your hair at the crown. This will create a lifted, voluminous look. Be careful not to backcomb too aggressively, as this can damage your hair.

You can also use hot rollers or a curling iron to add curls and volume to your hair. Wrap sections of your hair around the rollers or iron, being sure to start at the roots for maximum lift. Once your hair has cooled, gently brush out the curls for a softer, more natural look.

Section 4: Finish Your Look

To finish your fluffy hair look, use a lightweight hairspray to hold everything in place. Avoid using heavy, sticky products that can weigh down your hair and undo all your hard work.

You can also use a dry shampoo to add even more volume and texture to your hair. Simply spray onto your roots and massage in with your fingertips.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve fluffy hair with naturally curly hair?

A: Absolutely! Just be sure to use products and techniques that enhance and define your curls, rather than weighing them down.

Q: How often should I wash my hair to achieve fluffy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Generally, washing your hair every other day or every three days is sufficient to maintain a healthy scalp and hair.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking its fluffiest, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using heavy, greasy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to prevent breakage and damage.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve hair that's so fluffy, you'll feel like you're walking on clouds. So go ahead, give it a try and see the amazing results for yourself!
