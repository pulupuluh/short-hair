How To Fade A Mullet A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In 2023, mullets have made a comeback in the fashion world. However, not everyone wants to keep their mullet looking the same all the time. If you want to know how to fade a mullet, you've come to the right place. This guide will take you through the steps to achieving a fade that looks good and lasts.

How to Fade a Mullet

Before we dive into the steps, let's define what a mullet is. A mullet is a hairstyle that is short on the top and sides and long in the back. Fading a mullet means gradually decreasing the length of the hair on the sides and back.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start with clean, dry hair. This is important because it allows you to see the hair's natural texture and thickness.

2. Use clippers with a guard to trim the hair on the sides and back of the head. Start with a higher guard and work your way down to a lower guard as you move towards the bottom.

3. Use scissors to trim the hair around the edges of the fade. This will help blend the shorter hair into the longer hair.

4. Use a comb to check for any uneven areas and make adjustments as needed.

5. If you want to add more texture to the hair, use thinning shears to remove some of the bulk.

6. Use a razor to create a smooth transition between the shorter hair and longer hair. Go slowly and be careful not to cut too much hair.

7. Use a trimmer to clean up any stray hairs around the ears and neckline.

8. Style the hair as desired.

Tips

- Start with a higher guard and work your way down to a lower guard. This will help you achieve a more gradual fade.

- Take your time and go slowly. It's better to take off less hair than to take off too much.

- Use a mirror to check the back of your head.

- If you're not sure how short to go, start with a longer guard and work your way down.

FAQ

Q: Can I fade my own mullet?

A: Yes, you can fade your own mullet. However, it's important to go slowly and be careful not to cut too much hair.

Q: How often should I fade my mullet?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to change your look. Some people fade their mullets every few weeks, while others do it every few months.

Hair Care

After you've faded your mullet, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the hair and cause split ends.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends from forming.

- Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to add moisture to your hair.

Now that you know how to fade a mullet, you can experiment with different styles and looks. Remember to take your time, go slowly, and be careful not to cut too much hair. With a little practice, you'll be able to fade your mullet like a pro.
