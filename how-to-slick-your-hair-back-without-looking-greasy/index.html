How To Slick Your Hair Back Without Looking Greasy A 2023 Guide

Written by Petter Mar 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a fan of the slicked-back hairstyle, you know how challenging it can be to achieve the perfect look without looking like you just dipped your head in a vat of oil. Luckily, there are a few tricks to help you achieve a sleek, polished look without the unwanted shine. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps for how to slick your hair back without looking greasy, as well as tips for hair care and frequently asked questions.

How to Slick Your Hair Back Without Looking Greasy

The key to achieving a slicked-back hairstyle without looking greasy is in the products you use and how you apply them. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to remove any excess oil or buildup from your scalp and hair.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hands and distribute it evenly throughout your hair. Choose a product that is designed for your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, opt for a lightweight product. If you have thick or curly hair, a heavier product may be necessary.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a comb, comb your hair back away from your face. Start at the front and work your way to the back of your head. Make sure your hair is evenly distributed and free of any tangles or knots.

Step 4: Use a Hair Dryer

Using a hair dryer, blow dry your hair on a low heat setting. Use your fingers to shape your hair as you go. This will help to set the style and remove any excess moisture.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is dry, apply a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place. Hold the can about 10-12 inches away from your head and spray evenly. Avoid spraying too much, as this can lead to a greasy look.

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back Without Looking Greasy

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Use a matte or low-shine product to avoid a greasy look
  • Don't apply too much product â a little goes a long way
  • Use a comb to distribute the product evenly
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day, as this can lead to a greasy look
  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any buildup or excess oil

FAQ: Slicking Your Hair Back Without Looking Greasy

Q: What kind of product should I use to slick my hair back?

A: The type of product you use will depend on your hair type and texture. For fine hair, opt for a lightweight product like a pomade or gel. For thicker or curly hair, a heavier product like a wax or cream may be necessary.

Q: Can I slick my hair back without using any product?

A: It is possible to slick your hair back without using any product, but it may not stay in place as well. Using a styling product will help to hold the style and give it a polished look.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I slick it back?

A: It is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid buildup and excess oil. If you have particularly oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Hair Care for Slicked-Back Hair

In addition to following the steps above, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated

By following these steps and tips, you'll be able to achieve a sleek, polished look without the unwanted shine. Whether you're getting ready for a night out or just want to switch up your everyday look, the slicked-back hairstyle is a classic and versatile option.
