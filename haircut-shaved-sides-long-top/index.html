Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top A Trendy Look For 2023

Written by Petter Aug 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is both trendy and easy to maintain? Look no further than the haircut shaved sides long top. This versatile style can be tailored to your individual preferences and is suitable for both men and women. In this article, we will explore the different aspects of this trendy haircut, including how to achieve it, tips for maintenance, and frequently asked questions.

Description

The haircut shaved sides long top is characterized by short or closely shaved sides and longer hair on top. The length of the top can vary, ranging from a few inches to several inches long. This style can be customized to your liking, whether you prefer a slicked-back look or a messy, textured style. The shaved sides add a touch of edginess to the overall look, while the longer top provides versatility in styling.

How to Achieve the Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top

If you are interested in getting the haircut shaved sides long top, it is important to find a skilled hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you desire. The process typically involves cutting or shaving the sides and back of the head, leaving the top section longer. The length and style of the top can be customized to your liking, whether you prefer a pompadour, quiff, or messy, textured look.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the haircut shaved sides long top:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best style and length for your hair type and face shape.
  2. Section off the hair on top and clip it out of the way.
  3. Use clippers or scissors to cut or shave the sides and back of the head. Gradually increase the length as you move up towards the top.
  4. Blend the top section with the sides using scissors or a razor, creating a seamless transition between the two lengths.
  5. Style the top section using your preferred method, whether it be a blow dryer, pomade, or texturizing spray.
  6. Use a comb or your fingers to create the desired shape and texture.
  7. Finish with hairspray or other styling products as desired.

Tips for Maintenance

To maintain your haircut shaved sides long top, it is important to keep the sides and back trimmed regularly to prevent a shaggy or unkempt appearance. You can visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for touch-ups, or use clippers at home to keep the sides and back tidy. To style the top section, experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and desired look.

FAQ

Is the haircut shaved sides long top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle can be customized to suit a variety of hair types, from straight to curly. However, it is important to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your individual hair type and face shape.

Can women rock the haircut shaved sides long top?

Absolutely! This haircut is versatile and suitable for both men and women. Women can add a feminine touch to the style by leaving the top section longer and styling it in loose waves or curls.

Is the haircut shaved sides long top easy to maintain?

Yes, this style is relatively low-maintenance compared to other hairstyles. However, it is important to keep the sides and back trimmed regularly to prevent a shaggy or unkempt appearance.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it is important to follow a regular hair care routine. This may include washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a moisturizing hair mask once a week, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. It is also important to protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf when outdoors.

Conclusion

The haircut shaved sides long top is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your individual preferences. Whether you prefer a slicked-back look or a messy, textured style, this haircut is sure to turn heads. By following a regular hair care routine and keeping the sides and back trimmed, you can maintain the look and feel confident in your stylish new haircut.
