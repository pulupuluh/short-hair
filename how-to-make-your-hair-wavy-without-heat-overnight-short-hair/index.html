How To Make Your Hair Wavy Without Heat Overnight Short Hair

Written by William May 15, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of using heat styling tools to achieve wavy hair? Heat can damage your hair and cause split ends and breakage. Fortunately, there are ways to make your hair wavy without heat overnight, even if you have short hair. In this article, we’ll show you how to get beachy waves without damaging your hair. Let’s get started!

How to Make Your Hair Wavy Without Heat Overnight

There are several ways to make your hair wavy without heat overnight. Here are some of the most effective methods:

Braids

One of the easiest ways to get wavy hair overnight is by braiding your hair before you go to bed. Here’s how:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it’s damp.
  3. Divide your hair into two sections.
  4. Braid each section of hair tightly.
  5. Secure the ends of the braids with hair ties.
  6. Go to bed and let your hair dry overnight.
  7. In the morning, remove the hair ties and gently unravel the braids.
  8. Use your fingers to separate the waves and add some texture.
  9. Spray some sea salt spray or texturizing spray to hold the waves in place.

Voila! You now have gorgeous, beachy waves without using any heat.

Twists

If you don’t want to braid your hair, you can also use twists to create waves. Here’s how:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it’s damp.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Twist each section of hair tightly.
  5. Secure the ends of the twists with hair ties.
  6. Go to bed and let your hair dry overnight.
  7. In the morning, remove the hair ties and gently unravel the twists.
  8. Use your fingers to separate the waves and add some texture.
  9. Spray some sea salt spray or texturizing spray to hold the waves in place.

Twists are a great alternative to braids, especially if you have shorter hair.

Buns

If you want smaller, tighter waves, you can use buns to create them. Here’s how:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it’s damp.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections.
  4. Twist each section of hair tightly.
  5. Wrap the twisted hair around itself to create a small bun.
  6. Secure the bun with a hair tie.
  7. Go to bed and let your hair dry overnight.
  8. In the morning, remove the hair ties and gently unravel the buns.
  9. Use your fingers to separate the waves and add some texture.
  10. Spray some sea salt spray or texturizing spray to hold the waves in place.

Buns are great if you want tighter, more defined waves.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to making your hair wavy without heat overnight:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it’s damp.
  3. Decide which method you want to use (braids, twists, or buns).
  4. Divide your hair into small sections.
  5. Follow the instructions for your chosen method.
  6. Go to bed and let your hair dry overnight.
  7. In the morning, remove the hair ties and gently unravel the braids, twists, or buns.
  8. Use your fingers to separate the waves and add some texture.
  9. Spray some sea salt spray or texturizing spray to hold the waves in place.

Tips for Making Your Hair Wavy Without Heat

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before you start.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Don’t make the braids, twists, or buns too tight, or you’ll end up with crimped hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause the waves to fall out.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about making your hair wavy without heat overnight:

Can I use hairspray instead of sea salt spray?

Yes, you can use hairspray to hold your waves in place. However, sea salt spray will give you a more natural, beachy look.

Will this work on all hair types?

Yes, these methods will work on all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How long will the waves last?

The waves should last until you wash your hair again. You can refresh them with some more sea salt spray or texturizing spray if needed.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to keep your wavy hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your hair wavy without heat overnight and keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Give it a try and see how easy it is to get beachy waves without damaging your hair!
