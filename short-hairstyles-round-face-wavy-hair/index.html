Short Hairstyles For Round Face Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jul 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face and wavy hair? Look no further! In this blog post, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks for short hairstyles for round face wavy hair that will leave you feeling confident and beautiful. So, let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks and a round chin. Wavy hair, on the other hand, has a natural texture that adds volume and movement to the hair. Combining these two features can be tricky, but with the right short hairstyle, you can emphasize your best features and make your face appear slimmer.

How To

The key to finding the perfect short hairstyle for round face wavy hair is to focus on creating vertical lines that elongate the face. This can be achieved by adding layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts. It's also important to avoid adding volume to the sides of the face, as this can make the face appear wider.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect short hairstyle for round face wavy hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for wavy hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the roots and smooth out any frizz.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Curl sections of hair away from your face to create a natural-looking wave.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray to add definition and hold to your waves.
  6. Using a comb, create a deep side part to add dimension to your hair.
  7. If you want bangs, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on one side than the other. This will create a diagonal line that elongates the face.
  8. Finish off with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for round face wavy hair:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as they can make the face appear wider.
  • Opt for layers that start at the cheekbone or higher to create vertical lines.
  • Consider an asymmetrical cut that is longer on one side than the other to create a diagonal line that elongates the face.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural waves.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the one that flatters your face shape the most.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for round face wavy hair:

What is the best short hairstyle for round face wavy hair?

The best short hairstyle for round face wavy hair is one that creates vertical lines and elongates the face. This can be achieved with layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts.

Should I avoid adding volume to the sides of my face?

Yes, adding volume to the sides of the face can make a round face appear wider. Instead, focus on creating vertical lines that elongate the face.

What products should I use for wavy hair?

For wavy hair, it's best to use products that enhance your natural texture and add definition to your waves. Look for texturizing sprays, curl-enhancing creams, and leave-in conditioners.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your short, round face wavy hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent over-drying.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent heat damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking neat and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect short hairstyle for your round face wavy hair. Remember to experiment and have fun with your hair, and don't be afraid to try something new!
