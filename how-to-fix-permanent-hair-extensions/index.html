How To Fix Permanent Hair Extensions A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 12, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of constantly struggling with hair extensions that don't stay put? Permanent hair extensions can be a game-changer, giving you long, luscious locks that look and feel natural. However, fixing them in place can be a bit tricky. In this article, we'll show you how to fix permanent hair extensions in easy-to-follow steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Fix Permanent Hair Extensions
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Permanent Hair Extensions
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Permanent Hair Extensions

How to Fix Permanent Hair Extensions

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's first understand what we mean by "permanent" hair extensions. These are extensions that are fixed to your natural hair using a variety of methods, including glue, tape, or beads.

The key to fixing permanent hair extensions is to ensure that they are attached securely to your natural hair. This will prevent them from slipping or falling out, which can be both frustrating and embarrassing. Here are the basic steps you need to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Clean and prepare your hair
Before you begin fixing your permanent hair extensions, you need to make sure your natural hair is clean and dry. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual, and let it air dry or blow dry it. Avoid using any styling products that can make your hair greasy or heavy, as this can affect the bonding of the extensions.

Step 2: Section your hair
Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any spots when attaching the extensions.

Step 3: Attach the extensions
Take a small section of your natural hair and apply a small amount of glue or tape to the root. Attach the extension to the root of your natural hair and press firmly. Repeat this process until all the extensions are attached.

Step 4: Blend the extensions with your natural hair
Use a comb to blend the extensions with your natural hair. This will create a seamless and natural-looking finish.

Step 5: Style your hair
Once the extensions are attached and blended, you can style your hair as usual. However, avoid using any heat styling tools directly on the bonds, as this can weaken the glue or tape.

Tips for Maintaining Permanent Hair Extensions

Now that you know how to fix permanent hair extensions, here are some tips to help you maintain them:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging the bonds
  • Avoid using heat styling tools directly on the bonds
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair to avoid pulling on the extensions
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose braid to prevent tangling
  • Get regular maintenance appointments with your hairstylist to ensure the extensions are in good condition

FAQs

Q: How long do permanent hair extensions last?
A: The lifespan of permanent hair extensions depends on several factors, including the method of attachment, the quality of the extensions, and how well you maintain them. Generally, they can last anywhere from 6 weeks to 6 months.

Q: Can I color my hair with permanent extensions?
A: Yes, you can color your hair with permanent extensions. However, it's best to consult with your hairstylist to ensure that the color doesn't damage the bonds or the extensions.

Hair Care for Permanent Hair Extensions

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining permanent hair extensions. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools directly on the bonds
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, and use a detangling brush
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out the extensions
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose braid to prevent tangling
  • Get regular maintenance appointments with your hairstylist

By following these tips, you can ensure that your permanent hair extensions look and feel great for as long as possible.

Now that you know how to fix permanent hair extensions, you can enjoy long, luscious locks that look and feel natural. Remember to take good care of your extensions to ensure that they last as long as possible.
