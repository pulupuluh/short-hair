How To Cut Men s Haircut Long On Top Short On Sides

Written by Luffy Aug 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for men? The long on top and short on sides hairstyle is a popular choice for men who want to look fashionable and sleek. This versatile hairstyle can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types. In this article, we will discuss how to cut men's haircut long on top short on sides in relaxed English language.

Description

The long on top and short on sides hairstyle is also known as the undercut. It features longer hair on the top of the head, which is usually styled in a pompadour, quiff, or slick back. The sides and back of the head are shaved or cut short, creating a sharp contrast with the longer hair on top. This hairstyle has been popular for several years and is still going strong in 2023.

How to

If you want to cut a men's haircut long on top short on sides, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consultation

Before you start cutting the hair, it's important to have a consultation with the client. Discuss the style they want and check their hair type and face shape. This will help you determine the best length and style for the haircut.

Step 2: Sectioning

Section the hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start with the sides and back of the head, and then move to the top section. This will make it easier to cut the hair evenly.

Step 3: Cutting the Sides

Using clippers, cut the sides and back of the head to the desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if necessary. Blend the hair at the top of the head with the sides to create a seamless transition.

Step 4: Cutting the Top

Using scissors, cut the hair on top of the head to the desired length. The hair should be longer on the front and gradually get shorter towards the back. Use a point-cutting technique to add texture and movement to the hair.

Step 5: Styling

Style the hair on top of the head using a pomade, wax, or gel. Use a comb or your fingers to create the desired shape and volume. The hair on the sides can be styled with a clipper or trimmer to create a fade or undercut effect.

Tips

  • Use a mirror to check the haircut from different angles.
  • Take your time and don't rush the haircut.
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen the hair before cutting it.
  • Invest in high-quality clippers and scissors for a professional finish.
  • Use a hair cape and towel to protect the client's clothes from hair clippings.

FAQ

Q: What face shape suits a long on top short on sides hairstyle?

A: This hairstyle suits most face shapes, but it looks especially good on oval, square, and diamond-shaped faces.

Q: Can I cut a men's haircut long on top short on sides at home?

A: Yes, you can cut this hairstyle at home if you have some experience with cutting hair. However, it's recommended to visit a professional barber or stylist for best results.

Q: How often do I need to cut a men's haircut long on top short on sides?

A: It depends on the client's hair growth and desired length. Generally, this hairstyle needs to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care

To keep a men's haircut long on top short on sides looking sharp and sleek, it's important to maintain a good hair care routine. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and wash your hair regularly. Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage the hair. Instead, use a hair dryer on a low heat setting and style the hair using a comb or your fingers. Apply a hair oil or serum to add shine and moisture to the hair.

Now that you know how to cut a men's haircut long on top short on sides, it's time to try it out on your clients or yourself. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully for best results.
