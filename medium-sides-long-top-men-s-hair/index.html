Medium Sides Long Top Men s Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy May 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile hairstyle, then medium sides long top men's hair is the perfect choice for you. With this hairstyle, you can keep the sides short while leaving the top long, giving you a stylish and modern look. In this guide, we will cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to style it, tips, and hair care.

How to Style Medium Sides Long Top Men's Hair

Styling medium sides long top men's hair is relatively easy and straightforward. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in styling your medium sides long top men's hair is to wash it thoroughly. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to clean your hair and make it more manageable.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel as this can damage your hair and cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply Hair Product

Next, apply a hair product of your choice to your hair. You can use hair wax, pomade, or gel to style your hair. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now, it's time to style your hair. With medium sides long top men's hair, you can choose to comb your hair to the side, slick it back, or create a messy look. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.

Tips for Medium Sides Long Top Men's Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your medium sides long top men's hair:

  • Choose a hair product that works well with your hair type
  • Trim the sides regularly to maintain the hairstyle
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you use heat styling tools

FAQs About Medium Sides Long Top Men's Hair

Is Medium Sides Long Top Men's Hair Suitable for All Face Shapes?

Yes, medium sides long top men's hair is suitable for all face shapes. However, you may need to adjust the length and style to suit your face shape and hair type.

How Often Should I Trim the Sides?

You should trim the sides every 2-3 weeks to maintain the hairstyle.

What Hair Products Should I Use?

You can use hair wax, pomade, or gel to style your hair. Choose a product that works well with your hair type and provides the desired hold.

Hair Care for Medium Sides Long Top Men's Hair

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and appearance of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your medium sides long top men's hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair

With these tips and guidelines, you can rock the medium sides long top men's hair look with confidence and style. So, go ahead and try out this trendy and versatile hairstyle for yourself!
