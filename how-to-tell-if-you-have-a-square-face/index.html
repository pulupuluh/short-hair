How To Tell If You Have A Square Face

Written by Frank May 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're wondering whether you have a square face, you're not alone. Many people want to know what face shape they have so they can choose the right hairstyles and makeup to flatter their features. In this post, we'll show you how to tell if you have a square face and give you some tips on how to enhance your natural beauty.

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and straight sides. If you have a square face, your face is roughly the same width from top to bottom and side to side.

How to Tell if You Have a Square Face

There are a few simple ways to tell if you have a square face:

  • Measure your face: Use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If your face is roughly the same width in all three areas, you have a square face.
  • Look at your jawline: If you have a strong, defined jawline, you likely have a square face.
  • Check your forehead: If you have a broad forehead that is roughly the same width as your jawline, you have a square face.

If you're still unsure, take a selfie and look at the shape of your face. You can also ask a friend or family member for their opinion.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you determine if you have a square face:

  1. Measure your forehead, cheeks, and jawline with a tape measure.
  2. Compare the three measurements. If they're roughly the same width, you have a square face.
  3. Look at your jawline. Is it strong and defined?
  4. Check your forehead. Is it broad and roughly the same width as your jawline?
  5. Take a selfie and look at the shape of your face.
  6. Ask a friend or family member for their opinion.

Tips for Enhancing Your Natural Beauty

If you have a square face, you can enhance your natural beauty with these tips:

  • Choose hairstyles that soften your features. Soft curls or waves can help balance out a strong jawline.
  • Avoid hairstyles that emphasize your jawline, such as slicked-back ponytails.
  • Use makeup to contour your face. Apply bronzer along your jawline and temples to soften your features.
  • Choose glasses with rounded edges to balance out your strong jawline.
  • Experiment with bold lip colors to draw attention to your lips.

FAQs

What other face shapes are there?

There are several other face shapes, including oval, round, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped.

Can I change my face shape?

No, you can't change your face shape. However, you can use makeup and hairstyles to enhance your natural features.

Hair Care for Square Faces

When it comes to hair care for square faces, it's important to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Soft, wavy hairstyles can help soften a strong jawline, while straight, blunt cuts can emphasize it. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Remember to keep your hair healthy by using a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking its best.

Conclusion

If you have a square face, don't worry! With the right hairstyles and makeup, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your skin. Use the tips in this post to help you determine if you have a square face and how to flatter your features.
