Asian Hair Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia May 20, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring hairstyle? Do you want to try something new and trendy? Look no further than the Asian hair short sides long top look! This style has been gaining popularity in recent years and is a great way to switch up your look. In this article, we’ll go through a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this style, tips for maintenance, and commonly asked questions.

Description

The Asian hair short sides long top style is exactly what it sounds like. The sides of the hair are cut short, while the top is left longer. This creates a contrast in length that is both edgy and stylish. This look is versatile and can be worn in many different ways, from slicked back to messy and textured.

How to Achieve the Look

The first step in achieving the Asian hair short sides long top look is to find a reliable hairstylist who is experienced in cutting Asian hair. Once you’ve found someone you trust, follow these steps:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

Before getting started, decide on the length you want for the sides and the top. This will depend on your personal preference and face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to keep the sides shorter to create a more angular look.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

The sides of the hair should be cut short, usually with clippers. This will create a clean, sharp look. Ask your hairstylist to fade the hair from short to long, creating a gradient effect.

Step 3: Cut the Top

The top of the hair should be left longer, usually around 2-3 inches. This will give you enough length to style your hair in different ways. Ask your hairstylist to layer the top so that it blends seamlessly with the sides.

Step 4: Style the Hair

Once the haircut is done, it’s time to style your hair. Use a hair product that suits your hair type and the look you’re going for. For a slicked-back look, use a pomade or gel. For a messy, textured look, use a wax or paste. The possibilities are endless!

Tips for Maintenance

Now that you’ve achieved the Asian hair short sides long top look, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for touch-ups.
  • Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Try different styling products to achieve different looks.
  • Consider investing in a good hairdryer and styling tools.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about the Asian hair short sides long top look:

Can this style be worn by anyone?

Yes, this style can be worn by anyone. However, it’s important to consider your face shape and hair type when deciding on the length and style.

What kind of products should I use?

The type of product you use will depend on your hair type and the look you’re going for. Experiment with different products to find what works best for you.

How often should I visit my hairstylist?

You should visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for touch-ups.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and health.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to achieve the Asian hair short sides long top look and keep it looking great!
