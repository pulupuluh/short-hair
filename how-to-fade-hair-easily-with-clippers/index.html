How To Fade Hair Easily With Clippers

Written by Christine May 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

In this blog post, we will be discussing how to fade hair easily with clippers. Fading hair has been a trend for a long time, and it is not going anywhere anytime soon. It is a technique that involves cutting the hair shorter on the sides and gradually blending it with longer hair on top. Fading hair can be done with scissors, but clippers make the process easier and quicker.

Description

Fading hair is a technique that involves cutting the hair shorter on the sides and gradually blending it with longer hair on top. Fading hair can be done with scissors, but clippers make the process easier and quicker.

How to

Here are the steps to fade hair easily with clippers:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

The first step in fading hair is choosing the right clippers. Clippers come in different sizes, and you need to choose the right one for the job. A clipper with a #1 guard is ideal for a high fade, while a #2 guard is suitable for a medium fade. A #3 guard is perfect for a low fade.

Step 2: Wash the Hair

Before you start fading the hair, you need to wash it thoroughly. Use a shampoo that suits your hair type and rinse it with warm water.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Section the hair by using a comb and clips. The top section of the hair should be clipped away, leaving the sides and the back.

Step 4: Start Fading

Start fading the hair by using the clippers with the #1 guard. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use a flicking motion to blend the hair, and keep the clippers parallel to the head.

Step 5: Change the Guard

Once you have blended the hair with the #1 guard, change it to the #2 guard and repeat the process. Continue changing the guards until you have achieved the desired look.

Step 6: Blend the Top

Blend the top section of the hair with the scissors. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle.

Step 7: Clean Up

Clean up any stray hairs with the clippers and a comb. Use a mirror to check the back of the head.

Tips

Here are some tips to make the process of fading hair easier:

  • Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle.
  • Keep the clippers parallel to the head.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head.
  • Work in small sections to ensure that you blend the hair correctly.
  • Practice makes perfect. Don't be afraid to make mistakes.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fading hair with clippers:

Q: How long does it take to fade hair with clippers?

A: It depends on the length of the hair and the desired look. It can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour.

Q: Can I fade my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can. Fading hair with clippers is easy to do at home.

Q: Do I need to use guards when fading hair?

A: Yes, guards help you achieve the desired length of the hair.

Hair Care

Fading hair with clippers can be damaging to the hair if not done correctly. Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair after fading it:

  • Use a conditioner to keep the hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on the hair.
  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain the fade.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish the hair.

In conclusion, fading hair with clippers is easy to do at home. It takes practice and patience to achieve the desired look. Follow the steps mentioned above and take care of your hair after fading it.
