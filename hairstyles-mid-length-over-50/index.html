Hairstyles Mid Length Over 50 A Guide To Looking Fabulous

Written by Robby Jun 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for a hairstyle that's not too short or too long? Look no further than mid-length hairstyles! This length is versatile and flattering for different face shapes and hair types. In this article, we'll share how to style mid-length hair, step-by-step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice.

How to Style Mid-Length Hair Over 50

Styling mid-length hair is easy and fun. You can wear it straight, wavy, or curly. Here are some steps to follow depending on your desired look:

Straight

To achieve a sleek, straight look, start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from hot tools. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair in small sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Finish with a shine serum to add gloss to your locks.

Wavy

To create soft waves, apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and curl the ends under. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Finish with a texturizing spray to add definition and hold.

Curly

If you have natural curls, enhance them with a curl-defining cream. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to encourage curl formation. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting the curls. Finish with a lightweight hair oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Mid-Length Hair Over 50

Here are some tips to make the most of your mid-length hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Add layers to create movement and texture in your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to change up your look.
  • Accessorize with hair clips, headbands, or scarves to add interest to your hairstyle.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQ About Hairstyles Mid Length Over 50

What Face Shapes Suit Mid-Length Hair?

Mid-length hair suits most face shapes, but if you have a round face, avoid chin-length hair as it can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for longer hair that grazes your shoulders.

What Hair Types Are Suitable for Mid-Length Hair?

Mid-length hair works well for most hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and fine hair. If you have thick hair, consider adding layers to reduce the weight and make your hair more manageable.

Can I Style Mid-Length Hair for a Formal Occasion?

Absolutely! Mid-length hair is versatile and can be styled in different ways for formal events. You can wear it in an updo, half-up, or side-swept style. Add some hair accessories like a sparkly clip or a flower crown to make your hairstyle stand out.

Hair Care Tips for Mid-Length Hair Over 50

To keep your mid-length hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and soft.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent heat damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Conclusion

Mid-length hairstyles are a great choice for women over 50 who want a stylish and low-maintenance look. Whether you prefer straight, wavy, or curly hair, you can achieve your desired style with the right tools and products. Follow our tips and hair care advice to keep your locks looking fabulous at any age!
