How To Fake Short Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you want to try out short curly hair without committing to a haircut? Thankfully, it's easy to fake short curls with the right tools and techniques. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve a natural-looking short curly hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  1. Tools You'll Need
  2. Preparation
  3. Curling Your Hair
  4. Styling Your Curls
  5. Finishing Touches
  6. Tips for Maintaining Your Fake Short Curls
  7. FAQs
  8. Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Tools You'll Need

Before you get started, make sure you have these tools on hand:

  • Curling iron or wand
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray
  • Comb or brush
  • Clips or bobby pins

Preparation

Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. Blow dry your hair straight, using a comb or brush to smooth out any tangles.

Next, separate your hair into small sections using clips or bobby pins. This will make it easier to curl your hair.

Curling Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends. Hold for a few seconds and release.

Repeat this process all around your head, curling your hair in different directions for a more natural look. Don't worry if the curls are tight at first - we'll loosen them up in the next step.

Styling Your Curls

Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently loosen the curls. You can also use a wide-tooth comb or pick to separate the curls and add volume.

If you want more definition, you can use a curl-defining cream or mousse. Apply a small amount to your curls and scrunch your hair to distribute the product evenly.

Finishing Touches

Finally, spritz your hair with hairspray to hold the curls in place. You can also use a bit of shine serum to add some extra gloss to your curls.

Tips for Maintaining Your Fake Short Curls

  • Don't wash your hair every day - this can dry out your curls and make them lose their shape.
  • Sleep with a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without disturbing their shape.
  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends, which can make your curls look frizzy and unruly.

FAQs

Q: How long will my fake short curls last?

A: Your curls should last for a few days if you take care of them properly. You can refresh them with a bit of hairspray and water if they start to lose their shape.

Q: Can I use this technique on all hair types?

A: Yes, this technique should work on all hair types. However, if you have very fine or thin hair, you may need to use a smaller curling iron or wand to achieve the desired effect.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

To keep your short curly hair looking healthy and vibrant, it's important to use the right products and techniques. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry - this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or pick to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause it to lose its curl pattern.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily fake short curly hair and enjoy the look without committing to a drastic haircut. Have fun experimenting with different styles and products, and don't be afraid to try something new!
