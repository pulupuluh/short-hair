Long On Top Short On Sides No Fade A Guide To The Latest Trend

Written by Frank Jun 21, 2023 · 6 min read

Welcome to our guide to the latest hair trend – long on top short on sides no fade. This style is becoming increasingly popular among men, and for good reason. It’s versatile, easy to maintain, and looks great on almost anyone. In this article, we’ll tell you everything you need to know about this trend, including how to get the look, tips for styling, and how to take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Get the Look

The long on top short on sides no fade is a simple and timeless hairstyle that is easy to achieve. To get the look, you’ll need to have longer hair on the top of your head and shorter hair on the sides and back. The key to this style is the absence of a fade, which creates a clean and classic look.

Tools You’ll Need

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair gel or pomade

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving the long on top short on sides no fade:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it’s damp.
  2. Use a comb to part your hair on the side or in the middle, depending on your preference.
  3. Using scissors, trim the top of your hair to your desired length. You can leave it longer for a more dramatic look or shorter for a more subtle look.
  4. Next, use clippers to trim the sides and back of your head to a shorter length. Make sure to blend the hair on the sides and back so that it blends seamlessly with the longer hair on top.
  5. Use a comb to style your hair and add some gel or pomade to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling your long on top short on sides no fade:

  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use a hairdryer on a low heat setting to style your hair for a more polished look.
  • Apply hair gel or pomade sparingly to avoid a greasy or weighed-down look.
  • Consider adding some highlights or coloring your hair for a more unique look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the long on top short on sides no fade:

Can anyone pull off this style?

Yes, this style is versatile and can be adapted to suit almost any face shape or hair type.

How often do I need to get a haircut?

You’ll need to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the style.

What products should I use to style my hair?

Use a hair gel or pomade to hold the style in place. Avoid using too much product, as this can create a greasy or weighed-down look.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair with this style:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished.

Now that you know everything you need to know about the long on top short on sides no fade, it’s time to book your next haircut and try out this trend for yourself!
