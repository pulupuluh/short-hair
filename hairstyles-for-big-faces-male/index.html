Hairstyles For Big Faces Male Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are a man with a big face, you might be wondering what hairstyle would suit you best. Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on choosing the perfect hairstyle for big faces male.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Big Faces Male

Description

Men with big faces have a unique set of challenges when it comes to choosing a hairstyle. The goal is to balance the face's width, creating a more symmetrical appearance. There are several options for big faces male, ranging from short to long haircuts. The key is finding a style that flatters your face shape and works with your hair type and lifestyle.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle, consider the following factors:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair type
  • Your lifestyle and daily routine
  • Your personal style and preferences

Men with big faces may want to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of the head, as this will only make the face appear wider. Instead, opt for styles that create height and length, such as a slicked-back undercut or a messy textured crop.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some popular hairstyles for big faces male:

1. Slicked-Back Undercut

To achieve this look, ask your barber for a short back and sides with a longer length on top. Use a comb and a high-shine pomade to slick your hair back and create height at the crown. This style is perfect for men with thick, straight hair.

2. Messy Textured Crop

This style is ideal for men with wavy or curly hair. Ask your stylist for a short crop with plenty of texture and movement. Use a salt spray or texturizing paste to create a messy, tousled look.

3. Side-Parted Quiff

The side-parted quiff is a classic look that flatters most face shapes. Ask your barber for a fade on the sides with a longer length on top. Use a comb and a matte pomade to create a side part and add height to the front of the hair.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Invest in high-quality hair products that suit your hair type and styling needs.
  • Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber or stylist for advice on which style would suit you best.
  • Maintain regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for big faces male:

Q: Can men with big faces pull off long hair?

A: Yes, men with big faces can certainly pull off long hair. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair type. Consider a style with layers and texture to add movement and volume to your hair.

Q: What is the best hair product for slicked-back hairstyles?

A: A high-shine pomade or hair gel is ideal for creating a slicked-back hairstyle. Look for products that offer a strong hold and a glossy finish.

Hair Care for Big Faces Male

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers and straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

There you have it, your ultimate guide to hairstyles for big faces male. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style when choosing a new look. With the right haircut and styling products, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look that suits you perfectly.
