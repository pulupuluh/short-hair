How To Thin Out Men s Hair With Regular Scissors

Written by Eveline Feb 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thinning out hair is a great way to achieve a more natural and less bulky look. Typically, men with thick hair tend to prefer a more relaxed and thinner look. While thinning out men's hair may seem like a daunting task, it is actually quite simple with regular scissors. In this blog post, we will discuss how to thin out men's hair with regular scissors, including tips, step-by-step guide and frequently asked questions.

How to Thin Out Men's Hair with Regular Scissors

Before proceeding to thin out your hair, it is essential to have a clear understanding of the process. Here are step-by-step instructions to help you get started:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Begin by washing and drying your hair with a towel. Ensure that the hair is entirely dry before proceeding to the next step. Wet hair can cause uneven cuts, making it harder to achieve the desired look.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to detangle your hair, ensuring that it is free of knots and tangles. Comb your hair in the direction opposite to the one it naturally falls. This will help you identify the areas that require thinning out.

Step 3: Identify the Areas to Thin Out

The next step is to identify the areas that require thinning out. Typically, the areas around the crown, sides and back of the head tend to be thicker than the rest of the hair. Use a comb to lift the hair and identify the areas that need thinning out.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

To cut the hair, hold the scissors at an angle and cut the hair in a downward motion. Ensure that the scissors are not held straight, as this may result in uneven cuts. Start with small cuts, and gradually increase the amount of hair you cut. Remember to cut only a small amount of hair at a time, as it is easier to cut more if required than to fix a cut that is too short.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

After cutting the hair, use a comb to blend it with the rest of the hair. This will help you achieve a natural and relaxed look.

Tips for Thinning Out Men's Hair with Regular Scissors

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when thinning out men's hair with regular scissors:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure smooth and even cuts.
  • Start with small cuts and gradually increase the amount of hair you cut.
  • Ensure that the hair is dry before cutting it.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and identify the areas that require thinning out.
  • Blend the hair after cutting it to achieve a natural and relaxed look.

FAQs About Thinning Out Men's Hair with Regular Scissors

Q: How much hair should I cut when thinning out my hair?

A: It is best to start with small cuts and gradually increase the amount of hair you cut. Cutting too much hair at once may result in an uneven cut, making it harder to achieve the desired look.

Q: Can I use regular scissors to thin out my hair?

A: Yes, regular scissors can be used to thin out men's hair. However, it is essential to use sharp scissors to ensure smooth and even cuts.

Q: How often should I thin out my hair?

A: The frequency of thinning out hair depends on individual hair growth and thickness. Typically, it is recommended to thin out hair every six to eight weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Thinning Out Men's Hair with Regular Scissors

Thinning out hair can make it more susceptible to damage. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using heat-styling tools frequently, as they can cause hair damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as it is gentler on the hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos and conditioners, as they can strip the hair of its natural oils.

Thinning out men's hair with regular scissors is a simple and effective way to achieve a natural and relaxed look. Follow the tips and step-by-step guide outlined in this blog post to achieve the best results. Remember to take care of your hair by following the hair care tips to keep it healthy and strong.
