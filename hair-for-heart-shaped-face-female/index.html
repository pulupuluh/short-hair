Hair For Heart Shaped Face Female Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Aug 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you already know that choosing the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want to find a style that flatters your features and balances your face shape. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for finding the perfect hair for heart-shaped face female.

How to Determine if You Have a Heart Shaped Face

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces, let's make sure you know if you actually have a heart-shaped face. This face shape is defined by a wider forehead and cheekbones that taper down to a narrow chin. If you're still not sure, take a selfie and draw an imaginary line from your temples down to your jawline. If the line forms a heart shape, then you have a heart-shaped face.

Best Haircuts for Heart Shaped Face Female

When it comes to haircuts for heart-shaped faces, you want to create balance and symmetry. Here are some of the best haircuts for heart-shaped face female:

Long Layers

Long layers that start at the cheekbones can help balance out a heart-shaped face. This style adds movement and volume to the lower half of the face, creating a more balanced look.

Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs can help soften a heart-shaped face and draw attention to the eyes. Make sure the bangs are long enough to graze the eyebrows for a flattering look.

Bob with Volume

A bob with volume at the crown can help balance out a heart-shaped face. This style creates width at the top of the head, which helps to balance out the narrow chin.

Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut can be a great option for a heart-shaped face. This style draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones, while also creating balance with a short, tapered back.

Tips for Styling Hair for Heart Shaped Face Female

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your heart-shaped face, it's important to style it in a way that flatters your features. Here are some tips for styling hair for heart-shaped face female:

Add Volume at the Crown

Adding volume at the crown of your head can help balance out a narrow chin. Use a volumizing mousse or spray and blow dry your hair upside down for maximum volume.

Avoid Heavy Layers around the Jawline

Heavy layers around the jawline can make a heart-shaped face look bottom-heavy. Instead, opt for layers that start at the cheekbones and add movement to the lower half of the face.

Emphasize the Eyes

Heart-shaped faces often have beautiful eyes, so make sure to emphasize them with your hairstyle. Side-swept bangs and layered hair around the face can draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

FAQs about Hair for Heart Shaped Face Female

What hairstyles should heart-shaped faces avoid?

Heart-shaped faces should avoid hairstyles that add volume around the jawline, as this can create an unbalanced look. They should also avoid short, straight bangs that cut across the forehead.

Can heart-shaped faces pull off a center part?

Yes, heart-shaped faces can pull off a center part. However, it's important to add volume at the crown to balance out the narrow chin.

Should heart-shaped faces wear their hair up or down?

Heart-shaped faces can wear their hair either up or down, as long as the style is balanced and flattering. Updos that add volume at the crown can be especially flattering for heart-shaped faces.

Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Face Female

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips for heart-shaped face female:

Use a Heat Protectant

Whether you're blow drying, straightening, or curling your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

Wash Your Hair Regularly

It's important to wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. However, be careful not to over-wash, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, depending on your hair's needs.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hair for heart-shaped face female. Whether you opt for long layers, side-swept bangs, or a pixie cut, make sure to style your hair in a way that flatters your features and adds balance to your face shape.
