Best Hairstyles For A Round Face

Written by Frank Sep 06, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape and accentuates your best features. Luckily, there are plenty of options available. In this article, we'll share some of the best hairstyles for a round face, along with tips and tricks for styling and caring for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for a Round Face
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair

  • Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we get into the best hairstyles for a round face, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a style that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. To determine your face shape, start by pulling your hair back and looking in the mirror. Take note of the shape of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Here are the most common face shapes:

  • Round: Your face is nearly as wide as it is long, with soft curves and no angles.
  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and jawline.
  • Square: Your face is roughly as wide as it is long, with a strong, square jawline.
  • Heart: Your face is widest at the forehead and gradually narrows toward the jawline.
  • Diamond: Your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at the forehead and jawline.

Best Hairstyles for a Round Face

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some of the best hairstyles for a round face:

1. Long Layers

Long layers create movement and dimension, which can help elongate a round face. Layers should start at the jawline or cheekbones and gradually get longer toward the ends of the hair. Avoid blunt cuts or heavy bangs, which can make your face appear shorter and wider.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help balance out a round face by drawing attention to the eyes and cheekbones. The bangs should be long and swept to one side, rather than straight across the forehead. Avoid short, blunt bangs, which can make your face appear wider.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves can add texture and volume to a round face, giving the illusion of a more oval shape. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves, and tousle your hair with your fingers for a natural, effortless look.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold and stylish choice for a round face. The key is to keep the cut short and textured, with plenty of layers and volume at the crown. Avoid a blunt, straight-across cut, which can make your face appear wider.

5. High Ponytail

A high ponytail can elongate a round face, especially when paired with long, face-framing layers. Pull your hair back tightly at the crown of your head, and let the rest of your hair fall loosely around your face.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, it's important to style it properly. Here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy, sticky styling products, which can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and lift at the roots.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQs About Hairstyles for a Round Face

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs, chin-length cuts, and straight-across bobs. Instead, opt for styles that add length and dimension, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, and beachy waves.

Can I still wear my hair in a bun or updo if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Just make sure to keep the style loose and textured, rather than tight and slicked back. Experiment with different styles, such as messy buns or braided updos, to find what works best for you.

Do I need to have a certain hair type to wear these hairstyles?

No. These hairstyles can work for a variety of hair types, from straight to curly, fine to thick. The key is to choose a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural beauty.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Caring for your hair is just as important as choosing the right hairstyle. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling wands, as much as possible.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental pollutants by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your round face and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Whether you opt for long layers, side-swept bangs, or a bold pixie cut, remember to embrace your natural beauty and have fun with your hair!
