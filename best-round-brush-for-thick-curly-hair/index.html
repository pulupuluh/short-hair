Best Round Brush For Thick Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Jun 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to style it. However, with the right tools, you can achieve the perfect look. One of the most important tools you need is a round brush. But with so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this article, we will guide you through the best round brush for thick curly hair.

  • How To Choose the Best Round Brush for Thick Curly Hair

Description

A round brush is a hair styling tool that is used to create volume and curl. It is an essential tool for thick curly hair as it helps to detangle and smooth out the hair while creating defined curls. The best round brush for thick curly hair is one that has a ceramic or tourmaline barrel, which helps to distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz.

How To Choose the Best Round Brush for Thick Curly Hair

Choosing the best round brush for thick curly hair can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some factors to consider when choosing a round brush:

  1. Barrel size: The size of the barrel will determine the size of the curls. For thick curly hair, a larger barrel size is recommended.
  2. Bristle type: For thick curly hair, a round brush with natural boar bristles is recommended as it helps to distribute natural oils and reduce frizz.
  3. Handle grip: Choose a round brush with a comfortable handle grip to ensure that you have control while styling.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using a Round Brush for Thick Curly Hair

Using a round brush for thick curly hair may seem daunting, but it is easy once you get the hang of it. Here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and clip them away.
  3. Take one section of hair and wrap it around the round brush.
  4. Blow-dry your hair while holding the brush in place.
  5. Repeat the process for each section of hair.
  6. Once done, use a hair spray to set the curls.

Tips for Using a Round Brush for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your round brush:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use the cool setting on your blow dryer to set the curls.
  • Use a light-hold hairspray to avoid weighing down your curls.
  • Choose a round brush with a larger barrel size for looser curls and a smaller barrel size for tighter curls.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about using a round brush for thick curly hair:

  • Can I use a round brush on wet hair? No, using a round brush on wet hair can cause damage and breakage. Make sure your hair is dry before using a round brush.
  • How often should I clean my round brush? It is recommended to clean your round brush after every use to avoid product buildup.
  • Can I use a round brush on short hair? Yes, round brushes come in different sizes and can be used on all hair lengths.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your thick curly hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry to prevent frizz.
  • Use a deep-conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

Using the best round brush for thick curly hair can make all the difference in achieving the perfect look. Consider the factors mentioned above and choose the right round brush for your hair type. With the right technique and hair care routine, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls that will last all day!
