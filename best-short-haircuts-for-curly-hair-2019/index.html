Best Short Haircuts For Curly Hair 2019 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 19, 2023 · 9 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it’s undeniably beautiful, it can also be a challenge to style and manage, especially when it comes to short haircuts. But fear not! In this article, we’ll be discussing the best short haircuts for curly hair in 2019, along with tips on how to style and care for your curls.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Your Curly Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Hair
  3. Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair
  4. FAQs About Short Haircuts for Curly Hair
  5. Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Your Curly Hair

When it comes to choosing a short haircut for your curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Face shape: Depending on the shape of your face, certain haircuts may be more flattering than others. For example, a bob may look great on someone with an oval face, but may not be as flattering on someone with a round face.
  • Curl type: Different curl types may require different haircuts. For example, someone with tight curls may benefit from a shorter haircut that allows their curls to bounce and spring, while someone with looser curls may prefer a longer, layered cut.
  • Personal style: Ultimately, the best short haircut for your curly hair is one that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Consider your personal style and what will make you feel most comfortable.

With these factors in mind, here are some of the best short haircuts for curly hair in 2019:

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that can look great on curly hair. This cut is typically short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top that can be styled in a variety of ways. The pixie cut is a great option for someone with tight curls, as it allows their curls to bounce freely.

The Bob

The bob is another classic short haircut that can look great on curly hair. This cut is typically chin-length and can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous. The bob is a great option for someone with looser curls or wavy hair.

The Shag

The shag is a layered haircut that can look great on curly hair. This cut is typically shoulder-length or shorter and features lots of layers throughout. The shag is a great option for someone with medium to long curls, as it allows their curls to have movement and texture.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Hair

Styling short curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, it’s possible to create a variety of looks. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling short curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  2. Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and prevent frizz.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define any curls that need a little extra help.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair

Maintaining short curly hair requires some extra care and attention. Here are some tips to keep your curls looking their best:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day. Curly hair tends to be dry, and washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. Heat can damage your curls and make them look limp and lifeless.

FAQs About Short Haircuts for Curly Hair

Q: Will a short haircut make my curly hair look frizzy?

A: Not necessarily. With the right styling products and techniques, it’s possible to create a frizz-free look with short curly hair.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short curly haircut?

A: Yes! There are plenty of ways to style short curly hair, including updos and half-up styles.

Q: Will a short haircut make my curls look less defined?

A: It depends on the haircut and your natural curl pattern. Some short haircuts can actually enhance your curls and make them look more defined.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

When it comes to caring for short curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and manageable short haircut for your curly hair. Whether you prefer a pixie cut, a bob, or a shag, there’s a short haircut out there that’s perfect for you.
