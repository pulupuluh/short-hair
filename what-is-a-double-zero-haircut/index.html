What Is A Double Zero Haircut

Written by Christine Sep 13, 2023 · 6 min read

In the world of barbershop lingo, a double zero haircut is a very short haircut where the hair is cut to a length of 1/16th of an inch or shorter. This style is also known as "bald fade" or "skin fade" and is often used by men who want a low-maintenance haircut that requires little styling.

How to Get a Double Zero Haircut?

Getting a double zero haircut is not something you should try to do on your own. It's best to visit a professional barber or hairstylist who has experience in this type of haircut. Here's what to expect during your appointment:

Step 1: Consultation

Before your barber starts cutting, he or she will ask you about the style you want and the length you prefer. Be specific about the length and style you want to achieve so that the barber can understand your vision and give you the best service possible.

Step 2: Clippers

The barber will start with clippers, using a shorter guard for the top of your head and gradually switching to a shorter guard as he or she moves down the side and back of your head. The clippers will be used to create the fade effect and to remove most of the hair.

Step 3: Scissors

After the clippers, the barber will use scissors to trim the remaining hair to achieve the desired length and style. This step is important to ensure the haircut is even and looks good from all angles.

Step 4: Neckline and Sideburns

The barber will then trim your neckline and sideburns to complete the look. This step is often overlooked, but it's important to give the haircut a polished finish.

Tips for Maintaining a Double Zero Haircut

After you've gotten a double zero haircut, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking fresh. Here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

  • Visit your barber regularly to keep the haircut looking sharp.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp healthy.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Consider using a scalp oil or serum to keep your scalp moisturized and healthy.

FAQs

Is a double zero haircut suitable for all hair types?

A double zero haircut is suitable for most hair types, but it's best for men with straight or wavy hair. Men with curly or coarse hair may find it difficult to maintain this style.

Can I get a double zero haircut if I have a receding hairline?

A double zero haircut can work well with a receding hairline, but it's important to discuss your options with your barber. Your barber may suggest a different style that will work better for your hairline.

How often should I get a double zero haircut?

It's recommended to get a double zero haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and style. However, this may vary depending on your hair growth rate and personal preference.

Hair Care for a Double Zero Haircut

When it comes to hair care for a double zero haircut, less is more. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp healthy and avoid heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down. Consider using a scalp oil or serum to keep your scalp moisturized and healthy.

Getting a double zero haircut can be a refreshing change, especially for men who prefer low-maintenance hairstyles. With the right care and maintenance, this style can look great for weeks to come.
