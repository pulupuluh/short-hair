How To Make Your Hair Curlier For Black Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter May 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a black guy with straight hair, you may have always wanted to add some curl to your hair. But you may be wondering how to make your hair curlier without damaging it or spending a fortune on hair products. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the curly look you desire, as well as tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Curlier
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs

How to Make Your Hair Curlier

There are several ways to make your hair curlier, including using hair products, getting a perm, or using heat tools. However, the most natural and safest way to achieve curly hair is by using hair products specifically designed for curly hair.

Product Recommendations

Here are some products that you can use to make your hair curlier:

  • Curl Defining Creams
  • Curl Enhancing Gels
  • Curl Activating Sprays
  • Leave-In Conditioners
  • Curl Refreshing Sprays

When choosing a product, look for ingredients such as coconut oil, shea butter, and argan oil, which help to moisturize and define curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make your hair curlier:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a curl enhancing product to your damp hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.
  4. Scrunch your hair with your hands as you dry it to enhance the curl pattern.
  5. Apply a curl refreshing spray to your hair throughout the day to maintain the curl.

If you have longer hair, you may want to consider getting a haircut that is more suitable for curly hair.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your curly hair:

  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair, as it can break up the curl pattern. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as they can damage the hair and cause breakage.

FAQs

Can I make my hair curlier without using hair products?

While it is possible to use alternative methods such as twisting your hair or using rollers to create curls, these methods may not be as effective or long-lasting as using hair products.

Can I make my hair curlier without damaging it?

Yes, by using hair products specifically designed for curly hair, you can achieve the curly look you desire without damaging your hair.

Is it necessary to get a perm to make my hair curlier?

No, a perm is not necessary to make your hair curlier. There are several hair products that can help you achieve the curly look you desire.

Hair Care Advice

To maintain healthy hair, it is important to follow a hair care routine that includes regular washing, conditioning, and deep conditioning treatments. It is also important to protect your hair from heat damage and avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.

By following the tips and steps outlined in this article, you can achieve the curly look you desire without damaging your hair or spending a fortune on hair products.
