How To Put Shoulder Length Layered Hair Up A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Having shoulder-length layered hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks great when styled, it can be a challenge to put up in a way that looks effortless and chic. In this post, we'll show you how to put shoulder length layered hair up in a relaxed style that's perfect for any occasion.

Table of Contents

  • How to Put Shoulder Length Layered Hair Up
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Layered Hair

How to Put Shoulder Length Layered Hair Up

Putting up shoulder length layered hair can be a bit of a challenge. However, with the right tools and a little bit of practice, you can achieve a relaxed and effortless look that's perfect for any occasion. Here's what you'll need:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hair elastics
  • Hair spray
  • Texturizing spray (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to put your shoulder length layered hair up:

  1. Start by spraying your hair with texturizing spray. This will help give your hair some grip and make it easier to style.
  2. Gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair elastic. This will create a half-up, half-down style.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the base of the elastic. Secure it with a bobby pin.
  4. Take another small section of hair from the ponytail and twist it. Bring the twisted section over the top of the ponytail and secure it with a bobby pin.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until all of the hair in the ponytail is twisted and pinned.
  6. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and twist it. Bring the twisted section back and over the top of the ponytail. Secure it with a bobby pin.
  7. Repeat step 6 on the other side of your head.
  8. Spray your hair with hairspray to set the style.
  9. Take a step back and admire your work!

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Layered Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your shoulder length layered hair:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to help them blend in.
  • Don't worry if your style isn't perfect - a slightly messy look is on-trend right now.
  • If you're having trouble getting your hair to stay in place, try teasing the sections before you twist and pin them.
  • Experiment with different placement and sizes of twists to create a unique look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about putting shoulder length layered hair up:

Q: Can I put my shoulder length layered hair up in a bun?

A: Yes, you can put your hair up in a bun. However, it may be a bit more challenging than other styles. Try a low bun or a messy bun for a more relaxed look.

Q: How do I prevent my hair from slipping out of bobby pins?

A: Make sure your bobby pins are tight and secure. You can also try spraying them with hairspray before you put them in your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Layered Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your shoulder length layered hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before you use hot styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray or other styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Consider getting a deep conditioning treatment once a month to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Now that you know how to put shoulder length layered hair up, you can create a chic and effortless look for any occasion. With a little bit of practice and some patience, you'll be able to master this style in no time.
