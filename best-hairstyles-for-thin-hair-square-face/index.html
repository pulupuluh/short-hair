Best Hairstyles For Thin Hair Square Face

Written by Robby Jun 09, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the right hairstyle for your thin hair square face? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a variety of hairstyles that will suit your face shape and make your thin hair look voluminous.

Description

Having a square face means that you have a strong jawline and a broad forehead. This face shape looks best with hairstyles that soften the angles and add length to the face. Additionally, having thin hair can pose a challenge when it comes to finding hairstyles that add volume and make your hair look fuller.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for your thin hair square face, it’s important to keep in mind the following:

  • Hairstyles that add volume and texture to your hair are ideal
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or blunt, as they can accentuate the squareness of your face
  • Soft waves or layers that frame your face will help to soften your features

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of the best hairstyles for thin hair square face:

Long Layered Cut

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Part your hair down the middle
  3. Take a small section of hair at the front of your face and twist it away from your face
  4. Continue twisting the section and pin it at the back of your head
  5. Repeat on the other side
  6. Using a curling iron, create loose waves throughout your hair
  7. Finish with a texturizing spray to add volume and hold

Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Part your hair on the side
  3. Take a section of hair at the front of your face and comb it forward
  4. Trim the section of hair to the desired length for your bangs
  5. Blow dry the bangs using a round brush, sweeping them to the side
  6. Curl the ends of your hair away from your face using a curling iron
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your thin hair square face:

  • Use volumizing products such as mousse or root lifting spray
  • Avoid using heavy products such as gels or waxes, as they can weigh down your hair
  • Create volume at the crown of your head by backcombing or teasing
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair

FAQ

What are the best haircuts for a square face?

The best haircuts for a square face shape are those that soften the angles and add length to the face. This can include long layered cuts, side-swept bangs, or soft waves.

How can I add volume to my thin hair?

You can add volume to your thin hair by using volumizing products, backcombing or teasing, or getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip the natural oils
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

In conclusion, there are a variety of hairstyles that can suit your thin hair square face. By following these tips and step-by-step instructions, you can achieve a voluminous and flattering hairstyle that will enhance your features.
