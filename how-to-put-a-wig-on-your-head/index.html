How To Put A Wig On Your Head Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank May 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Wearing wigs has become a common trend nowadays, especially for those who want to change their hairstyles or cover up hair loss. However, putting on a wig for the first time can be a daunting experience. This guide will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to put a wig on your head, including tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Put a Wig on Your Head
  2. Step-by-Step Instructions
  3. Tips for Wearing Wigs
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Put a Wig on Your Head

Before putting on a wig, you need to prepare your hair and scalp. Here are the things you need to do:

  • Put your hair in a low ponytail or braid it.
  • Cover your hair with a wig cap. This will prevent your natural hair from sticking out and make the wig fit more securely.
  • Adjust the wig cap to fit your head. Make sure it's not too tight or too loose.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now it's time to put the wig on. Follow these steps:

  1. Hold the wig from the front and back and shake it gently to loosen the hair.
  2. Put the wig on your head and adjust it so that the front of the wig is aligned with your natural hairline.
  3. Secure the wig by adjusting the straps or combs inside the wig. Some wigs come with clips or adhesive tape for extra security.
  4. Style the wig according to your preference. You can use a wig brush or comb to detangle the hair and create your desired look.

Tips for Wearing Wigs

Here are some tips to help you wear wigs more comfortably:

  • Choose a wig that fits your head size and shape. Wigs come in different sizes, so make sure to measure your head before buying one.
  • Wash your wig regularly to keep it clean and fresh. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner and let it air dry.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your wig, as this can damage the fibers.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape and prevent tangling.

FAQs

How long can I wear a wig?

You can wear a wig for as long as you want, but it's recommended to take it off at night to let your scalp breathe.

Can I swim or shower with a wig on?

No, it's not recommended to wear a wig while swimming or taking a shower as the water can damage the wig and affect its style.

How do I clean my wig?

You can clean your wig by washing it with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Rinse it thoroughly and let it air dry on a wig stand or mannequin head.

Hair Care Tips

Wearing wigs can be a great way to protect your natural hair, but it's important to take care of both your wig and your natural hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp and hair regularly to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your natural hair, as this can damage it.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.

With these tips and instructions, you should now be able to put a wig on your head with ease. Remember to take care of your wig and natural hair to keep them healthy and beautiful.
