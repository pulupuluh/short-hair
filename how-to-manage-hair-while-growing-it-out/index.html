How To Manage Hair While Growing It Out Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jun 20, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of having short hair and want to grow it out? Growing out hair can be a challenging experience, especially when you're dealing with the awkward in-between stages. However, with the right hair care routine and a few simple tips, you can manage your hair and make the growing out process much easier.

Description

Growing out your hair can take time and patience, but with the right care and attention, you can make the process smoother. This article will provide you with tips and tricks on how to manage your hair while growing it out, including ways to reduce breakage and promote healthy hair growth.

How to Manage Hair While Growing It Out

Here are some essential tips to help you manage your hair while growing it out:

1. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims is essential to maintaining healthy hair while growing it out. Trimming your hair every six to eight weeks will help to prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down your hair's growth.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush. A wide-tooth comb will help to prevent breakage and minimize damage to your hair.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and slow down its growth. Try to avoid using hair dryers, straighteners, and curling irons as much as possible. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

4. Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep conditioning your hair once a week will help to keep it healthy and prevent breakage. Use a deep conditioning treatment that is designed for your hair type and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

5. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles such as braids, buns, and ponytails can cause breakage and damage to your hair. Opt for looser hairstyles that don't put too much tension on your hair.

6. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure to include plenty of fruits, vegetables, and protein in your diet.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to manage your hair while growing it out:

Step 1: Get Regular Trims

Make an appointment with your hairstylist every six to eight weeks to get your hair trimmed. This will help to prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down your hair's growth.

Step 2: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots to minimize breakage.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage to your hair.

Step 4: Deep Condition Your Hair

Once a week, deep condition your hair to keep it healthy and prevent breakage. Leave the deep conditioning treatment on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 5: Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage and damage to your hair. Opt for looser hairstyles that don't put too much tension on your hair.

Step 6: Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth. Include plenty of fruits, vegetables, and protein in your diet.

Tips for Managing Hair While Growing It Out

Here are some additional tips for managing your hair while growing it out:

1. Be Patient

Growing out your hair takes time, so be patient and don't get discouraged if you don't see results right away.

2. Experiment with Hair Accessories

Try different hair accessories such as headbands, hair clips, and scarves to add some variety to your hairstyles.

3. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can help to prevent breakage and damage to your hair while you sleep.

4. Try Hair Growth Supplements

Consider taking hair growth supplements such as biotin or collagen to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs

Q: How long does it take to grow out hair?

A: The length of time it takes to grow out hair varies depending on factors such as genetics, age, and hair type. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month.

Q: Can I still color my hair while growing it out?

A: Yes, you can still color your hair while growing it out. However, be mindful of the chemicals in the hair dye and try to avoid excessive coloring.

Q: Is it okay to use hair extensions while growing out my hair?

A: Yes, using hair extensions is a great way to add length to your hair while you're growing it out. Just make sure to use high-quality extensions that won't damage your hair.

Hair Care While Growing Out Hair

Growing out your hair can be a challenging experience, but with the right care and attention, you can make the process smoother. Follow these tips and tricks, and you'll be well on your way to healthy, long hair.
