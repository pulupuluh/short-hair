How To Style Men s Curly Frizzy Hair A Complete Guide

How to Style Men's Curly Frizzy Hair

When it comes to styling curly frizzy hair, the key is to embrace your natural texture. Here are some hairstyles that work well for men with curly frizzy hair:

  • Short and textured
  • Long and layered
  • Afro
  • Undercut
  • Man bun
  • Crew cut

Now, let's dive into the step-by-step guide on how to style these hairstyles.

Step-by-Step Guide

Short and Textured

To achieve this look, start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Squeeze out any excess water and apply a small amount of curl-defining cream throughout your hair. Use your fingers to scrunch and define your curls. Finish off with a matte hair clay to add texture and hold.

Long and Layered

If you prefer longer hair, go for a layered cut to help reduce frizz. Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle and nourish your hair. Apply a curl-enhancing mousse to define your curls and let your hair air dry. Once dry, use a hair serum to add shine and control frizz.

Afro

To achieve an afro, start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner to moisturize your curls. Apply a curl-defining cream and use your fingers to shape your hair into an afro. Finish off with a hair pick to add volume.

Undercut

The undercut works well for men with curly frizzy hair as it keeps the sides and back short and the top longer. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Apply a curl-enhancing cream to define your curls and use a hairdryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair. Finish off with a hair wax to add hold and texture.

Man Bun

To achieve a man bun, start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Use a leave-in conditioner to detangle and moisturize your hair. Apply a curl-defining cream and use your fingers to pull your hair into a bun. Secure with a hair tie and finish off with a hair oil to add shine.

Crew Cut

The crew cut is a classic hairstyle that works well for men with curly frizzy hair. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Apply a curl-enhancing cream and use a hairdryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair. Finish off with a hair wax to add hold and texture.

Tips for Styling Men's Curly Frizzy Hair

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair as it can cause frizz. Instead, use your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Apply products to damp hair to help define your curls.
  • Invest in a diffuser attachment for your hairdryer to help dry your hair without causing frizz.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and control frizz.

FAQs

Can I straighten my curly frizzy hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly frizzy hair using a straightening iron. However, it is important to use a heat protectant spray and not straighten your hair too often as it can cause damage.

How often should I wash my curly frizzy hair?

It is recommended to wash your curly frizzy hair once or twice a week to avoid drying out your hair. However, you can rinse your hair with water and apply a leave-in conditioner in between washes.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Curly Frizzy Hair

  • Avoid using hot water as it can dry out your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid causing breakage.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a month to nourish your curls.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol as it can dry out your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to help reduce split ends and breakage.

Now that you have all the tips and tricks to style your curly frizzy hair, go ahead and try out some of these hairstyles. Remember, embrace your natural texture and have fun with your hair!
