Men s Haircut Long On Top Short On Sides A Relaxed Style Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Long on top and short on the sides is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. This style is popular among men because it is versatile and easy to maintain. It is perfect for men who want a stylish yet low-maintenance haircut. In this article, we’ll discuss everything you need to know about the men's haircut long on top short on sides.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Men's Haircut Long on Top Short on Sides
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

The men's haircut long on top short on sides is a style that involves having longer hair on the top of the head while the sides are trimmed short. This style is often referred to as an undercut or a disconnected haircut. The length on top can vary depending on the individual's preference, but it is typically longer than the sides. This style can be worn in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a fringe.

How to Get the Men's Haircut Long on Top Short on Sides

If you want to get the men's haircut long on top short on sides, you should visit a professional hairstylist. They will be able to give you the perfect cut that suits your face shape and hair type. Before you go to the salon, it's a good idea to gather some inspiration pictures to show your stylist. This will help them understand the look you're going for.

Choosing the Right Length

The length on top can vary from a few inches to several inches. The length you choose will depend on your face shape, hair texture, and personal preference. If you have a round face, you may want to keep the top shorter to create a more balanced look. If you have a longer face, you can go for a longer length on top.

Choosing the Right Style

The men's haircut long on top short on sides can be worn in various styles, including slicked back, messy, or with a fringe. You should choose a style that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal preference. If you have curly hair, you may want to go for a messy style. If you have straight hair, you can go for a slicked-back look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get the men's haircut long on top short on sides:

  1. Visit a professional hairstylist
  2. Show them inspiration pictures
  3. Choose the right length and style
  4. Sit back and relax while they work their magic
  5. After the haircut, style your hair as desired

Tips for Maintaining the Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining the men's haircut long on top short on sides:

  • Visit the salon regularly to keep the sides trimmed
  • Use a good quality hair product to style the hair
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair

FAQs

Can I style the men's haircut long on top short on sides differently?

Yes, you can style the men's haircut long on top short on sides in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a fringe.

What face shape suits the men's haircut long on top short on sides?

The men's haircut long on top short on sides can suit various face shapes. If you have a round face, you may want to keep the top shorter to create a more balanced look. If you have a longer face, you can go for a longer length on top.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining the men's haircut long on top short on sides:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a good quality hair product to style the hair
  • Visit the salon regularly to keep the sides trimmed

The men's haircut long on top short on sides is a classic and versatile style that can suit various face shapes and hair types. By following the tips mentioned above, you can maintain this style and keep your hair healthy and stylish. Remember to visit a professional hairstylist to get the perfect cut that suits your face shape and hair type.
