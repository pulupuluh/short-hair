Easy Ponytail Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Daniel Aug 03, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have medium length hair, you know how versatile it can be. You can wear it curly, straight, up, or down. One of the most classic and easy hairstyles for medium length hair is the ponytail. In this article, we will discuss easy ponytail hairstyles for medium length hair that you can create in a matter of minutes.

Description

A ponytail is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you’re going to work, school, or a night out, a ponytail can be dressed up or down. It’s also a great hairstyle for those days when you don’t have time to wash your hair.

How to

Creating a ponytail is easy, but there are a few things to keep in mind.

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Decide where you want to place your ponytail.
  4. Pull your hair back into a ponytail holder.
  5. Secure the ponytail holder tightly to prevent your hair from falling out.

Step by Step

There are many different ways to create a ponytail, so here are a few step-by-step instructions for some easy ponytail hairstyles for medium length hair.

The Classic Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair back and gather it in your hand at the nape of your neck.
  2. Secure it with a ponytail holder.
  3. For a sleek look, use a bit of hairspray to tame any flyaways.

The Messy Ponytail

  1. Start with a low ponytail.
  2. Use your fingers to loosen the hair on the top of your head for a messy look.
  3. Pull a few strands of hair out of the ponytail for an effortless look.

The Braided Ponytail

  1. Start with a low ponytail.
  2. Braid the ponytail and secure it with a small elastic.
  3. For a more polished look, wrap a strand of hair around the elastic and secure it with a bobby pin.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you create the perfect ponytail:

  • Use a brush to smooth out any bumps or lumps in your hair.
  • Use a hair tie that won’t damage your hair, like a scrunchie or a fabric hair tie.
  • Add volume to your ponytail by teasing the hair at the crown of your head.

FAQ

Can I create a ponytail with wet hair?

It’s best to create a ponytail with dry hair, as wet hair is more prone to breakage.

How can I make my ponytail look fuller?

Tease the hair at the crown of your head or use a volumizing spray to add volume to your ponytail.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy, make sure to use a conditioner after shampooing and avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.

In conclusion, the ponytail is a classic, easy hairstyle that is perfect for medium length hair. With a few tips and tricks, you can create a variety of ponytail hairstyles that will take you from day to night.
