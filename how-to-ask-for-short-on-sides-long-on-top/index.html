How To Ask For Short On Sides Long On Top A Relaxed English Language Guide

Written by Robby Mar 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read
If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, the short on sides long on top style might be perfect for you. This cut features shorter hair on the sides with longer hair on the top of the head. It's popular among men and women of all ages, and it can be styled in many different ways to suit your personal taste. But before you head to the salon, you need to know how to ask for short on sides long on top. In this guide, we'll show you how to do it in relaxed English language.

Description

Short on sides long on top is a haircut that features shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on the top of the head. The length of the hair on the top can vary, but it's typically longer than the hair on the sides. This style is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, including slicked back, messy, or even with a pompadour.

How to Ask for Short on Sides Long on Top

Asking for a short on sides long on top haircut is easy once you know the right words to use. Here's how to do it in relaxed English language: 1. Start by telling your stylist that you want a short on sides long on top haircut. You can say something like, "I want my hair to be shorter on the sides and longer on top." 2. Be specific about the length you want. You can say something like, "I want a number two or three on the sides and about two inches of length on the top." 3. If you have a specific style in mind, describe it to your stylist. You can say something like, "I want my hair to be slicked back on the top with a fade on the sides." 4. If you're not sure what style you want, ask your stylist for recommendations. They can suggest a style that will suit your face shape and hair type. 5. Finally, be open to suggestions from your stylist. They are professionals and can offer valuable advice on what will look best on you.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it. 2. Use a comb to part your hair on one side. 3. Use clippers to cut the sides of your hair. Start with a higher guard and work your way down to a lower guard for a fade effect. 4. Use scissors to trim the hair on the top of your head. Cut the hair to the desired length, starting with longer sections and working your way to shorter sections. 5. Style your hair as desired. You can use pomade, gel, or wax to create different looks.

Tips

- Bring a picture of the style you want to show your stylist. - Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing a style. - Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair. - Visit your stylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

FAQ

Q: Is short on sides long on top a good haircut for curly hair? A: Yes, it can be a great haircut for curly hair. Just be sure to ask your stylist to leave more length on top to accommodate for the curls. Q: How often should I get a short on sides long on top haircut? A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the style loses its shape. Generally, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style. Q: Can short on sides long on top be styled for formal events? A: Yes, this style can be versatile enough to be styled for formal events. You can slick back the hair on top and add some shine for a polished look.

Hair Care

To keep your short on sides long on top haircut looking its best, you should follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, using a styling product that works for your hair type, and visiting your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups. In conclusion, asking for a short on sides long on top haircut is easy once you know the right words to use. Be specific about the length and style you want, and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for recommendations. With a little bit of communication and some styling products, you can rock this trendy and versatile haircut.
