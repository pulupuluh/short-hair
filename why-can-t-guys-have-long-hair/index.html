Description

Long hair has long been associated with femininity, and as such, many people believe that men should have short hair. This belief is deeply ingrained in our society and has been reinforced by gender norms and stereotypes. Men with long hair are often seen as rebellious, unprofessional, or even unclean.

However, the truth is that long hair can be just as stylish and professional as short hair. It's all about how you wear it and how you take care of it. Many male celebrities, such as Jared Leto and Chris Hemsworth, have rocked long hair on the red carpet, proving that it can be just as fashionable as any other hairstyle.

Q: Is it true that long hair is harder to maintain?
