How To Cut Your Own Hair With Clippers Male

Written by William Apr 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In this year 2023, cutting your own hair has become increasingly popular. Not only does it save money and time, but it also allows for more control over the final result. However, it can be intimidating to start cutting your own hair, especially for men who are used to going to a barber. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to cut your own hair with clippers (male).

Description

Cutting your own hair with clippers is a simple process that can be done at home. It involves using a set of clippers to trim your hair to your desired length. This can be done on various parts of your head, including the sides and back. With the right tools and techniques, cutting your own hair can be easy and effective.

How to Cut Your Own Hair with Clippers (Male)

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step to cutting your own hair with clippers is to gather your tools. You will need a set of clippers, a comb, a mirror, and a towel. Make sure your clippers have different guard sizes to allow for different lengths of hair.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Before starting, determine the length you want to cut your hair to. Use the different guard sizes to achieve the desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard if needed.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Begin by starting with the sides of your head. Use the comb to lift the hair and run the clippers over it. Work your way up to the top of your head, using different guard sizes as needed. Make sure to keep the clippers level and use the mirror to check for any missed spots.

Step 4: Move to the Back

After finishing the sides, move to the back of your head. Use the same technique as before, starting with a longer guard and working your way down. Use the mirror to check for any missed spots.

Step 5: Trim the Top

After finishing the sides and back, it's time to trim the top of your head. Use the same technique as before, but be careful not to take off too much hair. Use the mirror to check for any uneven spots.

Step 6: Clean Up the Edges

After finishing the main parts of your head, use the clippers without a guard to clean up the edges around your hairline and neck.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid taking off too much hair.
  • Use the mirror to check for any missed spots.
  • Take your time and be patient.
  • Ask for help if needed.

FAQ

Q: Can I use regular scissors to cut my own hair?
A: It is not recommended to use regular scissors to cut your own hair, as they can be difficult to control and can result in uneven cuts.

Q: How often should I cut my own hair?
A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you prefer to have it trimmed. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is a good time frame.

Hair Care

After cutting your own hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type, and avoid using excessive heat on it. Regular trims can help maintain the health of your hair.
