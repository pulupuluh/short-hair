How To Cut Bangs On A Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old wig style and looking to switch things up? Adding bangs to your wig is a great way to transform your look. However, cutting bangs on a wig can be tricky, especially if you don't have any experience with DIY haircuts. Don't worry, though, we've got you covered with this step-by-step guide on how to cut bangs on a wig.

Description

Adding bangs to your wig can give your hair a fresh new look. However, it is important to know how to cut bangs on a wig properly to avoid any damage to the wig and to ensure that the bangs are the right length and style for your face shape.

How to Cut Bangs on a Wig

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut bangs on a wig:

Step 1: Choose Your Wig

Choose a wig that you want to add bangs to. Make sure that the wig is made of human hair or high-quality synthetic hair, as low-quality synthetic hair can melt or burn when exposed to heat.

Step 2: Determine the Length of Your Bangs

Determine the length of your bangs based on your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, longer bangs that sweep to the side can help elongate your face.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by pulling the rest of the wig back with a clip. This will ensure that you only cut the bangs and not the rest of the wig.

Step 4: Wet Your Bangs

Wet your bangs with a spray bottle or by dipping them in a bowl of water. This will make them easier to cut and ensure that they are even.

Step 5: Cut Your Bangs

Using a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, cut your bangs in a straight line, starting from the center of your forehead and working your way outwards. Be sure to cut small sections at a time to avoid cutting too much off.

Step 6: Check Your Bangs

Check your bangs by letting them dry and seeing if they are even. If they are uneven, you can always go back and trim them.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting bangs on a wig:

  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Start with a longer length and cut gradually to avoid cutting too much off
  • Work in small sections to ensure evenness
  • Wet your bangs before cutting to make them easier to manage
  • Consider getting a wig stand to make the process easier

FAQ

Can I cut bangs on a synthetic wig?

Yes, you can cut bangs on a synthetic wig, but make sure that the wig is made of high-quality synthetic hair that can withstand heat. Also, keep in mind that synthetic hair does not grow back, so be sure to cut slowly and gradually.

How do I clean my wig after cutting bangs?

You can clean your wig by gently washing it with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Be sure to avoid getting the bangs wet and use a wig brush to comb out any tangles or knots.

Hair Care

After cutting bangs on your wig, it is important to take care of your new style. Use gentle hair care products and avoid using heat styling tools, as excessive heat can damage the wig. Also, be sure to store your wig properly to avoid tangling or damage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut bangs on your wig like a pro. Experiment with different styles and lengths to find the perfect look for you. Happy cutting!
