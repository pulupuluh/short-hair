How To Make Your Fringe Look Thicker Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Aug 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your thin and lifeless fringe? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with thin fringes that lack volume and fullness. Fortunately, there are several things you can do to make your fringe look thicker and more voluminous. In this blog post, we'll show you how to achieve a thicker fringe with some simple tips and tricks.

What Causes Thin Fringes?

Before we get into the tips on how to make your fringe look thicker, it's important to understand what causes thin fringes. Some of the factors that contribute to thin fringes include:

  • Genetics
  • Diet
  • Hormonal changes
  • Stress
  • Heat damage from styling tools

How to Make Your Fringe Look Thicker

1. Get a Good Haircut

The first step in achieving a thicker fringe is getting a good haircut. It's important to choose a haircut that works with your hair type and face shape. A good haircut can add volume and fullness to your fringe.

2. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help add volume and fullness to your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin and keratin, which can help strengthen and thicken your hair.

3. Blow Dry Your Fringe

Blow drying your fringe can help add volume and fullness. Use a round brush to gently lift the roots of your fringe while blow drying. This will create volume and give your fringe a thicker appearance.

4. Use a Root Lifting Spray

A root lifting spray can help add volume to your fringe. Spray the product at the roots of your hair and massage it in. This will help lift your hair and give your fringe a thicker appearance.

5. Try Hair Extensions

If you're looking for a more dramatic change, hair extensions can help add volume and fullness to your fringe. Clip-in extensions are a great option because they're easy to use and can be removed at any time.

Tips for Maintaining a Thicker Fringe

Once you've achieved a thicker fringe, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining a thicker fringe:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Make My Fringe Look Thicker Without Using Products?

Yes, you can make your fringe look thicker without using products. Blow drying your hair and using a round brush can help add volume and fullness to your fringe.

How Often Should I Wash My Hair?

It's important to find a washing schedule that works for your hair type. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair every day. If you have dry hair, you may be able to wash your hair every other day.

Hair Care Tips for Thicker Fringes

Here are some additional hair care tips for maintaining a thicker fringe:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or elastics that can pull on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather with a hat or scarf

Conclusion

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a thicker and more voluminous fringe. Remember to take care of your hair with a healthy diet, regular trims, and gentle styling. With a little effort, you can achieve the look you want and feel confident in your appearance.
