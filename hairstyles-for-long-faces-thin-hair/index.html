Hairstyles For Long Faces Thin Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Christine Mar 31, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a long face and thin hair, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that will accentuate your features and add volume to your hair without overwhelming your face. In this article, we will explore some of the best hairstyles for long faces and thin hair that are trending in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long face and thin hair can be a tricky combination to work with, but there are plenty of hairstyles that can work for you. The key is to find a style that adds volume to your hair and complements the shape of your face. In this guide, we will explore some of the best hairstyles for long faces thin hair that are trending in 2023.

How To

The first step in finding a hairstyle that works for you is to identify your face shape. If you have a long face, you want to avoid styles that elongate your face even further. Instead, opt for styles that add volume to the sides of your face and create the illusion of width.

Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great hairstyle for long faces thin hair. The layers add volume to your hair, while the bob length creates the illusion of width. This style works well with both straight and wavy hair. To style, use a round brush to blow dry your hair, and finish with a texturizing spray for added volume.

Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, long layers are a great option for long faces thin hair. The layers add volume and movement to your hair, while the length balances out your face shape. This style works well with both straight and wavy hair. To style, use a curling iron to add loose waves, and finish with a volumizing hairspray.

Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are a great way to add volume to your hair and balance out a long face. The angled cut creates the illusion of width, while the side sweep adds movement and volume. This style works well with both straight and wavy hair. To style, blow dry your hair with a round brush, and finish with a texturizing spray.

Step By Step

To achieve these hairstyles, follow these simple steps:

Layered Bob:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Towel dry your hair
  3. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush
  5. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair

Long Layers:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Towel dry your hair
  3. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush
  5. Use a curling iron to add loose waves to your hair
  6. Finish with a volumizing hairspray

Side Swept Bangs:

  1. Wash and condition your hair
  2. Towel dry your hair
  3. Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush
  5. Use a flat iron to create a side part
  6. Blow dry your bangs to the side
  7. Finish with a texturizing spray

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots
  • Avoid heavy, blunt cuts that can elongate your face
  • Use a round brush to add volume and create movement
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what works best for you

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face and thin hair?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too blunt, as these can elongate your face even further. Also avoid styles that are too short, as these can make your hair look thinner.

What hair care products should I use?

Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair. Also use a texturizing spray to add texture and movement.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It is recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your style and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

In addition to styling your hair, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently, as these can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner

With these tips and hairstyles, you can create a look that complements your long face and thin hair. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to try something new!
