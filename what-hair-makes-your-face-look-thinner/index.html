What Hair Makes Your Face Look Thinner Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Mar 10, 2023 · 9 min read

If you are looking for ways to make your face appear slimmer, changing your hairstyle can do wonders. By choosing the right cut and style, you can create an illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones. In this article, we will discuss what hair makes your face look thinner and how to achieve it.

  • Step by Step

How to Make Your Face Look Thinner with Hair

The key to making your face look thinner with hair is to create volume on top while keeping the sides and bottom sleek. This can be achieved through layering, side-swept bangs, and strategic placement of highlights and lowlights. Here are some hairstyles that can help make your face look slimmer:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help create a vertical illusion, making your face appear longer and slimmer. Ask your stylist to add layers that start at your chin or below and blend them seamlessly with the rest of your hair. Avoid layers that start at your cheekbones as they can add width to your face.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help shorten a long forehead and create a diagonal line that slims down your face. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle that follows your brow line and sweep them to the side. Avoid blunt bangs that end at your eyebrows as they can make your face appear wider.

3. Bob with Face-Framing Layers

A bob with face-framing layers can help soften sharp angles and add dimension to your face. Ask your stylist to cut your hair into a chin-length bob and add layers that frame your face. Avoid bobs that end at your jawline as they can make your face appear wider.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can help accentuate your cheekbones and jawline, making your face appear slimmer. Ask your stylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back and leave longer layers on top. Avoid pixie cuts that are too short as they can make your face appear wider.

Step by Step Guide to Achieve Thinner-Looking Face with Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving a thinner-looking face with hair:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to choosing a hairstyle that makes your face look thinner is to determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and tracing the outline of your face on a mirror with a lipstick or eyeliner.

Step 2: Choose the Right Cut

Once you have determined your face shape, choose a cut that flatters your features. As a general rule, choose cuts that add height on top and keep the sides and bottom sleek.

Step 3: Add Layers

Add layers to your hair to create volume on top and add movement. Layers can help create an illusion of a longer and slimmer face.

Step 4: Get Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help create a diagonal line that slims down your face. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle that follows your brow line and sweep them to the side.

Step 5: Add Highlights and Lowlights

Strategic placement of highlights and lowlights can help create depth and dimension to your hair, making your face appear slimmer. Ask your stylist to add highlights and lowlights that frame your face and add movement.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut to Make Your Face Look Thinner

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut to make your face look thinner:

  • Choose a cut that adds height on top and keeps the sides and bottom sleek.
  • Avoid cuts that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs and bobs that end at your jawline.
  • Choose cuts that flatter your face shape, such as long layers for oval faces and pixie cuts for heart-shaped faces.
  • Consult with your stylist to determine the best cut and style for your face shape and hair texture.

FAQ about What Hair Makes Your Face Look Thinner

Q: Can a haircut really make my face look thinner?

A: Yes, choosing the right haircut can create an illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones, making your face appear slimmer.

Q: What face shape looks best with a pixie cut?

A: A pixie cut looks best on heart-shaped and oval faces.

Q: How often should I get a haircut to maintain a thinner-looking face?

A: It depends on your hair growth rate and the style of your haircut. Generally, it is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining a Thinner-Looking Face

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when maintaining a thinner-looking face:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it appear flat.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume on top.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull your hair back and widen your face.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut and style can make a big difference in how your face looks. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a slimmer-looking face with hair.
