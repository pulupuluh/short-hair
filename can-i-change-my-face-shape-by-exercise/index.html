Can I Change My Face Shape By Exercise

Written by William Jul 22, 2023 · 7 min read

If you are wondering, “can I change my face shape by exercise?” the answer is yes! With the right exercises and techniques, you can alter the shape of your face and achieve a more defined, youthful look. In this article, we will explore how to change your face shape through exercise, step by step guides, helpful tips, FAQs and hair care tips.

Description

Your face shape is determined by the structure of your bones, muscles, and fat distribution. While you may not be able to change the underlying bone structure of your face, you can still alter the appearance of your face by targeting and toning specific muscles in your face.

How to Change Your Face Shape through Exercise?

One of the easiest and most effective ways to change your face shape is through facial exercises. These exercises are designed to target specific muscles in your face, helping to tone and strengthen them. Here are some exercises you can try:

1. Jawline Exercise

This exercise helps to tone and define your jawline. Place your thumb under your chin and press upwards, while simultaneously pressing your tongue to the roof of your mouth. Hold for 10 seconds and repeat 10 times.

2. Cheek Exercise

This exercise helps to tone and lift your cheeks. Suck your cheeks in and pucker your lips, as if you were about to kiss someone. Hold for 10 seconds and repeat 10 times.

3. Forehead Exercise

This exercise helps to smooth out wrinkles and lines on your forehead. Place your palms on your forehead and gently press down, using your eyebrows to resist the pressure. Hold for 10 seconds and repeat 10 times.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to help you get started with facial exercises:

Step 1: Warm-up

Before you start any facial exercises, it’s important to warm up your facial muscles. You can do this by gently massaging your face with your fingertips, using circular motions.

Step 2: Choose Your Exercises

Choose the exercises that you want to do based on your goals. For example, if you want to target your jawline, focus on exercises that target the muscles in that area.

Step 3: Start Slowly

Start with just a few repetitions of each exercise and gradually increase the number of repetitions as your facial muscles get stronger.

Step 4: Be Consistent

Consistency is key when it comes to facial exercises. Try to do them every day for best results.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your facial exercises:

1. Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your skin hydrated and plump, which can help enhance the effects of your facial exercises.

2. Avoid Overexertion

Overexerting your facial muscles can lead to strain and even injury. Start slowly, and don’t push yourself too hard.

3. Be Patient

Changing your face shape through exercise takes time and patience. Don’t get discouraged if you don’t see results right away.

FAQ

Q: Can facial exercises really change my face shape?

A: Yes, facial exercises can help tone and define specific muscles in your face, which can alter your face shape.

Q: How long does it take to see results from facial exercises?

A: It can take several weeks or even months to see noticeable results from facial exercises.

Q: Are facial exercises safe?

A: Facial exercises are generally safe, but it’s important to start slowly and avoid overexertion.

Hair Care

While facial exercises can help change your face shape, taking care of your hair can also have a big impact on your overall appearance. Here are some tips:

1. Keep Your Hair Healthy

Regular haircuts, deep conditioning treatments, and avoiding heat styling can help keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Choose a Flattering Style

Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. If you’re not sure what style is best for you, consult with a hairstylist.

3. Experiment with Accessories

Hair accessories like headbands, clips, and scarves can add interest and style to your hair.

In conclusion, changing your face shape through exercise is possible with the right techniques and exercises. By targeting specific muscles in your face, you can achieve a more defined, youthful look. Remember to start slowly, be consistent, and be patient. With time and effort, you can achieve your desired results.
