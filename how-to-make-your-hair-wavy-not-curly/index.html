How To Make Your Hair Wavy Not Curly

Written by Robby Jul 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a way to switch up your hairstyle, waves are a great option. Waves can add volume and texture to your hair without requiring too much effort. However, if you have naturally curly hair, it can be challenging to achieve the wavy look you desire. In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair wavy not curly.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Wavy Not Curly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Your Hair Wavy
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair

How to Make Your Hair Wavy Not Curly

The first step to making your hair wavy is to choose the right hair products. You'll need a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture to your hair. Avoid using heavy products like oils and creams that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Next, you'll need to use the right tools to create waves. A curling iron or wand with a large barrel is ideal for creating loose waves. You can also use a flat iron to create waves, but it requires more skill and practice.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make your hair wavy:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a texturizing spray or mousse to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair until it's about 80% dry.
  4. Section your hair and use a large barrel curling iron or wand to create loose waves. Start at the middle of your hair and wrap your hair around the barrel, leaving the ends out.
  5. Hold the curling iron for a few seconds, then release the curl.
  6. Repeat the process until all sections of your hair are curled.
  7. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the waves.
  8. Spray your hair with a light hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips for Making Your Hair Wavy

Here are some tips to help you make your hair wavy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Curl your hair away from your face for a more natural look.
  • Don't curl the ends of your hair to create a more relaxed wave.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural wave pattern.
  • Sleep with your hair in braids or twists to create natural waves overnight.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your hair wavy:

Can you make curly hair wavy?

Yes, you can make curly hair wavy by using a texturizing spray or mousse and a large barrel curling iron or wand.

How long do wavy hairstyles last?

Wavy hairstyles can last up to 2-3 days with the right maintenance and product use.

Can you make wavy hair curly?

Yes, you can make wavy hair curly by using a smaller barrel curling iron or wand and curling your hair from the root to the ends.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to hydrate and define your waves.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to make your hair wavy not curly, you can switch up your hairstyle and try something new. With the right products and tools, you can achieve the perfect wavy look in no time.
