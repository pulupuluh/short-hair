Guys Haircut Long On Top A Trending Style In 2023

Written by Luffy Mar 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the guys haircut long on top! This style has been trending for a few years now and is still going strong in 2023. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about this style, including how to get it, tips for maintaining it, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

The guys haircut long on top is a style where the hair on the top of the head is left longer than the hair on the sides and back. The length on top can vary, but typically it is at least a few inches long. The sides and back are usually tapered or faded, creating a contrast between the longer top and shorter sides. This style can be worn with a variety of hair textures and can be styled in many different ways, making it a versatile choice for men.

How to

If you're interested in getting a guys haircut long on top, the first step is to find a skilled barber or stylist who can create the look for you. When you go in for your appointment, bring a picture of the style you want so that your stylist can see exactly what you're looking for. From there, the process will vary depending on your hair type and the specific style you want. In general, though, your stylist will likely use clippers to taper or fade the sides and back, leaving the hair on top longer. They may also use scissors to trim and shape the longer hair on top.

Step by Step:

1. Find a skilled barber or stylist.

2. Bring a picture of the style you want.

3. Discuss the specifics of the style with your stylist.

4. Your stylist will likely use clippers to taper or fade the sides and back.

5. They will use scissors to trim and shape the longer hair on top.

Tips

Once you have your guys haircut long on top, it's important to take care of it so that it stays looking great. Here are a few tips for maintaining this style:

- Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

- Use a styling product like pomade or wax to help hold your hair in place and add texture.

- Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

- Visit your barber or stylist for regular trims to keep your style looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about the guys haircut long on top:

What face shape does this style work best on?

This style can work on a variety of face shapes, but it tends to look especially good on men with oval or square-shaped faces.

How do I style my hair once I have this cut?

There are many ways to style hair that's long on top. Some popular options include slicking it back, combing it to the side, or leaving it messy and textured.

Can I still wear a hat with this style?

Yes, you can still wear a hat with a guys haircut long on top. Just make sure to choose a hat that fits well and doesn't flatten your hair too much.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for any style, but it's especially crucial for a guys haircut long on top. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.

- Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as this can damage your hair.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking neat.

- Finally, be gentle with your hair! Avoid pulling or tugging on it too much, and use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle it when it's wet.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a guys haircut long on top with confidence and style.
