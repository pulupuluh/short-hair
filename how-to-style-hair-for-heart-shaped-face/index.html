How To Style Hair For Heart Shaped Face

Written by William Aug 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Heart shaped faces are characterized by a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. If you have this face shape, you may be wondering how to style your hair to flatter your features. Fortunately, there are many hairstyles that can complement a heart shaped face. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to style hair for heart shaped face.

Table of Contents:

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Style Hair for Heart Shaped Face

When it comes to styling hair for heart shaped face, the goal is to balance out the width of the forehead with the narrowness of the chin. You can achieve this by creating volume at the bottom of the hair and avoiding volume at the top.

Short Hair

If you have short hair, a pixie cut or a bob that hits at the jawline can look great on a heart shaped face. A side-swept fringe can also help to balance out the forehead. Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous at the top, as this can make the forehead appear even wider.

Medium Length Hair

For medium length hair, a layered cut with soft, face-framing layers can be flattering. You can also try a lob (long bob) that hits at the collarbone. Again, avoid adding too much volume at the top.

Long Hair

If you have long hair, a layered cut with layers that start at the chin can be flattering. You can also try a side-swept fringe or a middle part with soft waves or curls. Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous at the top.

Step by Step: How to Style Hair for Heart Shaped Face

Here's a step by step guide on how to style hair for heart shaped face:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  2. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on creating volume at the bottom of the hair. Avoid adding too much volume at the top.
  3. If you have short hair, use a flat iron to create soft waves or curls. If you have medium or long hair, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls.
  4. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling hair for heart shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back, as this can accentuate the wideness of the forehead.
  • Avoid heavy bangs, as this can make the forehead appear even wider.
  • Consider a side part, as this can help to balance out the width of the forehead.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous at the top, as this can make the forehead appear even wider.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and balance out the face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to style hair for heart shaped face:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a heart shaped face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous at the top, heavy bangs, and hairstyles that are slicked back.

What hairstyles are flattering for heart shaped face?

Hairstyles that create volume at the bottom of the hair, such as a bob, lob, or layered cut with face-framing layers, can be flattering for heart shaped face.

Should I part my hair in the middle or on the side?

Consider a side part, as this can help to balance out the width of the forehead. However, you can also try a middle part with soft waves or curls.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is important for any hairstyle. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to style hair for heart shaped face, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident. Remember to balance out the width of the forehead with the narrowness of the chin, and avoid adding too much volume at the top of the hair. With the right hairstyle and hair care, you can rock any look!
