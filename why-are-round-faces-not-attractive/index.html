Why Are Round Faces Not Attractive A Comprehensive Guide

Description

Round faces have often been associated with baby faces, and while that may be cute in some instances, it is not always considered attractive in the adult world. The perception of beauty varies from culture to culture and has evolved over time. However, there are some universal standards of beauty, and having a round face may not be one of them.

How To Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into why round faces are not considered attractive, it is essential to determine your face shape. There are six primary face shapes:

  1. Round
  2. Oval
  3. Square
  4. Rectangle
  5. Heart
  6. Diamond

Determining your face shape is crucial as it helps you understand what hairstyles, makeup, and glasses suit you the most.

Step By Step Guide To Identify Round Faces

Here is a step-by-step guide to identifying round faces:

  1. Measure your face from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin and from cheek to cheek.
  2. If the length and width of your face are almost equal, you have a round face.
  3. The cheekbones are the widest part of your face.
  4. Your jawline is rounded, and your chin may be slightly pointed.

Tips To Enhance Round Faces

Just because round faces are not considered attractive does not mean that you cannot enhance them. Here are some tips to enhance round faces:

  • Hairstyle: Opt for hairstyles that add length to your face. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and volume at the crown of the head can create an illusion of a longer face.
  • Makeup: Use contouring techniques to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones.
  • Glasses: Choose angular glasses frames to contrast with the softness of your face.
  • Jewelry: Wear earrings that are longer and more angular to elongate your face.
  • Clothing: Wear v-neck tops or dresses to elongate your neck and create the illusion of a longer face.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about round faces:

  • Q: Are round faces unattractive?
  • A: Round faces are not necessarily unattractive, but they may not be considered conventionally attractive.

  • Q: Can you change your face shape?
  • A: No, you cannot change your face shape, but you can enhance it with certain hairstyles, makeup, and clothing.

  • Q: What hairstyles suit round faces?
  • A: Hairstyles that add length to the face, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, and volume at the crown of the head, suit round faces.

Hair Care For Round Faces

Here are some hair care tips for round faces:

  • Use hair products that add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs and bobs.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add length to your face.
  • Use heat protectant products to protect your hair from heat damage.

In conclusion, round faces may not be considered conventionally attractive, but beauty is subjective. It is essential to embrace and enhance your natural features and find what works best for you.
