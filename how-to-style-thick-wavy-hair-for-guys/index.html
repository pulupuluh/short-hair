How To Style Thick Wavy Hair For Guys

Written by Robby May 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. On the one hand, it looks great when styled properly. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage if you don't know what you're doing. In this blog post, we'll give you some tips on how to style your thick wavy hair so that you can look your best every day.

Description

Thick wavy hair is characterized by its volume and texture. It can be difficult to manage and style, but with the right techniques, you can achieve a variety of looks. Whether you want a sleek and sophisticated style or a more casual, relaxed look, there are options for every occasion.

How to Style Thick Wavy Hair for Guys

First things first, you need to find the right haircut for your hair type. A good haircut can make all the difference when it comes to styling your hair. Look for a stylist who has experience working with thick wavy hair, and ask for their recommendations.

Once you have the right cut, it's time to start styling. Here are some tips:

Step by Step

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

Step 2: Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair. This will help to define your waves and control frizz.

Step 3: Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your waves and create volume.

Step 5: Once your hair is dry, use a styling wax or pomade to define your waves and add texture.

Tips

- Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

- Don't be afraid to use a little bit of hairspray to keep your style in place.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it difficult to style.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my thick wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have an active lifestyle and sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more frequently than someone who leads a more sedentary lifestyle.

Q: Can I style my thick wavy hair without using heat?

A: Yes, there are plenty of heat-free styling techniques that you can use to achieve a variety of looks.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

- Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and nourished.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it difficult to style.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

By following these tips, you can style your thick wavy hair with confidence and achieve a variety of looks that suit your personal style.
