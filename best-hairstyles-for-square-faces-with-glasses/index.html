Best Hairstyles For Square Faces With Glasses

Written by Aprilia Apr 17, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for the best hairstyles for square faces with glasses? Look no further! In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about finding the perfect hairstyle to complement your square face and glasses. From how to determine your face shape to the best styles for your unique features, we’ve got you covered.

Description

Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and straight sides. Glasses can be a stylish accessory for square faces, but it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that complements both features. The right hairstyle can soften the angles of your face and balance out your features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for square faces with glasses, it’s essential to determine your face shape. One way to do this is by pulling your hair back and standing in front of a mirror. Examine the shape of your face and note the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have a square face.

Best Hairstyles for Square Faces with Glasses

1. Soft Layers

Soft, flowing layers can add movement and dimension to a square face. Layers that start at the chin or collarbone can soften a strong jawline and balance out your features. Opt for side-swept bangs to help draw attention away from your forehead and towards your eyes.

2. Side Part

A side part can also help soften a square face. Try a deep side part to create asymmetry and shift the focus away from your jawline.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold and stylish choice for square faces with glasses. Short hair can help elongate your face and draw attention to your eyes. Opt for a textured pixie cut with side-swept bangs to add movement and softness.

4. Long Layers

Long, flowing layers can also be a great choice for square faces. Layers that start at the collarbone or lower can add length to your face and soften your features.

5. Bob

A bob can also be a flattering choice for square faces with glasses. Opt for a chin-length bob with side-swept bangs to balance out your features.

Tips

When choosing a hairstyle for square faces with glasses, keep the following tips in mind:

- Avoid blunt cuts that end at your jawline, as they can emphasize your square features.

- Opt for soft, flowing layers to add movement and dimension to your face.

- Side-swept bangs can help draw attention away from your forehead and towards your eyes.

- A deep side part can create asymmetry and shift the focus away from your jawline.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear glasses if I have a square face?

A: Absolutely! Glasses can be a stylish accessory for square faces. Just be sure to choose a frame that complements your features.

Q: Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a square face?

A: Not necessarily. Short haircuts can be a great choice for square faces, but be sure to choose a style with texture and movement to soften your features.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, be sure to use high-quality hair care products and schedule regular haircuts to maintain your style. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools and avoid over-styling your hair to prevent damage.

In conclusion, there are many great hairstyles for square faces with glasses. Choose a style that complements your features and highlights your unique beauty. With the right hairstyle, you can feel confident and stylish every day.
