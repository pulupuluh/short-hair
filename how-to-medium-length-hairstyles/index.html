How To Medium Length Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of the same old boring hairstyle? Do you want to switch things up but don't know where to start? Look no further, because we've got you covered with our guide on how to medium length hairstyles. Whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair, we've got tips and tricks for every hair type. So, let's get started!

How to Medium Length Hairstyles

Medium length hairstyles are perfect for those who want a versatile look without sacrificing too much length. The key to achieving the perfect medium length hairstyle is to find a style that complements your face shape and hair texture. Here are some popular medium length hairstyles to consider:

1. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a classic style that has been around for decades. This hairstyle is perfect for those with straight or wavy hair and can be customized to suit your face shape. Whether you want a blunt bob or a layered bob, this style is sure to turn heads.

2. Lob Haircut

A lob haircut, also known as a long bob, is a great option for those who want a slightly longer hairstyle. This style is perfect for those with thick hair and can be customized with layers or bangs.

3. Shag Haircut

A shag haircut is a great option for those with curly or wavy hair. This style features layers throughout the hair and can be customized to suit your face shape. The shag haircut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance style.

Step-by-Step Process

Now that you've decided on a medium length hairstyle, it's time to learn how to achieve it. Here's a step-by-step process for achieving the perfect medium length hairstyle:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step in achieving any hairstyle is to start with clean hair. Wash and condition your hair with a product that is suited to your hair type.

2. Apply Heat Protectant

If you plan on using heat tools to style your hair, be sure to apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using any tools.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair until it is about 80% dry. Use a round brush to help smooth out your hair as you dry it.

4. Style Your Hair

Use a curling iron or straightener to style your hair as desired. If you're going for a curly or wavy look, be sure to use a curling iron with a barrel size that is appropriate for your hair length.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray to help hold your style in place.

Tips for Medium Length Hairstyles

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect medium length hairstyle:

1. Use the Right Products

Using products that are suited to your hair type can help you achieve your desired hairstyle. For example, if you have curly hair, use a product that is designed to enhance and define curls.

2. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the perfect medium length hairstyle for you. Try out different cuts, colors, and styling techniques until you find a look that you love.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are essential for maintaining the health and shape of your medium length hairstyle. Be sure to visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim.

FAQs

1. Can I achieve a medium length hairstyle with curly hair?

Absolutely! There are many medium length hairstyles that are perfect for curly hair, such as a shag haircut or a layered bob.

2. How do I choose the right medium length hairstyle for my face shape?

The best way to choose a medium length hairstyle that complements your face shape is to consult with your stylist. They can recommend styles that will look best on you.

3. How can I add volume to my medium length hair?

Use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying your hair to add volume and body to your hair.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hairstyles

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the health and beauty of your medium length hairstyle. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat tools to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip your hair of its natural oils, leading to dry and damaged hair. Try to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect medium length hairstyle in no time. Remember to experiment with different styles and products until you find a look that you love.
