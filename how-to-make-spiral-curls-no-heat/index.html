How To Make Spiral Curls No Heat A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a way to achieve beautiful, bouncy spiral curls without using heat, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps for creating gorgeous curls that are sure to turn heads.

Description

Spiral curls are a popular hairstyle that can add volume and texture to your hair. While many people use heat tools like curling irons or hot rollers to create these curls, there are also no-heat methods that can be just as effective. Not only are these methods gentler on your hair, but they can also save you time and money in the long run.

How to Make Spiral Curls No Heat

Here's what you'll need to get started:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair ties
  • Hair spray (optional)

Follow these steps to create your spiral curls:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

Wash your hair and towel-dry it until it's about 80% dry. You want your hair to be damp, but not dripping wet.

Step 2: Section your hair

Using a wide-tooth comb, divide your hair into sections. The size of the sections will depend on how tight you want your curls to be. Smaller sections will create tighter curls, while larger sections will create looser curls.

Step 3: Twist each section of hair

Take one section of hair and twist it tightly from the roots to the ends. Once you've twisted the entire section, wrap it around itself to create a spiral shape. Secure the end of the spiral with a hair tie. Repeat this process for all of the sections of hair.

Step 4: Let your hair dry completely

Leave the hair ties in place and let your hair air-dry completely. This can take several hours, so you may want to do this before you go to bed and sleep on the spirals overnight.

Step 5: Remove the hair ties

Once your hair is completely dry, remove the hair ties and gently separate the spirals with your fingers. You can also use a wide-tooth comb to gently loosen the curls if they're too tight.

Step 6: Finish with hair spray (optional)

If you want your curls to hold their shape longer, you can finish with a light mist of hair spray. Be sure to hold the can at least 12 inches away from your head to avoid getting too much product on your hair.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results:

  • For tighter curls, use smaller sections of hair and twist them more tightly.
  • For looser curls, use larger sections of hair and twist them less tightly.
  • If you want to reduce frizz, use a leave-in conditioner or anti-frizz serum before you start twisting your hair.
  • For a more natural look, don't worry too much about making the spirals perfect. A little bit of imperfection can add character to your curls.

FAQ

Can you make spiral curls without heat?

Yes, you can create spiral curls without using heat. The method we've outlined in this guide involves twisting damp hair into spirals and letting it air-dry.

How long does it take for spiral curls to dry?

It can take several hours for spiral curls to dry completely, depending on your hair type and the size of the sections you've twisted. You may want to let your hair air-dry overnight to ensure that the curls are fully set.

How can you make spiral curls last longer?

To make your spiral curls last longer, avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day. You can also use a light mist of hair spray to help hold the curls in place.

Hair Care

Creating spiral curls without heat can be a great way to protect your hair from damage. However, it's still important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Be sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too many styling products that can weigh your hair down. If you have dry or damaged hair, consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help restore moisture and shine.
