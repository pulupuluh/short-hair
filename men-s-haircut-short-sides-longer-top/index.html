Men s Haircut Short Sides Longer Top A Trendy Style In 2023

Written by William Apr 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and low-maintenance look, then the men's haircut short sides longer top is the perfect style for you. This hairstyle has been popular for a few years now and continues to be a go-to choice for many men. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways, making it suitable for all occasions. In this article, we'll be discussing how to achieve this look, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care to keep it looking sharp.

Description

The men's haircut short sides longer top is a style where the sides and back of the hair are cut short, while the top is left longer. This haircut is also known as an undercut or a disconnected haircut. The length of the top can vary, ranging from a few inches to several inches, depending on your preference. This style can be worn with a variety of hair textures and types, from straight to curly, making it a versatile look for many men.

How to Achieve the Men's Haircut Short Sides Longer Top

Before you start cutting your hair, you should decide on the length of the top that you'd like to achieve. Once you've decided, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly. This will make it easier to cut and style. If you have curly hair, you can apply a leave-in conditioner to help tame your curls.

Step 2: Cut the sides and back

Using clippers, trim the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. You can choose to have a fade or a more blunt cut. Remember to blend the hair on the sides and back with the top of your hair.

Step 3: Cut the top

Using scissors, trim the top of your hair to your desired length. You can choose to have layers or a more uniform length. Remember to leave the hair on top longer than the sides and back.

Step 4: Style your hair

You can style your hair in various ways, depending on your preference. You can use a pomade or a wax to create a slicked-back look or a messy style. You can also use a hairdryer to create volume.

Tips for Maintaining the Men's Haircut Short Sides Longer Top

To keep your haircut looking sharp, you should follow these tips:

  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve this haircut at home?

A: It's best to have this haircut done by a professional barber, as it requires precision and skill.

Q: Will this haircut suit my face shape?

A: The men's haircut short sides longer top suits most face shapes, but it's best to consult with your barber to find the best style for you.

Q: How long does this haircut take to style?

A: The styling time can vary, depending on the length and texture of your hair. On average, it takes about 10-15 minutes to style.

Hair Care for Men's Haircut Short Sides Longer Top

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, you should follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

In conclusion, the men's haircut short sides longer top is a trendy and versatile style that's perfect for many men. It's a low-maintenance look that can be styled in many different ways. By following these simple steps, tips, and hair care, you can achieve a stylish and sharp look that will turn heads.
