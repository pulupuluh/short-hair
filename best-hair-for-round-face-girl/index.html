Best Hair For Round Face Girl Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 31, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you a girl with a round face and wondering what hairstyle would suit you best? Look no further because we have got you covered! In this article, we will discuss the best hair for round face girl and provide tips and tricks on how to rock them. From long to short, curly to straight, we have got all the options covered. So, let’s dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Best Hair for Round Face Girl
  • Step by Step Guide on Styling Your Hair Based on Your Face Shape

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, it is important to determine your face shape. To do so, follow these steps:

  1. Pull your hair back in a ponytail or tie it back.
  2. Stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or marker.
  3. Compare the shape to the following face shapes: oval, square, heart, diamond, and round.

Now that you have determined that you have a round face shape, let’s move on to the best hair for round face girl.

Best Hair for Round Face Girl

When it comes to hairstyles for round face girls, the goal is to create an illusion of length and angles to balance out the roundness of the face. Here are some options:

Long Layers

Long layers are one of the best hairstyles for round face girls as they create an illusion of length and volume. The layers add movement and texture to the hair, making it look fuller and thicker. You can choose to wear your hair straight or add some loose waves for an effortless look.

Side-Swept Bangs

Adding side-swept bangs to your hair draws attention away from the roundness of your face and creates an illusion of angles. They frame the face beautifully and add a touch of sophistication to any hairstyle.

Messy Bob

A messy bob is a perfect hairstyle for round face girls who want to add volume and texture to their hair. The uneven layers add movement to the hair, making it look fuller and thicker. You can choose to add some loose waves or leave it straight for an effortless look.

High Ponytail

A high ponytail is a great hairstyle for round face girls as it creates an illusion of length and adds height to the face. To make the ponytail look even more stylish, you can add some loose waves to the hair or wrap a piece of hair around the base of the ponytail to hide the hair tie.

Step by Step Guide on Styling Your Hair Based on Your Face Shape

Now that you know the best hair for round face girl, let’s go through a step by step guide on how to style your hair based on your face shape:

Oval Face Shape

For an oval face shape, you can pretty much rock any hairstyle. Lucky you! However, if you want to add some angles to your face, you can opt for a long bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs.

Square Face Shape

For a square face shape, you want to soften the angles of your face. You can do so by wearing your hair in loose waves, a messy bun, or adding layers to your hair.

Heart Face Shape

For a heart face shape, you want to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. You can do so by wearing your hair in a messy updo or adding some volume to the crown of your head.

Diamond Face Shape

For a diamond face shape, you want to soften the angles of your face. You can do so by wearing your hair in a messy bun, adding layers to your hair, or opting for a side-swept fringe.

Tips and Tricks to Keep Your Hair Healthy

Now that you know the best hair for round face girl, it is important to keep your hair healthy. Here are some tips and tricks:

  • Invest in a good shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on a daily basis.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that put pressure on your hairline.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that creates an illusion of length and angles. You can opt for long layers, side-swept bangs, a messy bob, or a high ponytail.

What hairstyles should round faces avoid?

Round faces should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face. This includes blunt bangs, bobs that end at the chin, and straight hairstyles that fall along the face.

Can round faces pull off short hair?

Yes, round faces can pull off short hair as long as it adds volume to the top of the head and creates an illusion of length. You can opt for a pixie cut or a short bob with layers.

Now that you know the best hair for round face girl, it’s time to rock your new hairstyle with confidence. Remember to keep your hair healthy by following the tips and tricks we have provided. Happy styling!
