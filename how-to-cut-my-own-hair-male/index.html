How To Cut My Own Hair Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to save some money and try something new, cutting your own hair is a great option. However, it can be daunting, especially if you've never done it before. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of cutting your own hair male, including how to prepare, what tools you'll need, and the steps to take.

Table of Contents

  • Preparation
  • Tools Needed
  • Step 1: Start with the Sides
  • Step 2: Move to the Top
  • Step 3: Trim the Bangs
  • Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting

Preparation

Before you start cutting your own hair male, you need to prepare yourself and your surroundings. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a well-lit area where you can see what you're doing.
  • Cover the floor and any surfaces with a sheet or towel to catch stray hairs.
  • Wash your hair and dry it completely before you start cutting.
  • Decide on the length and style you want to achieve.

Tools Needed

To cut your own hair male, you'll need the following tools:

  • Clippers with guard attachments
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror

Step 1: Start with the Sides

Begin by attaching a guard to your clippers. Start with the longest guard first and work your way down until you achieve the desired length. Start at the sideburns and move the clippers up and around your head in a straight line. Repeat on the other side.


Step 2: Move to the Top

Next, switch to a longer guard and work on the top of your head. Start from the front and move to the back, using the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to cut it. Work in small sections, making sure to blend the hair as you go.


Step 3: Trim the Bangs

Finally, use scissors to trim your bangs. Start by combing your bangs forward and holding them between your index and middle finger. Cut the hair in a straight line, working from the center outwards. Make sure to cut small sections at a time and check your progress in the mirror.


Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you cut your own hair male:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one.
  • Work in a well-lit area with a mirror.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and make it easier to cut.
  • Cut small sections at a time to avoid mistakes.
  • Check your progress in the mirror often.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair male:

  • Can I cut my own hair with just scissors?
  • How often should I cut my own hair?
  • What should I do if I make a mistake?
  • Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your own hair male, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair to remove any stray hairs.
  • Style your hair as desired using products like gel or pomade.
  • Maintain your hair by trimming it regularly.

With this guide, you now know how to cut your own hair male. Remember to take your time, work in a well-lit area, and follow the steps carefully. With some practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect haircut every time.
