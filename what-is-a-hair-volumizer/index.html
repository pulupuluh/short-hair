What Is A Hair Volumizer

Written by Luffy Apr 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

A hair volumizer is a hair product that helps to increase the volume of your hair. It can be in the form of a spray, mousse, powder, or a cream. The main goal of a hair volumizer is to lift your hair at the roots, giving it more body and fullness. This article will go through the basics of a hair volumizer, how to use it, and some tips for the best results.

How to Use a Hair Volumizer

The first step is to choose the right type of hair volumizer for your hair type. If you have fine hair, then a spray or mousse would be better as it won't weigh your hair down. If you have thick hair, then a powder or cream would work better as it can give more hold and texture.

Next, start by washing your hair and applying a small amount of conditioner to the ends. Rinse thoroughly and gently towel dry your hair. Apply a small amount of the volumizer to the roots of your hair, working in sections. Use your fingers to massage the product in and lift your hair at the roots.

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting your hair at the roots as you go. This will help to create more volume. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer if you have curly or wavy hair. Finish off with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and gently towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply the Volumizer

Apply a small amount of the volumizer to the roots of your hair, working in sections. Use your fingers to massage the product in and lift your hair at the roots.

Step 3: Blow-dry your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting your hair at the roots as you go. This will help to create more volume. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer if you have curly or wavy hair.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Best Results

Here are some tips for the best results when using a hair volumizer:

  • Use a volumizer that is specifically designed for your hair type
  • Apply the product to the roots of your hair, working in sections
  • Use your fingers to massage the product in and lift your hair at the roots
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting your hair at the roots as you go
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer if you have curly or wavy hair
  • Finish off with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place

FAQ

Can I use a hair volumizer every day?

Yes, you can use a hair volumizer every day. However, it's important to choose a product that is gentle enough for daily use.

Will a hair volumizer make my hair look greasy?

No, a hair volumizer should not make your hair look greasy. However, it's important to use the right amount of product and to apply it to the roots of your hair.

Can I use a hair volumizer on wet hair?

Yes, you can use a hair volumizer on wet hair. However, it's important to towel dry your hair first before applying the product.

Hair Care

Using a hair volumizer is a great way to add volume and fullness to your hair. However, it's important to use the right product for your hair type and to apply it correctly. Remember to always towel dry your hair before applying the product and to use a round brush when blow-drying. With these tips, you'll be able to achieve the perfect voluminous hairstyle!
