How to Cut Hair on Top of Head with Scissors

Cutting hair on top of the head involves trimming the hair on the crown area and the sides. It's important to have the right tools and some basic knowledge of hair cutting techniques. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • Mirror
  • Hair spray (optional)

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you have a clean canvas to work with. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and knots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, create a part in the middle of your head. Then, use your sectioning clips to separate the hair on the sides from the hair on the crown area. Clip the hair on the sides out of the way and focus on the hair on the top of your head.

Step 3: Trim the Crown Area

Start by trimming the crown area. Use your comb to lift a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Then, use your scissors to trim the ends to your desired length. Repeat this process, working your way from the back to the front of your head. Remember to go slow and trim small sections at a time.

Step 4: Blend the Sides

Once you're done with the crown area, unclip the hair on the sides and blend it with the hair on top. Use your comb to lift a small section of hair from the side of your head and hold it between your fingers. Then, use your scissors to trim the ends to match the length of the hair on top. Repeat this process, working your way from the back to the front of your head.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Use a mirror to check your work. Make sure that the hair on top is even and the sides are blended with the crown area. If you notice any uneven sections, go back and trim them accordingly.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting your hair on top of your head with scissors:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair.
  3. Trim the crown area.
  4. Blend the sides.
  5. Check your work.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own hair:

  • Invest in a good pair of scissors. Sharp scissors will make it easier to cut your hair and ensure a clean cut.
  • Go slow. It's better to trim small sections at a time than to cut off too much hair.
  • Use a mirror. Make sure that you can see the back of your head in the mirror to ensure an even cut.
  • Don't cut wet hair. Wet hair is more difficult to cut and can result in an uneven cut.
  • Ask for help. If you're having trouble cutting the back of your head, ask a friend or family member for help.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair on top of my head with regular scissors?

While you can technically cut your hair with regular scissors, it's best to invest in a pair of hair cutting scissors. Hair cutting scissors are designed to give you a clean cut and won't damage your hair.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go back and trim the section again. If the mistake is significant, you may need to trim more hair to even it out.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair

After cutting your own hair, it's important to take care of it to ensure that it stays healthy and looks great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can confidently cut your own hair on top of your head with scissors. Happy cutting!
