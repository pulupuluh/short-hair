Mens Hairstyles For Curly Hair 2021 How To Style Your Curls

Written by Luffy Jul 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have curly hair, you may find it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that suits both your personality and your curls. However, with the right guidance and tips, you can rock a stylish look that enhances your natural curls. In this post, we will explore the best mens hairstyles for curly hair 2021, and give you tips on how to style and take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Your Curls
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips for Curly Hair
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Curly Hair

Description

Mens hairstyles for curly hair 2021 are all about embracing natural curls and adding a touch of modernity to the classic cuts. 2021 is all about the messy and textured look, with a focus on short haircuts and taper fades.

Whether you have loose curls, tight ringlets, or a mix of both, there is a hairstyle that will perfectly complement your natural hair. From slicked-back hair to textured quiffs, here are some of the best mens hairstyles for curly hair in 2021.

How to Style Your Curls

The key to styling your curly hair is to work with its natural texture and not against it. Here are some tips to help you style your curls:

  • Start with damp hair to avoid frizz.
  • Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your curls gently.
  • Avoid brushing your curls as it can cause frizz and damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling some of the best mens hairstyles for curly hair:

Short Curly Hair

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.
  3. Use a hairdryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your curls gently.
  4. Scrunch your curls with your hands to enhance their shape.

Textured Quiff

  1. Start with damp hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  3. Apply a hair product that provides texture and hold.
  4. Use your fingers to create a messy and textured quiff.

Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some extra tips to help you maintain and style your curly hair:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your curls in shape and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for curly hair?

The best haircut for curly hair depends on the individual's face shape, hair type, and personal style. However, some popular haircuts for curly hair include the curly shag, tapered fade, and messy crop.

How do I make my curls look defined?

To make your curls look defined, use a curl-enhancing product and a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer. Scrunch your curls with your hands to enhance their shape and add volume.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and hydrated. Here are some tips to help you take care of your curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can dry out your curls.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel to prevent frizz.

With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can rock a stylish look that complements your natural curls. Follow these tips and tricks, and you'll be sure to turn heads with your curly hair in 2021.
