Short Sides Long Top Haircut For Black Females A Guide

Written by Luffy Mar 03, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking to switch things up? A short sides long top haircut may be just what you need. This edgy and versatile cut is perfect for black females who want to make a statement with their hair. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about this haircut, from how to get it to how to take care of it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Short Sides Long Top Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

The short sides long top haircut is a trendy and stylish look that has become increasingly popular among black females. This haircut features short or shaved sides and a longer top, which can be styled in a variety of ways. It's a great choice for those who want a low-maintenance yet edgy hairstyle.

How to Get the Short Sides Long Top Haircut

The first step in getting the short sides long top haircut is to find a stylist who is experienced in this type of cut. Look for someone who has worked with black hair before and has a good understanding of the texture and thickness of your hair.

When you go to the salon, bring pictures of the haircut you want and be clear about what you're looking for. Your stylist will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair short or shaving them completely. Then, they'll leave the top longer, which can be styled in a variety of ways.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the short sides long top haircut:

  1. Find a stylist who is experienced in this type of cut.
  2. Bring pictures of the haircut you want and be clear about what you're looking for.
  3. Your stylist will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair short or shaving them completely.
  4. Then, they'll leave the top longer, which can be styled in a variety of ways.
  5. To style your hair, you can use a variety of products, such as pomade, gel, or wax.
  6. You can also experiment with different hairstyles, such as a slicked-back look, a messy top, or a curly top.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your short sides long top haircut:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.
  • Use high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the short sides long top haircut:

  • Is this haircut easy to maintain? Yes, this haircut is relatively low-maintenance and easy to style on a daily basis.
  • Can this haircut work for all hair types? Yes, this haircut can work for a variety of hair types, including curly, straight, and wavy hair.
  • What face shapes does this haircut work best for? This haircut can work for a variety of face shapes, including oval, round, and square-shaped faces.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to the tips mentioned earlier, here are some additional hair care tips for black females with short sides long top haircuts:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as relaxers and perms, on your hair, as these can damage it over time.

In conclusion, the short sides long top haircut is a stylish and edgy look that can work for black females of all ages and hair types. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can achieve and maintain this trendy haircut with ease.
