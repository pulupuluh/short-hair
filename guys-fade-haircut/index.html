Guys Fade Haircut A Trendy Style For Men In 2023

Written by Eveline Apr 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, a guys fade haircut might be the perfect choice for you. This popular hairstyle involves gradually cutting the hair shorter towards the neck, creating a faded effect. In this article, we will discuss how to get a guys fade haircut, important tips to keep in mind, and how to take care of your new 'do.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get a Guys Fade Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Important Tips to Keep in Mind
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your New Fade

How to Get a Guys Fade Haircut

The first step in getting a guys fade haircut is finding a skilled barber who can create the look you want. Make sure to bring in photos of the style you're interested in, as well as any specific requests you have.

It's also important to decide on the type of fade you want. There are several popular styles, including the high fade, low fade, and mid fade. Each style creates a slightly different look, so choose the one that best suits your face shape and personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a guys fade haircut:

  1. Choose the type of fade you want.
  2. Discuss your preferences with your barber.
  3. Start with a longer length on top and gradually fade the hair shorter towards the neck.
  4. Blend the hair between the different lengths for a smooth transition.
  5. Finish the look with a sharp edge-up around the hairline.

Important Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some important tips to keep in mind when getting a guys fade haircut:

  • Be specific about the type of fade you want.
  • Choose a barber who has experience with fades.
  • Communicate with your barber throughout the process.
  • Consider your hair texture and face shape when choosing a fade style.
  • Maintain your haircut with regular trims and touch-ups.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about guys fade haircuts:

What is a guys fade haircut?

A guys fade haircut involves gradually cutting the hair shorter towards the neck, creating a faded effect.

What are the different types of fades?

There are several popular types of fades, including the high fade, low fade, and mid fade.

How often should you get a fade haircut?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your haircut. Generally, it's recommended to get a touch-up every 2-4 weeks to keep your fade looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Fade

Once you've got your guys fade haircut, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking sharp. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hairdryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair oil or leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Try not to touch your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz and mess up your style.

By following these tips and maintaining your new haircut, you can enjoy a stylish and low-maintenance look with a guys fade haircut.
