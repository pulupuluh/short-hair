What Hairstyles Suit A Chubby Face

Written by Oliver Aug 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a chubby face, you may be wondering what hairstyles will flatter your features. Don't worry, there are plenty of options available that can slim down your face and make you look your best. In this article, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for a chubby face, how to style them, and tips for maintaining these hairstyles.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The right hairstyle can make all the difference when it comes to enhancing your facial features. A chubby face can be slimmed down with the right cut and style. The key is to choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. There are many different hairstyles that can work for a chubby face, from short to long, and everything in between.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for a chubby face is to determine your face shape. If you have a round face, you will want to choose a hairstyle that will add length and height to your face. This can be achieved with a layered cut or a long bob.

If you have a square face, you will want to soften your features with a wispy, layered cut. This will add movement and texture to your hair, creating a more feminine look. Bangs can also be a great option for a square face, as they can help to balance out the features.

Heart-shaped faces can be elongated with a center part and long, loose waves. This will draw attention away from the forehead and chin, creating a more balanced look. A side-swept bang can also be a great option for a heart-shaped face, as it will soften the features.

Step by step

Once you have determined your face shape and chosen a hairstyle that will flatter your features, it's time to style your hair. Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the most popular hairstyles for a chubby face:

Layered Bob

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on creating volume at the crown.
  4. Use a straightener to create loose waves throughout your hair.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Long Layers

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a smoothing serum to the ends of your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on creating volume at the roots.
  4. Use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout your hair.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle and keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a chubby face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs or a bob that ends at chin length. These styles can make your face appear wider and rounder.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a chubby face?

A high ponytail can elongate your face and draw attention to your cheekbones, making it a great option for a chubby face.

Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a chubby face?

No, short haircuts can actually be a great option for a chubby face. Just make sure to choose a style that will elongate your face, such as a pixie cut or a layered bob.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained is key to achieving the perfect hairstyle for a chubby face. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type, and get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Now that you know what hairstyles suit a chubby face, it's time to try out some new styles and find the perfect look for you. With the right cut and style, you can flatter your features and look your best.
