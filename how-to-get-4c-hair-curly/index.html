How To Get 4C Hair Curly A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jul 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have 4C hair, you may have been told that getting your hair curly is impossible. But we're here to tell you that it's not! With a little patience and the right techniques, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps of how to get 4C hair curly.

What is 4C Hair?

4C hair is a hair type that is tightly coiled and has a lot of shrinkage. It is common in people of African descent and can be difficult to manage. Because of its tight coils, it can be hard to get 4C hair to curl.

How to Get 4C Hair Curly

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin the process of getting your hair curly, make sure your hair is clean. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup from your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help moisturize it. A leave-in conditioner will also help to define your curls.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections to make it easier to work with.

Step 4: Apply a Curl Defining Cream

Apply a curl defining cream to each section of your hair. Make sure to distribute the cream evenly through your hair.

Step 5: Use a Denman Brush

Use a Denman brush to define your curls. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

Step 6: Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to diffuse your hair. This will help to set your curls and give them more volume.

Step 7: Air Dry

Let your hair air dry. Avoid touching your hair as it dries to prevent frizz.

Tips for Getting 4C Hair Curly

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying to get your 4C hair curly:

  • Use a deep conditioner regularly to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates, as they can be drying to your hair.

FAQ

Can you get 4C hair curly without heat?

Yes, you can get 4C hair curly without heat. The key is to use the right products and techniques, such as curl defining creams and diffusers.

How often should you wash 4C hair?

You should wash 4C hair once a week to prevent buildup and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care for 4C Hair

Taking care of 4C hair can be a challenge, but it's important to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Here are some tips for taking care of your 4C hair:

  • Use a deep conditioner regularly to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using products that contain sulfates, as they can be drying to your hair.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin or silk bonnet or scarf.

In conclusion, getting 4C hair curly is possible with the right techniques and products. Follow the steps outlined in this article and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
