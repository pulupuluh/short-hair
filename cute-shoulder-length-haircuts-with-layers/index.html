Cute Shoulder Length Haircuts With Layers A Guide To The Perfect Hairstyle

Written by Luffy Jul 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your long locks but not ready to go for a short haircut? Shoulder length haircuts with layers might be the perfect solution for you. This hairstyle is versatile, easy to maintain, and can suit any face shape. In this article, we’ll guide you through how to get the perfect cute shoulder length haircut with layers, answer some frequently asked questions, and provide some tips on how to take care of your new hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Cute Shoulder Length Haircuts with Layers
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Haircuts with Layers
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Shoulder Length Haircuts with Layers

How to Get Cute Shoulder Length Haircuts with Layers

Before you go to your hairstylist, it’s important to know what type of shoulder length haircut with layers you want. Look for inspiration in magazines, social media, or consult with your hairstylist for their opinion. Some popular types of shoulder length haircuts with layers include:

  • Long-layered haircut
  • Choppy layers
  • Blunt cut with layers
  • Side-swept bangs with layers

Once you decide on a hairstyle, it’s time to make an appointment with your hairstylist. Make sure to communicate with them clearly about what type of haircut you want and what length you prefer. They will be able to guide you through the best options for your hair type and face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting a cute shoulder length haircut with layers:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts - two at the front and two at the back.
  3. Begin cutting the back sections, starting at the nape of your neck and working upwards.
  4. Move onto the front sections, cutting downwards and angling the layers towards your face.
  5. Once you’ve finished cutting, style your hair as desired.

Remember to be patient and take your time when cutting your own hair. If you’re not confident in your skills, it’s best to go to a professional hairstylist.

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Haircuts with Layers

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your new shoulder length haircut with layers:

  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to help detangle and style your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as curling or straightening your hair.
  • Try adding some hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length haircuts with layers:

What are the benefits of shoulder length haircuts with layers?

Shoulder length haircuts with layers are versatile, easy to maintain, and can suit any face shape. They can also add volume and texture to your hair, which can help create a more dynamic look.

What type of products should I use on my shoulder length haircut with layers?

It’s important to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Some common products for shoulder length haircuts with layers include volumizing mousse, texturizing spray, and heat protectant spray.

Can I still wear my hair up with a shoulder length haircut with layers?

Absolutely! Shoulder length haircuts with layers can still be styled in a variety of ways, including ponytails, braids, and messy buns.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Haircuts with Layers

Here are some tips on how to take care of your shoulder length haircut with layers:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent over-drying.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

Cute shoulder length haircuts with layers are a great way to switch up your look without committing to a drastic haircut. With the right styling techniques and hair care routine, you can rock this hairstyle with confidence.
