Which Beard Suits Me Online A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jul 26, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you wondering which beard style would suit you the best? With so many options available online, it can be challenging to find the perfect match. However, there are various factors to consider, such as face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we'll give you some tips on how to find the beard style that suits you best, whether you're using an online beard simulator or trying different styles in real life.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before deciding which beard style to choose, it's essential to know your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and triangular. You can determine your face shape by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and chin with a tape measure or ruler. Alternatively, you can use an online face shape calculator to make things easier.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you should opt for a beard style that adds some length to your face. A beard with a pointy chin or a goatee can help elongate your face and make it look more oval.

Square Face Shape

For a square face shape, you should aim for a beard style that softens your angles. A beard with rounded edges or a goatee can help balance your face's proportions and make it look less sharp.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck as almost any beard style will suit you. You can experiment with different styles, from a short beard to a long one, and find the one that fits your personal style.

Heart Face Shape

A heart-shaped face has a broad forehead and narrow chin. You should opt for a beard style that balances your face's proportions, such as a goatee or a beard with a pointy chin. Avoid styles that add too much volume to your chin area.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and chin with wide cheekbones. You should aim for a beard style that adds volume to your chin area while keeping the sides trimmed. A beard with a pointed chin or a goatee can help balance your face's proportions.

Triangular Face Shape

A triangular face shape has a wide forehead and narrow chin. You should aim for a beard style that adds volume to your chin area while keeping the sides trimmed. A beard with a pointed chin or a goatee can help balance your face's proportions.

Using an Online Beard Simulator

If you're not sure which beard style to choose, you can use an online beard simulator. These simulators use your uploaded photo to show you how different beard styles would look on you. Some of the most popular online beard simulators are:

  • BeardStyle
  • Virtual Beard
  • BeardSage
  • BeardWizard

Using an online beard simulator can be a fun way to experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you the best. However, keep in mind that these simulators may not be 100% accurate, and the final result may vary depending on your hair type and growth pattern.

Trying Different Styles in Real Life

If you prefer to try different beard styles in real life, there are some tips you should follow to achieve the best results:

  • Start with a short beard and let it grow gradually. This way, you can see how different lengths and styles suit you without committing to a specific style.
  • Keep your beard well-groomed and trimmed regularly to maintain its shape and avoid looking unkempt.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a goatee, a Van Dyke, a full beard, or a mustache, and find the one that fits your personal style and face shape.
  • Consider your hair type and growth pattern when choosing a beard style. Some styles may require more maintenance and styling than others, so choose the one that fits your lifestyle and grooming routine.

FAQ about Which Beard Suits Me Online

Q: Can I use an online beard simulator on my mobile phone?

A: Yes, most online beard simulators are compatible with mobile devices, and you can upload your photo from your phone's gallery or take a new one.

Q: How long does it take to grow a beard?

A: The time it takes to grow a beard depends on your genetics, age, and other factors. On average, it takes about two to four months to grow a full beard.

Q: Can I change my beard style frequently?

A: Yes, you can change your beard style as often as you like. However, keep in mind that frequent trimming and styling can damage your hair and skin, so it's essential to use the right tools and products and take breaks between styles.

Final Thoughts

Choosing the right beard style can enhance your facial features and give you a unique and stylish look. Whether you're using an online beard simulator or trying different styles in real life, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style. By following the tips and guidelines in this article, you can find the beard style that suits you best and enjoy a new and confident look.
