The Ultimate Guide To Men s Fade Haircut Short

Written by Oliver Feb 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut? Look no further than the men's fade haircut short. This popular hairstyle has been around for decades, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the men's fade haircut short, from how to get it to how to maintain it.

Description

The men's fade haircut short is a hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and back of the head that gradually gets longer as it reaches the top. The hair is faded, meaning there is a smooth transition from short to long hair. This type of haircut can be done with different lengths on top, depending on your preference.

How to Get a Men's Fade Haircut Short

Getting a men's fade haircut short is easy, but it's important to go to a professional barber or hairstylist to ensure the best results. Here are the steps to getting a men's fade haircut short:

Step 1: Choose Your Fade Type

There are different types of fades, including high, low, and mid fades. Decide which one you want before going to the barber. High fades start at the top of the head, while low fades start near the ears. Mid fades are somewhere in between.

Step 2: Decide on Your Hair Length on Top

Do you want short hair on top or a longer length? Communicate this to your barber before they start cutting. You can also bring a picture of the style you want to show them.

Step 3: Get the Haircut

The barber will start cutting your hair with clippers on the sides and back, gradually increasing the guard size as they move up. They will then use scissors to cut the hair on top to your desired length.

Tips for Maintaining a Men's Fade Haircut Short

Once you have your men's fade haircut short, it's essential to maintain it to keep it looking fresh. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Get Regular Touch-Ups

To keep the fade looking sharp, you'll need to get touch-ups every two to three weeks. This will prevent the hair on the sides and back from growing out too much.

Tip 2: Use the Right Styling Products

Depending on your hair type, you may need to use different styling products to keep your hair in place. Choose a product that works well with your hair type, whether it's pomade, gel, or wax.

Tip 3: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly will keep it looking clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

FAQs

What Face Shapes Work Best with a Men's Fade Haircut Short?

Men's fade haircut short works best with oval and square-shaped faces. It can also work for round faces if the hair on top is kept longer.

How Long Does a Men's Fade Haircut Short Last?

A men's fade haircut short can last up to two to three weeks before needing a touch-up.

Can I Style a Men's Fade Haircut Short?

Yes, you can style a men's fade haircut short with different products, depending on your hair type.

Hair Care for Men's Fade Haircut Short

Proper hair care is essential to maintain a men's fade haircut short. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Look for products that are specifically designed for men's hair.

Tip 2: Avoid Overusing Styling Products

Using too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Use only a small amount and work it through your hair evenly.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

Conclusion

The men's fade haircut short is a versatile and stylish hairstyle that's easy to maintain. Whether you want a high, low, or mid fade, communicate your preferences to your barber or hairstylist to get the best results. Remember to get regular touch-ups, use the right styling products, and take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
