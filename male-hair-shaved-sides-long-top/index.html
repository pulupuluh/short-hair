Male Hair Shaved Sides Long Top A Trend That s Here To Stay

Written by Eveline Mar 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a bold, modern hairstyle that's sure to turn heads, look no further than the male hair shaved sides long top trend. This edgy look has been around for a few years now, but it shows no signs of slowing down. In this article, we'll explore everything you need to know about this style, from how to achieve it to how to care for it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Achieve The Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips For Maintaining The Style
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

The male hair shaved sides long top style is exactly what it sounds like: the sides of the head are shaved or buzzed short, while the top is left long and styled. This creates a striking contrast that draws attention to the top of the head. The length of the top can vary, from a few inches to several inches, depending on personal preference and face shape.

This look is popular with men of all ages and can be adapted to suit different hair types and textures. It's also versatile enough to be dressed up or down, making it a great choice for everything from a casual day out to a formal event.

How To Achieve The Look

The male hair shaved sides long top look is best achieved by a professional barber or stylist, but it can also be done at home with the right tools and technique. To get started, you'll need:

  • Clippers or a razor to shave the sides
  • Scissors or a razor to trim the top
  • Styling products, such as pomade or wax

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the male hair shaved sides long top look:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Using clippers or a razor, shave the sides of your head. You can choose how short you want to go, but a #2 or #3 guard is a good place to start.
  3. Next, trim the top of your hair. You can use scissors or a razor to achieve the desired length and shape.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair and style as desired.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold the style in place.

Tips For Maintaining The Style

Once you've achieved the male hair shaved sides long top look, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips to help you do just that:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly to keep the sides trimmed and neat.
  • Use high-quality styling products that won't weigh down your hair or make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Consider getting a touch-up every few weeks to keep the top looking fresh and trim any split ends.

FAQs

What face shape does this style suit best?

The male hair shaved sides long top look can suit a variety of face shapes, but it tends to work best for those with square or oval faces. If you have a round face, consider keeping the top shorter to balance out your features.

Can this style be done with curly hair?

Absolutely! The male hair shaved sides long top look can be adapted to suit curly hair, although you may need to experiment with different products and techniques to get the desired result.

What products should I use to style my hair?

There are a variety of products you can use to style your hair, including pomade, wax, and gel. It's important to choose a high-quality product that won't weigh down your hair or make it look greasy. You may need to experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and texture.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few hair care tips to help you do just that:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve and maintain the male hair shaved sides long top look with ease. Whether you're looking to make a bold statement or simply switch up your style, this trend is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish.
