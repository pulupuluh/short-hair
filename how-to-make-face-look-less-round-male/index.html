How To Make Face Look Less Round Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Mar 11, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you a male with a round face and wondering how to make it look less round? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many men struggle with this issue, but the good news is that there are a variety of ways to achieve a more angular appearance. In this article, we'll explore different methods and techniques to help you achieve a more chiseled and defined look.

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks and a soft, curved jawline. While this may look cute on some people, it can be a source of frustration for men who want a more masculine and angular appearance. Fortunately, there are several ways to make a round face look less round, including through changes in hairstyle, facial hair, and makeup. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a more chiseled and defined look that will leave you feeling confident and attractive.

How to

1. Choose the right hairstyle

The right hairstyle can make all the difference when it comes to making your face look less round. Generally, you'll want to go for a style that adds height and volume to the top of your head, while keeping the sides short and tapered. This will create the illusion of a more angular face shape. Avoid styles that are too flat or round, as these will only emphasize the roundness of your face.

2. Grow facial hair

Facial hair can also help to make your face look less round. A well-groomed beard or goatee can add definition to your jawline and create a more angular look. If you're not a fan of facial hair, consider growing some stubble instead. This can help to break up the roundness of your face and create a more rugged, masculine appearance.

3. Use makeup

If you're looking for a quick fix, makeup can be a great option. By using contouring techniques, you can create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones. Use a matte bronzer to shade the sides of your face and under your cheekbones, and a highlighter to bring out the center of your face. This will create a more angular and chiseled look.

4. Lose weight

If you're carrying extra weight around your face, losing weight can help to create a more defined and angular look. As you lose weight, your face will naturally become more sculpted and defined. Combine a healthy diet with regular exercise to achieve your weight loss goals and create a more chiseled appearance.

Tips

- Avoid round or flat hairstyles

- Experiment with different facial hair styles to find what works best for you

- Use matte makeup products to avoid adding shine to your face

- Drink plenty of water to keep your skin looking healthy and hydrated

- Consider getting a professional haircut and styling advice from a barber or stylist

FAQ

Q: Can losing weight really make my face look less round?

A: Yes, losing weight can help create a more defined and angular look in your face. As you lose weight, your face will naturally become more sculpted and defined.

Q: Is there a particular type of facial hair that's best for making my face look less round?

A: The best type of facial hair will depend on your face shape and personal style. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. A well-groomed beard or goatee can add definition to your jawline and create a more angular look.

Q: Can makeup really make a difference in making my face look less round?

A: Yes, makeup can be a great way to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones. Use contouring techniques and matte products to create a more angular and chiseled look.

Hair Care

When it comes to making your face look less round, choosing the right hairstyle is key. Keep your hair well-groomed and styled to create height and volume at the top of your head. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. And don't forget to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

Conclusion

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily make your face look less round and achieve a more chiseled and defined appearance. Whether you choose to grow facial hair, use makeup, or lose weight, there are plenty of ways to achieve the look you want. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and always stay confident and true to yourself.
