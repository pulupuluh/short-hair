Best Beard Styles For Oblong Face A Comprehensive Guide

Description

An oblong face is characterized by a long and narrow shape with slightly rounded edges. It is also known as a rectangular face. If you have this face shape, you may find that some beard styles don't look good on you. However, there are plenty of beard styles that can complement your facial structure and make you look more handsome and stylish.

How to Choose the Best Beard Styles for Oblong Face?

When choosing a beard style for your oblong face, you need to consider your facial features, hair growth pattern, and personal preferences. Some beard styles can make your face appear longer and narrower, while others can balance out your features and add width to your face.

Here are some of the best beard styles for oblong face:

  • The Full Beard
  • The Anchor Beard
  • The Chin Strap Beard
  • The Circle Beard
  • The Short Boxed Beard

The Full Beard

The full beard is a classic and versatile style that can suit most face shapes, including an oblong face. It features a full, thick beard that grows on the cheeks, jawline, and neck. The length and shape can vary depending on your preference, but it's best to keep it well-groomed and neat to avoid looking unkempt.

The Anchor Beard

The anchor beard is a stylish and edgy beard style that can add definition and balance to an oblong face. It features a pointed chin beard and a mustache that connects to the beard but doesn't extend past the corners of the mouth. This style works best for men with a strong chin and jawline.

The Chin Strap Beard

The chin strap beard is a minimalist and low-maintenance beard style that can suit an oblong face. It features a thin strip of hair that runs along the jawline and connects to the sideburns. This style can add width to your face and make your chin appear more prominent.

The Circle Beard

The circle beard is a classic and timeless beard style that can complement an oblong face. It features a neatly trimmed beard that surrounds the mouth area, including the mustache and soul patch. This style can add a touch of sophistication and maturity to your look.

The Short Boxed Beard

The short boxed beard is a stylish and modern beard style that can suit an oblong face. It features a short, well-groomed beard that is trimmed into a square shape on the chin and jawline. This style can add definition to your face and make your features appear more balanced.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Best Beard Styles for Oblong Face

Here are some steps to help you achieve the best beard styles for oblong face:

  1. Grow out your facial hair for at least one month to see how it grows and to determine your beard style.
  2. Choose a beard style that complements your facial features and hair growth pattern.
  3. Trim your beard regularly to maintain its shape and length.
  4. Use a beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft, moisturized, and healthy.
  5. Consider using a beard comb or brush to keep your beard neat and tidy.
  6. Shave the areas that you don't want to grow hair, such as your cheeks and neck.
  7. Experiment with different beard styles to find the one that suits you the best.

Tips for Maintaining the Best Beard Styles for Oblong Face

Here are some tips for maintaining the best beard styles for oblong face:

  • Wash your beard regularly with a beard shampoo or soap to remove dirt, oil, and bacteria.
  • Avoid over-trimming your beard, as it can lead to patchy or uneven growth.
  • Use a high-quality razor or trimmer to avoid skin irritation, cuts, or ingrown hairs.
  • Avoid touching or scratching your beard too much, as it can damage the hair follicles and cause frizz or split ends.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote beard growth and health.

FAQs

Q: Can an oblong face shape wear a beard?

A: Yes, an oblong face shape can wear a beard. The key is to choose a beard style that complements your facial features and hair growth pattern.

Q: What beard styles should I avoid if I have an oblong face?

A: You should avoid beard styles that make your face appear longer and narrower, such as the long goatee, the ducktail beard, and the extended goatee.

Hair Care Tips for a Healthy Beard

Here are some hair care tips for a healthy beard:

  • Use a beard oil or balm to keep your beard moisturized and soft.
  • Brush or comb your beard regularly to prevent tangling and matting.
  • Trim your beard regularly to maintain its shape and length.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote beard growth and health.
  • Exercise regularly to improve blood circulation and stimulate hair growth.

With these tips and beard styles, you can easily achieve a handsome and stylish look that complements your oblong face shape. Experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you the best.
