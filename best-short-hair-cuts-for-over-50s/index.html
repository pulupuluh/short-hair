Best Short Hair Cuts For Over 50S A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Aprilia Apr 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, it's natural to start thinking about changing our hairstyle to suit our changing style and needs. For many women over 50, short hair cuts offer both convenience and style. But with so many options out there, how do you know which style is right for you? In this guide, we'll explore the best short hair cuts for over 50s and provide tips on how to care for your hair to keep it looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Short Hair Cut for You
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair Cut
  • FAQs About Short Hair Cuts for Over 50s

How to Choose the Best Short Hair Cut for You

When it comes to short hair cuts for over 50s, there are a few factors to consider to find the right style for you:

Face shape

The first thing to consider is your face shape. Different hair styles flatter different face shapes, so it's important to choose a style that complements your features. For example, if you have a round face, a shorter style with layers and height on top can help elongate your face.

Hair texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider. If you have fine hair, a shorter style with layers can create volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can help tame your locks.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is also important to consider. If you're active and on-the-go, a low-maintenance style like a pixie cut or short bob may suit you best. If you're looking for something more formal, a sleek bob or layered cut can be versatile enough for both work and play.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Styling short hair can be quick and easy with the right tools and techniques. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a chic and polished look:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling to ensure it's clean and free of any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair to help tame flyaways and add texture. Use a dime-sized amount of product for fine hair and a quarter-sized amount for thicker hair.

Step 3: Use a round brush

Use a round brush to create volume and movement in your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to lift and shape your hair as you go.

Step 4: Style with heat

Use a hair dryer, flat iron, or curling iron to create your desired style. Use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish your look with a light spritz of hairspray to help hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair Cut

Short hair cuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips to help you care for your hair:

Get regular trims

Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Use heat protectant

When using heat styling tools, always use a heat protectant spray to help prevent damage to your hair.

Invest in quality products

Invest in quality hair care products that are specifically designed for your hair type and needs.

FAQs About Short Hair Cuts for Over 50s

Q: Will a short hair cut make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. The right short hair cut can actually make you look more youthful and modern.

Q: Can I still style my short hair for special occasions?

A: Yes! Short hair cuts can be versatile and stylish for both everyday wear and special occasions.

Q: What if I don't like my short hair cut?

A: Talk to your stylist about your concerns and work together to find a solution that works for you.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Caring for short hair is easy with a few simple tips:

Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

Avoid over-styling

Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools and harsh chemicals, which can damage your hair over time.

Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

With these tips and style suggestions, you can confidently choose a short hair cut that flatters your features and suits your lifestyle. Remember to care for your hair regularly to keep it looking its best.
