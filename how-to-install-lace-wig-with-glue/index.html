How To Install Lace Wig With Glue

Written by Petter Mar 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of struggling with your lace wig every morning? Do you want a more secure and natural-looking installation? Then it's time to learn how to install lace wig with glue! In this article, we will guide you through the process step by step, and provide you with some tips and tricks to make it easier.

Table of Content

  • How to Install Lace Wig with Glue
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Step by Step Guide

Before we start, make sure you have all the necessary tools and products:

  • Lace wig
  • Lace wig adhesive
  • Scissors
  • Rat tail comb
  • Alcohol
  • Cotton swabs

Now, let's get started:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair and Skin

First, make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products. If you have long hair, braid it or put it in a low bun. Next, clean your hairline with alcohol and let it dry completely. This will remove any oils or dirt that could affect the adhesive's hold.

Step 2: Cut the Lace

Using scissors, carefully cut the excess lace around the perimeter of the wig. Be sure to leave a small amount of lace, as you will need it later to attach the wig.

Step 3: Apply Adhesive

Apply a thin layer of lace wig adhesive along your hairline, starting from the center and working your way outwards. Use a cotton swab to spread the adhesive evenly, and be careful not to apply too much, as this will make the wig look bulky and unnatural.

Step 4: Position the Wig

Place the wig on your head, starting from the front and working your way back. Use a rat tail comb to adjust the wig's position and make sure it's centered on your head. Use your fingers to press the wig firmly into place, and use the comb to smooth out any bumps or wrinkles.

Step 5: Secure the Wig

Once the wig is in place, use a small amount of adhesive to secure the lace to your skin. Use a cotton swab to apply the adhesive, and be sure to press the lace firmly into place to create a natural-looking hairline.

Step 6: Style Your Wig

Now that your wig is securely in place, you can style it however you like! Use a flat iron or curling iron to create your desired look, and add some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make your lace wig installation easier and more comfortable:

  • Always use a high-quality lace wig adhesive to ensure a secure hold.
  • Apply the adhesive in thin layers to avoid a bulky look.
  • Don't apply too much pressure when securing the wig, as this can cause discomfort and irritation.
  • Use a silk or satin scarf or bonnet at night to protect your wig and prevent tangling.
  • Wash your wig regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about lace wig installation:

Q: How long will my lace wig stay in place?

A: With proper installation and care, your lace wig can stay in place for up to three weeks.

Q: Can I swim or exercise with a lace wig installed?

A: Yes, you can swim and exercise with a lace wig installed, but be sure to use a waterproof adhesive and protect your wig from excessive sweating.

Q: Can I install a lace wig myself?

A: Yes, you can install a lace wig yourself, but it may take some practice to get the hang of it.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your lace wig looking beautiful and natural. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash your wig.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your wig, as this can damage the hair fibers.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or in a hair net to prevent tangling and damage.
  • Use a detangling brush or wide-tooth comb to remove knots and tangles.

Now that you know how to install lace wig with glue, you can enjoy a more secure and natural-looking hairstyle every day. With practice and patience, you'll be a pro in no time!
