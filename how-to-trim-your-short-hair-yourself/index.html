How To Trim Your Short Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 23, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking to save some money and time by cutting your short hair at home, you've come to the right place. With the right tools and a little bit of patience, you can trim your short hair yourself and achieve the look you want. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to trim your short hair yourself, as well as some tips, FAQs and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Trim Your Short Hair Yourself
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Trimming Your Short Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Advice

How to Trim Your Short Hair Yourself

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over the tools you'll need to trim your short hair at home:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Mirror
  • Towel or cape

Once you have all your tools ready, you can start trimming your short hair. Here's how:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wash your hair and let it dry completely before you start trimming. This will help you see the length and shape of your hair more clearly.
  2. Section your hair. Use hair ties or clips to divide your hair into manageable sections.
  3. Start with the sides. Use your scissors or clippers to trim the hair around your ears and down the sides of your head. Use the comb to hold the hair in place and cut it straight across.
  4. Move to the back of your head. Use your mirror to see the back of your head and trim the hair in a straight line across the nape of your neck.
  5. Trim the top. Use your scissors to cut the hair on top of your head to your desired length. Start at the front and work your way back, taking small sections of hair at a time.
  6. Blend the sides and top. Use your scissors to blend the hair on the sides and top of your head together. Hold the hair with the comb and cut it at an angle to create a seamless transition.
  7. Check your work. Use your mirror to check all angles of your hair and make any additional adjustments if necessary.
  8. Style your hair. Once you're happy with the length and shape of your hair, style it as desired.

Tips for Trimming Your Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when trimming your short hair at home:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed. It's easier to trim more hair than to try to fix a mistake.
  • Use a comb to hold the hair in place while you cut. This will help you achieve a straight, even cut.
  • Don't rush. Take your time and make small adjustments as needed.
  • Use caution when using clippers. They can be powerful and it's easy to accidentally cut too much hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people ask when trimming their short hair at home:

Q: How often should I trim my short hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. As a general rule, you should trim your short hair every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I trim my short hair when it's wet?

A: It's best to trim your short hair when it's dry. Wet hair can be harder to cut evenly and you may end up cutting more than you intended.

Q: Should I use scissors or clippers to trim my short hair?

A: It's up to you and the look you're going for. Scissors are better for achieving a softer, more textured look, while clippers are better for a more precise, uniform cut.

Hair Care Advice

Now that you know how to trim your short hair yourself, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair in shape.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can achieve a great look without having to spend time and money at the salon.
