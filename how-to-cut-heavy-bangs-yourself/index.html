How To Cut Heavy Bangs Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of always having to go to the salon to get your bangs trimmed? Or maybe you just want to try something new with your hair? Whatever your reason may be, cutting your own heavy bangs can be a fun and rewarding experience. And the best part? You don't need to be a professional hairstylist to do it!

Description

Cutting heavy bangs is a great way to update your look without making a drastic change to your hair. Heavy bangs are perfect for framing your face and adding some edge to your style. Plus, they're easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways.

How to Cut Heavy Bangs Yourself

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your bangs, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to section off your hair. It's also a good idea to have a mirror and some hair spray on hand.

Step 2: Section Off Your Hair

Use the comb to section off the hair you want to cut into bangs. Make sure the section is even and straight. You can use hair clips to hold the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want your bangs to be. It's a good idea to start with longer bangs and then gradually cut them shorter if needed. Remember, you can always cut more hair off, but you can't put it back on!

Step 4: Start Cutting

Using your scissors, start cutting your bangs. Make small, vertical cuts to create a textured look. It's important to cut slowly and carefully, as you don't want to cut too much hair off at once.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

Once you've cut your bangs, check your progress in the mirror. Make sure they're even and at the length you want. If you need to make any adjustments, go ahead and do so.

Step 6: Style Your Bangs

Now that you've cut your bangs, it's time to style them. Use a hair dryer and a round brush to blow dry them straight or use a straightener to create a sleek look. You can also use hair spray or styling products to add some volume or texture.

Tips

  • Start with longer bangs and then gradually cut them shorter if needed.
  • Cut slowly and carefully, as you don't want to cut too much hair off at once.
  • Use a comb to section off your hair and hair clips to hold the rest of your hair out of the way.
  • Check your progress in the mirror often to make sure your bangs are even and at the length you want.
  • Use a hair dryer and a round brush to blow dry your bangs straight or use a straightener to create a sleek look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and lengths.

FAQ

Can I cut heavy bangs on curly hair?

Yes, you can cut heavy bangs on curly hair. Just make sure to cut them when your hair is dry and in its natural state, so you can see how they'll look when they're styled.

What if I mess up my bangs?

If you mess up your bangs, don't panic! You can always go to a salon and have them fixed. Or, if you're feeling brave, you can try to fix them yourself by trimming them slowly and carefully.

How often should I trim my bangs?

It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and style.

Hair Care

After cutting your bangs, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. You can also use hair masks or treatments to nourish your hair and keep it looking its best.

Now that you know how to cut heavy bangs yourself, it's time to give it a try! Remember to take it slow and be patient, and soon you'll have a fresh new look that you'll love.
