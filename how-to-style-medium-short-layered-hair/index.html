How To Style Medium Short Layered Hair

Written by Luffy Feb 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you want to switch things up and try something new? Medium short layered hair is a great option for those who want to add some volume and texture to their hair. In this article, we will show you how to style medium short layered hair in different ways.

Description

Medium short layered hair is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. It is a great option for those who want to add some volume and texture to their hair. The layers in this hairstyle add dimension and movement to the hair, making it look more lively and playful.

How to

Styling medium short layered hair is not as difficult as it may seem. With the right tools and products, you can achieve different looks that will suit your mood and personality. Here are some steps on how to style medium short layered hair:

Step 1: Start with clean hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner, and then towel dry it gently. Avoid using a hair dryer as it can damage your hair.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a mousse or a gel, to add some hold and texture. Use a small amount of product and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use the brush to lift your hair and create some movement.

Step 4: Use a curling iron

If you want to create some curls, use a curling iron to add some texture to your hair. Start at the roots and wrap small sections of hair around the curling iron. Hold for a few seconds and then release. Continue this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 5: Finish with a hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a hairspray to hold it in place. Choose a hairspray that is lightweight and flexible, so it doesn't weigh down your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your medium short layered hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you
  • Don't be afraid to try different hairstyles and techniques
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my medium short layered hair?

A: Yes, you can use a hair straightener to straighten your hair. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage.

Q: How often should I wash my medium short layered hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less often. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I color my medium short layered hair?

A: Yes, you can color your hair. Just make sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the color.

Hair Care

To keep your medium short layered hair healthy and shiny, follow these tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Get enough sleep and exercise

Styling medium short layered hair is a fun and creative way to switch up your look. With these tips and techniques, you can achieve different hairstyles that will suit your personality and mood.
