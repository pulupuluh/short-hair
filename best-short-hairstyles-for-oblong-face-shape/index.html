Best Short Hairstyles For Oblong Face Shape In 2023

Written by Frank Jun 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, you know that finding the perfect hairstyle can be a bit of a challenge. But fear not, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the best short hairstyles for oblong face shapes that will flatter your features and make you look your best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with prominent cheekbones and a slightly rounded chin. The goal when choosing a short hairstyle for an oblong face shape is to add volume to the sides of the face to create the illusion of width. This can be achieved through layers, curls, and waves.

How To

When choosing a short hairstyle for an oblong face shape, it's important to keep in mind that you want to add volume to the sides of your face. This can be achieved through layers, curls, and waves. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that has volume at the sides and on top.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or too long.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create volume.
  • Curls and waves can also add volume and width to your face.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a short hairstyle for an oblong face shape:

  1. Identify your face shape.
  2. Research hairstyles that flatter your face shape.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for you.
  4. Take into consideration your hair texture and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle.
  5. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect one for you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for an oblong face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or severe.
  • Consider a side part to create the illusion of width.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories to add interest to your hairstyle.
  • Keep your hair healthy and nourished with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or too long. Also avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back or severe.

What hairstyles are best for oblong face shapes?

Hairstyles that have volume at the sides and on top are best for oblong face shapes. Consider adding layers, curls, or waves to create volume and width.

Can I wear a pixie cut if I have an oblong face shape?

Yes! A pixie cut can be a great option for an oblong face shape. Just make sure to add volume to the sides and on top.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is important when choosing a short hairstyle for an oblong face shape. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect short hairstyle for your oblong face shape. Remember, the key is to add volume to the sides and on top to create the illusion of width. Experiment with different styles and have fun!
