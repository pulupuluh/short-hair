How To Style Thick Men s Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing or a curse. It can be a sign of good health and vitality, but it can also be a pain to manage. Men with thick hair often struggle to find the right style that suits their hair type and face shape. In this guide, we'll show you how to style thick men's hair, step by step. We'll cover everything from choosing the right cut to styling products and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Thick Men's Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Thick Men's Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Thick Men's Hair

How to Style Thick Men's Hair

Before we get started, it's important to understand that not all hairstyles work for thick hair. You need to choose a hairstyle that complements your hair type and face shape. Here are some popular hairstyles for men with thick hair:

  • Textured Crop
  • Pompadour
  • Side Part
  • Quiff
  • Long Wavy Hair

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, it's time to start styling. Here are some general tips for styling thick men's hair:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Use a dime-sized amount for short hair and a quarter-sized amount for long hair.
  3. Spread the product evenly through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.
  4. If you're using a pomade or wax, use a comb to distribute the product evenly through your hair.
  5. If you're using a gel, use your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  6. Blow-dry your hair using a medium to low heat setting. Use a comb or brush to style your hair as you dry it.
  7. Once your hair is dry, apply a small amount of finishing product, such as hairspray or a matte pomade, to keep your style in place.

Tips for Styling Thick Men's Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your thick men's hair:

  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Consider getting a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your hair, as this will help prevent breakage and split ends.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling thick men's hair:

Q: What's the best styling product for thick men's hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and desired style. Pomade, wax, and clay are popular choices for men with thick hair.

Q: How often should I wash my thick men's hair?

A: This depends on your lifestyle and hair type. If you have an active lifestyle and sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: Can I style my thick men's hair without blow-drying it?

A: Yes, you can air-dry your hair and style it without blow-drying. However, blow-drying can help add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Men's Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your thick men's hair looking healthy:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach and hair dye, as these can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to style your thick men's hair with ease. Remember to choose a hairstyle that complements your hair type and face shape, and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
