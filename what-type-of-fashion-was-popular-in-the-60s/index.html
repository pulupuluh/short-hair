What Type Of Fashion Was Popular In The 60S A Guide To Retro Style

Written by Luffy May 10, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you curious about what type of fashion was popular in the 60s? Look no further! In this article, we’ll explore the iconic styles of the decade and how you can incorporate them into your modern wardrobe. From mini skirts to tie-dye shirts, the 60s were a time of bold experimentation and self-expression through fashion.

Description

The 60s were a time of significant cultural change, and fashion was no exception. From the mod style of London to the bohemian vibes of San Francisco, there were many different fashion trends to choose from. Some of the most iconic styles of the decade included mini skirts, bell-bottoms, and tie-dye shirts. The 60s also saw the rise of youth culture, and fashion became a way for young people to rebel against the conservative values of their parents.

How to Incorporate 60s Fashion into Your Wardrobe

If you’re looking to add some retro flair to your wardrobe, there are many ways to incorporate 60s fashion into your everyday style. Here are a few tips:

1. Mini Skirts

Mini skirts were a staple of 60s fashion, and they’re still popular today. Pair a mini skirt with a turtleneck sweater and knee-high boots for a classic look. Or, dress it down with a graphic t-shirt and sneakers.

2. Bell-Bottoms

Bell-bottoms were another iconic style of the 60s. They’re a great way to add some retro flair to your wardrobe. Pair them with a flowy blouse and platform shoes for a bohemian look.

3. Tie-Dye

Tie-dye was a popular DIY trend in the 60s, and it’s still a fun way to add some color to your wardrobe. You can find tie-dye shirts and dresses at most stores, or you can make your own using a tie-dye kit.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide to incorporating 60s fashion into your wardrobe:

1. Research

Research different 60s fashion trends to find the ones that appeal to you. Look for inspiration online, in vintage shops, or in old magazines.

2. Choose Your Pieces

Choose a few key pieces that represent the 60s style you want to emulate. This could be a mini skirt, a pair of bell-bottoms, or a tie-dye shirt.

3. Mix and Match

Mix and match your 60s pieces with modern pieces to create a unique look. For example, pair a mini skirt with a modern t-shirt and sneakers for a fun, casual outfit.

4. Accessorize

Accessorize with 60s-inspired accessories like headbands, hoop earrings, or round sunglasses.

Tips for Wearing 60s Fashion

Here are a few tips for wearing 60s fashion:

- Don’t be afraid to mix and match different styles. - Keep it modern by incorporating modern pieces into your outfit. - Experiment with bold colors and patterns. - Have fun with it!

FAQ

What were the most popular colors in 60s fashion?

The most popular colors in 60s fashion were bright, bold colors like orange, yellow, and green.

What were popular accessories in 60s fashion?

Popular accessories in 60s fashion included headbands, round sunglasses, and hoop earrings.

What was the mod style?

The mod style was a fashion trend that originated in London in the early 60s. It was characterized by bold, geometric patterns, mini skirts, and knee-high boots.

Hair Care

If you’re going for a full 60s look, don’t forget about your hair! Here are a few tips for achieving 60s-inspired hairstyles:

- Try a bouffant or beehive updo for a classic look. - Experiment with hair accessories like headbands or scarves. - Embrace your natural hair texture and go for a curly or wavy look.

In conclusion, 60s fashion was a time of bold experimentation and self-expression through fashion. From mini skirts to tie-dye shirts, there are many ways to incorporate 60s style into your modern wardrobe. Don’t be afraid to mix and match different styles and have fun with it!
