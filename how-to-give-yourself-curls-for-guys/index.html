How to Give Yourself Curls for Guys | Step by Step Guide

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to switch things up? Well, you're in luck. This step-by-step guide will show you how to give yourself curls for guys. Whether you have short or long hair, this guide will work for you.

How to

To give yourself curls, you will need:

  • Hair Gel
  • Hair Dryer
  • Curling Iron
  • Hairspray

Step by Step

Step 1: Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Once you have washed your hair, pat it dry with a towel.

Step 2: Apply hair gel to your hair. Make sure you apply it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Using a hair dryer, dry your hair until it is about 80% dry. Make sure you use a diffuser on your hair dryer to prevent frizz.

Step 4: Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on how much hair you have. If you have long hair, you will need to make more sections.

Step 5: Take one section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for about 10-15 seconds, then release the hair. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.

Step 6: Once you have curled all sections of your hair, spray it with hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

Tip 1: Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 2: Don't use too much hair gel, as this can weigh your hair down and prevent the curls from forming.

Tip 3: If you have short hair, use a smaller curling iron for tighter curls.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a hair straightener instead of a curling iron?

A: No, a hair straightener will not give you the same results as a curling iron.

Q: How long will the curls last?

A: The curls should last all day if you use enough hairspray to hold them in place.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Make sure you use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Additionally, try to avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.

Now that you know how to give yourself curls for guys, it's time to try it out. With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to create the perfect curls every time.
