The Best Haircut For Fat Round Faces A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Luffy Aug 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your round face? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with fuller faces find it difficult to choose a style that flatters their features. Fortunately, there are plenty of haircuts that can help create a more balanced and slimming effect. In this article, we'll explore the best haircut for fat round faces, as well as tips for styling and maintaining your new 'do.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Haircut for Fat Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Fat Round Faces

Description

Before we dive into the best haircuts for round faces, it's important to understand what makes a face appear round. Typically, round faces have a wider forehead and cheekbones, with a rounded chin and jawline. The goal of a good haircut is to create angles and length in the face, which can help balance out these features and create a more defined look.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Fat Round Faces

The best haircut for a round face will vary depending on your hair type, personal style, and lifestyle. However, there are a few general guidelines to keep in mind when choosing a style:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as these can make the face appear wider
  • Choose styles with layers and volume at the crown, which can help elongate the face
  • Consider bangs or face-framing layers to draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones
  • Opt for asymmetrical cuts or styles that create angles in the face, such as a side-swept bob or pixie cut

It's also important to talk to your stylist about your face shape and any concerns you may have. They can offer personalized advice and help you choose a style that works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Haircut

Once you've chosen the perfect haircut for your face shape, it's important to know how to style it to get the most flattering look. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, lifting the crown to add volume
  4. If you have bangs, use a flat iron to straighten them and create a smooth, sleek look
  5. Use a curling iron or wand to add loose waves or curls to the ends of your hair
  6. Finish with a lightweight hairspray or texturizing spray

Remember, the key to styling any haircut is to use the right tools and products for your hair type. Consult with your stylist or a hair care professional to find the best products for your needs.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut

Once you've got your new haircut, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking fresh and flattering. Here are a few tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking sharp and prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray or serum
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style

FAQs

Here are a few frequently asked questions about haircuts for round faces:

What hair length is best for a round face?

While there's no one-size-fits-all answer, generally speaking, mid-length to long haircuts with layers and volume at the crown are flattering for round faces.

Can I wear a bob or pixie cut with a round face?

Absolutely! Bobs and pixie cuts can be flattering for round faces, as long as they're asymmetrical or have face-framing layers to create angles in the face.

Do I have to have bangs with a round face?

No, bangs aren't necessary for round faces. However, if you do choose to have bangs, opt for side-swept or wispy styles that draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

Hair Care for Fat Round Faces

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often (2-3 times a week is ideal)
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage and breakage

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for your face shape, you can create a flattering and stylish look that highlights your best features. Good luck!
