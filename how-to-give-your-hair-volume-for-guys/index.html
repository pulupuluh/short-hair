How To Give Your Hair Volume For Guys

Written by Frank Jun 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a guy with thin or flat hair, you may be wondering how to give your hair volume. Don't worry, there are plenty of ways to achieve a fuller, more voluminous look. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for adding volume to your hair.

Description

Having voluminous hair is a great way to make a statement and stand out from the crowd. However, not all of us are blessed with naturally thick and voluminous hair. Fortunately, there are ways to give your hair volume and achieve the fullness you desire.

How to

The first step in adding volume to your hair is to choose the right haircut. A layered cut can create the illusion of volume and thickness. Talk to your hairstylist about the best options for your hair type and face shape.

Another way to give your hair volume is by blow-drying it. Use a round brush to lift the roots and create volume. Dry your hair in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Finish with a blast of cold air to set the style.

Styling products can also help add volume to your hair. Mousse, volumizing spray, and hair powder are all great options. Apply the product to damp hair and work it through from roots to ends. Blow-dry your hair as usual, using a round brush to create volume.

You can also use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair a boost. These products are designed to add volume and thickness to thin, flat hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like keratin and biotin, which can help strengthen and thicken hair.

Step by Step

1. Choose a layered haircut to create the illusion of volume and thickness.

2. Use a round brush to lift the roots and create volume while blow-drying your hair.

3. Apply a volumizing product, like mousse or hair powder, to damp hair.

4. Blow-dry your hair as usual, using a round brush to create volume.

5. Finish with a blast of cold air to set the style.

6. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair a boost.

Tips

- Don't use too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

- Avoid using heavy oils and creams, as these can also weigh down your hair.

- Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a volumizing product on dry hair?

A: While it's best to apply volumizing products to damp hair, you can also use them on dry hair for a quick pick-me-up.

Q: How often should I use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner?

A: It's best to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner every time you wash your hair, or as directed on the packaging.

Hair Care

In addition to using the right styling products and techniques, it's important to take good care of your hair. This means washing it regularly, using a conditioner to keep it moisturized, and avoiding excessive heat styling and chemical treatments.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve the voluminous look you desire. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you!
