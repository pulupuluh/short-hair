How To Style Side Swept Bangs With Round Brush

If you have side swept bangs, you might be wondering how to style them with a round brush. This technique can help give your bangs a sleek, polished look that's perfect for any occasion. In this blog post, we'll go over the steps to styling your side swept bangs with a round brush, along with some tips and hair care advice to keep your bangs looking their best.

How to Style Side Swept Bangs with Round Brush

To style your side swept bangs with a round brush, you'll need a few tools: a round brush, a blow dryer, and some hair styling products like hairspray or heat protectant spray. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you begin styling your bangs, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can wash your hair the night before and let it air dry, or you can blow dry it before styling. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it with a flat iron before starting.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant Spray

Before using any heat styling tools, it's important to protect your hair from damage. Apply a heat protectant spray to your bangs and the rest of your hair to prevent breakage and split ends.

Step 3: Section Off Your Bangs

Use a comb to section off your bangs from the rest of your hair. You can clip the remaining hair back so it doesn't get in the way.

Step 4: Use a Round Brush to Style Your Bangs

Take your round brush and place it underneath your bangs at the roots. Hold the brush in place with one hand and use your other hand to blow dry your bangs using a low heat setting. Use the brush to guide your bangs in the direction you want them to go.

Step 5: Repeat Until Your Bangs are Dry

Continue using the round brush and blow dryer until your bangs are completely dry. Make sure to keep the heat setting on low to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 6: Apply Hairspray (Optional)

If you want to keep your bangs in place, you can apply a light mist of hairspray. Hold the can about 6 inches away from your hair and spray lightly. Don't use too much or your hair may become stiff and crunchy.

Step by Step Guide

To summarize, here is a step-by-step guide for styling your side swept bangs with a round brush:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply heat protectant spray
  3. Section off your bangs
  4. Use a round brush to style your bangs
  5. Repeat until your bangs are dry
  6. Apply hairspray (optional)

Tips for Styling Side Swept Bangs

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your side swept bangs:

  • Use a round brush with natural bristles to prevent damage to your hair
  • Blow dry your bangs in the opposite direction first to add volume
  • Don't use too much heat or your hair may become damaged
  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place without making them stiff

FAQ about Styling Bangs with a Round Brush

Can I use a flat iron to style my bangs instead of a round brush?

Yes, you can use a flat iron to style your bangs, but a round brush will give you a more natural and polished look.

Do I need to use heat protectant spray?

Yes, it's important to use heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

How often should I style my bangs with a round brush?

You can style your bangs with a round brush as often as you like, but try not to use too much heat or you may damage your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Side Swept Bangs

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your side swept bangs looking their best:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent oil buildup

With these tips and techniques, you can easily style your side swept bangs with a round brush for a sleek and polished look. Remember to always use heat protectant spray and be gentle with your hair to prevent damage. Happy styling!
