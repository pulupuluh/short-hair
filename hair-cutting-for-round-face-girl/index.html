Hair Cutting For Round Face Girls A Complete Guide

If you have a round face, you might be struggling to find the perfect haircut that complements your facial features. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about hair cutting for round face girls. From how to choose the right hairstyle to tips on hair care, we've got it all. So, let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle for a round face can be tricky, but there are a few things you can keep in mind to make the process easier:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head, like a layered bob or a pixie cut.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, like a blunt bob or a center part.
  • Consider getting bangs that hit above your eyebrows to draw attention to your eyes.

Remember, these are just general guidelines. At the end of the day, you should choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Hair for Round Face Girls

While it's always best to leave hair cutting to the professionals, we understand that not everyone has the time or money to visit a salon regularly. So, if you decide to cut your hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back.
  3. Starting at the front, cut your hair at an angle, moving towards the back. Be sure to cut in small sections and use sharp scissors.
  4. To add volume, cut some layers into your hair. Again, cut in small sections and angle the scissors downwards to create a feathered effect.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
  6. For the back, bring all your hair to the front and cut it in a straight line. This will create layers and add volume.
  7. Trim any uneven ends and adjust the length as needed.
  8. Style your hair as desired!

Remember, if you're not comfortable cutting your own hair, it's always best to leave it to the professionals. A bad haircut can be difficult and expensive to fix.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you have your perfect haircut, you'll want to maintain it. Here are a few tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in quality hair care products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant for longer.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I still get a bob if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still get a bob if you have a round face! Just make sure to get one that is layered and has some volume at the top.

2. Will bangs suit my round face?

Yes, bangs can look great on round faces! Just make sure to get bangs that hit above your eyebrows, as this will draw attention to your eyes.

3. How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Round Face Girls

Finally, here are a few tips on how to take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

That's it for our guide to hair cutting for round face girls. We hope you found it helpful and informative! Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Happy styling!
