How To Trim Your Beard To Elongate Your Face

Written by Frank Mar 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Having a beard can be a great way to express your personal style and enhance your facial features. However, if you have a round or square face, your beard may be making your face appear shorter and wider. Fortunately, with a few simple trimming techniques, you can elongate your face and create a more balanced look. In this article, we'll show you how to trim your beard to elongate your face.

How to Trim Your Beard to Elongate Your Face

The key to elongating your face with your beard is to create a more defined jawline and chin. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you start trimming your beard, you need to determine your face shape. This will help you decide which areas of your beard to trim to create the illusion of a longer face. If you have a round or square face, you'll want to focus on creating a more defined jawline and chin. If you have an oblong or rectangular face, you'll want to avoid trimming your beard too short on the sides.

Step 2: Trim Your Neckline

The first step in trimming your beard is to define your neckline. Use a trimmer or razor to create a clean line just above your Adam's apple. This will help create a more defined jawline and make your face appear longer.

Step 3: Trim Your Cheeks

Next, focus on trimming your cheeks. Use a longer guard on your trimmer to create a gradual taper from your sideburns down to your neckline. Avoid trimming your beard too high on your cheeks, as this can make your face appear wider.

Step 4: Define Your Jawline

To create a more defined jawline, trim the hair around your jawbone with a shorter guard. This will help create a more angular look and make your face appear longer.

Step 5: Trim Your Chin

Finally, trim your chin to create a more defined shape. Use a shorter guard to trim any stray hairs and create a more pointed shape. This will help elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Trimming Your Beard

Here is a step-by-step guide to trimming your beard:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Trim your neckline.
  3. Trim your cheeks.
  4. Define your jawline.
  5. Trim your chin.

Tips for Trimming Your Beard

Here are some tips for trimming your beard:

  • Use a quality trimmer or razor.
  • Trim your beard when it is dry.
  • Trim your beard in a well-lit area.
  • Take your time and trim slowly.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress.
  • Don't trim too much off at once.

FAQs

How often should I trim my beard?

You should trim your beard every 2-3 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

What kind of trimmer should I use?

You should use a quality trimmer with different guard lengths to help you achieve the perfect look.

Can I trim my beard when it is wet?

No, you should always trim your beard when it is dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to trim and can lead to uneven results.

Hair Care Tips for Your Beard

Here are some hair care tips for your beard:

  • Wash your beard regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a beard oil to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Comb your beard daily to prevent tangles.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your beard, as this can damage the hair.
  • Trim your beard regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips and techniques, you can trim your beard to elongate your face and create a more balanced look. Remember to take your time and trim slowly, and always use quality tools to achieve the best results.
