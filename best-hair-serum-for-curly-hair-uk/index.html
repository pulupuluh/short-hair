Best Hair Serum For Curly Hair Uk The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Mar 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you searching for the best hair serum for curly hair UK? Look no further. In this article, we will explore the top hair serums that can give your curly hair the nourishment it needs.

Description

Curly hair requires special attention as it is prone to frizz, dryness, and breakage. Hair serums can help to tame frizz, add shine, and protect your hair from damage. However, finding the right serum can be a daunting task. We have compiled a list of the best hair serums for curly hair UK to help you make an informed decision.

How to Choose the Right Hair Serum for Curly Hair

Before we dive into the best hair serums, let's first understand how to choose the right serum for your curly hair.

1. Look for Ingredients

Choose a serum that contains natural ingredients such as argan oil, coconut oil, and shea butter. Avoid serums that have alcohol or sulfates as they can dry out your hair.

2. Hair Type

Consider your hair type when choosing a serum. If you have fine hair, choose a lightweight serum. If you have thick hair, go for a serum that is richer in texture.

3. Purpose

What do you want to achieve with your serum? Do you want to add shine, tame frizz, or protect your curls from heat damage? Choose a serum that caters to your specific needs.

Top 5 Best Hair Serums for Curly Hair UK

1. Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie

This curl-enhancing smoothie is perfect for defining curls, reducing frizz, and adding shine. It contains natural ingredients such as coconut oil, shea butter, and hibiscus flower extract.

2. Cantu Shea Butter Leave-In Conditioning Repair Cream

This leave-in conditioning cream is ideal for restoring and repairing damaged hair. It contains shea butter, argan oil, and jojoba oil.

3. OGX Argan Oil of Morocco Curling Perfection Defining Cream

This curl defining cream contains argan oil that nourishes and hydrates curly hair. It also helps to reduce frizz and add shine.

4. Moroccanoil Curl Defining Cream

This curl defining cream is perfect for creating bouncy, defined curls. It contains argan oil that helps to hydrate and nourish curly hair.

5. L'Oreal Paris Elvive Extraordinary Oil Curl Nourishment Serum

This nourishing serum is perfect for adding shine and taming frizz. It contains a blend of coconut oil and flower oils that leave your curls feeling soft and smooth.

Step by Step Guide on How to Use Hair Serum for Curly Hair

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry.

Step 2: Apply the Serum

Take a small amount of serum and rub it between your palms. Apply it to your damp hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

You can either air dry your hair or use a diffuser to dry your curls. Once your hair is dry, style it as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

To maintain healthy, bouncy curls, follow these tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a scarf.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I use hair serum?

You can use hair serum every time you wash your hair. However, avoid using too much serum as it can weigh down your hair.

2. Can I use hair serum on dry hair?

Yes, you can use hair serum on dry hair to tame frizz and add shine.

3. Can hair serum damage my hair?

No, hair serum does not damage your hair. However, using too much serum can make your hair look greasy.

The Bottom Line

Choosing the right hair serum for your curly hair can make a huge difference in how your hair looks and feels. With this guide, you can find the best hair serum for curly hair UK that suits your specific needs. Remember to follow the tips for maintaining healthy, bouncy curls, and you'll have beautiful hair all year round.
