How To Wear Braids With No Edges Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi May 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling with hair loss or thinning edges but still want to rock braids? Don't worry, you can still achieve a fabulous braided hairstyle even without a full set of edges. In this article, we'll share our top tips and tricks for wearing braids with no edges. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Braids with No Edges
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Flawless Braided Look

How to Wear Braids with No Edges

If you have thinning or no edges, it's important to choose a braiding style that won't put too much tension on your hairline. Here are some of the best braiding styles for those with no edges:

  • Box braids
  • Twists
  • Crochet braids
  • Faux locs
  • Havana twists

These styles are all great because they don't require tight braids at the hairline or lots of pulling and tugging. They also tend to be more forgiving if your edges are uneven or sparse.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for achieving a braided hairstyle with no edges:

  1. Start with clean, detangled hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  2. Part your hair into sections. Use a rat-tail comb to create neat, even parts. You can make the sections as small or large as you like, depending on the size of the braids you want.
  3. Begin braiding. Take a small section of hair and divide it into three pieces. Cross the right piece over the middle piece, then cross the left piece over the middle piece. Keep repeating this pattern, adding more hair to each section as you go.
  4. Continue braiding all the way down to the ends of your hair. Secure the ends with a hair elastic or a small rubber band.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 for each section of hair.
  6. Style your braids as desired. You can leave them loose, tie them up in a ponytail, or create an updo.

Remember to be gentle as you braid, and avoid pulling too tightly on your hairline. This will help prevent further hair loss and breakage.

Tips for a Flawless Braided Look

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a flawless braided look:

  • Use a light, non-greasy oil or serum on your hair before braiding. This will help keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Don't braid your hair too tightly. This can cause tension and damage to your hairline.
  • Wrap your hair at night with a silk or satin scarf or bonnet to protect your braids and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid wearing your braids for too long. Take them out after 6-8 weeks to give your hair a break and prevent damage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing braids with no edges:

  • Q: Can I still wear braids if I have no edges?
  • A: Yes, you can! Just be sure to choose a style that won't put too much tension on your hairline.
  • Q: How can I prevent further hair loss?
  • A: Be gentle with your hair, avoid tight hairstyles, and use gentle products that won't dry out or damage your hair.
  • Q: Can I still use hair extensions with no edges?
  • A: Yes, you can! Just be sure to choose a lightweight, non-damaging option like crochet braids.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to follow when wearing braids:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your scalp clean and healthy.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or moisturizing spray to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Take breaks between braided styles to give your hair a chance to rest and recover.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a beautiful braided hairstyle even if you have no edges. Remember to be gentle with your hair, choose the right style for you, and take good care of your hair and scalp. Happy braiding!
