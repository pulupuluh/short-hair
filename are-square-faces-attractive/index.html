Are Square Faces Attractive

Description

Square faces are often characterized by a prominent jawline, wide forehead, and angular features. This face shape is considered to be one of the most attractive and desirable, as it exudes confidence, strength, and sophistication. In fact, many celebrities and models have square faces, such as Angelina Jolie, Olivia Wilde, and Chris Hemsworth. However, some people may feel self-conscious about their square face and wonder if it is attractive. The answer is yes, square faces are indeed attractive, and with the right styling and grooming techniques, you can enhance your features and look your best.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to enhancing your square face is to determine your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back from your face. Look at the overall shape of your face and the proportions of your features. There are five main face shapes: square, round, oval, heart, and diamond.

If you have a square face, your jawline will be the most prominent feature, and your forehead and cheekbones will be of a similar width. Your face will be angular and defined, with minimal curves.

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Using a tape measure or ruler, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Write down each measurement.

Step 2: Determine Your Face Length

Measure the length of your face from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin. Write down the measurement.

Step 3: Analyze Your Results

If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of a similar width, and your face length is approximately equal to your face width, you have a square face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Enhance Your Square Face

Now that you have determined your face shape, it's time to enhance your features. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Emphasize Your Eyes

One of the best ways to enhance your square face is to emphasize your eyes. Use eyeliner, mascara, and eyeshadow to create a dramatic and sultry look. Avoid harsh lines and angular shapes, and instead, opt for soft and rounded lines.

Step 2: Contour Your Cheekbones

Contouring your cheekbones can help define your face and create a more sculpted look. Use a bronzer or contouring powder that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone, and apply it to the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline.

Step 3: Soften Your Jawline

If you have a prominent jawline, you may want to soften it slightly. Use a blush or highlighter to create a soft, rounded shape on the apples of your cheeks. This will draw attention to your cheeks and away from your jawline.

Step 4: Choose the Right Hairstyle

The right hairstyle can make all the difference when it comes to enhancing your square face. Choose a hairstyle that adds softness and curves to your face, such as long layers or a side-swept fringe. Avoid short, angular cuts that emphasize your jawline.

Step 5: Experiment with Accessories

Accessories can be a great way to enhance your square face. Choose earrings and necklaces that are round or curved, and avoid angular shapes. Hats and headbands can also soften your features and add a touch of femininity.

Tips to Accentuate Your Square Face

Here are some additional tips to accentuate your square face:

  • Wear bold lipstick to draw attention to your lips.
  • Try a cat-eye or winged eyeliner to elongate your eyes.
  • Wear clothing with a V-neck or scoop neck to create a more rounded neckline.
  • Avoid square or angular glasses frames, and instead, opt for round or oval frames.

FAQs About Square Faces

Q: Are square faces masculine?

A: Square faces are often associated with masculinity due to their angular and defined features. However, this does not mean that square faces are exclusively masculine. In fact, many women have square faces, and they can be just as attractive and feminine as any other face shape.

Q: Can square faces wear bangs?

A: Yes, square faces can wear bangs. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape. Soft, side-swept bangs can help soften your features and add a touch of femininity.

Q: What kind of makeup looks best on square faces?

A: Soft, rounded makeup looks best on square faces. Avoid harsh lines and angular shapes, and opt for soft, blended colors. Use bronzer or contouring powder to define your features, and emphasize your eyes with eyeliner, mascara, and eyeshadow.

Hair Care for Square Faces

Here are some hair care tips for square faces:

  • Avoid harsh chemical treatments that can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Choose a hairstyle that adds softness and curves to your face, such as long layers or a side-swept fringe.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look dry and frizzy.

In conclusion, square faces are indeed attractive, and with the right styling and grooming techniques, you can enhance your features and look your best. Emphasize your eyes, contour your cheekbones, soften your jawline, choose the right hairstyle, and experiment with accessories. With a little effort and creativity, you can accentuate your square face and exude confidence, strength, and sophistication.
