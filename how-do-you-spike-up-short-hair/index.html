How To Spike Up Short Hair Tips And Tricks In 2023

Written by Christine Feb 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have short hair, one way to add some edge and style to your look is by spiking it up. Whether you're going for a punk rock vibe or just want to switch up your usual hairstyle, spiking up short hair is a fun and easy way to do it. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for how to spike up short hair in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • How to Spike Up Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Spiking Up Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Spiked Hair

How to Spike Up Short Hair

The first step to spiking up short hair is to choose the right product. Look for a strong hold gel, pomade, or wax that's designed for short hair. You want something that will provide enough hold to keep your hair in place without making it look greasy or weighed down.

Next, you'll want to start with clean, dry hair. Apply a small amount of product to your fingertips and work it through your hair, focusing on the roots and ends. Use your fingers to create the desired shape and texture, pulling your hair up and away from your scalp.

If you have longer sections of hair that won't stay in place, you can use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to help set the style. Use your fingers or a comb to direct the hair where you want it to go, and use the blow dryer to dry it in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for how to spike up short hair:

  1. Choose the right product for your hair type
  2. Start with clean, dry hair
  3. Apply a small amount of product to your fingertips
  4. Work the product through your hair, focusing on the roots and ends
  5. Use your fingers to create the desired shape and texture
  6. Use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to help set the style
  7. Use your fingers or a comb to direct the hair where you want it to go
  8. Use the blow dryer to dry the hair in place
  9. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style

Tips for Spiking Up Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when spiking up short hair:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed
  • Don't overdo it with the product, as it can make your hair look greasy
  • Experiment with different textures and shapes to find the look that works best for you
  • Use a comb or brush to create a more polished look, or use your fingers for a more tousled look
  • Don't be afraid to try new things and have fun with your style!

FAQs

Q: Can I spike up short hair if it's curly?

A: Yes! You may need to use a stronger hold product and a blow dryer to help set the style, but it's definitely possible to spike up short curly hair.

Q: Will spiking up my hair damage it?

A: If you use the right products and don't overdo it, spiking up your hair shouldn't cause any damage. However, if you're using a lot of product or using heat tools frequently, you may notice some damage over time.

Q: How do I remove product buildup from my hair?

A: Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove product buildup from your hair. You can also use a mixture of baking soda and water as a DIY clarifying treatment.

Hair Care Tips for Spiked Hair

When you're spiking up your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to prevent damage and keep it healthy. Here are some hair care tips for spiked hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove product buildup
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat tools too frequently, as they can cause damage
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking healthy

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily spike up your short hair and create a fun, edgy look that's perfect for any occasion. Have fun experimenting with different styles and products, and don't be afraid to get creative with your hair!
