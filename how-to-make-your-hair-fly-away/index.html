How To Make Your Hair Fly Away A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having flat and lifeless hair? Do you want to add some volume and movement to your locks? Look no further than the art of making your hair fly away!

Description

Making your hair fly away is a technique that involves creating lightness and texture in your hair, resulting in a playful and carefree look. This style works best on medium to long length hair and can be achieved through a variety of methods.

How To

There are several ways to make your hair fly away, depending on your hair type and desired look. Here are some steps to follow:

Blow-Dry Technique

1. Start by washing your hair and applying a volumizing mousse or spray to damp hair.

2. Blow-dry your hair upside down, using your fingers to tousle your hair as you go.

3. Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add more volume and texture.

4. Flip your head back up and use a light hairspray to set the style.

Curling Iron Technique

1. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.

2. Use a curling iron to curl small sections of your hair away from your face.

3. Once you have curled your entire head, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.

4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Braiding Technique

1. Start by braiding your hair in several small braids.

2. Use a flat iron to gently heat the braids, creating a crimped effect.

3. Undo the braids and use your fingers to tousle your hair, creating a textured and voluminous look.

4. Finish with a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add more volume and texture.

Step by Step

1. Choose the technique that best suits your hair type and desired look.

2. Prepare your hair by washing and applying the necessary products.

3. Follow the steps outlined for your chosen technique.

4. Use your fingers to tousle and create texture in your hair.

5. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the style in place.

Tips

- Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add more body to your hair.

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different techniques to find what works best for you.

- Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add more volume and texture to your hair.

- For a more drastic effect, try teasing your hair at the roots before styling.

FAQ

Q: Will making my hair fly away damage my hair?

A: No, these techniques are safe for your hair as long as you use a heat protectant spray and don't overuse heat styling tools.

Q: Can I make my hair fly away if I have short hair?

A: It may be more difficult to achieve the same effect with short hair, but you can still add volume and texture through styling products and techniques.

Q: How long will the style last?

A: The style should last for a few hours to a full day, depending on the products used and your hair type.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and texture. Here are some tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

- Limit your use of heat styling tools and always use a heat protectant spray.

- Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can lead to breakage and damage.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

Now that you know how to make your hair fly away, give it a try and have some fun with your locks! Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vitality.
