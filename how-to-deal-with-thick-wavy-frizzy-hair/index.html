How To Deal With Thick Wavy Frizzy Hair

If you have thick wavy frizzy hair, you know how challenging it can be to manage it. But don't worry, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve beautiful, manageable hair. In this article, we will share with you our expert tips on how to deal with thick wavy frizzy hair.

How to Deal with Thick Wavy Frizzy Hair

The first step to dealing with thick wavy frizzy hair is to understand your hair type and its needs. Thick wavy hair tends to be dry and prone to frizz, so it needs moisture and nourishment. Here are some tips to help you manage your hair:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for thick and curly hair. Look for products that contain natural oils like coconut or argan oil, which will help moisturize your hair and reduce frizz. Avoid using products that contain sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it even drier.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to give your hair a boost of moisture. Apply the treatment to your hair, focusing on the ends, and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out. This will help restore your hair's natural moisture and make it more manageable.

3. Don't Wash Your Hair Too Often

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy. Instead, try to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or even less if you can. This will help your hair retain its natural oils and keep it moisturized.

4. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner is a great way to add moisture to your hair throughout the day. Apply it to your hair when it's damp and focus on the ends. This will help prevent tangles and reduce frizz.

5. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When you're combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid tugging and pulling. This will help prevent breakage and reduce frizz. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to help manage your thick wavy frizzy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  3. Don't wash your hair too often.
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair when it's damp.
  5. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  6. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry.
  7. Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  8. Try not to touch your hair too much throughout the day.
  9. Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as these can dry out your hair.
  10. Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

Top Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you manage your thick wavy frizzy hair:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry it out.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair, as these are gentler on your hair than a regular towel.
  • Avoid using hair elastics that have metal parts, as these can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting a silk or satin pillowcase, as these are gentler on your hair than cotton.
  • Try using a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my thick wavy frizzy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant spray and not use heat styling tools too often. Straightening your hair too often can cause damage and make it more prone to frizz.

Q: Can I use a hair mask instead of a deep conditioning treatment?

A: Yes, a hair mask can be a great way to add moisture to your hair. Look for a mask that is specifically formulated for thick and curly hair.

Q: How often should I get a trim?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

Hair Care Routine

Here's a sample hair care routine for thick wavy frizzy hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair when it's damp.
  • Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, manageable hair even if you have thick wavy frizzy hair. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you'll see results in no time.
