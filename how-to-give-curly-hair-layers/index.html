How To Give Curly Hair Layers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 30, 2023 · 6 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On the one hand, it’s voluminous and full of personality. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. If you’re looking to give your curly hair some shape and definition, layers might just be the answer. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the process of giving curly hair layers, from start to finish.

Table of Contents

  • What are layers?
  • How to prepare your hair for layering
  • How to give your curly hair layers
  • Tips for maintaining your layered curly hair
  • FAQs
  • Final thoughts

What are layers?

Layers are a popular haircutting technique that involve cutting different lengths of hair throughout the head, creating depth and dimension. For curly hair, layers can help define the curls and reduce bulk. Layers can also help to frame the face and add movement to the hair.

How to prepare your hair for layering

Before you start cutting, it’s important to prepare your hair for layering. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair as you normally would.
  • Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or brush.
  • Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.
  • Consider cutting your hair when it’s dry, as curly hair can shrink when it’s wet.

How to give your curly hair layers

Now that your hair is prepped and ready to go, it’s time to start cutting. Here’s how to give your curly hair layers:

  1. Divide your hair into sections. Use clips or hair ties to keep each section separate.
  2. Start at the bottom of your hair and work your way up.
  3. Use a pair of sharp scissors to cut a small amount of hair at a time. Start with longer layers and work your way up to shorter layers.
  4. Be sure to cut each section evenly to maintain balance throughout your hair.
  5. Use a mirror to check your progress as you go.
  6. When you’re finished, use your fingers to shake out your curls and see how they fall.

Tips for maintaining your layered curly hair

Now that you’ve given your curly hair layers, it’s important to take care of it properly to maintain the style. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair, as it can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to enhance your curls.

FAQs

1. How often should I get my curly hair layered?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule of thumb, you should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

2. Can I cut my curly hair myself?

While it’s possible to cut your own hair at home, it’s recommended that you see a professional stylist for best results. Curly hair can be tricky to cut, and a stylist will have the experience and expertise to give you the best possible outcome.

3. Will layers make my curly hair frizzy?

Layers can actually help reduce frizz by reducing bulk and defining your curls. However, if you don’t take care of your hair properly, it can become frizzy regardless of whether you have layers or not.

Final thoughts

Giving your curly hair layers can be a great way to enhance your natural texture and add shape to your hair. By following the steps outlined in this guide and taking care of your hair properly, you can enjoy your new layered look for weeks to come.
