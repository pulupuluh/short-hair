How To Do Your Own Undercut Male A Step By Step Guide For 2023

Written by Robby Apr 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune for a haircut every month? Want to try a new look without breaking the bank? Or are you just feeling adventurous and ready to take on a DIY project? Whatever your reason may be, learning how to do your own undercut male is easier than you think! Follow this step-by-step guide for a fresh new look.

Description

The undercut hairstyle has been a popular trend for men in recent years, and for good reason. This edgy style features short sides and back with longer hair on top, creating a contrast that is both stylish and versatile. While it may seem intimidating to try at first, doing your own undercut male can save you time and money in the long run.

How to

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You will need clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also helpful to have a spray bottle filled with water and a towel nearby.

Step 1: Determine Your Desired Length

Before you start cutting, decide on the length you want for your hair. Use the clippers to buzz the sides and back of your head to your desired length. Start with a higher guard and work your way down until you find the perfect length.

Step 2: Create a Guide

Next, use the comb to separate the longer hair on top from the buzzed sides and back. Create a guide by combing the longer hair straight up and cutting it to your desired length. Use the scissors to carefully trim the ends, working from the back to the front.

Step 3: Blend the Sides and Top

To blend the sides and top, use the clippers to create a fade. Start with a higher guard and work your way down, blending the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back. Use a comb to check for any uneven spots and make adjustments as needed.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting, style your hair as desired. Use a pomade, wax, or gel to create a sleek or messy look. Don't be afraid to experiment and find the style that works best for you.

Tips

- Start with a higher guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.

- Check for any uneven spots by using a comb and mirror.

- Take your time and be patient. It's better to go slow and get it right than rush and make a mistake.

- Don't be afraid to ask for help if needed, especially for hard-to-reach areas.

FAQ

Q: How often should I do my own undercut male?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain the look. Most men choose to cut their hair every 3-4 weeks.

Q: Can I do an undercut with curly or wavy hair?

A: Yes, an undercut can work with any hair type. However, it may require more maintenance and styling to keep the shape and texture.

Hair Care

To keep your undercut looking fresh and stylish, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and avoid over-washing. Trim the sides and back regularly to maintain the shape and avoid split ends. And don't forget to style your hair with a product that works for you.

With these tips and steps, you can confidently do your own undercut male and rock a fresh new look. Happy cutting!
