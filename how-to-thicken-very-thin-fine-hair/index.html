How To Thicken Very Thin Fine Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel May 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you have very thin, fine hair and are looking for ways to add volume and thickness? You're not alone! Many people struggle with thin hair, but there are several tips and tricks you can use to make your hair look thicker and fuller. In this article, we'll explore some of the best ways to thicken very thin fine hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Thicken Very Thin Fine Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thin Hair

How to Thicken Very Thin Fine Hair

There are several ways to thicken very thin fine hair, including:

  • Adding layers to your haircut
  • Using volumizing products
  • Blow drying your hair upside down
  • Using a root lifter
  • Trying hair extensions or a hair piece

Keep reading for a step-by-step guide on how to use these methods to achieve thicker hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Get a layered haircut

Adding layers to your hair can make it look thicker and fuller. Ask your stylist for a layered cut that will add volume to your hair.

2. Use volumizing products

Choose shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are specifically formulated to add volume to hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like keratin, biotin, and collagen.

3. Blow dry your hair upside down

Blow drying your hair upside down can help create volume at the roots. Use a round brush to lift your hair as you blow dry it.

4. Use a root lifter

A root lifter is a spray or mousse that is applied to the roots of your hair to add volume. Apply it to damp hair before blow drying.

5. Try hair extensions or a hair piece

If you're looking for a more dramatic change, consider trying hair extensions or a hair piece. These can add instant volume and thickness to your hair.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips for thickening very thin fine hair:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils that make it look thicker.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to avoid pulling out hair and causing breakage.
  • Avoid using heat tools like straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Consider taking hair growth supplements or changing your diet to include more protein and nutrients that promote hair growth.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about thickening very thin fine hair:

Can I thicken my hair naturally?

Yes, there are several natural remedies that can help thicken hair, including using castor oil, onion juice, and aloe vera.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every other day or every two days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.

Hair Care for Thin Hair

In addition to the tips and tricks mentioned above, it's important to take good care of your hair if you have very thin fine hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dyes, as these can weaken your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can also damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Consider using a silk pillowcase to avoid hair breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly into a ponytail or bun, as this can cause breakage and make your hair look thinner.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve thicker, fuller hair, even if you have very thin fine hair. Experiment with different methods to find what works best for you, and remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
