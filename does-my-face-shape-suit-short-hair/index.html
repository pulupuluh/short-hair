Does My Face Shape Suit Short Hair

Written by Daniel Jul 23, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Short hair is an incredibly stylish and trendy look, but it's not always easy to know if it will suit your face shape. If you're considering a chop, you might be wondering: does my face shape suit short hair? In this article, we'll explore the answer to that question, and provide you with some tips and tricks for rocking short hair with confidence.

Description

Short hair can be a great option for those looking for an easy-to-manage, low-maintenance hairstyle. However, it's important to consider your face shape when deciding if short hair is right for you. A haircut that looks great on one person might not necessarily work on another, so it's important to take your unique features into account.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're not sure what your face shape is, there are a few easy ways to find out. One option is to stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner. Another option is to take a photo of yourself and use an app or website that can analyze your features and determine your face shape.

There are generally six different face shapes: oval, round, square, diamond, heart, and long. Each shape has its own unique features and characteristics, which can help you determine what hairstyles will look best on you.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck â almost any hairstyle will suit you! Your face is longer than it is wide, with gently rounded features and a slightly narrower jawline. You can pull off short hair with ease, whether it's a pixie cut or a bob.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a short haircut that adds definition and angles to your features. A layered bob or a side-swept pixie cut can work well, as can a hairstyle with lots of volume at the crown.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to soften your features with a short haircut that has lots of texture and movement. A choppy bob or a tousled pixie cut can work well, as can a style with long, side-swept bangs.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you'll want to choose a short haircut that balances out your features. A layered bob or a choppy pixie cut can work well, as can a style with lots of volume at the crown and soft, side-swept bangs.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to choose a short haircut that adds volume to your lower face and balances out your forehead. A textured bob or a pixie cut with lots of volume at the crown can work well, as can a style with side-swept bangs.

Long Face Shape

If you have a long face shape, you'll want to choose a short haircut that adds width and volume to your features. A layered bob or a choppy pixie cut can work well, as can a style with lots of volume at the crown and soft, side-swept bangs.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape and which hairstyles will suit you best, it's time to take the plunge and chop off your locks! Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get the perfect short haircut:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that will suit your face shape.
  2. Bring photos of the style you want to your stylist.
  3. Discuss your hair texture and thickness with your stylist.
  4. Ask your stylist to show you how to style your new haircut.
  5. Invest in good-quality hair products to keep your new style looking fresh.

Tips for Rocking Short Hair

Short hair can be a big change, so it's important to be prepared for the transition. Here are some tips to help you rock your new look:

  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, clips, and scarves.
  • Be patient â it may take some time to get used to your new look.
  • Embrace your unique features and be confident!

Frequently Asked Questions

Will short hair make my face look wider?

Short hair can actually make your face look narrower, especially if you choose a style that adds volume at the crown and around the sides of your face.

Can I still style my short hair for special occasions?

Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways for special occasions, from sleek and sophisticated to tousled and textured.

How often will I need to get my short hair cut?

This will depend on your hair texture and how quickly it grows. Generally, you'll want to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires a slightly different hair care routine than longer hair. Here are some tips to keep your short locks looking their best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and use it on a low heat setting.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Overall, whether or not short hair will suit your face shape will depend on a variety of factors, including your unique features, hair texture, and personal style. By taking the time to determine your face shape and choose a haircut that flatters your features, you can confidently rock a stylish and trendy short hairstyle.
