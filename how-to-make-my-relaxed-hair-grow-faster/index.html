How To Make My Relaxed Hair Grow Faster Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver May 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having short, brittle hair? Do you dream of having long, luscious locks? If so, you're not alone. Many women struggle with growing their hair, and it can be especially challenging when you have relaxed hair. But don't worry, there are ways to make your relaxed hair grow faster. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you achieve your hair growth goals.

Table of Contents

  • How Hair Growth Works
  • How to Make Your Relaxed Hair Grow Faster

How Hair Growth Works

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, let's take a quick look at how hair growth works. Hair growth is a complex process that involves several stages. The first stage is the anagen phase, which is the active growth phase. During this phase, your hair grows about half an inch per month. This phase can last anywhere from two to six years, depending on your genetics and other factors.

After the anagen phase comes the catagen phase, which is a transitional phase. During this phase, your hair stops growing and the follicle starts to shrink. This phase lasts about two weeks. Finally, there's the telogen phase, which is the resting phase. During this phase, your hair falls out and the follicle remains inactive for about three months. Then, the cycle starts all over again.

How to Make Your Relaxed Hair Grow Faster

If you want to make your relaxed hair grow faster, there are several things you can do. Here are some tips:

Eat a Healthy Diet

One of the most important things you can do for your hair is to eat a healthy, balanced diet. Your hair needs nutrients like protein, iron, and biotin to grow strong and healthy. Make sure you're getting plenty of lean protein, leafy greens, and whole grains in your diet. You may also want to consider taking a multivitamin or hair growth supplement.

Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help stimulate blood flow to the hair follicles, which can promote hair growth. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for a few minutes each day. You can also try using a scalp massager for extra stimulation.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause breakage. If you want to make your hair grow faster, try to avoid using heat styling as much as possible. If you must use a heat styling tool, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

Protect Your Hair

Protecting your hair from damage is crucial if you want it to grow faster. Wear a silk or satin scarf or bonnet to bed to prevent friction and breakage. Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, like braids and ponytails. And be gentle when you brush or comb your hair.

FAQ

How long does it take for hair to grow?

On average, hair grows about half an inch per month. However, this can vary depending on genetics, diet, and other factors.

Can relaxed hair grow as fast as natural hair?

Relaxed hair can grow just as fast as natural hair, but it may require extra care and attention to keep it healthy and strong.

Do hair growth supplements really work?

Hair growth supplements can be helpful for some people, but they're not a magic solution. It's still important to eat a healthy diet and take care of your hair to promote growth.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you care for your relaxed hair:

  • Wash your hair once a week with a gentle shampoo.
  • Deep condition your hair once a month to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, which can dry out your hair.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Be patient! Hair growth takes time, so don't get discouraged if you don't see results right away.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your relaxed hair grow faster and achieve the long, healthy hair you've always wanted. Remember to be patient and consistent, and your hair will thank you!
