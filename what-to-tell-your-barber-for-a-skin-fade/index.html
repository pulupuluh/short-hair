What To Tell Your Barber For A Skin Fade

Written by Eveline Apr 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to get a skin fade haircut but don't know what to tell your barber? Look no further, because we've got you covered. In this article, we'll walk you through what to say and give you tips on how to get the perfect skin fade.

Description

A skin fade is a popular haircut that involves gradually fading the hair from short to shorter or even bald on the sides and back, while leaving the hair on top longer. It's a trendy and stylish look that can be worn by men of all ages.

How To

When you're ready to get a skin fade, here's what to tell your barber:

  • Ask for a skin fade on the sides and back
  • Specify how short you want the fade to be (high, mid, or low)
  • Decide on the length of hair you want on top
  • Discuss any additional details, such as a hard part or a line-up

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get the perfect skin fade:

  1. Find a reputable barber who specializes in skin fades
  2. Bring a photo of the style you want or be prepared to describe it in detail
  3. Discuss any concerns or questions you have with your barber
  4. Sit back and relax while your barber works their magic
  5. Ask for any styling tips or product recommendations to maintain your new haircut

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a skin fade:

  • Don't be afraid to communicate with your barber
  • Be specific about the length and style you want
  • Consider your face shape and hair type when deciding on a skin fade
  • Bring in a photo of the style you want for reference
  • Take care of your hair before and after your haircut to keep it looking fresh

FAQ

What is a skin fade?

A skin fade is a haircut that gradually fades the hair from short to shorter or even bald on the sides and back, while leaving the hair on top longer.

How short should I ask for the fade to be?

You can ask for a high, mid, or low fade depending on your preference. A high fade starts at the temples and goes up to the top of the head, a mid fade starts in the middle of the head, and a low fade starts just above the ears.

What products should I use to maintain my skin fade?

Use a good quality pomade or hair wax to style your hair and keep it in place. You can also use a hair spray to set your style.

Hair Care

After getting a skin fade, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking fresh. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners
  • Use a hair oil or serum to keep your hair shiny and healthy
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your skin fade

Now that you know what to tell your barber for a skin fade, you're ready to rock this trendy and stylish haircut. Remember to communicate with your barber, be specific about what you want, and take care of your hair after your haircut to keep it looking fresh.
