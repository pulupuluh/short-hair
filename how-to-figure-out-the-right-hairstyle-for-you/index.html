How To Figure Out The Right Hairstyle For You

Written by Christine Jul 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. With a little bit of research and an understanding of your hair type and face shape, you can find the right hairstyle that suits you perfectly. In this blog post, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to figure out the right hairstyle for you.

Table of Contents

  • Understanding Your Hair Type
  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Hairstyle Ideas for Different Face Shapes
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Understanding Your Hair Type

The first step to finding the right hairstyle is to understand your hair type. Your hair type will determine the texture, thickness, and curl pattern of your hair. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. To determine your hair type, take a strand of your hair and examine it closely.

If your hair is straight, it will be smooth and without any curl pattern. If your hair is wavy, it will have a slight wave pattern. If your hair is curly, it will have a definite curl pattern. And if your hair is coily, it will have a tight curl pattern.

Once you've determined your hair type, you can then look for hairstyles that work best for your hair type.

Determining Your Face Shape

The next step is to determine your face shape. Your face shape will determine which hairstyles will look best on you. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong.

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Take a close look at the shape of your face. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oblong face shape. If your face is as wide as it is long, you have a square face shape. If your face is longer than it is wide and has a pointed chin, you have a heart face shape. If your face is widest at the cheekbones and tapers towards the forehead and chin, you have a diamond face shape.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can then look for hairstyles that complement your face shape.

Hairstyle Ideas for Different Face Shapes

Now that you know your hair type and face shape, it's time to find the right hairstyle for you. Here are some hairstyle ideas for different face shapes:

  • Oval Face Shape: Oval face shapes can pull off almost any hairstyle. Opt for long layers or a blunt bob.
  • Round Face Shape: Round face shapes look best with hairstyles that add height and length. Choose a side-swept bang or a layered bob.
  • Square Face Shape: Square face shapes look best with hairstyles that soften the angles of your face. Try a side-parted long bob or soft waves.
  • Heart Face Shape: Heart face shapes look best with hairstyles that add volume and width to the lower half of the face. Opt for a chin-length bob or loose curls.
  • Diamond Face Shape: Diamond face shapes look best with hairstyles that balance the width of the forehead and chin. Try a pixie cut or a chin-length bob with bangs.
  • Oblong Face Shape: Oblong face shapes look best with hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face. Choose a layered cut or a side-swept bang.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the right hairstyle for you, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding the right hairstyle:

What if I have thin hair?

If you have thin hair, consider getting a layered cut to add volume and texture. You can also try using volumizing products to give your hair a fuller look.

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, embrace your natural texture and opt for a hairstyle that works with your curls. Consider getting a DevaCut or using a diffuser to enhance your curls.

What if I have a receding hairline?

If you have a receding hairline, consider getting a buzz cut or a short, cropped hairstyle. You can also try using hair fibers or a hairpiece to fill in any bald patches.

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By understanding your hair type and face shape, and experimenting with different hairstyles, you can find the perfect hairstyle that suits you best.
