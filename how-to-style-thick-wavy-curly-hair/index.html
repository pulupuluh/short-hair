How To Style Thick Wavy Curly Hair

Written by Frank Jul 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick wavy curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can be voluminous and beautiful, it can also be difficult to style and maintain. In this article, we will provide you with tips on how to style thick wavy curly hair, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Style Thick Wavy Curly Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Instructions
  4. Tips for Styling Thick Wavy Curly Hair
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Thick Wavy Curly Hair

Description

Thick wavy curly hair is hair that has a lot of body and can be difficult to manage. This type of hair can be prone to frizz and can be challenging to style. However, with the right techniques and products, you can style your hair in a way that enhances its natural beauty.

How to Style Thick Wavy Curly Hair

The first step in styling thick wavy curly hair is to start with clean, well-moisturized hair. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and healthy. Once your hair is clean, use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and keep them moisturized.

Next, decide on the style you want to achieve. There are many different styles you can create with thick wavy curly hair, including braids, buns, and half-up half-down styles. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

When styling thick wavy curly hair, it's important to use the right tools. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, and use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without causing frizz. You can also use a curling iron or wand to create defined curls or waves.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style thick wavy curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, well-moisturized hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls.
  3. Decide on the style you want to achieve.
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  5. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.
  6. Use a curling iron or wand to create defined curls or waves.
  7. Finish with a hairspray or other styling product to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Thick Wavy Curly Hair

Here are some tips for styling thick wavy curly hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel to reduce frizz.
  • Don't brush your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce hair breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my thick wavy curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with thick wavy curly hair can go several days without washing their hair, while others may need to wash their hair every other day.

Q: Can I straighten my thick wavy curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your thick wavy curly hair, but it's important to use a heat protectant and not straighten your hair too often, as this can damage your hair.

Q: How can I reduce frizz in my thick wavy curly hair?

A: Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to keep your hair moisturized, and avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without causing frizz.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Curly Hair

To keep your thick wavy curly hair healthy and beautiful, it's important to follow a hair care routine that includes regular shampooing, conditioning, and moisturizing. Here are some tips:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and keep them moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can style your thick wavy curly hair in a way that enhances its natural beauty and keeps it healthy and strong.
