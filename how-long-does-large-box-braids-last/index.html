How Long Does Large Box Braids Last

Written by Luffy Aug 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Box braids have been a popular hairstyle for decades, and there is no sign of it slowing down. Large box braids are a trendy and stylish version of this classic hairstyle. They are larger in size and can be styled in various ways. However, many wonder how long does large box braids last?

Description

Large box braids are braids that are typically thicker and more prominent than regular box braids. They can vary in size, but they are usually larger than pencil-sized braids. They can be styled in various ways, such as updos, ponytails, or left loose. Large box braids are a popular protective hairstyle that can help to protect your natural hair from damage.

How to

Large box braids can be done at home or in a salon. If you choose to do it yourself, here are the steps:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair.

Before you start braiding, make sure that your hair is clean and moisturized. This will help to prevent breakage and damage.

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections.

Divide your hair into sections using a comb. Make sure that the sections are even in size.

Step 3: Start braiding.

Start braiding each section, making sure that the braids are tight and secure. Use hair extensions if you want longer braids.

Step 4: Seal the ends.

Seal the ends of the braids using hot water or a lighter. This will prevent the braids from unraveling.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your large box braids last longer:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly to prevent it from becoming dry and itchy.
  • Wear a silk or satin bonnet or scarf at night to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Avoid swimming or getting your braids wet as much as possible.

FAQ

1. How long do large box braids last?

Large box braids can last anywhere from 4-8 weeks, depending on how well you take care of them.

2. Can I wash my hair while wearing large box braids?

It is best to avoid getting your braids wet as much as possible. However, if you need to wash your hair, you can use a dry shampoo or a gentle sulfate-free shampoo.

3. Can I style my large box braids in different ways?

Yes, you can style your large box braids in various ways, such as updos, ponytails, or left loose. Just make sure that the style is not too tight, as this can cause breakage and damage.

Hair Care

When wearing large box braids, it is essential to take care of your natural hair underneath. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly to prevent it from becoming dry and itchy.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your braids.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote blood flow and hair growth.
  • Avoid wearing your braids for too long to prevent breakage and damage.

Conclusion

Large box braids are a popular protective hairstyle that can last anywhere from 4-8 weeks. It is essential to take care of your natural hair underneath and avoid getting your braids wet as much as possible. With proper care, you can enjoy your large box braids for weeks and protect your natural hair from damage.
