How To Cut Your Own Hair A Step By Step Guide With Youtube Videos

Written by Aprilia Feb 25, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on haircuts? Do you want to learn how to give yourself a fresh, new haircut without breaking the bank? Look no further than YouTube, where you can find a plethora of videos on how to cut your own hair. In this blog post, we’ll guide you through the process of cutting your own hair with the help of YouTube videos.

Description: What You Need to Know Before Cutting Your Own Hair

Before you start cutting your own hair, there are a few things you need to know. First, cutting your own hair can be challenging, especially if you’ve never done it before. Second, you’ll need the right tools, including a pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Finally, be prepared to make mistakes – it’s all part of the learning process!

How to Cut Your Own Hair: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose the Right YouTube Video

The first step in cutting your own hair is to find the right YouTube video. There are a lot of videos out there, so take your time to find one that matches your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Step 2: Gather Your Tools

Once you’ve found the right video, it’s time to gather your tools. You’ll need a pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Make sure your scissors are sharp and your comb is clean.

Step 3: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, you’ll need to wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier to cut and will ensure that you’re working with clean hair.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair. Divide it into smaller sections, so that you can work on one section at a time. Use clips to hold each section in place.

Step 5: Follow the YouTube Video

Now it’s time to follow the YouTube video. Take your time and follow the instructions carefully. If you’re unsure about anything, pause the video and re-watch the section.

Step 6: Trim the Ends

Once you’ve followed the video, it’s time to trim the ends. Start with small snips and work your way up. Remember, it’s better to cut too little than too much.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair. Use your comb and scissors to create the style you want. Don’t be afraid to experiment – it’s your hair, after all!

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting your own hair:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up.
  • Don’t be afraid to make mistakes – it’s all part of the learning process.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting your own hair can take longer than going to a salon.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is it hard to cut your own hair?

A: Cutting your own hair can be challenging, especially if you’ve never done it before. However, with the help of YouTube videos, you can learn how to do it successfully.

Q: What tools do I need to cut my own hair?

A: You’ll need a pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Q: Should I cut my hair dry or wet?

A: It’s best to cut your hair when it’s dry, as wet hair can be more difficult to cut evenly.

Hair Care: Taking Care of Your Hair After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote strong, healthy hair.

With the help of YouTube videos, cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. Just remember to be patient, take your time, and don’t be afraid to make mistakes. Happy cutting!
