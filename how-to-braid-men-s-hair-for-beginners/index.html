How To Braid Men s Hair For Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Braiding hair is an excellent way to keep your hair neat and tidy, especially for men with long hair. However, if you're new to braiding, it can be a bit intimidating. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing a step-by-step guide on how to braid men's hair for beginners. Let's get started!

  • How to Braid Men's Hair

How to Braid Men's Hair

Braiding men's hair is a simple process that involves weaving three or more strands of hair together. Braids can be worn in a variety of styles, including cornrows, French braids, and twists.

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's discuss the tools you'll need:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Hair clips
  • Braid spray or hair gel
  • Hair ties or rubber bands

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to braid men's hair:

  1. Start by combing your hair to remove any tangles and knots. Use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage.
  2. Section your hair. Use hair clips to divide your hair into sections. The size of the section will depend on your hair thickness and the braid style you want to achieve.
  3. Apply braid spray or hair gel. This will help keep the hair in place and prevent frizzing.
  4. Take three strands of hair. Start with the section closest to your forehead. Cross the right strand over the middle strand, then cross the left strand over the middle strand.
  5. Add more hair to the braid. As you cross each strand over the middle section, add a small amount of hair to the strand. This will create a tight, neat braid.
  6. Continue braiding. Keep adding hair to each strand as you cross them over the middle section. Make sure to keep the braid tight and close to the scalp.
  7. Tie off the braid. Once you reach the end of the section, tie off the braid with a hair tie or rubber band.
  8. Repeat the process. Continue braiding each section of hair until you've achieved your desired style.
  9. Style your braids. You can leave your braids loose or style them in a bun or ponytail.
  10. Finish with a hairspray. This will help keep your braids in place and prevent frizzing.

Tips for Braiding Men's Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when braiding men's hair:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use a comb to detangle your hair before braiding.
  • Apply braid spray or hair gel to help keep the braid neat and prevent frizzing.
  • Make sure to add hair to each strand as you braid to create a tight, neat braid.
  • Keep the braid close to the scalp to prevent it from unraveling.
  • Practice makes perfect! Don't be discouraged if your braids don't turn out perfectly the first time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can men have cornrows?

Yes, men can have cornrows. Cornrows are a popular braid style for men with long hair.

How long does it take to braid men's hair?

The time it takes to braid men's hair will depend on the length and thickness of the hair, as well as the braid style. On average, it can take anywhere from 30 minutes to several hours to braid men's hair.

How long do braids last?

Braids can last anywhere from a few days to several weeks, depending on the braid style and how well they're maintained.

Hair Care for Braided Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your braided hair:

  • Don't wash your hair for at least two weeks after getting braids.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizzing.
  • Apply oil to your scalp to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your braids.
  • Remove your braids carefully to prevent breakage.

That's it! With these tips and a bit of practice, you'll be able to braid men's hair like a pro. Have fun experimenting with different braid styles and don't be afraid to get creative!
