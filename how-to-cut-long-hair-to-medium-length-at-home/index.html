How To Cut Long Hair To Medium Length At Home

Description

If you have long hair and want to change up your look, cutting it to a medium length can be a great option. Going to a salon to get your hair cut can be expensive, so why not try cutting it at home? With the right tools and techniques, it's possible to achieve a beautiful medium-length cut on your own. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut your long hair to medium length at home, as well as tips and tricks to ensure a successful outcome.

How to

The first thing you'll need to do is gather all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair ties, and clips to section your hair. You may also want to invest in a pair of hair cutting shears if you plan on cutting your hair regularly.

Next, it's important to decide on the length you want to cut your hair. A medium length can vary from just below the shoulders to just above the collarbone. Use a tape measure to determine the length you want, or simply mark it with a hair tie.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any tangles.

2. Section your hair into four equal parts: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair ties or clips to keep each section separate.

3. Take one of the back sections and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers at the length you want to cut it.

4. Using your scissors, cut straight across the hair. Be sure to cut small sections at a time to ensure accuracy.

5. Repeat this process with the other sections of your hair, working your way from the back to the front.

6. Once you've cut all the sections, carefully blend the hair together by combing it straight down and making small, gentle snips with your scissors.

7. Check the length of your hair to make sure it is even.

Tips

- Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections to avoid cutting too much off at once.

- Take your time and be patient. Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with practice, you'll get better.

- If you're unsure about cutting your hair, start with a small trim and work your way up to a medium length.

- Consider watching tutorials on YouTube to get a better understanding of the process.

FAQ

Q: How much hair should I cut off?

A: This depends on the length you want to achieve. If you're going from long to medium length, start with a small trim and work your way up to the desired length.

Q: Is it easy to cut your own hair?

A: Cutting your own hair can be challenging, but with practice, it can be done. Start with a small trim and work your way up to a medium length.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims will also help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful medium-length cut on your own. Remember to take your time and be patient, and always seek help from a professional if you're unsure about cutting your own hair.
