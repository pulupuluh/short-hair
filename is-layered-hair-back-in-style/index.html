Is Layered Hair Back In Style

Written by Christine Jun 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Layered hair has been a popular hairstyle for decades, but many people are wondering if it's still in style. In this article, we'll explore whether or not layered hair is back in style and provide tips on how to achieve this look.

Description

Layered hair is a hairstyle where the hair is cut into different lengths, creating layers. This style can add volume and texture to the hair, making it a popular choice for many women. Layered hair can be styled in many ways, from beachy waves to sleek and straight.

How to Achieve Layered Hair

There are several ways to achieve layered hair. You can go to a hairstylist and ask for layers, or you can do it yourself at home. If you're doing it yourself, it's important to use sharp scissors and be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Divide your hair into sections.

3. Decide how many layers you want and where you want them.

4. Take a small section of hair and hold it straight up.

5. Cut the hair at an angle, starting at the bottom and working your way up.

6. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

7. Use a comb to blend the layers together.

Tips for Layered Hair

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.

2. Use a texturizing spray to add texture to your hair.

3. Use a curling iron or flat iron to create different styles.

4. Use a hair serum to add shine to your hair.

5. Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh.

FAQ

1. Is layered hair back in style?

Yes, layered hair is back in style. It's a timeless hairstyle that can be customized to fit any face shape or hair type.

2. What face shape is best for layered hair?

Layered hair can be flattering on any face shape, but it's especially great for those with long, oval faces. It can help balance out the face and add volume to the hair.

3. Can layered hair be styled in different ways?

Yes, layered hair can be styled in many ways. You can create beachy waves, sleek and straight styles, or even add braids or updos.

Hair Care for Layered Hair

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you have layers. Here are some tips for hair care:

1. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

3. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.

4. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet.

5. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

So, is layered hair back in style? Absolutely! It's a versatile and timeless hairstyle that can be customized to fit any face shape or hair type. With the right care and styling products, you can achieve the perfect layered look.
