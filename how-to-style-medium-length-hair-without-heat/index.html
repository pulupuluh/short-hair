How To Style Medium Length Hair Without Heat

Written by Frank Mar 10, 2023 · 9 min read

Medium length hair is versatile and easy to manage, but it can be challenging to style without using heat. Heat styling tools can cause damage to your hair, making it dry and brittle. However, there are many ways to style your medium length hair without heat that are easy, quick, and will leave your hair looking great. In this article, we will show you how to style medium length hair without heat.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Medium Length Hair Without Heat
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

How to Style Medium Length Hair Without Heat

Here are some easy ways to style your medium length hair without using any heat:

1. Braids

Braids are a great way to add texture and style to your hair without using heat. There are many different types of braids that you can try, such as French braids, Dutch braids, and fishtail braids. You can also try different braiding patterns, such as braiding your hair in a zigzag pattern or braiding your hair into a crown.

2. Twist Outs

Twist outs are another great way to style your hair without using heat. To create a twist out, simply twist sections of your hair and let them dry overnight. In the morning, unravel the twists and fluff your hair to create volume and texture.

3. Rollers

Rollers are a great way to add volume and curl to your hair without using heat. Simply roll sections of your hair onto the rollers and leave them in overnight. In the morning, remove the rollers and gently fluff your hair to create volume and texture.

4. Headbands

Headbands are a quick and easy way to style your hair without using heat. Simply place a headband over your hair and tuck your hair into the band. You can also use a headband to create a sleek and polished look by smoothing your hair back and tucking it into the band.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your medium length hair without using heat:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. It’s important to start with clean hair to ensure that your style lasts longer.

2. Choose Your Style

Choose the style that you want to create, such as braids, twist outs, rollers, or headbands.

3. Prep Your Hair

Prep your hair by applying a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to help hold your style in place.

4. Style Your Hair

Style your hair using your chosen method. Follow the instructions for your chosen style, whether it’s braiding, twisting, rolling, or using a headband.

5. Let Your Hair Dry

Let your hair dry completely overnight. This will ensure that your style holds and lasts longer.

6. Fluff Your Hair

In the morning, gently fluff your hair to create volume and texture. Use your fingers to separate the curls or twists and create a natural look.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair

Here are some tips for styling your medium length hair without using heat:

1. Use the Right Products

Use styling products that are designed for your hair type and the style you want to create. For example, if you want to create curls, use a curl-enhancing product.

2. Don’t Overdo It

Don’t try to style your hair too much. Over-styling can cause damage to your hair and make it look dry and brittle.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from the elements, such as wind and sun, by wearing a hat or scarf.

4. Experiment with Different Styles

Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for your hair. Don’t be afraid to try something new!

FAQs

Q. How can I make my hair grow faster?

A. There are many things you can do to promote hair growth, such as eating a healthy diet, taking vitamins, and reducing stress.

Q. How often should I wash my hair?

A. It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people wash their hair every day, while others wash it once a week.

Q. How can I prevent split ends?

A. To prevent split ends, trim your hair regularly and avoid using heat styling tools.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

Here are some hair care tips for medium length hair:

1. Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. You can make your own hair mask using natural ingredients like avocado, honey, and coconut oil.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools, or if you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause damage, such as tight ponytails or braids.

4. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your medium length hair without using any heat. Not only will your hair look great, but it will also be healthier and more vibrant. Try these styles today and see the difference for yourself!
