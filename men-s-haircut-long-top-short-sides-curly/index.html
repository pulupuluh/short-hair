Men s Haircut Long Top Short Sides Curly A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to spice up your look? A men's haircut long top short sides curly might just be what you need! This hairstyle is trendy and versatile, perfect for those who want to make a statement. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this hairstyle, as well as tips on how to maintain it. Read on to learn more!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A men's haircut long top short sides curly is a hairstyle that features a longer top section of hair that is curly or wavy, while the sides and back are kept short. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look. It can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to

The men's haircut long top short sides curly is not as complicated as it may seem. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Choose the right length

The first step in achieving this hairstyle is deciding on the length of your hair. The top section should be long enough to create curls or waves, while the sides and back should be short. You can work with your hairstylist to determine the best length for your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Cut the sides and back

Next, your hairstylist will use clippers or scissors to cut the sides and back of your hair. The length will depend on your preference, but typically it ranges from a #1 to a #4 guard. Make sure to blend the sides and back into the top section to create a seamless transition.

Step 3: Cut the top section

Once the sides and back are cut, your hairstylist will move on to the top section. They'll use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length and create layers to add texture. The length will depend on your preference, but typically it ranges from 3 to 6 inches.

Step 4: Style the hair

Finally, it's time to style your hair. Apply a styling product to the top section, such as a curl-enhancing cream or mousse. Use your fingers or a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer to enhance the curls or waves. You can also use a hair straightener to create more defined waves or curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your men's haircut long top short sides curly:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls or waves hydrated and healthy.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to prevent frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess moisture.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut long top short sides curly:

Q: Will this hairstyle work for all hair types?

A: This hairstyle works best for those with naturally curly or wavy hair. However, if you have straight hair, you can still achieve this look by using a curling iron or hair rollers.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Q: What styling products should I use?

A: Use a curl-enhancing cream or mousse to define your curls or waves. You can also use a hair straightener to create more defined waves or curls.

Hair Care

To keep your men's haircut long top short sides curly looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess moisture.
  • Get a trim every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Now that you know how to achieve and maintain a men's haircut long top short sides curly, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try it out for yourself. With the right care and styling products, you can rock this trendy hairstyle with confidence!
