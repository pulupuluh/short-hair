How To Style Your Hair When It s Short Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Bardi Aug 25, 2023 · 8 min read

If you've recently cut your hair short or are looking for new ways to style it, you've come to the right place. Short hair can be incredibly versatile and easy to style, but it can also be a bit daunting if you're used to longer locks. In this article, we'll walk you through how to style your hair when it's short, including step-by-step instructions and helpful tips. Whether you have a pixie cut or a bob, we've got you covered.

Hair Care

Before we dive into styling tips, it's important to take care of your short hair. Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs some TLC to stay healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too frequently.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By taking care of your short hair, you'll be able to style it more easily and with better results.

How to Style Your Hair When It's Short

Now that you know how to take care of your short hair, it's time to start experimenting with different styles. Here are some popular ways to style short hair:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle that's perfect for low maintenance styling. Here's how to style a pixie cut:

  1. Apply a styling product like mousse or gel to damp hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair using your fingers to create volume and texture.
  3. Use a pomade or wax to define your hair's texture and add shine.

Bob

A bob is a classic short hairstyle that can be styled in a variety of ways. Here's how to style a bob:

  1. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  2. Curl your hair using a curling iron or hot rollers.
  3. Brush out your curls for a soft, natural look.
  4. Use a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Braids

Braids are a fun and easy way to style short hair. Here's how to braid short hair:

  1. Create a deep side part in your hair.
  2. Take a small section of hair at the front of your head and braid it.
  3. Secure the braid with a small elastic band.
  4. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  5. Pin the braids together at the back of your head.

Helpful Tips

Here are some helpful tips for styling short hair:

  • Invest in quality styling tools like a good blow dryer and flat iron.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and techniques.
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, and other hair accessories.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear a ponytail with short hair?

A: Yes, you can! Use a small elastic band to gather your hair into a ponytail at the back of your head.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.

Q: Can I still color my short hair?

A: Absolutely! Just be sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your color looking fresh.

Q: Is it possible to straighten short hair?

A: Yes, you can use a flat iron to straighten short hair. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Caring for Your Short Hair

Styling short hair can be fun and easy, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. By following these tips and techniques, you'll be able to create a variety of stylish looks that showcase your short hair.
