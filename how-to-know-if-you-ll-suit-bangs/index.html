How To Know If You ll Suit Bangs

Written by Eveline Jul 08, 2023 · 10 min read

If you're thinking about getting bangs, you're probably wondering if they'll suit your face shape and hair type. Luckily, there are a few things you can consider to help you make the decision. In this article, we'll go over how to know if you'll suit bangs, as well as some tips for styling and caring for them.

Consider Your Face Shape

The first thing to consider when deciding if bangs will suit you is your face shape. Bangs can help balance out certain features and create a more symmetrical look. Here are some guidelines to help you determine if bangs will work for your face shape:

  • If you have a round face, go for long, side-swept bangs that hit at your cheekbones. Avoid blunt bangs that hit straight across.
  • If you have a square face, go for soft, wispy bangs that are longer on the sides. Avoid heavy, straight-across bangs.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, go for long, side-swept bangs that are tapered at the ends. Avoid blunt, heavy bangs.
  • If you have an oval face, you can pull off just about any type of bangs! Try blunt, straight-across bangs or long, side-swept bangs.

Remember, these are just guidelines – ultimately, you should choose the bangs that make you feel the most confident and comfortable.

Think About Your Hair Type

The next thing to consider is your hair type. Bangs can look different on different types of hair, so it's important to choose a style that will work with your hair's natural texture and thickness.

  • If you have thin hair, go for wispy, layered bangs that won't weigh your hair down.
  • If you have thick hair, go for blunt, heavy bangs that will add structure to your hair.
  • If you have curly hair, go for longer, side-swept bangs that will blend in with your curls.
  • If you have straight hair, you can pull off just about any type of bangs! Try blunt, straight-across bangs or long, side-swept bangs.

Take Your Forehead Size into Account

Another factor to consider when deciding on bangs is your forehead size. Bangs can help shorten a long forehead or make a small forehead appear larger.

  • If you have a large forehead, go for blunt, straight-across bangs that hit just above your eyebrows.
  • If you have a small forehead, go for longer, side-swept bangs that will create the illusion of more forehead space.

Consider Your Eye Shape

Your eye shape can also play a role in determining if bangs will suit you. Bangs can help draw attention to your eyes and create a frame for your face.

  • If you have round eyes, go for long, side-swept bangs that will elongate your eye shape.
  • If you have almond-shaped eyes, go for blunt, straight-across bangs that will create a bold frame for your eyes.
  • If you have hooded eyes, go for wispy, layered bangs that will draw attention to your eyes without overwhelming them.

Think About Your Hair Length

The length of your hair can also affect how bangs will look on you. Bangs can work with any hair length, but it's important to choose a style that will complement your haircut.

  • If you have short hair, go for blunt, short bangs that will add structure to your haircut.
  • If you have medium-length hair, go for longer, side-swept bangs that will blend in with your layers.
  • If you have long hair, go for longer, side-swept bangs that will add movement to your hair.

Take Your Hair Texture into Account

Finally, your hair texture can also affect how bangs will look on you. Bangs can work with any texture, but it's important to choose a style that will work with your hair's natural texture.

  • If you have fine hair, go for wispy, layered bangs that won't weigh your hair down.
  • If you have thick hair, go for blunt, heavy bangs that will add structure to your hair.
  • If you have curly hair, go for longer, side-swept bangs that will blend in with your curls.
  • If you have straight hair, you can pull off just about any type of bangs! Try blunt, straight-across bangs or long, side-swept bangs.

Styling Tips for Bangs

Once you've decided on the type of bangs you want, it's important to know how to style them. Here are some tips for styling bangs:

  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume.
  • Use a flat iron to smooth out your bangs and create a sleek look.
  • Apply a small amount of styling cream to your bangs to add texture and hold.

Hair Care for Bangs

Keeping your bangs looking great requires some special care. Here are some tips for caring for your bangs:

  • Wash your bangs daily to keep them looking fresh.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to absorb oil and add volume.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day, as this can transfer oil from your fingers onto your hair.

FAQs

Can I get bangs if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Just make sure to choose a style that will work with your curls, such as longer, side-swept bangs that will blend in with your curls.

Will bangs make me look younger?

Bangs can help soften your features and create a more youthful look. However, the effect will depend on the type of bangs you choose and how you style them.

How do I know if I'll like bangs on myself?

The best way to know if you'll like bangs is to try them out! Consider getting clip-in bangs or trying out a virtual hairstyle app before committing to a haircut.

Conclusion

Deciding if you'll suit bangs can seem daunting, but with these tips, you'll be able to make an informed decision. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, forehead size, eye shape, and hair length when choosing a style. And once you've got your bangs, be sure to follow our styling and hair care tips to keep them looking great!
