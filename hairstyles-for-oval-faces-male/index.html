Hairstyles For Oval Faces Male A Complete Guide

Written by William Sep 03, 2023 · 10 min read

Are you tired of the same old haircut? Do you want to switch things up and try a new look? One of the best ways to do this is by choosing a hairstyle that complements your face shape. If you have an oval face, you're in luck because there are plenty of great hairstyles that will suit you perfectly. In this article, we'll take a closer look at hairstyles for oval faces male, including how to choose the right style, step-by-step instructions, tips, and more.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Short Hairstyles for Oval Faces Male
  • Medium Hairstyles for Oval Faces Male
  • Long Hairstyles for Oval Faces Male
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, it's important to understand how to choose the right one for your face shape. Oval faces are considered the "ideal" shape because they have balanced proportions and are versatile when it comes to hairstyles. However, not all hairstyles will work for every oval face. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, as this will make your face appear longer.
  • Choose styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will create a more balanced look.
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a style. Some styles work better with thin or thick hair, so make sure to choose one that suits your hair type.
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair each day. Some styles require more maintenance than others, so choose one that fits your routine.

Short Hairstyles for Oval Faces Male

Short hairstyles are a great choice for oval faces because they accentuate your features and create a clean, polished look. Here are a few options to consider:

Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic, low-maintenance style that works well for oval faces. It's also a great option if you're looking to show off your facial hair. Simply ask your barber to use clippers to trim your hair to a uniform length all over.

Crew Cut

The crew cut is a slightly longer version of the buzz cut, with more length on the top of your head. This style works well for oval faces because it adds volume to the sides of your face, creating a more balanced look.

Side Part

The side part is a timeless style that works well for oval faces. It's easy to maintain and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. To achieve this look, simply comb your hair to one side and use a bit of product to keep it in place.

Medium Hairstyles for Oval Faces Male

If you're looking for a style that's a bit longer than a buzz or crew cut, medium hairstyles are a great option. Here are a few to consider:

Textured Crop

The textured crop is a trendy style that works well for oval faces. It's a bit longer than a crew cut, with textured layers on top that add volume and dimension. To achieve this look, ask your barber to cut your hair into short, choppy layers on top and leave the sides and back shorter.

Quiff

The quiff is a stylish, versatile style that works well for oval faces. It's longer on top with shorter sides, creating a sharp, polished look. To achieve this look, ask your barber to cut your hair into a longer, layered style on top and use a bit of product to create volume and hold.

Slicked Back

The slicked back style is a classic look that works well for oval faces. It's longer on top with shorter sides, and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. To achieve this look, simply comb your hair back and use a bit of product to keep it in place.

Long Hairstyles for Oval Faces Male

If you're looking for a longer style, there are plenty of great options that will work well for oval faces. Here are a few to consider:

Man Bun

The man bun is a trendy, stylish look that works well for oval faces. It's longer on top with shorter sides, and can be worn either high or low on the head. To achieve this look, simply pull your hair back into a bun and secure it with a hair tie.

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for oval faces because they add volume and dimension to your hair. To achieve this look, ask your barber to cut your hair into long layers that frame your face.

Wavy or Curly

If you have wavy or curly hair, consider embracing your natural texture with a longer style. This will add volume and dimension to your hair, creating a more balanced look.

FAQ

What if I have a receding hairline?

If you have a receding hairline, short hairstyles like the buzz cut or crew cut are great options. They will help to minimize the appearance of your receding hairline and create a clean, polished look.

What if I have thinning hair?

If you have thinning hair, consider a shorter style like the buzz cut or crew cut. These styles will help to create the illusion of thicker hair and are easy to maintain.

Hair Care Tips

No matter what style you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or flat irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a bit of product to style your hair, but avoid using too much as it can weigh your hair down.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can make a huge difference in your overall look. By following these tips and trying out a few different styles, you're sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your oval face.
