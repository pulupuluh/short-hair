Best Straighteners For Fine Hair 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy May 24, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you tired of dealing with frizzy, unruly hair every morning? Do you have fine hair that seems to break or get damaged easily? If so, investing in a high-quality hair straightener can be a game-changer for you. However, with so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this article, we'll be discussing the best straighteners for fine hair in 2020 that will give you salon-worthy results without damaging your hair.

How to Choose the Right Straightener for Fine Hair?

Before we dive into the list of best straighteners for fine hair, let's first understand what features you should look for while choosing a straightener.

Plate Material:

The material of the plates can impact the health of your hair. Ceramic plates are a popular choice as they distribute heat evenly and have a smoother surface that reduces the risk of hair damage. Titanium plates are also great for fine hair as they heat up quickly and retain heat well.

Temperature:

High temperatures can damage fine hair, so it's important to choose a straightener with adjustable temperature settings. Look for a straightener that has a maximum temperature of 350-380 degrees Fahrenheit.

Size:

The size of the plates can impact how well the straightener works on your hair. For fine hair, it's best to go for a straightener with narrow plates that can glide smoothly through the hair.

Top 5 Best Straighteners for Fine Hair 2020

1. BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium-Plated Ultra-Thin Straightening Iron

The BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium-Plated Ultra-Thin Straightening Iron is a top choice for fine hair. The titanium plates heat up quickly and glide smoothly through the hair without causing any damage. The iron also has adjustable temperature settings that range from 300-450 degrees Fahrenheit.

2. ghd Platinum+

The ghd Platinum+ straightener is a favorite among hairstylists as it uses patented ultra-zone technology that ensures even heat distribution. It also has a maximum temperature of 365 degrees Fahrenheit that's perfect for fine hair.

3. CHI Original 1" Flat Hair Straightening Ceramic Hairstyling Iron

The CHI Original 1" Flat Hair Straightening Ceramic Hairstyling Iron is a classic choice for fine hair. The ceramic plates distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz, leaving your hair smooth and shiny. The straightener also has a maximum temperature of 392 degrees Fahrenheit that's suitable for fine hair.

4. Remington S5500 1" Anti-Static Flat Iron

The Remington S5500 1" Anti-Static Flat Iron is an affordable option that's perfect for fine hair. The ceramic plates are infused with anti-static technology that reduces frizz and flyaways. The straightener also has adjustable temperature settings that range from 310-410 degrees Fahrenheit.

5. HSI Professional Glider Ceramic Tourmaline Ionic Flat Iron

The HSI Professional Glider Ceramic Tourmaline Ionic Flat Iron is a versatile option that works well on all hair types, including fine hair. The ceramic plates are infused with tourmaline and produce negative ions that reduce frizz and static. The straightener also has adjustable temperature settings that range from 140-450 degrees Fahrenheit.

Step by Step Guide to Straighten Fine Hair

Now that you've chosen the right straightener for your fine hair, let's take a look at how to straighten it without causing any damage.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair and use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the straightener.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to straighten each section and ensure that you don't miss any spots.

Step 3: Straighten Each Section

Take a small section of hair and clamp it between the plates of the straightener. Slowly glide the straightener down the length of the hair, making sure to keep the plates parallel to each other. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Finish with a Serum

Once you've straightened all sections of your hair, finish off with a hair serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Straightening Fine Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind while straightening your fine hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before straightening to prevent damage.
  • Don't straighten wet hair as it can cause the hair to break.
  • Use a lower temperature setting for fine hair to prevent damage.
  • Don't straighten the same section of hair multiple times as it can cause damage.
  • Finish with a serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

FAQs

1. Can I straighten fine hair every day?

Straightening fine hair every day can cause damage and breakage. It's best to limit straightening to once or twice a week and use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

2. Can I use a straightener on wet hair?

No, it's not safe to use a straightener on wet hair as it can cause damage and breakage. Always make sure your hair is completely dry before straightening.

3. Can I use a straightener on colored or chemically treated hair?

Yes, you can use a straightener on colored or chemically treated hair. However, it's best to use a lower temperature setting and a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Fine Hair

Using the right hair care products can make a huge difference in the health of your fine hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for fine hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

By following these tips and using the best straighteners for fine hair in 2020, you can achieve smooth, frizz-free hair without causing any damage. Remember to always use a heat protectant spray and choose a straightener with adjustable temperature settings for the best results.
