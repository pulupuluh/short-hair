How To Make Your Hair Big And Fluffy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 06, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of flat, lifeless hair? Do you want to achieve that big and fluffy look that you see in magazines and on TV? With a few simple tricks and tips, you can make your hair look voluminous and full of life. In this article, we’ll show you how to make your hair big and fluffy in just a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Big and Fluffy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Big and Fluffy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Big and Fluffy Hair

How to Make Your Hair Big and Fluffy

In order to make your hair look big and fluffy, you need to focus on adding volume and texture. Here are some tips to help you achieve that look:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, which can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to create lift at the roots.
  • Tease your hair at the crown to add height and volume.
  • Add curls or waves to create texture and movement.
  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add extra lift and hold.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you create big and fluffy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair, then apply a volumizing spray or mousse to your roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair upside down, using your fingers to lift and separate your hair at the roots.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or hot rollers to add curls or waves.
  5. Tease your hair at the crown to create height and volume.
  6. Spray your hair with a light-hold hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips for Maintaining Big and Fluffy Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your big and fluffy hair:

  • Avoid touching or brushing your hair too much, as this can cause it to lose its volume.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help maintain its shape and volume.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make it look flat.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair to maintain its volume?

A: It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils, which can help add volume and texture.

Q: What type of styling products should I use to make my hair look big and fluffy?

A: Look for volumizing sprays, mousses, and dry shampoos that are designed to add texture and body to your hair.

Hair Care for Big and Fluffy Hair

In order to keep your hair looking its best, you need to take good care of it. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy, big, and fluffy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in protein and vitamins, which can help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your hair look big and fluffy in no time. Follow our step-by-step guide, and don’t forget to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
