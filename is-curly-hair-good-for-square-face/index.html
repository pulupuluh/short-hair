Is Curly Hair Good For Square Face

Written by Frank Apr 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a square face, you might be wondering whether curly hair is a good choice for you. The answer is yes! In fact, curly hair can be a great choice for those with square faces, as it helps to soften angular features and create a more balanced look. In this article, we'll explore why curly hair is a great choice for square faces, and provide some tips for styling and caring for your curly locks.

Description

If you have a square face, you have a strong jawline and angular features. Curly hair can help soften these features and create a more symmetrical look. The curls add volume and texture to your hair, which can help balance out your face shape. Additionally, curly hair creates a more playful and youthful look, which can be especially flattering for those with square faces.

How to Style Curly Hair for Square Faces

If you have curly hair and a square face, there are a few things to keep in mind when styling your hair. First, you want to make sure your curls are well-defined and voluminous. This can be achieved through the use of styling products like mousse or curling cream, as well as by diffusing your hair with a hair dryer.

Second, you want to avoid hairstyles that emphasize your jawline or create harsh angles. This means avoiding slicked-back styles, as well as hairstyles that are too short or too long. Instead, opt for hairstyles with soft layers and volume at the crown of your head.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair for Square Faces

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling curly hair for square faces:

  1. Begin by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Gently towel-dry your hair, being careful not to rub or tangle your curls.
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing product like mousse or curling cream to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define any loose or frizzy curls.
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Caring for Curly Hair on Square Faces

Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair on a square face:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can create frizz.
  • Invest in a good diffuser attachment for your hair dryer to create defined curls.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my curly hair in a ponytail if I have a square face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a square face. However, it's best to avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can emphasize your jawline.

Q: What kind of bangs should I get if I have curly hair and a square face?

A: Soft, side-swept bangs can be a great choice for those with curly hair and square faces. These help to soften angular features and create a more balanced look.

Hair Care for Curly Hair on Square Faces

When caring for curly hair on a square face, it's important to choose products that are designed for your hair type. Look for sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners, which will help to keep your curls hydrated and defined. Additionally, invest in a good diffuser attachment for your hair dryer, which will help to create defined curls without causing frizz. Finally, be sure to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, curly hair can be a great choice for those with square faces. By following the tips and guidelines outlined in this article, you can create a flattering and stylish look that enhances your natural features.
