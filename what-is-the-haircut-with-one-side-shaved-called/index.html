What Is The Haircut With One Side Shaved Called

Written by William Jul 31, 2023 Â· 6 min read

A popular trend in recent years is the haircut with one side shaved. This edgy and bold style has taken the fashion world by storm, and many people are curious about what it's called. In this article, we'll explore the answer to this question and provide tips and tricks for maintaining this look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Haircut with One Side Shaved
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining this Look
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for this Style

Description

The haircut with one side shaved is also known as an undercut. This style involves shaving one side of the head while leaving the other side longer. The longer side can be styled in a variety of ways, such as a bob, pixie cut, or even long layers. This style is popular among both men and women and can be customized to suit individual preferences.

How to Achieve the Haircut with One Side Shaved

The haircut with one side shaved can be achieved by visiting a professional hairstylist. However, it's important to bring in a picture of the desired style to ensure that the stylist understands your vision. If you're feeling brave, this style can also be achieved at home with clippers and a steady hand. However, it's important to take your time and go slowly to avoid any mistakes.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're interested in achieving the haircut with one side shaved at home, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Section off the hair that you want to keep longer, using hair clips or bands
  3. Use clippers with a guard to shave the other side of the head
  4. Take your time and go slowly, making sure to check your progress frequently
  5. Once you've achieved the desired length, use scissors to trim the longer side of the hair
  6. Style the longer side as desired

It's important to note that achieving this style at home can be challenging and may not result in a professional-looking cut. It's always best to visit a stylist if you're unsure or don't have experience with using clippers.

Tips for Maintaining this Look

Maintaining the haircut with one side shaved requires regular maintenance. It's important to visit a stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep the shaved side looking fresh. Additionally, using a high-quality hair product, such as pomade or wax, can help to keep the longer side styled and in place. Finally, avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, on the shaved side to prevent damage to the hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the haircut with one side shaved suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this style can be customized to suit all hair types, including straight, curly, and wavy hair.

Q: Is the haircut with one side shaved appropriate for the workplace?

A: It depends on the workplace and the dress code. Some workplaces may frown upon edgy hairstyles, while others may be more accepting. It's best to check with your employer before making any drastic changes to your hair.

Hair Care for this Style

To keep the haircut with one side shaved looking its best, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing the hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, using a high-quality hair product to style the longer side, and avoiding heat styling tools on the shaved side. Additionally, regular trims every 4-6 weeks will help to maintain the shape of the cut and prevent split ends.

Overall, the haircut with one side shaved is a bold and edgy style that can be customized to suit individual preferences. Whether you choose to visit a stylist or attempt this style at home, with regular maintenance and proper hair care, you can enjoy this look for years to come.
