Is A Taper Fade A Thing A Comprehensive Guide To This Popular Hairstyle

Written by Frank Aug 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a fan of trendy hairstyles, you've probably heard of the taper fade. This hairstyle has been around for years, but it's still popular today. If you're wondering, "Is a taper fade a thing?" the answer is yes! In this comprehensive guide, we'll take a closer look at this style, how to achieve it, and how to take care of it.

What is a Taper Fade?

A taper fade is a hairstyle that is shorter on the sides and longer on top. The hair gradually gets shorter as it goes down the sides of the head, creating a tapering effect. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be worn by men of all ages and hair types.

How to Achieve a Taper Fade

To achieve a taper fade, you'll need to visit a barber. The process involves cutting the hair on the sides of the head with clippers, gradually going from a longer length to a shorter length. The hair on top of the head is left longer and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Here are the steps a barber will typically take to achieve a taper fade:

Step 1: Determine the Length

The barber will start by determining the length of hair you want on the top of your head. This will help them determine how short to cut the sides.

Step 2: Start with a Longer Guard

The barber will start with a longer guard on their clippers and cut the hair around the temples and sideburns. This will create a gradual tapering effect.

Step 3: Gradually Shorten the Length

The barber will gradually shorten the length of the guard on their clippers as they move down the sides of the head. This will create a gradual tapering effect.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

The barber will then blend the hair on the sides of the head with a pair of scissors or clippers to create a seamless transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides.

Step 5: Style the Hair on Top

Finally, the barber will style the hair on top of the head to your liking. This can be done with a comb, brush, or your fingers.

Tips for Maintaining a Taper Fade

Now that you have a taper fade, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips to keep your hairstyle looking fresh:

  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups. Depending on how fast your hair grows, you may need to visit every 2-4 weeks.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Consider using a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to help hold your hair in place.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as a hair dryer, too frequently as they can damage your hair.

FAQ

Is a taper fade suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a taper fade can be worn by men with all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and kinky hair.

Can I achieve a taper fade at home?

We do not recommend trying to achieve a taper fade at home. It's best to visit a professional barber who has experience with this hairstyle.

How do I know if a taper fade is right for me?

If you're interested in a taper fade, we recommend consulting with a barber. They can help you determine if this hairstyle will work with your hair type and face shape.

Hair Care

A taper fade is a trendy hairstyle that requires a bit of maintenance to keep it looking its best. By following the tips we've outlined in this guide, you can keep your hairstyle looking fresh and stylish for weeks to come.
