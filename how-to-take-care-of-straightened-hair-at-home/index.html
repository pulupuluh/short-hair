How To Take Care Of Straightened Hair At Home

Written by Aprilia Jul 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Straightened hair looks sleek, stylish, and polished. However, it requires extra care to maintain its shine and health. If you have recently straightened your hair at home, you must be wondering how to take good care of it. In this article, we will guide you on how to take care of straightened hair at home. Let's get started!

How to Straighten Hair at Home

Straightening hair at home is a convenient and affordable option for many people. Here are the steps to straighten hair at home:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or dirt.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent heat damage.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections and use a flat iron to straighten each section. Make sure to start from the roots and move towards the tips.
  4. Once you have straightened all sections, use a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Congratulations! You have successfully straightened your hair at home. Now, let's move on to the next step, which is taking care of straightened hair.

Step-by-Step Guide for Taking Care of Straightened Hair at Home

To maintain the health and shine of your straightened hair, follow these steps:

  1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for straightened hair. Sulfates can strip away the natural oils from your hair, making it dry and brittle.
  2. Avoid washing your hair too frequently. Washing hair every other day or twice a week is sufficient to keep it clean and healthy.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing. Do not use a brush as it can lead to breakage and damage.
  4. Avoid using heat styling tools frequently. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage.
  5. Avoid tying your hair tightly in a ponytail or bun as it can lead to hair breakage.
  6. Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and maintain its shape.
  7. Avoid over-processing your hair with chemicals, such as hair dye or bleach.

By following these steps, you can maintain the health and shine of your straightened hair at home.

Tips for Maintaining Straightened Hair

Here are some additional tips for maintaining straightened hair:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid using towel to dry your hair, instead use a soft t-shirt to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to harsh sunlight as it can lead to dryness and damage. Use a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week to provide deep nourishment to your hair.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a healthy diet to promote hair health from within.

By following these tips, you can maintain the health and shine of your straightened hair at home.

FAQs

Q. Can I straighten my hair every day?

A. No, it is not recommended to straighten your hair every day as it can lead to heat damage and breakage. Try to limit heat styling to once or twice a week.

Q. How can I prevent frizz in my straightened hair?

A. Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz. Also, avoid touching your hair too much as it can lead to frizz.

Q. How often should I trim my straightened hair?

A. It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and get rid of split ends.

Hair Care Products for Straightened Hair

Here are some hair care products that are specifically designed for straightened hair:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Heat protectant spray or serum
  • Hair serum or oil
  • Leave-in conditioner or hair mask

Choose these products to maintain the health and shine of your straightened hair at home.

Conclusion

Straightened hair requires extra care to maintain its shine and health. By following the steps and tips mentioned in this article, you can take good care of your straightened hair at home. Remember to use hair care products that are specifically designed for straightened hair and avoid using heat styling tools frequently. With proper care, you can enjoy your sleek and stylish straightened hair for a long time.
