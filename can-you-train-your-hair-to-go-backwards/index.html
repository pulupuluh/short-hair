Can You Train Your Hair To Go Backwards Tips And Tricks To Achieve The Look

Written by Petter Jun 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your hair always falling forward and covering your face? Do you want to achieve that trendy, slicked-back hairstyle? If so, you may be wondering: can you train your hair to go backwards? The answer is yes, and in this article, we'll provide you with the tips and tricks to achieve the look.

Description

Training your hair to go backwards requires some effort and patience. You'll need to change your hair's natural growth pattern, which can take time. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the look you want.

How To Train Your Hair to Go Backwards

Here are the steps to follow to train your hair to go backwards:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup, and then condition your hair to keep it hydrated.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair in the opposite direction of its natural growth pattern. This will help to train your hair to go backwards. Use a round brush to create volume and hold the hair in place.

Step 3: Apply Product

Apply a styling product that will hold your hair in place. A pomade or wax will work well for this look. Use a small amount and work it evenly into your hair, starting from the back and moving forward.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair Backwards

Use a comb to brush your hair backwards, starting from the front and moving towards the back. Keep combing until your hair stays in place. You may need to use a hair clip to hold the hair in place while it sets.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish the look with a light mist of hairspray to hold the hair in place. Avoid using too much hairspray, as it can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips for Training Your Hair to Go Backwards

Here are some additional tips to help you train your hair to go backwards:

  • Be patient â it can take several weeks for your hair to get used to the new growth pattern.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about training your hair to go backwards:

Can You Train Any Hair Type to Go Backwards?

Yes, you can train any hair type to go backwards. However, the process may be easier or more difficult depending on your hair's natural texture and thickness.

How Long Does It Take to Train Your Hair to Go Backwards?

It can take several weeks or even months to train your hair to go backwards. Be patient and consistent with your styling routine.

Can You Train Your Hair to Go Backwards Without Products?

It's possible to train your hair to go backwards without products, but it may be more difficult to hold the hair in place. Using a styling product can help to create the desired look and hold the hair in place.

Hair Care for Training Your Hair to Go Backwards

Training your hair to go backwards can put stress on your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to remove split ends and keep it looking healthy.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, which is essential for healthy hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can train your hair to go backwards and achieve the trendy, slicked-back look you've been dreaming of. Just remember to be patient and consistent with your styling routine, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
