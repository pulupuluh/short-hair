How To Do Hairstyle Messy Bun A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jun 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Messy bun is one of the most popular hairstyles that can make you look chic and stylish. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn on different occasions. Whether you want to create a casual or formal look, messy bun can do the trick. In this article, we will guide you on how to do hairstyle messy bun, step by step. We will also provide some tips, FAQs, and hair care tips that you should know.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Do Hairstyle Messy Bun

Before we proceed with the steps, here are some things that you need:

  • Hairbrush or comb
  • Hair tie or elastic band
  • Bobby pins
  • Hair spray (optional)

Step by Step

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to create the messy bun.

Step 2: Tie Your Hair into a High Ponytail

Pull your hair back and tie it into a high ponytail. Make sure to position it where you want your bun to be. Use an elastic band or hair tie to secure it in place.

Step 3: Create the Messy Bun

Twist the ponytail around the base to create a bun. Secure it in place with bobby pins. Don't worry if some strands fall out or if it's not perfectly neat. After all, it's called a messy bun for a reason!

Step 4: Adjust the Bun

Loosen the bun by gently pulling on the sides. This will create a more relaxed and messy look. You can also pull out a few strands to frame your face.

Step 5: Set the Look

If you want your messy bun to last longer, use a hairspray to set the look. Just spritz a little bit on the bun and around the edges to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you create the perfect messy bun:

  • For a more textured look, use a sea salt spray on your hair before creating the bun.
  • Don't pull your hair back too tightly. This can cause headaches and hair breakage.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Experiment with different bun sizes and positions to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Can I create a messy bun on short hair?

Yes, you can! Just follow the same steps, but instead of tying your hair into a high ponytail, create a small bun at the back of your head. Use bobby pins to keep it in place.

How do I make my messy bun look fuller?

You can add volume to your messy bun by using a hair donut or bump. Simply place it under your ponytail before creating the bun.

Hair Care

Creating a messy bun can be a great way to style your hair without causing too much damage. However, it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often. Excessive heat can cause hair breakage and split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to moisturize your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals. This will help promote hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

Now that you know how to do hairstyle messy bun, you can create this chic and stylish look anytime, anywhere. With our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice, you can rock the messy bun with confidence. Happy styling!
