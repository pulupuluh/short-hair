Short Hairstyles For Long Faces Over 60 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 19, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a long face and are over 60, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that will flatter your facial features, but also work with your age and lifestyle. In this guide, we will go over some of the best short hairstyles for long faces over 60. From pixie cuts to bobs, we’ve got you covered!

Description

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner and lose its volume. This is why short hairstyles are a great option for women over 60 with long faces. Short hair can add volume and texture to your hair, making it appear fuller and thicker. Short hair is also easier to manage and style, which is perfect for those who are always on the go.

How To

The first step in finding the perfect short hairstyle for your long face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by looking in the mirror and measuring the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have a long face shape.

Once you have determined your face shape, it’s time to start looking at different short hairstyles. Pixie cuts, bobs, and shags are all great options for women over 60 with long faces.

Pixie Cuts

Pixie cuts are a popular short hairstyle for women of all ages. They are versatile, easy to style, and can work with any face shape. For women over 60 with long faces, a pixie cut can help to balance out the length of your face.

When getting a pixie cut, make sure to ask your stylist for layers and texture. This will help to add volume and movement to your hair, making it appear fuller and thicker.

Bobs

Bobs are another great short hairstyle for women over 60 with long faces. A bob can help to add width to your face, making it appear less long. Bobs can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous.

When getting a bob, make sure to ask your stylist for layers and texture. This will help to add volume and movement to your hair, making it appear fuller and thicker.

Shags

Shags are a great option for women over 60 with long faces who want a more relaxed and casual look. A shag can help to add volume and texture to your hair, making it appear fuller and thicker.

When getting a shag, make sure to ask your stylist for layers and texture. This will help to add volume and movement to your hair, making it appear fuller and thicker.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape by measuring the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.

2. Choose a short hairstyle that works with your face shape. Pixie cuts, bobs, and shags are all great options for women over 60 with long faces.

3. Talk to your stylist about adding layers and texture to your hair to add volume and movement.

4. Style your hair using products that add volume and texture, such as mousse or texturizing spray.

Tips

- Don’t be afraid to try something new. Short hairstyles can be a great way to change up your look and add some variety to your style.

- Choose a style that works with your lifestyle. If you’re always on the go, a low-maintenance pixie cut may be the best option for you.

- Use products that add volume and texture to your hair, such as mousse or texturizing spray.

FAQ

Q: What is the best short hairstyle for a long face?

A: Pixie cuts, bobs, and shags are all great options for women over 60 with long faces.

Q: How do I add volume and texture to my short hair?

A: Use products that add volume and texture to your hair, such as mousse or texturizing spray.

Hair Care

When it comes to maintaining your short hairstyle, it’s important to keep up with regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your style. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect short hairstyle for your long face and rock it with confidence. Remember, it’s all about finding a style that works for you and makes you feel your best!
