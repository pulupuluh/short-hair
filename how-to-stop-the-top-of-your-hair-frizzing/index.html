How To Stop The Top Of Your Hair Frizzing

Written by Bardi Jul 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're tired of dealing with frizzy hair, you're not alone. Frizz is a common problem, especially for those with curly or wavy hair. It can be frustrating to spend time washing and styling your hair, only to have it turn into a frizzy mess. In this article, we'll show you how to stop the top of your hair frizzing.

How to Stop the Top of Your Hair Frizzing

To stop the top of your hair frizzing, you need to follow a few simple steps:

1. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner

Frizz is often caused by dry hair, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter or coconut oil, which can help hydrate and nourish your hair.

2. Don't wash your hair too often

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to frizz. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days instead of every day.

3. Use a leave-in conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help keep your hair hydrated throughout the day. Apply it to damp hair after washing and styling.

4. Use a wide-tooth comb

A wide-tooth comb is gentler on your hair than a brush and can help prevent frizz. Use it to detangle your hair after washing and before styling.

5. Use a heat protectant

If you use heat tools like a blow dryer or flat iron, it's important to use a heat protectant to prevent damage and frizz. Apply it to your hair before using any heat tools.

6. Avoid touching your hair too much

The oils and dirt from your hands can transfer onto your hair, making it greasy and frizzy. Try to avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to prevent the top of your hair from frizzing:

Step 1: Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Don't wash your hair too often.

Step 3: Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair after washing.

Step 4: Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

Step 5: Apply a heat protectant to your hair before using any heat tools.

Step 6: Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Tips to Prevent Hair Frizzing

Here are some additional tips to help prevent hair frizzing:

1. Use a silk or satin pillowcase

Cotton pillowcases can cause friction, which can lead to frizz. A silk or satin pillowcase is gentler on your hair and can help prevent frizz.

2. Use a hair mask once a week

A weekly hair mask can help hydrate and nourish your hair, preventing frizz. Look for a mask that contains ingredients like argan oil or avocado oil.

3. Don't rub your hair with a towel

Instead of rubbing your hair with a towel after washing, gently squeeze out the excess water. Rubbing can cause friction and lead to frizz.

4. Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair

A diffuser can help distribute the heat evenly and prevent frizz when blow drying your hair.

5. Use a hair oil

A hair oil can help tame frizz and add shine to your hair. Apply a small amount to the ends of your hair after styling.

FAQs

Q: Can humidity cause frizz?

A: Yes, humidity can cause frizz. When the air is humid, it can cause your hair to absorb moisture, leading to frizz.

Q: Can brushing my hair cause frizz?

A: Yes, brushing your hair too much or using the wrong type of brush can cause frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush.

Best Hair Care Practices

Here are some best hair care practices to follow to prevent hair frizzing:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims can help prevent split ends, which can lead to frizz.

2. Use a clarifying shampoo once a month

A clarifying shampoo can help remove buildup from styling products and other pollutants, preventing frizz.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

The sun's rays can dry out your hair and cause frizz. Wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to protect your hair.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can prevent the top of your hair from frizzing. Remember to be gentle with your hair and avoid touching it too much throughout the day. With a little effort, you can have smooth, frizz-free hair.
