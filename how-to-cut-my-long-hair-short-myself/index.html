How To Cut My Long Hair Short Myself A Step By Step Guide

Do you want to switch up your look and cut your long hair short? Going to a salon can be pricey, but with the right tools and techniques, you can do it yourself at home. In this article, we will show you how to cut your own hair and achieve the perfect short hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Your Hair Short Yourself
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Your Hair Short
  • FAQ

Description

Cutting your hair short yourself may seem daunting, but with the right preparation and guidance, it can be done. The key is to have the right tools, including sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Additionally, it's essential to understand the technique and follow a step-by-step guide to ensure that you achieve the look you want.

How to Cut Your Hair Short Yourself

The first step in cutting your hair short yourself is to decide on the length you want. It's helpful to have a picture or idea in mind to guide you. Then, gather the necessary tools, including scissors, a comb, and hair clips.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four quadrants by parting it down the middle and then across the crown of your head.
  3. Clip three of the quadrants out of the way, leaving one section to work with.
  4. Start cutting from the back, using the comb to separate a small section of hair from the rest.
  5. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to cut straight across.
  6. Continue working through the section, cutting small sections of hair at a time.
  7. Repeat the process for the other three quadrants.
  8. Once you've finished cutting, use the scissors to trim any uneven pieces.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Short

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Start with small cuts, as you can always trim more.
  • Invest in high-quality scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Be patient and take your time.
  • Use a mirror to ensure that you are cutting evenly.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair:

  • Can I cut my hair short myself if I have curly hair?
  • Yes, but it's important to cut curly hair when it's dry to avoid any surprises when it dries. It's also essential to cut curly hair in small sections to ensure that you get an even cut.
  • What is the best length for short hair?
  • The best length for short hair depends on your face shape and personal style. However, a length between the chin and shoulders is usually flattering for most people.
  • Do I need to wash my hair before cutting it?
  • It's best to cut your hair when it's clean and dry to ensure that you get an accurate cut.

Hair Care Tips

After cutting your hair short, it's essential to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Limit heat styling to avoid damage.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking fresh.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush to avoid breakage.

By following this step-by-step guide and our hair care tips, you can cut your hair short yourself and achieve a stylish and flattering look. Remember to be patient and take your time, and you'll be rocking your new short hairdo in no time!
