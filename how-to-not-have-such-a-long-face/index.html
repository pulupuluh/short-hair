How To Not Have Such A Long Face

Written by Aprilia May 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you feel like you have a long face? Maybe you're self-conscious about it and wish there was something you could do to change it. Well, the good news is that there are ways to make your face look shorter and more proportional. In this article, we'll go over tips and tricks on how to not have such a long face.

Description

Having a long face is a common issue that many people face. It can make you feel self-conscious and affect your confidence. However, there are ways to make your face look shorter and more proportional. In this article, we'll go over how to not have such a long face and provide tips and tricks to help you feel more confident.

How to

There are several ways to make your face look shorter and more proportional. Here are some tips:

1. Hairstyles

One of the easiest ways to make your face look shorter is by getting a new hairstyle. A short bob or pixie cut can help to balance out the length of your face. Avoid long, straight hair as it can make your face look longer.

2. Makeup

Makeup can also be used to make your face look shorter. Use contouring to create shadows and make your face appear more proportional. Apply bronzer under your cheekbones and along your jawline to create definition.

3. Glasses

If you wear glasses, opt for frames that are wider than your face. This will help to balance out the length of your face and make it appear shorter.

4. Accessories

Accessories can also be used to make your face look shorter. Wear statement earrings or a necklace to draw attention away from your face.

Step by Step

Here is a step by step guide on how to not have such a long face:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that balances out the length of your face. A short bob or pixie cut can help to make your face appear shorter.

Step 2: Use Makeup to Create Shadows

Use contouring to create shadows and make your face appear more proportional. Apply bronzer under your cheekbones and along your jawline to create definition.

Step 3: Choose the Right Glasses

If you wear glasses, choose frames that are wider than your face to balance out the length of your face.

Step 4: Wear Accessories

Wear statement earrings or a necklace to draw attention away from your face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you not have such a long face:

1. Smile

Smiling can help to lift your face and make it appear shorter.

2. Stand Up Straight

Standing up straight can help to elongate your torso and make your face appear shorter.

3. Avoid Long, Straight Hair

Long, straight hair can make your face appear longer. Opt for a shorter hairstyle instead.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my face appear shorter without getting a haircut?

A: Yes, you can use makeup and accessories to make your face appear shorter.

Q: What kind of glasses should I choose?

A: Choose frames that are wider than your face to balance out the length of your face.

Q: Will smiling really make a difference?

A: Yes, smiling can help to lift your face and make it appear shorter.

Hair Care

When trying to make your face appear shorter, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. If you're unsure about what type of hairstyle to get, consult with a hairstylist who can recommend a style that will work best for you.

By following these tips and tricks, you can learn how to not have such a long face and feel more confident in your appearance.
