Best Hair Length For Round Chubby Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Having a round, chubby face can make it challenging to choose the right hairstyle. A good hairstyle can make your face look slimmer and more defined, while a bad one can make it look rounder and fuller. In this article, we'll discuss the best hair length for round chubby faces, as well as some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect hairstyle.

  2. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Best Hair Length for Round Chubby Faces

  6. Hair Care Tips for Round Chubby Faces

Description

Round chubby faces have soft curves and a rounded chin, which can make the face look fuller. The best way to create the illusion of a slimmer face is to choose a hairstyle that adds length and height to the face. The right hair length and style can also highlight your best features and balance out your face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hair length for round chubby faces, it's important to determine your face shape. Here's how to do it:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you've drawn.

Your face shape will fall into one of the following categories:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

Once you've determined your face shape, you can move on to choosing the best hair length for your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Best Hair Length for Round Chubby Faces

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the best hair length for round chubby faces:

Step 1: Consider Your Hair Texture

The first step in choosing the right hair length is to consider your hair texture. If you have thin, fine hair, a shorter cut may be more flattering. On the other hand, if you have thick, curly hair, a longer cut may be more suitable.

Step 2: Look for Length and Height

The key to choosing the right hair length for round chubby faces is to add length and height to the face. A long, layered cut that falls below the shoulders can help elongate the face, while a short pixie cut can create the illusion of height.

Step 3: Avoid Blunt Cuts and Heavy Bangs

Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as they can make the face look rounder and fuller. Instead, opt for soft, layered cuts that frame the face and highlight your best features.

Step 4: Consider Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers can help draw attention to your best features and create the illusion of a slimmer face. Ask your stylist to add layers around your face, starting at the cheekbones and tapering down towards the ends.

Step 5: Don't Be Afraid to Experiment

Finally, don't be afraid to experiment with different hair lengths and styles. What works for one person may not work for another, so don't be afraid to try something new.

Tips for Styling Your Hair with a Round Chubby Face

Here are some tips for styling your hair with a round chubby face:

  • Use volumizing products to add height to your hair and create the illusion of a slimmer face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or pulled back, as they can make the face look rounder.
  • Try a side part or deep side part to create the illusion of length.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too bulky or heavy, as they can overwhelm a round face.

FAQs

What is the best hair length for a round chubby face?

The best hair length for a round chubby face is one that adds length and height to the face. A long, layered cut that falls below the shoulders can help elongate the face, while a short pixie cut can create the illusion of height.

What hairstyles should I avoid with a round chubby face?

Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as they can make the face look rounder and fuller. Instead, opt for soft, layered cuts that frame the face and highlight your best features.

Hair Care Tips for Round Chubby Faces

Here are some hair care tips for round chubby faces:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add height and fullness to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to prevent tangles and breakage.

Choosing the best hair length for a round chubby face can be challenging, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle that highlights your best features and flatters your face shape. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
