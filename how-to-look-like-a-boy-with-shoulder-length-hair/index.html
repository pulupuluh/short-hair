How To Look Like A Boy With Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Aprilia Feb 28, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're a girl with shoulder-length hair who wants to achieve a more masculine look, this is the article for you. In this post, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to look like a boy with shoulder length hair.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a great length to work with if you want to achieve a more masculine look. It's long enough to style in different ways, but not too long that it becomes unmanageable. With the right styling techniques and a few key accessories, you can transform your look and start feeling more confident in your appearance.

How to

Here are some steps you can take to look like a boy with shoulder length hair:

Step 1: Cut your hair

If your hair is currently longer than shoulder length, you may want to consider getting it cut. A shorter haircut will give you a more masculine look, and it will also be easier to style. Talk to your stylist about what kind of cut would best suit your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Style your hair

Once you have your hair cut, it's time to start styling it in a more masculine way. One option is to slick it back with gel or pomade. Another option is to create a side part and comb your hair to one side. You could also try a messy, textured look by using a texturizing spray and scrunching your hair with your hands.

Step 3: Wear masculine clothing

Your hair is just one part of your overall look. To really look like a boy, you'll want to wear clothing that is more masculine. This could mean wearing button-down shirts, jeans, and sneakers instead of dresses, skirts, and heels.

Step 4: Accessorize

To complete your look, consider adding some masculine accessories. A watch, a belt, and a pair of sunglasses can all help to give you a more masculine vibe.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a more masculine look:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that you like
  • Invest in a good quality haircut
  • Consider wearing a binder to flatten your chest
  • Practice good posture to help you look more confident and masculine

FAQ

Q: Will cutting my hair short damage it?

A: Cutting your hair short won't damage it, as long as you go to a professional stylist who knows how to work with your hair type.

Q: What kind of clothing should I avoid wearing?

A: To look more masculine, you'll want to avoid clothing that is overly feminine, such as dresses, skirts, and high heels.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, no matter what style you're going for. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or oil to help prevent split ends

With these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to looking like a boy with shoulder length hair. Remember to have fun with your style and experiment until you find a look that you love.
