How To Cover Beard With Makeup A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Apr 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Beards are a popular trend among men in recent years, but for some, it may not fit their personal style or work environment. However, removing a beard can be a daunting task, especially for those who have grown attached to it. Fortunately, there is a solution: covering your beard with makeup. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cover your beard with makeup, as well as some tips and frequently asked questions.

Description

When you want to change your look or style, it can be difficult to know where to start. If you have a beard, it may seem impossible to cover it up without shaving it off completely. However, with the right makeup products and techniques, you can achieve the look you desire without having to sacrifice your beloved beard. In this article, we will guide you through the process of covering your beard with makeup, giving you a new, fresh look while keeping your facial hair intact.

How to Cover Beard with Makeup: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Cleanse and moisturize your face and beard

The first step in covering your beard with makeup is to cleanse and moisturize your face and beard. This will ensure that your skin is free of dirt, oil, and debris, and that your beard is soft and pliable. Use a gentle cleanser that is suitable for your skin type, and follow up with a lightweight moisturizer that won't clog your pores.

Step 2: Apply primer to your face and beard

The next step is to apply primer to your face and beard. This will help to smooth out your skin's texture, minimize the appearance of pores, and create a smooth, even canvas for your makeup. When applying primer to your beard, be sure to work the product in thoroughly, making sure to cover every hair.

Step 3: Apply foundation to your face and beard

The third step is to apply foundation to your face and beard. Choose a foundation that matches your skin tone, and apply it in thin, even layers, blending it in with a damp makeup sponge or brush. When applying foundation to your beard, use a small brush or your fingers to work the product into your beard, making sure to cover every hair.

Step 4: Conceal any remaining beard hairs

The fourth step is to conceal any remaining beard hairs that may be showing through your foundation. Use a concealer that matches your skin tone, and apply it in thin, even layers, dabbing it onto any areas where your beard may be showing through. Blend the concealer in with a small brush or your fingers, making sure to cover every hair.

Step 5: Set your makeup with powder

The final step is to set your makeup with powder. Use a translucent powder that matches your skin tone, and apply it in thin, even layers, using a large fluffy brush. This will help to lock in your makeup and prevent it from smudging or transferring throughout the day.

Tips for Covering Beard with Makeup

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when covering your beard with makeup:

  • Choose makeup products that are suitable for your skin type
  • Blend your makeup thoroughly, making sure to cover every hair
  • Use a light touch when applying makeup to your beard
  • Set your makeup with powder to prevent smudging and transfer
  • Experiment with different makeup techniques to find what works best for you

FAQ about Covering Beard with Makeup

Can I cover my beard with makeup if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, you can still cover your beard with makeup if you have sensitive skin. Just be sure to choose products that are formulated for sensitive skin, and do a patch test before applying makeup all over your face and beard.

Will covering my beard with makeup damage my facial hair?

No, covering your beard with makeup will not damage your facial hair. Just be sure to use a light touch when applying makeup to your beard, and avoid using products that are too heavy or greasy.

Can I wear makeup over my beard every day?

Yes, you can wear makeup over your beard every day if you wish. Just be sure to cleanse and moisturize your skin and beard thoroughly before applying makeup, and follow up with a gentle cleanser to remove makeup at the end of the day.

Hair Care for Covered Beard

When covering your beard with makeup, it's important to maintain good hair care habits to keep your beard healthy and strong. Here are some tips for taking care of your covered beard:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free beard shampoo to cleanse your beard
  • Apply a lightweight beard oil to your beard to keep it soft and moisturized
  • Trim your beard regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your beard

By following these tips, you can enjoy a covered beard while keeping your facial hair healthy and strong.
