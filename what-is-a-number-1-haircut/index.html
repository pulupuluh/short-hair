What Is A Number 1 Haircut

Written by Frank Mar 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you thinking about getting a new haircut but not sure what a number 1 haircut is? Look no further! In this article, we'll explain what a number 1 haircut is, how to get it, and some tips for maintaining your new style.

Description of Number 1 Haircut

A number 1 haircut is a very short buzz cut that leaves about 1/8 inch of hair on your scalp. It's the shortest clipper guard size available and is often used for military-style haircuts or for men who want a low-maintenance style. The number 1 haircut is also known as a "crew cut" or a "butch cut."

How to Get a Number 1 Haircut

If you're interested in getting a number 1 haircut, the first step is to find a barber or stylist who is experienced in cutting short hair. When you arrive for your appointment, let them know that you want a number 1 haircut and they will use clippers with a guard size of 1 to cut your hair evenly all over your head. You can also choose to have your hair tapered around the sides and back for a more polished look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Number 1 Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a number 1 haircut:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before your haircut, wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any product buildup or oils that may interfere with the clippers.

Step 2: Choose Your Length

Let your barber know that you want a number 1 haircut and discuss any additional styling or tapering you want.

Step 3: Begin the Cut

Your barber will begin the cut at the front of your hairline and work their way back, moving the clippers in an upward motion against the grain of your hair.

Step 4: Taper the Sides and Back

If you want a tapered look, your barber will use scissors or clippers without a guard to gradually shorten your hair around the sides and back of your head.

Step 5: Clean Up the Neckline

Your barber will clean up your neckline with clippers or scissors, depending on your preference.

Tips for Maintaining Your Number 1 Haircut

Here are some tips for keeping your number 1 haircut looking sharp:

Tip 1: Keep Your Hair Clean

Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent buildup and maintain a healthy scalp.

Tip 2: Use a Styling Product

If you want a more polished look, use a styling product like pomade, wax, or gel to add texture and hold to your hair.

Tip 3: Get Regular Touch-Ups

Because a number 1 haircut is so short, it can grow out quickly. Visit your barber every 2-4 weeks for a touch-up to keep your style looking fresh.

FAQs

Q: Is a number 1 haircut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a number 1 haircut is versatile and can complement any face shape. It's a great option for men who want a low-maintenance style that's easy to maintain.

Q: Can I get a number 1 haircut at home?

A: While it's possible to cut your hair at home with clippers, we recommend visiting a professional barber or stylist for the best results.

Hair Care for a Number 1 Haircut

Keeping your hair healthy and looking great after a number 1 haircut is important. Here are some hair care tips:

Tip 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated to moisturize and nourish your hair. Look for ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, and argan oil.

Tip 2: Protect Your Scalp from the Sun

Because a number 1 haircut leaves your scalp exposed, it's important to protect it from the sun's harmful rays. Wear a hat or use a scalp sunscreen to prevent sunburn and skin damage.

Tip 3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your number 1 haircut looking sharp, visit your barber every few weeks for a touch-up. This will also prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, a number 1 haircut is a short buzz cut that's perfect for men who want a low-maintenance style. With the right care and maintenance, your number 1 haircut can look great and feel comfortable all year round.
