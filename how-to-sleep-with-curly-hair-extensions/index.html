How To Sleep With Curly Hair Extensions A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curly hair extensions are a great way to add volume and length to your natural hair. However, sleeping with curly hair extensions can be a challenge, especially if you don't know how to take care of them properly. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to sleep with curly hair extensions without damaging them. We will also share some useful tips and tricks to make the process easier for you. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Sleep with Curly Hair Extensions
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

How to Sleep with Curly Hair Extensions

Sleeping with curly hair extensions can be a bit tricky, as they can easily get tangled and matted while you sleep. However, there are some simple steps you can follow to keep your curly hair extensions looking great:

  • Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf before going to bed.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent tangling.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet or damp hair, as this can cause frizz and damage your extensions.
  • Try sleeping on your back to avoid putting pressure on your hair extensions.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to sleep with curly hair extensions:

  1. Brush your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or a curl-defining cream to your hair extensions to keep them moisturized and defined.
  3. Section your hair into small twists or braids to prevent tangling while you sleep.
  4. Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf, making sure to cover all your hair extensions.
  5. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent tangling.
  6. Avoid sleeping with wet or damp hair, as this can cause frizz and damage your extensions.
  7. Try sleeping on your back to avoid putting pressure on your hair extensions.
  8. In the morning, gently remove the scarf and untwist or unbraid your hair.
  9. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to style your hair extensions.
  10. Apply a light mist of water or a curl refreshing spray to revive your curls.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you sleep with curly hair extensions:

  • Use a silk or satin bonnet instead of a scarf if you find it more comfortable.
  • Avoid using hair ties or clips that can cause tension and breakage.
  • Try sleeping with a silk or satin shirt or nightgown to reduce friction and prevent tangling.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair extensions, as this can damage them.
  • Wash your hair extensions at least once a week to keep them clean and fresh.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about sleeping with curly hair extensions:

Q: Can I sleep with my curly hair extensions down?

A: It is not recommended to sleep with your curly hair extensions down, as this can cause tangling and matting. It is best to wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf or bonnet to protect your extensions while you sleep.

Q: Can I use a regular pillowcase instead of a silk or satin one?

A: It is not recommended to use a regular cotton pillowcase, as it can cause friction and tangle your hair extensions. A silk or satin pillowcase is much gentler on your hair and will help prevent tangling and matting.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair extensions?

A: It is recommended to wash your curly hair extensions at least once a week to keep them clean and fresh. However, depending on your lifestyle and how much you sweat or exercise, you may need to wash them more frequently.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you take care of your curly hair extensions:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos or conditioners that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair extensions moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling wands on your hair extensions, as this can damage them.
  • Trim your hair extensions regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your curls and make them look dull.

Following these tips and tricks will help you sleep with curly hair extensions without damaging them. With a little bit of care and attention, you can keep your extensions looking great for weeks or even months!
