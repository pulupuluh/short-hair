How To Style Men s Medium Wavy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Apr 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Men's medium wavy hair can be a bit of a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can make the most of your natural waves. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to create a stylish and effortless look that suits your hair type and personal style. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Men's Medium Wavy Hair

How to Style Men's Medium Wavy Hair

Before we dive into the styling process, it's important to understand your hair type and what products work best for it. Men's medium wavy hair requires a bit of moisture to enhance the natural texture and reduce frizz. Here are some products that can help:

  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Sea salt spray
  • Wax or pomade

Once you have your products ready, it's time to start styling.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with clean, towel-dried hair. Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair to add moisture and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Spray sea salt spray throughout your hair to enhance the natural waves. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create more texture.

Step 3: Use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair. This will help enhance the natural waves without creating too much frizz. Use low heat and low speed settings to avoid over-drying your hair.

Step 4: Once your hair is dry, apply a small amount of wax or pomade to your fingertips. Rub your hands together to warm up the product and apply it to your hair. Use your fingers to create the desired shape and texture.

Step 5: Finish off with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place throughout the day.

Tips for Styling Men's Medium Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling men's medium wavy hair:

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and style.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause frizz and disrupt the natural waves.
  • Consider getting a haircut that complements your natural waves, such as a layered cut or a side-swept fringe.
  • Don't be afraid to embrace the natural texture of your hair. Wavy hair can add a unique and stylish touch to any look.

FAQs

Q: Can I style my hair without using heat tools?

A: Yes, you can enhance your natural waves by using sea salt spray and air-drying your hair. However, using a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment can help create more defined waves and reduce frizz.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. However, over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

Hair Care for Men's Medium Wavy Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and stylish hair. Here are some tips to keep your men's medium wavy hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can cause dryness and frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to add moisture and nourishment to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. If you must use heat tools, use a heat protectant spray and low heat settings.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy look.

With these tips and techniques, you can style men's medium wavy hair with ease and confidence. Embrace your natural waves and experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
