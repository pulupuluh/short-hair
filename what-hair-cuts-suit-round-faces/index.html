What Hair Cuts Suit Round Faces

Written by Eveline Jul 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering what hair cuts will flatter your features. While there are no hard and fast rules, there are some styles that tend to work well with round faces. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the most popular hair cuts for round faces and provide some tips on how to style them.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Popular Hair Cuts for Round Faces
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Round Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a hair cut that flatters your features is to determine your face shape. To do this, start by pulling your hair back away from your face and taking a close look at your features.

If the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly equal and you have a soft, round chin, you likely have a round face. Other common face shapes include square, oval, heart, and diamond.

Popular Hair Cuts for Round Faces

When it comes to choosing a hair cut for a round face, the goal is to create the illusion of length and definition. Here are some popular styles to consider:

Long Layers

Long layers can help to add texture and movement to your hair, which can in turn create a more elongated look for your face. To achieve this style, ask your stylist to add layers that start at or below your chin.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of length and draw attention away from the roundness of your face. To achieve this style, ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle so that they start longer on one side and gradually get shorter on the other.

Bob

A bob can be a great choice for a round face, especially if it is cut just above the chin. To create a more elongated look, consider adding some layers or subtle angles to the cut.

Pixie

A pixie cut can be a bold choice for a round face, but it can also be very flattering. To avoid creating a "bubble" effect, ask your stylist to add some texture and volume to the crown of your head.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen a hair cut that flatters your round face, it's important to learn some styling tips to keep your hair looking its best. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add lift to your roots.
  • Avoid blunt cuts, which can create a "bubble" effect.
  • Consider adding subtle highlights or lowlights to create depth and definition.
  • Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, which can help to add volume and movement.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair cuts for round faces:

Should I avoid short hair cuts if I have a round face?

Not necessarily. Short hair cuts can be very flattering for round faces, as long as they are styled properly. Consider a pixie cut or a bob that is cut just above the chin.

Do bangs work well with round faces?

Yes, bangs can be a great choice for round faces. Consider side-swept bangs that are cut at an angle to create the illusion of length.

How can I add volume to my hair?

There are a few ways to add volume to your hair, including using a volumizing mousse or spray, blow drying your hair with a round brush, and adding subtle highlights or lowlights to create depth and definition.

Hair Care for Round Faces

When it comes to caring for your hair, it's important to choose products that are right for your hair type and texture. Consider using a shampoo and conditioner that are designed to add volume and moisture to your hair.

In addition to using the right products, it's important to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using hot tools. You should also try to avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

By following these tips and choosing a hair cut that flatters your round face, you can create a look that is both stylish and flattering.
