How To Get Levi Haircut Tips Steps And Hair Care In 2023

Written by Bardi Jun 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a fan of the anime and manga series, Attack on Titan, then you're probably familiar with the main protagonist, Levi Ackerman. Aside from his impressive fighting skills, Levi is also known for his iconic short haircut. If you want to achieve a similar look, then you're in the right place. In this article, we'll be sharing tips, steps, and hair care advice on how to get Levi haircut in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • Tips for Getting Levi Haircut
  • How to Get Levi Haircut: Step by Step Guide
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Levi Haircut

Tips for Getting Levi Haircut

Before getting a Levi haircut, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Make sure that the haircut will suit your face shape and hair type.
  • Bring a reference photo of Levi's haircut to show your stylist.
  • Consider getting a fade or undercut for a more modern look.
  • Find a stylist who has experience in cutting men's hair.

How to Get Levi Haircut: Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow for getting a Levi haircut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It's important to start with clean and dry hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and rinse thoroughly. Then, towel dry your hair or use a hair dryer on low heat to remove excess moisture.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to divide your hair into sections. Clip the top section away and focus on the sides and back.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Ask your stylist to use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair. Start with a longer guard and gradually go shorter until you achieve the desired length. For a more modern look, consider getting a fade or undercut.

Step 4: Cut the Top

For the top section, your stylist will use scissors to cut your hair to the desired length. Make sure to show them a reference photo of Levi's haircut to get the right style. You can also ask for texture or layers to add volume and movement.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

After cutting your hair, your stylist will style it according to your preference. You can ask for a side part or a messy, textured look. Use a hair product that suits your hair type and style to keep your hair in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How short should I cut my hair for a Levi haircut?

A: The length of your hair will depend on your face shape and hair type. Generally, the sides and back are cut shorter while the top is left longer. Bring a reference photo of Levi's haircut to show your stylist.

Q: Can I get a Levi haircut if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still get a Levi haircut if you have curly hair. However, it may require more maintenance to keep the style in place. Ask your stylist for advice on how to style and maintain your curly hair.

Q: How often do I need to get a Levi haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Levi Haircut

Here are some tips to keep your Levi haircut looking great:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and avoid harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant before styling.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style and prevent split ends.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips, steps, and hair care advice, you can now achieve a Levi haircut in 2023. Remember to find a stylist who can help you achieve the look and maintain it with proper hair care.
