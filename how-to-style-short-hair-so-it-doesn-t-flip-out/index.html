How To Style Short Hair So It Doesn t Flip Out

Written by Christine Aug 23, 2023 · 6 min read

Short hair is a great look, but it can be frustrating when it starts to flip out. Whether you have a pixie cut or a bob, there are ways to style your hair so it stays in place all day. In this blog post, we'll discuss how to style short hair so it doesn't flip out.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Short Hair So It Doesn't Flip Out
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Short Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Style Short Hair So It Doesn't Flip Out

If you have short hair that flips out, it's important to start with a good haircut. Make sure your hair is cut evenly and has a good shape. A stylist can give you a great cut that will work with your hair type and face shape.

Another key to styling short hair is to use the right products. Look for products that offer hold and control, such as mousse or a styling paste. Apply these products to your hair while it's damp, and use your fingers to work the product through your hair.

When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to create volume and shape. Blow-dry your hair in the opposite direction of where you want it to flip out. This will help to train your hair to stay in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair so it doesn't flip out:

  1. Start with a good haircut that works with your hair type and face shape.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair while it's damp.
  3. Blow-dry your hair in the opposite direction of where you want it to flip out.
  4. Use a round brush to create volume and shape.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of styling paste or hairspray to hold it in place.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling short hair:

  • Use a flat iron to smooth out any flyaways or frizz.
  • Experiment with different parting options to change up your look.
  • Try using a headband or hair accessory to keep your hair in place.
  • Don't overdo it with product – too much can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short hair:

Q: Can I still style my short hair if it's thin or fine?

A: Yes, you can still style short hair if it's thin or fine. Look for products that offer volume and texture, and use a round brush to create body and shape.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with short hair wash it every day, while others can go a few days between washes. Experiment to see what works best for you.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often – this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider getting a deep conditioning treatment once a month to keep your hair healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can style your short hair so it doesn't flip out. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.
