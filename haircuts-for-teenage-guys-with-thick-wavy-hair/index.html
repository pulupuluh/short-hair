Haircuts For Teenage Guys With Thick Wavy Hair

Written by William Mar 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a teenage guy with thick wavy hair, you might be wondering what haircut would suit you best. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this article, we'll go through some of the best haircuts for teenage guys with thick wavy hair, and provide tips and tricks to help you maintain your hairstyle. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs About Haircuts for Teenage Guys with Thick Wavy Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Thick Wavy Hair

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When it comes to choosing a haircut for teenage guys with thick wavy hair, there are a few things to consider:

  • Face Shape: Certain haircuts look better on certain face shapes, so it's important to determine your face shape before choosing a haircut.
  • Hair Texture: Thick wavy hair can be difficult to manage, so it's important to choose a haircut that works with your hair texture.
  • Personal Style: Your haircut should reflect your personal style and personality.

Some of the best haircuts for teenage guys with thick wavy hair include:

Short Textured Crop

This haircut is perfect for teenage guys who want a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish. The hair is cut short on the sides and back, with a longer length on top. The top is then styled into a textured crop, which adds volume and movement to the hair.

Long Wavy Hair

If you want to embrace your thick wavy hair, consider growing it out. Long wavy hair can look great on teenage guys, especially when styled with a bit of product to add definition and hold.

Side Part

The side part is a classic haircut that works well with thick wavy hair. The hair is cut short on the sides and back, with a longer length on top. The top is then styled into a side part, which gives the haircut a polished and sophisticated look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair

If you're looking to cut your own hair at home, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, left side, right side, and back.
  3. Using a pair of scissors or clippers, start cutting the sides and back of your hair. Use a comb to guide your cuts and ensure that you're cutting evenly.
  4. Move on to the top of your hair. Depending on the haircut you want, you can either cut the hair straight across or create layers.
  5. Once you've finished cutting, style your hair using a bit of product.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

To keep your haircut looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use the right products for your hair type.
  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.

FAQs About Haircuts for Teenage Guys with Thick Wavy Hair

What haircut is best for thick wavy hair?

Some of the best haircuts for thick wavy hair include the short textured crop, long wavy hair, and the side part.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

What products should I use for thick wavy hair?

It's best to use products that are specifically designed for thick wavy hair, such as mousse, curl cream, or sea salt spray.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Wavy Hair

Thick wavy hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right hair care routine, you can keep your hair looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine to your hair.

With these haircuts and tips, you'll be able to manage your thick wavy hair with ease. Remember to choose a haircut that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, and to maintain your haircut with regular trims and the right products. Happy styling!
