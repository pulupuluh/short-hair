Do Side Swept Bangs Look Good On Round Faces

Description

Side swept bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add a touch of glam to any look. However, if you have a round face, you might be wondering whether they will suit you or not. The answer is yes! Side swept bangs can look great on round faces, but it's all about the way they are cut and styled.

How to

The key to pulling off side swept bangs on a round face is to make sure they are not too short or too blunt. Opt for longer bangs that are cut on an angle, starting at the outside corner of your eyebrow and gradually getting shorter towards the middle. This will help to elongate your face and create a slimming effect.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get side swept bangs for a round face:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to decide on the length and angle of your bangs.
  2. Wash and dry your hair as usual.
  3. Part your hair to one side.
  4. Take a section of hair from the front and comb it forward.
  5. Hold the scissors at an angle and cut the hair, starting at the outside corner of your eyebrow and getting gradually shorter towards the middle.
  6. Repeat on the other side.
  7. Style the rest of your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling side swept bangs on a round face:

  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to give them a little lift.
  • Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz.
  • Add some texture to your bangs by using a sea salt spray or texturizing spray.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different angles and lengths to find the most flattering style for your face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about side swept bangs on round faces:

Q: Will side swept bangs make my face look even rounder?

No, as long as they are cut and styled correctly, side swept bangs can actually help to slim down a round face.

Q: What other hairstyles look good on round faces?

Other flattering hairstyles for round faces include long layers, a lob (long bob), and a side part.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking healthy.
  • Consider getting regular deep conditioning treatments to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

So, the next time you're thinking about getting side swept bangs, don't be afraid to give them a try! With the right cut and styling, they can look great on any face shape, including round faces.
