What Hairstyle Suits For Round Face Female

Written by Petter Jul 23, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As a woman with a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a haircut that flatters your face shape and brings out your best features. In this blog post, we will discuss what hairstyle suits for round face female, how to achieve it, and some tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

A round face is characterized by a circular shape, with a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The goal of a good hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles, which can help to elongate the face and make it appear slimmer.

How to

The best hairstyles for round face female are those that add height and volume to the crown of the head, while keeping the sides and back of the hair close to the head. Here are some hairstyles that are perfect for women with round faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are great for women with round faces because they add volume and movement to the hair. They also help to create the illusion of length, which can make the face appear slimmer. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to add long layers to your hair, starting at the chin and working their way down.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, layered haircut that can be styled in a variety of ways. It's a great hairstyle for women with round faces because it adds height to the top of the head, which can help to elongate the face. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a short, layered cut with long bangs that can be swept to the side.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add angles to a round face. They can be styled in a variety of ways and can be worn with almost any haircut. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long, side-swept bangs that start at the temple and sweep across the forehead.

Step by Step

Here are the step by step instructions to achieve the perfect hairstyle for round face female:

1. Determine your face shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. A round face is characterized by a circular shape, with a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin.

2. Choose the right haircut

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to choose the right haircut. The best hairstyles for round faces are those that add height and volume to the crown of the head, while keeping the sides and back of the hair close to the head.

3. Consider adding layers

Adding layers to your hair can help to add volume and movement, which can be especially flattering for women with round faces.

4. Opt for side-swept bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to add angles to a round face, which can make it appear slimmer.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for round face female:

1. Avoid blunt cuts

Blunt cuts can make a round face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for layered cuts that add volume and movement to the hair.

2. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

3. Consider your hair texture

Your hair texture can play a big role in determining which hairstyle will work best for you. If you have thick, coarse hair, you may want to opt for a shorter, layered cut. If you have fine hair, a longer, layered cut may be more flattering.

FAQ

1. Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

Not necessarily. A short haircut can be very flattering for women with round faces, as long as it's layered and styled in a way that adds height and volume to the crown of the head.

2. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail?

A high ponytail can be very flattering for women with round faces, as it adds height to the top of the head. However, avoid wearing your hair in a low ponytail, as this can make your face appear rounder.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

1. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's right for your hair type

Using the right shampoo and conditioner can help to keep your hair healthy and looking its best. If you have fine hair, look for a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have thick, coarse hair, look for a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Avoid using too much heat on your hair

Excessive heat can damage your hair and make it look dry and frizzy. If you must use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using them every day.

3. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, finding the perfect hairstyle for round face female can be a challenge, but with the right cut and styling techniques, you can create a look that flatters your face shape and brings out your best features. Remember to experiment with different styles and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
