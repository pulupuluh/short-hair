How Many Mm Is A Grade 2 Haircut Uk

Written by Bardi Apr 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Have you ever walked into a barbershop and been asked what grade you want for your haircut? If you're not familiar with the grading system, it can be confusing to know what to ask for. In the UK, the grade 2 haircut is one of the most popular choices for men. But how many mm is a grade 2 haircut UK? In this article, we'll break down everything you need to know about this popular haircut.

Description

The grade 2 haircut is a short, buzzed hairstyle that leaves hair around 6mm in length. It's a versatile cut that suits many face shapes and hair types. The grade 2 haircut is a popular choice for men who want a low-maintenance style that looks neat and tidy.

How To

To get a grade 2 haircut, you'll need to go to a barbershop or salon that offers clipper cuts. You can ask for a grade 2 all over or have a fade or taper added to the sides and back. Here's how to get a grade 2 haircut:

Step 1: Choose Your Style

Decide whether you want a simple all-over cut or a more complex style with a fade or taper. You can bring in pictures or ask your barber for advice on what will suit you best.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before your haircut, it's a good idea to wash your hair to remove any product buildup or dirt. This will make it easier for your barber to work with your hair.

Step 3: Get the Cut

Your barber will use clippers to trim your hair to the desired length. For a grade 2, they'll use a guard that leaves hair around 6mm in length. They'll work around the sides, back, and top of your head to create a neat, uniform cut. If you're having a fade or taper, they'll blend the hair at the sides and back for a smooth transition.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your haircut is complete, your barber will style your hair using products like wax or pomade. They'll show you how to style your hair at home and give you tips on how to maintain your new haircut.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your grade 2 haircut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups and trims
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for you
  • Try different beard styles to complement your new haircut

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the grade 2 haircut:

What other grades are there?

The grading system goes from 0 (no hair) to 8 (long hair). Each grade corresponds to a different clipper guard size, with 0 being the shortest and 8 being the longest.

How often should I get a grade 2 haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your style. Most people get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep their hair looking neat and tidy.

Will a grade 2 haircut suit me?

The grade 2 haircut is a versatile style that suits many face shapes and hair types. However, it's always a good idea to ask your barber for advice on what will suit you best.

Hair Care

After your grade 2 haircut, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to detangle your hair
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage

Now that you know how many mm is a grade 2 haircut UK, you can confidently walk into your next barber appointment and ask for the style you want. With the right care and maintenance, your grade 2 haircut will look great and keep you looking sharp for weeks to come.
