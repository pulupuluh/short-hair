Hairstyles For Rectangle Face Shape Male The Ultimate Guide In 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a male with a rectangle face shape? Are you tired of searching for the perfect hairstyle that suits you? Look no further because we have got you covered. In this ultimate guide, we will be sharing the best hairstyles for rectangle face shape male that will enhance your features and make you look your best. Here is what you can expect from this article:

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Choose A Hairstyle
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips For Styling
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

A rectangle face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and a forehead that is about the same width as the cheekbones. The length of the face is longer than its width. This face shape is also known as an oblong face shape.

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for rectangle face shape male, the goal is to create balance and proportion. You want to avoid hairstyles that elongate your face even further or make it look too narrow. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face and shorten the appearance of your face length.

How To Choose A Hairstyle

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for rectangle face shape male, it is important to understand how to choose a hairstyle that suits you. Here are some factors to consider:

  • Face shape
  • Hair texture and thickness
  • Personal style and preferences
  • Age and lifestyle

In addition to these factors, it is also important to consult with a hairstylist who can recommend the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.

Step By Step Guide

Here are some of the best hairstyles for rectangle face shape male:

Short Back and Sides

A short back and sides haircut is a classic hairstyle that looks great on rectangle face shapes. The short sides and back create volume and add width to the face, while the longer hair on top adds height and balances out the face length.

The Quiff

The quiff is a popular hairstyle that looks great on rectangle face shapes. The hair is brushed upwards and back, creating height and volume on top. This hairstyle adds balance to the face and looks great with a side part or a fade on the sides.

Side Part

A side part adds volume to the sides of the face and creates a balanced look. This hairstyle works well with short or medium-length hair and can be styled with a pomade or a wax.

Textured Crop

The textured crop is a modern hairstyle that looks great on rectangle face shapes. The hair is cut short on the sides and back, while the top is left longer and textured. This hairstyle adds volume and texture to the face and is easy to style with a matte finish product.

Tips For Styling

Here are some tips for styling your hair if you have a rectangle face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short
  • Use a matte finish product for a natural look
  • Add volume to the sides of your face with a side part or textured crop
  • Balance out your face length with height on top

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a rectangle face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that elongate your face even further or make it look too narrow. This includes hairstyles that are too long or too short, as well as hairstyles that are slicked back or parted in the middle.

What is the best haircut for rectangle face shape male?

The best haircut for rectangle face shape male is one that adds volume to the sides of the face and balances out the face length. Short back and sides, the quiff, side part, and textured crop are all great options.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy

With these tips and hairstyle options, you are sure to find a hairstyle that suits your rectangle face shape male and enhances your features. Remember to consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect haircut for you.
