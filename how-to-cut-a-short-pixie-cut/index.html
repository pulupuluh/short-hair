How To Cut A Short Pixie Cut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Mar 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your long, boring hair? Do you want to try something new and edgy? A short pixie cut might be the perfect solution! But before you grab those scissors and start snipping away, read this guide on how to cut a short pixie cut. We'll give you all the tips and tricks you need to create the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut a Short Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A short pixie cut is a style where the hair is cut very short, usually no longer than 2-3 inches. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look that still makes a statement. It's also a great way to show off your facial features and bone structure.

How to Cut a Short Pixie Cut

Cutting a short pixie cut is not as complicated as it may seem. However, it does require some skill and practice. If you're not confident in your abilities, it's best to go to a professional stylist. But if you're feeling brave, here's how to cut a short pixie cut:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair at the crown of your head and clip it up.
  3. Using scissors, cut the hair at the nape of your neck to the desired length.
  4. Move up the head and continue cutting the hair in small sections, making sure to blend the hair as you go.
  5. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  6. Unclip the hair at the crown of your head and cut it to the desired length.
  7. Blend the hair at the crown with the hair on the sides and back.
  8. Use thinning shears to create texture and volume.
  9. Trim the bangs to the desired length.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips to make sure you achieve the perfect short pixie cut:

  • Invest in good quality scissors and thinning shears.
  • Don't cut your hair too short, as it can be difficult to style.
  • Make sure to blend the hair as you go to create a seamless look.
  • Use a comb and your fingers to help guide your scissors as you cut.
  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a short pixie cut:

Q: Will a short pixie cut work with my face shape?

A: Short pixie cuts can work with any face shape, but you may need to adjust the length or style to suit your features.

Q: Can I cut my hair myself?

A: You can, but it's best to go to a professional stylist if you're not confident in your abilities.

Q: How often do I need to get my hair cut?

A: Short pixie cuts require more maintenance than longer styles, so you may need to get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape.

Hair Care

Short pixie cuts require minimal maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair.

Now that you know how to cut a short pixie cut, it's time to take the plunge and try out this edgy and stylish look!
