Short Curly Hairstyles For Round Faces With Double Chin

Written by Frank Jul 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face with a double chin, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. However, short curly hairstyles can be a great option for those looking to accentuate their features and create a more defined look. In this article, we will explore some of the best short curly hairstyles for round faces with double chins, along with tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

Short curly hairstyles are perfect for those with round faces and double chins because they add texture and volume to the hair, which can help to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline. With the right cut and styling techniques, short curly hair can be both stylish and flattering.

How to

The first step in achieving a short curly hairstyle is to find the right cut for your face shape. For those with round faces and double chins, a layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can be a great option. These cuts help to create volume on top while drawing attention away from the chin.

Once you have your cut, it's important to properly style your hair. To enhance your curls, apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and scrunch your hair with your fingers. Use a diffuser on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls. Finally, use a small amount of pomade or hair wax to define your curls and add shine.

Step by Step

Step 1: Find the right cut

Consult with your hairstylist to find a cut that is both flattering and easy to maintain. A layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can be a great option for those with round faces and double chins.

Step 2: Apply curl-enhancing product

Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and scrunch your hair with your fingers to enhance your natural curls.

Step 3: Use a diffuser on your blow dryer

Use a diffuser on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.

Step 4: Define your curls

Use a small amount of pomade or hair wax to define your curls and add shine.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short curly hair:

  • Invest in a good quality curl-enhancing product to help define your curls.
  • Use a diffuser on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.
  • Avoid using a brush or comb on your curls, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a small amount of pomade or hair wax to define your curls and add shine.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight if I have a round face with a double chin?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair straight if you have a round face with a double chin. However, it's important to choose a cut that flatters your face shape and to avoid hairstyles that add volume to your chin area.

Q: How often should I wash my short curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your short curly hair every two to three days to avoid drying out your curls.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keeping your short curly hair looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can cause damage to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality curl-enhancing product to help define your curls.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.

Overall, short curly hairstyles can be a great option for those with round faces and double chins. With the right cut and styling techniques, short curly hair can be both stylish and flattering. If you're considering a short curly hairstyle, be sure to consult with your hairstylist to find a cut that works for your face shape and hair type.
