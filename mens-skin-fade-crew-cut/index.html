Mens Skin Fade Crew Cut The Ultimate Guide

Written by William May 05, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, the mens skin fade crew cut might just be the perfect option for you. This classic hairstyle has been around for decades, and it’s still as popular as ever. In this article, we’ll go over everything you need to know about this haircut, from how to get it to how to take care of it.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Get a Mens Skin Fade Crew Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Skin Fade Crew Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for a Mens Skin Fade Crew Cut

Description

The mens skin fade crew cut is a short and tapered haircut that’s cut close to the scalp on the sides and back, while leaving a bit more length on top. It’s a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways, from messy and textured to slick and polished. The skin fade on the sides and back of the head creates a clean and sharp look that’s perfect for any occasion.

How to Get a Mens Skin Fade Crew Cut

If you’re interested in getting a mens skin fade crew cut, the first step is to find a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in cutting this style. It’s important to choose someone who understands your hair type and can give you the look you want.

When you go to your appointment, be sure to bring a photo of the haircut you want. This will give your barber a clear idea of what you’re looking for and ensure that you get the exact style you want.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting a mens skin fade crew cut:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  2. Decide on the length you want for the hair on top of your head.
  3. Using clippers, your barber will start by cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head very short, gradually fading it down to the skin.
  4. The hair on top of your head will be cut to the length you’ve chosen.
  5. Your barber will then blend the hair on top with the faded sides and back to create a seamless transition.
  6. The hairline around your ears and at the nape of your neck will be cleaned up with a trimmer.
  7. Finish off with styling the top of your hair with a good hair product.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Skin Fade Crew Cut

Here are a few tips to help you keep your mens skin fade crew cut looking sharp:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Use a hair product that works well for your hair type to style your hair on top.
  • Consider investing in a pair of clippers to touch up the faded sides and back between haircuts.

FAQs

1. Is the mens skin fade crew cut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the mens skin fade crew cut can work well for all hair types, from straight to curly. Your barber will adjust the length and texture of your hair to create a look that suits you.

2. How often should I get a mens skin fade crew cut?

It’s recommended to get a mens skin fade crew cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and keep it looking sharp.

Hair Care Tips for a Mens Skin Fade Crew Cut

Here are a few tips to help you take care of your hair after getting a mens skin fade crew cut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a hair product that works well for your hair type to style your hair on top.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a mens skin fade crew cut with confidence and ease. Whether you’re going for a casual or formal look, this classic haircut is sure to make a statement.
