Are Bangs Good For Oblong Faces

Written by Frank May 15, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you considering getting bangs for your oblong face but unsure if it’s a good idea? Bangs can be a great way to add volume and balance to an oblong face shape, but it’s important to choose the right style that works for you. In this blog post, we will discuss whether bangs are good for oblong faces, how to choose the right style, and hair care tips to keep your bangs looking great.

Description

Oblong faces are longer than they are wide, with a narrow chin and forehead. Bangs can help to shorten the appearance of the face, balance out features, and add volume. However, not all bangs are created equal, and some styles may not work well with oblong faces.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Oblong Faces

When choosing bangs for your oblong face shape, consider the following:

1. Length

Choose bangs that hit above the eyebrows or just below them. Shorter bangs can help to make your face appear shorter and wider, while longer bangs can make your face appear longer.

2. Thickness

Thicker bangs can help to balance out a longer face, while thinner bangs can make your face appear even longer. Consider getting side-swept bangs or curtain bangs that are thicker in the middle and taper towards the ends.

3. Shape

Choose bangs that are curved or angled to soften the appearance of your face. Avoid straight across bangs that can make your face appear even longer.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to choose the right bangs for your oblong face:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Use a measuring tape to measure the width and length of your face. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oblong face shape.

2. Consider Your Hair Type

Thick, curly hair may not work well with certain bang styles, while fine, straight hair may not hold certain styles. Consider your hair type when choosing a bang style.

3. Look for Inspiration

Browse online or in magazines for inspiration and ideas. Save pictures of styles that you like and bring them to your hairstylist for guidance.

4. Consult with a Hairstylist

Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best bang style for your face shape and hair type. They can also give you tips on how to style and maintain your bangs.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs

Follow these tips to keep your bangs looking great:

1. Trim Regularly

Get your bangs trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy.

2. Use Heat Protectant

When using heat tools to style your bangs, use a heat protectant to prevent damage and breakage.

3. Wash and Style Properly

Wash your bangs separately from the rest of your hair to prevent them from becoming greasy. Use a small amount of shampoo and conditioner, and style with a round brush and blow dryer for volume.

FAQ

Q: What type of bangs work best for oblong faces?

A: Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs that are thicker in the middle and taper towards the ends work best for oblong faces.

Q: How do I know if bangs will suit me?

A: Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best bang style for your face shape and hair type.

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have a big forehead?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs if you have a big forehead. Consider getting side-swept bangs or curtain bangs to balance out your features.

Hair Care for Bangs

Follow these hair care tips to keep your bangs looking great:

1. Use a Dry Shampoo

Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh and free of oil.

2. Avoid Touching Your Bangs

Avoid touching your bangs too much, as oils from your hands can transfer onto your hair and make it look greasy.

3. Brush Regularly

Brush your bangs regularly with a boar bristle brush to distribute natural oils and prevent tangles.

In conclusion, bangs can be a great way to add volume and balance to an oblong face shape. When choosing bangs for your face shape, consider the length, thickness, and shape of the bangs. Consult with a professional hairstylist for guidance and follow our hair care tips to keep your bangs looking great.
