Hairstyles For Long Thin Faces Tips Ideas And Care

Written by Bardi Apr 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Having a long, thin face can be both a blessing and a curse. While it can give you a model-like appearance, it can also make it difficult to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features. The good news is, there are plenty of hairstyles that work well for long thin faces. In this article, we'll be sharing some tips, ideas and care for hairstyles that flatter your face shape.

How to Determine if You Have a Long Thin Face?

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's important to know if you have a long thin face. Here are some characteristics:

  • Your face is longer than it is wide
  • You have a pointy chin
  • Your forehead is higher than the average

Hairstyles for Long Thin Faces

1. Layered Haircuts

Layered haircuts are perfect for long thin faces. They add volume and shape to your hair, making your face appear fuller. Ask your hairstylist to add layers around your face to create a more balanced look.

2. Bob Haircuts

Bob haircuts are versatile and look great on long thin faces. Opt for a chin-length bob or a longer bob that reaches your shoulders. This will create the illusion of width and add volume to your hair.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for long thin faces. They draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, creating a more balanced look. Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face look longer.

4. Wavy Hair

Wavy hair adds texture and volume to your hair, making your face appear fuller. Use a curling iron or hair rollers to create loose waves that frame your face. Avoid tight curls as they can make your face look longer.

Tips for Hairstyles for Long Thin Faces

1. Avoid Straight Hair

Straight hair can make your face look longer and thinner. If you have naturally straight hair, add some texture and volume by using a volumizing mousse or spray.

2. Embrace Layers

Layers are your best friend when it comes to long thin faces. They add volume and shape to your hair, creating a more balanced look. Ask your hairstylist for layers around your face.

3. Add Volume

Adding volume to your hair is key to creating a more balanced look. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.

FAQs

What Hairstyles Should I Avoid if I Have a Long Thin Face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long, straight and flat. Blunt bangs can also make your face look longer. Opt for hairstyles that add volume and shape to your hair.

Can I Wear a Ponytail if I Have a Long Thin Face?

Yes, you can wear a ponytail if you have a long thin face. However, avoid pulling your hair back tightly as it can make your face look longer. Instead, opt for a loose ponytail that adds volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care for Long Thin Faces

1. Use Volumizing Products

Use volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. Avoid heavy hair products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

2. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. Avoid using hot tools every day as it can damage your hair and make it look thinner.

3. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This will also help your hair look fuller and thicker.

Final Thoughts

Having a long thin face can be challenging, but there are plenty of hairstyles that work well for this face shape. Embrace layers, add volume and texture to your hair, and avoid hairstyles that make your face look longer. With these tips and ideas, you can find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features and makes you feel confident.
