Would I Look Good With Short Hair Quiz Buzzfeed A Guide For 2023

If you're thinking about chopping off your locks and going for a shorter haircut, you may be wondering if it will suit you. Fortunately, Buzzfeed has come up with a quiz to help you figure it out. In this article, we'll take a look at the 'would I look good with short hair quiz Buzzfeed' and provide tips and advice for those considering a shorter hairstyle.

How to Take the Quiz

Taking the 'would I look good with short hair quiz Buzzfeed' is easy. Simply head to the Buzzfeed website and search for the quiz. Once you find it, click on the start button to begin. The quiz will ask you a series of questions about your hair type, face shape, and personal style. Once you answer all the questions, the quiz will provide you with a result, indicating whether or not a short hairstyle would suit you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for taking the 'would I look good with short hair quiz Buzzfeed':

  1. Head to the Buzzfeed website and search for the quiz.
  2. Click on the start button to begin the quiz.
  3. Answer all the questions honestly.
  4. Submit your answers and wait for the result.

Tips for Deciding on a Short Hairstyle

If the 'would I look good with short hair quiz Buzzfeed' result indicates that a shorter hairstyle would suit you, here are some tips to help you decide on the best style:

  • Consider your face shape - some styles suit certain face shapes better than others.
  • Think about your hair texture - some styles require more maintenance than others.
  • Look for inspiration - browse social media and magazines for ideas and inspiration.
  • Consult with a hairstylist - they can provide advice on what styles would suit you best.

FAQ

What if the quiz result indicates that short hair wouldn't suit me?

Don't worry! The quiz is just a guide, and ultimately the decision is up to you. If you still want to try a shorter hairstyle, go for it!

I'm nervous about cutting my hair short. What should I do?

It's normal to feel nervous about such a big change. Consider starting with a slightly shorter style and gradually working your way up to a shorter cut.

How can I take care of my short hair?

Short hair requires less maintenance than longer hair, but it still needs regular trims to keep it looking its best. Be sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type and consider using styling products to add texture and volume.

Hair Care Tips

Whether you have long or short hair, taking care of your locks is important. Here are some general hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a suitable shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

Overall, the 'would I look good with short hair quiz Buzzfeed' is a fun tool to help you decide on a shorter hairstyle. Remember, the decision is ultimately up to you, so trust your instincts and choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
