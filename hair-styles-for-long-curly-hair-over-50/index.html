Hair Styles For Long Curly Hair Over 50

Written by Petter Jun 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have long curly hair and are over 50, you might be looking for some new hair styles to try out. There are plenty of options available, from classic cuts to trendy updos. In this article, we'll go over some popular hair styles for long curly hair over 50, as well as tips for caring for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

As we age, our hair can become thinner and lose elasticity, which can make it more difficult to style. However, with the right techniques and products, you can still achieve a beautiful, healthy head of long curly hair.

How To

The first step in finding a hair style that works for you is to consider your face shape and hair texture. Oval faces tend to work well with most hair styles, while round faces look best with longer hair and layers. If you have thick hair, you might want to consider a layered cut to add movement and prevent your hair from looking too heavy.

Popular Hair Styles for Long Curly Hair Over 50

Here are some popular hair styles for long curly hair over 50:

Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great option for thick, curly hair. It adds movement and texture, while also preventing your hair from looking too heavy. You can choose to have long layers or shorter layers, depending on the look you're going for.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to frame your face and add movement to your hair. They're also a great option if you're looking to add some volume to your hair.

Updo

If you're looking for a more formal hair style, an updo is a great option. You can choose to have a messy bun, a sleek chignon, or a braided updo.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your long curly hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. This will help to hydrate and define your curls.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product, such as a curl cream or mousse, to your damp hair. This will help to define your curls and prevent frizz.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to maintain your curls' shape and prevent frizz.

Step 4: Style

Once your hair is dry, you can style it using a curling iron or other styling tools. You can also opt for a more natural look by scrunching your hair with your hands.

Tips

Here are some tips for caring for your long curly hair:

  • Use products designed for curly hair to help define and hydrate your curls.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair long if I'm over 50?

A: Yes, you can definitely still wear your hair long if you're over 50. However, you might want to consider adding layers or side-swept bangs to add movement and prevent your hair from looking too heavy.

Q: How often should I wash my long curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or exercise frequently, you might want to wash your hair every other day. If you have drier hair or don't sweat as much, you can probably get away with washing your hair once or twice a week.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful long curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use products designed for curly hair to help define and hydrate your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat styling tools by using a heat protectant spray.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

With these tips and hair styles for long curly hair over 50, you can rock your beautiful curls with confidence.
