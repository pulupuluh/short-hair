How To Hide Grey Hair At Temples Tips And Tricks

Written by William May 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you have grey hair at your temples that you want to hide? You're not alone! Many people experience grey hair at their temples as they age. Fortunately, there are several ways to conceal grey hair and restore your youthful appearance. In this article, we'll show you how to hide grey hair at temples with easy and effective methods.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Hide Grey Hair at Temples
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Hide Grey Hair at Temples
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Grey hair is a natural part of aging, and it can be especially noticeable at the temples. Grey hair is caused by a decrease in melanin production, which gives hair its color. While many people embrace their grey hair, others prefer to hide it. There are several ways to conceal grey hair at temples, including hair dye, root touch-up products, and styling techniques.

How to Hide Grey Hair at Temples

One of the most effective ways to hide grey hair at temples is to dye your hair. You can use permanent hair dye, semi-permanent hair dye, or temporary hair dye. Permanent hair dye lasts the longest but requires more maintenance. Semi-permanent hair dye lasts for several weeks and fades gradually. Temporary hair dye washes out after one or two washes.

You can also use root touch-up products to conceal grey hair at temples. Root touch-up products come in the form of sprays, powders, and pens. They can be used to cover grey roots between coloring sessions.

Another way to hide grey hair at temples is to use styling techniques. You can create a side part or style your hair in layers to conceal grey hair. You can also use a hair accessory, such as a headband or scarf, to cover grey hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to hide grey hair at temples:

  1. Choose a hair dye that matches your natural hair color.
  2. Follow the instructions on the hair dye package to prepare the dye.
  3. Apply the hair dye to your hair, focusing on the temples.
  4. Wait for the recommended amount of time before rinsing the dye out.
  5. If using root touch-up products, choose a product that matches your natural hair color.
  6. Apply the root touch-up product to your grey roots.
  7. Blend the root touch-up product with your natural hair color.
  8. If using styling techniques, part your hair to conceal grey hair at temples.
  9. Style your hair in layers to conceal grey hair at temples.
  10. Use a hair accessory to cover grey hair at temples.

Tips to Hide Grey Hair at Temples

Here are some tips to help you hide grey hair at temples:

  • Choose a hair dye that matches your natural hair color.
  • Use root touch-up products to cover grey roots between coloring sessions.
  • Style your hair in layers to conceal grey hair.
  • Use a hair accessory, such as a headband or scarf, to cover grey hair.
  • Try different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Can I dye my hair at home to hide grey hair at temples?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home to hide grey hair at temples. Just be sure to follow the instructions on the hair dye package carefully.

What is the best hair dye to hide grey hair at temples?

The best hair dye to hide grey hair at temples is one that matches your natural hair color and provides full coverage.

How often should I touch up my roots to hide grey hair at temples?

You should touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to hide grey hair at temples.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

In conclusion, there are several ways to hide grey hair at temples, including hair dye, root touch-up products, and styling techniques. Choose the method that works best for you and remember to follow proper hair care practices to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
