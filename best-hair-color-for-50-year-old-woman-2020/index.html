Best Hair Color For 50 Year Old Woman 2020 A Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 16, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you a 50 year old woman looking to switch up your hair color in 2020? With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one for you. In this guide, we’ll go over some of the most popular hair colors for 50 year old women and provide tips on how to maintain them.

Description

As we age, our hair naturally loses pigment and becomes thinner. This means that the hair color we may have rocked in our 20s and 30s may no longer be the best option for us. However, that doesn’t mean we can’t have fun with our hair! There are plenty of hair colors that can complement our skin tones and make us feel confident and beautiful.

How to Choose the Best Hair Color for 50 Year Old Woman 2020

When choosing a hair color, it’s important to consider your skin tone, natural hair color, and personal style. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone. If you have warm undertones (yellow or peach), opt for warmer hair colors like honey blonde or copper. If you have cool undertones (pink or blue), try cooler hair colors like ash blonde or platinum.

Think About Your Natural Hair Color

Some hair colors require more maintenance than others. If you have naturally dark hair, you may want to avoid going too light as it can be damaging to your hair. On the other hand, if you have naturally lighter hair, going darker can be a great option.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose a hair color that fits your lifestyle. If you’re someone who is always on-the-go, you may want to opt for a low maintenance hair color that doesn’t require frequent touch ups.

Step by Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair

While it’s always recommended to visit a professional hairstylist, you can also color your hair at home with the right tools and products. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Choose Your Color

Pick a hair color that complements your skin tone and natural hair color. Make sure to read the instructions on the box carefully.

Step 2: Do a Strand Test

Before applying the hair dye all over, do a strand test to make sure you’re happy with the color. Apply a small amount of hair dye to a section of hair and wait the recommended time before washing it out.

Step 3: Get Your Tools Ready

You’ll need gloves, a mixing bowl, a brush, and a towel to protect your clothing.

Step 4: Apply the Hair Dye

Divide your hair into sections and apply the hair dye to each section. Make sure to follow the instructions on the box carefully and leave the hair dye on for the recommended time.

Step 5: Rinse and Condition

Once the time is up, rinse your hair thoroughly with water and apply the conditioner that came with the hair dye. Leave it on for the recommended time before rinsing it out.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

To keep your hair color looking fresh and vibrant, here are some tips:

Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Regular shampoos and conditioners can strip away color, so it’s important to use products that are specifically designed for color-treated hair.

Avoid Washing Your Hair Too Often

Washing your hair too often can cause your hair color to fade. Try to wash your hair every other day or use dry shampoo in between washes.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling wands can damage your hair and cause your color to fade. If you must style your hair, use a heat protectant spray and lower the heat setting.

Get Regular Touch-Ups

To keep your hair color looking fresh, schedule regular touch-ups with your hairstylist.

FAQ

What is the best hair color for 50 year old women?

The best hair color for 50 year old women depends on skin tone, natural hair color, and personal style. Some popular options include honey blonde, copper, ash blonde, and platinum.

Can I color my hair at home?

Yes, you can color your hair at home with the right tools and products. However, it’s always recommended to visit a professional hairstylist to avoid any damage to your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

Washing your hair too often can cause your hair color to fade. Try to wash your hair every other day or use dry shampoo in between washes.

Hair Care Tips for 50 Year Old Women

As we age, our hair requires extra care and attention. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage.

Avoid Over-Styling

Over-styling your hair can cause damage and breakage. Try to let your hair air dry and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hair color for you and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant. Don't be afraid to switch it up and have fun with your hair!
