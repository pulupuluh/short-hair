How To Use Hair Texturizing Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Feb 20, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your hair feeling flat and lifeless? Do you want to add some volume and texture to your locks? Look no further than hair texturizing scissors. These specialized scissors can help you achieve a layered, trendy look without having to visit a salon. In this article, we’ll walk you through how to use hair texturizing scissors at home.

How to Use Hair Texturizing Scissors

Before we get started, let’s talk about what hair texturizing scissors are and how they work. These scissors have teeth-like notches on one or both blades, which help to remove hair in a more subtle way than traditional haircutting scissors. This creates a softer, more natural look.

Now, onto the steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to see where you need to create layers or remove bulk.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Grab a section of hair at the top of your head and clip the rest of your hair away. This will help you see what you’re doing and prevent you from accidentally cutting too much hair at once.

Step 3: Hold the Scissors Correctly

Hold the texturizing scissors at a 45-degree angle and point them downward. This will help you achieve a more natural-looking cut. Make sure the teeth are facing up towards the ceiling.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Starting at the bottom of your section, take small, vertical snips. Work your way up to the top of the section, taking care not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

When you’ve finished cutting, use a comb to blend the layers together. This will help create a seamless, natural look.

Tips for Using Hair Texturizing Scissors

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you use hair texturizing scissors:

  • Start with small sections of hair, especially if you’re new to using texturizing scissors.
  • Don’t cut too much hair at once. It’s better to take your time and go slowly.
  • Use a comb to blend the layers together and create a natural look.
  • Always cut at a 45-degree angle to achieve the best results.
  • Don’t forget to trim your hair regularly to maintain your new, textured look.

FAQs

Do I need to be a professional hairstylist to use hair texturizing scissors?

No, anyone can use hair texturizing scissors. Just make sure to go slowly and start with small sections of hair.

Can I use hair texturizing scissors on any hair type?

Yes, hair texturizing scissors can be used on any hair type. Just make sure to use them correctly to achieve the desired look.

Hair Care

After using hair texturizing scissors, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and texture. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools like curling irons or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to give your hair some extra nourishment.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a trendy, textured look from the comfort of your own home. Happy cutting!
