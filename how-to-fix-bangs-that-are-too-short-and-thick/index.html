How To Fix Bangs That Are Too Short And Thick

Written by Petter Apr 28, 2023 ¬∑ 7 min read

If you have recently cut your bangs too short or you have naturally thick hair, you may be struggling with how to style them. Don't worry, you're not alone! In this article, we will go over how to fix bangs that are too short and thick. We'll cover everything from how to style them, to tips on how to care for your bangs.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Fix Bangs that are Too Short and Thick
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Bangs

How to Fix Bangs that are Too Short and Thick

One of the most important things you can do when styling bangs that are too short and thick is to use the right tools. A good quality hair dryer, flat iron, and round brush are essential for achieving the perfect style. Here's how to fix bangs that are too short and thick:

Step by Step Guide:

Step 1: Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume to your hair and make it easier to style your bangs.

Step 2: Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. This will help to lift your hair at the roots and add volume to your bangs. Use the brush to pull your bangs forward and down, using the heat from the hair dryer to set them in place.

Step 3: Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz in your bangs. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using a comb to help guide the hair through the flat iron.

Step 4: Apply a styling product, such as a hair serum or wax, to your bangs. This will help to add shine and hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Bangs:

Tip 1: Use a little bit of hairspray to hold your bangs in place. This will help to keep them from falling flat or getting in your eyes.

Tip 2: Experiment with different styles, such as a side-swept bang or a wispy fringe. This will help you find the perfect look for your face shape and hair type.

Tip 3: Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for help. They can provide you with tips and tricks for styling your bangs, as well as recommend the best products for your hair type.

FAQs:

Q: How long does it take for bangs to grow out?

A: It can take anywhere from 4-6 months for bangs to grow out completely. In the meantime, try styling them in different ways to make them less noticeable.

Q: Can I cut my own bangs?

A: While it is possible to cut your own bangs, it is generally recommended that you leave it to a professional hairstylist. They have the skills and experience needed to create a flattering and natural-looking style.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs:

Tip 1: Use a heat protectant spray before using any heated styling tools. This will help to protect your hair from damage and breakage.

Tip 2: Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more prone to breakage. Instead, try washing your hair every other day or every few days.

Tip 3: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished. This will help to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to fix bangs that are too short and thick, you can confidently rock your new style! Remember to experiment with different looks and products to find the perfect style for you. With a little bit of practice and patience, you'll be able to achieve the perfect bangs in no time.
