Are Pixie Cuts Hard To Style Tips For Easy Maintenance And Styling

Written by Oliver Sep 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're considering getting a pixie cut, you might be wondering if they are hard to style. While they may require some extra effort compared to longer hair, pixie cuts can actually be quite easy to style with the right techniques and products. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about styling and maintaining a pixie cut.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style a Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Pixie Cut
  • Tips for Maintaining and Styling a Pixie Cut

Description

A pixie cut is a short haircut that typically features short layers and a tapered back. This style can be worn in a variety of ways, from a messy, textured look to a sleek and polished style. While some people may find it challenging to style a pixie cut, with the right techniques and products, you can create a variety of looks that are easy to maintain.

How to Style a Pixie Cut

There are many ways to style a pixie cut, depending on your personal style and preferences. Some of the most popular styling techniques include:

  • Using a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to your hair
  • Blow-drying your hair with a round brush to create volume and shape
  • Creating a side-part or adding bangs to change up your look
  • Using a flat iron to create a sleek, polished style
  • Adding accessories, such as headbands or barrettes, to add interest to your hairstyle

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Pixie Cut

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling a pixie cut:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are designed for short hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently, being careful not to rub or pull on your hair.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that need extra hold or texture.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  5. Create a side-part or add bangs to change up your look.
  6. Use a flat iron to create a sleek, polished style.
  7. Add accessories, such as headbands or barrettes, to add interest to your hairstyle.

Tips for Maintaining and Styling a Pixie Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining and styling a pixie cut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Invest in high-quality styling products that are designed for short hair.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques and products to find what works best for you.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Q: Are pixie cuts hard to style?

A: While they may require some extra effort compared to longer hair, pixie cuts can be quite easy to style with the right techniques and products.

Q: How often should I get a trim with a pixie cut?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Q: Can I still wear hair accessories with a pixie cut?

A: Yes, you can still wear hair accessories with a pixie cut! Headbands, barrettes, and other accessories can add interest and style to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining a healthy and stylish pixie cut:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with products that are designed for short hair.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or conditioners that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush or comb that is designed for short hair.
  • Avoid using elastic hair ties that can cause breakage or damage to your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily maintain and style your pixie cut for a chic and effortless look. Whether you prefer a messy, textured style or a sleek and polished look, there are many ways to create a flattering and stylish pixie cut. So go ahead and take the plunge - you won't regret it!
