How To Make Your Face Look Heart Shaped

Written by Aprilia Aug 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to create a more defined and feminine facial shape, making your face look heart shaped is a great way to achieve that. This article will give you a step-by-step guide on how to make your face look heart shaped, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice. By following these simple steps, you'll be able to create the perfect heart-shaped face in no time!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Contour Your Cheeks
  • Step 2: Highlight Your Forehead
  • Step 3: Define Your Jawline
  • Step 4: Accentuate Your Lips
  • Step 5: Use the Right Haircut
  • Tips for Making Your Face Look Heart Shaped
  • FAQs About Making Your Face Look Heart Shaped
  • Hair Care Tips for Heart-Shaped Faces

Step 1: Contour Your Cheeks

The first step in making your face look heart shaped is to contour your cheeks. Use a contouring powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone. Apply the contouring product to the hollows of your cheeks, blending it out towards your temples. This will create the illusion of higher cheekbones and a more defined jawline.

Step 2: Highlight Your Forehead

Next, use a highlighting powder or cream to accentuate your forehead. Apply the highlighter to the center of your forehead, blending it out towards your temples. This will help to balance out your facial features and create a more heart-shaped face.

Step 3: Define Your Jawline

To further define your jawline, use a contouring product to shade the area just below your jawbone. This will help to create a more chiseled and defined jawline, which is a key feature of a heart-shaped face.

Step 4: Accentuate Your Lips

One of the most important features of a heart-shaped face is a full and defined set of lips. Use a lip liner to outline your lips and fill them in with a lipstick or gloss that complements your skin tone. This will help to create a more defined and feminine look.

Step 5: Use the Right Haircut

The right haircut can also help to make your face look heart shaped. Consider a haircut with layers that fall around your cheekbones and jawline, which will help to accentuate those areas. A side-swept fringe can also help to soften your features and create a more feminine look.

Tips for Making Your Face Look Heart Shaped

  • Use a light hand when applying your makeup, as too much can make your face look overly done.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and makeup looks to find what works best for you.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a healthy diet to maintain a clear and glowing complexion.

FAQs About Making Your Face Look Heart Shaped

Q. Can I make my face look heart shaped without makeup?

A. Yes, you can make your face look heart shaped by using the right haircut and styling techniques. Consider a haircut with layers around your cheekbones and jawline, and use a side-swept fringe to soften your features.

Q. What kind of makeup should I use to make my face look heart shaped?

A. Use a contouring powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone to contour your cheeks and define your jawline. Use a highlighting powder or cream to accentuate your forehead, and a lip liner and lipstick or gloss to accentuate your lips.

Hair Care Tips for Heart-Shaped Faces

  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head, as this can make your face look top-heavy.
  • Consider a haircut with layers around your cheekbones and jawline to accentuate those areas.
  • Use a side-swept fringe to soften your features and create a more feminine look.

By following these simple steps, tips, and hair care advice, you'll be able to make your face look heart shaped in no time. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and makeup looks to find what works best for you, and always stay hydrated and eat a healthy diet to maintain a clear and glowing complexion.
