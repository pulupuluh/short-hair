Do Dreads Make You Look Better

Description

Dreads, or dreadlocks, have been around for centuries and are a popular hairstyle choice for many people. They are formed by allowing hair to grow and mat together into thick, rope-like strands. While some people may think that dreads are not a good look, others believe that they can actually make you look better. In this article, we will explore whether or not dreads make you look better and provide tips on how to take care of them.

How To

If you are interested in getting dreads, there are a few different methods to choose from:

1. Natural Method

The natural method involves letting your hair grow out and allowing it to twist and mat on its own. This method takes the longest, but it is also the healthiest for your hair.

2. Backcombing Method

The backcombing method involves sectioning your hair and backcombing each section to create knots. This method is quicker than the natural method, but it can be damaging to your hair if not done properly.

3. Twist and Rip Method

The twist and rip method involves sectioning your hair and twisting each section before ripping it apart. This method is also quicker than the natural method, but it can cause some damage to your hair.

Step By Step

If you decide to get dreads, here is a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

1. Wash Your Hair

Before starting the process, make sure your hair is clean and free of any products.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties.

3. Choose Your Method

Choose the method you want to use and start backcombing or twisting and ripping each section.

4. Maintenance

Maintain your dreads by washing them regularly and using a residue-free shampoo. You can also use a crochet hook to tighten any loose knots.

Tips

Here are some tips to consider if you are thinking about getting dreads:

  • Do your research on the different methods and choose the one that is best for your hair type and lifestyle.
  • Be patient. Dreads take time to form and may not look how you want them to right away.
  • Take care of your dreads by washing them regularly and using a residue-free shampoo.
  • Consider using a crochet hook to tighten any loose knots.

FAQ

Q: Will dreads make me look better?

A: This is subjective and depends on personal preference. Some people believe that dreads can make you look better, while others do not.

Q: Can anyone get dreads?

A: Yes, anyone can get dreads regardless of hair type or texture.

Q: How long does it take to get dreads?

A: This depends on the method you choose and how fast your hair grows. The natural method can take several months to a year, while the backcombing and twist and rip methods are quicker.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your dreads to prevent them from becoming damaged or dirty. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy dreads:

  • Wash your dreads regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Use a crochet hook to tighten any loose knots.
  • Avoid using products that contain heavy oils or waxes.
  • Avoid using a hair dryer on high heat.
  • Protect your dreads from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf.

In conclusion, whether or not dreads make you look better is a personal preference. If you are interested in getting dreads, do your research and choose the method that is best for you. Take care of your dreads by washing them regularly and using a residue-free shampoo to maintain healthy, beautiful locks.
