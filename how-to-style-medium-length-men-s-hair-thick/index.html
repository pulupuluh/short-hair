How To Style Medium Length Men s Hair Thick A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Jun 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Medium length hair is a popular choice for men, but it can be challenging to style if you have thick hair. Thick hair can be unruly, making it difficult to manage and style. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a stylish and manageable look that will turn heads. In this article, we will explore how to style medium length men's hair thick, step-by-step, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Style Medium Length Men's Hair Thick

Styling medium length men's hair thick requires the right products, tools, and techniques. Here are some tips to help you achieve a stylish and manageable look:

Choose the Right Haircut

The first step to styling medium length men's hair thick is to choose the right haircut. A layered haircut can help to thin out thick hair and make it more manageable. You can also consider getting a textured cut, which can add volume and movement to your hair.

Use the Right Products

Using the right products is essential for styling medium length men's hair thick. A lightweight styling cream or pomade can help to control frizz and add definition to your hair. You can also use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help to add volume and shape to your hair. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots while blow drying. You can also use a diffuser attachment to add texture and definition to your curls.

Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it using your preferred technique. You can use a comb or your fingers to create a messy, textured look. You can also use a hair straightener or curling iron to create defined waves or curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling medium length men's hair thick:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a lightweight styling cream or pomade.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to lift your hair at the roots.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to create a messy, textured look.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray or texturizing spray.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Men's Hair Thick

Here are some additional tips for styling medium length men's hair thick:

  • Avoid using heavy products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the look that works best for you.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of your hair.

FAQs

How often should I wash my hair?

It is recommended to wash your hair every other day or every three days, depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

Can I use a hair straightener or curling iron on thick hair?

Yes, you can use a hair straightener or curling iron on thick hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

What is the best haircut for thick hair?

A layered or textured haircut is best for thick hair, as it can help to thin out the hair and make it more manageable.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Here are some tips for caring for thick hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping the natural oils from your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can style your medium length men's hair thick and achieve a look that is both stylish and manageable. Experiment with different products and hairstyles to find the look that works best for you, and don't forget to take care of your hair with regular haircuts and a good hair care routine.
