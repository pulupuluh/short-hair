2021 Long Hairstyles For Over 50 A Guide To Rocking Your Locks

Written by Robby Mar 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for some new hairstyle inspiration? If you're over 50 and have long hair, you're in luck! Long hair can be incredibly versatile, and there are so many ways to style it. In this article, we'll be sharing some of the best 2021 long hairstyles for over 50. Whether you want to keep your hair straight, add some waves, or try out a new updo, we've got you covered.

Description

As we age, our hair may become thinner or lose its natural shine. But that doesn't mean we can't still rock a fabulous hairstyle! Long hair can be a great option for women over 50, as it can help to frame the face and create a youthful look. Plus, there are so many different ways to style it, from braids and updos to loose waves and straight locks.

How to

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle in 2021, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your hair texture and face shape. Certain styles may work better for you depending on these factors. Next, think about how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling your hair. Some styles may require more maintenance than others. Finally, don't be afraid to experiment! Trying out a new hairstyle can be a fun and exciting way to refresh your look.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of our favorite long hairstyles for women over 50:

1. Loose Waves

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray. Using a curling iron or wand, curl small sections of hair away from your face. Once all sections are curled, run your fingers through your hair to create loose waves. Finish with a light-hold hairspray.

2. Braided Updo

Start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail. Divide the ponytail into three sections and braid each section. Cross the braids over each other and secure with bobby pins. Repeat with any remaining hair. Finish with a strong-hold hairspray.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

Start by parting your hair down the middle. Take a small section of hair from each side of your head and tie them together at the back of your head. Repeat with another section of hair underneath the first. Continue until you reach the desired amount of hair tied back. Finish with a light-hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your long hair:

- Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools. - Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, barrettes, and scarves. - Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

FAQ

Q: What are some good long hairstyles for fine hair?

A: Women with fine hair may want to consider styles that add volume and texture, such as loose waves or layered cuts.

Q: Can I still wear my hair long after 50?

A: Absolutely! There's no age limit on long hair. As long as you feel confident and comfortable, you can rock any hairstyle you like.

Hair Care

Keeping your long hair healthy and shiny is key to achieving any hairstyle. Here are some tips for taking care of your locks:

- Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils. - Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage. - Avoid using hot tools every day, as this can lead to breakage and split ends. - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush. - Consider using a silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage while you sleep.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you'll be able to rock your long hair in style in 2021 and beyond.
