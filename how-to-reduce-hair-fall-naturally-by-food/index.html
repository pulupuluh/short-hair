How To Reduce Hair Fall Naturally By Food

Written by Luffy Apr 25, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of seeing your hair on your pillow, brush, or shower drain? Hair fall is a common problem faced by many people, and it can be caused by stress, pollution, genetics, hormonal changes, and poor nutrition. While there are many treatments available to reduce hair fall, the best way to tackle the problem is by eating the right foods. In this article, we will discuss how to reduce hair fall naturally by food.

Table of Content

  1. How to Reduce Hair Fall Naturally by Food
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips to Reduce Hair Fall Naturally by Food
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Reduce Hair Fall Naturally by Food

To reduce hair fall naturally, it is essential to consume foods rich in vitamins, minerals, and proteins. Here are some foods that can help reduce hair fall:

1. Eggs

Eggs are an excellent source of protein, which is essential for hair growth. They also contain biotin, which helps strengthen hair and prevent hair fall. You can eat boiled, scrambled, or poached eggs to get the benefits.

2. Spinach

Spinach is rich in iron, which helps improve blood circulation and supply oxygen to hair follicles. It also contains vitamins A and C, which help produce sebum, a natural hair conditioner. You can add spinach to salads, smoothies, or sautéed vegetables.

3. Nuts

Nuts like almonds, walnuts, and cashews are rich in omega-3 fatty acids, which help nourish hair and prevent hair fall. They also contain biotin, vitamin E, and zinc, which promote hair growth. You can eat them as a snack or add them to salads or smoothies.

4. Sweet Potatoes

Sweet potatoes are rich in beta-carotene, which the body converts to vitamin A. Vitamin A helps promote hair growth and prevent hair fall. You can bake, roast, or steam sweet potatoes and eat them as a side dish or snack.

5. Lentils

Lentils are an excellent source of protein, iron, and biotin, which help prevent hair fall and promote hair growth. You can add lentils to soups, stews, or salads.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to reduce hair fall naturally by food:

Step 1: Incorporate protein-rich foods like eggs, chicken, fish, and lentils into your diet.

Step 2: Eat foods rich in iron like spinach, kale, broccoli, and sweet potatoes.

Step 3: Include foods rich in omega-3 fatty acids like nuts, seeds, and fatty fish like salmon.

Step 4: Consume foods rich in vitamins A, C, and E like sweet potatoes, carrots, citrus fruits, and nuts.

Step 5: Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water, coconut water, and fresh fruit juices.

Tips to Reduce Hair Fall Naturally by Food

Here are some tips to reduce hair fall naturally by food:

1. Avoid processed foods, sugary drinks, and alcohol as they can weaken hair and cause hair fall.

2. Incorporate foods rich in antioxidants like berries, dark chocolate, and green tea as they help prevent hair fall.

3. Eat a balanced diet that includes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, and healthy fats.

4. Take supplements like biotin, iron, and vitamin D after consulting with your doctor.

5. Massage your scalp with warm coconut or olive oil to improve blood circulation and nourish hair follicles.

FAQ

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

A. Yes, stress can cause hair fall by disrupting the hair growth cycle.

Q. How long does it take to see results by eating the right foods?

A. It may take a few months to see visible results as hair growth is a slow process.

Q. Can hair fall be prevented by eating the right foods?

A. While eating the right foods can help prevent hair fall, it may not be the only solution. Other factors like genetics, hormonal changes, and lifestyle habits also play a role in hair fall.

Hair Care

Along with eating the right foods, it is essential to take care of your hair to prevent hair fall. Here are some tips:

1. Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools as they can damage hair.

2. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type.

3. Brush your hair gently and avoid pulling or tugging.

4. Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and pollution by wearing a hat or scarf.

5. Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, reducing hair fall naturally by food is a safe and effective way to promote hair growth and prevent hair fall. By incorporating the right foods into your diet and taking care of your hair, you can achieve healthy and luscious locks.
