How To Do A Messy Bun With Thin Hair Step By Step

Written by Luffy Jul 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of trying to do a messy bun with your thin hair and not achieving the desired look? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many women struggle with creating a messy bun with thin hair. But with the right technique and products, you can achieve the perfect messy bun with ease. In this article, we'll walk you through a step-by-step guide on how to do a messy bun with thin hair.

Table of Content

  • Supplies You'll Need
  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Creating the Messy Bun
  • Tips for Maintaining the Messy Bun
  • FAQs

Supplies You'll Need

Before starting, make sure you have the following supplies:

  • Comb
  • Bobby pins
  • Clear elastic bands
  • Hairspray
  • Dry shampoo (optional)

Prepping Your Hair

The key to creating a perfect messy bun with thin hair is to add volume and texture to your hair. Here's how to prep your hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume.
  3. If you have oily hair, apply dry shampoo to your roots to add texture.
  4. Brush your hair and create a side parting (optional).

Creating the Messy Bun

Now that your hair is prepped, it's time to create the messy bun. Follow these steps:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail using a clear elastic band.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band, creating a bun shape.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins, making sure to hide the elastic band.
  4. Pull out some strands of hair from the bun to create the messy look.
  5. Spray with hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Messy Bun

Here are some tips to help you maintain your messy bun throughout the day:

  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
  • Apply a small amount of hairspray to the bun to keep it in place.
  • Recreate the messy look throughout the day by pulling out some strands of hair from the bun.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing a messy bun with thin hair:

Can I do a messy bun with short hair?

Yes, you can! Follow the same steps and create a smaller bun. You can also use hair extensions to add volume.

How can I add more volume to my thin hair?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-dry your hair upside down, and apply dry shampoo to your roots. You can also use hair extensions or a volumizing mousse.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

Here are some tips to help you take care of your thin hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too many styling products.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

And there you have it - a step-by-step guide on how to do a messy bun with thin hair! With the right products and technique, you can achieve the perfect messy bun every time.
