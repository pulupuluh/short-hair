How To Get Curls With Straight Hair Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Apr 13, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're a guy with straight hair who's always dreamed of having luscious curls, you're in luck! With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the curly look you've always wanted. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to get curls with straight hair guys, including step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  • How to Get Curls with Straight Hair Guys: Step-by-Step Guide

  • How to Get Curls with Straight Hair Guys: Hair Care

How to Get Curls with Straight Hair Guys: Overview

Before we dive into the specifics, let's talk about what you'll need to get curls with straight hair guys. Here's a list of the essentials:

  • Curling iron
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Styling mousse or cream
  • Hair spray
  • Wide-tooth comb

With these tools in hand, you'll be well on your way to achieving the curly hair of your dreams.

How to Get Curls with Straight Hair Guys: Step-by-Step Guide

Now, let's get into the nitty-gritty of how to get curls with straight hair guys. Follow these steps for the best results:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, then let it air dry completely. Don't use any styling products yet – you want your hair to be completely dry before you begin curling.

Step 2: Apply heat protectant spray

Spray your hair with heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron. Make sure to distribute the spray evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Divide your hair into sections

Use the wide-tooth comb to divide your hair into small sections. The smaller the sections, the tighter the curls will be.

Step 4: Apply styling mousse or cream

Apply a small amount of styling mousse or cream to each section of hair. This will help hold the curls in place and prevent frizz.

Step 5: Curl your hair

Now it's time to start curling! Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for 10-15 seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process for all sections of hair.

Step 6: Apply hair spray

Once you've finished curling your hair, apply hair spray to hold the curls in place.

How to Get Curls with Straight Hair Guys: Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the best possible results:

  • Use a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline barrel, as these materials distribute heat more evenly and cause less damage to your hair.
  • If you're going for a more natural look, use a larger curling iron barrel for loose waves.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly – this can cause damage and make your curls look unnatural.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

How to Get Curls with Straight Hair Guys: FAQ

Can I get curls with straight hair guys without a curling iron?

While a curling iron is the most effective way to get curls with straight hair guys, you can also try using foam rollers or braiding your hair overnight for a more natural look.

How often should I curl my hair?

Curling your hair too often can cause damage, so it's best to limit it to once or twice a week.

How to Get Curls with Straight Hair Guys: Hair Care

To keep your curls looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause breakage and split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to get curls with straight hair guys in no time. Whether you're going for a natural look or something more dramatic, you'll love the way your new curls make you feel.
