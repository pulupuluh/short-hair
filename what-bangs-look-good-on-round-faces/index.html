What Bangs Look Good On Round Faces

Written by William Apr 18, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you wondering what type of bangs would suit your round face? Bangs are a great way to switch up your hairstyle and add some personality to your look. However, not all bangs are created equal, and not all bangs look good on round faces. In this article, we'll explore what bangs look good on round faces and provide you with some tips on how to style them.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Bangs
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

  • Hair Care Tips for Healthy Bangs

Description

Round faces have soft, curved features, and the width and length are almost equal. The goal is to create an illusion of length and balance out the width of the face. The right bangs can help achieve this by drawing attention away from the width of your face and highlighting your best features.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Round Face

The key to choosing the right bangs for your round face is to find a style that creates an illusion of length. Here are some types of bangs that look good on round faces:

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for round faces. They create a diagonal line that breaks up the roundness of the face and elongates the overall appearance. Make sure the bangs are long enough to reach the cheekbones or jawline and blend seamlessly with the rest of your hair.

Long, Textured Bangs

Long, textured bangs that fall just above the eyebrows are another great option for round faces. The texture adds dimension and movement to the hair, while the length helps to elongate the face. You can style them straight, wavy, or curly.

Asymmetrical Bangs

Asymmetrical bangs are another great way to add length to a round face. These bangs are longer on one side and shorter on the other, creating an asymmetrical shape that draws attention away from the width of the face. They can be styled straight or with a slight wave.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Bangs

Now that you've chosen the right bangs for your face shape, it's time to learn how to style them. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling your bangs. This will help to eliminate any excess oil or product buildup that can weigh down your bangs.

Step 2: Section your bangs

Using a comb, section off your bangs from the rest of your hair. Use hair clips to hold the rest of your hair back.

Step 3: Blow-dry your bangs

Using a round brush, blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall. Make sure to dry them completely to avoid any frizz or flyaways.

Step 4: Style your bangs

Once your bangs are dry, you can style them to your liking. Use a flat iron for a sleek look or a curling iron for a more textured look. You can also use a small amount of styling product to add volume or hold.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Here are some tips to keep your bangs looking great:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh
  • Use dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil between washes
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent oil buildup
  • Blow-dry your bangs in the opposite direction for added volume
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron or curling iron for styling

FAQs about Bangs and Round Faces

Q: Can bangs make my round face look even rounder?

A: Yes, if you choose the wrong type of bangs, they can accentuate the roundness of your face. Stick to side-swept, textured, or asymmetrical bangs to create an illusion of length.

Q: Can I pull off blunt bangs with a round face?

A: Blunt bangs can be challenging to pull off with a round face. However, if you have your heart set on them, make sure they are longer on the sides and blend seamlessly with the rest of your hair.

Q: Do bangs require a lot of maintenance?

A: Bangs do require some maintenance, but it's not too time-consuming. You'll need to trim them every 4-6 weeks and style them daily. However, the payoff is worth it - bangs can transform your look and add some personality to your hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Bangs

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your scalp clean and healthy
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner for your hair type

Now that you know what bangs look good on round faces, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist and try out a new look! Remember, the key is to find a style that elongates your face and highlights your best features. Have fun experimenting and don't be afraid to step out of your comfort zone.
