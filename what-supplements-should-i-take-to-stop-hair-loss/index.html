What Supplements Should I Take To Stop Hair Loss

Written by Luffy Apr 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss and looking for a natural solution? Hair loss can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, stress, and poor nutrition. While there are many products on the market that claim to stop hair loss, some people prefer to take a more natural approach. In this article, we will discuss the best supplements for hair loss.

Biotin

Biotin is a B vitamin that is essential for healthy hair, skin, and nails. It helps to strengthen hair and prevent breakage. Biotin also helps to improve the health of the scalp, which can promote hair growth. You can find biotin in foods such as eggs, nuts, and whole grains, or you can take a supplement.

Vitamin D

Vitamin D is important for many functions in the body, including hair growth. Studies have shown that vitamin D deficiency can lead to hair loss. You can get vitamin D from the sun, but many people don't get enough. You can also find vitamin D in foods such as fatty fish, egg yolks, and fortified dairy products. If you're not getting enough vitamin D, you can take a supplement.

Iron

Iron is essential for the production of hemoglobin, which carries oxygen to the hair follicles. Iron deficiency can lead to hair loss, so it's important to get enough iron in your diet. You can find iron in foods such as red meat, poultry, and leafy green vegetables. If you're not getting enough iron, you can take a supplement.

Zinc

Zinc is important for the health of the hair follicles. It helps to keep the oil glands around the follicles working properly, which can prevent hair loss. Zinc also helps to strengthen hair and prevent breakage. You can find zinc in foods such as oysters, beef, and pumpkin seeds, or you can take a supplement.

Selenium

Selenium is an antioxidant that helps to protect the hair follicles from damage. It also helps to regulate the thyroid gland, which can affect hair growth. You can find selenium in foods such as Brazil nuts, tuna, and whole grains, or you can take a supplement.

Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Omega-3 fatty acids are essential for healthy hair and skin. They help to reduce inflammation, which can contribute to hair loss. Omega-3s also help to keep the scalp healthy, which can promote hair growth. You can find omega-3s in foods such as fatty fish, flaxseeds, and walnuts, or you can take a supplement.

Collagen

Collagen is a protein that is important for the health of the hair and skin. It helps to strengthen hair and prevent breakage. Collagen also helps to keep the scalp healthy, which can promote hair growth. You can find collagen in foods such as bone broth, or you can take a supplement.

Ashwagandha

Ashwagandha is an herb that is known for its stress-reducing properties. Stress can contribute to hair loss, so reducing stress levels can be beneficial. Ashwagandha also helps to regulate hormone levels, which can affect hair growth. You can take ashwagandha in supplement form.

Probiotics

Probiotics are beneficial bacteria that support gut health. A healthy gut can help to improve nutrient absorption, which can benefit hair growth. Probiotics also help to reduce inflammation, which can contribute to hair loss. You can find probiotics in foods such as yogurt and kefir, or you can take a supplement.

Conclusion

There are many supplements that can help to stop hair loss. Biotin, vitamin D, iron, zinc, selenium, omega-3 fatty acids, collagen, ashwagandha, and probiotics are all beneficial for hair health. However, it's important to remember that supplements should be taken in conjunction with a healthy diet and lifestyle. If you're experiencing hair loss, it's important to talk to your doctor to determine the underlying cause and develop a treatment plan.
