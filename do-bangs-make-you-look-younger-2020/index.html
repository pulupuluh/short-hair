Do Bangs Make You Look Younger In 2020

Written by Robby Apr 30, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’re looking for a way to refresh your appearance, bangs are a great option to consider. Not only can they help frame your face and accentuate your features, but they may also make you look younger. In this blog post, we’ll explore whether bangs can truly help you achieve a more youthful appearance in 2020.

How To Get Bangs?

Before we dive into the topic of whether bangs can make you look younger, let’s talk about how you can get them. If you’re considering bangs, you’ll need to decide on the right type of bangs for your face shape and hair type.

You can opt for blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, or curtain bangs, among other styles. Once you’ve decided on the type of bangs you want, you can either cut them yourself or go to a professional hairstylist.

Step By Step Guide

If you’re planning to cut your own bangs, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you:

Step 1:

Start with clean, dry hair.

Step 2:

Use a comb to section off the front part of your hair that you want to cut into bangs.

Step 3:

Decide on the length and thickness of your bangs and use a pair of sharp scissors to cut them.

Step 4:

Trim your bangs until you achieve your desired length and shape.

Tips For Styling Your Bangs

Now that you have your bangs, here are some tips to help you style them:

  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to achieve a smooth, polished look.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs if you want a sleek, modern look.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to your bangs to tame any flyaways.

FAQ About Bangs

Now, let’s answer some frequently asked questions about bangs:

Q: Do bangs make you look younger?

A: Bangs can make you look younger by covering up wrinkles on your forehead and framing your face. However, this effect may vary depending on the type of bangs you choose and your face shape.

Q: How often should you trim your bangs?

A: You should trim your bangs every four to six weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Q: Can you wear bangs with curly hair?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with curly hair, but you should choose a style that complements your curls and doesn’t require too much styling.

Hair Care Tips For Bangs

If you have bangs, it’s important to take good care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips for bangs:

  • Wash your bangs regularly to prevent them from becoming greasy.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling on your bangs to prevent damage and breakage.
  • If you have oily skin, use a blotting paper or dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh throughout the day.

Conclusion

So, do bangs make you look younger? The answer is yes, they can. Bangs can help to give you a more youthful appearance by framing your face and covering up wrinkles on your forehead. However, it’s important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape and hair type, and to take good care of them to keep them looking their best.

If you’re thinking of getting bangs, be sure to consult with a professional hairstylist to help you choose the perfect style for you. With the right style and care, you can enjoy the many benefits of having bangs, including a more youthful appearance.
