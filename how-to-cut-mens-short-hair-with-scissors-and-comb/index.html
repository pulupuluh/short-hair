How To Cut Men s Short Hair With Scissors And Comb

Written by Aprilia Jun 27, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you looking for a way to cut your own hair? Do you want to save money by not going to the barbershop? Cutting your own hair can be a bit intimidating, but with some practice and the right tools, it can be done. In this article, we will show you how to cut men's short hair with scissors and comb.

  • How to Cut Men's Short Hair with Scissors and Comb
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Cutting men's short hair with scissors and comb is a simple and straightforward process. You will need a good pair of scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to hold the hair in place. This method is perfect for men who want a quick and easy haircut without having to use clippers or other complicated tools.

How to Cut Men's Short Hair with Scissors and Comb

The first step to cutting men's short hair with scissors and comb is to wash and dry the hair. This will help you see the natural texture and shape of the hair. Make sure the hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Next, comb the hair straight down over the forehead and cut a straight line across the bangs. This will be your guide for the rest of the haircut.

Using your comb, separate a section of hair from the top of the head. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it in a straight line, following the guide you created with the bangs. Repeat this process for the rest of the hair on the top of the head.

Next, move to the sides of the head. Use your comb to separate a section of hair from the side and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair in a straight line, following the guide you created with the top of the head. Repeat this process for the other side of the head.

Finally, move to the back of the head. Use your comb to separate a section of hair from the back and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair in a straight line, following the guide you created with the top and sides of the head. Continue this process until all of the hair on the back of the head has been cut.

Step-by-Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to cutting men's short hair with scissors and comb:

  1. Wash and dry the hair.
  2. Comb the hair straight down over the forehead and cut a straight line across the bangs.
  3. Using your comb, separate a section of hair from the top of the head. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it in a straight line, following the guide you created with the bangs.
  4. Repeat step 3 for the rest of the hair on the top of the head.
  5. Move to the sides of the head. Use your comb to separate a section of hair from the side and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair in a straight line, following the guide you created with the top of the head.
  6. Repeat step 5 for the other side of the head.
  7. Move to the back of the head. Use your comb to separate a section of hair from the back and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair in a straight line, following the guide you created with the top and sides of the head.
  8. Continue this process until all of the hair on the back of the head has been cut.
  9. Use your scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven spots.
  10. Style the hair as desired.

Tips

  • Invest in a good pair of scissors. Cheap scissors can result in uneven cuts and damaged hair.
  • Use hair clips to hold the hair in place while you cut. This will make the process easier and prevent any mistakes.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting hair can be a slow process, but it's worth it to get a good result.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's short hair with scissors and comb:

  • Can I cut my own hair? Yes, you can cut your own hair with scissors and comb.
  • Do I need any special tools? You will need a good pair of scissors, a comb, and some hair clips.
  • How long does it take to cut hair with scissors and comb? Cutting hair with scissors and comb can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the length of the hair and the style you want to achieve.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair with scissors and comb, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and styled. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape.

Now that you know how to cut men's short hair with scissors and comb, you can save money and get a great haircut at home. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and be patient. Happy cutting!
