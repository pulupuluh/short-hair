How To Style Big Pearl Hair Clips A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Mar 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Big pearl hair clips have taken the fashion world by storm, and for good reason. They add an instant touch of elegance and sophistication to any outfit, and they're incredibly versatile. Whether you're going for a casual look or a more formal one, big pearl hair clips can be the perfect accessory. In this guide, we'll show you how to style big pearl hair clips like a pro, so you can look your best no matter where you go.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Big Pearl Hair Clips
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Big Pearl Hair Clips
  • FAQs

How to Style Big Pearl Hair Clips

Big pearl hair clips can be styled in many different ways, depending on the occasion and your personal preference. Here are some ideas to get you started:

1. Half-Up Hairstyle

A half-up hairstyle is a classic look that can be made even more chic with the addition of a big pearl hair clip. Simply pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a clip, leaving the bottom half down. This look is perfect for a date night or a fancy dinner.

2. Low Ponytail

If you're looking for a simple yet elegant hairstyle, a low ponytail with a big pearl hair clip is the way to go. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band. Then, clip a big pearl hair clip over the elastic band to hide it. This look is perfect for a day at the office or a brunch with friends.

3. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a great hairstyle option for those days when you don't want to spend too much time on your hair. Simply gather your hair into a loose bun and secure it with an elastic band. Then, clip a big pearl hair clip over the elastic band to add some glamour. This look is perfect for a casual day out or a weekend getaway.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have some hairstyle ideas, here's a step-by-step guide on how to style big pearl hair clips:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Big pearl hair clips work best on clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose the hairstyle you want to create. You can use the ideas listed above or come up with your own.
  3. Section your hair as needed for the hairstyle you've chosen.
  4. Place the big pearl hair clip where you want it to go. Make sure it's secure and won't fall out.
  5. Adjust your hairstyle as needed to make sure the big pearl hair clip is visible.
  6. Finish with hairspray or other styling products, if desired.

Tips for Styling Big Pearl Hair Clips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling big pearl hair clips:

  • Choose a big pearl hair clip that matches your outfit. If you're wearing a black dress, for example, a black big pearl hair clip would be a great choice.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match different styles of big pearl hair clips. You can create a unique look by combining different sizes and shapes.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to see which ones work best with big pearl hair clips.
  • Use bobby pins to secure the big pearl hair clip in place, if needed.

FAQs

Can big pearl hair clips be worn with short hair?

Yes, big pearl hair clips can be worn with short hair. Simply clip the hair clip onto a small section of hair and style as desired.

Can big pearl hair clips be worn with curly hair?

Yes, big pearl hair clips can be worn with curly hair. Simply style your hair as desired and then clip the hair clip onto a small section of hair.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep it looking healthy.

With these tips and ideas, you can style big pearl hair clips like a pro and look your best no matter where you go. So go ahead and add some glamour to your hair with a big pearl hair clip today!
