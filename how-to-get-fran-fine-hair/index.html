How To Get Fran Fine Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a fan of the classic sitcom The Nanny, you'll remember Fran Fine's iconic hairstyle. Her voluminous, wavy locks were the envy of many women in the '90s, and they still are today. If you want to achieve the same look, you're in luck. In this article, we'll show you how to get Fran Fine hair in just a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Fran Fine Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Fran Fine Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products for Fran Fine Hair

How to Get Fran Fine Hair

Fran Fine's signature hairstyle is all about volume and texture. To achieve this look, you'll need to start with the right cut and style, then add some waves and texture. Here's how:

1. Get the Right Cut

The first step to achieving Fran Fine hair is to get the right cut. You'll want to ask your stylist for long layers that frame your face and add volume to your hair. If you have thin hair, you can also ask for some soft, face-framing layers to add dimension.

2. Add Some Waves

Once you have the right cut, you can start adding some waves to your hair. You can use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves throughout your hair. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools to protect your hair from damage.

3. Create Texture

To create the signature texture of Fran Fine hair, you'll want to use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. Spray it throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths, and use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, here's a step-by-step guide to getting Fran Fine hair:

Step 1: Wash and Blow-Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and blow-drying your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some extra volume to your hair.

Step 2: Add Some Waves

Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves throughout your hair. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.

Step 3: Create Texture

Spray a texturizing spray or dry shampoo throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths. Use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair to create texture.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your waves and texture in place.

Tips for Maintaining Fran Fine Hair

Now that you have Fran Fine hair, you'll want to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add extra volume to your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look limp.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to absorb excess oil and add texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it look frizzy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting Fran Fine hair:

What kind of hair works best for Fran Fine hair?

Any hair type can achieve Fran Fine hair with the right cut and styling techniques. However, if you have thin or fine hair, you may need to use more volumizing products to add extra volume and texture to your hair.

Can I achieve Fran Fine hair without using hot tools?

Yes, you can achieve Fran Fine hair without using hot tools. You can create loose waves by braiding your hair when it's wet and letting it air dry. You can also use foam rollers to create curls without heat.

Hair Care Products for Fran Fine Hair

If you want to keep your Fran Fine hair looking its best, you'll want to use the right products. Here are some hair care products that work well for this hairstyle:

  • Volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Texturizing spray or dry shampoo
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Light hold hairspray

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the iconic Fran Fine hair that you've always wanted. Whether you're going for a casual, everyday look or a more formal style, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and beautiful.
