What Does The Purple Hair Mean

Written by Daniel Apr 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a fan of bold hair colors, you may have been considering purple hair for your next look. But before you take the plunge, it's important to understand what the color represents and what it can say about you. In this article, we'll explore the meaning of purple hair and provide some tips on how to care for it.

Description

Purple hair is a vibrant and eye-catching hair color that can range from a soft lavender to a deep violet hue. It's a popular choice for those looking to make a bold statement with their hair and can be achieved through a variety of techniques, including dyeing, extensions, and wigs.

How to

If you're interested in trying out purple hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it's important to work with a professional stylist who can help you achieve the desired shade without damaging your hair. They can also recommend products and techniques to help maintain the color and prevent fading.

Before your appointment, it's also a good idea to do some research on the different shades of purple and decide which one you want to go for. You can bring pictures to show your stylist or ask for their recommendations based on your skin tone and natural hair color.

Step by Step

Step 1: Consultation

Before dyeing your hair purple, schedule a consultation with a professional stylist to discuss your desired shade and the process they will use to achieve it. They can also assess the health of your hair and recommend any necessary treatments or products.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

To prepare your hair for the dyeing process, your stylist may recommend a deep conditioning treatment or a trim to remove any split ends. They may also ask you to come in with clean, dry hair to ensure the dye takes evenly.

Step 3: Dye Your Hair

Once your hair is prepped, your stylist will begin the dyeing process. They may use a semi-permanent or permanent dye, depending on your preference and the desired longevity of the color. The process can take several hours, so be prepared to sit in the chair for a while.

Step 4: Maintenance

After dyeing your hair purple, it's important to take care of it properly to prevent fading and maintain the vibrancy of the color. This may include using a color-safe shampoo and conditioner, avoiding excessive heat styling, and getting regular touch-ups to maintain the color.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you rock your purple hair:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to show off your new color.
  • Accessorize with complementary colors to make your hair pop.
  • Wear makeup that flatters your skin tone and complements your hair color.
  • Be prepared for some extra attention and compliments on your hair!

FAQ

What does purple hair mean?

Purple hair can represent creativity, individuality, and a willingness to take risks. It can also be associated with mysticism and spirituality.

Is purple hair damaging?

Dyeing your hair any color can cause damage if not done properly. It's important to work with a professional stylist and take proper care of your hair after dyeing it to prevent damage and maintain its health.

How long does purple hair last?

The longevity of purple hair depends on the type of dye used, how well it's maintained, and how often it's washed. Semi-permanent dyes may last a few weeks to a few months, while permanent dyes can last several months to a year or more.

Hair Care

To keep your purple hair looking its best, it's important to use products specifically designed for color-treated hair. This may include a color-safe shampoo and conditioner, a hair mask or treatment, and a heat protectant spray if you use heat styling tools. You should also avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the color and cause it to fade more quickly.

Overall, purple hair can be a fun and bold choice for anyone looking to switch up their look. With proper care and maintenance, you can rock your purple locks for months to come.
