Do Square Glasses Suit Oval Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Mar 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you someone with an oval face shape? Are you contemplating getting square glasses but not sure if they will suit your face? Look no further, as we have got you covered! In this blog post, we will discuss everything you need to know about whether square glasses are a good fit for oval faces.

Description

Oval face shapes are characterized by balanced proportions with slightly wider cheekbones and a gently curved jawline. People with oval faces tend to have a high forehead and a chin that is narrower than the forehead. It is one of the most versatile face shapes that can pull off almost any style of glasses.

How to Determine your Face Shape

If you are unsure about your face shape, you can determine it by following these simple steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Take a lipstick or eyeliner and trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Compare the shape you made with the following face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing Square Glasses for Oval Face Shape

While oval face shapes can carry off almost any frame style, square glasses can add a touch of sophistication and elegance. Here's how you can choose the perfect square glasses for your oval face:

  1. Consider the size of the glasses: Choose a size that is proportionate to your face. If you have a smaller face, opt for smaller glasses, and if you have a larger face, go for bigger glasses.
  2. Pay attention to the frame style: Square glasses come in a variety of styles, such as thick-rimmed, thin-rimmed, and rimless. Choose one that complements your personal style and the shape of your face.
  3. Think about the color: Choose a color that complements your skin tone and hair color. Neutral colors like black, brown, and grey are versatile and can be paired with any outfit.

Tips for Wearing Square Glasses with Oval Face Shape

Here are some tips on how to wear square glasses with an oval face shape:

  • Wear glasses that are wider than your cheekbones but do not extend beyond the width of your face.
  • Choose glasses that have rounded edges to soften the angles of your face.
  • Avoid glasses with very thick frames that overpower your delicate features.
  • Experiment with different frame styles to find the perfect fit for your face shape.

FAQs

1. Can oval face shapes wear any style of glasses?

Yes, oval face shapes can wear almost any style of glasses. However, it is important to choose glasses that complement your face shape and personal style.

2. Are square glasses suitable for oval faces?

Yes, square glasses can be a great fit for oval faces. They can add a touch of sophistication and elegance to your look.

3. What are some tips for choosing the perfect square glasses for oval faces?

Consider the size, frame style, and color of the glasses. Choose a size that is proportionate to your face, a frame style that complements your personal style, and a color that complements your skin tone and hair color.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shapes

While choosing the right glasses can enhance your look, taking care of your hair is just as important. Here are some hair care tips for oval face shapes:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect fit for your face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head as they can make your face appear longer.
  • Consider getting a side-swept fringe or layers to add dimension to your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that cover your forehead as they can make your face appear longer.

In conclusion, square glasses can be a great fit for oval faces. It is important to choose glasses that complement your face shape and personal style. Experiment with different styles and colors to find the perfect fit for you. Don't forget to take care of your hair to enhance your overall look!
