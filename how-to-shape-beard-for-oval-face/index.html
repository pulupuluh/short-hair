How To Shape Beard For Oval Face A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Apr 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an oval face and want to grow a beard, it's essential to know how to shape it correctly. A well-groomed beard can enhance your facial features and make you look more attractive. However, if you don't shape it correctly, it can make your face look rounder or elongated. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to shape a beard for an oval face.

Description

An oval face is considered to be the most balanced face shape, with the forehead being slightly wider than the jawline. If you have an oval face, you have the advantage of being able to pull off different beard styles. However, you need to keep in mind that not all beard styles will suit your face type. It's essential to choose a beard style that complements your facial features.

How to Shape Beard for Oval Face

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to shape a beard for an oval face:

Step 1: Grow Your Beard

The first step is to grow your beard to the desired length. It's recommended to grow your beard for at least four to six weeks before shaping it. This will give you enough hair to work with and help you determine which beard style suits your face type.

Step 2: Determine Your Face Shape

Once your beard has grown, it's essential to determine your face shape. You can do this by standing in front of a mirror and analyzing the shape of your face. If you're not sure, you can ask your barber or hairstylist to help you determine your face shape.

Step 3: Choose the Right Beard Style

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the right beard style. If you have an oval face, you have the advantage of being able to pull off different beard styles. However, it's essential to choose a style that complements your facial features. A short beard or a goatee can make your face look more elongated, while a full beard can make your face look rounder.

Step 4: Trim Your Beard

Once you've chosen the right beard style, it's time to trim your beard. Use a beard trimmer to trim your beard to the desired length. Start by trimming the sides of your beard and then move to the chin and neck area. Make sure to trim your beard evenly on both sides.

Step 5: Shape Your Beard

Now it's time to shape your beard. Use a beard comb to brush your beard in the direction of hair growth. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs that are sticking out. Use a beard trimmer to shape the edges of your beard. Make sure to shape your beard in a way that complements your facial features.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when shaping a beard for an oval face:

  • Choose a beard style that complements your facial features.
  • Trim your beard regularly to maintain its shape.
  • Use a beard oil or balm to keep your beard moisturized.
  • Don't forget to trim your mustache to keep it in line with your beard.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaping a beard for an oval face:

What beard style suits an oval face?

Short beards, goatees, and full beards can suit an oval face. It's essential to choose a style that complements your facial features.

How often should I trim my beard?

You should trim your beard every two to three weeks to maintain its shape.

Can I use a regular trimmer to trim my beard?

No, it's recommended to use a beard trimmer as it's designed specifically for trimming facial hair.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your beard to keep it healthy and well-groomed. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your beard with a beard shampoo or a mild soap.
  • Use a beard conditioner to keep your beard soft and moisturized.
  • Brush your beard daily to prevent tangles and promote hair growth.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your beard as it can damage the hair.

By following these tips and steps, you can shape your beard for an oval face and enhance your facial features. Remember to choose a beard style that complements your facial features and keep your beard well-groomed and healthy.
