How To Know Diamond Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're wondering how to know diamond face shape, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll guide you through the step-by-step process of determining if you have a diamond face shape. We'll also provide tips on how to style your hair to flatter your face shape and answer some frequently asked questions.

What is a Diamond Face Shape?

A diamond face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a pointed chin. This face shape is often considered the most unique and rare, making it a coveted feature in the modeling industry. However, if you have a diamond face shape, you might struggle to find the right hairstyles and makeup looks that complement your features.

How to Determine if You Have a Diamond Face Shape

There are a few key features that can help you determine if you have a diamond face shape:

  • High cheekbones: Your cheekbones should be the widest part of your face.
  • Narrow forehead: Your forehead should be smaller than your cheekbones.
  • Pointed chin: Your chin should be slightly pointed or narrow.
  • Straight or angular jawline: Your jawline should be defined and angular.

If you have these features, it's likely that you have a diamond face shape. However, if you're still unsure, you can use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Tips for Styling Diamond Face Shape Hair

Now that you know how to know diamond face shape, it's important to learn how to style your hair to flatter your features. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid styles that add volume to the top of your head, as this can make your forehead appear wider.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as layered cuts or side-swept bangs.
  • Consider a middle part, as this can help balance out your features.
  • Avoid styles that are too long, as this can elongate your face even more.

With these tips in mind, you can find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your diamond face shape.

FAQ About Diamond Face Shape

Here are some frequently asked questions about diamond face shape:

What hairstyles look best on diamond face shape?

Hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as layered cuts or side-swept bangs, look best on diamond face shape.

Can you wear a bob with a diamond face shape?

Yes, you can wear a bob with a diamond face shape, but make sure it's not too long and adds volume to the sides of your face.

What makeup looks best on diamond face shape?

Makeup that emphasizes your cheekbones, such as bronzer or blush, looks best on diamond face shape. You can also try a bold lip to draw attention to your mouth.

Diamond Face Shape Hair Care

When it comes to hair care for diamond face shape, it's important to keep your hair healthy and nourished. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent breakage, and avoid heat styling tools as much as possible. If you do use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Now that you know how to know diamond face shape, you can find the perfect hairstyle and makeup looks that flatter your unique features. Remember to embrace your diamond face shape and showcase your natural beauty!
