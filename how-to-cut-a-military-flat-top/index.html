How To Cut A Military Flat Top A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver May 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to cut your hair into a military flat top? Whether you're in the military or just love the look, a flat top is a classic and timeless hairstyle. But cutting a military flat top can be a bit tricky, so we've put together this comprehensive guide to help you achieve the look you want. Read on for step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut a Military Flat Top
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Flat Top
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for a Military Flat Top

How to Cut a Military Flat Top

A military flat top is a short haircut with the hair on top of the head cut flat, usually with a slight slope towards the front. The sides and back of the head are usually cut very short or buzzed. Here's what you'll need to get started:

  • Clippers with guards
  • A comb
  • Scissors
  • A mirror
  • Patience and a steady hand

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut a military flat top:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb it straight up so the hair on top of the head is standing straight up.
  2. Use clippers with a #2 or #3 guard to trim the sides and back of the head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, going against the direction of the hair growth.
  3. Blend the sides and top using a #4 or #5 guard. Use a comb to lift the hair and trim it evenly.
  4. Use scissors to trim the top of the head. Start in the middle and work your way outwards, cutting the hair to the desired length.
  5. Use the clippers to cut the hair on top of the head flat. Start at the front and work your way back, cutting the hair to the desired length. Make sure it's even on both sides.
  6. Blend the top and sides using the clippers with a #3 or #4 guard. Go around the head, trimming the hair evenly.
  7. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven spots.
  8. Clean up the neckline and sideburns with the clippers and a #1 or #2 guard.
  9. Check your work in the mirror and make any final adjustments.

Tips for a Perfect Flat Top

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect military flat top:

  • Take your time and go slowly. It's better to make small, gradual cuts than to take off too much at once.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help. Cutting the back of your own head can be tricky, so ask a friend or family member to help you.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and trim it evenly.
  • Make sure the hair on top of the head is cut flat and even.
  • Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven spots.
  • Clean up the neckline and sideburns with the clippers and a #1 or #2 guard.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a military flat top:

How long does a military flat top last?

A military flat top usually lasts about 2-4 weeks before it needs to be trimmed again.

What length should the hair be on top of the head?

The hair on top of the head should be between 1/4 to 1/2 inch long.

Can I cut a military flat top myself?

Yes, you can cut a military flat top yourself, but it can be tricky. It's best to ask for help or go to a professional stylist.

Hair Care for a Military Flat Top

Here are some tips for caring for your military flat top:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Apply a styling product, like hair gel or pomade, to keep your flat top looking sharp.
  • Use a comb to style your hair into a flat top shape.
  • Visit a professional stylist every 2-4 weeks to keep your flat top looking fresh.

With these tips and techniques, you should be able to achieve a perfect military flat top. Remember to take your time, go slowly, and use the right tools for the job. And don't forget to take care of your hair between cuts to keep it looking its best.
