Black Mens Haircut Fade A Complete Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Jun 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a black man looking for a new haircut, you might want to consider a fade. A fade is a popular hairstyle that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving the hair longer on top. This style is versatile, easy to maintain, and looks good on most face shapes. In this post, we'll go over everything you need to know about black mens haircut fade, including how to get one, the different types of fades, and how to care for your hair after getting a fade.

What is a Black Mens Haircut Fade?

A fade is a haircut where the hair on the sides and back gradually tapers from a longer length to a shorter length. This creates a fade effect, hence the name. The hair on top can be styled in various ways, such as a pompadour, slick back, or messy fringe. The fade can be done with clippers or scissors, depending on the desired look.

How to Get a Black Mens Haircut Fade

The first step in getting a fade is to choose the type of fade you want. There are several types of fades, including low fade, high fade, skin fade, and drop fade. Once you've decided on the type of fade, you'll need to find a barber who is experienced in cutting black men's hair. It's important to communicate clearly with your barber about the type of fade you want and how short you want your hair to be.

During the haircut, your barber will use clippers or scissors to gradually cut the hair shorter on the sides and back. They will blend the hair from the longer length on top to the shorter length on the sides and back. The fade can be as subtle or dramatic as you want, depending on your style preferences.

Types of Fades

There are several types of fades to choose from, depending on your style preferences:

  • Low fade: The hair gradually tapers down to the neckline.
  • High fade: The hair is cut very short on the sides and back, usually up to the temple.
  • Skin fade: The hair is cut very short, almost to the skin, on the sides and back.
  • Drop fade: The hair drops down behind the ear, creating a curved shape.

Tips for Maintaining a Black Mens Haircut Fade

Here are some tips for keeping your fade looking fresh:

  • Get regular trims: Fades require maintenance, so it's important to get regular trims every few weeks to keep the style looking sharp.
  • Use the right products: Use a styling product that works for your hair type to keep your hair in place and looking good.
  • Wash your hair regularly: Keep your hair clean by washing it every 2-3 days.
  • Protect your hair: Wear a hat or use a scarf to protect your hair from the sun and wind.

FAQ About Black Mens Haircut Fade

Here are some frequently asked questions about black mens haircut fade:

  • Can anyone get a fade?
  • How long does a fade last?
  • What should I tell my barber when getting a fade?
  • How can I style my hair after getting a fade?
  • What products should I use for my hair?

Hair Care for Black Mens Haircut Fade

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after getting a fade:

  • Moisturize your scalp: Use a moisturizer to keep your scalp from getting dry and itchy.
  • Trim your hair regularly: Keep your hair looking neat by trimming it every few weeks.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: Wear a hat or use a scarf to protect your hair from the sun and wind.
  • Avoid using too much heat: Don't use too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and cause it to break.

With these tips, you'll be able to keep your black mens haircut fade looking fresh and stylish. Remember to communicate clearly with your barber about the type of fade you want, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
