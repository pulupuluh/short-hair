What Hair Part Looks Best On Round Face

Written by Bardi Jul 09, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round face, you know the struggle of finding a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. One of the easiest ways to enhance your features and create balance is by choosing the right hair part. In this article, we'll share some tips on what hair part looks best on round face and how to style it to perfection.

Description

A round face typically has a wide hairline and full cheeks, with the width and length of the face being roughly equal. The goal is to create the illusion of length and slimness, so you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. Instead, opt for styles that elongate your face and draw attention to your best features.

How to

The first step in determining what hair part looks best on round face is to identify your face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back, so you can see the full shape of your face. Take note of the widest points, as well as the length of your face.

Next, experiment with different hair parts to see what works best for you. Here are some tips:

Center Part

A center part is a classic and timeless choice that works well for round faces. It creates symmetry and draws the eye down the center of your face, making it appear longer. Be sure to keep your hair sleek and straight to avoid adding unwanted volume to the sides of your face.

Side Part

A side part is another great option for round faces, as it adds dimension and creates the illusion of length. However, be mindful of which side you part your hair on. If you have a round face, opt for a deep side part that falls close to the arch of your eyebrow. This will create a diagonal line that elongates your face and draws attention to your cheekbones.

Diagonal Part

A diagonal part is a less common but effective choice for round faces. It creates an angled line that slims your face and adds definition to your features. Experiment with different angles to find the one that works best for you.

Step by Step

Once you've determined what hair part looks best on round face, it's time to style your hair to perfection. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a sleek and flattering look:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly, using a lightweight conditioner to avoid weighing down your strands. Blow-dry your hair in the direction of your desired part, using a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head.

Step 2: Part your hair

Using a fine-tooth comb, create your desired part. Remember to keep it as straight and precise as possible, using your eyebrows as a guide.

Step 3: Style your hair

Depending on your hair type and length, style your hair in loose waves, sleek and straight, or a chic updo. Avoid adding too much volume to the sides of your face, as this will only accentuate your roundness.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when deciding what hair part looks best on round face:

  • Experiment with different angles and depths to find the most flattering part for your face shape.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and texture.
  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they only emphasize the width of your face.
  • Accessorize with statement earrings or a bold lip to draw attention to your best features.

FAQ

Can a round face pull off a middle part?

Absolutely! A middle part is a great choice for round faces, as it creates symmetry and lengthens the face. Just be sure to keep your hair sleek and straight to avoid adding volume to the sides of your face.

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that creates the illusion of length and slimness. Consider a layered haircut with soft, face-framing layers or a long bob that falls just below the chin.

Do I have to stick to one hair part?

No! Feel free to switch up your hair part depending on your mood or outfit. Just be sure to keep in mind what hair part looks best on round face and adjust accordingly.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining luscious locks:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

By following these tips and choosing the right hair part, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance. Have fun experimenting with different styles and finding what works best for you!
