Best Hair Gel For Thick Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 06, 2023 · 10 min read

Are you tired of trying different hair gels but none of them seem to work for your thick curly hair? Look no further because we have compiled a list of the best hair gels that will work wonders for your locks. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about the best hair gel for thick curly hair, including how to apply it, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Description

Thick curly hair can be challenging to manage, and finding the right hair gel can make all the difference. The best hair gel for thick curly hair is one that provides hold, definition, and moisture without weighing down your curls. It should also be free of harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

How to Choose the Best Hair Gel for Thick Curly Hair

Choosing the right hair gel for your hair type can be overwhelming, but here are some factors to consider when selecting the best hair gel for thick curly hair:

  • Hold: Look for a hair gel that provides a strong hold without making your curls feel stiff or crunchy.
  • Moisture: Thick curly hair needs moisture to stay healthy and defined, so choose a hair gel that contains hydrating ingredients like aloe vera, glycerin, or honey.
  • Ingredients: Avoid hair gels that contain harsh chemicals like sulfates, parabens, and alcohol, which can dry out your hair and cause damage.
  • Texture: Choose a hair gel that has a lightweight, non-greasy texture that won't weigh down your curls.

Top 5 Best Hair Gels for Thick Curly Hair

1. Eco Styler Olive Oil Gel

The Eco Styler Olive Oil Gel is a cult favorite among curly girls for its strong hold, moisture, and affordable price. It contains olive oil, which provides nourishment and shine to your curls without leaving them crunchy or flaky.

2. Cantu Moisturizing Twist & Lock Gel

The Cantu Moisturizing Twist & Lock Gel is perfect for those with thick curly hair looking for a gel that provides a lightweight hold while keeping their curls moisturized. It contains shea butter, which helps to nourish and strengthen your curls.

3. Aunt Jackie's Flaxseed Don't Shrink Curling Gel

Aunt Jackie's Flaxseed Don't Shrink Curling Gel is a great option for those with thick curly hair that tends to shrink up. It contains flaxseed oil, which helps to elongate your curls while providing moisture and hold.

4. Kinky-Curly Original Curling Custard

Kinky-Curly Original Curling Custard is a cult favorite among curly girls for its natural ingredients and ability to define and hold curls without leaving them stiff or crunchy. It contains agave nectar, which helps to moisturize your curls and prevent frizz.

5. DevaCurl Ultra Defining Gel

The DevaCurl Ultra Defining Gel is a popular choice among those with thick curly hair for its lightweight, non-greasy texture and ability to define and hold curls without leaving any residue. It contains wheat and soy proteins, which help to nourish and strengthen your curls.

How to Apply Hair Gel for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some steps to follow when applying hair gel for thick curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to help define your curls and provide moisture.
  3. Take a small amount of hair gel and rub it between your palms.
  4. Scrunch the hair gel into your curls, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  5. Flip your head upside down and scrunch your curls some more to encourage definition.
  6. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry your curls.

Tips for Using Hair Gel for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using hair gel for thick curly hair:

  • Use a small amount of hair gel to avoid weighing down your curls.
  • Apply hair gel to soaking wet hair for best results.
  • Scrunch your curls with a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to avoid frizz.
  • Don't touch your curls while they're drying to avoid disrupting the curl pattern.
  • Refresh your curls with a water and leave-in conditioner mixture throughout the week to keep them moisturized.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your thick curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to provide extra moisture and nourishment.
  • Avoid heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid tangles and breakage while you sleep.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
