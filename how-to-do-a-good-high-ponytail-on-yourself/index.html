How To Do A Good High Ponytail On Yourself

Written by Aprilia Jul 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and want to mix things up? A high ponytail is a classic and easy look to achieve. In this article, we will provide you with the steps, tips, and hair care advice to create a fabulous high ponytail on yourself.

Table of Contents

  • How to Create a High Ponytail
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect High Ponytail
  • FAQs

How to Create a High Ponytail

A high ponytail is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn for any occasion. Here are the basic steps to create a high ponytail:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Using a comb, create a straight part down the middle of your hair.
  3. Gather all of your hair upwards to the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie.
  4. Adjust the ponytail to your desired height and tightness.

These are the basic steps, but keep reading for a more detailed guide on creating a high ponytail.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these detailed steps to create a perfect high ponytail:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before starting, make sure your hair is clean, dry, and free from tangles. Use a brush or wide-tooth comb to remove any knots, and apply a small amount of hair oil or serum for added shine and smoothness.

Step 2: Create a Part

Using a comb, create a straight part down the middle of your hair. You can also create a side part if you prefer.

Step 3: Gather your Hair

Using a brush, gather all of your hair upwards to the crown of your head. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or lumps in your hair.

Step 4: Secure with a Hair Tie

Secure your hair with a hair tie. Make sure to choose a hair tie that matches your hair color, and avoid using a rubber band as it can damage your hair.

Step 5: Adjust the Ponytail

Adjust the ponytail to your desired height and tightness. Use a brush or your fingers to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Tips for a Perfect High Ponytail

Here are some tips to help you achieve a perfect high ponytail:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color.
  • For added volume, tease the hair at the crown of your head before gathering it into a ponytail.
  • Use hairspray or hair gel to smooth out any flyaways and keep your hair in place.
  • Accessorize your ponytail with a scrunchie, ribbon, or hair clip for a cute and stylish look.

FAQs

Can I wear a high ponytail with short hair?

Yes, you can wear a high ponytail with short hair. Simply use hair extensions or a clip-in ponytail to add length and volume to your hair.

How do I prevent my hair tie from slipping out?

To prevent your hair tie from slipping out, use a hair tie that is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not too tight that it causes discomfort. You can also use bobby pins to secure the hair tie in place.

How can I make my high ponytail last longer?

To make your high ponytail last longer, use hairspray or hair gel to keep your hair in place. You can also wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie to hide it and create a more polished look.

Hair Care for High Ponytails

A high ponytail can be a great hairstyle, but it can also cause damage to your hair if not done properly. Here are some tips to care for your hair when wearing a high ponytail:

  • Avoid wearing a high ponytail every day to prevent breakage and hair loss.
  • Use a hair tie that is gentle on your hair, such as a scrunchie or elastic band.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tight, as it can cause tension headaches and damage your hair follicles.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.

With these tips and steps, you can create a beautiful high ponytail on yourself. Remember to take care of your hair, and have fun experimenting with different styles!
