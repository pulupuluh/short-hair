How Do I Get Rid Of Brassy Hair

Written by Aprilia Apr 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you've ever dyed your hair blonde, you know that brassy tones can be a real problem. But fear not, there are several ways to get rid of brassy hair and maintain your beautiful blonde locks. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks on how to get rid of brassy hair and keep your hair looking fabulous.

Table of Contents

  • Why Brassy Hair Occurs
  • How to Get Rid of Brassy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Process
  • Tips to Prevent Brassy Hair

Why Brassy Hair Occurs

Brassy hair is a common problem for those with blonde hair. It occurs when the hair turns yellow or orange instead of the desired blonde color. Brassy hair can be caused by several factors, including:

  • Exposure to chlorine or saltwater
  • Using the wrong hair products
  • Using heat styling tools too often
  • Not using enough toner after bleaching

Now that we've covered why brassy hair occurs, let's move on to how to get rid of it.

How to Get Rid of Brassy Hair

There are several ways to get rid of brassy hair, including:

  • Using a purple shampoo or toning conditioner
  • Using a toner
  • Applying a hair mask
  • Using a color-correcting product

Each of these methods can be effective in getting rid of brassy tones. However, it's important to find the method that works best for you and your hair type. Let's dive into a step-by-step process on how to get rid of brassy hair.

Step-by-Step Process

Here's a step-by-step process on how to get rid of brassy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a purple shampoo or toning conditioner. These products are specifically designed to neutralize brassy tones and enhance your blonde color.
  2. If your hair is still brassy after using a purple shampoo or toning conditioner, try using a toner. Toners work by depositing cool tones into your hair to counteract the warm, brassy tones.
  3. Apply a hair mask to your hair once a week. Hair masks are a great way to nourish your hair and keep it healthy. Look for a hair mask that's designed to target brassy hair.
  4. If all else fails, try using a color-correcting product. These products are designed to correct unwanted tones in your hair and can be a quick fix for brassy hair.

Now that you know how to get rid of brassy hair, let's talk about some tips to prevent it from occurring in the first place.

Tips to Prevent Brassy Hair

Here are some tips to prevent brassy hair:

  • Avoid exposure to chlorine and saltwater as much as possible.
  • Use hair products specifically designed for blonde hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Be sure to use enough toner after bleaching.

Following these tips can help prevent brassy hair from occurring and keep your blonde hair looking its best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting rid of brassy hair:

Q: Can I use regular shampoo on blonde hair?

A: Yes, you can use regular shampoo on blonde hair. However, it's important to use a shampoo specifically designed for blonde hair to prevent brassy tones.

Q: How often should I use a toner?

A: It depends on your hair type and how often you wash your hair. Generally, toner can be used once every two to four weeks.

Q: Can I use a hair mask instead of a toner?

A: Hair masks and toners serve different purposes, but both can be effective in getting rid of brassy tones. It's best to use both for optimal results.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your blonde hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use hair products specifically designed for blonde hair.
  • Be sure to use enough toner after bleaching.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Following these hair care tips can help keep your blonde hair healthy and free from brassy tones.

Now that you know how to get rid of brassy hair and maintain your blonde locks, you can enjoy beautiful, healthy hair all year round. Remember to follow these tips and tricks for optimal results.
