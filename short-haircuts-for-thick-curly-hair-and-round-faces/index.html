Short Haircuts For Thick Curly Hair And Round Faces

Written by Petter May 26, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you someone with thick curly hair and a round face? Are you looking for a new haircut that will complement your features? If so, you’re in the right place. In this article, we’ll be discussing the best short haircuts for thick curly hair and round faces. We’ll be covering everything from how to choose the right haircut to tips on how to care for your new style. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Short Haircut Ideas
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Thick Curly Hair

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When it comes to choosing a haircut for thick curly hair and a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to make sure the haircut complements your face shape. Second, you want to find a style that works with your natural texture. Here are a few tips to help you choose the right haircut:

  • Look for hairstyles that feature layers. Layers can help to add volume to your hair and create a more flattering shape for your face.
  • Avoid haircuts that are too short. A shorter cut can make your face appear fuller, so it’s best to stick with a style that’s at least chin-length.
  • Consider a side-swept bang. A side-swept bang can help to elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

By keeping these tips in mind, you’ll be able to choose a haircut that flatters your face and works with your natural texture.

Short Haircut Ideas

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut, it’s time to explore some short haircut ideas. Here are a few styles to consider:

1. Long Bob

A long bob, or “lob,” is a great option for thick curly hair and a round face. This style features longer layers in the front and shorter layers in the back, which helps to create a more flattering shape for your face. Plus, the longer length allows you to still have some length to work with.

2. Pixie Cut

If you’re looking for a more dramatic change, a pixie cut can be a great option. This short hairstyle features layers that are cut close to the head, which can help to create a more defined shape for your face. Plus, it’s a low-maintenance style that’s perfect for busy mornings.

3. Shaggy Bob

A shaggy bob is another great option for those with thick curly hair and a round face. This style features choppy layers throughout the hair, which helps to add volume and texture. Plus, it’s a versatile style that can be worn straight or curly.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Once you’ve chosen your new haircut, it’s important to know how to style it. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling short haircuts for thick curly hair and round faces:

  • Apply a moisturizing hair product to damp hair to help control frizz and define your curls.
  • Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help to add volume to your curls.
  • Use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need a little extra definition.
  • Finish with a hairspray to help hold your style in place.

By following these steps, you’ll be able to style your new haircut with ease.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you have your new haircut, it’s important to take care of it to ensure it looks its best. Here are a few tips and tricks for maintaining your short haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

By following these tips, you’ll be able to keep your new haircut looking great for weeks to come.

FAQs

Here are a few frequently asked questions about short haircuts for thick curly hair and round faces:

What is the best haircut for a round face with curly hair?

The best haircut for a round face with curly hair is one that has layers to add volume and shape to your hair. A long bob, pixie cut, or shaggy bob are all great options.

Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A very short haircut can make your face look rounder, so it’s best to go with a style that’s at least chin-length.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Curly Hair

Thick curly hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for caring for your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to help keep your curls moisturized.
  • Detangle your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.

By following these tips, you’ll be able to keep your thick curly hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Final Thoughts

Choosing the right haircut for thick curly hair and a round face can be a challenge, but with the tips and ideas in this article, you’ll be able to find a style that works for you. Remember to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and natural texture, and to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. With the right haircut and care, you’ll be able to embrace your curls and feel confident in your new style.
