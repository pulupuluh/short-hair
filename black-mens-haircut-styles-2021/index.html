Black Mens Haircut Styles 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jul 23, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you ready to try out a new hairstyle? Black mens haircut styles 2021 are diverse and on-trend. From buzz cuts to fades, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve some of the most popular black mens haircut styles 2021, as well as tips on hair care and maintenance. Let's dive in!

  • How to Achieve Black Mens Haircut Styles 2021
  • Step-by-Step Instructions for Popular Black Mens Haircut Styles 2021
  • Tips for Maintaining Black Mens Haircut Styles 2021
  • FAQs about Black Mens Haircut Styles 2021
  • Hair Care for Black Mens Haircut Styles 2021

How to Achieve Black Mens Haircut Styles 2021

Before we get into the specific styles, let's talk about how to achieve them. The first step is to find a barber or stylist who specializes in black hair. You want someone who has experience working with your hair type and can recommend styles that will complement your face shape and personal style.

Once you've found the right barber or stylist, communicate your preferences clearly. Bring in pictures or examples of hairstyles you like and be specific about the length and texture you want. Don't be afraid to ask questions or voice any concerns you may have.

Finally, be patient. Some styles may require several visits to the barber to perfect. And remember, a good haircut is only the beginning. Proper hair care and maintenance are key to keeping your new style looking great.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Popular Black Mens Haircut Styles 2021

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic style that requires little maintenance. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose the desired length for your buzz cut. A 1 or 2 guard will give you a short buzz, while a 3 or 4 guard will leave more hair on top.
  3. Start at the sides and work your way up to the top, using the clippers in a sweeping motion.
  4. Blend the sides and top with scissors or clippers, depending on your preference.
  5. Use a trimmer to clean up the hairline and sideburns.

The Fade

The fade is a popular style that blends hair from short to long. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose the desired length for the top of your hair.
  3. Use clippers to create a fade at the sides and back of your head. Start with a higher guard and work your way down to create a gradual fade.
  4. Blend the top with scissors or clippers.
  5. Use a trimmer to clean up the hairline and sideburns.

The Afro

The afro is a bold and iconic style that requires some upkeep. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Use a pick or a wide-tooth comb to create volume and texture.
  3. Apply a moisturizing product, like a leave-in conditioner or curl cream, to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  4. Use a trimmer to clean up the hairline and sideburns, if desired.

Tips for Maintaining Black Mens Haircut Styles 2021

Now that you've achieved your new style, it's important to keep it looking fresh. Here are some tips for maintaining black mens haircut styles 2021:

  • Invest in quality hair care products. Look for products that are specifically designed for black hair and that address your specific hair concerns, like dryness or breakage.
  • Protect your hair while you sleep. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and tangling.
  • Visit your barber regularly. Even low-maintenance styles require some upkeep, and regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

FAQs about Black Mens Haircut Styles 2021

What hairstyles are popular for black men in 2021?

Some popular styles for black men in 2021 include fades, buzz cuts, and textured styles like the curly top.

What should I ask my barber when getting a haircut?

Be specific about the length and texture you want, and ask for recommendations based on your face shape and personal style. Don't be afraid to ask questions or voice any concerns you may have.

How often should I get a haircut?

This depends on your individual hair growth and style. Some styles may require more frequent trims, while others can go several weeks between appointments. Your barber can recommend a schedule that works for you.

Hair Care for Black Mens Haircut Styles 2021

In addition to regular trims and quality hair care products, there are some other steps you can take to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair. Black hair can be prone to dryness, so aim to wash your hair no more than once or twice a week.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage. Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools like flat irons or blow dryers.
  • Stay hydrated. Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair and scalp healthy.

With these tips and a little patience, you can achieve and maintain a stylish and on-trend black mens haircut style in 2021. Happy styling!
