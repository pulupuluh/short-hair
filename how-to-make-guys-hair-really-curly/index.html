How To Make Guys Hair Really Curly A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curly hair is a popular hairstyle for guys nowadays. However, not all guys are blessed with naturally curly hair. For those who want to achieve curly hair, there are several ways to do it without spending too much money in a salon. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to make guys hair really curly.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Guys Hair Really Curly
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Guys Hair Really Curly
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Make Guys Hair Really Curly

There are several ways to make guys hair really curly. Some of these methods include the use of hair products, styling tools, and natural methods. Here are some of the most effective ways to make guys hair really curly.

1. Use Curl Enhancing Products

Using curl enhancing products such as curl creams, gels, and mousse can help define and enhance curls in guys hair. These products work by adding moisture to the hair and creating a barrier to protect it from humidity. Apply the product to damp hair and scrunch it with your fingers to create curls.

2. Blow Dry with a Diffuser

A diffuser attachment on a blow dryer can help enhance curls in guys hair. Apply a curl enhancing product to damp hair and use the diffuser attachment to dry the hair. Use a low heat setting and scrunch the hair with the diffuser to create curls.

3. Use a Curling Iron

A curling iron is a great tool to create curls in guys hair. Use a 1-inch curling iron and wrap small sections of hair around the barrel. Hold the hair for 10-15 seconds and release. Repeat this process until all sections of hair are curled. Use a hairspray to set the curls in place.

4. Braid Hair Overnight

Braiding hair overnight can create natural curls in guys hair. Divide damp hair into sections and braid each section tightly. Leave the braids in overnight and remove them in the morning. Use your fingers to separate the curls and apply a hairspray to set them in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to make guys hair really curly.

Step 1: Wash Hair

Wash hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to add moisture to the hair.

Step 2: Apply Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product such as curl cream, gel, or mousse to damp hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry with Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on a blow dryer to dry the hair. Use a low heat setting and scrunch the hair with the diffuser to create curls.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

Use a 1-inch curling iron to curl small sections of hair. Hold the hair for 10-15 seconds and release. Repeat this process until all sections of hair are curled.

Step 5: Braid Hair Overnight

Divide damp hair into sections and braid each section tightly. Leave the braids in overnight and remove them in the morning. Use your fingers to separate the curls and apply a hairspray to set them in place.

Tips for Making Guys Hair Really Curly

Here are some tips for making guys hair really curly.

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner can add moisture to the hair and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

2. Use a Heat Protectant

Using a heat protectant can protect the hair from damage caused by styling tools such as curling irons and blow dryers.

3. Experiment with Different Products

Experiment with different curl enhancing products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can guys with straight hair make their hair curly?

Yes, guys with straight hair can make their hair curly using curl enhancing products, styling tools, and natural methods.

How long will the curls last?

The duration of the curls depends on the method used and the hair type. Curls created using a curling iron or braids can last up to 24 hours. Curls created using curl enhancing products can last up to 2-3 days.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

1. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle hair. Avoid using brushes as they can cause frizz and breakage.

2. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to add moisture to the hair and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

3. Avoid Using Heat Styling Tools too Often

Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can cause damage to the hair and make it dry and brittle.

In conclusion, making guys hair really curly is possible with the use of curl enhancing products, styling tools, and natural methods. Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for your hair type. Don't forget to take care of your curly hair by using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, a heat protectant, and a leave-in conditioner.
