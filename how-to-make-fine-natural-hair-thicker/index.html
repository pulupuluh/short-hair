How To Make Fine Natural Hair Thicker

Written by Eveline Apr 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have fine natural hair, you may feel like your hair is thin and lacks volume. However, there are ways to make your hair appear thicker and fuller. In this article, we will discuss how to make fine natural hair thicker with some simple tips and tricks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Fine Natural Hair Thicker
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Make Fine Natural Hair Thicker

The first step to making fine natural hair thicker is to use the right products. Look for products that are designed to add volume and thickness to your hair. These products may include volumizing shampoos and conditioners, hair mousse, and styling sprays.

Another way to make your hair appear thicker is to use a root lifter. A root lifter is a product that is designed to lift the roots of your hair and add volume to your hairstyle. Apply the root lifter to your damp hair, then blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.

Another way to make your hair appear thicker is to add layers to your hairstyle. Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, making it appear thicker and fuller. Work with your hair stylist to create a layered haircut that is tailored to your hair type and face shape.

Step by Step

Here are some simple steps to follow to make fine natural hair thicker:

  1. Choose the right products that are designed to add volume and thickness to your hair.
  2. Use a root lifter to lift the roots of your hair and add volume.
  3. Add layers to your hairstyle to create volume and movement.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help make your fine natural hair appear thicker:

  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons too frequently, as they can damage your hair and make it appear thinner.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and thickness.
  • Consider taking a hair growth supplement to help promote hair growth and thickness.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making fine natural hair thicker:

Can I make my hair thicker without using products?

While using the right products can help make your hair appear thicker, there are other ways to add volume to your hair. For example, blow drying your hair upside down can create lift and volume at the roots.

Can cutting my hair shorter make it appear thicker?

Cutting your hair shorter can create the illusion of thicker hair, but it may not actually make your hair thicker. Adding layers to your hairstyle may be a more effective way to create volume and thickness.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair and promoting hair growth. Here are some tips for taking care of fine natural hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly into ponytails or braids, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your fine natural hair appear thicker and fuller. Remember to choose the right products, add layers to your hairstyle, and take good care of your hair to promote healthy hair growth and thickness.
