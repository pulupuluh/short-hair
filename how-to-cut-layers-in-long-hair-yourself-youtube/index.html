How To Cut Layers In Long Hair Yourself Youtube Diy Guide

Written by Robby Mar 11, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of having long hair that just hangs there? Do you want to add some life and movement to your locks? If so, you may want to consider cutting layers into your hair. Layers can add volume, texture, and dimension to long hair, making it much more interesting to look at and style. But, how can you cut layers in long hair yourself, without having to go to a salon and spend a fortune? The answer is simple: follow our DIY guide and use Youtube as your teacher.

Description

Learning how to cut layers in long hair yourself Youtube can be a bit intimidating at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it's actually quite easy to do. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting layers in long hair, and provide you with some helpful tips and tricks along the way. We'll also answer some frequently asked questions about layer cutting, and give you some advice on how to care for your newly layered locks.

How to Cut Layers in Long Hair Yourself Youtube

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, you'll need to gather a few essential tools. These include sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, sectioning clips, and a mirror. You may also want to have a spray bottle filled with water, to mist your hair as you cut it.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, you'll want to section your hair into manageable pieces. Start by parting your hair down the middle, and then dividing it into two sections. Clip the top section of hair out of the way, and focus on the bottom section first.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Using your comb and scissors, trim the bottom layer of your hair to the desired length. This will be the foundation layer for your layers. Be sure to cut straight across, and avoid angling the scissors upward or downward, as this can create uneven layers.

Step 4: Cut the Middle Layer

Release the top section of your hair, and clip the freshly cut bottom layer out of the way. Next, take a section of hair from the middle of your head, and comb it straight up. Using the bottom layer as a guide, cut this section of hair to the same length.

Step 5: Cut the Top Layer

Finally, release the top layer of your hair, and once again clip the freshly cut middle layer out of the way. Take a section of hair from the top of your head, and comb it straight up. Using the middle layer as a guide, cut this section of hair to the same length.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best possible result when cutting layers in long hair yourself Youtube:

  • Take your time, and don't rush the process.
  • Use sharp scissors, as dull ones can create split ends.
  • Don't cut too much off at once. It's better to take small sections and cut gradually, rather than taking off too much and regretting it later.
  • Make sure your hair is dry and straight before cutting, as wet hair can appear longer than it actually is.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting layers in long hair yourself Youtube:

Is it easy to cut layers in long hair yourself?

Yes, with the right tools and techniques, cutting layers in long hair yourself can be quite easy.

How do I know how many layers to cut?

This will depend on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as your personal preference. As a general rule, start with one or two layers, and add more if desired.

Will cutting layers damage my hair?

No, cutting layers should not damage your hair, as long as you use sharp scissors and avoid cutting too much off at once.

Hair Care

After cutting layers in long hair yourself Youtube, it's important to take good care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips for caring for layered hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-washing your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep them moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools.

By following these tips, you can enjoy beautiful, layered hair that looks and feels great. So, go ahead and try cutting layers in long hair yourself Youtube - you might just surprise yourself with how easy and fun it can be!
