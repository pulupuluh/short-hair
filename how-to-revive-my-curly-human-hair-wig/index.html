How To Revive My Curly Human Hair Wig

Written by Oliver Apr 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Curly human hair wigs are a great way to switch up your look, but over time, they can lose their bounce and shine. If you're wondering how to revive your curly human hair wig, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll discuss the best ways to bring your wig back to life.

Description

Before we delve into the steps on how to revive your curly human hair wig, it's essential to understand why wigs lose their curl pattern. There are many reasons why this can happen, including overwashing, using the wrong hair products, and excessive heat styling. These factors can cause the wig's hair strands to become dry, brittle, and prone to tangling, resulting in a dull and lifeless appearance.

How to Revive My Curly Human Hair Wig

Step 1: Detangle the Wig

The first step to revive your curly human hair wig is to detangle it gently. Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to remove any knots or tangles. Start from the bottom of the wig and work your way up to avoid damaging the hair strands.

Step 2: Deep Condition the Wig

After detangling your wig, it's time to deep condition it. Apply a generous amount of deep conditioner to the wig's hair strands, focusing on the ends. Cover the wig with a shower cap and leave it on for at least 30 minutes. Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water to lock in the moisture.

Step 3: Air Dry the Wig

Allow the wig to air dry naturally. Avoid using a hair dryer or any heat styling tools as they can cause further damage to the hair strands. Once the wig is dry, use your fingers to fluff the curls gently.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep your curly human hair wig looking great:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to maintain the wig's curl pattern.
  • Avoid brushing or combing the wig when it's wet as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Store the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling the wig.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a curling iron on my curly human hair wig?

A: While it's possible to use a curling iron on your wig, we recommend avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. Excessive heat can damage the hair strands, resulting in a loss of curl pattern.

Q: How often should I wash my curly human hair wig?

A: It's best to wash your wig every 7-10 wears or when it starts to feel greasy or dirty. Overwashing can strip the hair strands of their natural oils, leading to dryness and breakage.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and appearance of your wig. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair strands of their natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your wig as it can cause tangling and matting.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to detangle your wig gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.

By following these tips, you can keep your curly human hair wig looking fabulous for years to come!
