How To Air Dry Curly Hair And Get Volume A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Apr 26, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools to style your curly hair? Air drying your hair is the perfect solution to achieve healthy and voluminous curls without damaging them. In this guide, we will show you how to air dry curly hair and get volume effortlessly. Read on for our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

How To Air Dry Curly Hair And Get Volume

Air drying curly hair can seem daunting at first, but it’s actually quite simple. Here’s what you need to do:

1. Start With Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy. Rinse your hair with cool water to seal the cuticles and prevent frizz.

2. Apply A Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair to enhance your curls and add volume. Choose a product that suits your hair type and texture. You can use a curl cream, mousse, gel, or leave-in conditioner.

3. Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your curls to form. Don’t use a towel to dry your hair, as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use an old t-shirt or a microfiber towel to gently squeeze out excess water.

4. Let Your Hair Air Dry

Let your hair air dry naturally. Don’t touch your hair while it’s drying, as this can disrupt your curls and make them frizzy. Avoid using a hairdryer or diffuser, as the heat can damage your hair and cause frizz.

5. Fluff Your Hair

Once your hair is completely dry, fluff your hair with your fingers to add volume. Don’t use a brush or a comb, as it can break up your curls and make them less defined.

Step-By-Step Guide

Here’s a detailed step-by-step guide on how to air dry curly hair and get volume:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair with cool water to seal the cuticles and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Apply A Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair to enhance your curls and add volume. Choose a product that suits your hair type and texture.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your curls to form. Use an old t-shirt or a microfiber towel to gently squeeze out excess water.

Step 4: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections to help it dry evenly. Clip each section up with a hair clip or a hairband.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Let your hair air dry naturally. Don’t touch your hair while it’s drying, as this can disrupt your curls and make them frizzy.

Step 6: Fluff Your Hair

Once your hair is completely dry, fluff your hair with your fingers to add volume. Don’t use a brush or a comb, as it can break up your curls and make them less defined.

Tips For Air Drying Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you air dry your curly hair and get volume:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Apply a styling product to your damp hair to enhance your curls and add volume.
  • Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your curls to form.
  • Let your hair air dry naturally and avoid using heat tools.
  • Fluff your hair with your fingers to add volume once it’s completely dry.

FAQs

Q: Can I air dry my curly hair overnight?

A: Yes, you can air dry your curly hair overnight. Just make sure to use a satin pillowcase or a silk scarf to avoid frizz and breakage.

Q: How long does it take to air dry curly hair?

A: It can take anywhere from 2-4 hours to air dry curly hair, depending on your hair type and texture. If you have thick or long hair, it may take longer.

Q: Can I use a diffuser to air dry my curly hair?

A: While a diffuser can help speed up the drying process, it can also cause frizz and damage your hair. It’s best to let your hair air dry naturally.

Hair Care Advice For Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your curly hair healthy and voluminous:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat tools, such as hairdryers and flat irons, on your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or a swim cap.

Air drying your curly hair is a great way to achieve voluminous and healthy curls without damaging them. Follow our guide and tips for the best results. With a little patience and care, you can have beautiful hair every day.
