How To Do A Love Heart In Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 04, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to try out for your next date night or special occasion? Look no further than the love heart in hair! This cute and romantic hairstyle is perfect for any occasion and is sure to turn heads. In this article, we'll show you just how easy it is to create the love heart in hair, and give you some tips for making it look its best.

Description

The love heart in hair is a hairstyle that features two hearts made out of hair, one on each side of the head. It's a cute and playful look that's perfect for romantic occasions like Valentine's Day or a date night.

How to

Here's how to create the love heart in hair:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before starting this hairstyle. If your hair is naturally curly or wavy, you may want to straighten it first for a smoother look.

Step 2: Section your hair

Take a section of hair from the top of your head and divide it into two equal parts. This will be the top of the heart.

Step 3: Create the heart shape

Take each section of hair and create a loop, pulling it down towards your neck. The loops should meet in the middle to create a heart shape. Use bobby pins to secure the loops in place.

Step 4: Create the bottom of the heart

Take a section of hair from each side of your head, just below the top of the heart. Pull them towards the middle of your head and secure them with bobby pins. This will create the bottom of the heart shape.

Step 5: Finish the hairstyle

Use hairspray to hold the hairstyle in place, and add any additional styling touches you like. You can leave your hair down or pull it up into a ponytail or bun for a different look.

Tips

Here are some tips for making your love heart in hair look its best:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • Try using a heart-shaped hair clip or accessory to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different hair textures and lengths to create a unique look.
  • Practice the hairstyle a few times before wearing it out in public to make sure you get the hang of it.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the love heart in hair:

Is this hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle can be done on any hair type, but it may be easier to achieve on straight or slightly wavy hair.

Can I do this hairstyle on myself?

Yes, this hairstyle can be done on yourself, but it may be easier to have someone else help you with the back sections.

How long does this hairstyle take to do?

Depending on your hair length and skill level, this hairstyle can take anywhere from 5-20 minutes to complete.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair before and after doing this hairstyle. Use a heat protectant if you're using a straightener, and don't use too many hair accessories that could damage your hair.

Now that you know how to do a love heart in hair, you can try this cute and romantic hairstyle for your next special occasion. Have fun experimenting with different textures and accessories, and don't forget to take care of your hair in the process.
