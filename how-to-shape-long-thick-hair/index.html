How To Shape Long Thick Hair Tips For A Perfect Look

Written by Eveline May 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have long and thick hair, you know how difficult it can be to manage and style it. However, with the right techniques and tools, you can shape your hair into a perfect look that will turn heads. In this article, we will show you how to shape long thick hair, step by step.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Shape Long Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Shaping Long Thick Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Thick Hair

How to Shape Long Thick Hair

Shaping long thick hair requires patience, the right tools, and some techniques. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Before you start shaping your hair, you need to wash and condition it to remove any dirt, oil, or buildup. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Once you have washed and conditioned your hair, dry it with a towel to remove excess water. Then, use a blow dryer to dry it completely. You can also let it air dry if you have time.

Step 3: Use a Heat Protectant

Before you start using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will prevent damage from the heat and keep your hair healthy.

Step 4: Use a Round Brush

Use a round brush to shape your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use a blow dryer on low heat to dry your hair as you brush it.

Step 5: Use a Flat Iron

Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and make it more manageable. Section your hair and use the flat iron on each section, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 6: Use a Curling Iron

To add some volume and texture to your hair, use a curling iron. Section your hair and use the curling iron on each section, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to shape long thick hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Dry your hair with a towel and then use a blow dryer to dry it completely.
  3. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  4. Use a round brush to shape your hair and dry it with a blow dryer on low heat.
  5. Straighten your hair with a flat iron to make it more manageable.
  6. Add volume and texture to your hair with a curling iron.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Shaping Long Thick Hair

Here are some tips to help you shape your long thick hair:

  • Use a heat protectant to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  • Section your hair to make it easier to manage.
  • Use a round brush to shape your hair and a flat iron to make it more manageable.
  • Add volume and texture to your hair with a curling iron.
  • Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my long thick hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If your hair tends to get oily quickly, you may need to wash it every other day. If your hair is dry, you can wash it once or twice a week.

Q: Can I shape my long thick hair without heat styling tools?

A: Yes, you can use rollers or braids to shape your hair without heat styling tools. However, this may take longer and may not give you the same results as heat styling tools.

Hair Care for Long Thick Hair

Here are some tips to keep your long thick hair healthy:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products as they can weigh down your hair.

By following these tips, you can shape your long thick hair into any style you want. With a little patience and practice, you can achieve a perfect look that will turn heads.
