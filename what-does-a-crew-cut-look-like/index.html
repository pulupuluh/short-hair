What Does A Crew Cut Look Like A Relaxed Guide To The Timeless Hairstyle

Written by Robby Mar 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a classic and timeless hairstyle that's easy to maintain and looks good on almost everyone, a crew cut might be the perfect option for you. But what exactly is a crew cut, and how does it differ from other hairstyles? In this guide, we'll answer all of your questions about what a crew cut looks like, how to get one, and how to take care of it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Crew Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Crew Cut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Crew Cut
  • FAQs About Crew Cuts
  • Hair Care Tips for Crew Cuts

Description

A crew cut is a short hairstyle that's typically cut with clippers and features shorter hair on the sides and back of the head, with slightly longer hair on top. The hair on top is usually cut in a uniform length, and the overall look is clean and neat. The crew cut is often associated with military and sports teams, but it's a versatile hairstyle that can be adapted to suit any personal style.

How to Get a Crew Cut

If you're interested in getting a crew cut, the first step is to find a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in cutting this style. Look for someone who has experience with men's haircuts and who can recommend the best crew cut style for your face shape and hair type. You can also bring in photos of crew cuts that you like to give your barber a better idea of what you're looking for.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Crew Cut

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a crew cut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it so that it's damp but not dripping wet.
  2. Sit in the barber's chair and explain the crew cut style that you want. Your barber will determine the best length for your hair and the shape of your head.
  3. Your barber will then use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length. The hair on top will be left slightly longer.
  4. The barber will then use scissors to trim the hair on top to the desired length and shape.
  5. Finally, your barber will use a comb and scissors to blend the hair on top with the hair on the sides and back, creating a smooth and clean transition.

Tips for Maintaining Your Crew Cut

Maintaining a crew cut is relatively easy, but there are a few tips that can help you keep your hair looking its best:

  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to keep your crew cut looking sharp.
  • Use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Consider using a styling product, like hair wax or pomade, to add texture and hold to your crew cut.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer on high heat, as this can damage your hair and scalp.

FAQs About Crew Cuts

What face shapes are best for a crew cut?

A crew cut can work well on almost any face shape, but it's especially flattering on those with oval or square-shaped faces. If you have a round face, you may want to consider a crew cut with slightly longer hair on top to balance out your features.

Can women get crew cuts?

Yes, women can certainly get crew cuts! While it's often associated with men's haircuts, the crew cut is a versatile style that can look great on women as well. If you're considering a crew cut, talk to your hairstylist about the best length and shape for your face and hair type.

What are some variations of the crew cut?

There are several variations of the crew cut, including the high and tight, the Ivy League, and the butch cut. These styles all feature shorter hair on the sides and back of the head, with slightly longer hair on top, but the length and shape of the top can vary depending on the style.

Hair Care Tips for Crew Cuts

Here are some additional hair care tips for keeping your crew cut looking its best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like curling irons or straighteners, as these can damage your hair and scalp.
  • If you have dandruff or an itchy scalp, use a medicated shampoo to soothe and treat the problem.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

In conclusion, a crew cut is a timeless and classic hairstyle that's easy to maintain and looks great on almost everyone. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance style for everyday wear or a sharp and clean look for a special occasion, a crew cut might be the perfect option for you. Just be sure to find an experienced barber or hairstylist who can help you achieve the perfect crew cut for your face shape and hair type.
