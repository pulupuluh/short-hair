How To Cut A Man s Hair In The Back Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to give a man a fresh haircut, it's essential to know how to cut hair in the back properly. The back of the head can be a little tricky to manage, but with the right techniques and tools, you'll be able to achieve a clean and professional cut. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to cut a man's hair in the back.

Description

Cutting a man's hair in the back can be a little tricky, especially if you're new to hair cutting. The back of the head is not visible, which makes it difficult to determine the length and style. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a clean and professional cut. This guide will help you learn how to cut a man's hair in the back and achieve a stylish and trendy look.

How to Cut a Man's Hair in the Back

Follow these steps to cut a man's hair in the back:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step to cutting a man's hair in the back is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Haircutting Cape

Step 2: Section the Hair

Section the hair by using the comb to create a part down the middle of the head. Then, create two more parts on each side of the head to separate the hair into four sections.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting the hair at the nape of the neck. Use the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length, starting from the bottom and moving upward. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut in a straight line.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Blend the hair by using the scissors to cut the hair at an angle. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim the ends. Blend the hair by cutting at different lengths to create a natural look.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

Check for evenness by combing the hair and looking for any uneven parts. Use the scissors to trim any uneven parts until the hair is even.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the clippers to clean up the neckline by trimming the hair around the ears and the back of the neck. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut in a straight line.

Step 7: Style the Hair

Style the hair by using the scissors to trim any stray hairs and create the desired look. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim it to the desired length.

Tips

  • Use a haircutting cape to keep the hair from getting on the client's clothes.
  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut the hair shorter.
  • Use a mirror to check for any uneven parts of the hair.

FAQ

1. Can I cut my own hair in the back?

It's not recommended to cut your hair in the back by yourself, as it can be difficult to see and reach. It's best to have someone else do it for you.

2. How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on the length of your hair and the style you want to achieve. Typically, men's hair should be cut every four to six weeks.

3. What if I make a mistake while cutting the hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting the hair, don't panic. You can always trim it again or go to a professional for help.

Hair Care

After cutting the hair, it's essential to take care of it properly. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using too much heat on the hair, as it can damage it. Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools. Trim the hair every four to six weeks to maintain the style.
