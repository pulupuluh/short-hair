How To Hide Bald Spots With Braids A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have bald spots on your scalp, you may feel self-conscious and want to find ways to cover them up. One popular option is to use braids to hide the bald spots. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to hide bald spots with braids. We will also provide you with tips and answers to frequently asked questions about braids and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Hide Bald Spots with Braids
  • Tips for Braiding Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions about Braids and Hair Care
  • Hair Care for Braided Hair

How to Hide Bald Spots with Braids

Braids can be a great way to cover up bald spots on your scalp. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Choose the Right Type of Braids

There are many types of braids, but not all of them are suitable for hiding bald spots. The best type of braids for this purpose is box braids, as they can cover a large area of the scalp. You can also consider getting cornrows or twists, but they may not be as effective at covering up bald spots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Before you start braiding, you need to section your hair. Use a comb to create even sections of hair on your scalp. Make sure that the sections are not too large or too small, as this can affect the final result.

Step 3: Start Braiding

Once you have sectioned your hair, you can start braiding. Take one section of hair and divide it into three equal parts. Cross the right section over the middle section, and then cross the left section over the middle section. Continue braiding until you reach the end of the section.

Step 4: Repeat

Continue braiding each section of hair until you have covered all the bald spots on your scalp. Make sure to braid tightly to ensure that the braids cover the bald spots effectively.

Step 5: Style Your Braids

Once you have finished braiding, you can style your braids as desired. You can wear them in a ponytail, bun, or any other style that you prefer.

Tips for Braiding Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when braiding your hair:

  • Use a good quality hair product to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Don't braid too tightly, as this can cause hair breakage and damage.
  • Take breaks between braiding sections to avoid putting too much stress on your scalp.

Frequently Asked Questions about Braids and Hair Care

Q: How long can I keep my braids in?

A: You can keep your braids in for up to 8 weeks, but it's important to take breaks between braiding to give your scalp a rest.

Q: Can braids cause hair loss?

A: If braids are done too tightly, they can cause hair breakage and damage. It's important to braid gently and take breaks between braiding to avoid putting too much stress on your scalp.

Q: How can I maintain my braids?

A: To maintain your braids, make sure to keep your scalp clean and moisturized. You can use a scalp oil or spray to keep your scalp healthy.

Hair Care for Braided Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your braided hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat on your braids, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your braids from getting tangled.

By following these tips and using braids to cover up your bald spots, you can feel confident and beautiful in your own skin.
