Easy Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Petter Apr 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Medium length hair is a versatile length that allows for many easy hairstyles. Whether you're going for a casual, everyday look or a more formal style, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this article, we'll go over some easy hairstyles for medium length hair that you can try out at home.

Description

Medium length hair is typically defined as hair that falls between the shoulders and the collarbone. This length is very popular because it allows for a variety of hairstyles that are easy to maintain and style. Whether your hair is straight, wavy, or curly, there are many options to choose from when it comes to easy hairstyles for medium length hair.

How to

One of the easiest hairstyles for medium length hair is the messy bun. To create this look, simply gather your hair into a loose ponytail at the nape of your neck. Then, twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face and you're done!

Another easy hairstyle for medium length hair is the half-up, half-down style. To create this look, gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip. Leave the bottom half of your hair down and you're done! This style is perfect for a casual day out or a day at the office.

Step by Step

Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a loose ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face.
  5. You're done!

Half-Up, Half-Down

  1. Gather the top half of your hair.
  2. Secure it with a hair tie or clip.
  3. Leave the bottom half of your hair down.
  4. You're done!

Tips

When creating easy hairstyles for medium length hair, it's important to use the right products. A good hairspray or mousse can help hold your style in place all day. It's also important to use the right tools, such as a curling iron or straightener, to achieve the look you want.

FAQ

What are some easy hairstyles for medium length hair?

Some easy hairstyles for medium length hair include the messy bun, half-up, half-down style, and the simple braid.

What products should I use to create easy hairstyles for medium length hair?

It's important to use the right products, such as a good hairspray or mousse, to hold your style in place all day.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. This means using a good shampoo and conditioner, and avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools as much as possible. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking its best.

With these easy hairstyles for medium length hair, you can change up your look anytime you want. Whether you're going for a casual, everyday look or a more formal style, there are plenty of options to choose from. Just remember to use the right products and tools, and take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
