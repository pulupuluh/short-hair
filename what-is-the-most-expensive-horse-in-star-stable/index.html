Description

The most expensive horse in Star Stable is the Friesian Sport Horse. This majestic horse has a sleek black coat and a muscular build, making it a favorite among players who love powerful and beautiful horses. How to Acquire

Acquiring the Friesian Sport Horse is easy. You can buy it from the Star Stable online store using your Star Coins. Step by Step Guide

  2. Go to the Star Stable online store.
  3. Select the Friesian Sport Horse from the list of horses.
  4. Click on the "Buy" button.
  5. Confirm your purchase by entering your payment details.
  6. Wait for the horse to be added to your stable.

Tips

Now that you have the most expensive horse in Star Stable, here are some tips on how to take care of it:

  • Feed it regularly to keep its health and energy levels high.
  • Groom it daily to keep its coat shiny and healthy.
  • Train it regularly to improve its skills and abilities.
  • Take it to the vet if it gets sick or injured.
  • Participate in races and quests to earn rewards and improve your horse's skills.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the most expensive horse in Star Stable:

Hair Care

One important aspect of taking care of your Friesian Sport Horse is its hair care. Here are some tips on how to keep your horse's hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Brush its hair daily to prevent tangles and matting.
  • Use a detangler spray to make brushing easier.
  • Trim its mane and tail regularly to keep them neat and tidy.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for horses to keep their hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, on your horse's hair.

With these tips, you can ensure that your Friesian Sport Horse stays healthy, beautiful, and ready for any adventure in Star Stable!
