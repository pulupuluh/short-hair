How To Hide Your Hair With Extensions A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi May 15, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, extensions can be a great option. However, sometimes you don't want your natural hair to show through the extensions. In this guide, we'll go over how to hide your hair with extensions to achieve a seamless look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Hide Your Hair with Extensions
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Hiding Your Hair with Extensions
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Extensions

How to Hide Your Hair with Extensions

There are a few different methods you can use to hide your natural hair with extensions. The most popular methods are using a weave or a wig.

With a weave, your natural hair is braided close to the scalp and the extensions are sewn onto the braids. This method is great for adding length and volume to your hair.

Wigs are another popular option for hiding your natural hair. With a wig, your natural hair is cornrowed or braided close to the scalp and the wig is placed on top. This method is great for protecting your natural hair while still being able to change up your hairstyle.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for hiding your hair with extensions using a weave:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Braid your hair close to the scalp.
  3. Sew the extensions onto the braids.
  4. Cut and style the extensions to your desired length and shape.

Here's a step-by-step guide for hiding your hair with extensions using a wig:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Cornrow or braid your hair close to the scalp.
  3. Place the wig on top of your cornrows or braids.
  4. Adjust the wig to your desired style.

Tips for Hiding Your Hair with Extensions

Here are some tips to keep in mind when hiding your hair with extensions:

  • Make sure your natural hair is clean and conditioned before braiding or cornrowing it.
  • Use a wig cap to help keep your natural hair in place and to prevent the wig from slipping.
  • Choose the right type of extensions for your hair type and texture.
  • Be gentle when styling your extensions to prevent damage.
  • Don't leave your extensions in for too long. Take them out after a few weeks to give your natural hair a break.

FAQs

Can I hide my short hair with extensions?

Yes, you can hide short hair with extensions. However, you may need to use a wig if your hair is too short to braid.

Can I wash my hair with extensions?

Yes, you can wash your hair with extensions. However, you'll need to be gentle and use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent damage to the extensions.

How long can I leave my extensions in?

It's best to take your extensions out after a few weeks to give your natural hair a break. Leaving them in for too long can cause damage and breakage.

Hair Care Tips for Extensions

Here are some tips to keep your extensions looking their best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to the extensions.
  • Be gentle when brushing or styling your extensions to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your extensions as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Don't sleep with wet extensions as this can cause tangling and damage.
  • Take your extensions out after a few weeks to give your natural hair a break.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to hide your hair with extensions and achieve a seamless look.
