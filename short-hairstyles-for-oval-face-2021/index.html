Description

Oval faces are considered the perfect face shape as they are well proportioned and balanced. However, selecting the right hairstyle can make them look even more attractive. In 2021, short hairstyles for oval faces have become extremely popular due to their easy maintenance and modern look. Whether you want a choppy, layered cut or a sleek bob, there are plenty of options to choose from. This article will guide you through the process of selecting the perfect short hairstyle for your oval face in 2021.

How to Choose Short Hairstyles for Oval Face 2021

When selecting a short hairstyle for oval face, it is important to consider the following factors:

  • Face Shape: Although oval face shapes are versatile, it is important to select a hairstyle that complements your face shape. For instance, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Texture: The texture of your hair plays a crucial role in selecting a short hairstyle. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a layered cut to add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a sleek bob to keep your hair under control.
  • Lifestyle: Your lifestyle also plays a crucial role in selecting a short hairstyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, you may want to opt for a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling time.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to selecting the perfect short hairstyle for your oval face:

  1. Start by determining your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and studying the shape of your face in the mirror.
  2. Consider your hair texture and thickness. This will help you select a hairstyle that complements your hair type.
  3. Research short hairstyles for oval faces online or in magazines. Look for styles that complement your face shape and hair type.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which short hairstyle would suit you best. They can provide recommendations based on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.
  5. Once you have selected a short hairstyle, take care of your hair by washing and conditioning it regularly. Use a hair serum to add shine and protect your hair from heat damage.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Oval Face 2021

Here are some tips for maintaining short hairstyles for oval faces:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Use a hair serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and style.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will a short hairstyle make my oval face look too long?

A: No, a short hairstyle can actually enhance the proportions of your oval face and make it look more balanced.

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a short hairstyle for oval face?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs with a short hairstyle. However, it is important to select bangs that complement your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Oval Face 2021

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair with a short hairstyle:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly with high-quality products.
  • Use a hair serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.

Overall, short hairstyles for oval face in 2021 are a great way to update your look and enhance your natural beauty. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can select a style that complements your unique features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
