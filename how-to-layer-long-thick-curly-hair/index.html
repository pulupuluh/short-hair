How To Layer Long Thick Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Feb 23, 2023 · 6 min read

Layering long thick curly hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right technique, you can achieve a stylish and manageable look. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the process of layering your long, thick, curly hair to help you achieve your desired look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Layer Long Thick Curly Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Layering Long Thick Curly Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Long Thick Curly Hair

How to Layer Long Thick Curly Hair

Layering long thick curly hair involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create depth and volume. The main goal is to remove some of the bulk while keeping the curls intact. Here are some things to keep in mind when layering your long, thick, curly hair:

  • Consider the length of your hair and the final look you want to achieve.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Work with small sections of hair to ensure even layering.
  • Keep the curls intact while cutting to maintain the texture of your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to layer your long, thick, curly hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair, then gently towel-dry it.
  2. Part your hair as you normally would.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the top of your head and comb it out.
  4. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, then cut it at an angle.
  5. Move to the next section and repeat the process, cutting at a slightly different angle to create depth and volume.
  6. Continue this process until you’ve layered all of your hair.
  7. Check for any uneven spots and trim them as needed.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Layering Long Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when layering your long, thick, curly hair:

  • Consider getting a professional haircut if you’re not confident in your own abilities.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and enhance your curls.
  • Don’t overdo it with the layers, as too many can make your hair look thin and stringy.
  • Be patient and take your time, as rushing can lead to uneven layers.

FAQs

How often should I get my long thick curly hair layered?

It’s recommended to get your hair layered every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Can I layer my own long thick curly hair?

While it’s possible to layer your own hair, it’s recommended to seek the help of a professional stylist to ensure an even and balanced cut.

Will layering damage my long thick curly hair?

No, as long as you use a sharp pair of scissors and work with small sections of hair, layering should not damage your hair.

Hair Care for Long Thick Curly Hair

Maintaining healthy long thick curly hair requires a consistent hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent tangling and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and manageable look for your long thick curly hair. Remember to be patient and take your time, as layering requires precision and attention to detail.
