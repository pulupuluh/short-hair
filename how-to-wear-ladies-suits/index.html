How To Wear Ladies Suits

Written by Bardi May 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a woman looking to make a statement with your outfit, then a ladies suit is definitely the way to go. A well-tailored suit can make you look confident, professional, and stylish all at the same time. In this article, we'll take you through the steps on how to wear ladies suits and provide you with some tips on how to style them.

How to Choose the Right Ladies Suit

The first step in wearing a ladies suit is choosing the right one. When shopping for a suit, keep the following tips in mind:

Choose a Suit That Fits Your Body Type

Not all suits are created equal, and not all suits will flatter your body type. For instance, if you have an hourglass figure, opt for a suit that cinches at the waist to accentuate your curves. If you have a rectangular figure, go for a suit that has a bit of structure to give the illusion of curves.

Choose a Suit That Matches Your Skin Tone

Wearing a suit that complements your skin tone is important. If you have a warm skin tone, go for suits in colors like brown, beige, and gold. For cooler skin tones, opt for suits in colors like blue, green, and silver.

Choose a Suit That Fits Your Personal Style

Your suit should reflect your personal style. If you prefer a more classic look, opt for a navy or black suit. If you like to stand out, go for a suit in a bright color or with a bold pattern.

How to Wear a Ladies Suit

Now that you've chosen the right suit, it's time to put it on. Here's how:

Start with a Good Foundation

Make sure you have the right undergarments to wear with your suit. A good bra and panties can make all the difference in how your suit looks.

Accessorize with the Right Shoes

The right shoes can make or break your outfit. Opt for pumps or loafers in a neutral color, or choose a pair of statement shoes to add some personality.

Choose the Right Jewelry

Jewelry can help tie your outfit together. Choose simple pieces for a more professional look, or go for bolder pieces to make a statement.

Finish with a Handbag

A handbag is the perfect finishing touch to any outfit. Opt for a structured bag in a neutral color, or choose a bold color to add some personality.

Tips for Wearing Ladies Suits

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when wearing a ladies suit:

- Make sure your suit is properly tailored to your body - Avoid wearing too much jewelry - Choose a suit in a wrinkle-resistant fabric for easy maintenance - Experiment with different accessories to change up your look

Frequently Asked Questions

Can ladies suits be worn to casual events?

Yes, ladies suits can be worn to casual events. Opt for a suit in a lighter fabric like linen, and pair it with sandals and a casual handbag.

Do I need to wear a tie with my ladies suit?

No, you don't need to wear a tie with your ladies suit. Opt for a blouse or button-up shirt instead.

What hairstyle should I wear with a ladies suit?

When wearing a ladies suit, opt for a hairstyle that complements your outfit. A sleek ponytail or a low bun can look professional and polished.

Hair Care for Ladies Suits

When wearing a ladies suit, it's important to keep your hair looking polished. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny. If you're short on time, try a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes. And don't forget to use a heat protectant when using hot tools on your hair.

Now that you know how to wear ladies suits, it's time to go out and make a statement with your outfit. Remember to choose the right suit for your body type and personal style, and accessorize with the right shoes, jewelry, and handbag. With these tips, you'll be sure to turn heads wherever you go!
