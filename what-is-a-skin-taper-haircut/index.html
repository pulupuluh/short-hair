What Is A Skin Taper Haircut

Written by Robby May 22, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, a skin taper might be just what you need. This haircut is versatile and works for all hair types, including straight, curly, and wavy hair. In this article, we’ll explain what a skin taper haircut is, how to get it, and how to care for it.

Description

A skin taper haircut is a type of fade haircut that involves shaving the hair on the sides and back of the head very close to the skin, gradually increasing the length as it approaches the top of the head. The hair at the top of the head is left longer and can be styled in many ways. The result is a clean, sharp, and stylish look that is perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

How to Get a Skin Taper Haircut

If you want to get a skin taper haircut, you can either visit a professional barber or do it yourself at home. However, we recommend visiting a barber who specializes in this type of haircut to ensure that you get the best results.

Here’s what you can expect during your appointment:

Step 1: Consultation

The barber will ask you what type of skin taper haircut you want and will recommend the best style for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Preparation

The barber will shampoo and condition your hair to prepare it for the haircut.

Step 3: Cutting

The barber will use clippers to shave the sides and back of your head, gradually increasing the length as it approaches the top. They will then use scissors to trim the hair at the top of your head to the desired length.

Step 4: Styling

The barber will style your hair using a comb and hair gel or pomade to achieve the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining a Skin Taper Haircut

Here are some tips to keep your skin taper haircut looking sharp:

  • Visit your barber every 2-3 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Style your hair using a comb and hair gel or pomade for a polished look.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and cold weather by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

FAQ

What is the difference between a skin taper and a bald fade?

A skin taper involves shaving the hair on the sides and back of the head very close to the skin, gradually increasing the length as it approaches the top. A bald fade, on the other hand, involves shaving the hair on the sides and back of the head completely bald, gradually increasing the length as it approaches the top.

Can I style my skin taper haircut in different ways?

Yes, you can style your skin taper haircut in many ways, depending on your personal preferences and the occasion. For a casual look, you can leave your hair messy and tousled. For a formal look, you can slick your hair back or part it to the side.

Is a skin taper haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a skin taper haircut works for all face shapes. However, your barber may recommend a specific style based on your face shape to enhance your features and balance your proportions.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain a healthy and stylish skin taper haircut. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair 2-3 times a week with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners, curling irons, and blow dryers, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair protectant spray when you’re exposed to the sun, wind, or cold weather.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation and promote hair growth.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to nourish your hair from the inside out.

A skin taper haircut is a stylish and versatile haircut that can work for all hair types and face shapes. With proper maintenance and care, you can keep your hair looking sharp and polished. Visit your barber regularly and follow these tips to maintain a healthy and stylish skin taper haircut.
