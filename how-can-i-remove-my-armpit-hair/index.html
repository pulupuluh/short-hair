How Can I Remove My Armpit Hair

Written by Petter Jul 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Armpit hair is a common concern for many people, especially during the summer months when sleeveless shirts and dresses are in fashion. If you're wondering how to remove your armpit hair, you're not alone. There are several methods to choose from, including shaving, waxing, and using hair removal creams. In this article, we'll explore these options and provide tips for hair care after removal.

How to Remove Armpit Hair

Shaving

Shaving is the most common method of removing armpit hair. It's quick, easy, and can be done at home. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Cleanse the area with warm water and soap to soften the hair.
  2. Apply shaving cream or gel to the armpit.
  3. Use a sharp razor to shave the hair in the direction of growth.
  4. Rinse the area with cool water and pat dry with a clean towel.

Shaving can cause irritation and ingrown hairs. To prevent this, exfoliate the area before shaving and avoid using dull razors. Also, use a moisturizing lotion after shaving to soothe the skin.

Waxing

Waxing is a longer-lasting method of removing armpit hair. It involves applying warm wax to the area and then removing it with a strip of cloth. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Cleanse the area with warm water and soap to soften the hair.
  2. Apply warm wax to the armpit in the direction of hair growth.
  3. Place a cloth strip over the wax and press down firmly.
  4. Quickly pull the strip off in the opposite direction of hair growth.
  5. Rinse the area with cool water and pat dry with a clean towel.

Waxing can be painful and may cause redness and irritation. To prevent this, avoid waxing during your period and take a pain reliever before waxing. Also, avoid waxing over cuts or irritated skin.

Hair Removal Creams

Hair removal creams are another option for removing armpit hair. They work by dissolving the hair at the root. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Cleanse the area with warm water and soap to soften the hair.
  2. Apply the hair removal cream to the armpit.
  3. Leave the cream on for the recommended time.
  4. Wipe off the cream with a damp cloth.
  5. Rinse the area with cool water and pat dry with a clean towel.

Hair removal creams can cause skin irritation and should not be used on broken or irritated skin. To prevent this, do a patch test before using the cream and follow the instructions carefully.

Tips for Hair Care After Removal

After removing your armpit hair, it's important to take care of the skin to prevent irritation and ingrown hairs. Here are some tips:

  • Exfoliate the area regularly to prevent ingrown hairs.
  • Avoid tight clothing that can irritate the skin.
  • Moisturize the area with a hydrating lotion to soothe the skin.
  • Avoid deodorants and antiperspirants for at least 24 hours after hair removal.

FAQ

Is it safe to remove armpit hair?

Yes, it is safe to remove armpit hair. However, some methods, such as waxing and hair removal creams, can cause skin irritation and ingrown hairs. It's important to follow the instructions carefully and take care of the skin after removal.

How often should I remove my armpit hair?

It depends on your personal preference and hair growth rate. Shaving should be done every few days, while waxing and hair removal creams can last for several weeks.

Can I remove my armpit hair at home?

Yes, all the methods mentioned in this article can be done at home. However, if you're unsure about the process or have sensitive skin, it's recommended to consult a professional.

Hair Care is Important

Removing armpit hair is a personal choice, and there are several methods to choose from. It's important to take care of the skin after removal to prevent irritation and ingrown hairs. Remember to follow the instructions carefully and consult a professional if you're unsure about the process. With these tips, you can keep your armpits smooth and hair-free all year round.
