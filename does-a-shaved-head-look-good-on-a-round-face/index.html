Does A Shaved Head Look Good On A Round Face

Written by Frank Jul 30, 2023 · 10 min read

Are you considering shaving your head but unsure if it will suit your round face? Well, you’re not alone. Many people wonder if a shaved head will look good on a round face. In this article, we will explore this question and provide tips on how to rock a bald look with a round face.

Description

A shaved head can be a bold and empowering look. It’s a style that requires confidence and a bit of maintenance. However, if you have a round face, you might be hesitant to try it out. Round faces are characterized by their soft, curved lines, and lack of sharp angles.

So, does a shaved head look good on a round face? The answer is yes! A shaved head can look great on any face shape, including a round face. It’s all about finding the right style and embracing the look with confidence.

How to

If you’re ready to take the plunge and shave your head, here are some tips to ensure you rock the bald look with a round face:

1. Find the right style

There are many styles to choose from when it comes to a shaved head. However, for a round face, it’s best to go for a style that adds some height to the top of your head. This will create the illusion of length and balance out the roundness of your face.

You can also consider adding some facial hair to create contrast and definition. A well-groomed beard or mustache can complement a shaved head and add some edge to your look.

2. Embrace your confidence

Rocking a bald look requires confidence. It’s a bold move that will definitely turn heads. However, if you’re not confident in your new look, it will show. So, embrace your confidence and own your new style.

Remember, a shaved head can be a powerful and empowering look. It’s a symbol of strength and self-assurance. So, wear it with pride.

3. Maintain your look

A shaved head requires some maintenance to keep it looking sharp. Be sure to shave regularly to keep your head smooth and free of stubble. You can also consider using a moisturizer to keep your scalp hydrated and healthy.

Invest in a good razor and shaving cream to ensure a clean and smooth shave. And, if you’re not confident in your shaving skills, consider going to a professional barber for a clean shave.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow if you want to shave your head:

Step 1: Trim your hair

Before you shave your head, it’s best to trim your hair as short as possible. This will make it easier to shave and reduce the risk of clogging your razor. Use clippers or scissors to trim your hair evenly.

Step 2: Wet your head

Wet your head with warm water to soften the hair and open up the pores. This will make it easier to shave and reduce the risk of irritation.

Step 3: Apply shaving cream

Apply shaving cream evenly over your head. This will help lubricate the skin and reduce the risk of cuts and irritation. Be sure to choose a shaving cream that is designed for sensitive skin.

Step 4: Shave your head

Using a sharp razor, shave your head in the direction of hair growth. Be sure to use light pressure and take your time. Rinse the razor frequently to prevent clogging.

Step 5: Rinse and moisturize

Once you’ve finished shaving, rinse your head with cool water to close the pores. Pat your head dry with a towel and apply a moisturizer to keep your scalp hydrated and healthy.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you rock a shaved head with a round face:

1. Experiment with different styles

Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best. You can try adding some texture to your hair or playing around with different lengths.

Remember, a shaved head doesn’t have to be boring. There are many ways to make it unique and stylish.

2. Consider your skin tone

When choosing a style, consider your skin tone. If you have a lighter skin tone, a bald head can create a striking contrast. However, if you have a darker skin tone, you might want to consider adding some facial hair to create definition.

3. Wear sunscreen

When you shave your head, your scalp is exposed to the sun. Be sure to wear sunscreen to protect your skin from UV rays. You can also wear a hat or headscarf to provide additional protection.

FAQ

Q: Will shaving my head make my hair grow back thicker?

A: No, shaving your head will not make your hair grow back thicker. This is a common myth. Shaving only cuts the hair at the surface level and does not affect the hair follicles or growth rate.

Q: Will shaving my head make me look bald?

A: Yes, shaving your head will make you look bald. However, it can also create a bold and stylish look. It all depends on how you wear it.

Q: Is a shaved head high maintenance?

A: Shaving your head requires some maintenance to keep it looking sharp. You will need to shave regularly and moisturize your scalp to keep it healthy. However, it’s not necessarily high maintenance.

Hair Care

Even though you’ve shaved your head, it’s still important to take care of your scalp. Here are some tips for maintaining a healthy scalp:

1. Moisturize

Use a moisturizer to keep your scalp hydrated and healthy. Dry scalp can lead to itching and flaking.

2. Protect your scalp

When you’re out in the sun, be sure to wear a hat or headscarf to protect your scalp from UV rays.

3. Exfoliate

Exfoliate your scalp regularly to remove dead skin cells and prevent clogged pores.

In conclusion, a shaved head can look good on a round face. It’s all about finding the right style and embracing your confidence. With the right maintenance, a shaved head can be a powerful and empowering look. So, go ahead and rock that bald look!
