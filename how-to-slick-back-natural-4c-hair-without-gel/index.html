How To Slick Back Natural 4C Hair Without Gel

Written by Frank Aug 01, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have natural 4c hair, you may have struggled to find a way to slick back your hair without using gel. While gel can be a useful tool, it can also cause flaking and dryness. Fortunately, there are other methods you can use to slick back your hair without gel. In this article, we’ll go over how to slick back your natural 4c hair without gel, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Back Natural 4c Hair Without Gel
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Back Natural 4c Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Natural 4c Hair

How to Slick Back Natural 4c Hair Without Gel

There are a few different methods you can try to slick back your natural 4c hair without using gel. Here are a few options:

Bobby Pins

One option is to use bobby pins to slick back your hair. Start by parting your hair into sections and brushing it back. Then, use bobby pins to secure the hair in place. You can also use a headband to hold the hair back.

Edge Control

Another option is to use edge control products. These products are designed to slick back the hair and hold it in place. Look for products that are formulated for natural hair and don’t contain harsh chemicals.

Twists or Braids

You can also try twists or braids to slick back your hair. Simply part your hair into sections and twist or braid each section. Then, use pins or hair ties to secure the twists or braids in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to slicking back your natural 4c hair without gel:

  1. Start by parting your hair into sections.
  2. Brush your hair back using a soft-bristled brush.
  3. Use bobby pins or a headband to secure the hair in place.
  4. If using edge control, apply a small amount to the edges of your hair and brush it back.
  5. If using twists or braids, part your hair into sections and twist or braid each section. Secure with pins or hair ties.

Tips for Slicking Back Natural 4c Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when slicking back your natural 4c hair:

  • Use a soft-bristled brush to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don’t apply too much edge control or other products, as this can cause buildup and dryness.
  • Experiment with different methods to find what works best for your hair.
  • Be gentle when removing bobby pins or hair ties to avoid pulling or breaking your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicking back natural 4c hair:

Can I use gel to slick back my natural 4c hair?

While gel can be used to slick back your hair, it can also cause flaking and dryness. We recommend trying other methods first, such as bobby pins or edge control products.

What is the best edge control product for natural 4c hair?

There are many edge control products designed specifically for natural hair. Look for products that don’t contain harsh chemicals and are formulated for your hair type.

How often should I slick back my natural 4c hair?

You can slick back your hair as often as you’d like, but be sure to give your hair a break in between styles to avoid damage.

Hair Care for Natural 4c Hair

It’s important to take care of your natural 4c hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it strong.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin cap or using a satin pillowcase.

Slicking back your natural 4c hair without gel is possible with the right techniques and products. Experiment with different methods to find what works best for your hair, and be sure to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
