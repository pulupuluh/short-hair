Can Hair Pattern Change As You Get Older

Written by Bardi Jul 20, 2023 · 7 min read

As we age, our bodies go through a lot of changes, and our hair is no exception. One of the most common questions people ask is whether their hair pattern can change as they get older. The answer is yes, it can. In this blog post, we’ll take a closer look at why hair pattern changes and what you can do to care for your hair as you age.

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Hair pattern refers to the way your hair grows and falls naturally. This can include the direction it grows, the texture, and the thickness of individual strands. Hair pattern can change throughout your life due to a variety of factors, including hormonal changes, genetics, and environmental factors.

As you age, your body goes through hormonal changes that can affect your hair pattern. For example, menopause can cause hair to become thinner and drier. Additionally, as we age, our hair follicles can become less active, leading to a decrease in hair growth and a change in hair texture.

How to

If you’re concerned about changes in your hair pattern, there are a few things you can do to care for your hair:

1. Use gentle hair products

Choose hair products that are gentle on your hair and scalp. Look for products that are sulfate-free, paraben-free, and alcohol-free. These ingredients can strip your hair of its natural oils, leading to dryness and breakage.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and lead to a change in hair pattern. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature on the low side.

3. Get regular trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can lead to a change in hair pattern. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Step by step

If you’re noticing a change in your hair pattern, here are some steps you can take:

1. Consult with a stylist

A professional stylist can help you determine what’s causing the change in your hair pattern and recommend products and treatments to help.

2. Consider a new hairstyle

If you’re unhappy with your new hair pattern, consider changing your hairstyle. A new cut or style can help you embrace your new hair pattern and feel more confident.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for caring for your hair as you age:

1. Eat a healthy diet

A healthy diet can help keep your hair healthy and strong. Make sure to eat plenty of fruits and vegetables, lean protein, and healthy fats.

2. Stay hydrated

Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated from the inside out.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

The sun can damage your hair and lead to a change in hair pattern. Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray when you’re out in the sun.

FAQ

Can stress cause a change in hair pattern?

Yes, stress can cause a change in hair pattern. Stress can lead to hair loss, thinning, and breakage.

Can medication cause a change in hair pattern?

Yes, certain medications can cause a change in hair pattern. Talk to your doctor or pharmacist if you’re concerned about the side effects of a medication.

Is it normal for hair to become thinner as you age?

Yes, it’s normal for hair to become thinner as you age. This is due to hormonal changes and a decrease in hair follicle activity.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair as you age is important to maintain a healthy hair pattern. Use gentle hair products, avoid heat styling, and get regular trims. Consult with a stylist if you’re concerned about changes in your hair pattern, and consider a new hairstyle to embrace your new hair pattern.

Remember to eat a healthy diet, stay hydrated, and protect your hair from the sun. And if you’re experiencing hair loss or other significant changes in your hair pattern, consult with a healthcare professional to rule out underlying medical issues.
