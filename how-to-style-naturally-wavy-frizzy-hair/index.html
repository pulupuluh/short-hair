How To Style Naturally Wavy Frizzy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jun 12, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have naturally wavy frizzy hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it. However, with the right techniques and products, you can turn your wild waves into beautiful, bouncy curls. In this article, we’ll discuss how to style naturally wavy frizzy hair, from the basics to advanced techniques.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Naturally Wavy Frizzy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Naturally Wavy Frizzy Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Naturally Wavy Frizzy Hair

How to Style Naturally Wavy Frizzy Hair

The first step to styling naturally wavy frizzy hair is to embrace your natural texture. Don’t fight your waves – work with them. Follow these steps to style your hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse with cool water to help seal the cuticle and reduce frizz.
  2. Gently towel-dry your hair, then apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and reduce frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp and scrunch your hair upward to encourage your curls to form. Use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to touch up any areas that need more definition. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to protect your hair from damage.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to help your curls hold their shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a more detailed step-by-step guide to styling naturally wavy frizzy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically formulated for curly hair, as they will help enhance your natural texture.
  2. Gently towel-dry your hair. Don’t rub your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair. Work the product through your hair with your fingers, using a wide-tooth comb to detangle any knots. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  4. Flip your head upside down and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp and scrunch your hair upward to encourage your curls to form. Use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to touch up any areas that need more definition. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the barrel of the curling iron or wand, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to protect your hair from damage.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to help your curls hold their shape. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look flat.

Tips for Styling Naturally Wavy Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling naturally wavy frizzy hair:

  • Don’t brush your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it’s wet.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz. Try to air dry your hair as much as possible.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • If you have particularly stubborn frizz, try using a hair serum or oil to smooth your hair and add shine.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about styling naturally wavy frizzy hair:

What’s the best way to dry naturally wavy frizzy hair?

The best way to dry naturally wavy frizzy hair is to use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help enhance your curls and reduce frizz. You can also try air drying your hair, but be prepared for more frizz and less defined curls.

How often should I wash my naturally wavy frizzy hair?

It’s best to wash your naturally wavy frizzy hair every 2-3 days. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make it more prone to frizz and damage.

What’s the best haircut for naturally wavy frizzy hair?

The best haircut for naturally wavy frizzy hair is one that enhances your curls and reduces bulk. Layers can help add movement and shape to your hair, while a blunt cut can make your hair look thicker and fuller.

Hair Care for Naturally Wavy Frizzy Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of naturally wavy frizzy hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help hydrate your hair and reduce frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz. Try to air dry your hair as much as possible.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Get regular trims to help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your naturally wavy frizzy hair with confidence. Embrace your natural texture, and experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you.
