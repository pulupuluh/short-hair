How Long Would It Take To Grow Dreads

Table of Contents:

Description

Dreads, also known as dreadlocks, are a popular hairstyle that has become increasingly popular in recent years. This hairstyle involves twisting or matting hair into long, ropelike strands that can be worn loose or styled into various updos. Many people wonder how long it would take to grow dreads, and the answer varies depending on several factors such as hair texture, thickness, and length.

How to Grow Dreads

Growing dreads involves a natural process that requires patience and care. The first step is to stop combing or brushing your hair, which allows it to begin matting and twisting on its own. Some people choose to use wax or other products to help speed up the process, but this can be damaging to hair and is not recommended.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to growing dreads:

  1. Stop combing or brushing your hair.
  2. Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  3. Separate your hair into sections and twist or braid each section tightly.
  4. Leave your hair alone and let it begin to mat and twist on its own.
  5. Continue to wash your hair regularly and separate sections as needed.
  6. Be patient and let your dreads grow naturally.

Tips for Growing Dreads

Here are some tips to help you grow healthy dreads:

  • Avoid using wax or other products that can damage hair.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo to prevent buildup.
  • Use a light oil or spray to keep dreads moisturized.
  • Separate sections regularly to prevent matting.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons or curling irons.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing dreads:

How long does it take to grow dreads?

The length of time it takes to grow dreads varies depending on hair texture, thickness, and length. It can take anywhere from six months to two years to fully grow and mature dreads.

Can you grow dreads with relaxed hair?

Yes, you can grow dreads with relaxed hair, but it may take longer to achieve the desired texture and thickness. It is important to use gentle, natural hair care products and avoid using heat styling tools.

Can you wash your hair with dreads?

Yes, you can and should wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo to prevent buildup and keep dreads clean and healthy.

Hair Care for Dreads

Proper hair care is essential for healthy dreads. Here are some tips for taking care of your dreads:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Avoid using wax or other products that can damage hair.
  • Use a light oil or spray to keep dreads moisturized.
  • Separate sections regularly to prevent matting.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons or curling irons.

With patience and proper care, you can achieve healthy, beautiful dreads that will be a unique expression of your personal style.
