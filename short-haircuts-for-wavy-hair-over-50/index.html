Short Haircuts For Wavy Hair Over 50 A Guide For Effortless Style

Written by Aprilia Mar 13, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you a woman over 50 who has wavy hair and wants to switch things up with a short haircut? Look no further! In this article, we will explore the best short haircuts for wavy hair over 50, as well as provide tips and tricks for maintaining your new ‘do.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for You
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Wavy Hair Over 50

  • Hair Care for Short Haircuts for Wavy Hair Over 50

Description

Short haircuts for wavy hair over 50 are perfect for women who want to simplify their hair care routine and achieve a chic, low-maintenance look. Wavy hair can be prone to frizz, but with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can embrace your natural texture and rock a trendy, youthful style.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for You

When choosing a short haircut for wavy hair over 50, it’s important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Some popular options include:

  • Pixie cut: This short, layered haircut is perfect for women with thick, wavy hair who want a low-maintenance style that highlights their facial features.
  • Bob cut: The classic bob is a timeless style that works well for wavy hair. Whether you opt for a chin-length or shoulder-length cut, a bob can flatter any face shape and add volume to your hair.
  • Shag cut: The shag is a layered, textured cut that works well for wavy hair. It’s a great option for women who want a playful, edgy style that’s easy to maintain.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Wavy Hair Over 50

Styling short haircuts for wavy hair over 50 doesn’t have to be complicated. Follow these simple steps for effortless style:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a quarter-sized amount of styling cream or mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots and ends.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to gently dry your hair, scrunching it as you go to encourage your natural waves.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to define your waves and add texture.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

To keep your short haircut looking fresh and stylish, try these tips:

  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain your haircut’s shape.
  • Invest in a high-quality diffuser attachment for your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair.

FAQs about Short Haircuts for Wavy Hair Over 50

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for wavy hair over 50:

  • Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?
  • A: Not necessarily! A well-executed short haircut can actually make you look more youthful and vibrant.
  • Q: Can I still style my hair if I have a short cut?
  • A: Yes! Short haircuts are versatile and can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and polished to messy and textured.
  • Q: What should I do if my wavy hair is prone to frizz?
  • A: Use a smoothing serum or cream before styling to tame frizz and add shine.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts for Wavy Hair Over 50

Short haircuts for wavy hair over 50 require minimal maintenance, but it’s still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet, as this is when it’s most vulnerable to breakage.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair too much when styling, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock a short haircut for wavy hair over 50 with confidence and ease. Remember, the key to effortless style is embracing your natural texture and finding a haircut that flatters your unique features.
