Will My Face Look Fat With Short Hair

Written by William Mar 29, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're considering cutting your hair short, you might be wondering whether it will flatter your face shape or make it look rounder. The truth is, short hair can be just as flattering as long hair, but it all depends on your face shape and the right haircut for you. In this article, we'll answer the question "will my face look fat with short hair" and give you some tips and tricks to make sure you choose the perfect short haircut for your face shape.

Description

If you have a round face shape, you may be hesitant to try out a short haircut, fearing it will only make your face look fuller. However, the right short haircut can actually help to elongate your face and make it look slimmer. It's all about finding the right style that complements your face shape and features.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Face Shape

The key to finding the right short haircut for your face shape is to choose a style that complements your features and helps to elongate your face. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right short haircut is to determine your face shape. There are six face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. You can determine your face shape by measuring the width and length of your face and looking for certain features that are unique to each shape.

2. Research Hairstyles for Your Face Shape

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to research hairstyles that will flatter your features. Look for hairstyles that are designed for your face shape and consider the length that will work best for you.

3. Consider Your Hair Texture

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a shorter cut that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a longer bob or shaggy cut may be more flattering.

4. Consult with a Professional

If you're still unsure about which short haircut will flatter your face shape, consider consulting with a professional hairstylist. They can help you determine your face shape and recommend a haircut that will work best for you.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing a Short Haircut

Here is a step by step guide to help you choose the perfect short haircut:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Measure the width and length of your face and look for unique features that correspond to each face shape.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles for Your Face Shape

Look for hairstyles that are designed for your face shape and consider the length that will work best for you.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Texture

Think about your hair texture and what length and style will work best for you.

Step 4: Consult with a Professional

If you're still unsure, consider consulting with a professional hairstylist for expert advice.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Once you've found the perfect short haircut, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut:

1. Keep Your Hair Clean

Wash your hair regularly to keep it looking fresh and clean.

2. Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for your hair type and texture to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

3. Schedule Regular Trims

Regular trims will help to keep your short haircut looking sharp and prevent split ends.

4. Style Your Hair with Care

Be gentle when styling your hair to avoid damaging it and use heat protectant products when using hot tools.

FAQ

Q: Will my face look fat with short hair?

A: Not necessarily. The right short haircut can actually help to elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

Q: What short haircut is best for a round face?

A: A pixie cut, angled bob, or layered bob can be a flattering short haircut for a round face.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it looking fresh and clean.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your short hair looking its best:

1. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a protective spray to prevent sun damage to your hair.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Too Often

Try to limit the use of hot tools to avoid damaging your hair.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

4. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will help to keep your short haircut looking sharp and prevent split ends.

Overall, there's no need to worry about whether your face will look fat with short hair. By following these tips and choosing the right short haircut for your face shape, you can rock a stylish and flattering short haircut with confidence!
