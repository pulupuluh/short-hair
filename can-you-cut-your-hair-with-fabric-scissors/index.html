Can You Cut Your Hair With Fabric Scissors

Written by Oliver Jun 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're in desperate need of a haircut and you're stuck at home without proper hair cutting tools, you may be considering using fabric scissors to get the job done. But can you really cut your hair with fabric scissors? In this article, we'll explore this question and provide you with everything you need to know about cutting your hair with fabric scissors.

Description

Fabric scissors are a popular tool used for cutting fabric, but can they be used to cut hair? The short answer is, technically, yes. However, just because you can, doesn't mean you should. Using fabric scissors to cut your hair can result in uneven cuts and damage to your hair.

How to Cut Your Hair with Fabric Scissors

If you've decided to cut your hair with fabric scissors, here's a step-by-step guide to help you through the process:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will help you see any split ends or uneven areas that need to be trimmed.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using hair clips, section your hair into manageable parts. This will make it easier for you to cut your hair evenly.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Using the fabric scissors, start cutting your hair in small increments. It's important to remember to only cut a little at a time to avoid any drastic mistakes.

Step 4: Check Your Progress

After you've cut a small section of your hair, check your progress in the mirror. If you're happy with the length and shape, move on to the next section. If not, make any necessary adjustments.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You may want to use a straightener or curling iron to add volume or texture.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair with Fabric Scissors

While we don't recommend using fabric scissors to cut your hair, if you're going to do it anyway, here are some tips to help you:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair
  • Cut small sections at a time
  • Make sure you're cutting your hair evenly
  • Don't cut too much off at once
  • Be patient and take your time

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with any type of scissors?

While you technically can cut your hair with any type of scissors, we recommend using proper hair cutting scissors to avoid any damage or uneven cuts.

Will cutting my hair with fabric scissors damage it?

Yes, using fabric scissors to cut your hair can result in damage to your hair, split ends, and uneven cuts.

Can I fix a bad haircut if I use fabric scissors?

If you've made a mistake while cutting your hair with fabric scissors, it may be difficult to fix. We recommend seeking professional help to avoid making the situation worse.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to avoid any damage or breakage. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy
  • Use hair masks and oils to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair

Conclusion

While you technically can cut your hair with fabric scissors, it's not recommended. Using proper hair cutting scissors will give you better results and avoid any damage to your hair. If you're unsure about cutting your hair yourself, it's best to seek professional help to avoid any mistakes.
