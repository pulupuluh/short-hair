Best Short Haircuts For Thick Straight Hair

Written by Oliver May 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of dealing with your thick straight hair every day? Do you want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks great? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for thick straight hair that will make your mornings a breeze.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

If you have thick straight hair, you know that finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that will work with your hair's natural texture without requiring too much maintenance. Fortunately, there are several short haircuts that are perfect for thick straight hair.

How to

When considering a short haircut for thick straight hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want a style that will flatter your face shape. Second, you want a cut that will work with your hair's natural texture. Finally, you want a style that is easy to maintain.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow to get the best short haircut for your thick straight hair:

  1. Find inspiration: Look online or in magazines for short haircuts that catch your eye.
  2. Consult with your stylist: Bring your inspiration photos to your stylist and discuss which cuts will work best for your face shape and hair texture.
  3. Choose a length: Decide how short you want to go. A bob or a pixie cut are both great options for thick straight hair.
  4. Add layers: Layers can add texture and movement to your hair. Ask your stylist to add layers to your cut if you want a more dynamic look.
  5. Style your hair: Once your cut is complete, experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut for your thick straight hair:

  • Consider your face shape: Some cuts will flatter certain face shapes better than others.
  • Think about your lifestyle: Choose a cut that will work with your daily routine and won't require too much maintenance.
  • Be open to change: If you're used to longer hair, a short cut can be a big change. Be open to trying something new.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for thick straight hair:

What are the best short haircuts for thick straight hair?

The best short haircuts for thick straight hair are bobs, pixie cuts, and layered cuts.

Will a short haircut work with my face shape?

There are short haircuts that will flatter every face shape. Consult with your stylist to find the best cut for you.

How should I style my short haircut?

Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the look that works best for you. Your stylist can also give you tips on how to style your new cut.

Hair Care

Once you have your new short haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. You may also want to invest in a good styling product to help you achieve your desired look.

Overall, there are many great short haircuts for thick straight hair. Consult with your stylist to find the best cut for you and enjoy your new low-maintenance style!
