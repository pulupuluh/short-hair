How To Style Spiky Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Mar 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Spiky hair has been a popular hairstyle for decades, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. Whether you're going for a punk rock look or just want to add some edge to your style, spiky hair is a great option. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to style spiky hair, from the basics to advanced techniques.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Spiky Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Spiky Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Spiky Hair

How to Style Spiky Hair

Before we get into the details of how to style spiky hair, let's go over the basics. Spiky hair is a short hairstyle in which the hair is cut short on the sides and long on top, allowing for the hair to be spiked up. There are a few different ways to style spiky hair, but the most common method involves using hair gel or wax to hold the hair in place.

Tools You'll Need

To style spiky hair, you'll need a few basic tools:

  • Hair gel or wax
  • A comb or brush
  • Hairspray (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your tools ready, it's time to learn how to style spiky hair. Follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow-dry it before you begin.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to your fingertips and rub it between your hands to warm it up.
  3. Starting at the back of your head, use your fingertips to work the gel or wax into your hair, making sure to get it evenly distributed.
  4. Use a comb or brush to lift the hair at the front of your head and push it forward. This will create the spiky look.
  5. Continue working the gel or wax into your hair, making sure to get the hair at the sides and back of your head.
  6. Use your fingertips to create spikes by pulling small sections of hair upward and twisting them slightly.
  7. Once you have created the desired spikes, use a small amount of hairspray to hold them in place.

And there you have it - spiky hair! Of course, there are many different variations of spiky hair, so feel free to experiment and find the style that works best for you.

Tips for Styling Spiky Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling spiky hair:

  • Use a small amount of product to avoid making your hair look greasy or weighed down.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to use hairspray to hold your spikes in place.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut to ensure that your hair is the right length for spiky hair.
  • Try different styles, such as messy spikes or a more polished look.

FAQs

How long does spiky hair last?

Spiky hair can last for several hours to a full day, depending on the product you use and how well you maintain it.

Can I style spiky hair if my hair is thin or fine?

Yes, you can still style spiky hair if you have thin or fine hair. Just be sure to use a lightweight product that won't weigh your hair down.

How do I maintain spiky hair throughout the day?

Carry a small amount of product with you and touch up your spikes throughout the day as needed.

Hair Care for Spiky Hair

To keep your spiky hair looking its best, be sure to follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove product buildup.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it harder to style.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair when styling it, as this can cause breakage and damage.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to rock spiky hair like a pro. Remember to experiment and have fun with your style, and don't be afraid to try new things!
