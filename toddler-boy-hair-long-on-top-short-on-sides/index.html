Toddler Boy Hair Long On Top Short On Sides A Practical Guide

Written by Bardi May 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Long on top and short on sides toddler boy haircuts are a popular trend that has been around for a while now. If you're looking to give your little one a stylish and trendy haircut, then this might be the perfect choice for you. In this article, we'll be discussing how to achieve this look, hair care tips, and answering some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut Toddler Boy Hair Long on Top Short on Sides
  2. Tips for Maintaining Toddler Boy Hair Long on Top Short on Sides
  3. Frequently Asked Questions

How to Cut Toddler Boy Hair Long on Top Short on Sides

The first step in achieving the toddler boy hair long on top short on sides look is to choose the right haircut. You can either take your little one to a professional hairdresser or do it yourself at home. Here's how to cut toddler boy hair long on top short on sides:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Hair

Start by washing your toddler's hair and drying it with a towel. Make sure the hair is completely dry before you start cutting it.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

Use clippers to cut the sides of your toddler's hair. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down until you reach the desired length. Make sure to trim the hair around the ears and the back of the neck.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Use scissors to cut the top of your toddler's hair. Start by sectioning off the hair at the crown of the head and work your way towards the front. Cut the hair to the desired length, leaving it longer at the front and shorter towards the back.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Top

Use scissors to blend the sides and the top of your toddler's hair. Cut the hair at a diagonal angle to create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 5: Finish the Haircut

Use a comb and scissors to trim any stray hairs and create a clean finish. You can also use a trimmer to clean up the hairline at the back of the neck.

Tips for Maintaining Toddler Boy Hair Long on Top Short on Sides

Maintaining toddler boy hair long on top short on sides requires some effort to keep it looking neat and tidy. Here are some tips to help you:

Tip 1: Trim Regularly

To keep your toddler's hair looking neat and tidy, you should trim it every four to six weeks. This will prevent the hair from becoming too long and straggly.

Tip 2: Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is gentle on your toddler's hair. This will keep it soft and manageable.

Tip 3: Style with a Comb or Brush

Use a comb or brush to style your toddler's hair every day. This will prevent tangles and knots from forming.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is it safe to cut toddler boy hair long on top short on sides at home?

A: Yes, it is safe to cut toddler boy hair long on top short on sides at home as long as you use the right tools and follow the instructions carefully.

Q: What length should the hair be on top?

A: The hair on top should be about two to three inches long.

Q: How often should I wash my toddler's hair?

A: You should wash your toddler's hair two to three times a week.

Hair Care for Toddler Boy Hair Long on Top Short on Sides

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining toddler boy hair long on top short on sides. Here are some tips to help you:

Tip 1: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your toddler's hair. This will prevent breakage and damage.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools on your toddler's hair as this can damage the hair and make it dry and brittle.

Tip 3: Trim Split Ends

Regularly trim split ends to prevent them from getting worse and causing damage to the hair.

In conclusion, toddler boy hair long on top short on sides is a stylish and trendy look that is easy to achieve. With the right tools and techniques, you can give your little one a haircut that will make him look and feel great. Remember to maintain the hair regularly with proper care and styling to keep it looking neat and tidy.
