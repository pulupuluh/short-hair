How To Put Hair Extensions In Thin Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Feb 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have thin hair and are looking to add some volume and length, hair extensions can be a great option. However, putting hair extensions in thin hair can be a bit tricky. In this guide, we will show you how to put hair extensions in thin hair in a few easy steps. We will also provide you with tips on how to care for your hair extensions and answer some frequently asked questions about hair extensions in thin hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Put Hair Extensions in Thin Hair
  2. Tips for Putting Hair Extensions in Thin Hair
  3. FAQs about Hair Extensions in Thin Hair
  4. Hair Care for Hair Extensions in Thin Hair

How to Put Hair Extensions in Thin Hair

Before you start putting in your hair extensions, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need:

  • Hair extensions
  • Clips
  • Rat-tail comb
  • Hairspray

Now, follow these steps:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair. Use the rat-tail comb to create a horizontal parting at the nape of your neck. Clip the rest of your hair up and out of the way.

Step 2: Backcomb Your Hair

Take a small section of hair from the horizontal parting and backcomb it. This will create a base for the hair extension to clip onto. Repeat this step for all the sections of hair you want to add extensions to.

Step 3: Clip in the Hair Extensions

Clip in the hair extensions by sliding the clips onto the backcombed section of hair. Make sure the clips are secure and the hair extensions blend in with your natural hair.

Step 4: Repeat the Process

Repeat steps 1-3 for all the sections of hair you want to add extensions to. Once you've finished, release the clipped up hair and blend it in with the hair extensions.

Tips for Putting Hair Extensions in Thin Hair

Here are some tips to make putting hair extensions in thin hair easier:

  • Choose hair extensions that are specifically designed for thin hair. They should be lightweight and easy to blend in with your natural hair.
  • Backcomb your hair before clipping in the extensions. This will create a base for the clips to attach to and make the extensions more secure.
  • Keep the clips close to your scalp. This will help the extensions blend in better with your natural hair.
  • Use hairspray to keep the extensions in place and prevent them from slipping out.

FAQs about Hair Extensions in Thin Hair

Can you put hair extensions in thin hair?

Yes, you can put hair extensions in thin hair. However, you need to choose the right type of extensions and follow the correct procedure to make sure they blend in with your natural hair and don't damage your hair.

What type of hair extensions are best for thin hair?

The best type of hair extensions for thin hair are clip-in extensions. They are lightweight, easy to attach, and won't damage your hair.

How many hair extensions do I need for thin hair?

The number of hair extensions you need depends on the length and volume of your natural hair. It's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine how many extensions you need.

Hair Care for Hair Extensions in Thin Hair

Here are some tips to keep your hair extensions in good condition:

  • Brush your extensions regularly with a soft-bristle brush to prevent tangling.
  • Wash your extensions with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat on your extensions, such as curling irons or straighteners.
  • Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use.

By following these tips, you can enjoy beautiful, voluminous hair with hair extensions in thin hair.
