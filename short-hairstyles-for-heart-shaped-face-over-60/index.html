Short Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Face Over 60

Written by Petter Mar 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face and you're over 60, you may be looking for a short hairstyle that flatters your features. Short haircuts are a great option for older women because they're easy to maintain and can make you look younger. In this article, we'll discuss some of the best short hairstyles for heart-shaped faces over 60.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Heart-shaped faces are wider at the forehead and narrow at the chin. Short hairstyles that work well for this face shape include pixie cuts, bob cuts, and layered cuts. These haircuts can help balance out your features and make your face appear more proportional.

How To

When choosing a short haircut for a heart-shaped face over 60, it's important to consider your hair texture and personal style. You may want to consult with a hairstylist to find a cut that suits you best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Consider your hair texture

If you have thin hair, a layered cut can add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut can help control the weight of your hair.

Think about your personal style

Do you prefer a classic or edgy look? A pixie cut can give you a more daring look, while a bob cut can be more traditional.

Take into account your lifestyle

If you're an active person, you may want a low-maintenance haircut that doesn't require a lot of styling. If you have a lot of social events, you may want a haircut that's more versatile and can be styled in different ways.

Step by Step

Here are some popular short haircuts for heart-shaped faces over 60:

Pixie cut

A pixie cut is a bold and daring haircut that can make you look younger. It's a short, cropped cut that's easy to maintain and style. To achieve this haircut, your hairstylist will cut your hair short on the sides and back, leaving longer hair on top. You can style it with a bit of product for a messy, textured look.

Bob cut

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that's always in style. It's a short haircut that's longer in the front and shorter in the back. This cut can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. You can add layers for more volume or keep it blunt for a sleek look.

Layered cut

A layered cut is a great option if you have thin hair. This haircut adds volume and movement to your hair. Your hairstylist will cut different lengths of hair to create layers. You can style it with a bit of product for a messy, textured look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your short haircut:

  • Use a volumizing product to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots.
  • Use a hair straightener to create a sleek, polished look.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, to add interest to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: Short haircuts can make you look younger if they're styled correctly. A well-styled short haircut can help balance out your features and give you a more youthful appearance.

Q: What is the best haircut for a heart-shaped face over 60?

A: The best haircut for a heart-shaped face over 60 is a short haircut that flatters your features. Pixie cuts, bob cuts, and layered cuts are all great options.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It's recommended to get your hair cut every 6 to 8 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require less maintenance than longer haircuts, but it's still important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or flat irons.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut and prevent split ends.

Overall, short hairstyles are a great option for heart-shaped faces over 60. They can make you look younger and are easy to maintain. Whether you choose a pixie cut, bob cut, or layered cut, make sure to choose a haircut that flatters your features and suits your personal style.
