Faces That Look Good With Short Hair

Written by William Jul 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

In 2023, short hair is still a popular trend. However, not everyone can pull off the look. If you are considering a short haircut, it is important to choose a style that compliments your facial features. In this article, we will discuss the faces that look good with short hair and provide tips on how to care for your new hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts can be flattering for many different face shapes. Some of the faces that look good with short hair include:

  • Oval faces
  • Square faces
  • Heart-shaped faces
  • Diamond-shaped faces

Short haircuts can also work for round faces, but it is important to choose a style that adds volume at the crown and temples to elongate the face.

How To

When choosing a short haircut, it is important to consider your face shape and hair texture. Some popular short hairstyles for different face shapes include:

Oval Faces

Oval faces can pull off almost any short hairstyle. A pixie cut, bob, or lob (long bob) can all look great on oval faces. Experiment with different lengths and textures to find the perfect style for you.

Square Faces

Short hairstyles with soft layers and texture can help soften the angles of a square face. A chin-length bob or a layered pixie cut are both great options for square faces.

Heart-Shaped Faces

A chin-length bob with layers can help balance out a heart-shaped face. If you want to go shorter, a pixie cut with longer layers on top can also look great.

Diamond-Shaped Faces

Short hairstyles with soft layers and a side-swept fringe can help balance out a diamond-shaped face. A pixie cut with textured layers or a short bob with a side part are both great options.

Step By Step

If you have decided to go for a short haircut, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Consult with a professional hair stylist to find the best short hairstyle for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Bring pictures of short hairstyles you like to your hair appointment to give your stylist an idea of what you are looking for.
  3. Ask your stylist for tips on how to style and maintain your new haircut.
  4. Invest in quality hair products that are designed for short hair, such as a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray.
  5. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the perfect look for you.

Tips

  • Short haircuts require more frequent maintenance, so be prepared for regular trips to the salon.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to help style your short hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your short hair.
  • Accessorize your short hair with hair clips, headbands, or scarves.

FAQ

Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

Yes! There are many ways to style short hair up, such as with a small bun or a half-up, half-down style.

Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

It depends on the style you choose. A short haircut with volume at the crown and temples can actually elongate a round face.

What if I don't like my short haircut?

It can take some time to get used to a new haircut. If you are unhappy with your short haircut, talk to your stylist about making some adjustments.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to keep your short haircut looking fresh.

With the right styling and maintenance, short hair can be a flattering and low-maintenance hairstyle for many different face shapes. If you are considering a short haircut, talk to a professional stylist to find the perfect style for you.
