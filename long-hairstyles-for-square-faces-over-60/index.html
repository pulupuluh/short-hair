Long Hairstyles For Square Faces Over 60

Written by Daniel Aug 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are a woman over 60 with a square face shape, you may be wondering what hairstyles would suit you best. Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline, broad forehead, and cheekbones that are almost as wide as the forehead. Long hairstyles can help soften the angles of a square face, while also adding volume and movement to the hair. In this article, we will explore some of the best long hairstyles for square faces over 60.

Description

Long hairstyles are a great choice for women over 60 with square faces because they can help balance out the proportions of the face. Square faces tend to have a strong jawline, so adding length to the hair can soften the angles and create a more feminine look. Long hairstyles can also add volume to the hair, which can be especially beneficial for women with thinning hair.

How to

If you are considering a long hairstyle for your square face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the texture of your hair. If you have fine hair, a layered cut can add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut can help control the volume. Second, consider your lifestyle. If you are active and prefer low-maintenance hairstyles, a simple layered cut may be best. If you enjoy styling your hair, a more complex cut with layers and angles may be more suitable.

Step by Step

Step 1: Consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with square face shapes and women over 60.

Step 2: Consider the texture and thickness of your hair, as well as your lifestyle and preferred level of maintenance.

Step 3: Choose a long hairstyle that suits your face shape and personal style.

Step 4: Work with your hairstylist to achieve the desired cut and style.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a long hairstyle for a square face over 60:

  • Consider adding layers or angles to create movement and soften the angles of the face.
  • A side-swept fringe can help balance out the proportions of the face.
  • A long bob or lob can be a great option for women who want a shorter hairstyle without sacrificing length.
  • A center part can elongate the face, while a side part can create volume and balance.

FAQ

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face over 60?

A: Avoid hairstyles that emphasize the squareness of the face, such as blunt bobs or severe, straight-across bangs.

Q: Can I still wear my hair long if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes! Adding layers to a long hairstyle can create the illusion of volume and movement, even if the hair is thinning.

Hair Care

Long hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for women with long hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too frequently, as this can damage the hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip the natural oils from the scalp and lead to dryness and breakage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and healthy.

Long hairstyles can be a great choice for women over 60 with square face shapes. By choosing the right cut and style, you can create a look that flatters your face shape and suits your personal style. Remember to consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with women over 60 and square face shapes, and to keep up with regular hair care to keep your long locks looking their best.
