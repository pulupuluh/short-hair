How To Play Sports With Long Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Sep 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As an athlete with long hair, keeping your mane out of your face and under control can be a challenge. Whether you're playing soccer, basketball, or running, long hair can be distracting and get in the way. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to play sports with long hair without any hassle.

How to

1. Tie it up: The most obvious solution is to tie your hair up in a ponytail or bun. This will keep your hair out of your face and prevent it from getting tangled. Make sure you use a hair tie that won't slip out of place while you're playing.

2. Use a headband: If you have shorter hair or bangs that tend to fall out, a headband can be a lifesaver. Choose one that's made of a sweat-wicking fabric to keep your forehead dry and prevent breakouts.

3. Braid it: Braids are a great option for long hair because they keep your hair in place and prevent tangles. You can try a simple three-strand braid or get creative with fishtail braids or Dutch braids.

4. Wear a hat: If you're playing outdoors, wearing a hat can help keep your hair out of your face and protect your scalp from the sun. Choose a hat with a brim that's wide enough to shade your face and neck.

Step by Step

1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.

2. Decide how you want to wear your hair. Do you want to tie it up, braid it, or wear a headband?

3. If you're tying your hair up, gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie. Make sure it's not too tight or it can cause headaches.

4. If you're braiding your hair, start by dividing your hair into three sections. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section. Repeat until you reach the end of your hair and secure it with a hair tie.

5. If you're wearing a headband, place it over your forehead and slide it back until it's snug. Make sure it's not too tight or it can cause headaches.

Tips

- Use hair ties that won't slip out of place

- Choose a headband made of sweat-wicking fabric

- Experiment with different braids to find what works best for you

- Wear a hat with a wide brim to protect your face and neck from the sun

FAQ

Q: How can I prevent my hair from getting tangled while playing sports?

A: Brush your hair before tying it up or braiding it. You can also use a detangling spray or conditioner to make it easier to brush.

Q: What should I do if my hair keeps falling out of my ponytail or braid?

A: Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands in place. You can also try using a hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.

Hair Care

Playing sports can be tough on your hair, so it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

- Wash your hair after every game or practice to remove sweat and dirt

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and healthy

- Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

By following these tips and tricks, you can play sports with long hair without any distractions. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great!
