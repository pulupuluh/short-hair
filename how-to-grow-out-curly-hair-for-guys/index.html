How To Grow Out Curly Hair For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 30, 2023 · 8 min read

If you are a guy with curly hair, you might have struggled with finding the right way to grow out your locks. Growing out curly hair requires patience, proper care, and a lot of trial and error. In this article, we will guide you through the process of growing out curly hair for guys, with tips, FAQ, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Grow Out Curly Hair for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Growing Out Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Grow Out Curly Hair for Guys

Growing out curly hair is not as simple as just letting it grow. You need to have a plan, take care of your hair, and be patient. Here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Decide on Your Desired Hair Length

The first step is to decide on the length you want your hair to be. This will help you decide how long you need to grow it out and how to style it along the way.

Step 2: Find a Good Hairstylist

Find a hairstylist who is experienced in working with curly hair. They can give you advice on how to grow out your hair and help you choose the right products.

Step 3: Start with a Good Haircut

Before you start growing out your hair, get a good haircut. This will make it easier to manage and style as it grows out.

Step 4: Be Patient

Curly hair takes time to grow out, so be patient. Don't get frustrated if it doesn't grow as fast as you want it to.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to grow out your curly hair:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition Regularly

Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent breakage. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner.

Step 2: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz. Apply it after washing your hair and before styling.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons. They can damage your hair and cause breakage.

Step 4: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep it healthy. This will prevent breakage and make it easier to manage.

Step 5: Experiment with Different Styles

As your hair grows out, experiment with different styles. Try different hair products, part your hair in different ways, and try out different haircuts.

Tips for Growing Out Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you grow out your curly hair:

Tip 1: Be Patient

As we mentioned earlier, growing out curly hair requires patience. Don't get frustrated if it doesn't grow as fast as you want it to.

Tip 2: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush. This will prevent breakage and keep your curls intact.

Tip 3: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Instead, wash it every two to three days.

Tip 4: Protect Your Hair While Sleeping

Protect your hair while sleeping by using a satin or silk pillowcase. This will prevent breakage and frizz.

FAQ

How long does it take to grow out curly hair?

The time it takes to grow out curly hair varies from person to person. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month.

What products should I use on my curly hair?

Use hair products that are specifically designed for curly hair, like curl creams, leave-in conditioners, and anti-frizz serums.

How often should I trim my curly hair?

Trim your curly hair every three to four months to get rid of split ends and keep it healthy.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

Tip 1: Use Products Specifically Designed for Curly Hair

Use hair products that are specifically designed for curly hair to keep it moisturized and prevent frizz.

Tip 2: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Instead, wash it every two to three days.

Tip 3: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush. This will prevent breakage and keep your curls intact.

Tip 4: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF. The sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Follow these tips and steps, and you'll be on your way to growing out your curly hair into a healthy and stylish mane!
