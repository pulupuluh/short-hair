What Colour Eyeshadow Suits My Skin Tone

Written by Luffy Apr 19, 2023 · 7 min read

Welcome to our blog post on what colour eyeshadow suits your skin tone. Finding the right eyeshadow colour can be a challenge, but with our guide, you'll be able to choose the perfect shade for your skin tone. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to determine your skin tone to the best eyeshadow shades for your complexion. Let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Cool Skin Tones
  • Warm Skin Tones
  • Neutral Skin Tones
  • Eyeshadow Shades for Different Skin Tones
  • Tips for Applying Eyeshadow
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right eyeshadow shade is to determine your skin tone. There are three main skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral. Here's how to determine your skin tone:

  • Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell, you have a neutral skin tone.
  • Another way to determine your skin tone is to look at the undertones in your skin. If your skin has pink, red, or blue undertones, you have a cool skin tone. If your skin has yellow, peach, or golden undertones, you have a warm skin tone. If you have a mix of both, you have a neutral skin tone.

Cool Skin Tones

If you have a cool skin tone, you'll want to choose eyeshadow shades that are cool-toned as well. Cool-toned eyeshadows have a blue or purple undertone. Some great eyeshadow shades for cool skin tones include:

  • Lilac
  • Plum
  • Blue-grey
  • Pink
  • Silver

Warm Skin Tones

If you have a warm skin tone, you'll want to choose eyeshadow shades that are warm-toned as well. Warm-toned eyeshadows have a yellow or orange undertone. Some great eyeshadow shades for warm skin tones include:

  • Gold
  • Copper
  • Bronze
  • Peach
  • Coral

Neutral Skin Tones

If you have a neutral skin tone, you can wear both cool and warm-toned eyeshadows. Some great eyeshadow shades for neutral skin tones include:

  • Beige
  • Taupe
  • Grey
  • Soft pink
  • Champagne

Eyeshadow Shades for Different Skin Tones

Now that you know your skin tone and which eyeshadow shades work best, let's take a closer look at some specific shades for each skin tone:

  • Cool Skin Tones: Lilac, Plum, Blue-grey, Pink, Silver
  • Warm Skin Tones: Gold, Copper, Bronze, Peach, Coral
  • Neutral Skin Tones: Beige, Taupe, Grey, Soft pink, Champagne

Tips for Applying Eyeshadow

Now that you've chosen the perfect eyeshadow shade for your skin tone, here are some tips for applying it:

  • Use a primer before applying eyeshadow to help it last longer.
  • Use a light shade on your lid and a darker shade in the crease for a natural look.
  • Blend your eyeshadow well for a seamless look.
  • Use a small brush to apply eyeshadow to your lower lash line.
  • Experiment with different eyeshadow textures, such as matte, shimmer, and metallic.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing eyeshadow shades for different skin tones:

  • Can I wear any eyeshadow shade regardless of my skin tone?

    • While you can wear any eyeshadow shade you'd like, certain shades will be more flattering on your skin tone than others.

  • What if I have a cool undertone, but I want to wear a warm-toned eyeshadow?

    • It's okay to experiment with different eyeshadow shades, but warm-toned eyeshadows may clash with your cool undertones and make your skin look sallow.

  • Can I wear the same eyeshadow shade every day?

    • Of course! Find an eyeshadow shade you love and wear it as often as you'd like.


Hair Care

While choosing the right eyeshadow shade is important, don't forget about your hair! Here are some hair care tips to keep your locks looking their best:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

That's it for our guide on what colour eyeshadow suits your skin tone. We hope you found it helpful and feel confident in choosing the perfect eyeshadow shade for your complexion. Happy shopping!
