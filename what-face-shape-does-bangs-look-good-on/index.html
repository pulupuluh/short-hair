What Face Shape Does Bangs Look Good On

Written by Robby Mar 08, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you thinking of getting bangs but wondering if they will look good on your face shape? Bangs can be a great way to change up your hairstyle and add a new dimension to your look. However, not all bangs are created equal, and certain styles can flatter some face shapes more than others. In this article, we will explore what face shape bangs look good on and provide tips for choosing the perfect bangs for your face.

Description:

Bangs can be a versatile hairstyle that can suit a variety of face shapes. However, not all bangs are created equal, and certain styles can flatter some face shapes more than others. Choosing the right bangs for your face shape can enhance your features and create a more balanced look. In this article, we will explore what face shape bangs look good on and provide tips for choosing the perfect bangs for your face.

How to Choose the Perfect Bangs for Your Face Shape:

When choosing the perfect bangs for your face shape, it's important to consider the length, thickness, and style of the bangs. Here are some general guidelines for what face shape bangs look good on:

Round Face:

Bangs that are longer and swept to the side can help elongate a round face and create the illusion of a more angular shape. Avoid blunt bangs that end at the cheekbones, as they can make the face appear wider.

Square Face:

Soft, wispy bangs that are shorter in the center and longer on the sides can help soften the angles of a square face. Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs that can accentuate the squareness of the face.

Heart-Shaped Face:

Side-swept bangs that are longer on one side than the other can help balance out a heart-shaped face. Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs that can make the forehead appear wider.

Oval Face:

Oval faces are versatile and can pull off a variety of bang styles, from blunt bangs to side-swept bangs. However, it's important to consider the thickness and length of the bangs to ensure they don't overwhelm the face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Bangs:

Styling bangs can be a bit challenging, especially if you're new to the world of bangs. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using a comb, section off your bangs from the rest of your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or waves in your bangs.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs:

Maintaining your bangs can help keep them looking great between haircuts. Here are some tips for maintaining your bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Avoid touching or playing with your bangs throughout the day, as this can cause them to become greasy or frizzy.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your bangs between washes.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs, as this can weigh them down and make them look greasy.

FAQs:

Q: Can bangs make my face look slimmer?

A: Yes, bangs can help create the illusion of a slimmer face, especially if you have a round or square face shape. Longer, side-swept bangs can help elongate the face and create the illusion of a more angular shape.

Q: Do bangs require a lot of maintenance?

A: Bangs do require more maintenance than other hairstyles, as they need to be trimmed regularly and styled daily. However, with the right products and techniques, you can keep your bangs looking great with minimal effort.

Q: Can I wear bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, curly hair can look great with bangs. However, it's important to choose a bang style that works with your natural curl pattern and doesn't require too much heat styling.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs:

In addition to regular trims and styling, taking care of your hair can help keep your bangs looking great. Here are some hair care tips for bangs:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming dry or damaged.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, on your bangs too often, as this can cause them to become dry or frizzy.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your bangs to keep them moisturized and shiny.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

By following these tips and guidelines, you can choose the perfect bangs for your face shape and keep them looking great between haircuts. Whether you opt for blunt, straight-across bangs or side-swept bangs, bangs can be a great way to change up your hairstyle and add a new dimension to your look.
