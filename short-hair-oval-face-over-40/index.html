Short Hair Oval Face Over 40 Rocking The Perfect Look

Written by Robby Apr 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a woman over 40 with an oval face, you're in luck! Your face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off a range of hairstyles. And if you're looking for a low-maintenance yet chic look, short hair is the way to go. In this post, we'll guide you on how to rock the perfect short hair oval face over 40 look.

Description

Oval faces are characterized by balanced proportions and a slightly narrow chin. This face shape can handle almost any haircut and style, making it a popular choice among hairstylists. Short hair, on the other hand, is a low-maintenance option that can save you time and money on styling products and salon visits. It's also a great way to show off your facial features and neck.

How to

To achieve the perfect short hair oval face over 40 look, follow these steps:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

If you're not sure whether you have an oval face, measure the length and width of your face with a tape measure. If the measurements are almost equal, with a slightly narrower chin, you have an oval face.

Step 2: Browse hairstyles

Search for short hairstyles that flatter oval faces. Look for inspiration from magazines, social media, or consult with your hairstylist. Consider your hair texture, face shape, and lifestyle when choosing a style.

Step 3: Choose a haircut

Choose a haircut that accentuates your facial features and complements the shape of your face. A layered bob, pixie cut, or a short shag are popular options for an oval face. Ask your hairstylist for their recommendations.

Step 4: Style your hair

Short hair is low-maintenance, but it still requires some styling to look its best. Use a styling product that suits your hair type and style. Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to fall, and use a round brush to add volume to the crown. Finish with hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips for rocking the perfect short hair oval face over 40 look:

  • Consider your lifestyle when selecting a hairstyle. If you're on-the-go or have an active lifestyle, choose a low-maintenance style.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and nourishment to your hair.
  • Accessorize with headbands, clips, or scarves to add a personal touch to your hairstyle.
  • Invest in a good quality hair dryer and flat iron for styling.

FAQ

What are the best short hairstyles for oval faces over 40?

The best short hairstyles for oval faces over 40 include a layered bob, pixie cut, or a short shag. These styles accentuate your facial features and complement your face shape.

How do I style short hair?

To style short hair, use a styling product that suits your hair type and style. Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to fall, and use a round brush to add volume to the crown. Finish with hairspray.

Hair care

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs proper care to stay healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and nourishment to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and health.

Overall, short hair oval face over 40 is a chic and low-maintenance look that suits women of all ages. Follow our tips and advice to rock the perfect short hair look that flatters your oval face.
