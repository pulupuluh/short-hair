How Do You Hide Long Hair Under A Hat

Written by Aprilia Apr 08, 2023 · 6 min read

Long hair can sometimes be difficult to manage, especially when you want to wear a hat. Whether you are going on a hike, running errands, or simply need a bad hair day solution, wearing a hat can be a great option. However, figuring out how to hide long hair under a hat can be a challenge. In this article, we’ll provide you with some tips and tricks on how to do just that!

Description

If you have long hair, you know how challenging it can be to manage. Not only can it get in the way, but it can also be difficult to hide. Wearing a hat can be a great solution to this problem, but it’s essential to know how to do it correctly. In this article, we’ll go over how to hide long hair under a hat, step by step.

How to Hide Long Hair Under a Hat

Step 1: Choose the Right Hat

The first step in hiding long hair under a hat is choosing the right hat. Baseball caps and beanies are great options for this. Make sure to choose a hat that fits your head snugly, but not too tight. If the hat is too loose, it won’t stay in place, and your hair will peek out.

Step 2: Tie Your Hair Back

The next step is to tie your hair back. You can put it in a ponytail, bun, or braid. Make sure to keep it as flat as possible. If it’s too bulky, it will be difficult to hide under the hat.

Step 3: Pull the Hat Down

Once your hair is tied back, put the hat on and pull it down. Make sure it covers your hairline and ears. You may need to adjust your hair a bit to make sure it’s all tucked under the hat.

Step 4: Use Bobby Pins

If you have shorter layers or bangs that won’t stay under the hat, you can use bobby pins to secure them. Make sure to use neutral-colored bobby pins that match your hair color. You don’t want them to be visible.

Tips

  • Choose a hat that fits your head snugly, but not too tight.
  • Keep your hair as flat as possible to make it easier to hide under the hat.
  • Use bobby pins to secure shorter layers or bangs that won’t stay under the hat.
  • Use a hair tie or scrunchie that matches your hair color to blend in better.

FAQ

How do you keep your hair from falling out of the hat?

To keep your hair from falling out of the hat, make sure to choose a hat that fits your head snugly, and tie your hair back as flat as possible. You can also use bobby pins to secure shorter layers or bangs that won’t stay under the hat.

What kind of hat is best for hiding long hair?

Baseball caps and beanies are great options for hiding long hair. Make sure to choose a hat that fits your head snugly, but not too tight.

Can I wear a hat with wet hair?

It’s best to avoid wearing a hat with wet hair. Wet hair is more likely to get tangled and damaged, and wearing a hat can make it worse.

Hair Care

Make sure to take care of your hair, especially if you are tying it back or wearing a hat frequently. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy, and avoid tying it back too tightly. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage.
