What To Do With Very Thin Curly Hair

Written by Petter Aug 12, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have very thin curly hair, you may feel like it's difficult to style and manage. However, with the right techniques and products, you can make your hair look voluminous, healthy, and beautiful. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on what to do with very thin curly hair. We'll cover everything from hair care to styling, so keep reading to find out more.

Description

Very thin curly hair is hair that has a fine texture and a lot of curls. This hair type can be challenging to work with because it can easily become flat and lifeless. However, with the right techniques, you can add volume and bounce to your hair.

How to

1. Choose the Right Products

The first step in managing very thin curly hair is choosing the right products. Look for products that are designed for thin hair and curly hair. These products will help to add volume and bounce to your hair without weighing it down.

2. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner will help to make your hair look thicker and fuller. These products work by lifting the hair strands and adding volume to the roots. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, panthenol, and keratin, which will help to strengthen your hair and make it more resistant to breakage.

3. Avoid Heavy Products

Avoid using heavy products like hair oils and serums, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Instead, opt for lightweight products like mousses and gels that will help to define your curls without weighing them down.

4. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush. This will help to prevent breakage and damage to your hair. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots, gently detangling each section.

5. Blow Dry with a Diffuser

Blow drying your hair with a diffuser attachment will help to enhance your curls and add volume to your hair. Start by applying a volumizing mousse to your hair, then use the diffuser to dry your hair on a low heat setting. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you dry it, which will help to enhance your curls.

Step by Step

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any product buildup.

2. Apply a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your damp hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Use a Diffuser to Dry Your Hair

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and use a low heat setting to dry your hair. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you dry it, which will help to enhance your curls.

4. Apply a Lightweight Hair Spray

Once your hair is dry, apply a lightweight hair spray to hold your curls in place. Be careful not to apply too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.

Tips

- Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it look flat.

- Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from becoming tangled and frizzy while you sleep.

- Consider getting a haircut that is designed for thin curly hair. A layered haircut can help to add volume and bounce to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will using a hair oil make my hair look flat?

A: Yes, using a hair oil can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Instead, use a lightweight product like a mousse or gel to define your curls.

Q: Can I still use a brush on my thin curly hair?

A: It's best to avoid using a brush on your thin curly hair, as this can cause breakage and damage. Use a wide-tooth comb instead to detangle your hair.

Q: Can I use a hair dryer without a diffuser attachment?

A: Using a hair dryer without a diffuser attachment can cause your curls to become frizzy and undefined. It's best to use a diffuser to enhance your curls and add volume to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your very thin curly hair looking healthy and beautiful, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

- Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.

- Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make your very thin curly hair look voluminous, healthy, and beautiful. With the right products and care, you can achieve the perfect curls and keep your hair looking its best.
