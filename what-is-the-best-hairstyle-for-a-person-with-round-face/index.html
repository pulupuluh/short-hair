What Is The Best Hairstyle For A Person With Round Face

Written by Eveline May 22, 2023 · 7 min read
What is the Best Hairstyle for a Person with Round Face?

If you have a round face, you might be wondering what the best hairstyle for you is. While there are no hard and fast rules, there are some tips and tricks you can use to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best hairstyles for a person with a round face.

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a softer jawline. If you have a round face, you want to avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, as this can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, you want to look for hairstyles that elongate your face and create the illusion of more defined cheekbones and jawline.

How to

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for a round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, and comparing them to the length of your face. If your face is roughly the same width and length, you likely have a round face.

Once you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to start looking for hairstyles that flatter your features. Some of the best hairstyles for a person with a round face include:

Step by step:

1. Long Layers: Long, layered hair can help elongate your face and create the illusion of more defined cheekbones. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as these can add width to your face.

2. Side-Swept Bangs: If you want to try bangs, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle. This will help to break up the roundness of your face.

3. Bob: A long bob that falls just below the chin can be flattering for a round face. Avoid bobs that are too short, as these can add width to your face.

4. Pixie Cut: If you’re feeling daring, a pixie cut can be a great option for a round face. Look for a style that is longer on top and shorter on the sides, as this will help elongate your face.

5. Beachy Waves: Beachy waves can be a great option for a round face, as they add texture and dimension to your hair. Avoid tight curls, as these can add volume to your face.

Tips

When styling your hair, there are a few tips you can keep in mind to flatter your round face:

- Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face.

- Look for hairstyles that elongate your face and create the illusion of more defined cheekbones and jawline.

- Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs.

- Opt for side-swept bangs and long layers.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a round face. Just make sure to keep it loose and low, rather than tight and high on your head.

Q: Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a round face?

A: Not necessarily. A short haircut can be flattering for a round face if it’s styled correctly. Look for a style that is longer on top and shorter on the sides, as this will help elongate your face.

Hair Care

When it comes to taking care of your hair, there are a few things you can do to keep it healthy and looking its best:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s appropriate for your hair type.

- Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

- Use a heat protectant spray before using heat tools.

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

- Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips and finding the right hairstyle for your face shape, you can create a look that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
