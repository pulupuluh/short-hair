How To Add Volume To Short Layered Hair

Written by Eveline Jun 03, 2023 · 8 min read

Short layered hair is a trendy and stylish look that many women love. However, sometimes it can look flat and lifeless. If you’re struggling to add volume to your short layered hair, don’t worry, there are easy solutions to this problem. In this article, we’ll give you tips and tricks on how to add volume to short layered hair.

Description

Short layered hair is a type of haircut that involves cutting the hair into layers, creating a textured and voluminous look. However, if you have fine or thin hair, your short layered hair may look flat and lifeless. To add volume to your hair, you need to use the right products and styling techniques.

How to Add Volume to Short Layered Hair

Here are some tips on how to add volume to your short layered hair:

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products is essential to add volume to your short layered hair. Look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for fine or thin hair. These products will help to add volume and thickness to your hair.

You can also use styling products like mousse and volumizing sprays to add volume to your hair. Apply these products to your roots and work them through your hair before blow-drying.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow-drying your hair upside down is a great way to add volume to your short layered hair. Start by flipping your head upside down and blow-drying your hair with a round brush. This will help to lift your roots and create volume.

3. Use Velcro Rollers

Velcro rollers are a great tool to add volume to your hair. After blow-drying your hair, roll sections of your hair around the Velcro rollers and leave them in for a few minutes. This will help to create volume and lift at the roots.

4. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is an easy way to add volume to your short layered hair. Start by sectioning your hair and teasing the roots with a comb. This will create volume and texture that will last all day.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to add volume to your short layered hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products will help to add volume and thickness to your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair with a round brush. This will help to lift your roots and create volume.

Step 4: Use Velcro Rollers

Roll sections of your hair around Velcro rollers and leave them in for a few minutes. This will help to create volume and lift at the roots.

Step 5: Tease Your Hair

Section your hair and tease the roots with a comb. This will create volume and texture that will last all day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to add volume to your short layered hair:

  • Use a volumizing dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair between washes.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every other day or even less frequently. If you exercise regularly or live in a humid climate, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Can I add volume to my short layered hair without using heat?

Yes, you can use Velcro rollers or twist your hair into a bun while it’s damp and let it air dry. This will create natural volume and texture without using heat.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair. And don’t forget to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can add volume to your short layered hair and achieve a stylish and voluminous look.
