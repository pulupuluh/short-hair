How To Care For Wavy Frizzy Hair

Written by Christine Jul 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with wavy frizzy hair that just won't cooperate? You're not alone. Many people struggle with this hair type, but with the right care, you can tame your locks and achieve the beautiful waves you desire. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to care for wavy frizzy hair, including tips and FAQs.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Shampoo and Conditioner
  • Step 2: Styling Products
  • Step 3: Drying Techniques
  • Step 4: Overnight Care
  • Tips for Managing Wavy Frizzy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Conclusion

Step 1: Shampoo and Conditioner

The first step in caring for wavy frizzy hair is to choose the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and contain moisturizing ingredients like argan oil, shea butter, or coconut oil. Avoid products with sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it even frizzier.

When washing your hair, be gentle and use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your locks. Rinse thoroughly to ensure that all product is removed from your hair.

Step 2: Styling Products

After washing and conditioning your hair, it's time to apply styling products. Start with a leave-in conditioner to keep your locks moisturized and help prevent frizz. Then, apply a styling gel or mousse to define your waves and provide hold. Be sure to distribute the products evenly throughout your hair.

If you're looking for a more natural approach, you can use a small amount of coconut oil or argan oil instead of styling products. These oils will moisturize your hair and provide a slight hold.

Step 3: Drying Techniques

The way you dry your hair can make a big difference in how it looks. Instead of rubbing your hair with a towel, gently squeeze the excess water out and pat your hair dry. Avoid using a hair dryer if possible, as the heat can damage your hair and make it even frizzier.

If you must use a hair dryer, use a diffuser attachment and set it to a low heat setting. This will help to distribute the heat evenly and prevent your hair from becoming too dry.

Step 4: Overnight Care

One of the best ways to care for wavy frizzy hair is to be gentle with it while you sleep. Avoid sleeping with your hair in a tight ponytail or bun, as this can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, sleep with your hair loose or in a loose braid.

You can also use a silk or satin pillowcase, as these materials are gentler on your hair and won't cause as much friction as cotton.

Tips for Managing Wavy Frizzy Hair

  • Don't brush your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your locks.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as these can damage your hair and make it even frizzier.
  • Try not to touch your hair too much throughout the day, as the oils from your hands can make it greasy and frizzy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQs

Q: Can I still use a hair dryer if I have wavy frizzy hair?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a diffuser attachment and set it to a low heat setting to prevent damage and frizz.

Q: Should I wash my hair every day?

A: It's not necessary to wash your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it even frizzier. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

Conclusion

Caring for wavy frizzy hair may seem like a challenge, but with the right care and products, you can achieve beautiful, defined waves. Remember to choose products that are designed for your hair type, be gentle when washing and drying your hair, and use styling products to define and hold your waves. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to manage your wavy frizzy hair with ease.
