How To Cut Men s Hair With Receding Hairline With Clippers

Written by Luffy Jul 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a man with a receding hairline? Do you find it challenging to find the right hairstyle that suits your hair type and face shape? One solution is to cut your hair with clippers! In this article, we will guide you on how to cut men's hair with receding hairline with clippers.

Description

A receding hairline is a common issue among men. It is characterized by the hairline moving back from the forehead, creating a V-shape. This can make styling your hair difficult, but with the right technique, you can cut your hair with clippers and still look stylish.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Once you have everything ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clipper Guard

The first step is to choose the right clipper guard. The guard will determine how short your hair will be. If you have a receding hairline, we recommend using a longer guard to avoid exposing too much of your scalp. A number 3 or 4 guard is a good choice.

Step 2: Start at the Sides

Start cutting your hair at the sides. Use the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length. Make sure you move the clippers in an upward motion to create a natural-looking fade. Use a comb to lift the hair and trim any excess hair with scissors.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Once you have finished cutting the sides, move to the top of your head. Use the same clipper guard to trim the hair to the desired length. Make sure you blend the top with the sides to create a natural-looking cut.

Step 4: Blend and Style

Once you have finished cutting your hair, use the scissors to blend any uneven areas. Style your hair using your preferred product.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you need to.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • If you're not confident in cutting your own hair, ask a friend or family member for help.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair with a receding hairline:

Can I use clippers if I have a receding hairline?

Yes, clippers are a great tool for cutting men's hair with a receding hairline. Just make sure you use a longer guard to avoid exposing too much of your scalp.

What guard should I use?

If you have a receding hairline, we recommend using a longer guard such as a number 3 or 4 guard.

Do I need to use scissors?

Yes, scissors are necessary to trim any excess hair and blend any uneven areas.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's essential to take care of it to ensure it stays healthy and looks great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner suited for your hair type.
  • Don't wash your hair too often as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair with a receding hairline with clippers, you can confidently achieve a stylish and natural-looking haircut. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully for the best results.
