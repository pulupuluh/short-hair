How To Style Shoulder Length Thick Wavy Hair A Guide For Relaxed Locks

Written by Frank May 12, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have shoulder length thick wavy hair, you know that styling it can be a challenge. Finding the right products and techniques to tame your locks and make them look their best can seem like an uphill battle. But fear not – with the right approach, you can turn your unruly mane into a gorgeous, relaxed style that will turn heads.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Shoulder Length Thick Wavy Hair

The key to styling shoulder length thick wavy hair is finding the right balance between control and movement. You want your hair to look relaxed and effortless, but you also want it to stay in place and not frizz out. Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Start with clean, damp hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for wavy hair to keep your locks hydrated and frizz-free.
  • Apply a styling product that is specifically formulated for wavy hair, such as a curl cream or mousse. This will help define your waves and keep them from getting too frizzy.
  • Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat. This will help enhance your natural waves and prevent them from getting too puffy.
  • Use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need more definition. Just wrap small sections of hair around the iron and hold for a few seconds, then release.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your waves in place without weighing them down.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling shoulder length thick wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for wavy hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a curl cream or mousse evenly throughout.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat until it is about 80% dry.
  4. Use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need more definition.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your shoulder length thick wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Don't use too much product – a little goes a long way with wavy hair.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling shoulder length thick wavy hair:

Q: Can I straighten my wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your wavy hair using a flat iron. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using the flat iron on wet hair.

Q: How often should I wash my wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people with wavy hair can go several days between washes, while others need to wash their hair daily. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: What types of haircuts work best for wavy hair?

A: Shoulder length haircuts with layers tend to work well for wavy hair, as they help enhance the natural texture and movement of the hair.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Thick Wavy Hair

In addition to styling your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your shoulder length thick wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your shoulder length thick wavy hair to perfection. Embrace your natural texture and movement, and you'll be turning heads wherever you go.
