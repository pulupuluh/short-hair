How To Style A Pixie Cut Step By Step

Written by Frank Mar 17, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’ve recently gotten a pixie cut, you may be wondering how to style it. It can be a bit intimidating at first, but with the right techniques and products, you can create a variety of looks that will keep your new ‘do looking fresh and stylish. In this article, we’ll walk you through how to style a pixie cut step by step, as well as provide some tips and hair care advice to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style a Pixie Cut
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling a Pixie Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

How to Style a Pixie Cut

When it comes to styling a pixie cut, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you’ll want to consider your face shape and hair type. Certain styles may look better on certain face shapes, and some hair types may require more or less styling.

Next, you’ll want to choose the right products for your hair. This might include a volumizing mousse, a texturizing spray, or a smoothing serum, depending on the look you’re going for.

Finally, you’ll want to experiment with different techniques and styles to find what works best for you. Whether you’re looking for a sleek and polished look or something more tousled and messy, there are plenty of options to choose from.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide to styling your pixie cut:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Before you begin styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help your products work better and ensure that your style lasts longer.

2. Apply product.

Next, apply your chosen styling product to your hair. This might include a volumizing mousse, a texturizing spray, or a smoothing serum, depending on the look you’re going for.

3. Blow dry your hair.

Using a blow dryer, begin drying your hair in small sections, using a round brush to add volume and shape. Continue until your hair is completely dry.

4. Use a flat iron or curling iron (optional).

If you want to add some extra texture or curl to your hair, you can use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls.

5. Finish with hairspray.

Finally, finish your style with a light hold hairspray to keep it in place throughout the day.

Tips for Styling a Pixie Cut

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your pixie cut:

  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new – whether it’s a bold color or an edgy cut, a pixie cut is a great opportunity to express your personal style.
  • Invest in quality styling tools, such as a good blow dryer and flat iron, to help you achieve the best possible results.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get my pixie cut trimmed?

A: It’s a good idea to get your pixie cut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and maintain its shape.

Q: Can I style my pixie cut without heat?

A: Yes! There are plenty of heat-free styling options for pixie cuts, such as braids, twists, and buns.

Q: Will a pixie cut work for my face shape?

A: Pixie cuts can work for a variety of face shapes, but it’s important to choose a style that flatters your unique features. Talk to your stylist to find the best style for you.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

Here are a few tips to keep your pixie cut looking healthy and strong:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you’ll be able to style your pixie cut like a pro in no time. Whether you opt for a sleek and polished look or something more tousled and messy, a pixie cut is a versatile and stylish option for anyone looking to switch up their look.
