What Hair Cut Would Suit Me

Written by Daniel Jul 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle? Are you looking for a change but not sure what haircut would suit you? Look no further! In this article, we will go over how to determine what haircut would suit you, step by step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

Description

Choosing a haircut can be daunting, but with the right guidance, it can be a fun experience. The first step is determining your face shape to find a style that complements your features. Haircuts can accentuate your best features or detract from them, so it's important to find a style that works for you.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The easiest way to determine your face shape is to pull your hair back and study your face in the mirror. There are five main face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Take note of your prominent features and compare them to the descriptions below:

  • Round: full cheeks, rounded chin, and equal width and length of the face.
  • Oval: balanced features, slightly rounded jaw, and a forehead that's wider than the chin.
  • Square: strong jawline, square chin, and a forehead and jawline that are the same width.
  • Heart: wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin.
  • Diamond: narrow forehead and jawline, with wide cheekbones.

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to find a haircut that flatters your features.

Step by Step

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find a haircut that suits you. Here are some step by step instructions:

  1. Research hairstyles that work well with your face shape.
  2. Consult with a hairstylist to get their opinion and recommendations.
  3. Consider your hair texture and lifestyle when choosing a haircut.
  4. Bring a picture of the desired haircut to your hairstylist.
  5. Communicate your preferences and concerns to your hairstylist.
  6. Trust your hairstylist's professional opinion.
  7. Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims and using the right hair care products.

Following these steps will ensure that you get a haircut that suits you and complements your features.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut:

  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a haircut.
  • Think about how much time and effort you want to put into styling your hair every day.
  • Choose a haircut that aligns with your personal style and fashion sense.
  • Be open to trying new things and experimenting with different hairstyles.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a haircut:

  • What if I don't like my new haircut?
  • Can I change my haircut if I don't like it?
  • How often should I get my hair cut?
  • What hair care products should I use?

Hair Care

After getting a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Consider using hair masks and oils to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know what haircut would suit you, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and get the perfect haircut for your face shape and features. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
