Short Back And Sides Haircut The Classic Style That Never Goes Out Of Fashion

Table of Contents:

Description

The short back and sides haircut is a classic style that has been popular for many years. It is a versatile cut that can be worn by men of all ages and hair types. The style involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the hair on top longer. This creates a contrast between the top and the sides, which is what gives the style its signature look.

The short back and sides haircut is a great choice for men who want a low-maintenance style that looks neat and tidy. It is also a popular choice for men who work in professional settings, as it gives off a clean and polished appearance.

How to

To get a short back and sides haircut, you will need to visit a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in this style. They will use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back, while using scissors to trim the hair on top. The length of the hair on top can vary, depending on your personal preference and the shape of your face.

Choosing the Right Length

When choosing the length of your short back and sides haircut, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to go for a shorter length on top to create a more angular look. If you have a longer face, you can go for a longer length on top to balance out your features.

It's also important to consider your hair type. If you have thick hair, you may want to go for a shorter length on top to make the style more manageable. If you have thin hair, you can go for a longer length on top to create the illusion of more volume.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a short back and sides haircut:

  1. Visit a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in this style.
  2. Decide on the length of the hair on top.
  3. Sit in the barber's chair and let them get to work.
  4. They will use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back.
  5. They will use scissors to trim the hair on top.
  6. Once they are finished, they will show you the final result and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short back and sides haircut:

  • Visit your barber or hairstylist every four to six weeks for a trim.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Style your hair with a pomade or wax to keep it in place.
  • If you have a receding hairline, consider going for a shorter length on top to create the illusion of a fuller head of hair.

FAQ

Is the short back and sides haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the short back and sides haircut can be adapted to suit all face shapes. It's important to choose the right length of hair on top to balance out your features.

What is the best way to style a short back and sides haircut?

The best way to style a short back and sides haircut is with a pomade or wax. Apply a small amount to your fingertips and work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair after getting a short back and sides haircut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as hair dryers and straighteners.
  • If you have a dry scalp, use a moisturizing hair oil to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

Overall, the short back and sides haircut is a classic style that never goes out of fashion. It is a low-maintenance and versatile cut that can be adapted to suit all face shapes and hair types. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can maintain your short back and sides haircut and keep it looking sharp and stylish.
