How To Make A Pixie Cut Look Masculine

Written by Daniel Jul 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In recent years, the pixie cut has become a popular hairstyle for women, but it can also be a great option for men who want a shorter, more low-maintenance haircut. The key to making a pixie cut look masculine is all in the styling. Here's how to achieve a masculine look with a pixie cut:

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle where the hair is cut very short on the sides and back and left longer on the top. It's a low-maintenance hairstyle that's easy to style and perfect for those who don't want to spend a lot of time on their hair. To make a pixie cut look masculine, you'll need to focus on the texture and styling of the hair.

How To

Step 1: Choose the right cut

The first step to achieving a masculine look with a pixie cut is to choose the right cut. A pixie cut with shorter sides and a longer top will look more masculine than a cut with longer sides and a shorter top. You can also consider adding some texture to the hair by asking your stylist to use a razor or scissors to create choppy layers.

Step 2: Use the right products

The right styling products can make all the difference when it comes to achieving a masculine look with a pixie cut. For a textured, messy look, use a pomade or wax. For a sleek, polished look, use a styling cream or gel. Be sure to choose products that are specifically designed for men's hair.

Step 3: Style the hair

To achieve a masculine look with a pixie cut, you'll need to style the hair in a way that emphasizes the texture and shape of the cut. Use your fingers to apply the styling product to the hair, then use a comb or your fingers to create the desired style. For a messy, textured look, use your fingers to tousle the hair. For a polished, sleek look, use a comb to smooth the hair down.

Tips

  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Consider using a blow dryer to add volume and texture to the hair.
  • Keep the hair trimmed regularly to maintain the shape of the cut.

FAQ

Can I still color my hair with a pixie cut?

Yes, you can still color your hair with a pixie cut. Just be sure to choose a color that complements your skin tone and the shape of the cut.

How often should I wash my hair with a pixie cut?

It's best to wash your hair every other day with a pixie cut to prevent it from becoming too dry or damaged. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and hydrated.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie cut looking its best, be sure to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using too much heat when styling the hair, as this can damage the hair and cause it to become brittle. Finally, be sure to get regular trims to maintain the shape of the cut and keep the hair looking neat and tidy.
