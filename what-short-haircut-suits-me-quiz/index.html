What Short Haircut Suits Me Quiz

Written by Aprilia May 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're considering getting a short haircut, but you're not sure which style suits you best, taking a short haircut quiz can help you decide. Short haircuts come in many different styles, from pixie cuts to bobs, and finding the perfect style for you can be overwhelming. This article will guide you on how to take a "what short haircut suits me quiz," provide you with tips on selecting the right cut, and how to care for your new short hair.

Description

A "what short haircut suits me quiz" is an online questionnaire that analyzes your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle to determine the best short haircut for you. The quiz typically asks you a series of questions about your hair type, face shape, and personal style preferences. Based on your answers, the quiz will recommend a short haircut that will flatter your features and suit your lifestyle.

How to Take a Short Haircut Quiz?

To take a short haircut quiz, you need to find a reliable quiz online. There are many quizzes available on the internet, but not all of them are accurate or reliable. To ensure that you get the best results, look for a quiz from a reputable source or a hair salon website. Once you find a quiz, follow these steps:

  1. Read the instructions carefully before starting the quiz.
  2. Answer all the questions truthfully and accurately.
  3. Submit your answers and wait for the results.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to take a short haircut quiz:

  1. Go to a reliable website that offers a "what short haircut suits me quiz."
  2. Read the instructions and start the quiz.
  3. Answer the questions honestly and accurately.
  4. Submit your answers and wait for the results.
  5. Read the recommendations and choose the haircut that best suits you.
  6. Print the recommendations or take a screenshot of them.
  7. Bring the recommendations to your hair stylist.
  8. Discuss the recommendations with your stylist and ask for their opinion.
  9. Get the haircut that you and your stylist agree on.
  10. Enjoy your new short haircut!

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results from a "what short haircut suits me quiz":

  • Answer the questions honestly and accurately.
  • Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing a haircut.
  • Think about your lifestyle and daily routine when selecting a haircut.
  • Bring the recommendations to your hair stylist for their opinion.
  • Ask your stylist for tips on how to style and maintain your new haircut.

FAQ

Q: Can a short haircut suit any face shape?

A: Yes, there is a short haircut that can suit any face shape. However, the style and length of the cut may vary depending on your face shape and hair type.

Q: Can I change my short haircut if I don't like it?

A: Yes, you can change your short haircut if you don't like it. However, it's best to wait a few weeks before making any drastic changes to allow your hair to grow out a bit.

Q: How often should I trim my short haircut?

A: It's recommended to trim your short haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs proper care to look its best. Here are some tips to take care of your short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day or as needed.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner formulated for your hair type.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

A "what short haircut suits me quiz" can help you find the perfect short haircut for your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. By following the tips and taking good care of your hair, you can enjoy your new short haircut for months to come.
