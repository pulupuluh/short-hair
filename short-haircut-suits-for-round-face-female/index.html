Short Haircut Suits For Round Face Female

Written by Robby Jul 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face, you might be wondering what haircut would suit you best. The good news is, short haircuts can look great on women with round faces. In this article, we will show you some of the best short haircuts that are perfect for women with round faces. Let's get started!

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a wide forehead, and a rounded chin. The goal of a good haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of length and to make the face appear slimmer. Short haircuts can achieve this by adding volume and texture to the hair, which can create the illusion of a longer face.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect short haircut for a round face is to determine your face shape. Look in the mirror and take note of the width of your forehead, the fullness of your cheeks, and the roundness of your chin. Once you have determined that you have a round face, you can start looking for the perfect haircut.

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider the texture of your hair. If you have fine hair, you might want to opt for a layered bob or a pixie cut. If you have thick hair, a choppy bob or a shaggy cut might be more suitable.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape

2. Consider the texture of your hair

3. Look for inspiration

4. Consult with a hairstylist

5. Choose the perfect short haircut for your face shape and hair texture

Tips

- Avoid blunt cuts that will make your face look rounder

- Add volume and texture to your hair to create the illusion of length

- Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair

- Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one

FAQ

What short haircuts are best for round faces?

Some of the best short haircuts for round faces include pixie cuts, layered bobs, choppy bobs, and shaggy cuts.

Should I get highlights or lowlights?

Highlights or lowlights can add dimension to your hair and make it appear less flat. Talk to your hairstylist about what would look best on you.

What should I avoid when getting a short haircut for a round face?

Avoid blunt cuts that will make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for cuts that add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

Once you have your perfect short haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Use a good shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished. And don't forget to get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

So there you have it, some of the best short haircuts that suit round face females. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and consult with a hairstylist before making a decision. With the right haircut and hair care, you can look and feel your best!
