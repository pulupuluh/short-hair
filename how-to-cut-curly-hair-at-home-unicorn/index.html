How To Cut Curly Hair At Home Unicorn

Written by Robby Mar 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be a bit tricky to cut, but with a few tips and tricks, you can do it at home with ease. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting curly hair at home in a unicorn style. So, let's get started!

Description

If you're tired of paying high prices for haircuts, or you're trying to maintain social distance during the pandemic, cutting your curly hair at home is a great option. Unicorn hairstyles are popular among curly-haired people, and with a little bit of practice, you can create a new and unique look for yourself.

How to Cut Curly Hair at Home Unicorn

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a wide-tooth comb, hair clips, a spray bottle filled with water, and a mirror. Make sure to use sharp scissors, as dull ones can cause split ends and damage to your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Detangle Your Hair

Wash your hair and make sure it's clean and detangled. Comb your hair with a wide-tooth comb to remove any knots or tangles. Wet your hair with a spray bottle filled with water to make the hair easier to cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair by dividing it into four parts. Use hair clips to secure each section. Start by dividing your hair in the middle of your head, then split each side into two sections. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Start by cutting the tips of your hair. Hold a section of hair between your fingers and cut the tips straight across. Repeat this process for each section. Then, use your scissors to create a unicorn shape by cutting the hair at an angle on the sides of your head.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After cutting your hair, check your work in the mirror. Make sure the length and shape are even on both sides. If you notice any unevenness, use your scissors to trim the hair until it looks even.

Tips

  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need to. You can always go back and cut more if needed.
  • Practice on small sections of hair before cutting larger ones.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time to ensure the best results.

FAQ

Can I cut curly hair when it's dry?

It's best to cut curly hair when it's wet or damp. This will make it easier to cut and ensure even results.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended to cut curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Is it difficult to cut curly hair at home?

Cutting curly hair can be a bit challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, it's definitely doable. Just take your time and practice on small sections of hair before cutting larger ones.

Hair Care

After cutting your curly hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Cutting your curly hair at home unicorn style can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a unique and stylish look for yourself. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and practice on small sections of hair before cutting larger ones.
