Mens Haircuts 2020 Mid Fade A Guide To Effortlessly Stylish Hair

Written by Bardi Mar 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking for a fresh new look to take on the world this 2023? Look no further than the mid fade! In this article, we’ll go over everything you need to know about the popular mens haircuts 2020 mid fade, including how to get it, tips for styling, and hair care.

How to Get a Mid Fade Haircut

The mid fade haircut is a stylish and versatile haircut that works well for almost any hair type or face shape. It features a gradual taper from the sides to the back of the head, leaving a longer section of hair on top. Here’s how to get the look:

  1. Start by deciding how high you want your fade to be. The mid fade typically starts halfway up your head.
  2. Ask your barber to use clippers to fade the hair on the sides and back of your head. They’ll gradually blend the hair from short to long, leaving the longest hair on top.
  3. Once the fade is complete, your barber will then trim and style the hair on top to your desired length and shape.

And there you have it! You now have a stylish mid fade haircut that’s sure to turn heads.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Mid Fade

Now that you’ve got your mid fade haircut, it’s time to learn how to style it. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by towel-drying your hair after showering.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or styling cream to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up to the tips.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into your desired shape.
  4. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

And just like that, you’ve got a stylish and effortless look that’s perfect for any occasion.

Tips for Styling a Mid Fade

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your mid fade haircut:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to create texture and volume in your hair.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask your barber for styling tips or product recommendations.

FAQs About Mens Haircuts 2020 Mid Fade

What is a mid fade haircut?

A mid fade haircut is a popular mens haircut that features a gradual taper from the sides to the back of the head, leaving a longer section of hair on top.

What hair types work best with a mid fade?

A mid fade works well for almost any hair type, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily hair.

How often should I get a mid fade haircut?

It’s recommended to get a mid fade haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Mid Fade

Here are a few hair care tips to help you maintain your mid fade haircut:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and dryness.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your mid fade.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to maintain your stylish mid fade haircut for months to come.
