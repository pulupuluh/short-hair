What Is The Men s Haircut Called With Shaved Sides

Written by Aprilia Jul 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our blog post about the popular men's haircut with shaved sides. This is a trendy and stylish haircut that has become increasingly popular in recent years. In this post, we will be discussing what this haircut is called, how to get it, tips for styling it, and how to take care of it. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Haircut
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

The men's haircut with shaved sides is also known as the undercut. It features short or shaved sides and longer hair on top. The top hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. This haircut is versatile and can be worn in both formal and casual settings. It's a great option for men who want to add some edge and style to their look.

How to Get the Haircut

To get the men's haircut with shaved sides, you will need to visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in this style. It's important to find someone who understands your hair type and can create a customized cut that suits your face shape and personal style. Before your appointment, it's a good idea to bring in some pictures of the style you like to give your stylist a clear idea of what you're looking for.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to getting the men's haircut with shaved sides:

  1. Choose the length of the sides you want. You can go for a full shave or just a short buzz.
  2. Decide on the length and style of the top hair. You can keep it long, medium, or short and style it in a variety of ways.
  3. Ask your barber or stylist to blend the top and sides to create a seamless transition.
  4. Once the cut is complete, ask your stylist to style your hair in the desired way.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling the men's haircut with shaved sides:

  • Use a high-quality styling product, such as pomade or wax, to create the desired look.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Consider adding some texture to the hair on top for a more casual, laid-back look.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber or stylist for styling tips and advice.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut with shaved sides:

Is the men's haircut with shaved sides suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can be customized to suit any hair type, from straight to curly to wavy.

How often will I need to get this haircut?

The frequency of haircuts will depend on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. Generally, you'll need to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh.

Can I style this haircut in different ways?

Yes, the men's haircut with shaved sides is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your men's haircut with shaved sides looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and nourished.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools, such as a hairdryer or straightener.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

That concludes our blog post about the men's haircut with shaved sides. We hope you found this information helpful and that you feel inspired to try out this trendy and stylish haircut!
