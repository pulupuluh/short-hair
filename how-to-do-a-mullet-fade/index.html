How To Do A Mullet Fade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Feb 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a fresh new hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the mullet fade! This trendy hairstyle combines the classic mullet with a modern fade, creating a look that is both edgy and stylish. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect mullet fade.

Description

The mullet fade is a hairstyle that features longer hair on the top and shorter hair on the sides and back. The fade refers to the gradual transition from longer to shorter hair, with the hair on the sides and back being buzzed or shaved to create a sharp contrast. This hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years and is favored by many celebrities and musicians.

How To

Before you begin, gather the necessary tools: clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Now, follow these steps:

Step 1: Determine the Length

The first step in achieving a mullet fade is to determine how long you want your hair to be on top. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. Use a comb to separate the top section of your hair from the sides and back.

Step 2: Cut the Top

Using scissors, cut the top section of your hair to your desired length. It is best to do this while your hair is dry, so you can see the final result.

Step 3: Create the Fade

Next, use clippers to create the fade. Start with a longer guard and gradually switch to a shorter guard as you move down the sides and back of your head. Be sure to blend the hair so that there are no harsh lines.

Step 4: Blend the Top and Sides

Use scissors to blend the top section of your hair with the shorter hair on the sides and back. This will create a seamless transition between the two sections.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Now that you have achieved the perfect mullet fade, it is time to style your hair. Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best possible mullet fade:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard to ensure a smooth transition
  • Use a comb to separate the top section of your hair from the sides and back
  • Blend the top section of your hair with the shorter hair on the sides and back to create a seamless transition
  • Use a styling product to add texture and hold

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mullet fade:

How long does the mullet fade last?

With proper maintenance and upkeep, the mullet fade can last up to a month or more.

Can anyone pull off a mullet fade?

Yes, the mullet fade can be tailored to suit any face shape or hair type.

What styling products should I use?

Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to your hair.

Hair Care

To maintain your mullet fade, you should wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Use a styling product to add texture and hold, but be sure to wash it out thoroughly at the end of the day. Visit your hairstylist for regular trims to keep your mullet fade looking fresh and stylish.

Now that you know how to achieve a mullet fade, it's time to try it out for yourself! With a little bit of practice and patience, you can achieve a stylish and edgy look that is sure to turn heads.
