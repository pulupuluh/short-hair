How To Make Your Face Look Round Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Aug 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have an angular or square face, you may want to soften your features and make your face look rounder. Fortunately, there are many ways to achieve this look without resorting to plastic surgery. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make your face look round.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Face Look Round
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

How to Make Your Face Look Round

There are several ways to make your face look rounder:

  • Use makeup to contour your face
  • Choose the right hairstyle
  • Wear the right accessories
  • Take care of your skin

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make your face look round:

Step 1: Use Makeup to Contour Your Face

Contouring is a makeup technique that can help you create the illusion of a rounder face. Use a contouring powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone to create shadows on your face. Apply the contouring product on the sides of your forehead, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline. Blend the product well for a natural look.

Step 2: Choose the Right Hairstyle

The right hairstyle can make a big difference in the shape of your face. If you want to make your face look rounder, choose a hairstyle that has soft layers and volume on the sides. Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they can make your face look longer and more angular.

Step 3: Wear the Right Accessories

Accessories can also help you create the illusion of a rounder face. Choose earrings that are round or hoop-shaped, as they can draw attention to the roundness of your face. Avoid earrings that are too long or angular, as they can elongate your face. You can also wear glasses with round frames to soften your features.

Step 4: Take Care of Your Skin

Healthy skin can make your face look rounder and more youthful. Make sure to moisturize your skin daily and protect it from the sun with a sunscreen. You can also use a face mask once a week to keep your skin looking fresh and radiant.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to make your face look rounder:

  • Use blush on the apples of your cheeks to create a natural flush
  • Highlight the center of your forehead, nose, and chin to draw attention to the center of your face
  • Choose clothing with round necklines or collars to balance the angularity of your face
  • Avoid wearing hats that are too flat or angular, as they can make your face look longer

FAQs

Q: Can I make my face look round without makeup?

A: Yes, you can make your face look round by choosing the right hairstyle, wearing the right accessories, and taking care of your skin.

Q: Do I have to contour my face every day?

A: No, you don't have to contour your face every day. Contouring is a technique that is best used for special occasions or when you want to create a dramatic look.

Q: Can I make my face look round if I have a square jawline?

A: Yes, you can make your face look round by using makeup to contour your jawline and choosing a hairstyle with soft layers and volume on the sides.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

If you have a round face, here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they can make your face look longer
  • Choose a hairstyle with soft layers and volume on the sides
  • Avoid hairstyles with a lot of volume on top, as they can make your face look rounder
  • Consider getting a side-swept fringe to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your face look rounder and more youthful. Remember to choose a hairstyle and makeup look that makes you feel confident and comfortable.
