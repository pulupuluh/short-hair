How To Do Messy Space Buns With Thin Hair

Written by Aprilia Sep 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In this year 2023, messy space buns have become one of the trendiest hairstyles for women with thin hair. They are easy to do, look adorable, and are perfect for any casual or formal occasion. However, if you have thin hair, you might think that this hairstyle is not for you. Worry not, because with the right technique, you can achieve the perfect messy space buns with thin hair. In this article, we will guide you on how to do messy space buns with thin hair.

Description

Space buns are a hairstyle that involves twisting your hair into two buns on either side of your head. The messy space bun is a variation of this hairstyle that involves creating a more relaxed and effortless look. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add a fun and playful touch to their look. However, if you have thin hair, creating this hairstyle can be a challenge.

How to

Here are the steps to create messy space buns with thin hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and applying a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Blow dry your hair to add volume and texture.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into two sections, parting it down the middle. Use a hair tie to tie one section of your hair while you work on the other section.

Step 3: Create the Bun

Grab the section of hair you are working on and twist it into a bun. Secure it with bobby pins. Do the same with the other section of hair.

Step 4: Mess It Up

Use your fingers to loosen up the buns and create a messy look. Pull out some strands of hair around your face to soften the look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating messy space buns with thin hair:

- Use dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair. - Tease your hair before creating the bun to add more volume. - Use bobby pins that match the color of your hair to hide them. - Finish the look with hairspray to keep the buns in place.

FAQ

Q: Can I create messy space buns with short hair?

A: Yes, you can create messy space buns with short hair. However, you might need to use more bobby pins to secure the buns in place.

Q: Can I create messy space buns with curly hair?

A: Yes, you can create messy space buns with curly hair. However, you might need to straighten your hair before creating the buns to make the look more polished.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, make sure to wash your hair regularly and use products that are suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and protect it from the sun. A healthy diet and lifestyle can also contribute to healthy and strong hair.

Now that you know how to do messy space buns with thin hair, try it out and have fun with your new hairstyle!
