How To Cut Hair Short With Layers At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Apr 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and want to learn how to cut hair short with layers at home, you've come to the right place! This guide will provide you with everything you need to know to achieve a salon-worthy haircut from the comfort of your own home.

How to Cut Hair Short with Layers at Home

Cutting your own hair can seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done successfully. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A fine-toothed comb
  • Hair clips to section hair
  • A spray bottle filled with water

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then, dry it completely using a towel or hair dryer.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your fine-toothed comb, section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. Clip each section to keep them separated.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Starting with the back sections, use your comb to pull out a small section of hair at the desired length. Cut the hair using your scissors, keeping the blades parallel to the hair for a clean cut. Work your way up, section by section, until you've cut all of the hair in the back.

Step 4: Cut the Front

Once you're done with the back, unclip the front sections and repeat the same process, cutting the hair at the desired length. For layers, pull out small sections of hair at an angle and cut them slightly shorter than the rest of the hair.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Using your scissors, blend the layers by cutting into the hair at an angle. This will create a softer, more natural look.

Step 6: Check for Symmetry

After you've finished cutting, check to make sure that both sides are symmetrical by comparing them in the mirror.

Tips for Cutting Hair Short with Layers at Home

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use a comb to keep the hair straight and even while cutting.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it will appear longer when it dries.
  • Be patient and take your time to ensure a precise cut.

FAQ: Cutting Hair Short with Layers at Home

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

No, it's important to use sharp hair cutting scissors to achieve a clean cut and avoid damaging the hair.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! You can always fix it by trimming the hair a little more or seeking professional help.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Hair Care after Cutting Your Hair Short with Layers at Home

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and shine. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools and let your hair air dry whenever possible.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to deeply nourish your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you'll be able to cut your hair short with layers at home like a pro. Good luck!
