How To Get Wavy Hair With A Curling Iron

Written by Oliver Jul 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to add some waves to your locks? Look no further than your trusty curling iron! With a few simple steps, you can achieve effortless beach waves or bouncy curls that will elevate your hair game. In this article, we will go over how to get wavy hair with a curling iron, step by step.

How to:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, it's important to prep your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then apply a heat protectant spray or serum. This will help protect your hair from heat damage and prevent frizz. Blow dry your hair until it's completely dry and tangle-free.

Step 2: Choose Your Curling Iron

There are many types of curling irons available, each with different barrel sizes and shapes. For beach waves, choose a curling iron with a larger barrel size, around 1.5 inches. For tighter curls, go for a smaller barrel size, around 1 inch.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable parts, starting from the bottom of your head. Use hair clips to hold each section in place, leaving a small section of hair out to curl at a time.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel, holding it for a few seconds. Release the curl and move on to the next section, alternating the direction of the curl for a more natural look. Repeat until all sections are curled.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've curled all your hair, finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Tips:

  • Use a curling iron with adjustable heat settings to prevent heat damage.
  • Experiment with different curling iron barrel sizes to find the perfect size for your hair type and desired look.
  • For a more natural look, leave the ends of your hair uncurled.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to gently comb through your curls for a softer look.

FAQ:

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: The longevity of your curls depends on your hair type and the products you use. Typically, curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Q: Can I curl my hair without a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can achieve wavy hair without a curling iron by using braids or buns.

Hair Care:

It's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain healthy locks. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week and avoid using heat on your hair too often. Additionally, try to use a heat protectant spray or serum before using any hot tools.

Now that you know how to get wavy hair with a curling iron, it's time to try it out for yourself! With these simple steps and tips, you'll have gorgeous waves in no time.
