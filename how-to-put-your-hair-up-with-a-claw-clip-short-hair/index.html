How To Put Your Hair Up With A Claw Clip Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Apr 25, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and want to mix things up? Claw clips can be a great solution for those with short hair who want to put their hair up in a stylish and easy way. In this guide, we will show you how to put your hair up with a claw clip in just a few simple steps.

How to Use a Claw Clip

Before we get started with the step-by-step guide, it’s important to understand how to use a claw clip. Claw clips are designed to hold your hair in place, using two interlocking combs that snap together. They come in various sizes and designs, so it’s essential to choose the right one for your hair type and length.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now, let’s get started with the step-by-step guide. Follow these simple steps to put your hair up with a claw clip:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather your hair in your hand and twist it upwards towards the crown of your head.
  3. Hold the twisted hair with one hand and use the other hand to open the claw clip with your thumb and forefinger.
  4. Place the open claw clip over the twisted hair and secure it in place by snapping the combs together.
  5. Adjust the claw clip as needed to ensure it’s secure and comfortable.
  6. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair if necessary.
  7. Finish the look by adding hairspray or hair accessories if desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using a claw clip:

  • Choose the right size claw clip for your hair type and length.
  • Use a claw clip with teeth to ensure a secure hold.
  • Experiment with different styles and placements for a unique look.
  • Don’t pull your hair too tightly when twisting it to avoid damaging it.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about putting your hair up with a claw clip:

  • Can I use a claw clip on wet hair?
  • How long can I wear a claw clip for?
  • What other hair accessories can I use with a claw clip?

Hair Care

It’s essential to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to put your hair up with a claw clip, it’s time to experiment with different styles and looks. With a little practice, you’ll be able to create a variety of hairstyles that are both stylish and easy to do.
