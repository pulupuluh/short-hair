How To Thicken Your Thin Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi May 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thin hair, you know how frustrating it can be to style and maintain. But don't worry, there are plenty of ways to add volume and thickness to your locks. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you achieve fuller, thicker hair.

How to Thicken Your Thin Hair

Here are some tips to help you thicken your thin hair:

1. Use volumizing shampoo and conditioner

Start by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products contain ingredients that help to lift the hair and add volume. Look for products that contain keratin, biotin, or collagen, as these ingredients can help to strengthen and thicken the hair.

2. Avoid using heavy products

Avoid using heavy styling products, such as oils and gels. These products can weigh down your hair and make it look even thinner. Instead, opt for lightweight products, such as mousses and sprays.

3. Try a thickening spray

Thickening sprays are a great way to add volume and thickness to your hair. These sprays contain polymers that coat the hair and make it appear thicker. Apply the spray to damp hair, then blow dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.

4. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent it from looking thin and stringy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

5. Use a wide-toothed comb

Using a wide-toothed comb can help to prevent breakage and damage to your hair. Brushing your hair with a regular brush can cause your hair to break and become even thinner.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you thicken your thin hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products will help to add volume and thickness to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a thickening spray

Apply a thickening spray to damp hair. This will help to add even more volume and thickness to your locks.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair upside down

Blow dry your hair upside down to add even more volume. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots.

Step 4: Use a wide-toothed comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and damage to your locks.

Tips for Thicker Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve thicker hair:

1. Eat a healthy diet

Eating a healthy diet can help to promote hair growth and thickness. Make sure to include plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools

Heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, can damage your hair and make it appear thinner. Try to limit your use of these tools as much as possible.

3. Get enough sleep

Getting enough sleep is important for overall hair health. Aim to get 7-8 hours of sleep per night to help promote hair growth and thickness.

4. Use hair masks

Using a hair mask once a week can help to nourish and strengthen your hair. Look for masks that contain ingredients like coconut oil, avocado, and honey.

FAQs

1. Can I thicken my thin hair naturally?

Yes, there are several natural ways to thicken your thin hair. Eating a healthy diet, using hair masks, and avoiding heat styling tools can all help to promote hair growth and thickness.

2. Will cutting my hair make it thicker?

Getting regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage, but it won't necessarily make your hair thicker. However, having healthy hair can make it appear thicker and fuller.

3. How often should I wash my hair?

This depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people can go several days without washing their hair, while others prefer to wash their hair every day. Aim to wash your hair as often as needed to keep it clean and healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Thicker Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you achieve thicker hair:

1. Use a silk pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can help to prevent breakage and damage to your hair. Silk is gentle on the hair and can help to prevent tangles and knots.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

The sun's rays can damage your hair and make it appear thinner. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to prevent damage.

3. Avoid tight hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles, such as ponytails and braids, as these can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

4. Use a hair serum

Using a hair serum can help to add shine and smoothness to your hair. Look for serums that contain natural oils, such as argan oil or jojoba oil.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve thicker, fuller hair in no time.
