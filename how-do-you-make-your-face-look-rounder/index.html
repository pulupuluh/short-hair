How Do You Make Your Face Look Rounder

Written by Daniel Jun 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you have a naturally angular face and want to make it look rounder? There are several ways to achieve a rounder face shape without resorting to surgical procedures. In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks to make your face look rounder, from facial exercises to makeup techniques.

Table of Contents

  1. Facial Exercises
  2. Makeup Techniques
  3. Hairstyle Tips
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care

Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone and strengthen the muscles in your face, which can help create a rounder appearance. Here are a few exercises to try:

Cheek Puff Exercise

Take a deep breath and puff your cheeks out as much as possible. Hold for a few seconds, then release the air slowly. Repeat this exercise 10-15 times.

Jaw Release Exercise

Open your mouth as wide as possible, then move your jaw up and down as if you were chewing. Repeat this exercise 10-15 times.

Tongue Press Exercise

Press your tongue against the roof of your mouth and hold for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this exercise 10-15 times.

Makeup Techniques

Makeup can also help create a rounder face shape. Here are some techniques to try:

Contouring

Contouring involves using makeup to create shadows and highlights on your face, which can help create a more defined, rounder shape. Use a bronzer or contour powder to shade the areas below your cheekbones, along your hairline, and around your jawline.

Blush

Applying blush to the apples of your cheeks can help create a rounder, more youthful look. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone and apply it in a circular motion on the apples of your cheeks.

Hairstyle Tips

Your hairstyle can also play a role in how round your face appears. Here are some tips to consider:

Layers

Layered haircuts can help create movement and volume, which can help make your face look rounder. Talk to your hairstylist about adding layers to your hair.

Soft Waves

Soft waves can also help create a rounder face shape. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves in your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can facial exercise really help make my face look rounder?

A: Yes, facial exercises can help tone and strengthen the muscles in your face, which can help create a rounder appearance.

Q: How long does it take to see results from facial exercises?

A: It can take a few weeks to a few months to see results from facial exercises. Consistency is key.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your hair can also help create a rounder face shape. Here are some tips:

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth.

Use a Heat Protectant

Using a heat protectant before using hot tools on your hair can help prevent damage and breakage.

Overall, there are several ways to make your face look rounder, from facial exercises to makeup techniques and hairstyle tips. Experiment with different methods to find what works best for you.
