How To Do Party Hairstyles For Long Hair At Home

Written by Eveline Jul 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have long hair, you have the perfect canvas to create stunning party hairstyles. However, going to the salon to get your hair styled can be expensive and time-consuming. So why not learn how to do party hairstyles for long hair at home? With a little practice, you can create gorgeous hairstyles that will turn heads at any party.

Table of Contents:

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to:

The first step to doing party hairstyles for long hair at home is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a comb, a hairbrush, bobby pins, hair elastics, and hair spray. Once you have all the tools, follow these steps:

1. Decide on the Hairstyle

Before you start styling your hair, decide on the hairstyle you want to create. You can browse online for inspiration or create your own style. Once you have decided on the hairstyle, make sure you have a clear picture in your mind of how it should look.

2. Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.

3. Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using a comb. This will make it easier to style your hair. You can use hair elastics to secure the sections.

4. Start Styling

Take one section of hair and start styling it as per the hairstyle you have decided. Use bobby pins to secure the hair in place. Repeat this step for all the sections of hair.

5. Finish with Hair Spray

Once you have styled your hair, finish with a good quality hair spray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step:

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to do party hairstyles for long hair at home:

Step 1:

Decide on the hairstyle you want to create.

Step 2:

Wash and dry your hair.

Step 3:

Divide your hair into sections using a comb.

Step 4:

Take one section of hair and start styling it as per the hairstyle you have decided.

Step 5:

Use bobby pins to secure the hair in place.

Step 6:

Repeat steps 4 and 5 for all the sections of hair.

Step 7:

Finish with a good quality hair spray to hold the style in place.

Tips:

Here are some tips to help you do party hairstyles for long hair at home:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying your hair.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • If you have thin hair, use a volumizing mousse to add texture.
  • Practice the hairstyle before the party to ensure you get it right.

FAQ:

Q. How long will the hairstyle last?

A. The length of time the hairstyle will last depends on the hairstyle, the products you use, and your hair type. However, a good quality hair spray will help hold the style in place for longer.

Q. What if I make a mistake while styling my hair?

A. Don't worry if you make a mistake. You can always undo the hairstyle and start again. Just make sure to have enough time before the party to get it right.

Hair Care:

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can weigh down your hair.

With these tips and steps, you can now confidently do party hairstyles for long hair at home. So next time you have a party to attend, save some money and time by styling your hair at home.
