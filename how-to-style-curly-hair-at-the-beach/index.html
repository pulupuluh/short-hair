How To Style Curly Hair At The Beach A Guide For Effortless Beachy Waves

Written by Aprilia Jul 27, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Styling curly hair can be a challenge, especially when you're at the beach. The combination of humidity, saltwater, and wind can easily turn your beautiful curls into a frizzy mess. But with the right techniques, you can achieve effortless beachy waves that will make you look and feel amazing. In this guide, we'll show you how to style curly hair at the beach and keep it looking fabulous all day long.

Table of Contents:

  • Hair Care
  • How to Style Curly Hair at the Beach
  • Step-by-Step Tutorial
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Beachy Waves
  • FAQs About Styling Curly Hair at the Beach

Hair Care:

Before you head to the beach, it's important to take care of your curls. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and hydrated:

1. Deep Condition:

Deep conditioning is essential for curly hair, especially if you're going to the beach. Use a hydrating mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized and prevent breakage.

2. Detangle:

Detangling your hair before you go to the beach will make it easier to style and prevent knots and tangles. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently remove any knots.

3. Protect:

Protect your hair from the sun and saltwater by wearing a hat or scarf. You can also apply a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from UV damage.

How to Style Curly Hair at the Beach:

Now that your hair is prepped and ready, it's time to style it for the beach. Follow these steps to achieve effortless beachy waves:

1. Start with Wet Hair:

The best time to style your curly hair is right after you wash it. Wet hair is easier to manipulate and will hold its shape better. If you don't want to wash your hair, you can wet it down with a spray bottle.

2. Apply Styling Product:

Apply a styling product to your hair to help define your curls and prevent frizz. Use a curl cream, gel, or mousse, depending on your hair type and desired look. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

3. Scrunch:

Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your curls to form. Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to gently squeeze out any excess water.

4. Air Dry:

Air dry your hair to avoid heat damage and preserve your curls' shape. If you're in a hurry, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to speed up the drying process.

Step-by-Step Tutorial:

Follow these steps for a more detailed tutorial on how to style your curly hair at the beach:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as a curl cream or gel. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 3: Part Your Hair

Part your hair in the desired style. You can part it down the middle, to the side, or create a zigzag part for a more bohemian look.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two or three parts, depending on how thick your hair is. Secure each section with a hair clip or elastic band.

Step 5: Twist and Pin

Twist each section of your hair and pin it up with bobby pins. Leave the pins in place until your hair is dry.

Step 6: Let Your Hair Down

Once your hair is dry, remove the bobby pins and gently shake out your hair. Use your fingers to separate your curls and create volume.

Tips for Maintaining Your Beachy Waves:

To keep your beachy waves looking great all day long, follow these tips:

1. Avoid Touching Your Hair:

Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can cause frizz and disrupt your curls' shape.

2. Use a Sea Salt Spray:

A sea salt spray can help enhance your curls and give them a beachy texture. Spray it on your hair and scrunch with your hands to create volume.

3. Refresh Your Curls:

If your curls start to lose their shape, you can refresh them by spraying your hair with water and scrunching with your hands.

FAQs About Styling Curly Hair at the Beach:

Q: Can I use a hair straightener or curling iron at the beach?

A: It's not recommended to use heat styling tools at the beach, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz. Stick to natural styling techniques, like scrunching and twisting.

Q: How often should I wash my hair at the beach?

A: It's best to wash your hair after every beach day to remove any saltwater and sand that can cause damage and dryness.

Q: What can I do if my hair gets too frizzy at the beach?

A: If your hair gets too frizzy, you can apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to tame the frizz and hydrate your hair.

Styling curly hair at the beach doesn't have to be difficult. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve beautiful beachy waves that will make you feel confident and gorgeous. Remember to take care of your hair before and after your beach day to keep it healthy and hydrated. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at styling your curly hair at the beach in no time!
