How To Style Thick Wavy Hair Guys A Complete Guide

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing or a curse, depending on how you style it. If you're a guy with thick wavy hair, you might have trouble figuring out how to style it properly. Fortunately, we're here to help. In this article, we'll show you how to style thick wavy hair guys in a way that looks great and is easy to maintain. Let's get started!

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Thick Wavy Hair Guys

The first step in styling thick wavy hair guys is to choose a haircut that suits your hair type. You want to avoid haircuts that are too short or too long, as they can make your hair appear unruly. Instead, opt for a haircut that is medium-length, with layers to help reduce the weight of your hair.

Once you have the right haircut, you can start styling your hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use the Right Products

Thick wavy hair guys should use products that are designed to enhance the natural texture of their hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil, shea butter, or coconut oil. These ingredients will help to moisturize your hair and reduce frizz.

2. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Thick wavy hair guys should avoid washing their hair too often, as this can strip the natural oils from their hair and make it more prone to frizz. Instead, aim to wash your hair every other day, or even less frequently if your hair tends to be dry.

3. Use a Diffuser

Using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can help to enhance the natural texture of your hair and reduce frizz. Simply attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and use it to dry your hair on a low or medium heat setting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling thick wavy hair guys:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for thick wavy hair. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any product buildup.

2. Apply Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, focusing on the ends and working your way up to the roots. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on a low or medium heat setting. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you dry it, to enhance the natural texture of your hair.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your style with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold your hair in place and reduce frizz.

Tips for Styling Thick Wavy Hair Guys

Here are some additional tips for styling thick wavy hair guys:

1. Don't Use Too Much Product

Using too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.

2. Embrace the Natural Texture of Your Hair

Thick wavy hair guys should embrace the natural texture of their hair, rather than trying to fight it. Use styling products that enhance your hair's natural texture, rather than trying to make it look straight.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. Aim to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

FAQs

1. Can I Straighten My Thick Wavy Hair?

Yes, you can straighten your thick wavy hair if you want to. However, be aware that using heat styling tools too often can damage your hair over time.

2. What Products Should I Use for Styling Thick Wavy Hair?

Thick wavy hair guys should use products that are designed to enhance the natural texture of their hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil, shea butter, or coconut oil.

3. How Often Should I Wash My Thick Wavy Hair?

Thick wavy hair guys should avoid washing their hair too often, as this can strip the natural oils from their hair and make it more prone to frizz. Aim to wash your hair every other day, or even less frequently if your hair tends to be dry.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair Guys

Here are some tips for taking care of your thick wavy hair:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help moisturize your hair and reduce frizz.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, rather than a brush. This will help to prevent breakage and reduce frizz.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools on your hair, to help prevent damage.

Now that you know how to style thick wavy hair guys, you can rock your natural texture with confidence. Follow these tips and you'll have great-looking hair that's easy to maintain.
