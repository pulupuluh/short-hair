How To Cut A Stacked Wedge Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you interested in learning how to cut a stacked wedge haircut? Look no further! This comprehensive guide will provide you with everything you need to know to achieve the perfect stacked wedge haircut.

Description

The stacked wedge haircut is a classic look that has been popular for decades. It is characterized by a short back and long front, creating a wedge-like shape. The haircut is perfect for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair while also keeping it stylish and modern.

How to

Before you start cutting, it is essential to have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off your hair. Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Section Off Your Hair

Start by sectioning off your hair into three parts: the top, middle, and bottom. Use hair clips to keep each section secure.

Step 2: Cut the Bottom Layer

Start by cutting the bottom layer of your hair. Use a comb to create a straight line, and then cut your hair in a horizontal motion. Make sure to cut your hair evenly and avoid cutting too much.

Step 3: Cut the Middle Layer

Next, move on to the middle layer of your hair. Use the same technique as before, but this time, cut your hair at an angle. This will create the stacked effect.

Step 4: Cut the Top Layer

Finally, move on to the top layer of your hair. Use the same technique as before, but this time, cut your hair at a steeper angle. This will create the wedge shape.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Once you have cut all three layers of your hair, use a comb to blend them together. This will create a seamless look and ensure that your hair looks natural.

Tips

Here are some tips to make sure you achieve the perfect stacked wedge haircut:

  • Start with small sections of hair, especially if you are new to cutting hair.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Comb your hair before cutting to avoid tangles.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or frustrated. Cutting hair takes time and patience.

FAQ

Can I Cut My Own Stacked Wedge Haircut?

While it is possible to cut your own stacked wedge haircut, it is recommended that you visit a professional stylist. They have the experience and skill necessary to give you the perfect haircut.

How Often Should I Get a Stacked Wedge Haircut?

It depends on the length and texture of your hair. Generally, it is recommended that you get a haircut every six to eight weeks to maintain your hairstyle.

Hair Care

To maintain your stacked wedge haircut, it is important to take care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.

Congratulations! You now know how to cut a stacked wedge haircut. With a bit of practice and patience, you will be able to achieve the perfect haircut every time.
