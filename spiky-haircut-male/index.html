Spiky Haircut Male How To Get And Maintain The Look

Written by Christine Apr 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both edgy and trendy, a spiky haircut for men might be just what you need. This hairstyle has been popular for years and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about spiky haircuts for men, from how to get the look to how to maintain it.

Description

A spiky haircut male is a hairstyle where the hair is cut short and styled into spikes using gel or wax. The spikes can be subtle or bold, depending on the individual's preference. This hairstyle is popular among men of all ages and works well with any hair type.

How to Get a Spiky Haircut Male

If you're interested in getting a spiky haircut male, the first step is to find a barber or stylist who is experienced in this type of hairstyle. Once you've found someone you trust, here's what the process typically looks like:

Step 1: Consultation

During the consultation, you'll discuss your hair type, face shape, and personal style with your barber or stylist. They will then recommend the best length and style for your hair.

Step 2: Haircut

Next, your hair will be cut to the desired length. Typically, spiky haircuts are shorter on the sides and longer on top. Your barber or stylist may use clippers or scissors to achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Styling

Once your hair is cut, your barber or stylist will style it into spikes using gel or wax. They may use a blow dryer to help set the spikes in place.

Tips for Maintaining a Spiky Haircut Male

Now that you have your spiky haircut male, it's important to know how to maintain it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a high-quality gel or wax to keep your spikes in place.
  • Reapply gel or wax throughout the day as needed.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the length and shape of your spiky haircut.
  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup of product.

FAQ

What hair types work best for a spiky haircut male?

Spiky haircuts work well with all hair types, but they look particularly good on thick, straight hair.

How long does a spiky haircut male last?

A spiky haircut male can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, depending on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the style.

Can I still get a spiky haircut male if I have thinning hair?

Yes, but you may need to use more product to achieve the desired look. Your barber or stylist can recommend products that will work well with your hair type.

Hair Care for a Spiky Haircut Male

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a spiky haircut male. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make the spikes fall flat.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right haircut, styling products, and care, you can rock a spiky haircut male with confidence. Whether you're going for a subtle or bold look, this hairstyle is a timeless classic that will never go out of style.
