How To Get Curly Hair For Thick Hair

Written by Frank Jun 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to switch things up with some gorgeous curls? If you have thick hair, you might think that getting curls is impossible. But fear not, because we have some tips and tricks to help you get those beautiful, bouncy curls you've always wanted. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to get curly hair for thick hair.

How to Get Curly Hair for Thick Hair

The first step to getting curly hair for thick hair is to choose the right products. You'll need a good shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain moisturizing ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil. These will help keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.

Next, you'll need to invest in a good curling iron or set of hot rollers. Choose a barrel size that is appropriate for your hair length and thickness. If you have very thick hair, you may need a larger barrel size to create bigger, more voluminous curls.

Finally, you'll need to learn some techniques for creating curls. This can take some practice, but with a little patience and perseverance, you'll be able to create beautiful, bouncy curls that last all day.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting curly hair for thick hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair. Rinse thoroughly.
  2. Blot your hair dry with a towel, but don't rub it vigorously. This can cause frizz.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair.
  4. Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on the thickness of your hair. Use clips to hold each section in place.
  5. Starting with one section, wrap a small section of hair around the curling iron barrel or hot roller. Hold for a few seconds, then release.
  6. Repeat with each section, working your way around your head.
  7. Once all sections are curled, run your fingers through your hair to break up the curls and create a more natural, tousled look.
  8. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Getting Curly Hair for Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best curls possible:

  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look limp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Try using different barrel sizes to create different types of curls.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different techniques, such as twisting or braiding your hair before curling.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting curly hair for thick hair:

Can I get curly hair without heat styling?

Yes, you can create curls without heat styling by using techniques like braiding or twisting your hair while damp and letting it dry overnight. You can also use foam rollers or flexi rods to create curls without heat.

How do I keep my curls from falling flat?

Use a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place. You can also try using a texturizing spray to add more volume and hold.

How often should I wash my hair?

This will depend on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair prefer to wash their hair less often to avoid drying it out. Try washing your hair every 2-3 days and see how it feels. If your hair feels dry or greasy, adjust your washing frequency accordingly.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or blow dryers too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Try using a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too often, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to get gorgeous, curly hair for thick hair in no time. Remember to choose the right products, invest in a good curling iron or hot rollers, and practice your techniques. With a little patience and perseverance, you'll have beautiful, bouncy curls that last all day.
