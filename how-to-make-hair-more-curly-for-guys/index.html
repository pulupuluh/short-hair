How To Make Hair More Curly For Guys Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jun 03, 2023 Â· 12 min read

Curly hair is often considered attractive and stylish, but not all men are blessed with natural curls. If you're a guy with straight or wavy hair, you might be wondering how to achieve those luscious curls. Fortunately, there are several ways to make your hair more curly, whether you want loose waves or tight ringlets. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for getting curly hair that will make heads turn.

Description

Many men with straight or wavy hair envy those with curly locks. Curly hair is often considered more attractive and stylish, but it can be challenging to achieve those curls, especially if you're not used to styling your hair. Fortunately, there are several ways to make your hair more curly, whether you want loose waves or tight ringlets. From using hair products to changing your hair care routine, we'll show you how to achieve the curly hair you've always wanted.

How to Make Hair More Curly for Guys

1. Use a Curl-Enhancing Product

If you want to make your hair more curly, the first step is to use a curl-enhancing product. These products are specially formulated to define and enhance your natural curls, making them more visible and bouncy. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil, as these can help moisturize and nourish your hair while enhancing your curls. You can choose from gels, creams, mousses, or sprays, depending on your hair type and styling preferences.

2. Try a Diffuser Attachment

If you're using a hair dryer to style your hair, consider using a diffuser attachment. A diffuser is a tool that attaches to your hair dryer and distributes the air flow evenly, reducing frizz and enhancing your curls. To use a diffuser, apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair, then use the diffuser to dry your hair on low heat. Scrunch your hair with your hands as you dry it to enhance your curls.

3. Use a Curling Wand or Iron

If you want to create more defined curls, you can use a curling wand or iron. These tools allow you to wrap sections of your hair around a heated barrel, creating curls that can last for hours. Choose a barrel size that matches the type of curl you want, whether it's loose waves or tight ringlets. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using a curling wand or iron to prevent heat damage.

4. Experiment with Hairstyles

If you're not ready to commit to a permanent change, you can experiment with different hairstyles that make your hair more curly. For example, you can try a perm, which is a chemical treatment that creates curls that last for several months. Alternatively, you can try braiding your hair when it's wet and leaving it overnight to create natural-looking waves. You can also try twisting your hair with your fingers and securing it with bobby pins to create curls without heat.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you want to make your hair more curly, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. These products will help define your natural curls and make them more visible. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel-dry it gently, leaving it slightly damp.

Step 2: Apply a curl-enhancing product

Apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair, making sure to distribute it evenly. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to comb the product through your hair, from root to tip.

Step 3: Use a diffuser attachment

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and set it to low heat. Use the diffuser to dry your hair, scrunching it with your hands as you go. Make sure to dry your hair completely, but don't over-dry it, as this can cause frizz.

Step 4: Use a curling wand or iron

If you want more defined curls, you can use a curling wand or iron. Choose a barrel size that matches the type of curl you want, then wrap sections of your hair around the barrel, holding it for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat until all of your hair is curled, then run your fingers through your hair to separate the curls.

Step 5: Finish with a hairspray

Finish with a light hairspray to hold your curls in place. Avoid using a heavy hairspray, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look stiff.

Tips for Making Hair More Curly for Guys

Here are some tips to help you make your hair more curly:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this will help prevent breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause heat damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends, which can make your hair look frizzy and dull.

FAQs

Can I make my hair more curly naturally?

Yes, you can make your hair more curly naturally by changing your hair care routine and using a curl-enhancing product. You can also try braiding your hair or twisting it with your fingers to create curls without heat.

Is it possible to make straight hair curly permanently?

Yes, you can make straight hair curly permanently by getting a perm, which is a chemical treatment that creates curls that last for several months. However, perms can be damaging to your hair if not done properly, so make sure to research the process and find a reputable stylist.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should wash your curly hair every 2-3 days, as frequent washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and frizzy. Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls defined and moisturized.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and defined. Here are some tips for hair care for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause heat damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends, which can make your hair look frizzy and dull.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your hair more curly and achieve the luscious locks you've always wanted. Whether you want loose waves or tight ringlets, there are several ways to enhance your natural curls and make them more visible. Remember to take care of your hair properly to keep it healthy and defined, and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
