How To Know If You Would Look Good With Bangs

Are you considering getting bangs but not sure if they would suit your face shape, hair texture, or personal style? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to know if you would look good with bangs and provide tips on how to care for them.

How to Know If You Would Look Good With Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to change up your look and frame your face, but they're not for everyone. Here are some factors to consider when deciding if bangs would suit you:

Face Shape

The shape of your face is a crucial factor in determining if bangs would suit you. Generally, bangs are most flattering on oval, heart, and square-shaped faces. If you have a round face, blunt or heavy bangs may make your face appear even rounder, so it's best to opt for side-swept or wispy bangs that create an illusion of length.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also affect how bangs will look on you. If you have thick, straight hair, you can pull off blunt or heavy bangs. However, if you have fine or curly hair, it's best to opt for wispy or feathered bangs that won't weigh down your hair.

Personal Style

Finally, your personal style should also be considered when deciding on bangs. If you prefer a low-maintenance look, bangs may not be for you since they require regular trims and styling. However, if you enjoy experimenting with your hair and trying new looks, bangs can be a fun way to switch things up.

Step by Step Guide to Determine If You Would Look Good With Bangs

Still not sure if bangs are for you? Follow these steps to get a better idea:

Step 1: Take a Selfie

Take a selfie of your face with your hair pulled back. This will give you a clear view of your face shape and forehead.

Step 2: Use a Virtual Hairstyle Tool

There are many virtual hairstyle tools available online that allow you to upload your photo and try on different hairstyles, including bangs. This can give you a good idea of how you would look with different types of bangs.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

Think about your daily routine and whether bangs would fit into it. If you have an active lifestyle or work in a job that requires a lot of movement, bangs may not be practical for you.

Step 4: Consult with Your Stylist

If you're still unsure, consult with your hairstylist. They can recommend the best type of bangs for your face shape and hair texture and give you a professional opinion on whether bangs would suit you.

Tips for Choosing and Styling Bangs

Once you've decided to go for bangs, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose the right type of bangs for your face shape and hair texture.
  • Don't cut your own bangs unless you're a professional stylist.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your bangs' shape and prevent them from becoming too long.
  • Style your bangs using a round brush and blow dryer to create volume and shape.
  • Avoid using too much product on your bangs, as this can weigh them down and make them look greasy.

FAQs About Bangs

Can I get bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still get bangs if you have curly hair. Just make sure to choose a wispy or feathered style that won't weigh down your curls. Consult with your stylist for recommendations.

How often should I trim my bangs?

You should trim your bangs every four to six weeks to maintain their shape.

Can I wear my bangs straight or do I have to style them?

You can wear your bangs straight or style them using a round brush and blow dryer for volume and shape.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Here are some tips to keep your bangs looking their best:

  • Wash your bangs every other day to prevent them from becoming oily.
  • Avoid touching or playing with your bangs, as this can make them greasy and messy.
  • Blow dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Avoid using too much product on your bangs, as this can weigh them down and make them look greasy.

With these tips, you'll be able to determine if bangs are right for you and keep them looking great.
