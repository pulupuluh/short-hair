How To See What Hair Color Suits You Tips Steps And Hair Care In 2023

Written by Frank Feb 28, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking to change up your hair color, you may be wondering what shade will suit you best. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right color for your skin tone, eye color, and personal style. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this post, we'll walk you through the steps to determine what hair color suits you best, as well as offer tips and hair care advice to ensure your new look stays healthy and vibrant.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine Your Ideal Hair Color
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

How to Determine Your Ideal Hair Color

The first step in finding the perfect hair color for you is to consider your skin tone. People with warm skin tones tend to look best in shades like honey blonde, copper, and chocolate brown, while those with cool skin tones may prefer ash blonde, platinum, or burgundy. If you're not sure what your skin tone is, take a look at the veins on the underside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you likely have cool undertones, while green veins indicate warm undertones.

Another factor to consider is your eye color. If you have blue or green eyes, you may want to opt for a warmer hair color to complement them, while those with brown or hazel eyes may prefer cooler shades like ash blonde or chestnut brown. Of course, personal style and preference should also be taken into account when choosing a hair color.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you determine what hair color suits you best:

  1. Identify your skin tone and undertones by looking at the veins on your wrist.
  2. Consider your eye color and how it will complement different hair colors.
  3. Look at pictures of people with similar skin tones and eye colors to see what shades look good on them.
  4. Try on wigs or use virtual hair color apps to see how different shades will look on you.
  5. Consult with a professional hairstylist to get their opinion and advice on what hair color would suit you best.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

When choosing a hair color, keep these tips in mind:

  • Consider your lifestyle and whether a high-maintenance color will work for you.
  • Don't stray too far from your natural color, as this can be difficult to maintain and may not suit you as well.
  • Choose a color that complements your skin tone and eye color.
  • Think about the season and how different colors may look in different lighting.
  • Consider the cost and time commitment of maintaining your new hair color.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair color:

  • What hair color looks best on fair skin? People with fair skin tend to look best in cool-toned shades like platinum blonde, ash brown, and burgundy.
  • What hair color looks best on dark skin? Dark skin tones look great with warm shades like caramel, honey blonde, and chestnut brown.
  • How do I know if I should go lighter or darker? Consider your skin tone and eye color, as well as your personal style and preference. If you're unsure, consult with a professional hairstylist.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

Once you've chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of it to ensure it stays healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the color and natural oils from your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as these can damage your hair and cause color fading.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips and steps, you'll be able to find the perfect hair color to suit your skin tone, eye color, and personal style. Don't be afraid to experiment and try something new – after all, hair color is a fun and easy way to change up your look!
