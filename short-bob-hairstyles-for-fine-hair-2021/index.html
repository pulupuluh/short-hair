Short Bob Hairstyles For Fine Hair 2021 A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 30, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of having fine hair that always seems to lack volume? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with fine hair struggle to find the right hairstyle that will help add volume and texture to their locks. One of the best solutions for this is a short bob. In this article, we'll be discussing short bob hairstyles for fine hair 2021 and how you can achieve the perfect look for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The short bob hairstyle has been a popular choice for many years now. It's versatile, easy to maintain, and can be customized to suit any face shape and hair type. For those with fine hair, a short bob is a great option as it can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker. Short bob hairstyles for fine hair 2021 are all about adding layers and texture to your hair, creating a tousled and effortless look.

How To

The first step in achieving the perfect short bob hairstyle for fine hair is to find the right haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for you. Once you have the right cut, you can start styling your hair to add volume and texture.

Blow Drying

Blow drying your hair is one of the best ways to add volume and texture to your short bob hairstyle. Start by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. After washing your hair, towel dry it gently and apply a root lifting spray. Then, blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to create volume. Finish with a texturizing spray to add texture and hold.

Curling

If you want to add more texture to your short bob hairstyle, you can also use a curling iron or wand. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair. Then, curl small sections of hair away from your face, holding the curling iron or wand vertically. Once you've curled all of your hair, run your fingers through it to loosen the curls and create a tousled look.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a short bob hairstyle for fine hair:

  1. Get the right haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  3. Towel dry your hair gently.
  4. Apply a root lifting spray to your hair.
  5. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to create volume.
  6. Curl small sections of hair away from your face using a curling iron or wand.
  7. Run your fingers through your hair to create a tousled look.
  8. Finish with a texturizing spray to add hold and texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your short bob hairstyle for fine hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Apply a root lifting spray to your hair before blow drying to add even more volume.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the roots.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to add texture to your hair.
  • Finish with a texturizing spray to add hold and texture.

FAQ

Can I get a short bob if I have fine hair?

Yes, a short bob is a great hairstyle for those with fine hair as it can add volume and texture to your locks.

What length should my short bob be?

The length of your short bob will depend on your face shape and hair type. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length for you.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to keep your short bob hairstyle looking great:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect short bob hairstyle for fine hair in 2021. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to find the best cut and style for you, and take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and voluminous.
