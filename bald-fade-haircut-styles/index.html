Bald Fade Haircut Styles The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern haircut, the bald fade is a great option. This haircut has been popular for years and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this guide, we'll take a look at different bald fade haircut styles and how to achieve them. We'll also provide tips and advice on how to take care of your hair after getting a bald fade.

Description

The bald fade haircut is a popular men's hairstyle that involves cutting the hair very short on the sides and back of the head while leaving the hair longer on top. The hair gradually fades from a longer length to a shorter length until it blends seamlessly with the skin. There are different types of bald fades, including high, mid, and low fade. Each type of fade has a different level of gradient, with the high fade being the most dramatic and the low fade being the most subtle.

How to Get a Bald Fade Haircut

If you want to get a bald fade haircut, the first step is to find a good barber. Look for a barber who has experience cutting men's hair and who understands the different types of fades. Once you've found a barber, schedule an appointment and bring a picture of the hairstyle you want. This will help your barber understand what you're looking for and ensure that you get the haircut you want.

Step-by-Step Guide to Bald Fade Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a bald fade haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Next, your barber will use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head very short.
  3. The clippers will be gradually moved up until the hair blends seamlessly with the skin.
  4. The hair on the top of your head will be cut to your desired length and style.
  5. Your barber will then use scissors to blend the top and sides of your hair together.
  6. Finally, your barber will use a straight razor to clean up the hairline and add any finishing touches.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bald Fade Haircut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your bald fade haircut:

  • Visit your barber every 2-4 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Consider using a hair trimmer to touch up your haircut between appointments.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about bald fade haircut styles:

Q: What is the difference between a high fade and a low fade?

A: A high fade is a dramatic fade that starts very high on the head, while a low fade is a more subtle fade that starts lower on the head.

Q: Can I get a bald fade if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can get a bald fade with curly hair. However, you may need to find a barber who is experienced with cutting curly hair to ensure that the haircut looks good.

Q: How do I style my hair after getting a bald fade?

A: The great thing about a bald fade is that it's very versatile and can be styled in many different ways. You can use a comb or your fingers to style your hair, depending on the look you want to achieve.

Hair Care Tips for Bald Fade Haircut

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your bald fade haircut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, as these can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your bald fade haircut with confidence!
