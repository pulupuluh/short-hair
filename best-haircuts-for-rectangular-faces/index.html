Best Haircuts For Rectangular Faces A Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Mar 03, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a rectangular face, you may be wondering what are the best haircuts to flatter your features. Rectangular faces are characterized by a long and narrow shape, with a strong jawline and forehead. In this guide, we will give you tips on the best haircuts for rectangular faces, including a step-by-step guide, FAQ, and hair care tips to keep your locks looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Haircuts for Rectangular Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide for Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Rectangular Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best haircut for your rectangular face is to determine your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. You can then use these measurements to determine your face shape.

Rectangular faces are typically longer than they are wide, with a forehead, cheekbones, and jawline that are roughly the same width. The jawline is typically strong and defined, and the forehead is often high and prominent.

Best Haircuts for Rectangular Faces

When it comes to choosing a haircut for a rectangular face, the goal is to soften the strong angles and create a more balanced look. Here are some of the best haircuts for rectangular faces:

Long Layers

Long layers can add movement and texture to long hair, helping to soften the strong angles of a rectangular face. The layers should start at or above the cheekbones and frame the face to create a more balanced look.

Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a classic style that can work well on rectangular faces. The key is to choose a length that hits at or above the chin, with soft layers that frame the face. This will help to create a more balanced look by drawing attention away from the strong jawline.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to soften the forehead and create a more balanced look for rectangular faces. The key is to choose a length that hits just above the eyebrows, with a soft, wispy texture that blends seamlessly into the rest of the hair.

Layered Pixie Cut

A layered pixie cut can be a great option for rectangular faces, especially for those with shorter hair. The layers should be soft and textured, with longer pieces around the face to help soften the strong angles.

Step-by-Step Guide for Styling

Once you have chosen a haircut that flatters your rectangular face, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here is a step-by-step guide for styling your hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, working from the roots to the ends.
  4. If you have long hair, create loose waves using a curling iron or a straightener.
  5. If you have short hair, use a texturizing or molding paste to create definition and texture.
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you have found the perfect haircut for your rectangular face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your locks looking their best:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQ

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair and a rectangular face, you can still rock a great haircut. Consider a shoulder-length cut with layers to add movement and texture, or a curly bob with side-swept bangs to soften the forehead.

What if I have a receding hairline?

If you have a receding hairline and a rectangular face, consider a short, textured cut with a side-swept fringe. This will help to draw attention away from the hairline and create a more balanced look.

Hair Care Tips for Rectangular Faces

In addition to choosing the right haircut and styling your hair properly, it's important to take care of your locks to keep them healthy and looking their best. Here are some hair care tips for rectangular faces:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping the natural oils from your scalp.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid damaging your hair when brushing it.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for your rectangular face, you can keep your locks looking their best and feel confident in your appearance.
