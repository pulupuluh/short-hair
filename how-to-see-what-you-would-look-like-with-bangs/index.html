How To See What You Would Look Like With Bangs

Written by Petter Aug 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you thinking about getting bangs, but not sure how they would look on you? It's a common concern, but luckily there are several ways to see what you would look like with bangs before making the cut. In this article, we'll go over the steps and tips to help you visualize whether bangs are right for you.

How to

There are a few different ways to see what you would look like with bangs:

1. Use a Virtual Makeover Tool

One of the easiest ways to see what you would look like with bangs is by using a virtual makeover tool. These tools allow you to upload a photo of yourself and then see what you would look like with different hairstyles, including bangs. Some popular virtual makeover tools include:

  • Virtual Hairstyler
  • TAAZ
  • ModiFace

Simply upload a photo of yourself, select the bangs option, and try out different styles until you find one that you like.

2. Try on a Wig

If you're not ready to commit to bangs just yet, try on a wig to see what you would look like with them. You can find wigs in a variety of styles and colors, including bangs. Wear the wig around the house and see how you feel with bangs.

3. Use Photoshop

If you have some Photoshop skills, you can use the program to see what you would look like with bangs. Simply take a photo of yourself, upload it to Photoshop, and add in some bangs. This method requires a bit more skill, but it can be a fun way to experiment with different styles.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions for using a virtual makeover tool:

  1. Choose a virtual makeover tool
  2. Upload a photo of yourself
  3. Select the bangs option
  4. Try out different styles
  5. Save your favorite looks

When trying on a wig, here are the steps:

  1. Choose a wig with bangs
  2. Put the wig on
  3. Style the wig as desired
  4. Wear the wig around the house
  5. Decide if you like the look

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when trying to see what you would look like with bangs:

  • Consider your face shape - not all bangs are created equal, so make sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape.
  • Think about maintenance - bangs require regular trims to keep them looking neat, so make sure you're willing to commit to the upkeep.
  • Start small - if you're not sure about bangs, start with a smaller fringe and see how you like it before going for a full set of bangs.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about seeing what you would look like with bangs:

Can I see what I would look like with bangs at a salon?

Many salons offer virtual makeover tools that allow you to see what you would look like with different hairstyles, including bangs. Ask your stylist if they offer this service.

How can I tell if bangs will look good on me?

Consider your face shape and features when choosing a bangs style. Some styles may look better on certain face shapes, so do your research before committing to a cut.

What if I don't like how I look with bangs?

If you try out bangs and decide they're not for you, don't worry - hair grows back! You can either wait for your bangs to grow out or visit a stylist to have them trimmed or styled differently.

Hair Care

If you do decide to get bangs, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are some tips:

  • Trim them regularly - bangs grow quickly, so make sure to schedule regular trims to keep them looking neat.
  • Avoid using heat tools - using a flat iron or curling iron on your bangs can damage them, so try to let them air dry and style them with your fingers.
  • Wash them frequently - bangs can get greasy quickly, so make sure to wash them often to keep them looking fresh.

Now that you know how to see what you would look like with bangs, you can experiment with different styles and find the perfect look for you. Remember to consider your face shape, maintenance, and personal preferences when choosing a bangs style, and don't be afraid to try something new!
