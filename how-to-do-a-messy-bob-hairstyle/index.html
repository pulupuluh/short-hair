How To Do A Messy Bob Hairstyle Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Apr 20, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you looking for a chic and effortless hairstyle that you can rock on any occasion? Look no further than the messy bob! This versatile haircut is a great way to add texture and movement to your hair while keeping it low maintenance. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect messy bob hairstyle.

What is a Messy Bob?

A messy bob is a shoulder-length haircut that's characterized by its textured, tousled look. It's a great option for anyone who wants a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks polished and put-together. The key to a messy bob is to keep it slightly uneven, with choppy layers and loose waves or curls.

How to Achieve the Messy Bob Look

To achieve the perfect messy bob, you'll need to start with the right haircut. Ask your stylist for a shoulder-length cut with choppy layers and textured ends. You can also add some face-framing layers if you like.

Once you have the right cut, it's all about styling. To create the messy, textured look, you'll need to use the right products and techniques. Here are some tips to get you started:

1. Use a Texturizing Spray

A texturizing spray is a must-have for achieving the messy bob look. This type of product adds volume and texture to your hair, making it look tousled and effortless. Spray it on damp hair and scrunch with your hands to create waves and curls.

2. Embrace Your Natural Texture

If you have naturally wavy or curly hair, embrace it! The messy bob is all about embracing your hair's natural texture and enhancing it with the right products and techniques. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls and create volume.

3. Use a Curling Wand

If you have straight hair, you can still achieve the messy bob look with a curling wand. Wrap small sections of hair around the wand and hold for a few seconds before releasing. This will create loose waves and curls that look natural and effortless.

Step-by-Step Guide to Doing a Messy Bob

Now that you know the basics of achieving the messy bob look, let's dive into the step-by-step process of actually doing the hairstyle:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair as usual. You can also use a dry shampoo to add some volume and texture to your roots.

Step 2: Apply Texturizing Spray

Spray a texturizing spray all over your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. Use your hands to scrunch your hair and create waves and curls.

Step 3: Create Loose Waves

Use a curling wand to create loose waves throughout your hair. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the wand, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Be sure to alternate the direction of the curl for a more natural look.

Step 4: Add Some Hairspray

Finish off your look with a light hold hairspray. This will help to set your waves and keep them in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining a Messy Bob

Now that you've achieved the perfect messy bob, here are some tips for maintaining the look:

1. Use a Silk Pillowcase

A silk pillowcase will help to prevent frizz and keep your hair looking smooth and shiny. It also helps to prevent breakage and split ends.

2. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep it looking fresh.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. This will help to nourish your hair and prevent damage from heat styling and other factors.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Can I achieve the messy bob look with short hair?

Yes! The messy bob works well on all hair lengths, including short hair. Just make sure to keep it slightly uneven and textured for the perfect look.

2. Can I achieve the messy bob look without using heat styling tools?

Absolutely! You can achieve the messy bob look without using heat styling tools by using a texturizing spray and scrunching your hair with your hands. You can also braid your hair overnight to create loose waves and curls.

3. How do I prevent my messy bob from looking too frizzy?

To prevent your messy bob from looking too frizzy, use a smoothing serum or oil on the ends of your hair. This will help to tame any flyaways and keep your hair looking smooth and polished.

Hair Care for Messy Bob Hairstyles

To keep your messy bob looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and shiny:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

If you're using heat styling tools on your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant spray or cream. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your messy bob looking fresh and polished, it's important to trim your hair regularly. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your ends looking neat and tidy.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can be damaging to your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. To protect your hair from the sun, wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect messy bob hairstyle in no time. Whether you're headed to work, a night out with friends, or just running errands, this chic and effortless hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
