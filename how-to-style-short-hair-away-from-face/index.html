How To Style Short Hair Away From Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Aug 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short hair can be a fun and easy hairstyle to maintain, but sometimes you may want to switch things up and pull your hair away from your face. Whether you are going for a casual or formal look, we have got you covered with these tips and tricks on how to style short hair away from your face.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Style Short Hair Away from Face
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs

How to Style Short Hair Away from Face

There are several ways to style short hair away from your face. Some popular styles include:

  • Ponytail
  • Braids
  • Half-up, Half-down
  • Top Knot
  • Side Part
  • Slicked Back

The key to pulling off these hairstyles is to use the right products and tools. You will need a good hairspray, bobby pins, hair ties, and a comb or brush.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style short hair away from your face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hairspray to your hair to help hold the style in place.
  3. Pull your hair back into a ponytail, braid, or half-up, half-down style. Secure with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  4. If you are creating a top knot, gather your hair at the crown of your head and twist it into a bun. Secure with bobby pins or a hair tie.
  5. For a side part, use a comb or brush to create a part on one side of your head. Brush your hair away from your face and secure with bobby pins.
  6. To achieve a slicked-back look, apply a generous amount of gel or pomade to your hair and comb it back away from your face. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your short hair away from your face:

  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place without weighing it down.
  • Invest in good quality hair ties and bobby pins to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to accessorize with headbands, clips, or scarves.
  • If you have bangs, try pinning them back with bobby pins or incorporating them into your hairstyle.

FAQs

Q: Can I style my short hair away from my face without using any products?

A: While products can help hold your hair in place, you can still style your short hair away from your face without using any products. Try using bobby pins and hair ties to secure your hair in place.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from looking greasy when using products?

A: Use a light hold hairspray or a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil and prevent your hair from looking greasy.

Q: Can I wear a hat with my hair styled away from my face?

A: Yes, you can wear a hat with your hair styled away from your face. Just make sure to adjust the hat so that it sits comfortably on your head without disturbing your hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and stylish:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of buildup.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style your short hair away from your face and achieve a variety of different looks. Whether you are going for a casual or formal style, these hairstyles are sure to turn heads.
