How To Style My Hair Medium Length Tips And Tricks

Written by William Mar 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have medium length hair, you may be wondering how to style it in a way that is both easy and chic. Fear not, we have compiled a list of tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect medium length hairstyle.

Description

Medium length hair is hair that falls between your shoulders and your collarbone. This hair length is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. It can be curled, straightened, and braided, among other styles.

How to Style My Hair Medium Length

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

Before you begin styling your medium length hair, it is important to determine your hair type. This will help you determine the best products to use and the best styling techniques to employ. If you have curly hair, for example, you may want to use a curl defining cream to enhance your natural curls.

Step 2: Choose Your Styling Tools

Once you have determined your hair type, it is time to choose your styling tools. If you want to straighten your hair, you will need a flat iron. If you want to curl your hair, you will need a curling iron. If you want to create waves, you can use a flat iron or a curling iron.

Step 3: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before styling your hair, it is important to wash and condition it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type. If you have dry hair, for example, you may want to use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner.

Step 4: Apply Styling Products

Apply styling products to your damp hair. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage and also help to hold your style in place. If you are straightening your hair, you may want to use a heat protectant spray. If you are curling your hair, you may want to use a curl enhancing cream.

Step 5: Begin Styling

Once you have applied your styling products, you can begin styling your hair. If you are straightening your hair, divide your hair into sections and run the flat iron over each section. If you are curling your hair, divide your hair into sections and wrap each section around the curling iron. If you are creating waves, use a flat iron to create a slight bend in your hair.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

After you have styled your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold your style in place and also add shine to your hair.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Don't use too much heat on your hair. This can cause damage and breakage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

What are some easy hairstyles for medium length hair?

Some easy hairstyles for medium length hair include a low ponytail, a messy bun, and a half-up half-down style.

How often should I wash my medium length hair?

It is recommended that you wash your hair every 2-3 days. This will help to prevent your hair from becoming too dry.

Hair Care

In addition to styling your hair, it is important to take care of your hair. This includes using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect medium length hairstyle. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
