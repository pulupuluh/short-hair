How To Cut Short Layered Bob Hairstyles Step By Step Guide

Are you thinking of getting a short layered bob haircut? This hairstyle is a classic and versatile look that can suit any face shape and hair type. But before you head to the salon, it's important to know how to cut short layered bob hairstyles properly. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect short layered bob haircut.

How to Cut Short Layered Bob Hairstyles

The key to cutting a short layered bob haircut is to create layers that add volume and texture to the hair. This will give the bob haircut a more modern and edgy look. Here are the basic steps to follow when cutting a short layered bob:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help you see the natural shape and texture of your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Separate Hair into Sections

Separate your hair into sections by parting it down the middle and then from ear to ear. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Cut the Back Section

Start with the back section of your hair. Use a comb to lift a small section of hair and cut it straight across. Repeat this process for the entire back section of your hair.

Step 4: Cut the Side Sections

Move on to the side sections of your hair. Use the back section as a guide and cut each side section at an angle. This will create layers that frame your face.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Use scissors to blend the layers together by cutting into the hair at an angle. This will create a seamless transition between the layers.

Step 6: Check for Balance

Check your hair for balance by looking in the mirror and making sure both sides are even. Make any necessary adjustments.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair as desired. You can use a curling iron or straightener to add texture and volume to your short layered bob haircut.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to cut short layered bob hairstyles:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

You will need a few tools to cut a short layered bob haircut, including:

  • Hair scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to part your hair down the middle and then from ear to ear. This will create four sections of hair. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 4: Cut the Back Section

Start with the back section of your hair. Spray a small section of hair with water and use a comb to lift it up. Cut the hair straight across using your scissors as a guide. Repeat this process for the entire back section of your hair.

Step 5: Cut the Side Sections

Move on to the side sections of your hair. Use the back section as a guide and cut each side section at an angle. This will create layers that frame your face.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Use scissors to blend the layers together by cutting into the hair at an angle. This will create a seamless transition between the layers.

Step 7: Check for Balance

Check your hair for balance by looking in the mirror and making sure both sides are even. Make any necessary adjustments.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Finally, style your hair as desired. You can use a curling iron or straightener to add texture and volume to your short layered bob haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Short Layered Bob Hairstyles

Here are some tips for maintaining your short layered bob haircut:

  • Get regular trims to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Try different styling techniques, such as blow-drying your hair upside down, to add volume to your roots.

FAQs

1. Is a short layered bob haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a short layered bob haircut can suit any hair type, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

2. How often should I get a trim for a short layered bob haircut?

You should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your short layered bob haircut.

3. Can I style my short layered bob haircut in different ways?

Yes, you can style your short layered bob haircut in many different ways, such as straight, curly, or wavy. You can also try different partings and accessories to change up your look.

Hair Care Tips for Short Layered Bob Hairstyles

Here are some hair care tips for short layered bob hairstyles:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent your hair from becoming too oily or dry.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.

Now that you know how to cut short layered bob hairstyles, you can confidently head to the salon and get the perfect haircut for your face shape and hair type. Just remember to maintain your hairstyle with regular trims and proper hair care.
