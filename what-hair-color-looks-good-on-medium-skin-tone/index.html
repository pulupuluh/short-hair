What Hair Color Looks Good On Medium Skin Tone Tips Faqs And Hair Care

Written by Daniel Jun 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing the right hair color can make all the difference to your overall look. If you have medium skin tone, you may be wondering which hair color would suit you best. In this article, we will be discussing the best hair colors for medium skin tones and how to care for your hair post-coloring.

Table of Contents

  1. What is Medium Skin Tone?
  2. Best Hair Colors for Medium Skin Tone
  3. How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  4. How to Choose the Right Hair Color
  5. Hair Care Tips Post-Coloring
  6. FAQs

What is Medium Skin Tone?

Medium skin tones are those that are not too light or too dark. They typically have a warm undertone and range from olive to caramel in color. People with medium skin tones can have varying shades of brown, black, and even blonde hair.

Best Hair Colors for Medium Skin Tone

The best hair colors for medium skin tones are warm shades that complement the natural undertones. Some of the best hair colors for medium skin tones are:

  • Warm browns
  • Caramel highlights
  • Honey blonde
  • Copper red
  • Auburn
  • Chocolate brown

These shades work well because they complement the natural undertones of medium skin tones. They also add warmth and dimension to the hair, making it look healthy and vibrant.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The easiest way to determine your skin tone is to look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If your veins appear green, you have warm undertones, and if they are blue, you have cool undertones. If you can't quite tell, you may have neutral undertones, which means you can pull off both cool and warm shades.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

When choosing the right hair color for your medium skin tone, consider your undertones and the natural color of your hair. If you have warm undertones, opt for warm shades like honey blonde or copper red. If you have cool undertones, try ash brown or platinum blonde. If you have neutral undertones, you can experiment with both cool and warm shades.

It's also important to consider your lifestyle and maintenance preferences when choosing a hair color. If you don't have the time or resources to visit the salon every few weeks, opt for a low-maintenance shade that will grow out seamlessly.

Hair Care Tips Post-Coloring

After coloring your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy. Here are some hair care tips post-coloring:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the color.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently as it can cause the color to fade faster.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you must use heat styling tools.

FAQs

1. Can I dye my hair if I have a medium skin tone?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have a medium skin tone. Just make sure to choose a shade that complements your natural undertones and fits your lifestyle and maintenance preferences.

2. Should I dye my hair at home or at a salon?

If you're new to coloring your hair, it's best to go to a salon to avoid any mishaps. If you're experienced with coloring your hair, you can dye your hair at home using a high-quality hair dye kit.

3. How often should I color my hair?

This depends on your hair growth and maintenance preferences. If you want to maintain the color, touch-ups every 4-6 weeks are recommended. However, if you prefer a low-maintenance look, you can space out your touch-ups to every 8-12 weeks.



Choosing the right hair color for your medium skin tone can be challenging, but with these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect shade for you. Remember to take care of your hair post-coloring to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy.
