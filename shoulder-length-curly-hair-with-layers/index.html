Shoulder Length Curly Hair With Layers A Guide To Effortless Style

Written by Robby Apr 25, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have shoulder length curly hair with layers, you’ve got yourself a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle that can take you from day to night with ease. But to keep your curls looking their best, you need to know how to style them properly. In this guide, we’ll show you how to achieve effortless style with your shoulder length curly hair with layers.

Description

Shoulder length curly hair with layers is a trendy and chic hairstyle that suits any face shape. The layers help to add volume and movement to your curls, making them look more defined and bouncy. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair at a manageable length while still enjoying the benefits of having curly hair.

How to Style

To style your shoulder length curly hair with layers, you’ll need a few styling products and tools. Here’s how to get started:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a leave-in conditioner and a curl-defining cream to your damp hair. This will help to define your curls and keep them looking bouncy and shiny.

Step 3: Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your style with a light hold hairspray. This will help to keep your curls in place without making them look stiff or crunchy.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you maintain your shoulder length curly hair with layers:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls while they’re wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make your curls look dull.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your layers looking fresh.

FAQ

How often should I wash my shoulder length curly hair with layers?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from getting too dry or oily.

Can I straighten my shoulder length curly hair with layers?

Yes, you can straighten your hair occasionally, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and a low heat setting to prevent damage.

How can I make my curls last longer?

Try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help preserve your curls overnight.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length curly hair with layers looking its best, you need to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve effortless style with your shoulder length curly hair with layers. So embrace your natural curls and show them off with confidence!
