How To Style Wavy Hair With Curtain Bangs

Written by Oliver Jul 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is both trendy and easy to maintain? Look no further than wavy hair with curtain bangs. This style combines loose, natural waves with long, parted bangs that frame the face. Not sure how to achieve this look? Keep reading for a step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

How to Style Wavy Hair with Curtain Bangs

To achieve wavy hair with curtain bangs, you will need:

  • A curling iron or wand
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray
  • A comb or brush

Follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for wavy hair. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to create a center part in your hair. This will create the curtain effect of your bangs.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling iron or wand, curl sections of your hair away from your face. Be sure to leave the ends of your hair uncurled for a natural look.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Using a comb or brush, style your bangs to frame your face. You can choose to leave them loose and natural or pin them back for a different look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Spray your hair with a light or medium hold hairspray to keep your waves in place without weighing them down.

Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair with Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to keep your wavy hair with curtain bangs looking great:

  • Use products designed for wavy hair to enhance and define your natural waves.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Limit how often you wash your hair to prevent stripping it of natural oils.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your bangs.

FAQs

Can I achieve this look without a curling iron?

Yes, you can achieve natural waves by braiding your hair while it is damp and allowing it to dry. Once the braids are dry, gently unravel them and style your hair as desired.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with curtain bangs?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with curtain bangs. Simply pull back the rest of your hair and leave your bangs loose to frame your face.

Hair Care Advice for Wavy Hair with Curtain Bangs

Here are some additional tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful wavy hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner before swimming.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair to avoid pulling or breaking it.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a stylish and low-maintenance look with wavy hair and curtain bangs. Experiment with different products and styling techniques to find what works best for your hair type and texture. Happy styling!
