How To Deal With Short Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Mar 09, 2023 · 7 min read

Short, thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's easy to style and maintain. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and can look unflattering if not styled properly. If you're struggling with your short, thick locks, don't worry – we're here to help. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to deal with short thick hair, from styling tips to hair care routines.

Description

Short, thick hair can be a challenge to deal with, but with the right tools and techniques, it can also be a source of pride and confidence. Whether you're looking to add volume, tame your curls, or simply keep your hair healthy and shiny, we've got you covered.

How to Deal with Short Thick Hair

Step 1: Choose the Right Cut

When it comes to short thick hair, the right cut can make all the difference. Look for a style that suits your face shape and hair texture, and that is easy to maintain. A layered bob or a pixie cut are both great options for short, thick hair, as they add volume and texture while keeping your hair looking neat and polished.

Step 2: Invest in the Right Products

Short, thick hair requires the right products to keep it looking and feeling healthy. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are designed specifically for your hair type, and that contain ingredients like keratin and biotin to strengthen and nourish your locks. A good leave-in conditioner can also help to keep your hair soft and manageable.

Step 3: Use the Right Styling Tools

To style your short thick hair, you'll need the right tools. A good quality hair dryer, a round brush, and a straightening iron or curling wand are all essential tools for creating a variety of styles. Look for tools that are designed specifically for short hair, as they will be easier to use and will give you better results.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for dealing with short thick hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add extra body and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage your locks and make them look dry and brittle.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my short thick hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your short thick hair. However, it's important to choose a hair dye that is gentle and won't damage your hair. Look for a dye that is specifically designed for short hair, and that contains ingredients like keratin and biotin to nourish and protect your locks.

Q: How often should I wash my short thick hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, it's best to wash your hair every other day or every three days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. However, if your hair tends to get oily quickly, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your short thick hair to keep it looking and feeling healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and avoid over-washing your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and keep it soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.

Dealing with short thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right tools, products, and techniques, you can create a variety of stylish looks that are easy to maintain. Whether you're looking for a sleek bob, a messy pixie cut, or anything in between, we hope this guide has given you the inspiration and confidence you need to rock your short thick locks with pride.
