How To Make Wet Straight Hair Wavy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair all the time? Do you want to switch up your look and try something new? One great option is to make your wet straight hair wavy. Not only does this add some texture and volume to your hair, but it also gives you a fun and flirty style that can be perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve wavy hair from straight hair, so you can get the look you want.

Description

Wavy hair is a great way to add some texture and volume to your hair, and it's a fun and flirty style that can be perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll show you how to make your wet straight hair wavy, so you can get the look you want.

How To Make Wet Straight Hair Wavy

Here are the steps you need to follow to make wet straight hair wavy:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step is to wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed to add volume and texture. This will help to give your hair some extra body, which is important when trying to create waves.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, use a towel to dry it off. You don't want your hair to be completely dry, but you also don't want it to be dripping wet. Aim for it to be about 60-70% dry.

Step 3: Apply a Wave-Enhancing Product

Next, apply a wave-enhancing product to your hair. This could be a mousse, a spray, or a gel. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Divide Your Hair into Sections

To make sure that you're curling all of your hair and not missing any sections, divide your hair into several sections using clips or hair ties. This will make the process much easier.

Step 5: Use a Curling Wand

Now it's time to start curling your hair. Use a curling wand to create waves in each section of your hair. Start at the top and work your way down, making sure to hold the curling wand in place for a few seconds to create a defined wave.

Step 6: Repeat on All Sections of Your Hair

Once you've curled all of the sections of your hair, use your fingers to gently comb through the waves and create a more natural look. You can also use a sea salt spray to add some texture to your hair.

Step 7: Let Your Hair Dry

Now it's time to let your hair dry naturally. Don't use a hair dryer, as this can cause your hair to frizz and lose its shape. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

Step 8: Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is completely dry, finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep the waves in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your wet straight hair wavy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage
  • Experiment with different wave-enhancing products to find the one that works best for your hair
  • Don't be afraid to play around with the size of the curling wand to create different sized waves
  • Use bobby pins to hold any sections of hair that aren't cooperating

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making wet straight hair wavy:

Can I make my hair wavy without a curling wand?

Yes, you can use a hair straightener to create waves in your hair. Simply twist the straightener as you run it through your hair to create a wave.

How long will my wavy hair last?

Your wavy hair should last for several days, especially if you use a hairspray to help hold the waves in place.

Can I make my hair wavy if it's already dry?

Yes, you can still make your hair wavy if it's already dry. Simply dampen your hair with some water or a wave-enhancing spray before you start curling.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you're using heat tools to create waves. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair every day
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers

With these tips and tricks, you can easily make your wet straight hair wavy and achieve a fun and flirty look. Whether you're going out for the night or just want to switch up your style, wavy hair is a great option.
