Best Haircuts For Round Face And Curly Hair In 2023

Written by Daniel Sep 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face and curly hair, finding the perfect hairstyle can be challenging. You want a cut that flatters your features and enhances your natural curls. In this article, we will discuss some of the best haircuts for round face and curly hair in 2023, including how to style and care for your locks.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide hairline. Curly hair, on the other hand, is voluminous and tends to have a lot of texture. When combined, these two features can create a beautiful, soft look that is both feminine and flattering. However, it can be challenging to find the right haircut that complements both features.

How to

When choosing a haircut for round face and curly hair, there are a few things to consider. First, you want a cut that elongates your face and creates the illusion of a more defined jawline. Second, you want a style that enhances your curls without adding too much volume to your cheeks. Finally, you want a cut that is easy to maintain and style.

Consider Your Face Shape

Before choosing a haircut, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you determine which styles will work best with your features. For a round face, you want to create the illusion of length. This can be achieved with a long, layered cut or a side-swept fringe.

Enhance Your Curls

Curly hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right cut and styling products, you can enhance your natural curls and create a beautiful, textured look. Look for a cut that adds layers and movement to your hair. This will help your curls fall in a natural, flattering way.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving the perfect haircut for round face and curly hair:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a measuring tape to determine the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three are roughly the same width, you have a round face.

Step 2: Choose Your Cut

Once you've determined your face shape, choose a cut that complements your features. A long, layered cut with side-swept bangs can create the illusion of length and a more defined jawline.

Step 3: Enhance Your Curls

To enhance your curls, look for a cut that adds layers and movement to your hair. This will help your curls fall in a natural, flattering way. Use a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel, to define your curls and add shine.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect haircut for round face and curly hair:

  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that add volume to your cheeks.
  • Opt for a side-swept fringe that creates a diagonal line across your face.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Invest in a quality hairbrush to detangle your curls without creating frizz.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face with curly hair?

The best haircut for a round face with curly hair is a long, layered cut with side-swept bangs. This style elongates the face and creates the illusion of a more defined jawline.

How do you style curly hair with a round face?

To style curly hair with a round face, use a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel, to define your curls and add shine. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.

Hair Care

To keep your curls looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Apply a leave-in conditioner to hydrate your curls and prevent frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing your curls when they are dry.

In conclusion, finding the perfect haircut for round face and curly hair can be a challenge, but by considering your face shape and enhancing your natural curls, you can achieve a beautiful, flattering look. Use the tips and techniques outlined in this article to create a style that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty.
