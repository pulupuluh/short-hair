How To Stop Genetic Hair Fall Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Aug 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are one of those who suffer from hair fall due to genetics, it can be a frustrating and depressing experience. You may have tried various hair care products and remedies, but nothing seems to work. However, there are ways to stop genetic hair fall. In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks to help you stop genetic hair fall.

Table of Content

  • How to Stop Genetic Hair Fall
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Prevent Genetic Hair Fall

How to Stop Genetic Hair Fall

Genetic hair fall can be difficult to prevent, but there are some things you can do to slow down or even stop it. Here are some tips:

1. Use Hair Growth Products

One of the best ways to stop genetic hair fall is to use hair growth products. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and vitamins like Vitamin A, C, and E. These ingredients nourish the scalp and hair follicles, promoting healthy hair growth.

2. Take Supplements

Taking supplements can also help combat genetic hair fall. Supplements like biotin, collagen, and omega-3 fatty acids can help strengthen the hair and prevent breakage. Consult with your doctor before taking any supplements.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles like braids, buns, and ponytails can put pressure on the hair follicles, leading to hair fall. Avoid tight hairstyles and opt for loose hairstyles instead.

4. Don't Overuse Hair Styling Products

Hair styling products like gels, mousses, and hairsprays can damage the hair and cause hair fall. Avoid overusing these products and choose natural hair styling alternatives instead.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you stop genetic hair fall:

Step 1: Identify the Cause of Your Hair Fall

Before you start treating your hair fall, it's important to identify the cause. If it's genetic, you may need to take a different approach than if it's due to other factors like stress or hormonal changes.

Step 2: Consult with a Dermatologist

Consult with a dermatologist to determine the best treatment plan for your hair fall. They may prescribe medications or recommend hair growth products.

Step 3: Use Hair Growth Products

Start using hair growth products that contain nourishing ingredients like biotin and keratin. These products can help stimulate hair growth and prevent hair fall.

Step 4: Take Supplements

Consult with your doctor and start taking supplements like biotin and collagen to help strengthen your hair.

Step 5: Avoid Tight Hairstyles and Overuse of Styling Products

Avoid tight hairstyles and overuse of hair styling products, as these can damage your hair and cause hair fall.

Tips to Prevent Genetic Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips to help prevent genetic hair fall:

  • Get plenty of sleep
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  • Reduce stress through meditation, yoga, or other relaxation techniques
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons

FAQs

Q. Is genetic hair fall reversible?

A. Genetic hair fall is not reversible, but it can be slowed down or even stopped with the right treatment plan.

Q. Can stress cause genetic hair fall?

A. Stress can exacerbate genetic hair fall, but it is not the primary cause.

Q. How long does it take to see results from hair growth products?

A. It can take several weeks to several months to see results from hair growth products, so be patient and consistent with your treatment plan.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to help keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to help nourish your hair
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it is more prone to breakage
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair

Remember, genetic hair fall can be frustrating, but there are ways to stop it. Follow the tips and tricks outlined in this article and be patient with your hair care routine. With time and consistency, you can achieve healthy, beautiful hair.
