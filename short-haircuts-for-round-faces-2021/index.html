Short Haircuts For Round Faces 2021 The Ultimate Guide

Are you tired of your long hair and want to try something different? Short haircuts for round faces 2021 might just be the answer you're looking for. In this article, we'll guide you through the best short haircuts for round faces, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair. So, let's get started!

  • How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Round Faces

Description

Short haircuts for round faces 2021 are perfect for those who want to make their features stand out. They can add volume, texture, and depth to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker. These haircuts can also make your face look slimmer and more defined. There are several types of short haircuts that you can try, such as pixie cuts, bob cuts, and lob cuts.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Round Faces

When it comes to choosing the right short haircut for round faces, there are a few things to keep in mind. You want to find a haircut that will flatter your face shape and highlight your best features. Here are some tips:

  • Look for haircuts with layers and texture to add volume and depth to your hair.
  • Avoid haircuts that end at your chin or jawline, as they can make your face look wider.
  • Consider a side-parted or asymmetrical haircut to create the illusion of a slimmer face.
  • Choose a haircut that compliments your natural hair texture and thickness.

Short Haircuts for Round Faces 2021

Now that you know what to look for in a short haircut for round faces, let's take a look at some of the most popular styles for 2021.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that has been popular for decades. It's a great option for those with round faces, as it adds height and dimension to your hair. You can style it in a variety of ways, from sleek and polished to messy and textured.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another timeless short haircut that looks great on round faces. It's a versatile style that can be tailored to your preferences. You can opt for a chin-length bob or a longer bob that falls just above your shoulders. The key is to add layers and texture to create movement and volume.

Lob Cut

The lob cut, or long bob, is a trendy short haircut that's perfect for those who want to keep some length to their hair. It's a great option for round faces, as it can create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face. You can add layers and texture to create volume and movement.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Round Faces

Styling short haircuts for round faces is easy and quick. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting at the roots to create volume.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create texture and waves.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Round Faces

Short haircuts for round faces require minimal maintenance, but there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs

Can I still wear bangs with a short haircut for round faces?

Yes, you can wear bangs with a short haircut for round faces. However, make sure that the bangs are layered and textured to create movement and volume.

What hair color looks best on short haircuts for round faces?

Any hair color can look great on short haircuts for round faces, but highlights and lowlights can add dimension and depth to your hair.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts for Round Faces

Short haircuts for round faces require minimal hair care, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, and always use a heat protectant.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Short haircuts for round faces 2021 are a great way to update your look and make a statement. With the right haircut, styling, and hair care, you can create a look that's uniquely you. So, what are you waiting for? Book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out one of these short haircuts for round faces!
