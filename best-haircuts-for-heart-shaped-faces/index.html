Best Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, it's important to choose a haircut that complements your features. In this article, we'll cover the best haircuts for heart shaped faces, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Faces

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a narrow chin and wide forehead. If you have this face shape, you'll want to choose a haircut that balances out your features. The best haircuts for heart shaped faces are those that add volume to the chin area and create width around the forehead.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a heart shaped face, you'll want to avoid styles that accentuate the forehead or chin. Instead, opt for hairstyles that create balance and proportion.

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts like pixie cuts and bobs can work well for heart shaped faces. These styles add volume to the chin area and create width around the forehead. To make the look more feminine, add some soft layers or bangs.

Medium Length Haircuts

Medium length haircuts like long bobs and shags can also work well for heart shaped faces. These styles add volume around the chin and create width around the forehead. To make the look more flattering, add some face-framing layers or side-swept bangs.

Long Haircuts

Long haircuts like layers and waves can also work well for heart shaped faces. These styles add volume to the chin area and create balance around the forehead. To make the look more flattering, add some face-framing layers or side-swept bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Once you've chosen a haircut that works well for your heart shaped face, it's important to know how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Use a curling iron or straightener to create the desired style.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

To keep your new haircut looking great, follow these tips:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid with a heart shaped face?

You should avoid hairstyles that accentuate the forehead or chin. This includes styles with a blunt fringe or middle part.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail with a heart shaped face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail with a heart shaped face. Just make sure to add some volume around the chin area to balance out your features.

Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Faces

In addition to choosing the right haircut and styling your hair properly, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut, you can create a flattering look that accentuates your best features. So, go ahead and try out some of these best haircuts for heart shaped faces!
