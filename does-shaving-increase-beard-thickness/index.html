Does Shaving Increase Beard Thickness

Written by Daniel Jul 31, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're someone who is trying to grow a thick beard, you may have heard that shaving regularly can help. But is there any truth to this? In this article, we'll explore the question, "does shaving increase beard thickness?"

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Many people believe that shaving your beard regularly can make it grow thicker and fuller. However, this is actually a myth. Shaving does not affect the thickness or density of your beard. In fact, the opposite is true: the longer you let your beard grow, the thicker and fuller it will become.

The reason why people believe that shaving can increase beard thickness is because when you shave, the hair that grows back is often coarser and darker than the hair that was previously there. This is simply because the hair has been cut at its thickest point, so the new growth appears thicker.

How to

If you want to grow a thick beard, the best thing you can do is to let it grow. This means avoiding shaving or trimming your beard for at least a few weeks, if not a few months. During this time, your beard will grow in thicker and fuller, and you can then trim it to your desired length and shape.

It's also important to take care of your beard during the growing process. This means keeping it clean and moisturized, and using products like beard oil and beard balm to promote healthy growth.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to growing a thick beard:

  1. Stop shaving or trimming your beard.
  2. Keep your beard clean and moisturized.
  3. Use beard oil and beard balm to promote healthy growth.
  4. Wait at least a few weeks, if not a few months, for your beard to grow in thick and full.
  5. Trim your beard to your desired length and shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for growing a thick beard:

  • Be patient. Growing a thick beard takes time.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as these can damage your hair follicles.
  • Consider using a beard growth supplement, such as biotin or vitamin E.

FAQ

Q: Does shaving increase beard thickness?

A: No, shaving does not increase beard thickness.

Q: How can I make my beard grow thicker?

A: The best way to make your beard grow thicker is to let it grow and take care of it during the growing process. This means keeping it clean and moisturized, and using products like beard oil and beard balm to promote healthy growth.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take care of your beard once it's grown in thick and full. This means continuing to keep it clean and moisturized, and using products like beard oil and beard balm to keep it healthy and soft.

Remember, growing a thick beard takes time and patience. But with the right care and attention, you can achieve the beard of your dreams.
