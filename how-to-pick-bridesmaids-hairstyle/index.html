How To Pick Bridesmaids Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 22, 2023 · 8 min read

Choosing the perfect bridesmaids hairstyle can be a daunting task. There are so many options to choose from, and you want to make sure that your bridesmaids look and feel their best on your big day. In this guide, we’ll take you through a step-by-step process to help you pick the perfect bridesmaids hairstyle.

Step 1: Consider Your Wedding Theme

The first step in picking the perfect bridesmaids hairstyle is to consider your wedding theme. If you’re having a boho wedding, you may want to consider loose waves or a messy bun. If you’re having a formal wedding, an updo or sleek straight hair may be more appropriate. Your bridesmaids’ hairstyles should complement the overall theme and vibe of your wedding.

Step 2: Look at Your Bridesmaids’ Hair Types

The next step is to look at your bridesmaids’ hair types. If your bridesmaids have fine hair, an updo may be a better choice to add volume. If your bridesmaids have curly hair, you may want to embrace their natural texture or opt for a half-up, half-down style. It’s important to work with your bridesmaids’ natural hair to create a style that will last throughout the day.

Step 3: Determine the Length of Your Bridesmaids’ Hair

The length of your bridesmaids’ hair will also play a role in the hairstyle you choose. If your bridesmaids have long hair, you may want to consider an updo or a braided style. If your bridesmaids have short hair, you may want to keep it simple with loose waves or a sleek bob.

Step 4: Consider Your Bridesmaids’ Personal Style

It’s important to consider your bridesmaids’ personal style when choosing a hairstyle. If your bridesmaids prefer a more natural look, you may want to opt for loose waves or a messy bun. If your bridesmaids prefer a more polished look, an updo or sleek straight hair may be more appropriate.

Step 5: Look for Inspiration

Look for inspiration from wedding magazines, Pinterest, and Instagram. Save photos of hairstyles that you like and share them with your bridesmaids. This will help you and your hairstylist create a clear vision of what you want.

Step 6: Schedule a Hair Trial

Schedule a hair trial with your bridesmaids to try out different styles. This will give you and your hairstylist the opportunity to make any changes or adjustments before the big day.

Step 7: Consider Your Hairstylist’s Recommendations

Your hairstylist is a professional and will have experience with different hairstyles and hair types. It’s important to listen to their recommendations and trust their expertise.

Step 8: Make Sure the Hairstyle Complements the Dress

The bridesmaids’ hairstyles should complement the dress. If your bridesmaids are wearing a dress with a high neckline, an updo may be a better choice to show off the dress. If your bridesmaids are wearing a dress with a low neckline, loose waves or a half-up, half-down style may be more appropriate.

Step 9: Don’t Forget About Accessories

Accessories can add a special touch to your bridesmaids’ hairstyles. Consider adding a flower crown, hairpins, or a headband to your bridesmaids’ hair to tie everything together.

Step 10: Hair Care Tips for Your Bridesmaids

Make sure your bridesmaids are taking care of their hair leading up to the wedding. Encourage them to use a deep conditioning treatment, avoid using heat tools, and get regular trims to keep their hair healthy and looking its best.

FAQ

What if my bridesmaids have different hair types?

If your bridesmaids have different hair types, consider choosing a hairstyle that can be customized to each individual. For example, a half-up, half-down style can work for both curly and straight hair.

How do I choose a bridesmaids hairstyle that complements my wedding dress?

Consider the neckline of your wedding dress when choosing a bridesmaids hairstyle. If you have a high neckline, an updo may be more appropriate to show off the dress. If you have a low neckline, a half-up, half-down style or loose waves may be more appropriate.

What kind of accessories can I add to my bridesmaids’ hairstyles?

You can add a flower crown, hairpins, a headband, or even a tiara to your bridesmaids’ hairstyles to tie everything together.

Hair Care

Make sure your bridesmaids are taking care of their hair leading up to the wedding. Encourage them to use a deep conditioning treatment, avoid using heat tools, and get regular trims to keep their hair healthy and looking its best.
