Point Cut Short Hairstyles A Trendy And Timeless Look

Written by Bardi Sep 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your long, unruly hair? Do you want a fresh new look that's low maintenance and easy to style? Look no further than point cut short hairstyles! This trendy and timeless look is perfect for anyone who wants to make a bold change without sacrificing style. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about point cut short hairstyles, from how to get the look to tips for maintaining your new 'do.

What are Point Cut Short Hairstyles?

Point cut short hairstyles are a type of haircut that involves cutting the hair at an angle, creating a textured, layered look. The hair is cut using a technique called "point cutting," which involves using scissors to cut into the hair at an angle, rather than straight across. This creates a more natural, undone look that's perfect for anyone who wants a low-maintenance yet stylish haircut.

How to Get the Look

If you're interested in getting a point cut short hairstyle, the first step is to find a stylist who is experienced in this technique. You'll want to bring in pictures of the hairstyle you're interested in, as well as any other styles you like or dislike. Your stylist will work with you to determine the best length and angle for your face shape and hair type. From there, they'll use the point cutting technique to create a layered, textured look that's both stylish and easy to maintain.

Step by Step

1. Find a stylist who is experienced in point cut short hairstyles.

2. Bring in pictures of the hairstyle you're interested in, as well as any other styles you like or dislike.

3. Work with your stylist to determine the best length and angle for your face shape and hair type.

4. Your stylist will use the point cutting technique to create a layered, textured look.

5. Style your new look using your favorite products and tools!

Tips

1. Use a texturizing spray or mousse to enhance the natural texture of your hair.

2. Experiment with different styling techniques, such as tousling your hair with your fingers or using a diffuser to create soft, natural waves.

3. Regular trims are essential for maintaining a point cut short hairstyle. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

4. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.

FAQ

Q: Can point cut short hairstyles work for any hair type?

A: Yes! Point cut short hairstyles can work for any hair type, from straight to curly to wavy.

Q: Will a point cut short hairstyle require a lot of maintenance?

A: No! Point cut short hairstyles are designed to be low-maintenance and easy to style.

Q: How often should I get a trim to maintain my point cut short hairstyle?

A: Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hairstyle looking fresh and stylish.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and strong is key to maintaining any hairstyle, including point cut short hairstyles. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in top condition:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

- Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.

- Limit your use of heat styling tools, as excessive heat can damage your hair.

- Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With proper care and maintenance, a point cut short hairstyle can be a stylish and timeless look that you'll love for years to come. So why not take the plunge and try out this trendy new haircut for yourself?
