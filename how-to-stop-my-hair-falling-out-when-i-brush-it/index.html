How To Stop My Hair Falling Out When I Brush It

Written by Eveline Apr 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're experiencing hair loss or noticing excessive hair shedding when you brush your hair, you're not alone. Many people struggle with hair loss and it can be a frustrating and stressful experience. Fortunately, there are steps you can take to prevent hair loss and protect your hair from damage. In this article, we'll explore how to stop your hair falling out when you brush it, including tips, step by step guide, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Stop Hair Falling Out When Brushing
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Preventing Hair Loss

How to Stop Hair Falling Out When Brushing

The first step in preventing hair loss when brushing your hair is to be gentle. Avoid using harsh brushes or pulling on your hair too hard. Instead, use a soft-bristled brush or a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair. This will help prevent hair breakage and reduce the amount of hair that falls out when you brush.

Another way to prevent hair loss is to avoid brushing your hair when it's wet. Wet hair is more fragile and prone to breakage, so wait until your hair is dry before brushing or combing it. If you must brush your hair when it's wet, use a wide-tooth comb and be very gentle.

It's also important to avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, such as flat irons or curling irons. These tools can damage your hair and cause it to break and fall out. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and avoid using them every day.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to prevent hair loss when brushing your hair:

  1. Choose a soft-bristled brush or wide-tooth comb.
  2. Gently detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  3. Wait until your hair is dry before brushing or combing it.
  4. If you must brush your hair when it's wet, use a wide-tooth comb and be very gentle.
  5. Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair.
  6. Use a heat protectant spray if you must use heat styling tools.
  7. Avoid pulling on your hair too hard or using harsh brushes.
  8. Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
  9. Consider taking a hair growth supplement or using a hair growth treatment.
  10. Consult with a dermatologist or hair loss specialist if you're experiencing excessive hair loss.

Tips for Preventing Hair Loss

Here are some additional tips for preventing hair loss:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth, such as biotin, vitamin D, and iron.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, such as braids or ponytails.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a protective hair spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair loss and hair care:

  • What causes hair loss? Hair loss can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, stress, medication, and medical conditions.
  • Can hair loss be prevented? While not all hair loss can be prevented, there are steps you can take to reduce your risk of hair loss and promote healthy hair growth.
  • What are some hair growth treatments? Hair growth treatments include topical minoxidil, oral medications, and hair transplant surgery.
  • How can I promote healthy hair growth? You can promote healthy hair growth by eating a healthy diet, staying hydrated, avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools, and using hair growth supplements or treatments.

Hair Care Advice

In addition to following the tips and advice outlined in this article, it's important to take good care of your hair overall. This includes washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo, conditioning your hair to keep it moisturized, and using hair masks or treatments to nourish your hair. Avoid over-washing your hair or using too many styling products, as this can damage your hair and cause it to break and fall out. With the right care and attention, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful for years to come.
