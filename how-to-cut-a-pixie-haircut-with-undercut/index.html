How To Cut A Pixie Haircut With Undercut A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, a pixie haircut with an undercut may be just what you need. This trendy style is popular among women of all ages and can be customized to suit your unique preferences. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting a pixie haircut with an undercut, including tips for styling and caring for your new 'do.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Cut a Pixie Haircut with Undercut
  • Step-by-Step Instructions

How to Cut a Pixie Haircut with Undercut

Before you begin cutting, it's important to decide how short you want your hair to be. Pixie cuts can range from a few inches long to just a few millimeters. You'll also need to choose the location of the undercut. This style typically involves shaving or cutting the hair on one or both sides of the head, creating a dramatic contrast with the longer hair on top.

Once you've decided on the length and location of the undercut, you're ready to begin cutting. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors or clippers
  • A comb or brush
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • A mirror

Step-by-Step Instructions:

Follow these steps to cut a pixie haircut with an undercut:

  1. Start by wetting your hair with the spray bottle. This will make it easier to cut and help you see the natural texture of your hair.
  2. Section off the longer hair on top of your head and clip it out of the way.
  3. Using scissors or clippers, cut the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length. You may need to use different blade sizes to create a gradual fade or a more dramatic contrast.
  4. Next, create the undercut by shaving or cutting the hair on one or both sides of your head. This can be done with scissors, clippers, or a razor, depending on your preference.
  5. Blend the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back. You may need to use thinning shears or a razor to create a seamless transition.
  6. Trim the hair on top to the desired length. This can be done with scissors or clippers, depending on your preference.
  7. Use the comb or brush to style your hair as desired. Pixie cuts can be worn sleek and straight or tousled and textured.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining Your Pixie Cut:

Now that you've cut your pixie haircut with an undercut, here are some tips for styling and maintaining your new 'do:

  • Invest in high-quality styling products, such as a texturizing spray or a pomade, to create different looks.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups and trims. Pixie cuts require more frequent maintenance than longer hairstyles.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or barrettes, to add some flair to your look.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons.

FAQs:

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a pixie haircut with an undercut:

Q: Will an undercut suit my face shape?

A: Pixie haircuts with undercuts can be customized to suit any face shape. Your stylist can help you choose the best length and location of the undercut based on your features.

Q: How long does it take to grow out a pixie cut?

A: It can take several months to a year to grow out a pixie cut, depending on the length you're aiming for. Regular trims and maintenance can help speed up the process.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts:

Here are some general hair care tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair after cutting a pixie haircut with an undercut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers or flat irons, too frequently, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to confidently cut and style your own pixie haircut with an undercut. Whether you're after a bold and edgy look or a chic and sophisticated style, this versatile haircut is sure to turn heads.
