Best Flat Iron For Thick Curly Frizzy Hair

Written by William Jul 08, 2023 · 9 min read

If you have thick curly frizzy hair, you know how difficult it can be to find a flat iron that can handle your hair type. But fear not, we've done the research and put together a list of the best flat irons for thick curly frizzy hair. Whether you're looking for a straightener that can handle your curls, tame your frizz, or protect your hair from heat damage, we've got you covered. Read on for our top picks and tips for styling your hair.

How to Choose the Best Flat Iron for Thick Curly Frizzy Hair

Before we get into our top picks, let's talk about what to look for when choosing a flat iron for thick curly frizzy hair. Here are some key factors to consider:

Plate Size

The size of the plates on your flat iron can make a big difference in how quickly and effectively you can straighten your hair. For thick hair, look for plates that are at least 1.5 inches wide to cover more surface area in less time.

Temperature

The temperature range of your flat iron is important for achieving the right level of heat to straighten your hair without damaging it. For thick curly frizzy hair, look for a flat iron that can heat up to at least 400 degrees Fahrenheit.

Material

The material of the plates on your flat iron can affect the smoothness of your hair and the amount of frizz it produces. Ceramic and tourmaline plates are great for reducing frizz and providing a smooth finish, while titanium plates are better for thick hair and can handle higher temperatures.

Our Top Picks for the Best Flat Iron for Thick Curly Frizzy Hair

1. BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium-Plated Ultra-Thin Straightening Iron

This flat iron is a favorite among hairstylists and for good reason. The titanium plates heat up quickly and evenly, and the ultra-thin design allows for more precise styling. Plus, the 5-inch plates are long enough to straighten even the thickest hair in fewer passes.

2. HSI Professional Glider Ceramic Tourmaline Ionic Flat Iron

This flat iron features ceramic and tourmaline plates that produce negative ions to reduce frizz and static. The adjustable temperature settings allow for customizable heat levels, and the 1-inch plates are perfect for thick curly hair.

3. Remington S5500 1" Anti-Static Flat Iron

This affordable flat iron features ceramic plates infused with anti-static technology to reduce frizz and flyaways. The 1-inch plates are great for thick curly hair, and the temperature control allows for customizable heat levels.

How to Straighten Thick Curly Frizzy Hair

Now that you have your flat iron, it's time to learn how to straighten your hair. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles and make sure it's completely dry.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or elastics. Start at the bottom of your hair and work your way up to the top.

Step 3: Straighten Your Hair

Take a section of hair and clamp the flat iron down as close to the roots as possible. Slowly glide the flat iron down the length of your hair, repeating as necessary until the section is straight. Continue with the rest of your hair.

Tips for Styling Thick Curly Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling your hair:

1. Use a Deep Conditioner

Thick curly frizzy hair can be prone to dryness, so it's important to use a deep conditioner regularly to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Don't Overdo It

Avoid straightening your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage. Aim for once or twice a week, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to extend your style.

3. Embrace Your Curls

If you're tired of straightening your hair, embrace your curls! Use a curl-enhancing product and let your natural texture shine.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best flat iron for thick curly frizzy hair?

The best flat iron for thick curly frizzy hair is one with large plates, adjustable temperature settings, and ceramic or titanium plates. Our top picks include the BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium-Plated Ultra-Thin Straightening Iron, HSI Professional Glider Ceramic Tourmaline Ionic Flat Iron, and Remington S5500 1" Anti-Static Flat Iron.

Can I straighten my hair if it's curly and frizzy?

Yes, you can straighten curly and frizzy hair with a flat iron. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and work in small sections to avoid damage.

How do I prevent frizz when straightening my hair?

To prevent frizz when straightening your hair, use a flat iron with ceramic or tourmaline plates, apply a smoothing serum or oil, and avoid touching your hair too much after straightening.

Conclusion

With the right flat iron and styling techniques, you can achieve sleek, straight hair even if you have thick curly frizzy hair. Remember to choose a flat iron with the right plate size, temperature range, and material, and use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. And don't forget to embrace your natural texture – curls and all!
