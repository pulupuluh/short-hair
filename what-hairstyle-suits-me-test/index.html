What Hairstyle Suits Me Test Find The Perfect Hairstyle For Your Face Shape

Description

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure which one suits your face shape. That's why the "what hairstyle suits me test" is a popular tool that many hairstylists and beauty experts use to help their clients find the perfect hairstyle. This test takes into account your face shape, hair texture, and personal style to recommend the most flattering hairstyle for you. In this article, we'll take you through the steps of taking the "what hairstyle suits me test" and give you some tips on how to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape.

How to Take the Hairstyle Suits Me Test

Taking the "what hairstyle suits me test" is easy and can be done in just a few minutes. Here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in taking the "what hairstyle suits me test" is to determine your face shape. There are five basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Take a good look at the shape of your face and compare it to the following descriptions:
  • Round: Your face is nearly as wide as it is long, with full cheeks and a rounded chin.
  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and a slightly pointed chin.
  • Square: Your face is roughly as wide as it is long, with a strong jawline and a square hairline.
  • Heart: Your face is wider at the temples and narrows at the chin, with a pointed chin.
  • Diamond: Your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at the forehead and chin.

Step 2: Determine Your Hair Texture

The next step is to determine your hair texture. Hair texture refers to the natural curl pattern of your hair. There are three basic hair textures: straight, wavy, and curly. To determine your hair texture, let your hair air dry without any styling products. Take a good look at the natural curl pattern of your hair and compare it to the following descriptions:
  • Straight: Your hair is naturally straight and has little or no curl.
  • Wavy: Your hair has a natural wave pattern that is visible when it air dries.
  • Curly: Your hair has a natural curl pattern that is visible when it air dries.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you've determined your face shape and hair texture, it's time to take the "what hairstyle suits me test." Here's how:
  1. Visit a website that offers a "what hairstyle suits me test," such as Hairstyleonpoint.com or Instyle.com.
  2. Select your face shape and hair texture from the drop-down menus.
  3. Answer a few questions about your personal style, such as whether you prefer a classic or trendy look.
  4. Upload a photo of yourself or use the virtual try-on tool to see how different hairstyles would look on you.
  5. Review the recommended hairstyles and select the one that you like the most.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape:
  • Round faces look best with hairstyles that add height and length, such as long layers and side-swept bangs.
  • Oval faces can pull off just about any hairstyle, but look best with styles that frame the face and highlight the cheekbones.
  • Square faces look best with hairstyles that soften the jawline, such as long layers and soft waves.
  • Heart-shaped faces look best with hairstyles that balance the width of the forehead and the narrowness of the chin, such as chin-length bobs and side-swept bangs.
  • Diamond-shaped faces look best with hairstyles that soften the cheekbones and balance the forehead and chin, such as chin-length bobs and layered cuts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I take the "what hairstyle suits me test" on my phone?
A: Yes, most websites that offer the test are mobile-friendly and can be accessed on your phone. Q: What if I don't like the recommended hairstyles?
A: Remember, the "what hairstyle suits me test" is just a tool to help you find the most flattering hairstyle for your face shape. Ultimately, the decision is up to you. If you don't like the recommended hairstyles, try tweaking them to better suit your personal style. Q: Can I take the test if I have short hair?
A: Yes, the test can be taken by anyone regardless of hair length.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
Taking the "what hairstyle suits me test" is a fun and easy way to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape. By following the steps outlined in this article and keeping our tips in mind, you'll be well on your way to a fabulous new look.
