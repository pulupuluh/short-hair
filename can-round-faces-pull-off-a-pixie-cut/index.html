Can Round Faces Pull Off A Pixie Cut

Written by William Mar 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When it comes to haircuts, the pixie cut is one of the most popular and versatile styles. It's a short hairstyle that's easy to manage, and it's perfect for those who want to show off their facial features. But can round faces pull off a pixie cut? The answer is yes!

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that's cut close to the scalp. It's a low-maintenance style that's perfect for those who don't want to spend a lot of time styling their hair. The pixie cut is also a great way to show off your facial features, especially if you have a round face.

How To

If you're thinking about getting a pixie cut, there are a few things to consider. First, you'll need to find a stylist who specializes in short haircuts. You'll also need to decide on the length and style of your pixie cut. Once you've made these decisions, your stylist will cut your hair to the desired length and shape.

Step By Step

1. Find a stylist who specializes in short haircuts.

2. Decide on the length and style of your pixie cut.

3. Your stylist will cut your hair to the desired length and shape.

Tips

Here are a few tips for those with round faces who want to pull off a pixie cut:

- Consider adding layers to your pixie cut. This will add volume and texture to your hair, which can help balance out the roundness of your face.

- Opt for a side-swept bang. This will help elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

- Keep your hair at least an inch long on the top. This will help create height and balance out the roundness of your face.

FAQ

Q: Will a pixie cut make my face look even rounder?

A: No, a properly styled pixie cut can actually help elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

Q: How often will I need to get my pixie cut trimmed?

A: You'll need to get your pixie cut trimmed every four to six weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care

Since a pixie cut is a short hairstyle, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

- Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.

- Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

- Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your pixie cut.

In conclusion, round faces can definitely pull off a pixie cut. With the right stylist, length, and style, a pixie cut can help elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. Follow these tips to keep your pixie cut looking its best, and you'll be sure to turn heads wherever you go!
