How To Make Your Hair Wavy If It s Naturally Straight

Written by Petter Feb 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to our blog post on "how to make your hair wavy if it's naturally straight." If you're tired of your straight locks and want to add some waves to your hair, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll show you how to create effortless, beachy waves that will make you look like you just stepped off the sand. Before we get started, let's take a look at what you'll need.

What You'll Need

To create wavy hair, you'll need a few tools and products:

  • A curling iron
  • Hairspray
  • Heat protectant
  • Sea salt spray
  • Texture spray

How to Make Your Hair Wavy If It's Naturally Straight

Step 1: Protect Your Hair

Before using any heat styling tool, it's important to protect your hair from heat damage. Apply a heat protectant spray to your locks to prevent breakage and keep your hair healthy.

Step 2: Add Texture

Next, add texture to your hair by applying a sea salt spray. This will give your hair some grit and make it easier to style. Spray the product onto your hair evenly, scrunching your locks as you go.

Step 3: Create Waves

Using a curling iron, take small sections of your hair and wrap them around the barrel. Hold for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process throughout your hair, alternating the direction of the curls to create a natural wave.

Step 4: Add Definition

Once you've curled all of your hair, apply a texture spray to give your waves some definition. This will help hold the style in place and prevent frizziness.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To finish off your wavy hair look, apply a light mist of hairspray to your locks. This will keep your waves in place all day long.

Tips for Making Your Hair Wavy If It's Naturally Straight

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind as you style your hair:

  • Don't curl your hair too tightly. Loose waves look more natural and effortless.
  • Use a smaller barrel curling iron to create tighter waves, and a larger barrel for looser waves.
  • For a more dramatic look, curl your hair away from your face.
  • Remember to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your locks.

FAQs

Q: Can I make my hair wavy without using heat?

A: Yes, you can make your hair wavy without heat by braiding your hair while it's damp and leaving it overnight. In the morning, undo the braids and you'll have natural-looking waves.

Q: How long will my wavy hair last?

A: Your wavy hair should last all day, but you may need to touch it up with hairspray throughout the day to keep the waves in place.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Now that you know how to make your hair wavy if it's naturally straight, it's important to take care of your locks to keep them healthy and looking their best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your locks of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage to your locks.

We hope you found this article helpful in creating wavy hair. Remember to have fun with your hair and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
