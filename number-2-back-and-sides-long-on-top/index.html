Number 2 Back And Sides Long On Top The Trendy Hairstyle For Men In 2023

Written by William May 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring haircut? Do you want to switch things up and try something new? Look no further than the number 2 back and sides long on top hairstyle. This trendy haircut is taking the fashion world by storm and is perfect for men who want a stylish yet low-maintenance look. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about this hairstyle, from how to get it to tips for maintaining it.

What is the Number 2 Back and Sides Long on Top Hairstyle?

The number 2 back and sides long on top hairstyle is a variation of the classic undercut. The sides and back are buzzed with a number 2 clipper, while the hair on top is left longer and styled in a variety of ways. This hairstyle is versatile and can be tailored to your personal preferences, making it a popular choice for men of all ages.

How to Get the Number 2 Back and Sides Long on Top Hairstyle

Getting the number 2 back and sides long on top hairstyle is easy and can be done at any barbershop or salon. Here's how:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length you want for the hair on top. This can vary depending on your personal style and the shape of your face. Talk to your barber or stylist to determine the best length for you.

Step 2: Buzz the Sides and Back

Next, the barber or stylist will buzz the sides and back with a number 2 clipper. This will leave the hair short and low-maintenance.

Step 3: Cut and Style the Hair on Top

Finally, the barber or stylist will cut and style the hair on top to your desired length and style. This may involve using scissors or a razor to create layers or texture. They will then style the hair using a pomade, gel, or other product to keep it in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Number 2 Back and Sides Long on Top Hairstyle

The number 2 back and sides long on top hairstyle is relatively low-maintenance, but there are still some tips you should follow to keep it looking its best:

Tip 1: Wash and Condition Regularly

Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

Use the right hair products to maintain your hairstyle. A pomade or wax can help add texture and hold to the hair on top, while a hair oil can keep the sides and back looking shiny and healthy.

Tip 3: Get Regular Touch-Ups

Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for touch-ups. This will keep the sides and back looking neat and tidy, and prevent the hair on top from becoming too long and unmanageable.

FAQ

What Face Shapes Suit the Number 2 Back and Sides Long on Top Hairstyle?

The number 2 back and sides long on top hairstyle is versatile and can suit a variety of face shapes. However, it is particularly well-suited to men with square, oval, or diamond-shaped faces.

Can I Style the Hair on Top in Different Ways?

Absolutely! The hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, from a messy, textured look to a slicked-back style. Talk to your barber or stylist to determine the best style for you.

Is the Number 2 Back and Sides Long on Top Hairstyle Professional?

Yes! The number 2 back and sides long on top hairstyle is a stylish and professional look that is suitable for any workplace. It can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining any hairstyle, including the number 2 back and sides long on top. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Avoid Overwashing

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and damage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

Tip 2: Use Heat Protection

If you use heat tools like a hair dryer or straightener, be sure to use a heat protection spray to prevent damage and breakage.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

The number 2 back and sides long on top hairstyle is a trendy and versatile look that is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance yet stylish haircut. By following these tips and taking proper care of your hair, you can keep this hairstyle looking its best. So why not give it a try and switch up your look today?
