How To Give Thin Curly Hair Volume A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your thin curly hair falling flat and lacking volume? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many people with thin curly hair struggle to achieve the volume they desire. However, with the right techniques and products, you can give your hair the lift and body it needs. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to give thin curly hair volume.

Table of Contents

  • How to Give Thin Curly Hair Volume
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Achieving Volume
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Thin Curly Hair

How to Give Thin Curly Hair Volume

There are several ways to give thin curly hair volume:

  • Blow-drying your hair upside down
  • Using volumizing products
  • Teasing your hair at the roots
  • Using a diffuser when air-drying

Incorporating these techniques into your hair care routine can help you achieve the volume you desire. Let's dive into the step-by-step guide below.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo. Look for products that contain ingredients like keratin, biotin, or caffeine, which can help strengthen and thicken your hair. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.

2. Apply a volumizing conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a volumizing conditioner to the ends of your hair. Avoid applying conditioner to your roots, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Leave the conditioner in for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Use a volumizing mousse or foam

Apply a volumizing mousse or foam to your damp hair. These products can help add body and texture to your hair without weighing it down. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

4. Blow-dry your hair upside down

Flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This can help lift your roots and add volume to your hair. Use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

5. Tease your hair at the roots

Using a teasing comb, gently backcomb your hair at the roots. This can help create volume and lift at your crown. Be sure to tease your hair in small sections to avoid damaging it.

6. Finish with a volumizing hairspray

To keep your hair in place and maintain the volume you've created, finish with a volumizing hairspray. Look for products that offer a strong hold without leaving your hair stiff or crunchy.

Tips for Achieving Volume

Here are some additional tips for achieving volume in thin curly hair:

  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as these can weigh down your hair and make it look flat
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can create static and frizz
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to create natural waves and volume
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look limp

FAQs

Can I achieve volume in my thin curly hair without using heat?

Yes! Try air-drying your hair with a diffuser attachment or sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to create natural waves and volume.

What type of brush should I use to style my thin curly hair?

A wide-tooth comb is the best option for detangling and styling thin curly hair. Avoid using a brush, which can create static and frizz.

Hair Care for Thin Curly Hair

Thin curly hair requires special care to maintain its health and volume. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look limp
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair
  • Avoid using heat tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair and make it look dull
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape and volume

By following these tips and incorporating volumizing techniques into your hair care routine, you can achieve the volume and body you desire in your thin curly hair.
