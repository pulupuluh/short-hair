How To Get Curly Hair To Lay Down For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 19, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're a guy with curly hair, you know that sometimes it can be difficult to get your curls to lay down smoothly. Whether you're looking for a sleek, polished look or just want to tame your curls a bit, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you exactly how to get curly hair to lay down for guys, using relaxed language and easy-to-follow steps.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Get Curly Hair to Lay Down for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Get Curly Hair to Lay Down for Guys

If you want to get your curly hair to lay down smoothly, you'll need to follow a few simple steps. Here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Conditioned Hair

The first step to getting your curly hair to lay down is to make sure it's clean and conditioned. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's designed for curly hair, to help keep your curls moisturized and healthy.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. This will help to hold your curls in place and give them a sleek, polished look. There are a variety of styling products that work well for curly hair, including gels, creams, and pomades. Choose the product that works best for your hair type and texture.

Step 3: Use a Comb or Brush to Smooth Your Hair

Once you've applied your styling product, use a comb or brush to smooth your hair down. Make sure to comb or brush your hair in the direction you want it to lay down. If you have particularly stubborn curls, you may want to use a wide-toothed comb to help detangle your hair before smoothing it down.

Step 4: Use a Hair Dryer to Set Your Style

Finally, use a hair dryer to set your style in place. Use a low heat setting and hold the dryer about 6 inches away from your hair. As you dry your hair, use your comb or brush to continue smoothing it down. Once your hair is dry, you should have a smooth, polished look that will last all day.

Step-by-Step Guide

For those who prefer a more detailed guide, here's a step-by-step breakdown of how to get curly hair to lay down:

  1. Start with clean, conditioned hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair.
  3. Use a comb or brush to smooth your hair down.
  4. Use a hair dryer to set your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair

Once you've gotten your curly hair to lay down, you'll want to make sure it stays that way. Here are a few tips for maintaining your curly hair:

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to help prevent frizz.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your curls.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about getting curly hair to lay down:

Can I use a hairbrush to smooth my curls?

Yes, you can use a hairbrush to smooth your curls. Just make sure to use a brush that's designed for curly hair, and be gentle to avoid damaging your curls.

What type of styling product should I use?

The type of styling product you should use depends on your hair type and texture. Gels, creams, and pomades are all good options for getting curly hair to lay down.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

You should aim to wash your curly hair no more than once or twice a week, to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a little extra care and attention to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Don't brush your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to get your curly hair to lay down smoothly and stay looking great all day long. Whether you're getting ready for work or a night out, you'll have a polished, put-together look that's sure to turn heads.
