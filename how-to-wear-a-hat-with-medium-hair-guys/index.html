How To Wear A Hat With Medium Hair Guys

Written by Daniel Aug 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a guy with medium-length hair, you might be wondering how to wear a hat without messing up your hairdo. Well, look no further! In this article, we'll show you the best ways to wear a hat with medium hair guys. We'll cover everything from how to choose the right hat to how to style your hair after taking off the hat.

How to Choose the Right Hat

The first step to wearing a hat with medium hair is to choose the right hat. Here are some tips:

1. Consider your face shape

When choosing a hat, consider your face shape. If you have a round face, go for a hat with a brim that's wider than your face. If you have a long face, go for a hat with a shorter brim.

2. Choose the right size

Make sure to choose a hat that fits your head properly. If it's too big or too small, it won't look good.

3. Pick a hat that matches your style

Choose a hat that matches your personal style. If you're into streetwear, go for a snapback or a beanie. If you're more of a classic guy, a fedora or a newsboy cap might be more your style.

How to Wear a Hat with Medium Hair

Now that you've chosen the right hat, it's time to put it on. Here are some tips:

1. Tilt the hat

Tilt the hat slightly to one side or the other. This will add some personality to your look.

2. Don't flatten your hair

When putting on the hat, make sure not to flatten your hair too much. Use your fingers to gently lift your hair and create some volume.

3. Experiment with different hairstyles

Try out different hairstyles with your hat. You can wear your hair down, slicked back, or in a messy bun. The possibilities are endless!

Step-by-Step Guide to Wearing a Hat with Medium Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to wearing a hat with medium hair:

1. Start with clean hair

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before putting on the hat. You don't want to trap any moisture under the hat.

2. Choose the right hat

Choose a hat that fits your style and face shape.

3. Tilt the hat

Tilt the hat slightly to one side or the other.

4. Lift your hair

Use your fingers to gently lift your hair and create some volume.

5. Experiment with different hairstyles

Try out different hairstyles with your hat.

Tips for Wearing a Hat with Medium Hair

Here are some additional tips for wearing a hat with medium hair:

1. Don't wear the hat too tight

Make sure the hat isn't too tight. If it's too tight, it can cause your hair to flatten.

2. Use a hairspray

If you're worried about your hair getting messed up under the hat, use a hairspray to keep it in place.

3. Don't wear the hat for too long

Don't wear the hat for too long. If you wear it for too long, it can cause your hair to become flattened and greasy.

Hair Care after Wearing a Hat

After taking off the hat, here are some hair care tips:

1. Brush your hair

Use a brush to gently brush out any tangles or knots in your hair.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.

3. Style your hair

Style your hair as desired. You can use a hair dryer, straightener, or curler to achieve your desired look.

FAQs

1. Can I wear a hat with medium hair?

Yes, you can wear a hat with medium hair. Just make sure to choose the right hat and follow the tips in this article.

2. Will wearing a hat damage my hair?

No, wearing a hat won't damage your hair. However, wearing a hat for too long can cause your hair to become flattened and greasy.

3. How do I choose the right hat for my face shape?

Choose a hat with a brim that's wider than your face if you have a round face. If you have a long face, go for a hat with a shorter brim.

Now that you know how to wear a hat with medium hair guys, go out there and rock that hat with confidence!
