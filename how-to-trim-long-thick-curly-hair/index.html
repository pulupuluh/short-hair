How To Trim Long Thick Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long, thick, curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful and can be styled in so many ways, it can also be difficult to manage and maintain. One of the most important things you can do to keep your hair healthy and looking its best is to trim it regularly. In this article, we'll show you how to trim long thick curly hair in a way that will help you maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

  • Tips for Trimming Long Thick Curly Hair

Description

Trimming your hair regularly is essential for keeping it healthy and preventing split ends. For those with long thick curly hair, trimming can be a bit more challenging. The key is to trim your hair while it's dry and stretched out, so you can see the shape of your hair and how much you need to trim. You'll also want to use sharp scissors to prevent damage to your hair. In this article, we'll show you how to trim your long thick curly hair step-by-step.

How to Trim Long Thick Curly Hair

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A mirror

It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle with water to help you dampen your hair as needed.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to trim your long thick curly hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will make it easier to comb and detangle.
  2. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.
  3. Section your hair. Use clips or hair ties to separate your hair into manageable sections.
  4. Take one section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the ends of the hair between your fingers.
  5. Using sharp scissors, trim the ends of the hair. Only trim a small amount at a time to avoid cutting off too much.
  6. Repeat with the remaining sections of hair.
  7. Check your hair in the mirror to ensure that it's even.
  8. If needed, make any additional trims to even out your hair.

Remember to only trim a small amount at a time. It's better to make multiple small trims than one big one.

Tips for Trimming Long Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you trim your long thick curly hair:

  • Trim your hair when it's dry and stretched out. This will help you see the shape of your hair and how much you need to trim.
  • Use sharp scissors to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Don't trim too much off at once. It's better to make multiple small trims than one big one.
  • If you're not comfortable trimming your own hair, consider going to a professional stylist.

FAQs

How often should I trim my long thick curly hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Can I trim my hair when it's wet?

No, it's better to trim your hair when it's dry and stretched out. This will help you see the shape of your hair and how much you need to trim.

What type of scissors should I use to trim my hair?

Use sharp scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair.

Hair Care for Long Thick Curly Hair

In addition to trimming your hair regularly, there are other things you can do to keep your long thick curly hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine in pools.

By following these tips and regularly trimming your hair, you can keep your long thick curly hair looking and feeling healthy.
