How To Cut A Man s Hair With Thinning Shears

Written by Luffy Jul 02, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of using regular scissors to cut men's hair? Do you want to achieve a more natural and textured look? Thinning shears might be the solution for you. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut a man's hair with thinning shears. We will also provide tips and hair care advice to ensure that your client's hair looks and feels great.

How to

Before you start cutting, make sure that you have the right tools. You will need a pair of thinning shears, regular scissors, a comb, and hair clippers.

Step 1: Wash and dry the hair. Wet hair is harder to cut, and it can also affect the results.

Step 2: Use the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length. You can use different clipper sizes to create layers and texture.

Step 3: Use the regular scissors to cut the top section of the hair. Determine the length you want to achieve and cut it straight across.

Step 4: Use the thinning shears to create texture and remove bulk from the hair. Take small sections of the hair and use the thinning shears to cut into the hair.

Step 5: Blend the hair by using the regular scissors and thinning shears together. Comb the hair and use the scissors to trim any stray hairs. Use the thinning shears to create a natural and textured look.

Tips

Tip 1: Use the thinning shears sparingly. Overusing them can make the hair look too thin and stringy.

Tip 2: Use a comb to make sure that the hair is evenly distributed.

Tip 3: Practice makes perfect. It might take some time to get used to using thinning shears, but with practice, you will be able to achieve the desired results.

FAQ

Q: Can thinning shears be used to cut all types of hair?

A: Thinning shears work best on thick and coarse hair. They might not be as effective on thin and fine hair.

Q: Can thinning shears damage the hair?

A: Thinning shears can cause damage if they are not used properly. Make sure to use them sparingly and follow the steps above.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of the hair after cutting it with thinning shears. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and moisturized.

Tip 2: Avoid using heat styling tools too often. Heat can cause damage to the hair and make it look dry and brittle.

Tip 3: Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of the haircut.

With these tips and steps, you can confidently cut a man's hair with thinning shears. Remember to take your time and practice to achieve the best results.
