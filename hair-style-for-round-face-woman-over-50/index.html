Best Hair Style For Round Face Woman Over 50

Written by Bardi Mar 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our hair also changes. It becomes thinner, drier, and more delicate. Moreover, our face shape also changes, and many women over 50 have a round face shape. Finding the right hair style that suits your face shape and hair type can be challenging. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this blog post, we'll show you the best hair style for round face woman over 50. Keep reading to find out more.

Description

Round face shapes are characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a soft jawline. The ideal hair style for round face woman over 50 should create the illusion of a longer face shape, add volume to the top of the head, and draw attention away from the chin area. Moreover, the hair style should be low-maintenance and easy to style.

How To

The best hair style for round face woman over 50 is a layered bob. Here's how to get this look:

Step 1: Consult with Your Hair Stylist

Before getting this hair style, consult with your hair stylist. They will assess your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to determine if this hair style is suitable for you.

Step 2: Cut Your Hair into a Layered Bob

Your hair stylist will cut your hair into a layered bob. The layers will add volume to the top of your head, elongate your face shape, and draw attention away from your chin area. Moreover, the layers will make your hair look fuller and thicker.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

You can style your layered bob in many ways. You can blow-dry your hair for a sleek look, or you can add some curls or waves for a more textured look. You can also use hair products like mousse, hairspray, or serum to add volume, shine, and hold to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair style:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools like a curling iron or straightener.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to moisturize and nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much hair products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair style for round face woman over 50:

What other hair styles are suitable for round face woman over 50?

Other hair styles that are suitable for round face woman over 50 include a long bob, a pixie cut, a shoulder-length cut with layers, and a side-swept bangs.

Can I color my hair if I have a round face shape?

Yes, you can color your hair if you have a round face shape. However, make sure to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and eye color. Moreover, avoid coloring your hair too often as it can damage your hair.

Do I need to use a specific hair product for my hair type?

Yes, it's essential to use a hair product that suits your hair type. For example, if you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have thin hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray. Consult with your hair stylist or a hair care specialist to determine the best hair products for your hair type.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy and beautiful hair, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it's wet.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair and let it air dry whenever possible.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

Now that you know the best hair style for round face woman over 50, it's time to book an appointment with your hair stylist and get this look. Remember to maintain your hair style by following the tips and hair care routine we've shared with you. Enjoy your new hair style and feel confident and beautiful at any age.
