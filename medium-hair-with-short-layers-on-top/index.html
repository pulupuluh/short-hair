Medium Hair With Short Layers On Top

Written by Robby Jun 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a fresh, new hairstyle that's easy to maintain and looks great? Consider medium hair with short layers on top! This trendy hairstyle is perfect for those who want a little bit of volume and texture without sacrificing length. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care. Let's get started!

Description

Medium hair with short layers on top is a versatile haircut that works well with many different hair types and face shapes. The layers on top add volume and movement to the hair, while the length allows for easy styling and versatility. This haircut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish and put together.

How to

If you're interested in getting a medium hair with short layers on top, the first step is to find a hairstylist who specializes in this cut. Once you've found a stylist, you can discuss your desired length, layering, and overall style. Your stylist will then use a combination of scissors and a razor to create the layers on top, while leaving the length intact.

Styling this haircut is easy and straightforward. Simply blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume, and use a flat iron or curling iron to add some texture and waves. You can also use a texturizing spray or pomade to add even more texture and hold.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a nourishing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair healthy and hydrated, which is essential for any hairstyle.

Step 2: Towel Dry

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Be careful not to rub your hair too hard, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product of your choice, such as a texturizing spray or mousse, to your damp hair. This will help to add volume and texture while also providing hold.

Step 4: Blow Dry

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, starting with the bottom layers and working your way up to the top layers. Be sure to lift the hair at the roots to create volume.

Step 5: Style

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add some texture and waves. You can also use a texturizing spray or pomade to add even more texture and hold.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling and caring for your medium hair with short layers on top:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium hair with short layers on top:

Is this hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle works well with many different hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your layers looking fresh and prevent split ends.

What styling products should I use?

Experiment with different styling products, such as texturizing sprays, mousses, and pomades, to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips for caring for your medium hair with short layers on top:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a nourishing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and low-maintenance medium hair with short layers on top. Whether you're heading to the office or out on the town, this versatile haircut is sure to turn heads.
