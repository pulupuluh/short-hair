How To Put Your Hair In A Man Bun Short Hair

Written by Petter Mar 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are looking for a stylish and practical hairstyle that is perfect for both casual and formal occasions, then look no further than the man bun. Whether you have long or short hair, you can rock this trendy hairstyle with ease. However, if you have short hair, you may be wondering how to put your hair in a man bun. In this blog post, we will guide you through the steps to achieve the perfect man bun on short hair.

Description

A man bun is a popular hairstyle that involves tying your hair into a bun at the crown of your head. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Although it is typically associated with longer hair, short hair can also create a stylish man bun.

How to Put Your Hair in a Man Bun Short Hair

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step in achieving the perfect man bun is to start with clean hair. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any product buildup or dirt. This will help your hair to be more manageable and easier to style.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair Back

Using a brush or comb, brush your hair back away from your face. Make sure your hair is smooth and free of knots or tangles.

Step 3: Gather Your Hair at the Crown of Your Head

Using your hands, gather your hair at the crown of your head. Use a hair tie to secure your hair in a ponytail. Make sure the ponytail is tight and secure.

Step 4: Twist Your Hair and Wrap It into a Bun

Twist your ponytail and wrap it around the base of your ponytail to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place. Make sure the bun is tight and secure.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To ensure that your man bun stays in place all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to set the style and prevent any flyaways.

Tips

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • Add some texture to your hair by using a sea salt spray or texturizing paste before styling.
  • Experiment with different types of buns, such as a half-bun or a messy bun, to find the perfect style for you.

FAQ

Can you put short hair in a man bun?

Yes, you can put short hair in a man bun. Follow the steps outlined above to achieve the perfect man bun on short hair.

What products should I use to style a man bun?

You can use a variety of products to style a man bun, such as a hair tie, bobby pins, hairspray, sea salt spray, and texturizing paste.

How long does my hair need to be to put it in a man bun?

Although longer hair is typically associated with a man bun, you can still achieve the style with shorter hair. A length of at least 6 inches is recommended for a full man bun, but you can create a smaller bun with shorter hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and manageable, it is important to follow a proper hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner, using heat protectant products when styling with heat tools, and avoiding harsh chemicals and treatments that can damage your hair.

By following these steps and tips, you can confidently rock a man bun on short hair. Whether you are going to the office or a night out with friends, this stylish and practical hairstyle is sure to turn heads. So, give it a try and see how easy it is to put your hair in a man bun on short hair.
