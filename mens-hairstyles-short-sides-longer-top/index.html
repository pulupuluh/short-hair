Mens Hairstyles Short Sides Longer Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 28, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're looking to update your hairstyle, consider the popular trend of mens hairstyles short sides longer top. This versatile look can be customized to suit your personal style, and is perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this style, from how to achieve it, to tips for maintenance. Let's get started!

Description

The mens hairstyles short sides longer top is exactly what it sounds like – a haircut that features shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on top. This style can be worn with a variety of lengths on top, from a few inches to several inches, and can be styled in many different ways. It's a popular choice for men of all ages and is suitable for any face shape.

How to Achieve the Look

Getting the mens hairstyles short sides longer top look is easy with the right tools and a skilled hairstylist. Here's how to achieve the look:

Step 1: Choose the Length

The first step is to decide how long you want your hair on top to be. This will depend on your personal style and face shape. You can choose to have a few inches of length, or go for a more dramatic look with several inches of hair on top.

Step 2: Cut the Sides Short

The next step is to cut the sides short. This can be done with clippers or scissors, depending on your preference. The length of the sides will depend on the length of the hair on top, but a common length is a #2 or #3 guard on the clippers.

Step 3: Blend the Top and Sides

Once the sides are cut, the hairstylist will blend the top and sides together for a seamless look. This is typically done with scissors, and the stylist will work from the sides up to the top, blending the hair as they go.

Step 4: Style the Top

Finally, it's time to style the top of your hair. This can be done with a variety of products, including pomade, wax, or gel. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and desired style.

Tips for Maintenance

Once you have your mens hairstyles short sides longer top, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep the sides short and the top in shape.
  • Use quality hair products to style your hair and keep it looking great throughout the day.
  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. The mens hairstyles short sides longer top is versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about the mens hairstyles short sides longer top:

What face shape is best for this style?

The mens hairstyles short sides longer top is suitable for any face shape. However, if you have a round face, you may want to keep the hair on top shorter to avoid adding width to your face.

Do I need to use product to style this look?

While you don't necessarily need to use product to style this look, it can help to keep your hair in place and looking great throughout the day. Experiment with different products to find what works best for you.

Can I wear this style to a formal event?

Yes! The mens hairstyles short sides longer top can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too frequently, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips, you can achieve and maintain the mens hairstyles short sides longer top look. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice.
