How To Style Flip Out Bob

Written by Aprilia Aug 28, 2023 · 6 min read

Welcome to our blog post on how to style flip out bob. A flip out bob is a popular hairstyle that features flipped out ends, giving it a playful and fun look. If you're looking to achieve this style, then you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll guide you through the steps to create a flip out bob that looks chic and stylish.

Table of Content

  1. How to Style Flip Out Bob?
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

Step by Step

Here are the steps to create a flip out bob:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  2. Divide your hair into sections. Use a clip or hair tie to separate the top section from the bottom section.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the bottom section and clamp it with a flat iron close to the roots.
  4. Flip the flat iron outwards and slide it down to the ends of your hair. This will create a flipped out effect.
  5. Repeat step 3 and 4 for all sections of hair in the bottom section.
  6. Release the top section of hair and repeat steps 3-5 for all sections of hair in the top section.
  7. Once you've flipped out all sections of hair, use a comb or your fingers to blend the sections together.
  8. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

And voila! You've created a gorgeous flip out bob that's perfect for any occasion.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while styling your flip out bob:

  • Use a high-quality flat iron with ceramic plates for best results.
  • Don't clamp the flat iron too hard on your hair as it can cause damage.
  • Make sure to section your hair properly to ensure that no areas are missed.
  • For more volume, tease the crown of your hair before styling.
  • Experiment with different angles while flipping out your hair to create a more natural look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about flip out bobs:

Q. Will the flip out bob work on all hair types?

A. Yes, the flip out bob works on all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q. Can I achieve a flip out bob without a flat iron?

A. While a flat iron is the easiest way to achieve a flip out bob, you can also use a round brush and blow dryer to create the same effect.

Q. How often should I style my flip out bob?

A. It's best to style your flip out bob every few days to avoid heat damage to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your flip out bob looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant product before styling.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your style.
  • Avoid using too many styling products on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

By following these tips and using the step-by-step instructions above, you'll be able to create a gorgeous flip out bob that's perfect for any occasion. With a little practice, you'll be able to style your hair like a pro!
