Short Bob Haircuts For Round Faces 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Aug 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face shape and are looking for a new haircut that will flatter your features, a short bob haircut may be the perfect choice for you. In this guide, we will cover everything you need to know about short bob haircuts for round faces in 2020, including how to choose the right style for your face shape, how to style and maintain your bob, and more.

Description

A short bob haircut is a classic hairstyle that has been popular for decades. It is a versatile style that can be customized to suit a variety of face shapes and hair textures. For round faces, a short bob can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

How to Choose the Right Bob for Your Face Shape

When choosing a short bob haircut for a round face, there are a few key factors to consider. The first is the length of the bob. A shorter bob that falls between the chin and the jawline can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

The second factor to consider is the shape of the bob. A bob with layers and texture can help to add volume and movement to the hair, which can help to create a more balanced look on a round face. Additionally, a side-swept fringe or asymmetrical cut can help to create the illusion of a longer face shape.

Step by Step Guide to Styling a Short Bob Haircut

Once you have chosen the right bob haircut for your round face, it is important to know how to style and maintain your new look. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling a short bob haircut:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair using products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, working in sections to add volume and shape to your bob.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and movement to your bob. You can also use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition to your layers.
  5. Finish with a flexible hold hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Bob Haircut

Here are a few tips to help you maintain your short bob haircut:

  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your bob looking fresh and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.

FAQ

1. Can I wear a short bob if I have a round face?

Yes! A short bob can be a great choice for round faces. The key is to choose the right length and shape to flatter your features.

2. How do I know if a short bob is right for my hair texture?

A short bob can work for a variety of hair textures, but it is important to choose a style that will work well with your natural texture. Consult with your stylist to determine the best bob for your hair type.

3. How do I style a short bob for a special occasion?

You can dress up your short bob for a special occasion by adding accessories like hair clips or headbands. You can also experiment with different updos and braids to create a more formal look.

Hair Care for Short Bob Haircuts

Keeping your short bob looking its best requires a good hair care routine. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid heat styling tools too frequently.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

In conclusion, a short bob haircut can be a great choice for round faces in 2020. By choosing the right length and shape, and following the proper styling and maintenance techniques, you can create a flattering and stylish look that will make you feel confident and beautiful.
