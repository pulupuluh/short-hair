How to Give Yourself a High and Tight Fade

The high and tight fade is a military-inspired haircut that features shaved sides and a longer top. This style is perfect for those who want a low maintenance look that is both edgy and professional. Here's how to give yourself a high and tight fade:

What You Will Need

  • Clippers with guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Towel
  • Barber cape (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to give yourself a high and tight fade:

Step 1: Start with a Clean and Dry Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and style. Use a towel to dry your hair thoroughly.

Step 2: Determine the Length of the Top

Decide how long you want the top of your hair to be. Use a comb to lift the hair up and cut it to the desired length using scissors. Make sure the top is even.

Step 3: Trim the Sides with Clippers

Use clippers with the appropriate guard to trim the sides and back of your hair. Start with the longest guard and work your way down to a shorter guard until you get the desired length. Make sure the sides and back are even.

Step 4: Create the Fade

To create the fade, switch to a shorter guard and trim the hair around the bottom of the sides and back. Gradually move the clippers up, using shorter guards as you go. Make sure to blend the hair evenly to create a smooth transition from the longer top to the shorter sides and back.

Step 5: Clean Up the Edges

Use scissors to trim any stray hairs around the edges of the cut. You can also use clippers without a guard to create a clean line around the ears and neck.

Step 6: Style the Top

Use a comb to style the top of your hair as desired. You can use a pomade or wax to add texture and hold.

Tips for a Perfect High and Tight Fade

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high and tight fade:

  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time to ensure an even and smooth cut.
  • Experiment with different guards and lengths to find the perfect look for you.
  • Clean your clippers and scissors after each use to keep them in good condition.

FAQ

Can I give myself a high and tight fade if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still give yourself a high and tight fade if you have curly hair. However, it may be more difficult to achieve a smooth transition from the longer top to the shorter sides and back. Consider seeking the help of a professional stylist if you're not confident in doing it yourself.

How often should I get a high and tight fade?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the style loses its shape. On average, you should get a high and tight fade every 2-4 weeks to maintain the look.

Can I style my hair differently with a high and tight fade?

Yes, the beauty of the high and tight fade is that it's versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the perfect look for you.

Hair Care Tips for a High and Tight Fade

Maintaining a high and tight fade requires some basic hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to avoid damaging it.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Now that you know how to give yourself a high and tight fade, it's time to go for it. Follow the steps above and don't forget to experiment with different styles to find the perfect look for you. Good luck!
