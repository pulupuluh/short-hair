How To Style Short Wavy Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Aprilia Mar 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short wavy hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can create a beautiful and effortless look. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about styling short wavy hair, including how to achieve the perfect waves, tips and tricks for styling, and hair care tips for keeping your hair healthy and shiny.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Wavy Hair
  • Tips and Tricks for Styling Short Wavy Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Wavy Hair

How to Style Short Wavy Hair

The first step in styling short wavy hair is to start with clean, dry hair. If you have naturally wavy hair, you can enhance your waves with a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, scrunching as you go. If you have straight hair, you will need to use a curling iron to create waves.

Once you have applied the product, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your waves and prevent frizz. Alternatively, you can air dry your hair and use a texturizing spray to enhance your waves.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Wavy Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling short wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a mousse or gel.
  3. Scrunch your hair as you apply the product.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair.
  5. Alternatively, air dry your hair and use a texturizing spray to enhance your waves.
  6. Use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need more definition.
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Tips and Tricks for Styling Short Wavy Hair

Here are some tips and tricks for styling short wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or blow dryer.
  • Experiment with different curling iron sizes to achieve different types of waves.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to prevent frizz.
  • Apply a texturizing spray to enhance your waves.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your waves in place without weighing them down.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short wavy hair:

Can I straighten my short wavy hair?

Yes, you can straighten your short wavy hair using a flat iron. However, keep in mind that using heat styling tools frequently can damage your hair. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat settings.

How often should I wash my short wavy hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may be able to go longer between washes. Generally, it is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care Tips for Short Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips for keeping your short wavy hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to moisturize your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your short wavy hair with ease and keep it looking healthy and shiny. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the perfect routine for your hair type and personal style.
