Best Long Layered Haircuts For Oval Faces

Written by Robby Feb 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Oval faces are the most versatile face shape when it comes to haircuts. This face shape is well-balanced and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, long layered haircuts are the best for oval faces as they accentuate the facial features and add volume to the hair. In this article, we will discuss the best long layered haircuts for oval faces in 2023.

Description

Long layered haircuts are perfect for oval faces as they can add volume to the hair and accentuate the facial features. The layers can be added to the hair to create movement and texture. This type of haircut is perfect for those who want to add volume to their hair and make it look fuller.

How to

The best way to get a long layered haircut is to visit a professional hairstylist. They will be able to assess your hair type and face shape and create a customized haircut that suits your needs. However, if you want to try it at home, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly
  2. Divide your hair into sections using hair clips
  3. Start cutting the hair in sections, starting from the back
  4. Use a comb to create the layers and cut them at an angle
  5. Repeat the process for all sections of the hair

Step by Step

Here are the step by step instructions to get the best long layered haircut for oval faces:

  1. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly
  2. Divide your hair into sections using hair clips
  3. Start cutting the hair in sections, starting from the back
  4. Use a comb to create the layers and cut them at an angle
  5. Repeat the process for all sections of the hair
  6. After cutting, use a hair dryer to style your hair
  7. Apply a hair serum to add shine to your hair

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a long layered haircut for an oval face:

  • Consult with a professional hairstylist to get the best haircut for your face shape
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools
  • Avoid using too much hair products as it can weigh down your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long layered haircuts for oval faces:

What is the best length for long layered haircuts for oval faces?

The best length for long layered haircuts for oval faces is between the shoulders and the waistline. This length will accentuate the facial features and add volume to the hair.

What is the difference between long layers and short layers?

Long layers are cut at an angle and are longer in length. Short layers are cut at a sharper angle and are shorter in length. Long layers are perfect for oval faces as they add volume and movement to the hair.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your long layered haircut looking healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends

Long layered haircuts are perfect for oval faces as they add volume and movement to the hair. Follow these tips and tricks to keep your long layered haircut looking healthy and shiny.
