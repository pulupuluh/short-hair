Why Does My Hair Flick Out On One Side

Written by Oliver Jul 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're someone who's been dealing with hair that flicks out on one side, then you're not alone. This hair problem is quite common and can be quite frustrating to deal with. In this blog post, we'll go over why your hair flicks out on one side and what you can do to prevent it from happening.

Description

When we say hair flicks out on one side, we're talking about hair that tends to curl or flip outwards on one side. This can happen to any hair type, whether curly or straight, and can be caused by a variety of factors.

How to

There are a few things you can do to help prevent your hair from flicking out on one side:

1. Blow-dry your hair in the opposite direction

One of the easiest ways to prevent your hair from flicking out on one side is to blow-dry it in the opposite direction. This means that if your hair tends to flick outwards on the right side, you should blow-dry it towards the left. This will help to train your hair to lay flat in the direction you want it to.

2. Use a flat iron

If your hair tends to flick outwards on one side, using a flat iron can help to smooth it down. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent any damage to your hair.

3. Use a hair serum

A hair serum can help to smooth down any flyaways and keep your hair looking sleek and smooth. Be sure to choose a serum that's lightweight and won't weigh down your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to prevent your hair from flicking out on one side:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant spray

If you're going to blow-dry or use a flat iron on your hair, be sure to apply a heat protectant spray first.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair in the opposite direction

Blow-dry your hair in the opposite direction of the side that tends to flick outwards. Use a round brush to help smooth down any flyaways.

Step 4: Use a flat iron

If needed, use a flat iron to further smooth down any flicked-out pieces of hair.

Step 5: Apply a hair serum

Finish off by applying a hair serum to help keep your hair looking smooth and sleek.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help prevent your hair from flicking out on one side:

1. Use a silk pillowcase

A silk pillowcase can help to prevent your hair from getting tangled and prevent any friction that can cause hair to flick out on one side.

2. Use a leave-in conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent any dryness that can cause hair to flick out.

3. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help to prevent split ends, which can cause hair to flick out on one side.

FAQ

Q: Why does my hair flick out on one side?

A: Hair can flick out on one side due to a variety of factors, including genetics, hair type, and the way you style your hair.

Q: Can using a flat iron help prevent hair from flicking out on one side?

A: Yes, using a flat iron can help to smooth down any flyaways and prevent hair from flicking out on one side. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent any damage to your hair.

Q: Can using a hair serum help prevent hair from flicking out on one side?

A: Yes, using a hair serum can help to smooth down any flyaways and keep your hair looking sleek and smooth.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important if you want to prevent it from flicking out on one side. Here are a few tips to help keep your hair healthy:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help to remove any buildup of oil and dirt that can weigh down your hair.

2. Use a conditioner

Using a conditioner can help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent any dryness that can cause hair to flick out on one side.

3. Avoid using too much heat

Using too much heat on your hair can cause damage and lead to hair that flicks out on one side. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat on your hair.

In conclusion, hair that flicks out on one side can be frustrating to deal with, but there are things you can do to prevent it from happening. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this blog post, you can keep your hair looking sleek and smooth.
