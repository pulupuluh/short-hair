Easy Care Short Haircuts For Wavy Hair

Written by Petter May 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have wavy hair, you know how difficult it can be to find a haircut that looks good and is easy to maintain. Short haircuts are a great option for those with wavy hair, as they can help to control frizz and provide a sleek, polished look. In this article, we will discuss some easy care short haircuts for wavy hair that you can try out.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short haircuts for wavy hair are perfect for those who want to achieve a low-maintenance look. With the right cut, you can easily style your hair in just a few minutes each day. Short haircuts can also help to enhance the natural texture of your hair, making it easier to style and manage.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for wavy hair, it is important to consider your face shape and hair texture. A good stylist will be able to recommend a cut that will flatter your face and work well with your hair type. You can also bring in pictures of haircuts that you like to help give your stylist an idea of what you are looking for.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.

3. Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to your hair.

4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to gently dry your hair.

5. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

- Try a layered haircut to add volume and texture to your hair.

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

- Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: Will a short haircut make my wavy hair look frizzy?

A: Not necessarily. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can achieve a sleek, polished look.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. Try a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style.

Hair Care

When caring for your wavy hair, it is important to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are moisturizing and sulfate-free. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Overall, there are many easy care short haircuts for wavy hair that you can try out. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can achieve a low-maintenance look that is both stylish and practical.
