Is It Bad To Wax Facial Hair

Written by Petter Aug 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Facial hair is a common problem for many women. While some women choose to embrace their facial hair, others may choose to remove it. Waxing is a popular method of hair removal, but is it bad to wax facial hair?

Description

Waxing involves applying hot wax to the skin and then removing it quickly, taking the hair with it. While this method can be effective for removing facial hair, it can also cause some negative side effects.

How to Wax Facial Hair

Waxing facial hair can be done at home or at a salon. If you choose to do it at home, you will need to purchase a waxing kit that includes wax, strips, and an applicator. Follow these steps to wax your facial hair:

Step 1: Clean the area

Before waxing, clean the area thoroughly. Use a gentle cleanser to remove any dirt or oil. Dry the area completely.

Step 2: Apply the wax

Apply the wax to the area in the direction of hair growth. Be sure to apply a thin layer, as a thick layer can make it more difficult to remove the hair.

Step 3: Place the strip

Place the strip over the wax and press down firmly. Smooth the strip in the direction of hair growth.

Step 4: Remove the strip

Hold the skin taut and quickly remove the strip in the opposite direction of hair growth. Repeat as necessary.

Tips for Waxing Facial Hair

If you choose to wax your facial hair, here are some tips to help make the process easier and more effective:

  • Exfoliate the area before waxing to help remove dead skin cells and prevent ingrown hairs.
  • Wait until your hair is at least 1/4 inch long before waxing, as shorter hair may not be removed effectively.
  • Apply a cold compress to the area after waxing to help reduce redness and inflammation.
  • Avoid sun exposure or tanning for at least 24 hours after waxing.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about waxing facial hair:

Is it bad to wax facial hair?

Waxing facial hair can cause some negative side effects, such as redness, irritation, and ingrown hairs. However, if done correctly, it can be an effective method of hair removal.

How often should I wax my facial hair?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows. Typically, facial hair should be waxed every 4-6 weeks.

What should I do if I experience redness or irritation after waxing?

If you experience redness or irritation after waxing, apply a cold compress to the area and avoid touching or picking at the skin. You can also apply aloe vera gel to help soothe the skin.

Hair Care

After waxing your facial hair, it is important to take care of your skin to prevent further irritation. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using harsh or fragranced products on the area.
  • Moisturize the area regularly to help prevent dryness.
  • Avoid touching or picking at the skin.
  • Use sunscreen to protect the skin from sun damage.

Overall, while waxing facial hair can cause some negative side effects, it can be an effective method of hair removal. By following the tips and taking care of your skin, you can help minimize the negative effects and enjoy smooth, hair-free skin.
