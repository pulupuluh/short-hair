What Length Hair For Oblong Face A Guide For 2023

Are you wondering what length hair would suit your oblong face shape? You're not alone! Many people with an oblong face struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters their features. In this guide, we'll explore what length hair is best for an oblong face and provide you with some helpful tips and tricks to achieve your desired look.

Table of Contents

  • How To Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long, narrow face with a high forehead, prominent chin, and straight cheekbones. If you have an oblong face, you want to choose a hairstyle that will balance out your features and create the illusion of a more oval face shape. This can be achieved by selecting a hairstyle that adds width to the sides of your face and softens your features.

How To Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the perfect hairstyle for your oblong face, you need to determine your face shape. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back off your face with a headband or clip.
  2. Take a lipstick or marker and trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you've drawn.
  4. Compare your shape to the following face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and oblong.

If your face is longer than it is wide, with a high forehead and straight cheekbones, you have an oblong face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know you have an oblong face shape, it's time to choose the perfect hairstyle. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that adds width to the sides of your face.
  2. Consider a layered haircut, as this will add volume and movement to your hair.
  3. Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they will make your face look even longer.
  4. Try a shoulder-length or chin-length bob, as this will create the illusion of a wider face.
  5. If you prefer longer hair, add some layers around your face to soften your features.
  6. Avoid straight, sleek hairstyles, as they will only emphasize the length of your face.
  7. Consider adding some waves or curls to your hair, as this will add volume to the sides of your face.
  8. Choose a side part rather than a center part, as this will create the illusion of a wider face.
  9. If you want to wear your hair up, try a messy bun or a side ponytail to add width to your face.
  10. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find the perfect look for you!

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they will emphasize the length of your face.
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to your hair to create dimension and add width to your face.
  • Try a side-swept fringe or bangs to add width to your forehead.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous on top, as they will make your face look even longer.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personal style and suits your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have when choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face shape:

Q: Can I wear my hair long if I have an oblong face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair long, but it's best to add some layers around your face to soften your features and create the illusion of a wider face.

Q: What is the best hairstyle for an oblong face with curly hair?

A: A shoulder-length or chin-length bob with layers is a great choice for oblong faces with curly hair, as it will add volume and movement to your hair while balancing out your features.

Q: Can I wear bangs with an oblong face shape?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs or a fringe can be a great choice for oblong faces, as they will add width to your forehead and balance out your features.

Hair Care Tips for Oblong Faces

When it comes to caring for your hair as an oblong face shape, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage and make your hair look flat and lifeless.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and movement to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush that will help create volume and add shape to your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your hair looking its best.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to choose the perfect hairstyle for your oblong face shape and feel confident and beautiful every day!
