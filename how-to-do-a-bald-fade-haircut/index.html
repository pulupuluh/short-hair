How To Do A Bald Fade Haircut Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Feb 20, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking to achieve a clean and sharp look with your hair? The bald fade haircut is a popular style that is easy to maintain and can be customized to suit your preferences. In this guide, we will take you through a step-by-step process on how to do a bald fade haircut.

How to Do a Bald Fade Haircut

The bald fade haircut is a style where the hair gradually fades from a longer length at the top to a shorter length at the sides and back, eventually blending into the skin. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Electric clippers
  • Clipper guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Barber cape or towel

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose the Length

Decide on the length of hair you want to keep on the top of your head. Use a clipper guard to trim the hair to your desired length. For a bald fade, the hair should be longer at the top and gradually shorter towards the sides and back.

Step 2: Create a Guideline

Using the longest clipper guard, create a guideline around the head by trimming the hair around the temples and sideburns. This will serve as a reference point for the fade.

Step 3: Fade the Sides

Switch to a shorter clipper guard and start fading the sides by trimming the hair in an upward motion, starting from the guideline and working your way down. Gradually switch to shorter clipper guards until the hair blends into the skin.

Step 4: Blend the Top

Use scissors to blend the top section of hair with the faded sides. Use a comb to lift the hair and trim any uneven parts to achieve a smooth blend.

Step 5: Clean Up the Neckline

Trim the hair around the neckline and ear area to create a clean and sharp look. Use a razor or trimmer to create a defined line.

Tips for a Perfect Bald Fade Haircut

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one to achieve a natural-looking fade.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and cut in a straight line to avoid uneven cuts.
  • Take your time and be patient with the process.
  • Practice makes perfect. Keep practicing to improve your skills.

FAQs

What is a bald fade haircut?

A bald fade haircut is a style where the hair gradually fades from a longer length at the top to a shorter length at the sides and back, eventually blending into the skin.

What equipment do I need to do a bald fade haircut?

You will need electric clippers, clipper guards, scissors, a comb, and a barber cape or towel.

Can I do a bald fade haircut on my own?

It is possible to do a bald fade haircut on your own, but it is recommended to seek the help of a professional barber for the best results.

Hair Care Tips for a Bald Fade Haircut

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly to prevent dryness and flakiness.
  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups and maintenance.

Now that you have a step-by-step guide on how to do a bald fade haircut, you can achieve a clean and sharp look with ease. Remember to take your time and be patient with the process, and you’ll be sure to achieve great results. Happy styling!
