How To Use Thinning Shears On Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thinning shears are an essential tool for anyone who wants to add texture and volume to their hair. They are particularly useful when it comes to trimming bangs, as they allow you to create a softer, more natural look without sacrificing length. In this article, we'll show you how to use thinning shears on bangs in a few simple steps.

How to Use Thinning Shears on Bangs

Before you begin, make sure you have a good pair of thinning shears that are sharp and in good condition. You'll also need a comb, some hair clips, and a steady hand.

Step 1: Prepare Your Bangs

Start by washing and drying your hair as you normally would. When your hair is dry, use a comb to separate your bangs from the rest of your hair. Clip the rest of your hair back so it doesn't get in the way.

Step 2: Determine How Much Hair to Cut

Decide how much hair you want to cut off your bangs. Thinning shears are designed to remove some of the hair without cutting it all the way through, so you'll need to take this into account when deciding how much to cut. As a general rule, it's best to start with a small amount and work your way up.

Step 3: Use the Thinning Shears

Hold the thinning shears at a slight angle and begin cutting into your bangs. Start at the ends and work your way up, cutting small sections at a time. Be careful not to cut too much at once, as this can lead to uneven results. Keep checking your progress in the mirror to make sure you're happy with the shape and length of your bangs.

Step 4: Blend the Ends

Once you've cut all the way through your bangs, use the thinning shears to blend the ends. Hold the shears at a slight angle and run them over the ends of your bangs, taking care not to cut too much off. This will help to create a softer, more natural look.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Now that you've thinned out your bangs, it's time to style them. Use a brush or your fingers to tousle your bangs and create the look you want. You can also use a styling product to add volume and hold if desired.

Tips for Using Thinning Shears on Bangs

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when using thinning shears on your bangs:

  • Start with a small amount of hair and work your way up
  • Be careful not to cut too much at once
  • Check your progress in the mirror frequently
  • Hold the shears at a slight angle for the best results
  • Blend the ends for a softer, more natural look

FAQ

Can I use regular scissors to thin out my bangs?

While you can use regular scissors to trim your bangs, thinning shears are designed specifically for this purpose and will give you better results.

Can I use thinning shears on other parts of my hair?

Yes, you can use thinning shears on other parts of your hair to add texture and volume. However, it's best to leave this to a professional stylist unless you have experience using thinning shears.

Hair Care Tips

Thinning out your bangs can be a great way to freshen up your look, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

By following these tips and using thinning shears correctly, you can achieve a stylish and natural-looking set of bangs that will enhance your overall look.
