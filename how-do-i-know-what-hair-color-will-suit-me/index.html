How Do I Know What Hair Color Will Suit Me

Written by Daniel Apr 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing a hair color can be quite challenging, especially if you are not sure which color will suit you the best. There are many factors you need to consider before making a decision, such as your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to know what hair color will suit you the best, giving you tips and tricks to help you make the right decision.

How To Determine Your Skin Tone?

Your skin tone is an essential factor in choosing the right hair color. There are three categories of skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins in your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones, if they appear green, you have warm undertones, and if they are a mix of both, you have neutral undertones.

Warm Undertones

If you have warm undertones, you will look best in hair colors that have golden, red, or copper tones. These colors will bring out the warmth in your skin and make you look more vibrant. Shades like golden blonde, copper brown, and auburn are perfect for you.

Cool Undertones

If you have cool undertones, you will look best in hair colors that have ashy, cool, or blue-based tones. These colors will complement your skin and make you look more radiant. Shades like platinum blonde, ash brown, and burgundy are perfect for you.

Neutral Undertones

If you have neutral undertones, you can pull off both warm and cool hair colors. You can experiment with different shades and find the one that suits you best.

Step By Step Guide To Choosing The Right Hair Color

Now that you know your skin tone, you can follow these steps to choose the right hair color:

Step 1: Determine Your Natural Hair Color

If you have never dyed your hair before, determine your natural hair color. This will help you choose a color that suits you the best.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also affect how certain hair colors look on you. For example, if you have blue eyes, warm hair colors like golden blonde and copper brown will make your eyes pop.

Step 3: Determine Your Skin Tone

Determine your skin tone following the steps mentioned above.

Step 4: Choose A Hair Color That Complements Your Skin Tone

Based on your skin tone, choose a hair color that complements it. If you have warm undertones, choose warm hair colors, and if you have cool undertones, choose cool hair colors.

Tips To Keep In Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Don't go too far from your natural hair color if you are not sure about the change.
  • Consider the maintenance and upkeep of the hair color you choose.
  • Choose a hair color that suits your lifestyle and personality.
  • Ask your hairstylist for advice.

FAQ

What should I do if I am still not sure?

If you are still not sure, ask your hairstylist for advice. They can help you choose a hair color that suits you the best.

Can I change my hair color if I don't like it?

Yes, you can change your hair color if you don't like it. However, keep in mind that frequent hair dyeing can damage your hair.

Hair Care Tips

After dyeing your hair, it is essential to take care of it to maintain its color and health. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when going out in the sun.

Choosing the right hair color can make a significant difference in your overall appearance. By following the steps mentioned in this article and keeping the tips in mind, you can find the perfect hair color that suits you the best.
