Should I Use Mousse Or Gel On Curly Hair

Written by Petter Aug 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to manage, but it can also be beautiful and full of life. However, with so many styling products on the market, it can be difficult to know which one to choose. In this article, we will explore whether mousse or gel is the best option for styling curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Mousse and gel are both popular styling products for curly hair. Mousse is a lightweight foam that adds volume and hold to hair, while gel is a thicker, more concentrated product that provides more hold and definition. Both products can be used to enhance curls and reduce frizz, but they work differently and have different benefits and drawbacks.

How to

The best way to determine whether mousse or gel is right for your curly hair is to experiment with both products. Start by applying a small amount of mousse to damp hair and scrunching it into your curls. If you want more hold, you can apply more mousse, but be careful not to apply too much, as this can make your hair sticky and crunchy.

If you prefer a stronger hold or more definition, you can try using gel instead. Apply a small amount of gel to damp hair and use your fingers to distribute it evenly. Then, scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation. You can also use a diffuser to dry your hair, which can help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for using mousse and gel on curly hair:

Using Mousse

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel-dry your hair to remove excess moisture.
  3. Dispense a small amount of mousse into your palm.
  4. Flip your head upside down and scrunch the mousse into your hair.
  5. Flip your head back up and use your fingers to scrunch your hair again.
  6. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser to dry it.

Using Gel

  1. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel-dry your hair to remove excess moisture.
  3. Dispense a small amount of gel into your palm.
  4. Use your fingers to distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair.
  5. Scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation.
  6. Use a diffuser to dry your hair or allow it to air dry.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when using mousse or gel on curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help prevent dryness and damage.
  • Apply styling products to damp hair for best results.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while it's drying, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or T-shirt to dry your hair, as these materials are gentler on curls than regular towels.
  • Experiment with different amounts of product to find the right balance for your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using mousse and gel on curly hair:

Can I use mousse and gel together on curly hair?

Yes, you can use mousse and gel together to create a customized styling routine for your curly hair. Start by applying a small amount of mousse to damp hair and then follow up with a small amount of gel. Scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation, and then allow it to air dry or use a diffuser to dry it.

Can mousse and gel cause buildup on curly hair?

Yes, mousse and gel can cause buildup on curly hair if they are not properly washed out. To prevent buildup, make sure to use a clarifying shampoo regularly to remove any product residue.

How often should I use mousse or gel on curly hair?

It's best to use styling products like mousse or gel on curly hair as needed, rather than every day. Overuse of these products can cause buildup and weigh down curls, so it's important to find the right balance for your hair.

Hair Care

In addition to using the right styling products, it's important to take good care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can cause damage to curly hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment regularly to hydrate and nourish your hair.

By following these tips and using the right styling products, you can achieve beautiful, healthy curls that you'll love to show off.
