Best Haircuts For Thick Wavy Hair Square Face

If you have a square face shape with thick wavy hair, it can be challenging to find the perfect haircut that flatters your features. The goal is to soften the angles of your face and enhance your natural waves. In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for thick wavy hair square face, including how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Haircuts for Thick Wavy Hair Square Face

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a haircut, it's essential to determine your face shape. If you have a square face shape, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width. Your jawline has a sharp angle, and your face is relatively short.

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Take a close look at the shape of your face and measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are all about the same width, you have a square face shape.

Best Haircuts for Thick Wavy Hair Square Face

The best haircuts for thick wavy hair square face are those that add softness to your features and enhance your natural waves. Here are some of the best haircuts for square faces with thick wavy hair:

Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for square faces with thick wavy hair. The layers add movement and softness to your hair, framing your face beautifully. You can wear your hair straight or style it with loose waves.

Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob is another great option for square faces with thick wavy hair. The length of the bob falls just below your chin, which helps to soften the angles of your jawline. You can wear your hair straight or style it with loose waves.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are an excellent addition to any haircut for square faces with thick wavy hair. The bangs add softness to your forehead and help to balance out your features. You can wear your hair straight or style it with loose waves.

How to Style Your Hair

Once you have chosen your haircut, it's essential to know how to style your hair. Here are some tips for styling thick wavy hair:

  • Apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair and blow-dry with a round brush.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves.
  • Apply a texturizing spray to add definition and hold to your waves.
  • Use a light hairspray to set your style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your thick wavy hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a square face with thick wavy hair?

The best haircuts for square faces with thick wavy hair are long layers, shoulder-length bob, and side-swept bangs.

How do I style my thick wavy hair?

You can style your thick wavy hair by applying a volumizing mousse, blow-drying with a round brush, and using a curling iron or wand to create loose waves.

What can I do to maintain my thick wavy hair?

To maintain your thick wavy hair, wash it every other day, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoid using hot tools too often.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for your thick wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to keep them moisturized.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut, you can enhance your natural waves and soften the angles of your square face shape. Experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
