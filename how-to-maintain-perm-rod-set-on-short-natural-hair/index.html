How To Maintain Perm Rod Set On Short Natural Hair

Written by Christine Aug 28, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’re looking for a way to jazz up your short natural hair, a perm rod set can be a great option. Not only does it give your hair volume and definition, it’s also a low-maintenance style that lasts for a few days. However, to maintain your perm rod set, you’ll need to follow a few tips and tricks that we’ll be discussing in this article.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A perm rod set is a styling technique that involves rolling small sections of hair around perm rods and allowing them to dry. This creates defined curls and waves that can last for a few days. This style works best on short natural hair, as longer hair can be more difficult to roll around the rods.

How to

To maintain your perm rod set, you’ll need to follow a few steps. Firstly, you should avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and disrupt the curl pattern. Secondly, you should sleep with a satin bonnet or scarf to protect your curls while you sleep. Finally, you should refresh your curls each day by using a moisturizing spray and re-rolling any sections that have lost their shape.

Step by step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to maintain your perm rod set:

  1. Avoid touching your hair too much.
  2. Sleep with a satin bonnet or scarf.
  3. Refresh your curls each day with a moisturizing spray.
  4. Re-roll any sections that have lost their shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your perm rod set:

  • Use a lightweight, moisturizing product to set your hair.
  • Don’t use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to avoid frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about maintaining a perm rod set on short natural hair:

How long does a perm rod set last?

A perm rod set can last for a few days, depending on how well you maintain it.

Can I use a hair oil on my perm rod set?

Yes, you can use a hair oil on your perm rod set to add shine and moisture.

How do I prevent frizz on my perm rod set?

To prevent frizz, you should avoid touching your hair too much and use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, you should follow a few hair care tips. Firstly, you should wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Secondly, you should deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized. Finally, you should trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, maintaining a perm rod set on short natural hair requires a few simple steps, such as avoiding touching your hair too much and sleeping with a satin bonnet or scarf. With these tips and tricks, you can keep your curls defined and beautiful for days.
