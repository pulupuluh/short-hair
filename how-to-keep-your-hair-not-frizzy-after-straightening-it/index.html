How To Keep Your Hair Not Frizzy After Straightening It

Written by Luffy Jul 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of straightening your hair only to end up with a frizzy mess? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with keeping their hair smooth and straight after straightening it. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you keep your hair looking sleek and frizz-free.

  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Keeping Your Hair Not Frizzy After Straightening It

How to Keep Your Hair Not Frizzy After Straightening It

The key to keeping your hair smooth and frizz-free after straightening it is to use the right products and techniques. Below are some tips to help you achieve this:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

Before straightening your hair, it's essential to use a heat protectant spray or serum. This will help protect your hair from heat damage and prevent frizz.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair Before Straightening It

Blow-drying your hair before straightening it can help reduce frizz. This is because blow-drying can help smooth out the hair cuticle and prepare it for straightening.

3. Use the Right Temperature

Using the right temperature for your hair type is crucial. Fine hair requires lower heat settings, while thick, coarse hair can handle higher heat. Using the wrong temperature can lead to heat damage and frizz.

4. Use a Quality Straightener

Investing in a quality straightener can make a significant difference in your hair's appearance. Look for a straightener that has ceramic plates and temperature control settings.

5. Don't Overdo It

Straightening your hair every day can cause damage and frizz. Try to limit straightening to once or twice a week.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you keep your hair frizz-free after straightening it:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair before blow-drying it.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. This will help smooth out your hair and prepare it for straightening.

Step 4: Straighten your hair

Straighten your hair using a quality straightener. Use the right temperature setting for your hair type.

Step 5: Apply an anti-frizz serum

Apply an anti-frizz serum to your hair after straightening it. This will help keep your hair smooth and frizz-free.

Tips for Keeping Your Hair Not Frizzy After Straightening It

Here are some additional tips to help you keep your hair looking sleek after straightening it:

1. Use a silk pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help reduce frizz by preventing your hair from rubbing against a rough surface.

2. Avoid touching your hair

Avoid touching your hair throughout the day. This can cause frizz and disrupt the shape of your hairstyle.

3. Apply a hair oil

Apply a hair oil to the ends of your hair to help keep them hydrated and prevent frizz.

4. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use hairspray to prevent frizz?

A: Yes, you can use hairspray to help keep your hair in place and prevent frizz. However, be careful not to use too much as it can make your hair stiff and crunchy.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go several days without washing it. Try to find a washing frequency that works for your hair type and lifestyle.

Hair Care Tips for Frizz-Free Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you keep your hair frizz-free:

1. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner

Using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner can help keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.

2. Avoid using hot tools too often

Using hot tools too often can cause damage and frizz. Try to limit your use of hot tools to once or twice a week.

3. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

4. Drink plenty of water

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair looking sleek and frizz-free after straightening it. Remember to use the right products and techniques, and take good care of your hair.
