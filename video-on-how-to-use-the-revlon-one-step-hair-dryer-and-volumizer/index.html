How To Use The Revlon One Step Hair Dryer And Volumizer A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a game-changing hair styling tool that can help you achieve salon-worthy results at home, the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer might just be what you need. This innovative hair dryer and volumizer in one can help you achieve a smooth, frizz-free blowout with tons of volume and body in just one step. But how do you use it properly? In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to use the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer, along with some tips and FAQs to help you get the most out of this amazing tool.

Description

The Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is a unique hair styling tool that combines a hair dryer and a round brush in one. It features a ceramic coating and ionic technology that helps reduce frizz and static, while also adding shine to your hair. The oval brush design helps create volume and body, while the multiple heat and speed settings allow you to customize your styling experience depending on your hair type and texture.

How to Use

Step 1: Prep your hair

Before using the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer, make sure your hair is clean and free from tangles. You can either air-dry your hair or towel-dry it until it's damp, but not soaking wet. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start with the bottom layers and work your way up. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Turn on the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer

Plug in your Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer and choose your desired heat and speed settings. If you have fine or thin hair, use a lower heat and speed setting. If you have thick or coarse hair, use a higher heat and speed setting. Wait for the tool to heat up before using it.

Step 4: Start styling

Take a section of hair and place the brush underneath it. Use your other hand to hold the hair taut while you glide the brush through your hair from the root to the tip. Rotate the brush as you go to create volume and body. Repeat this process with each section of hair until your entire head is styled.

Step 5: Finish with a cool shot

Once you've styled your hair, use the cool shot button to set the style and add extra shine. Hold the cool shot button for a few seconds before releasing it.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the heat
  • Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to style
  • Start with the bottom layers and work your way up
  • Use a lower heat and speed setting if you have fine or thin hair
  • Use a higher heat and speed setting if you have thick or coarse hair
  • Rotate the brush as you go to create volume and body
  • Use the cool shot button to set the style and add extra shine

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer:

Q: Can I use the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on wet hair?

A: Yes, you can use the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on wet hair. However, it's recommended that you towel-dry your hair first to remove excess moisture.

Q: How do I clean my Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer?

A: To clean your Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer, unplug it and let it cool down completely. Use a soft, damp cloth to wipe the brush head and body. Do not immerse the tool in water or other liquids.

Q: Can I use the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on all hair types?

A: Yes, the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer can be used on all hair types, but you should adjust the heat and speed settings depending on your hair texture and thickness.

Hair Care

Using the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer can be a game-changer for your hair styling routine, but it's also important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Make sure to use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. Regular trims can also help keep your hair in top shape.
