What Hair Length Is Best For Fat Faces

Written by Oliver May 22, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Having a fat face can make it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that flatters your face shape. You want a hairstyle that slims down your face and highlights your best features. So, what hair length is best for fat faces? In this blog post, we will explore different hair lengths that can enhance your appearance and make you feel confident.

Description

People with round or chubby faces often struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that suits their face shape. They want a hairstyle that accentuates their facial features and makes their face look slimmer. Choosing the right hair length is crucial in achieving this goal. Short, medium, and long hair lengths can all work with a fat face, given that you choose the right style that complements your face shape and features.

How to Choose the Right Hair Length for Fat Faces

Choosing a hairstyle that suits your face shape requires some consideration. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair length for your fat face:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, determine your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and examining your face's shape in the mirror. If your face is round, you have a fat face, and you should choose a hairstyle that adds height and elongates your face.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture plays a significant role in determining the right hair length for your face shape. If you have thick hair, a short hairstyle can add volume to your face, making it look even more round. On the other hand, thin hair can benefit from a medium or long hairstyle that adds volume and texture to your hair.

3. Look for Hairstyles that Slim Down Your Face

Hairstyles that slim down your face include layered haircuts, side-swept bangs, and hairstyles that add height to your crown. Avoid blunt cuts and center-parted hairstyles that can make your face look rounder.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Length for Fat Faces

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right hair length for your fat face:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If these measurements are the same or nearly the same, you have a round face shape.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Determine your hair texture by examining your hair's thickness and texture. If your hair is thick, a short hairstyle can add volume to your face, making it look even more round. On the other hand, thin hair can benefit from a medium or long hairstyle that adds volume and texture to your hair.

3. Look for Hairstyles that Slim Down Your Face

Hairstyles that slim down your face include layered haircuts, side-swept bangs, and hairstyles that add height to your crown. Avoid blunt cuts and center-parted hairstyles that can make your face look rounder.

Tips for Styling Hair for Fat Faces

Here are some tips to help you style your hair for your fat face:

1. Add Volume to Your Crown

Adding volume to your crown can elongate your face and make it look slimmer. Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add volume to your hair's roots.

2. Use Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can draw attention away from your round face and make it look slimmer. Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face look even rounder.

3. Avoid Tight Updos

Tight updos can make your face look rounder. Opt for loose, messy updos that add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQs

Q. Can Short Hair Look Good on a Fat Face?

A. Yes, short hair can look good on a fat face if you choose the right style that suits your face shape and features. Layered bobs, pixie cuts, and asymmetrical haircuts can all work with a fat face.

Q. Can Long Hair Make a Fat Face Look Slimmer?

A. Yes, long hair can make a fat face look slimmer if you choose the right hairstyle that adds height and volume to your hair. Layers, waves, and curls can all add texture and volume to your hair.

Hair Care for Fat Faces

Proper hair care is essential in maintaining healthy hair and preventing hair damage. Here are some tips for hair care for fat faces:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Avoid shampoos that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.

2. Condition Your Hair

Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny. Apply conditioner to the ends of your hair and avoid applying it to your scalp.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling tools such as blow dryers, flat irons, and curling irons as they can damage your hair. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

4. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Schedule a haircut every six to eight weeks.

Choosing the right hair length for your fat face can enhance your appearance and make you feel confident. Short, medium, and long hair lengths can all work with a fat face, given that you choose the right style that complements your face shape and features. Follow these tips and tricks, and you'll have a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features.
