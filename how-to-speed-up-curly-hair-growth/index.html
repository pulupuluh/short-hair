How To Speed Up Curly Hair Growth Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jul 06, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of waiting for your curly hair to grow longer? Do you wish you could speed up the process? Well, the good news is that you can! With the right hair care routine and a few lifestyle changes, you can promote healthy hair growth and get those luscious curls you’ve been dreaming of. In this article, we’ll discuss how to speed up curly hair growth and provide you with some useful tips and tricks to help you along the way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Speed Up Curly Hair Growth
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Faster Hair Growth
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair Growth

How to Speed Up Curly Hair Growth

Curly hair tends to grow slower than straight hair due to its texture and shape. However, there are several things you can do to speed up the process and promote healthy hair growth. Here are some tips:

Eat a Balanced Diet

Your diet plays a significant role in the health of your hair. Eating a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals can help promote hair growth. Some foods that are good for hair growth include salmon, eggs, nuts, leafy greens, and sweet potatoes.

Take Hair Growth Supplements

If you’re not getting enough nutrients from your diet, you can take hair growth supplements to help promote hair growth. Biotin, vitamin D, and iron are some of the most popular supplements for hair growth.

Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help stimulate blood flow to the hair follicles, which can promote hair growth. Use your fingers to massage your scalp for a few minutes every day.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and slow down hair growth. Try to avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to speed up curly hair growth:

  1. Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  2. Take hair growth supplements if necessary.
  3. Massage your scalp for a few minutes every day.
  4. Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  5. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  6. Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair.
  7. Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.
  8. Get regular hair trims to prevent split ends.
  9. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  10. Be patient and consistent with your hair care routine.

Tips for Faster Hair Growth

Here are some additional tips for faster hair growth:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dyes.
  • Get enough sleep to promote overall health and wellbeing.
  • Reduce stress levels, as stress can impact hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as they can damage your hair.

FAQ

How long does it take for curly hair to grow?

The average rate of hair growth is about half an inch per month. However, curly hair may grow slower due to its texture and shape.

Can hair growth be accelerated?

Yes, hair growth can be accelerated by following a healthy hair care routine and lifestyle changes.

What are some good hair growth supplements?

Biotin, vitamin D, and iron are some of the most popular supplements for hair growth.

Hair Care for Curly Hair Growth

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as it can cause breakage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and frizz-free.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair while you sleep by using a satin or silk pillowcase.

By following these tips and tricks, you can speed up curly hair growth and achieve the luscious curls you’ve been dreaming of. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you’ll see results in no time.
