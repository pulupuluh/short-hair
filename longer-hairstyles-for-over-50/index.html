Longer Hairstyles For Over 50 How To Rock Your Locks In Style

Written by Bardi Jun 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for a new hairstyle that will give you a fresh, youthful look?

If so, then you're in luck! In this article, we'll be discussing some of the best longer hairstyles for women over 50. From classic looks to modern styles, we've got you covered!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Longer hairstyles are a great option for women over 50, as they can help to add volume and texture to your hair. Plus, they're easy to maintain and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your individual taste.

Whether you prefer a sleek and sophisticated look or something more playful and tousled, there's a longer hairstyle out there that will work for you. And the best part? Many of these styles are low-maintenance and can be worn on a day-to-day basis without much fuss.

How to

The key to rocking a longer hairstyle over 50 is to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type. Some styles may look great on one person but not on another, so it's important to experiment with different looks until you find one that works for you.

When it comes to styling longer hair, there are a few tips to keep in mind. First, invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your locks healthy and shiny. Second, use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair gently, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Finally, consider using a heat protectant spray if you plan on using hot styling tools like a curling iron or straightener.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for a few popular longer hairstyles:

Beachy Waves

  1. Wash and condition your hair using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Braid your hair while it's still damp and allow it to air dry.
  3. Once your hair is dry, take out the braids and use your fingers to tousle your hair and create loose waves.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Sleek and Straight

  1. Wash and condition your hair using a smoothing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair straight using a round brush.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten any remaining frizz or waves.
  4. Finish with a shine spray to add gloss and smoothness.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when styling longer hair:

  • Invest in regular trims to keep split ends at bay and maintain the health of your hair.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add movement and texture.
  • Try out different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and looks until you find one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

FAQ

What are some good longer hairstyles for women over 50?

Some popular longer hairstyles for women over 50 include layered cuts, beachy waves, and sleek and straight styles.

How do I know which longer hairstyle will suit me?

The best way to find out which longer hairstyle will suit you is to experiment with different looks and consult with a hairstylist who can offer personalized advice based on your face shape and hair type.

How can I keep my longer hair healthy and shiny?

To keep your longer hair healthy and shiny, use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, invest in regular trims, and avoid using hot styling tools too often.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for longer hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type. Second, avoid using hot styling tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair over time. Finally, be sure to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.

With these tips and tricks in mind, you'll be able to rock your longer hair in style no matter your age or hair type. So go ahead and experiment with different looks until you find one that makes you feel confident and beautiful â you deserve it!
