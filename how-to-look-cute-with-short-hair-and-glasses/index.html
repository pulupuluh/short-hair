How To Look Cute With Short Hair And Glasses

Written by William Jun 01, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have short hair and wear glasses, you may think that it's challenging to look cute and stylish. However, with a few tips and tricks, you can rock this look and feel confident in your appearance.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair and glasses are a trendy and practical combination. However, styling short hair can be tricky, and glasses can sometimes dominate the face, making it hard to look cute. Fortunately, there are many ways to enhance your features, make your glasses work for you, and show off your short hair's unique style.

How to

Here are some tips on how to look cute with short hair and glasses:

1. Choose the right glasses

The first step in looking cute with short hair and glasses is selecting the right frames. Choose glasses that match your face shape, complement your hair, and highlight your best features. If you have a round face, opt for angular frames. If you have a square face, try rounded or oval frames. If you have short hair, pick glasses that are not too heavy or thick, as they may overwhelm your face.

2. Consider your hair texture

Your hair texture can affect your glasses' appearance and how cute you look. If you have straight hair, consider adding some layers to add volume and movement to your hair. If you have curly hair, keep your hair hydrated and use a styling product to define your curls. If you have wavy hair, go for a bob or pixie cut that will show off your hair's texture.

3. Experiment with accessories

Accessories can enhance your short hair and glasses' look and add some personality to your outfit. Try a cute headband, a statement necklace, or a pair of bold earrings to draw attention to your face and glasses.

4. Play with makeup

Makeup can also help you look cute with short hair and glasses. Consider wearing bright lipstick, bold eyeliner, or a pop of color on your cheeks. These makeup techniques will make your features stand out and complement your glasses.

Step by Step

Follow these steps to achieve a cute and stylish look with short hair and glasses:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some body to your hair. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and movement.

Step 2: Style your hair

Use a styling product to add texture and hold to your hair. If you have straight hair, use a texturizing spray or a curling iron to add some waves. If you have curly hair, use a hair oil or cream to define your curls. If you have wavy hair, use a pomade or wax to create a messy look.

Step 3: Put on your glasses

Put on your glasses and adjust them to fit your face comfortably. Make sure that the glasses' frames are not too heavy or thick, as they may overwhelm your face.

Step 4: Add some accessories

Add some accessories to complete your look. Try a headband, a necklace, or a pair of earrings that complement your glasses and hair.

Step 5: Apply makeup

Apply makeup that highlights your features and complements your glasses. Try a bright lipstick, a bold eyeliner, or a pop of color on your cheeks.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you look cute with short hair and glasses:

  • Choose glasses that match your personal style and complement your hair color.
  • Use hair accessories, such as bobby pins or hair clips, to create different hairstyles.
  • Experiment with different makeup looks to find what works best for you.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine to your hair and reduce frizz.
  • Try a layered haircut to add volume and movement to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear glasses with short hair?

A: Yes, you can wear glasses with short hair. In fact, short hair and glasses are a trendy and practical combination.

Q: How can I choose the right glasses for my face?

A: To choose the right glasses for your face, consider your face shape, hair color, and personal style. Try on different frames to see what looks best on you.

Q: How can I make my glasses look cute?

A: You can make your glasses look cute by choosing frames that complement your face shape and hair color, adding some accessories, and wearing makeup that highlights your features.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy and stylish. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and add shine.

By following these tips and tricks, you can look cute and stylish with short hair and glasses. With the right glasses, hair care routine, and accessories, you can rock this look and feel confident in your appearance.
