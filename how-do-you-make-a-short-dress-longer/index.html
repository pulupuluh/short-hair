How To Make A Short Dress Longer Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of having to constantly pull down your short dress? Do you wish it was just a few inches longer? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we'll show you how to make a short dress longer in a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make a Short Dress Longer
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Making a Short Dress Longer
  • FAQs

How to Make a Short Dress Longer

There are several ways to make a short dress longer, including:

  • Add a slip or petticoat underneath
  • Add a lace or crochet trim to the bottom
  • Attach a piece of matching fabric to the hemline
  • Wear leggings or tights underneath
  • Accessorize with knee-high boots or ankle booties

Depending on the style of your dress, some methods may work better than others. Let's dive into a step-by-step guide on how to make a short dress longer.

Step by Step Guide

1. Choose your method

Decide which method you want to use to make your dress longer. Do you want to add a slip or petticoat underneath? Or maybe you want to add a piece of fabric to the hemline. Choose the method that works best for your dress and personal style.

2. Gather your materials

Once you've chosen your method, gather the materials you'll need. For example, if you want to add a slip underneath, you'll need to purchase a slip that matches the length and style of your dress.

3. Measure and cut

If you're adding fabric to the hemline, measure the length you want to add and cut a piece of matching fabric to that length. Make sure to add a little extra length for hemming.

4. Hem the fabric

If you're adding fabric to the hemline, hem the fabric by folding the edges under and sewing them in place. Make sure the hemline matches the hemline of the dress.

5. Attach the fabric

Attach the fabric to the hemline of the dress by sewing it in place. Make sure the fabric is evenly distributed around the hemline.

6. Try it on

Try on the dress and make sure the length is now to your liking. Make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Making a Short Dress Longer

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when making a short dress longer:

  • Choose a slip or petticoat that matches the length and style of your dress
  • Use fabric that matches the color and texture of your dress
  • When adding fabric to the hemline, make sure the fabric is the same weight and drape as the dress fabric
  • Accessorize with knee-high boots or ankle booties to elongate your legs

FAQs

Can I make a short dress longer without sewing?

Yes, you can make a short dress longer without sewing by wearing leggings or tights underneath. You can also accessorize with knee-high boots or ankle booties.

Can I use any type of fabric to make a short dress longer?

It's best to use fabric that matches the color and texture of your dress. When adding fabric to the hemline, make sure the fabric is the same weight and drape as the dress fabric.

Hair Care Tips to Match Your New Look

Now that you've made your short dress longer, you may want to update your hairstyle to match your new look. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Try a sleek ponytail or bun to elongate your neck
  • Accessorize with a headband or hair clip to add some sparkle
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair
  • Try a new hair color or highlights to freshen up your look

With these tips and tricks, you can make a short dress longer and feel confident and comfortable in your new look. Whether you're wearing it to a formal event or a casual day out, your longer dress will be sure to turn heads.
