Men s Haircut Long On Top And Back A Guide For 2023

Written by Bardi Jul 12, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile men's haircut, you might want to consider the long on top and back style. This haircut features longer hair on the top and back of the head, while the sides are kept shorter. It's a great choice for guys who want to experiment with different hairstyles, as the longer hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured. In this article, we'll discuss how to get a men's haircut long on top and back, as well as some tips for styling and maintaining this look.

Description

The men's haircut long on top and back is a modern take on the classic short back and sides. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be tailored to suit different hair types and face shapes. The length of the hair on top and back can vary, depending on personal preference, but typically ranges from 2 to 6 inches. The sides are usually cut short, either with clippers or scissors, and can be tapered or faded for a more gradual transition. This haircut is popular among men of all ages and can be dressed up or down, making it suitable for any occasion.

How to Get a Men's Haircut Long on Top and Back

If you're interested in getting a men's haircut long on top and back, the first step is to find a skilled hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. It's important to communicate your preferences clearly, so your hairstylist can create a style that suits you. Here are the basic steps involved in getting a men's haircut long on top and back:

Step 1: Consultation

The first step is to have a consultation with your hairstylist. During this consultation, you can discuss your hair type, face shape, and personal preferences. Your hairstylist can then recommend a style that will suit you and provide advice on styling and maintenance.

Step 2: Cutting the Sides

The next step is to cut the sides of your hair. This is usually done with clippers or scissors, depending on the desired length and style. Your hairstylist may use a technique called tapering or fading to create a gradual transition from the longer hair on top to the shorter hair on the sides.

Step 3: Cutting the Top and Back

Once the sides are done, your hairstylist will move on to the top and back of your hair. Depending on the length you want, they may use scissors or a razor to create layers and texture. It's important to communicate your preferences during this step, as the length and texture of the hair on top will determine how the style looks once it's finished.

Step 4: Finishing Touches

Finally, your hairstylist will add any finishing touches, such as blending the sides and top, trimming the hairline, and styling the hair. They may use products such as pomade, wax, or gel to create the desired look.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining a Men's Haircut Long on Top and Back

Once you've got your men's haircut long on top and back, it's important to know how to style and maintain it. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Choosing the right hair products can make all the difference when it comes to styling your men's haircut long on top and back. Experiment with different pomades, waxes, and gels to find the product that works best for your hair type and desired look.

Tip 2: Try Different Styles

One of the best things about the men's haircut long on top and back is its versatility. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles, from slicked back to messy and textured. You can also try adding a side part or a fringe to change up your look.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

To keep your men's haircut long on top and back looking sharp, it's important to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will help prevent split ends and keep the shape of the style intact.

FAQs

Q: Is the men's haircut long on top and back suitable for all face shapes?

A: The men's haircut long on top and back can be tailored to suit different face shapes. Your hairstylist can recommend a style that flatters your features.

Q: How do I know if the men's haircut long on top and back is right for me?

A: The men's haircut long on top and back is a versatile style that can work for many different hair types and personal styles. It's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine if this style is right for you.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your men's haircut long on top and back looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy. Avoid using hot water, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Condition Your Hair

Use a conditioner after shampooing to keep your hair moisturized and soft. This will also help prevent tangles and breakage.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

If you're using heat styling tools such as a blow dryer or flat iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 4: Get Regular Haircuts

As mentioned earlier, regular haircuts are important to maintain the shape and style of your men's haircut long on top and back.

Conclusion

The men's haircut long on top and back is a stylish and versatile choice for guys who want to experiment with different hairstyles. With the right cut, products, and maintenance, you can achieve a look that suits your personal style and flatters your features. Whether you prefer a slicked back or messy and textured look, the men's haircut long on top and back is a great option for any occasion.
