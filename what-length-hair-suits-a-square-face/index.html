What Length Hair Suits A Square Face

Written by William Mar 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you a proud owner of a square face? Square faces are characterized by strong jawlines, wide foreheads, and angular features. The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for square faces is to soften the angular features and balance the proportions of your face. In this article, we will discuss what length hair suits a square face and provide tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • How to Choose the Right Length for Square Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide on How to Style Your Hair
  • Tips on How to Care for Your Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Determining your face shape is the first step to finding the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a photo of your face straight on. Then, use a ruler to measure the length and width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

If the length and width of your face are roughly equal, you have a square face. If your jawline is significantly narrower than your forehead and cheekbones, you have a heart-shaped face. If your forehead is wider than your jawline, you have a triangular or pear-shaped face. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oval face.

How to Choose the Right Length for Square Faces

The right length for square faces is one that softens the angles of the face and balances the proportions. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

Short Hair

Short haircuts work well for square faces as they draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones while minimizing the jawline. A short bob or pixie cut with side-swept bangs can soften the angles of the face. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline as they can make the face look wider.

Medium Hair

Medium-length hair can work well for square faces as long as it is layered and soft around the face. A shoulder-length bob with layers can add movement and volume to the hair while framing the face. Avoid straight cuts that end at the jawline as they can make the face look wider.

Long Hair

Long hair can also work for square faces as long as it is layered and soft around the face. Long layers can add movement and volume to the hair while balancing the proportions of the face. Avoid straight cuts that end at the jawline as they can make the face look wider.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Style Your Hair

Once you have chosen the right length for your square face, it's time to style your hair. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that suit your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement.
  4. Use a curling iron or flat iron to add loose waves or curls to your hair.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips on How to Care for Your Hair

Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day as it can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can square faces pull off a middle part?

Yes, square faces can pull off a middle part as long as the hair is soft and layered around the face. A middle part can elongate the face and balance the proportions.

2. Can square faces have bangs?

Yes, square faces can have bangs as long as they are soft and side-swept. Bangs can add movement and volume to the hair while framing the face.

3. Can square faces have straight hair?

Yes, square faces can have straight hair as long as it is soft and layered around the face. Straight hair can elongate the face and balance the proportions.

In conclusion, finding the perfect length for square faces is all about softening the angles and balancing the proportions. Short, medium, and long hair can all work for square faces as long as they are layered and soft around the face. Use the tips and guidelines in this article to find the perfect hairstyle for your square face.
