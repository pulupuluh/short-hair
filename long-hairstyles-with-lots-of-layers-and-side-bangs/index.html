Long Hairstyles With Lots Of Layers And Side Bangs A Trending Style In 2023

Written by Oliver May 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hairstyles with lots of layers and side bangs have been a popular trend for the past few years, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. This style is perfect for those who want to add volume, movement, and texture to their hair. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this style, tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve Long Hairstyles with Lots of Layers and Side Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Long Hairstyle with Layers and Side Bangs
  • FAQ

How to Achieve Long Hairstyles with Lots of Layers and Side Bangs

The first step towards achieving this style is to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting long hair. Layering long hair can be tricky, and you want to make sure that your stylist knows what they're doing. It's also important to have a consultation with your stylist before the cut to discuss the specific look you're going for.

When it comes to side bangs, you'll want to decide how long you want them to be and where you want them to fall. Your stylist can help you determine the best length and placement based on your face shape and hair texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Wash and condition your hair using products that are suitable for your hair type.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.

3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.

4. Section off your hair and start cutting the layers. Your stylist will use different techniques to create the layers, such as slicing or point cutting.

5. Once the layers are in place, your stylist will cut the side bangs. They may use a razor or scissors to create a soft, textured look.

6. Style your hair using a curling iron, straightener, or any other styling tool of your choice.

Tips for Maintaining Your Long Hairstyle with Layers and Side Bangs

1. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh and clean.

4. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: Will long hairstyles with lots of layers and side bangs work for all hair types?

A: This style can work for most hair types, but it's important to consult with your stylist to determine the best layering technique for your hair texture.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with this style?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail. However, it may be more difficult to achieve a sleek, polished look due to the layers and side bangs.

Hair Care for Long Hairstyles with Lots of Layers and Side Bangs

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Avoid brushing your hair while it's wet to prevent breakage.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.

4. Apply a leave-in conditioner to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends.

5. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Long hairstyles with lots of layers and side bangs are a great way to add movement and texture to your hair. With the proper care and maintenance, you can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish. Consult with your stylist to determine the best layering technique and side bang placement for your face shape and hair texture.
