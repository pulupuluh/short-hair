Most Flattering Hairstyles For Over 60

Written by Bardi Jun 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair changes along with our skin and body. It's important to choose a hairstyle that flatters us at any age, but even more so after the age of 60. In this article, we'll explore some of the most flattering hairstyles for women over 60, including how to achieve them, tips for styling, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose a Flattering Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving a Flattering Hairstyle
  • Tips for Styling
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Aging Hair

How to Choose a Flattering Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle after the age of 60, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider a shorter hairstyle to help frame your face and highlight your features.
  • Opt for a style with layers to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Choose a style that complements your face shape - for example, a bob for a square face or a pixie cut for a heart-shaped face.
  • Take your hair texture into account - for example, a wavy hairstyle may be easier to maintain than a straight hairstyle.
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair each day. A low-maintenance hairstyle may be a better option for some.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving a Flattering Hairstyle

Once you've chosen a flattering hairstyle, here's a step-by-step guide to achieving it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently to avoid damage.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to damp hair to add volume and hold.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add lift and shape. Use the brush to direct your hair away from your face.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add texture and shape as desired.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling your hair to keep it looking healthy and flattering:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time. Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo or other styling product to extend the time between washes and keep your hair looking fresh.
  • If you have thinning hair, consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add fullness.
  • Try a new hair accessory, such as a headband or hair clip, to add interest to your hairstyle.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a flattering hairstyle after the age of 60:

What kind of hairstyles are best for thinning hair?

Shorter hairstyles with layers are a great option for thinning hair, as they can add volume and texture. Avoid hairstyles that are too long or heavy, as these can weigh down your hair and make it look thinner.

What hairstyles should I avoid after the age of 60?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as these can make you look older. Also avoid hairstyles that are too severe or harsh, such as a blunt bob or a slicked-back ponytail. These can accentuate wrinkles and make you look older.

Can I dye my hair after the age of 60?

Yes, you can dye your hair at any age! However, it's important to choose a color that is flattering to your skin tone and complements your features. Consider consulting with a stylist to find the best color for you.

Hair Care for Aging Hair

As we age, our hair may become more dry, brittle, and thin. Here are some tips for caring for aging hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that support healthy hair growth.
  • Consider taking a hair supplement, such as biotin or collagen, to support hair health.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals, such as bleach or perm solution, which can damage your hair.

In conclusion, choosing a flattering hairstyle after the age of 60 can be a fun and exciting way to refresh your look and boost your confidence. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can find a style that works for you. With the right care and styling products, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful at any age!
