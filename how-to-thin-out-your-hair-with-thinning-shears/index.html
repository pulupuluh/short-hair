How To Thin Out Your Hair With Thinning Shears

Written by Christine Mar 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thinning out your hair can help you achieve a more natural look and reduce the bulkiness of thick hair. One way to thin out your hair is by using thinning shears. In this article, we will guide you on how to thin out your hair with thinning shears and provide you with some tips and tricks to achieve the best results.

Description

Thinning shears are scissors designed to remove bulk from hair without altering the length too much. They have one serrated blade and one straight blade, which work together to cut some of the hair while leaving the rest intact. Thinning shears are ideal for people with thick hair who want to reduce the volume, but also for those who want to add texture to their hair.

How to Thin Out Your Hair with Thinning Shears

Before you start thinning out your hair with thinning shears, you need to make sure that your hair is clean and dry.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into small sections, using clips to hold them in place. This will help you work on one section at a time and achieve a more even look.

Step 2: Determine the Amount of Hair to Cut

Take a small section of hair and determine how much you want to cut. Remember that thinning shears will remove more hair than regular scissors, so start with a small amount and work your way up.

Step 3: Hold the Thinning Shears Correctly

Hold the thinning shears with the serrated blade facing up and the straight blade facing down. This will help you achieve a more natural look.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Start cutting the hair by placing the thinning shears about an inch away from the roots and cutting halfway down the length of the hair. Repeat this process until you have thinned out the desired amount of hair.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you have thinned out the hair, use regular scissors to blend the hair and create a more natural look. This will help you avoid any choppy or uneven sections.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when thinning out your hair with thinning shears:

  • Start with a small amount of hair and work your way up.
  • Use the right type of thinning shears for your hair type.
  • Hold the thinning shears correctly to achieve a more natural look.
  • Use regular scissors to blend the hair and create a more even look.
  • Consult a professional if you are unsure about how to thin out your hair with thinning shears.

FAQ

Can thinning shears damage your hair?

Thinning shears can damage your hair if they are not used properly. It is important to use the right type of thinning shears for your hair type and to consult a professional if you are unsure about how to use them.

How often should you thin out your hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, you should thin out your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and natural.

Is it possible to thin out curly hair with thinning shears?

Yes, it is possible to thin out curly hair with thinning shears. However, it is important to use the right type of thinning shears for your hair type and to consult a professional if you are unsure about how to use them.

Hair Care

Thinning out your hair can be a great way to achieve a more natural look and reduce the bulkiness of thick hair. However, it is important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

Thinning out your hair with thinning shears can be a great way to achieve a more natural look and reduce the bulkiness of thick hair. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the best results and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
