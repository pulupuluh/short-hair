What Haircut Goes Best With A Round Face

Written by Christine Jun 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut that flatters your round face? Look no further! In this article, we'll discuss the best haircuts for round faces, how to style them, and tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description:

A round face typically has a softer jawline and fuller cheeks, so it's important to choose a haircut that creates the illusion of a longer, slimmer face. The ideal haircut for a round face is one that adds height and volume on top while keeping the sides and back shorter.

How To:

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for your round face is to determine your face shape. This can be done by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If these measurements are roughly equal, then you have a round face.

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to choose a haircut. The best haircuts for round faces include:

1. Long layers

Long layers help to create movement and add volume to the top of your head, which can make your face appear longer and slimmer.

2. Side-swept bangs

Side-swept bangs help to break up the roundness of your face and draw attention to your eyes.

3. Bob with volume

A bob with volume on top can help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer jawline.

4. Pixie cut

A pixie cut with volume on top can help to balance out a round face and create the illusion of a longer face.

Step by Step:

Here's how to style some of the best haircuts for round faces:

1. Long layers

Start by applying a volumizing mousse to damp hair. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to create volume. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Side-swept bangs

Blow dry your hair using a round brush, sweeping your bangs to the side as you go. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Bob with volume

Apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on adding volume to the top of your head. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

4. Pixie cut

Apply a small amount of styling cream to damp hair. Blow dry your hair using your fingers to create volume on top. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips:

Here are some additional tips for finding the best haircut for your round face:

  • Avoid blunt bangs or straight haircuts that end at your chin, as these can make your face appear rounder.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth in your hair.
  • Consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with round faces to find the perfect haircut for your unique features.

FAQ:

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight, but make sure to add volume on top to balance out your face shape.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a center part if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a center part, but make sure to add volume on top to create the illusion of a longer face.

Hair Care:

It's important to maintain your new haircut with regular trims and styling. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair, and avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

Now that you know what haircut goes best with a round face, it's time to book your next appointment with your hairstylist. Embrace your unique features and rock your new look with confidence!
