Is Curly Or Straight Hair Better For Round Faces

Written by Bardi Aug 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

In this year 2023, one of the most common questions we receive is whether curly or straight hair is better for round faces. It's a valid question, as the shape of your face can greatly impact the way your hair looks. But the answer is not always straightforward. In this article, we will explore the pros and cons of both curly and straight hair for round faces, and provide tips on how to style and care for each hair type.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

First, let's define what we mean by "round face." A round face is characterized by a soft, circular shape with a width and length that are almost equal. Round faces tend to have fuller cheeks and a rounded jawline. When it comes to hair, the goal is to create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face.

So, is curly or straight hair better for round faces? The answer is...it depends. Both hair types can work well for round faces, but it depends on the specific features of your face and the style you are going for.

How to

If you have a round face and want to determine whether curly or straight hair is better for you, here are some tips:

  • Consider your hair texture: If you have naturally curly hair, it may be easier to embrace your curls and work with them. If you have straight hair, you may need to use heat styling tools to achieve curls or waves.
  • Think about the length: Short haircuts can work well for round faces, but they may not be the best choice if you have curly hair. Longer hair can help elongate the face, but it may require more maintenance if you have curly hair.
  • Consider your personal style: Ultimately, the best hairstyle for you is one that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Consider your personal style and the amount of time you are willing to spend styling your hair.

Step by step

If you have decided to go with curly hair, here are some step-by-step instructions:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a curl-defining product to your hair, such as curl cream or mousse.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, scrunching as you go to encourage the curls.
  4. Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling iron or wand to touch up any areas that need more definition.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

If you have decided to go with straight hair, here are some step-by-step instructions:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair in sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.
  4. Finish with a smoothing serum or spray to keep your hair sleek and shiny.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling curly or straight hair for round faces:

  • For curly hair, avoid bangs that are too short or blunt, as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • For straight hair, consider adding layers to create movement and texture.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to see what works best for your face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous at the sides, as they can make your face appear wider.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about whether curly or straight hair is better for round faces:

Can I have bangs with curly hair if I have a round face?

Yes, but it's best to go for longer, side-swept bangs rather than short, blunt bangs. This will help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

Will straight hair make my face look longer?

Straight hair can help to create the illusion of a longer face, but it depends on the length and style of your hair. Longer, straight hair can be very elongating, while shorter, straight hair may not have the same effect.

Can I wear a bob with a round face?

Yes, but it's important to choose the right length and style. A chin-length bob can work well for round faces, but avoid bobs that are too short or too voluminous.

Hair Care

Regardless of whether you choose curly or straight hair, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

So, is curly or straight hair better for round faces? The answer is that both can work well, depending on the specific features of your face and the style you are going for. Use the tips and techniques outlined in this article to find the perfect hairstyle for you.
