Short Black Hairstyles For Thin Hair Over 50

Written by Bardi Mar 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, our hair becomes thinner, and it can be a struggle to find a hairstyle that looks good and suits our face shape. But don't worry! There are plenty of short black hairstyles for thin hair over 50 that are chic and easy to maintain. In this article, we'll go through some of the best styles, how to achieve them, tips, and hair care to make sure your hair looks healthy and fabulous.

How to Choose a Short Black Hairstyle for Thin Hair Over 50

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, it's essential to understand that not all styles suit everyone. When choosing a short black hairstyle, consider your face shape, lifestyle, and hair texture. For instance, if you have a round face, you might want to opt for a style that elongates your face, like a pixie cut. If you have a busy lifestyle, you might want a low maintenance style that doesn't require too much styling.

Step by Step Guide for Short Black Hairstyles for Thin Hair Over 50

Once you've chosen a style, it's time to achieve it. Here is a step-by-step guide for some of the most popular short black hairstyles for thin hair over 50:

Pixie Cut

  • Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  • Apply a styling cream to your hair to add volume.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create texture.
  • Apply a styling wax to your hair to hold the style in place.

Bob Cut

  • Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  • Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair to add volume.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and create texture.
  • Apply a styling spray to your hair to hold the style in place.

Cropped Cut

  • Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  • Apply a styling cream to your hair to add volume.
  • Use a hair clipper to create a short, cropped cut.
  • Apply a styling wax to your hair to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Black Hairstyles for Thin Hair Over 50

To keep your short black hairstyle looking fresh and healthy, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming greasy.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends.

FAQs on Short Black Hairstyles for Thin Hair Over 50

What are the best short black hairstyles for thin hair over 50?

Some of the best short black hairstyles for thin hair over 50 include the pixie cut, bob cut, and cropped cut.

How can I add volume to my thin hair?

You can add volume to your thin hair by using volumizing products like mousse, styling cream, and styling spray. Blow-drying your hair using a round brush can also add volume.

Can I color my thin hair?

Yes, you can color your thin hair, but be sure to use gentle, sulfate-free hair dye, and avoid coloring it too often, as this can damage your hair.

Conclusion

Short black hairstyles for thin hair over 50 can be chic, stylish, and easy to maintain. By choosing the right style, following the right steps, and following the tips and hair care advice, you can have a healthy and fabulous hairstyle that suits your face shape and lifestyle.
