How To Cover Hair Loss On Top Of Head Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Sep 02, 2023 · 6 min read

Dealing with hair loss on top of the head can be a challenge, but there are several ways to cover it up. In this article, we will explore how to cover hair loss on top of the head using different methods and hair care tips to maintain healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cover Hair Loss on Top of Head
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Covering Hair Loss on Top of Head
  • FAQs on Hair Loss on Top of Head
  • Hair Care Tips to Prevent Hair Loss

How to Cover Hair Loss on Top of Head

There are several ways to cover hair loss on top of the head, including:

Hair Fibers:

Hair fibers are a quick and easy way to cover up hair loss. They are made of keratin and can be sprinkled onto the hair to create a fuller look. Hair fibers come in a variety of colors and can be matched to your hair color.

Hair Pieces:

Hair pieces or toupees are another option for covering hair loss on top of the head. They are made of synthetic or human hair and can be attached to the scalp with clips or adhesives. Hair pieces can be styled to match your natural hair and can be removed and reattached as needed.

Hair Extensions:

Hair extensions can be used to add volume and length to the hair, which can help cover up hair loss. They can be attached to the hair with clips, glue, or sewn in. Hair extensions come in a variety of colors and lengths to match your natural hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to cover hair loss on top of the head:

  1. Clean and dry your hair.
  2. Select the method you want to use to cover hair loss.
  3. Apply hair fibers, hair pieces, or hair extensions to the affected area.
  4. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Covering Hair Loss on Top of Head

Here are some tips to help you cover hair loss on top of the head:

  • Choose a method that matches your lifestyle and budget.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Choose a color that matches your natural hair for a more natural look.
  • Invest in good quality products to ensure they last longer.

FAQs on Hair Loss on Top of Head

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair loss on top of the head:

What Causes Hair Loss on Top of the Head?

Hair loss on top of the head can be caused by genetics, hormones, stress, and aging.

Can Hair Loss on Top of the Head be Reversed?

While there is no cure for hair loss, there are treatments that can help slow down the process and promote hair growth.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Hair Loss

Here are some hair care tips to help prevent hair loss:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the hair.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Reduce stress through exercise and relaxation techniques.

By following these tips and using the methods mentioned above, you can cover up hair loss on top of the head and maintain healthy hair.
