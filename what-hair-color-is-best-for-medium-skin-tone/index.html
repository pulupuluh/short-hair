What Hair Color Is Best For Medium Skin Tone A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jun 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing the right hair color that complements your skin tone can be a tricky task. If you have a medium skin tone, you might be wondering what hair color suits you best. In this guide, we will discuss the best hair colors for medium skin tone, how to choose them, and hair care tips to maintain your colored tresses.

Description

Medium skin tone refers to skin that has a warm, olive undertone. It can range from light to dark brown, and sometimes with a hint of yellow or green. When it comes to choosing the right hair color, it's essential to consider your skin's undertones to ensure that the color complements your natural complexion and brings out your best features.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

The key to choosing the right hair color for medium skin tone is to opt for shades that complement your undertones. If you have warm undertones, choose warm hair colors like golden blonde, copper, or caramel. If you have cool undertones, opt for cool hair colors like ash blonde, dark brown, or burgundy. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hair color:

Tip #1: Consider Your Eye Color

When choosing the right hair color, it's essential to consider your eye color. If you have warm-toned eyes like brown, hazel, or green, opt for warm hair colors like golden blonde or caramel. If you have cool-toned eyes like blue or gray, go for cool hair colors like ash blonde or dark brown.

Tip #2: Determine Your Skin's Undertones

To determine your skin's undertones, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If you can't tell whether they're green or blue, you might have neutral undertones, which means you can rock both warm and cool hair colors.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Color

Step 1: Determine your skin's undertones.

Step 2: Consider your eye color.

Step 3: Choose warm or cool hair colors based on your undertones and eye color.

Step 4: Consult a professional hair colorist to help you choose the best shade for your skin tone.

Tips for Maintaining Colored Hair

Colored hair requires extra care to maintain its vibrancy and prevent damage. Here are some tips to keep your colored tresses healthy:

Tip #1: Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair color from fading. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, or shea butter.

Tip #2: Avoid Hot Showers

Hot showers can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause color fading. Opt for lukewarm or cool showers instead to keep your hair and scalp healthy.

Tip #3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can cause hair color to fade faster, so it's essential to protect your hair from the sun. Wear a hat or use a hair serum with SPF protection when you're out in the sun.

Tip #4: Schedule Regular Hair Trims

Regular hair trims can help maintain your hair's health and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

FAQs

What hair colors should I avoid if I have medium skin tone?

If you have a medium skin tone, avoid hair colors that are too light, like platinum blonde or pastel shades. They can wash you out and make you look pale. You should also avoid hair colors that are too dark, like jet black or dark brown, as they can make your skin look dull.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While you can dye your hair at home, it's best to leave it to the professionals. They have the expertise and knowledge to choose the right hair color for your skin tone and ensure that the dye application is even and long-lasting.

Hair Care Products for Colored Hair

If you have colored hair, it's essential to use hair care products that are specially formulated for colored hair. Here are some products you can try:

  • Color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Hair oil or serum
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Hair mask

Choosing the right hair color for medium skin tone can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips and recommendations, you can find a hair color that complements your natural complexion and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
