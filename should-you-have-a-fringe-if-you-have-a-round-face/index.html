Should You Have A Fringe If You Have A Round Face

Written by Petter Feb 18, 2023 · 7 min read

Having a round face can sometimes make it difficult to choose the right hairstyle. One of the most common questions that people with round faces ask is whether or not they should have a fringe. In this article, we will explore the pros and cons of having a fringe if you have a round face, and provide you with some helpful tips and tricks to keep your hair looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Having a fringe can be a great way to change up your look and add some personality to your hairstyle. However, when it comes to deciding whether or not to have a fringe, it’s important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, you may be hesitant about getting a fringe as it can accentuate the roundness of your face.

How to

If you do decide to get a fringe, there are a few things you can do to make sure it complements your round face:

1. Choose the Right Type of Fringe

There are many different types of fringes to choose from, but not all of them will suit a round face. The best type of fringe for a round face is one that is longer on the sides and shorter in the middle, such as a side-swept fringe. This will help to elongate your face and make it appear less round.

2. Avoid Heavy, Blunt Fringes

Heavy, blunt fringes can make a round face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for a softer, more feathered fringe that blends into the rest of your hair.

3. Consider the Length of Your Fringe

The length of your fringe can also make a difference. A shorter fringe can make a round face appear rounder, while a longer fringe can help to elongate your face. If you’re unsure, speak to your hairstylist for advice.

Step by Step

If you’ve decided to get a fringe, here are some step-by-step instructions to help you style it:

1. Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair as you normally would. If you have a round face, you may want to add some volume at the top of your head to help elongate your face.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into the length of fringe you want, and clip the rest of your hair back.

3. Cut Your Fringe

If you’re cutting your own fringe, make sure you do it slowly and carefully. If you’re unsure, it’s best to leave it to a professional hairstylist.

4. Style Your Fringe

Once your fringe is cut, you can style it using a hair straightener, curling iron, or simply blow-drying it into place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind if you have a round face and are considering a fringe:

  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair and help elongate your face.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to the top of your head.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different types of fringes until you find one that works for you.

FAQ

Q: Will a fringe make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the type of fringe you get. A side-swept fringe that is longer on the sides and shorter in the middle can help to elongate your face and make it appear less round.

Q: Should I cut my own fringe?

A: If you’re unsure or inexperienced, it’s best to leave it to a professional hairstylist to avoid any mishaps.

Hair Care

Regardless of whether or not you have a fringe, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Make sure you use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

In conclusion, if you have a round face, you can still have a fringe as long as you choose the right type and length. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
