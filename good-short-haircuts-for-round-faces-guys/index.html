Good Short Haircuts For Round Faces Guys

Written by Oliver Aug 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a guy with a round face, you might find that some haircuts just don't seem to work for you. But fear not, there are plenty of good short haircuts for round faces guys that can help you look your best. In this article, we'll explore some of the best short haircuts for round faces guys, how to get them, and how to take care of your hair once you've got your new look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a round chin, and a wider forehead. When choosing a short haircut for a round face, it's important to choose a style that will complement your face shape and balance out your features. Some good short haircuts for round faces guys include the buzz cut, the crew cut, and the textured crop.

How to

Getting a good short haircut for a round face starts with finding a skilled barber or stylist who understands your face shape and can recommend a style that will work for you. When you're in the chair, be sure to communicate your preferences and ask questions if you're not sure about something.

Step by step

Here are some steps you can follow to get a good short haircut for a round face:

  1. Find a skilled barber or stylist who understands your face shape.
  2. Discuss your preferences and ask questions if you're not sure about something.
  3. Choose a style that will complement your face shape and balance out your features.
  4. Sit back and let the barber or stylist work their magic!

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a good short haircut for a round face:

  • Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face look even rounder.
  • Choose styles that add height to the top of your head, as this can help elongate your face.
  • Consider adding texture to your hair with layers or styling products, as this can create the illusion of more angular features.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about good short haircuts for round faces guys:

What is the best short haircut for a round face?

The best short haircut for a round face depends on your personal preferences and your hair type. Some good options include the buzz cut, the crew cut, and the textured crop.

How can I make my face look less round with a short haircut?

You can make your face look less round with a short haircut by choosing a style that adds height to the top of your head and avoiding styles that add volume to the sides of your head.

Do I need to use styling products with a short haircut?

It's not necessary to use styling products with a short haircut, but they can help you achieve the look you want and add texture to your hair.

Hair Care

Once you've got your new short haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly for trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

With the right short haircut and hair care routine, guys with round faces can look their best and feel confident in their appearance. So why not try out one of these good short haircuts for round faces guys and see how it works for you?
