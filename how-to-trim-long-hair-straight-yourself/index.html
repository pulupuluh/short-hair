How To Trim Long Hair Straight Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon every time you need a trim? Trimming long hair straight is not as hard as it seems. With a few tools and the right technique, you can achieve a perfect trim at home. In this guide, we will show you how to trim long hair straight yourself in a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  1. Tools You Will Need
  2. Tips Before You Start
  3. How to Trim Long Hair Straight Yourself
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care After Trimming

Tools You Will Need

Before you start trimming your hair, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb or hairbrush
  • Clips to section off hair
  • Water spray bottle
  • Mirror

Tips Before You Start

Here are some tips to keep in mind before you start trimming your hair:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry.
  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up.
  • Trim less than you think you need to, as you can always cut more later.
  • Be patient and take breaks when needed.

How to Trim Long Hair Straight Yourself

Now that you have your tools and tips in place, let's get started with the step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Brush and Section Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Then, using clips, section your hair into 4-6 parts, depending on the thickness of your hair.

Step 2: Wet Your Hair

Spray water on the section of hair you want to trim. This will help to keep your hair in place and make it easier to cut.

Step 3: Cut the Ends

Take a small section of hair and twist it tightly. Then, using your sharp scissors, cut the ends of the twisted hair straight across. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

Once you have finished trimming all sections, release your hair and check for evenness. If you notice any uneven sections, repeat the previous steps to fix them.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about trimming long hair straight:

How often should I trim my hair?

It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

Can I use regular scissors to trim my hair?

No, regular scissors are not sharp enough to cut through hair and can cause damage to your hair.

Should I trim my hair wet or dry?

It is recommended to trim your hair when it is dry, as wet hair can stretch and appear longer than it actually is.

Hair Care After Trimming

After trimming your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair for a few days after trimming.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

Trimming long hair straight yourself is not as difficult as it seems. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a perfect trim in the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you will have beautiful, healthy hair in no time!
