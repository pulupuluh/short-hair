How To Get Fluffy Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 19, 2023 · 8 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to get fluffy curly hair! If you’ve been dreaming of having bouncy, voluminous curls that make heads turn, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll go over everything you need to know to achieve that perfect fluffy curly hairstyle, from basic hair care tips to step-by-step instructions on how to create those beautiful curls. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How to Get Fluffy Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining Fluffy Curls
  • FAQs

Hair Care

Before we dive into how to get fluffy curly hair, let’s start with the basics: hair care. Taking good care of your hair is essential to achieving and maintaining healthy, bouncy curls. Here are some tips:

1. Moisturize

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it’s important to keep it moisturized. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider adding a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your routine.

2. Avoid Heat

Heat can damage curly hair and cause it to lose its bounce, so avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons as much as possible. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

3. Detangle Gently

Curly hair is prone to tangles, but it’s important to detangle gently to avoid damaging your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle, and start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

How to Get Fluffy Curly Hair

Now that you know how to take care of your hair, let’s move on to how to get fluffy curly hair. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Start with Clean, Moisturized Hair

Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

2. Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product like a curl cream or mousse to your damp hair. This will help define your curls and add volume.

3. Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and add volume. Hold the diffuser close to your hair and move it around to dry your hair evenly.

4. Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your hands to scrunch your hair from the ends to the roots. This will help enhance your curls and add volume.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting fluffy curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product like a curl cream or mousse to your damp hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use your hands to scrunch your hair from the ends to the roots.
  5. Flip your hair upside down and shake it out to add volume.

Tips for Maintaining Fluffy Curls

Once you’ve achieved your perfect fluffy curly hairstyle, you’ll want to maintain it. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases are gentler on your hair and won’t cause frizz or breakage while you sleep.

2. Refresh Your Curls

If your curls start to lose their bounce, refresh them by spraying your hair with water and scrunching it with your hands.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Elements

Wind, rain, and humidity can all cause your curls to lose their shape, so protect your hair with a hat or scarf when you’re out in the elements.

FAQs

Q: Can I get fluffy curls if my hair is naturally straight?

A: Yes, you can! Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create curls, and then follow the steps above to enhance and maintain your curls.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people can go several days between washes, while others may need to wash their hair every day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: What if my curls are too tight?

A: If you prefer looser, more fluffy curls, try using a larger curling iron or hot rollers to create bigger curls. You can also use a wide-tooth comb to gently loosen your curls after they’ve cooled.

And that’s it! We hope this guide has been helpful in teaching you how to get fluffy curly hair. Remember, the key to healthy, bouncy curls is taking good care of your hair and using the right products and techniques. Happy styling!
