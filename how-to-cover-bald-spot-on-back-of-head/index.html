How To Cover Bald Spot On Back Of Head A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Sep 01, 2023 Â· 11 min read

Are you struggling with a bald spot on the back of your head? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people experience hair loss, and it can be a source of frustration and embarrassment. But there are ways to cover up bald spots and regain your confidence. In this guide, we will discuss how to cover bald spots on the back of your head and provide tips for hair care to promote healthy growth.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cover Bald Spot on Back of Head
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Covering Bald Spots
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Healthy Growth

Description

Bald spots can occur for various reasons, including genetics, aging, medication, stress, and poor nutrition. They can be located on different parts of the head, but the back of the head is a common area. Bald spots can be small or large, and they can be visible or hidden under the hair. Covering up a bald spot on the back of the head can be challenging, but it's not impossible. There are several techniques and products that can help you hide the bald spot and improve your appearance.

How to Cover Bald Spot on Back of Head

There are several ways to cover up a bald spot on the back of your head, including:

Hairstyles

Choosing a suitable hairstyle can help you cover up the bald spot. You can try a short or medium-length haircut that is tapered or layered at the back. This will create volume and texture that can conceal the bald patch. You can also try a buzz cut or a shaved head if you're comfortable with it. These styles are trendy and can make baldness look intentional.

Hairpieces

If you want to cover up the bald spot temporarily, you can use a hairpiece. Hairpieces are synthetic or natural hair that can be attached to your existing hair or scalp with clips, tape, or glue. Hairpieces come in different styles, colors, and lengths, and you can choose the one that matches your natural hair. Hairpieces are easy to use and can be removed or replaced anytime.

Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are a type of cosmetic product that can be sprinkled on the bald spot to make it less visible. Hair fibers are made of keratin or cotton and come in different colors that match your hair. Hair fibers stick to the existing hair and create an illusion of fuller hair. Hair fibers are easy to apply and can last for a day or two.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cover up a bald spot on the back of your head:

  1. Clean your hair and scalp with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Dry your hair with a towel or a hairdryer.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair to create volume and texture.
  4. Choose a suitable hairstyle that covers the bald spot, such as a short or medium-length haircut that is tapered or layered at the back.
  5. If you want to use a hairpiece, select the one that matches your hair color and style.
  6. Attach the hairpiece to your existing hair or scalp with clips, tape, or glue. Make sure it's secure and comfortable.
  7. If you want to use hair fibers, sprinkle them on the bald spot and pat gently to distribute them evenly. Avoid using too much or rubbing vigorously.
  8. Style your hair as desired and use a hairspray to hold it in place.
  9. Check your hair from different angles to make sure the bald spot is covered properly.
  10. Enjoy your new look and feel confident!

Tips for Covering Bald Spots

Here are some tips for covering up bald spots on the back of your head:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Consult a hairstylist for advice on the best hairstyle and hair care products for your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and hairpieces to find the one that works for you.
  • Avoid using too much hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make the bald spot more visible.
  • Avoid exposing your scalp to the sun, as it can cause further damage and hair loss.
  • Eat a balanced and nutritious diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and biotin, which are essential for healthy hair growth.
  • Practice stress-management techniques, such as yoga or meditation, to reduce stress and anxiety, which can contribute to hair loss.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as they can damage your hair and scalp.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about covering bald spots on the back of the head:

Can I use makeup to cover up the bald spot?

Yes, you can use makeup, such as concealer or foundation, to cover up the bald spot temporarily. However, makeup can be messy and may not last long, especially if you sweat or get wet.

Can I use hair extensions to cover up the bald spot?

Yes, you can use hair extensions to cover up the bald spot if they are attached to your existing hair. However, if the bald spot is located on the top of your head, hair extensions may not be suitable.

Can I use hair regrowth products to grow hair on the bald spot?

Yes, you can use hair regrowth products, such as minoxidil or finasteride, to stimulate hair growth on the bald spot. However, these products may take several months to show results and may have side effects.

Hair Care for Healthy Growth

To promote healthy hair growth and prevent further hair loss, you can follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water or harsh chemicals on your hair, as they can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a hairbrush with soft bristles to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid pulling or twisting your hair too tightly, as it can cause hair breakage and loss.
  • Avoid using hair dryers, curling irons, or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Avoid wearing tight hats or helmets that can restrict blood flow to your scalp.
  • Massage your scalp regularly with your fingertips to improve blood circulation and stimulate hair growth.
  • Eat a balanced and nutritious diet that contains protein, vitamins, and minerals that are essential for healthy hair growth.
  • Get enough sleep and exercise regularly to reduce stress and improve overall health.
  • Consult a dermatologist or a hair specialist if you experience severe hair loss or scalp problems.

Now that you know how to cover up a bald spot on the back of your head and take care of your hair, you can feel more confident and comfortable in your own skin. Remember, baldness is not a curse, but a natural part of life. Embrace your uniqueness and rock your style!
