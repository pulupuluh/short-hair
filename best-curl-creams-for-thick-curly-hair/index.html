The Best Curl Creams For Thick Curly Hair In 2023

Written by Bardi May 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with frizz and unmanageable curls? Look no further! We have compiled a list of the best curl creams for thick curly hair that will keep your curls defined, moisturized, and under control. In this article, we will discuss how to use curl creams, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care for thick curly hair. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use Curl Creams
  • Step-by-Step Instructions

  • FAQs

Description

Curl creams are a must-have for anyone with thick curly hair. They help to define your curls, reduce frizz, and add moisture to your hair. The best curl creams for thick curly hair are those that are lightweight, non-greasy, and provide long-lasting hold without leaving a sticky residue. They should also contain natural ingredients that nourish and protect your hair.

How to Use Curl Creams

To get the most out of your curl cream, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a small amount of curl cream to your palms and rub them together to emulsify the product.
  3. Distribute the curl cream evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to comb your hair and distribute the product evenly.
  5. Scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation.
  6. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use curl creams:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Take a small amount of curl cream and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
  4. Apply the cream to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  5. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to comb through your hair and distribute the product evenly.
  6. Scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation.
  7. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair.
  8. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to scrunch your hair and break up any crunchy areas.

Tips for Using Curl Creams

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your curl cream:

  • Less is more. Start with a small amount of product and add more if necessary.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly.
  • Scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation.
  • Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curl creams:

Q: Can curl creams be used on all hair types?

A: Curl creams are best suited for thick curly hair, but they can be used on all hair types.

Q: How often should I use curl cream?

A: You can use curl cream every time you wash your hair or as needed to refresh your curls.

Q: Can I use curl cream on dry hair?

A: Curl cream is designed to be used on damp hair, but you can use it on dry hair to tame frizz and add shine.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

In addition to using curl creams, here are some tips for caring for thick curly hair:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.

With the right curl cream and hair care routine, you can have beautiful, defined curls that are easy to manage. Try one of the best curl creams for thick curly hair and see the difference for yourself!
