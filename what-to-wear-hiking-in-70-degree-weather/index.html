What To Wear Hiking In 70 Degree Weather

Written by William Mar 19, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you planning a hiking trip in 70-degree weather and wondering what to wear? Look no further! In this article, we’ll provide you with the ultimate guide on what to wear hiking in 70-degree weather. We’ll cover everything from clothing to footwear, so you can be well-prepared for your next adventure.

Description

When it comes to hiking, wearing the right clothing is crucial. Not only will it keep you comfortable, but it will also protect you from the elements. In 70-degree weather, you’ll want to wear clothing that is breathable and lightweight, so you don’t overheat.

How To

Here are some tips on what to wear hiking in 70-degree weather:

1. Clothing

Start with a moisture-wicking base layer, such as a lightweight t-shirt or tank top. This will help keep you cool and dry. On top of that, wear a lightweight, long-sleeved shirt to protect your arms from the sun and insects. For bottoms, opt for lightweight shorts, pants, or leggings, depending on your preference.

2. Footwear

Choose comfortable and sturdy hiking shoes or boots with good traction. Make sure they are broken in before your hiking trip to avoid blisters.

3. Accessories

Don’t forget to protect your head and eyes from the sun with a hat and sunglasses. You may also want to bring a lightweight jacket or raincoat in case the weather changes.

Step by Step

Follow these steps to properly dress for a hike in 70-degree weather:

Step 1:

Start with a moisture-wicking base layer, such as a lightweight t-shirt or tank top.

Step 2:

Add a lightweight, long-sleeved shirt to protect your arms from the sun and insects.

Step 3:

Choose lightweight shorts, pants, or leggings for bottoms.

Step 4:

Select comfortable and sturdy hiking shoes or boots with good traction.

Step 5:

Protect your head and eyes from the sun with a hat and sunglasses.

Step 6:

Consider bringing a lightweight jacket or raincoat in case the weather changes.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

1. Dress in layers:

If the temperature changes throughout the day, you can easily remove or add layers as needed.

2. Avoid cotton:

Cotton absorbs sweat and moisture, making it uncomfortable to wear. Opt for moisture-wicking fabrics instead.

3. Bring a backpack:

Carry a backpack with essentials such as water, snacks, and a map.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear sandals for hiking in 70-degree weather?

A: While sandals may be comfortable, they don’t provide enough support or protection for hiking. Stick with sturdy hiking shoes or boots.

Q: Do I need to wear sunscreen?

A: Yes, it’s important to protect your skin from the sun’s harmful rays. Apply sunscreen with at least SPF 30 before heading out on your hike.

Q: Should I bring extra clothing?

A: It’s always a good idea to bring extra clothing, such as a lightweight jacket or raincoat, in case the weather changes.

Hair Care

When hiking, it’s important to keep your hair out of your face and protected from the sun. Tie your hair back in a ponytail or braid, and wear a hat to protect your scalp from the sun’s rays.

Now that you know what to wear hiking in 70-degree weather, you’re ready for your next adventure. Remember to dress in layers, wear moisture-wicking fabrics, and protect yourself from the sun. Happy hiking!
