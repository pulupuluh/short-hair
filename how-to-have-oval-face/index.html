How To Have An Oval Face A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of having a round or square face? Do you wish to have an oval face shape? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to have an oval face. We will also provide you with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve your desired face shape. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine your face shape
  • Step 2: Opt for a shorter haircut
  • Step 3: Contour your face
  • Step 4: Choose the right hairstyle
  • Step 5: Maintain a healthy lifestyle
  • Tips for achieving an oval face shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair care for oval face shape

Step 1: Determine your face shape

The first step in achieving an oval face shape is to determine your current face shape. You can do this by looking at yourself in the mirror and examining your facial features. A round face has a circular shape with full cheeks, a square face has a strong jawline, and a heart-shaped face has a wider forehead and a narrower chin. Once you identify your face shape, you can move on to the next steps.

Step 2: Opt for a shorter haircut

If you have a round or square face, a shorter haircut can help you achieve an oval face shape. A shorter haircut will help to create the illusion of a longer face. However, make sure to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape. You can consult with a hairstylist to find the right haircut for you.

Step 3: Contour your face

Contouring is a makeup technique that can help you achieve an oval face shape. By using a darker shade of foundation or bronzer, you can create shadows on your face to make it appear slimmer. Apply the darker shade along your jawline, temples, and under your cheekbones. Blend it well to create a natural look.

Step 4: Choose the right hairstyle

The right hairstyle can make a big difference in achieving an oval face shape. Opt for a hairstyle that is longer on the top and shorter on the sides. This will help to elongate your face and create a more oval shape. Avoid hairstyles that have too much volume on the sides, as this can make your face appear wider.

Step 5: Maintain a healthy lifestyle

Maintaining a healthy lifestyle is important for achieving an oval face shape. Exercise regularly to keep your body in shape and maintain a healthy weight. This will help to prevent excess fat from accumulating on your face. Also, make sure to drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to keep your skin looking its best.

Tips for achieving an oval face shape

  • Use a lighter shade of foundation or concealer to highlight the center of your face, such as your forehead, nose, and chin, to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Wear earrings that are longer and more angular to help elongate your face.
  • Choose glasses that have a wider top than bottom to help balance out a round face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that have too much volume on the sides, as this can make your face appear wider.

FAQs

1. Can I achieve an oval face shape without surgery?

Yes, you can achieve an oval face shape without surgery. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can contour your face, choose the right hairstyle, and maintain a healthy lifestyle to achieve your desired face shape.

2. How long does it take to achieve an oval face shape?

The time it takes to achieve an oval face shape varies depending on your current face shape and the methods you use to achieve your desired shape. It can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months to achieve an oval face shape.

3. Will losing weight help me achieve an oval face shape?

Losing weight can help to prevent excess fat from accumulating on your face, which can make your face appear rounder. However, it is important to maintain a healthy weight and exercise regularly to achieve an oval face shape.

Hair care for oval face shape

Once you achieve an oval face shape, it is important to maintain it. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your new look:

  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and complements your features.
  • Avoid hairstyles that make your face appear wider or rounder.
  • Use hair products that add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it harder to style.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking its best.

By following the steps and tips outlined in this article, you can achieve an oval face shape and maintain it. Remember to be patient and consistent in your efforts, and you will see results!
