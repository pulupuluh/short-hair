How To Cut A Short Curly Bob Yourself

Written by Oliver Aug 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both trendy and easy to maintain, a short curly bob might be just what you need. Not only does it give you a fresh new look, but it also works great for those with naturally curly hair. In this blog post, we'll go over the steps on how to cut a short curly bob yourself, as well as some tips and frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

How To

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair
Before you start cutting your hair, make sure to wash and condition it to get rid of any dirt or build-up. This will also make your hair easier to manage and cut.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair
Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. This will help you see the natural shape of your curls and make it easier to cut them evenly.

Step 3: Section Your Hair
Divide your hair into four sections: two on the top of your head and two on the sides. Use hair clips or ties to keep each section in place.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair
Starting with the back sections, cut your hair to the desired length. You can use hair shears or regular scissors, but make sure they are sharp. Cut your hair in a straight line, following the natural shape of your hair.

Step 5: Cut the Front Sections
Moving on to the front sections, cut your hair at an angle to create a subtle layering effect. Make sure the length of the front sections is shorter than the back sections.

Step 6: Check for Balance
Check your hair for balance by comparing each section to the opposite side. Trim any uneven sections until you achieve the desired look.

Tips

Tip 1: Cut Your Hair When It's Dry
Cutting your hair when it's dry will help you see how your curls will fall naturally. This will also prevent your hair from looking shorter than expected after it dries.

Tip 2: Use Hair Shears
Using hair shears will give you a cleaner cut and prevent split ends.

Tip 3: Start with Small Cuts
It's better to start with small cuts than to cut too much hair at once. You can always trim more if needed.

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut my hair?
A: It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I cut my hair wet?
A: It's not recommended to cut curly hair when it's wet because it can shrink when it dries and lead to uneven cuts.

Q: Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?
A: While it's possible to cut your hair with regular scissors, it's recommended to use hair shears for a cleaner cut.

Hair Care

Now that you've cut your hair into a short curly bob, it's important to take care of it to maintain its shape and health. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner
Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep it hydrated.

Tip 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner
Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing it to keep it moisturized and prevent frizz.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling
Heat styling can damage your hair and lead to split ends. Try to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.

By following these tips and steps, you can cut a short curly bob yourself and maintain its health and shape. Have fun experimenting with your new hairstyle!
