How To Grow A Man Bun With Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine May 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your curly hair always being in your face? Want to try something new and trendy? Look no further than the man bun. While it may seem daunting to grow a man bun with curly hair, it's actually quite simple with the right steps and care. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to grow a man bun with curly hair, from start to finish.

Table of Contents

  • How to Grow a Man Bun with Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Growing a Man Bun with Curly Hair

How to Grow a Man Bun with Curly Hair

First things first, you need to have enough hair to create a man bun. Depending on how long you want your bun to be, you may need to grow your hair out for a few months to a year. Once you have enough length, follow these steps:

Step 1: Cleanse Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to cleanse your hair without stripping it of its natural oils, which can cause dryness and damage. Make sure to massage your scalp to promote blood flow and stimulate hair growth.

Step 2: Moisturize Your Hair

After washing, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair. This will help to moisturize and define your curls, making them easier to manage and style. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Brush Your Hair

To create a sleek and smooth man bun, you'll need to brush your hair regularly. Use a boar bristle brush to gently brush your hair from the roots to the ends. This will help to distribute your natural oils and prevent tangles.

Step 4: Tie Your Hair

Once your hair is long enough, you can start tying it into a bun. Use a hair tie or elastic band to secure your hair at the crown of your head. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or flyaways for a clean look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed breakdown of how to grow a man bun with curly hair:

Step 1: Set a Goal

Decide how long you want your hair to be and set a goal. This will help to keep you motivated and track your progress.

Step 2: Choose the Right Products

Invest in high-quality hair products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Look for sulfate-free shampoos, moisturizing conditioners, and leave-in treatments or curl creams.

Step 3: Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and nutrients can help to promote healthy hair growth. Focus on foods that are high in protein, iron, and vitamin C.

Step 4: Get Regular Trims

While it may seem counterintuitive, getting regular trims can actually help your hair to grow faster and healthier. Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

Tips for Growing a Man Bun with Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you grow a healthy and stylish man bun:

  • Be patient. Growing out your hair takes time, so don't get discouraged if it doesn't happen overnight.
  • Protect your hair from heat and sun damage by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and hair accessories to keep your look fresh and versatile.

FAQ

How long does it take to grow a man bun with curly hair?

It can take anywhere from a few months to a year or more, depending on how long you want your hair to be and how fast it grows.

Do I need to straighten my hair to create a man bun?

No, you can create a man bun with curly hair. In fact, many people prefer the natural texture and volume of curly hair.

What if my hair is too short to tie into a bun?

If your hair is too short to tie into a bun, you can try using hair extensions or a hairpiece to add length and volume.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair less frequently to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to cleanse and moisturize your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, and avoid using a brush.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, which can damage your hair and cause breakage.

By following these steps and tips, you can grow a stylish and healthy man bun with curly hair. Remember to be patient, take care of your hair, and have fun experimenting with different styles and accessories.
