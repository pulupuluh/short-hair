Mens Hair Style Long Top Short Sides A Trendy Look For The Modern Man

Written by Eveline Mar 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a haircut that is both stylish and low maintenance, then the mens hair style long top short sides might just be the perfect option for you. This haircut has been gaining popularity in recent years, and for good reason. It's versatile, easy to style, and suits a wide range of face shapes and hair types. In this article, we'll break down everything you need to know about this trendy haircut, including how to get it, style it, and care for it.

Description

The mens hair style long top short sides features longer hair on top that is styled in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. The sides and back are cut short, usually with clippers, and can be faded or tapered for a more gradual transition. This haircut is often paired with a beard or stubble for a rugged, masculine look.

How to Get the Mens Hair Style Long Top Short Sides

To get the mens hair style long top short sides, you'll need to visit a salon or barber shop. Make sure to bring a photo of the style you want, so your stylist can understand your vision. The stylist will use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair short, and scissors to trim the top to your desired length. Depending on the look you're going for, you may also ask for a fade, which is a gradual transition from short to long hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Mens Hair Style Long Top Short Sides

Step 1: Bring a photo of the mens hair style long top short sides to your stylist.

Step 2: Communicate your vision clearly to your stylist.

Step 3: The stylist will use clippers to cut the sides and back short.

Step 4: The stylist will use scissors to trim the top to your desired length.

Step 5: If desired, ask for a fade to create a gradual transition from short to long hair.

Tips for Styling the Mens Hair Style Long Top Short Sides

One of the great things about the mens hair style long top short sides is that it's versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Here are some tips for styling your hair:

- For a sleek look, use a pomade or wax to slick back the hair on top. - For a messy, textured look, use a sea salt spray or texturizing spray to add volume and texture. - Experiment with different parting styles to find what looks best on you. - Use a hair dryer and a round brush to add volume and shape to the top of your hair. - Don't be afraid to try different styles and products until you find what works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get my hair cut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your hair. However, this can vary depending on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to change your look.

Can I still get the mens hair style long top short sides if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! The mens hair style long top short sides can be adapted to suit a wide range of hair types, including curly hair. Talk to your stylist about the best way to cut and style your curly hair to achieve the look you want.

Do I need to use specific products to style my hair?

Not necessarily. The mens hair style long top short sides can be styled with a variety of products, including pomades, waxes, gels, and sprays. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and styling needs.

Hair Care Tips for the Mens Hair Style Long Top Short Sides

Maintaining your mens hair style long top short sides is relatively easy, but here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

- Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy. - Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools, such as a hair dryer or flat iron. - Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. - Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. - Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with UV protection.

The mens hair style long top short sides is a trendy and versatile haircut that can suit a wide range of men. Whether you prefer a sleek, polished look or a messy, textured style, this haircut can be adapted to suit your personal style and needs. With the right products and hair care routine, you can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish for weeks to come.
