How To Get Different Hairstyles In Animal Crossing Pocket Camp

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle for your character in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp? Do you want to switch it up and try out some new looks? Well, you're in luck because there are several ways to get different hairstyles in this game. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know to change up your character's hairstyle.

How to Get Different Hairstyles

There are a few different ways to get new hairstyles in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp. Here are your options:

  • Visit Harriet's Salon
  • Complete Goals
  • Purchase Leaf Tickets

Let's take a closer look at each of these options.

Visit Harriet's Salon

Harriet's Salon is located on the right side of the map, next to the Marketplace. You'll need to have a Salon Chair in your campsite in order to visit her. You can purchase a Salon Chair from the Market Place for 3,000 Bells.

Once you have a Salon Chair, you can visit Harriet to change your hairstyle. She will offer you a few different options, and you can choose the one you like best. Keep in mind that some hairstyles are only available to certain genders.

Complete Goals

Completing certain goals in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp can unlock new hairstyles. Check your goals frequently to see if any of them offer this reward.

Purchase Leaf Tickets

Finally, you can purchase Leaf Tickets with real money to buy new hairstyles. Leaf Tickets can be used to purchase items and services in the game, including hairstyles.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know your options, here's a step by step guide for changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp:

  1. Purchase a Salon Chair from the Marketplace for 3,000 Bells
  2. Place the Salon Chair in your campsite
  3. Tap on the Salon Chair to visit Harriet's Salon
  4. Select the hairstyle you want from the options available
  5. Confirm your choice

Tips for Getting the Hairstyle You Want

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when trying to get the hairstyle you want:

  • Some hairstyles are only available to certain genders, so make sure you're selecting from the right options
  • Check your goals frequently to see if any of them offer new hairstyles as a reward
  • If you're really set on a particular hairstyle, you may need to purchase Leaf Tickets to get it

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about getting different hairstyles in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp:

Can I change my hairstyle at any time?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle whenever you want as long as you have a Salon Chair in your campsite.

Do I need to pay real money to get new hairstyles?

No, you can get new hairstyles by completing certain goals or visiting Harriet's Salon. However, some hairstyles may only be available for purchase with Leaf Tickets, which you can buy with real money.

Hair Care in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp

In Animal Crossing Pocket Camp, your character's hair can get messy if you don't take care of it. To keep your hair looking its best, make sure to visit Harriet's Salon regularly and keep an eye on your goals for opportunities to unlock new hairstyles.

With these tips, you should be able to easily get the hairstyle you want in Animal Crossing Pocket Camp. Have fun experimenting with different looks and showing off your new 'do to your friends!
