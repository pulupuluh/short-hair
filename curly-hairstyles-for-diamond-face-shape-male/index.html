Curly Hairstyles For Diamond Face Shape Male

Written by Bardi Sep 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are a male with a diamond face shape, finding the right hairstyle can be a bit of a challenge. The diamond face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead, wide cheekbones, and a pointed chin. However, with the right curly hairstyle, you can accentuate your features and look your best. In this article, we will discuss some of the best curly hairstyles for diamond face shape male.

Description

The diamond face shape is one of the most unique and rare face shapes. If you have a diamond face shape, your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrower. Your face also has a pointed chin. The key to finding the right hairstyle for a diamond face shape male is to balance out the width of the cheekbones with the narrowness of the forehead and jawline.

How To

The first step in finding the right curly hairstyle for a diamond face shape male is to determine the length of your hair. Curly hairstyles work best for medium to long hair lengths. If you have short hair, you can still opt for a curly hairstyle, but you may need to add some length to your hair first.

Once you have determined the length of your hair, you can start looking for curly hairstyles that will accentuate your features. One of the best curly hairstyles for diamond face shape male is the curly fringe. This hairstyle features curly hair on top of the head that is styled into a fringe. The curly fringe adds volume to the forehead and balances out the width of the cheekbones.

Step By Step

To achieve the curly fringe hairstyle, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.
  4. Style the top of your hair into a curly fringe using a curling iron or your fingers.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

When choosing a curly hairstyle for a diamond face shape male, it's important to consider the width of your cheekbones. Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as the curly fringe.

Another tip is to consider the shape of your hairline. If you have a high hairline, avoid hairstyles that expose your forehead, as this can make your face appear even longer. Instead, opt for hairstyles that have a side-swept fringe or a longer fringe that covers your forehead.

FAQ

What other curly hairstyles work for diamond face shape male?

Other curly hairstyles that work well for diamond face shape male include the curly undercut, curly quiff, and curly side part. These hairstyles add volume to the top of the head and balance out the width of the cheekbones.

Can I wear my curly hair down if I have a diamond face shape?

Yes, you can wear your curly hair down if you have a diamond face shape. However, it's important to avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your head. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as the curly fringe.

Hair Care

To maintain your curly hairstyle, it's important to use the right hair care products. Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair before blow-drying with a diffuser attachment. Use a hairspray to hold the style in place.

It's also important to avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make your curls dry and frizzy. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days and use dry shampoo in between washes to refresh your hair.

Conclusion

If you have a diamond face shape male and curly hair, there are many hairstyles that can accentuate your features and make you look your best. The key is to balance out the width of your cheekbones with the narrowness of your forehead and jawline. Experiment with different curly hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.
