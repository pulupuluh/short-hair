Short Haircuts For Round Face Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Are you tired of dealing with long, unruly curls on a round face? It's time to switch up your look with a short haircut that complements your features. In this guide, we'll discuss the best short haircuts for round face curly hair, how to style them, and tips for maintaining your new 'do. Let's dive in!

Types of Short Haircuts for Round Face Curly Hair

There are plenty of short haircuts that flatter a round face and curly hair. Here are some popular options:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, low-maintenance haircut that's perfect for busy women. It looks great on round faces and curly hair because it adds height to the crown and creates a more elongated face shape.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic, versatile haircut that can be tailored to suit any face shape and hair type. For round face curly hair, a chin-length bob with layers is a great option because it creates movement and volume without adding width to the face.

Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut is a bold, edgy haircut that's perfect for women who want to make a statement. It's also a great option for round face curly hair because it creates the illusion of a slimmer face shape.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Face Shape and Hair Type

When choosing a short haircut for round face curly hair, there are a few key factors to consider:

  • Face Shape: Round faces look best with haircuts that add height and length to the face. Avoid haircuts that add width or volume to the sides.
  • Hair Type: Curly hair looks best with haircuts that create movement and volume without adding too much bulk.
  • Lifestyle: Consider your daily routine and how much time you want to spend styling your hair. Choose a low-maintenance haircut if you're always on the go.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Hair

Styling short curly hair may seem daunting at first, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a gorgeous look in no time. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as mousse or gel.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls while maintaining their shape.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need more definition.
  5. Finish off with a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair

Maintaining short curly hair requires a little extra care and attention, but it's worth it for the gorgeous results. Here are some tips:

  • Invest in a good-quality shampoo and conditioner that's specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls when they're wet.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause it to lose its curl pattern.

FAQs About Short Haircuts for Round Face Curly Hair

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the haircut. Avoid haircuts that add width to the sides of your face, as this can make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for haircuts that add height and length to your face.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with a short haircut?

A: Depending on the length of your hair, you may be able to wear it in a small ponytail or bun. However, shorter haircuts may not allow for this style.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

Short curly hair requires a little extra care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Use lukewarm water instead.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or coloring products on your hair, as these can damage your curls.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as this can cause it to become dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

There you have it - everything you need to know about short haircuts for round face curly hair. We hope this guide has inspired you to switch up your look and embrace your natural curls. Remember, the key to beautiful hair is taking care of it!
