How To Cut Hair At Home With Youtube A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to save some money on haircuts or simply want to learn a new skill, cutting your own hair at home can be a great option. Fortunately, with the help of YouTube and some basic tools, you can achieve a salon-worthy look from the comfort of your own home. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut hair at home using YouTube as your guide.

How to Cut Hair at Home: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before getting started, you'll need to gather all the necessary tools. This includes a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. You may also want to invest in a hair cutting cape or a towel to protect your clothing.

Step 2: Choose Your YouTube Tutorials

Next, you'll want to find YouTube tutorials that match the style you're looking to achieve. There are a multitude of channels and videos available, so make sure to do your research and find the ones that are most helpful for your specific needs.

Step 3: Watch and Learn

Once you've found the tutorials you want to follow, watch them a few times to get a good idea of what you'll need to do. Make sure to pay attention to the techniques used and the tools required.

Step 4: Start with Small Snips

When you're ready to start cutting, it's best to begin with small snips. This will help you avoid making any major mistakes and allow you to gradually work your way towards the final look.

Step 5: Take Your Time

Cutting hair at home can be stressful, but it's important to take your time and be patient. Rushing through the process can lead to uneven cuts and mistakes that are difficult to fix.

Step 6: Use the Right Techniques

As you work through the haircut, make sure to use the right techniques for each section of hair. This may include point cutting, layering, or using thinning shears.

Step 7: Check Your Progress

Throughout the haircut, take breaks to check your progress in the mirror. This will help you make sure you're on track and catch any mistakes before they become too difficult to fix.

Step 8: Clean Up and Finish

Once you've finished cutting, take some time to clean up any stray hairs and make sure the cut is even. You may also want to style the hair to complete the look.

Tips for Cutting Hair at Home

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting your hair at home:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or overwhelmed
  • Consider investing in professional-grade tools if you plan to cut your hair regularly
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you get stuck

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really cut my own hair at home?

Yes! With the right tools and techniques, cutting your own hair at home can be a great way to save money and learn a new skill.

What tools do I need to cut my hair at home?

You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. You may also want to invest in a hair cutting cape or a towel to protect your clothing.

How do I choose the right YouTube tutorials?

Do your research and find tutorials that match the style you're looking to achieve. Look for videos that are clear and easy to follow, and watch them a few times before getting started.

Hair Care After Cutting Hair

After cutting your hair, it's important to take good care of it to maintain the look and keep it healthy. This may include using a quality shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and getting regular trims to keep the ends healthy.

Overall, cutting your own hair at home can be a rewarding experience that saves you money and gives you a sense of accomplishment. With the help of YouTube and some basic tools, you can achieve great results and enjoy a fresh new look without leaving your house.
