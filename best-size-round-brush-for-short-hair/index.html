Best Size Round Brush For Short Hair

Written by Christine May 20, 2023 · 7 min read

In this article, we will discuss the best size round brush for short hair. We will provide you with a detailed guide on how to choose the right size, how to use it, and how to take care of your hair. If you have short hair and want to add some volume and body to your hair, keep reading.

How to Choose the Right Size Round Brush for Short Hair

Choosing the right size round brush is essential when styling short hair. The size of the brush will depend on the length of your hair and the desired style. Here are some tips on how to choose the right size:

1. Length of Hair

If you have short hair, you should choose a round brush with a smaller barrel size. A barrel size of 1 to 1.5 inches is perfect for short hair. If your hair is shoulder-length or longer, you can opt for a larger barrel size.

2. Hair Texture

If you have fine hair, you should choose a smaller barrel size to create more volume. If you have thick hair, you can opt for a larger barrel size to tame your hair.

How to Use a Round Brush on Short Hair

Using a round brush on short hair can be a bit tricky, but with a little practice, you can achieve a salon-worthy look. Here are some steps to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide on Using a Round Brush on Short Hair

1. Prep Your Hair

Before using a round brush, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to style your hair.

3. Start Styling

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the brush. Use a hairdryer to blow-dry your hair while keeping the brush in place. Repeat this process until all sections of hair are styled.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Using a Round Brush on Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using a round brush on short hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling.
  • Choose a brush with natural bristles to avoid static and frizz.
  • Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go.
  • Don't use too much tension when using a round brush to prevent breakage.
  • Use a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

FAQ

What is the best size round brush for short hair?

The best size round brush for short hair is a barrel size of 1 to 1.5 inches.

What type of brush should I use for short hair?

You should use a round brush with natural bristles for short hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep your short hair healthy and looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and the elements by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Now that you know how to choose the right size round brush for short hair and how to use it, you can achieve a salon-worthy look at home. Just remember to use a heat protectant spray, choose a brush with natural bristles, and finish your look with a light hairspray. Follow our hair care tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and gorgeous.
