How To Cut My Own Hair Military Style A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Mar 09, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts every few weeks? Do you want to maintain a short, clean-cut look without having to visit the barber regularly? If you answered yes to these questions, then you might be interested in learning how to cut your own hair military style. In this article, we’ll walk you through the process of achieving the perfect military haircut at home. Here’s what we’ll cover:

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Your Own Hair Military Style
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Great Military Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After the Cut

How to Cut Your Own Hair Military Style

The military haircut is a classic, low-maintenance style that can be achieved with a few basic tools and techniques. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Clipper set with guards
  • Mirror
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or towel

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your tools ready, let’s get started on the haircut:

  1. Begin by washing your hair and towel drying it until it’s damp. This will make it easier to cut.
  2. Attach the longest guard to your clippers and start cutting your hair at the top of your head. Move the clippers in the direction of your hair growth to create an even cut.
  3. Gradually work your way down to the sides and back of your head, using shorter guards as you go. Start with a #4 guard for the sides and back, and then switch to a #3 guard for a shorter cut.
  4. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers missed. Comb the hair up and trim across the top to create a straight line.
  5. Blend the sides and back into the top by using a #2 guard to taper the hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a flicking motion to create a seamless transition.
  6. Use the clippers without a guard to clean up the back of your neck and around your ears.
  7. Comb the hair forward and trim the bangs straight across, leaving them slightly longer than the rest of the hair.
  8. Check your work in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.
  9. Clean up any loose hairs with a brush or towel.

Tips for a Great Military Haircut

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect military haircut:

  • Always cut your hair in a well-lit area with a mirror that allows you to see the back of your head.
  • Use a steady hand and take your time to ensure an even cut.
  • Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Keep the clippers clean and oiled to maintain their performance.
  • Experiment with different guards to find the perfect length for your hair.

FAQs

Here are some common questions and answers about cutting your own hair military style:

  • Can I cut my hair military style without clippers?
    While it’s possible to cut your hair with scissors, clippers make the process much easier and more precise.
  • What if I mess up my haircut?
    If you make a mistake, don’t panic. You can always go shorter or visit a barber to fix any issues.
  • How often should I cut my hair military style?
    Most people opt for a military haircut every two to three weeks to maintain the look.

Hair Care After the Cut

After you’ve cut your hair military style, it’s important to take care of it properly to maintain the look. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner every two to three days.
  • Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair to keep it in place.
  • Visit a barber regularly to maintain the shape of the haircut.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a perfect military haircut at home. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and practice regularly to improve your skills. Good luck!
