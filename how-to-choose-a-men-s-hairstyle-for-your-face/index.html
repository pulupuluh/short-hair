How To Choose A Men s Hairstyle For Your Face

Written by Petter Jul 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle, it's not just about what's trending or popular. The key to finding the perfect hairstyle is to choose one that complements your face shape. In this article, we'll show you how to choose a men's hairstyle for your face shape, provide tips for maintaining your hair, and answer common questions about hair care.

Table of Contents

  1. Determining Your Face Shape
  2. Hairstyle Options for Different Face Shapes
  3. Tips for Hair Care
  4. FAQs

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six common face shapes:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Oval
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Look at the shape of your face and compare it to the descriptions above.

Hairstyle Options for Different Face Shapes

Once you've determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that complements it. Here are some options:

Round Face

For a round face, choose a hairstyle that adds length to your face. A short back and sides with a longer top can help elongate your face. Avoid hairstyles that add width, such as a full fringe or a buzz cut.

Square Face

A square face has a strong jawline, so choose a hairstyle that softens it. A textured crop or a side-swept fringe can help balance your features. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too sharp.

Oval Face

An oval face is considered the ideal face shape, so you have more hairstyle options. A classic side part or a messy fringe can work well. Avoid hairstyles that cover your forehead, as this can make your face appear shorter.

Heart Face

A heart-shaped face has a wider forehead and a narrow chin. A short back and sides with a longer top can help balance your features. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as this can make your forehead appear even wider.

Diamond Face

A diamond-shaped face has a narrow forehead and chin, with wider cheekbones. A textured crop or a messy fringe can help balance your features. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long.

Triangle Face

A triangle face has a narrow forehead and wider jawline. A textured crop or a side-swept fringe can help balance your features. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your jawline appear even wider.

Tips for Hair Care

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as hair dryers or straighteners.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy.
  • Use a styling product that works for your hair type, such as gel or pomade.

FAQs

How often should I get my hair cut?

You should get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and maintain your hairstyle.

What's the best styling product for my hair?

The best styling product depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Ask your hairstylist for recommendations.

Can I dye my hair?

Yes, you can dye your hair, but it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and natural hair color.

How can I prevent hair loss?

To prevent hair loss, maintain a healthy diet, avoid smoking, and reduce stress levels. You can also use hair loss prevention products, such as minoxidil or finasteride.

Choosing the right hairstyle can make a big difference in your appearance. By determining your face shape and choosing a hairstyle that complements it, you can look and feel your best. Remember to take care of your hair by washing it regularly, using conditioner, and avoiding too much heat. With these tips, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.
