Best Short Haircuts For Square Faces Female A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Feb 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Square faces have a strong and angular jawline, which can make it difficult to find the perfect haircut that complements your facial features. However, with the right short haircut, you can soften your features and highlight your best attributes. In this article, we have compiled a list of the best short haircuts for square faces female that will flatter your face shape and enhance your beauty.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Square Faces Female
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintenance and Styling
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Square Faces Female

Description

Square faces are characterized by a distinct jawline that is almost as wide as the forehead. This face shape can look strong and angular, but with the right short haircut, you can soften your features and create a more feminine look. Short haircuts that add volume to the top of the head or have layers around the face can create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. The key is to find a haircut that balances your features and creates harmony with your face shape.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Square Faces Female

When choosing a short haircut for square faces female, it's important to consider the following factors:

  • Face Shape: Square faces need haircuts that soften the angles and create a more oval shape.
  • Hair Texture: Straight hair can be difficult to style, while curly or wavy hair can add volume and texture to a short haircut.
  • Personal Style: Your short haircut should reflect your personal style and enhance your natural beauty.

Some of the best short haircuts for square faces female are:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that can look great on square faces. This style is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top that can be styled in different ways. The pixie cut can add volume to the top of the head, which can create the illusion of a longer face.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular short haircut for square faces. A chin-length bob can soften the jawline and create a more feminine look. Adding layers around the face can also create the illusion of a longer face shape.

Short Shag

The short shag is a layered haircut that can add volume and texture to a short hairstyle. This style can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair textures. For square faces, a short shag with layers around the face can soften the angles and create a more oval shape.

Step by Step Guide

If you have decided to get a short haircut for square faces female, follow these steps:

  • Research different short haircuts for square faces female and find a style that you like.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best short haircut for your face shape and hair texture.
  • Bring a photo of the haircut you want to your appointment.
  • Ask your hairstylist to customize the haircut to suit your face shape and hair texture.
  • After the haircut, style your hair according to your preferences.

Tips for Maintenance and Styling

Here are some tips for maintaining and styling your short haircut:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume to the roots.
  • Use a curling iron or flat iron to create different hairstyles.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your short haircut.

FAQs

Can square faces female wear bangs with short haircuts?

Yes, square faces female can wear bangs with short haircuts. However, it's important to choose a bang style that flatters your face shape. Side-swept bangs or wispy bangs can soften the angles of a square face.

What hair color looks best on square faces female with short haircuts?

The best hair color for square faces female with short haircuts is one that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best hair color for your face shape and skin tone.

Hair Care Tips for Square Faces Female

To keep your short haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your short haircut.

By following these tips and choosing the right short haircut, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a more feminine look. Whether you prefer a pixie cut, a bob cut, or a short shag, there is a perfect short haircut for square faces female that will flatter your face shape and highlight your best features.
