How To Style Short Natural Black Hair At Home

Written by Eveline Jul 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short natural black hair can be tricky to style, especially if you're used to having longer hair. However, with the right techniques and products, you can create a variety of beautiful and versatile looks right in the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll show you how to style short natural black hair at home, step-by-step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Natural Black Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Natural Black Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Short Natural Black Hair

How to Style Short Natural Black Hair

Before you begin styling your short natural black hair, it's important to understand your hair type and texture. Natural black hair can range from coarse and kinky to soft and curly, so it's essential to use products that work well with your specific hair type. Here are some popular styling options for short natural black hair:

  • Bantu knots
  • Twist-outs
  • Braid-outs
  • Finger coils
  • Afro puffs

Each of these styles can be achieved with products like curl defining creams, gels, and edge control. You can also incorporate accessories like headbands, scarves, and hairpins to add some extra flair to your look.

Step-by-Step Guide

To achieve a twist-out on short natural black hair, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a curl defining cream or gel to your damp hair, making sure to distribute the product evenly from root to tip.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections and twist each section tightly from root to tip.
  4. Allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until completely dry.
  5. Untwist each section carefully, using a small amount of oil or serum to prevent frizz.
  6. Fluff and style your hair as desired.

For a braid-out, follow the same steps but instead of twisting your hair, braid each section tightly from root to tip. You can also experiment with different parting techniques and braid sizes to create a variety of unique looks.

Tips for Styling Short Natural Black Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short natural black hair:

  • Use products that work well with your specific hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping with a satin bonnet or scarf.
  • Experiment with different styles and accessories to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short natural black hair:

How often should I wash my hair?

It's generally recommended to wash natural hair once a week or every other week, depending on your hair type and texture. Overwashing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and breakage.

What are some good hair care products for short natural black hair?

Some popular hair care products for short natural black hair include curl defining creams, leave-in conditioners, and edge control. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture.

Hair Care for Short Natural Black Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful natural black hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping with a satin bonnet or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and overall health.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently style your short natural black hair at home and show off your unique and beautiful look.
