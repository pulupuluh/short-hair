How To Tell If A Hairstyle Will Suit You

Written by Robby Sep 03, 2023 Â· 12 min read

Are you tired of constantly changing hairstyles and never feeling satisfied with the results? It can be frustrating to spend time and money on a new haircut or style, only to realize it doesn't flatter your face shape or overall look. Fortunately, there are some easy ways to determine whether a hairstyle will suit you before you commit. In this article, we'll provide step-by-step instructions and tips for how to tell if a hairstyle will suit you.

Table of Contents

  2. Consider Your Hair Type
  3. Think About Your Lifestyle
  4. Schedule a Hair Consultation
  5. Try On Wigs or Use Virtual Try-On Tools
  6. Take Care of Your Hair
  7. FAQs

1. Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in figuring out whether a hairstyle will suit you is to determine your face shape. This will help you understand which types of hairstyles will flatter your features and which won't. The four main face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart-shaped

Once you've figured out your face shape, you can research hairstyles that suit your specific features. For example, if you have a round face, you might want to avoid short, blunt cuts and opt for longer layers instead.

2. Consider Your Hair Type

In addition to your face shape, you'll also want to consider your hair type when choosing a hairstyle. Thick, curly hair may require a different cut than thin, straight hair. You'll also want to think about whether your hair is fine or coarse, oily or dry, and whether you have any specific hair concerns like split ends or breakage.

3. Think About Your Lifestyle

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your lifestyle. If you're someone who prefers low-maintenance styles, you'll want to opt for a cut that requires minimal styling. On the other hand, if you enjoy experimenting with different looks and have the time to style your hair every day, you might want to consider a more complex cut or style.

4. Schedule a Hair Consultation

If you're still unsure about which hairstyle will suit you best, consider scheduling a consultation with a hair stylist. A professional stylist can assess your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, and recommend styles that will flatter your features and fit your daily routine. They can also provide tips for styling and maintaining your new cut or style.

5. Try On Wigs or Use Virtual Try-On Tools

If you're hesitant to commit to a new cut or style, consider trying on wigs or using virtual try-on tools to see how different hairstyles will look on you. This can be a fun and low-risk way to experiment with different looks before committing to a permanent change.

6. Take Care of Your Hair

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that suits you, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. This includes regular washing and conditioning, using heat protectant products when styling, and getting regular trims to avoid split ends and breakage.

FAQs

How can I tell if a short haircut will suit me?

Short haircuts can be flattering on a variety of face shapes and hair types. However, it's important to choose a style that complements your features and fits your lifestyle. Consider factors like your face shape, hair texture, and daily routine when choosing a short haircut.

What hairstyles are best for round faces?

Hairstyles that elongate the face and create the illusion of length are typically flattering on round faces. This may include long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts. Avoid blunt, chin-length cuts that can make the face appear rounder.

Can I pull off a pixie cut?

Pixie cuts can be a bold and stylish choice for those with the right face shape and hair type. If you have a heart-shaped face or defined cheekbones, a pixie cut can be especially flattering. However, if you have a round or square face, you may want to opt for a longer cut or style.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

Regular trims are important for maintaining the health and appearance of your hair. It's generally recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth. However, the frequency of trims may vary depending on your hair type and length.

What products should I use to protect my hair from heat damage?

To protect your hair from heat damage when styling, use a heat protectant product before using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil or keratin, which help to nourish and protect the hair.

Can I dye my hair if it's damaged?

If your hair is already damaged, it's important to take steps to repair and strengthen it before coloring. Consider using a deep conditioning treatment or hair mask to restore moisture and reduce breakage. It's also a good idea to consult with a stylist to determine whether coloring is safe for your hair type and condition.

How can I style my hair quickly in the morning?

If you're short on time in the morning, consider opting for low-maintenance hairstyles like messy buns or ponytails. You can also try using dry shampoo or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to second-day hair.

What types of hairstyles are best for curly hair?

Curly hair can be styled in a variety of ways, depending on your hair type and personal preference. Consider styles like loose waves, bouncy curls, or a curly bob. It's important to use products that nourish and define curls, such as curl-enhancing creams or gels.

Can I style my hair without using heat?

Absolutely! There are many heat-free styling options that can help you achieve a variety of looks. Consider styles like braids, buns, or twists, which can be done without using hot tools. You can also use accessories like headbands or scarves to add interest to your hairstyle.

What should I do if I don't like my new haircut?

If you're unhappy with your new haircut, don't panic! Talk to your stylist about what you don't like and see if they can make any adjustments or suggest a different style. It's important to communicate openly with your stylist to ensure you're both on the same page.

How can I prevent hair breakage?

There are several ways to prevent hair breakage, including using a wide-toothed comb or detangling brush, avoiding tight hairstyles, and protecting your hair from heat damage. It's also important to maintain a healthy diet and lifestyle, as well as using products that nourish and strengthen the hair.

What should I do if my hair is thinning?

If you're experiencing hair thinning or loss, it's important to consult with a medical professional to determine the underlying cause. In some cases, thinning hair may be a sign of a medical condition or hormonal imbalance. There are also products and treatments available to help promote hair growth and improve the overall appearance of thinning hair.

How can I prevent frizzy hair?

To prevent frizzy hair, it's important to use products that moisturize and nourish the hair, such as leave-in conditioners or oils. You can also try using a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to help distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz. Avoid brushing or touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz to worsen.

What can I do if my hair color fades too quickly?

If your hair color fades too quickly, consider using a color-protecting shampoo and conditioner. You can also try using a hair mask or treatment specifically formulated for colored hair to help maintain vibrancy. Avoid using hot tools or washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause color to fade more quickly.
