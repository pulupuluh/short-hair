Best Hair Products For Volume Fine Hair 2020 How To Get That Perfect Bounce

Written by Daniel Apr 14, 2023 · 10 min read

Are you tired of dealing with flat, lifeless hair? Do you want to add volume and bounce to your locks? If you have fine hair, finding the right hair products can be a challenge. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll share with you the best hair products for volume fine hair 2020.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Volume in Fine Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Volume
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

Description

When it comes to adding volume to fine hair, the right hair products can make all the difference. But with so many products on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right ones. That’s why we’ve put together a list of the best hair products for volume fine hair 2020. These products have been tried and tested by professionals and consumers alike, and they have proven to be effective in adding volume and bounce to fine hair. From shampoos and conditioners to styling products and tools, we’ve got you covered.

How to Get Volume in Fine Hair

Before we dive into the best hair products for volume fine hair 2020, let’s talk about how to get volume in fine hair. Here are some tips:

  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Tease your hair at the crown for added volume.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying to add volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know how to get volume in fine hair, let’s dive into the best hair products for volume fine hair 2020. Here is a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

The foundation of any good hair routine is a good shampoo and conditioner. For fine hair, look for products that are specifically designed to add volume. Some of the best options include:

  • Living Proof Full Shampoo and Conditioner: This duo is designed to add volume to fine hair without weighing it down. It also contains a patented healthy hair molecule that helps to smooth and protect hair.
  • Bumble and Bumble Thickening Shampoo and Conditioner: This duo is infused with ingredients like panthenol and wheat protein to help plump up fine hair.
  • Oribe Shampoo for Magnificent Volume: This shampoo is designed to gently cleanse fine hair while adding volume and shine. It also contains keratin and hydrolyzed vegetable protein to strengthen hair.

Step 2: Use a Volumizing Mousse or Spray

To add even more volume to your fine hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray. These products are designed to be applied to damp hair and work by adding texture and lift. Some of the best options include:

  • Kenra Volume Mousse: This mousse provides long-lasting volume and hold without leaving hair feeling sticky or crunchy.
  • Oribe Maximista Thickening Spray: This spray is designed to add volume, thickness, and texture to fine hair. It also provides heat protection up to 450 degrees.
  • Redken Guts 10 Volumizing Spray: This spray provides root lift and volume without weighing down hair. It also contains a UV filter to protect hair from sun damage.

Step 3: Finish with a Volumizing Hairspray

To set your style and ensure that your volume lasts all day, finish with a volumizing hairspray. These sprays are designed to provide hold and lift without weighing down your hair. Some of the best options include:

  • Moroccanoil Luminous Hairspray Strong: This hairspray provides long-lasting hold and shine without leaving hair feeling sticky or stiff.
  • TRESemmé Compressed Micro Mist Hairspray: This hairspray provides a flexible hold that lasts all day. It also comes in an eco-friendly compressed can.
  • Kenra Volume Spray 25: This hairspray provides super hold and humidity resistance without weighing down hair. It also dries quickly and leaves no residue.

Tips for Maintaining Volume

Now that you know the best hair products for volume fine hair 2020, here are some tips for maintaining volume:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it more difficult to add volume.
  • Avoid heavy products like oils and serums, as these can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil and add volume.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can flatten your roots and undo your volume.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to maintain volume overnight.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair products for volume fine hair 2020:

Q: Can I use these products if I have color-treated hair?

A: Yes, all of the products on this list are safe for color-treated hair.

Q: Can I use these products if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, but keep in mind that some of the products may be more effective than others for curly hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, like a curl-enhancing mousse or spray.

Q: How often should I use these products?

A: It depends on your hair type and how often you wash your hair. As a general rule, you should use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner every time you wash your hair, and a volumizing mousse or spray as needed.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for fine hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling wands too often, as these can damage your hair and make it more difficult to add volume.
  • Avoid using heavy conditioners, as these can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this is less likely to cause breakage than a brush.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids, as these can pull on your hair and make it more difficult to add volume.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best hair products for volume fine hair 2020, it’s time to start adding some bounce to your locks. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve the volume and body you’ve always wanted.
