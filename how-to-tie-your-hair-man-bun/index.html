How To Tie Your Hair Man Bun A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking for something new? The man bun is a popular hairstyle that has been around for a while now and is still going strong. The man bun is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways, from sleek and sophisticated to messy and casual. In this article, we will show you how to tie your hair man bun in a relaxed English language step-by-step guide.

How to tie your hair man bun

The man bun is a simple hairstyle that can be achieved in just a few steps. Follow the steps below to create the perfect man bun:

Step 1: Gather your hair

The first step in creating a man bun is to gather your hair into a ponytail. Use a brush or your fingers to smooth out any bumps or tangles in your hair. This will help to create a sleek and polished look.

Step 2: Twist your hair

Once you have gathered your hair into a ponytail, twist it tightly. The tighter you twist your hair, the smaller your man bun will be. If you prefer a larger bun, twist your hair loosely.

Step 3: Wrap your hair around the base of the ponytail

Wrap your twisted hair around the base of the ponytail, forming a bun. Use a hair tie to secure the bun in place. Make sure that the hair tie is tight enough to keep the bun in place, but not so tight that it pulls on your hair.

Step 4: Tuck in any loose ends

If you have any loose ends sticking out of your bun, tuck them in using bobby pins. This will give your bun a more polished look.

Tips for tying your hair man bun

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect man bun:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • If you have shorter hair, use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
  • Experiment with different bun sizes to find the one that works best for you.
  • If you have thick hair, use a hair tie and bobby pins to secure your bun in place.

FAQ about tying your hair man bun

What length should my hair be to create a man bun?

Your hair should be at least shoulder-length to create a man bun. If your hair is shorter than that, you may have trouble securing it into a bun.

Can I create a man bun with curly hair?

Yes, you can create a man bun with curly hair. Just make sure to smooth out any bumps or tangles in your hair before tying it into a bun.

How long does my hair need to be to create a large man bun?

Your hair should be at least mid-back length to create a large man bun. If your hair is shorter than that, you may not be able to achieve a large bun.

Hair care for tying your hair man bun

Tying your hair into a man bun can put stress on your hair and scalp. To keep your hair healthy, follow these tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to tie your hair man bun, you can experiment with different styles and sizes to find the one that works best for you. Whether you prefer a sleek and sophisticated look or a more casual and messy style, the man bun is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways.
