How To Control Hair Fall During Menopause Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia May 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Menopause is a natural process that every woman goes through, but it can come with some unpleasant symptoms, such as hair fall. Hair fall during menopause is common due to hormonal changes, but there are ways to control it. In this article, we will discuss how to control hair fall during menopause and provide some tips and tricks to help you maintain healthy and beautiful hair.

Table of Contents

  • What is Menopause?
  • Why Does Hair Fall During Menopause?
  • How to Control Hair Fall During Menopause
  • Hair Care Tips During Menopause
  • FAQ

What is Menopause?

Menopause is a natural biological process that marks the end of a woman's fertility. It typically occurs between the ages of 45 and 55, but can happen earlier or later. During menopause, a woman's body goes through hormonal changes that can cause a variety of symptoms, including hair fall.

Why Does Hair Fall During Menopause?

Estrogen is a hormone that helps promote hair growth and thickness. During menopause, a woman's estrogen levels decrease, which can cause hair to become thinner and fall out more easily. Additionally, menopause can cause stress, which can also contribute to hair fall.

How to Control Hair Fall During Menopause

There are several ways to control hair fall during menopause. Here are some tips:

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet that is rich in nutrients can help promote hair growth and prevent hair fall. Some foods that are good for your hair include:

  • Protein-rich foods like meat, fish, and eggs
  • Leafy greens like spinach and kale
  • Whole grains like brown rice and quinoa
  • Fruits and vegetables like blueberries, carrots, and sweet potatoes

Take Supplements

Taking supplements can also help promote hair growth and prevent hair fall. Some supplements that are good for your hair include:

  • Biotin
  • Vitamin D
  • Vitamin E
  • Iron

Use Hair Products for Thinning Hair

Using hair products that are specifically designed for thinning hair can help make your hair look thicker and fuller. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and niacin.

Reduce Stress

Reducing stress can also help prevent hair fall. Some ways to reduce stress include:

  • Meditation
  • Yoga
  • Deep breathing exercises
  • Taking a warm bath

Consult a Doctor

If your hair fall is severe, you may want to consult a doctor. They can prescribe medications or recommend treatments that can help promote hair growth and prevent hair fall.

Hair Care Tips During Menopause

In addition to the tips listed above, there are some hair care tips that can help prevent hair fall during menopause:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair
  • Avoid chemical treatments like coloring and perms
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet

FAQ

Q: Is hair fall during menopause normal?

A: Yes, hair fall during menopause is normal due to hormonal changes.

Q: Will my hair grow back after menopause?

A: It is possible for your hair to grow back after menopause, but it may take some time and effort.

Q: Should I see a doctor if I am experiencing severe hair fall?

A: Yes, if your hair fall is severe, you should consult a doctor.


By following these tips and tricks, you can control hair fall during menopause and maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Remember to eat a healthy diet, take supplements, use hair products for thinning hair, reduce stress, and consult a doctor if necessary. With a little effort and care, you can keep your hair looking its best during this natural transition.
