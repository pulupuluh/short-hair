How To Change Hairstyle In Pokemon Go

Pokemon Go is one of the most popular mobile games in the world, with millions of players worldwide. One of the most exciting features of the game is the ability to customize your character's appearance, including changing their hairstyle. In this article, we will show you how to change your hairstyle in Pokemon Go, step by step.

How to Change Your Hairstyle in Pokemon Go

Changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go is a simple process that can be done in just a few steps. Here's what you need to do:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Open the Pokemon Go app on your mobile device.

Step 2: Tap on the "Main Menu" icon in the bottom right corner of the screen.

Step 3: Tap on the "Style" option.

Step 4: Tap on the "Hair" option.

Step 5: Choose your preferred hairstyle from the available options.

Step 6: Tap on the "Confirm" button to save your new hairstyle.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

Choosing the perfect hairstyle for your Pokemon Go character can be a fun and exciting process. Here are some tips to help you make the right choice:

  • Consider your character's personality and style.
  • Think about the type of Pokemon your character likes to catch.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your character's outfit.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits your character best.

FAQs

Q: How many hairstyles are available in Pokemon Go?

A: There are currently 10 different hairstyles available in the game.

Q: Can I change my hairstyle more than once in Pokemon Go?

A: Yes, you can change your hairstyle as many times as you like.

Q: Do I need to pay real money to change my hairstyle in Pokemon Go?

A: No, changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go is completely free.

Hair Care Tips for Pokemon Go Players

Playing Pokemon Go can be a lot of fun, but it can also be tiring for your body and your hair. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair while playing the game:

  • Wear a hat or headband to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, such as straighteners or curling irons.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great while enjoying all the fun and excitement of Pokemon Go.

Changing your hairstyle in Pokemon Go is a great way to customize your character and make them stand out from the crowd. With these easy steps, you can change your hairstyle as often as you like and experiment with different looks. So why not give it a try today?
