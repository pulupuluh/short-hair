Is Square Face Pretty Everything You Need To Know About This Face Shape

Written by Robby Aug 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Having a square face shape is not uncommon, but it can sometimes leave you wondering if it's pretty or not. The good news is that beauty lies in the eyes of the beholder, and there is no right or wrong answer to this question. However, if you're looking for tips and tricks to enhance your square face shape, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about square face shape and some tips on how to make it look even more beautiful.

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and angular features. This face shape is often compared to a rectangle or a square, hence the name. People with square face shape tend to have a more prominent bone structure, which can sometimes make them look more masculine. However, with the right makeup and hairstyle, you can soften the angles and enhance your natural beauty.

How to Determine if You Have a Square Face Shape?

The easiest way to determine if you have a square face shape is to look at yourself in the mirror. Here are some characteristics that are common in people with square face shape:

  • A prominent jawline
  • A broad forehead
  • Straight cheekbones
  • A square chin

If you have these features, then there's a good chance that you have a square face shape.

Step by Step Guide to Enhance Your Square Face Shape

Here are some tips to enhance your square face shape:

1. Soften Your Jawline

The most prominent feature of a square face shape is the jawline. To soften the angles, try applying a lighter shade of foundation or contouring powder along the jawline. This will create an illusion of a more rounded face shape.

2. Add Some Volume to Your Hair

Adding volume to your hair can help balance out a square face shape. Try a volumizing shampoo and conditioner or use a round brush to blow dry your hair. Soft waves or curls can also help create a more feminine look.

3. Play with Your Hair Parting

Changing your hair parting can also help balance out your square face shape. Try a side part or a middle part to create a different look. This will also help draw attention away from your jawline.

4. Use Soft Makeup

Using soft makeup can help soften your square face shape. Try using a light pink or peach blush to add some color to your cheeks. Use a soft eyeshadow color and a light lipstick shade to complete the look.

Tips for Square Face Shape

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when it comes to square face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too blunt, as they can emphasize your square jawline.
  • Avoid wearing earrings that are too angular or square-shaped.
  • Wear clothes that balance out your upper and lower body, such as A-line dresses or tops with a V-neckline.

FAQ

1. Can You Have a Square Face Shape and Still Be Pretty?

Absolutely! Beauty comes in all shapes and sizes, and having a square face shape is no exception. With the right makeup and hairstyle, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your own skin.

2. Can You Change Your Face Shape?

No, you cannot change your face shape. However, you can use makeup and hairstyle to enhance your natural features and create a different look.

3. What Are Some Celebrity Examples of Square Face Shape?

Some celebrities with square face shape include Angelina Jolie, Demi Moore, and Olivia Wilde.

Hair Care for Square Face Shape

When it comes to hair care for square face shape, it's important to keep your hair healthy and well-nourished. Here are some tips:

  • Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the hair and make it look dull.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment to nourish your hair and keep it looking shiny.

In conclusion, having a square face shape is not a bad thing. With the right makeup and hairstyle, you can enhance your natural features and feel confident in your own skin. Remember, beauty comes in all shapes and sizes, and having a square face shape is no exception.
