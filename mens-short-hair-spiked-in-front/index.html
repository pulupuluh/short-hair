Mens Short Hair Spiked In Front A Guide To Achieving A Cool And Edgy Look

Written by Eveline Aug 12, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re looking for a hairstyle that exudes confidence and style, then the mens short hair spiked in front might just be the perfect one for you. This hairstyle has been a popular choice among men for years because of its versatility and edgy look. In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this hairstyle, as well as some tips and tricks to maintain it. So, let’s get started!

Description

The mens short hair spiked in front is a hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on top that is spiked up and styled forward. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look. It is also versatile enough to be styled in different ways, depending on your mood and preference.

How To

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the mens short hair spiked in front:

Step 1: Get the Right Haircut

The first step is to get the right haircut. You’ll need a short cut on the sides and back, with longer hair on top. The length of the hair on top will depend on your preference, but make sure that it is long enough to be spiked up.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure that it is clean and dry. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Then, towel dry your hair until it is damp.

Step 3: Apply Hair Product

Apply a small amount of hair product, such as wax or gel, to your hair. Make sure to distribute it evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the hair on top. This will help hold the spikes in place.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Using your fingers or a comb, style your hair by spiking it up and forward. Make sure that the spikes are even and well-defined. You can also add some texture to your hair by using your fingers to create a messy look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mens short hair spiked in front:

  • Use a high-quality hair product that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Don’t wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different hair products and styling techniques to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens short hair spiked in front:

What hair products should I use?

You can use wax, gel, or pomade to style your hair. Choose a product that is suitable for your hair type and provides a strong hold.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and lifestyle. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.

By following these tips and using the right hair products, you can achieve a cool and edgy look with your mens short hair spiked in front. So, go ahead and try out this hairstyle – you might just love it!
