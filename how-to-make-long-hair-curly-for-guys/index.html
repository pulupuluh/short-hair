How To Make Long Hair Curly For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 17, 2023 ¬∑ 7 min read

Are you tired of your long straight hair and want to switch things up with some curls? Look no further! In this article, we'll be providing you with a step-by-step guide on how to make long hair curly for guys. We'll also be answering some commonly asked questions and providing tips on how to care for your curly locks. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Long Hair Curly for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs

How to Make Long Hair Curly for Guys

There are several methods to make long hair curly for guys. Some of the most popular methods include:

  1. Using a curling iron
  2. Using a hair straightener
  3. Using rollers or flexi-rods
  4. Natural methods such as braiding or twisting damp hair overnight

Each method has its pros and cons, and the best method for you will depend on your hair type, the amount of time you have, and the type of curls you want to achieve. In this guide, we'll be focusing on using a hair straightener to create curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make long hair curly for guys using a hair straightener:

Step 1: Clean and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and avoid using a hairdryer to prevent heat damage. It's best to let your hair air dry naturally or use a microfiber towel to dry your hair gently.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before using a hair straightener, apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to prevent heat damage. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and clamp the hair straightener at the root. Twist the straightener away from your face and slowly glide it down the length of your hair. Repeat until you've curled all sections of your hair.

Step 5: Apply Hair Spray

Finish off by applying a light hold hair spray to set your curls in place. Avoid using too much product as this can weigh down your curls and make them look unnatural.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Now that you've got your curls, it's essential to take care of them to maintain their shape and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos and conditioners that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer, as this can cause frizz and damage your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz while you sleep.

FAQs

How long will the curls last?

The longevity of your curls will depend on your hair type and the method you used to curl your hair. Generally, curls created with a hair straightener can last up to two days with proper care.

Can I use a hair straightener on wet hair?

No, it's not safe to use a hair straightener on wet hair as this can damage your hair and cause breakage. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using any heat styling tools.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires unique care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to prevent frizz and enhance your curls.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as this can damage your hair and cause breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you're well on your way to achieving luscious, bouncy curls. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Have fun experimenting with different styles and enjoy your new curly hair!
