Is It Good To Keep Your Hair In Braids

Written by Eveline Apr 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Keeping your hair in braids is a popular hair styling technique that has been around for centuries. Braids come in many different styles and can be worn for various reasons, including convenience, protection, and fashion. Some people believe that keeping their hair in braids is good for their hair health, while others think that it can cause damage over time. In this article, we will explore the pros and cons of keeping your hair in braids and answer the question, "is it good to keep your hair in braids?"

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Keep Your Hair in Braids
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Braids
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Braids

Description

Braids are a hairstyle that involves weaving three or more strands of hair together to create a pattern. Braids can be worn in many different styles, including box braids, cornrows, and twists. Braids are often used to protect natural hair from damage and can also be worn as a fashion statement. Keeping your hair in braids for an extended period of time can help to reduce the need for daily styling and manipulation, which can lead to breakage and damage.

How to Keep Your Hair in Braids

Before you start braiding your hair, it's essential to ensure that your hair is clean and free of tangles. You should also use a deep conditioner to help detangle your hair and make it more manageable. Once your hair is clean and conditioned, you can start braiding your hair. The best way to keep your hair in braids is to use a braiding technique that is suitable for your hair type and desired style. You can either braid your hair yourself or go to a professional stylist to get it done.

Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow when braiding your hair:

  1. Start by parting your hair into sections using a comb.
  2. Take a small section of hair at the front of your head and divide it into three equal parts.
  3. Cross the right strand over the middle strand.
  4. Cross the left strand over the middle strand.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you reach the end of the section of hair.
  6. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie or rubber band.
  7. Repeat these steps with each section of hair until your hair is fully braided.

Tips for Maintaining Braids

Here are some tips for maintaining braids:

  • Don't keep your braids in for too long.
  • Use a silk scarf or bonnet to protect your braids while you sleep.
  • Wash your scalp regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using heavy oils and products on your hair.
  • When you take your braids out, be gentle and use a detangling spray to avoid breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping your hair in braids:

Can keeping your hair in braids cause damage?

Yes, keeping your hair in braids for an extended period of time can cause damage to your hair. Braids can create tension on your hairline, which can lead to breakage and hair loss. It's essential to take breaks between braiding your hair and to avoid keeping your braids in for too long.

How long should you keep your hair in braids?

You should not keep your hair in braids for more than eight weeks. After eight weeks, you should take a break from braiding your hair to allow your scalp and hair to rest.

Can you wash your hair while it's in braids?

Yes, you can wash your hair while it's in braids. However, you should avoid using heavy oils and products on your hair, as this can create buildup and cause your braids to unravel.

Hair Care for Braids

Keeping your hair in braids requires proper hair care to maintain healthy hair. Here are some hair care tips for braids:

  • Wash your scalp regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heavy oils and products on your hair, as this can create buildup and cause your braids to unravel.
  • When you take your braids out, be gentle and use a detangling spray to avoid breakage.

In conclusion, keeping your hair in braids can be a convenient and stylish way to protect your hair and reduce daily styling and manipulation. However, it's essential to take breaks between braiding your hair and to avoid keeping your braids in for too long to prevent damage. By following proper hair care techniques, you can keep your hair healthy and strong while keeping it in braids.
