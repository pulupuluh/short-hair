How To Cut Fringe For Round Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a way to flatter your round face shape with a new hairstyle? One of the best ways to do so is to cut fringe or bangs. However, cutting fringe for round face can be tricky, as you want to make sure you don't end up with a style that emphasizes the roundness of your face. In this guide, we will walk you through how to cut fringe for round face, including tips, FAQ, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Fringe for Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Fringe for Round Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Fringe and Round Face

How to Cut Fringe for Round Face

The key to cutting fringe for round face is to create angles that will elongate your face shape. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • Avoid straight, blunt bangs that cut across your forehead.
  • Opt for side-swept or asymmetrical bangs that create angles.
  • Choose a length that falls just above your eyebrows or longer.
  • Avoid too much volume or thickness, which can add width to your face.
  • Consider a choppy or wispy texture to add movement and softness to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut fringe for round face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a comb to section off the hair you want to cut into fringe.
  3. Hold the hair between your fingers and decide on the length you want to cut.
  4. Using sharp scissors, cut the hair at an angle, starting from the middle and working your way outward.
  5. Repeat on the other side, making sure the length and angle match.
  6. Check your work in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Style your new fringe by blow-drying or air-drying and using a round brush to smooth out any kinks.

Tips for Cutting Fringe for Round Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting fringe for round face:

  • Go slow and cut small amounts at a time.
  • Use sharp, professional-grade scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Don't cut too short, as you can always trim more later.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut if you're not comfortable cutting your own hair.
  • Experiment with different lengths and angles to find the best style for your face shape and personal preferences.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting fringe for round face:

Can I cut fringe for round face if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Just make sure to cut your fringe when your hair is dry and in its natural form. You may also want to leave a bit more length than you would with straight hair, as curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries.

What if I don't like my new fringe?

If you're not happy with your new fringe, don't panic! You can always grow it out or have it reshaped by a professional stylist. Just be patient and give your hair time to grow.

How often should I trim my fringe?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your style. Generally, you should trim your fringe every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care for Fringe and Round Face

To keep your new fringe looking its best, use these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the ends and cause split ends.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and moisture to your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Cutting fringe for round face can be a great way to update your hairstyle and flatter your face shape. By following these tips and guidelines, you can create a look that's both stylish and flattering.
