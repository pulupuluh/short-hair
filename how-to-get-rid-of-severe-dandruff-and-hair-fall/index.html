How To Get Rid Of Severe Dandruff And Hair Fall

Written by Robby Jul 17, 2023 · 8 min read

Dandruff and hair fall are common hair problems that can affect anyone, regardless of age or gender. However, severe dandruff and hair fall can be quite frustrating and can affect your confidence. In this post, we’ll provide you with tips on how to get rid of severe dandruff and hair fall.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get Rid of Severe Dandruff and Hair Fall
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips to Prevent Severe Dandruff and Hair Fall
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips

Description

Dandruff is a common scalp condition that causes flakes of skin to appear. It is often accompanied by itching and irritation. Severe dandruff can lead to hair fall, as the constant scratching can damage the hair follicles. Hair fall, on the other hand, is the excessive loss of hair, which can be caused by various factors such as stress, poor nutrition, and hormonal changes.

How to Get Rid of Severe Dandruff and Hair Fall

Getting rid of severe dandruff and hair fall requires a combination of lifestyle changes, hair care practices, and the use of specialized products. Here are some tips:

1. Use a specialized shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is specifically designed for people with dandruff and hair fall. These shampoos contain active ingredients that can help reduce dandruff and strengthen hair. Look for shampoos that contain ketoconazole, salicylic acid, or coal tar.

2. Keep your scalp clean

Cleanse your scalp regularly to remove excess oil, dirt, and dead skin cells. Use a gentle shampoo and avoid using hot water, as it can strip your scalp of its natural oils. Massage your scalp gently while shampooing to help improve blood circulation.

3. Avoid harsh hair treatments

Avoid using harsh hair treatments such as chemical relaxers, hair dyes, and hot styling tools. These can damage your hair and scalp, leading to hair fall and dandruff. If you must use them, use them sparingly and follow the instructions carefully.

4. Eat a healthy diet

A healthy diet is essential for healthy hair and scalp. Include foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals such as iron, zinc, and vitamin D. Eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.

5. Manage stress

Stress can cause hormonal changes that can lead to hair fall and dandruff. Manage your stress levels by practicing relaxation techniques such as meditation or yoga.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide on how to get rid of severe dandruff and hair fall:

Step 1: Choose a specialized shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is specifically designed for people with dandruff and hair fall. Look for shampoos that contain active ingredients such as ketoconazole, salicylic acid, or coal tar.

Step 2: Cleanse your scalp regularly

Cleanse your scalp regularly with a gentle shampoo to remove excess oil, dirt, and dead skin cells. Massage your scalp gently while shampooing to help improve blood circulation.

Step 3: Avoid harsh hair treatments

Avoid using harsh hair treatments such as chemical relaxers, hair dyes, and hot styling tools. These can damage your hair and scalp, leading to hair fall and dandruff.

Step 4: Eat a healthy diet

Include foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals such as iron, zinc, and vitamin D. Eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.

Step 5: Manage stress

Manage your stress levels by practicing relaxation techniques such as meditation or yoga.

Tips to Prevent Severe Dandruff and Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips to prevent severe dandruff and hair fall:

  • Avoid using too much hair styling products
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles
  • Avoid scratching your scalp
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends

FAQs

Q: Can dandruff cause hair fall?

A: Yes, severe dandruff can cause hair fall, as the constant scratching can damage the hair follicles.

Q: Can stress cause hair fall?

A: Yes, stress can cause hormonal changes that can lead to hair fall.

Q: Can a poor diet cause hair fall?

A: Yes, a poor diet can cause hair fall. A healthy diet is essential for healthy hair and scalp.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips:

  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, instead, pat it dry
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

By following these tips, you can get rid of severe dandruff and hair fall and enjoy healthy, beautiful hair.
