Best Round Brush For Thick Hair Blowout Achieve Salon Worthy Hair At Home

Written by Luffy Mar 30, 2023 · 11 min read

Welcome to our guide to the best round brush for thick hair blowout! If you have thick hair, then you know how difficult it can be to style it. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve salon-worthy hair at home. In this article, we will discuss the best round brush for thick hair blowout, how to use it, tips for achieving the perfect blowout, and more. So, let’s get started!

Description

A round brush is an essential tool for achieving a perfect blowout. It is especially important for those with thick hair because it helps to evenly distribute heat and tension, which can be difficult to do with other types of brushes. A round brush also helps to create volume, bounce, and curl, depending on the size of the brush and the technique used. When selecting the best round brush for thick hair blowout, consider the size, material, and bristle type.

How to Use a Round Brush for Thick Hair Blowout

Before you start blow-drying your hair with a round brush, make sure your hair is clean and conditioned. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from damage. Then, follow these steps:

  1. Section your hair into manageable pieces using clips or hair ties.
  2. Start with the bottom section of your hair and work your way up.
  3. Hold the round brush at the ends of your hair and roll it towards your scalp.
  4. Use a blow dryer with a concentrator nozzle to direct the heat towards your hair and the brush.
  5. Roll the brush towards your scalp, pulling the hair taut as you go.
  6. Hold the brush in place for a few seconds to set the curl or wave.
  7. Carefully release the brush from your hair and repeat on the next section.

Remember to use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage. Also, be careful not to overheat your hair or hold the brush in one spot for too long, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Step-by-Step Guide to Blow-Drying Your Hair with a Round Brush

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide to blow-drying your hair with a round brush:

  1. Start with clean, conditioned hair. Towel-dry your hair to remove excess water.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start with the bottom section of your hair and work your way up.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the round brush.
  5. Hold the brush at the ends of your hair and roll it towards your scalp.
  6. Use a blow dryer with a concentrator nozzle to direct the heat towards your hair and the brush.
  7. Roll the brush towards your scalp, pulling the hair taut as you go.
  8. Hold the brush in place for a few seconds to set the curl or wave.
  9. Carefully release the brush from your hair and repeat on the next section.
  10. Once you have finished blow-drying all sections of your hair, use a cool shot to set the style.
  11. Apply a hair serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Remember to be patient and take your time. It may take a few tries to get the hang of using a round brush for thick hair blowout, but with practice, you will be able to achieve salon-worthy hair at home.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Blowout

Here are some tips for achieving the perfect blowout:

  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from damage.
  • Section your hair into manageable pieces using clips or hair ties.
  • Start with the bottom section of your hair and work your way up.
  • Hold the brush at the ends of your hair and roll it towards your scalp.

FAQs About Using a Round Brush for Thick Hair Blowout

What size round brush should I use for thick hair?

The size of the round brush you use will depend on the length of your hair and the style you want to achieve. For shorter hair, a smaller round brush (1-1.5 inches) may be more appropriate, while longer hair may require a larger brush (2-3 inches).

What material should the bristles be for a round brush for thick hair blowout?

The material of the bristles can be either natural or synthetic. Natural bristles, such as boar or horsehair, are gentler on the hair and help to distribute natural oils. Synthetic bristles, such as nylon, are more durable and can withstand heat better.

How often should I clean my round brush?

You should clean your round brush at least once a week to remove any buildup of product or oil. To clean the brush, remove any hair with a comb or brush, then soak the brush in warm water and shampoo for 10-15 minutes. Rinse thoroughly and let it air dry.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

Here are some hair care tips for those with thick hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help retain moisture.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.

By following these hair care tips and using the best round brush for thick hair blowout, you can achieve beautiful, healthy hair at home.
