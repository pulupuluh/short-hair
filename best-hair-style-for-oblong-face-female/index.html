Best Hair Style For Oblong Face Female

Are you an oblong face female looking for the best hairstyle that accentuates your facial features? Look no further! In this article, we'll be discussing the different hairstyles that complement an oblong face. We'll also provide tips on how to style your hair and maintain its health.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oblong face has a longer length than width, with a high forehead, long nose, and pointed chin. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face is to create width and balance. Hairstyles that work best for this face shape include those that add volume to the sides, have layers, or are shorter in length.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face, consider the following:

  • Choose a style that adds volume to the sides of your face
  • Consider layers to add dimension and texture
  • Shorter hairstyles can help balance out a longer face shape
  • Side-swept bangs can help shorten the appearance of a high forehead
  • Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to create a hairstyle that complements an oblong face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type
  2. Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume to the sides of your face
  3. If you have long hair, consider adding layers to create dimension and texture
  4. If you want a shorter hairstyle, consult with your stylist to find a suitable length that balances out your face shape
  5. Consider side-swept bangs to shorten the appearance of your forehead
  6. Finish off your hairstyle with a hairspray or styling product to hold it in place

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Consult with your stylist to find a hairstyle that works best for your face shape and hair type
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair
  • Use styling products that are suitable for your hair type to avoid damage and breakage
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find the perfect one

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for oblong face females:

What hairstyles work best for oblong faces?

Hairstyles that work best for oblong faces include those that add volume to the sides, have layers, or are shorter in length.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face?

Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear even longer.

Hair Care

To maintain the health of your hair, consider the following:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly with products that are suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote hair growth and strength
  • Consider using hair masks or treatments to nourish and hydrate your hair

Now that you know the best hairstyle for oblong face females, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist and try out a new look! Remember to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and strong.
