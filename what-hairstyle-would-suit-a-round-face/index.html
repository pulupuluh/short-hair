What Hairstyle Would Suit A Round Face

Written by William Jul 18, 2023 · 6 min read

Having a round face can be a challenge when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle that complements your features. Fortunately, there are many hairstyles that can flatter a round face and enhance your natural beauty. In this blog post, we’ll explore the different hairstyles that would suit a round face, along with tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step-by-Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of a more elongated face shape. This can be achieved by adding volume and height to the crown of the head, creating a side part, and incorporating layers that frame the face.

How To

The first step in choosing a hairstyle for a round face is to determine your face shape. This can be done by pulling your hair back and examining the shape of your face in the mirror. Once you have determined that you have a round face, you can begin to explore different hairstyles that would suit your features.

Step-by-Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating hairstyles that would suit a round face:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help to elongate a round face. To create this look:

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair straight.
  2. Create a deep side part.
  3. Add long layers that frame your face.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A bob with side-swept bangs can help to create a more angular look for a round face. To create this look:

  1. Cut your hair into a bob that falls just above your shoulders.
  2. Create a deep side part.
  3. Add side-swept bangs that fall just above your eyebrows.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. High Ponytail

A high ponytail can help to elongate a round face. To create this look:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Secure with a hair tie.
  3. Add volume to the crown of your head by teasing your hair.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs or straight hair with no layers.
  • Experiment with different partings to create the illusion of a more angular face shape.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs or straight hair with no layers.

What hairstyles would suit a round face?

Long layers, a bob with side-swept bangs, and a high ponytail are all hairstyles that would suit a round face.

Hair Care

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, it’s important to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, which can cause damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance.
