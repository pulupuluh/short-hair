Best Hairstyle For Long Hair Oval Face

Best Hairstyle for Long Hair Oval Face | Hair Care Tips | 2023 Guide

Long hair is a blessing and can be styled in many ways. However, if you have an oval face, you need to be careful while choosing a hairstyle that complements your face shape. In this article, we will guide you through the best hairstyle for long hair oval face and share some tips on hair care.

Description

An oval face is considered the perfect face shape as it has balanced features and a narrow forehead. If you have an oval face, you can experiment with various hairstyles. However, the best hairstyle for long hair oval face is the one that accentuates your features and adds volume to your hair.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Long Hair Oval Face

Choosing the best hairstyle for long hair oval face is all about creating the illusion of an oval face shape. You can achieve this by adding volume to the top of your head and avoiding styles that elongate your face. Here are some hairstyles that work best for long hair oval face:

  • Layered Haircut: A layered haircut adds volume and texture to your hair, making your face look fuller.
  • Side Swept Bangs: Side-swept bangs create an illusion of a shorter forehead, balancing the face.
  • Beachy Waves: Beachy waves add texture and volume to your hair, making it look fuller.
  • High Ponytail: A high ponytail creates an illusion of a longer face, balancing the oval face shape.

Step by Step Guide to Style Long Hair for Oval Face

Follow these steps to style your long hair for an oval face:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant to avoid damage from hot styling tools.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the top of your head.
  4. Curl your hair using a curling iron to create loose curls or beachy waves.
  5. If you have side-swept bangs, blow-dry them in the direction you want them to fall.
  6. Tie your hair in a high ponytail or half updo to create an illusion of a longer face.

Tips to Maintain Long Hair for Oval Face

Long hair requires proper care and maintenance to look healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to maintain long hair for oval face:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and maintain the length.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles as they can put pressure on your hair and cause breakage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for long hair oval face:

  • Can I wear my hair straight if I have an oval face? Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have an oval face. However, avoid styles that elongate your face.
  • Can I wear a center part if I have an oval face? Yes, you can wear a center part if you have an oval face. However, add volume to the top of your head to balance the face shape.
  • Is a bob haircut suitable for long hair oval face? Yes, a bob haircut is suitable for long hair oval face if it adds volume to the top of your head.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair Oval Face

Here are some additional hair care tips for long hair oval face:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and control frizz.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant with SPF.

Long hair oval face can be styled in many ways, but the best hairstyle is the one that complements your face shape. Follow our guide to choose the best hairstyle for long hair oval face and maintain healthy hair.
