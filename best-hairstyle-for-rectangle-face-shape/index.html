Best Hairstyle For Rectangle Face Shape

Written by William May 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a rectangle face shape, you may be wondering what the best hairstyle is for you. Don't worry, there are plenty of options that will flatter your face shape and leave you feeling confident and beautiful. In this article, we will be discussing the best hairstyle for rectangle face shape, how to achieve it, and tips for maintaining your hair.

Description

A rectangle face shape is characterized by a long and narrow shape with a strong jawline. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width, which can make the face appear longer than it is wide. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a rectangle face shape is to create the illusion of width and soften the angles of the face.

How To: Best Hairstyle for Rectangle Face Shape

The best hairstyle for a rectangle face shape is one that adds volume and width to the sides of the face while softening the angles. Here are some steps to achieve this look:

Step 1: Choose a Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a rectangle face shape, you want to add volume to the sides of the face. This can be achieved with layered cuts, choppy ends, or soft waves. Avoid straight cuts and blunt edges that will only emphasize the angular shape of the face.

Step 2: Add Layers

Adding layers to your haircut will help create the illusion of width and volume. Layers can be added to the sides of the face, the crown, or throughout the hair to create movement and texture.

Step 3: Soft Waves

Soft waves can help soften the angles of the face and add volume to the sides. You can achieve this look with a curling iron or by braiding your hair overnight for natural waves.

Step 4: Side Part

A side part can help balance the width of the face and create the illusion of volume. Simply part your hair to one side and let your hair fall naturally.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a rectangle face shape:

  • Avoid straight cuts and blunt edges
  • Add layers to create volume and movement
  • Soft waves can help soften the angles of the face
  • A side part can help balance the width of the face
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid with a rectangle face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can emphasize the length of the face. Blunt cuts and straight edges should also be avoided, as they can emphasize the angular shape of the face.

What hairstyles are best for a rectangle face shape?

The best hairstyles for a rectangle face shape are those that add volume and width to the sides of the face while softening the angles. Layered cuts, choppy ends, and soft waves are all great options.

How do I know if I have a rectangle face shape?

A rectangle face shape is characterized by a long and narrow shape with a strong jawline. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width, which can make the face appear longer than it is wide.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

By following these tips and choosing the best hairstyle for your rectangle face shape, you can achieve a look that is both flattering and stylish.
