How To Contour Oval Face For Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're new to makeup, the world of contouring can be intimidating. But don't worry, contouring an oval face is actually quite easy once you know the basics. In this article, we'll break down how to contour oval face for beginners and provide some tips and tricks along the way. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Contour Oval Face for Beginners
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Oval Face Shapes

Description

Before we dive into how to contour oval face, let's first define what an oval face shape is. An oval face is characterized by a slightly wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. It's considered the most balanced face shape and is often seen as the ideal shape for contouring. Contouring an oval face helps to enhance its natural features and create a more defined look.

How to Contour Oval Face for Beginners

When it comes to contouring an oval face, the key is to focus on the areas where shadows naturally occur. This includes the hollows of the cheeks, temples, and jawline. By applying darker shades of makeup to these areas, you can create the illusion of depth and dimension.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to contour oval face:

Step 1: Prep Your Face

Before you start contouring, make sure your skin is clean and moisturized. Apply a primer to create a smooth base for your makeup.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Apply your usual foundation and blend it well into your skin. This will create an even base for your contouring.

Step 3: Apply Contour

Using a contour brush, apply a darker shade of makeup to the hollows of your cheeks. Start at your ears and blend inwards towards the center of your face. Apply some contour to your temples and along your jawline as well.

Step 4: Blend

Use a blending brush to blend the contour outwards towards the edges of your face. You want to create a natural-looking shadow, so make sure to blend well.

Step 5: Highlight

Apply a lighter shade of makeup to the high points of your face, including your cheekbones, brow bone, and the bridge of your nose. This will help to create contrast and bring out your natural features.

Step 6: Set Your Makeup

Finally, set your makeup with a setting spray or powder to ensure it lasts all day.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for contouring an oval face:

  • Choose a contour shade that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone.
  • Blend, blend, blend! The key to natural-looking contour is to blend well.
  • Use a small brush for precise application.
  • Apply highlighter sparingly to avoid a shiny, oily look.
  • Try using a cream contour for a more natural look.

FAQs

Q: Can you contour an oval face with powder?

A: Yes, you can use either cream or powder contour to contour an oval face. It all depends on your personal preference and the type of look you're going for.

Q: How often should I contour my face?

A: It's up to you how often you contour your face. Some people contour every day, while others only do it for special occasions. It's important to listen to your skin and give it a break if it needs it.

Hair Care for Oval Face Shapes

In addition to contouring, there are also some hair care tips to keep in mind for oval face shapes. Here are a few:

  • Consider a middle part to elongate your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Try a layered bob or long, loose waves to add texture and movement.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to contour your oval face like a pro in no time. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don't be afraid to experiment and find what works best for you!
