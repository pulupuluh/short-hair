Do Square Faces Look Good With Short Hair

Written by Eveline Mar 06, 2023 · 7 min read

Short hair has been a popular style for women for many years now. However, not all women may feel comfortable with the idea of cutting their long locks short. One of the biggest concerns women have is whether a short hairstyle will suit their face shape or not. In this article, we will explore the question of whether square faces look good with short hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step by Step Guide on Choosing Short Hair for Square Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair on Square Faces
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hair on Square Faces

Description

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular cheekbones. Women with square faces often worry about whether short hair will look good on them or not. However, the good news is that square faces can look great with short hair as long as the right style is chosen.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before you decide on a short hairstyle, it is important to determine your face shape. To determine whether you have a square face or not, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and examine the outline. If your jawline is strong and your forehead is wide with angular cheekbones, you have a square face.

Step by Step Guide on Choosing Short Hair for Square Faces

Choosing the right short hairstyle for a square face can be a daunting task. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you choose the perfect short hairstyle:

  1. Consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, a short bob or pixie cut can add volume and dimension to your hair. If you have thick hair, a short layered cut can help to thin out your hair.
  2. Choose a style that softens your strong jawline. A side-swept fringe or a layered bob can help to soften your jawline.
  3. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your head. This can make your face appear wider than it actually is. Instead, opt for styles that add volume to the top of your head.
  4. Consider your personality and lifestyle. If you’re a busy mom or have a hectic work schedule, a low maintenance pixie cut may be the perfect option for you.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair on Square Faces

Once you’ve chosen the perfect short hairstyle for your square face, it’s important to maintain it properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair straightener or curling iron to style your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about short hair on square faces:

1. Can I still have long hair if I have a square face?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a square face. However, it’s important to choose a style that complements your face shape.

2. Will a short bob suit my square face?

Yes, a short bob can look great on a square face. However, make sure to choose a style that softens your jawline.

3. Can I still have bangs with short hair on a square face?

Yes, you can still have bangs with short hair on a square face. However, make sure to choose a style that softens your jawline.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair on Square Faces

Here are a few hair care tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and shiny:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

In conclusion, square faces can look great with short hair as long as the right style is chosen. By following the tips and guidelines in this article, you can choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape and enhances your features.
