How To Keep Hair From Falling Out During Chemo

Written by Daniel Mar 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Chemo can be a very difficult time for anyone going through it. One of the most common side effects of chemotherapy is hair loss. Losing your hair can be emotionally and physically challenging. Fortunately, there are steps you can take to help prevent hair loss during chemo. In this blog post, we will discuss how to keep hair from falling out during chemo, including tips and FAQ.

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Hair from Falling Out During Chemo
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Prevent Hair Loss During Chemo
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care During Chemo

How to Keep Hair from Falling Out During Chemo

Chemotherapy works by attacking rapidly dividing cells, which unfortunately includes hair follicles. This is why hair loss is a common side effect of chemotherapy. However, there are a few ways you can help prevent hair loss.

1. Scalp Cooling

Scalp cooling is a method used to reduce the amount of chemotherapy that reaches the hair follicles. It involves wearing a cold cap during chemotherapy treatments, which can help protect the hair follicles from damage. Talk to your doctor or oncology nurse to see if scalp cooling is an option for you.

2. Minoxidil

Minoxidil is a medication that can help promote hair growth. It is typically used to treat hair loss, but some studies have found that it can also help prevent hair loss during chemotherapy. Talk to your doctor to see if minoxidil is right for you.

3. Hair Loss Prevention Shampoo

There are several hair loss prevention shampoos on the market that are designed to help prevent hair loss. These shampoos contain ingredients that can help nourish and strengthen the hair follicles. Look for shampoos that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and niacin.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to keep hair from falling out during chemo:

Step 1: Talk to Your Doctor

Before starting chemotherapy, talk to your doctor about your concerns regarding hair loss. They may be able to recommend treatments or medications that can help prevent hair loss.

Step 2: Consider Scalp Cooling

If scalp cooling is an option for you, talk to your doctor or oncology nurse about how to use it. They will be able to provide you with instructions on how to use the cold cap.

Step 3: Use Hair Loss Prevention Shampoo

Start using a hair loss prevention shampoo as soon as possible. Look for shampoos that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and niacin.

Step 4: Consider Minoxidil

If your doctor recommends minoxidil, follow their instructions on how to use it.

Tips to Prevent Hair Loss During Chemo

Here are a few additional tips to help prevent hair loss during chemo:

1. Be Gentle

Be gentle with your hair. Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools. Use a soft-bristled brush and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

2. Wear a Hat

Wearing a hat can help protect your scalp from the sun and other elements. Look for hats made from soft, breathable materials like cotton.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help promote hair growth. Make sure to eat plenty of protein, fruits, and vegetables.

FAQs

Q: Will all chemotherapy drugs cause hair loss?

A: No, not all chemotherapy drugs cause hair loss. However, many of them do. Talk to your doctor to find out if your particular chemotherapy drug is likely to cause hair loss.

Q: How long does it take for hair to grow back after chemo?

A: Hair usually starts to grow back a few weeks after chemotherapy ends. However, it may take several months for your hair to fully grow back.

Hair Care During Chemo

Even if you take steps to prevent hair loss during chemo, it is still important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

1. Be Gentle

Be gentle with your hair. Use a soft-bristled brush and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair. Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools.

2. Use Mild Shampoo

Use a mild shampoo that is designed for sensitive scalps. Avoid using shampoos that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.

3. Moisturize Your Scalp

Chemotherapy can dry out your scalp, so it is important to keep it moisturized. Use a gentle moisturizer or oil to keep your scalp hydrated.

By following these tips, you can help prevent hair loss during chemo and take care of your hair during this difficult time.
