How To Cut Your Hair By Yourself With Clippers

Description

With the pandemic still ongoing, more and more people are looking for ways to cut their hair at home. This is where clippers come in. However, cutting your own hair with clippers can be tricky, especially if you don't have any experience. In this article, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut your hair by yourself with clippers.

How to Cut Your Hair by Yourself with Clippers

The first thing you need to do is to make sure you have the right tools. You will need a good pair of clippers, a comb, a mirror, and a cape or towel to cover your clothes. Once you have everything you need, you can start cutting your hair.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to wash and dry it. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that there are no tangles.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length Guard

Clippers usually come with different length guards. Choose the one that will give you the desired length. If you're not sure, start with a longer guard and work your way down.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start at the back of your head and work your way up. Use the clippers to trim the hair in a straight line. Make sure to go against the direction of hair growth for a closer cut. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Once you're done with the back, move on to the sides. Use the same technique and make sure to blend the hair at the back with the hair on the sides.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, move on to the top of your head. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly. If you want a shorter cut, use a smaller guard.

Tips

Here are some tips to make sure you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Take your time and don't rush.
  • Don't cut too much at once.
  • Make sure to clean your clippers after each use.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with kitchen scissors?

No, it's not recommended to use kitchen scissors to cut your hair. They are not sharp enough and can damage your hair.

Do I need to use a cape or towel?

Yes, it's recommended to use a cape or towel to cover your clothes and protect them from hair clippings.

Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

It's recommended to cut your hair when it's dry. Wet hair can make it difficult to cut evenly.

Hair Care

After you're done cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy.

Now that you know how to cut your hair by yourself with clippers, you can save time and money by doing it at home. Just remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully.
