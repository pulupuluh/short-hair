Best Long Hair Cut For Oval Face A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Aug 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky enough to pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, choosing the perfect long hair cut can be overwhelming. In this article, we will guide you on the best long hair cut for oval face shape.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile and balanced face shape. It means that you can try any hairstyle without worrying about it not suiting your face. However, there are certain long haircuts that can enhance your features and make you look even better.

How To

The first thing you need to do is determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are almost equal, then you have an oval face shape.

Step by Step

Step 1: Decide on the length of your hair. Long hair is ideal for oval face shapes, but you can choose the length that suits you the best.

Step 2: Choose a haircut that adds volume to the sides of your face. This will balance out your features and make your face look even more symmetrical.

Step 3: Consider adding layers to your hair. Layers will add movement and texture to your hair, making it look more voluminous.

Step 4: If you want to keep your hair straight, go for a blunt cut. This will make your hair look thicker and fuller.

Step 5: If you want to add some curls to your hair, go for loose, beachy waves. This will add volume and texture to your hair without making it look too voluminous.

Tips

Tip 1: Avoid a center parting as it can make your face look longer.

Tip 2: Avoid heavy bangs as they can overpower your face. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or a long fringe.

Tip 3: If you have thin hair, add some highlights to create the illusion of thickness.

FAQ

Q: Can oval face shapes pull off any hairstyle?

A: Yes, oval face shapes are considered to be the most versatile face shape and can pull off almost any hairstyle.

Q: What is the best haircut for oval face shapes?

A: The best haircut for oval face shapes is a long haircut with volume on the sides. Adding layers and loose waves can enhance your features even more.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair. Avoid using hot tools too often and protect your hair from the sun and pollution.

In conclusion, choosing the best long hair cut for oval face shape can be easy if you follow these simple steps. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine.
