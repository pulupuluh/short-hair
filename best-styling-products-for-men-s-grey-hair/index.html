Best Styling Products For Men s Grey Hair

Written by William Apr 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As men age, grey hair becomes a part of their natural hair color. Grey hair can be stylish and sophisticated, and it's important to find the right products to maintain its health and appearance. In this article, we will explore the best styling products for men's grey hair, including shampoos, conditioners, and styling gels.

Table of Contents

  1. Shampoo
  2. Conditioner
  3. Styling Gel
  4. How to Style Grey Hair
  5. Tips for Styling Grey Hair
  6. FAQs
  7. Grey Hair Care

Shampoo

Choosing the right shampoo is important for men with grey hair. Look for shampoos that are specially formulated for grey hair, as they contain ingredients that help to brighten and enhance the color. Some good options include:

  • Jack Black True Volume Thickening Shampoo
  • Redken Brews Silver Shampoo
  • Kiehl's Grooming Solutions Nourishing Shampoo & Conditioner

Conditioner

Conditioner is essential for keeping grey hair healthy and hydrated. It helps to prevent breakage and adds shine to the hair. Look for conditioners that are specifically designed for grey hair, such as:

  • Aveda Invati Men Nourishing Exfoliating Shampoo & Conditioner
  • Paul Mitchell Tea Tree Lavender Mint Moisturizing Conditioner
  • Baxter of California Daily Fortifying Conditioner

Styling Gel

Styling gel can help to add texture and hold to grey hair. Look for gels that are lightweight and won't weigh down the hair. Some good options include:

  • American Crew Firm Hold Styling Gel
  • Bumble and Bumble Sumotech
  • Redken Brews Extreme Gel

How to Style Grey Hair

Styling grey hair can be easy and effortless. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair
  2. Apply a small amount of styling gel to your hands and rub them together
  3. Work the gel through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends
  4. Use your fingers to create your desired style
  5. Allow your hair to air dry or use a blow dryer to finish the look

Tips for Styling Grey Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling grey hair:

  • Avoid using too much styling product, as it can weigh down your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as it is gentler on your strands
  • Don't overwash your hair, as it can strip away natural oils
  • Invest in a good-quality hairbrush or comb

FAQs

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my grey hair?

A: While you can use regular shampoo and conditioner on your grey hair, it's best to use products that are specifically formulated for grey hair to enhance its color and prevent yellowing.

Q: How often should I wash my grey hair?

A: It's best to wash your grey hair every other day or every few days to prevent it from drying out.

Grey Hair Care

Grey hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth
  • Stay hydrated to keep your hair and scalp healthy

Using the right styling products can make all the difference when it comes to styling men's grey hair. By following these tips and using the right products, you can keep your grey hair looking its best.
