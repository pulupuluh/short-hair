How To Manage Thick Poofy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 10, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have thick, poofy hair, you know that it can be a challenge to keep it under control. But don't worry, with the right techniques and products, you can manage your hair and achieve the look you want. In this article, we'll take you through a step-by-step guide on how to manage thick poofy hair.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Manage Thick Poofy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Managing Thick Poofy Hair

How to Manage Thick Poofy Hair

Managing thick poofy hair starts with understanding your hair type and texture. Thick hair can be challenging to style, but with the right techniques, it can look amazing. Here are some tips on how to manage thick poofy hair:

1. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

When it comes to managing thick poofy hair, it's essential to use the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are designed for thick or curly hair. These products usually contain ingredients that help to moisturize and define curls, making them easier to manage.

2. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and prone to frizz. Try to wash your hair every two to three days, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb or Brush

When combing or brushing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb or brush. This will help to detangle your hair without causing breakage. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

4. Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, such as a flat iron or curling iron, it's essential to use a heat protectant. This will help to prevent damage to your hair and keep it looking healthy.

5. Use Hair Oil

Hair oil can help to tame frizz and add shine to your hair. Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your hair, and work your way up to the roots. Avoid applying too much oil, as this can make your hair look greasy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to manage thick poofy hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for thick or curly hair.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-in Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help detangle and moisturize your hair.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Step 4: Apply Hair Oil

Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your hair, working your way up to the roots.

Step 5: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on a low heat setting. This will help to define your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a flat iron or curling iron, if desired. Remember to use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Managing Thick Poofy Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing thick poofy hair:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry it out.
  • Wrap your hair in a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry it, rather than rubbing it with a regular towel.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

FAQs

Q: Can thick poofy hair be straightened?

A: Yes, thick poofy hair can be straightened using a flat iron. However, it's important to use a heat protectant and to not overuse heat styling tools, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Q: How can I reduce frizz in my thick poofy hair?

A: To reduce frizz in your thick poofy hair, avoid overwashing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb or brush, and apply a hair oil to the ends of your hair.
