Best Haircuts For Oval Faces Over 40

Written by Petter Apr 04, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you over 40 and looking for a new haircut? Do you have an oval face shape and want to find the best haircut to compliment your features? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for oval faces over 40. Keep reading to find the perfect style for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oval face shape is considered the ideal shape for women. It is versatile and can pull off many different haircuts and styles. However, as we age, our hair may thin and lose its luster. That's why it's important to find a haircut that flatters our face shape and adds volume to our hair. In this article, we will provide you with the best haircuts for oval faces over 40 to help you look and feel your best.

How To

When choosing a haircut, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Oval faces are longer than they are wide, with a rounded jawline and high cheekbones. The best haircuts for oval faces over 40 are those that highlight your best features while adding volume and texture to your hair. Here are some tips on how to choose the perfect haircut:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

As mentioned earlier, oval faces are longer than they are wide. If you have an oval face shape, you can pull off many different haircuts and styles. You can go short or long, curly or straight. However, it's best to avoid haircuts that add length to your face, such as a long straight bob.

2. Think About Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture will also play a role in choosing the perfect haircut. If you have thin hair, you may want to go for a layered haircut that adds volume and movement to your hair. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a blunt cut that will emphasize your hair's natural texture.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

Lastly, consider your lifestyle when choosing a haircut. If you're a busy mom or have a hectic work schedule, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance haircut that doesn't require a lot of styling. On the other hand, if you have more time to spend on your hair, you may want to choose a more intricate haircut that requires more styling.

Step By Step

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a haircut, let's dive into the best haircuts for oval faces over 40. Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve these haircuts:

1. The Long Layered Cut

The long layered cut is perfect for oval faces over 40. It adds volume and movement to your hair while flattering your face shape. Here's how to achieve this look:

  • Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry.
  • Section your hair and curl the ends with a curling iron or hot rollers.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

2. The Lob

The lob, or long bob, is a chic and stylish haircut that looks great on oval faces over 40. It's also low-maintenance, making it perfect for busy women. Here's how to achieve this look:

  • Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry.
  • Use a straightening iron to straighten your hair.
  • Apply a smoothing serum to tame any frizz or flyaways.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

3. The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a bold and daring haircut that looks great on oval faces over 40. It's also low-maintenance and perfect for women who want a dramatic change. Here's how to achieve this look:

  • Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry.
  • Apply a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Use a styling cream to mold your hair into the desired shape.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut:

  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect haircut for your face shape and hair texture.
  • Bring in pictures of hairstyles you like to show your hairstylist.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.
  • Invest in quality hair products to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for oval faces over 40:

What is the best haircut for an oval face over 40?

The best haircut for an oval face over 40 is one that flatters your face shape while adding volume and texture to your hair. Some popular haircuts for oval faces over 40 include the long layered cut, the lob, and the pixie cut.

Can I still wear long hair if I have an oval face over 40?

Yes, you can still wear long hair if you have an oval face over 40. However, it's best to avoid haircuts that add length to your face, such as a long straight bob. Instead, opt for a layered cut that adds volume and movement to your hair.

Hair Care

Now that you have the perfect haircut for your oval face over 40, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to protect your hair from damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a quality hair mask to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and haircuts for oval faces over 40, you're sure to look and feel your best. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect haircut for you, and invest in quality hair products for healthy, shiny hair.
