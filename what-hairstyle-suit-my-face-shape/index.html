What Hairstyle Suits My Face Shape

Written by Christine Mar 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially when you are not sure what suits your face shape. Our face shape plays a crucial role in the hairstyle we choose, as it can either accentuate our features or make them appear less flattering. In this article, we will guide you on how to determine your face shape and what hairstyle suits it best.

Determining Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's determine your face shape. The easiest way to do this is by pulling your hair back and standing in front of a mirror. Take note of the following:

  • Forehead: Measure the width of your forehead from one eyebrow arch to the other.
  • Cheekbones: Measure the widest part of your cheekbones.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the area below your earlobe where your jawline angles up.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Once you have these measurements, determine which of the following face shapes best describes you:

Hairstyles for Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, consider yourself lucky as you can pull off almost any hairstyle. The oval face shape has balanced proportions, with a slightly curved jawline. Here are some hairstyles that complement an oval face shape:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob with layers
  • Pixie cut

Hairstyles for Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, your face is widest at the cheeks and ears, with a rounded chin. The goal is to elongate your face and create the illusion of a more defined jawline. Here are some hairstyles that work well with a round face shape:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Wavy lob
  • High ponytail with volume at the crown

Hairstyles for Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong jawline, with the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline being of similar width. The goal is to soften the angles of the face and create balance. Here are some hairstyles that complement a square face shape:

  • Soft layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long bob
  • Messy updo

Hairstyles for Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape has a wide forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow chin. The goal is to create the illusion of a wider jawline, balancing the face. Here are some hairstyles that suit a heart face shape:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long layers
  • Bob
  • Low bun with volume at the nape

Hairstyles for Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and chin, with wide cheekbones. The goal is to balance the cheekbones and create the illusion of a more defined chin. Here are some hairstyles that complement a diamond face shape:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long layers
  • Bob with volume at the crown
  • Low ponytail with side parting

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle, keep the following tips in mind:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you have to style your hair.
  • Take into account your hair type and texture.
  • Be open to trying new things, but also consider what makes you feel comfortable and confident.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of your face shape or hairstyle, taking care of your hair is crucial. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools frequently, and always use a heat protectant.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions regarding face shape and hairstyles:

  • Can I have bangs with a round face shape? Yes, but opt for side-swept bangs instead of blunt bangs.
  • Will a bob suit a heart face shape? Yes, a bob that ends at the chin or collarbone will complement a heart face shape.
  • Can I pull off a pixie cut with a square face shape? Absolutely, but opt for a longer pixie with side-swept bangs to soften the angles of your face.

In conclusion, determining your face shape is the first step in choosing the right hairstyle. Consider your lifestyle, hair type, and texture when selecting a hairstyle, and don't be afraid to try new things. Remember to take care of your hair with regular haircuts and nourishing treatments to keep it healthy and looking its best.
