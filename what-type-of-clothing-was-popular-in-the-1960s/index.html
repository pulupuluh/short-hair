What Type Of Clothing Was Popular In The 1960S

Written by Petter Aug 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our fashion blog post where we will be discussing what type of clothing was popular in the 1960s. The 1960s was a decade of change in fashion, music, and culture. This era was known for its bold and colorful fashion statements and it influenced the fashion industry in many ways. In this article, we will be discussing the clothing styles that were popular in the 1960s, how to wear them, and where to find them. So, let's get started!

What Clothing Styles Were Popular in the 1960s?

The 1960s was a decade of revolution in fashion, it was a time when clothing styles were changing rapidly. Women's clothing styles became more daring and unconventional, while men's clothing styles became more relaxed and comfortable. Here are some of the popular clothing styles that were worn in the 1960s:

1. Mod Style

The Mod Style was a popular fashion trend in the 1960s, it was characterized by bold and colorful patterns, mini skirts, and go-go boots. The Mod Style was worn by both men and women, and it represented a new era of fashion that was influenced by the youth culture.

2. Hippie Style

The Hippie Style was a fashion trend that emerged in the mid-1960s, it was characterized by loose-fitting clothing, long hair, and natural fabrics such as cotton and linen. The Hippie Style was worn by both men and women, and it represented a new era of fashion that was influenced by the hippie counterculture.

3. Psychedelic Style

The Psychedelic Style was a fashion trend that emerged in the late 1960s, it was characterized by bright colors, bold patterns, and unconventional designs. The Psychedelic Style was worn by both men and women, and it represented a new era of fashion that was influenced by the psychedelic rock music scene.

How to Wear 1960s Clothing Styles

If you want to wear 1960s clothing styles, here are some tips on how to do it:

1. Mix and Match

The 1960s was all about mixing and matching different clothing styles, so don't be afraid to experiment with different patterns and colors.

2. Accessorize

Accessories were an important part of 1960s fashion, so don't forget to add some statement jewelry, hats, and sunglasses to your outfit.

3. Be Bold

The 1960s was a decade of bold fashion statements, so don't be afraid to wear bright colors and unconventional designs.

Where to Find 1960s Clothing Styles

If you want to find 1960s clothing styles, here are some places to look:

1. Vintage Stores

Vintage stores are a great place to find 1960s clothing styles, as they often have a wide selection of vintage clothing from this era.

2. Online Retailers

There are many online retailers that specialize in vintage clothing, so you can easily find 1960s clothing styles online.

3. Thrift Stores

Thrift stores are another great place to find 1960s clothing styles, as you can often find vintage clothing at an affordable price.

Hair Care in the 1960s

Hairstyles were an important part of 1960s fashion, here are some popular hairstyles from this era:

1. The Bouffant

The Bouffant was a popular hairstyle in the 1960s, it was characterized by a large, teased, and voluminous look.

2. The Beehive

The Beehive was another popular hairstyle in the 1960s, it was characterized by a high, rounded look that was created by backcombing the hair.

3. Long Hair

Long hair was a popular hairstyle for both men and women in the 1960s, it was often worn with a middle part and loose waves.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the 1960s was a decade of change in fashion, and it influenced the fashion industry in many ways. The popular clothing styles from this era include the Mod Style, Hippie Style, and Psychedelic Style. If you want to wear 1960s clothing styles, remember to mix and match, accessorize, and be bold. You can find 1960s clothing styles at vintage stores, online retailers, and thrift stores. Hairstyles were also an important part of 1960s fashion, popular hairstyles from this era include the Bouffant, Beehive, and Long Hair.
