Description

A pear shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead and a wide jawline. Women with pear shaped faces usually have round chins, which makes it essential to choose the right hairstyle that complements their facial features. In this blog post, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for pear shaped face female, how to determine if you have a pear shaped face, step-by-step guide on how to style your hair, tips to keep in mind, frequently asked questions, and hair care for pear shaped face female.

How to Determine if You Have a Pear Shaped Face

To determine if you have a pear shaped face, follow these steps:

  1. Take a full-face photo of yourself.
  2. Draw an imaginary line across your forehead and another line across your cheekbones.
  3. Measure the distance between the two lines. If the distance is greater than the distance between your cheekbones and jawline, you have a pear shaped face.

Best Hairstyle for Pear Shaped Face Female

The best hairstyle for pear shaped face female is one that adds volume to the top of the head and narrows the jawline. Some of the best hairstyles for pear shaped face female are:

  • Long Layers: Long layers add volume to the top of the head and create an illusion of a narrower jawline.
  • Side Swept Bangs: Side swept bangs draw attention to the eyes and away from the jawline.
  • Bob: A bob that ends at the chin or above the chin adds volume to the top of the head and narrows the jawline.
  • Pixie Cut: A pixie cut that is longer on top and shorter on the sides adds volume to the top of the head and narrows the jawline.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Style Your Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair if you have a pear shaped face:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume to the top of the head.
  4. If you have long hair, add layers to add volume to the top of the head.
  5. If you have bangs, style them to the side to draw attention to the eyes.
  6. If you have short hair, use a texturizing spray to add volume to the top of the head.
  7. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for pear shaped face female:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the bottom of the head, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs.
  • Avoid center parting as it makes the face look wider.
  • Add volume to the top of the head to create an illusion of a narrower jawline.
  • Try different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I have long hair if I have a pear shaped face?

A: Yes, you can have long hair if you have a pear shaped face. Just make sure to add layers to add volume to the top of the head.

Q: Can I have blunt bangs if I have a pear shaped face?

A: It is best to avoid blunt bangs if you have a pear shaped face. Instead, go for side swept bangs to draw attention to the eyes.

Hair Care for Pear Shaped Face Female

Here are some hair care tips for pear shaped face female:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a volumizing mousse to add volume to the top of the head.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and to prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best hairstyle for pear shaped face female, it's time to try different hairstyles and find the one that suits you the best. Remember to keep in mind the tips we mentioned and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
