Is Big Hair Making A Comeback The Truth About This Hairstyle Trend

Description

Big hair was a popular hairstyle trend in the 80s and early 90s, characterized by voluminous curls, waves, and teased hair. Over the years, this trend slowly faded away, but recently, there has been a resurgence in big hair. Many celebrities and influencers have been spotted rocking this retro hairstyle, and it seems like it's making a comeback.

How to Achieve Big Hair

If you're interested in jumping on the big hair trend, here are some tips on how to achieve the look:

Curling Iron

One of the easiest ways to achieve big hair is to use a curling iron. Start by sectioning your hair and curling small sections at a time. Once you've curled your entire head, flip your hair upside down and gently tease the roots to create more volume. Finish off with hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Teasing Comb

Another way to add volume to your hair is to tease it using a teasing comb. Start by sectioning your hair and backcombing at the roots. Once you've teased all of your hair, smooth out the top layer with a comb or brush. Finish off with hairspray to hold the volume in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve big hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with volumizing products.
  2. Blow dry your hair upside down to add more volume.
  3. Section your hair and curl small sections using a curling iron.
  4. Once you've curled your entire head, flip your hair upside down and tease the roots using a teasing comb.
  5. Smooth out the top layer of your hair with a comb or brush.
  6. Finish off with hairspray to hold the volume and curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Big Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining big hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can cause it to become flat and limp.
  • Use dry shampoo to absorb oil and add volume to your roots.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products, as they can weigh your hair down.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs About Big Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about big hair:

Is big hair suitable for all hair types?

Big hair can be achieved with any hair type, but it may be more challenging for those with fine or straight hair. Using volumizing products and teasing your hair can help add more volume and texture.

How long does big hair last?

Big hair can last for a few days with proper maintenance and styling. Using hairspray and avoiding heavy hair products can help hold the volume in place.

Can big hair damage your hair?

Teasing your hair can cause damage, but using the right tools and techniques can minimize the risk. Avoid backcombing too aggressively and use a teasing comb instead of a brush to prevent breakage.

Hair Care for Big Hair

Maintaining big hair requires proper hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more volume to your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

In conclusion, big hair is making a comeback, and it's a fun and retro hairstyle trend that can be achieved with the right tools and techniques. Whether you have curly or straight hair, you can rock big hair by following the tips and steps outlined in this article.
