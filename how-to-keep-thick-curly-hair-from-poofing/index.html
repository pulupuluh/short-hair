How to Keep Thick Curly Hair from Poofing - Tips and Tricks

If you have thick curly hair, you know that it can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, your curls are beautiful and unique. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and prevent it from poofing. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to keep thick curly hair from poofing.

How to Prevent Thick Curly Hair from Poofing

The key to preventing your curly hair from poofing is to keep it moisturized and defined. When your hair is dry, it tends to frizz and poof. Here are some ways to keep your hair moisturized and defined:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

When washing your hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair. These products will help to hydrate your hair and keep it moisturized.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner. This will help to keep your hair moisturized throughout the day. Look for a leave-in conditioner that is lightweight and won't weigh your hair down.

3. Use a Styling Gel or Cream

To define your curls and prevent them from poofing, use a styling gel or cream. Apply the product to wet hair and scrunch your curls. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to prevent your thick curly hair from poofing:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly and gently towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Gel or Cream

Apply a styling gel or cream to your wet hair. Use a dime-sized amount and distribute it evenly throughout your hair. Scrunch your curls to define them.

Step 4: Air Dry Your Hair

Avoid using a blow dryer as it can cause your hair to poof. Instead, let your hair air dry naturally. Once your hair is completely dry, scrunch your curls to refresh them.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep your thick curly hair from poofing:

1. Use a Satin Pillowcase

Using a satin pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and breakage. Satin is gentle on your hair and won't cause friction like cotton pillowcases.

2. Avoid Touching Your Hair

Touching your hair can cause it to poof. Try to avoid touching your hair throughout the day. If you need to refresh your curls, scrunch them with your hands.

3. Use a Diffuser

If you need to use a blow dryer, use a diffuser attachment. This will help to distribute the heat evenly and prevent your hair from poofing.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often.

Q: Can I use a regular towel to dry my hair?

A: No, using a regular towel can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot your hair.

Hair Care Products for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some hair care products that are specifically designed for thick curly hair:

  • Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie
  • DevaCurl SuperCream Coconut Curl Styler
  • Cantu Coconut Curling Cream
  • Ouidad Advanced Climate Control Heat & Humidity Gel

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your thick curly hair from poofing. Remember to keep your hair moisturized and defined, avoid touching it throughout the day, and use the right hair care products. With a little bit of effort, you can have beautiful, defined curls that don't poof.
