How To Style Hair Like 60S A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Mar 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

The 60s era was known for its iconic hairstyles that are still popular today. From the beehive to the bouffant, the 60s saw a rise in voluminous and sleek hairstyles. If you want to achieve a 60s-inspired hairdo, follow this step-by-step guide to achieve the perfect look.

Description

The 60s was a time of revolution, and the hairstyles of the era reflected this. Women's hair was no longer just a practical necessity, but a statement of individuality and style. The beehive, bouffant, and big curls were all popular looks that defined the era. These hairstyles were both elegant and bold, and can still be seen on runways and red carpets today.

How to Style Hair Like 60s

To achieve a 60s-inspired hairstyle, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with clean hair

The first step is to wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume to your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply mousse or foam

Apply a volumizing mousse or foam to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. This will help to add volume and hold to your hair.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots. You can also use a diffuser attachment to create soft waves.

Step 4: Tease your hair

Tease your hair at the crown to create height and volume. Use a fine-tooth comb to tease small sections of hair, and then smooth the top layer with a brush.

Step 5: Create a beehive or bouffant

To create a beehive or bouffant, gather the hair at the crown and pin it in place. Then, take sections of hair from the sides and back of your head and tease them at the roots. Smooth the top layer and pin the hair in place to create a rounded shape.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

Finish your 60s-inspired hairstyle with a strong hold hairspray. This will help to keep your hair in place and maintain the shape of your beehive or bouffant.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 60s-inspired hairstyle:

  • Use a tail comb to create a precise parting.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to keep your style in place.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as the half-up bouffant or the side-swept beehive.
  • Accessorize with a headband or scarf to complete your look.

FAQ

Can I achieve a 60s-inspired hairstyle with short hair?

Yes, you can still achieve a 60s-inspired hairstyle with short hair. Opt for a pixie cut or a bob with volume at the crown to create a 60s-inspired look.

Is it necessary to tease my hair?

Teasing your hair is an important step in creating a 60s-inspired hairstyle. It helps to add volume and shape to your hair, making it easier to style.

Hair Care

To maintain your 60s-inspired hairstyle, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from environmental damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a 60s-inspired hairstyle that is both timeless and trendy. Experiment with different styles and accessories to make the look your own, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
