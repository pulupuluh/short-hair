Medium Haircuts For Over 60 With Glasses A Guide To A New Look

Written by Christine Jul 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you over 60 and wear glasses? Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will flatter your face and glasses? Look no further than medium haircuts! Medium-length hair is versatile and easy to style, making it a great choice for older women with glasses. Here's a guide to the best medium haircuts for over 60 with glasses.

Description

Medium haircuts are hairstyles that fall between short and long hair. They usually reach between the collarbone and the shoulders. Medium haircuts are great for over 60 women because they are easy to maintain and style. They also help to create a youthful and feminine look. Wearing glasses can be a challenge when choosing a haircut, but medium haircuts are perfect for women who wear glasses.

How to

Before choosing a haircut, it's important to consider your face shape, hair type, and glasses style. Oval, round, and heart-shaped faces look great with medium haircuts. If you have thin hair, a layered medium haircut can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a blunt medium haircut can help to thin it out. When choosing glasses, go for frames that complement your face shape and haircut.

Step by step

1. Look for inspiration - Browse magazines, social media, or ask your hairstylist for ideas about medium haircuts for over 60 with glasses.

2. Choose a haircut - Decide on a medium haircut that suits your face shape and hair type. Some popular options include layered, bob, shag, and lob haircuts.

3. Consult a hairstylist - Bring a photo of your chosen haircut to your hairstylist and discuss the best options for your hair.

4. Style your hair - Once you have your new haircut, experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. You can try blow-drying, curling, or straightening your hair.

Tips

- Choose a haircut that suits your lifestyle and personality.

- Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type.

- Wear glasses that complement your face shape and haircut.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear bangs with medium haircuts for over 60 with glasses?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with medium haircuts. However, it's important to choose bangs that suit your face shape and glasses style.

Q: Are there any medium haircuts that are low maintenance?

A: Yes, shag haircuts are low maintenance and perfect for over 60 women with glasses.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for hair care:

- Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

- Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

- Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best medium haircuts for over 60 with glasses, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and rock a new look!
