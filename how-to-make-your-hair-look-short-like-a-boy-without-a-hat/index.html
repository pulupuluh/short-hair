How To Make Your Hair Look Short Like A Boy Without A Hat

Written by William Jun 15, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your long hair and in the mood for a change? Do you want to try out a short look, but you’re not ready to make the commitment to chop it off? You’ve come to the right place! In this article, we will show you how to make your hair look short like a boy without a hat. Follow these steps and get ready for a new look!

Description

Many women want to try out a short haircut but hesitate because they don’t want to commit to a drastic change. With the tips we’re about to share, you can achieve the look of short hair without actually cutting your hair. This is a great way to experiment with a new style without the permanent commitment.

How To

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

You’ll need a few items to create the illusion of short hair. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray
  • Comb or brush

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Begin by washing and drying your hair. Once your hair is dry, apply some hairspray to give it some texture and hold. This will make it easier to style.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Starting from the front, section off a portion of your hair and pin it back with bobby pins. Do this on both sides of your head, leaving the back of your hair down. This will create the look of short hair on the sides.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have pinned back the sides of your hair, you can style the back. You can either leave it straight or add some texture by curling it with a curling iron or flat iron. You can also add some volume by teasing it with a comb or brush.

Step 5: Add Some Final Touches

Once you have styled your hair, give it a final spritz of hairspray to hold the look in place. You can also add some accessories like a headband or hair clip to complete the look.

Tips

  • Practice makes perfect! Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the look you love.
  • Invest in high-quality bobby pins that will hold your hair in place without slipping.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Don’t forget to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

FAQ

Can I do this with any hair length?

This style works best on medium to long hair. If you have very short hair, you may not be able to achieve the look of short hair without actually cutting it.

How long will this style last?

This style should last all day with the help of some hairspray. If you need to touch up throughout the day, simply re-pin any sections that have come loose and add some more hairspray.

What if I don’t have bobby pins?

You can use small hair clips or even a small elastic band to pin back the sides of your hair.

Hair Care

Remember to take good care of your hair by washing it regularly and using a good quality conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and always use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it healthy.

Now that you know how to make your hair look short like a boy without a hat, go ahead and try it out! It’s a fun way to experiment with a new style without making a permanent change. Have fun and enjoy your new look!
