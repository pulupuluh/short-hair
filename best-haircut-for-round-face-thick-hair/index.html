Best Haircut For Round Face Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Mar 30, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round face and thick hair, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that will flatter your round features while also managing your thick locks. Luckily, there are plenty of options available that can do just that. In this article, we'll explore the best haircut for round face thick hair, as well as provide tips and tricks for maintaining your new 'do.

  2. How to Choose the Best Haircut for Round Face Thick Hair
  3. Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Cut

  6. Hair Care Tips for Thick, Round Hair

Description

When it comes to haircuts for round face thick hair, there are a few key things to consider. First and foremost, you want a style that will elongate your face and draw attention away from your round cheeks. Additionally, you need a cut that can manage your thick locks without adding any unnecessary bulk. The right haircut can make all the difference, giving you a flattering and low-maintenance style that enhances your natural features.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Round Face Thick Hair

So, what exactly should you be looking for in a haircut for round face thick hair? Here are a few tips:

1. Consider length

Longer hairstyles tend to elongate the face, making them a great option for round faces. However, you also want to make sure your hair isn't too long, as this can add unnecessary weight and make your face look wider. A medium-length cut that falls just below the shoulders is a great option for round face thick hair.

2. Opt for layers

Layers can help to add movement and texture to your hair, making it more manageable and flattering. For round face thick hair, consider asking your stylist for long layers that frame the face. This will help to draw attention away from your round cheeks and towards your features.

3. Avoid blunt cuts

Blunt haircuts can add bulk and weight to your hair, making your face look wider. Instead, opt for a cut with soft, subtle layers that will help to enhance your natural features.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Cut

Ready to get the perfect haircut for your round face thick hair? Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Do your research

Before you head to the salon, do some research to find inspiration for your new cut. Look for styles that flatter your face shape and hair type, and bring pictures to show your stylist.

2. Consult with your stylist

When you arrive at the salon, consult with your stylist about the best haircut for your face shape and hair type. They can provide recommendations and help you find the perfect style.

3. Consider your hair texture

Thick hair can be challenging to manage, so make sure to discuss your hair texture with your stylist. They may recommend thinning or layering your hair to make it more manageable.

4. Get the cut

Once you've settled on a style, sit back and relax as your stylist works their magic. They'll carefully cut and shape your hair to create a flattering and low-maintenance style.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Your New Haircut

Now that you have the perfect haircut for your round face thick hair, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are a few tips:

1. Use the right products

Thick hair can be prone to dryness and damage, so it's important to use the right products to keep it healthy. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are designed for thick, coarse hair.

2. Style your hair properly

To maintain your haircut's shape, style your hair properly using a blow dryer, curling iron, or straightener as needed. Avoid using too much heat, as this can damage your hair.

3. Get regular trims

To keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy, make sure to get regular trims every 6-8 weeks. This will help to prevent split ends and maintain your style's shape.

FAQs About Haircuts for Round Face Thick Hair

What is the best haircut for a round face with thick hair?

The best haircut for a round face with thick hair is one that elongates the face and manages your thick locks. Consider a medium-length cut with long layers that frame the face.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face and thick hair?

Avoid blunt haircuts that add bulk and weight to your hair, as well as short hairstyles that accentuate your round cheeks.

Hair Care Tips for Thick, Round Hair

1. Use a deep conditioner

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and hydrated.

2. Dry your hair gently

Pat your hair dry gently with a towel instead of rubbing it vigorously.

3. Brush your hair regularly

Brush your hair regularly to keep it detangled and healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect haircut for your round face thick hair and maintain it properly for a flattering and low-maintenance style.
