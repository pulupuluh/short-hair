How To Cut The Sides Of Men s Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Mar 16, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Cutting men's hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're a beginner. But with the right tools and some practice, you can master the art of cutting hair in no time. In this article, we'll discuss how to cut the sides of men's hair, step by step. We'll also provide some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect cut.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • How to Cut the Sides of Men's Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Men's Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting hair, you'll need to gather some essential tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Clipper guards (optional)

Make sure to use high-quality tools to ensure a clean and precise cut. You don't want to end up with a choppy or uneven haircut.

How to Cut the Sides of Men's Hair

When it comes to cutting the sides of men's hair, there are a few different techniques you can use. Some people prefer to use clippers, while others prefer to use scissors. Here's a general overview of how to cut the sides of men's hair:

1. Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut and style, so it's best to work with dry hair.

2. Comb the Hair

Use a comb to part the hair on the sides. You can choose how far up the hair you want to cut, but a good rule of thumb is to cut up to the point where the hair starts to curve.

3. Choose Your Technique

Next, choose your cutting technique. You can use clippers or scissors, depending on your preference. Clippers are faster and easier to use, but scissors give you more control over the cut.

4. Start Cutting

Start cutting the hair, using either the clippers or scissors. Work in small sections, cutting the hair at an angle to create a gradual, tapered look. Make sure to cut evenly on both sides.

5. Blend the Hair

Once you've finished cutting both sides, use the clippers or scissors to blend the hair at the back and the top. This will create a smooth, even look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed, step-by-step guide to cutting the sides of men's hair:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Start by washing the hair with shampoo and conditioner. Then, towel dry the hair until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Comb the Hair

Use a comb to create a part on the sides of the hair. You can choose how far up the hair you want to cut, but a good rule of thumb is to cut up to the point where the hair starts to curve.

Step 3: Choose Your Technique

Choose whether you want to use clippers or scissors. Clippers are faster and easier to use, but scissors give you more control over the cut.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting the hair, working in small sections. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut at an angle to create a tapered look. Make sure to cut evenly on both sides.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've finished cutting both sides, use the clippers or scissors to blend the hair at the back and the top. This will create a smooth, even look.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Use sharp scissors and clippers to ensure a clean cut.
  • Work in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Use clipper guards to create a consistent length.
  • Blend the hair at the back and the top to create a smooth, even look.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.

FAQs

How often should men get their hair cut?

Men should get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain a clean, polished look.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting hair, don't panic. You can always use scissors to trim the hair and even out the cut.

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair, but it's important to use caution and go slowly. It's always best to have a professional cut your hair if possible.

Hair Care Advice

After you've finished cutting the hair, it's important to take care of it to ensure it stays healthy and looks great. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy appearance.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut the sides of men's hair and achieve a polished, professional look. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced hair stylist, these tips will help you achieve the perfect cut every time.
