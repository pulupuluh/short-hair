How To Spice Up A Pixie Cut Tips And Tricks

Written by William May 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a way to give your pixie cut a fresh and stylish new look, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll be discussing how to spice up a pixie cut with simple tips and tricks that you can try at home. Whether you want a bold and edgy look or a subtle and sophisticated one, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

How to Spice Up a Pixie Cut

1. Add Some Texture

Pixie cuts can sometimes look flat and lifeless, especially if you have fine or thin hair. To add some texture and volume, try using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. These products will give your hair some grip and make it easier to style. You can also try using a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves or curls.

2. Experiment with Color

Changing your hair color is a great way to spice up a pixie cut. You can go for a bold and bright color, like red or purple, or opt for a more subtle shade, like caramel or honey blonde. If you're not ready to commit to a permanent color, try using temporary hair dye or colored hair extensions.

3. Play with Accessories

Accessories can instantly elevate your pixie cut and make it look more stylish. Try adding a headband, scarf, or hairpin to your hair. You can also experiment with different types of hats, like fedoras or beanies, to add some extra flair.

4. Try a Bold Makeup Look

Your pixie cut is a great canvas for bold and dramatic makeup looks. Try experimenting with bright eyeshadow colors, bold lipsticks, or graphic eyeliner. You can also add some extra sparkle with glitter or metallic eyeshadows.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to spice up your pixie cut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair some extra lift.

Step 2: Apply Texturizing Spray

Spray a texturizing spray or dry shampoo on your hair to add some texture and grip.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves or curls. You can also use a hairbrush and blow dryer to add volume and texture.

Step 4: Add Accessories

Add a headband, scarf, or hairpin to your hair to add some extra style.

Step 5: Finish with Makeup

Complete your look with a bold makeup look. Try using bright eyeshadow colors, bold lipsticks, or graphic eyeliner.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining your pixie cut:

1. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your pixie cut looking fresh and stylish.

2. Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for short hair, like texturizing sprays and dry shampoos.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from damage by using heat protectant sprays and avoiding excessive heat styling.

4. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and looks. Your pixie cut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

FAQs

Q. Can I still wear hair extensions with a pixie cut?

A. Yes, you can still wear hair extensions with a pixie cut. Try using clip-in hair extensions or tape-in extensions for a temporary look.

Q. What type of makeup looks best with a pixie cut?

A. Bold and dramatic makeup looks best with a pixie cut. Try using bright eyeshadow colors, bold lipsticks, or graphic eyeliner.

Conclusion

We hope these tips and tricks have inspired you to spice up your pixie cut and try something new. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles and looks. With the right products and accessories, you can create a unique and stylish look that is all your own.
