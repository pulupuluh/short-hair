How Many Years Does It Take To Grow Dreads

Written by Frank Jun 28, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're considering growing dreadlocks, one of the first questions you may have is, "How many years does it take to grow dreads?" The answer to this question is not a straightforward one, as it depends on several factors, including your hair type, the length and thickness of your hair, and the method you use to create your dreads. In this blog post, we'll explore the different factors that can affect how long it takes to grow dreads and provide you with tips on how to care for your hair during the process.

Factors Affecting How Long it Takes to Grow Dreads

Hair Type

The type of hair you have can have a significant impact on how long it takes to grow dreads. If you have fine or straight hair, it may take longer for your hair to lock into place than if you have coarse or curly hair. This is because fine hair has less texture and can be more slippery, making it harder for the hair to interlock. On the other hand, curly hair has more texture, making it easier for the hair to tangle and lock into dreadlocks.

Length and Thickness of Hair

The length and thickness of your hair can also affect how long it takes to grow dreads. If you have short hair, it may take longer for your hair to lock into place because there is less hair to work with. Similarly, if you have thin hair, it may take longer for your hair to create thick, full-looking dreadlocks. On the other hand, if you have long, thick hair, your dreads may start to take shape more quickly.

Method Used to Create Dreads

The method you use to create your dreads can also impact how long it takes for them to form. There are several different methods for creating dreadlocks, including backcombing, twist and rip, and freeform. Backcombing involves teasing the hair and rolling it into dreadlocks, while twist and rip involves twisting sections of hair and then ripping them apart to create knots. Freeform dreads are created by allowing the hair to naturally lock and tangle over time.

How to Grow Dreads

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're ready to start growing dreadlocks, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. It's important to have clean hair before you start the dreadlocking process. You can wash your hair with a residue-free shampoo to remove any product buildup.
  2. Choose a method for creating your dreads. Decide which method you want to use to create your dreadlocks.
  3. Section your hair. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. The size of the sections will depend on the thickness of your hair and the size of the dreadlocks you want to create.
  4. Begin the dreadlocking process. Use your chosen method to create your dreadlocks. Make sure to work carefully and methodically to avoid damaging your hair.
  5. Maintain your dreads. Once your dreadlocks have formed, it's important to maintain them properly to keep them looking healthy and neat. This may involve regular washing, conditioning, and retwisting.

Tips for Growing Dreads

Here are some tips to help you grow healthy, beautiful dreadlocks:

  • Be patient. Growing dreadlocks takes time, so don't expect to see results overnight.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or products on your hair. These can damage your hair and slow down the dreadlocking process.
  • Keep your hair moisturized. Dry hair is more prone to breakage, so make sure to moisturize your hair regularly to keep it healthy.
  • Retwist your dreads regularly. This will help to keep them looking neat and prevent them from unraveling.

FAQs About Growing Dreads

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing dreadlocks:

How long does it take to grow dreads?

The amount of time it takes to grow dreadlocks varies depending on several factors, including your hair type, the length and thickness of your hair, and the method you use to create your dreads. It can take anywhere from a few months to several years to grow fully formed dreadlocks.

Can you grow dreadlocks with short hair?

Yes, you can grow dreadlocks with short hair. However, it may take longer for your hair to form fully formed dreadlocks than if you have longer hair.

Can you wash your hair with dreadlocks?

Yes, you can and should wash your hair regularly when you have dreadlocks. It's important to use a residue-free shampoo to avoid buildup and keep your hair looking and feeling clean.

Hair Care for Dreadlocks

Caring for your hair when you have dreadlocks is crucial to keeping them looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining your dreadlocks:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner specifically designed for dreadlocks to keep them moisturized.
  • Retwist your dreads regularly to keep them looking neat.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or products on your hair.
  • Protect your hair when sleeping or wearing hats by covering your hair with a silk or satin scarf.

Now that you know the answer to the question, "How many years does it take to grow dreads?" you can start your journey towards beautiful, healthy dreadlocks. Remember to be patient, take care of your hair, and enjoy the process!
