What Hair Type Is Thick Wavy Hair

Written by William Aug 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you wondering if your hair is considered thick wavy hair? In this article, we will delve into the characteristics of this hair type, how to care for it, and some tips and tricks to enhance its natural beauty.

Description of Thick Wavy Hair

Thick wavy hair is a hair type that has a lot of volume and body. It is characterized by loose, large waves that are not very defined, but give the hair a natural, beachy look. This hair type can be difficult to manage, as it tends to get frizzy and tangled easily, especially in humid weather.

How to Determine if Your Hair is Thick Wavy

If you are unsure if you have thick wavy hair, there are a few things you can look for. First, check the thickness of your individual hair strands. If they are thicker than average, you likely have thick hair. Then, examine the texture of your hair. If it has a natural wave pattern that is not very defined, it is likely wavy. If you have both of these characteristics, you have thick wavy hair.

Step-by-Step Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

Now that you know you have thick wavy hair, it's important to learn how to care for it properly. Follow these steps for healthy, beautiful hair:

Step 1: Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for thick hair. Avoid products that contain sulfates, as they can strip the hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Step 2: Detangle

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair while it is wet. Be gentle, as thick wavy hair can easily get tangled and break.

Step 3: Air Dry

Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves.

Step 4: Nourishing Products

Use nourishing hair products like leave-in conditioners and hair oils to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Tips for Enhancing Thick Wavy Hair

Want to make the most of your thick wavy hair? Try these tips:

Tip 1: Use Sea Salt Spray

Sea salt spray can enhance your natural waves and give your hair a beachy, textured look.

Tip 2: Try a Braid

Braiding your hair while it is still damp can help define your waves and prevent frizz.

Tip 3: Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to gently detangle your hair to prevent breakage and frizz.

FAQ About Thick Wavy Hair

Q: Can I straighten my thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair, but it's important to use heat protectant products to prevent damage.

Q: Can I dye my thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, but it's important to use hair dye formulated for thick hair and to follow the instructions carefully to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: Can I use a hair mask on my thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, using a hair mask once a week can help nourish and moisturize your hair.

Conclusion

Thick wavy hair is a beautiful hair type that requires proper care and attention to keep it healthy and looking its best. By following the steps outlined in this article and incorporating some of the tips and tricks, you can enhance your natural waves and keep your hair looking beautiful.
