Hairstyles That Suit Big Faces A Guide For 2023

Written by Petter Aug 07, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you someone with a big face and struggling to find the perfect hairstyle? You are not alone. Many people with a round or wide face shape have a hard time deciding which hairstyle to go for. In this article, we will help you find the best hairstyles that suit big faces and give you some tips on how to take care of your hair.

Description

A big face is characterized by having a wide forehead and cheeks, a round chin, and a soft jawline. The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for a big face is to create an illusion of length and height. This means going for hairstyles that add volume to the crown or the top of the head and elongate the face shape.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a big face, there are a few things to consider:

Face Shape

Before deciding on a hairstyle, determine your face shape. A big face can be round, square, or heart-shaped. Different face shapes suit different hairstyles, so it's essential to know which one you have.

Hair Texture

Another crucial factor to consider is hair texture. Straight hair, curly hair, and wavy hair all have different requirements when it comes to styling. Choose a hairstyle that works with your hair texture.

Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. If you have a busy schedule, go for a low-maintenance style that doesn't require much upkeep. If you have more time, you can experiment with different hairstyles.

10 Hairstyles That Suit Big Faces

Here are some of the best hairstyles that suit big faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers add volume to the crown and elongate the face shape. They work well on straight or wavy hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs soften the forehead and make the face appear longer. They work well on any hair texture.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves add texture and volume to the hair and create a relaxed, effortless look. They work well on wavy or curly hair.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, low-maintenance hairstyle that works well on straight or wavy hair. It adds volume to the crown and elongates the face shape.

5. High Ponytail

A high ponytail adds height to the crown and elongates the face shape. It works well on any hair texture.

6. Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that works well on straight or wavy hair. It adds volume to the crown and elongates the face shape.

7. Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down hairstyle adds volume to the crown and elongates the face shape. It works well on any hair texture.

8. Side Part

A side part adds volume to the crown and elongates the face shape. It works well on any hair texture.

9. Updo

An updo adds height to the crown and elongates the face shape. It works well on any hair texture.

10. Straight and Sleek

Straight and sleek hair elongates the face shape and works well on straight hair.

Tips for Taking Care of Your Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products that are suitable for your hair type. For example, use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for straight or curly hair.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid excessive heat styling, as it can damage your hair. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray.

3. Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

4. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs

1. Can I wear a short hairstyle with a big face?

Yes, you can wear a short hairstyle with a big face. A short hairstyle can add volume to the crown and elongate the face shape.

2. Can I wear bangs with a big face?

Yes, you can wear bangs with a big face. Side-swept bangs can soften the forehead and make the face appear longer.

3. How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Use the right products, avoid excessive heat styling, get regular trims, and protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.

With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident every day.
