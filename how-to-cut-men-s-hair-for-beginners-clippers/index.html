How To Cut Men s Hair For Beginners Clippers A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel May 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of spending money at the barber every month to get your hair cut? Or maybe you just want to learn a new skill? Cutting men's hair with clippers is not as hard as it seems. In fact, with the right tools and some practice, you can give yourself or someone else a great haircut right at home. In this article, we will walk you through the steps to cut men's hair for beginners clippers.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You Will Need
  • How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Great Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After the Haircut

Tools You Will Need

Before you start cutting, you will need the following tools:

  • Clippers with guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Cape or towel to cover the person's clothes
  • Clipper oil
  • A spray bottle with water

Make sure your clippers are sharp and clean before you start. Dull clippers can pull the hair and hurt the person. Clean clippers will cut better and last longer. You can use a small brush to clean the blades and a few drops of oil to keep them lubricated.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers

Cutting men's hair with clippers is easy and quick. You can choose from different clipper guards to get the desired length. If you are not sure which guard to use, start with the longest one and work your way down until you reach the desired length.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut men's hair with clippers:

  1. Prepare the person's hair by washing it and combing it. Wet hair is easier to cut and combing it will remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Cover the person with a cape or towel to protect their clothes from hair clippings.
  3. Choose the guard size you want to use and attach it to the clippers.
  4. Start at the back of the head and work your way up. Use the clippers to cut the hair against the direction it grows. Move the clippers from the bottom of the head upwards.
  5. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim the longer parts with the scissors. This will give a more even look to the haircut.
  6. Move to the sides of the head and follow the same pattern. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim it with the scissors.
  7. Cut the hair at the top of the head. Use the longest guard and cut the hair from the front to the back. This will give a more natural look to the haircut.
  8. Blend the hair from the sides to the top. Use a shorter guard and move the clippers in an upward motion to blend the hair from the sides to the top.
  9. Trim the hairline around the ears and the neck. Use a shorter guard or no guard at all to trim the hairline around the ears and the neck. This will give a more polished look to the haircut.
  10. Clean the clippers and scissors with a brush and oil. Remove any hair clippings from the person's clothes.

Tips for a Great Haircut

Here are some tips to help you get a great haircut:

  • Practice makes perfect. Don't worry if your first haircut is not perfect. It takes time to learn how to cut hair with clippers.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head and the sides. This will help you see any uneven parts and adjust them.
  • Keep the clippers and scissors clean and sharp. This will make the haircut easier and faster.
  • Use a spray bottle to wet the hair if it gets dry. Dry hair is harder to cut and can cause the clippers to pull the hair.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to cut small sections and check the length than to cut too much and have to start over.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. Use a mirror to see the back of your head and follow the same steps as you would with someone else.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't worry if you make a mistake. Hair grows back and you can always go to a barber to fix it. Just try to learn from your mistake and adjust it next time.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on the person's hair type and style. Some people need a haircut every month, while others can go up to three months. If you are not sure, ask your barber for advice.

Hair Care After the Haircut

After the haircut, wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any hair clippings and keep your hair healthy. You can also use a styling product to give your hair a desired look.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair for beginners clippers, you can save money and time by doing it yourself. Remember to practice, be patient, and have fun. Happy cutting!
