What To Do If Your Hair Is Sticking Up Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Sep 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Have you ever woken up in the morning, looked in the mirror, and found that your hair is sticking up in all directions? Don't worry, you're not alone. This is a common problem that many people face, especially those with curly or frizzy hair. In this article, we'll provide you with some tips and tricks on what to do if your hair is sticking up.

Table of Content

  • How to Fix Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Fix Your Hair

If your hair is sticking up, there are a few things you can do to fix it. Here are some options:

1. Wet Your Hair

If your hair is sticking up because it's dry or frizzy, try wetting it down. You can either take a shower or wet your hair with a spray bottle. Once your hair is wet, you can style it as normal.

2. Use a Comb

If your hair is sticking up in certain areas, use a comb to smooth it down. Start at the roots and gently comb through your hair until it's lying flat.

3. Use Hair Products

There are many hair products available that can help tame frizz and keep your hair lying flat. Look for products like hair gel, pomade, or hairspray that are designed to hold your hair in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on what to do if your hair is sticking up:

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Start by wetting your hair down with water. You can either take a shower or wet your hair with a spray bottle.

Step 2: Apply Hair Products

Apply a hair product like gel or pomade to your hair. Use a dime-sized amount and work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to smooth your hair down. Start at the roots and gently comb through your hair until it's lying flat.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is lying flat, style it as normal. You can blow dry it or let it air dry.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help keep your hair from sticking up:

1. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and keep your hair lying flat.

2. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to frizz and flyaways. Try to wash your hair every other day instead of every day.

3. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

A wide-tooth comb is gentler on your hair than a brush and can help prevent breakage and frizz.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and lead to frizz and flyaways. Try to avoid using them as much as possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why is my hair sticking up?

A: There are many reasons why your hair might be sticking up, including dryness, frizz, and static electricity.

Q: Can I use hairspray to keep my hair from sticking up?

A: Yes, hairspray can help hold your hair in place and prevent it from sticking up.

Q: Should I use a brush or a comb to fix my hair?

A: It's best to use a comb instead of a brush to fix your hair, as a brush can cause breakage and frizz.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Look for a shampoo and conditioner that are designed to moisturize your hair and prevent dryness.

2. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals can help keep your hair looking healthy and strong.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair from sticking up and looking its best. Remember to be gentle with your hair, avoid using too much heat, and protect it from the sun and other harsh elements.
