How To Cut A Long Layered Hair Cut A 2023 Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 15, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your long hair looking flat and lifeless? A long layered hair cut can add volume, texture, and movement to your locks! In this guide, we’ll show you how to cut a long layered hair cut step by step. From tips to frequently asked questions, we’ve got you covered!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Cut a Long Layered Hair Cut
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A long layered hair cut is a hairstyle that features longer hair with shorter layers cut into it. The layers can add volume, texture, and movement to the hair. This style can work for all hair types and face shapes, and can be customized to fit your personal preferences.

How to Cut a Long Layered Hair Cut

To cut a long layered hair cut, you will need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Water spray bottle
  • Styling products (optional)

Step by Step

Follow these steps to cut a long layered hair cut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle or on the side, depending on your preference.
  3. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and comb it straight down.
  4. Hold the section of hair between your fingers at the length you want the shortest layer to be.
  5. Using your scissors, cut the hair straight across at the length you are holding it.
  6. Release the hair and let it fall back into place.
  7. Take another section of hair from the front of your head, just behind the first section.
  8. Comb it straight down and hold it between your fingers, making sure it is at the same length as the first section you cut.
  9. Cut the hair straight across at the same length as the first section.
  10. Continue taking sections of hair from the front of your head and cutting them to the same length until you reach the back of your head.
  11. Release the hair and comb it out to see the layers.
  12. If you want shorter layers, repeat the process on the top layer of your hair.
  13. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a long layered hair cut:

  • Start with small sections of hair to ensure you get an even cut.
  • Don’t cut too much hair at once.
  • Keep your scissors sharp to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take breaks if your hand gets tired.
  • If you’re not confident in cutting your own hair, consider going to a professional stylist.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long layered hair cuts:

How often should I get a long layered hair cut?

You can get a long layered hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the layers and keep your hair healthy.

Will a long layered hair cut work for my hair type?

Yes! A long layered hair cut can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and thick hair.

What face shape does a long layered hair cut work best for?

A long layered hair cut can work for all face shapes! The layers can be customized to flatter your features.

Hair Care

After you’ve cut your long layered hair cut, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the layers and keep your hair healthy.

Congratulations, you’ve learned how to cut a long layered hair cut! With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful, textured hairstyle that will make you feel confident and stylish.
