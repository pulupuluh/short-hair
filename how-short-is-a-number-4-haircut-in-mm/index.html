How Short Is A Number 4 Haircut In Mm

Written by Aprilia May 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

When it comes to getting a haircut, it's important to know what you're asking for. One common haircut that many men get is a number 4 haircut. But how short is a number 4 haircut in mm? In this article, we'll explore everything you need to know about this popular haircut.

Description

A number 4 haircut is typically around 12mm in length. This means that the hair on top of your head will be cut to a length of around 12mm, or just under half an inch. The sides and back of your head will be tapered shorter, usually around 9mm or 6mm, depending on your preference.

How to Get a Number 4 Haircut

To get a number 4 haircut, you'll need to visit a barber or hair stylist who is familiar with this style. When you arrive, you can simply ask for a number 4 haircut. The stylist will then use clippers to cut your hair to the appropriate length, and will taper the sides and back as needed.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to get a number 4 haircut:

  1. Visit a barber or hair stylist
  2. Ask for a number 4 haircut
  3. Sit in the chair and let the stylist get to work
  4. The stylist will use clippers to cut your hair to the appropriate length
  5. The sides and back will be tapered shorter, usually around 9mm or 6mm
  6. Double check with the stylist if you're happy with the length
  7. Pay for your haircut and leave the salon looking sharp!

Tips for Maintaining a Number 4 Haircut

If you want to keep your number 4 haircut looking fresh, there are a few things you can do:

  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim
  • Invest in a good quality set of clippers to touch up your hair in between appointments
  • Use a pomade or wax to style your hair and keep it in place
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy

FAQs

Can I get a number 4 haircut with scissors?

While it's technically possible to get a number 4 haircut with scissors, it's not recommended. Clippers are the best tool for achieving the precise length and tapering required for a number 4 haircut.

Will a number 4 haircut suit me?

A number 4 haircut is a versatile style that can suit most face shapes and hair types. However, it's always a good idea to consult with your stylist before making any major changes to your hair.

How short is a number 4 haircut compared to other haircuts?

A number 4 haircut is shorter than a number 5 or 6 haircut, but longer than a number 3 or 2 haircut. It's also longer than a buzz cut, which is typically around 3mm in length.

Hair Care for a Number 4 Haircut

To keep your number 4 haircut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. This includes washing it regularly with a gentle shampoo, using a conditioner to keep it soft and manageable, and avoiding heat styling tools that can damage your hair.

With the right care and maintenance, your number 4 haircut can look great for weeks at a time. So why not give it a try and see how it works for you?
