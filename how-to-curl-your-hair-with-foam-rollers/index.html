How To Curl Your Hair With Foam Rollers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a way to curl your hair without using heat, foam rollers are a great option. Not only are they gentle on your hair, but they're also easy to use and can create a variety of different curl styles. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of curling your hair with foam rollers, from start to finish. So grab your rollers and let's get started!

How to Use Foam Rollers to Curl Your Hair

Before we get into the step-by-step process of using foam rollers, let's talk about the different types of rollers you can use. Foam rollers come in a variety of sizes and shapes, from small and tight to large and loose. The size of the roller you choose will determine the type of curl you create. Small rollers will create tight curls, while larger rollers will create looser waves.

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start rolling, you'll need to prep your hair. Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. Next, towel dry your hair until it's damp but not dripping wet. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you can skip the washing and just dampen your hair with water.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, you'll want to section your hair. Using a comb, divide your hair into small sections, starting at the bottom and working your way up. The size of the section will depend on the size of the roller you're using. For small rollers, you'll want to use smaller sections of hair. For larger rollers, you can use larger sections.

Step 3: Roll Your Hair

Now it's time to start rolling! Take a section of hair and place the roller at the end of the hair. Roll the hair up towards your scalp, making sure to keep the roller tight against your head. Once you reach the scalp, use the clips that come with the rollers to hold the roller in place. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Dry

Once all of your hair is rolled, you'll need to let it dry. You can either let your hair air dry or use a hair dryer to speed up the process. If you're using a hair dryer, be sure to use a diffuser attachment to avoid frizz and damage.

Step 5: Remove the Rollers

Once your hair is completely dry, it's time to remove the rollers. Start at the bottom and work your way up, carefully removing each roller and gently teasing out the curls with your fingers.

Tips for Using Foam Rollers to Curl Your Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when using foam rollers to curl your hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to avoid damage to your hair
  • Roll your hair tightly for tighter curls
  • Roll your hair more loosely for looser waves
  • Let your hair dry completely before removing the rollers
  • Use a diffuser attachment when using a hair dryer
  • Tease out your curls gently with your fingers to avoid frizz

FAQs About Curling Your Hair with Foam Rollers

Q: How long do you leave foam rollers in your hair?

A: Foam rollers should be left in your hair until your hair is completely dry. This can take anywhere from a few hours to overnight.

Q: How do you sleep with foam rollers in your hair?

A: Sleeping with foam rollers in your hair can be uncomfortable, but it's possible. You can try using a satin pillowcase to reduce friction and keep the rollers in place while you sleep.

Q: Can you use foam rollers on short hair?

A: Yes, foam rollers can be used on short hair. Just make sure to use smaller rollers and smaller sections of hair.

Hair Care Tips for Curled Hair

Now that you've curled your hair with foam rollers, it's important to take care of your curls to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to keep your curls hydrated and defined
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your curls too often, as this can cause damage

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls with foam rollers. So go ahead and give it a try!
