How To Cut Wavy Curly Hair Yourself A Guide For 2023

Written by Petter Feb 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of shelling out big bucks at the hair salon every few months? Or maybe you just prefer the comfort of your own home when it comes to haircuts. Whatever the reason, cutting wavy curly hair yourself is easier than you might think. With a few simple tools and some basic knowledge, you can achieve a fresh, stylish cut that will leave you feeling confident and beautiful. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step by step, from preparing your hair to finishing your cut.

How to Prepare Your Hair

The first step in cutting wavy curly hair yourself is to prepare your hair. This means washing it and letting it air dry naturally. Avoid using any styling products or heat tools, as these can affect the texture of your hair and make it more difficult to cut. Once your hair is dry, comb it carefully to remove any tangles or knots.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Wavy Curly Hair

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using hair clips. Start with the top section of your hair and clip it up and out of the way. Then, divide the rest of your hair into small sections, clipping each one as you go.

Step 2: Cut the Bottom Layer

Start by cutting the bottom layer of your hair. Take a small section of hair from the back of your head and hold it between your index and middle fingers. Use a pair of hair cutting scissors to trim the ends of the hair, cutting at a slight angle to create layers. Repeat this process on all of the sections in the bottom layer of your hair.

Step 3: Cut the Middle Layer

Once you've finished the bottom layer, release the top section of your hair and clip the bottom layer out of the way. Then, start cutting the middle layer of your hair using the same technique as before. Take small sections of hair and trim the ends at a slight angle to create layers.

Step 4: Cut the Top Layer

Finally, release the bottom layer of your hair and clip the middle layer out of the way. Cut the top layer of your hair using the same technique as before, taking small sections and trimming the ends at a slight angle to create layers.

Tips for Cutting Wavy Curly Hair Yourself

- Use sharp, high-quality hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut.
- Cut your hair when it's dry, as cutting wet hair can lead to unevenness.
- Start with small sections and work your way up to larger ones as you gain confidence.
- Don't be afraid to make mistakes! Hair grows back, and you can always fix any mistakes later on.
- Take breaks as needed to avoid fatigue and maintain focus.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut wavy curly hair myself?

A: Yes! With the right tools and techniques, cutting wavy curly hair yourself is completely doable.

Q: How often should I cut my wavy curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair's individual growth rate and how quickly you want to maintain your style. As a general rule, cutting your hair every 6-8 weeks is a good idea.

Q: What if I mess up my haircut?

A: Don't panic! If you make a mistake, you can always visit a professional stylist to fix it. Alternatively, you can wait for your hair to grow out and try again.

Hair Care for Wavy Curly Hair

Now that you know how to cut wavy curly hair yourself, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty. Here are a few tips:

- Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
- Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can lead to frizz.
- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
- Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to enhance your hair's natural texture.
- Avoid using heat tools whenever possible, as these can damage your hair and lead to breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful, stylish haircut that showcases your wavy curly hair's natural beauty. Happy cutting!
