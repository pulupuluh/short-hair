How To Get The Best Haircut For Your Face Shape

Written by Aprilia May 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Getting a new haircut can be exciting, but it can also be daunting. With so many different haircuts to choose from, it can be difficult to know which one will suit you best. The key to getting the best haircut for your face shape is to understand what works for you and what doesn't. In this article, we'll take you through some tips on how to get the best haircut for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Your face shape plays a big part in determining which haircut will suit you best. Whether you have a round, square, oval, or heart-shaped face, there's a haircut that will flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we'll take you through the steps to finding the best haircut for your face shape, as well as some tips on how to care for your hair.

How To

The first step to getting the best haircut for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. You can do this by pulling your hair back and examining your face in the mirror. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for haircuts that will complement your features.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want a haircut that will elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Long layers or a side-swept fringe can help achieve this. Avoid blunt cuts and short styles that will make your face look wider.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and angular features. Soften your features with a layered haircut that falls just below your chin. Avoid blunt cuts and short styles that will accentuate your jawline.

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is considered the ideal face shape, as it's well-proportioned and versatile. You can wear almost any haircut with an oval face shape, but a layered bob or long layers can accentuate your cheekbones and enhance your features.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want a haircut that balances out your features. A long bob or a side-swept fringe can help soften your forehead and jawline. Avoid short styles that will make your forehead appear wider.

Step by Step

Now that you know which haircut will suit your face shape, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Research haircuts that will suit your face shape.
  2. Bring pictures of the haircut you want to show your hairstylist.
  3. Communicate with your hairstylist about what you want and what you don't want.
  4. Ask for their professional opinion on what will suit you best.
  5. Be open to suggestions, but also trust your instincts.
  6. Ask your hairstylist for tips on how to style your new haircut.
  7. Book your next appointment to maintain your new haircut.

Tips

Getting the best haircut for your face shape is just the first step. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

It's not uncommon to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, you'll want to choose a haircut that will balance out your features. Your hairstylist can help you determine which haircut will suit you best.

Can I still wear a certain haircut if it doesn't suit my face shape?

Of course! At the end of the day, it's all about what makes you feel confident and beautiful. If you love a certain haircut, go for it! Just be aware that it may not be the most flattering for your face shape.

How often should I get a haircut?

This depends on your hair type and the style of your haircut. As a general rule, you should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Hair Care

Getting the best haircut for your face shape is just the first step. Taking care of your hair is essential to keeping it healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid tying your hair up too tightly, as this can cause tension on your scalp.
  • Eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair stays healthy and beautiful, no matter what haircut you choose.
