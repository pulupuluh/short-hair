How To Cut Your Own Hair Straight Across The Back

Written by Eveline Sep 10, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts every few weeks? Do you want to learn how to cut your own hair straight across the back? Look no further, as we have put together a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home.

Description

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially when it comes to cutting the back. However, with the right tools and technique, it is achievable. This guide will help you achieve a straight cut at the back of your hair, leaving you with a clean and polished look.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Straight Across the Back

To achieve a straight cut at the back of your hair, you will need the following tools:

  • A pair of sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A comb
  • A hand-held mirror
  • A hair tie or clip

Once you have gathered your tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before beginning the cutting process. It is important to have clean and dry hair, as wet hair can be more difficult to cut.

Step 2: Part Your Hair in the Middle

Using your comb, create a part in the middle of your hair. This will help to ensure that your cut is straight.

Step 3: Tie Your Hair Up

Using a hair tie or clip, tie your hair up into a ponytail. Make sure that the ponytail is at the height where you want your hair to be cut. If you want a shorter cut, tie your hair higher up on your head.

Step 4: Cut Across the Ponytail

Using your scissors, cut straight across the ponytail. Be sure to cut slowly and carefully, checking that your cut is straight as you go along. If you are unsure, you can use your hand-held mirror to check the back of your head.

Step 5: Check for Any Unevenness

After you have cut across the ponytail, release your hair and check for any unevenness. If you notice any unevenness, trim the hair until it is even.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a professional-looking cut:

  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Cut slowly and carefully to avoid any mistakes
  • Check for any unevenness and trim as necessary
  • Use a hand-held mirror to check the back of your head
  • Start with a small cut and gradually work your way up to your desired length

FAQ

Can I cut my hair straight across the back without a ponytail?

It is possible to cut your hair straight across the back without a ponytail, but it can be more difficult to achieve an even cut. Using a ponytail will help to ensure that your cut is straight.

What should I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim the hair until it is even. If you are unsure, it is always best to start with a small cut and gradually work your way up to your desired length.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and smooth
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too frequently
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and health

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and cost-effective way to achieve a new look. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home.
