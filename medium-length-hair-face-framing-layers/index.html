Medium Length Hair Face Framing Layers A Guide To Perfect Hair

Written by Frank Jul 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a change? Do you want to add some style to your medium length hair? Look no further, as face framing layers are the perfect solution! This article will guide you through what face framing layers are, how to get them, and how to take care of them.

What are Medium Length Hair Face Framing Layers?

Face framing layers are a type of haircut that features shorter layers around the face, adding depth and dimension to the hair. They help to highlight your facial features and add movement to your hair. Medium length hair is the perfect length for face framing layers, as it allows for versatility and easy styling.

How to Get Medium Length Hair Face Framing Layers?

The best way to get medium length hair face framing layers is by visiting a professional hair stylist. They will be able to assess your hair type and face shape to create the perfect layers for you. If you want to try it at home, make sure to use sharp scissors and take your time. Start by sectioning your hair and cutting the shorter layers around your face. Remember, less is more, and you can always add more layers later.

Step by Step Guide to Cutting Medium Length Hair Face Framing Layers

Step 1: Section your hair into two parts, the top and the bottom.

Step 2: Take a small section of hair from the front and comb it straight down.

Step 3: Hold the hair between your index and middle finger and cut it at an angle, starting from the cheekbone to the chin.

Step 4: Continue this process on both sides, making sure the layers are even.

Step 5: Blend the layers with the rest of your hair by combing through them with a brush or your fingers.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair Face Framing Layers

1. Use a heat protectant before styling your hair with heat tools like a curling iron or flat iron.

2. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and volume to your hair.

3. Trim your layers every six to eight weeks to maintain their shape and prevent split ends.

4. Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid tugging and breaking your hair.

5. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ about Medium Length Hair Face Framing Layers

Q. Who can get Medium Length Hair Face Framing Layers?

A. Anyone with medium length hair can get face framing layers. It's a versatile haircut that suits all face shapes and hair types.

Q. Can I style my Medium Length Hair Face Framing Layers in different ways?

A. Yes, you can style your layers in different ways, such as straightening, curling, or adding waves. Face framing layers are versatile and can be styled to suit your mood and occasion.

Q. How long does it take to grow out Medium Length Hair Face Framing Layers?

A. It can take up to six months to grow out face framing layers completely. During this time, you can trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair Face Framing Layers

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.

2. Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

3. Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip away its natural oils.

4. Use a leave-in conditioner or serum to protect your hair from environmental damage.

5. Don't brush your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage and damage.

Overall, medium length hair face framing layers are a great way to add style and depth to your hair. With the right care and maintenance, they can last for months and make you look and feel great. So, what are you waiting for? Book an appointment with your hairstylist today and get ready to rock your new look!
