Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Long On Top A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Sep 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, then the mens haircut shaved sides long on top is the way to go. This style has been popular for years and is still going strong in 2023. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about this trendy haircut, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Long on Top
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling
  • FAQs

Description

The mens haircut shaved sides long on top is a popular style that gives men a trendy and versatile look. This style features short or shaved sides and a longer top that can be styled in many ways. The shaved sides create an edgy and masculine look, while the longer top provides more styling options.

How to Get the Mens Haircut Shaved Sides Long on Top

If you want to get the mens haircut shaved sides long on top, you will need to visit a barber or hairstylist. They will be able to give you the perfect cut and style your hair to your liking. Before your appointment, it's a good idea to bring in a picture of the style you want. This will give your barber or hairstylist a better idea of what you're looking for.

Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow to get the mens haircut shaved sides long on top:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it
  2. Visit your barber or hairstylist
  3. Show them a picture of the style you want
  4. Ask for a fade or undercut on the sides
  5. Ask for the top to be left longer
  6. Choose a styling product you like
  7. Style as desired

Tips for Styling

Here are some tips for styling the mens haircut shaved sides long on top:

  • Use a pomade or wax to style your hair
  • Experiment with different parting styles
  • Blow dry your hair for added volume
  • Tousle your hair for a messy, textured look
  • Comb your hair back for a classic, sophisticated look

FAQs

Is the mens haircut shaved sides long on top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this style is suitable for all hair types. However, if you have thin or fine hair, you may need to use a volumizing product to create more texture and volume.

How often should I get my hair cut?

You should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

Yes, you can style your hair differently for different occasions. For example, you can slick your hair back for a formal event or tousle it for a casual day out.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut shaved sides long on top looking its best, you will need to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind
  • Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut

Overall, the mens haircut shaved sides long on top is a versatile and stylish haircut that can suit any man. By following our guide and tips, you can achieve the perfect look and keep your hair looking great.
