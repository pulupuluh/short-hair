Why Is My Hair Dry And Frizzy On Top

Written by Bardi Aug 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're struggling with dry and frizzy hair on top, you're not alone. Many people experience this issue, and it can be frustrating to deal with. In this blog post, we'll explore the reasons why your hair may be dry and frizzy on top and provide you with some tips on how to care for your hair to prevent this issue from happening in the future.

Why Is My Hair Dry and Frizzy on Top?

There are several reasons why your hair may be dry and frizzy on top:

  • Heat styling: Using hot tools like straighteners, curling irons, and blow dryers can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and prone to frizz.
  • Humidity: High humidity can cause your hair to absorb moisture from the air, leading to frizz.
  • Chemical treatments: Relaxers, perms, and other chemical treatments can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and frizzy.
  • Poor hair care habits: Not washing your hair frequently enough, using harsh shampoos, and not conditioning your hair properly can all contribute to dryness and frizz.

How to Care for Dry and Frizzy Hair on Top

If you're dealing with dry and frizzy hair on top, there are several things you can do to care for your hair:

Step 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Look for a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for dry, damaged hair. These products can help to restore moisture to your hair and reduce frizz.

Step 2: Use a Hair Mask or Deep Conditioner

Once a week, use a hair mask or deep conditioner to give your hair an extra dose of moisture. Leave the product on your hair for at least 10 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 3: Avoid Heat Styling

If possible, avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers and straighteners. If you must use these tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

Step 4: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help to keep your hair moisturized throughout the day. Apply the product to your hair after washing and conditioning it.

Tips for Preventing Dry and Frizzy Hair on Top

Here are some additional tips for preventing dry and frizzy hair on top:

  • Avoid overwashing: Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz. Try to wash your hair no more than every other day.
  • Avoid harsh shampoos: Look for a shampoo that is gentle and sulfate-free.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel: Instead, gently squeeze your hair with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to remove excess water.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

FAQ About Dry and Frizzy Hair on Top

Q: Can dry and frizzy hair on top be fixed?

A: Yes, with the right hair care routine, dry and frizzy hair on top can be fixed.

Q: How often should I use a hair mask?

A: Use a hair mask once a week to give your hair an extra dose of moisture.

Q: Can I still use heat styling tools?

A: Yes, you can still use heat styling tools, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

Hair Care for Dry and Frizzy Hair on Top

If you're struggling with dry and frizzy hair on top, don't worry - there are plenty of things you can do to care for your hair. By using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling, and using a leave-in conditioner, you can help to restore moisture to your hair and reduce frizz. And by following the tips we've provided, you can prevent dry and frizzy hair on top from happening in the first place.
