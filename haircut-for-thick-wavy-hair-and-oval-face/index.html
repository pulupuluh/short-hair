Haircut For Thick Wavy Hair And Oval Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 29, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect haircut for your thick wavy hair and oval face? Well, worry no more! We have compiled a comprehensive guide to help you find the perfect hairstyle that will flatter your face shape and enhance your natural curls.

Description

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse at the same time. While it adds volume and texture to your hair, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Coupled with an oval face shape, finding the right haircut can be a daunting task. Fortunately, there are many hairstyles that can complement your hair type and face shape.

How To

Before you head to the salon, it is essential to have a clear idea of the type of haircut you want. You can browse through magazines, websites, or Pinterest for inspiration. It’s also important to consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend on styling your hair.

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a great option for thick wavy hair and an oval face shape. It adds movement and texture to your hair while framing your face perfectly. Ask your stylist to add layers to your hair, starting from your chin and gradually getting longer towards the back.

2. Long Layers

If you prefer to keep your hair long, opt for long layers. This will add volume and texture to your hair without sacrificing the length. Make sure the layers start at your cheekbones and blend seamlessly into the rest of your hair.

3. Shoulder-Length Cut

A shoulder-length cut is another option that can flatter your face shape and hair type. It’s a versatile style that can be worn straight or wavy. Ask your stylist to add layers to create movement and volume.

Step by Step

1. Wash and condition your hair with products suitable for thick wavy hair.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.

3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help enhance your natural curls and add volume.

4. Use a curling iron to define your curls if needed.

5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips

- Use products that are specifically designed for thick wavy hair to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

- Avoid using a brush to detangle your hair as it can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

- Don’t over-wash your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for thick wavy hair and an oval face?

The best haircut for thick wavy hair and an oval face is a layered bob, long layers, or a shoulder-length cut.

How can I enhance my natural curls?

You can enhance your natural curls by using products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as a curl-enhancing cream or mousse. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to enhance your curls.

How often should I wash my hair?

If you have thick wavy hair, aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, it’s important to take care of it. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends. And finally, avoid using heat styling tools too often as it can damage your hair.

Now that you have the ultimate guide to finding the perfect haircut for your thick wavy hair and oval face, it’s time to head to the salon and get the haircut of your dreams!
