Hair Styles To Suit Round Faces

Written by Luffy Sep 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that don't seem to flatter your round face? Well, worry no more! We have compiled a list of hair styles that are perfect for round faces. Keep reading to find out which ones suit you best.

Description

Round faces are characterized by the width of the cheekbones, jawline, and forehead being almost equal. The goal is to find hairstyles that add length and angles to the face to create the illusion of an oval face shape.

How to

Before choosing any hairstyle, it is essential to determine your face shape. One way to do this is by pulling your hair back into a ponytail and tracing your face shape onto a mirror with a lipstick. Once you have determined that you have a round face, you can choose from the following hair styles:

1. Long Layers

Long layers add depth and dimension to the hair, which helps to elongate the face. Layers should start from the chin to create an angular look.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs break up the width of the face, making it appear slimmer. The bangs should be long enough to graze the eyebrows and blend in with the rest of the hair.

3. Bob Cut

A bob cut that falls just below the chin helps to elongate the face while adding volume to the crown. The ends should be slightly angled to create a more angular look.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut with volume at the crown and shorter sides helps to elongate the face. The sides should be tapered to create a more angular look.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape by tracing it on a mirror with lipstick.

2. Choose a hairstyle that adds length and angles to your face.

3. Long layers add depth and dimension to the hair, which helps to elongate the face.

4. Side-swept bangs break up the width of the face, making it appear slimmer.

5. A bob cut that falls just below the chin helps to elongate the face while adding volume to the crown.

6. A pixie cut with volume at the crown and shorter sides helps to elongate the face.

Tips

1. Avoid blunt bangs as they tend to make the face look rounder.

2. Add volume to the crown of your head to create the illusion of a longer face.

3. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as a wide afro.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part?

A: Yes, but make sure to add volume to the crown to create the illusion of a longer face.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail?

A: Yes, but make sure to add volume to the crown and leave some hair out around the face to create angles.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair to maintain healthy locks. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Lastly, trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
