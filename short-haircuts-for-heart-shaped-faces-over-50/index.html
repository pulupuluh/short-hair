Short Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces Over 50

Written by Luffy Apr 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a heart shaped face and are over 50, you may be wondering what short haircuts will flatter your face shape and age. Short haircuts can be a great option for heart shaped faces as they can help to balance out the wider forehead and narrower jawline. In this post, we will be discussing some of the best short haircuts for heart shaped faces over 50.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Heart Shaped Face

Before we dive into specific haircut options, it's important to understand how to choose the right haircut for your heart shaped face. The key is to find a haircut that balances out the proportions of your face. This means choosing a style that adds width to the lower half of your face and avoids adding volume to the top half.

When choosing a short haircut, consider the following:

  • Length: Look for haircuts that fall just below the chin or shorter. Anything longer can add too much volume to the top half of your face.
  • Layers: Layers can help to add volume to the lower half of your face.
  • Side-swept bangs: Side-swept bangs can help to balance out a wider forehead.

Step by Step Guide for Getting a Short Haircut

If you've decided on a short haircut, here is a step by step guide for getting the perfect cut:

  1. Find inspiration: Look for pictures of short haircuts that you like and bring them to your stylist.
  2. Consult with your stylist: Talk to your stylist about your face shape and what haircut would work best for you.
  3. Wash your hair: Wash your hair before your appointment so your stylist can see your natural texture.
  4. Trust your stylist: Let your stylist take the lead and make suggestions based on your hair and face shape.
  5. Maintenance: Short haircuts require more frequent maintenance, so be prepared to schedule regular appointments to keep your cut looking fresh.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Short haircuts require more maintenance than longer styles. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair looking great:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the lower half of your face.
  • Invest in a good dry shampoo to keep your hair looking fresh between washes.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQs About Short Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces Over 50

What is a heart shaped face?

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow jawline.

What are the best short haircuts for heart shaped faces over 50?

The best short haircuts for heart shaped faces over 50 are those that add volume to the lower half of the face, such as a bob with layers or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs.

Can I still wear bangs with a heart shaped face?

Yes, side-swept bangs can help to balance out a wider forehead and are a great option for heart shaped faces.

Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Faces

In addition to choosing the right haircut, here are some hair care tips to keep in mind for heart shaped faces:

  • Avoid adding too much volume to the top half of your face.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can accentuate a wider forehead.

Overall, there are many great short haircut options for heart shaped faces over 50. By choosing the right cut and following these tips for maintenance and care, you can have a stylish and flattering look that enhances your natural beauty.
