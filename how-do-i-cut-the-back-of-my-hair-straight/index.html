How Do I Cut The Back Of My Hair Straight A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Apr 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially when it comes to the back. It can be difficult to see what you're doing and even harder to make sure everything is straight. In this article, we will go over a step-by-step guide on how to cut the back of your hair straight.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Cut the Back of Your Hair Straight
  • Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair

How to Cut the Back of Your Hair Straight

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror.

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to see and cut.
  2. Using the comb, separate the back of your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on the thickness and length of your hair.
  3. Take one section at a time and comb it straight down. Use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make sure the section is straight.
  4. Hold the scissors at a 45-degree angle and cut the hair in a straight line. Be sure to cut small sections at a time.
  5. Repeat this process for each section, making sure to check the back of your head with the handheld mirror.
  6. Once you have finished cutting the back of your hair, use the comb to blend the back and front sections together.
  7. Check the back of your head in the mirror to make sure everything is even and straight.
  8. If you notice any uneven sections, go back and trim them until everything is straight.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

It is important to take your time and be patient when cutting your own hair. Remember, it is always better to cut less than to cut too much.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

  • Invest in a good pair of scissors. This will make a huge difference in the quality of your cut.
  • Start with small sections and work your way up to larger ones.
  • Use a comb to guide your scissors and make sure everything is straight.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet as it can be more difficult to see what you're doing.
  • Ask a friend or family member for help if you're nervous about cutting the back of your hair.

FAQs

Can I cut the back of my hair without a handheld mirror?

While it is possible to cut the back of your hair without a handheld mirror, it is not recommended. It is important to be able to see the back of your head to ensure everything is straight and even.

How often should I cut the back of my hair?

This will depend on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and looking great.

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can ensure that your DIY haircut looks great and stays healthy.
