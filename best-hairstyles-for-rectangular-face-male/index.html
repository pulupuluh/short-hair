Best Hairstyles For Rectangular Face Male A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 10, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you a male with a rectangular face and struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on the best hairstyles for rectangular face male that will complement your features and enhance your overall appearance. Read on to discover some great hairstyle ideas and tips!

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to choose the right hairstyle for a rectangular face male
  • Step by step guide for styling the best hairstyles for rectangular face male
  • Tips for maintaining the best hairstyles for rectangular face male
  • FAQs
  • Hair care for rectangular face male

Description

A rectangular face is characterized by a strong jawline, prominent forehead, and elongated shape. The aim of choosing the right hairstyle is to soften these features and create a balanced look. Different hairstyles can create different effects on your face shape, so it is crucial to choose the hairstyle that complements your unique features. Keep reading to find out how to choose the right hairstyle and some great hairstyle ideas for rectangular face males.

How to choose the right hairstyle for a rectangular face male

When selecting a hairstyle, it is essential to consider the shape of your face. Rectangular face males should aim for hairstyles that add width to the face and create an illusion of a shorter face. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

  • Consider a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face
  • Avoid hairstyles that create too much height on top of your head
  • Shorter hairstyles work better for rectangular face males
  • Try a hairstyle with layers or textured haircuts to add dimension
  • Consider keeping your hair longer on top and shorter on the sides to balance the features

Step by step guide for styling the best hairstyles for rectangular face male

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, here are some great hairstyle ideas and a step-by-step guide to styling them:

1. Side-parted hairstyle

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

Step 2: Comb your hair over to one side

Step 3: Use a hair product to add texture and hold to your hair

Step 4: Use a blow dryer to set the hair in place

2. Short textured haircut

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Step 2: Use scissors or clippers to cut your hair short on the sides and back

Step 3: Use a texturizing product to add volume and texture to the top of your head

Step 4: Use a comb or your hands to style your hair

3. Buzz cut

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Step 2: Use clippers to buzz your hair to a short length all around

Step 3: Use a comb or your hands to style your hair

Step 4: Add a hair product for shine and hold (optional)

Tips for maintaining the best hairstyles for rectangular face male

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hairstyle looking fresh
  • Use high-quality hair products that suit your hair type
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable

FAQs

Q. Can a rectangular face male have long hair?

A. While shorter hairstyles work better for rectangular face males, long hair can work if styled correctly. Consider keeping your hair layered and textured to add dimension and avoid a flat look.

Q. What hair products should I use for the best hairstyles for rectangular face male?

A. Use a hair product that suits your hair type and the hairstyle you choose. For example, a texturizing product can add volume and texture to your hair, while a pomade can provide a sleek and polished look.

Hair care for rectangular face male

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for any hairstyle. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes nutrients essential for healthy hair, such as vitamins A, C, and E, biotin, and iron

In conclusion, choosing the best hairstyles for rectangular face male requires careful consideration of your face shape and hair type. With the right hairstyle and hair care, you can enhance your features and look your best. Try out some of the hairstyle ideas provided in this article and see which one works best for you. Remember to maintain your hairstyle with regular haircuts and using high-quality hair products. Good luck!
