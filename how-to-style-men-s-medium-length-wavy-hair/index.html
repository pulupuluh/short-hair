How to Style Men's Medium Length Wavy Hair | Tips and Tricks

Having medium length wavy hair can be a great canvas for styling. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a variety of looks that suit your personal style. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for styling men's medium length wavy hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Men's Medium Length Wavy Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide

How to Style Men's Medium Length Wavy Hair

The key to styling men's medium length wavy hair is to work with your natural texture. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for wavy hair. This will help enhance your natural waves and make them more defined.

Next, towel dry your hair and apply a styling product. A lightweight mousse or sea salt spray can help enhance your waves and add volume. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.

Use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair, using your fingers to scrunch your hair as you go. This will help enhance your waves and create a more tousled, undone look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to styling men's medium length wavy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner formulated for wavy hair
  2. Towel dry your hair
  3. Apply a lightweight mousse or sea salt spray
  4. Dry your hair with a hair dryer on low heat, scrunching your hair as you go
  5. Finish with a light styling product, like a wax or pomade, to add definition and control

Tips for Styling Men's Medium Length Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for styling men's medium length wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, like a messy quiff or a side part
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling men's medium length wavy hair:

  • Can I straighten my wavy hair? Yes, you can use a hair straightener to straighten your hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  • Can I color my wavy hair? Yes, you can color your wavy hair, but be sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • How often should I wash my wavy hair? It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but most people with wavy hair can wash their hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care for Men's Medium Length Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of men's medium length wavy hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner formulated for wavy hair
  • Avoid using hot tools, like hair dryers and straighteners, too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a variety of stylish looks for your medium length wavy hair. Remember to work with your natural texture and use products that enhance your waves without weighing them down. With a little practice, you'll be able to create the perfect style for any occasion.
