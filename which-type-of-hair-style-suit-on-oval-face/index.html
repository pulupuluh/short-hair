Which Type Of Hair Style Suit On Oval Face

Are you looking for the perfect hairstyle that suits your oval face? If yes, then you are in the right place. An oval face is considered one of the most versatile shapes, which means you can try a variety of hairstyles. However, not all hairstyles will flatter your face shape.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by its balanced proportions, with the forehead and jawline being slightly narrower than the cheekbones. This face shape is considered the most versatile, as it can pull off almost any hairstyle with ease.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your oval face is to determine your face shape. To do this, follow these simple steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Take a ruler and measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Note down the measurements and compare them.
  4. If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face and your forehead and jawline are slightly narrower, then you have an oval face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your oval face. Follow these steps:

  1. Consider your hair type and texture.
  2. Look for hairstyles that will balance out your face shape.
  3. Choose a hairstyle that will highlight your best features.
  4. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you can devote to styling your hair.
  5. Try different hairstyles and see which one looks the best on you.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips that will help you choose the right hairstyle for your oval face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as this will make your face look longer.
  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face, as this will balance out your face shape.
  • Consider getting a side-swept fringe, as this will highlight your cheekbones and jawline.
  • Try a bob haircut, as it will accentuate your face shape and highlight your neck.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can make your face look unbalanced.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for oval faces:

Q. Can I try a pixie cut with an oval face shape?

A. Yes, you can try a pixie cut with an oval face shape, as it will highlight your facial features.

Q. Which hair color suits oval faces?

A. Any hair color can suit an oval face shape, but warm hair colors like caramel, honey, and chocolate brown can add warmth to your complexion.

Q. Can I try a center parting hairstyle with an oval face shape?

A. Yes, you can try a center parting hairstyle with an oval face shape, as it will accentuate your facial features.

Hair Care Tips for Your Oval Face

Here are some hair care tips that will help you maintain your oval face shape:

  • Keep your hair clean and well-maintained.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tight, as it can damage your hair and affect your face shape.

Now that you know which type of hair style suits oval face, it's time to try out some new hairstyles and experiment with different looks. Remember, the key is to choose a hairstyle that highlights your best features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
