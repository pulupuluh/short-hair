How To Style Hair Short Sides Long Top A Relaxed Guide

Written by Frank Jul 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, the short sides long top hairstyle might be perfect for you. This style has been popular for years, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this guide, we'll show you how to style hair short sides long top in a relaxed way, without too much fuss or effort.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Hair Short Sides Long Top
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Tips

Description

The short sides long top hairstyle is characterized by short hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. This style can be worn in many different ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy, textured style. It's a great option for men who want a versatile haircut that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

How to Style Hair Short Sides Long Top

The first step in styling a short sides long top haircut is to make sure you have the right length of hair. The sides should be cut short, usually with clippers, while the top should be left longer. This can vary depending on your personal preference, but a good rule of thumb is to have at least two inches of hair on top.

To style your hair, you'll need a few products, including a pomade or wax for hold and a hair dryer for volume. Start by applying the product to your hair, starting at the back and working your way forward. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, making sure to evenly distribute it.

Step by Step Guide

  1. Apply pomade or wax to your hair, starting at the back and working your way forward.
  2. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, making sure to evenly distribute it.
  3. Blow-dry your hair, using your fingers to lift and style the hair on top.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a comb or brush to style it into your desired look.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or styling spray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect short sides long top hairstyle:

  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go to create volume and texture.
  • Use a comb or brush to style your hair while blow-drying for a more polished look.
  • For a more relaxed look, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, textured style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the short sides long top hairstyle:

  • Is this hairstyle easy to maintain?

    • This hairstyle is fairly easy to maintain, as long as you keep the sides trimmed regularly and style your hair daily.

  • What face shapes does this hairstyle suit?

    • This hairstyle suits most face shapes, but works particularly well for those with oval or square-shaped faces.

  • Can I wear this hairstyle to work?

    • Yes, this hairstyle is versatile enough to wear to work or any other occasion.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your short sides long top hairstyle looking great, make sure to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair in good condition and maintain the shape of your haircut.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your short sides long top haircut with ease. Whether you're going for a polished or relaxed look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
