Hairstyle For Long Face Asian Girl Tips And Tricks

Written by William Apr 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you an Asian girl with a long face and struggling to find the perfect hairstyle? Look no further! This article is all about hairstyles that suit long face Asian girls. Read on to discover some fantastic hairdos that will complement your features and enhance your beauty.

Description

Long face Asian girls have a unique beauty that needs to be highlighted with the right hairstyle. Choosing the right hairstyle can be challenging as it needs to balance out the face's length and make it look proportional. But don't worry, we've got you covered with some great hairstyles that will bring out the best in you.

How to

When picking a hairstyle, it's essential to consider the shape of your face. For long face Asian girls, hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face are ideal. Bangs and layers can also work wonders in creating an illusion of width around the face.

Step-by-step Guide:

  1. Consult a stylist to determine the right hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Choose hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as layered cuts or side-swept bangs.
  3. Experiment with different styles to see which one best suits your personality and lifestyle.
  4. Use styling products such as volumizing mousse, texturizing spray, and hairspray to hold your hair in place.
  5. Invest in quality hair care products to maintain the health and shine of your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for long face Asian girls:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long and flat as they can make the face look longer.
  • Try out different types of bangs such as side-swept, blunt, or wispy to add width to the face.
  • Accessorize your hair with hairpins, clips, and headbands to add interest and texture to your hairstyle.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair and create an illusion of width around the face.
  • Don't be afraid to try out new hairstyles and experiment with different looks.

FAQ

What hairstyles suit long face Asian girls?

Hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face such as layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and textured waves are ideal for long face Asian girls.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a long face?

A high ponytail or bun can elongate the face further. It's best to go for a low ponytail or a side ponytail to create an illusion of width around the face.

Can I get a bob haircut if I have a long face?

A bob haircut can work well for long face Asian girls if it's styled with layers and texture to add volume to the sides of the face.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly and condition it to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many hair products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, long face Asian girls can look stunning and confident. Try out different styles and experiment with different looks to find the perfect hairstyle that suits you.
