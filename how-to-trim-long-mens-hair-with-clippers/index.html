How To Trim Long Men s Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine May 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're one of the many men out there with long hair, you know that it can be a challenge to keep it looking neat and tidy all the time. One easy way to give yourself a fresh look is by using clippers to trim your hair down to a more manageable length. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps of how to trim long men's hair with clippers. From preparation to finishing touches, we'll cover everything you need to know to get the job done right.

Table of Contents

  • Preparation
  • Sectioning the Hair
  • Choosing the Right Guard
  • Trimming the Hair
  • Blending the Hair
  • Cleaning Up
  • Tips for Success
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Trimming

Preparation

Before you start trimming your hair, make sure you have all the tools you need. You'll need a set of clippers with various guards, a comb, hair clips (to keep sections of hair separated), and a mirror.

It's also important to have a clean and dry head of hair before you start. Take a shower or wash your hair to remove any excess oils or dirt that may make it difficult to cut.

Sectioning the Hair

Start by separating your hair into sections. Use the comb to create a part down the center of your head, from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Then, create a horizontal part from ear to ear. Clip the top section of hair up and out of the way.

Choosing the Right Guard

Choose the guard size you want to use. If you're not sure which guard to use, start with a larger one and work your way down. Remember, you can always go shorter, but you can't go longer once you've cut your hair.

Trimming the Hair

Start at the bottom of your head and work your way up. Hold the clippers against your scalp, with the guard flat against your head. Move the clippers in an upward motion, against the direction of hair growth.

Work your way around your head, using the comb to lift sections of hair as needed. Be sure to keep the guard flat against your head to ensure an even cut.

Blending the Hair

Once you've trimmed the bottom section of your hair, move on to the top section. Use a smaller guard size than you did on the bottom section, and blend the two sections together by running the clippers over the area where they meet.

Cleaning Up

After you've finished trimming your hair, use the clippers without a guard to clean up around your ears and the back of your neck. Use the comb to lift any stray hairs and trim them with the clippers.

Tips for Success

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you trim your hair:

  • Start with a larger guard size and work your way down to prevent cutting too much off at once.
  • Use the mirror to check your progress and ensure an even cut.
  • Take breaks if your hands get tired or your neck starts to ache.
  • Don't forget to clean your clippers after each use to keep them in good working condition.

FAQs

Here are a few frequently asked questions about trimming long men's hair with clippers:

  • Can I trim my hair wet or dry?
  • How often should I trim my hair?
  • Can I use the same clippers to trim my beard?

Hair Care After Trimming

After you've finished trimming your hair, be sure to wash it thoroughly to remove any loose hairs. Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated, and avoid using any heat styling tools for at least a few days after trimming. This will give your hair a chance to recover from the cutting process and help prevent damage.

Now that you know how to trim long men's hair with clippers, you can save time and money by doing it yourself at home. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to create a fresh, stylish look that's all your own.
