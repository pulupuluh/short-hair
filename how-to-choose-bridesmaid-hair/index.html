How to Choose Bridesmaid Hair

Choosing bridesmaid hair can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some things to consider when choosing the right hairstyles for your bridesmaids:

Consider the Dress

The first thing you should consider when choosing bridesmaid hair is the dress. The hairstyle you choose should complement the dress and the overall theme of your wedding. For example, if your bridesmaids are wearing long, flowy dresses, you may want to consider loose, beachy waves or a low bun.

Think About Your Own Hair

You should also consider your own hairstyle when choosing bridesmaid hair. If you're wearing your hair up, you may want to consider having your bridesmaids wear their hair down. If you're wearing your hair down, you may want to consider having your bridesmaids wear their hair up.

Consider Your Bridesmaids' Hair Types

It's also important to consider your bridesmaids' hair types when choosing their hairstyles. If you have bridesmaids with different hair types, you may want to consider choosing a hairstyle that works for all hair types. For example, a low bun or a simple updo can work well for all hair types.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Choose a Theme

The first step in choosing bridesmaid hair is to choose a theme for your wedding. This will help you narrow down your choices and make it easier to choose the right hairstyles for your bridesmaids.

Step 2: Consider the Dress

Consider the dress your bridesmaids will be wearing. The hairstyle you choose should complement the dress and the overall theme of your wedding.

Step 3: Think About Your Own Hair

Consider your own hairstyle when choosing bridesmaid hair. If you're wearing your hair up, you may want to consider having your bridesmaids wear their hair down. If you're wearing your hair down, you may want to consider having your bridesmaids wear their hair up.

Step 4: Consider Your Bridesmaids' Hair Types

Consider your bridesmaids' hair types when choosing their hairstyles. If you have bridesmaids with different hair types, you may want to consider choosing a hairstyle that works for all hair types.

Step 5: Schedule a Hair Trial

Schedule a hair trial for your bridesmaids so they can try out different hairstyles and find the perfect one for them.

Tips for Choosing Bridesmaid Hair

  • Consider the overall theme of your wedding when choosing bridesmaid hair.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements the bridesmaids' dresses.
  • Consider your own hairstyle when choosing bridesmaid hair.
  • Choose a hairstyle that works for all hair types.
  • Schedule a hair trial for your bridesmaids.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my bridesmaids have different hair types?

Choose a hairstyle that works for all hair types. A low bun or a simple updo can work well for all hair types.

What if my bridesmaids have different lengths of hair?

Choose a hairstyle that works for different lengths of hair. A low bun or loose waves can work well for both short and long hair.

Hair Care Tips for Bridesmaids

Make sure your bridesmaids take care of their hair leading up to the wedding. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition their hair regularly.
  • Avoid using too much heat on their hair.
  • Use heat protectant when using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Consider a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment to nourish their hair.

Choosing bridesmaid hair can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. By considering the dress, your own hairstyle, and your bridesmaids' hair types, you can choose the perfect hairstyle for your bridesmaids. And don't forget to schedule a hair trial so your bridesmaids can find the perfect hairstyle for them. With these tips and tricks, your bridesmaids are sure to look stunning on your special day!
