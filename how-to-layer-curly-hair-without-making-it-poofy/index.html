How To Layer Curly Hair Without Making It Poofy

Written by Petter Jun 08, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that it can be difficult to manage. One of the biggest challenges is figuring out how to layer your curls without making them poofy. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks for how to layer curly hair without making it poofy.

Table of Contents

  • How to Layer Curly Hair Without Making it Poofy
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Layer Curly Hair Without Making it Poofy

The key to layering curly hair is to do it when your hair is wet. This will help you avoid creating frizz and poofy curls. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Be sure to use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair while it’s wet. This will help you avoid creating knots and tangles.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

Once you’re done washing your hair, use a microfiber towel to dry it. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel as this can cause frizz and poofy curls. Instead, gently blot your hair to remove excess water.

Step 3: Apply a curl-enhancing product

Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair while it’s still wet. This will help define your curls and prevent them from becoming poofy. Be sure to use a product that suits your hair type.

Step 4: Section your hair

Section your hair into four parts using clips. This will make it easier to layer your hair without missing any sections.

Step 5: Layer your hair

Starting from the bottom section, use a pair of hair cutting scissors to cut your hair at an angle. This will create layers in your hair without making it poofy. Repeat this process for each section of your hair.

Step 6: Style your hair

Once you’re done layering your hair, style it as desired. You can use a diffuser to dry your hair or let it air dry. Avoid using a hairbrush as this can cause frizz and poofy curls.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when layering curly hair:

  • Use hair cutting scissors instead of regular scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as this can damage your hair and make it poofy.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from becoming tangled while you sleep.

FAQ

How often should I layer my curly hair?

You should layer your curly hair every 3-4 months. This will help you maintain the shape of your curls and prevent them from becoming poofy.

Can I layer my curly hair myself?

While it’s possible to layer your curly hair yourself, we recommend going to a professional hairstylist. They have the experience and tools needed to layer your hair without making it poofy.

Can I use a hairbrush on my curly hair?

No, you should avoid using a hairbrush on your curly hair as this can cause frizz and poofy curls. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful curly hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your curly hair looking its best and avoid making it poofy when layering.
