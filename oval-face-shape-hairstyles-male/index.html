Oval Face Shape Hairstyles For Men A Guide For 2023

Written by Frank Aug 21, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you a man with an oval face shape? If so, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. There are a variety of options that can complement your features and make you look your best. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about oval face shape hairstyles for men in 2023.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, with a slightly curved jawline and forehead. This shape is often considered the most attractive, as it is associated with symmetry and harmony. Men with oval face shapes have the advantage of being able to pull off a wide range of hairstyles, from short and neat to long and tousled.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle for your oval face shape, there are a few things to consider. First, think about your hair type and texture. Do you have thick or thin hair? Is it straight or curly? These factors can influence the way your hair falls and how it looks with certain styles.

Next, consider your personal style and the vibe you want to give off. Do you prefer a classic, clean-cut look or something more edgy and modern? This can help guide your choice of hairstyle.

Finally, take into account any other features you want to highlight or downplay. For example, if you have a prominent nose, you may want to opt for a style that draws attention away from that area.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some popular oval face shape hairstyles for men in 2023:

Short and Neat

A short, neat cut is a classic choice for men with oval face shapes. This style is easy to maintain and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

To achieve this look, ask your barber for a tapered cut with short sides and a slightly longer top. Use a styling product like pomade or wax to keep your hair in place and add texture.

The Undercut

The undercut is a trendy hairstyle that works well with oval face shapes. This style features short sides and a longer top that can be styled in a variety of ways.

To get the undercut look, ask your barber to shave the sides of your head and leave the top longer. You can style the top in a slicked-back or messy look, depending on your preference.

The Side Part

The side part is a classic men's hairstyle that can work well with oval face shapes. This style features a deep part on one side of the head, with the hair combed over to the other side.

To achieve this look, ask your barber for a cut with a side part. Use a comb to create the part and style your hair to one side, using a styling product to keep it in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your oval face shape, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain the shape and style.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and soft.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can damage it.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the best one for your hair type and style.

FAQs

Q: Can men with oval face shapes wear long hair?

A: Yes, men with oval face shapes can pull off long hairstyles. However, it's important to choose a style that complements your features and doesn't overwhelm your face.

Q: Is it better to have short or long hair with an oval face shape?

A: Either short or long hair can work well with an oval face shape. It's more important to choose a style that suits your hair type and personal style.

Hair Care Tips

When it comes to hair care, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, men with oval face shapes can look their best in 2023 and beyond. Experiment with different styles and products to find the perfect look for you.
