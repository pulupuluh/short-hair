Can You Get A Fringe With Curly Hair

Written by Christine Apr 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

In this year 2023, the trend of having a fringe with curly hair is still going strong. The question remains: Can you get a fringe with curly hair? The answer is yes, you can get a fringe with curly hair. However, there are a few things you need to consider before going for it. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about getting a fringe with curly hair.

Description

A fringe, also known as bangs, is a trendy hairstyle that involves cutting the front portion of your hair and letting it fall over your forehead. This style can be customized to suit any face shape and hair texture. However, for those with curly hair, getting a fringe can be a bit tricky.

How to Get a Fringe with Curly Hair

Getting a fringe with curly hair requires a bit of extra effort and consideration. Here are the steps you need to follow to get a perfect fringe with curly hair:

Step 1: Consult with a Hairstylist

Before getting a fringe, it is important to consult with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair. They will be able to advise you on the best fringe style that will suit your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Choose the Right Fringe Style

There are different types of fringe styles that you can choose from, such as side-swept, blunt, choppy, and curtain. For those with curly hair, it is recommended to go for a side-swept or curtain fringe. These styles will blend well with your curls and make your hair look more natural.

Step 3: Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims is essential to maintain your fringe and keep it looking fresh. This is especially important for those with curly hair, as curls tend to shrink up when they are dry. Getting regular trims will help prevent your fringe from looking too short or uneven.

Tips for Maintaining a Fringe with Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your fringe with curly hair:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to avoid frizz and preserve your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your fringe when it is dry, as this can cause frizz and disrupt the curl pattern.
  • Use a lightweight styling product to enhance your curls and keep them looking defined.
  • Avoid touching your fringe too much, as this can cause it to lose its shape and become oily.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and prevent buildup.

FAQs

Can I get a fringe with tight curls?

Yes, you can get a fringe with tight curls. However, it is important to choose the right fringe style and consult with a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair.

Will a fringe make my hair look shorter?

Yes, a fringe can make your hair look shorter, especially if you have curly hair that tends to shrink up when it is dry. However, getting regular trims can help maintain the length of your hair and prevent it from looking too short.

Can I style my fringe with curly hair?

Yes, you can style your fringe with curly hair using a lightweight styling product, such as a curl cream or mousse. However, avoid using heavy styling products or tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and disrupt the curl pattern.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires extra care to maintain its shape and definition. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos and opt for sulfate-free or low-poo formulas.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to moisturize and soften your curls.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and disrupt the curl pattern.
  • Avoid towel-drying your hair, as this can cause frizz and disrupt the curl pattern. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to blot your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.

With the right care and maintenance, you can get a fringe with curly hair and rock this trendy hairstyle with confidence!
