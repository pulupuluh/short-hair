How To Cut Short Pixie Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and go for a bold, edgy look, a short pixie cut may be perfect for you. Pixie cuts are low maintenance and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. However, constant salon visits can be expensive, which is why learning how to cut short pixie hair yourself can be a game-changer. In this article, we'll outline the steps, tips, and hair care advice you need to achieve the perfect short pixie cut at home.

How to Cut Short Pixie Hair Yourself

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, especially if you're not used to it. However, with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a flawless pixie cut. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clipper or trimmer
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Handheld mirror or large mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you get started, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Follow these steps:

  1. Section off the top of your hair with hair ties or clips, leaving the bottom section loose.
  2. Using your clipper or trimmer, cut the bottom section of your hair to your desired length. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard to avoid any mistakes.
  3. Remove the hair ties or clips and blend the top section of your hair with the bottom section. Use your scissors to cut the hair in a diagonal direction, creating layers that gradually get longer towards the top of your head.
  4. Using your comb, lift small sections of hair and cut them to your desired length, making sure to keep the scissors parallel to your head for an even cut.
  5. Trim the hair around your ears and neck, making sure to create a clean, even line.
  6. Check your work using a handheld mirror or large mirror. Make any necessary adjustments and trim any stray hairs.

Tips for Cutting Short Pixie Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting short pixie hair yourself:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter to avoid any mistakes.
  • Use a comb to lift small sections of hair for an even cut.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or overwhelmed.
  • Invest in quality scissors and clippers to ensure a clean cut.
  • When in doubt, err on the side of caution and cut less rather than more.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting short pixie hair yourself:

Q: Can I cut my hair wet or dry?
A: It's best to cut your hair dry, as wet hair can appear longer and may result in a shorter cut than intended. Q: How often should I trim my short pixie hair?
A: It depends on how fast your hair grows, but most people need a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain their pixie cut. Q: What if I make a mistake?
A: Don't panic! It's just hair and it will grow back. If you make a mistake, try to fix it or seek professional help if needed.

Hair Care Tips for Short Pixie Hair

Short pixie hair requires minimal maintenance, but here are some tips to keep it looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid over-drying.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Invest in a quality heat protectant if you plan on using hot tools.
  • Use a dry shampoo or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your short pixie cut.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut your own short pixie hair at home. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun with your new style!
