How To Cut Spiky Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and go for a spiky look, you might be wondering how to cut spiky hair. Luckily, it's a fairly simple process that you can do at home with the right tools and techniques. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve the perfect spiky hairstyle.

Description

Spiky hair is a popular hairstyle that has been around for decades. It's a trendy, edgy look that works well for both men and women. To achieve this look, you'll need to cut your hair short and use a styling product to create spikes. Cutting spiky hair can be tricky, but with a little practice, you can do it yourself at home.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to gather all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and clippers if you want to cut your hair short. Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 2: Section your hair

Use a comb to section your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an even cut all around.

Step 3: Cut your hair

Using your scissors, cut your hair to your desired length. If you want a shorter spiky look, you can use clippers to shave the sides and back of your head.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once your hair is cut, it's time to style it. Use a styling product like hair gel or mousse to create spikes. Apply the product to your hair and use your fingers to style it into spikes.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect spiky hairstyle:

  • Use a mirror to ensure that you are cutting your hair evenly
  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut shorter if you want a more dramatic look
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting spiky hair:

Can I cut spiky hair myself?

Yes, you can cut spiky hair yourself at home. Just make sure you have the necessary tools and follow the steps outlined in this article.

How do I make my spikes stay in place?

Use a strong-hold styling product like hair gel or mousse to make your spikes stay in place. Apply the product to your hair and use your fingers to create the spikes.

Hair Care

To keep your spiky hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining your spiky hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean
  • Use a conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to cut spiky hair like a pro. Whether you're looking for a bold, edgy look or just want to switch up your hairstyle, spiky hair is a great choice. So grab your scissors and styling products and get ready to rock your new spiky 'do!
