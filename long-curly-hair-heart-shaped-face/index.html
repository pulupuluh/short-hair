Long Curly Hair For Heart Shaped Face A Complete Guide

Are you someone with a heart-shaped face and long curly hair? If yes, then this post is for you. In this post, we will be discussing everything you need to know about long curly hair for heart-shaped faces. From how to style it to some helpful tips, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wide forehead and cheekbones, and a narrow chin. Long curly hair can be the perfect hairstyle for this face shape as it helps to balance out the face and add some width to the jawline. However, styling long curly hair can be a challenge, especially if you're new to it. That's why we've put together this guide to help you style your long curly hair with ease.

How to Style Long Curly Hair for Heart Shaped Face

Styling long curly hair for heart-shaped faces can be done in various ways. Here are some of the ways you can style your long curly hair:

1. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down style is a classic and easy way to style your long curly hair. To achieve this look, take the top half of your hair and tie it into a bun or ponytail, leaving the rest of your hair loose.

2. High Ponytail

A high ponytail is a simple but elegant way to style your long curly hair. Pull all your hair up into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie. You can also add some curls to the ends of your ponytail to add some volume and dimension.

3. Braids

Braids are a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many ways. You can try a fishtail braid, a French braid, or a simple three-strand braid. Braids not only add some texture and dimension to your hair but also help to keep it out of your face.

4. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a great way to style your long curly hair if you're in a hurry. Pull your hair up into a high ponytail, twist it around, and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands to create a messy, effortless look.

Step by Step: How to Style Long Curly Hair for Heart Shaped Face

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your long curly hair for heart-shaped faces:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step to styling your long curly hair is to wash and condition it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and follow the instructions on the label.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help detangle it and reduce frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the conditioner evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to help define your curls. You can use a curl cream, mousse, or gel, depending on your hair type and preference. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to help enhance your curls. Use a low heat setting and move the diffuser around your head, scrunching your curls as you go.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Choose one of the styling options mentioned above and style your hair accordingly. You can also experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for you.

Tips for Styling Long Curly Hair for Heart Shaped Face

Here are some tips to help you style your long curly hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush. A brush can cause breakage and frizz, while a wide-tooth comb gently removes tangles without damaging your curls.

2. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Use a silk pillowcase to help reduce frizz and breakage while you sleep. Silk is gentle on your hair and helps to retain moisture.

3. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Don't wash your hair too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Instead, wash your hair every 2-3 days and use a dry shampoo in between washes.

4. Use Heat Protectant

If you're using heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair. Heat protectants help to reduce breakage and keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

1. Can I straighten my long curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your long curly hair if you want to. However, make sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using heat styling tools too often as it can damage your hair.

2. How can I reduce frizz in my long curly hair?

To reduce frizz in your long curly hair, use a leave-in conditioner, avoid overwashing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and use a silk pillowcase.

3. Can I color my long curly hair?

Yes, you can color your long curly hair. However, make sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner and avoid using heat styling tools too often as it can damage your hair.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair for Heart Shaped Face

Here are some tips for taking care of your long curly hair:

1. Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair. Deep conditioners help to keep your hair healthy and prevent breakage.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your hair healthy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray. The sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle.

In conclusion, long curly hair can be a great hairstyle for heart-shaped faces. With the right styling techniques and hair care tips, you can rock your long curly hair with confidence. We hope this guide has been helpful to you. Happy styling!
