Mens Hair Cut Short Sides Long Top A Relaxed Guide For 2023

Written by Petter Apr 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile hairstyle that's easy to maintain, the mens hair cut short sides long top is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been popular for several years now and shows no signs of slowing down. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, tips for styling it, and how to take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Mens Hair Cut Short Sides Long Top
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQs

Description

The mens hair cut short sides long top is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. The sides and back of the head are cut short, while the hair on top is left longer. This creates a contrast between the two lengths that is both stylish and easy to maintain. This hairstyle can be worn with a variety of textures, from straight to curly, and is suitable for all face shapes.

How to Get the Mens Hair Cut Short Sides Long Top

The mens hair cut short sides long top can be achieved at any barber shop or hair salon. Just show your stylist a picture of the style you want, and they will be able to create it for you. If you prefer to cut your hair at home, you can use clippers to trim the sides and back, and scissors to trim the top. However, we recommend going to a professional stylist for the best results.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair

If you're cutting your hair at home, follow these steps to achieve the mens hair cut short sides long top:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Use clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard until you achieve your desired length.
  3. Use scissors to trim the hair on top of your head. Start by combing the hair straight up, and then trim the ends with scissors. You can also use thinning shears to create texture.
  4. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some tips for styling your mens hair cut short sides long top:

  • Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to create definition and hold.
  • Experiment with different textures, such as messy and tousled or slicked back and polished.
  • Ask your stylist for advice on which products to use and how to style your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens hair cut short sides long top:

What face shapes does this hairstyle suit?

The mens hair cut short sides long top is suitable for all face shapes.

What hair types does this hairstyle work with?

This hairstyle can be worn with a variety of hair types, from straight to curly.

How often do I need to get my hair cut?

We recommend getting your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your style.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your mens hair cut short sides long top looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as this can damage the hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve and maintain the mens hair cut short sides long top with ease. Whether you're looking for a casual or polished look, this hairstyle is sure to impress.
