How To Dry Curly Hair To Make It Straight

Written by Aprilia Aug 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have curly hair, you might want to switch up your look every now and then. While you can always use a flat iron to straighten your hair, this can damage your hair if you do it too often. Another option is to dry your curly hair to make it straight. In this blog post, we'll show you how to do just that.

  • Step by Step Guide

How to Dry Curly Hair to Make it Straight

The first thing you need to do is wash your hair with a straightening shampoo and conditioner. This will help to smooth out your hair and make it easier to straighten. After washing your hair, wrap it in a towel to absorb excess water.

Next, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will protect your hair from heat damage when you use a hair dryer. You can also apply a straightening cream or serum to your hair to help make it straighter.

Now it's time to dry your hair. Use a round brush and a hair dryer to dry your hair. You should start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Make sure you use the nozzle attachment on your hair dryer to direct the heat to the roots of your hair.

As you dry your hair, use the round brush to smooth out any kinks or curls. This will help to make your hair straighter. Keep the hair dryer at least six inches away from your hair to avoid heat damage.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to drying your curly hair to make it straight:

  1. Wash your hair with a straightening shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Wrap your hair in a towel to absorb excess water.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  4. Apply a straightening cream or serum to your hair.
  5. Use a round brush and a hair dryer to dry your hair.
  6. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.
  7. Use the round brush to smooth out any kinks or curls.
  8. Keep the hair dryer at least six inches away from your hair.
  9. Repeat until your hair is completely dry and straight.

Tips for Drying Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you dry your curly hair to make it straight:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before you wash it.
  • Use a microfiber towel to dry your hair instead of a regular towel. This will help to reduce frizz.
  • Don't rub your hair with the towel. Instead, gently squeeze out excess water.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair if you want to keep some of your curls.
  • Don't use a hair dryer on wet hair. This can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a lower heat setting on your hair dryer to avoid heat damage.

FAQs

Can I dry my curly hair to make it straight without using a hair dryer?

While a hair dryer can help to make your hair straighter, you can also air dry your hair. However, this will take longer and your hair might not be as straight as it would be if you used a hair dryer.

How often can I dry my curly hair to make it straight?

You should avoid drying your curly hair to make it straight too often. This can cause damage to your hair. Instead, try to limit it to once or twice a week.

What should I do if my hair is still curly after drying it?

If your hair is still curly after drying it, you can try using a flat iron to straighten it further. However, be careful not to use the flat iron too often as this can damage your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when you wash your hair as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid friction and reduce frizz.

By following these tips, you can keep your curly hair looking healthy and beautiful.
