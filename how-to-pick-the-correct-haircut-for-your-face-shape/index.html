How To Pick The Correct Haircut For Your Face Shape

Written by Luffy Jul 12, 2023 · 9 min read

When it comes to getting a haircut, choosing the right one for your face shape can make all the difference. Not only does it enhance your features, but it can also bring balance and symmetry to your overall look. In this article, we’ll give you tips on how to pick the correct haircut for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Pick the Correct Haircut for Your Face Shape
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Tips

Description

Choosing the right haircut for your face shape can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to know which one is right for you. However, by understanding your face shape and the hairstyles that suit it, you can make an informed decision that will enhance your look.

How to Pick the Correct Haircut for Your Face Shape

The first step in picking the right haircut for your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Once you have identified your face shape, you can then choose a haircut that complements your features and brings balance to your overall look.

Face Shapes and Haircuts

Oval Face: An oval face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off a variety of haircuts. You can go for a short, medium or long hairstyle, and it will look great. Avoid styles that cover your face too much, and choose hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.

Round Face: A round face shape is characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. To create the illusion of a longer face, opt for a haircut that adds height to the crown of your head. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will only make your face appear wider.

Square Face: A square face shape has a strong jawline and a square forehead. To soften your features, choose a haircut that adds volume to the top of your head and has soft, rounded edges. Avoid styles that are too angular or structured.

Heart Face: A heart-shaped face has a wide forehead and a narrow chin. To create balance, choose a haircut that adds volume to the chin area, such as a chin-length bob or a layered haircut. Avoid styles that add volume to the top of your head, as this will only make your forehead appear wider.

Diamond Face: A diamond face shape has wide cheekbones and a narrow forehead and chin. To create balance, choose a haircut that adds volume to the forehead and chin area, such as a pixie cut or a shoulder-length bob. Avoid styles that add volume to the cheekbones.

Oblong Face: An oblong face shape has a long and narrow shape. To create balance, choose a haircut that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as a layered cut or a side-swept bang. Avoid styles that are too long and straight, as this will only elongate your face shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to pick the correct haircut for your face shape:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Research hairstyles that suit your face shape
  3. Consider your hair type and texture
  4. Consult with a hairstylist
  5. Choose a hairstyle that complements your personality and lifestyle

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when picking the right haircut for your face shape:

  • Experiment with different haircuts and styles to find what works best for you
  • Consider your hair color and skin tone when choosing a hairstyle
  • Take care of your hair to ensure it looks its best
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about picking the right haircut for your face shape:

  • Q: Can I pull off a short haircut if I have a round face shape? A: Yes, you can. Just make sure the haircut adds height to the crown of your head to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Q: Can I wear bangs if I have a square face shape? A: Yes, you can. Just make sure the bangs are soft and rounded to soften your features.
  • Q: What should I do if I can’t decide on a haircut? A: Consult with a hairstylist. They can give you recommendations based on your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Hair Care Tips

Once you’ve picked the right haircut for your face shape, it’s important to take care of your hair to ensure it looks its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Picking the right haircut for your face shape can enhance your look and bring balance to your features. By following the tips and guidelines in this article, you can make an informed decision and choose a haircut that suits you best.
