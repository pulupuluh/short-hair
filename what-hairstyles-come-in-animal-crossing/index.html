What Hairstyles Come In Animal Crossing

Written by Daniel Mar 26, 2023 · 5 min read

Animal Crossing has been a popular game since its release in 2001, and its latest version, Animal Crossing: New Horizons, has taken the world by storm. One of the most exciting aspects of the game is the ability to customize your character's appearance, including their hairstyle. In this article, we'll explore what hairstyles come in Animal Crossing and how to get them.

Description

Animal Crossing offers a wide variety of hairstyles to choose from. These include short and long styles, curly and straight styles, and even some unique and quirky styles. You can also choose from a range of hair colors, including natural colors like brown and blonde, as well as brighter colors like pink and blue.

How to

Getting a new hairstyle in Animal Crossing is relatively easy. You can visit Harriet at the Shampoodle salon in the city and ask her to give you a new look. Harriet will ask you a few questions about the kind of hair you want and then show you a range of styles to choose from. Once you've selected your new look, Harriet will give you a mirror so you can admire your new hairdo.

Step by Step

1. Visit the Shampoodle salon in the city.

2. Speak to Harriet and tell her you want a new hairstyle.

3. Answer Harriet's questions about the kind of hair you want.

4. Choose a hairstyle from the range of styles Harriet shows you.

5. Admire your new look in the mirror.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle in Animal Crossing:

- Experiment with different styles to find one that suits your character's personality.

- Choose a color that complements your character's skin tone.

- Consider the time of year when choosing a hairstyle. For example, a shorter style may be more comfortable in the summer months.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles in Animal Crossing:

Q: Can I change my hairstyle more than once?

A: Yes, you can change your hairstyle as many times as you like.

Q: Can I change the color of my hair?

A: Yes, you can choose from a range of hair colors at the Shampoodle salon.

Q: Is there a limit to the number of hairstyles I can choose from?

A: No, there is a wide range of hairstyles to choose from at the Shampoodle salon.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a new hairstyle in Animal Crossing, it's important to take care of your hair. To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, try using a hair care product like shampoo and conditioner. You can also visit the salon regularly to get a trim and keep your hair looking its best.

Overall, Animal Crossing offers a wide range of hairstyles to choose from, making it easy to find a look that suits your character's personality. Whether you prefer short and sweet or long and flowing, there's a hairstyle for everyone in Animal Crossing.
