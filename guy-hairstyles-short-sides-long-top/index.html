Guy Hairstyles Short Sides Long Top

Short sides long top hairstyles have been a popular trend among men for quite some time now. This hairstyle is versatile and can be adapted to suit any face shape and hair type. In this article, we'll explore how to achieve the perfect guy hairstyles short sides long top in relaxed English language. We'll cover how to style it, what to ask for at the barber, and some tips for maintaining the look.

Description

Guy hairstyles short sides long top is a hairstyle where the hair on the sides of the head is cut short, while the hair on the top is left long. This hairstyle can be styled in many different ways, such as slicked back, textured, or messy. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be adapted to suit any face shape and hair type. This hairstyle is popular among men because it's easy to style and maintain.

How to Achieve Guy Hairstyles Short Sides Long Top

The first step in achieving guy hairstyles short sides long top is to ask your barber for a short fade on the sides and back of your head. Depending on your preference, you can ask for a high, medium, or low fade. The hair on the top of your head should be left long enough to be styled in the desired way.

Once you have the right haircut, it's time to style your hair. Apply some hair product to your damp hair and use a comb or your fingers to style it. You can slick it back for a classic look or add some texture for a more modern look.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Visit the Barber

The first step in achieving guy hairstyles short sides long top is to visit your barber. Ask for a short fade on the sides and back of your head, leaving the hair on the top long enough to be styled in the desired way. You can also ask your barber for recommendations based on your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner, and then dry it with a towel. Apply some hair product to your damp hair to make it easier to style.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Using a comb or your fingers, style your hair in the desired way. You can slick it back for a classic look, add some texture for a more modern look, or leave it messy for a relaxed look.

Tips for Maintaining Guy Hairstyles Short Sides Long Top

Here are some tips for maintaining guy hairstyles short sides long top:

- Use a good quality hair product to keep your hair in place.

- Wash your hair regularly, but not too often, as this can strip the natural oils from your hair.

- Visit your barber regularly to maintain the short sides and back.

- Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

1. What hair type is best for guy hairstyles short sides long top?

Guy hairstyles short sides long top can work for any hair type, whether it's straight, curly, or wavy. It's important to choose a style that suits your hair type and face shape.

2. How often should I visit the barber to maintain the short sides and back?

You should visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain the short sides and back. This will keep your hairstyle looking fresh and tidy.

3. What hair products should I use to style my hair?

You can use a variety of hair products to style your hair, such as pomade, wax, or gel. Choose a product that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Hair Care

To maintain the health of your hair, it's important to wash it regularly with shampoo and conditioner. Use a good quality hair product to protect your hair from heat damage and to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as this can damage your hair over time.

Guy hairstyles short sides long top is a versatile and stylish hairstyle that can be adapted to suit any face shape and hair type. With the right haircut and styling products, you can achieve the perfect look for you. Remember to visit your barber regularly to maintain the short sides and back, and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
