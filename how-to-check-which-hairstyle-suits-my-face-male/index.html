How To Check Which Hairstyle Suits My Face Male

Written by Bardi May 03, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're a man who is looking to spice up your hairstyle, you might be wondering which hairstyle suits your face shape best. After all, different face shapes suit different hairstyles, and it's important to know which one is the most flattering for you. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide to determine which hairstyle suits your face, along with tips and hair care advice to keep your locks looking great!

Table of Contents

  • Step 2: Look for Hairstyles that Suit Your Face Shape
  • Step 3: Consider Your Hair Type
  • Step 4: Try on Different Hairstyles
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to finding the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Look at the outline of your face and compare it to the descriptions of the different face shapes. You can also use an online face shape calculator to determine your face shape.

Step 2: Look for Hairstyles that Suit Your Face Shape

Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that suit your face shape. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round face: Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, such as a faux hawk or a pompadour.
  • Oval face: Lucky you! Almost any hairstyle will suit your face shape, so feel free to experiment with different styles.
  • Square face: Look for hairstyles that soften your jawline, such as a side-parted hairstyle or a slicked-back undercut.
  • Heart face: Try hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a messy quiff or a textured fringe.
  • Diamond face: Look for hairstyles that add width to your forehead and chin, such as a side-swept fringe or a messy side part.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair type. Different hair types suit different hairstyles, and some hairstyles require more maintenance than others. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Straight hair: Try hairstyles with texture, such as a messy fringe or a slicked-back undercut.
  • Curly hair: Look for hairstyles that embrace your curls, such as a curly fringe or a short curly crop.
  • Thick hair: Opt for hairstyles that thin out your hair, such as a textured crop or a layered cut.
  • Thin hair: Look for hairstyles that add volume to your hair, such as a messy quiff or a slicked-back undercut.

Step 4: Try on Different Hairstyles

Once you have narrowed down your options, it's time to try on different hairstyles. You can either visit a hair salon or try on virtual hairstyles using a hairstyle app. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

  • Bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to your hair salon appointment.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend on styling your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles until you find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

Q: Can I pull off a buzz cut?

A: If you have a round face, a buzz cut may not be the most flattering hairstyle for you. However, if you have an oval or square face shape, a buzz cut can look great.

Q: Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

A: Absolutely! You can experiment with different hairstyles for different occasions, such as a formal event or a casual night out.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and the length of your hair. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips

  • Use shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on a daily basis.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your hairstyle.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.

By following these tips, you can easily determine which hairstyle suits your face shape and hair type best. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that makes you look and feel your best!
