What Is The Best Semi Permanent Hair Dye To Cover Grey Hair

Written by Christine Jun 02, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of seeing grey hair on your head? Don't worry! You can cover them up with a semi-permanent hair dye. But, what is the best semi-permanent hair dye to cover grey hair? In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about semi-permanent hair dye and which one is the best for grey hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cover Grey Hair with Semi-Permanent Hair Dye
  • Step-by-step Guide
  • Tips for Covering Grey Hair with Semi-Permanent Hair Dye
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care after Using Semi-Permanent Hair Dye

Description

Semi-permanent hair dye is a type of hair dye that lasts for a shorter period than permanent hair dye. It usually lasts for about 6-8 weeks and gradually fades away. It is a great option for those who want to change their hair color temporarily without damaging their hair. Semi-permanent hair dyes are also great for covering grey hair as they do not penetrate the hair shaft like permanent hair dye. They only coat the hair shaft and give it a new color.

How to Cover Grey Hair with Semi-Permanent Hair Dye

The process of covering grey hair with semi-permanent hair dye is quite easy. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step-by-step Guide

  1. Choose the right shade of semi-permanent hair dye that matches your natural hair color.
  2. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any build-up and oils from your hair.
  3. Towel dry your hair and apply petroleum jelly or conditioner around your hairline to prevent the dye from staining your skin.
  4. Mix the semi-permanent hair dye according to the instructions on the package.
  5. Apply the dye to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  6. Leave the dye on your hair for the recommended time according to the instructions on the package.
  7. Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water until the water runs clear.
  8. Apply a conditioner to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it off.
  9. Style your hair as usual.

Tips for Covering Grey Hair with Semi-Permanent Hair Dye

Here are some tips that you can follow to get the best results while covering your grey hair with semi-permanent hair dye:

  • Choose a semi-permanent hair dye that is ammonia-free and does not contain harsh chemicals as they can damage your hair.
  • Do a patch test before applying the dye to your hair to avoid any allergic reactions.
  • If you have a lot of grey hair, you may need to leave the dye on for a longer time than recommended.
  • Use a shower cap to cover your hair while the dye is processing to prevent it from drying out.
  • Do not wash your hair for at least 24-48 hours after applying the dye to let the color set in.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about covering grey hair with semi-permanent hair dye:

Q. How long does semi-permanent hair dye last?

A. Semi-permanent hair dye usually lasts for about 6-8 weeks.

Q. Can semi-permanent hair dye cover grey hair?

A. Yes, semi-permanent hair dye is great for covering grey hair as it coats the hair shaft and does not penetrate it like permanent hair dye.

Q. Is semi-permanent hair dye safe for your hair?

A. Yes, semi-permanent hair dye is safe for your hair as it does not contain harsh chemicals like ammonia that can damage your hair.

Hair Care after Using Semi-Permanent Hair Dye

After using semi-permanent hair dye, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain the color and prevent damage. Here are some tips you can follow:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent the color from fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners as they can damage your hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip the color from your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

In conclusion, semi-permanent hair dye is a great option for covering grey hair. Choose a shade that matches your natural hair color, follow the instructions on the package, and take care of your hair after using the dye to maintain the color and prevent damage.
