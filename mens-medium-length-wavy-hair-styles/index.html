Mens Medium Length Wavy Hair Styles A Guide For 2023

Written by Petter Jun 03, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you a man with medium length wavy hair looking for some inspiration on how to style it? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about mens medium length wavy hair styles, including how to achieve them, tips for maintaining them, and answers to common questions. Let’s dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Mens Medium Length Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Mens Medium Length Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Mens Medium Length Wavy Hair

Description

Mens medium length wavy hair styles are perfect for those who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look. This hair type is versatile and can be worn in a variety of ways, from messy and casual to sleek and sophisticated.

One of the best things about mens medium length wavy hair is that it doesn’t require a lot of styling products or tools. With just a few key products and some basic styling techniques, you can achieve a great look in no time.

How to Style Mens Medium Length Wavy Hair

Before we dive into the specific styles, let’s talk about some general tips for styling mens medium length wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as this will help maintain your natural waves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are some popular mens medium length wavy hair styles and how to achieve them:

1. Messy Waves

To achieve messy waves, start by towel-drying your hair so that it’s damp but not dripping. Apply a sea salt spray or texturizing cream to your hair, then scrunch it with your hands to enhance your natural waves. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry it on a low heat setting.

2. Slicked Back

To achieve a slicked back look, apply a small amount of pomade or gel to your hair and comb it back away from your face. You can also use a hair dryer to blow dry your hair back for a sleeker look.

3. Curly Fringe

To achieve a curly fringe, use a curl-enhancing product like a mousse or cream and apply it to your hair. Use your fingers or a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural curls. Style your hair forward and to one side to create a curly fringe.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Medium Length Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens medium length wavy hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy appearance.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about mens medium length wavy hair:

Q: Can I straighten my wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your wavy hair if you want to achieve a different look. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It’s recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy appearance.

Q: What kind of shampoo and conditioner should I use?

A: Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Mens Medium Length Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your mens medium length wavy hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as this will help maintain your natural waves.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a great look with your mens medium length wavy hair. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don’t be afraid to ask your stylist for advice!
