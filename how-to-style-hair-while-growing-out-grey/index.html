How To Style Hair While Growing Out Grey Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine May 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you in the process of growing out your grey hair but struggling to style it? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many women are embracing their natural grey hair, but it can be challenging to figure out how to style it during the transitional period. In this blog post, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to style hair while growing out grey.

  • How to Style Hair While Growing Out Grey
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Hair While Growing Out Grey

The first thing to keep in mind when it comes to styling grey hair is that it's a different texture than your natural hair. Grey hair tends to be coarser, drier, and more fragile, which means you need to treat it with extra care.

Here are some things you can do to style your hair while growing out grey:

1. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Grey hair has a unique texture that can be quite beautiful. Try embracing your natural texture and working with it instead of against it. You can use products like curl cream or mousse to enhance your natural curls or waves.

2. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles while you're growing out your grey hair. You can try a sleek bob, a messy bun, or even a braided hairstyle. The key is to find a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

3. Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can be a great way to add some style to your hair while you're growing out your grey. You can try a headband, hair clips, or even a scarf to add some color and texture to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to style your hair while growing out grey:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling. Make sure it's completely dry, so it's easier to work with.

2. Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product like mousse or curl cream to enhance your natural texture.

3. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using clips or hair ties.

4. Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a brush, comb, or your fingers. Use a hair dryer, if necessary, to set the style.

5. Finish with a Hair Spray

Finish your style with a light hold hair spray to keep it in place.

Tips for Styling Grey Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling grey hair:

1. Use a Purple Shampoo

Grey hair can sometimes turn yellow or brassy. Use a purple shampoo to keep your hair looking bright and vibrant.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Try to avoid heat styling your hair as much as possible. Heat can damage your hair and make it even more fragile.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: Should I dye my hair while growing out grey?

A: It's up to you. Some women choose to dye their hair while growing out grey, while others embrace their natural hair color.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture. Generally, you should wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care Tips for Grey Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for grey hair:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Grey hair tends to be drier than other hair types. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

2. Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to give your hair some extra hydration and nourishment.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your hair while growing out grey and embrace your natural hair color with confidence.
