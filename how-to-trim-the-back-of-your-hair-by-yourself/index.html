How To Trim The Back Of Your Hair By Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Trimming your hair at home can be a challenging task, especially when it comes to the back of your head. However, with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to achieve a professional-looking haircut without leaving your home. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to trim the back of your hair by yourself in relaxed English language.

Description

Trimming the back of your hair by yourself can be a daunting task, but it is not impossible. With the COVID-19 pandemic, many hair salons have closed down, and getting a professional haircut has become a challenge. However, with the right tools, technique, and patience, you can trim your hair at home.

How to Trim the Back of Your Hair by Yourself

Before you start trimming the back of your hair, you need to gather the necessary tools. These include:

  • Scissors or clippers
  • Comb
  • Mirror (preferably a full-length mirror)
  • Water spray bottle

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow when trimming the back of your hair:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Wet hair is easier to cut, but make sure it is entirely dry before you start trimming.
  2. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and make it easier to cut.
  3. Stand in front of a full-length mirror or set up two mirrors to see the back of your head.
  4. Take a section of hair from the back of your head and comb it straight.
  5. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and make a horizontal cut with scissors or clippers at the desired length.
  6. Repeat this process with small sections of hair until you achieve your desired length.
  7. Move to the sides of your head and repeat the same process.
  8. Check the back of your hair using the mirrors and make any necessary adjustments.
  9. Trim the hairline around your neck to complete the look.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Trimming the Back of Your Hair

Trimming the back of your hair can be tricky, but the following tips can make the process easier:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen your hair if it is too dry or difficult to manage.
  • Use a comb to guide your scissors or clippers for a more precise cut.
  • Take breaks in between to check your progress and avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Ask someone for help if you are finding it hard to see the back of your head.

FAQs

1. How often should I trim the back of my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. As a general rule, it is recommended to trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy.

2. Can I use regular scissors to trim my hair?

While regular scissors can be used to trim your hair, it is recommended to use hair cutting scissors or clippers for a more precise cut.

3. How can I avoid cutting too much hair?

Start with small sections of hair and take breaks in between to check your progress. It is better to cut less hair and make multiple passes than to cut too much at once.

Hair Care after Trimming

After trimming the back of your hair, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. This includes:

  • Regularly washing and conditioning your hair
  • Using a heat protectant when styling your hair
  • Avoiding tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair
  • Using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair

Trimming the back of your hair by yourself can be a challenging task, but with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to achieve a professional-looking haircut. Follow our step-by-step guide, use our tips, and take care of your hair after trimming to maintain its health and shine.
