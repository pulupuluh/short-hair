How To Cut Short Side Bangs Yourself Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and want to switch things up? Cutting short side bangs is a great way to freshen up your look without making a drastic change. Plus, you can do it yourself at home! In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of cutting short side bangs yourself.

How to Cut Short Side Bangs Yourself

Cutting short side bangs can be a bit intimidating, especially if you've never done it before. But with the right tools and technique, you can achieve the look you want. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these easy steps to cut short side bangs yourself:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and comb it forward.
  4. Decide how short you want your bangs to be and make a small snip. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't put hair back!
  5. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you reach your desired length.
  6. Repeat on the other side.
  7. Check to make sure both sides are even.
  8. Style your bangs as desired.

That's it! You now have short side bangs that you cut yourself.

Tips for Cutting Short Side Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting short side bangs:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up. It's better to cut too little than too much.
  • Don't cut your bangs straight across. Instead, cut them at an angle for a more natural look.
  • Use hair clips to keep the rest of your hair out of the way while you're cutting.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp to avoid split ends.
  • If you're not confident in your cutting skills, consider practicing on a wig or hair extension before cutting your own hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting short side bangs:

How short should I cut my side bangs?

The length of your side bangs is up to you, but typically they should fall just above your eyebrows.

Can I cut my bangs when they're wet?

Yes, you can cut your bangs when they're wet. Wet hair is easier to work with and you'll be able to see the length better.

What if I mess up?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! You can always visit a hair salon to have a professional fix it for you.

Hair Care After Cutting Short Side Bangs

After cutting your short side bangs, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking neat.
  • If you have curly hair, be sure to use products that are specifically designed for curly hair to prevent frizz.

Cutting short side bangs yourself is a great way to switch up your hairstyle and save money on salon visits. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a professional-looking result. Just remember to take your time and start with small snips. Happy cutting!
