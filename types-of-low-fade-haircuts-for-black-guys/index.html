Types Of Low Fade Haircuts For Black Guys

Written by Daniel May 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are looking for a fresh and trendy haircut, a low fade is a great option. This style is versatile and can be worn in many different ways, making it perfect for all types of hair. In this article, we will discuss the types of low fade haircuts for black guys and provide tips on how to maintain them.

Description

A low fade haircut involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the top longer. The hair gradually tapers down from the top to the skin, creating a fade effect. This style is popular among black guys as it highlights their natural curls and texture.

How to

Before getting a low fade, you should decide which type of fade you want. There are different types of fades such as high fade, mid fade, and low fade. A low fade starts just above the ear and tapers down to the skin.

To get a low fade, you need to visit a professional barber or stylist. They will use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair short, and gradually blend it into the longer hair on top. You can ask for a specific length on the top, depending on your preference.

Step by Step

Step 1: Visit a professional barber or stylist
Step 2: Decide which type of fade you want
Step 3: Communicate your preferences to the barber or stylist
Step 4: Let the barber or stylist work their magic
Step 5: Use hair products to style your hair as desired

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain your low fade haircut:

- Visit your barber or stylist regularly to maintain the shape of the haircut
- Use hair products such as pomade or wax to style your hair
- Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it
- Use a silk or satin pillowcase to protect your hair while sleeping
- Keep your hair moisturized to prevent dryness and breakage

FAQ

Q: Can I get a low fade if I have curly hair?
A: Yes, a low fade is perfect for curly hair as it highlights the natural texture and curls.

Q: How often do I need to visit the barber to maintain my low fade?
A: It is recommended to visit the barber every 2-3 weeks to maintain the shape of the haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, it is important to follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips:

- Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo
- Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
- Apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat and environmental damage
- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
- Avoid using too much heat on your hair

By following these tips and getting a low fade haircut, you can achieve a stylish and trendy look. Remember to communicate your preferences to your barber or stylist and maintain the shape of the haircut by visiting them regularly.
