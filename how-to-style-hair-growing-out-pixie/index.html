How To Style Hair Growing Out Pixie A Complete Guide

Written by Christine May 14, 2023 · 9 min read

If you’ve recently chopped your hair into a pixie cut, you may be wondering how to style your hair as it grows out. The process of growing out a pixie cut can be challenging, but with a little patience and the right styling techniques, you can make the transition smoother. In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style hair growing out pixie, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Hair Growing Out Pixie
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Hair Growing Out Pixie
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Growing Out Pixie Cut

How to Style Hair Growing Out Pixie

As your hair grows out of a pixie cut, it can be tempting to throw in the towel and just wait for it to grow out completely. But with a little effort, you can style your hair in a way that looks chic and stylish. Here’s how:

1. Use Hair Accessories

One of the easiest ways to style hair growing out pixie is to use hair accessories. Headbands, hair clips, and bobby pins can help keep your hair in place and add some personality to your look. You can also experiment with scarves, hats, and other accessories to add some variety to your hairstyles.

2. Experiment with Parting

Changing your parting can make a big difference in how your hair looks. Try parting your hair in the middle, on the side, or even in a zigzag pattern. This can help add volume and texture to your hair and make it look less like you’re growing out a pixie cut.

3. Use a Curling Iron or Flat Iron

A curling iron or flat iron can help add some texture and shape to your hair. You can use a curling iron to create loose waves or use a flat iron to create sleek, straight hair. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

4. Try a Half-Up, Half-Down Style

A half-up, half-down style is a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still showing off your length. You can experiment with different variations, such as a messy bun or a braided half-up style.

5. Get Regular Trims

While it may seem counterintuitive to get regular trims when you’re trying to grow out your hair, it’s actually important to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. A trim every 6-8 weeks can help keep your hair looking its best and prevent breakage.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some of the best ways to style hair growing out pixie, here’s a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any product buildup and keep your hair healthy.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair to help detangle and protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair. You can also use a diffuser attachment to help enhance your natural texture.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron or Flat Iron

Use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture and shape to your hair. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 5: Apply Hair Accessories

Add some personality to your look by using hair accessories such as headbands, hair clips, or bobby pins.

Tips for Styling Hair Growing Out Pixie

Here are some additional tips to help you style your hair as it grows out:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Be patient and don’t get discouraged if your hair doesn’t look exactly how you want it to.
  • Use a texturizing spray to help add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Try using a dry shampoo to help absorb oil and refresh your hair between washes.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut to help shape your hair and make the transition easier.

FAQs

Q: How long does it take to grow out a pixie cut?

A: It can take anywhere from 6 months to a year to grow out a pixie cut, depending on how fast your hair grows and how long you want it to be.

Q: Can I dye my hair while it’s growing out?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair while it’s growing out. Just be sure to use a gentle, nourishing dye and avoid over-processing your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair while it’s growing out?

A: You should wash your hair as often as you feel comfortable, but try not to wash it too frequently as this can strip your hair of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days or as needed.

Hair Care Tips for Growing Out Pixie Cut

Here are some general hair care tips to help keep your hair healthy and promote growth:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently as this can damage your hair and slow down growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Consider taking a hair growth supplement or vitamin to help support healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can style hair growing out pixie in a way that looks chic and stylish. Remember to be patient and experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
