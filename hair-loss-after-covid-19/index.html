Hair Loss After Covid 19 Causes Prevention And Treatment

Written by Aprilia Jun 21, 2023 · 7 min read

If you are someone who has recently recovered from Covid 19, then you might have noticed a sudden hair loss. Although it is not a common symptom of Covid 19, hair loss after Covid 19 can affect your confidence and self-esteem. In this article, we will discuss the causes, prevention, and treatment of hair loss after Covid 19.

What Causes Hair Loss After Covid 19?

Hair loss after Covid 19 can be caused by various factors. The most common causes are:

  • Physical stress caused by Covid 19
  • Emotional stress due to the pandemic
  • Nutritional deficiencies
  • Medications used to treat Covid 19

When you are infected with Covid 19, your body goes through a lot of physical stress. This can cause your hair to enter the resting phase of the hair growth cycle, leading to hair loss. Emotional stress due to isolation, financial problems, and fear of the virus can also cause hair loss. Nutritional deficiencies such as low levels of iron, vitamin D, and biotin can also contribute to hair loss. Finally, some medications used to treat Covid 19 such as Remdesivir and Dexamethasone can also cause hair loss.

How to Prevent Hair Loss After Covid 19?

While it is not always possible to prevent hair loss after Covid 19, there are some steps you can take to reduce your risk.

  • Eat a balanced and nutritious diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Manage your stress levels through meditation, exercise, or therapy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools and harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair

How to Treat Hair Loss After Covid 19?

If you are already experiencing hair loss after Covid 19, then you can try the following treatments:

  • Minoxidil: A topical medication that can promote hair growth
  • Finasteride: An oral medication that can prevent hair loss
  • Platelet-rich plasma (PRP) therapy: A procedure that involves injecting your own blood plasma into your scalp to stimulate hair growth
  • Hair transplant: A surgical procedure that moves hair from one part of your scalp to another

It is important to consult a dermatologist or a hair specialist before trying any of these treatments.

Hair Care Tips for Hair Loss After Covid 19

In addition to the above treatments, you can also take care of your hair to prevent further hair loss.

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation
  • Avoid exposing your hair to excessive heat and sunlight
  • Wear a hat or a scarf when going out in the sun
  • Use a hair serum or oil to nourish your hair

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Is hair loss after Covid 19 permanent?

A. It depends on the cause of hair loss. If it is due to physical or emotional stress, then it is usually temporary and your hair will grow back once your body recovers. However, if it is due to genetics or hormonal imbalances, then it might require long-term treatment.

Q. Can Covid 19 vaccines cause hair loss?

A. There is no evidence that Covid 19 vaccines cause hair loss. In fact, some studies suggest that Covid 19 vaccines might actually improve hair growth by boosting the immune system.

Q. Can hair loss after Covid 19 be prevented?

A. While it is not always possible to prevent hair loss after Covid 19, you can reduce your risk by eating a balanced diet, managing your stress levels, and taking care of your hair.

Conclusion

Hair loss after Covid 19 can be a distressing experience, but it is usually temporary and can be treated with the right approach. By taking care of your hair and consulting a specialist, you can regain your confidence and self-esteem.
