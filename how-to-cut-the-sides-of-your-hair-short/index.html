How To Cut The Sides Of Your Hair Short A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Mar 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and want to cut the sides of your hair short, you're in the right place. This guide will walk you through the process of cutting your hair, from preparation to hair care tips. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned hair cutter, you'll find useful information in this post.

Table of Contents

  1. Preparation
  2. Tools Needed
  3. Sectioning Your Hair
  4. Cutting Your Hair
  5. Tips for a Great Haircut
  6. FAQs
  7. Hair Care Tips

Preparation

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to prepare properly. First, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be tricky to cut and may result in uneven sections. Second, find a well-lit area to work in. Natural light is best, but if that's not possible, use a bright lamp. Finally, wear a shirt that you don't mind getting hair on.

Tools Needed

Here are the tools you'll need to cut the sides of your hair short:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Sectioning clips

Sectioning Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair. Use a comb to create a part down the middle of your head, from the front to the back. Then, create another part from the top of each ear to the center part. You should now have four sections of hair. Use hair ties to tie up the top two sections and sectioning clips to clip the bottom two sections to your shirt.

Cutting Your Hair

Now it's time to start cutting. Begin with the bottom left section of hair. Use clippers to trim the hair to your desired length. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Repeat on the bottom right section of hair.

Next, use scissors to trim the hair above the ears. Comb the hair straight down and cut along the hairline. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once, as it's easier to cut more hair than to add it back.

Finally, use scissors to blend the top sections of hair with the shorter sides. Comb the hair forward and cut at a diagonal angle to create a seamless transition.

Tips for a Great Haircut

  • Start with longer hair and gradually work your way shorter. You can always trim more hair, but you can't add it back.
  • Use sharp scissors and clippers to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take your time and be patient. Rushing can result in uneven sections.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from different angles.

FAQs

How often should I cut the sides of my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. As a general rule, aim to cut the sides of your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the same length.

Can I cut my hair myself or should I go to a salon?

You can certainly cut your hair yourself, but if you're not confident or have never done it before, it may be best to go to a salon. A professional hair stylist can help you achieve the look you want and ensure a clean cut.

Hair Care Tips

After you've cut the sides of your hair short, it's important to take care of your hair properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut the sides of your hair short like a pro. Happy cutting!
