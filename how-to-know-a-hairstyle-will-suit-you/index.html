How To Know A Hairstyle Will Suit You Tips And Tricks

How to Know a Hairstyle Will Suit You

Before you commit to a new hairstyle, there are a few things you should consider:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair texture and thickness
  • Your lifestyle and daily routine

Let's take a closer look at each of these factors.

Face Shape

Your face shape is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a new hairstyle. Different hairstyles will flatter different face shapes. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have a round face, go for a hairstyle with height at the crown and long, layered bangs. This will elongate your face.
  • If you have a square face, go for a hairstyle with soft, rounded layers. This will soften your features.
  • If you have an oval face, lucky you! You can pull off almost any hairstyle.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, go for a hairstyle with lots of layers and a side-swept bang. This will balance out your features.

Hair Texture and Thickness

Your hair texture and thickness will also play a role in determining which hairstyle will suit you. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have fine hair, go for a short or medium-length hairstyle with layers. This will create the illusion of volume.
  • If you have thick hair, go for a long hairstyle with minimal layers. This will help to control the volume.
  • If you have curly hair, go for a hairstyle that works with your natural texture. This will save you time and effort in styling.
  • If you have straight hair, go for a hairstyle with layers and texture. This will add some interest to your hair.

Lifestyle and Daily Routine

Your lifestyle and daily routine will also play a role in determining which hairstyle will suit you. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have an active lifestyle, go for a low-maintenance hairstyle that you can easily style on the go.
  • If you have a busy schedule, go for a hairstyle that requires minimal styling time.
  • If you work in a professional environment, go for a hairstyle that looks polished and put-together.
  • If you're a stay-at-home parent, go for a hairstyle that is comfortable and easy to wear.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a new hairstyle, let's walk through the steps:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Consider your hair texture and thickness
  3. Think about your lifestyle and daily routine
  4. Research hairstyles that suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle
  5. Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion
  6. Try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to see how different hairstyles will look on you
  7. Choose a hairstyle that you love and feel confident in

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you choose a hairstyle that will suit you:

  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hairstylist
  • Consider getting a haircut in stages if you're nervous about making a big change
  • Don't be afraid to try something new - hair grows back!
  • Consider your skin tone when choosing a hair color

FAQs

How do I know if a short hairstyle will suit me?

Short hairstyles can be a great way to refresh your look, but they're not for everyone. To determine if a short hairstyle will suit you, consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Short hairstyles work best on oval and heart-shaped faces, and can be flattering on fine or thick hair. If you have a busy lifestyle or don't have time to style your hair every day, a short hairstyle may be a good option for you.

What should I consider when choosing a hair color?

When choosing a hair color, consider your skin tone. Generally, warm skin tones look best with warm hair colors like golden blondes or rich browns, while cool skin tones look best with cool hair colors like ash blondes or cool browns. It's also important to consider your natural hair color and how much maintenance you're willing to do to maintain your new hair color.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen a hairstyle that suits you, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Invest in high-quality hair care products
  • Use heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, which can strip it of its natural oils
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can find a hairstyle that will make you feel confident and beautiful. And remember, if you don't love your new hairstyle, it's only hair - it will grow back!
