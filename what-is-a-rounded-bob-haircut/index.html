What Is A Rounded Bob Haircut

Written by Robby Jul 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that is both trendy and timeless, then you might want to consider getting a rounded bob haircut. This haircut has been around for decades and is still one of the most popular styles today. The rounded bob is a shorter haircut that falls between the chin and the shoulders. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in a variety of ways and looks great on all face shapes. In this article, we'll take a closer look at what a rounded bob haircut is, how to get it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The rounded bob haircut is a classic hairstyle that has been around for many years. It's a shorter haircut that is typically cut to fall between the chin and the shoulders. This haircut is characterized by its rounded shape, which is created by cutting the hair at an angle. The rounded bob is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways. It can be straightened, curled, or left natural. It can also be worn with or without bangs.

How to Get a Rounded Bob Haircut

If you're interested in getting a rounded bob haircut, the first step is to find a stylist who is experienced in this type of haircut. You should bring pictures of the style you want to show your stylist. This will help them understand what you're looking for and how to achieve it. Once you and your stylist have agreed on the style, they will start by washing and blow-drying your hair. They will then cut your hair at an angle to create the rounded shape. They may also add layers to give your hair more volume and movement. Once your hair is cut, your stylist will style it and show you how to maintain the look at home.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a rounded bob haircut:

Step 1: Find a Stylist

Look for a stylist who is experienced in cutting rounded bob haircuts. Bring pictures of the style you want to show your stylist.

Step 2: Wash and Blow-Dry

Your stylist will wash and blow-dry your hair to prepare it for cutting.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Your stylist will cut your hair at an angle to create the rounded shape. They may also add layers to give your hair more volume and movement.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Your stylist will style your hair and show you how to maintain the look at home.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining a rounded bob haircut:

  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about rounded bob haircuts:

What face shapes look best with a rounded bob?

A rounded bob looks great on all face shapes, but it's especially flattering on those with oval, heart, or square-shaped faces.

How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Can I wear a rounded bob with bangs?

Yes, a rounded bob can be worn with or without bangs. Talk to your stylist about which option would work best for you.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your rounded bob haircut:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage

In conclusion, a rounded bob haircut is a classic and versatile hairstyle that looks great on all face shapes. If you're considering getting this haircut, be sure to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting rounded bobs. Follow the tips and advice in this article to maintain the shape and health of your haircut.
