Most Flattering Haircuts For Round Faces

Written by Petter Sep 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face, you may have struggled to find a haircut that flatters your features. But fear not! With the right cut and style, you can highlight your best features and create a flattering look. In this article, we will discuss the most flattering haircuts for round faces, how to achieve them, and hair care tips to maintain your style.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How-To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by fullness in the cheeks and a rounded jawline. The goal of a flattering haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of a more elongated face shape. This can be achieved by adding height and volume at the crown, creating angles with layers, and avoiding styles that add width to the face.

How-To

The first step in achieving a flattering haircut for a round face is to consult with a professional stylist. They can help you determine your face shape and recommend styles that will work best for you. It's also important to consider your hair texture and lifestyle when choosing a cut.

Step-by-Step

1. Determine your face shape

2. Consult with a professional stylist

3. Consider your hair texture and lifestyle

4. Choose a cut that adds height and volume at the crown

5. Create angles with layers

6. Avoid styles that add width to the face

Tips

When choosing a cut for a round face, it's important to consider your hair texture and lifestyle. If you have fine hair, a shorter cut with layers can add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a longer cut with minimal layers can help control the volume. If you lead an active lifestyle, a low-maintenance cut may be best for you.

FAQ

Q: What are the best haircuts for round faces?

A: The best haircuts for round faces are those that add height and volume at the crown, create angles with layers, and avoid styles that add width to the face.

Q: Should I choose a short or long haircut for a round face?

A: The length of your haircut will depend on your hair texture and lifestyle. If you have fine hair, a shorter cut with layers can add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, a longer cut with minimal layers can help control the volume.

Hair Care

To maintain your flattering haircut, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. This includes regular trims to keep your style looking fresh, using quality hair products that are appropriate for your hair type, and protecting your hair from heat damage with a heat protectant spray.

With the right cut and care, you can rock a flattering hairstyle that highlights your best features and complements your round face shape. Consult with a professional stylist to find the perfect cut for you, and enjoy your new look!
