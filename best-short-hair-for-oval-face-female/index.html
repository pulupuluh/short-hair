Best Short Hair For Oval Face Female

Written by Frank Jul 21, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your long hair and want to try something new? Short hair is a great option, especially if you have an oval face. In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for oval face females. From how to style them to tips on maintaining them, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oval face is considered to be the most versatile face shape, as it can pull off almost any haircut. Short haircuts are a great way to accentuate your features and show off your face shape. The right short haircut can make your face look slimmer and more defined. It can also add volume and texture to your hair. In this article, we will be discussing the best short haircuts for oval face females.

How to

When it comes to choosing the right short haircut for an oval face, there are a few things to consider. First, you want to choose a cut that will balance out your face shape. This means avoiding haircuts that are too short or too long. You also want to choose a cut that will flatter your features. For example, if you have a prominent nose, you may want to choose a haircut that adds volume to the top of your head, to draw attention away from your nose.

Step by Step

Now that you know what to look for in a short haircut for an oval face, here are some of the best options:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that looks great on oval faces. This cut is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. It adds volume to the top of the head, which can make your face look slimmer. It’s also a low-maintenance cut that is easy to style.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another great option for oval faces. This cut is chin-length or shorter, with layers that frame the face. It can add volume to the sides of your face, which can balance out your features. The bob cut is also a versatile cut that can be styled in many different ways.

Shaggy Cut

The shaggy cut is a layered cut that looks great on oval faces. This cut is longer on top, with shorter layers throughout. It adds volume and texture to the hair, which can make your face look slimmer. The shaggy cut is also a great option if you have thick hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut for an oval face:

  • Consider your hair texture when choosing a cut
  • Choose a cut that flatters your features
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new
  • Consult with a stylist to find the perfect cut for you

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for oval face females:

What is the best short haircut for an oval face?

The best short haircut for an oval face is one that balances out your face shape and flatters your features. Some great options include the pixie cut, bob cut, and shaggy cut.

Will a short haircut make my face look slimmer?

Yes, a short haircut can make your face look slimmer, especially if you choose a cut that adds volume to the top of your head.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your short haircut to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a heat protectant when styling with heat tools
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut
  • Consider using a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair

Now that you know the best short haircuts for oval face females, it’s time to book your appointment with a stylist. Remember to consider your hair texture and features when choosing a cut, and don’t be afraid to try something new. With the right short haircut, you can accentuate your features and show off your beautiful face shape.
