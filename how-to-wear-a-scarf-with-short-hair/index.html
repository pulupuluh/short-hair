How To Wear A Scarf With Short Hair

Written by Aprilia Apr 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Scarves are a versatile accessory that can elevate any outfit. They come in various colors, patterns, and materials, making them perfect for any season. However, wearing a scarf with short hair can be a challenge for some. In this article, we'll show you how to wear a scarf with short hair and give you some tips on how to take care of your hair while doing so.

Table of Content:

  • How to Wear a Scarf with Short Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Scarf with Short Hair
  • FAQs

How to Wear a Scarf with Short Hair

There are several ways to wear a scarf with short hair. The most popular ways are:

1. Headband

Use a scarf as a headband to add some color and texture to your hair. Fold the scarf into a thin strip and tie it around your head. You can either tie it at the top of your head or at the nape of your neck.

2. Ponytail Wrap

If you have a ponytail, wrap a scarf around it to create a chic and effortless look. You can either wrap the scarf around the base of your ponytail or tie it around the entire ponytail.

3. Turban

A turban is a great way to keep your hair off your face while looking stylish. Fold the scarf into a triangle and place it on your head with the pointy end facing forward. Tie the two ends together at the nape of your neck and tuck the remaining fabric underneath the knot.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to wear a scarf as a headband:

  1. Choose a scarf that complements your outfit.
  2. Fold the scarf into a thin strip.
  3. Place the scarf over your head, positioning it where you want it to sit.
  4. Tie the scarf in a knot at the top of your head or at the nape of your neck.
  5. Tuck any loose fabric underneath the scarf.

Here's a step by step guide on how to wear a scarf as a turban:

  1. Choose a scarf that complements your outfit.
  2. Fold the scarf into a triangle.
  3. Place the scarf on your head with the pointy end facing forward.
  4. Tie the two ends together at the nape of your neck.
  5. Tuck any loose fabric underneath the knot.

Tips for Wearing a Scarf with Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing a scarf with short hair:

  • Choose a scarf that complements your outfit and hair color.
  • Experiment with different tying styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use bobby pins to secure the scarf in place.
  • Don't tie the scarf too tight as it may cause headaches or damage your hair.
  • Wear a silk or satin scarf to prevent breakage and frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a scarf with short hair:

Can I wear a scarf with short hair?

Yes, you can wear a scarf with short hair. There are several ways to wear a scarf with short hair, such as a headband, ponytail wrap, or turban.

What type of scarf should I wear with short hair?

You can wear any type of scarf with short hair, but silk or satin scarves are ideal as they prevent breakage and frizz.

How do I tie a scarf as a headband?

To tie a scarf as a headband, fold the scarf into a thin strip and tie it at the top of your head or at the nape of your neck.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to take care of your hair when wearing a scarf:

  • Wear a silk or satin scarf to prevent breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid tying the scarf too tight as it may cause headaches or damage your hair.
  • Moisturize your hair before wearing a scarf to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup and oiliness.

Now that you know how to wear a scarf with short hair, it's time to add some flair to your hairstyle. Remember to choose a scarf that complements your outfit, experiment with different tying styles, and take care of your hair while doing so. With these tips, you'll be rocking a scarf with short hair in no time.
