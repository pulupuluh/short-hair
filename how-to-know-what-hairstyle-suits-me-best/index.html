How To Know What Hairstyle Suits Me Best A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 25, 2023 Â· 11 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. However, with a few tricks and tips, you can easily figure out what hairstyle suits you best and flaunt your locks with confidence. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to know what hairstyle suits me best, along with some hair care tips and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • Know Your Face Shape
  • Consider Your Hair Texture
  • Define Your Personal Style
  • Hairstyle Ideas for Different Face Shapes
  • Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair
  • FAQ: Common Questions About Hairstyles

Know Your Face Shape

One of the most important factors in choosing the right hairstyle is your face shape. There are six main face shapes: round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror.

  • Round: Your face is circular, with full cheeks and a rounded chin.
  • Square: Your face is angular, with a strong jawline and forehead.
  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and jaw.
  • Heart: Your face is wider at the forehead and temples, with a pointed chin.
  • Diamond: Your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at the forehead and jaw.
  • Oblong: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a straight hairline and jawline.

Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and balances out your proportions.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Another important factor in choosing a hairstyle is your hair texture. There are four main hair textures: straight, wavy, curly, and kinky. Your hair texture affects how your hair looks and behaves, and can also influence the types of hairstyles that work best for you.

  • Straight: Your hair is smooth and lacks natural volume.
  • Wavy: Your hair has gentle waves and some natural volume.
  • Curly: Your hair has tight curls and lots of natural volume.
  • Kinky: Your hair has a coarser texture and tight, springy curls.

Depending on your hair texture, you may want to choose a hairstyle that enhances your natural texture or adds more volume and movement to your hair.

Define Your Personal Style

Aside from your face shape and hair texture, your personal style is also an important factor in choosing a hairstyle. Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and the image you want to project. Do you prefer a classic, timeless look, or something edgier and more modern? Are you a low-maintenance person or do you enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles and trends?

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your lifestyle, profession, and personal preferences. A hairstyle that looks great on someone else may not suit you if it doesn't align with your personal style.

Hairstyle Ideas for Different Face Shapes

Now that you know your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, it's time to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some hairstyle ideas for different face shapes:

Round

Round faces look best with hairstyles that add height and length to the face. Avoid hairstyles that add width to the cheeks and jawline. Good hairstyle choices for round faces include:

  • Long, layered haircuts
  • Side-swept bangs
  • High ponytails
  • Top knots and buns

Square

Square faces look best with hairstyles that soften the angular features and add roundness to the face. Avoid hairstyles that accentuate the square jawline. Good hairstyle choices for square faces include:

  • Soft, layered haircuts
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Loose waves and curls
  • Bob haircuts

Oval

Oval faces are considered the most versatile face shape, as they can pull off a wide range of hairstyles. Good hairstyle choices for oval faces include:

  • Long, straight haircuts
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Beachy waves
  • Pixie cuts

Heart

Heart faces look best with hairstyles that balance out the forehead and narrow chin. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the forehead. Good hairstyle choices for heart faces include:

  • Long, side-swept bangs
  • Bob haircuts
  • Chin-length haircuts
  • Updos with volume at the crown

Diamond

Diamond faces look best with hairstyles that add width to the forehead and jawline, while minimizing the cheekbones. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the cheekbones. Good hairstyle choices for diamond faces include:

  • Chin-length bobs
  • Long, side-swept bangs
  • Updos with volume at the crown
  • Layered haircuts

Oblong

Oblong faces look best with hairstyles that add width to the face and break up the length. Avoid hairstyles that elongate the face. Good hairstyle choices for oblong faces include:

  • Shoulder-length haircuts
  • Layered haircuts
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Loose waves and curls

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

Choosing the right hairstyle is only half the battle - you also need to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools (like flat irons and curling irons) too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ: Common Questions About Hairstyles

Q: How do I know if a hairstyle will suit me?

A: Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle. You can also try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to see how different hairstyles look on you.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and the length of your hair. Generally, you should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: How do I choose a hair color that suits me?

A: Consider your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color when choosing a hair color. You can also consult with a professional colorist to find the best shade for you.

Q: How can I make my hair look thicker and fuller?

A: Use volumizing products, like mousse and root lifters, to add volume to your hair. You can also try teasing your hair at the roots or getting a layered haircut to add movement and texture.

Q: What is the best hairstyle for curly hair?

A: The best hairstyle for curly hair depends on your face shape and personal style. Generally, curly hair looks great with layered haircuts, side-swept bangs, and updos.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily figure out what hairstyle suits you best and feel confident and fabulous every day
