Mens Short Hair Long On Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

In recent years, the mens short hair long on top trend has taken the world by storm. This versatile haircut can be styled in many different ways and is perfect for guys who want to keep their hair short but still have some length on top. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this popular hairstyle, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The mens short hair long on top style is characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. The length of the hair on top can vary, but generally ranges from 2-4 inches. This style can be worn with a variety of textures, from straight to curly, and can be styled in many different ways.

How to Get Mens Short Hair Long on Top

If you're interested in getting the mens short hair long on top style, the first step is to find a good barber or stylist who is experienced in this type of haircut. When you go in for your appointment, be sure to bring pictures of the style you want so that your barber can get a clear idea of what you're looking for.

During the haircut, your barber will use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length. They will then use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length and style it according to your preferences.

Step by Step Guide to Style Mens Short Hair Long on Top

Styling mens short hair long on top is relatively easy and can be done in just a few simple steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair healthy and moisturized, which is important for this style.

Step 2: Towel Dry

After washing your hair, towel dry it until it is damp but not dripping wet. This will make it easier to style and will help to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 3: Apply Product

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as wax or pomade, to your hair. Work the product evenly through your hair, making sure to focus on the hair on top.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Using your fingers or a comb, style your hair on top in the desired direction. You can create a variety of different looks with this style, from slicked back to messy and textured.

Tips for Mens Short Hair Long on Top

To keep your mens short hair long on top looking great, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Regularly trim the hair on the sides and back to maintain the shape of the style.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Try different styling techniques, such as blow drying or using a straightener, to create different looks.

FAQ About Mens Short Hair Long on Top

Q: Is mens short hair long on top easy to maintain?

A: Yes, this style is relatively easy to maintain with regular haircuts and simple styling techniques.

Q: Can this style be worn by guys with curly hair?

A: Yes, mens short hair long on top can be styled with curly hair and can look great with a variety of textures.

Q: Can I style my hair on top in different directions?

A: Yes, you can style your hair on top in a variety of different directions to create different looks.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Short Hair Long on Top

To keep your mens short hair long on top looking its best, here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape of the style.
  • Use styling products that are designed for your hair type.

With these tips and techniques, you can rock the mens short hair long on top style with confidence and ease. Whether you're looking for a classic or modern take on this popular hairstyle, the options are endless. So why not give it a try and see how it works for you?
