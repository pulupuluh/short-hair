How To Tie A Maxi Dress That s Too Long

Written by Bardi Apr 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of tripping over the hem of your maxi dress? Or are you afraid to wear your favorite maxi dress because it's too long? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll show you how to tie a maxi dress that's too long using simple steps and tips.

Description

A maxi dress is a long, flowy dress that is perfect for any occasion. However, a common problem many people face is that the dress is too long, which can cause tripping, discomfort, and damage to the dress. To solve this problem, you can tie a knot at the bottom of your dress to shorten it and create a stylish look.

How to Tie a Maxi Dress That's Too Long

Here are the steps to tie a maxi dress that's too long:

Step 1: Put on the Dress

Before tying the dress, make sure to put it on and adjust it to your desired length.

Step 2: Gather the Fabric

Grab the fabric at the center of the hemline and pull it up towards your waist.

Step 3: Tie a Knot

Tie a knot using the excess fabric at your desired height. Make sure to tie the knot tightly to prevent it from coming undone.

Step 4: Adjust the Knot

Adjust the knot and the fabric to ensure that it looks neat and doesn't bunch up.

Step 5: Style the Dress

Style the dress by adding a belt or leaving it as is. You can also tuck in the excess fabric to create a more polished look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you tie a maxi dress that's too long:

  • Choose a dress that is made from a lightweight and flowy fabric to make it easier to tie.
  • Opt for a solid-colored dress to make the knot more prominent.
  • Experiment with different types of knots, such as a bow or a double knot, to create different looks.
  • Accessorize your dress with jewelry, a hat, or a bag to complete your outfit.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about tying a maxi dress that's too long:

Can I tie a maxi dress that is too short?

No, if the dress is too short, it's better to get it hemmed or choose a different dress that fits you better.

What if I don't want to tie a knot?

If you don't want to tie a knot, you can get the dress hemmed or wear it with heels to add height.

Can I wear a maxi dress to a formal event?

Yes, you can wear a maxi dress to a formal event as long as you choose the right fabric, color, and style. Opt for a dress that is made from a luxurious fabric, such as silk or chiffon, and pair it with elegant accessories.

Hair Care

When wearing a maxi dress that's too long, it's important to pay attention to your hair as well. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements the dress, such as a loose bun or beachy waves.
  • Avoid wearing your hair down if it's windy outside to prevent it from getting tangled in the dress.
  • Use a hair tie or bobby pins to keep your hair in place and prevent it from falling in your face.
  • Consider wearing a hat or a headband to add some flair to your outfit.

Now that you know how to tie a maxi dress that's too long, you can confidently wear your favorite dress without worrying about tripping or damaging it. Remember to experiment with different knots and styles to find the one that suits you best. Happy styling!
