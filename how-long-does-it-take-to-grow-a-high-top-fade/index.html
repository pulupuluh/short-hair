How Long Does It Take To Grow A High Top Fade

Are you looking to grow a high top fade but unsure about how long it will take? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss how long it typically takes to grow a high top fade, as well as provide tips and hair care advice to help you achieve your desired look.

How to Grow a High Top Fade

The high top fade is a popular hairstyle that features a high top with short or faded sides. To grow a high top fade, you will need to have enough hair length on top to create the signature style. This typically takes around 6-8 months, depending on how fast your hair grows.

During this time, it's important to avoid cutting the top of your hair and only trim the sides and back as necessary to maintain the shape of the fade. You should also avoid using heat tools or harsh chemicals on your hair, as this can cause damage and slow down the growth process.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to growing a high top fade:

  1. Start with a fresh haircut to create an even base.
  2. Avoid cutting the top of your hair and only trim the sides and back as necessary.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.
  4. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  5. Avoid using heat tools or harsh chemicals on your hair.
  6. As your hair grows, use a hair tie or headband to keep the top section of your hair in place.
  7. Once your hair has reached the desired length, visit a professional stylist to shape your high top fade.
  8. Maintain your high top fade by trimming the sides and back regularly and using hair products to keep your hair looking healthy.

Tips for Growing a High Top Fade

Here are some tips to help you grow a high top fade:

  • Be patient. It takes time to grow out your hair, so don't get discouraged if it takes longer than expected.
  • Avoid using heat tools or harsh chemicals on your hair, as this can cause damage and slow down the growth process.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.
  • Use hair products, such as leave-in conditioner, hair oil, and hair masks, to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Visit a professional stylist to shape your high top fade once your hair has reached the desired length.

FAQs

How long does it take to grow a high top fade?

It typically takes around 6-8 months to grow a high top fade, depending on how fast your hair grows.

What if my hair is not growing as fast?

If your hair is not growing as fast as you would like, try eating a healthy diet, drinking plenty of water, and using hair products to promote hair growth.

Can I use heat tools on my hair?

Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and slow down the growth process. Instead, opt for air-drying your hair or using heat-free styling methods.

Hair Care for a High Top Fade

To keep your high top fade looking its best, it's important to follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools or harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Trim the sides and back of your hair regularly to maintain the shape of the fade.
  • Visit a professional stylist every 4-6 weeks to shape your high top fade.

In conclusion, growing a high top fade takes time and patience. It typically takes around 6-8 months to grow enough hair length to create the signature style. Follow a hair care routine, avoid using heat tools or harsh chemicals on your hair, and be patient. With these tips and advice, you can achieve the high top fade you've always wanted.
