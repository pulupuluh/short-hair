What Hairstyle Makes Your Face Look Smaller

Are you tired of feeling like your face looks too big? Do you wish you could find a hairstyle that makes your features look more balanced? If so, you're in luck! In this article, we'll be discussing what hairstyle makes your face look smaller, along with tips on how to achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How To Make Your Face Look Smaller With Hairstyles

How To Make Your Face Look Smaller With Hairstyles

The key to finding a hairstyle that makes your face look smaller is to focus on creating balance. You'll want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head or make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for styles that elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks and jawline.

Hairstyles That Make Your Face Look Smaller

There are several hairstyles that can make your face look smaller, including:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob with face-framing layers
  • Pixie cut with long side bangs
  • Low ponytail with side part

These hairstyles work by creating vertical lines that elongate your face and draw attention upward. They also add volume to the crown of your head, which helps balance out a round face shape.

Step By Step Guide To Achieving The Perfect Look

Now that you know what hairstyles work best for making your face look smaller, it's time to learn how to achieve the perfect look. Follow these steps for salon-worthy hair:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which hairstyle will work best for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Show your stylist pictures of the hairstyle you want to achieve.
  3. Ask your stylist to add long layers or face-framing layers to your haircut.
  4. If you're getting bangs, ask for side-swept bangs that are longer on one side.
  5. If you're styling your hair at home, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the crown of your head.
  6. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to add volume.
  7. If you have curly hair, use a diffuser to enhance your curls without adding volume to your roots.
  8. Finish off your look with a texturizing spray or serum to add definition and shine.

Tips For Maintaining Your New Hairstyle

Once you've achieved the perfect hairstyle, you'll want to maintain it to keep your face looking smaller. Here are some tips to help you do just that:

  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh.
  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your hairstyle between washes.
  • Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make your face appear wider.
  • Experiment with different partings to create the illusion of a smaller face.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Face Shape Is Best Suited For Long Layers?

Long layers work well on most face shapes, but they're particularly flattering on oval and heart-shaped faces. If you have a round face, try adding layers that start at your cheekbones to create the illusion of a longer face.

Can Bangs Make Your Face Look Smaller?

Yes, bangs can make your face look smaller if you choose the right style. Side-swept bangs that are longer on one side are particularly flattering for round faces, while blunt bangs can add width to your forehead and make your face appear wider.

Hair Care Tips For Healthy Locks

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy locks:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent heat damage.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush to prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
