What Faces Suit Curtain Bangs A Guide For The Perfect Look

Written by Eveline Jul 22, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you thinking of getting curtain bangs but not sure if it will suit your face shape? Don't worry; we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss what faces suit curtain bangs and how to achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Style Curtain Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Description

Curtain bangs are a trendy hairstyle that has been around for decades. It's a versatile style that suits different face shapes, hair types, and lengths. Curtain bangs are long, parted in the middle, and frame your face beautifully. They give a youthful and carefree vibe to your overall look.

However, not all face shapes can pull off curtain bangs. You need to consider your face shape, hair type, and length before getting this hairstyle. In the next section, we will discuss what faces suit curtain bangs.

How to Style Curtain Bangs

Before we dive into what faces suit curtain bangs, let's talk about how to style them. Curtain bangs require minimal effort and can be styled in different ways. Here are some styling options:

  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and a soft wave.
  • Straighten your bangs for a sleek and modern look.
  • Use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  • Add texturizing spray to create a messy and effortless look.

Now that you know how to style your curtain bangs, let's discuss what faces suit curtain bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on what faces suit curtain bangs:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look at your face in the mirror.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

The length of your curtain bangs depends on your face shape. Here are some guidelines:

  • Oval face shape: Any length works well.
  • Round face shape: Go for longer bangs that reach your cheekbones.
  • Square face shape: Opt for longer and wispy bangs that soften your jawline.
  • Heart face shape: Choose shorter, choppy bangs that frame your face.
  • Diamond face shape: Go for longer and side-swept bangs that balance your face shape.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Type

Another factor to consider is your hair type. Curtain bangs work well with straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, if you have thin or fine hair, opt for shorter and choppy bangs that create volume.

Step 4: Consult with Your Hair Stylist

The best way to determine what faces suit curtain bangs is to consult with your hair stylist. They can recommend the best length and style that suits your face shape, hair type, and length.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

Now that you have curtain bangs, here are some tips to maintain them:

  • Trim your bangs every six to eight weeks to maintain the length and shape.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your bangs from getting greasy.
  • Blow-dry your bangs in the opposite direction to create volume.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much to prevent them from getting oily.

FAQs

Q: Can I get curtain bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, curly hair works well with curtain bangs. However, make sure to consult with your hair stylist to determine the best length and style that suits your hair type.

Q: Can I style curtain bangs with a middle part?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can be styled with a middle part. However, it's best to style them with a side part to frame your face better.

Q: Can I wear my curtain bangs up?

A: Yes, you can wear your curtain bangs up. However, make sure to use bobby pins to keep them in place.

Hair Care Tips for Curtain Bangs

Here are some hair care tips for curtain bangs:

  • Wash your hair twice a week to prevent your bangs from getting oily.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs as it can damage them.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain the length and shape.

Now that you know what faces suit curtain bangs, it's time to book an appointment with your hair stylist and get this trendy hairstyle. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and length before getting curtain bangs to achieve the perfect look. Happy styling!
