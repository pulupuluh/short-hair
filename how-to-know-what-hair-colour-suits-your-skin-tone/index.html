How To Know What Hair Colour Suits Your Skin Tone

Written by Petter Mar 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking to change your hair color but not sure which shade to go for? Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, but it can make a huge difference in your overall look. Your skin tone plays a crucial role in determining which hair color will suit you the best. In this article, we will show you how to know what hair color suits your skin tone.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. There are three main categories of skin tone: cool, warm, and neutral.

If your skin has pink or blue undertones and burns easily under the sun, you have a cool skin tone. If your skin has yellow or golden undertones and tans easily under the sun, you have a warm skin tone. If you have a mix of both cool and warm undertones, you have a neutral skin tone.

You can also determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If your veins appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell whether your veins appear blue or green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Color

Now that you have determined your skin tone, it's time to choose the right hair color. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

Step 1: Determine Your Natural Hair Color

Take a look at your natural hair color to determine whether your skin tone is warm or cool. If your hair is black, dark brown, or deep chestnut brown, you have a cool skin tone. If your hair is light brown, golden blond, or strawberry blond, you have a warm skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also help you determine which hair color suits you best. If you have blue or green eyes, shades of blond or light brown will suit you best. If you have brown or hazel eyes, darker hues of brown or black will complement your skin tone.

Step 3: Look at Your Skin Undertones

Take a closer look at your skin undertones to determine the right hair color. If you have a cool skin tone, shades of ash blond, platinum blond, or burgundy will complement your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, shades of golden blond, honey blond, or chestnut brown will suit you best.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair color:

  • Consider the season. Warmer tones like caramel and chestnut brown are great for fall and winter, while cooler tones like platinum blond and ash brown are perfect for spring and summer.
  • Go for a gradual change. If you're not sure about a drastic change, start with highlights or lowlights to see how you like the new color.
  • Consider your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, choose a low-maintenance hair color that doesn't require frequent touch-ups.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, but make sure to choose a hair dye that is gentle on sensitive skin. Look for products that are free from ammonia, peroxide, and other harsh chemicals.

Q: How do I maintain my hair color?

A: Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair color. Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip the color. Also, protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, but make sure to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test first to avoid any allergic reactions.

Hair Care Tips After Coloring Your Hair

Here are some hair care tips to follow after coloring your hair:

  • Wait at least 48 hours before washing your hair after coloring it.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as these can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Choosing the right hair color can be a game-changer for your overall look. By following these tips and guidelines, you can find the perfect hair color that complements your skin tone and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
