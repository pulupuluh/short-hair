What Hair Colour Do Most Guys Prefer

Written by Christine Apr 21, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hair colour but not sure which one will catch the attention of the guys? Look no further as we delve into what hair colour do most guys prefer in this year 2023.

Description

Hair colour can make or break your overall appearance, and it's no secret that guys pay attention to it. While some may have a particular preference, there are a few popular colours that most guys tend to gravitate towards. In this blog post, we will explore those hair colours and why guys prefer them.

How to

If you're considering changing your hair colour, there are a few things to consider before making the jump. Firstly, determine your skin tone to choose a colour that compliments your complexion. Next, research the upkeep required for your chosen colour to ensure you're willing to commit to the maintenance. Finally, schedule an appointment with a professional hairstylist to ensure you achieve the desired look.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your skin tone

Step 2: Research upkeep for your chosen colour

Step 3: Schedule an appointment with a professional hairstylist

Tips

1. Consider your natural hair colour when choosing a new shade.

2. Keep in mind that some colours may require more maintenance than others.

3. Consult with a professional hairstylist for their expert opinion.

FAQ

What hair colour do most guys prefer?

Most guys tend to prefer natural-looking hair colours such as brunette and blonde. However, some may be attracted to bold and vibrant colours such as red or purple.

What hair colour looks best on me?

Your hair colour should complement your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, consider warm hair colours such as honey blonde or chestnut brown. For cool undertones, opt for cool colours such as ash blonde or black.

How often should I touch up my hair colour?

The frequency of touch-ups depends on the colour and individual hair growth rate. Generally, it's recommended to touch up your hair colour every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After making the switch to a new hair colour, it's essential to adjust your hair care routine to maintain the vibrancy and health of your locks. Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading and nourish your hair. Avoid washing your hair too often and use heat protectant products before styling with hot tools. Lastly, schedule regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain a polished look.

Conclusion

In conclusion, while there's no one-size-fits-all answer to what hair colour do most guys prefer, there are a few colours that tend to catch their attention. Remember to consider your skin tone, research upkeep, and consult with a professional hairstylist before making the switch. Lastly, maintain your new hair colour with a proper hair care routine to keep it looking vibrant and healthy.
