Good Short Hairstyles For Oval Faces

Written by Robby May 27, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you’re lucky because it’s the most versatile face shape. You can pull off almost any hairstyle, but short hairstyles are a great way to show off your face shape. In this post, we’ll share the best short hairstyles for oval faces, how to style them, and tips to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hairstyles are perfect for oval faces because they highlight your best features. You can choose from a variety of styles, from pixie cuts to bobs. The key is to find a style that complements your face shape and brings out your best features.

How to

Before choosing a short hairstyle, you need to consider your hair type, texture, and personal style. You should also look for inspiration from magazines, social media, and celebrities with similar face shapes. Once you’ve found a style you like, bring a photo to your stylist and discuss how to adapt it to your face shape and hair type.

Step by step

Here are some of the best short hairstyles for oval faces:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well with oval faces. It’s a low-maintenance style that’s perfect for busy women. To style, apply a volumizing mousse and blow dry your hair with a round brush. Use a small amount of pomade to define your layers and add texture.

Bob

A bob is a versatile short hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. For oval faces, a chin-length bob with layers and side-swept bangs is a great option. To style, blow dry your hair with a round brush and use a flat iron to add volume and texture.

Layered Lob

A layered lob is a longer version of the bob that falls just above the shoulders. It’s a great option for oval faces because it adds volume and texture to your hair. To style, apply a texturizing spray and blow dry your hair with a round brush. Use a curling iron to add loose waves.

Tips

When choosing a short hairstyle for your oval face, consider your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, choose a style with layers and texture to add volume. If you have thick hair, choose a style that flatters your face shape and reduces bulk. You should also consider your personal style and how much time you’re willing to spend styling your hair.

FAQ

Can oval faces pull off any short hairstyle?

Yes, oval faces can pull off almost any short hairstyle. The key is to choose a style that complements your face shape and brings out your best features.

What’s the best way to style short hair?

The best way to style short hair is to use a combination of products and tools. Start by applying a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to damp hair. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and texture. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add definition and shape.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair looking its best, you should wash it every other day with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools and avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair. You should also schedule regular haircuts to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.
