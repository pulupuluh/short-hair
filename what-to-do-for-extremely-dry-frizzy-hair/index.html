What To Do For Extremely Dry Frizzy Hair

Written by Bardi Jul 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Having dry and frizzy hair can be frustrating and can affect your overall appearance. It can be caused by various factors such as genetics, lack of moisture, overuse of hair products, and excessive heat styling. If you're experiencing extremely dry and frizzy hair, don't worry! There are numerous ways to bring back the moisture and shine to your hair. In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks on what to do for extremely dry frizzy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in treating dry and frizzy hair is to identify the cause. Once you have identified the cause, you can take the necessary steps to treat it. Here are some general tips on what to do for extremely dry frizzy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week to provide your hair with extra moisture.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. If you need to use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on what to do for extremely dry frizzy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair and leave it on for the recommended time.
  3. Rinse your hair with cool water.
  4. Gently towel dry your hair, avoiding rubbing or pulling on it.
  5. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to provide extra moisture.
  6. Avoid using heat styling tools. If you need to use them, use a heat protectant spray.
  7. Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.
  8. Apply a hair serum or oil to the ends of your hair to provide extra moisture and shine.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on what to do for extremely dry frizzy hair:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid damaging your hair while you sleep.
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal clasps as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet as it can cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what to do for extremely dry frizzy hair:

Can I use natural remedies to treat dry and frizzy hair?

Yes, you can use natural remedies such as coconut oil, avocado, and honey to provide extra moisture to your hair. However, it's important to note that not all natural remedies work for everyone, so it's best to do a patch test first before applying anything to your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces. If you have dry hair, you can wash your hair once or twice a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Can I use a hair mask instead of a deep conditioning treatment?

Yes, you can use a hair mask instead of a deep conditioning treatment. Hair masks usually provide extra moisture and nourishment to your hair, similar to deep conditioning treatments.

Hair Care

Aside from the tips and tricks mentioned above, it's important to take care of your hair on a regular basis to prevent it from becoming dry and frizzy. Here are some general hair care tips:

  • Drink plenty of water to keep your body and hair hydrated.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote hair growth and health.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and tricks on what to do for extremely dry frizzy hair, you can bring back the moisture and shine to your hair and improve its overall health and appearance.
