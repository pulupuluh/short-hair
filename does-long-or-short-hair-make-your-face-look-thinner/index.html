Does Long Or Short Hair Make Your Face Look Thinner

Written by Robby Jul 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to slim down your face, changing your hairstyle can be an easy and effective way to do it. But does long or short hair make your face look thinner? In this article, we'll explore the answer to that question and provide tips on how to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape.

Description

Having a slim and toned face is a beauty standard that many people aspire to achieve. While there are many ways to achieve this, changing your hairstyle is one of the easiest and most cost-effective options. Whether you choose long or short hair, the right haircut can help accentuate your best features and slim down your face.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

Before deciding on a hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. Here are the common face shapes and the best hairstyles to complement them:

Round Face

If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that creates angles and elongates your face. Long, layered hairstyles that frame the face and add volume at the crown are perfect for this face shape.

Square Face

If you have a square face, you'll want to soften your strong jawline with a hairstyle that has layers and volume around the crown. Long, wavy hairstyles or short, layered bobs are great options.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that balances your features. Long hairstyles with layers and side-swept bangs are perfect for this face shape.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you're lucky! This face shape can pull off almost any hairstyle. Long, straight hair or a short, layered bob are great options.

Step by Step: How to Style Your Hair to Look Thinner

Now that you know which hairstyles work best for your face shape, let's go over some styling tips to help you achieve a thinner-looking face:

1. Add Volume

Adding volume to your hair can help balance out your face and make it appear slimmer. Use a volumizing mousse or spray on your roots and blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume.

2. Side Part

A side part can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. Part your hair on the side and sweep it across your forehead to create a flattering angle.

3. Layered Haircuts

Layered haircuts can help create the illusion of a slimmer face by adding texture and movement. Ask your hairstylist for long layers or short, choppy layers depending on your face shape.

4. Highlights

Adding highlights to your hair can draw attention away from your face and create a slimming effect. Ask your hairstylist for highlights that frame your face and add dimension.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Whether you choose long or short hair, it's important to maintain healthy hair to achieve your desired look. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

1. Regular Trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

2. Use Heat Protectant

Using a heat protectant spray before styling your hair can help prevent damage from heat styling tools.

3. Deep Condition

Deep conditioning treatments can help nourish and strengthen your hair. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

4. Avoid Overstyling

Overstyling your hair with heat tools and chemicals can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools and opt for natural hairstyles instead.

FAQs

1. Does long hair make your face look thinner?

Long hair can make your face look thinner if styled correctly. Long, layered hairstyles that add volume at the crown can create the illusion of a slimmer face.

2. Does short hair make your face look thinner?

Short hair can make your face look thinner if styled correctly. Short, layered bobs that add volume at the crown can create the illusion of a slimmer face.

3. How can I make my face look thinner with my hair?

You can make your face look thinner by choosing a hairstyle that complements your face shape and adding volume to your hair. A side part, layered haircut, and highlights can also help create a slimming effect.

Hair Care

When it comes to achieving a thinner-looking face with your hair, the key is to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and adds volume. Whether you choose long or short hair, the right haircut and styling techniques can help you achieve your desired look. And don't forget to take care of your hair with regular trims, heat protectant, and deep conditioning treatments to keep it healthy and shiny!
