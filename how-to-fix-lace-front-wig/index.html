How To Fix Lace Front Wig A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 10, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you struggling to fix your lace front wig? Do you want to know how to do it the right way? Well, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll teach you how to fix your lace front wig step by step. We’ll also give you some tips on how to take care of your wig and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Fix Lace Front Wig
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Fixing Lace Front Wig
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Lace Front Wig

How to Fix Lace Front Wig

Fixing a lace front wig can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Here are some things you will need:

  • A wig stand or mannequin head
  • A comb or brush
  • A pair of scissors
  • A wig cap
  • A wig adhesive or tape

Now that you have all the tools you need, let’s get started.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to fix your lace front wig:

  1. Place your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to make it easier to work on.
  2. Put on a wig cap to keep your hair in place and to protect your scalp.
  3. Use a comb or brush to detangle the hair on your wig.
  4. If your wig has any loose or frayed strands, trim them with a pair of scissors.
  5. Apply wig adhesive or tape to the hairline of your wig. Be careful not to put too much as it can cause irritation to your scalp.
  6. Align the wig with your natural hairline and press it onto your scalp. Use a comb or brush to smooth out any bumps or lumps.
  7. Adjust the wig to fit your head properly. Use the adjustable straps or clips to secure it in place.
  8. Style your wig as desired.

Voila! Your lace front wig is fixed.

Tips for Fixing Lace Front Wig

Here are some tips to make fixing your lace front wig easier:

  • Always use a wig cap to protect your scalp and keep your hair in place.
  • Try different wig adhesives or tapes to see which one works best for you.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your wig to prevent damage to the hair fibers.
  • Trim any loose or frayed strands with a pair of scissors to make your wig look neater.
  • When styling your wig, use heat protectant sprays or serums to prevent damage to the hair fibers.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about fixing lace front wigs:

Q: Can I fix my lace front wig without using any adhesive or tape?

A: It’s not recommended as the wig may not stay in place properly.

Q: How long will my lace front wig stay in place?

A: It depends on the type of adhesive or tape you use and how often you wear your wig. Generally, it can stay in place for up to a week.

Q: Can I sleep with my lace front wig on?

A: It’s not recommended as it can damage the hair fibers and cause tangling.

Hair Care for Lace Front Wig

Here are some tips on how to take care of your lace front wig:

  • Wash your wig every 7-10 wears with a wig shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can damage the hair fibers.
  • Use a wig brush or comb to detangle your wig.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as it can damage the hair fibers.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape.

Following these tips will help to keep your lace front wig looking great for longer.

We hope this article has been helpful in teaching you how to fix your lace front wig. Remember to take care of your wig properly to keep it looking great for longer.
