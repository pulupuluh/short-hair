Does Short Hair Make Guys Look Older

Written by Petter Apr 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair is a popular hairstyle for men, but some may wonder if it makes them look older. In this article, we'll explore the question, "Does short hair make guys look older?" and provide some tips for men who want to maintain a youthful appearance.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Many people believe that short hair makes men look older because it is often associated with a more mature look. However, this is not always the case. Short hair can actually make men look younger if styled correctly.

The perception of whether short hair makes guys look older also depends on factors such as face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Some men may look better with short hair, while others may look better with longer hair.

How to

If you're considering getting a short haircut but are worried about looking older, here are some tips to keep in mind:

Consider Your Face Shape

Short hair can be very flattering for certain face shapes. For example, men with square or oval faces often look great with short haircuts. However, men with round faces may want to avoid very short haircuts, as they can make the face look even rounder.

Choose the Right Length

The length of your hair can also affect how old you look. Generally, shorter haircuts tend to look more professional and mature, while longer hair can give a more laid-back, youthful vibe. Consider how you want to be perceived and choose a length that aligns with that.

Experiment with Styling

The way you style your hair can also affect how old you look. For example, slicking your hair back can give a more mature look, while messy, textured styles can give a more youthful vibe.

Step by Step

If you're unsure how to style your short hair for a more youthful appearance, here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any product buildup and leave your hair looking clean and healthy.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair while it's still damp. Use a product that's appropriate for your hair type and desired style.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using your fingers or a comb, depending on the look you're going for. If you want a more textured look, use your fingers to create a messy, tousled style. If you want a more polished look, use a comb to slick your hair back or to the side.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your style with a light mist of hairspray to hold it in place throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you look youthful with short hair:

  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and texture.
  • Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture.
  • Accessorize with hats or sunglasses to add a trendy, youthful touch to your look.

FAQ

Does short hair make guys look older?

Not necessarily. Short hair can be very flattering for men and can actually make them look younger if styled correctly.

What face shape looks best with short hair?

Square and oval faces tend to look great with short haircuts. Men with round faces may want to avoid very short haircuts, as they can make the face look even rounder.

What length of hair makes guys look younger?

Generally, longer hair can give a more laid-back, youthful vibe. However, the length of your hair ultimately depends on your personal style and how you want to be perceived.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and healthy.

Overall, short hair can be a great choice for men of all ages. By choosing the right haircut and styling it correctly, you can maintain a youthful appearance and look your best.
