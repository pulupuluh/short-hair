How To Make Your Hair Curly Boys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline May 20, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to try something new? Having curly hair can completely transform your look and give you a fresh new style. In this article, we’ll show you how to make your hair curly boys with a step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Curly Boys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Your Hair Curly Boys

There are a few different ways to make your hair curly, so it’s important to choose the method that works best for your hair type and texture. Here are some of the most popular ways to make your hair curly:

  • Using a curling iron
  • Using hot rollers
  • Using flexi rods
  • Using a perm
  • Using a salt spray

Each of these methods will give you a different type of curl, so it’s important to choose the one that will give you the look you want. For example, a curling iron will give you tight, defined curls, while flexi rods will give you looser, bouncier curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair curly using a curling iron:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up.
  3. Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, holding it for 10-15 seconds.
  4. Release the curl and repeat on the rest of your hair.
  5. Once all of your hair is curled, use a hairspray to set the curls in place.

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair curly using flexi rods:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a styling product, like a mousse or curl cream.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and wrap each section around a flexi rod.
  3. Leave the rods in your hair overnight or for several hours.
  4. Remove the rods and use your fingers to separate the curls.
  5. Use a hairspray to set the curls in place.

Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your curls.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Q: How long do curls last?

A: The length of time that curls last depends on the method used and your hair type. Generally, curls will last for a day or two, but can be refreshed with a curling iron or hairspray.

Q: Can all hair types be curly?

A: Yes, all hair types can be curly with the right styling methods.

Q: Will curly hair require more maintenance than straight hair?

A: Yes, curly hair typically requires more maintenance than straight hair, as it tends to be drier and more prone to frizz.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your curls.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define and hydrate your curls.
  • Visit a professional stylist for regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily make your hair curly boys and achieve a stylish new look. Whether you opt for a perm or use hot tools to create curls, there’s a method that will work for you.
