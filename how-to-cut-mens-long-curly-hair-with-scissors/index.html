How To Cut Men s Long Curly Hair With Scissors

Written by William Aug 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking to change up your long curly hair but don't want to go to a professional? Cutting long curly hair can be tricky, but with the right tools and technique, you can do it at home. In this article, we'll go over step-by-step instructions on how to cut men's long curly hair with scissors.

Description

Long curly hair can be difficult to manage, but a good haircut can make all the difference. With the right technique, you can achieve a stylish and manageable cut that suits your face shape and personal style. Cutting long curly hair with scissors is a great way to achieve a natural and organic look, without the harsh lines of clippers or razors.

How to Cut Men's Long Curly Hair with Scissors

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting men's long curly hair with scissors is to gather your tools. You will need a good pair of scissors, preferably with serrated blades that can grip the hair. You will also need a comb, hair clips, and a spray bottle of water.

Step 2: Wet the Hair

Before you start cutting, wet the hair with the spray bottle of water. This will make the hair more manageable and easier to work with.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Use the comb to section the hair into manageable sections. Clip each section of hair out of the way, leaving only the section you are working on.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting the hair, using the scissors to snip small sections at a time. Work from the bottom up, cutting the hair to the desired length.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Once you have cut the hair to the desired length, use the scissors to blend the layers. This will create a more natural and seamless look.

Step 6: Check for Balance

Check the balance of the cut, making sure that both sides are even. Make any necessary adjustments as needed.

Step 7: Finish the Cut

Once you are satisfied with the cut, remove the clips and comb through the hair. Make any final adjustments as needed, and then style the hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting men's long curly hair with scissors:

  • Start with small snips, and work your way up to larger ones.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and create tension before cutting.
  • Take breaks as needed to avoid fatigue and ensure accuracy.
  • Be patient and take your time, especially if you are new to cutting hair.

FAQ

Q: What type of scissors should I use?

A: Use a pair of scissors with serrated blades that can grip the hair. This will make it easier to cut through the hair without causing damage.

Q: How often should I cut my long curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Generally, it's a good idea to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and well-maintained.

Hair Care

Once you have cut your long curly hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips for caring for long curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, which can damage the hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or detangling brush to gently comb through wet hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine.

By following these tips, you can keep your long curly hair looking great between haircuts.
