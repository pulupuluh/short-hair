How To Style Thin Straight Shoulder Length Hair

Are you tired of your thin straight shoulder length hair falling flat and lifeless? Don't worry, with the right styling techniques, you can achieve a voluminous and chic look. In this article, we will guide you through step by step on how to style thin straight shoulder length hair.

Table of Contents

How to Style Thin Straight Shoulder Length Hair

Before we dive into the styling process, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You will need a hairbrush, a hairdryer, a heat protectant spray, a volumizing mousse, and a hairspray.

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent any heat damage from the hairdryer. Use your fingers to apply a volumizing mousse through your hair, focusing on the roots.

Next, use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots to create volume. Once your hair is dry, use a straightener to straighten your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. This will create a sleek and polished look.

Step by Step Guide

1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.

3. Apply a volumizing mousse through your hair, focusing on the roots.

4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the roots.

5. Use a straightener to straighten your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.

6. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks

- Use a volumizing dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

- Invest in a good quality straightener to prevent heat damage.

- Don't overuse hairspray, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

- Experiment with different partings to create a different look.

FAQs

Q: Can I curl my hair after straightening it?

A: Yes, you can use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls.


Q: Can I use a hair serum instead of a heat protectant spray?

A: No, a hair serum does not offer the same level of protection from heat damage as a heat protectant spray.

Hair Care for Thin Straight Shoulder Length Hair

Thin straight shoulder length hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy and voluminous. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to create volume at the roots.

- Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can cause heat damage and breakage.

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

By following these styling tips and hair care tips, you can achieve a voluminous and chic look with your thin straight shoulder length hair.
