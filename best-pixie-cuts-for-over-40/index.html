Best Pixie Cuts For Over 40 A Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 07, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're over 40 and looking for a fresh, low-maintenance hairstyle, a pixie cut might just be the perfect option for you. Not only is this style chic and trendy, but it's also a great way to show off your features and highlight your natural beauty. Here are some of the best pixie cuts for women over 40.

How to Choose the Best Pixie Cut for You

Before you head to the salon, it's important to consider a few factors when choosing the best pixie cut for you:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair texture
  • Your personal style
  • Your lifestyle

By taking these factors into account, you can work with your stylist to find the perfect pixie cut that suits your unique features and complements your lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Pixie Cut

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a pixie cut:

  1. Consult with your stylist to determine the best pixie cut for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Wash your hair and make sure it's clean and dry before your appointment.
  3. Bring in pictures of the pixie cut you're interested in so your stylist can see exactly what you want.
  4. Sit back and relax as your stylist cuts your hair into the perfect pixie cut.
  5. Add some styling product to give your new cut some shape and texture.

With just a few simple steps, you can have a fresh, trendy pixie cut that's perfect for women over 40.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Once you've got your new pixie cut, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips for maintaining your pixie cut:

  • Visit the salon every 4-6 weeks to keep your cut looking sharp.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Add some styling product to give your hair some texture and volume.
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your cut.

By following these tips, you can keep your pixie cut looking chic and stylish for months to come.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Pixie Cuts for Women Over 40

Q: Will a pixie cut make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. While some hairstyles can add years to your appearance, a well-executed pixie cut can actually make you look younger and more vibrant.

Q: Can I still style my pixie cut in different ways?

A: Yes! While a pixie cut is typically low-maintenance, there are still plenty of ways to style it to suit your mood or occasion. Try adding some texture with a styling product, or sweep your bangs to the side for a different look.

Q: Will a pixie cut work for all hair types?

A: While a pixie cut can work for many different hair types, it's important to consult with your stylist to determine the best cut for your specific hair texture and density.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

When it comes to hair care for pixie cuts, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Invest in a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your pixie cut looking fresh and sharp.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your pixie cut looking chic and stylish for weeks to come.

Conclusion

A pixie cut can be a great option for women over 40 who want a low-maintenance, chic hairstyle that highlights their natural beauty. By considering your face shape, hair texture, personal style, and lifestyle, you can work with your stylist to find the perfect pixie cut for you. With just a few simple maintenance tips and some high-quality hair care products, you can keep your pixie cut looking fresh and stylish for months to come.
