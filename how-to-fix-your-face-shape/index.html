How To Fix Your Face Shape Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jun 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to enhance your beauty and improve your facial features, then fixing your face shape might be the solution you've been looking for. In this article, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to fix your face shape in natural and effortless ways. So, let's get started!

Description: What is Face Shape?

Face shape refers to the shape and structure of your face, including your jawline, cheekbones, and forehead. There are six main types of face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and long. Knowing your face shape is crucial when it comes to choosing the right hairstyles, makeup, and accessories that complement your features.

How to Fix Your Face Shape

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to fixing your face shape is to determine what type of face shape you have. Use a measuring tape or ruler to measure the length and width of your face, and compare it to the six main types of face shapes. You can also use an online face shape calculator to help you determine your face shape.

Step 2: Choose the Right Hairstyles

Choosing the right hairstyles is essential when it comes to fixing your face shape. For example, if you have a round face shape, opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of your head, such as a high ponytail or updo. If you have a square face shape, go for soft and wavy hairstyles that frame your face, such as beachy waves or a layered bob.

Step 3: Use Makeup to Enhance Your Features

Makeup can help enhance your facial features and create the illusion of a more defined face shape. For example, contouring can help create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones. Use a contouring kit and a bronzer to create shadows on the areas you want to slim down, such as the sides of your nose and the hollows of your cheeks.

Step 4: Accessorize to Complement Your Features

Accessories can also help complement your facial features and fix your face shape. For example, if you have a heart-shaped face, opt for earrings that are wider at the bottom, such as chandelier earrings. If you have a square face shape, go for rounded or oval-shaped earrings that soften your features.

Tips: Dos and Don'ts

Here are some dos and don'ts to keep in mind when fixing your face shape:

  • Do choose hairstyles that complement your face shape.
  • Do use makeup to enhance your features.
  • Do accessorize to complement your features.
  • Don't choose hairstyles that make your face look wider or rounder.
  • Don't use too much makeup, as it can look unnatural.
  • Don't wear accessories that clash with your face shape.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I fix my face shape without surgery?

A: Yes, you can fix your face shape without surgery. By choosing the right hairstyles, makeup, and accessories, you can enhance your features and create the illusion of a more defined face shape.

Q: How do I determine my face shape?

A: You can determine your face shape by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing it to the six main types of face shapes. You can also use an online face shape calculator to help you determine your face shape.

Q: Can I wear any type of earrings?

A: No, you should choose earrings that complement your face shape. For example, if you have a heart-shaped face, opt for earrings that are wider at the bottom, such as chandelier earrings. If you have a square face shape, go for rounded or oval-shaped earrings that soften your features.

Hair Care: Keep Your Hair Healthy and Beautiful

Lastly, don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Also, consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Fixing your face shape is all about enhancing your natural beauty and creating a more defined look. By following these tips and tricks, you can fix your face shape in natural and effortless ways. Remember to choose hairstyles, makeup, and accessories that complement your features, and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
