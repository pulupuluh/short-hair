How To Relax Curly Hair Into Waves A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be beautiful, but sometimes you just want to switch things up and go for a more relaxed, wavy look. Luckily, it's possible to relax curly hair into waves with a few simple steps. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of how to relax curly hair into waves, including tips and tricks for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Relax Curly Hair into Waves
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Waves
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Relaxed Hair

How to Relax Curly Hair into Waves

Before we dive into the step by step process, it's important to note that relaxing curly hair into waves is not the same as straightening it. While straightening involves using heat to completely remove the curl pattern, relaxing simply loosens the curls to create a more wavy look.

The process of relaxing curly hair into waves can be done with a chemical relaxer or with heat. However, using heat can be damaging to your hair, so we recommend using a chemical relaxer for the best results.

Step by Step Guide

Here's how to relax curly hair into waves:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. Then, deep condition your hair to moisturize and protect it from the chemicals in the relaxer.

Step 2: Apply the Relaxer

Section your hair into four parts and apply the relaxer to each section, making sure to avoid your scalp. Leave the relaxer on for the recommended amount of time, which will vary depending on the strength of the relaxer and your hair type.

Step 3: Rinse and Neutralize

After the recommended time has passed, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water to remove the relaxer. Then, apply a neutralizing shampoo to stop the chemical process and restore your hair's natural pH balance.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now that your hair is relaxed, it's time to style it into waves. Start by applying a styling cream or mousse to your hair and scrunching it gently to create waves. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your waves and add volume.

Tips for Maintaining Your Waves

Here are some tips for maintaining your newly relaxed waves:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent damage.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent further damage to your hair.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Refresh your waves in the morning with a spray bottle of water and a little bit of styling cream.

FAQs

Can I relax my hair into waves without chemicals?

While it is possible to use heat to relax your hair into waves, it can be damaging to your hair. We recommend using a chemical relaxer for the best results.

How often should I relax my hair?

It's important to give your hair time to recover between relaxer treatments. We recommend waiting at least 6-8 weeks between treatments.

Will relaxing my hair make it straight?

No, relaxing your hair will simply loosen the curls to create a more wavy look. If you want completely straight hair, you'll need to use a straightening treatment.

Hair Care for Relaxed Hair

After relaxing your hair, it's important to take extra care to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for relaxed hair:

  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.

Now that you know how to relax curly hair into waves, you can switch up your hairstyle and enjoy your new look!
