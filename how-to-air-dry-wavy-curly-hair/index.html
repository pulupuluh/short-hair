How To Air Dry Wavy Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of using heat tools to style your wavy curly hair? Air drying is a great alternative that can help maintain your hair's natural texture and prevent damage. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of air drying wavy curly hair and share some tips for achieving the best results. Let's get started!

How to Air Dry Wavy Curly Hair

Air drying wavy curly hair may seem like a simple process, but there are a few things you should keep in mind to achieve the best results. Here's what you need to do:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  2. Remove excess water from your hair by gently squeezing it with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as this can cause frizz.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing cream to your hair. This will help define your curls and prevent frizz.
  4. Part your hair in sections and use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Be gentle to avoid pulling or breaking your hair.
  5. Let your hair air dry naturally. Avoid touching your hair as much as possible to prevent frizz. You can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the process, but make sure to use it on a cool setting.
  6. Once your hair is completely dry, you can scrunch your curls to enhance their shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to air dry wavy curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, causing dryness and frizz.
  2. Gently squeeze your hair with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair, as this can cause frizz.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing cream to your hair. This will help define your curls and prevent frizz.
  4. Part your hair in sections and use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Be gentle to avoid pulling or breaking your hair.
  5. Let your hair air dry naturally. Avoid touching your hair as much as possible to prevent frizz.
  6. If you're in a hurry, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the process. Make sure to use it on a cool setting to prevent heat damage.
  7. Once your hair is completely dry, you can scrunch your curls to enhance their shape. You can also use a lightweight oil or serum to add shine and tame frizz.

Tips for Air Drying Wavy Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips that can help you achieve the best results when air drying wavy curly hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to remove excess water from your hair. Regular towels can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Apply your leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing cream to wet hair to help distribute it evenly.
  • Don't touch your hair while it's drying to prevent frizz. If you need to adjust your hair, use a silk scarf or a satin pillowcase.
  • Try plopping your hair to help define your curls. To do this, wrap your hair in a t-shirt or a microfiber towel and leave it on for 10-20 minutes.
  • Consider using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the process. Make sure to use it on a cool setting to prevent heat damage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about air drying wavy curly hair:

How long does it take to air dry wavy curly hair?

The time it takes to air dry wavy curly hair varies depending on your hair length, thickness, and texture. On average, it can take anywhere from 2 to 4 hours.

Can I use a diffuser attachment on my blow dryer to speed up the process?

Yes, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the process. However, make sure to use it on a cool setting to prevent heat damage.

Can I touch my hair while it's drying?

Avoid touching your hair as much as possible to prevent frizz. If you need to adjust your hair, use a silk scarf or a satin pillowcase.

Hair Care for Wavy Curly Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful wavy curly hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using heat tools.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or relaxers, on your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, healthy, and natural-looking wavy curly hair without the use of heat tools. Give it a try and see the difference for yourself!
