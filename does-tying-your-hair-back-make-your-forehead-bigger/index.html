Does Tying Your Hair Back Make Your Forehead Bigger

Written by Christine Aug 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

For years, people have been asking whether tying their hair back makes their forehead appear bigger. It's a common belief that tying your hair back can cause your forehead to look larger, creating a disproportionate look to your face. In this article, we'll be exploring whether this belief is true or not.

Description

The belief that tying your hair back can make your forehead appear bigger is based on the idea that when you tie your hair back, it pulls your skin back, creating a stretched-out look. This stretched-out look can make your forehead appear bigger than it actually is. However, this belief is not entirely true.

How To

If you're worried about tying your hair back and making your forehead appear larger, there are a few things you can do to prevent this from happening. Firstly, make sure that you tie your hair back loosely. Avoid pulling your hair too tightly, as this can cause your skin to stretch out even further. Additionally, you can try wearing your hair in a different style, such as a side part or a low ponytail.

Step by Step

If you're not sure how to tie your hair back without making your forehead appear larger, follow these steps:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gently gather your hair at the nape of your neck.
  3. Secure your hair with a hair tie, making sure to leave a bit of slack so that your hair isn't pulled too tightly.
  4. If you have any loose strands, tuck them in behind your ears or use bobby pins to secure them in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid wearing your hair in a tight bun, as this can pull your skin back even further.
  • Consider adding some volume to your hair to create a more balanced look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find one that suits you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about whether tying your hair back can make your forehead appear larger:

Does tying your hair back make your forehead bigger permanently?

No, tying your hair back does not make your forehead bigger permanently. Any changes in the appearance of your forehead due to tying your hair back will be temporary.

Why does tying your hair back make your forehead look bigger?

Tying your hair back can make your forehead look bigger because it pulls your skin back, creating a stretched-out look. However, this effect is only temporary, and your forehead will return to its normal size once you let your hair down.

Can wearing a headband make your forehead look bigger?

Wearing a headband can make your forehead look bigger if it's worn too tightly. To avoid this, make sure that your headband fits comfortably and isn't pulling your skin back too much.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and looks its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, tying your hair back does not make your forehead appear permanently larger. However, it can create a temporary stretched-out look. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can tie your hair back without worrying about creating a disproportional look to your face.
