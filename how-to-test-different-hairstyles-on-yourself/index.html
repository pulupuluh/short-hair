How To Test Different Hairstyles On Yourself

Written by Robby Aug 11, 2023 · 7 min read
How to Test Different Hairstyles on Yourself | Step by Step Guide

Are you tired of wearing the same old hairstyle every day? Trying out new hairstyles can be a fun way to switch up your look and boost your confidence. However, it can be difficult to know where to start or how to test different hairstyles on yourself. In this step by step guide, we’ll share some tips and tricks to help you find your next favorite hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to Test Different Hairstyles on Yourself
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Keep in Mind
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step by Step Guide

Before you start testing different hairstyles on yourself, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your hair type: Certain hairstyles work better for different hair types, so make sure to choose styles that will work well with your hair.
  • Gather your tools: You’ll need a few tools to test different hairstyles such as a comb, hair ties, bobby pins and hair spray.
  • Find inspiration: Look for inspiration from magazines, social media or hair styling apps to find new hairstyles you want to try.

Now that you’ve got everything you need to test different hairstyles, let’s get started:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start testing different hairstyles, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will ensure that your hair is easier to style and will hold the hairstyle better.

Step 2: Choose your hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle you want to try. It can be helpful to start with simpler hairstyles such as ponytails, buns or braids before moving on to more complicated styles.

Step 3: Prep your hair

Depending on the hairstyle you choose, you may need to prep your hair by adding some product or using heat tools to curl or straighten your hair.

Step 4: Style your hair

Begin styling your hair, using your tools to create the desired look. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different techniques, such as twisting or backcombing, to achieve the look you want.

Step 5: Evaluate and adjust

Once you’ve styled your hair, take a look in the mirror to evaluate the results. Make any necessary adjustments to the hairstyle to make it perfect.

Step 6: Take pictures

Once you’ve found a hairstyle you love, take pictures from different angles to help you remember how you styled it. This will make it easier to recreate the hairstyle in the future.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when testing different hairstyles on yourself:

  • Don’t be afraid to try something new: Experimenting with new hairstyles can be a fun way to express yourself and boost your confidence.
  • Use hair accessories: Hair accessories such as headbands, clips or scarves can add some extra flair to your hairstyle.
  • Take care of your hair: Make sure to keep your hair healthy by using quality hair care products and getting regular trims.

FAQs

How do I know if a hairstyle will look good on me?

The best way to know if a hairstyle will look good on you is to try it out. Consider your face shape, hair type and personal style when choosing a hairstyle.

What if I mess up my hair?

Don’t worry if you mess up your hair while testing out a new hairstyle. You can always start over or try a different hairstyle.

How often should I try new hairstyles?

There’s no set rule for how often you should try new hairstyles. It can be helpful to switch up your hairstyle every few weeks or months to keep your look fresh and interesting.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Testing different hairstyles on yourself can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look. By following these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find your next favorite hairstyle in no time.
