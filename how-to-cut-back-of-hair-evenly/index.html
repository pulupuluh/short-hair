How To Cut Back Of Hair Evenly A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline May 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially when it comes to the back. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a perfectly even cut without having to go to the salon. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting the back of your hair evenly, step by step. We will also provide some tips and FAQs to help you achieve the best results.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Back of Hair Evenly
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Hair Evenly
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After Cutting

How to Cut Back of Hair Evenly

The first step in cutting your hair evenly is to gather the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a handheld mirror. It is also important to have good lighting and a clear view of the back of your head.

Once you have all the tools, it's time to get started. The key to cutting the back of your hair evenly is to work in small sections and take your time. Here are the basic steps:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle and combing it straight down your back.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the middle of your head and comb it straight out from your head.
  3. Hold the section of hair between your index and middle fingers, and use the scissors to trim the ends.
  4. Repeat steps 2-3, working your way out from the middle to the sides of your head.
  5. Use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make sure the hair is even.
  6. Once you have trimmed all the sections, comb your hair and check for any unevenness. If you find any, simply trim the ends until it is even.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Hair Evenly

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting the back of your hair:

  • Work in small sections to ensure evenness.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Make sure your hair is dry before cutting to avoid any surprises when it dries.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to take small trims until you reach your desired length.
  • Use hair clips to keep the sections of hair separated and organized.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting the back of your hair:

  • Can I cut the back of my hair myself? Yes, you can. With the right tools and techniques, it is possible to achieve an even cut.
  • How often should I cut the back of my hair? It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain your style. On average, people cut their hair every 6-8 weeks.
  • What if I make a mistake? If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim a little more until it's even. If you're not confident in cutting your own hair, consider going to a salon.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting the back of your hair evenly, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a perfectly even cut at home. Remember to take your time, work in small sections, and use sharp scissors for the best results.
