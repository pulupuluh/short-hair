How To Cut My Hair Short With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to save some money and cut your own hair short, using clippers is a great option. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut your hair short with clippers in easy-to-follow steps.

How to Cut Your Hair Short with Clippers

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather a few essential tools:

  • Clippers
  • Clipper guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clippers

Make sure your clippers are fully charged and clean before you begin.

Step 2: Decide on Your Desired Length

Decide on the length you want for your hair before you start cutting. It's a good idea to start with a longer guard and work your way down if necessary.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Start with the sides of your head, using the clipper with your chosen guard. Work from the bottom of your hairline up, using upward strokes to create an even cut. Be sure to lift the clippers away from your head at the end of each stroke to avoid creating a harsh line.

Step 4: Move to the Back

Once you've finished the sides, move to the back of your head. Use the same technique as before, working from the bottom of your hairline up.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Now it's time to cut the top of your head. Use scissors to cut any longer sections of hair, then use the clippers with a longer guard to trim the rest.

Step 6: Blend the Sides and Top

Blend the sides and top of your hair using a shorter guard. Use a comb to lift the hair and create a smoother transition between the two sections.

Step 7: Check for Any Uneven Spots

Check your hair in the mirror for any uneven spots or missed areas. Use the clippers and scissors to touch up any areas that need it.

Step 8: Clean Up

Once you're happy with your cut, use a brush to remove any loose hair from your head and neck.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair with Clippers

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if necessary.
  • Use upward strokes to create an even cut.
  • Lift the clippers away from your head at the end of each stroke to avoid creating a harsh line.
  • Blend the sides and top of your hair using a shorter guard.
  • Check for any uneven spots and touch up as needed.

FAQ

What if I mess up?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go shorter or visit a professional stylist for help.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair with Clippers

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Cutting your hair with clippers can be a cost-effective and convenient way to maintain your style. With these easy steps and tips, you'll be able to achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home.
