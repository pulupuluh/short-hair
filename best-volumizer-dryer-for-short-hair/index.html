If you have short hair and want to add some volume to it, investing in a good volumizer dryer is a must. With so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this article, we will guide you through the best volumizer dryer for short hair and provide tips on how to use it effectively.

Description

A volumizer dryer is a hair tool that combines a hair dryer and a round brush to create volume and texture in the hair. It is perfect for short hair as it allows you to add volume and lift to your hair without using a lot of products or spending too much time styling your hair.

The best volumizer dryers for short hair are lightweight, easy to use, and come with multiple heat and speed settings. They also have different brush sizes to accommodate different hair lengths and textures.

How to Use a Volumizer Dryer for Short Hair

Using a volumizer dryer for short hair is easy and straightforward. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Towel dry your hair and detangle it using a wide-tooth comb.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from heat damage. This is important, especially if you use a volumizer dryer regularly.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will help you work on one section at a time and prevent your hair from tangling.

Step 4: Start Drying Your Hair

Take a small section of your hair and place the volumizer dryer close to the roots. Start drying your hair by moving the brush down towards the ends. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume.

Step 5: Repeat on All Sections

Repeat the process on all sections of your hair until you have dried and styled your entire head. Use a cool shot button to set your style and add shine to your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you use a volumizer dryer for short hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair.
  4. Section your hair into small sections using hair clips.
  5. Take a small section of your hair and place the volumizer dryer close to the roots.
  6. Start drying your hair by moving the brush down towards the ends.
  7. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume.
  8. Repeat the process on all sections of your hair.
  9. Use a cool shot button to set your style and add shine to your hair.
  10. Finish with hairspray if desired.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get the most out of your volumizer dryer:

  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Start with the lowest heat setting and increase the heat as needed.
  • Use the cool shot button to set your style and add shine to your hair.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray before drying your hair to add more volume.
  • Brush your hair in the opposite direction of your natural part to add more volume.

FAQs

How often should I use a volumizer dryer?

You can use a volumizer dryer as often as you want. However, it is important to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray or serum.

Can I use a volumizer dryer on wet hair?

No, you should not use a volumizer dryer on wet hair. Towel dry your hair first and detangle it using a wide-tooth comb before using a volumizer dryer.

What is the best volumizer dryer for short hair?

The best volumizer dryer for short hair is the one that suits your hair type and needs. Some of the popular options in the market are the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer, the Dyson Airwrap, and the Hot Tools 24K Gold One Step Dryer and Volumizer.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to take care of your short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you know how to use a volumizer dryer for short hair, go ahead and try it out. With the right technique and tools, you can achieve a voluminous and bouncy hairstyle in no time.
