How To Style Curly Hair With Big Forehead Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Sep 01, 2023 · 7 min read

Curly hair can be challenging to style, especially if you have a big forehead. However, with the right techniques and products, you can make your curls look amazing and minimize the appearance of your forehead. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to style curly hair with a big forehead.

Table of Contents:

  • Hair Care
  • How To Style Curly Hair with Big Forehead
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

Hair Care

Before we dive into styling tips, it's essential to note that healthy hair is the foundation of any hairstyle. Here are a few hair care tips that can help you maintain healthy, curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush.

By following these hair care tips, you'll have a strong foundation for styling your curly hair with a big forehead.

How to Style Curly Hair with Big Forehead

Styling curly hair with a big forehead can be tricky, but it's not impossible. Here are a few things to keep in mind when styling your curls:

  • Choose hairstyles that flatter your face shape.
  • Use products that enhance your curls' natural texture.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style curly hair with a big forehead:

  1. Start with freshly washed and conditioned hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and define your curls.
  3. Use a curl-enhancing product like mousse or gel to define your curls further.
  4. If you want to part your hair, choose a side part or a middle part. Avoid a deep side part as it can make your forehead look more prominent.
  5. If you want to wear your hair up, opt for a messy bun or a ponytail. Avoid slicked-back hairstyles as they can accentuate your forehead.
  6. Accessorize your hair with headbands, scarves, or hairpins to draw attention away from your forehead.
  7. Blow-dry your hair on low heat to avoid frizz. If you must use a diffuser, use it on low speed and heat setting.
  8. Avoid brushing your hair too much as it can cause frizz and disrupt your curls' natural texture.
  9. If you have bangs, opt for side-swept bangs or long bangs that can be tucked behind your ears.
  10. Use hairspray to set your hairstyle in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips and tricks that can help you style your curly hair with a big forehead:

  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your curls without disrupting their natural texture.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and partings to find what works best for you.
  • If you're self-conscious about your forehead, try using a hairband or scarf to cover it instead of opting for a slicked-back hairstyle.
  • Don't overuse products as they can weigh down your curls and cause buildup.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and preserve your curls' natural texture.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about styling curly hair with a big forehead:

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have a big forehead?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs if you have a big forehead. Opt for side-swept bangs or long bangs that can be tucked behind your ears to minimize the appearance of your forehead.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a big forehead?

A: Avoid slicked-back hairstyles that can accentuate your forehead. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the crown of your head to balance out your facial features.

Q: What products should I use to style my curly hair?

A: Use products that enhance your curls' natural texture, such as mousse, gel, or curl cream. Avoid products that contain sulfates or alcohol as they can dry out your hair and cause frizz.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your curly hair with a big forehead and feel confident in your look. Remember to prioritize hair care, experiment with different hairstyles, and have fun with your curls!
