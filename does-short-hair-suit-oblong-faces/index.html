Does Short Hair Suit Oblong Faces

Written by Oliver Apr 28, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, you may have heard that shorter haircuts are not for you. However, this is a myth! Short hair can actually be very flattering for oblong faces, as long as you choose the right style. In this article, we will explore the different factors that determine whether short hair suits an oblong face, as well as tips for finding the perfect haircut.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Oblong faces are characterized by their elongated shape, with a forehead, cheekbones, and jawline that are all roughly the same width. This face shape can be very striking, but it can also be challenging to find hairstyles that flatter it. Short haircuts can work well for oblong faces, as long as they create the illusion of width at the sides of the face.

How To

The key to finding a short haircut that suits an oblong face is to look for styles that add volume and width to the sides of the face. This can be achieved through a variety of techniques, such as:

  • Adding layers to create movement and texture
  • Incorporating bangs or fringe to break up the length of the face
  • Creating volume at the crown of the head to balance out the length of the face

Step By Step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a short haircut for an oblong face:

  1. Consider your hair texture and thickness. Some styles may work better for fine hair, while others are better suited for thick or curly hair.
  2. Look for inspiration in magazines, online, or on social media. Save pictures of hairstyles that you like and that you think would look good on your face shape.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with oblong face shapes. They can help you choose a style that will flatter your features and give you tips on styling and maintenance.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut for an oblong face:

  • Avoid styles that are too blunt or straight, as they can emphasize the length of the face.
  • Consider a side part, which can create the illusion of width at the sides of the face.
  • Experiment with different styling products, such as volumizing mousse or texturizing spray, to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can oblong faces wear pixie cuts?

A: Yes! Pixie cuts can be very flattering for oblong faces, especially if they incorporate volume and texture at the sides of the face.

Q: Should oblong faces avoid short haircuts altogether?

A: No! Short haircuts can be very stylish and flattering for oblong faces. The key is to choose a style that adds width and volume to the sides of the face.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for caring for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Style your hair using a heat protectant spray and a hair dryer or styling tool.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

In conclusion, short hair can definitely suit oblong faces, as long as you choose the right style. By following these tips and consulting with a hairstylist, you can find a short haircut that flatters your features and complements your personal style.
