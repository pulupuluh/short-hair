What Length Hair Suits Me Quiz Find The Perfect Hair Length For Your Face Shape

Written by Oliver Sep 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle but not sure which length will suit you best? Take our "What Length Hair Suits Me Quiz" to find out! In this article, we will guide you step by step on how to take the quiz, provide some tips on how to choose the right hair length, answer some frequently asked questions, and give you some hair care advice.

How to Take the Quiz?

The quiz is easy and fun to take. You will need a mirror, a ruler, and a piece of paper and pen to record your measurements. Follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Measure your face shape with a ruler. Record the measurements of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Answer the quiz questions based on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.
  4. Get your result and find out which hair length suits you best!

Tips on Choosing the Right Hair Length

When choosing a new hairstyle, consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips:

  • If you have a round face, go for a long and layered hairstyle to create the illusion of length.
  • If you have a square face, opt for a soft and wavy hairstyle to soften your angular features.
  • If you have an oval face, you are lucky! Almost any hair length and style will suit you.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, choose a shoulder-length hairstyle with layers to balance out your features.
  • If you have a diamond-shaped face, go for a short and sleek hairstyle to accentuate your cheekbones.
  • If you have curly hair, embrace your natural texture and go for a layered and voluminous hairstyle.
  • If you have straight hair, try a blunt and sleek hairstyle for a polished look.
  • If you have a busy lifestyle, choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hair length:

What is the best hair length for fine hair?

A shorter hairstyle will give the illusion of thicker hair. Try a bob or a pixie cut.

What is the best hair length for thick hair?

A longer hairstyle will showcase your hair's volume and texture. Try a layered haircut or a beachy wave style.

What is the best hair length for a formal event?

A classic updo or a sleek ponytail will look elegant and sophisticated.

Hair Care Advice

No matter which hair length you choose, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or a swim cap.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the health of your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week for extra hydration.

Now that you know which hair length suits you best, go and book an appointment with your hairstylist. Embrace your new look with confidence!
