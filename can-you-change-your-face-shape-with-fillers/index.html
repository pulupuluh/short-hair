Can You Change Your Face Shape With Fillers

Written by Robby Mar 24, 2023 ¬∑ 6 min read

If you're looking to change your face shape, you might be wondering if fillers can help. The short answer is yes, fillers can be used to reshape the face and create a more balanced, youthful look. In this article, we'll explore how fillers work, what you can expect during the procedure, and some tips for achieving the best results.

Table of Contents

  • How Fillers Work
  • Areas to Treat
  • The Procedure
  • Recovery Time
  • Tips for Best Results
  • FAQ
  • Conclusion

How Fillers Work

Fillers are injectable gels made from hyaluronic acid, a substance that occurs naturally in the body. Hyaluronic acid helps to hydrate and plump the skin, giving it a more youthful, radiant look. When injected into the face, fillers can be used to add volume, contour the cheeks and jawline, and soften lines and wrinkles.

There are many different types of fillers available, each with their own unique properties and benefits. Your cosmetic surgeon or dermatologist can help you choose the right filler for your needs and goals.

Areas to Treat

Fillers can be used to treat a variety of areas on the face, including:

  • The cheeks
  • The temples
  • The jawline
  • The chin
  • The lips
  • The nasolabial folds (lines that run from the nose to the corners of the mouth)
  • The marionette lines (lines that run from the corners of the mouth to the chin)

The Procedure

The procedure for getting fillers is relatively simple and straightforward. First, your cosmetic surgeon or dermatologist will assess your face and determine where the fillers should be injected. They may mark the injection sites with a marker.

Next, they will clean and numb the area with a local anesthetic. Once the area is numb, they will inject the filler using a small needle or cannula. The injection process only takes a few minutes, and you should feel minimal discomfort.

After the injection, your cosmetic surgeon or dermatologist will massage the area to ensure that the filler is distributed evenly. They may also apply ice to reduce swelling and bruising.

Recovery Time

The recovery time for fillers is minimal. You may experience some swelling, redness, and bruising at the injection site, but this should subside within a few days. You can resume your normal activities immediately after the procedure, although you should avoid strenuous exercise and exposure to heat or cold for at least 24 hours.

Tips for Best Results

To achieve the best results with fillers, there are a few things you can do:

  • Choose an experienced cosmetic surgeon or dermatologist who has a proven track record of success with fillers.
  • Be clear about your goals and expectations for the procedure.
  • Follow all pre- and post-procedure instructions carefully.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, which can interfere with the healing process.
  • Take good care of your skin with a healthy diet, regular exercise, and a good skincare routine.

FAQ

Q: How long do fillers last?

A: The length of time that fillers last varies depending on the type of filler used and the individual patient. Generally, fillers last anywhere from 6 to 18 months.

Q: Are fillers safe?

A: Yes, fillers are safe when administered by a qualified and experienced cosmetic surgeon or dermatologist.

Q: Is there any downtime after getting fillers?

A: No, there is no downtime after getting fillers. You can resume your normal activities immediately after the procedure.

Conclusion

If you're looking to change your face shape, fillers can be a safe and effective option. By adding volume and contouring key areas of the face, fillers can create a more youthful, balanced look. To achieve the best results, be sure to choose an experienced cosmetic surgeon or dermatologist, follow all pre- and post-procedure instructions, and take good care of your skin.
