How To Thin Long Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy May 18, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have long curly hair, you know that it can be difficult to manage. One way to make it more manageable is to thin it out. Thinning long curly hair can help reduce the weight, prevent tangling, and make styling easier. In this article, we’ll provide a step-by-step guide on how to thin long curly hair.

How to Thin Long Curly Hair

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to thinning your long curly hair is to wash it. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and residue from your hair. This will help make it easier to cut and thin.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Once your hair is clean, use a wide-tooth comb to section it off. Divide it into four sections, two in the front and two in the back.

Step 3: Start Thinning

Take a pair of thinning shears and start at the bottom of one of the back sections. Hold a small section of your hair and cut into it with the thinning shears. Make sure to cut at an angle and not straight across.

Continue working your way up, taking small sections of hair and cutting into them with the thinning shears. Be sure to work slowly and carefully to avoid cutting too much hair.

Step 4: Move to the Front

Once you’ve finished thinning the back sections, move to the front. Take small sections of hair and thin them out using the same technique.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you’ve finished thinning your hair, check your progress in the mirror. If you need to thin it out more, go back and repeat the process.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you’re happy with the thinness of your hair, style it as desired. Use a curl-defining cream to enhance your curls and give your hair a polished look.

Tips for Thinning Long Curly Hair

Thinning long curly hair can be a tricky process. Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use sharp thinning shears to avoid damaging your hair
  • Work slowly and carefully, taking small sections of hair at a time
  • Don’t thin your hair too much to avoid creating thin spots
  • Experiment with different thinning techniques to find what works best for you

FAQ

How often should I thin my long curly hair?

It’s recommended to thin your long curly hair every 3 to 4 months. This will help keep it looking healthy and prevent tangling.

Can I thin my long curly hair at home?

Yes, you can thin your long curly hair at home. However, if you’re not comfortable doing it yourself, it’s best to go to a professional stylist.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

In addition to thinning your long curly hair, it’s important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for caring for long curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat tools as much as possible to prevent damage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf at night to prevent frizz and breakage

With these tips and techniques, you can keep your long curly hair looking its best. Whether you want to thin it out or just keep it healthy, taking good care of your hair is essential.
