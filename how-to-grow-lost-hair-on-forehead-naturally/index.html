How To Grow Lost Hair On Forehead Naturally Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 25, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you concerned about your receding hairline or thinning hair on your forehead? Don't worry; you're not alone. Many people experience hair loss on their forehead due to various reasons, such as genetics, stress, hormonal changes, and more. Fortunately, there are several natural remedies that can help you grow lost hair on your forehead without spending a fortune on hair transplants or chemical treatments.

How to Grow Lost Hair on Forehead Naturally

If you're looking for natural ways to grow lost hair on your forehead, you'll be surprised to know that there are several home remedies that can help stimulate hair growth. Here are some of the best natural remedies for growing hair on your forehead:

1. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is an excellent natural remedy for hair growth as it contains enzymes that can help nourish your hair follicles and promote hair growth. Simply extract fresh aloe vera gel and apply it to your scalp and hair twice a week for best results.

2. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is rich in essential fatty acids that can help nourish your scalp and hair follicles, promoting healthy hair growth. Massage warm coconut oil onto your scalp and hair and leave it on for at least an hour before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

3. Onion Juice

Onion juice is a well-known natural remedy for hair growth as it contains sulfur, which can help promote collagen production and stimulate hair growth. Apply onion juice to your scalp and hair and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

4. Fenugreek Seeds

Fenugreek seeds are rich in protein, which can help strengthen your hair and promote healthy hair growth. Soak fenugreek seeds overnight and grind them into a fine paste. Apply the paste to your scalp and hair and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some of the best natural remedies for growing hair on your forehead, here's a step-by-step guide to help you grow lost hair naturally:

Step 1: Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help stimulate blood flow to your hair follicles, promoting healthy hair growth. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp for at least 5-10 minutes every day.

Step 2: Apply Natural Remedies

Apply natural remedies such as aloe vera, coconut oil, onion juice, or fenugreek seeds to your scalp and hair twice a week for best results. Leave the remedies on for at least 30 minutes before washing them off with a mild shampoo.

Step 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet rich in vitamins, minerals, and protein can help promote healthy hair growth. Eat foods such as eggs, nuts, fish, leafy greens, and berries to nourish your hair from the inside out.

Step 4: Avoid Stress

Stress can contribute to hair loss, so try to avoid stress as much as possible. Practice relaxation techniques such as yoga, meditation, or deep breathing exercises to reduce stress levels.

Tips for Growing Hair on Forehead Naturally

Here are some additional tips to help you grow lost hair on your forehead:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid using hair styling products that contain harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles such as braids, ponytails, or cornrows that can pull on your hair and damage the hair follicles.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to heat styling tools such as hair dryers, curling irons, or straighteners.

FAQs

Q. Can stress cause hair loss on forehead?

A. Yes, stress can contribute to hair loss on your forehead and other areas of your scalp.

Q. Can I use natural remedies for hair growth on my forehead if I have sensitive skin?

A. Yes, you can use natural remedies for hair growth on your forehead, but make sure to do a patch test first to avoid any allergic reactions.

Q. How long does it take to see results with natural remedies for hair growth on my forehead?

A. It may take several weeks or months to see visible results with natural remedies for hair growth on your forehead. Consistency is key, so make sure to apply the remedies regularly for best results.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair Growth

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you promote healthy hair growth:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your scalp clean and healthy.
  • Condition your hair after every wash to avoid dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet as it can cause damage to your hair follicles.

In conclusion, growing lost hair on your forehead naturally is possible with the help of natural remedies, a healthy diet, and proper hair care. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you'll soon see visible results.
