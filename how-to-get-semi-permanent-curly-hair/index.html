How To Get Semi Permanent Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Apr 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of straight hair and want to switch it up with some curls? Getting semi permanent curly hair is a great way to achieve a new look without committing to a full perm. In this article, we'll go over the steps to get semi permanent curly hair, as well as tips for hair care and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Semi Permanent Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Hair Care and Maintenance
  • FAQs

How to Get Semi Permanent Curly Hair

Semi permanent curly hair can be achieved through a chemical process that alters the structure of your hair. This process is similar to a perm, but the curls are not as tight and will gradually fade over time. Here are some steps to get semi permanent curly hair:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional Stylist

Before attempting any chemical process on your hair, it's important to consult with a professional stylist. They can assess your hair type and determine if semi permanent curly hair is a good option for you. They can also recommend the best products and techniques to achieve your desired look.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before the chemical process, your hair will need to be washed and dried. Avoid using any styling products or heat tools, as these can interfere with the process. Your stylist may also apply a protective treatment to your hair to minimize damage.

Step 3: Apply the Chemical Solution

Next, your stylist will apply a chemical solution to your hair. This solution will break down the bonds in your hair, allowing it to be reshaped into curls. The solution will be left on your hair for a specified amount of time, depending on your hair type and desired curl pattern.

Step 4: Rinse and Style

After the solution has been applied and processed, your hair will be rinsed thoroughly. Your stylist may then apply a neutralizing solution to stop the chemical process. Finally, your hair will be styled into curls using rollers or other styling tools.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide for getting semi permanent curly hair:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist
  2. Prepare your hair by washing and drying it
  3. Apply the chemical solution
  4. Leave the solution on for a specified amount of time
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly
  6. Apply a neutralizing solution
  7. Style your hair into curls using rollers or other styling tools
  8. Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours
  9. Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to maintain your curls
  10. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

Tips for Hair Care and Maintenance

Here are some tips for caring for and maintaining your semi permanent curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can damage the curls
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the curls of their shape
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without disrupting their shape
  • Sleep with a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to keep your curls hydrated and defined

FAQs

How long will my semi permanent curls last?

Semi permanent curls can last anywhere from 3-6 months, depending on how well you care for them and how fast your hair grows.

Can I color my semi permanent curly hair?

It's best to wait at least 2 weeks after getting semi permanent curls before coloring your hair. Be sure to consult with a professional stylist to avoid damaging your curls.

Can I swim with semi permanent curly hair?

Yes, but it's important to rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming in a pool or the ocean. Chlorine and salt can dry out and damage your curls.

Now that you know how to get semi permanent curly hair, it's time to book an appointment with a professional stylist and try out this new look for yourself. With proper care and maintenance, you can enjoy your curls for months to come.
