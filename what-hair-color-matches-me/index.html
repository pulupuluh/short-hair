What Hair Color Matches Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Looking for a new hair color that matches your skin tone and personality can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this article, we'll break down the factors that determine what hair color matches you and provide tips on how to find the perfect shade.

Table of Content:

  • Factors that Determine Hair Color
  • How to Find the Right Hair Color
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Your Hair Color
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Factors that Determine Hair Color

Before we dive into how to find the right hair color for you, it's important to understand the factors that determine what shades will look best. These include:

  • Skin tone
  • Eye color
  • Natural hair color
  • Personality and desired look


Your skin tone plays a significant role in what hair color will look best on you. Cool skin tones typically look best with cooler shades such as platinum blonde, ash brown, or black. Warm skin tones look best with warmer shades such as golden blonde, honey brown, or auburn.


Eye color can also be a factor in choosing your hair color. Blue or green eyes can be complemented by cool shades, while brown or hazel eyes look best with warm shades.


Your natural hair color can also impact what shades will look best on you. If you have naturally dark hair, it may be more difficult to achieve lighter shades without causing damage to your hair. If you have lighter hair, you may have more options when it comes to coloring.


Lastly, your personality and desired look should also be considered when choosing a hair color. If you're looking for a dramatic change, you may want to consider a bold color such as purple or red. If you want a more subtle change, a natural-looking shade may be best.

How to Find the Right Hair Color

Now that you understand the factors that determine what hair color matches you, it's time to start looking for the perfect shade. Here are some tips to help you find your ideal hair color:

  • Look at pictures of people with similar skin tones and eye colors to get an idea of what shades may look best on you.
  • Speak with a stylist to get their professional opinion on what shades will be most flattering for you.
  • Consider trying a temporary hair color to see how you like the shade before committing to a permanent change.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Your Hair Color

Follow these steps to find your perfect hair color:

  1. Determine your skin tone
  2. Consider your eye color
  3. Think about your natural hair color
  4. Decide on a desired look or personality
  5. Look at pictures of people with similar features to get an idea of what shades will look best on you
  6. Speak with a stylist to get their professional opinion
  7. Consider trying a temporary hair color before committing
  8. Choose your shade and schedule your appointment
  9. Prepare your hair for coloring by following your stylist's instructions
  10. Maintain your new hair color with proper hair care

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Congratulations, you've found your perfect hair color! Now it's important to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair color:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently
  • Avoid hot tools that can damage your hair and fade your color
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Get regular touch-ups to maintain your color

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding the right hair color:

What's the best hair color for fair skin?

If you have fair skin, cool shades such as platinum blonde, ash brown, or black will look best on you.

What's the best hair color for olive skin?

If you have olive skin, warm shades such as golden blonde, honey brown, or auburn will look best on you.

How often should I touch up my hair color?

It depends on the type of color and how fast your hair grows. Speak with your stylist to determine the best touch-up schedule for your hair.

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it's possible to dye your hair at home, it's recommended to have a professional do it to ensure the best results and avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Colored hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your colored hair:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently
  • Avoid hot tools that can damage your hair and fade your color
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Get regular touch-ups to maintain your color


In conclusion, finding the right hair color that matches you is all about understanding your skin tone, eye color, natural hair color, and desired look. Use the tips in this article to help you find your perfect shade, and remember to maintain your color with proper hair care.
