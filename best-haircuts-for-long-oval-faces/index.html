Best Haircuts For Long Oval Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Jul 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of trying different haircuts that don't seem to suit your long oval face? Finding the right haircut that complements your face shape can be a daunting task, but fret not, we're here to help you out. In this article, we'll be discussing the best haircuts for long oval faces, along with tips and tricks to help you choose the perfect haircut for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Oval Faces

Description

Long oval faces are characterized by a longer forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The best haircuts for this face shape are those that add width and volume to the sides of the face, while minimizing the length of the forehead. Avoid haircuts that elongate the face or draw attention to the length of the chin.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a long oval face, it's important to consider the following:

  • Face Shape: Determine your face shape before choosing a haircut. If you have a long oval face, you'll want to avoid haircuts that add length to the face.
  • Hair Texture: Consider your hair texture when choosing a haircut. Curly hair may require a different type of haircut than straight hair.
  • Personal Style: Choose a haircut that matches your personal style and aesthetic.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get the perfect haircut for your long oval face:

  1. Research different haircuts that are suitable for long oval faces.
  2. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair texture.
  3. Bring a picture of the desired haircut to the hairstylist.
  4. Communicate your preferences and concerns with the hairstylist.
  5. Ask for advice on how to style and maintain the haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your haircut:

  • Visit the hairstylist regularly to keep the haircut looking fresh.
  • Use the right hair products for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.

FAQs

Q: What are the best haircuts for long oval faces?

A: The best haircuts for long oval faces are those that add width and volume to the sides of the face, while minimizing the length of the forehead. Examples include layers, side-swept bangs, and bob haircuts.

Q: Can I pull off a pixie cut with a long oval face?

A: It's possible to pull off a pixie cut with a long oval face, but it's important to choose a style that adds volume to the sides of the face to balance out the length. Consult with a hairstylist to determine if a pixie cut would suit your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Long Oval Faces

Here are some hair care tips for long oval faces:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent heat damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Now that you know the best haircuts for long oval faces, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and get the perfect haircut for you. Remember to choose a haircut that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
