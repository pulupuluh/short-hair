Why Is Thick Hair Attractive

Written by Aprilia Jul 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Thick hair has always been a desirable trait for both men and women. It adds volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and healthier. In this blog post, we will discuss why thick hair is attractive and share some tips on how to take care of your thick hair.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Get Thick Hair
  3. Tips for Maintaining Thick Hair
  4. FAQs About Thick Hair
  5. Hair Care for Thick Hair

Description of Thick Hair

Thick hair refers to the density of hair on your scalp. It means that you have a lot of hair follicles on your head, which translates to having more hair strands. Thick hair can come in many different textures and types, including curly, straight, wavy, coiled, and kinky. It is also more resistant to damage and breakage compared to thin hair.

Thick hair can be genetic or acquired. Some people are born with naturally thick hair, while others can achieve it through proper hair care and lifestyle changes.

How to Get Thick Hair

If you were not born with thick hair, there are several ways to achieve it:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals that promote hair growth.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Use hair care products that are specifically designed for thick hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to increase blood flow to your hair follicles.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Hair

Once you have achieved thick hair, it is important to maintain it properly:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Avoid using hair ties that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with UV protection.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy.

FAQs About Thick Hair

1. Is thick hair better than thin hair?

It depends on personal preference. Some people prefer thick hair because it looks fuller and provides more volume, while others prefer thin hair because it is easier to manage and style.

2. Can I make my hair thicker?

Yes, you can make your hair thicker by following the tips mentioned earlier in this post.

3. Does thick hair fall out more than thin hair?

No, thick hair is more resistant to breakage and fall out compared to thin hair.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

When it comes to hair care for thick hair, it is important to use products that are specifically designed for your hair type. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are sulfate-free and contain ingredients that promote hair growth and hydration, such as biotin and argan oil.

You should also avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle. Instead, wash your hair every 2-3 days and use a dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil.

Deep conditioning your hair once a week is also important to keep it hydrated and healthy. You can use a hair mask or an oil treatment to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.

In conclusion, thick hair is attractive because it adds volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and healthier. By following the tips mentioned in this post, you can achieve and maintain thick hair that looks and feels great.
