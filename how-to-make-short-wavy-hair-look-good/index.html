How To Make Short Wavy Hair Look Good

Written by Frank Apr 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have short wavy hair, you may be wondering how you can make it look good. Don't worry, there are plenty of things you can do to enhance your natural waves and have a fabulous hair day every day. Below are some tips that will help you achieve the perfect short wavy hair look.

Description

Short wavy hair can be challenging to style, but with the right techniques, you can make it look fabulous. Having wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse, as you have a natural texture to work with, but it can also be difficult to manage. However, with the right products and styling techniques, you can make your short wavy hair look good every day.

How to

The first step in making short wavy hair look good is to find the right haircut. A layered haircut is perfect for wavy hair as it creates movement and volume. The layers should be cut to enhance your natural waves and make them more defined. Once you have the right haircut, you can start styling your hair.

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for wavy hair. This will help to enhance your natural waves and make them more defined. After washing, towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage.

Next, blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help to enhance your natural waves and create volume. Use a low heat setting and scrunch your hair as you dry it to create more defined waves.

Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling iron to enhance your waves further. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. This will create more defined waves and add volume to your hair.

Step by Step

Step 1: Find the right haircut

The first step in making short wavy hair look good is to find the right haircut. A layered haircut is perfect for wavy hair as it creates movement and volume. The layers should be cut to enhance your natural waves and make them more defined.

Step 2: Wash your hair with the right products

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for wavy hair. This will help to enhance your natural waves and make them more defined. After washing, towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment

Next, blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help to enhance your natural waves and create volume. Use a low heat setting and scrunch your hair as you dry it to create more defined waves.

Step 4: Use a curling iron to enhance your waves

Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling iron to enhance your waves further. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. This will create more defined waves and add volume to your hair.

Tips

  • Use products that are designed for wavy hair to enhance your natural waves.
  • Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to create volume and enhance your waves.
  • Use a curling iron to create more defined waves and add volume to your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my short wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your short wavy hair, but it's best to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Keep in mind that straightening your hair too often can cause damage and breakage, so make sure to use a quality flat iron and avoid using it every day.

Q: How often should I wash my short wavy hair?

A: It's best to wash your short wavy hair every two to three days to prevent it from becoming too oily. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every four to five days.

Hair Care

To keep your short wavy hair looking good, it's essential to take care of it properly. Use products that are designed for wavy hair, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage, and try to wash your hair every two to three days. With the right care and styling techniques, you can have fabulous short wavy hair every day.
