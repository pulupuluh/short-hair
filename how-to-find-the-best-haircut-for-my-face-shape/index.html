How To Find The Best Haircut For My Face Shape

Written by Christine Mar 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of getting haircuts that just don't seem to suit you? It could be because you haven't considered your face shape when choosing a hairstyle. Finding the right haircut for your face shape can make all the difference in how you look and feel. In this article, we'll go over how to determine your face shape and how to find the best haircut for it.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best haircut for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart-shaped, diamond-shaped, and long. Here's how to determine which one you have.

Round Face

If you have a round face, your face will be about as wide as it is long. Your jawline will be rounded rather than angled, and your cheekbones will be the widest part of your face.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, your face will be longer than it is wide. Your jawline will be slightly rounded, and your cheekbones will be the widest part of your face.

Square Face

If you have a square face, your face will be about as wide as it is long. Your jawline will be squared off rather than rounded, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline will all be about the same width.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, your forehead will be the widest part of your face, and your face will gradually taper down to a narrow chin. Your cheekbones may be high and prominent.

Diamond-Shaped Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, your forehead and jawline will be narrow, and your cheekbones will be the widest part of your face.

Long Face

If you have a long face, your face will be longer than it is wide. Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline will all be about the same width.

Haircuts for a Round Face

If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a haircut that adds some angles and length to your face. Here are some great options:

  • Long, layered cuts
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical cuts
  • Short, spiky cuts

Haircuts for an Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you're in luck. Almost any haircut will look great on you! Here are some popular options:

  • Long, straight cuts
  • Short pixie cuts
  • Layered cuts
  • Bob cuts

Haircuts for a Square Face

If you have a square face, you'll want to soften your angles with your haircut. Here are some great options:

  • Long, layered cuts
  • Soft waves
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Curly hair

Haircuts for a Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to balance out your forehead and narrow chin. Here are some great options:

  • Side-swept bangs
  • Layered cuts
  • Long, wavy cuts
  • Pixie cuts

Haircuts for a Diamond-Shaped Face

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you'll want to soften your angles and balance out your forehead and chin. Here are some great options:

  • Layered cuts
  • Long, wavy cuts
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Short, textured cuts

Tips for Choosing the Best Haircut for Your Face Shape

Here are a few general tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for your face shape:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness. Some haircuts may not work well with certain hair types.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you don't have a lot of time to style your hair every day, choose a low-maintenance cut.
  • Bring pictures to your stylist. It can be helpful to show your stylist exactly what you're looking for.
  • Be open to suggestions. Your stylist may have some ideas you haven't considered.

FAQ

What if my face doesn't fit neatly into one category?

It's not uncommon for people to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, focus on the features that are most prominent and choose a haircut that flatters those features.

Can I still wear a certain haircut even if it's not recommended for my face shape?

Of course! These guidelines are just suggestions. Ultimately, you should choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and happy.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your face shape, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips and choosing a haircut that flatters your face shape, you'll be well on your way to great-looking hair!
