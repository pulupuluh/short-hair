How To Apply Gel To Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 06, 2023 · 6 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can be gorgeous, it can also be difficult to manage. One of the best ways to tame curly hair is by using gel. Gel can help define your curls and keep them in place all day long. However, applying gel to curly hair can be tricky. In this article, we’ll show you how to apply gel to curly hair step by step. We’ll also give you some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

How to Apply Gel to Curly Hair

Before we get started, it’s important to choose the right gel for your hair. Look for a gel that is specifically designed for curly hair. These gels are usually lightweight, and they won’t weigh your curls down.

Step 1: Start with clean, wet hair.

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make your curls dry and frizzy. After you’ve washed your hair, gently pat it dry with a towel. Don’t rub your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply the gel.

Take a quarter-sized amount of gel and rub it between your palms. Then, apply the gel to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Be sure to distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Scrunch your hair.

Use your hands to scrunch your hair. This will help define your curls and give them shape. Be gentle when you scrunch your hair, as rough handling can cause frizz.

Step 4: Let your hair air dry.

Air drying is the best way to dry curly hair. Using a blow dryer can cause frizz and damage your hair. If you’re in a hurry, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. This will help distribute the heat evenly and minimize frizz.

Tips for Applying Gel to Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when applying gel to curly hair:

  • Don’t use too much gel. A little goes a long way.
  • Don’t touch your hair while it’s drying. This can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase. This will help reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Try plopping. Plopping is a technique where you wrap your hair in a t-shirt or microfiber towel to help define your curls.

FAQ About Applying Gel to Curly Hair

Can I apply gel to dry hair?

It’s best to apply gel to wet hair. This will help distribute the gel evenly and prevent clumping.

How often should I apply gel to my hair?

You can apply gel to your hair every day if you’d like. Just be sure to wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup.

What should I do if my hair feels crunchy after applying gel?

This is a common problem. To fix it, scrunch your hair with your hands. This will break up the gel and make your hair feel softer.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Using gel is just one part of a good hair care routine for curly hair. Here are some other tips to keep your curls looking their best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

By following these tips and using gel, you can have beautiful, defined curls that last all day long.
