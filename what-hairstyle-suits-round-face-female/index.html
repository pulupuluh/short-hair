What Hairstyle Suits Round Face Female

Written by Eveline May 08, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round face, you might be looking for the perfect hairstyle to flatter your features. A round face is characterized by equal width and length, with soft curves and no pronounced angles. Luckily, there are many hairstyles that can complement a round face and help you look your best. In this article, we'll explore different hairstyles that suit a round face female and provide tips on how to style them.

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The right hairstyle can make all the difference when it comes to enhancing your facial features. For round faces, it's important to choose a hairstyle that adds length and angles to balance out the soft curves of your face. This can be achieved with layers, angles, and height at the crown. However, it's also essential to choose a hairstyle that suits your personality, lifestyle, and hair type.

How to

To determine which hairstyle suits round face female, you need to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. It's also crucial to consult with a hairstylist who can recommend the best hairstyle for your features and guide you on how to style it. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height and volume at the crown, such as high ponytails, half-up hairstyles, and voluminous updos.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, and one-length haircuts.
  • Choose hairstyles with layers and angles that frame your face, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical bobs.
  • Consider hairstyles that elongate your face, such as long straight hair, beachy waves, and sleek buns.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best and makes you feel confident.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style some of the best hairstyles for round face females:

High Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and create a smooth base.
  2. Gather your hair at the crown and secure it with an elastic band.
  3. Tease the roots of your ponytail to add height and volume.
  4. Smooth out the top layer of your hair with a comb or brush.
  5. Wrap a small section of hair around the elastic band to hide it and secure it with a bobby pin.
  6. Spray your hair with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Part your hair to the side, using your natural part as a guide.
  2. Section off a small triangle of hair at your hairline.
  3. Comb the rest of your hair back and secure it with a clip or elastic band.
  4. Trim your bangs to the desired length, starting from the middle and working towards the sides.
  5. Blow dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and a side-swept shape.
  6. Smooth out your bangs with a flat iron or straightening brush.
  7. Spray your bangs with hairspray to hold the shape in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for round face female:

  • Consider your hair texture and choose a hairstyle that works with it, such as curly hair with layers or straight hair with blunt ends.
  • Use hair accessories, such as headbands, clips, and scarves, to add interest and style to your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings, such as a center part or a deep side part, to change up your look.
  • Avoid hairstyles that require a lot of maintenance or styling, such as complicated updos or tight braids.
  • Invest in quality hair products, such as a good shampoo and conditioner, heat protectant spray, and styling tools.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face female?

The best haircut for a round face female is one that adds length and angles to balance out the soft curves of the face. This can be achieved with layers, angles, and height at the crown. Examples include long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical bobs.

Can a round face female have short hair?

Yes, a round face female can have short hair, but it's important to choose a short hairstyle that flatters the face shape. Short hairstyles with layers and angles that frame the face, such as pixie cuts and layered bobs, can work well for round faces.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and scalp.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling wands, too often or at high temperatures.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair while it's wet.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can enhance your features and feel confident in your appearance as a round face female.
