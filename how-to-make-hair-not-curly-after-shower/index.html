How To Make Hair Not Curly After Shower A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine May 09, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that washing your hair can be a hassle. After you step out of the shower, your hair can become frizzy and curly, making it difficult to style. Fortunately, there are several ways to make hair not curly after shower. In this article, we will discuss some of the best tips and tricks to help you achieve smooth, straight hair after washing.

Description

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can look beautiful and voluminous, it can also be difficult to maintain. After you wash your hair, the curls can become frizzy and unmanageable, making it challenging to style your hair the way you want it. This article will provide you with tips on how to make hair not curly after shower so that you can achieve the style you desire.

How to Make Hair Not Curly After Shower: Step by Step

Step 1: Use a Straightening Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a straightening shampoo and conditioner can help to reduce frizz and make your hair more manageable. These products contain ingredients that can help to smooth your hair cuticles and make your hair less prone to curls. When selecting a straightening shampoo and conditioner, look for products that contain keratin, which can help to strengthen your hair and make it smoother.

Step 2: Apply a Straightening Serum

After you have washed your hair, apply a straightening serum to your hair. This will help to make your hair more manageable and reduce frizz. Look for a serum that contains ingredients like argan oil or coconut oil, which can help to nourish your hair and make it smoother.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Once you have applied the straightening serum, blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will help to smooth your hair and reduce frizz. Make sure to blow dry your hair in sections and use a low heat setting to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron

After you have blow dried your hair, use a flat iron to straighten your hair. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using the flat iron to prevent damage to your hair. When straightening your hair, work in sections and use a low heat setting to prevent damage to your hair.

Tips for Making Hair Not Curly After Shower

Here are some additional tips to help you make your hair not curly after shower:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much after washing it, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using a hairbrush on wet hair, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.

FAQ

Can I use a straightening serum every day?

It is best to use a straightening serum every other day to prevent damage to your hair. Using it every day can cause your hair to become brittle and dry, which can lead to breakage.

Do I need to use a flat iron to make my hair not curly after shower?

No, you do not need to use a flat iron to make your hair not curly after shower. However, using a flat iron can help to smooth your hair and reduce frizz.

Can I use a hair dryer instead of a flat iron?

Yes, you can use a hair dryer instead of a flat iron to make your hair not curly after shower. However, using a flat iron will give you better results.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it is important to take care of your hair properly. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as regular brushes can cause breakage.

Conclusion

Making your hair not curly after shower can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve smooth, straight hair. Remember to use a straightening shampoo and conditioner, apply a straightening serum, blow dry your hair, and use a flat iron to achieve the style you desire. With proper care, you can keep your curly hair looking beautiful and healthy.
