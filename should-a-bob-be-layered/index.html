Should A Bob Be Layered A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy May 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Bob hairstyles have been a popular choice for decades, and for good reason. They are versatile, low maintenance, and can be customized to suit any face shape and hair type. One question that many people ask is whether a bob should be layered or not. In this article, we will answer that question and provide you with everything you need to know about layering a bob.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Layer a Bob
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Layering a Bob
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Layered Bobs

Description

A layered bob is a haircut that involves cutting the hair into different lengths. Layers can be added to the front, back, or sides of the hair to create texture and volume. Layering a bob can also make it easier to style and maintain, as the shorter layers can add movement and body to the hair.

How to Layer a Bob

Layering a bob is a technique that should only be done by a professional hairstylist. The stylist will use different cutting techniques, such as slicing or point cutting, to create layers that blend seamlessly into the rest of the hair. The length and placement of the layers will depend on the client's hair type, face shape, and desired style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to layering a bob:

  1. Start by washing and drying the hair.
  2. Section the hair into four parts: the top, sides, and back.
  3. Starting at the nape of the neck, take a small section of hair and cut it to the desired length.
  4. Continue working your way up the back of the head, taking small sections of hair and cutting them to the same length.
  5. Move to the sides of the head and repeat the same process.
  6. For the top of the head, take horizontal sections of hair and cut them to the desired length.
  7. Blend the layers together using a blending shear or scissors.
  8. Finish by styling the hair as desired.

Tips for Layering a Bob

Here are some tips for layering a bob:

  • Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best type of layering for your hair type and face shape.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair before getting a layered bob.
  • Keep the layers long if you have fine hair, as shorter layers can make the hair look thinner.
  • Use a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and definition to the layers.

FAQs

Is a layered bob suitable for all face shapes?

A layered bob can be customized to suit any face shape. The length and placement of the layers will depend on the client's individual features.

Can I still wear my hair straight with a layered bob?

Yes, you can still wear your hair straight with a layered bob. The layers will add movement and texture to the hair, making it easier to style.

Will a layered bob make my hair look thinner?

If the layers are too short, they can make the hair look thinner. It is important to consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best length and placement of the layers for your hair type.

Hair Care Tips for Layered Bobs

Maintaining a layered bob requires proper hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to the hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair and make the layers look limp.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of the layers.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair soft and moisturized.

In conclusion, a layered bob can be a great choice for anyone looking for a low-maintenance, versatile haircut. However, it is important to consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best type of layering for your hair type and face shape. With proper hair care and styling, a layered bob can look great for months to come.
