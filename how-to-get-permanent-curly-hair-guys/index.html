How To Get Permanent Curly Hair For Guys

If you're a guy who wants to add some texture and bounce to your hair, getting permanent curly hair might be a good option for you. In this article, we'll go over how to get permanent curly hair guys, step by step. We'll also cover some tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice to help you maintain your new curly locks.

How to Get Permanent Curly Hair Guys

There are a few different methods you can use to get permanent curly hair as a guy. One popular option is to get a perm. Here's how to do it:

1. Choose a Professional Stylist

Before you do anything, it's important to choose a professional stylist who has experience with perms. Look for reviews online or ask for recommendations from friends to find someone who you trust.

2. Consult with Your Stylist

Once you've found a stylist, schedule a consultation to discuss your hair type, desired curl pattern, and any concerns you may have. Your stylist may also do a strand test to determine how your hair will react to the perm solution.

3. Prep Your Hair

Before getting the perm, your stylist will need to wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup. They may also trim your hair to ensure even curls.

4. Apply the Perm Solution

Your stylist will apply the perm solution to your hair, which will break down the bonds that give your hair its natural shape. They'll then wrap your hair in rods to create the desired curl pattern.

5. Rinse and Style

After the perm solution has set, your stylist will rinse your hair thoroughly and apply a neutralizer to help set the curls. They'll then style your hair using a diffuser to enhance the curl pattern.

Step by Step

Here's a brief summary of the steps to getting permanent curly hair:

  1. Choose a professional stylist
  2. Consult with your stylist
  3. Prep your hair
  4. Apply the perm solution
  5. Rinse and style

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your permanent curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to help maintain your curls
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair to prevent damage to your curls
  • Get regular trims to keep your curls looking fresh
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized

FAQ

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of your curls.

Can I swim with a perm?

Yes, you can swim with a perm, but you should avoid exposing your hair to chlorine as it can damage your curls. Consider wearing a swim cap or using a leave-in conditioner before swimming to protect your hair.

Can I get a perm if I have dyed hair?

Yes, you can get a perm if you have dyed hair, but you should let your stylist know beforehand as the chemicals in the perm solution can affect the color of your hair.

Hair Care

Here are some additional hair care tips to help you maintain your permanent curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your curls
  • Consider using a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep

Getting permanent curly hair as a guy can be a great way to switch up your look and add some texture to your hair. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve long-lasting, bouncy curls that will turn heads.
