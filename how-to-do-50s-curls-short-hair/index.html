How To Do 50S Curls On Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a classic and chic hairstyle for your short hair, 50s curls might be just what you need. This retro look is perfect for formal events, parties, or even just for everyday wear. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps of achieving these vintage curls on short hair.

How to Create 50s Curls on Short Hair

Before we start, it's important to note that you'll need some tools to achieve this hairstyle. Here's what you'll need:

  • Curling iron
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair clips
  • Hairspray

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and blow-drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use hair clips to section your hair into small sections. This will help you to curl your hair more easily and to achieve a more uniform look.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Start curling your hair with a curling iron. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the barrel of the curling iron. Hold for a few seconds and then release. Continue curling all the sections of your hair.

Step 4: Pin Your Curls

Once you've curled all your hair, pin the curls up with hair clips. This will help them to set and cool down. Leave the pins in for about 10 minutes.

Step 5: Remove the Pins

Once your curls have cooled down, remove the hair clips. Use your fingers to gently separate and loosen the curls.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your curls in place all day, spray them with hairspray. This will also add some shine to your hair.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect 50s Curls

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 50s curls on short hair:

  • Use a smaller curling iron for tighter curls.
  • Don't curl your hair all the way to the roots. Leave some space to avoid a "poodle" look.
  • If your hair is naturally straight, use a texturizing spray to add some grip to your hair before curling.
  • Use bobby pins instead of hair clips to hold your curls in place.
  • Experiment with the direction of your curls to create a more natural-looking hairstyle.

FAQs

Can I achieve 50s curls on short hair without a curling iron?

While a curling iron is the easiest way to achieve 50s curls, you can also use hot rollers or flexi rods.

Can I achieve 50s curls on short hair if my hair is naturally curly?

Yes, you can still achieve 50s curls on short hair if your hair is naturally curly. You may need to use a straightening iron to straighten some parts of your hair before curling.

How long will my 50s curls last?

Your 50s curls should last all day if you use hairspray and don't touch your hair too much.

Hair Care for 50s Curls

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray before curling.

With these steps, tips, and FAQs, you're now ready to achieve perfect 50s curls on short hair. Whether you're going for a retro look or just want to switch up your hairstyle, this classic hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
