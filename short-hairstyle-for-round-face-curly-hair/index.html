Short Hairstyle For Round Face Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Are you tired of dealing with unruly curly hair and struggling to find the perfect hairstyle to flatter your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will guide you through the best short hairstyles for round face curly hair, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Description

Round faces are characterized by having a circular shape and soft features. When it comes to curly hair, it's important to choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the crown, while keeping the sides and back tapered and close to the head. Short hairstyles are the perfect choice for round face curly hair, as they provide a flattering shape and are easy to maintain.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Round Face Curly Hair

When choosing a short hairstyle for round face curly hair, consider the following:

  • Length: Short hairstyles work best, but avoid going too short as it can make your face appear wider.
  • Layers: Layers add volume and texture to your hair, but avoid too many as it can make your hair look frizzy and unkempt.
  • Bangs: Side-swept or asymmetrical bangs can help elongate your face, but avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Curls: Embrace your curls and allow them to add natural volume and shape to your hair.

Short Hairstyles for Round Face Curly Hair

Here are some of the best short hairstyles for round face curly hair:

  • Pixie Cut: A classic short hairstyle that's perfect for round face curly hair. Keep the sides and back tapered, while adding volume to the crown.
  • Bob: A chin-length bob with layers and side-swept bangs can help elongate your face and add volume to your hair.
  • Cropped Curls: A short crop with tight curls can add texture and volume to your hair, while keeping the sides and back tapered.
  • Asymmetrical Cut: An asymmetrical cut with longer layers on one side can help elongate your face and add volume to your hair.

Step by Step Guide on How to Style Short Hairstyle for Round Face Curly Hair

Follow these simple steps to style your short hairstyle for round face curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat.
  4. Scrunch your hair to enhance your curls.
  5. Use a curling iron to define any loose curls.
  6. Apply a hair serum to add shine and tame frizz.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyle for Round Face Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle for round face curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: Will short hair make my round face look wider?

A: It depends on the length and style of your hair. Avoid going too short and opt for styles that add height to the crown, while keeping the sides and back tapered.

Q: Can I still wear bangs with round face curly hair?

A: Yes, side-swept or asymmetrical bangs can help elongate your face and add volume to your hair.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyle for Round Face Curly Hair

Follow these tips to care for your short hairstyle for round face curly hair:

  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as it can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as it can dry out your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid tying your hair up too tightly, as it can cause tension on your scalp.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can embrace your round face curly hair and rock a flattering short hairstyle with ease. Remember to care for your hair and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
