Little Boy Haircuts 2021 Trending Styles For Your Kid

Are you looking for some inspiration for your little boy's next haircut? Look no further than the top trending styles for 2021! From classic cuts to modern twists, there's something for every little guy. In this article, we'll cover how to choose the perfect cut for your child, step-by-step instructions for achieving each style, tips for maintenance and care, and answers to frequently asked questions. Let's dive in!

How to Choose the Perfect Cut

Before you even step foot in the salon or grab the clippers, it's important to consider a few factors when choosing the right haircut for your little boy. Think about his face shape, hair texture, and overall style. Does he have a round face? Opt for a longer cut that adds height to the top of his head. Does he have curly hair? Keep it shorter on the sides and longer on top for a trendy, textured look. Does he prefer a classic, clean-cut style or something more modern and edgy? Take all of these things into account before making your decision.

Classic Cuts

If your little boy prefers a more traditional look, there are plenty of classic cuts that never go out of style. The crew cut, buzz cut, and side part are all timeless options that are easy to maintain and always look polished.

The Crew Cut

The crew cut is a popular choice for little boys because it's short, simple, and easy to manage. It's also versatile enough to be styled in a variety of ways. To achieve this classic cut, ask your barber to use clippers to trim the hair on the top of your child's head to about 1/2 inch. The sides and back should be tapered down to about 1/8 inch.

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is another classic style that's perfect for little boys who want a low-maintenance look. This cut is achieved by using clippers to trim the hair all over to the same length, usually around 1/4 inch. It's a great option for active kids who don't want to spend a lot of time styling their hair.

The Side Part

The side part is a more formal option that's perfect for special occasions or school pictures. To achieve this look, ask your barber to create a deep side part and leave the hair longer on top, gradually tapering it down to a shorter length on the sides and back.

Modern Twists

If your child wants something a little more trendy and modern, there are plenty of options to choose from. The textured crop, messy fringe, and long comb-over are all popular styles that are sure to make a statement.

The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a popular style for little boys because it's trendy, yet easy to maintain. To achieve this look, ask your barber to leave the hair longer on top and textured, while tapering the sides and back. This style works well with all hair types and can be styled with a little bit of product for added texture and hold.

The Messy Fringe

The messy fringe is a fun, playful style that's perfect for little boys with wavy or curly hair. To achieve this look, leave the hair longer on top and create a messy fringe by cutting the hair at an angle. The sides and back should be tapered down to a shorter length for a clean, polished look.

The Long Comb-Over

The long comb-over is a modern take on a classic style. To achieve this look, leave the hair longer on top and create a deep side part. Comb the hair over to one side and use a little bit of product to hold it in place. The sides and back should be tapered down to a shorter length for a clean, polished look.

Step-by-Step Instructions

No matter what style you choose, it's important to follow proper cutting techniques to achieve the best results. Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving some of the most popular little boy haircuts:

The Crew Cut

  1. Start by using clippers to trim the hair on the top of your child's head to about 1/2 inch.
  2. Taper the sides and back down to about 1/8 inch.
  3. Blend the hair on the sides and top using scissors or clippers with a guard.
  4. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs on top that need to be blended in.
  5. Finish by trimming around the ears and neckline with scissors or clippers.

The Textured Crop

  1. Leave the hair longer on top and textured, while tapering the sides and back.
  2. Use scissors to create texture on the top by cutting into the hair at an angle.
  3. Blend the hair on the sides and top using scissors or clippers with a guard.
  4. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs on top that need to be blended in.
  5. Finish by trimming around the ears and neckline with scissors or clippers.

Tips for Maintenance and Care

Now that you've achieved the perfect little boy haircut, it's important to keep it looking fresh and polished. Here are some tips for maintenance and care:

  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.
  • Use a gentle, kid-friendly shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy.
  • Apply a small amount of hair product, such as gel or pomade, to add texture and hold.
  • Brush or comb the hair daily to prevent tangles and keep it looking neat.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to common questions about little boy haircuts:

What is the best haircut for a little boy?

The best haircut for a little boy depends on his face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Some classic cuts, such as the crew cut and side part, are always a good choice, while modern styles like the textured crop and messy fringe are great for trendier kids.

How often should I cut my child's hair?

It's recommended to trim your child's hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

What products should I use on my child's hair?

Use a gentle, kid-friendly shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy. You can also use a small amount of hair product, such as gel or pomade, to add texture and hold.

How do I keep my child's hair looking neat and tidy?

Brush or comb your child's hair daily to prevent tangles and keep it looking neat. You can also use a little bit of product to add texture and hold, and trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

With these tips and styles in mind, you're sure to find the perfect little boy haircut for your child in 2021. Whether you opt for a classic cut or a modern twist, make sure to follow proper cutting techniques and maintenance to keep the hair looking fresh and polished. Happy styling!
