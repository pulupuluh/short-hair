What Is 60 S Music

Written by Christine May 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

The 1960s was an era of change and revolution, and this was reflected in the music of the time. 60's music has become iconic and continues to influence artists today. In this blog post, we will explore what 60's music is and why it is still relevant today.

What is 60's Music?

60's music refers to the popular music that was created and performed during the 1960s. This music was heavily influenced by the cultural and social changes that were happening at the time. It was a decade of great change, and the music reflected this. There was a shift towards more experimental and innovative styles, and the music became more politically charged.

What are the Characteristics of 60's Music?

There are several characteristics that define 60's music:

  • The use of electric instruments such as guitars, keyboards, and drums
  • The incorporation of experimental and innovative styles
  • The use of political and social themes in the lyrics
  • The use of harmonies and vocal arrangements
  • The influence of various genres such as rock, pop, folk, and soul

Why is 60's Music Still Relevant Today?

60's music is still relevant today because it was a time of great change and revolution. The music of the 60s reflected the social and cultural changes that were occurring at the time. The themes and messages in the lyrics are still relevant today, and the music continues to inspire artists across the world. 60's music has a timeless quality that transcends generations, and it continues to influence and inspire new music today.

What are Some Examples of 60's Music?

Some examples of 60's music include:

  • The Beatles - "Hey Jude"
  • The Rolling Stones - "Satisfaction"
  • Bob Dylan - "Blowin' in the Wind"
  • The Beach Boys - "Good Vibrations"
  • Janis Joplin - "Piece of my Heart"

How Can You Listen to 60's Music?

There are several ways to listen to 60's music:

  • Streaming services such as Spotify or Apple Music
  • Purchasing CDs or vinyl records
  • Listening to online radio stations that play 60's music

What is the Legacy of 60's Music?

The legacy of 60's music is vast and far-reaching. It has influenced countless artists and continues to inspire new music today. The music of the 60s reflected the social and cultural changes of the time, and it helped to shape the world we live in today. 60's music will always be remembered as a revolutionary and transformative time in music history.

In Conclusion

60's music is a timeless and iconic genre that continues to influence and inspire artists today. It was a time of great change and revolution, and the music reflected this. The characteristics of 60's music, such as the use of electric instruments and the incorporation of political and social themes, continue to define the genre. 60's music has a lasting legacy that will continue to inspire new music for generations to come.
