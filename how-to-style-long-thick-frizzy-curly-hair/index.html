How To Style Long Thick Frizzy Curly Hair

Written by Christine Aug 08, 2023 · 9 min read

Long thick frizzy curly hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to style long thick frizzy curly hair, along with some tips and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

How to Style Long Thick Frizzy Curly Hair

Before we begin, it’s important to understand that everyone’s hair is different, and what works for one person may not work for another. However, there are some general steps you can follow to style your long thick frizzy curly hair.

The key to styling curly hair is to keep it moisturized and defined. This means using products that provide moisture and hold, and avoiding products that can dry out your hair.

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Begin by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to frizz. Once you’ve washed your hair, gently squeeze out the excess water with a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as this can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair. This will help to moisturize your curls and provide some hold. Work the conditioner through your hair with your fingers, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Use a Styling Product

Choose a styling product that works for your hair type and desired look. If you want defined curls, use a curl cream or gel. If you prefer a more natural look, try a mousse or styling foam. Apply the product to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up. Be sure to apply enough product to coat all of your curls.

Step 4: Diffuse or Air Dry

Once you’ve applied your styling product, you can either diffuse your hair or let it air dry. If you’re in a hurry, use a diffuser to dry your hair quickly. If you have more time, let your hair air dry. Avoid touching your hair while it’s drying, as this can cause frizz.

Step 5: Scrunch Out the Crunch

After your hair is dry, use your fingers to scrunch out any crunchiness from the styling product. This will help to loosen up your curls and make them look more natural.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling long thick frizzy curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Squeeze out the excess water with a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair.
  4. Choose a styling product that works for your hair type and desired look.
  5. Apply the styling product to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  6. Diffuse your hair or let it air dry.
  7. Scrunch out the crunchiness from the styling product.
  8. Use a hair oil or serum to add shine and tame any flyaways.
  9. Style your hair as desired.
  10. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Styling Long Thick Frizzy Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your long thick frizzy curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, which can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Refresh your curls on non-wash days by spritzing them with water and applying a small amount of styling product.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of haircut is best for long thick frizzy curly hair?

A layered haircut can help to remove some of the bulk from your hair and create more defined curls. However, it’s important to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair, as a bad haircut can make your hair look even frizzier.

How often should I wash my long thick frizzy curly hair?

It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days, as washing it too often can strip your hair of its natural oils. However, this can vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.

What’s the best way to sleep with long thick frizzy curly hair?

Try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid to prevent tangles and frizz. You can also use a silk or satin scarf or bonnet to protect your curls while you sleep.

Hair Care for Long Thick Frizzy Curly Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your long thick frizzy curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, which can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, which can dry out your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your long thick frizzy curly hair with confidence. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
