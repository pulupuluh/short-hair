Haircuts For Long Faces Flattering Styles For Your Face Shape

Written by Bardi Jul 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut that flatters your long face shape? Look no further, as we've got you covered! In this article, we'll provide you with tips, tricks, and haircut ideas to make the most of your long face shape. Let's dive in!

Description: Understanding Long Face Shapes

First and foremost, it's important to understand what a long face shape is. A long face typically has more length than width, with a high forehead and a narrow chin. The aim of a good haircut for long faces is to balance out the proportions and create the illusion of width.

How to: Choosing the Right Haircut for Your Long Face Shape

When it comes to choosing a haircut for a long face, the key is to create width and volume around the sides of the face. This can be achieved through layers, waves, and curls. Avoid hairstyles with too much length, as this can elongate the face even more.

Step by Step: Getting a Haircut for a Long Face

1. Consult with your hairstylist about your face shape and the best haircut options for you.

2. Consider adding layers to create movement and volume around the sides of your face.

3. Opt for styles that fall just above or below the collarbone to avoid elongating your face.

4. Consider side-swept bangs to create the illusion of width.

5. Embrace waves and curls to add volume and softness to your look.

Tips: Maintaining Your Haircut for Long Faces

Once you've got the perfect haircut for your long face shape, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking fresh and flattering. Here are some tips:

- Schedule regular trims to keep your haircut looking sharp.

- Use volumizing products to add body and bounce to your hair.

- Avoid heavy, straight styles that can make your face look longer.

- Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ: Common Questions About Haircuts for Long Faces

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a long face. The key is to add layers and volume to create width and balance.

Q: Should I avoid center parts if I have a long face?

A: It's not necessary to avoid center parts if you have a long face, but side-swept bangs can be more flattering as they create the illusion of width.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

A: Avoid hairstyles with too much length, as this can elongate the face even more. Heavy, straight styles can also make your face look longer.

Hair Care: Keeping Your Long Hair Healthy

Finally, it's important to keep your long hair healthy and nourished. Here are some tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

- Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and shiny.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

- Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect haircut for your long face shape. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
