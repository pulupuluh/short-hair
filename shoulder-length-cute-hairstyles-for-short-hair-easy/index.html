Shoulder Length Cute Hairstyles For Short Hair Easy

Written by Aprilia Apr 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short hair can be a challenge when it comes to finding cute hairstyles, but shoulder length hair is the perfect length to try different styles. In this article, we will provide you with some easy and cute hairstyles that you can try on your shoulder length short hair.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Shoulder length hair is a great length to try different hairstyles because it's long enough to style but short enough to be manageable. The hairstyles we have included in this article are easy to do and perfect for those who are always on the go. These hairstyles can be done in minutes and are perfect for any occasion.

How to

The hairstyles we have included in this article are easy to do and require minimal effort. All you need is a few hair accessories and styling products to make your hair look fabulous. These hairstyles can be done on any hair texture and can be customized to suit your personal style.

Step by step

1. Half-up top knot

Start by separating the top section of your hair from the bottom section. Gather the top section of your hair and twist it into a knot. Secure the knot with bobby pins and leave the bottom section of your hair down.

2. Messy bun

Gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a bun. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look. Secure the bun with bobby pins and hairspray.

3. Braided headband

Take a small section of hair from the front of your hairline and braid it. Pin the braid behind your ear and repeat on the other side. This will create a braided headband effect.

4. Side swept bangs

If you have bangs, sweep them to the side and secure them with bobby pins. This will create a chic and effortless look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your short shoulder length hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.
  • Invest in some good quality bobby pins and hair elastics.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and accessories.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair in between washes.

FAQ

What hair texture is best for these hairstyles?

These hairstyles can be done on any hair texture, whether it's straight, wavy, or curly.

How long does it take to do these hairstyles?

These hairstyles can be done in minutes and are perfect for those who are always on the go.

Hair Care

To keep your short shoulder length hair looking healthy and beautiful, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Use a deep conditioning mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

With these easy and cute shoulder length hairstyles for short hair, you can change up your look and feel confident in no time. Remember to experiment with different styles and accessories to find the perfect look for you.
