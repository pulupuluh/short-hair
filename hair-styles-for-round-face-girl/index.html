Are you a round-faced girl searching for the perfect hair style that will complement your features? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best hair styles for round face girls along with how-to's, step-by-step guides, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hair styles for round face girls, let's first determine if you have a round face shape. Here's how:

  1. Take a photo of yourself facing the camera directly. Make sure your hair is pulled back and your face is not covered.
  2. Using a ruler or measuring tape, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Compare the measurements. If they are all about the same width, you have a round face shape.

Now that you know your face shape, let's move on to the fun part: choosing the best hair style for your round face!

Hair Styles for Round Face Girls

When it comes to hair styles for round face girls, the goal is to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. Here are some hair styles that will do just that:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for round face girls as they help to elongate the face. Keep the layers around your face longer to create the illusion of a more oval shape.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for round face girls. They help to create angles on the face and draw attention away from the width.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a great way to add texture and movement to your hair. They also help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the width.

4. High Ponytail

A high ponytail is a simple and easy hair style that works wonders for round face girls. It helps to elongate the face and draw attention upwards.

5. Bob with Volume

A bob with volume is a great way to create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. The volume at the crown helps to elongate the face, while the length helps to balance out the width.

Tips for Round Face Girls

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair style for your round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin as they can make the face appear wider.
  • Avoid center parts as they can also make the face appear wider.
  • Opt for soft layers and side parts to create angles on the face.
  • Consider highlights or lowlights to add dimension and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair styles for round face girls:

1. Can I still wear my hair in a bun?

Absolutely! Just make sure to leave some hair around your face to create angles.

2. Should I avoid short haircuts?

Not necessarily. Short haircuts can work for round face girls as long as they have some volume at the crown and are not too blunt.

3. Can I still wear a center part?

While a center part is not the most flattering for round face girls, it can still be worn. Just make sure to add some volume at the crown to create angles.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have chosen the perfect hair style for your round face, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.

With these tips and hair styles for round face girls, you are sure to find the perfect look that will complement your features and make you feel confident!
