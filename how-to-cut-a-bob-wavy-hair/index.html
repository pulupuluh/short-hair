How To Cut A Bob Wavy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Mar 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your long, wavy hair and want to switch up your look with a chic and trendy bob haircut? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut a bob wavy hair that will leave you feeling confident and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut a Bob Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting a Bob Wavy Hair

How to Cut a Bob Wavy Hair

Before we get started with the step-by-step guide, let's go over some of the tools you will need to cut a bob wavy hair. You will need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair.

It's important to note that cutting your hair can be tricky and it's always recommended to get your hair cut by a professional. However, if you are feeling confident and want to try it out for yourself, follow these steps below.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.
  2. Comb your hair to remove any tangles and part it in the middle.
  3. Section your hair into two parts and clip them up.
  4. Take one section of your hair and comb it straight down.
  5. Use your scissors to cut your hair in a straight line, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Remember to cut small sections at a time and to keep checking the length.
  6. Repeat the same process on the other side of your hair.
  7. Once you have cut both sides, unclip your hair and comb it straight down.
  8. Use your scissors to trim any uneven parts or strands that are longer than the rest.
  9. Style your hair to your liking. You can add some waves or curls to give it some texture.
  10. Lastly, use a hairspray or hair oil to keep your hair in place and give it some shine.

Tips for Cutting a Bob Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a bob wavy hair:

  • Start with small sections at a time to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • When cutting, hold your scissors at an angle to give your hair some texture.
  • Use hair clips to keep your hair in place and prevent it from falling in your face.
  • Take breaks in between cutting to avoid getting tired or making mistakes.
  • Always cut less than you think you need to. You can always go back and trim more if needed.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your own hair at home. However, it's always recommended to get your hair cut by a professional to avoid any mistakes or accidents.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain its health and shape.

How do I know if a bob wavy hair will suit me?

You can try on wigs or use online hair simulation tools to see how a bob wavy hair will look on you. It's also important to consult with a hair stylist to get their professional opinion.

Hair Care after Cutting a Bob Wavy Hair

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and style. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.
  • Use hair oils or serums to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.

And there you have it! A step-by-step guide on how to cut a bob wavy hair. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't forget to take care of your hair after cutting it.
