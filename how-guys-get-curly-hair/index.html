How Guys Can Get Curly Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Bardi Apr 28, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you a guy who's always wanted curly hair? Maybe you're tired of your straight locks and want to switch things up. Or perhaps you're just curious about what it would be like to have curly hair. Whatever the reason, you're not alone. In this guide, we'll show you how guys can get curly hair in 2023. From styling methods to hair care tips, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Get Curly Hair

Before we dive into the specifics, let's talk about what it means to have curly hair. Curly hair is hair that grows in a spiral shape, rather than straight. This shape is caused by the curvature of the hair follicle. While some people are born with naturally curly hair, others can achieve the look through styling methods.

If you're looking to get curly hair, there are a few different methods you can try:

Perms

A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the texture of your hair. During a perm, your hair is wrapped around rods and a solution is applied to break down the bonds in your hair. Once the bonds are broken, the hair is reshaped to form curls. Perms can last for several months, but they can also damage your hair if not done correctly.

Curling Irons

A curling iron is a tool that heats up and allows you to curl your hair manually. Curling irons come in different sizes, so you can choose the size of curl you want. While curling irons can be a quick way to achieve curls, they can also cause damage to your hair if used too often.

Natural Methods

If you're looking for a more natural way to get curly hair, there are a few things you can try. One method is to braid your hair before bed and then undo the braids in the morning. This can create natural-looking waves and curls. You can also try using a sea salt spray, which can enhance your hair's natural texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you've decided to try one of the methods listed above, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve curly hair:

Perms

  1. Find a reputable hair salon that offers perms.
  2. Consult with a stylist to determine if your hair is healthy enough for a perm.
  3. Select the size of rod you want to use for your curls.
  4. Sit through the perm process, which can take several hours.
  5. Care for your hair properly after the perm to prevent damage.

Curling Irons

  1. Choose the size of curling iron you want to use.
  2. Heat up the curling iron.
  3. Section off your hair and begin curling one section at a time.
  4. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release.
  5. Repeat until all sections of hair are curled.

Natural Methods

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section off your hair and begin braiding each section.
  3. Secure the end of each braid with a hair tie.
  4. Sleep with the braids in overnight.
  5. Undo the braids in the morning and run your fingers through your hair to create natural-looking waves.
  6. Spray your hair with a sea salt spray to enhance the texture.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Once you've achieved curly hair, you'll want to make sure you're taking care of it properly. Here are a few tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Sleep with a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent tangles.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are a few frequently asked questions about getting curly hair:

Can guys get perms?

Yes, guys can get perms. Perms are not just for women.

Will a perm damage my hair?

A perm can damage your hair if not done correctly. Make sure to consult with a stylist who has experience with perms before getting one.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's best to wash curly hair no more than two or three times per week to prevent dryness.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a specific type of care to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often.
  • Detangle your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to add moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your curly hair looking healthy and stylish.

Conclusion

If you're a guy who's interested in getting curly hair, there are several methods you can try. Whether you opt for a perm, a curling iron, or a natural method, make sure to take care of your hair properly to prevent damage. By following the tips in this guide, you can achieve and maintain curly hair in 2023 and beyond.
