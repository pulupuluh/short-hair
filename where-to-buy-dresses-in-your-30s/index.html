Where To Buy Dresses In Your 30S A Guide For Fashion Forward Women

Written by Frank Feb 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a woman in your 30s looking for the perfect dress? Whether you're attending a wedding, date night, or just want to add some new pieces to your wardrobe, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll explore the best places to buy dresses in your 30s, as well as tips on how to find the perfect dress for your body type and personal style.

How to Find the Perfect Dress

Before we dive into where to buy dresses in your 30s, let's talk about how to find the perfect dress. First, consider your body type. Are you hourglass, pear, apple, or straight? Knowing your body shape will help you choose a dress that flatters your figure. Next, think about your personal style. Do you prefer classic, trendy, or bohemian? Finally, consider the occasion. Is it formal or casual? Day or night? Once you have these factors in mind, you can start shopping for the perfect dress.

Where to Buy Dresses in Your 30s

1. Nordstrom

Nordstrom is a department store that offers a wide range of dresses for women in their 30s. They carry dresses from popular brands like Ted Baker, Rachel Parcell, and Eliza J. Nordstrom also offers free shipping and returns, so you can try on dresses at home and return them hassle-free.

2. Reformation

Reformation is a sustainable fashion brand that offers stylish dresses for women of all ages. Their dresses are made from eco-friendly materials and are designed to flatter a variety of body types. Reformation also offers free shipping and returns, as well as a virtual try-on feature that lets you see how the dress will look on your body.

3. ASOS

ASOS is an online retailer that offers a vast selection of dresses for women in their 30s. They carry dresses from both their in-house brand and other popular brands like French Connection and Free People. ASOS also offers free shipping and returns, as well as a feature that lets you filter dresses by body type and occasion.

4. Anthropologie

Anthropologie is a lifestyle brand that offers unique and elegant dresses for women in their 30s. Their dresses feature intricate details and high-quality materials, making them perfect for special occasions. Anthropologie also offers free shipping on orders over $50 and a generous return policy.

Tips for Buying Dresses in Your 30s

Now that you know where to buy dresses in your 30s, here are some tips to help you make the most of your shopping experience:

  • Try on dresses in person whenever possible to get a better idea of how they fit and feel.
  • Read reviews and look for photos of the dress on real people to get a better idea of how it looks in real life.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles, even if they're outside your comfort zone.
  • Invest in high-quality dresses that will last for several seasons.
  • Accessorize your dress with jewelry, shoes, and a clutch to complete the look.

FAQ: Where to Buy Dresses in Your 30s

Q: What type of dress is best for a wedding?

A: It depends on the dress code of the wedding. For a formal wedding, a long gown or cocktail dress is appropriate. For a semi-formal or daytime wedding, a knee-length or midi dress is a good choice.

Q: What type of dress is best for a date?

A: It depends on the location and vibe of the date. For a fancy dinner, a little black dress or a maxi dress is appropriate. For a casual date, a sundress or a denim dress is a good choice.

Hair Care for Dress Shopping

When shopping for dresses, it's essential to look and feel your best. One way to do this is by taking care of your hair. Here are some tips for healthy, shiny hair:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using heat tools like curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to hydrate and nourish your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and recommendations, you're ready to find the perfect dress for any occasion. Happy shopping!
