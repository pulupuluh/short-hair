Short Haircuts For Thin Curly Hair 2019 Tips How To And Hair Care

Written by Aprilia Feb 26, 2023 · 11 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to refresh your look? If you have thin curly hair, you might think that short haircuts are not for you. But don’t worry, there are plenty of short haircuts that can complement your curls and make your hair appear fuller. In this article, we will guide you through the best short haircuts for thin curly hair 2019, how to style them, and hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and beautiful.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Thin Curly Hair 2019

Before choosing a short haircut for your thin curly hair, there are a few things you need to consider to make sure the style complements your face shape and hair texture.

First, consider your face shape. Oval, heart-shaped, and square face shapes tend to suit most short haircuts. If you have a round face, opt for a longer bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs to elongate your face.

Second, consider your hair texture. Thin curly hair tends to lack volume, so you want to choose a haircut that creates the illusion of fuller hair. Layered haircuts, bobs, and pixie cuts are great options for thin curly hair because they add texture and movement to the hair.

Lastly, consider your lifestyle and how much time you want to spend styling your hair. Short haircuts require more maintenance than longer hair, so choose a style that you feel comfortable maintaining.

Bob Haircut for Thin Curly Hair

The bob haircut is a classic that never goes out of style. It’s a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types, including thin curly hair.

A chin-length bob with layers is a great option for thin curly hair because it adds volume and movement to the hair. You can also opt for a longer bob with side-swept bangs to frame your face and elongate your features.

To style a bob haircut for thin curly hair, apply a volumizing mousse to your hair and blow-dry with a diffuser attachment. Use a curling iron to define your curls and finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Pixie Cut for Thin Curly Hair

If you’re feeling bold, a pixie cut is a great way to show off your curls and add volume to your hair. A pixie cut is a short haircut that is typically cut close to the head with short layers.

A pixie cut for thin curly hair should be cut slightly longer on top with shorter layers on the sides and back to create texture and volume. You can also add side-swept bangs to soften the look and frame your face.

To style a pixie cut for thin curly hair, apply a curl-enhancing cream to damp hair and scrunch with your hands. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair and define your curls. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Layered Haircut for Thin Curly Hair

A layered haircut is a great option for thin curly hair because it adds volume and movement to the hair. A layered haircut involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create texture and dimension.

If you have thin curly hair, opt for a layered haircut that is shorter in the back and longer in the front to create the illusion of fuller hair. You can also add side-swept bangs to frame your face.

To style a layered haircut for thin curly hair, apply a curl-enhancing cream to damp hair and scrunch with your hands. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair and define your curls. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

How to Style Short Haircuts for Thin Curly Hair 2019

Styling short haircuts for thin curly hair can be tricky, but with the right tools and products, you can achieve a beautiful and voluminous look.

First, start with clean, damp hair. Apply a curl-enhancing cream to your hair to define your curls and add moisture. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair and avoid frizz.

Next, use a curling iron to define your curls and add volume to your hair. If you have a bob or a pixie cut, use a small curling iron to curl the ends of your hair for a polished look.

Finally, finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place. Avoid using too much hairspray to prevent your hair from looking stiff and unnatural.

Tips to Keep Your Thin Curly Hair Healthy

To keep your thin curly hair healthy and beautiful, follow these tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to add moisture and prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote hair growth.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet to prevent breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb instead.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I dye my thin curly hair?

Yes, you can dye your thin curly hair, but be cautious of using harsh chemicals that can damage your hair. Opt for a semi-permanent or demi-permanent hair color that is gentle on your hair and won’t cause breakage.

How often should I wash my thin curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Can I wear a short haircut if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear a short haircut if you have a round face. Opt for a longer bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs to elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer face shape.

Can I wear a short haircut if I have thin curly hair?

Yes, you can wear a short haircut if you have thin curly hair. Opt for a layered haircut, bob, or pixie cut to add volume and movement to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect short haircut for your thin curly hair in 2019. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right products to keep your locks healthy and beautiful.
