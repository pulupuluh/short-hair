How To Dress With Style Over 60

Written by Oliver May 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we get older, we may feel like we need to dress more conservatively or give up on fashion altogether. However, that couldn't be further from the truth! Age is just a number, and you can still dress with style over 60. Here are some tips and tricks to help you look and feel your best.

  • Tip #2: Embrace Color
  • Tip #3: Accessorize
  • Tip #4: Invest in Quality Pieces
  • Tip #5: Don't Be Afraid to Experiment

Tip #1: Dress for Your Body Type

One of the most important things to consider when dressing with style over 60 is your body type. As we age, our bodies may change, and it's important to dress in a way that flatters your figure. Whether you're pear-shaped, apple-shaped, or hourglass-shaped, there are plenty of clothing options that will make you look and feel amazing.

For example, if you're pear-shaped, you may want to choose pants with a wider leg to balance out your hips. If you're apple-shaped, you may want to choose tops with a v-neckline to elongate your torso. And if you're hourglass-shaped, you may want to choose clothing that accentuates your waist.

Tip #2: Embrace Color

Another way to dress with style over 60 is to embrace color. While black and other neutrals can be chic and sophisticated, adding a pop of color to your outfit can be a great way to show off your personality and make a statement.

Don't be afraid to experiment with different colors and patterns. Whether you choose a bright red coat or a floral blouse, adding some color to your wardrobe can help you stand out and feel confident.

Tip #3: Accessorize

Accessories can be a game-changer when it comes to dressing with style over 60. A statement necklace, a colorful scarf, or a stylish hat can take your outfit to the next level and help you express your creativity.

However, it's important not to go overboard with accessories. Choose one or two pieces that complement your outfit and make you feel good.

Tip #4: Invest in Quality Pieces

Investing in quality pieces is another key to dressing with style over 60. While it can be tempting to buy cheap, trendy clothing, investing in high-quality pieces that will last for years can save you money in the long run and help you look and feel your best.

When shopping, look for pieces made from high-quality materials like silk, cashmere, and leather. These materials not only look and feel luxurious but also have a timeless appeal that will never go out of style.

Tip #5: Don't Be Afraid to Experiment

Finally, don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and trends. Just because you're over 60 doesn't mean you have to stick to a certain type of clothing or style.

Try out new trends, mix and match different pieces, and have fun with your wardrobe. Fashion is all about expressing yourself, so don't be afraid to let your personality shine through.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Determine your body type and choose clothing that flatters your figure.

2. Experiment with different colors and patterns to add some personality to your wardrobe.

3. Invest in high-quality pieces that will last for years.

4. Accessorize with one or two statement pieces to take your outfit to the next level.

5. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and trends.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear trendy clothing over 60?

A: Absolutely! Just because you're over 60 doesn't mean you have to give up on trendy clothing. However, it's important to choose trends that work for your body type and personal style.

Q: How can I find clothing that fits well?

A: One option is to work with a tailor to ensure that your clothing fits you perfectly. You can also look for brands that offer petite or plus-size options, depending on your body type.

Q: What should I wear to a formal event?

A: For a formal event, you may want to choose a classic dress or suit in a neutral color like black or navy. You can also add some sparkle with statement jewelry or a clutch.

Hair Care

In addition to your wardrobe, your hair can also play a big role in your overall style. As we age, our hair may become thinner or more brittle, so it's important to take good care of it.

Some tips for healthy, stylish hair include using gentle shampoos and conditioners, avoiding heat styling tools, and using products designed for your hair type.

With these tips and tricks, you can dress with style over 60 and feel confident and fabulous at any age.
