How Long Does It Take For A Pixie Cut To Grow To Shoulder Length

Written by Frank May 17, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you've recently chopped your hair into a pixie cut, you may be wondering how long it will take for it to grow back to shoulder length. The answer isn't straightforward, as hair growth varies from person to person. However, on average, hair grows about half an inch per month. In this blog post, we'll dive deeper into how long it takes for a pixie cut to grow to shoulder length, and provide some tips on how to care for your hair during the growing process.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that typically sits above the ears and around the nape of the neck. It's a trendy and low-maintenance haircut that's perfect for those who want to make a statement with their hair. However, as with any haircut, there may come a time when you want to grow it out. The process of growing out a pixie cut can be frustrating, but it's important to be patient and take care of your hair along the way.

How to Grow Out a Pixie Cut

Growing out a pixie cut can be a daunting task, but there are a few things you can do to make the process easier. Here are some tips:

1. Get Regular Trims

While it may seem counterintuitive to cut your hair when you're trying to grow it out, getting regular trims can actually help it grow faster. Trimming your hair every six to eight weeks will prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down the growth process.

2. Be Patient

As mentioned earlier, hair grows about half an inch per month on average. This means that it can take anywhere from six months to a year to grow out a pixie cut to shoulder length. It's important to be patient and not get discouraged if your hair isn't growing as quickly as you'd like.

3. Experiment with Different Hairstyles

While you're growing out your pixie cut, it's a great time to experiment with different hairstyles. Try out different hair accessories, like headbands or clips, or play around with different ways to style your hair. This will help make the growing out process more fun and less frustrating.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to grow out a pixie cut:

Step 1: Get Regular Trims

As mentioned earlier, getting regular trims will help prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down the growth process. Make sure to schedule a trim every six to eight weeks.

Step 2: Be Patient

It's important to be patient when growing out a pixie cut. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month, so it may take several months or even a year to grow your hair out to shoulder length.

Step 3: Experiment with Different Hairstyles

While you're growing out your pixie cut, try out different hairstyles and hair accessories to make the process more fun. You can also play around with different ways to style your hair, like using a curling iron or straightening it.

Step 4: Take Care of Your Hair

It's important to take care of your hair during the growing out process. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat tools too often. You can also use hair masks and oils to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you grow out your pixie cut:

1. Be Gentle When Brushing Your Hair

When your hair is still short, it can be easier to accidentally pull on it when brushing. Be gentle when brushing your hair, and use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles.

2. Use Hair Accessories

Adding a headband, scarf, or clip to your hair can help add some variety to your look while you're growing out your pixie cut.

3. Get a Haircut When You Need One

Even if you're trying to grow out your hair, it's still important to get a haircut when you need one. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

How long does it take for a pixie cut to grow to shoulder length?

On average, hair grows about half an inch per month. This means that it can take anywhere from six months to a year to grow out a pixie cut to shoulder length.

Can I make my hair grow faster?

While there's no guaranteed way to make your hair grow faster, there are some things you can do to promote healthy hair growth. These include eating a balanced diet, getting enough sleep, and reducing stress.

Should I use heat tools on my hair while growing it out?

It's best to avoid using heat tools on your hair too often while you're growing it out. Heat can damage your hair and slow down the growth process.

Hair Care

While you're growing out your pixie cut, it's important to take care of your hair to promote healthy hair growth. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a gentle shampoo and conditioner will help keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain nourishing ingredients like argan oil or coconut oil.

2. Use Hair Masks and Oils

Using a hair mask or oil once a week can help keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter or avocado oil.

3. Avoid Using Heat Tools Too Often

Heat can damage your hair and slow down the growth process. Try to limit your use of heat tools, like curling irons and straighteners, and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

4. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth. Make sure to include plenty of protein, fruits, and vegetables in your diet.

In conclusion, growing out a pixie cut to shoulder length can take anywhere from six months to a year. It's important to be patient and take care of your hair during the growing process. By following the tips and hair care advice in this blog post, you can help promote healthy hair growth and make the process more manageable.
