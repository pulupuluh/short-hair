How To Use Mousse On Wavy Frizzy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Aug 28, 2023 · 9 min read

Wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful and effortless on some days, on others it can be a frizzy and unmanageable mess. That’s where mousse comes in. This versatile styling product can help you achieve defined waves, tame frizz, and add volume to your hair. In this article, we’ll show you how to use mousse on wavy frizzy hair for a flawless and polished look.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Use Mousse on Wavy Frizzy Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Using Mousse on Wavy Frizzy Hair
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Wavy Frizzy Hair

Description

Mousse is a styling product that comes in a foam form. It is lightweight and easy to apply, making it a popular choice for people with wavy or curly hair. Mousse works by adding volume, texture, and hold to your hair. It can also help define your waves or curls and control frizz. However, not all mousses are created equal. Look for a mousse that is specifically designed for wavy or curly hair, as these will contain ingredients that moisturize and nourish your hair while keeping it in place.

How to Use Mousse on Wavy Frizzy Hair

The best way to use mousse on wavy frizzy hair is to apply it to damp hair after washing. Here’s how:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Wavy hair tends to be dry, so it’s important to use products that hydrate and nourish your hair. Avoid using hot water, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more prone to frizz.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently squeeze out excess water from your hair using a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously, as it can cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply Mousse

Take a golf ball-sized amount of mousse and spread it evenly through your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. For best results, use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the mousse evenly.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Use your hands to scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp. This will help enhance your waves and create volume. Repeat this process until you’ve scrunched your entire head of hair.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Dry Naturally

Avoid using a hairdryer, as it can cause frizz. Instead, let your hair air dry naturally. Once your hair is completely dry, you can gently scrunch it again to break up any crunchiness caused by the mousse.

Tips for Using Mousse on Wavy Frizzy Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using mousse on wavy frizzy hair:

  • Use a mousse that is specifically designed for wavy or curly hair.
  • Avoid using too much mousse, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Don’t touch your hair too much while it’s drying, as it can cause frizz.
  • If you want more volume, flip your head upside down and scrunch your hair while it’s still damp.
  • Once your hair is dry, use a serum or oil to add shine and tame any flyaways.

FAQs

Q: Can I use mousse on dry hair?

A: While it’s best to use mousse on damp hair, you can also use it on dry hair to refresh your waves or curls. Simply apply a small amount of mousse to your hands and scrunch it through your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.

Q: Will mousse make my hair crunchy?

A: Mousse can make your hair feel crunchy if you use too much or don’t distribute it evenly. To avoid this, use a small amount of mousse and spread it evenly through your hair using a wide-tooth comb. Once your hair is dry, you can gently scrunch it to break up any crunchiness.

Q: How often should I use mousse?

A: It’s best to use mousse on wavy frizzy hair every time you wash your hair. However, if you have fine hair, you may want to use it every other wash to avoid weighing down your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep your wavy frizzy hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a weekly hair mask to hydrate and nourish your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair while it’s dry, as it can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it’s wet.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase, as it can help reduce frizz and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and make it more prone to frizz.

By following these tips and using mousse on wavy frizzy hair, you can achieve a beautiful and polished look that will turn heads. Remember to choose a mousse that is specifically designed for your hair type and use it sparingly to avoid weighing down your hair. With a little bit of effort and the right products, you can transform your wavy frizzy hair into a gorgeous and manageable mane.
