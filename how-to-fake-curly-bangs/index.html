How To Fake Curly Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Apr 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're hesitant to cut bangs but still want to try out the look, you're in luck. You can fake curly bangs without having to commit to a haircut. In this post, we'll go over some simple steps on how to achieve curly bangs without the commitment.

Description

Faking curly bangs can be a fun and easy way to switch up your hairstyle without having to commit to a haircut. This style is perfect for those who want to try out bangs but are hesitant to cut their hair. With a few simple steps, you can achieve the look of curly bangs and rock a new style.

How to Fake Curly Bangs

If you're ready to try out this fun and easy hairstyle, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Begin by washing and drying your hair. You'll want to start with clean, dry hair to ensure that your curls hold their shape.

Step 2: Section off your bangs

Next, section off your bangs. Use a comb to create a triangle shape in the front of your hair. Make sure that the triangle is centered and that the point of the triangle is at the top of your forehead.

Step 3: Curl your bangs

Now it's time to curl your bangs. Use a curling iron or wand to curl your bangs away from your face. Hold the curling iron for 10-15 seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process until all of your bangs are curled.

Step 4: Set your curls

Once you've curled your bangs, use a setting spray to keep them in place. This will ensure that your curls hold their shape throughout the day.

Step 5: Style the rest of your hair

Now that your bangs are curled, style the rest of your hair as desired. You can leave your hair down or pull it back into a ponytail or bun.

Tips for Faking Curly Bangs

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect curly bangs:

  • Use a smaller curling iron or wand to create tighter curls
  • Use a larger curling iron or wand to create looser curls
  • Don't over-curl your bangs - this can make them look too curly and unnatural
  • Use a light-hold hairspray to keep your curls in place without weighing them down

FAQs

Q: How long will my curly bangs last?

A: Your curly bangs should last all day as long as you use a setting spray to keep them in place.

Q: Can I fake curly bangs with straight hair?

A: Yes, you can fake curly bangs with straight hair. Just follow the steps above, and make sure to use a curling iron or wand to create the curls.

Q: Will faking curly bangs damage my hair?

A: No, faking curly bangs should not damage your hair as long as you use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Bangs

To keep your curly bangs looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid brushing your curls - use a wide-tooth comb instead
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often

With these tips and tricks, you can fake curly bangs and rock a new hairstyle without having to commit to a haircut. Have fun experimenting with different styles and remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
