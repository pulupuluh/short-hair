Little Boy Haircuts Short On Sides Longer On Top

Written by Luffy Mar 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for your little boy, then you've come to the right place. The little boy haircuts short on sides longer on top have been in trend for a while now, and it does not seem to be going anywhere. In this blog post, we will be discussing the how-to, tips, and hair care for this hairstyle.

Description

The little boy haircuts short on sides longer on top hairstyle is a modern take on the classic undercut. In this haircut, the sides are kept short, and the top is left longer, creating a contrast between the lengths. This style is versatile and can be styled in various ways, making it a popular choice among parents and their little boys.

How to

To achieve the little boy haircuts short on sides longer on top, you will need a few basic tools, such as a clipper, scissors, comb, and hair gel or pomade. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Wash and dry hair

Start by washing your little boy's hair and towel-drying it. It's easier to work with clean hair and to see the natural hairline.

Step 2: Clipper the sides

Using the clipper, cut the sides of your little boy's hair to the desired length. You can start with a higher number guard and work your way down until you reach the desired length.

Step 3: Cut the top

Using scissors, cut the top of your little boy's hair to the desired length. You can start by cutting the hair longer than you want and then trim it down gradually until you achieve the perfect length.

Step 4: Blend the sides and top

Use scissors to blend the sides and the top part of the hair. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 5: Style the hair

Apply hair gel or pomade to the hair and style it as desired. You can create a side part, slick it back, or leave it messy.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your little boy's short on sides longer on top haircut:

  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain its shape and style.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Apply hair gel or pomade sparingly to prevent the hair from looking greasy or clumpy.
  • Consider using a hair conditioner to keep the hair soft and manageable.

FAQ

What face shape suits this haircut?

The little boy haircuts short on sides longer on top hairstyle suits most face shapes, including round, oval, and square faces.

Is this hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle can be achieved on all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, the length and style may vary depending on the hair type.

How often should I trim my little boy's hair?

You should trim your little boy's hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care

To keep your little boy's hair healthy and shiny, you should follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a mild shampoo to remove dirt and oil buildup.
  • Use a conditioner to keep the hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons, as it can damage the hair.
  • Protect the hair from the sun's harmful UV rays by wearing a hat or applying a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

And that's it! You now know everything there is to know about little boy haircuts short on sides longer on top. This hairstyle is trendy, stylish, and easy to maintain, making it a popular choice among parents and kids alike. Remember to follow the tips and hair care advice to keep your little boy's hair healthy and shiny.
