Oval Face Shape Long Hair Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 29, 2023 · 7 min read

Long hair has always been a symbol of style and masculinity for men. However, not all men can pull off the long hair look, especially if they have an oval face shape. But fret not, because in this article, we will give you a complete guide on how to rock long hair if you have an oval face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Rock Long Hair with an Oval Face Shape
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Long Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Hair

Description

An oval face shape is considered the ideal face shape for men. It is characterized by a slightly curved jawline, high cheekbones, and a forehead that is wider than the chin. Men with oval face shapes have a lot of versatility when it comes to hairstyles. However, when it comes to long hair, there are certain things that you need to keep in mind to make sure that your hairstyle complements your face shape.

How to Rock Long Hair with an Oval Face Shape

The key to rocking long hair with an oval face shape is to create balance. You want to make sure that your hairstyle doesn't make your face look too long or too narrow. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as layered haircuts.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head, as this can elongate your face even more.
  • Keep your hair length below your shoulders to avoid adding too much length to your face.
  • Consider adding a fringe to your hairstyle to help shorten the appearance of your face.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your long hair if you have an oval face shape:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as a mousse or a styling cream, to add volume to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add more volume and create a wavy effect.
  4. If you have a fringe, use a flat iron to straighten it for a more polished look.
  5. Finish off your hairstyle with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Styling Long Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling long hair:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about oval face shape and long hair:

Can oval face shape men pull off long hair?

Yes, men with an oval face shape can pull off long hair, but they need to choose a hairstyle that complements their face shape.

What is the best hairstyle for oval face shape men with long hair?

The best hairstyle for oval face shape men with long hair is a layered haircut that adds volume to the sides of their face.

What is the ideal hair length for oval face shape men?

The ideal hair length for oval face shape men is below the shoulders, as this length helps to balance out the face shape.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Here are some tips to keep your long hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant before styling with hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips, you can confidently rock long hair even if you have an oval face shape. Remember to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
