What Do Guys Use To Slick Back Hair A Complete Guide

Are you wondering what do guys use to slick back hair? Slicked-back hair is a classic and timeless hairstyle that has been popular among men for decades. It's a versatile look that can be dressed up or down, making it a go-to style for many men. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about slicking back your hair, including the products you should use, how to style your hair, and tips for maintaining your look.

Description

Slicked-back hair is a hairstyle where the hair is combed back from the forehead and held in place with a product. It's a popular hairstyle among men because it's easy to maintain and can be worn in a variety of settings. Slicked-back hair can be achieved with a variety of products, including pomade, gel, wax, and hairspray. The type of product you use will depend on your hair type, the look you're going for, and your personal preferences.

How to Slick Back Hair

Slicking back your hair is a simple process that can be done in just a few steps. Here's what you'll need to do:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair and create a clean slate for styling.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to dry your hair until it's damp but not dripping wet. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz and damage your hair.

Step 3: Apply Your Product

Apply your product of choice to your hair. If you're using pomade or wax, rub a small amount between your palms and distribute it evenly through your hair. If you're using gel or hairspray, spray or apply a small amount to your hair and comb it through.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair Back

Use a comb to comb your hair back from your forehead. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back, making sure to comb your hair evenly and smooth out any bumps or tangles.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is combed back, style it to your liking. You can create a slicked-back look with a clean part, a messy look with textured hair, or a classic look with a side part. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to slick back hair:

Step 1: Choose Your Product

The first step in slicking back your hair is choosing the right product. As mentioned earlier, there are a variety of products you can use, including pomade, gel, wax, and hairspray. The type of product you choose will depend on your hair type, the look you're going for, and your personal preferences.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair and create a clean slate for styling.

Tips for Slicking Back Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back look:

  • Use a small amount of product at first and add more as needed. Too much product can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Start with damp hair. This will help your product distribute evenly and make it easier to comb your hair back.
  • Use a fine-tooth comb to create a clean and polished look.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to add some texture to your slicked-back hair. Textured hair can give your look a more modern and relaxed feel.

FAQs

What products should I use to slick back my hair?

The products you use to slick back your hair will depend on your hair type and personal preferences. Pomade, wax, gel, and hairspray are all good options.

How much product should I use?

Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. Too much product can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Can I slick back my hair if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can slick back your hair if you have curly hair. However, you may need to use more product and a stronger hold to keep your curls in place.

Hair Care for Slicked-Back Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup and maintain a clean and healthy scalp.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Now that you know what do guys use to slick back hair, you can achieve this classic and timeless look with ease. Experiment with different products and styles to find the one that works best for you, and don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and polished look.
