Do Layers Thin Out Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of dealing with a thick and heavy head of hair? Well, you're not alone. Many people with thick hair struggle to find hairstyles that suit them. Fortunately, layers can be a great solution to thin out thick hair while still maintaining its volume and texture. In this article, we'll take a closer look at how layers work and provide tips on how to style them.

How Do Layers Work?

Layers are a haircut technique where the hair is cut at different lengths, creating a "layered" effect. This technique can help thin out thick hair by removing some of the weight from the hair. By cutting the hair at different lengths, layers can also add movement and texture to the hair.

How to Cut Layers in Thick Hair

If you're considering getting layers to thin out your thick hair, it's important to get them cut by a professional hairstylist. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut layers:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  3. Starting at the back, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers.
  4. Using a pair of sharp scissors, cut the hair at an angle.
  5. Continue cutting small sections of hair at an angle, moving from the back to the sides and top of your head.
  6. Once you've finished cutting all the sections of hair, use a comb to blend the layers together.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

It's important to note that the length and angle of the layers will depend on your hair type and face shape. Your hairstylist will be able to advise you on what will work best for you.

Tips for Styling Layered Hair

Once you have your layers cut, here are some tips on how to style them:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair extra body.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.
  • Use a round brush to curl the ends of your hair for a bouncy, layered look.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and clips, to add interest to your hairstyle.

FAQs

Q: Will layers make my hair look thinner?

A: No, layers will not make your hair look thinner. In fact, they can help add volume and texture to your hair.

Q: Can I get layers if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, layers can work well with curly hair. However, it's important to get them cut by a hairstylist who is experienced with curly hair.

Q: How often do I need to get my layers trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your layers trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain their shape and texture.

Hair Care for Layered Hair

Finally, it's important to take care of your layered hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

In conclusion, layers can be a great way to thin out thick hair while still maintaining its volume and texture. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can achieve a beautiful, layered hairstyle that works for you.
