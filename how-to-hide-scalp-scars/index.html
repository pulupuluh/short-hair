How To Hide Scalp Scars A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Scalp scars can be a source of embarrassment and self-consciousness for many people. Whether caused by injury, surgery, or a medical condition, scars on the scalp can be difficult to hide. But with a few simple techniques and some clever styling tricks, you can learn how to hide scalp scars and feel confident again. In this article, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about concealing scalp scars, including tips, tricks, and hair care advice.

Description: What are Scalp Scars?

Scalp scars can occur for a variety of reasons, including injury, surgery, or medical conditions like alopecia or psoriasis. They can be raised or flat, and may be red or discolored. Scalp scars can be particularly challenging to hide because of the location of the scalp and the fact that hair may not grow evenly over the scar tissue.

How to Hide Scalp Scars: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle is key to hiding scalp scars. If your scar is on the top of your head, consider a style with volume or layers to create the illusion of thicker hair. If your scar is on the side or back of your head, try a style that incorporates braids or twists to cover the scar. A pixie cut or shaved head can also be a stylish option for those with scars on the top of their head.

Step 2: Use Concealer

Concealer can be a lifesaver when it comes to hiding scalp scars. Choose a shade that matches your skin tone and apply it directly to the scar with a small makeup brush. Be sure to blend the concealer in well to avoid any obvious lines or streaks.

Step 3: Try Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are an easy and effective way to cover up scalp scars. These tiny fibers attach to your hair and scalp to create the appearance of fuller, thicker hair. Simply shake the fibers onto your scalp and brush your hair to distribute them evenly.

Step 4: Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories like headbands, scarves, and hats can be a stylish way to hide scalp scars. Experiment with different styles and colors to find what works best for you.

Step 5: Consider Hair Transplants

If your scalp scar is particularly severe or you're unable to conceal it with styling or makeup, you may want to consider a hair transplant. This surgical procedure involves transferring hair follicles from one part of the scalp to the scarred area to create a more even hairline.

Tips for Hiding Scalp Scars

Here are some additional tips for hiding scalp scars:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to create the appearance of thicker hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the scalp and draw attention to the scar.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering style.
  • Avoid exposing your scalp to the sun, as this can make scars more noticeable.
  • Consider using a tinted dry shampoo to help cover up the scar.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Can scalp scars be removed?

Scalp scars can be surgically removed, but this may not always be practical or effective. In some cases, scar tissue may be too extensive to remove completely. Talk to your doctor or dermatologist about your options.

Are there any natural remedies for scalp scars?

There are several natural remedies that may help reduce the appearance of scalp scars, including aloe vera gel, coconut oil, and vitamin E oil. However, these remedies may not work for everyone and may take several weeks or months to see results.

Is it safe to use hair fibers on scalp scars?

Yes, hair fibers are safe to use on scalp scars. They are made from natural materials and are designed to be gentle on the scalp and hair.

Hair Care Tips for Scars on the Scalp

Here are some additional hair care tips for scars on the scalp:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on the scarred area.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair to avoid further irritation to the scalp.
  • Apply a moisturizing scalp treatment to keep the scalp and hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Wear a hat or scarf when outdoors to protect the scalp from the sun.

With these tips and tricks, you can learn how to hide scalp scars and feel confident again. Remember, everyone has scars, and they don't define who you are or your beauty. Embrace your uniqueness, and experiment with different styles until you find what works for you.
