Why Is My Hair Thinning on the Side of My Head?

If you have noticed that your hair is thinning on the side of your head, you may be wondering why this is happening. There are several reasons why hair can thin on the side of the head, and in this article, we will explore the most common causes and what you can do about them.

Table of Contents

  • Hormonal Changes
  • Genetics
  • Hairstyles
  • Medical Conditions
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Hormonal Changes

Hormonal changes can cause hair to thin on the side of the head. This is because hormones can affect the growth cycle of hair follicles. For example, during menopause, the decrease in estrogen levels can lead to hair loss or thinning. Similarly, hormonal changes during pregnancy or after giving birth can also cause hair to thin.

If you suspect that hormonal changes are causing your hair thinning, it is best to consult with a healthcare provider to determine the best course of treatment.

Genetics

If hair thinning runs in your family, genetics may be the cause of your hair thinning on the side of your head. This type of hair loss is known as androgenetic alopecia and is more common in men than women. Androgenetic alopecia is caused by a combination of genetic and hormonal factors and can be treated with medications or hair transplant surgery.

Hairstyles

Wearing tight hairstyles, such as braids, buns, or ponytails, can cause hair to thin on the side of the head. This is because the constant pulling on the hair follicles can cause them to become damaged over time. If you notice that your hair is thinning on the side of your head, it may be best to switch to looser hairstyles or give your hair a break from styling altogether.

Medical Conditions

Medical conditions such as thyroid disorders, anemia, or autoimmune diseases can also cause hair to thin on the side of the head. These conditions affect the body's ability to produce healthy hair follicles, leading to hair loss or thinning. If you suspect that a medical condition is causing your hair thinning, it is best to consult with a healthcare provider to determine the underlying cause and the best course of treatment.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of the cause of your hair thinning, there are several hair care tips that can help promote healthy hair growth. These include:

  • Using a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoiding heat styling tools
  • Eating a healthy diet rich in protein and vitamins
  • Reducing stress levels
  • Getting enough sleep

FAQs

Can hair thinning on the side of the head be reversed?

In some cases, hair thinning on the side of the head can be reversed with the proper treatment. However, the success of treatment depends on the underlying cause of the hair loss and the individual's response to treatment.

Is hair transplant surgery an option for hair thinning on the side of the head?

Yes, hair transplant surgery is an option for hair thinning on the side of the head caused by androgenetic alopecia. This procedure involves moving hair follicles from a donor area to the thinning area of the scalp.

Can hair thinning on the side of the head be prevented?

While hair thinning on the side of the head cannot always be prevented, there are several things you can do to promote healthy hair growth, such as following a healthy diet, reducing stress levels, and avoiding tight hairstyles.


In conclusion, hair thinning on the side of the head can be caused by several factors, including hormonal changes, genetics, hairstyles, and medical conditions. If you are experiencing hair thinning, it is best to consult with a healthcare provider to determine the underlying cause and the best course of treatment. Additionally, following a healthy hair care routine can help promote healthy hair growth and prevent further hair thinning.
