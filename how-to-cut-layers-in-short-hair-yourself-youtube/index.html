How To Cut Layers In Short Hair Yourself Youtube

Written by Eveline Aug 26, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and add some texture to your short hair, cutting layers can be a great option. While it's always best to seek the help of a professional hairstylist, it is possible to cut layers in short hair yourself by following some simple steps. In this article, we'll show you how to cut layers in short hair yourself using YouTube tutorials.

Description

Layered haircuts can add volume, movement, and dimension to your short hair. This hairstyle is versatile and suits most hair types and face shapes. Cutting layers in short hair yourself can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy look at home. In this article, we'll guide you through the process step by step.

How To Cut Layers in Short Hair Yourself YouTube

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. Here are the steps to cut layers in short hair yourself:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style. Use a towel to dry your hair, but don't blow-dry it completely as you don't want to cut your hair when it's too wet.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts – two at the front and two at the back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Layer

Start by cutting the bottom layer of your hair. Use a comb to separate the hair and cut it straight across. This will create a base for the rest of the layers.

Step 4: Cut the Top Layers

Use your comb to lift the top layer of your hair and cut it at an angle. Start from the front and work your way to the back. Make sure to cut the hair at an angle and not straight across to create a layered effect. Repeat this process for each section of your hair.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Blend the layers by combing through your hair and cutting any uneven parts. Use your scissors to blend the layers together and create a seamless look.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using your preferred method. You can use a straightening iron, curling iron, or blow-dryer to create your desired look. If you're unsure how to style your hair, you can find tutorials on YouTube.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting layers in short hair yourself:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Focus on creating a natural-looking layered effect.
  • Don't cut your hair too short or too close to your scalp.
  • Take your time and be patient.

FAQ

Can I cut layers in my short hair myself?

Yes, you can cut layers in your short hair yourself if you follow the right steps and use the right tools.

What tools do I need to cut layers in short hair?

You'll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror.

How do I know how many layers to cut?

The number of layers you cut depends on your hair length and thickness. You can start with a few layers and add more as needed.

Hair Care

Cutting your hair can be damaging, so it's important to take care of your hair after you cut it. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Trim your hair regularly to maintain the layered look.

Now that you know how to cut layers in short hair yourself using YouTube tutorials, you can experiment with different hairstyles and create a new look for yourself. Remember to take your time, be patient, and use the right tools to achieve a salon-worthy look.
