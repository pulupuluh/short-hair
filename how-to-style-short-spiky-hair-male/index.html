How To Style Short Spiky Hair Male

Written by Eveline Apr 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short spiky hair is a popular hairstyle for men. It is easy to maintain and versatile, making it suitable for different occasions. In this article, we will discuss how to style short spiky hair male.

Description

Short spiky hair is a haircut with short hair on the sides and back and longer hair on the top. The hair on the top is styled upwards to create spikes. This hairstyle is popular among men of all ages and is suitable for different hair types.

How to Style Short Spiky Hair Male

Here are the steps to style short spiky hair male:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to remove dirt and excess oil. Dry your hair with a towel until it is slightly damp.

Step 2: Apply styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as hair gel or pomade to your hair. Distribute the product evenly using your fingers.

Step 3: Create spikes

Using your fingers or a comb, create spikes on the top of your hair. Lift the hair upwards and push it towards the center to create spikes. You can also use a blow dryer to create more volume and hold.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

Finish the style with a light hold hairspray to keep the spikes in place. Hold the can 8-12 inches away from your hair and spray evenly.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style short spiky hair male:

  • Use a good quality styling product to achieve the desired hold and texture.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use a blow dryer to create more volume and hold.
  • Be careful not to use too much product as it can weigh down your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short spiky hair male:

What is the best styling product for short spiky hair?

The best styling product for short spiky hair is a high-quality hair gel or pomade. Look for a product that provides a strong hold and a matte finish.

Can short spiky hair be styled for formal occasions?

Yes, short spiky hair can be styled for formal occasions. Use a light hold styling product and create a more polished look with a comb or brush.

How often should I wash my short spiky hair?

You should wash your short spiky hair every other day or as needed. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and lead to dryness and damage.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to care for your short spiky hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Now you know how to style short spiky hair male. Follow these tips and tricks to achieve the perfect spiky hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle.
