What Are Round Layers In Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Feb 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Round layers in hair are a popular haircut style that creates a soft, layered look. This style is achieved by cutting the hair at an angle, creating layers that frame the face and add volume and movement to the hair. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about round layers in hair, including how to achieve the look, tips and tricks, and hair care.

Description

Round layers in hair are a versatile haircut that works for many hair types and lengths. This style involves cutting the hair at an angle, creating layers that are round and soft. The layers are usually shorter towards the top of the head and longer towards the bottom, creating a tapered effect. This style is often used to add volume and movement to the hair, making it a popular choice for those with thin or fine hair.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're interested in getting round layers in your hair, it's important to visit a professional stylist who has experience with this style. They will be able to cut your hair at the correct angle and ensure that the layers are even and symmetrical.

Before your appointment, it's a good idea to bring in pictures of the style you're hoping to achieve. This will give your stylist a clear idea of what you're looking for and help them create a custom look that works for you.

Step-by-Step

1. Start by washing and drying your hair.

2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.

3. Starting at the nape of your neck, use a comb to create a horizontal part.

4. Hold the hair at a 45-degree angle and cut the hair.

5. Move up the head, creating horizontal sections and cutting at a 45-degree angle.

6. Once you reach the top of your head, move to the sides and repeat the process.

7. Finish by cutting the top section of your hair, using the same technique.

Tips and Tricks

1. Use a sharp pair of scissors to ensure a clean cut.

2. Start with longer layers and work your way up to shorter layers.

3. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and movement to the hair.

4. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down the hair.

FAQ

How often should I get my hair cut to maintain round layers?

It's recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your round layers.

Will round layers work for my hair type?

Round layers can work for many hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, it's important to consult with a stylist to determine if this style will work for your specific hair type and texture.

Can I style my hair in different ways with round layers?

Yes, round layers are versatile and can be styled in many different ways, including straight, wavy, or curly.

Hair Care

To keep your round layers looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair. This includes using a quality shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible, and using a heat protectant spray when you do use them. It's also a good idea to get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your round layers.

Overall, round layers in hair are a popular and versatile style that can work for many hair types and lengths. By consulting with a professional stylist, maintaining regular trims, and taking care of your hair, you can achieve a beautiful, layered look that adds volume and movement to your hair.
