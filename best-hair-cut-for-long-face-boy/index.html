Best Hair Cut For Long Face Boy Tips And Styles

Written by Petter Apr 01, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you a boy with a long face and wondering which hairstyle suits you best? Look no further as we have researched and compiled the best haircuts for long face boys. In this article, we will guide you on how to choose the right haircut for your face shape, tips on maintaining it, and the latest styles in 2023.

Description

A long face is characterized by a forehead, cheekbones, and jawline that are almost equal in width. The length of the face is longer than its width, making it angular and narrow. The best haircut for a long face boy is one that adds width and volume to the sides of the face, balancing its length. It should also create a framing effect, drawing attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a long face boy, consider the following:

1. Face Shape

Understand your face shape, and choose a haircut that flatters it. Avoid hairstyles that elongate the face, such as long fringes, slicked-back hair, or straight cuts. Opt for styles that add width and volume to the sides, such as layered cuts, side-swept fringes, or textured hairstyles.

2. Hair Texture

Your hair texture also affects the type of haircut that suits you. If you have thick hair, you can go for longer hairstyles that add volume and movement. If you have fine hair, opt for shorter cuts that create the illusion of thickness and body.

3. Lifestyle

Consider your daily routine and choose a haircut that is easy to maintain and style. If you have a busy schedule, opt for low-maintenance haircuts that require minimal styling. If you have more time, experiment with different hairstyles and products.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to get the best haircut for a long face boy:

1. Consultation

Book a consultation with your hairstylist and discuss your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Show them pictures of hairstyles you like and ask for their opinion on what suits you best.

2. Preparing Your Hair

Wash and condition your hair before the appointment. Avoid using styling products, as they can alter the natural texture of your hair, making it difficult to cut and style.

3. Cutting Your Hair

Your hairstylist will cut your hair according to the agreed style. They may use scissors, clippers, or a combination of both, depending on the style and length of your hair.

4. Styling Your Hair

Once the haircut is done, your hairstylist will style your hair using products and tools. Ask for tips on how to maintain and style your hair at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

To keep your haircut looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

1. Regular Trims

Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your haircut. The frequency of trims depends on the style and length of your hair, but usually, every 4-6 weeks is recommended.

2. Use the Right Products

Use hair products that suit your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For example, use a volumizing mousse for fine hair or a texturizing spray for thick hair.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools. Also, avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as it can cause breakage and damage.

FAQ

1. Is a short or long haircut better for a long face boy?

A medium-length haircut is best for a long face boy, as it adds volume and width to the sides, balancing the length of the face. Avoid very short or very long hairstyles, as they can elongate the face.

2. Can I have a fringe with a long face?

Yes, you can have a fringe with a long face, but avoid straight fringes that cover the forehead, as they can make the face look longer. Opt for side-swept or textured fringes that add volume and framing to the face.

3. Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

Yes, you can experiment with different hairstyles and products for different occasions. However, make sure that the hairstyle flatters your face shape and suits the occasion.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, follow these hair care tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture.

2. Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. Apply the mask to clean, damp hair, and leave it on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it off.

3. Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using hot tools on your hair frequently, as it can cause heat damage and breakage. If you must use a hot tool, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

With these tips and styles, you are now ready to choose the best haircut for your long face. Embrace your unique face shape and rock the latest trends with confidence.
