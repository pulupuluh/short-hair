How To Wear Hair Away From Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Mar 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of constantly pushing your hair away from your face? Whether it's during a workout or just everyday activities, having hair in your face can be frustrating. Luckily, there are many ways to wear your hair away from your face that are both stylish and easy to do. In this article, we'll show you how to wear your hair away from your face in a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Hair Away from Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Keeping Hair Away from Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Wear Hair Away from Face

There are many ways to wear your hair away from your face. Some popular styles include ponytails, braids, and buns. You can also use hair accessories like headbands, clips, and bobby pins to keep your hair out of your face. When choosing a style, consider your hair type and what will be most comfortable for you.

Ponytails

Ponytails are a classic hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. To wear a ponytail away from your face, gather all of your hair at the back of your head and secure it with a hair tie. You can also leave a few strands of hair out around your face for a softer look. For a sleeker look, use a brush to smooth your hair back before securing it with a hair tie.

Braids

Braids are another popular hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. To wear a braid away from your face, start by parting your hair down the middle. Then, braid each section of hair and secure the ends with a hair tie. You can also braid your hair to the side for a more relaxed look. To keep your braid in place, use bobby pins to secure it to the side of your head.

Buns

Buns are a stylish and practical way to wear your hair away from your face. To wear a bun away from your face, start by gathering your hair into a high ponytail. Then, twist your hair around the hair tie and secure it with bobby pins. You can also use a hair donut to create a more voluminous bun. For a sleeker look, use a brush to smooth your hair back before creating your bun.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to wear your hair away from your face:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that works for your hair type and face shape.
  2. Gather all of your hair away from your face.
  3. Secure your hair with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  4. Use hair accessories like headbands, clips, or bobby pins to keep your hair in place.
  5. Smooth your hair back with a brush for a sleeker look.

Tips for Keeping Hair Away from Face

Here are some tips for keeping your hair away from your face:

  • Use hair accessories like headbands, clips, or bobby pins to keep your hair in place.
  • Choose a hairstyle that works for your hair type and face shape.
  • Use hair products like hairspray or gel to keep your hair in place.
  • Wrap a scarf or bandana around your head for a more bohemian look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing hair away from your face:

What hairstyles work best for keeping hair away from your face?

Ponytails, braids, and buns are all popular hairstyles for keeping hair away from your face. You can also use hair accessories like headbands, clips, or bobby pins to keep your hair in place.

What hair products can I use to keep my hair away from my face?

Hairspray, gel, and mousse can all be used to keep your hair in place. Choose a product that works for your hair type and hairstyle.

How do I keep my hair in place during a workout?

Use a headband or hair tie to keep your hair in place during a workout. You can also braid your hair or wear a bun to keep it out of your face.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily wear your hair away from your face in a stylish and comfortable way. Whether you prefer ponytails, braids, or buns, there's a hairstyle that will work for you. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and hair accessories until you find what works best for you.
