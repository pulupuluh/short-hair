How Do I Trim My Own Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 29, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of constantly visiting the salon for a simple haircut? Do you want to save money and time by trimming your own short hair at home? If yes, then you’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of trimming your own short hair, with easy-to-follow steps and helpful tips.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prepare
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Trimming Short Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Prepare

Before you start trimming your own short hair, there are a few things you need to do to prepare.

  • Gather all the necessary tools, such as scissors, a comb, and a mirror.
  • Wash and dry your hair thoroughly.
  • Wear comfortable clothing that you don’t mind getting hair on.
  • Choose a well-lit area to work in.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to trim your own short hair:

  1. Divide your hair into sections using clips.
  2. Start with the back of your head, using a comb to lift each section of hair.
  3. Hold the scissors vertically and trim the ends of the hair.
  4. Move on to the sides, using the same technique.
  5. Use the comb to blend any uneven spots.
  6. Check your progress in the mirror, and make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Finish with the bangs, if you have them.
  8. Use the scissors to cut them straight across or at an angle.
  9. Comb your hair to remove any stray hairs.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Remember to be patient and take your time when trimming your own short hair. It’s better to take small snips and check your progress frequently, rather than cutting too much at once.

Tips for Trimming Short Hair

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind when trimming your own short hair:

  • Invest in high-quality scissors specifically designed for hair cutting.
  • Use a comb to lift each section of hair, and cut it vertically with the scissors.
  • Don’t cut your hair when it’s wet, as it will appear longer when dry.
  • Trim your hair in a well-lit area to avoid missing any spots.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or frustrated.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about trimming short hair:

How often should I trim my short hair?

It’s recommended to trim your short hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Can I use regular household scissors to trim my hair?

No, it’s not recommended to use regular scissors to trim your hair, as they can cause uneven cuts and damage to your hair. Invest in a pair of high-quality hair cutting scissors instead.

How do I know if I’ve cut too much hair?

Check your progress frequently in the mirror, and take small snips instead of cutting too much at once. If you’re unsure, it’s better to err on the side of caution and cut less.

Hair Care Tips

Keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained is key to achieving a great haircut. Here are some tips for taking care of your short hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use hair masks or treatments to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid over-brushing or combing your hair, as this can cause breakage.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you’ll be able to trim your own short hair with confidence and ease. Happy cutting!
