How To Style Men s Curly Hairstyles

Written by Eveline Jun 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have curly hair, then you know that finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. However, with the right styling techniques and products, you can create a look that is both stylish and manageable. In this article, we will go over some tips on how to style men's curly hairstyles.

Description

Men's curly hairstyles are a popular choice for those with naturally curly hair. These hairstyles can range from short and sleek to long and wild, depending on your personal style. However, styling curly hair can be a challenge, as it tends to be more difficult to manage than straight hair.

How to Style Men's Curly Hairstyles

When it comes to styling men's curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it is important to choose the right haircut for your hair type. A good haircut can make all the difference in how easy it is to style your hair. Second, you will need to use the right products to help define your curls and keep them looking their best.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style men's curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair. This could be a curl defining cream, a gel, or a mousse. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls. This will help to keep your curls defined and reduce frizz.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.
  5. Use your fingers to style your curls. You can scrunch your hair to create more defined curls or tousle your hair for a more relaxed look.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to help hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling men's curly hair:

  • Don't over-wash your hair. Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so washing it too often can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Avoid using a brush, as it can cause frizz.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling men's curly hair:

What is the best haircut for men with curly hair?

The best haircut for men with curly hair is one that works with your natural curl pattern. A layered cut can help to reduce bulk and add definition to your curls.

How often should I wash my hair if I have curly hair?

It is recommended that you wash your hair no more than two to three times a week if you have curly hair. This will help to prevent your hair from becoming too dry.

What type of styling product should I use for curly hair?

The type of styling product you should use for curly hair depends on your hair type and the look you are trying to achieve. Curl defining cream, gel, and mousse are all good options for defining curls and reducing frizz.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, manageable curls. Here are some tips for taking care of curly hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to help keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

By following these tips and using the right styling techniques and products, you can create a look that is both stylish and manageable. Whether you prefer short and sleek or long and wild, there is a men's curly hairstyle out there for you.
