Short Back And Sides Haircut For Ladies A Bold And Edgy Look

Written by Luffy Aug 08, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd, then a short back and sides haircut for ladies might just be what you need! This style has been around for decades and has been popularized by iconic figures such as Audrey Hepburn, Mia Farrow, and more recently, Halle Berry. In this article, we'll go through everything you need to know about this classic haircut, from how to get it to how to take care of it.

Description

The short back and sides haircut for ladies is characterized by having short hair on the sides and at the back, while leaving some length on the top. This creates a sharp and clean look that is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish and modern. The length on top can vary depending on personal preference, but it usually ranges from a few inches to a pixie cut.

How to Get a Short Back and Sides Haircut for Ladies

If you're convinced that this is the haircut for you, then the first step is to find a hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts. Once you've found the right person, make sure to bring a picture of the specific style you want to achieve, as this will help your stylist understand your vision.

The haircut itself is pretty straightforward and usually involves cutting the sides and back with clippers, while using scissors to create texture and shape on the top. Depending on your hair type and the desired length, your stylist may also use a razor to add some more dimension to the cut.

Step by Step

To give you a better idea of what to expect during your haircut, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, left side, right side, and back
  3. Start with the sides and back, using clippers to cut the hair to the desired length (usually a #2 or #3 guard)
  4. Blend the sides and back into the top using scissors, creating a gradual transition
  5. Take the top section and cut it to the desired length, either with scissors or a razor
  6. Add texture and shape to the top using point cutting or slicing techniques
  7. Style your hair using a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you're considering a short back and sides haircut:

  • Make sure to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type
  • Consider the maintenance involved in this haircut, as you may need to get it trimmed more frequently than other styles
  • Invest in high-quality styling products to keep your hair looking sharp and defined
  • Experiment with different textures and lengths on top to find the perfect style for you

FAQ

Is a short back and sides haircut suitable for all hair types?

While this style can work for most hair types, it may not be the best option for those with very thick or curly hair. It's important to consult with your hairstylist to see if this style is a good fit for your hair type.

Will a short back and sides haircut make my face look too round or square?

Not necessarily! The key is to choose a style that complements your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to opt for a slightly longer length on top to create more height and length in the face. Your hairstylist can help you choose the right style for you.

Hair Care

Now that you've got your short back and sides haircut, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking sharp and defined. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the cuticle and make it look frizzy or dull
  • Use a small amount of styling product to define the hair and add shine
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your haircut

Overall, a short back and sides haircut for ladies is a great way to add some edge and personality to your look. With the right cut and styling products, you can create a chic and modern style that is both low-maintenance and eye-catching.
