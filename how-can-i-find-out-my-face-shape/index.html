How Can I Find Out My Face Shape

Written by Petter Jul 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you curious about your face shape and wondering what hairstyles or makeup looks would flatter your features? Identifying your face shape can be a helpful tool in determining the best styles for you. In this article, we will discuss how you can easily find out your face shape with simple steps and tips.

Table of Contents

  • Measurements
  • Mirror Method
  • Face Shape Software
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Measurements

One way to determine your face shape is by taking measurements of your face. Here are the steps:

  1. Take a flexible measuring tape and measure the width of your forehead at the widest point.
  2. Measure the width of your cheekbones at the widest point.
  3. Measure the length of your face from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.
  4. Measure the width of your jawline at the widest point.

After taking these measurements, you can compare them to the following guidelines to determine your face shape:

  • Oval: Your face length is greater than the width of your cheekbones, and your forehead is wider than your jawline.
  • Round: Your face length and cheekbone width are similar, and your forehead and jawline are also similar in width.
  • Square: Your face length, cheekbone width, forehead, and jawline are all similar in width.
  • Heart: Your forehead is wider than your cheekbones, and your face tapers to a narrow chin.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbones are the widest point of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrower.
  • Triangle: Your jawline is wider than your forehead and cheekbones.

Mirror Method

Another way to determine your face shape is by using the mirror method:

  1. Pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror.
  2. Trace the outline of your face onto the mirror using an erasable marker or lipstick.
  3. Step back and examine the shape you drew on the mirror.
  4. Compare the shape to the guidelines for face shapes.

Face Shape Software

If you prefer a more high-tech solution, there are several face shape software programs available online. These programs use a photo of your face to calculate your face shape. Simply upload a photo of yourself and let the software do the rest.

FAQ

Does face shape matter for hairstyles?

Yes, your face shape can affect which hairstyles will flatter your features. For example, if you have a round face, a short bob may not be the most flattering option as it can accentuate the roundness of your face. Instead, a longer style with layers could be a better choice.

Can face shape change over time?

No, your face shape is determined by your bone structure and will not change over time. However, weight gain or loss can affect the appearance of your face shape.

Hair Care Tips

Once you have identified your face shape, you can use this information to choose hairstyles that complement your features. Here are some tips:

  • Oval: Most hairstyles will flatter an oval face shape, but avoid styles that add too much volume at the top.
  • Round: Long, layered hairstyles can help elongate a round face. Avoid blunt cuts that add width.
  • Square: Soft, layered styles can help soften a square face shape. Avoid straight, blunt cuts.
  • Heart: Side-swept bangs or chin-length hairstyles can balance a heart-shaped face. Avoid styles that add volume at the top.
  • Diamond: Shorter hairstyles with volume at the top can balance a diamond face shape. Avoid styles that add width to the cheekbones.
  • Triangle: Chin-length or longer hairstyles with layers can balance a triangle face shape. Avoid styles that add volume at the jawline.

By following these tips and determining your face shape, you can confidently choose hairstyles and makeup looks that flatter your unique features.
