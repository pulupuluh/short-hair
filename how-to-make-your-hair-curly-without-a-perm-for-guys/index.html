How To Make Your Hair Curly Without A Perm For Guys

Written by William Sep 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to try out some curls? You don't have to go for a perm to achieve those luscious curls. In this article, we will guide you through how to make your hair curly without a perm for guys.

Description

Curls can add a lot of personality to your hair and make you stand out. However, if you have naturally straight hair, it can be challenging to get the curls you desire. Going for a perm is one option, but it can be damaging to your hair and expensive. Fortunately, there are many ways to make your hair curly without a perm for guys.

How to Make Your Hair Curly Without a Perm for Guys

1. Twist and Pin Method

One of the easiest ways to get curls is the twist and pin method. All you need to do is damp your hair and divide it into sections. Twist each section and pin it to your head. Leave it overnight or for a few hours, and then release the pins. You will have natural-looking curls.

2. Braid Method

The braid method is another simple way to get curls. Damp your hair and divide it into sections. Braid each section and leave it overnight or for a few hours. Once you release the braids, you will have bouncy curls.

3. Sponge Roller Method

You can also use sponge rollers to get curls. Damp your hair and wrap it around the sponge roller. Leave it overnight or for a few hours, and then release the rollers. You will have tight curls.

4. Curling Iron Method

If you want more control over the type of curls, you can use a curling iron. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair and section it. Use a curling iron to curl each section. The smaller the barrel, the tighter the curls will be.

Step by Step Guide

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start with clean hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse it thoroughly to avoid any residue.

2. Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove any excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel as it can cause damage.

3. Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to hold the curls. You can use a mousse, gel, or cream.

4. Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on the thickness of your hair.

5. Start Curling

Start curling your hair using the method of your choice. Be gentle and avoid pulling your hair.

6. Finish with Hairspray

Once you have curled all sections of your hair, finish it off with some hairspray. It will hold the curls in place for longer.

Tips

- Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron to avoid damaging your hair.

- Do not overuse styling products as it can weigh down your hair.

- Experiment with different methods to find the one that suits you the best.

FAQ

Q. Can I make my hair curly without using heat?

Yes, you can use methods like the twist and pin method or the braid method to make your hair curly without using heat.

Q. How long will my curls last?

It depends on the method you use and how well you maintain your curls. Some methods can last up to a week with proper hair care.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to maintain its shape and texture. Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as it can cause frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet. Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

By following these simple steps, you can make your hair curly without a perm for guys. Experiment with different methods and find the one that suits you the best.
