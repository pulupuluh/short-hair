Best Male Hair Products For Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Mar 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Thick hair is a blessing, but finding the right products to manage it can be a challenge. Whether you're looking for a styling product to tame unruly locks or a shampoo that won't weigh down your hair, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll discuss the best male hair products for thick hair, including how to use them and some helpful tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Shampoo
  • Conditioner
  • Styling Products
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Shampoo

When it comes to shampooing thick hair, it's important to use a product that will cleanse without stripping away natural oils. Look for a shampoo that's sulfate-free, as sulfates can be harsh on hair and scalp. Some of the best shampoos for thick hair include:

1. Redken Brews Extra Clean Shampoo

This shampoo is specifically designed for men with oily hair and scalp. It contains malt to strengthen hair and salicylic acid to remove excess oil and build-up.

2. American Crew Daily Moisturizing Shampoo

Infused with rosemary and thyme extracts, this shampoo moisturizes and soothes the scalp while gently cleansing hair. It's also great for men with sensitive skin.

3. Kiehl's Since 1851 Amino Acid Shampoo

This gentle shampoo is free of sulfates, parabens, and silicones, making it a great choice for men with thicker hair. It contains coconut oil and amino acids to moisturize and strengthen hair.

Conditioner

Conditioner is an important step in any hair care routine, but it's especially important for men with thick hair. A good conditioner will help detangle and soften hair, making it easier to manage. Here are some of the best conditioners for thick hair:

1. Dove Men+Care Thick and Strong Conditioner

This conditioner is infused with caffeine and calcium to strengthen hair and prevent breakage. It also has a refreshing menthol scent that leaves hair feeling clean and invigorated.

2. Paul Mitchell Tea Tree Lavender Mint Conditioner

This conditioner is formulated with tea tree oil, lavender, and mint to soothe and hydrate the scalp. It also detangles and softens hair, making it easier to style.

3. SheaMoisture Strengthen & Restore Conditioner

This conditioner is packed with natural ingredients like Jamaican black castor oil and shea butter to nourish and strengthen hair. It also helps to reduce breakage and promote healthy hair growth.

Styling Products

Styling thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right products, it's a breeze. Here are some of the best styling products for thick hair:

1. Baxter of California Clay Pomade

This pomade provides a strong hold with a matte finish, making it perfect for short hairstyles. It's also infused with natural ingredients like beeswax and clay to nourish and protect hair.

2. Hanz de Fuko Quicksand

This unique styling product provides a gritty texture that's perfect for messy, tousled styles. It's also infused with natural ingredients like kaolin clay and beeswax to keep hair healthy and strong.

3. Oribe Rough Luxury Molding Wax

This molding wax provides a flexible hold with a natural shine, making it perfect for longer hairstyles. It also contains natural ingredients like watermelon and lychee extracts to protect and nourish hair.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to using the right products, there are some simple things you can do to keep your thick hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair no more than three times a week to avoid stripping away natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle hair after washing.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs

Q: Can I use the same shampoo and conditioner for thick hair as I would for thin hair?

A: No, it's important to use products that are specifically formulated for thick hair. These products will provide extra moisture and nourishment to help manage your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have thick hair?

A: It's recommended to wash thick hair no more than three times a week, as washing too often can strip away natural oils and cause dryness.

Q: What's the best way to style thick hair?

A: The best way to style thick hair depends on your hair type and personal preferences. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.

In conclusion, managing thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and hair care routine, it's possible to have healthy, manageable locks. Try out some of the products mentioned in this article and see what works best for you.
