What Size Is A Number 3 Clipper Guard

Written by Robby Jun 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to buzz your hair at home, you may be wondering what size is a number 3 clipper guard. A clipper guard is an attachment that fits onto a hair clipper and determines the length of the hair that will be cut. The number 3 clipper guard is a popular choice for men's haircuts, but it can also be used for women's haircuts and even for pets. In this article, we will discuss what size is a number 3 clipper guard, how to use it, and some tips for getting the best results.

Description

A number 3 clipper guard is 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm long. This means that when you attach the clipper guard to your hair clipper, it will leave your hair at a length of 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm. The number 3 clipper guard is a popular choice for men's haircuts because it is long enough to leave some hair on the head, but short enough to give a neat and tidy appearance.

How to Use a Number 3 Clipper Guard

The first step in using a number 3 clipper guard is to make sure your hair is clean and dry. You should also make sure that your hair is free of tangles and knots. Once your hair is ready, attach the clipper guard to your hair clipper. Turn on the clipper and start at the bottom of your head, moving the clipper up and over your head. Make sure to go over each section of your head multiple times to ensure an even cut. If you want to fade your hair, you can use a number 2 or number 1 clipper guard around the sides and back of your head.

Step by Step Guide

1. Clean and dry your hair

2. Attach the number 3 clipper guard to your hair clipper

3. Turn on the clipper and start at the bottom of your head

4. Move the clipper up and over your head, going over each section multiple times

5. If you want to fade your hair, use a number 2 or number 1 clipper guard around the sides and back of your head

Tips for Using a Number 3 Clipper Guard

1. Go slow and take your time

2. Use a mirror to check the back of your head

3. If you're not sure which clipper guard to use, start with a longer one and work your way down

4. Clean your clipper guard after each use to prevent hair buildup

5. Don't apply too much pressure when using the clipper, as this can cause cuts and nicks

FAQ

Can I use a number 3 clipper guard for women's haircuts?

Yes, a number 3 clipper guard can be used for women's haircuts. However, it may not be suitable for all hair types and styles.

Can I use a number 3 clipper guard on my pet?

Yes, a number 3 clipper guard can be used on pets. However, it's important to use a clipper that is designed for pets and to be careful not to hurt your pet while clipping their hair.

How often should I clean my clipper guard?

You should clean your clipper guard after each use to prevent hair buildup. This will ensure that it continues to work properly and last longer.

Hair Care

Using a number 3 clipper guard can be a great way to maintain a neat and tidy appearance. However, it's important to remember that regular hair care is also important. This includes washing your hair regularly, using conditioner to keep it soft and moisturized, and trimming split ends as needed. By taking good care of your hair, you can ensure that it looks and feels healthy and strong.
