How To Style Hair Like Korean Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Apr 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a fan of K-pop or Korean dramas, you've probably noticed the unique and stylish hairstyles of Korean guys. From the classic bowl cut to the trendy textured fringe, Korean guys have mastered the art of hair styling. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect Korean-inspired hairstyle. Follow these steps to get the look!

How to

Step 1: Choose the Right Cut

The first step to achieving a Korean-inspired hairstyle is to choose the right cut. Popular styles include the bowl cut, the textured fringe, and the undercut. You can bring a picture of your desired hairstyle to your hairstylist or use a hair-cutting app to get an idea of how it will look on you.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo to add thickness. If you have curly hair, use a moisturizing conditioner to tame frizz.

Step 3: Apply Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. Korean guys often use hair wax or pomade to create texture and hold. Rub a small amount of product between your palms and apply it evenly throughout your hair. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray to hold it in place. Use a hairspray that won't make your hair look greasy or stiff. Korean guys prefer a natural and effortless look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect Korean-inspired hairstyle:

  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to try different cuts and styles until you find the one that suits you best.
  • Take good care of your hair by using a moisturizing conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and trimming it regularly.
  • Accessorize your hairstyle with a headband, hair clip, or bandana for a trendy and stylish look.

FAQ

What is the most popular Korean-inspired hairstyle?

The most popular Korean-inspired hairstyle is the textured fringe. It's a modern and versatile style that suits most face shapes and hair types.

What hair products do Korean guys use?

Korean guys often use hair wax or pomade to create texture and hold. They prefer a natural and effortless look, so they avoid using products that make their hair look greasy or stiff.

How do I choose the right hairstyle for me?

Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Bring a picture of your desired hairstyle to your hairstylist or use a hair-cutting app to get an idea of how it will look on you.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip the natural oils from your scalp.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style your hair like a Korean guy. Whether you want a classic bowl cut or a trendy textured fringe, these steps will help you achieve the perfect look. Remember to take good care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Happy styling!
