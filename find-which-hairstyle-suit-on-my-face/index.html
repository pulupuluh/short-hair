Find Which Hairstyle Suits Your Face A Complete Guide

Written by Robby Apr 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle is a daunting task, especially when you have no idea what suits your face shape. You can spend hours browsing through magazines, Pinterest, and Instagram for inspiration, but it's not always easy to know which style will look good on you. In this guide, we'll show you how to find which hairstyle suits your face shape and help you make an informed decision.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Here's how to figure out which one you have:

Round Face Shape

If your face is as wide as it is long and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have a round face shape. Hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head or have long layers that fall below your chin will elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

Oval Face Shape

If your face is longer than it is wide and your forehead is slightly wider than your chin, you have an oval face shape. Lucky you! Almost any hairstyle will suit your face shape.

Square Face Shape

If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, you have a square face shape. Soften your strong jawline with hairstyles that have layers around the face or wispy bangs.

Heart Face Shape

If your face is wider at the temples and narrows at the jawline, you have a heart face shape. Hairstyles that add fullness to the lower half of your face, like chin-length bobs or layered shoulder-length cuts, will balance out your face shape.

Diamond Face Shape

If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face and your forehead and jawline are narrow, you have a diamond face shape. Side-swept bangs and chin-length bobs with volume on the sides will soften your angular features.

Oblong Face Shape

If your face is longer than it is wide and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, you have an oblong face shape. Shorter hairstyles with volume on the sides or blunt bangs will help balance out your face shape.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, here are some tips for choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair type and texture. Some hairstyles work better on straight hair, while others are better suited for curly or wavy hair.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, a low-maintenance hairstyle might be a better option for you.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment. Trying out new hairstyles is a great way to find out what works for you.
  • Consult with a hairstylist. They can give you personalized recommendations and help you achieve your desired look.

FAQs

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

It's possible to have a combination of face shapes, so you may need to experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

Can I still wear a hairstyle that doesn't suit my face shape?

Of course! At the end of the day, it's all about how you feel. If you love a certain hairstyle, go for it!

How often should I change my hairstyle?

There's no set rule for how often you should change your hairstyle. It's up to you and your personal preferences.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair health.

With these tips, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
