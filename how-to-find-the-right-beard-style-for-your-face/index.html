How To Find The Right Beard Style For Your Face

Written by William May 22, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you looking to change up your beard style but not sure where to start? Finding the right beard style for your face can be a bit challenging, but with a few tips and tricks, you can easily find the perfect look. In this article, we will discuss how to find the right beard style for your face shape, as well as provide some helpful tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Popular Beard Styles
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Beard
  • FAQs

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right beard style for your face is to determine your face shape. There are six common face shapes: round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, take a look at your jawline, cheekbones, and forehead.

If your jawline is rounded, and your cheekbones and forehead are approximately the same width, you likely have a round face shape. If your jawline is angular and your forehead and cheekbones are approximately the same width, you likely have a square face shape. If your face is longer than it is wide, you likely have an oblong face shape. If your forehead is wider than your cheekbones and jawline, you likely have a heart-shaped face. If your cheekbones are wider than your forehead and jawline, you likely have a diamond-shaped face. And finally, if your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead and jawline are rounded, you likely have an oval face.

Popular Beard Styles

Once you have determined your face shape, you can start exploring different beard styles that will work for you. Here are some popular beard styles for each face shape:

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you want to create the illusion of a longer face. A beard that is shorter on the sides and longer on the chin can help elongate your face. A goatee or a soul patch can also work well with a round face shape.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you want to soften your features. A beard that is shorter on the chin and longer on the sides can help soften your jawline. A goatee or a circle beard can also work well with a square face shape.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile, and most beard styles will work well for you. You can try a full beard, a goatee, or a mustache.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to balance your features. A beard that is shorter on the chin and longer on the sides can help balance your face shape. A goatee or a soul patch can also work well with a heart-shaped face.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond-shaped face, you want to create the illusion of a wider chin. A beard that is shorter on the chin and longer on the sides can help widen your chin. A goatee or a soul patch can also work well with a diamond-shaped face.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, you want to create the illusion of a shorter face. A beard that is fuller on the sides and shorter on the chin can help shorten your face. A mustache or a chinstrap beard can also work well with an oblong face shape.

Tips for Maintaining Your Beard

Once you have found the right beard style for your face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining your beard:

  • Keep your beard clean by washing it regularly with a beard shampoo.
  • Trim your beard regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and hydrated.
  • Brush or comb your beard daily to prevent tangles and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid over-styling your beard with heat tools, as this can cause damage to your hair.

FAQs

What beard style is best for a patchy beard?

If you have a patchy beard, it's best to keep your beard short and neatly trimmed. A goatee or a chinstrap beard can also work well with a patchy beard.

How long does it take to grow a beard?

The amount of time it takes to grow a beard varies from person to person. On average, it takes about two to six months to grow a full beard.

How do I prevent beard dandruff?

To prevent beard dandruff, make sure to keep your beard clean and hydrated. Use a beard shampoo and conditioner, and apply a beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and moisturized.

Can I dye my beard?

Yes, you can dye your beard. However, it's important to use a dye specifically formulated for facial hair, as regular hair dye can be too harsh and cause skin irritation.

Finding the right beard style for your face can take a bit of trial and error, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be sure to find the perfect look. Remember to maintain your beard properly for healthy, happy hair.
