Fancy Hairstyles For Short Curly Hair Tips Step By Step Guide And Hair Care

Written by Petter Mar 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Short curly hair might seem challenging to style, but it's actually quite versatile. There are many fancy hairstyles for short curly hair that can elevate your look from ordinary to extraordinary. Whether you're looking for a chic updo or a playful ponytail, there's a style that will suit your taste and personality. In this article, we'll guide you through some of the best fancy hairstyles for short curly hair and provide tips on how to care for your curls.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Fancy Hairstyles for Short Curly Hair

How to Style Short Curly Hair

Before we dive into the fancy hairstyles, let's go over some tips on how to style short curly hair. The key to styling curly hair is to embrace its natural texture and work with it, rather than against it. Here are some general tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Consider using a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls' natural shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Fancy Hairstyles for Short Curly Hair

Now that you know how to care for your short curly hair let's get into the fun part: styling! Here are some step-by-step guides for fancy hairstyles:

1. Curly Bob with Bangs

This style is perfect for those with short bobs and curly hair. It's easy to achieve and looks great for any occasion.

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with sulfate-free products.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream.
  3. Use a diffuser to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go along.
  4. Use a curling iron to add some definition to your bangs, curling them away from your face.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

2. Braided Crown

This style is perfect for special occasions and is surprisingly easy to achieve.

  1. Part your hair down the middle and create two sections.
  2. French braid each section, starting from the hairline and working your way back.
  3. Once you reach the nape of your neck, secure the braids with bobby pins.
  4. Make sure to tuck the ends of the braids under each other to create a seamless look.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

This style is perfect for those who want to show off their curls while keeping their hair out of their face.

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with sulfate-free products.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream.
  3. Use a diffuser to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go along.
  4. Section off the top half of your hair, leaving the bottom half loose.
  5. Twist the top section of your hair and secure it with bobby pins, leaving some curls loose in the front.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair

Now that you've mastered some fancy hairstyles for short curly hair, here are some tips on how to maintain your curls:

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to avoid frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my short curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your short curly hair, but make sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Q: How often should I wash my short curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but generally, you can wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I color my short curly hair?

A: Yes, you can color your short curly hair, but make sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair's health and color.

Hair Care for Short Curly Hair

Finally, here are some products that can help you care for your short curly hair:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner or curl cream
  • Diffuser attachment for your hairdryer
  • Wide-toothed comb
  • Satin or silk pillowcase
  • Deep conditioning treatment

With the right products and techniques, you can achieve beautiful and fancy hairstyles for short curly hair. Remember to embrace your natural texture and have fun experimenting with different styles!
