How Do I Get Curly Hair Without A Perm

Written by Christine Aug 25, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you dreaming of having beautiful, bouncy curls but don't want to damage your hair with a perm? You're not alone! Many people crave curly hair without wanting to commit to a permanent change. Luckily, there are many ways to achieve curly hair without a perm, and we're here to guide you through them all!

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get Curly Hair Without a Perm

There are many ways to get curly hair without a perm, including:

  • Heat styling tools
  • Curling irons and wands
  • Rollers and twist-outs
  • Braids and bantu knots
  • Curl-enhancing products

Each of these methods has its own pros and cons, and the best one for you will depend on your hair type, lifestyle, and personal preferences. Let's dive into each method in more detail!

Heat Styling Tools

One of the easiest ways to get curly hair without a perm is by using heat styling tools. Curling irons and wands are great for creating defined, bouncy curls, while flat irons can be used to create looser waves. To get the best results, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and start with clean, dry hair.

Curling Irons and Wands

Curling irons and wands come in many sizes, from thin to thick, and can create a variety of curl types. To use a curling iron or wand, wrap small sections of hair around the barrel, hold for a few seconds, and release. Repeat until all of your hair is curled, then finish with a light hold hairspray.

Rollers and Twist-Outs

Rollers and twist-outs are great options for achieving curly hair without heat. Rollers can be used on wet or dry hair and come in varying sizes, while twist-outs involve twisting small sections of hair and leaving them to dry overnight. Both methods can create natural-looking, voluminous curls.

Braids and Bantu Knots

Braids and bantu knots are protective styles that can also create beautiful curls. To achieve this look, braid or twist small sections of damp hair and leave to dry overnight. In the morning, unravel the braids or knots and use a pick or your fingers to separate the curls.

Curl-Enhancing Products

There are many products on the market specifically designed to enhance curls and waves. Look for curl creams, mousses, and gels that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and aloe vera to nourish and define your curls. Apply to damp hair and scrunch to encourage curl formation.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to try one of these methods for yourself? Follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose your preferred method from the list above.
  3. If using heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant spray.
  4. Section your hair into small, manageable sections.
  5. For heat styling tools, wrap small sections of hair around the barrel and hold for a few seconds before releasing.
  6. For rollers and twist-outs, roll or twist small sections of hair and leave to dry overnight.
  7. For braids and bantu knots, braid or twist small sections of damp hair and leave to dry overnight.
  8. In the morning, unravel the curls and use a pick or your fingers to separate them.
  9. Finish with a light hold hairspray or curl-enhancing product.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Once you've achieved your dream curls, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause them to frizz and lose definition.

FAQs

Can I get curly hair without a perm if I have straight hair?

Yes! There are many methods for achieving curly hair without a perm, regardless of your hair texture.

Will my curls last all day?

This will depend on the method you choose and your hair type. Heat styling tools will typically last longer than twist-outs or bantu knots, but using a curl-enhancing product can help your curls last all day.

Can I still straighten my hair after using one of these methods?

Yes! You can still straighten your hair after using any of these methods, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a specific hair care routine to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can dry out your curls. Stick to warm or cool water instead.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your curls.
  • Try not to touch your hair too often, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curl pattern.

With these tips and methods, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls without a perm. Experiment with different methods and products to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to rock your natural curls!
