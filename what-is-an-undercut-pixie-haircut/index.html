What Is An Undercut Pixie Haircut

Written by Eveline Feb 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to our blog post about what is an undercut pixie haircut! If you're looking for a trendy and edgy hairstyle that is low-maintenance, then an undercut pixie haircut might be the perfect style for you. In this post, we'll cover everything you need to know about this popular hairstyle, including how to get it, tips for styling it, and how to care for your hair after getting this cut.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An undercut pixie haircut is a short hairstyle that features a shaved or closely-cropped section on one or both sides of the head, while the top is left longer. This style is perfect for those who want to create a bold and edgy look that is both versatile and low-maintenance. It is a popular choice among women who want a dramatic change in their appearance, and it can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to

If you're interested in getting an undercut pixie haircut, the first step is to find a reputable hairstylist who has experience cutting short hair. When you go to your appointment, bring a few pictures of the style you like to give your stylist an idea of what you're looking for.

Before your stylist starts cutting your hair, they will typically shave or clip the undercut section of your hair. You can choose to have the undercut on one side of your head or both sides, depending on your preference. Once the undercut is done, your stylist will begin cutting the top section of your hair to your desired length.

Step by Step

Here are the basic steps for getting an undercut pixie haircut:

  1. Find a reputable hairstylist who has experience cutting short hair.
  2. Bring pictures of the style you like to show your stylist.
  3. Decide if you want the undercut on one or both sides of your head.
  4. Your stylist will shave or clip the undercut section of your hair.
  5. Your stylist will begin cutting the top section of your hair to your desired length.

Tips

Here are a few tips for styling and maintaining your undercut pixie haircut:

  • Use a texturizing product to add volume and hold to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, like headbands or barrettes, to add interest to your style.
  • Be prepared to get regular trims to maintain your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some commonly asked questions about undercut pixie haircuts:

Is an undercut pixie haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, an undercut pixie haircut can be adapted to suit different face shapes. Your hairstylist can help you choose the best style for your face shape.

How often do I need to get my undercut pixie haircut trimmed?

You should plan on getting your undercut pixie haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for caring for your hair after getting an undercut pixie haircut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, like curling irons or flat irons, too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
