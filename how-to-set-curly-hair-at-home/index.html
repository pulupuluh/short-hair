How To Set Curly Hair At Home Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jun 03, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Curly hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right techniques, you can easily set your curls at home. Whether you have natural curls or you've permed your hair, setting your curls is an essential part of achieving the perfect hairstyle. In this article, we'll show you how to set curly hair at home, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • How to Set Curly Hair at Home
  • Step by Step
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Set Curly Hair at Home

Setting your curly hair at home is simple and easy. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are appropriate for your hair type
  2. Towel dry your hair or use a microfiber towel to gently remove excess water
  3. Apply a curl cream or mousse to your hair, focusing on the ends
  4. Section your hair into small sections, using clips or hair ties
  5. Take one section of hair and wrap it around your finger, starting from the ends and working your way up
  6. Once you reach the roots, secure the curl with a bobby pin or hair clip
  7. Continue this process with each section of hair
  8. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to speed up the process
  9. Once your hair is completely dry, remove the clips or bobby pins and gently finger comb your curls
  10. Finish with a light hairspray or curl defining spray to hold your curls in place

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Before you start setting your curls, it's important to wash and condition your hair with products that are appropriate for your hair type. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have oily hair, use a clarifying shampoo and a lightweight conditioner.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry your hair or use a microfiber towel to remove excess water. Do not rub your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply curl cream or mousse

Apply a curl cream or mousse to your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help define your curls and prevent frizz.

Step 4: Section your hair

Using clips or hair ties, section your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to set your curls and ensure that each curl is uniform.

Step 5: Wrap the hair around your finger

Take one section of hair and wrap it around your finger, starting from the ends and working your way up. This will create a spiral curl.

Step 6: Secure the curl

Once you reach the roots, secure the curl with a bobby pin or hair clip. This will hold the curl in place while it dries.

Step 7: Repeat the process

Continue this process with each section of hair, making sure that each curl is uniform.

Step 8: Allow your hair to air dry

Once you've set all of your curls, allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to speed up the process.

Step 9: Remove the clips or bobby pins

Once your hair is completely dry, remove the clips or bobby pins and gently finger comb your curls.

Step 10: Finish with hairspray

Finish with a light hairspray or curl defining spray to hold your curls in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you set your curly hair at home:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before you start setting your curls
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before applying curl cream or mousse
  • If you have naturally curly hair, consider using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls
  • Don't touch your hair while it's drying, as this can cause frizz
  • If you're setting your curls overnight, sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz

FAQ

How often should I set my curls?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly your curls fall out. Some people can set their curls once a week, while others may need to set their curls every day.

Can I set my curls with a hair straightener?

No, using a hair straightener to set your curls can damage your hair and cause breakage. Stick to using curl creams and mousse to define your curls.

Can I set my curls without products?

You can set your curls without products, but using a curl cream or mousse will help define your curls and prevent frizz.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use products that are appropriate for your hair type
  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to moisturize your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

By following these tips and tricks, you can easily set your curly hair at home and achieve the perfect hairstyle. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and following a proper hair care routine.
