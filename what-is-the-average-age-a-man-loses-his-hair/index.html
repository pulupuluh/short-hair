What Is The Average Age A Man Loses His Hair

Written by Robby May 08, 2023 · 8 min read

As men age, one of the most common concerns is hair loss. Although hair loss is a natural part of aging, it can be alarming for some men. If you are wondering what is the average age a man loses his hair, this article is for you. In this article, we’ll discuss the average age for hair loss, why it happens, and what you can do to slow down or prevent hair loss.

Table of Contents

  • What is the Average Age for Hair Loss in Men?
  • Why Do Men Lose Their Hair?
  • How to Slow Down Hair Loss?
  • How to Prevent Hair Loss?
  • Hair Care Tips for Men
  • FAQs

What is the Average Age for Hair Loss in Men?

The average age for hair loss in men is around 35 years old. However, some men can experience hair loss as early as their 20s, while others may not see any hair loss until their 50s or 60s. Hair loss can be influenced by genetics, lifestyle, and overall health.

Factors That Influence the Age of Hair Loss

Several factors can influence the age at which a man will start to lose his hair. These factors include:

  • Genetics: Hair loss can be hereditary, meaning if your father or grandfather experienced hair loss, you may be more likely to experience it too.
  • Hormonal Changes: Changes in hormones can cause hair loss, such as an increase in DHT (dihydrotestosterone).
  • Stress: High levels of stress can cause hair loss, especially if it is prolonged and chronic.
  • Diet: Poor nutrition can lead to hair loss, as the body requires nutrients to grow and maintain healthy hair.
  • Medical Conditions: Medical conditions such as thyroid disease or scalp infections can cause hair loss.

Why Do Men Lose Their Hair?

Hair loss in men is primarily caused by male pattern baldness, also known as androgenetic alopecia. This condition is caused by genetics and hormonal changes. Male pattern baldness typically starts with thinning hair at the temples and crown of the head, which can progress to complete baldness over time.

In addition to male pattern baldness, other factors that can contribute to hair loss in men include:

  • Stress
  • Poor nutrition
  • Medical conditions
  • Medications or treatments
  • Hairstyles that pull on the hair

How to Slow Down Hair Loss?

While hair loss is a natural part of aging, there are ways to slow down the process. Here are some tips:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Manage stress levels.
  • Avoid hairstyles that pull on the hair.
  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to harsh chemicals or heat styling tools.

How to Prevent Hair Loss?

Preventing hair loss is not always possible, but there are things you can do to reduce your risk. These include:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Limit your use of harsh chemicals and heat styling tools.
  • Avoid hairstyles that pull on the hair.
  • Take care of your scalp by keeping it clean and moisturized.
  • Consider using a hair growth treatment such as minoxidil.

Hair Care Tips for Men

In addition to slowing down or preventing hair loss, taking care of your hair can also help you maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to harsh chemicals or heat styling tools.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote blood flow.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: Is hair loss reversible?

A: While hair loss is not always reversible, there are treatments and medications that can help slow down or even stop hair loss.

Q: Does wearing a hat cause hair loss?

A: No, wearing a hat does not cause hair loss. However, wearing a tight hat for extended periods can cause hair breakage and damage.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, high levels of stress can cause hair loss, especially if it is prolonged and chronic.

Q: Is hair loss just a natural part of aging?

A: Yes, hair loss is a natural part of aging for most men. However, there are factors that can contribute to hair loss, such as genetics, lifestyle, and overall health.


In conclusion, the average age for hair loss in men is around 35 years old. However, hair loss can occur at any age, and is influenced by genetics, lifestyle, and overall health. While hair loss is a natural part of aging, there are ways to slow down or prevent it, such as eating a healthy diet, managing stress levels, and avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools. By taking care of your hair and scalp, you can maintain healthy hair and reduce your risk of hair loss.
