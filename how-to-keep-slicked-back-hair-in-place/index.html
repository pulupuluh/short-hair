How To Keep Slicked Back Hair In Place A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're someone who loves sporting slicked back hair, you know how frustrating it can be when your hairstyle falls out of place. But worry not, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about how to keep slicked back hair in place. From the products you need to the techniques you can use, we've got you covered.

Description

Slicked back hair is a timeless hairstyle that has been popular among men for decades. It's a classic look that exudes sophistication and style. However, keeping your slicked back hair in place can be challenging, especially if you have thick or curly hair. But with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and polished look that lasts all day.

How to Keep Slicked Back Hair in Place: Step by Step

Step 1: Start with clean hair

The first step to keeping your slicked back hair in place is to start with clean hair. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup, oil, or dirt from your hair. This will ensure that your hair is free from any impurities that may cause your hairstyle to fall out of place.

Step 2: Apply a strong hold pomade

Once your hair is clean, apply a strong hold pomade to your hair. Pomade is a styling product that is designed to provide hold and shine to your hair. It's perfect for creating slicked back hairstyles as it provides a strong hold that lasts all day. Apply the pomade evenly throughout your hair, making sure to distribute it from root to tip.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Using a comb, comb your hair back in the direction you want it to stay. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back towards the crown of your head. Make sure to comb your hair evenly, ensuring that there are no tangles or knots.

Step 4: Use a blow dryer

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while combing it back. This will help to set the pomade and ensure that your hair stays in place. Use a low to medium heat setting, and make sure to keep the blow dryer at least six inches away from your hair.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

To ensure that your slicked back hairstyle stays in place all day, finish with a light misting of hairspray. This will provide extra hold and help to keep your hair in place, even in windy or humid conditions.

Tips for Keeping Slicked Back Hair in Place

Here are some additional tips to help you keep your slicked back hair in place:

  • Use a strong hold pomade that is specifically designed for slicked back hairstyles
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy
  • Use a fine-tooth comb to create a sleeker look
  • If you have curly or thick hair, use a hair straightener to help smooth out your hair before applying pomade
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day as this can cause your hairstyle to fall out of place

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have a slicked back hairstyle?

A: It's recommended that you wash your hair every other day if you have a slicked back hairstyle. This will help to keep your hair clean and free from any impurities that may cause your hairstyle to fall out of place.

Q: Can I use hairspray instead of pomade?

A: While hairspray can provide hold, it's not ideal for creating slicked back hairstyles. Pomade is specifically designed for this type of hairstyle and provides a stronger hold that lasts all day.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips for slicked back hair:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or impurities from your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair straighteners or curling irons too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy

With these tips and techniques, you can keep your slicked back hair in place all day long. Whether you're heading to the office or out for a night on the town, you'll look sharp and stylish with your sleek and polished hairstyle.
