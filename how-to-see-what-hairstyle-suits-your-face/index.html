How To See What Hairstyle Suits Your Face

Written by Daniel Jun 11, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of constantly trying new hairstyles but never finding the perfect one? Look no further! In this post, we will guide you through the process of determining what hairstyle suits your face shape. By following our tips and steps, you’ll be able to find a hairstyle that will accentuate your best features and make you look and feel confident.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Find Hairstyles That Suit Your Face Shape
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, you can do the following:

  • Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  • Use a washable marker or lipstick to outline the shape of your face on the mirror.
  • Step back and look at the shape you drew.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can move on to the next step.

Step 2: Find Hairstyles That Suit Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it’s time to find hairstyles that suit it. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Oval: This is the most versatile face shape, and almost any hairstyle will suit it.
  • Round: Long hairstyles with layers or waves can help elongate the face.
  • Square: Soft, layered hairstyles that frame the face can soften the angles.
  • Heart: Side-swept bangs and chin-length bobs can balance the face.
  • Diamond: Hairstyles with volume at the crown or jaw-length bobs can enhance the cheekbones.
  • Oblong: Shorter hairstyles with volume on the sides can add width to the face.

Remember that these are just general guidelines, and you should choose a hairstyle that you feel comfortable with and that suits your personal style.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness. Some hairstyles may not work well with certain textures or thicknesses.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you’re constantly on the go, you may want a low-maintenance hairstyle.
  • Consult with a hairstylist. They can provide expert advice on what hairstyle will suit your face shape and personal style.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual hairstyle apps to see how different hairstyles will look on you.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear a hairstyle that doesn’t suit my face shape?

A: Of course! These guidelines are just suggestions, and you should wear a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Q: How often should I change my hairstyle?

A: It’s up to you! Some people like to switch up their hairstyle every few months, while others prefer to stick with a tried-and-true look.

Hair Care Tips

Once you’ve found the perfect hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

By following these tips and guidelines, you’ll be able to find a hairstyle that suits your face shape and personal style. Remember to have fun and experiment with different looks!
