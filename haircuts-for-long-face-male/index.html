Haircuts For Long Face Male The Ultimate Guide For 2023

Written by Frank Jul 12, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you a male with a long face shape and struggling to find the perfect haircut that compliments your features? Look no further! In this ultimate guide, we will discuss the best haircuts for long face male and provide you with tips on how to maintain your new look. Let’s get started!

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long face shapes are characterized by a narrow forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a pointed chin. The goal of a haircut for a long face male is to add width and volume to the sides of the face while keeping the length on top. This will create a balance and proportion to the face.

How to

When deciding on a haircut for a long face male, it’s essential to consider the face shape and hair type. A consultation with a professional hairstylist can help determine the best cut for your features. It’s also important to consider personal style and lifestyle when choosing a haircut.

Step by step

The following are the best haircuts for long face male:

1. The Side Part

The side part is a classic haircut that works well for long face shapes. The hair is parted to the side, and the sides are left longer to add width to the face. The top can be left longer or cut shorter, depending on personal preference.

2. The Pompadour

The pompadour is a popular haircut that works well for long face shapes. The hair is slicked back on the sides and voluminous on top. This style adds height to the face, creating a balanced look.

3. The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a low-maintenance haircut that works well for long face shapes. The hair is cut short all over, creating a clean and polished look. This style is perfect for those who don’t want to spend a lot of time styling their hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut for a long face male:

  • Ask your hairstylist to add volume to the sides of your face to create balance.
  • Consider your hair type when choosing a haircut. For example, straight hair may work better with a buzz cut, while curly hair may work better with a pompadour.
  • Use hair products, such as styling gel or mousse, to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a long face male?

The best haircut for a long face male is one that adds width and volume to the sides of the face while keeping the length on top. The side part, pompadour, and buzz cut are all great options.

How can I determine my face shape?

To determine your face shape, measure the length and width of your face and forehead. If the length is longer than the width, you have a long face shape.

Hair Care

To maintain your new haircut, it’s essential to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type and use hair products, such as styling gel or mousse, to add volume and texture. It’s also important to get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking fresh and polished.

In conclusion, getting a haircut for a long face male can be a daunting task, but with the right knowledge and tips, it can be a breeze. Consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style when choosing a haircut, and don’t forget to take care of your hair to maintain your new look. Good luck!
