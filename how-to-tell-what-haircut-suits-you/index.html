How To Tell What Haircut Suits You

Are you tired of getting haircuts that don't suit you? Do you want to know how to choose the perfect haircut for your face shape and hair type? Look no further! In this article, we will give you tips and tricks on how to tell what haircut suits you.

How to Tell What Haircut Suits You

Choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what suits you. Here are some tips on how to tell what haircut suits you:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right haircut is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. You can determine your face shape by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

2. Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type is another important factor to consider when choosing a haircut. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and kinky. Each hair type requires a different type of haircut to look its best.

3. Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is also an important factor to consider when choosing a haircut. If you have a busy schedule, you may want a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal styling. If you attend formal events regularly, you may want a more formal haircut that can be styled for different occasions.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to tell what haircut suits you:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline to determine your face shape.

2. Research Haircuts for Your Face Shape

Once you have determined your face shape, research haircuts that suit your face shape. There are many online resources that can help you find the perfect haircut for your face shape.

3. Consider Your Hair Type

Think about your hair type and choose a haircut that works well with your hair type. Straight hair typically looks best with blunt cuts, while curly hair looks best with layers.

4. Think About Your Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle and choose a haircut that suits your needs. If you have a busy schedule, choose a low-maintenance haircut. If you attend formal events regularly, choose a more formal haircut.

5. Consult with a Hairstylist

If you're still unsure about which haircut suits you, consult with a hairstylist. They can give you professional advice based on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

Tips

  • Bring a picture of the haircut you want to your hairstylist.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new.
  • Choose a haircut that makes you feel confident.

FAQ

What if I don't know my face shape?

You can determine your face shape by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. There are also many online resources that can help you determine your face shape.

What if I have multiple hair types?

If you have multiple hair types, choose a haircut that works well with the majority of your hair.

Can I still style my hair after getting a low-maintenance haircut?

Yes, even low-maintenance haircuts can be styled for different occasions.

Hair Care

Once you have chosen the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after washing to keep it hydrated.
  • Use heat protectant products before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and excessive heat styling.

Now that you know how to tell what haircut suits you, go out and find the perfect haircut for you! Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, and don't be afraid to try something new.
