What Is The Most Rare Face Shape

Written by Daniel Sep 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Have you ever wondered what the most rare face shape is? You may be surprised to learn that there is no definitive answer to this question. However, there are certain face shapes that are considered to be less common than others. In this article, we'll explore some of the most rare face shapes and provide tips on how to accentuate your features.

What are the Most Rare Face Shapes?

There are several face shapes that are considered to be less common than others. These include:

  • Diamond
  • Heart
  • Oblong
  • Pear

While these face shapes may be less common, they can be just as beautiful as more common shapes like oval and round. In fact, many celebrities have these face shapes and have learned how to accentuate their features to look their best.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you're not sure what your face shape is, there are a few simple steps you can follow to figure it out:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner.
  3. Compare the shape of your face to the common face shapes (oval, round, square, heart, diamond, oblong, and pear).

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start learning how to accentuate your features.

Tips for Accentuating Your Features

Each face shape has its own unique features that can be accentuated in different ways. Here are some tips for accentuating your features based on your face shape:

  • Diamond: Highlight your cheekbones with blush or bronzer.
  • Heart: Use a contouring powder to define your jawline.
  • Oblong: Add volume to your hair to balance out your face shape.
  • Pear: Highlight your forehead and cheekbones to draw attention upwards.

By following these tips, you can learn how to accentuate your features and feel more confident in your own skin.

FAQ About Face Shapes

Here are some frequently asked questions about face shapes:

Q: Can I have a combination of face shapes?
A: Yes, it's possible to have a combination of face shapes. For example, you may have a heart-shaped face with a strong jawline. Q: Is one face shape more attractive than others?
A: No, all face shapes can be attractive in their own way. It's more about how you accentuate your features than the shape of your face. Q: Can I change my face shape?
A: No, you can't change your face shape. However, you can use makeup and hairstyles to accentuate your features and create the illusion of a different face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Different Face Shapes

Your hair can also play a role in accentuating your features based on your face shape. Here are some hair care tips for different face shapes:

  • Oval: You can pull off just about any hairstyle, but avoid styles that create too much volume on top.
  • Round: Go for a longer hairstyle that elongates your face.
  • Square: Soften your features with a layered haircut.
  • Heart: Keep your hair away from your face to draw attention to your cheekbones.
  • Diamond: Add volume to the sides of your hair to balance out your face shape.
  • Oblong: Add volume to the top of your hair to balance out your face shape.
  • Pear: Keep your hair full and voluminous on top to draw attention upwards.

By following these hair care tips, you can create a hairstyle that complements your face shape and accentuates your features.

Conclusion

While there is no definitive answer to what the most rare face shape is, there are certain face shapes that are considered to be less common than others. By learning how to accentuate your features based on your face shape, you can look and feel your best.
