Medium Haircuts For Thick Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Feb 18, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your thick curly hair? Look no further than medium length haircuts! Not only do they offer versatility in styling, but they also help to tame unruly curls. In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about medium haircuts for thick curly hair, including how to achieve the look, tips for maintenance and care, and frequently asked questions.

How to Achieve the Look

When it comes to medium haircuts for thick curly hair, the possibilities are endless. However, some popular styles include:

  • Layered cuts: Layers can add texture and movement to your curls, making them more manageable.
  • Bob cuts: A medium-length bob can be a great option for those with thick curly hair, as it can help to control volume while still accentuating your curls.
  • Long shaggy cuts: This style is perfect for those with thick, voluminous curls, as it can help to create more definition and shape.

To achieve these looks, it’s important to work with a stylist who has experience cutting curly hair. They can help you determine which style will work best for your hair type and face shape, and can also offer advice on styling and maintenance.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re looking to achieve a medium haircut for thick curly hair at home, here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products specifically designed for curly hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  3. Section your hair and begin cutting. It’s important to cut your hair when it’s dry, as this will help you see the natural shape of your curls.
  4. Work in layers, cutting small sections at a time. This will help to create a textured, layered look.
  5. Once you’ve finished cutting, style your hair as desired using a curl-enhancing product.

Tips for Maintenance and Care

To keep your medium haircut looking its best, it’s important to take care of your hair on a daily basis. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when they’re wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to your hair while it’s still damp to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to help reduce friction and prevent breakage while you sleep.

FAQ

1. What is the best haircut for thick curly hair?

The best haircut for thick curly hair is one that takes your hair type and face shape into consideration. Some popular options include layered cuts, bob cuts, and long shaggy cuts.

2. Can you cut curly hair when it’s wet?

It’s generally not recommended to cut curly hair when it’s wet, as this can lead to uneven cuts and shrinkage. It’s best to cut your hair when it’s dry, as this will help you see the natural shape of your curls.

3. How often should you wash curly hair?

It’s recommended to wash curly hair every 2-3 days, as over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and frizz.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your thick curly hair, it’s important to use products specifically designed for your hair type. Look for sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners that are formulated to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. It’s also a good idea to invest in a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair. Finally, be gentle when detangling your curls and avoid using heat styling tools on a regular basis, as this can cause damage and breakage.
