Mens Short Haircut A Guide To The Best Styles In 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 15, 2023 · 8 min read

Short haircuts for men have become increasingly popular over the years, and it’s not hard to understand why. From low-maintenance styles to sleek and stylish looks, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this guide, we’ll take a closer look at some of the best mens short haircuts in 2023, as well as how to achieve them, tips for styling, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Mens short haircuts are versatile and can be tailored to suit any face shape, hair type, and personal style. Whether you’re looking for a classic crew cut, a textured crop, or a slick back undercut, there’s a short haircut out there for you. Short haircuts are also low-maintenance and easy to style, making them perfect for men who are always on the go.

How to

Before you head to the barber or start cutting your own hair, it’s important to know what kind of short haircut you want. Look through pictures online, ask friends for recommendations, or consult with your barber to find a style that will work best for you. Once you’ve decided on a style, you can follow these simple steps to achieve the look:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup and ensure that your hair is clean and healthy.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

Gently towel dry your hair until it’s damp but not wet. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Step 3: Apply styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as a pomade or wax. Use your fingers to work the product into your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up to the ends.

Step 4: Style your hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape. For a textured look, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create volume. For a sleek and polished look, use a comb to smooth your hair back.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish off your look by applying a light mist of hairspray to hold your hair in place. Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of the most popular mens short haircuts:

The Crew Cut

The crew cut is a classic short haircut that is both stylish and low-maintenance. Here’s how to achieve the look:

  • Start by trimming the sides and back of your hair with clippers, leaving the top longer.
  • Use scissors to cut the top of your hair to the desired length.
  • Use a comb to blend the sides and top together for a seamless look.
  • Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair and style as desired.

The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a modern short haircut that is perfect for men with thick, wavy hair. Here’s how to achieve the look:

  • Ask your barber to cut your hair to a length of 2-3 inches on top, and shorter on the sides and back.
  • Use scissors to create texture and movement in your hair by cutting into the ends of your hair.
  • Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair and use your fingers to tousle your hair and create volume.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a mens short haircut:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type.
  • Bring pictures to your barber to help communicate the look you want.
  • Invest in high-quality styling products to achieve the best results.
  • Maintain your short haircut by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ

What are the best short haircuts for men?

Some of the best short haircuts for men include the crew cut, the textured crop, the slick back undercut, and the buzz cut.

How often should I get a haircut?

Men with short haircuts should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of their haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your mens short haircut looking its best, it’s important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, as these can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to achieve the perfect mens short haircut in 2023. Remember to choose a style that works for you, take good care of your hair, and have fun experimenting with different looks!
