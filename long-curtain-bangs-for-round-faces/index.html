Long Curtain Bangs For Round Faces A Stylish Choice

Written by Oliver Jun 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long curtain bangs have been trending in the fashion industry for quite some time now. They are a versatile hairstyle that adds a touch of elegance to any look. However, if you have a round face, you might be hesitant to try it out. Fear not, because long curtain bangs are a great choice for round faces too! In this article, we will discuss how to get long curtain bangs for round faces, tips, and hair care.

Description

Long curtain bangs are a style that falls on either side of your face, framing your features. They are usually longer than your regular bangs, and the length can vary depending on the desired look. Long curtain bangs are versatile and can be styled in many ways. They can be straight, wavy or curly, and paired with any hairstyle.

How To

The first step in getting long curtain bangs for round faces is to choose the right length. The bangs should start from the crown of your head and fall on either side of your face. The length of the bangs should be longer than your eyebrows, and they should gradually become shorter towards the sides.

Next, you need to decide how you want to style your long curtain bangs. You can go for a sleek and straight look or add some texture and volume to them. If you have naturally curly hair, you can embrace your curls and let your long curtain bangs blend in with them.

It's important to keep in mind that long curtain bangs require regular trimming to maintain their length and shape. You can either trim them yourself or visit a hairstylist every few weeks.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting long curtain bangs for round faces:

  • Choose the right length for your bangs
  • Decide how you want to style your bangs
  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their length and shape

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting long curtain bangs for round faces:

  • Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face look rounder
  • Pair your long curtain bangs with layers to add dimension to your hair
  • Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs for a sleek look
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to see what works best for you

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long curtain bangs for round faces:

Q: Will long curtain bangs make my face look rounder?

A: No, long curtain bangs can actually elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

Q: How do I style my long curtain bangs?

A: You can style your long curtain bangs in many ways, such as straight, wavy or curly. Use a round brush to blow-dry them for a sleek look.

Q: How often do I need to trim my long curtain bangs?

A: You should trim your long curtain bangs every few weeks to maintain their length and shape.

Hair Care

Long curtain bangs require regular hair care to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny

Long curtain bangs for round faces are a stylish choice that can add a touch of elegance to any look. Follow these tips and hair care routines to keep your long curtain bangs looking their best.
