How To Know What Hair Color Suits You Best

Written by Oliver May 13, 2023 · 5 min read

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Choosing the right hair color is not an easy task, especially if you are doing it for the first time. You might be confused about which hair color will suit you best. However, to know what hair color suits you best, you need to consider several factors such as skin tone, eye color, natural hair color, and personal style. Here’s a guide that will help you choose the perfect hair color that complements your features.

How to

To know what hair color suits you best, you need to do the following:

  1. Identify your skin tone
  2. Consider your eye color
  3. Think about your natural hair color
  4. Consider your personal style

Step by step

Step 1: Identify your skin tone

Determine whether you have a cool, warm, or neutral skin tone. If you have a cool skin tone, your veins appear blue, and you might have pink or red undertones. If you have a warm skin tone, your veins appear green, and you might have yellow or golden undertones. If you have a neutral skin tone, your veins appear both blue and green, and you might have a combination of pink, red, yellow, or golden undertones.

Step 2: Consider your eye color

If you have blue or green eyes, consider warm hair colors such as golden blonde, honey blonde, or warm brown. If you have brown or hazel eyes, consider cool hair colors such as ash blonde, ash brown, or cool black.

Step 3: Think about your natural hair color

If you have dark hair, consider warm colors such as caramel, chestnut, or auburn. If you have light hair, consider cool colors such as platinum, ash blonde, or silver.

Step 4: Consider your personal style

If you have a classic style, consider natural hair colors such as brown or blonde. If you have an edgy style, consider bold hair colors such as pink or purple.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hair color:

  • Consult with a professional hairstylist
  • Consider the maintenance of the hair color
  • Experiment with temporary hair color before committing to permanent hair color
  • Consider your lifestyle and job

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have a sensitive scalp?

A: Yes, but make sure to use a hair dye that is specifically designed for sensitive scalps.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, but make sure to follow the instructions on the hair dye package carefully.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it’s essential to take care of it to maintain its color and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid hot styling tools
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Knowing what hair color suits you best can be a daunting task, but with these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hair color that complements your features and style. Remember to take care of your hair after dyeing it to maintain its color and health.
