Short Pixie Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces A Guide

Written by Petter Apr 28, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you know how important it is to find a haircut that complements your features. Short pixie haircuts are a great option for heart shaped faces, as they can highlight your cheekbones, jawline, and delicate chin. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about short pixie haircuts for heart shaped faces, including how to choose the right cut, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Right Cut
  2. Styling Tips
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintenance
  5. Frequently Asked Questions

How to Choose the Right Cut

When choosing a short pixie haircut for a heart shaped face, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. Heart shaped faces are widest at the forehead and narrowest at the chin, so you'll want to choose a cut that balances out these proportions.

A short pixie cut with longer layers on top can help create the illusion of width at the chin, while shorter layers around the ears can add volume to the sides of your face. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a choppy, textured cut to help thin out your hair and create movement. If you have fine hair, a layered cut can add volume and dimension to your hair.

Styling Tips

Short pixie haircuts for heart shaped faces can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and polished to messy and textured. Here are some styling tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and texture.
  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to create a messy, textured look.
  • For a more polished look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair and apply a shine serum.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style a short pixie haircut for a heart shaped face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the roots to create volume.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair, if desired.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray or pomade to create a messy, textured look.
  6. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create volume.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintenance

Short pixie haircuts for heart shaped faces require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your style between washes.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will a short pixie haircut make my face look even more heart shaped?

A: No, a short pixie haircut can actually help balance out the proportions of a heart shaped face. By adding volume to the sides of your face and creating width at the chin, a short pixie cut can help create a more balanced look.

Q: Can I still style my short pixie haircut if I have fine hair?

A: Yes, you can still style a short pixie haircut if you have fine hair. Just be sure to use products that add volume and texture, such as a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray.

Q: How often should I get my short pixie haircut trimmed?

A: You should get your short pixie haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking its best.

Q: Can I still wear accessories with a short pixie haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear accessories with a short pixie haircut, such as headbands, clips, and barrettes. Just be sure to choose accessories that complement your hairstyle and face shape.

Conclusion

Short pixie haircuts are a great option for heart shaped faces, as they can help balance out your proportions and highlight your best features. By choosing the right cut, styling your hair properly, and taking good care of your hair, you can rock a short pixie haircut with confidence.
