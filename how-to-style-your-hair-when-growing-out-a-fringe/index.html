How To Style Your Hair When Growing Out A Fringe

Written by Oliver Mar 03, 2023 · 7 min read

When you're growing out a fringe, it can be a difficult and frustrating process. You're stuck with a hairstyle that you don't love, and it seems like it's taking forever to grow out. But don't worry – there are plenty of ways to style your hair to make the transition easier. In this blog post, we'll go over some tips and tricks for styling your hair when growing out a fringe.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Your Hair When Growing Out a Fringe
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair When Growing Out a Fringe
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair When Growing Out a Fringe
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips When Growing Out a Fringe

How to Style Your Hair When Growing Out a Fringe

One of the best ways to style your hair when growing out a fringe is by using hair accessories. Headbands, clips, and bobby pins can all help keep your hair out of your face and make your fringe less noticeable. You can also try parting your hair in different ways to hide your fringe – a side part or a center part can work wonders.

If you're looking for a more natural look, try blow-drying your hair with a round brush. This will help blend your fringe in with the rest of your hair. You can also try using a curling iron or straightener to create waves or curls, which can help hide your fringe.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair When Growing Out a Fringe

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair when growing out a fringe:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair in a way that hides your fringe.
  3. Use hair accessories like headbands or clips to keep your hair out of your face.
  4. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to blend your fringe in with the rest of your hair.
  5. Use a curling iron or straightener to create waves or curls that can help hide your fringe.

Tips for Styling Your Hair When Growing Out a Fringe

Here are some additional tips for styling your hair when growing out a fringe:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to see what works best for you.
  • Try using dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Consider getting a trim to help shape your hair as it grows out.
  • Be patient – growing out a fringe takes time!

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing out a fringe:

Q: How long does it take to grow out a fringe?

A: It can take anywhere from a few months to a year or more to fully grow out a fringe. It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long your fringe is.

Q: Can I still style my hair while growing out a fringe?

A: Yes, absolutely! There are plenty of ways to style your hair when growing out a fringe. See our tips above for some ideas.

Q: Should I get a trim while growing out a fringe?

A: It can be helpful to get a trim every few months to help shape your hair as it grows out. Talk to your stylist about what's best for your hair.

Hair Care Tips When Growing Out a Fringe

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when growing out a fringe:

  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair to avoid pulling out your fringe.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make your fringe more noticeable.

Remember, growing out a fringe can be a frustrating process, but with patience and the right styling techniques, you can make the transition easier. Try out different hairstyles and hair accessories, and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice.
