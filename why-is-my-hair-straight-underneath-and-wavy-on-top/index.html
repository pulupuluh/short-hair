Why is My Hair Straight Underneath and Wavy on Top?

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

If you’re wondering why your hair is straight underneath and wavy on top, you’re not alone. This common hair type is called “mixed texture” hair, where the hair on the top is curlier or wavier than the hair on the bottom. Mixed texture hair can be frustrating to style and maintain, but with the right hair care routine and styling products, you can embrace your natural hair and make it work for you.

How to

The first step to managing mixed texture hair is understanding your hair type. If you have straight hair underneath and wavy hair on top, you likely have a combination of two hair types: Type 1 (straight) and Type 2 (wavy). Type 1 hair is straight and doesn’t have any curl or wave, while Type 2 hair has a defined S-shaped pattern.

Step by step

Here are some steps you can take to manage your mixed texture hair:

  1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to moisturize your hair and prevent frizz.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently detangle your hair.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves and curls.
  5. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you manage your mixed texture hair:

  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids, updos, and half-up half-down styles.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and preserve your natural hair oils.
  • Consider getting a haircut that works with your natural hair texture, such as layers or a curly bob.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mixed texture hair:

  1. Is mixed texture hair common? Yes, mixed texture hair is a common hair type that many people have.
  2. Can I straighten my wavy hair on top? Yes, you can straighten your hair using a flat iron or hair straightener. However, be sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using too much heat to prevent damage.
  3. What causes mixed texture hair? Mixed texture hair is caused by genetics and the natural shape of your hair follicles.

Hair Care

Here are some additional hair care tips for managing mixed texture hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than 2-3 times per week to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can strip away natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Invest in a deep conditioning treatment or hair mask to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Consider using a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup and prevent product buildup.
