What Size Barrel Curling Iron For Beachy Waves

Written by Luffy Jul 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Beachy waves are a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. Whether you have long or short hair, beachy waves are perfect for any occasion. The key to achieving this look is by using the right size barrel curling iron. In this blog post, we will discuss what size barrel curling iron for beachy waves and how to achieve this style.

Description

Beachy waves are a relaxed and effortless hairstyle that mimics the look of natural waves that you get from spending a day at the beach. This look is achieved by curling your hair with a curling iron and then brushing out the curls to create a soft and tousled look.

How to

To achieve beachy waves, you will need a curling iron with a barrel size between 1-1.5 inches. This size is perfect for creating loose and relaxed curls that will give you the desired beachy look.

Start by sectioning your hair into small sections and curl each section away from your face. Hold the curling iron for a few seconds and release the curl. Repeat this process until all your hair is curled.

Once you have finished curling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently brush out the curls. This will create a soft and tousled look that is perfect for beachy waves.

Step by Step

Step 1: Start by sectioning your hair into small sections using hair clips.

Step 2: Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel away from your face.

Step 3: Hold the curling iron for a few seconds and release the curl.

Step 4: Repeat this process until all your hair is curled.

Step 5: Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently brush out the curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect beachy waves:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage to your hair.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly, as this will create a different look.
  • Use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Don't forget to use hairspray to hold your curls in place.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve beachy waves with a flat iron?

A: Yes, you can achieve beachy waves with a flat iron, but it may take longer and be harder to achieve the desired look.

Q: What is the best hair length for beachy waves?

A: Beachy waves look great on any hair length, from short to long.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Limit the use of heat styling tools.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Don't use too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down.

By following these tips, you can achieve the perfect beachy waves and maintain healthy hair. Remember to use the right size barrel curling iron for beachy waves, and you'll be rocking this effortless hairstyle in no time.
