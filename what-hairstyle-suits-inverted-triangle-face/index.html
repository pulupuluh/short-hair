What Hairstyle Suits Inverted Triangle Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver May 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have an inverted triangle face, you'll know that it's wider at the top and narrows down towards the chin. This face shape can be tricky to style, as you don't want to accentuate the width of your forehead any further. However, with the right hairstyle, you can balance out your features and look your best. In this post, we'll cover everything you need to know about what hairstyle suits inverted triangle face.

Description

The inverted triangle face shape is also known as the heart-shaped face. It's characterized by a wide forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. This face shape is often seen on celebrities like Reese Witherspoon, Scarlett Johansson, and Jennifer Lawrence. If you have an inverted triangle face, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that balances out your features and draws attention away from your forehead.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for an inverted triangle face, you want to look for styles that add volume to the lower half of your face. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long
  • Choose hairstyles with side-swept bangs to cover your forehead
  • Opt for hairstyles with layers to add volume to the lower half of your face
  • Consider a bob or lob haircut that hits at your chin or collarbone
  • Avoid adding height to the top of your head, such as with a high ponytail or top knot

Step by Step Guide

Here are some step-by-step instructions for styling your hair if you have an inverted triangle face:

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush, focusing on the lower half of your hair to add volume.
  2. If you have bangs, blow-dry them to the side to cover your forehead.
  3. If you have long hair, consider adding layers to the bottom half to balance out your features.
  4. If you have short hair, consider a bob or lob haircut that hits at your chin or collarbone.
  5. If you want to add some texture, use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  6. Avoid adding height to the top of your head, such as with a high ponytail or top knot.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your hair if you have an inverted triangle face:

  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your forehead look even wider.
  • If you have curly hair, avoid adding volume to the top of your head, as this can make your face look more top-heavy.
  • If you have straight hair, consider adding some volume with a volumizing mousse or spray.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what hairstyle suits inverted triangle face:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an inverted triangle face?

Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, such as a high ponytail or top knot. Blunt bangs should also be avoided, as they can make your forehead look even wider.

What hairstyles are best for an inverted triangle face?

Hairstyles with side-swept bangs, layers, and volume in the lower half of your hair are best for an inverted triangle face. Consider a bob or lob haircut that hits at your chin or collarbone.

Should I add highlights or lowlights to my hair if I have an inverted triangle face?

Yes, adding highlights or lowlights can add dimension to your hair and balance out your features. Talk to your stylist about what would work best for your hair color and skin tone.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care for an inverted triangle face, it's important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner, and avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.

When styling your hair, use heat protectant products to prevent damage from hot tools. Avoid using hot tools every day, as this can cause damage to your hair over time. Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

And there you have it - everything you need to know about what hairstyle suits inverted triangle face. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
