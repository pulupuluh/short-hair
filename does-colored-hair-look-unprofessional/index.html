Does Colored Hair Look Unprofessional

Written by Bardi Jul 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you considering coloring your hair but are hesitant because you're worried about looking unprofessional? You're not alone. Many people wonder if colored hair is appropriate for the workplace. In this blog post, we'll explore whether or not colored hair looks unprofessional and provide tips on how to maintain your colored hair.

Why Colored Hair May Be Looked Down Upon

Before we dive into whether or not colored hair is unprofessional, let's address why some people may view it as such. There are a few reasons:

  • Tradition: In the past, natural hair colors were the norm in professional settings. Colored hair was seen as a rebellious or counter-cultural statement.
  • Perception: Some people may associate colored hair with a lack of professionalism, regardless of the individual's qualifications or work ethic.
  • Industry Standards: Certain industries, such as finance or law, may have strict dress codes that prohibit unnatural hair colors.

While these reasons may have some validity, it's important to remember that times have changed. Colored hair is becoming more and more common in the workplace, and many employers are embracing individuality and self-expression.

How to Style Colored Hair Professionally

If you're worried about looking unprofessional with colored hair, there are ways to style it in a way that's appropriate for the workplace. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a Natural-Looking Color: Opt for a color that's as close to your natural hair color as possible. This will make it easier to style your hair in a professional manner.
  • Keep it Tidy: Make sure your hair is well-kept and styled neatly. Avoid styles that are too wild or unkempt.
  • Accessorize Appropriately: Choose accessories that complement your hair color and overall look. Avoid anything too flashy or distracting.

Step-by-Step Guide to Colored Hair Care

One of the keys to maintaining professional-looking colored hair is proper hair care. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose a Quality Color: Make sure you choose a high-quality hair dye that won't fade or damage your hair.
  2. Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner: Invest in a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for colored hair. This will help prevent fading and keep your hair looking vibrant.
  3. Avoid Heat Styling: Heat styling can cause damage and fading to colored hair. Try to air-dry your hair as much as possible and avoid using hot tools.
  4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun: UV rays can cause colored hair to fade. If you're going to be spending time outside, wear a hat or use a UV-protective hair product.

Tips for Rocking Colored Hair in the Workplace

If you're still feeling unsure about whether or not colored hair is appropriate for your workplace, here are some tips to help you feel more confident:

  • Do Your Research: Find out if your workplace has a dress code or policy regarding hair color. If there's no policy in place, you're likely free to do as you please.
  • Start Small: If you're nervous about going all-in with a bold hair color, start with something subtle. For example, you could add some highlights or lowlights to your natural hair color.
  • Be Confident: Confidence is key! If you feel good about your hair, it will show in your demeanor and work performance.

FAQs About Colored Hair in Professional Settings

Q: Is it unprofessional to have colored hair in the workplace?

A: It depends on your workplace's dress code and policies. In general, colored hair is becoming more accepted in professional settings.

Q: What colors are considered unprofessional?

A: Bright, neon colors are generally considered unprofessional. Stick to more natural-looking colors or subtle highlights.

Q: Can I still get hired with colored hair?

A: Absolutely! Your qualifications and work experience are what matter most in the hiring process. As long as your hair is well-kept and styled appropriately, it shouldn't be a problem.

Q: Will coloring my hair damage it?

A: It can if you don't take proper care of it. Make sure you use high-quality hair dye and color-safe hair care products.

Q: Can I dye my hair back to its natural color if I change my mind?

A: Yes, you can always dye your hair back to its natural color if you decide you don't like the colored hair look.

Conclusion

So, does colored hair look unprofessional? The answer is no, as long as it's well-kept and styled appropriately. With the right hair care products and styling techniques, you can rock any hair color you choose in the workplace. Be confident, do your research, and remember that self-expression is important!
