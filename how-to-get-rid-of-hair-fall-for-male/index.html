How To Get Rid Of Hair Fall For Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel May 03, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Hair fall is a common problem that affects both men and women. However, male hair fall is often more severe and can lead to baldness if not treated properly. In this article, we will discuss how to get rid of hair fall for male using natural remedies and lifestyle changes. We will also answer some frequently asked questions about hair fall and provide tips on how to take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • What Causes Hair Fall in Men?
  • How to Get Rid of Hair Fall for Male
  • Natural Remedies for Hair Fall in Men
  • Lifestyle Changes to Prevent Hair Fall in Men

  • FAQs

What Causes Hair Fall in Men?

Hair fall in men can be caused by a variety of factors, including:

  • Genetics
  • Hormonal changes
  • Stress
  • Poor nutrition
  • Medical conditions
  • Medications

If you are experiencing hair fall, it is important to identify the underlying cause in order to choose the best treatment.

How to Get Rid of Hair Fall for Male

There are several ways to get rid of hair fall for male:

Natural Remedies for Hair Fall in Men

Natural remedies can help prevent hair fall and promote hair growth. Some effective remedies include:

  • Massage your scalp with essential oils such as rosemary or lavender oil.
  • Apply aloe vera gel to your scalp and hair.
  • Use onion juice on your scalp.
  • Apply coconut milk to your hair.

Lifestyle Changes to Prevent Hair Fall in Men

Lifestyle changes can also help prevent hair fall. Some effective changes include:

  • Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Reducing stress through exercise or meditation.
  • Avoiding harsh chemicals and heat on your hair.
  • Getting enough sleep.

Medications for Hair Fall in Men

If natural remedies and lifestyle changes do not work, medications may be recommended. Some common medications for hair fall in men include:

  • Finasteride
  • Minoxidil
  • Corticosteroids
  • Antifungal medications

Natural Remedies for Hair Fall in Men

Natural remedies can be effective in preventing hair fall and promoting hair growth. Here are some remedies you can try:

Massage Your Scalp with Essential Oils

Massaging your scalp with essential oils can improve blood circulation and promote hair growth. Some effective essential oils for hair growth include:

  • Rosemary oil
  • Lavender oil
  • Peppermint oil

Simply mix a few drops of your preferred essential oil with a carrier oil such as coconut oil or olive oil and massage it into your scalp. Leave it on for at least 30 minutes before washing it off with shampoo.

Apply Aloe Vera Gel to Your Scalp and Hair

Aloe vera gel has antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties that can help prevent hair fall. It also moisturizes the scalp and promotes hair growth. To use aloe vera gel for hair fall, simply apply it to your scalp and hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with shampoo.

Use Onion Juice on Your Scalp

Onion juice contains sulfur, which can improve blood circulation and promote hair growth. To use onion juice for hair fall, simply extract the juice from an onion and apply it to your scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with shampoo.

Apply Coconut Milk to Your Hair

Coconut milk is rich in vitamins and minerals that can nourish your hair and prevent hair fall. To use coconut milk for hair fall, simply apply it to your hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with shampoo.

Lifestyle Changes to Prevent Hair Fall in Men

Your lifestyle can have a big impact on your hair health. Here are some lifestyle changes you can make to prevent hair fall:

Eat a Balanced Diet

A balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can promote hair growth and prevent hair fall. Make sure to include foods that are rich in the following nutrients:

  • Vitamin A
  • Vitamin C
  • Vitamin E
  • Iron
  • Zinc
  • Protein

Reduce Stress

Stress can cause hair fall by disrupting the hair growth cycle. To reduce stress, try the following:

  • Exercise regularly
  • Meditate
  • Practice deep breathing

Avoid Harsh Chemicals and Heat on Your Hair

Harsh chemicals and heat can damage your hair and cause hair fall. To prevent this, avoid the following:

  • Using hair dyes or bleach
  • Using hair straighteners or curling irons
  • Using hair products that contain alcohol or other harsh chemicals

Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep is important for hair health. Aim to get at least 7-8 hours of sleep per night.

Tips for Hair Care in Men

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help prevent hair fall by removing dirt and oil from your scalp. However, washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, so aim to wash it every 2-3 days.

Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a gentle shampoo and conditioner can help prevent hair fall by keeping your hair and scalp healthy. Look for products that are free of sulfates and other harsh chemicals.

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles such as braids and ponytails can cause hair fall by pulling on your hair. To prevent this, avoid tight hairstyles or wear them less often.

Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Using a wide-toothed comb can prevent hair fall by reducing the amount of pulling and tugging on your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can stress cause hair fall in men?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair fall in men by disrupting the hair growth cycle.

Q: Is male hair fall hereditary?

A: Yes, male hair fall can be hereditary. If you have a family history of baldness, you may be more likely to experience hair fall.

Q: Can medications cause hair fall in men?

A: Yes, some medications can cause hair fall in men. If you are experiencing hair fall, talk to your doctor about any medications you are taking.

Q: How can I prevent hair fall in men?

A: To prevent hair fall in men, try the following:

  • Eat a balanced diet
  • Reduce stress
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and heat on your hair
  • Get enough sleep

Q: What are some natural remedies for hair fall in men?

A: Some natural remedies for hair fall in men include:

  • Massaging your scalp with essential oils
  • Applying aloe vera gel to your scalp and hair
  • Using onion juice on your scalp
  • Applying coconut milk to your hair

By following these tips and remedies, you can effectively get rid of hair fall for male and promote healthy hair growth.
