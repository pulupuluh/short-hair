Good Hair Color For 70 Year Old Woman A Guide

Written by Daniel May 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, our hair naturally loses its color and becomes gray. But that doesn't mean we have to settle for dull and lifeless hair. With the right hair color, a 70 year old woman can look and feel vibrant and youthful. In this article, we'll explore the best hair color options for mature women.

Why Choose a Good Hair Color?

A good hair color can enhance your features, hide imperfections, and make you feel more confident. It can also complement your skin tone and brighten up your complexion, making you look more youthful and radiant. Choosing the right hair color can be challenging, but with the right guidance, you can find the perfect shade that suits your personality and lifestyle.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color?

Choosing the right hair color can depend on a variety of factors, such as your skin tone, eye color, lifestyle, and personal preferences. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair color:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone can influence the hair color that looks best on you. For example, if you have warm undertones, you may look best with golden, honey, or caramel shades. If you have cool undertones, you may look best with ash, platinum, or champagne shades.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also affect your hair color choice. If you have an active lifestyle and spend a lot of time outdoors, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hair color that won't fade or require frequent touch-ups. If you have a more relaxed lifestyle, you may be able to experiment with more daring hair colors and styles.

Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also influence your hair color choice. For example, if you have blue or green eyes, you may look best with warm shades of blonde or brown. If you have brown or hazel eyes, you may look best with cool shades of brown, black, or red.

Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair

If you're ready to color your hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the best results:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hair Color

Choose a hair color that suits your skin tone, eye color, and lifestyle. If you're not sure which color to choose, consult a professional stylist.

Step 2: Gather Your Supplies

You'll need a hair dye kit, gloves, a cape or towel, a mixing bowl, a brush or comb, and a timer.

Step 3: Protect Your Skin and Clothes

Apply petroleum jelly or a barrier cream around your hairline and ears to protect your skin. Wear an old shirt or a cape to protect your clothes.

Step 4: Mix the Hair Dye

Follow the instructions on the hair dye kit and mix the dye in a mixing bowl.

Step 5: Apply the Hair Dye

Apply the hair dye to your hair using a brush or comb. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Be sure to cover all of your hair evenly.

Step 6: Set the Timer

Set the timer according to the instructions on the hair dye kit. Leave the dye on your hair for the recommended time.

Step 7: Rinse Your Hair

Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water until the water runs clear. Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Now that you've colored your hair, here are some tips to help you maintain your new look:

Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for color-treated hair. This will help preserve your hair color and prevent fading.

Avoid Hot Tools

Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons as they can damage your hair and cause your color to fade faster.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV-protective spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Best Hair Color for a 70 Year Old Woman?

The best hair color for a 70 year old woman depends on her skin tone, eye color, and lifestyle. Some popular options include warm shades of blonde, brown, and red.

Can I Color My Hair at Home?

Yes, you can color your hair at home using a hair dye kit. However, it's important to follow the instructions carefully and choose the right hair color for your needs.

How Often Should I Color My Hair?

The frequency of hair coloring depends on your hair type, hair color, and lifestyle. Some people may need to color their hair every 4-6 weeks, while others may only need to color their hair every 8-12 weeks.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair color can make a big difference in your appearance and confidence. By following these tips, you can find the perfect hair color that suits your personality and lifestyle. Remember to take care of your hair by using color-safe products and protecting it from damage.
