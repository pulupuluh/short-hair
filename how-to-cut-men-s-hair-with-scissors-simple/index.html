How To Cut Men s Hair With Scissors Simple A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Apr 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune at the barbershop every month? Or maybe you're just looking for a new skill to learn? Whatever the reason, cutting men's hair with scissors can be a simple and cost-effective way to maintain a fresh look. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to cut men's hair with scissors simple.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Scissors Simple: Step by Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools. This includes a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. It's also a good idea to have a mirror set up so you can see the back of the head.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the hair is clean and free of any product build-up.

Step 3: Comb the Hair

Using a comb, separate the hair into sections. Start at the bottom and work your way up, combing each section as you go.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Using your scissors, start cutting the hair. Begin at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb as a guide. Make sure to cut the hair evenly and at the desired length.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

As you cut, periodically check your progress in the mirror. This will help you ensure that the hair is being cut evenly and that you're not missing any spots.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut all the hair to the desired length, use the scissors to blend the hair. This will help create a more natural look and prevent any harsh lines.

Step 7: Trim the Sides and Back

Using the comb as a guide, trim the sides and back of the hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up, making sure to cut the hair evenly.

Step 8: Clean Up the Neckline

Using the scissors, clean up the neckline by trimming any stray hairs. This will help create a clean and polished look.

Step 9: Style the Hair

Once you've finished cutting the hair, it's time to style it. Use your fingers to apply any product and create the desired look.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors Simple

- Always use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut

- Cut the hair in small sections to ensure an even cut

- Use the comb as a guide to ensure that the hair is being cut evenly

- Check your progress in the mirror periodically to ensure that you're not missing any spots

FAQ: Cutting Men's Hair with Scissors Simple

Can I cut my own hair with scissors?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with scissors. However, it can be difficult to cut the back of your head, so you may want to ask a friend or family member to help.

What kind of scissors should I use?

Always use sharp scissors when cutting hair. Hair cutting scissors are designed specifically for this purpose and can be purchased at most beauty supply stores.

Hair Care Tips

- Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy

- Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable

- Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy

- Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy

With these simple steps and tips, you'll be able to cut men's hair with scissors simple in no time. Not only will you save money, but you'll also have the satisfaction of knowing that you did it yourself.
