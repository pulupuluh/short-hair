Best Hairstyles For Oval Faces Over 60

Written by Robby Feb 19, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have an oval-shaped face, then you are in luck! This is because an oval face shape is one of the most versatile when it comes to hairstyles. As you age, your hair changes, but that doesn't mean you can't have a great hairstyle. With the right cut, you can look and feel younger, more confident, and stylish. In this article, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for oval faces over 60, so keep reading.

  • Best Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 60

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for oval faces over 60, it's important to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Take a close look at your face and determine the widest point.
  3. If the widest point is at your cheekbones and your face narrows at the forehead and jawline, then you have an oval face shape.

Now that you know your face shape let's move on to the best hairstyles for oval faces over 60.

Best Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 60

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and chic hairstyle that is perfect for an oval face shape. This cut is both stylish and low maintenance, making it a great choice for women over 60. It highlights your face and showcases your features.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is another great hairstyle for an oval face shape. It's a versatile style that can be tailored to suit your individual needs. Whether you prefer a classic bob or a more modern take, this cut is perfect for women over 60.

3. Shoulder Length Cut

A shoulder-length cut is a great option if you prefer a longer hairstyle. It's a versatile style that can be worn straight or curly, depending on your mood. This cut frames your face and highlights your features, making it a great choice for women over 60.

4. Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great option if you want to add some volume and texture to your hair. This cut adds depth and dimension to your hair, giving it a more youthful and modern look. It's a great choice for women over 60 who want to look and feel their best.

5. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option if you want to change up your look without committing to a new hairstyle. This style is perfect for an oval face shape and can be worn with any length of hair. It's a great way to showcase your features and add some extra style to your look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you have an idea of the best hairstyles for oval faces over 60, let's talk about how to style your hair.

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair as desired.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you have a great hairstyle, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for an oval face shape?

The best haircut for an oval face shape is one that highlights your features and complements your bone structure. Pixie cuts, bob cuts, and shoulder-length cuts are all great options for women over 60 with oval face shapes.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

What styling products should I use?

You should use a heat protectant spray, a hairspray, and a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Hair Care Tips for Women Over 60

As you age, your hair changes, and it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips for women over 60:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for oval faces over 60, it's time to find the perfect cut for you. Remember to take care of your hair properly and maintain your hairstyle to keep it looking its best. With the right cut and styling, you can look and feel younger, more confident, and stylish.
