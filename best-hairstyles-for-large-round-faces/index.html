Best Hairstyles For Large Round Faces In 2023

Written by Eveline May 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect hairstyle for your large round face? Look no further because we have compiled a list of the best hairstyles for large round faces in 2023. Whether you have long or short hair, there is a hairstyle that will flatter your face shape and enhance your natural beauty.

Description

Large round faces can be tricky to style because you want to create the illusion of a more elongated face. The key is to find hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of your head while keeping the sides close to your face. This will help to balance out the proportions of your face and create a more flattering look.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's important to consider the texture and thickness of your hair. Some hairstyles work better for fine or thick hair, while others may require more maintenance. It's also important to consult with your hairstylist to see what works best for your hair type and face shape.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best hairstyles for large round faces in 2023:

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair for a large round face:

  • Try to avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear even rounder.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and texture.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to create a more elongated look.
  • Invest in good quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for large round faces:

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail with a large round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail as long as you keep it high on your head to create the illusion of a more elongated face.

Q: Are bangs a good option for a large round face?

A: Yes, bangs can be a great option for a large round face, especially if they are side-swept or layered.

Q: Can I wear my hair short with a large round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair short as long as you keep the sides close to your face and create volume at the crown of your head.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and shiny look. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and cause split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and maintain a healthy length.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can rock your large round face with confidence and style in 2023. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and hair type. Happy styling!
