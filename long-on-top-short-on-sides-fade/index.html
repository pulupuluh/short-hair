Long On Top Short On Sides Fade A Trendy Look For Men

Written by Petter Mar 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a man who is looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, the long on top short on sides fade is the perfect choice for you. This popular haircut combines the length on top with the short sides, creating a contrast that is both edgy and refined. In this blog post, we'll share with you everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, tips for styling, and hair care advice.

Description

The long on top short on sides fade is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your individual style. The long hair on top can be styled in many different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or with a side part. The sides are tapered and faded, creating a seamless blend between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides. This haircut is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance style that still looks great.

How to Get It

To get the long on top short on sides fade, it's best to visit a professional barber or hairstylist. They will be able to create the perfect fade for your hair type and face shape. When you go to the salon, ask for a fade that starts at a longer length on top and gradually tapers down to a shorter length on the sides. You can also bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to show your stylist for reference.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to get the long on top short on sides fade:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it
  2. Visit a professional barber or hairstylist
  3. Ask for a fade that starts at a longer length on top and gradually tapers down to a shorter length on the sides
  4. Choose the length of the fade
  5. Choose the length of the hair on top
  6. Style your hair using your preferred technique

Tips

Here are some tips for styling and maintaining your long on top short on sides fade:

  • Use a high-quality hair styling product to keep your hair in place
  • Regularly trim the sides and back to maintain the fade
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type
  • Visit your stylist every four to six weeks for touch-ups

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the long on top short on sides fade:

What hair types work best with this haircut?

The long on top short on sides fade works best for men with straight, wavy, or curly hair. It can be adapted for any hair type.

Does this haircut require a lot of maintenance?

No, this haircut is low-maintenance. You will need to visit your stylist every four to six weeks for touch-ups, and you should regularly trim the sides and back to maintain the fade, but other than that, it's easy to maintain.

Can this haircut be worn in a professional setting?

Yes, this haircut can be worn in a professional setting. It's a versatile style that can be adapted to suit any occasion.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair with the long on top short on sides fade:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as straighteners or curling irons
  • Regularly trim the sides and back to maintain the fade
  • Visit your stylist every four to six weeks for touch-ups
  • Use a high-quality hair styling product to keep your hair in place

The long on top short on sides fade is a trendy and stylish haircut that is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance style that still looks great. By following the tips and advice in this blog post, you can get the perfect long on top short on sides fade and keep it looking great for weeks to come.
