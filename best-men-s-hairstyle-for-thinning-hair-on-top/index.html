Best Men's Hairstyle for Thinning Hair on Top - Tips and Tricks

Are you experiencing thinning hair on top? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many men experience hair loss as they age, and it can be frustrating to find a hairstyle that works with your thinning hair. In this blog post, we'll be discussing the best men's hairstyle for thinning hair on top, as well as tips and tricks to make the most of your hair. Let's get started!

Best Hairstyles for Thinning Hair on Top

When it comes to hairstyles for thinning hair on top, the key is to avoid styles that highlight the thinning areas. Instead, opt for styles that add volume and texture to your hair. Here are some of the best hairstyles for thinning hair on top:

1. The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic hairstyle that works well for men with thinning hair on top. This style involves cutting your hair very short all over your head, which makes your hair look thicker and fuller. Plus, it's easy to maintain and can be done at home.

2. The Crew Cut

The crew cut is another short hairstyle that works well for thinning hair. This style is similar to the buzz cut, but it's slightly longer on top and tapered on the sides. This style adds texture and volume to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller.

3. The Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that works well for men with thinning hair. This style involves parting your hair to one side and keeping it short on the sides and back. The longer hair on top adds volume and texture to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller.

4. The Comb Over

The comb over is another classic hairstyle that works well for men with thinning hair. This style involves combing your hair to one side and using a styling product to hold it in place. The longer hair on top adds volume to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller.

How to Achieve These Hairstyles

Now that you know the best hairstyles for thinning hair on top, let's talk about how to achieve these styles. Here are some tips:

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make a big difference when it comes to styling your hair. Look for products that add volume and texture to your hair, such as volumizing shampoos, mousses, and texturizing sprays. Avoid heavy products, such as waxes and gels, which can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can also help add volume and texture. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots while blow drying. This will create volume and make your hair look thicker and fuller.

3. Use a Light Hold Styling Product

When styling your hair, use a light hold styling product that won't weigh down your hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for thinning hair, such as volumizing sprays and lightweight mousses.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to achieving the perfect hairstyle for thinning hair on top:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for thinning hair. This will help add volume and texture to your hair.

2. Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, towel dry it gently. Don't rub your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.

3. Apply a Volumizing Spray

Apply a volumizing spray to your hair to add volume and texture. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, focusing on the roots.

4. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots while blow drying. This will create volume and make your hair look thicker and fuller.

5. Style Your Hair

Use a light hold styling product to style your hair. Avoid heavy products, as these can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling thinning hair:

1. Avoid Over-washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it look dry and lifeless. Try to wash your hair every other day, or even less often if possible.

2. Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help keep your hair looking healthy and full. Ask your stylist to add some layers or texture to your hair to create the illusion of thickness.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, embrace it! These textures can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's hairstyles for thinning hair on top:

1. Will a hat make my hair fall out?

No, wearing a hat will not make your hair fall out. However, wearing a hat too often can cause damage to your hair and scalp, so it's important to give your hair a break from hats every once in a while.

2. Can I still dye my hair if it's thinning?

Yes, you can still dye your hair if it's thinning. However, be gentle when applying the dye, and avoid using harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

3. Will using hair products make my hair fall out?

No, using hair products will not make your hair fall out. However, using too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

Hair Care for Thinning Hair on Top

Here are some additional hair care tips for men with thinning hair on top:

1. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make it look even thinner.

2. Protect Your Scalp from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a sunscreen specifically designed for your scalp to protect it from the sun's harmful rays.

3. Consider Hair Loss Treatments

If you're experiencing significant hair loss, consider talking to your doctor or a hair loss specialist about hair loss treatments, such as Minoxidil or hair transplant surgery.

That's it! We hope this article has been helpful in finding the best men's hairstyle for thinning hair on top. Remember, there are plenty of options out there, so don't be afraid to experiment and find the style that works best for you.
