How To Wear Long Thick Hair Up

Written by Eveline Mar 31, 2023 · 7 min read

Long thick hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it’s beautiful and versatile, it can also be heavy and hot, especially during the summer months. Wearing your hair up is a great way to beat the heat and add some variety to your look. In this article, we’ll show you how to wear long thick hair up in a relaxed and effortless way.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Wear Long Thick Hair Up

When it comes to wearing your long thick hair up, there are countless options to choose from. Here are some of our favorite styles:

The Classic Bun

The classic bun is a simple and elegant style that can be dressed up or down. To achieve this look, start by pulling your hair back into a tight ponytail. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun and secure it with bobby pins. For a more relaxed look, pull out a few pieces of hair around your face and loosen the bun with your fingers.

The Messy Top Knot

The messy top knot is a fun and effortless style that’s perfect for casual occasions. To achieve this look, start by pulling your hair back into a high ponytail. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face and loosen the bun with your fingers to create a messy, undone look.

The Braided Crown

The braided crown is a bohemian-inspired style that’s perfect for festivals and outdoor events. To achieve this look, start by parting your hair down the middle and braiding each section into a tight braid. Bring each braid over the top of your head and secure them with bobby pins, tucking the ends under the opposite braid. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face and loosen the braids with your fingers for a more relaxed look.

Step by Step

Now that you know some of our favorite styles, here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving the classic bun:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head.
  3. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to frame it.
  6. Loosen the bun with your fingers to create a more relaxed look.
  7. Spray your hair with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for wearing your long thick hair up:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Don’t be afraid to use hair accessories like headbands, scarves, or clips to add some extra flair to your style.
  • Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you and your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing long thick hair up:

Can I wear my hair up if it’s too thick?

Absolutely! Just make sure to use strong bobby pins and hair ties to keep your style in place.

Will wearing my hair up damage it?

Wearing your hair up occasionally shouldn’t cause any damage, but it’s important to give your hair a break and let it down to prevent breakage and tangles.

Hair Care

Keeping your long thick hair healthy and strong is key to achieving beautiful updos. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can wear your long thick hair up in a variety of styles that are both effortless and chic. Whether you’re heading to the office or a night out on the town, these updos will have you looking and feeling your best.
