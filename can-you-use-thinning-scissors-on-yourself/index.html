Can You Use Thinning Scissors On Yourself A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Apr 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of having thick and bulky hair? Do you want to add some texture and volume to your locks? Thinning scissors might be the solution you are looking for. In this article, we will answer the question "Can you use thinning scissors on yourself?" and provide you with all the information you need to achieve the perfect haircut.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use Thinning Scissors on Yourself
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

Thinning scissors are a type of hair cutting tool that features two blades with teeth that are designed to remove bulk from the hair. They are commonly used by hairdressers and stylists to create texture and volume in the hair. However, you can also use thinning scissors on yourself at home.

How to Use Thinning Scissors on Yourself

Using thinning scissors on yourself requires a bit of skill and practice. However, with the right technique and tools, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. Here are the basic steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly.
  2. Section your hair into small sections using hair clips.
  3. Take one section at a time and comb it straight.
  4. Hold the thinning scissors horizontally and cut into the hair about an inch away from the scalp.
  5. Close the scissors and slide them down the hair shaft, removing some of the hair as you go.
  6. Repeat this process on each section until you achieve the desired look.
  7. Take a step back and look at your hair from different angles to make sure you have removed enough bulk.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Using Thinning Scissors on Yourself

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind when using thinning scissors on yourself:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger ones.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. You can always go back and remove more later.
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before using thinning scissors.
  • Practice on a small section of hair before moving on to the rest of your head.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you use regular scissors instead of thinning scissors?

Regular scissors can be used to cut hair, but they will not create the same texture and volume as thinning scissors. If you want to achieve a specific look, it's best to use the appropriate tool for the job.

How often should you use thinning scissors on your hair?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended to use thinning scissors every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut.

Can thinning scissors damage your hair?

Thinning scissors can damage your hair if not used properly. It's important to use sharp scissors and to not cut too much hair at once. If in doubt, it's best to consult a professional.

Hair Care Tips for Using Thinning Scissors

After using thinning scissors on your hair, it's important to take good care of your locks to keep them healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage.

Now that you know how to use thinning scissors on yourself, you can experiment with different haircuts and styles. With a bit of practice and patience, you can achieve the perfect look in no time.
