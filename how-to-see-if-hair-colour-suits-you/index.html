How To See If Hair Colour Suits You Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Mar 03, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of your old hair color and want to try something new? Or are you looking for a change that will suit you perfectly? Choosing a new hair color can be a daunting task, and you might be wondering how to see if hair color suits you. In this article, we will go over some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect hair color that complements your skin tone, eye color, and personality.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step 2: Choose the Right Hair Color for Your Skin Tone
  • Step 3: Consider Your Eye Color and Personality
  • Step 4: Try Temporary Hair Dye
  • Step 5: Consult a Professional Hairstylist
  • Tips for Maintaining Colored Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step to finding the perfect hair color is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell, you likely have a neutral skin tone.

Another way to determine your skin tone is to look at the undertones of your skin. If your skin has pink, red, or blue undertones, you have a cool skin tone. If your skin has yellow, peach, or golden undertones, you have a warm skin tone. If you have a mix of both, you have a neutral skin tone.

Step 2: Choose the Right Hair Color for Your Skin Tone

Once you have determined your skin tone, you can choose the right hair color that complements it. For cool skin tones, go for hair colors with cool undertones such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, and cool brown. For warm skin tones, go for hair colors with warm undertones such as golden blonde, honey blonde, and warm brown. For neutral skin tones, you can go for any hair color as long as it complements your eye color and personality.

Step 3: Consider Your Eye Color and Personality

Your eye color and personality also play a role in choosing the right hair color. If you have blue or green eyes, cool hair colors such as platinum blonde and ash brown will complement your eye color. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warm hair colors such as golden blonde and warm brown will complement your eye color.

Your personality also plays a role in choosing the right hair color. If you have a bold and adventurous personality, you can go for bright and bold hair colors such as pink, purple, and blue. If you have a more reserved and classic personality, you can go for natural hair colors such as blonde, brown, and black.

Step 4: Try Temporary Hair Dye

If you are not sure if a hair color will suit you, you can try temporary hair dye before committing to a permanent one. Temporary hair dye lasts for a few washes and allows you to see how the color looks on you. You can also experiment with different shades and see which one suits you best.

Step 5: Consult a Professional Hairstylist

If you are still unsure about which hair color suits you, you can consult a professional hairstylist. A hairstylist can help you determine your skin tone, eye color, and personality and recommend the right hair color for you. They can also give you tips on how to maintain your colored hair and recommend hair care products that are suitable for your hair type.

Tips for Maintaining Colored Hair

Once you have found the perfect hair color, it is important to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining colored hair:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip the color.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners as they can damage the hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine and saltwater as they can also cause the color to fade.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if you have sensitive skin, but you should be careful when choosing a hair dye. Look for hair dyes that are free of harsh chemicals such as ammonia and PPD. You can also do a patch test before applying the hair dye to make sure you are not allergic to it.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. On average, you should touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to maintain the color.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but you should follow the instructions carefully and use the right products. It is also recommended to do a patch test before applying the hair dye to make sure you are not allergic to it.

Finding the perfect hair color can be a fun and exciting journey. By following these tips and tricks, you can find a hair color that suits you and enhances your natural beauty. Remember to take care of your colored hair to keep it looking fresh and vibrant.
