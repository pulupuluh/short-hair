How To Make Curly Hair Look Good In Pictures

Written by Frank May 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to make it look good in pictures. Whether you're taking a selfie or posing for a professional photographer, curly hair can sometimes look frizzy, messy, or just plain unruly. But don't worry, with the right techniques and products, you can make your curls look fabulous in any picture.

How to

Follow these steps to make your curly hair look good in pictures:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Moisturized Hair

Before you take any pictures, make sure your hair is clean and well-moisturized. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Then, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and keep them hydrated.

Step 2: Style Your Curls

After your hair is clean and moisturized, it's time to style your curls. You can either air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disturbing your curls. If you want to add more definition to your curls, you can use a curling wand or flexi rods to create more uniform curls.

Step 3: Use the Right Lighting

Lighting is key when it comes to taking good pictures of curly hair. Natural light is always the best option, so try to take your pictures outside or near a window. Avoid harsh, artificial lighting that can wash out your curls or create unflattering shadows.

Step 4: Strike a Pose

Now that your hair is styled and the lighting is right, it's time to strike a pose. Play around with different poses and angles to find what works best for you and your curls. You can also use props like hats or sunglasses to add some extra flair to your pictures.

Step 5: Edit Your Pictures

Once you've taken your pictures, it's time to edit them. You can use editing apps like VSCO, Lightroom, or Snapseed to adjust the brightness, contrast, and saturation of your pictures. You can also use these apps to add filters or special effects to make your curls pop.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to make your curly hair look good in pictures:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls
  • Avoid touching your hair too much to prevent frizz
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent tangles and breakage
  • Experiment with different hair accessories like headbands, clips, or scarves
  • Don't be afraid to show off your natural curls!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making curly hair look good in pictures:

How can I prevent my curls from looking frizzy in pictures?

To prevent frizz, make sure your hair is well-moisturized and avoid touching it too much. You can also use a frizz-fighting product like a serum or oil to tame flyaways.

What's the best way to take pictures of curly hair?

The best way to take pictures of curly hair is to use natural light and experiment with different poses and angles. Don't be afraid to play around with props or accessories to add some extra flair to your pictures.

Can I wear my hair in a protective style for pictures?

Yes, you can definitely wear your hair in a protective style like braids or twists for pictures. Just make sure your hair is well-moisturized and the style is neat and tidy.

Hair Care

Taking care of your curly hair is essential for making it look good in pictures. Here are some tips for taking care of your curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair
  • Sleep with a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent tangles and breakage

With these tips and techniques, you can make your curly hair look fabulous in any picture. So go ahead and snap away, and don't forget to show off those gorgeous curls!
