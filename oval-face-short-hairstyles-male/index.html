Oval Face Short Hairstyles Male The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a man with an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. In this article, we'll be discussing the best short hairstyles for oval face males. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a slightly wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The length of the face is longer than the width. This face shape is considered to be the most symmetrical and balanced, which makes it easy to style. Short hairstyles work well with oval face shapes as they highlight the facial features and create a sharp, clean look.

How to

Before deciding on a hairstyle, it's important to consider your hair type and texture. Short hairstyles work best with straight or wavy hair. If you have curly hair, you may need to keep it slightly longer to avoid a frizzy look.

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your personal style and the image you want to portray. A short, clean-cut hairstyle gives off a professional and polished look, while a slightly messy hairstyle can give off a more casual and laid-back vibe.

Step by step

Here are some of the best short hairstyles for oval face males:

1. Crew Cut

The crew cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well with oval face shapes. This hairstyle is characterized by short hair on the sides and back and slightly longer hair on top. It's a low-maintenance hairstyle that's easy to style.

2. Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a super short hairstyle that's perfect for men who want a low-maintenance look. This hairstyle is characterized by hair that's cut to the same length all over the head. It's a great option for men who don't want to spend a lot of time styling their hair.

3. Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that works well with oval face shapes. This style is characterized by hair that's parted on the side and swept to one side. It's a polished and professional look that's perfect for the workplace.

4. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a modern and trendy hairstyle that works well with oval face shapes. This style is characterized by short hair on the sides and back and slightly longer hair on top. The hair on top is styled to create a messy, textured look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short hairstyles:

  • Use a good quality styling product to keep your hair in place.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find the one that works best for you.
  • Regular trims are important to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

What face shapes work best with short hairstyles?

Short hairstyles work well with oval, square, and heart-shaped faces.

What hair types work best with short hairstyles?

Straight and wavy hair work best with short hairstyles. If you have curly hair, you may need to keep it slightly longer to avoid a frizzy look.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important to keep your short hairstyle looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Regular trims are important to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and hairstyle options, you're sure to find the perfect short hairstyle for your oval face shape. Remember to experiment with different styles and products until you find the one that works best for you. Happy styling!
