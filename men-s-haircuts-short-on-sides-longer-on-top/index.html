Men s Haircuts Short On Sides Longer On Top A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short on sides and longer on top haircuts are one of the most popular hairstyles for men. This style is versatile and suitable for men of all ages and hair types. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about men's haircuts short on sides longer on top.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short on sides and longer on top haircuts are characterized by a shorter length on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on the top. This style can be customized to suit personal preferences, with varying lengths and textures on the top. This haircut can be styled in several ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side parting.

The short on sides and longer on top haircut is perfect for men who want a stylish, low-maintenance look. This haircut is easy to maintain and can be adapted to different occasions, from casual to formal events.

How to

To achieve a short on sides and longer on top haircut, you will need to visit a professional barber or stylist. They will use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair to the desired length, leaving the hair on the top longer. The length of the hair on the top can be adjusted to suit your preferences.

If you want to style your hair at home, you will need a few tools, including a comb, a hairbrush, and some styling products. Apply a small amount of product to your hair, and use your fingers to style your hair in your desired way. You can experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

Step by step

Here are the steps to achieve a short on sides and longer on top haircut:

  1. Visit a professional barber or stylist.
  2. Discuss your preferences and desired style with them.
  3. They will use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair.
  4. They will trim the hair on the top to the desired length.
  5. You can then style your hair at home using a comb, hairbrush, and styling products.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short on sides and longer on top haircut:

  • Bring a picture of your desired style to your barber or stylist.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.
  • Try different styling techniques to find the one that suits your face shape and personal style.

FAQ

1. Is a short on sides and longer on top haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this haircut is versatile and can be adapted to suit different face shapes. Your barber or stylist can help you choose the best style for your face shape.

2. How often should I trim my hair to maintain the length and shape of my haircut?

You should visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

3. Can I style my short on sides and longer on top haircut in different ways?

Yes, this haircut is versatile and can be styled in several ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side parting.

Hair Care

To keep your short on sides and longer on top haircut looking its best, you should follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair with a gentle shampoo, using a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth, and applying a styling product to help hold your hairstyle in place. You should also avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Short on sides and longer on top haircuts are a timeless and stylish choice for men. With a little maintenance and some styling products, you can achieve a versatile and low-maintenance look that suits your personal style.
