How To Gel Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Gelling short hair can give you a polished and put-together look, perfect for any occasion. But if you're new to the world of hair gels, it can be overwhelming to choose the right product and know how to use it properly. Fear not, we've got you covered with this step-by-step guide on how to gel short hair.

Description

Gel is a hair styling product that can help you achieve different hairstyles. It's perfect for short hair because it provides hold and control that you need to style your hair. Gel also adds shine and texture to your hair, making it look polished and put-together.

How to Gel Short Hair

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any product buildup or dirt. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp.

Step 2: Choose the Right Gel

There are different types of hair gels available in the market. Choose the one that works best for your hair type and the style you're going for. If you have thick hair, go for a strong-hold gel. If you have fine hair, use a lighter gel.

Step 3: Apply the Gel

Squeeze a small amount of gel onto your palm, and rub your hands together to distribute the product evenly. Start by applying the gel to the back of your head, and work your way forward. Make sure to distribute the gel evenly through your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair the way you want it. You can create spikes, slicked-back hair, or a messy look, depending on your preference. Make sure to work the gel into your hair, so it holds your style in place.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

If you want your style to last all day, finish with a light hairspray. This will add extra hold and keep your hair in place.

Tips

  • Use a pea-sized amount of gel for fine hair, and a dime-sized amount for thick hair.
  • Don't apply too much gel, or your hair will look greasy and weighed down.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to distribute the gel evenly through your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I use gel on wet hair?

A: Yes, you can use gel on wet hair. In fact, it's recommended to apply gel on damp hair, as it helps distribute the product evenly.

Q: Can I use gel every day?

A: Yes, you can use gel every day. However, make sure to wash your hair thoroughly at the end of the day to remove any product buildup.

Q: How do I remove gel from my hair?

A: Wash your hair with shampoo and warm water to remove gel from your hair. You may need to shampoo your hair twice to completely remove the product.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy, make sure to use a good shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage your hair. Also, make sure to trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
