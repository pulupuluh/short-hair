How To Do A Chignon With Medium Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a chic and sophisticated hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion, a chignon is a great option. This classic updo is versatile and can be customized to suit your hair length and texture. In this article, we'll show you how to do a chignon with medium hair in just a few easy steps.

Description

A chignon is a French term that refers to a low bun or knot that's usually positioned at the nape of the neck. It's a timeless hairstyle that's perfect for formal events or even for everyday wear. With medium hair, you can create a sleek and polished chignon that's elegant and effortless.

How to Do a Chignon with Medium Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do a chignon with medium hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

The first step is to prepare your hair by washing and conditioning it. Apply a volumizing mousse to your damp hair and blow-dry it with a round brush to create volume and texture.

Step 2: Create a Low Ponytail

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and create a low ponytail. Use a hair tie to secure it in place.

Step 3: Twist and Pin

Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off the look by applying a light-hold hairspray to keep the chignon in place.

Tips for Creating the Perfect Chignon

Here are a few tips to help you create the perfect chignon:

  • Use a smoothing serum or cream to tame any flyaways and create a sleek finish.
  • Add a hair accessory, such as a jeweled clip or headband, to dress up your chignon for a formal occasion.
  • If you have fine hair, use a teasing comb or volumizing powder to add texture and fullness to your chignon.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about chignons:

Can I do a chignon with short hair?

Yes, you can still create a chignon with short hair. Use bobby pins and hairspray to secure the hair in place and create a low bun or knot.

How do I make my chignon last all day?

Apply a strong-hold hairspray to keep your chignon in place. You can also try using hair pins or U-pins for added security.

Is a chignon suitable for a casual occasion?

Yes, a chignon can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. For a casual look, try a messy or tousled chignon with loose strands framing your face.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, make sure to use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot tools too frequently and use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair. Regular trims can also help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking its best.

With these simple steps and tips, you can create a beautiful chignon with medium hair that's perfect for any occasion. Give it a try and see how this classic hairstyle can elevate your look!
