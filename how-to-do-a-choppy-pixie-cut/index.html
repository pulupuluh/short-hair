How To Do A Choppy Pixie Cut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 02, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for a daring and edgy haircut that will accentuate your features and make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the choppy pixie cut! This popular hairstyle has been embraced by many celebrities, including Emma Watson, Katy Perry, and Miley Cyrus. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps of achieving the perfect choppy pixie cut.

How to

To achieve a choppy pixie cut, you’ll need the right tools, including a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a clip. You should also have a clear idea of what you want your final look to be, so bring a picture or two of your desired style to your hairstylist.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it is completely dry before you begin the cut.

2. Separate the top section of your hair from the sides and back, and clip it out of the way.

3. Using your comb, create a part on one side of your head.

4. Starting at the nape of your neck, use your scissors to cut the hair in small sections. Cut at an angle, so the hair is longer in the front and shorter in the back.

5. Move to the sides of your head and repeat the same process, cutting at an angle and creating a choppy, textured look.

6. Repeat on the other side of your head.

7. Unclip the top section of your hair and comb it forward.

8. Cut the top section of your hair at an angle, creating a long, choppy fringe.

9. Use your scissors to texturize the ends of your hair, creating a choppy, edgy look.

10. Style your new haircut with your favorite products.

Tips

- If you’re nervous about cutting your hair, visit a professional stylist.

- Keep in mind that a choppy pixie cut requires regular maintenance to keep it looking fresh and edgy.

- Experiment with different styling products to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

Q: Will a choppy pixie cut work for my face shape?

A: Choppy pixie cuts can work for a variety of face shapes, but it’s best to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best cut for your individual features.

Q: Can I style a choppy pixie cut in different ways?

A: Absolutely! There are many ways to style a choppy pixie cut, including slicked back, tousled, and accessorized with headbands or hair clips.

Hair Care

After getting a choppy pixie cut, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools. Regular trims will also help maintain your choppy pixie cut and keep it looking fresh.

By following these simple steps, you can achieve a daring and edgy choppy pixie cut that will turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish. Happy styling!
