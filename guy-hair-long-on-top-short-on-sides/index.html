Guys Hair Long On Top Short On Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby May 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to change up your hairstyle and try something new? Look no further than the classic "guy hair long on top short on sides" look. This style has been popular for years and continues to be a go-to for men of all ages. In this ultimate guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about pulling off this timeless look.

Description

The "guy hair long on top short on sides" look is exactly what it sounds like. The hair on top is left longer, while the sides and back are cut short. This creates a contrast between the two lengths and adds dimension to the style. The hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, from a slick back to messy waves. This style is popular among men of all ages and is versatile enough to be worn in a professional or casual setting.

How to

If you're interested in trying out the "guy hair long on top short on sides" look, here's how to get it:

Step 1: Choose your length

The first step is to decide how long you want your hair on top to be. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. If you have a round face, you may want to go shorter on top to avoid adding too much volume. If you have a longer face, you can go longer on top to balance out your features.

Step 2: Find a good barber

Once you've decided on your length, it's time to find a good barber. Look for someone who has experience with this style and can give you the cut you want. It's also important to communicate clearly with your barber and show them pictures of the look you're going for.

Step 3: Style your hair

Once you have your haircut, it's time to style your hair. The key to this look is to keep the hair on top longer and the sides shorter. You can use a pomade or wax to add texture and hold to your hair. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when going for the "guy hair long on top short on sides" look:

  • Regular trims are important to maintain the shape of the style.
  • Use a quality hair product to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Consider your face shape when choosing the length of your hair on top.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "guy hair long on top short on sides" look:

Is this style suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this style can work for most hair types. However, if you have very curly or thick hair, it may be more difficult to achieve the desired look.

How often should I get a trim?

You should aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the style.

What products should I use to style my hair?

You can use a pomade or wax to add texture and hold to your hair. Look for a product that works well with your hair type.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the "guy hair long on top short on sides" look, it's time to give it a try. With the right cut and styling, this classic look is sure to turn heads.
