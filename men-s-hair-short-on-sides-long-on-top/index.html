Description

The men's hair short on sides long on top trend has been around for quite some time now and remains popular in 2023. This haircut style features short hair on the sides and longer hair on top that can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back or messy. It's a versatile look that's perfect for any occasion, whether you're going to work or out for a night on the town.

How To Achieve the Look

If you're interested in getting the men's hair short on sides long on top look, the first step is to find a good barber or stylist who can execute the cut. It's important to communicate your desired length and style to ensure you get the look you want.

Once you have the cut, styling the hair is fairly easy. You can use a variety of products, such as pomade or wax, to achieve different looks. For a sleek, polished look, use a comb to slick the hair back. For a more relaxed, messy look, use your fingers to tousle the hair and apply product as needed.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to achieving the men's hair short on sides long on top look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using clippers, cut the sides of your hair to the desired length.
  3. Blend the sides and top using scissors or clippers.
  4. Leave the hair on top longer and style as desired.
  5. Apply product to achieve the desired look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for maintaining the men's hair short on sides long on top look:

  • Invest in quality hair products to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep the style looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques and products to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting a fade on the sides for a more dramatic look.

FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions about the men's hair short on sides long on top look:

  • Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?
  • How often do I need to get my hair trimmed to maintain the look?
  • Can I wear this style to work?
  • What products work best for this style?

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of protein and vitamins.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve and maintain the men's hair short on sides long on top look with ease. Whether you're going for a classic, polished look or something more relaxed and messy, this haircut style is sure to turn heads wherever you go.
