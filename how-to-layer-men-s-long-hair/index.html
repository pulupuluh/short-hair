How To Layer Men s Long Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long hair for men has become increasingly popular in recent years, and with good reason. It's versatile, stylish, and can be worn in a variety of ways. However, if you want your long hair to look its best, it's important to know how to layer it properly. In this article, we'll take a look at how to layer men's long hair, step-by-step.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Layer Men's Long Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Layering Men's Long Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Layered Men's Long Hair

How to Layer Men's Long Hair

Layering men's long hair involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create volume, texture, and movement. This can be done using various cutting techniques, such as point cutting or slicing.

Layering is ideal if you have thick, heavy hair, as it can help to reduce the weight and make it easier to manage. It's also a great option if you have curly or wavy hair, as it can enhance the natural texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to layer men's long hair:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that it's clean and free from any product buildup.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  3. Begin with the back section, and take a small section of hair (around 1 inch thick) and hold it between your fingers.
  4. Using scissors, cut the hair at a slight angle, starting from the mid-lengths and working your way down to the ends. This will create a subtle graduation.
  5. Repeat this process with the rest of the back section, working from the bottom up.
  6. Move on to the side sections, and repeat the same process as the back section, working from the bottom up.
  7. Finally, move on to the top section, and use the same technique, but this time cut the hair at a slightly steeper angle to create more volume.
  8. Once you've finished layering your hair, you can then use thinning shears to remove any excess weight and create more texture.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Layering Men's Long Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when layering men's long hair:

  • Start with small sections of hair, as it's easier to control the cutting and ensure that the layers are even.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure clean, precise cuts.
  • Take your time and work methodically, section by section.
  • Use thinning shears sparingly, as they can easily remove too much hair and create an uneven finish.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about layering men's long hair:

Q: How often should I get my long hair layered?

A: It's recommended that you get your long hair layered every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I layer my own long hair?

A: It's not recommended to layer your own long hair, as it can be difficult to achieve an even finish and you may end up cutting too much off. It's best to visit a professional stylist.

Hair Care for Layered Men's Long Hair

Once you've layered your men's long hair, it's important to take care of it properly to ensure that it stays healthy and looks its best.

Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for long hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a weekly hair mask to nourish and hydrate the hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Visit a professional stylist regularly to maintain the shape and condition of your layered long hair.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your layered men's long hair looks its best and stays healthy.
