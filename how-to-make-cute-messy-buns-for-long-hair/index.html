How To Make Cute Messy Buns For Long Hair

Written by William Aug 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Messy buns are a great hairstyle for long hair that can be worn in a variety of settings. Whether you're going to work, school, or out with friends, a cute messy bun can be a quick and easy hairstyle to create. In this post, we'll show you how to make cute messy buns for long hair step by step, along with tips for achieving the perfect look and answering some of the most frequently asked questions about this hairstyle. So, let's get started!

Description

A messy bun is a hairstyle where the hair is gathered and twisted into a bun shape, with some strands of hair left loose for a relaxed, tousled look. This style is great for long hair because it keeps the hair out of your face while still looking cute and stylish. With a few simple steps, you can create a cute messy bun in just a few minutes.

How To

To make a cute messy bun for long hair, follow these steps:

Step 1: Gather Your Hair

Start by gathering all of your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head. Use a brush or comb to smooth out any bumps or tangles.

Step 2: Twist and Wrap

Take the ponytail and twist it around itself to create a bun shape. Keep twisting until the entire length of the ponytail is wrapped around the base of the ponytail. Secure the bun with a hair tie or bobby pins.

Step 3: Mess It Up

Once the bun is secured, use your fingers to gently pull on some of the strands of hair to create a tousled, messy look. You can also use a comb or brush to tease the hair at the crown of your head for added volume.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your cute messy bun in place, finish with a light spritz of hairspray. This will help to hold the style without making it look stiff or crunchy.

Tips

Here are some tips for achieving the perfect cute messy bun:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • Don't worry if your bun isn't perfectly symmetrical - a messy bun is supposed to look, well, messy!
  • For added texture, use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo before creating the bun.
  • If you have layers in your hair, pull some of the shorter pieces out of the bun for a more relaxed look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cute messy buns:

How do I keep my bun from falling out?

Make sure to secure your bun with enough bobby pins or a hair tie that is tight enough to hold the hair in place. You can also use a bit of hairspray to help keep the style in place.

Can I wear a cute messy bun to a formal event?

While a messy bun is generally a more casual hairstyle, you can dress it up with some accessories like a sparkly hair clip or headband. Just make sure the overall look is still polished and put-together.

Will a cute messy bun work for all hair types?

A messy bun can work for most hair types, but it may be more difficult to create for very short or fine hair. If you have short hair, you can try a half-up, half-down style instead. For fine hair, use texturizing products to add volume and grip to the hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and moisturize the hair. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. When styling your hair, use products that are designed for your hair type and avoid pulling or tugging on the hair too much.
