How To Fix Shoulder Length Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to style your shoulder length curly hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it adds volume and texture to your hair, it can also be difficult to manage and style.

In this comprehensive guide, we'll take you through step-by-step instructions on how to fix shoulder length curly hair. From how to choose the right hair products to how to style your curls, we've got you covered.

How to Fix Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, there are a few things you need to know about styling curly hair.

Firstly, curly hair tends to be dry and frizzy, so it's essential to use hair products that are moisturizing and nourishing. Secondly, curly hair needs to be styled differently than straight hair. Instead of brushing it, you need to use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to detangle it.

Now that you know the basics, let's get started with the step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with Clean, Conditioned Hair

The first step in styling curly hair is to start with clean, conditioned hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair. This will help to hydrate your curls and prevent them from frizzing up.

3. Use a Curl Defining Cream

To enhance your curls, use a curl defining cream. Apply it to your hair, scrunching it up as you go. This will help to define your curls and give them a bouncy, voluminous look.

4. Diffuse Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to prevent your curls from getting too frizzy and will give them a more defined look.

5. Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, scrunch it up with your hands to enhance the curls. You can also use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need a little extra definition.

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Now that you know how to fix shoulder length curly hair, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent your curls from getting tangled and frizzy while you sleep.
  • Avoid brushing your hair, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Don't overdo it with the hair products. Too much product can weigh your curls down and make them look greasy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling shoulder length curly hair:

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you can get away with washing it once a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair, but it's essential to use a heat protectant and to not straighten it too often, as this can cause heat damage.

Q: How can I refresh my curls on days when I don't wash my hair?

A: To refresh your curls, you can spritz them with a water and leave-in conditioner mixture and scrunch them up with your hands.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Finally, here are some general hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause heat damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain sulfates, as they can dry out your curls.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the step-by-step guide we've outlined, you can easily fix your shoulder length curly hair and enjoy bouncy, defined curls every day.
