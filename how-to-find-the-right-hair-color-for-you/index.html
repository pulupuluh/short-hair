How To Find The Right Hair Color For You A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Feb 20, 2023 · 9 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's easy to feel overwhelmed. However, with a little bit of research and a few tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hair color that complements your skin tone, eye color, and personality. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of finding the right hair color for you.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color
  • Step 3: Assess Your Natural Hair Color
  • Step 4: Choose the Right Shade
  • Step 5: Try Temporary Hair Color
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in finding the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If they appear blue-green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Once you have determined your skin tone, you can choose a hair color that complements it. For warm skin tones, choose hair colors with warm undertones such as golden blonde, auburn, or chestnut. For cool skin tones, choose hair colors with cool undertones such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, or black. For neutral skin tones, you can choose either warm or cool hair colors.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining the right hair color for you. Certain hair colors can make your eyes pop and enhance their natural color. For example, if you have blue eyes, a warm hair color such as honey blonde or copper can make them stand out. If you have brown eyes, a cool hair color such as ash brown or platinum blonde can make them pop.

Step 3: Assess Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also give you clues about which hair colors will look best on you. If you have warm undertones in your natural hair color, such as red or golden highlights, warm hair colors will suit you best. If you have cool undertones in your natural hair color, such as ash or cool brown tones, cool hair colors will suit you best.

Step 4: Choose the Right Shade

Once you have determined your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color, you can start choosing the right shade of hair color. It's important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural features. If you're unsure about which shade to choose, consult a hairstylist who can help you find the perfect match.

Step 5: Try Temporary Hair Color

If you're still unsure about which hair color to choose, try a temporary hair color first. Temporary hair color can give you a preview of what a permanent hair color will look like without the commitment. This way, you can experiment with different shades and see which one looks best on you.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle and maintenance routine. Choose a hair color that fits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain.
  • Take inspiration from celebrities or models with similar skin tone and features.
  • Consider the season. Warmer hair colors such as reds and coppers are great for fall and winter, while cooler hair colors such as platinum blonde and ash brown are great for spring and summer.
  • Don't be afraid to go bold. If you're feeling adventurous, try a bold hair color such as purple or blue.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair color suits cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones look best with cool hair colors such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, or black.

What hair color suits warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones look best with warm hair colors such as golden blonde, auburn, or chestnut.

What hair color suits neutral skin tones?

Neutral skin tones can choose either warm or cool hair colors.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you've found the perfect hair color, it's important to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy and shine. Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your hair color:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip the color. Aim for 2-3 times a week.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons as they can damage the hair and fade the color.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid exposure to direct sunlight as it can fade the color. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray.

Choosing the right hair color can take some time and effort, but it's worth it to achieve a look that enhances your natural features and complements your personality. Follow these tips and tricks, and you'll be on your way to finding the perfect hair color for you.
