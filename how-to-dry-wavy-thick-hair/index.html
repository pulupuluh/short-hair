How To Dry Wavy Thick Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Wavy thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks amazing when styled, drying it can be a tedious and time-consuming task. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps on how to dry wavy thick hair, as well as provide some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

How to Dry Wavy Thick Hair

Drying wavy thick hair requires a slightly different approach than other hair types. Here's what you need to do:

1. Pre-Dry Your Hair

Before you start drying your hair, use a microfiber towel to gently blot your hair and remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel as this can cause frizz and damage.

2. Apply a Heat Protectant

Next, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to minimize damage from the heat of the hair dryer.

3. Use a Diffuser

A diffuser is an attachment that fits onto your hair dryer and helps to distribute the heat evenly. This is especially important for wavy thick hair as it can be prone to frizz.

4. Dry Your Hair in Sections

Divide your hair into sections and dry each section individually. Start with the bottom sections and work your way up. Use a low heat setting and a medium speed setting on your hair dryer.

5. Finish with a Cool Shot

Once you have dried your hair, use the cool shot button on your hair dryer to set the style and reduce frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to dry wavy thick hair:

Step 1: Pre-Dry Your Hair

Use a microfiber towel to gently blot your hair and remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant to your hair.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

Fit a diffuser attachment onto your hair dryer and use a low heat setting and a medium speed setting to dry your hair.

Step 4: Dry Your Hair in Sections

Divide your hair into sections and dry each section individually, starting with the bottom sections and working your way up.

Step 5: Finish with a Cool Shot

Use the cool shot button on your hair dryer to set the style and reduce frizz.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make drying wavy thick hair easier:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before drying. This will help to minimize breakage and damage.

2. Avoid Touching Your Hair

Try to avoid touching your hair as much as possible while drying. This will help to reduce frizz and damage.

3. Use a Hair Oil

Apply a hair oil to the ends of your hair before drying. This will help to prevent split ends and add shine.

FAQs

Is it better to air dry wavy thick hair?

Air drying can be a good option for wavy thick hair, but it can take longer and may not result in the same level of smoothness and definition as using a hair dryer.

What is the best type of hair dryer for wavy thick hair?

A hair dryer with a diffuser attachment is the best type of hair dryer for wavy thick hair.

How often should I wash my wavy thick hair?

Wavy thick hair can be prone to dryness, so it's best to wash it no more than twice a week.

Hair Care for Wavy Thick Hair

In addition to drying your hair properly, it's important to take care of your wavy thick hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Deep Condition Once a Week

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it nourished and prevent breakage.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Whenever Possible

Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to minimize damage to your hair.

4. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the right techniques, you can keep your wavy thick hair looking its best.
