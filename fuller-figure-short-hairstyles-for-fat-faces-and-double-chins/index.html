Fuller Figure Short Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins

Written by Aprilia Sep 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a fuller figure, fat face, and double chin, you might think that short hairstyles are not for you. However, this is not true! There are various short hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and accentuate your best features. In this article, we will guide you through some of the best fuller figure short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins.

Description

A fuller figure, fat face, and double chin are common features for many women. However, these features should not limit your hairstyle options. There are many short hairstyles that can enhance your facial features and make you look beautiful and confident.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle, you should consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. A good hairstylist can help you choose a hairstyle that suits your facial features and complements your overall appearance.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a short hairstyle for a fuller figure, fat face, and double chin:

  1. Determine your face shape: Oval, round, heart-shaped, or square-shaped
  2. Consider your hair texture: Straight, wavy, or curly
  3. Look for inspiration: Browse through magazines or online for short hairstyles that you like
  4. Consult a hairstylist: Ask for their opinion and recommendations based on your facial features and hair texture
  5. Choose a hairstyle: Select a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for a fuller figure, fat face, and double chin:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face
  • Opt for hairstyles with layers and texture to add volume to the top of your head
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long
  • Consider adding side-swept bangs to soften your facial features
  • Experiment with different hair colors to enhance your hairstyle

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fuller figure short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins:

What is the best short hairstyle for a round face with a double chin?

A layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs can flatter a round face with a double chin.

What is the best hair color for a short hairstyle for a fuller figure and fat face?

Warm and natural hair colors such as caramel, honey blonde, or chestnut brown can enhance a short hairstyle for a fuller figure and fat face.

Can I wear a short hairstyle if I have a square-shaped face and double chin?

Yes, you can wear a short hairstyle if you have a square-shaped face and double chin. A layered bob or a short shag can balance your facial features and soften your jawline.

Hair Care

To maintain your short hairstyle and keep it looking fresh, you should follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape

In conclusion, there are plenty of fuller figure short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins that can enhance your facial features and make you feel confident. By following our tips and consulting with a hairstylist, you can find a short hairstyle that flatters your face shape and complements your personal style.
