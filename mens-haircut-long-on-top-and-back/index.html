Mens Haircut Long On Top And Back A Guide To Stylish And Low Maintenance Hair

Description

The mens haircut long on top and back is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. It's a versatile and low-maintenance look that can be easily customized to suit your personal style. The key to this hairstyle is to keep the hair long on the top and back while keeping the sides shorter. This creates a sharp contrast and adds dimension to the look.

The mens haircut long on top and back is perfect for those who want a stylish and trendy look without having to spend too much time styling their hair. It's also a great option for those who have thick or curly hair, as it allows the hair to fall naturally and create a relaxed, effortless look.

How To

To achieve the mens haircut long on top and back, you'll need to visit a professional hair stylist. They will be able to determine the best length and style for your hair type and face shape. It's important to bring inspiration photos with you to the appointment, so the stylist can understand exactly what you're looking for.

Once you have the haircut, you can style it in different ways depending on the occasion. For a casual look, you can simply let your hair fall naturally and tousle it with your fingers. For a more polished look, you can use a matte hair product to add texture and hold to the hair.

Step by Step

  1. Visit a professional hair stylist and show them inspiration photos of the mens haircut long on top and back.
  2. Sit back and relax as the stylist cuts your hair, leaving the top and back long and the sides shorter.
  3. Once the haircut is complete, ask the stylist for tips on how to style the hair at home.
  4. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle the hair before styling.
  • Apply hair product to damp hair for better hold and texture.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Visit a professional hair stylist for regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut.

FAQ

Is the mens haircut long on top and back suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the mens haircut long on top and back can be customized to suit any face shape. Your hair stylist will be able to determine the best length and style for your individual features.

What hair products should I use to style the mens haircut long on top and back?

Matte hair products, such as clay or wax, work well for the mens haircut long on top and back. These products add texture and hold to the hair without making it look greasy or shiny.

How often should I visit a professional hair stylist to maintain the mens haircut long on top and back?

You should visit a professional hair stylist every 4-6 weeks for regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut long on top and back looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers and straighteners too often. To keep the hair healthy and shiny, incorporate a hair mask into your routine once a week.

The mens haircut long on top and back is a stylish and low-maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for those who want a trendy look without having to spend too much time styling their hair. With the right cut and styling products, this hairstyle can be easily customized to suit your personal style and face shape.
