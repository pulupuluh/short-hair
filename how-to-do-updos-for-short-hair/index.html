How To Do Updos For Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Apr 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have short hair, you might think that you're limited when it comes to updos. But the truth is, there are plenty of styles that you can try! In this guide, we'll go over some of the best ways to create updos for short hair. Whether you're heading to a formal event or just want to switch up your look, these styles are sure to impress.

Table of Contents

  • How to create updos for short hair
  • Step-by-step guide
  • Tips for creating updos for short hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair care tips for short hair

How to Create Updos for Short Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some of the best ways to create updos for short hair. Here are some styles to consider:

  • Messy bun
  • Braided updo
  • Top knot
  • Half-up, half-down
  • Pinned back

Each of these styles can be customized to fit your personal style and the occasion you're attending. Now, let's move on to the step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to create an updo for short hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is too slippery, try using a texturizing spray.
  2. Create a deep side part.
  3. Take a small section of hair at the front of your head and twist it away from your face. Pin it in place with a bobby pin.
  4. Take another small section of hair on the other side of your part and twist it away from your face. Pin it in place with a bobby pin.
  5. Continue taking small sections of hair and twisting them away from your face, pinning them in place as you go. You can create as many twists as you'd like!
  6. Once you've twisted and pinned all of the hair on one side of your head, repeat the process on the other side.
  7. Gather all of your hair at the back of your head and twist it into a messy bun. Pin it in place with bobby pins.
  8. Use your fingers to gently pull out some pieces of hair around your face for a more relaxed look.
  9. Spray your updo with a light hold hairspray to keep it in place.
  10. Admire your new updo!

Of course, you can always modify this style to fit your personal preferences. For example, you could create a braided updo instead of a messy bun, or you could leave some hair down for a half-up, half-down style.

Tips for Creating Updos for Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating updos for short hair:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color for a more seamless look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques!
  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your updo in place without making it look stiff.
  • If you have trouble creating volume in your hair, try using a volumizing powder.

FAQs

What if I have really short hair?

Even if your hair is very short, you can still create updos! Try using bobby pins to twist and pin small sections of hair in place.

Can I create updos for short hair without using heat?

Absolutely! In fact, many updos for short hair look best when created with natural texture. Try using a texturizing spray or volumizing powder to add some grit to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different kind of care than long hair. Here are some tips to keep your short hair healthy and looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to create stunning updos for short hair in no time. Whether you're getting ready for a special occasion or just want to switch up your look, these styles are sure to impress!
