Best Mousse To Hold Curls In Straight Hair

Written by Oliver Jul 23, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have straight hair and want to add some bouncy curls, you might have faced the challenge of keeping them in place. With the right mousse, you can achieve long-lasting curls that look natural and effortless. In this article, we will talk about the best mousse to hold curls in straight hair and how to use it for the best results.

Description

Mousse is a styling product that can help you create volume, shape and hold in your hair. The best mousse for holding curls in straight hair should provide a strong hold without making your hair feel crunchy or stiff. It should also be lightweight enough to avoid weighing down your hair or making it look greasy.

Some of the best mousses for holding curls in straight hair are:

  • Redken Guts 10 Volume Spray Foam: This mousse provides a long-lasting hold and adds volume to your hair without making it feel sticky or heavy.
  • TresemmÃ© Flawless Curls Mousse: This mousse is formulated to define curls and control frizz. It also adds shine and softness to your hair.
  • Bumble and Bumble Thickening Full Form Soft Mousse: This mousse is perfect for creating a voluminous, bouncy look. It also adds texture and definition to your curls.

When choosing a mousse, look for one that suits your hair type and desired style. If you have fine hair, you might want to choose a lightweight mousse that won't weigh your hair down. If you have thick hair, you might need a stronger hold to keep your curls in place.

How to Use Mousse to Hold Curls in Straight Hair

Using mousse to hold curls in straight hair is a simple process that can be done in a few steps:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair. Make sure your hair is free of tangles and knots.
  2. Apply a golf ball-sized amount of mousse to your palm. Rub your palms together to distribute the mousse evenly.
  3. Flip your head upside down and apply the mousse to the roots of your hair. Work the mousse through your hair with your fingers, making sure to distribute it evenly.
  4. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create curls. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance the curls.
  5. Allow your hair to air dry or use a hair dryer on a low heat setting. Avoid using a high heat setting as it can damage your hair and make your curls frizzy.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to separate the curls and create a natural look. Finish with a light hold hairspray if desired.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to using mousse to hold curls in straight hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. This will help create a good foundation for your curls.
  2. Towel dry your hair, but avoid rubbing it vigorously as it can damage your hair cuticles and create frizz.
  3. Apply a golf ball-sized amount of mousse to your palm. Rub your palms together to distribute the mousse evenly.
  4. Flip your head upside down and apply the mousse to the roots of your hair. Work the mousse through your hair with your fingers, making sure to distribute it evenly.
  5. Using a wide-tooth comb, gently comb your hair to detangle it. This will help create a smooth, frizz-free finish.
  6. Section your hair and use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Hold the diffuser close to your hair and use a low heat setting to enhance your curls.
  7. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to separate the curls and create a natural look. Avoid using a brush or comb as it can disrupt the curls.
  8. Finish with a light hold hairspray if desired.

Tips for Using Mousse to Hold Curls in Straight Hair

Here are some tips for using mousse to hold curls in straight hair:

  • Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to create a good foundation for your curls.
  • Apply the mousse to damp hair to help distribute it evenly.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid creating frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls and avoid using a high heat setting.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much once it's dry as it can disrupt the curls and create frizz.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to set your curls in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about using mousse to hold curls in straight hair:

Q: Can I use mousse to hold curls in short hair?

A: Yes, mousse can be used to hold curls in short hair. Apply the mousse to damp hair and use your fingers to create curls.

Q: Can I use mousse to hold curls in dry hair?

A: It's best to apply mousse to damp hair to help distribute it evenly. If you want to use mousse on dry hair, wet your hair slightly before applying the mousse.

Q: Can I use mousse to hold curls in fine hair?

A: Yes, but choose a lightweight mousse that won't weigh your hair down. Apply the mousse to damp hair and scrunch your hair to create curls.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Curls in Straight Hair

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining curls in straight hair:

  • Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to create a good foundation for your curls.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and create frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair and make your curls frizzy.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair as it can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

With the right mousse and hair care routine, you can enjoy long-lasting, bouncy curls in your straight hair. Experiment with different mousses and techniques to find the best one for your hair type and desired style.
