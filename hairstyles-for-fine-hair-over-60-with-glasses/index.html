Hairstyles For Fine Hair Over 60 With Glasses

Written by Eveline Apr 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our hair becomes finer and thinner, and it can be difficult to find the perfect hairstyle that complements our face shape and glasses. In this article, we'll share some of the best hairstyles for fine hair over 60 with glasses, and provide tips on how to care for your hair to maintain its health and vitality.

Description

Women over 60 with fine hair and glasses often struggle to find hairstyles that suit their face shape and lifestyle. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can achieve a flattering and low-maintenance hairstyle that makes you look and feel your best.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for fine hair over 60 with glasses is to determine your face shape. If you have a round face, a layered haircut with side-swept bangs can help elongate your face and create a more angular look. If you have a square face, a short bob or pixie cut can soften your features and add volume to your hair.

Next, consider your glasses. If you wear glasses with thick frames, opt for a shorter hairstyle that won't compete with your frames. If you wear glasses with thin frames or no frames at all, you can experiment with longer hairstyles and side-swept bangs.

When it comes to styling your hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume and texture to your fine hair. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create body and lift at the roots. For a sleeker look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair or create soft waves with a curling iron.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Use a mirror or take a selfie to determine your face shape. If you have a round face, your cheeks are the widest part of your face, and your chin is rounded. If you have a square face, your jawline is the most prominent feature of your face.

Step 2: Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and glasses

Based on your face shape and glasses, choose a hairstyle that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty.

Step 3: Use the right hair care products

Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume and texture to your fine hair. Use a heat protectant spray before using heated styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 4: Style your hair

Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create body and lift at the roots. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create sleek or wavy styles.

Tips

  • Opt for shorter hairstyles to add volume to your fine hair.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create lift at the roots.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heated styling tools.
  • Experiment with different styles and accessories to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for fine hair over 60 with glasses?

The best hairstyle for fine hair over 60 with glasses depends on your face shape and glasses. A layered haircut with side-swept bangs can complement a round face, while a short bob or pixie cut can soften a square face.

How can I add volume to my fine hair?

Use a volumizing mousse or spray, and blow dry your hair with a round brush to create lift at the roots.

Can I use heated styling tools on my fine hair?

Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care

To maintain the health and vitality of your fine hair, use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for fine hair. Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

With these tips and hairstyles for fine hair over 60 with glasses, you can achieve a look that is both stylish and low-maintenance. Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you!
