How To Sleep With Wet Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Sep 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Wet curly hair can be a hassle to deal with, especially when it comes to getting a good night's sleep. The good news is that there are ways to sleep with wet curly hair without waking up with a frizzy mess. In this article, we'll go through some tips and tricks on how to sleep with wet curly hair, as well as some FAQs and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Sleep with Wet Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Sleeping with Wet Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Sleep with Wet Curly Hair

Before we get into the tips and tricks, it's important to know that sleeping with wet hair can cause damage and breakage. However, if you must sleep with wet curly hair, here are some ways to do it:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using cotton pillowcases, as they can absorb moisture and cause frizz.
  • Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf or bonnet to protect your curls.
  • Try the "pineapple" method by gathering your hair on top of your head and securing it with a scrunchie or hair tie.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to sleep with wet curly hair:

  1. Shower and wash your hair as usual.
  2. Apply your favorite leave-in conditioner or styling product.
  3. Gently squeeze out excess water with a microfiber towel or t-shirt.
  4. Divide your hair into sections and detangle with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  5. Apply a curl-defining cream or gel to each section.
  6. Gather your hair on top of your head and secure it with a scrunchie or hair tie.
  7. Wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf or bonnet.
  8. Go to sleep and wake up with defined, frizz-free curls!

Tips for Sleeping with Wet Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for sleeping with wet curly hair:

  • Avoid using hair dryers or diffusers before bed, as they can cause frizz and damage.
  • Try the "plunking" method by using a t-shirt to scrunch and define your curls before going to bed.
  • Use a silk or satin scrunchie or hair tie to avoid creases and breakage.
  • If you have long hair, braid it loosely before going to bed to prevent tangling and breakage.
  • Avoid touching or manipulating your hair while you sleep to prevent frizz.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about sleeping with wet curly hair:

Is it bad to sleep with wet curly hair?

Yes, sleeping with wet curly hair can cause damage and breakage. However, if you must sleep with wet hair, use a silk or satin pillowcase and wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf or bonnet to protect your curls.

What is the best way to sleep with wet curly hair?

The best way to sleep with wet curly hair is to wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf or bonnet and use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz. You can also try the "pineapple" or "plunking" methods to protect your curls.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a diffuser on your hair dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can sleep with wet curly hair without waking up with a frizzy mess. Remember to take care of your curls by using the right products and techniques, and your hair will thank you for it!
