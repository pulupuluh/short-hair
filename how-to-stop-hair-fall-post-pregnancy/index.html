How To Stop Hair Fall Post Pregnancy Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Aug 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a new mom, you may have noticed that your hair is falling out more than usual. This is a common occurrence post-pregnancy, and it's nothing to be alarmed about. However, if you're looking for ways to stop hair fall post-pregnancy, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you keep your hair healthy and reduce hair fall.

How to Stop Hair Fall Post Pregnancy: Tips and Tricks

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

The first step in preventing hair fall post pregnancy is to make sure you're eating a healthy diet. This means consuming plenty of fruits and vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, and healthy fats. These foods provide the nutrients your hair needs to stay healthy and strong. Additionally, make sure you're drinking plenty of water to stay hydrated.

2. Take a Multivitamin

In addition to eating a healthy diet, taking a multivitamin can help ensure that you're getting all the nutrients your body needs. Look for a multivitamin that contains biotin, vitamin C, vitamin D, and iron. These vitamins and minerals are important for healthy hair growth.

3. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

If you're experiencing hair fall post pregnancy, it's important to avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair. This includes braids, ponytails, and buns. Instead, opt for loose hairstyles that don't put too much stress on your hair.

4. Be Gentle with Your Hair

When you're brushing or combing your hair, be gentle. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid pulling or tugging. Additionally, avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.

5. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Using a gentle shampoo can help prevent further hair fall post pregnancy. Look for a shampoo that's free of sulfates and parabens, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils. Additionally, avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause dryness and damage.

6. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help improve circulation and stimulate hair growth. You can do this by gently rubbing your scalp with your fingertips for a few minutes each day.

7. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can contribute to hair fall post pregnancy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

8. Manage Stress

Stress can contribute to hair fall post pregnancy, so it's important to find ways to manage stress. This can include exercise, meditation, or spending time with loved ones.

9. Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep is important for overall health, including hair health. Aim to get 7-8 hours of sleep each night to help reduce hair fall post pregnancy.

10. Consult a Doctor

If you're experiencing excessive hair fall post pregnancy, it's important to consult a doctor. They can help determine if there's an underlying medical condition that's causing your hair fall, and recommend treatment options.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does hair fall last after pregnancy?

Hair fall post pregnancy can last up to 6 months after giving birth.

Can breastfeeding cause hair fall?

Yes, breastfeeding can cause hair fall post pregnancy due to hormonal changes.

Can post-pregnancy hair fall be reversed?

Yes, with proper hair care and lifestyle changes, post-pregnancy hair fall can be reversed.

Hair Care Tips for Post-Pregnancy Hair Fall

Remember to eat a healthy diet, take a multivitamin, avoid tight hairstyles, be gentle with your hair, use a gentle shampoo, massage your scalp, get regular trims, manage stress, get enough sleep, and consult a doctor if necessary. By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair healthy and reduce hair fall post pregnancy.
