Best Curl Creams For Wavy Hair Uk A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Apr 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect curl cream for your wavy hair? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best curl creams for wavy hair in the UK, along with tips and tricks to achieve the perfect waves.

Description

Curl creams are a must-have for those with wavy hair. They help define and enhance your natural waves, while also reducing frizz and adding shine. With so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect one for your hair type. That's where we come in!

How to Choose the Right Curl Cream

Before we dive into our top picks for the best curl creams for wavy hair in the UK, let's talk about how to choose the right one for your hair type. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Hair Type

First and foremost, consider your hair type. Is it fine or thick? Do you have loose waves or tight curls? Different curl creams work better for different hair types.

2. Ingredients

Look for curl creams that are formulated with nourishing ingredients such as coconut oil, shea butter, and argan oil. Avoid products that contain sulfates, parabens, and alcohol, as these can dry out your hair and cause damage.

3. Hold

Depending on the level of hold you want, choose a curl cream that offers light, medium, or strong hold. Light hold curl creams work best for loose waves, while strong hold is ideal for tight curls.

Top 5 Best Curl Creams for Wavy Hair UK

Now that you know what to look for in a curl cream, let's take a look at our top picks:

1. Bumble and Bumble Bb. Curl Defining Cream

This curl cream is perfect for those with fine to medium wavy hair. It defines curls without weighing them down and adds a natural shine.

2. Cantu Shea Butter for Natural Hair Coconut Curling Cream

This curl cream is a cult favourite among those with wavy and curly hair. It's made with nourishing ingredients such as coconut oil and shea butter and provides a light hold.

3. Moroccanoil Curl Defining Cream

Moroccanoil is known for its nourishing hair products, and this curl defining cream is no exception. It's formulated with argan oil and provides a medium hold, perfect for those with medium to thick wavy hair.

4. Shea Moisture Coconut and Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie

This curl cream is perfect for those with thick and coarse wavy hair. It's made with natural ingredients such as coconut oil and hibiscus flower extract and provides a strong hold without leaving your hair crunchy.

5. Aussie Miracle Curls Curl Defining Cream

This affordable curl cream is perfect for those on a budget. It's formulated with Australian jojoba oil and sea kelp and provides a light hold for loose waves.

Tips for Achieving Perfect Waves

Now that you have the perfect curl cream, here are a few tips to help you achieve perfect waves:

1. Apply Curl Cream to Wet Hair

Apply curl cream to wet hair to help define your waves and reduce frizz. Scrunch your hair upwards to encourage your natural wave pattern.

2. Use a Diffuser

A diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can help enhance your waves and reduce frizz. Use it on a low heat setting and scrunch your hair as you go.

3. Sleep with a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help reduce frizz and keep your waves looking defined.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can curl creams be used on straight hair?

Curl creams are designed to enhance natural waves and curls. If you have straight hair, you won't see much of a difference.

2. How often should I use curl cream?

You can use curl cream as often as needed. However, overuse can lead to product buildup and greasy hair.

3. Can I use curl cream on dry hair?

Curl creams work best on wet hair. Applying it to dry hair can lead to crunchy and stiff curls.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Lastly, here are a few hair care tips to keep your wavy hair healthy and strong:

1. Use a Moisturising Shampoo and Conditioner

Wavy hair tends to be dry, so use a moisturising shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause frizz.

3. Regular Trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

With the right curl cream and hair care routine, you can achieve perfect waves every time. Give these products a try and let us know your favourite in the comments below!
