How To Cut Men s Long Curly Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of paying a fortune for a haircut every few weeks? Do you have long curly hair and want to learn how to cut it at home? Look no further! In this guide, we will walk you through how to cut men's long curly hair at home. We will provide you with the necessary tools, tips, and step-by-step instructions to achieve the perfect cut. Let's get started!

Tools You Will Need

Before we begin, make sure you have the necessary tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clipper
  • Hair ties

How to Cut Men's Long Curly Hair at Home

Step 1: Start with Clean and Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before starting to cut it. This will make it easier to cut and give you a better idea of how long your hair is.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to divide your hair into sections. Start by separating the top section from the bottom section. Then, divide the top section into smaller sections, and clip them up.

Step 3: Trim the Bottom

Start by trimming the bottom section of your hair. Use your scissors to cut the ends of your hair in a straight line. Take small sections at a time and be sure to cut evenly.

Step 4: Cut the Top and Sides

Unclip the top section of your hair and comb it straight. Cut the ends of your hair in a straight line, following the shape of your head. Use your clipper to trim the sides of your hair, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Be sure to use the same guard length on both sides to keep it even.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Use your comb to blend the hair between the top and bottom sections. Use your scissors to cut any stray hairs and make sure it looks even.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Take a step back and check your work. Make any necessary adjustments and trim any stray hairs.

Tips for Cutting Men's Long Curly Hair at Home

  • Start with small sections and cut slowly to avoid making mistakes.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to take off a little at a time than to cut off too much.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting hair can be tiring, so don't be afraid to take a break and come back to it later.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

While you can use regular scissors to cut your hair, it's best to use hair cutting scissors. These scissors are designed to cut hair cleanly and evenly, which will give you a better result.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! You can always make adjustments and trim any stray hairs. If you're not happy with the result, you can always go to a professional to have it fixed.

How often should I cut my hair?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want it to be. As a general rule, you should aim to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking its best.

Hair Care Tips

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair, such as hair dryers or straighteners, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

Congratulations! You now know how to cut men's long curly hair at home. With the right tools, tips, and instructions, you can achieve the perfect cut without ever leaving your house. Remember to take your time, start with small sections, and don't be afraid to make adjustments. Happy cutting!
