How To Cut Women s Hair With Clippers At Home The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 18, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re looking to save time and money by cutting your hair at home, using clippers can be a great option. However, it can be intimidating to use them, especially if you’re not a professional hairstylist. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut that looks like it was done by a pro. In this article, we’ll show you how to cut women’s hair with clippers at home, step by step.

How to Cut Women’s Hair with Clippers at Home:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You’ll need a good pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, a mirror, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow-drying it. This will help you see the length and texture of your hair better.

Step 3: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want to cut your hair. Use the clipper guard that corresponds to the desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Use your comb to section your hair. Start with the back of your head and work your way to the front. Clip the top section of your hair away from the bottom section.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair with the clippers. Begin at the nape of your neck and move upward, using the clipper guard to maintain the desired length. Use a slow, steady motion and make sure to cut in the direction of your hair growth.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Blend the hair by removing the clipper guard and using the clippers without it. Use a sweeping motion to blend the hair between the different sections.

Step 7: Cut the Sides

Move to the sides of your head and use the clippers to cut the hair. Start at the bottom and move upward, using the clipper guard to maintain the length.

Step 8: Cut the Top

Unclip the top section of your hair and use the scissors to cut it. Start at the front and work your way to the back. Cut the hair at an angle, using the previous sections as a guide.

Step 9: Check Your Work

Check your work in the mirror to make sure you’ve cut your hair evenly. Use the clippers and scissors to touch up any areas that need it.

Step 10: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired using your fingers or a styling product.

Tips for Cutting Women’s Hair with Clippers at Home:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Use a slow, steady motion when cutting your hair.
  • Make sure to cut in the direction of your hair growth.
  • Blend the hair between the different sections for a seamless look.
  • Use the scissors to cut the top section of your hair.
  • Check your work in the mirror to make sure you’ve cut your hair evenly.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with clippers without a guard?

It’s not recommended to use clippers without a guard, as it can result in an uneven cut or even injury.

Can I use the same clipper guard for the entire haircut?

No, you should use different clipper guards for different sections of your hair to achieve a graduated, layered look.

How often should I cut my hair with clippers at home?

It depends on your hair growth and the desired length. Generally, it’s recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair with clippers, make sure to wash and condition it. Use a hydrating hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to cut women’s hair with clippers at home, you can save time and money while achieving a great haircut. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow the steps carefully. Happy cutting!
