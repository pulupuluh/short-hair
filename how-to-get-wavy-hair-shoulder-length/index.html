How To Get Wavy Hair Shoulder Length

Written by Luffy Mar 11, 2023 · 9 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on how to get wavy hair shoulder length. Whether you’ve been blessed with naturally straight hair or have chemically relaxed your hair, creating waves can be a fun and easy way to switch up your look. From using heat tools to styling with products, we’ve got you covered.

Description

Wavy hair is a versatile and effortless style that can be achieved by anyone, regardless of hair type or length. Shoulder-length hair is the perfect length for creating soft, loose waves that can be dressed up or down for any occasion. In this guide, we’ll be showing you how to get wavy hair shoulder length using a variety of techniques and tools.

How To Get Wavy Hair Shoulder Length

There are several different ways to get wavy hair shoulder length, depending on your hair type and the tools you have available. Here are some of the most popular methods:

Method 1: Heat Tools

Heat tools like curling irons and wands are one of the most popular ways to create waves in your hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and clip them out of the way.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand.
  5. Hold for a few seconds, then release and repeat with the rest of your hair.
  6. Once all of your hair is curled, use your fingers or a wide-toothed comb to gently separate the curls and create waves.
  7. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the waves in place.

Method 2: Braids

Braids are another easy and heat-free way to create waves in your hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a styling product like mousse or sea salt spray to your hair to help create texture.
  3. Divide your hair into two or three sections.
  4. Braid each section of hair, starting at the top and working your way down.
  5. Secure the ends with a hair tie.
  6. Leave the braids in overnight or for several hours.
  7. Take out the braids and use your fingers or a wide-toothed comb to gently separate the waves.
  8. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the waves in place.

Method 3: Twist and Pin

This method is perfect for creating loose, beachy waves. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a styling product like mousse or sea salt spray to your hair to help create texture.
  3. Divide your hair into two or three sections.
  4. Twist each section of hair away from your face.
  5. Pin each twist to your head with a bobby pin.
  6. Leave the twists in for several hours or overnight.
  7. Take out the bobby pins and use your fingers or a wide-toothed comb to gently separate the waves.
  8. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the waves in place.

Step by Step

Now that you know the different methods for getting wavy hair shoulder length, let’s dive into a step-by-step guide on how to create waves using heat tools:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and clip them out of the way.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand.
  5. Hold for a few seconds, then release and repeat with the rest of your hair.
  6. Once all of your hair is curled, use your fingers or a wide-toothed comb to gently separate the curls and create waves.
  7. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the waves in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for getting wavy hair shoulder length:

  • Use a heat protectant spray or cream to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don’t hold the curling iron or wand on your hair for too long, as this can cause damage.
  • Experiment with different sized curling irons and wands to create different types of waves.
  • Add a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to your hair before styling to help create texture.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your twists or braids for a more comfortable and secure hold.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting wavy hair shoulder length:

How long will my waves last?

This depends on your hair type and the method you used to create the waves. Heat-styled waves will typically last 1-2 days, while braided or twisted waves can last up to a week.

Can I create waves without heat?

Yes! Braids and twists are a great heat-free way to create waves in your hair.

How often should I curl my hair?

We recommend limiting heat styling to once or twice a week to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray or cream.

Now that you know how to get wavy hair shoulder length, it’s time to give it a try! Whether you prefer heat tools, braids, or twists, there’s a method that will work for you. With a little practice and some styling products, you’ll be able to create beautiful, beachy waves in no time.
