Things To Do When Your Hair Is Falling Out

Written by Aprilia Sep 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you experiencing hair fall? Losing hair can be a stressful experience, but it is not the end of the world. There are a few things you can do to prevent further hair fall and promote hair growth. In this article, we will discuss some tips and tricks to help you deal with hair fall.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Determine the Cause of Hair Fall
  2. Tips to Prevent Hair Fall
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Promote Hair Growth
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

How to Determine the Cause of Hair Fall

The first step to dealing with hair fall is determining the cause. There are a few common reasons why hair falls out:

  • Stress
  • Poor diet
  • Hormonal changes
  • Medical conditions
  • Overuse of hair styling products

If you are unsure about the cause of your hair fall, it is best to consult a dermatologist.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are a few tips to help prevent hair fall:

  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair.
  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair.
  • Get enough sleep and manage stress levels.

Step-by-Step Guide to Promote Hair Growth

Here is a step-by-step guide to promote hair growth:

  1. Massage your scalp with warm oil for a few minutes.
  2. Wrap your hair with a warm towel and leave it for 30 minutes.
  3. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo.
  4. Apply a hair mask made with natural ingredients like eggs, yogurt, and honey.
  5. Leave the hair mask on for 30 minutes and then wash it off with a mild shampoo.
  6. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair and leave it on overnight.
  7. Rinse your hair with cool water in the morning.

FAQs

Q: Can hair fall be reversed?

A: Yes, hair fall can be reversed with proper care and treatment.

Q: Will cutting my hair help with hair fall?

A: Cutting your hair will not prevent hair fall, but it can make your hair look fuller and healthier.

Q: Can hair styling products cause hair fall?

A: Yes, overuse of hair styling products can cause hair fall. It is best to use them in moderation.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are a few hair care tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons frequently.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly when styling it.
  • Be gentle while brushing or combing your hair.
  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Use a satin pillowcase to prevent hair breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can prevent hair fall and promote hair growth. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine.
