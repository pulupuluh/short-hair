How To Do An Undercut Fade Girl A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd, an undercut fade might be just what you need. This trendy hairstyle has been popular among men for years, but it's now becoming increasingly popular among women as well. In this article, we'll show you how to do an undercut fade girl style, step by step.

  2. How to Do an Undercut Fade Girl

Description

An undercut fade girl is a haircut that features a shaved section on the side or back of the head, which gradually fades into longer hair on top. This style can be customized to suit your individual taste and preferences, and it can be worn in a variety of ways.

Whether you're looking for a dramatic and bold look or something more subtle and chic, an undercut fade girl is a versatile and stylish choice.

How to Do an Undercut Fade Girl

Before you start, you'll need a few tools and products to help you achieve the perfect undercut fade. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Hair trimmer
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Hair gel or styling product

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect undercut fade girl:

  1. Start by sectioning off the top portion of your hair, which you'll leave longer. Use a clip or hair tie to keep it out of the way.
  2. Next, use your clippers to shave the sides and back of your head. You can choose how high up you want to go, depending on how dramatic you want the undercut to be.
  3. Once you've shaved the sides and back, switch to your hair trimmer and use it to blend the shaved portion into the longer hair on top. Start with a high guard and work your way down slowly until you achieve the desired fade.
  4. Use your scissors to trim any longer pieces of hair on top, blending them into the shorter hair on the sides and back.
  5. Style your hair as desired, using hair gel or styling product to keep it in place.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Undercut Fade Girl

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when doing an undercut fade girl:

  • Choose the right length for the shaved portion. If you're not sure, start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter.
  • Be patient and take your time. Achieving the perfect fade takes practice and precision.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

FAQs

Q: How often do I need to touch up my undercut fade girl?

A: This depends on how quickly your hair grows and how dramatic your undercut is. In general, you'll want to touch it up every 4-6 weeks to maintain the look.

Q: Can I do an undercut fade girl on my own?

A: While it's possible to do an undercut fade girl on your own, it's recommended that you go to a professional stylist for the best results.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Undercut Fade Girl

Here are a few tips for keeping your undercut fade girl looking fresh and stylish:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and hydrated.
  • Use a styling product to keep your hair in place and prevent flyaways.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect undercut fade girl and keep it looking stylish and fresh for weeks to come. So why not give it a try?
