What Is The Best Shampoo For Curly Hair Uk

Written by Christine Sep 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to manage, but finding the right shampoo can make all the difference. With so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best shampoo for curly hair in the UK. In this article, we’ll explore the top shampoos for curly hair and provide tips on how to care for your curls.

Why is it Important to Use the Right Shampoo for Curly Hair?

Curly hair is naturally drier than straight hair because the oils from the scalp have a harder time traveling down the hair shaft. Using the wrong shampoo can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry, frizzy, and difficult to manage. The right shampoo will help to nourish and hydrate your curls, leaving them soft, defined, and bouncy.

How to Choose the Best Shampoo for Curly Hair?

When choosing a shampoo for curly hair, there are a few key things to look for. Firstly, you want to avoid shampoos that contain sulfates, as these can be harsh and drying on curly hair. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free and contain moisturizing ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, and argan oil. Secondly, look for shampoos that are specifically formulated for curly hair. These shampoos will help to define your curls and reduce frizz.

Top Shampoos for Curly Hair in the UK

1. Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl & Shine Shampoo

This sulfate-free shampoo is formulated with coconut oil, silk protein, and neem oil to nourish and hydrate curly hair. It helps to define curls and reduce frizz, leaving hair soft and bouncy.

2. Cantu Shea Butter for Natural Hair Sulfate-Free Cleansing Cream Shampoo

This sulfate-free shampoo is enriched with shea butter and coconut oil to moisturize and nourish curly hair. It gently cleanses the hair without stripping it of its natural oils, leaving it soft, shiny, and manageable.

3. Maui Moisture Curl Quench + Coconut Oil Shampoo

This curly hair shampoo is infused with coconut oil, papaya butter, and plumeria extract to hydrate and define curls. It’s sulfate-free and gentle, making it perfect for curly hair.

Tips for Caring for Curly Hair

Using the right shampoo is just one part of caring for curly hair. Here are some additional tips to keep your curls looking their best:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair

Curly hair is prone to tangling, so it’s important to use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to avoid pulling and damaging your hair.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can be damaging to curly hair, so try to avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons. If you must use heat, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

3. Sleep on a silk pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to reduce frizz and prevent breakage. The smooth surface of the silk won’t rub against your hair, leaving it smooth and shiny in the morning.

FAQ

Q: Can I use regular shampoo on curly hair?

A: Regular shampoo can be too harsh and drying for curly hair. It’s best to use a shampoo that’s specifically formulated for curly hair.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It’s best to wash your curly hair no more than once or twice a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: How can I reduce frizz in my curly hair?

A: Using a sulfate-free shampoo, avoiding heat styling, and sleeping on a silk pillowcase can all help to reduce frizz in curly hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Using the right shampoo is just one part of caring for curly hair. By following these tips and using the right products, you can keep your curls soft, defined, and bouncy.
