How To Style Hair With Gel For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Apr 20, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you looking for a way to style your hair that is easy and long-lasting? Look no further than hair gel! In this guide, we will walk you through the steps of how to style hair with gel for guys.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Hair with Gel for Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Hair with Gel

How to Style Hair with Gel for Guys

When it comes to styling hair with gel, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, you want to choose the right type of gel for your hair type and the look you want to achieve. Second, you want to apply the gel evenly throughout your hair to ensure that it holds the style in place. And finally, you want to use a blow dryer to set the style and give it a polished finish.

Choosing the Right Gel

When choosing a gel, there are a few things to consider. First, consider your hair type. If you have thin or fine hair, you may want to choose a lighter gel that won't weigh your hair down. If you have thick or curly hair, you may want to choose a stronger gel that will hold your hair in place.

Next, consider the look you want to achieve. If you want a natural, messy look, you may want to choose a texturizing gel. If you want a slicked-back, polished look, you may want to choose a high-shine gel.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have chosen the right gel for your hair type and style, it's time to get started. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair with gel:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair. Use a comb to remove any tangles and to part your hair if desired.
  2. Squeeze a dime-sized amount of gel into your palm and rub your hands together.
  3. Distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends. Use your fingers to shape your hair into the desired style.
  4. Use a blow dryer on low heat to set the style in place. Use a comb or your fingers to shape your hair as you blow dry.
  5. Once your hair is dry, you can use a bit more gel to add texture or hold to specific areas if needed.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil for added shine and hold.

Tips for Styling Hair with Gel

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your hair with gel:

  • Less is more when it comes to gel. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the gel evenly throughout your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair on low heat to avoid damaging your hair and to set the style in place.
  • To achieve a natural look, use your fingers to create texture and movement in your hair.
  • For a polished look, use a comb to create a sleek, smooth style.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use gel on wet hair?

Yes, you can use gel on wet hair. In fact, applying gel to wet hair can help distribute the product more evenly throughout your hair.

Can I use gel every day?

Yes, you can use gel every day, but it's important to wash your hair regularly to avoid build-up and to keep your hair healthy.

How do I remove gel from my hair?

To remove gel from your hair, simply wash your hair with shampoo and warm water.

Hair Care with Gel

Using gel to style your hair can be a great way to achieve a long-lasting, polished look. However, it's important to care for your hair to avoid damage and breakage. Here are a few tips for caring for your hair when using gel:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any build-up from gel.
  • Avoid using too much gel, as this can weigh your hair down and cause breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a great-looking style with hair gel. Experiment with different types of gel and styles to find the look that works best for you. Happy styling!
