Easy To Do Hairstyles For Long Hair At Home

Written by William Jun 27, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Long hair can be both a blessing and a curse. It looks beautiful, but it can be a challenge to style it every day. Whether you want to dress up for a special occasion or just want to change your look for the day, there are plenty of easy hairstyles you can do at home. Here are some ideas for easy to do hairstyles for long hair at home.

Description

In this blog post, we will discuss some easy hairstyles for long hair that you can do at home. From braids to buns, we will provide step-by-step instructions and tips to help you achieve a perfect hairstyle every time. We will also answer some frequently asked questions about hair care and provide some general tips to help you maintain healthy and beautiful hair.

How to

Before we dive into the different hairstyles you can do at home, let's talk about some basic hair care tips. First, make sure you are using the right shampoo and conditioner for your hair type. If you have long, thick hair, you may want to use a moisturizing shampoo to help keep your hair hydrated. Second, avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to create gentle waves.

1. Braided Crown

The braided crown is a beautiful and easy hairstyle that you can do at home. To create this hairstyle, start by parting your hair down the middle. Then, take a small section of hair on one side of your head and braid it. Repeat on the other side. Next, take one braid and wrap it over the top of your head, securing it with bobby pins. Do the same with the other braid, tucking the ends under the first braid. Voila! You now have a beautiful braided crown.

2. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for those days when you just don't feel like styling your hair. To create a messy bun, start by pulling your hair back into a high ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins, leaving some pieces of hair loose to create a messy look. You can also pull out some strands of hair around your face to frame it.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a simple but elegant look that you can do at home. To create this hairstyle, start by pulling the top half of your hair back and securing it with a hair tie. You can leave some strands loose to frame your face. Then, you can curl the bottom half of your hair for a more polished look or leave it straight for a more natural look.

Step-by-Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions to help you create these easy hairstyles for long hair at home.

Braided Crown

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair on one side of your head and braid it.
  3. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Take one braid and wrap it over the top of your head, securing it with bobby pins.
  5. Do the same with the other braid, tucking the ends under the first braid.

Messy Bun

  1. Pull your hair back into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins, leaving some pieces of hair loose to create a messy look.
  4. Pull out some strands of hair around your face to frame it.

Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a hair tie.
  2. Leave some strands loose to frame your face.
  3. Curl the bottom half of your hair for a more polished look or leave it straight for a more natural look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to create a more natural look.
  • Add some dry shampoo to your roots for extra volume.
  • Use a hairspray to help hold your hairstyle in place.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with long, thick hair may only need to wash it once a week, while others may need to wash it every other day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: How can I prevent split ends?

A: Regular haircuts can help prevent split ends. You can also use a hair oil or leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized.

Q: How can I make my hair grow faster?

A: Unfortunately, there is no magic solution to make your hair grow faster. However, maintaining a healthy diet and taking care of your hair can help promote healthy growth.

Hair Care

Here are some general tips to help you maintain healthy and beautiful hair:

  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein and vitamins.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.

Now that you have some ideas for easy to do hairstyles for long hair at home, it's time to start experimenting! Remember to take care of your hair and have fun with your new looks.
