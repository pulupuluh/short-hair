Best Colour Hair For Over 60 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 17, 2023 Â· 10 min read

As we age, our hair changes in texture and color. It's common for our hair to become thinner, drier, and lose its natural color. However, that doesn't mean we can't still look fabulous. Choosing the right hair color can make all the difference in enhancing our features and making us feel confident. In this article, we will discuss the best hair color for women over 60, including tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Content

  • How to Choose the Right Hair Color
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you've never colored your hair before. Here are some tips to help you choose the best color for you:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a significant role in determining which hair color will look best on you. If you have warm undertones, such as yellow or peach, warm hair colors like golden blonde, caramel, or auburn will look great on you. If you have cool undertones, such as pink or blue, cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum, or silver will complement your skin tone.

Think About Your Natural Hair Color

If you're looking for a subtle change, consider a hair color that's close to your natural color. If you want a more dramatic change, don't be afraid to try something completely different. Just make sure it complements your skin tone and features.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Consider how much time you're willing to spend on maintenance. If you don't want to spend a lot of time and money on touch-ups, choose a hair color that's close to your natural color or a low-maintenance color like balayage or highlights.

Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair

If you've decided to color your hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the best results:

Step 1: Choose Your Hair Color

Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and features. If you're not sure which color to choose, consult with a professional stylist.

Step 2: Do a Strand Test

Before applying the hair color all over, do a strand test to make sure you're not allergic to the product and to test the color. Apply the color to a small section of hair and wait for the recommended time before rinsing it off.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before applying the hair color. Make sure your hair is free of any products, such as gel or hairspray.

Step 4: Apply the Hair Color

Follow the instructions on the hair color box. Apply the color to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down. Use a comb to distribute the color evenly.

Step 5: Wait

Wait for the recommended time before rinsing the hair color off. Rinse your hair with cool water until the water runs clear.

Step 6: Condition Your Hair

Use the conditioner that came with the hair color to condition your hair. Leave it on for the recommended time before rinsing it off.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Now that you've colored your hair, here are some tips to help you maintain your color:

Use Color-Safe Products

Use shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for colored hair. These products are gentler on your hair and help to maintain your color.

Avoid Hot Tools

Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and flat irons, as they can damage your hair and cause your color to fade faster.

Avoid Chlorine

Avoid swimming in chlorinated water as it can cause your hair color to fade.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hair color for women over 60?

The best hair color for women over 60 is one that complements their skin tone and features. Warm colors like golden blonde, caramel, or auburn look great on women with warm undertones, while cool colors like ash blonde, platinum, or silver complement women with cool undertones.

Is it safe to color your hair at 60?

Yes, it's safe to color your hair at 60. However, it's essential to choose a gentle hair color that won't cause damage to your hair or scalp.

How often should I color my hair?

It depends on the type of hair color you choose. Permanent hair color lasts up to eight weeks, while semi-permanent hair color lasts up to four weeks. You should also consider how fast your hair grows and how noticeable the roots are.

Hair Care Tips for Women Over 60

Now that you've colored your hair, here are some hair care tips for women over 60:

Moisturize Your Hair

As we age, our hair becomes drier and more brittle. To keep your hair healthy and hydrated, use a deep conditioning mask once a week.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and breakage. Visit your stylist every six to eight weeks for a trim.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using hot tools as they can damage your hair and cause it to become more brittle. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF. The sun can cause your hair color to fade and damage your hair.

In conclusion, choosing the best hair color for women over 60 can be a daunting task, but with the right tips and hair care, you can achieve beautiful results. Consider your skin tone, natural hair color, and lifestyle when choosing a hair color. Follow the step-by-step guide to coloring your hair and use color-safe products to maintain your color. Don't forget to take care of your hair by moisturizing, trimming, and protecting it from the sun.
