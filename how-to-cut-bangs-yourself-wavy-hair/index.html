How To Cut Bangs Yourself For Wavy Hair

Written by Robby Sep 13, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of having the same old hairstyle? Cutting your bangs is a simple way to switch up your look. However, getting a professional haircut can be expensive and time-consuming. Fortunately, you can learn how to cut bangs yourself for wavy hair. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice for achieving the perfect bangs.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Cut Bangs Yourself for Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

Step-by-Step Guide:

Follow these simple steps to cut bangs yourself for wavy hair:

Step 1: Decide on the Bangs Style

Before you start cutting, decide on the bangs style you want. You can choose from straight, blunt, choppy, or side-swept bangs. Look for inspiration on social media or consult with a hairstylist.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly. Brush it to remove any tangles and knots. Use hair clips to separate the rest of your hair from the bangs section.

Step 3: Determine the Bangs Length

Start with a longer length and trim gradually. Cut less than you think you need and adjust accordingly. Remember, you can always cut more, but you can't add hair back.

Step 4: Cut the Bangs

Hold the scissors vertically and make small cuts. Avoid cutting straight across. Instead, follow the natural curve of your hairline. Cut from the center and work your way outwards.

Step 5: Check the Length

Release the hair clips and check the length. Make any necessary adjustments. Remember, it's better to cut less than to cut too much.

Tips for Cutting Bangs:

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect bangs:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Cut your bangs when your hair is dry to avoid shrinkage.
  • Start with a longer length and trim gradually.
  • Use a comb to keep the hair in place while cutting.
  • Don't cut too much at once.
  • Cut at an angle for a more natural look.

FAQs:

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting bangs:

Q: How often should I cut my bangs?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and the style you want. On average, cutting your bangs every 4-6 weeks is recommended.

Q: Can I cut my bangs with regular scissors?

A: While you can use regular scissors, it's recommended to use hair cutting scissors for a more precise cut.

Q: How do I fix mistakes when cutting bangs?

A: If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim a little more or hide it with hair accessories.

Hair Care Advice:

Here are some tips for taking care of your bangs:

  • Wash your bangs separately from the rest of your hair.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to avoid frizz.
  • Avoid using too much heat or styling products on your bangs.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to avoid split ends.

Cutting your own bangs can be a fun and rewarding experience. With these tips and step-by-step instructions, you can confidently cut your bangs yourself for wavy hair. Remember, take your time and be patient. Happy cutting!
