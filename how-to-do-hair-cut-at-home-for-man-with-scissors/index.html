How To Do Hair Cut At Home For Man With Scissors

Written by Bardi Mar 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a man who wants to save some money and get a fresh haircut in the comfort of your own home, then you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll show you how to do a haircut at home for men with scissors. We'll cover everything from the tools you need to the step-by-step process, tips, and hair care advice. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Preparing for the Haircut
  • Cutting the Hair
  • Blending the Hair
  • Finishing Touches
  • Tips for a Successful Haircut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After the Haircut

Tools You'll Need

Before you begin, you'll need to gather the necessary tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Mirror
  • Towel or cape
  • Water spray bottle


Make sure that your scissors are sharp and clean. You don't want to use dull or dirty scissors on your hair.

Preparing for the Haircut

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. You don't want it to be soaking wet, but it should be damp. If you have longer hair, you may want to tie it up or clip it back.


Next, put on a towel or cape to protect your clothes from any hair clippings. Use a water spray bottle to dampen your hair if it starts to dry out during the haircut.

Cutting the Hair

Begin by combing your hair straight down over your forehead. Take a small section of hair between your fingers and hold it straight up. Use your scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Repeat this process around your head, working from front to back and from side to side.


If you have longer hair, you may want to use clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one until you reach the desired length.

Blending the Hair

Once you've cut the hair to the desired length, it's time to blend the hair. Comb the hair straight out from your head and use your scissors to trim any longer pieces. Work your way around your head, blending the hair from the top to the sides and back.


If you have a fade haircut, you'll need to blend the hair from one length to another. Use a comb and scissors to blend the hair until you achieve a seamless transition.

Finishing Touches

After you've cut and blended the hair, it's time to add some finishing touches. Use your scissors to trim any stray hairs and create a more polished look. If you have a beard or mustache, you can use your scissors to trim and shape it.

Tips for a Successful Haircut

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Use a comb to guide your scissors and create even cuts.
  • Take breaks if you need them. Cutting hair can be tiring!
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or family member.

FAQ

Can I use any scissors to cut my hair?

No. You should use hair cutting scissors. These are specifically designed to cut hair and will give you better results than regular scissors.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on the style of haircut you have and how quickly your hair grows. On average, men should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

What if I mess up my haircut?

Don't worry! Hair grows back, and you can always go to a professional if you need to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care After the Haircut

After your haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type and style. Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils. If you have longer hair, you may want to use hair oil or serum to keep it healthy and shiny.


Congratulations! You now know how to do a haircut at home for men with scissors. With a bit of practice and patience, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut without leaving your home.
