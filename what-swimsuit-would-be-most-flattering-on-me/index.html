What Swimsuit Would Be Most Flattering On Me

Written by Aprilia Jul 25, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Welcome to our blog post about finding the perfect swimsuit that will flatter your body type. We understand that shopping for swimsuits can be a daunting task, but fear not, we have some tips and tricks that will make the process a lot easier. In this article, we will be discussing the different types of swimsuits that will work best for your body shape, how to measure yourself for the perfect fit, and some hair care tips to complete your beach look. Let's dive in!

How to Determine Your Body Shape

The first step in finding the perfect swimsuit is to determine your body shape. There are five main body shapes - apple, pear, hourglass, rectangle, and inverted triangle. Knowing your body shape will help you choose the right swimsuit that will accentuate your best features and downplay your problem areas.

Apple: If you carry most of your weight around your midsection, you have an apple-shaped body. Look for swimsuits that will draw attention away from your midsection, such as one-piece swimsuits with ruching or high-waisted bikini bottoms.

Pear: If your hips are wider than your shoulders, you have a pear-shaped body. Look for swimsuits that will balance out your proportions, such as a halter top bikini or a one-piece swimsuit with a plunging neckline.

Hourglass: If your shoulders and hips are in proportion, and you have a defined waist, you have an hourglass-shaped body. Look for swimsuits that will highlight your curves, such as a high-waisted bikini or a one-piece swimsuit with a belt.

Rectangle: If your shoulders, waist, and hips are roughly the same size, you have a rectangular-shaped body. Look for swimsuits that will create the illusion of curves, such as a bikini with ruffles or a one-piece swimsuit with cutouts.

Inverted Triangle: If your shoulders are wider than your hips, you have an inverted triangle-shaped body. Look for swimsuits that will balance out your proportions, such as a bikini with boy shorts or a one-piece swimsuit with a skirt.

How to Measure Yourself for the Perfect Fit

Once you know your body shape, the next step is to measure yourself for the perfect fit. Here are some tips for measuring yourself:

- Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your bust.

- Waist: Measure around the narrowest part of your waist.

- Hips: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.

- Torso: Measure from the top of your shoulder, down your back, and between your legs, and back up to the starting point.

When shopping for swimsuits, make sure to check the size chart for each brand, as sizing can vary. It's also a good idea to read reviews from other customers to see if the swimsuit runs true to size.

Types of Swimsuits That Will Flatter Your Body Shape

Now that you know your body shape and how to measure yourself, let's talk about the different types of swimsuits that will flatter your figure.

One-Piece Swimsuit: A one-piece swimsuit is a classic option that works well for all body types. Look for one with ruching or a belt to create the illusion of curves.

Bikini: A bikini is a great option if you want to show off your midsection. Look for a high-waisted bikini bottom if you have an apple-shaped body, or boy shorts if you have an inverted triangle-shaped body.

Halter Top: A halter top is a great option if you have a pear-shaped body, as it will balance out your proportions.

Plunging Neckline: A plunging neckline is a great option if you have an hourglass-shaped body, as it will highlight your curves.

Cutouts: Cutouts are a great option if you have a rectangular-shaped body, as they will create the illusion of curves.

Hair Care Tips to Complete Your Beach Look

Now that you have found the perfect swimsuit, it's time to complete your beach look with some hair care tips. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

- Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

- Rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming to remove chlorine or saltwater.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

- Avoid using hot tools, such as a curling iron or straightener, as they can damage your hair.

FAQ

A: Look for one-piece swimsuits with ruching or high-waisted bikini bottoms.

Q: What swimsuit will flatter my pear-shaped body?

A: Look for swimsuits that will balance out your proportions, such as a halter top bikini or a one-piece swimsuit with a plunging neckline.

Q: What swimsuit will flatter my hourglass-shaped body?

Conclusion

We hope this article has helped you find the perfect swimsuit that will flatter your body shape. Remember to measure yourself for the perfect fit, and to choose a swimsuit that will accentuate your best features and downplay your problem areas. And don't forget to complete your beach look with some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Have fun in the sun!
