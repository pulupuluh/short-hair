How To Get Rid Of Natural Waves In Hair For Guys

Written by Frank Jun 14, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of your natural waves in your hair and want to try something new? You’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll give you tips and tricks on how to get rid of natural waves in hair for guys. From hair care to step-by-step instructions, we’ve got you covered.

Description

Natural waves in hair can be a blessing and a curse. While some people love the effortless beachy look, others prefer a more polished and sleek hairstyle. If you’re in the latter category, you may be wondering how to get rid of your natural waves. There are a few methods you can try, but it’s important to note that some of these may damage your hair.

How to

Before we dive into the methods, it’s important to note that if you have naturally curly hair, it may not be possible to completely get rid of your waves. However, you can still try these methods to minimize them.

Method 1: Blow Drying

The first method to try is blow drying. This is a simple and quick way to straighten your hair. After washing your hair, use a heat protectant spray and then blow dry your hair using a round brush. Make sure to point the nozzle of the blow dryer downwards to avoid frizz.

Method 2: Flat Iron

If blow drying doesn’t do the trick, you can try using a flat iron. This method is a bit more time-consuming, but it will give you a sleek and straight look. Start by sectioning your hair and using a heat protectant spray. Then, slowly glide the flat iron down each section of hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Method 3: Chemical Straightening

If you’re looking for a more permanent solution, you can try chemical straightening. This method involves using chemicals to break down the bonds in your hair, which will permanently straighten it. However, it’s important to note that this method can be damaging to your hair and should only be done by a professional.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to use the blow drying method:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Section your hair and use a round brush to blow dry it.
  4. Point the nozzle of the blow dryer downwards to avoid frizz.
  5. Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style.

And here’s a step-by-step guide on how to use the flat iron method:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Section your hair and use a flat iron to straighten it.
  4. Start at the roots and slowly glide the flat iron down each section of hair.
  5. Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying to get rid of natural waves:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don’t use a flat iron or blow dryer on wet hair.
  • Use a round brush when blow drying to add volume to your hair.
  • Don’t use chemical straightening too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting a haircut that complements your straightened hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting rid of natural waves:

Can I use a hair straightener instead of a flat iron?

Yes, you can use a hair straightener instead of a flat iron. They essentially do the same thing.

Can I blow dry my hair without a round brush?

Yes, you can blow dry your hair without a round brush, but using one will give you more volume and help straighten your hair.

How often can I use chemical straightening?

You should only use chemical straightening every 6-12 months as it can be damaging to your hair.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair when trying to get rid of natural waves. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to get rid of your natural waves and achieve a sleek and polished hairstyle. Just remember to take care of your hair and use these methods in moderation to avoid damage.
