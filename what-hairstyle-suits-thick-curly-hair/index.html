What Hairstyle Suits Thick Curly Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Apr 04, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Having thick curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, you have voluminous and bouncy locks that can turn heads. On the other hand, it can be difficult to find the right hairstyle that suits your hair type, especially if you prefer to wear it natural. In this blog post, we will guide you through the best hairstyle options for thick curly hair in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Thick curly hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right hairstyle, you can make the most of your natural texture. The key is to find a haircut that works with your curls, rather than against them. This means avoiding styles that require a lot of heat styling or chemical treatments, as these can damage your hair over time.

How to

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your thick curly hair is to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a cut that flatters your features and enhances your natural texture. Some popular hairstyles for thick curly hair include:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for thick curly hair, as they can help to add movement and volume without sacrificing length. This style works best on oval or heart-shaped faces, as it can help to balance out your features.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic hairstyle that can work well with curly hair. This style is particularly flattering on round faces, as it can help to elongate your features. To make the most of your curls, ask your stylist to cut your hair into layers and add some texture.

3. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut can be a great way to show off your curly hair. This style works best on square or diamond-shaped faces, as it can help to soften your features. To keep your curls looking defined, use a curl-enhancing product and let your hair air dry.

Step by step

When it comes to styling your thick curly hair, there are a few steps you can take to enhance your natural texture:

1. Start with clean, moisturized hair.

To keep your curls looking their best, it's important to start with clean, moisturized hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Apply a curl-enhancing product.

To enhance your curls, apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair. This can be a mousse, gel, or cream, depending on your hair type and texture.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

To avoid frizz and enhance your curls, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will help to distribute the heat evenly and prevent your hair from getting too hot.

4. Finish with a hairspray or oil.

To hold your curls in place and add shine, finish with a hairspray or oil. This will help to keep your curls looking defined and bouncy all day long.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when styling your thick curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair over time.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my thick curly hair?

A: While it's possible to straighten your curly hair, it's not recommended on a regular basis. Heat styling tools can damage your hair over time, and straightening can also alter your natural texture. If you do choose to straighten your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using high heat settings.

Q: How often should I wash my thick curly hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and texture, but most people with thick curly hair can go 2-3 days between washes. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I color my thick curly hair?

A: Yes, you can color your thick curly hair, but it's important to choose a hair color that works well with your natural texture. Avoid using harsh chemicals or lightening your hair more than a few shades, as this can damage your hair and alter your curl pattern.

Hair Care

To keep your thick curly hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair over time.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and hairstyle options, you can make the most of your thick curly hair in 2023. Embrace your natural texture and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
