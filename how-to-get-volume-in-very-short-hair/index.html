How To Get Volume In Very Short Hair Tips And Tricks

If you have very short hair, you may think that getting volume is impossible. But with the right styling techniques and products, you can create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair. In this post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to get volume in very short hair.

How to Get Volume in Very Short Hair

Getting volume in very short hair requires a few key steps:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Choose the right styling products
  • Use a blow dryer to lift and volumize your hair
  • Finish with hairspray or other styling products

Let's dive deeper into each of these steps.

Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help your hair hold the style and prevent it from falling flat throughout the day. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup that might be weighing down your hair.

Choose the Right Styling Products

The right styling products can make all the difference when it comes to getting volume in very short hair. Look for products that are designed to add volume, such as mousses, root-lifting sprays, and volumizing powders. Apply these products to your roots and work them through your hair with your fingers.

Use a Blow Dryer to Lift and Volumize Your Hair

Using a blow dryer is essential for creating volume in very short hair. Use a round brush to lift and volumize your hair as you blow dry it. Focus the heat on your roots and use your fingers to lift your hair as you dry it. This will help create volume and fullness.

Finish with Hairspray or Other Styling Products

Once you've styled your hair, finish it off with a strong-hold hairspray or other styling product. This will help keep your hair in place and prevent it from falling flat throughout the day.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get volume in very short hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing product to your roots and work it through your hair.
  3. Use a round brush to lift and volumize your hair as you blow dry it.
  4. Focus the heat on your roots and use your fingers to lift your hair as you dry it.
  5. Once your hair is dry, apply a strong-hold hairspray or other styling product.

Tips for Getting Volume in Very Short Hair

Here are some additional tips for getting volume in very short hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and fullness to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create even more volume.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add grip and hold to your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to create the illusion of more volume.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to add volume and dimension to your hair.

FAQs

Can I get volume in very short hair without using heat?

While using a blow dryer is the most effective way to create volume in very short hair, you can still create some volume without heat. Try using a volumizing mousse or powder and working it through your hair with your fingers.

What's the best way to maintain volume in very short hair throughout the day?

Using a strong-hold hairspray or other styling product can help maintain volume in very short hair throughout the day. You can also use a texturizing spray or powder to refresh your hair and add volume throughout the day.

Hair Care for Very Short Hair

When you have very short hair, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat or styling products that can damage your hair.

Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy, and experiment with different styling techniques and products to find what works best for you.

With these tips and tricks, you can create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair in very short styles. Remember to start with clean, dry hair, choose the right styling products, use a blow dryer to lift and volumize your hair, and finish with a strong hold hairspray or other styling product.
