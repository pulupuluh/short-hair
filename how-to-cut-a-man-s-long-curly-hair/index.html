How To Cut A Man s Long Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Cutting a man's long curly hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're not a professional hairstylist. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut that will leave your client feeling confident and stylish. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut a man's long curly hair, step-by-step.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Cut a Man's Long Curly Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Long Curly Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

How to Cut a Man's Long Curly Hair

The first step in cutting a man's long curly hair is to gather the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section the hair. It's also helpful to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair as you work.

Before you start cutting, it's important to discuss with your client what kind of haircut they want. Do they want to maintain their length or do they want a shorter style? Are they looking for a layered cut or a more blunt cut? Once you have a clear understanding of their preferences, you can begin the cutting process.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Section the Hair

Use the comb to section the hair into manageable sections. Start at the nape of the neck and work your way up, clipping each section as you go.

Step 2: Cut the Hair

Starting at the back of the head, take a small section of hair and comb it straight. Hold the hair between your fingers and use the scissors to trim the ends. Work your way around the head, taking small sections and cutting them to the desired length.

Step 3: Layer the Hair

If your client wants a layered cut, you can create layers by taking diagonal sections of hair and cutting them at different lengths. This will create a textured look and add volume to the hair.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

To blend the hair, take a comb and comb through the hair to make sure there are no visible lines or uneven sections. Use the scissors to trim any areas that need to be blended.

Step 5: Finish the Haircut

Once you've cut the hair to the desired length and shape, you can finish the haircut by trimming the sideburns and neckline. Use the comb to create a clean line and trim any excess hair with the scissors.

Tips for Cutting Long Curly Hair

  • Always cut curly hair when it's dry, as wet hair can shrink when it dries and result in a shorter haircut than intended.
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen the hair as you work to make it more manageable.
  • Be conservative when cutting curly hair, as it can bounce up and become shorter once it's dry.
  • Keep the scissors pointed downwards when cutting curly hair to avoid creating choppy or uneven layers.

FAQs

Can I cut curly hair with clippers?

While clippers can be used to cut curly hair, they are not recommended as they can create an uneven, choppy look. Scissors are the best tool for cutting curly hair.

How often should I trim my long curly hair?

It's recommended to trim long curly hair every 8-12 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

After cutting your client's long curly hair, it's important to advise them on how to care for their new haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to maintain the hair's natural oils.
  • Avoid using a brush on curly hair, as it can break up the curls and create frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb instead.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair to help define the curls and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on curly hair, as it can damage the hair and create frizz. Instead, let the hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on a hair dryer.

Cutting a man's long curly hair can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut that your client will love. Remember to communicate with your client about their preferences and take your time to ensure a precise cut. With these tips and tricks, you'll be on your way to becoming a pro at cutting long curly hair.
