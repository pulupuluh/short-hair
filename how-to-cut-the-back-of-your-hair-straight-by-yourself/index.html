How To Cut The Back Of Your Hair Straight By Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi May 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting the back of your hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're doing it by yourself. However, with the right tools and technique, it is possible to achieve a straight, even cut. In this article, we'll go over how to cut the back of your hair straight by yourself, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut the Back of Your Hair Straight by Yourself
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Cut the Back of Your Hair Straight by Yourself

The first step to cutting the back of your hair straight is to gather the right tools. You'll need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror.

Next, you'll want to start with clean, dry hair. It's best to cut your hair in a well-lit area, with a mirror in front of you and behind you.

Before you start cutting, decide on the length you want to trim your hair. It's best to start with a small amount and work your way up. You can always cut more if needed, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by parting your hair down the middle and combing it straight down.

2. Hold the comb at the base of your hair, with the teeth facing up. Slide the comb down to the length you want to trim your hair.

3. Use the hair cutting scissors to cut along the length of the comb. Be sure to cut straight across, using the comb as a guide.

4. Repeat this process on the other side of your head, making sure to keep your hair even on both sides.

5. Use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head, adjusting any uneven spots.

6. If needed, repeat the process until you achieve the desired length.

Tips for Cutting the Back of Your Hair

- Start with a small amount of hair and work your way up to avoid cutting too much off at once.

- Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head periodically throughout the cutting process.

- If you have curly hair, be sure to cut it while it's dry to avoid any shrinkage.

- Don't use regular scissors to cut your hair, as they can cause split ends and damage.

FAQs

Q: Can I cut my hair with kitchen scissors?

A: No, you should use hair cutting scissors for the best results. Kitchen scissors can damage your hair and cause split ends.

Q: How often should I cut the back of my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. On average, it's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Advice

After cutting the back of your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

- Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

- Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Cutting the back of your hair straight by yourself may seem intimidating at first, but with the right tools and technique, it's definitely doable. Just remember to take your time, start with a small amount of hair, and use a mirror to check your progress. Happy cutting!
