Undercut Fade Black Girl A Stunning Hairstyle For Relaxed Hair

Written by Oliver Aug 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd, then an undercut fade is the perfect choice for you. And if you're a black girl with relaxed hair, then this is the perfect hairstyle to showcase your unique style and personality. In this article, we'll take you through everything you need to know about the undercut fade black girl hairstyle, including how to get it, tips for maintaining it, and more.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get an Undercut Fade
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Undercut Fade
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for an Undercut Fade

How to Get an Undercut Fade

The first step in getting an undercut fade is to find a skilled hairstylist who has experience cutting and styling black hair. You can ask for recommendations from friends, family, or check online reviews to find the right stylist.

Once you've found a stylist, you'll need to decide on the length and style of your undercut. Do you want a short or long undercut? Do you want a fade that blends in gradually or a sharp, defined fade? These are all things to consider before your appointment.

It's recommended to bring pictures of the hairstyle you want to show your stylist. This will help them understand your vision and ensure that you get the exact hairstyle you want.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting an undercut fade:

  1. Wash and condition your hair, and blow-dry it straight.
  2. Section off the hair that you want to keep long and clip it up out of the way.
  3. Use clippers to shave the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length.
  4. Use scissors to blend the fade into the longer hair on top.
  5. Style the longer hair on top as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Undercut Fade

Once you've got your undercut fade, you'll want to take care of it to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips for maintaining your undercut fade:

  • Get regular touch-ups to maintain the shape and style of your undercut.
  • Use a high-quality hair product, such as a pomade or wax, to style your hair and hold it in place.
  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

FAQ

1. Is an undercut fade suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, an undercut fade can be customized to suit any face shape. Your stylist can help you choose the right length and style to complement your features.

2. Can I wear my hair up with an undercut fade?

Absolutely! You can still wear your hair up in a ponytail or bun with an undercut fade. In fact, it can look even more stylish and edgy when styled up.

3. How often do I need to get a touch-up for my undercut fade?

It's recommended to get a touch-up every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your undercut fade.

Hair Care Tips for an Undercut Fade

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips for an undercut fade:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling or chemical treatments, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf when outdoors.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the undercut fade black girl hairstyle, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try it out for yourself. With the right care and maintenance, your undercut fade will make you look stylish and confident every day.
