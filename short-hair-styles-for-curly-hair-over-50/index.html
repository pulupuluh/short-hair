Short Hair Styles For Curly Hair Over 50

Written by Bardi Apr 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have curly hair and are over 50, you might think that your hair styling options are limited. However, that couldn't be further from the truth! There are plenty of short hair styles that are perfect for curly hair over 50. In this article, we'll go over some of the best short hair styles for curly hair over 50, as well as provide tips on how to care for your hair and maintain your style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Curly Hair over 50
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Hair over 50

How to Style Short Curly Hair over 50

The first step in styling short curly hair over 50 is to find a cut that works for your face shape and hair texture. Some popular short hair styles for curly hair over 50 include the pixie cut, the bob, and the shag. These cuts are versatile and can be adapted to suit your individual style.

Once you have your cut, you'll want to invest in some quality styling products. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl cream, mousse, or gel. These products will help define your curls and keep them looking their best.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Curly Hair over 50

Here's a step by step guide to styling short curly hair over 50:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a curl-defining product to your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths.
  3. Using a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer, dry your hair on a low heat setting. Be sure to scrunch your curls as you go to encourage definition.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of hair oil to smooth any frizz and add shine.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair over 50

Here are some tips for maintaining your short curly hair over 50:

  • Invest in a silk pillowcase to reduce frizz and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

FAQs about Short Curly Hair over 50

What short hair styles are best for curly hair over 50?

Some popular short hair styles for curly hair over 50 include the pixie cut, the bob, and the shag.

What products should I use to style my short curly hair over 50?

Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl cream, mousse, or gel.

How often should I trim my short curly hair over 50?

It's recommended that you trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair over 50

Here are some additional hair care tips for short curly hair over 50:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized throughout the day.

With these tips and short hair styles for curly hair over 50, you'll be able to maintain healthy, stylish hair that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
