How To Know If You Have A Round Or Square Face

Written by Bardi Jun 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you struggling to determine whether your face shape is round or square? Knowing your face shape is essential when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle, makeup, and accessories that complement your features. In this article, we will guide you on how to know if you have a round or square face and provide you with some tips on how to style your hair according to your face shape.

Description

Before we dive into how to determine your face shape, let's first discuss the two most common face shapes: round and square. A round face shape is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded jawline, and a width and length that are almost equal. On the other hand, a square face shape has a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a square-shaped hairline.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

There are several ways to determine your face shape, but the easiest method is to look at your jawline and forehead. Here's how:

Step 1: Pull Your Hair Back

Pull your hair back using a hair tie or a headband so that your entire face is visible.

Step 2: Look at Your Jawline

Take a close look at your jawline. If your jawline is rounded and soft, then you have a round face shape. If it's sharp and angular, then you have a square face shape.

Step 3: Look at Your Forehead

Now, look at your forehead. If it's wider than your jawline, then you have a round face shape. If it's the same width as your jawline, then you have a square face shape.

Step by Step Guide

If you're still unsure about your face shape, follow this step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Measure Your Face

Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Write down the measurements.

Step 2: Calculate the Ratios

Divide the measurement of your cheekbones by the measurement of your forehead. If the ratio is close to 1:1, then you have a round face shape. If the ratio is greater than 1:1, then you have a square face shape.

Step 3: Look at Your Face Length

Measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. If the length is almost the same as the width, then you have a round face shape. If it's longer than the width, then you have a square face shape.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Now that you know your face shape, here are some tips on how to style your hair accordingly:

For Round Faces:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to your crown, such as a high ponytail or an updo.
  • Choose hairstyles that create the illusion of length, such as long layers or a side-swept fringe.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a bob or a blunt cut.

For Square Faces:

  • Choose hairstyles that soften your features, such as a layered cut or a side-swept fringe.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the crown, such as a messy bun or a topknot.
  • Avoid hairstyles that accentuate your square jawline, such as a blunt bob or a straight cut.

FAQs

1. Can I have a mix of round and square features?

Yes, it's possible to have a mix of round and square features. In this case, try to identify which feature dominates your face shape and choose hairstyles accordingly.

2. Do face shapes change over time?

No, your face shape remains the same throughout your life.

3. Can makeup help me change the appearance of my face shape?

No, makeup can only enhance your natural features, but it cannot change the shape of your face.

Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Regardless of your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for different face shapes:

For Round Faces:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add height to your crown.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down.

For Square Faces:

  • Avoid hairstyles that accentuate your square jawline, as this can make your face appear wider.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to soften your features.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools that can damage your hair.

Knowing your face shape is the first step to looking your best. By following these tips and tricks for styling your hair according to your face shape, you can enhance your natural features and feel confident in your appearance.
