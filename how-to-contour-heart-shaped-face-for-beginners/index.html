How To Contour Heart Shaped Face For Beginners

Written by Luffy Apr 10, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you struggling to contour your heart shaped face? Do you want to highlight your best features and achieve a flawless look? Then, this guide is perfect for you! We will share step by step instructions on how to contour your heart shaped face for beginners. Follow these tips and tricks to enhance your natural beauty and create a stunning look.

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a broad forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a tapering chin. The goal of contouring is to create balance and symmetry by highlighting your best features and minimizing the ones you want to downplay. With the right tools and techniques, you can define your face shape and achieve a more sculpted look.

How to Contour Heart Shaped Face for Beginners: Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Prep Your Skin

The first step in contouring is to prep your skin. Start by cleansing, toning, and moisturizing your face. Apply a primer to create a smooth base for your makeup. This will help your contouring products to blend seamlessly and last longer.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Next, apply your foundation. Choose a shade that matches your skin tone and apply it evenly all over your face using a brush, sponge, or your fingers. Blend it well so that there are no harsh lines or patches.

Step 3: Highlight Your Features

The next step is to highlight your best features. Apply a light-reflecting concealer or highlighter to your forehead, under your eyes, and on your cheekbones. This will create a glowing effect and draw attention to these areas.

Step 4: Contour Your Cheekbones

To contour your cheekbones, start by sucking in your cheeks to find the hollows. Apply a matte bronzer or contour powder in these areas using an angled brush. Blend it well towards your ears to create a natural-looking shadow.

Step 5: Define Your Jawline

To define your jawline, apply the same bronzer or contour powder along your jawline using a small brush. Blend it well towards your neck to create a seamless look.

Step 6: Shape Your Forehead

If you have a broad forehead, you can use contouring to shape it. Apply the same bronzer or contour powder along your hairline using a small brush. Blend it well towards your temples to create a soft shadow.

Step 7: Blend, Blend, Blend

The key to successful contouring is blending. Use a clean brush or sponge to blend all the products together. This will create a natural-looking effect and prevent any harsh lines or patches.

Tips for Contouring Heart Shaped Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when contouring your heart shaped face:

  • Choose a matte bronzer or contour powder that is two shades darker than your skin tone.
  • Use a small brush for precision and control.
  • Blend well to avoid any harsh lines or patches.
  • Apply your contouring products in natural light to avoid any mistakes.
  • Keep your hair away from your face to make it easier to apply the products.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can contouring change the shape of my face?

A: Contouring can enhance your natural features and create the illusion of a different face shape. However, it cannot permanently change your bone structure or facial features.

Q: What should I do if I make a mistake during contouring?

A: If you make a mistake, don't worry! You can use a clean brush or sponge to blend it out or remove the excess product with a makeup wipe. Start over and take your time to achieve the desired look.

Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Face

Your hair can also complement your heart shaped face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle that balances your face shape, such as a bob or a layered cut.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as they can make your forehead look wider.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle to soften your forehead and draw attention to your eyes.
  • Keep your hair away from your face to show off your features and create a sleek look.

By following these tips and tricks, you can contour your heart shaped face like a pro. Remember to practice, experiment, and have fun with your makeup. With a little patience and creativity, you can achieve a flawless look that highlights your natural beauty.
