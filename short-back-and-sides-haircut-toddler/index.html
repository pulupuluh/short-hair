Short Back And Sides Haircut For Toddlers The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline May 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a parent with a toddler, you know how difficult it can be to keep their hair looking neat and tidy. That's where the short back and sides haircut comes in! This classic style is perfect for active little ones who need a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about the short back and sides haircut for toddlers, including how to do it, tips for keeping it looking fresh, and more.

Description

The short back and sides haircut is a classic men's hairstyle that has been around for decades. It involves cutting the hair short on the back and sides of the head, while leaving a bit more length on top. This creates a neat and tidy look that is perfect for active toddlers who don't want to spend too much time on their hair.

How to

Here's how to do a short back and sides haircut for your toddler:

Step 1: Gather your tools

You'll need a few tools to get started, including a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. You may also want to have a towel or cape to help keep your child clean during the haircut.

Step 2: Start with the sides

Begin by using the clippers to cut the hair on the sides of your child's head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to keep the hair even. You may need to use scissors to trim any stray hairs that the clippers miss.

Step 3: Move to the back

Next, move to the back of your child's head and use the clippers to trim the hair there. Again, start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to keep the hair even.

Step 4: Finish with the top

Finally, use the scissors to trim the hair on top of your child's head. You can leave the hair a bit longer here, or cut it shorter if you prefer. Use the comb to keep the hair even as you cut.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when doing a short back and sides haircut for your toddler:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a spray bottle to keep the hair damp as you cut
  • Take breaks if your child gets restless
  • Be patient and take your time

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the short back and sides haircut for toddlers:

Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the short back and sides haircut can work for all hair types, including straight, curly, and wavy hair.

How often should I get my child's hair cut?

This depends on how quickly your child's hair grows. Generally, you should plan to get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Can I do this haircut myself, or should I go to a salon?

You can do this haircut yourself if you have the right tools and feel confident in your abilities. However, if you're unsure or want a professional touch, you may want to go to a salon.

Hair Care

To keep your child's hair looking great after a short back and sides haircut, be sure to:

  • Wash their hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep their hair soft and manageable
  • Brush or comb their hair daily to prevent tangles
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or detangler if necessary

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to give your toddler a stylish and low-maintenance short back and sides haircut that will keep them looking great all year round. Good luck!
