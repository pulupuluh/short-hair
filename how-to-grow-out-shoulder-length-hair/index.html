How To Grow Out Shoulder Length Hair Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of having shoulder length hair and want to grow it out? It can be a long and frustrating process, but with the right tips and tricks, you can make it a little easier. In this article, we'll discuss how to grow out shoulder length hair, step by step. From how to take care of your hair to what hairstyles to try, we've got you covered.

How to Grow Out Shoulder Length Hair

Growing out shoulder length hair can take time and patience, but with a little effort, you can achieve the length you desire. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Trim your hair regularly

It may seem counterintuitive to cut your hair when you're trying to grow it out, but regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

2. Be gentle when brushing

When brushing your hair, be gentle to avoid pulling and breaking your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles.

3. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and slow down the growth process. Try to avoid them as much as possible or use a heat protectant spray.

4. Eat a healthy diet

A healthy diet can promote hair growth. Make sure you're getting enough protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet.

5. Take hair growth supplements

There are many hair growth supplements on the market that claim to promote hair growth. Consult with your doctor before taking any supplements.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of how to grow out shoulder length hair, let's dive into a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with a fresh cut

Before you begin the growing out process, start with a fresh cut. This will get rid of any split ends and ensure that your hair is healthy.

Step 2: Be patient

Growing out your hair can take time, so be patient. It may take several months or even a year to achieve your desired length.

Step 3: Experiment with hairstyles

As your hair grows out, it may go through awkward phases. Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you. Consider getting bangs or layers to switch up your look.

Step 4: Take care of your hair

Take care of your hair by washing it regularly and using a conditioner. Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to avoid pulling and breaking your hair.

Tips for Growing Out Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you grow out your shoulder length hair:

1. Use hair accessories

Use hair accessories like headbands, clips, and scrunchies to keep your hair out of your face and add some style to your look.

2. Be gentle when towel drying

When towel drying your hair, avoid rubbing your hair vigorously. This can cause breakage and damage. Instead, gently squeeze out excess water.

3. Avoid tight hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails, as they can cause damage and breakage.

4. Protect your hair from the sun

The sun can damage your hair and slow down the growth process. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time outdoors.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more frequently. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less often.

Q: Can I use hair extensions to help grow out my hair?

A: Yes, hair extensions can help you achieve the length you desire while you're growing out your hair.

Q: Will cutting my hair make it grow faster?

A: No, cutting your hair doesn't make it grow faster. However, regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause damage. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy.

2. Use a deep conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Avoid over-processing your hair

Over-processing your hair with perms, color treatments, and relaxers can cause damage and slow down the growth process.

With these tips and tricks, you can grow out your shoulder length hair and achieve the length you desire. Remember to be patient and take care of your hair along the way.
