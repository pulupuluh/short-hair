Description

Rose gold has been a popular color in fashion and beauty for years now. This warm, pinkish hue has been seen on everything from jewelry to clothing to home decor. But is rose gold out of style in 2020?

The short answer is no. Rose gold is still a trendy and fashionable color choice. In fact, it's still being used in new and creative ways by designers and influencers alike.

How to Wear Rose Gold in 2020

One of the best things about rose gold is its versatility. It can be dressed up or down, and can be worn in a variety of ways. Here are some ideas for how to wear rose gold in 2020:

1. Jewelry

Rose gold jewelry is a classic choice that will never go out of style. Earrings, necklaces, bracelets, and rings in rose gold can add a touch of warmth and glamour to any outfit.

2. Clothing

While rose gold clothing may seem bold, it can be surprisingly easy to wear. A rose gold skirt or top can be paired with neutral pieces to create a chic and sophisticated look.

3. Makeup

Rose gold makeup is another popular trend. A shimmery rose gold eyeshadow or blush can add a touch of glamour to any makeup look.

4. Hair

Rose gold hair has been a hot trend for a while now, and it's not going away anytime soon. This pinkish-gold hair color can be subtle or bold, depending on how it's applied.

Step by Step Guide to Rocking Rose Gold in 2020

If you're new to the rose gold trend, here's a simple step-by-step guide to help you get started:

1. Start Small

If you're unsure about wearing rose gold, start small. Try a rose gold accessory, like a bracelet or necklace, to add a touch of warmth to your outfit.

2. Mix with Neutrals

Rose gold looks great when paired with neutral colors like beige, black, or white. Try wearing a rose gold top with black pants, or a rose gold skirt with a white blouse.

3. Experiment with Makeup

If you're not ready to commit to rose gold clothing or accessories, try experimenting with rose gold makeup. A shimmery rose gold eyeshadow or blush can add a touch of glamour to any makeup look.

Tips for Wearing Rose Gold in 2020

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing rose gold:

1. Don't Overdo It

While rose gold is a beautiful color, it can be overwhelming if overused. Stick to one or two rose gold pieces per outfit.

2. Mix Metals

Don't be afraid to mix rose gold with other metals, like silver or gold. This can add interest and texture to your outfit.

3. Consider Your Skin Tone

Not all shades of rose gold will look good on all skin tones. Experiment with different shades to find the one that looks best on you.

FAQs about Rose Gold in 2020

1. What Colors Go with Rose Gold?

Rose gold looks great with neutral colors like black, white, and beige. It also pairs well with other warm colors like blush and peach.

2. Can Rose Gold Be Worn Year-Round?

Yes! Rose gold is a versatile color that can be worn year-round.

3. Is Rose Gold Out of Style for Weddings?

No, rose gold is still a popular choice for weddings. It's a warm and romantic color that works well in a variety of wedding styles and themes.

Hair Care for Rose Gold Hair in 2020

If you're considering trying rose gold hair, here are some tips for keeping it looking its best:

1. Use a Color-Safe Shampoo

Use a shampoo that's designed for color-treated hair to help preserve your rose gold color.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause your color to fade. Try air-drying your hair whenever possible.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look dull and damaged.

In conclusion, rose gold is still a fashionable and trendy color choice in 2020. Whether you're wearing it in your jewelry, clothing, makeup, or hair, there are plenty of ways to incorporate this warm and romantic color into your look.
