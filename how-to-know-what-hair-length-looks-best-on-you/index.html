How To Know What Hair Length Looks Best On You

Written by Daniel May 21, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of constantly changing your hair length, only to be disappointed with the result? Don't worry, finding the perfect hair length can be a daunting task for many people. In this article, we will guide you on how to know what hair length looks best on you.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

One of the most important aspects of choosing a hair length is to consider your face shape. Different face shapes have different hair length requirements. For instance, those with round faces may want to avoid short haircuts, while those with long faces may want to avoid long haircuts. Additionally, your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style will also play a role in determining the best hair length for you.

How to

Here are some tips on how to know what hair length looks best on you:

Consider Your Face Shape

As mentioned earlier, your face shape is an important factor in determining the best hair length for you. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have a round face, avoid short haircuts as they can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for longer haircuts that fall below the chin.
  • If you have a square face, avoid haircuts that end at your jawline as they can make your face appear boxy. Instead, opt for haircuts that fall just below your jawline.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, avoid haircuts that are too short or too long. Instead, opt for haircuts that fall just below your shoulders.
  • If you have an oval face, you're in luck! Almost any hair length will suit you.

Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type is another important factor to consider when choosing a hair length. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have thin hair, avoid long haircuts as they can make your hair appear even thinner. Instead, opt for shorter haircuts that add volume and texture.
  • If you have curly hair, avoid short haircuts as they can make your hair appear frizzy. Instead, opt for longer haircuts that allow your curls to shine.
  • If you have straight hair, almost any hair length will suit you. However, shorter haircuts may require more styling to maintain their shape.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to determine the best hair length for you:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair type.
  3. Think about your lifestyle and personal style. Do you have the time and energy to style your hair every day?
  4. Look for inspiration. Browse magazines and online galleries for hairstyles that match your face shape and hair type.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can give you personalized advice on the best hair length for you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair length:

  • Consider your skin tone. Certain hair lengths may complement your skin tone better than others.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment! Hair grows back, so you can always try a new hair length if you're not happy with the result.
  • Take care of your hair. No matter what hair length you choose, make sure to maintain it with regular haircuts, conditioning treatments, and styling products.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to know what hair length looks best on you:

What is the most flattering hair length?

The most flattering hair length depends on your face shape, hair type, and personal style. However, haircuts that fall just below the chin or just below the shoulders are generally considered flattering for most people.

Will short hair make my face look rounder?

Short haircuts can make a round face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for longer haircuts that fall below the chin to elongate your face.

Hair Care

No matter what hair length you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use a conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

In conclusion, finding the perfect hair length can be a challenge, but by considering your face shape, hair type, and personal style, you can narrow down your options. Remember to consult with a hairstylist for personalized advice, and don't be afraid to experiment with different hair lengths until you find the one that looks best on you!
