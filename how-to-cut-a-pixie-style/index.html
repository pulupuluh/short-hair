How To Cut A Pixie Style

Written by Christine Apr 01, 2023 · 5 min read

Cutting your hair short can be a daunting task, but a pixie cut is a great way to change up your look and embrace a new style. In this guide, we will walk you through how to cut a pixie style in relaxed English language. We’ll cover everything from the tools you’ll need, to the step-by-step process, to hair care tips for maintaining your new cut.

Table of Contents

  1. Tools You’ll Need
  2. How to Cut a Pixie Style
  3. Step-by-Step Process
  4. Tips for Cutting a Pixie Style
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Tips for a Pixie Cut

Tools You’ll Need

Before starting, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Sectioning clips
  • Water spray bottle

How to Cut a Pixie Style

The first step is to choose the right pixie style for your face shape and hair texture. It’s important to consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a pixie cut. Consult with a hair stylist if you’re unsure which style is best for you.

Step-by-Step Process

1. Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: the top, both sides, and the back. Use sectioning clips to keep each section separate.

2. Begin cutting the back section first. Use clippers to create a short, tapered cut. Start at the nape of the neck and work your way up.

3. Move on to the sides. Use scissors to create a textured, layered look. Start at the top of the ear and work your way down.

4. Cut the top section last. Use scissors to create a choppy, layered look. Start at the crown of the head and work your way forward.

5. Blend the sections together by using scissors to create a seamless transition between the layers.

6. Use clippers to clean up the nape of the neck and around the ears.

Tips for Cutting a Pixie Style

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use sharp scissors and clippers to avoid uneven cuts.
  • Work in small sections to ensure precision.
  • Don’t be afraid to take your time and make small adjustments as you go.

FAQ

Can I cut a pixie style myself?

We recommend consulting with a hair stylist if you’re unsure how to cut a pixie style. However, with the right tools and techniques, it is possible to cut a pixie style yourself.

How often do I need to cut my pixie style?

It’s recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your pixie cut.

Hair Care Tips for a Pixie Cut

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and nourishment to your hair.

Cutting a pixie style can be a fun and refreshing change to your look. By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut at home. Remember to take your time, work in small sections, and consult with a stylist if you’re unsure which style is best for you.
