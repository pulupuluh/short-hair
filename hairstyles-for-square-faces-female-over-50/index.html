Hairstyles For Square Faces Female Over 50

Written by Aprilia May 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a woman with a square face who is over 50? Do you find it difficult to choose the right hairstyle that suits your face shape and age? You have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss hairstyles that are perfect for square faces female over 50. So, let's dive in.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A square face has a strong jawline and a broad forehead. The length and width of the face are almost equal. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for a square face is to soften the strong features and create a balance. Women over 50 have unique hair needs, such as thinning hair, gray hair, and hair damage due to age. Therefore, the right hairstyle can make a significant difference in the overall appearance.

How to

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for a square face female over 50 is to determine the length of the hair. Short hairstyles are perfect for square faces as they create a balance with the strong jawline. However, long hairstyles are also an option as long as they have layers that soften the jawline. The next step is to choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the crown area and creates an illusion of length. This can be achieved by adding layers, curls, or waves. Lastly, avoid hairstyles that accentuate the squareness of the face, such as straight hair with blunt bangs.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine the length of hair
Step 2: Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the crown area
Step 3: Add layers, curls, or waves to create an illusion of length
Step 4: Avoid hairstyles that accentuate the squareness of the face

Tips

Tip 1: Short hairstyles such as pixie cuts, bobs, and layered hairstyles are perfect for square faces female over 50.
Tip 2: Long hairstyles with layers that soften the jawline are also a good option.
Tip 3: Avoid hairstyles with blunt bangs or straight hair that accentuate the squareness of the face.
Tip 4: Add volume to the crown area to create an illusion of length.
Tip 5: Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and age, such as volumizing mousse, hair serum, and hair spray.

FAQ

Q: Can women with square faces over 50 wear long hair?
A: Yes, as long as the hairstyle has layers that soften the jawline and create a balance.

Q: What are the best short hairstyles for square faces female over 50?
A: Pixie cuts, bobs, and layered hairstyles are perfect for square faces female over 50.

Q: How do I add volume to the crown area?
A: Use volumizing mousse or hair spray, and blow-dry the hair upside down.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy and beautiful hair, follow these hair care tips:
Tip 1: Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
Tip 2: Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and age.
Tip 3: Avoid using heat styling tools frequently.
Tip 4: Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
Tip 5: Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

Hairstyles for square faces female over 50 can be stylish, trendy, and age-appropriate. By following the tips and recommendations in this article, you can choose the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape and age. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right hair products to maintain healthy and beautiful hair.
