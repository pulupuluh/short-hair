How To Style Coarse Curly Hair Male A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 11, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Coarse curly hair can be a challenge to style, especially for men. It requires a lot of time and effort to get the desired look. But with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and manageable hairstyle that suits your personality. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style coarse curly hair for men. We will also share some tips and FAQs to help you take care of your hair. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Prepare Your Hair
  • Step 2: Choose the Right Product
  • Step 3: Apply the Product
  • Step 4: Style Your Hair
  • Tips for Styling Coarse Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Coarse Curly Hair

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

The first step in styling coarse curly hair is to prepare it properly. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to soften and hydrate your curls, making them easier to style. After washing, gently pat your hair dry with a towel. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out the excess water.

Step 2: Choose the Right Product

Choosing the right product is crucial for styling coarse curly hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl creams, gels, and mousses. These products will help to define your curls and reduce frizz. Avoid products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and cause it to become brittle. If you have thick and coarse hair, opt for a heavier product like a cream or butter. If your hair is finer, try a lighter product like a mousse or gel.

Step 3: Apply the Product

Once you have chosen the right product, it's time to apply it to your hair. Start by taking a small amount of product and rubbing it between your palms. Then, apply the product to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the tips. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair. Be careful not to use too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now that you have applied the product, it's time to style your hair. There are different ways to style coarse curly hair depending on your preferences. You can leave your hair loose for a more natural look, or you can create a defined and structured hairstyle. To create a defined look, use a comb or your fingers to shape your curls. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. If you prefer a more structured hairstyle, try using hair clips or bobby pins to create a defined part or shape. Remember to be gentle when styling your hair to avoid breakage.

Tips for Styling Coarse Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your coarse curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before applying product.
  • Don't use a brush on dry curly hair, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

FAQs

Q. How often should I wash my coarse curly hair?

A. It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you can wash your hair once or twice a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair more frequently. If you exercise regularly or live in a hot and humid climate, you may need to wash your hair more often to prevent sweat and dirt buildup.

Q. Can I straighten my coarse curly hair?

A. Yes, you can straighten your hair using a flat iron or a blow dryer with a round brush attachment. However, be careful not to use too much heat or straighten your hair too often, as this can damage your curls and cause breakage.

Q. How can I prevent frizz in my coarse curly hair?

A. To prevent frizz, avoid using hot tools too often, as they can dry out your hair and cause frizz. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls. Use a leave-in conditioner or anti-frizz serum to tame frizz and flyaways.

Hair Care Tips for Coarse Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you take care of your coarse curly hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can dry out your hair and cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

Styling coarse curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and manageable hairstyle. Remember to be gentle when styling your hair and take care of it regularly to keep it healthy and vibrant. We hope this guide has been helpful for you. If you have any questions or tips to share, feel free to leave a comment below!
