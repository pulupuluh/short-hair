What Glasses Suit A Heart Shaped Face

Written by Christine Jul 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you may be wondering what glasses will flatter your features. Heart shaped faces are characterized by a broad forehead and cheekbones that taper to a narrow chin. The key to finding the right glasses is to balance out the proportions of your face. In this blog post, we'll go over some tips for choosing glasses that will complement your heart shaped face.

Table of Contents

  • Shape Matters
  • Frame Styles
  • Colors that Pop
  • Hair Care
  • FAQ

Shape Matters

The shape of your glasses can make a big difference in how they look on your face. For heart shaped faces, it's best to go with frames that are wider at the top and narrow at the bottom. This will help balance out the width of your forehead and draw attention away from your chin. Cat-eye frames are a great option for heart shaped faces, as they have a similar shape to the face and will accentuate the cheekbones.

Another option is to go for oval frames, which will soften the angles of the face and create a more balanced look. Round frames can also work well, as long as they are not too small or too large. The key is to find frames that are proportionate to your face and complement your features.

Frame Styles

When it comes to frame styles, there are a few options that work particularly well for heart shaped faces. Rimless frames are a great choice, as they won't add any bulk to the face and will draw attention to the eyes. Another option is to go for frames with a low bridge, which will help balance out the proportions of the face. Browline frames can also work well, as they draw attention to the top of the face and away from the chin.

Colors that Pop

When it comes to choosing the color of your frames, there are a few things to keep in mind. Bright colors can work well on heart shaped faces, as they draw attention to the top of the face and away from the chin. Tortoiseshell frames are also a classic option that can work well, as they add warmth and depth to the face. Metallic frames can also be a good choice, as they add a touch of glamour and sophistication.

Hair Care

When choosing glasses for a heart shaped face, it's important to consider your hairstyle as well. Longer hairstyles can help balance out the proportions of the face, while shorter hairstyles can accentuate the chin. Side-swept bangs can be a good option, as they draw attention to the eyes and balance out the forehead. Updos can also work well, as they draw the eye up and away from the chin.

FAQ

What glasses should I avoid if I have a heart shaped face?

Avoid glasses with heavy, thick frames, as these can overwhelm the delicate features of a heart shaped face. Also avoid frames that are too small or too narrow, as these can make the face appear wider at the bottom.

Can I wear oversized glasses with a heart shaped face?

Yes, you can wear oversized glasses with a heart shaped face, as long as they are not too wide at the bottom. Look for frames that are wider at the top and taper to a narrower bottom.

Do I have to stick to neutral colors with a heart shaped face?

No, you can experiment with different colors and patterns when choosing glasses for a heart shaped face. Just make sure the frames are proportional to your face and complement your features.

Conclusion

When it comes to choosing glasses for a heart shaped face, the key is to find frames that balance out the proportions of the face and complement your features. Cat-eye frames, oval frames, and rimless frames are all great options, as are bright colors and tortoiseshell patterns. With a little experimentation, you're sure to find the perfect glasses for your heart shaped face.
