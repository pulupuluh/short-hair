How To Fix Frizzy Hair On Top Of Head

Written by Christine Mar 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Do you have frizzy hair on the top of your head that just won't seem to behave, no matter what you do? If so, you're not alone. Frizz is a common hair problem that can be caused by a variety of factors, including humidity, damage, and genetics. Fortunately, there are several things you can do to tame frizzy hair on the top of your head and achieve smooth, glossy locks. In this article, we'll show you how to fix frizzy hair on top of head with some easy tips and tricks.

Description

Frizzy hair can be frustrating, especially when it's on the top of your head where it's most noticeable. Frizz can make your hair look dull, dry, and unruly, and it can be difficult to style. But don't worry, there are several ways to fix frizzy hair on top of head and achieve smooth, silky locks.

How to Fix Frizzy Hair on Top of Head: Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

The first step to fixing frizzy hair on top of head is to use the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed to tame frizz and provide moisture to your hair. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals, such as sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils and make frizz worse.

Step 2: Apply a Hair Mask

Once a week, apply a deep conditioning hair mask to your hair. This will help to hydrate and nourish your hair, which will reduce frizz and make your hair softer and more manageable. Look for a hair mask that contains natural ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, or argan oil.

Step 3: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

After washing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and damage, which can lead to frizz. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up, gently combing through any tangles or knots.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair with a Diffuser

If you need to blow dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will help to distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz. Start by drying the roots of your hair, then work your way down to the ends. Use a low heat setting and avoid overheating your hair, which can cause damage and make frizz worse.

Step 5: Apply a Hair Serum

After blow drying your hair, apply a hair serum to the ends of your hair. This will help to smooth down any flyaways and add shine to your hair. Look for a serum that contains natural oils, like argan oil or jojoba oil, which will provide moisture to your hair and reduce frizz.

Tips for Fixing Frizzy Hair on Top of Head

Here are some additional tips for fixing frizzy hair on top of head:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools, like curling irons or straighteners, which can damage your hair and make frizz worse.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase, which will help to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Wear a hat or headscarf on humid days to protect your hair from moisture.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends, which can lead to frizz.

FAQs

Q: Can frizzy hair on top of head be genetic?

A: Yes, frizzy hair can be genetic. If your parents have frizzy hair, you may be more likely to have it too. However, environmental factors like humidity and damage can also contribute to frizz.

Q: Will using a hair oil reduce frizz on top of my head?

A: Yes, using a hair oil can help to reduce frizz on top of your head. Look for a hair oil that contains natural ingredients, like argan oil or coconut oil, which will provide moisture to your hair and help to smooth down any flyaways.

Hair Care for Frizzy Hair on Top of Head

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for hair care for frizzy hair on top of head:

  • Wash your hair no more than every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, which can damage your hair and make frizz worse.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, which can cause breakage and damage.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, which can dry out your hair and make frizz worse.

By following these tips and tricks, you can fix frizzy hair on top of head and achieve smooth, silky locks that will make you look and feel your best.
