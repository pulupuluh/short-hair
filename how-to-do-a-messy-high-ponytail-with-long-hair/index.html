How To Do A Messy High Ponytail With Long Hair

Written by Robby Sep 10, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’re looking for a quick and easy hairstyle that still looks stylish, then a messy high ponytail with long hair is a perfect choice. This hairstyle is versatile and can be worn for any occasion, whether you’re going to work, school or a night out with friends. In this post, we’ll show you how to create a messy high ponytail with long hair, step by step. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

To create a messy high ponytail with long hair, you’ll need a hairbrush, hair ties, bobby pins, and texturizing spray (optional). This hairstyle works best on second-day hair that hasn’t been washed recently. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles and knots. Make sure your hair is smooth and free of any bumps or lumps.


Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Next, gather all your hair at the crown of your head. Use your hands to create a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.


Step 3: Create a Messy Look

Now, it’s time to create the messy look. To do this, simply use your fingers to gently pull out a few strands of hair around your face. You can also use texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.


Step 4: Secure the Ponytail

Once you’re happy with the messy look, use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair around the base of the ponytail. This will help to keep your hair in place and prevent it from falling out of the hair tie.


Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish off your messy high ponytail with a spritz of hairspray. This will help to keep your hair in place and prevent it from falling out of the hair tie.


Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating a messy high ponytail with long hair:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • If you have thin hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add more texture to your hair.
  • To make your ponytail look even messier, use a teasing brush to create some volume at the crown of your head before gathering your hair into a ponytail.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about messy high ponytails with long hair:

Q: Will this hairstyle work on short hair?

A: No, this hairstyle works best on long hair that can be gathered into a ponytail.

Q: Can I wear this hairstyle to a formal event?

A: Yes, you can dress up a messy high ponytail with long hair by adding some sparkly hair accessories or a fancy hair tie.

Q: How do I prevent my hair tie from slipping out of my hair?

A: To prevent your hair tie from slipping out of your hair, try wrapping the hair tie around your ponytail a few times before securing it in place.


Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly. Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair with hot tools, and avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

By following these simple steps and tips, you can create a messy high ponytail with long hair that looks stylish and effortless. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
