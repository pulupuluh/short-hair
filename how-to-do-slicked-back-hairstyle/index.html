How To Do Slicked Back Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Are you looking for a sleek and polished hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion? Look no further than the classic slicked back hairstyle. Not only is it easy to achieve, but it's also a timeless look that never goes out of style. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to do slicked back hairstyle, including tips and hair care advice.

How to Do Slicked Back Hairstyle

To achieve the slicked back hairstyle, you'll need a few key products, including a strong hold pomade or gel, a comb, and a hair dryer.

Products You'll Need

  • Strong hold pomade or gel
  • Comb
  • Hair dryer

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or gel to your hands and work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends
  3. Using a comb, begin to slick your hair back, starting at the front and working your way to the back of your head
  4. Use a hair dryer on low heat to set the style in place
  5. Finish by adding a little more pomade or gel to any flyaways or areas that need extra hold

Tips for Perfecting Your Slicked Back Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you master the slicked back hairstyle:

  • Use a strong hold pomade or gel for best results
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different parting styles
  • Use a comb with fine teeth to create a sleek, polished look
  • If you have thick or curly hair, consider using a hair straightener before applying pomade or gel
  • To add texture and volume, consider using a sea salt spray

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the slicked back hairstyle:

How long does the slicked back hairstyle last?

With the right products and technique, the slicked back hairstyle can last all day.

Can I achieve the slicked back hairstyle with short hair?

Yes, the slicked back hairstyle can be achieved with short hair. However, you may need to use more pomade or gel to create the desired hold.

Is the slicked back hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the slicked back hairstyle is versatile and can be adapted to suit most face shapes.

Hair Care Tips for Slicked Back Hairstyle

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy
  • Take regular breaks from styling your hair, and let it air dry whenever possible
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate hair growth
  • Consider investing in a silk pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage

With these tips and techniques, you can easily achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle. Whether you're getting ready for a night out or just want to look polished and put together, this classic style is sure to impress.
