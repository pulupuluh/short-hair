How To Side Cut Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Description

Side swept bangs are a great way to change up your hairstyle without committing to a drastic cut. This type of bangs can be customized to suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Side cut bangs add volume and movement to your hair, making it look more dynamic and chic. Learning how to side cut bangs is a simple process that can be done at home with the right tools and techniques.

How to Side Cut Bangs

Side cut bangs are a trendy and edgy look that can be achieved with a few simple steps. Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean, dry, and detangled. You will need a sharp pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these easy steps to side cut your bangs:

  1. Part your hair down the middle and separate the section you want to cut into a triangle shape.
  2. Comb the section forward and hold it between your index and middle fingers.
  3. Hold the scissors vertically and make a small cut at a 45-degree angle near the bottom of the section.
  4. Gradually snip your way up the section, cutting at a slight angle and taking small sections at a time.
  5. Stop cutting when you reach your desired length or when you have cut all the way up to the crown of your head.
  6. Repeat on the other side, making sure to match the length and angle of the first side.
  7. Blend the bangs into the rest of your hair by lightly snipping into the ends with the scissors held horizontally.
  8. Check the length and shape of your side bangs in the mirror and make any additional cuts if necessary.

Tips for Cutting Side Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting side swept bangs:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if desired.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends and jagged edges.
  • Take small sections at a time to ensure evenness and control.
  • Cut at a slight angle for a softer look.
  • Don't cut too short or too high up, as this can make your bangs look choppy or uneven.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about side swept bangs:

  • What face shape is best for side swept bangs? Side swept bangs can work for any face shape, but they are especially flattering for round, oval, and heart-shaped faces.
  • How often should I trim my bangs? It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.
  • Can I cut my own side swept bangs? Yes, you can cut your own side swept bangs at home as long as you have the right tools and follow the proper techniques.

Hair Care Tips for Side Bangs

Once you have side swept bangs, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips for side bangs:

  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to smooth them out and add volume.
  • Use a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, to control flyaways and add texture.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them oily and flat.
  • Wash your bangs separately from the rest of your hair to avoid tangling and damage.
  • Consider using dry shampoo in between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh and voluminous.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently side cut your own bangs at home and rock this stylish look. Remember to take your time, have patience, and trust your instincts when it comes to cutting your own hair. And if you're ever unsure or need professional guidance, don't hesitate to visit a salon for a trim or consultation.
