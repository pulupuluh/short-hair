Who Looks Good With Curtain Bangs

Written by Petter Apr 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering getting curtain bangs but aren't sure if they'll suit your face shape or hair type? Look no further! In this article, we'll break down who looks good with curtain bangs and provide you with tips on how to style and care for them.

Table of Contents

  • Face Shape
  • Hair Type
  • How to Style
  • Hair Care
  • FAQ

Face Shape

When it comes to curtain bangs, they tend to suit most face shapes. However, if you have a round face, it's best to avoid blunt bangs and opt for curtain bangs instead. The soft, wispy layers will help elongate your face and create a more flattering look.

For those with oval or heart-shaped faces, curtain bangs are a great option as they can help balance out features and add dimension to the hair. If you have a square-shaped face, opt for longer curtain bangs that hit at the cheekbones to soften the angles of your face.

Overall, curtain bangs are a versatile style that can work for a variety of face shapes. It's all about finding the right length and shape to suit your unique features.

Hair Type

Curtain bangs work best on hair that is medium to long in length and has a slight wave or curl to it. If you have straight hair, you may need to add some texture to your bangs with a curling iron or styling product to achieve the perfect curtain effect.

If you have thick hair, consider getting your curtain bangs thinned out by a stylist to prevent them from looking too bulky. On the other hand, if you have fine hair, avoid getting your bangs too thin as this can make them look sparse and uneven.

How to Style

Styling curtain bangs is relatively easy and can be done with just a few products and tools. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then blow-dry it using a round brush to create volume and texture.

Next, use a curling iron or straightener to style your bangs, depending on the look you're going for. For a soft, wispy effect, use a curling iron to create loose waves in your bangs. If you prefer a sleek look, use a straightener to straighten your bangs and create a more polished finish.

Finish off your look with a light hairspray or texturizing spray to hold your bangs in place throughout the day.

Hair Care

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes regularly washing and conditioning your hair, as well as using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your locks healthy and hydrated.

Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage over time. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Finally, get regular trims to keep your curtain bangs looking fresh and prevent split ends from forming.

FAQ

Can I get curtain bangs if I have short hair?

Curtain bangs work best on medium to long hair, but if you have short hair, you can still achieve a similar look by opting for side-swept bangs instead.

Will curtain bangs work on curly hair?

Yes, curtain bangs can look great on curly hair! Just be sure to work with a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair to ensure that your bangs are cut in a way that complements your natural curls.

How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

It's best to get your curtain bangs trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best and prevent split ends from forming.

So there you have it, folks! Curtain bangs are a versatile and trendy style that can work for a variety of face shapes and hair types. Whether you prefer a soft, wispy effect or a more polished look, there's a curtain bang style out there for you. Just be sure to take good care of your hair and get regular trims to keep your bangs looking fresh and healthy.
