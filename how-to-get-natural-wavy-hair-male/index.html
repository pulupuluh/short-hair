How To Get Natural Wavy Hair Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 18, 2023 · 6 min read

Do you want to achieve natural wavy hair as a male? Well, you're in luck because in this article, we'll be sharing with you some tips and tricks on how to achieve those effortless waves. From hair care to styling, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  1. Hair Care
  2. How to Get Natural Wavy Hair Male
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Hair Care

Before we dive into how to style your hair to get those natural waves, let's talk about hair care. The foundation of any good hairstyle is healthy hair. Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair 2-3 times a week with a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Use conditioner every time you wash your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers and straighteners
  • Limit your use of hair products
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends

By following these hair care tips, you'll have a good base to start styling your hair into those natural waves.

How to Get Natural Wavy Hair Male

Now that you've taken care of your hair, it's time to start styling it. Here are some ways to get natural wavy hair as a male:

1. Salt Spray

Salt spray is a popular product among men who want to achieve natural beachy waves. Simply spray it on damp hair, scrunch it up, and let it air dry. This will give you those effortless waves that look like you just came from the beach.

2. Curl Enhancing Products

If you have naturally curly hair, you can enhance your curls by using curl enhancing products. Look for products that contain ingredients such as shea butter and coconut oil to keep your hair moisturized and defined.

3. Diffuser Attachment

If you have a hairdryer, you can use a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves. Simply attach the diffuser to your hairdryer and use it on a low heat setting. This will help to define your waves without causing frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get natural wavy hair male:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp
  3. Spray salt spray or curl enhancing product onto your hair
  4. Scrunch your hair up using your hands
  5. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on a low heat setting

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve natural wavy hair:

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type
  • Don't brush your hair after it's dry to avoid frizz
  • Use a microfiber towel or old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel
  • Avoid touching your hair too much once it's dry to prevent frizz

FAQ

Can I achieve natural wavy hair if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can achieve natural wavy hair even if you have straight hair. By using salt spray or curl enhancing products, you can add texture and definition to your hair.

Do I need to use a diffuser attachment?

No, you don't need to use a diffuser attachment to achieve natural wavy hair, but it can help to enhance your waves without causing frizz.

Can I use hair products every day?

It's best to limit your use of hair products to avoid buildup, but if you need to use them every day, make sure to use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup.

Hair Care

Remember, the key to achieving natural wavy hair is to take care of your hair and experiment with different styling techniques and products. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock those effortless waves in no time.
