Short Hairstyles For Wavy Hair Over 50

Written by Petter May 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to refresh your look? Short hairstyles for wavy hair over 50 can be a great option for those who are looking for a low maintenance but stylish haircut. In this article, we will give you some tips on how to choose the right short hairstyle for your wavy hair and provide you with some examples of popular styles.

Description

Wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. It has a natural texture that can add volume and movement to your hair, but it can also be hard to manage. Short hairstyles for wavy hair over 50 can help you to embrace your natural texture while keeping your hair looking chic and modern. These hairstyles are easy to style and require minimal maintenance, making them perfect for busy women who still want to look their best.

How-To

Before choosing a short hairstyle for your wavy hair, it is important to consider your face shape and hair texture. Short hairstyles can be flattering for almost any face shape, but it is important to choose a style that complements your features. If you have a round face, a short pixie cut can help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. If you have a square face, a short bob with soft layers can help to soften your features.

When it comes to hair texture, wavy hair can be a great base for many short hairstyles. If you have thick and coarse hair, a short layered bob can help to add movement and texture to your hair. If you have fine hair, a short pixie cut can help to add volume and height to your hair.

Step by Step

1. Choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair texture.

2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your hair.

3. Use a styling product, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to enhance your natural waves.

4. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to help define your waves.

5. Use a curling iron or wand to add more definition to your waves if needed.

Tips

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

3. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.

4. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What short hairstyles are best for wavy hair over 50?

Short layered bobs, pixie cuts, and shaggy hairstyles are all great options for wavy hair over 50.

How do I style my short wavy hair?

Use a styling product to enhance your natural waves, then blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. Use a curling iron or wand to add more definition if needed.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It is recommended to get your hair trimmed every six to eight weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your short wavy hair, it is important to use gentle products that won't dry out your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to give your hair an extra boost of hydration. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Short hairstyles for wavy hair over 50 can be a great way to embrace your natural texture while keeping your hair looking chic and modern. By choosing a style that complements your face shape and hair texture, and by following a few simple styling and hair care tips, you can have a stylish and low maintenance hairstyle that will make you look and feel your best.
