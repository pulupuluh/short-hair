What Hat Suits A Round Face

Written by Christine Feb 28, 2023 · 10 min read

Are you wondering what hat suits a round face? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll guide you on how to choose the perfect hat that complements your face shape. We’ll also provide tips on how to take care of your hair while wearing a hat. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • What Hat Suits A Round Face?
  • How to Choose the Right Hat
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips on Wearing Hats for Round-Faced People
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Choose the Right Hat

Choosing the right hat for your round face is all about finding the right balance. You want to avoid hats that make your face look even rounder, but you also don’t want a hat that’s too tight or too loose.

The first step is to determine your face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and look at the shape of your face. If your face is widest at the cheeks and tapers towards the chin and forehead, you have a round face.

Once you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to start looking for hats that complement your features. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Look for hats with height

Hats with height, such as fedoras and top hats, can help elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer profile.

2. Choose hats with a wide brim

Hats with a wide brim, such as floppy hats and sun hats, can help balance out your round face and create a more proportional look.

3. Avoid hats with a round shape

Hats with a round shape, such as bowler hats and beanies, can make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for hats with a more angular shape, such as fedoras and cowboy hats.

4. Consider the size of the hat

A hat that’s too big or too small can make your face look rounder. Make sure to choose a hat that fits snugly on your head without being too tight or too loose.

5. Experiment with different styles

Don’t be afraid to try on different hat styles to see what works best for you. You may be surprised by what looks good!

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to choose the perfect hat for your round face:

1. Determine your face shape

Stand in front of a mirror and determine your face shape. If your face is widest at the cheeks and tapers towards the chin and forehead, you have a round face.

2. Decide on the style of hat you want to wear

Do you want a hat with height or a wide brim? Do you prefer a more angular shape or a rounder shape? Decide on the style of hat that you want to wear.

3. Try on different hats

Try on different hats in the style that you’ve chosen. Make sure to choose hats that fit snugly on your head without being too tight or too loose.

4. Look for the perfect balance

Look for a hat that creates the perfect balance for your face shape. The hat should elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer profile, but it shouldn’t make your face look even rounder.

5. Consider the occasion

Consider the occasion that you’ll be wearing the hat for. A floppy hat may be perfect for the beach, but a fedora may be more appropriate for a night out.

Tips on Wearing Hats for Round-Faced People

Here are some tips on how to wear hats for round-faced people:

1. Wear your hat at a slight angle

Wearing your hat at a slight angle can help elongate your face and create a more flattering look.

2. Avoid wearing your hat too far down on your forehead

Wearing your hat too far down on your forehead can make your face look even rounder.

3. Consider your hairstyle

Consider your hairstyle when choosing a hat. If you have short hair, a hat with height may be more flattering. If you have long hair, a hat with a wide brim may be more appropriate.

4. Keep it simple

When it comes to hats, less is often more. Keep your outfit and accessories simple to let your hat be the focal point.

FAQ

What type of hat should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hats with a round shape, such as bowler hats and beanies. These hats can make your face look even rounder.

Can I wear a baseball cap if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear a baseball cap if you have a round face. Just make sure to wear it at a slight angle to help elongate your face.

How can I make my face look slimmer when wearing a hat?

Wearing a hat with height, such as a fedora or top hat, can help elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer profile.

Hair Care Tips

Wearing a hat can sometimes cause damage to your hair. Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair while wearing a hat:

1. Use a leave-in conditioner

Using a leave-in conditioner can help protect your hair from the friction caused by wearing a hat.

2. Avoid wearing a hat for extended periods of time

Avoid wearing a hat for extended periods of time to prevent sweat from building up on your scalp.

3. Choose the right hat material

Choose a hat made from a breathable material, such as cotton or straw, to prevent your scalp from becoming too hot and sweaty.

Now that you know what hat suits a round face, you can confidently choose the perfect hat for any occasion. Remember to keep these tips in mind and experiment with different styles to find the perfect hat that complements your features. Happy hat shopping!
