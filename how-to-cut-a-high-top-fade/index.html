How To Cut A High Top Fade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine May 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut that's trendy and stylish, a high top fade might be just what you need. The high top fade is a popular haircut that has been around for decades, and it's still going strong today. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut a high top fade step by step, so you can achieve the look you want.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut a High Top Fade
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting a High Top Fade
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for High Top Fade

How to Cut a High Top Fade

A high top fade is a short haircut that has longer hair on top. The sides and back are shaved or cut very short, while the top is left longer. The hair on top is usually styled into a flat top or a box shape. To cut a high top fade, you'll need a few tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape
  • Haircutting spray bottle

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut a high top fade:

  1. Wash and dry the hair. Make sure the hair is clean and dry before you start cutting.
  2. Use clippers to cut the sides and back. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you get the desired length. Use the comb to blend the hair on the sides and back.
  3. Cut the hair on top. Use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. Make sure the hair is even and straight.
  4. Create the box shape. Use the clippers to create the box shape on top. Start at the front and work your way back.
  5. Blend the sides and top. Use the comb to blend the sides and top together. Make sure there are no harsh lines.
  6. Trim the hairline. Use scissors to trim the hairline around the ears and neck. Make sure it's even and clean.
  7. Style the hair. Use a comb and hair product to style the hair on top.

Tips for Cutting a High Top Fade

Here are some tips to help you cut a perfect high top fade:

  • Use a mirror to check your work as you go.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting a high top fade takes time and practice.
  • Keep the hair damp with the spray bottle to make it easier to cut.

FAQs

How long does a high top fade last?

A high top fade can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks, depending on how fast the hair grows.

Can I cut a high top fade at home?

Yes, you can cut a high top fade at home, but it's recommended to seek the help of a professional if you're not experienced in cutting hair.

What products should I use to style a high top fade?

You can use hair gel, pomade, or hair wax to style a high top fade.

Hair Care for High Top Fade

To keep your high top fade looking fresh, you should:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply hair product to keep the style in place.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape.

Now that you know how to cut a high top fade, you can try it out for yourself. Remember to take your time, be patient, and don't be afraid to seek the help of a professional if you need it. With practice and the right tools, you can achieve a stylish and trendy high top fade.
