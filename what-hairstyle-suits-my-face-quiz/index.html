What Hairstyle Suits My Face Quiz Discover Your Perfect Look

Written by Bardi Aug 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle but don't know where to start? Taking a "what hairstyle suits my face quiz" can be a helpful tool in determining the best haircut for your features. In this article, we'll go over how to take the quiz, step by step instructions, tips, and hair care to maintain your new look. Let's get started!

How to Take the Quiz

Taking a "what hairstyle suits my face quiz" is easy and can be done in just a few minutes. There are several online quizzes available that ask a series of questions to determine the best haircut for your face shape. Some popular quizzes include:

  • The HairCode Quiz
  • The InStyle Hairstyle Finder
  • The Good Housekeeping Virtual Hairstyle Try-On

The HairCode Quiz

The HairCode Quiz is a comprehensive quiz that asks questions about your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to determine the best haircut for you. It takes about 10 minutes to complete and provides personalized recommendations for both cut and color.

The InStyle Hairstyle Finder

The InStyle Hairstyle Finder asks questions about your hair type, texture, and face shape to provide personalized hairstyle recommendations. It also includes a virtual try-on feature so you can see yourself with different haircuts before committing.

The Good Housekeeping Virtual Hairstyle Try-On

The Good Housekeeping Virtual Hairstyle Try-On allows you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles virtually. It also includes a quiz to help determine your face shape and provide personalized recommendations.

Step by Step Instructions

To take the quiz:

  1. Choose a quiz that appeals to you
  2. Answer the questions honestly and to the best of your ability
  3. Review your personalized recommendations
  4. Choose a hairstyle that you love and make an appointment with your hairstylist

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when taking a "what hairstyle suits my face quiz":

  • Be honest about your hair type, texture, and lifestyle
  • Consider your face shape when choosing a hairstyle
  • Don't be afraid to try something new
  • Bring a picture of your desired hairstyle to your hairstylist

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about "what hairstyle suits my face quiz":

What face shapes are there?

There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong.

What if I don't like my recommended hairstyle?

If you don't like your recommended hairstyle, don't worry! You can always take another quiz or consult with your hairstylist for additional recommendations.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Once you have your new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to maintain healthy ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week

Now that you know how to take a "what hairstyle suits my face quiz," it's time to discover your perfect look! Remember to be honest, consider your face shape, and don't be afraid to try something new. Good luck!
