How To Cut Soft Wispy Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Mar 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting your own bangs can be a daunting task, especially if you're looking to achieve a soft and wispy look. But with the right tools and technique, you can easily achieve this trendy hairstyle without having to visit a salon. In this article, we'll walk you through the step-by-step process of cutting soft wispy bangs at home.

How to Cut Soft Wispy Bangs

Before you start, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Water spray bottle

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and blow-dry your hair before you start cutting your bangs. This will give you a better idea of how your hair falls and where your natural part is.

Step 2: Section your hair

Use a comb to section off the hair that you want to cut into bangs. Start from the center of your forehead and comb your hair forward towards your nose. Then, use hair clips to separate the rest of your hair from your bangs.

Step 3: Wet your hair

Spray your bangs with water using a spray bottle. This will make your hair easier to cut and help you achieve a more precise cut.

Step 4: Cut your bangs

Hold your scissors at an angle and start cutting your bangs in small sections. Take your time and make sure you're cutting your hair evenly. Remember, it's better to cut less hair at first and then go back and trim more if needed.

Step 5: Check the length

Once you've cut your bangs, check the length to make sure it's even. If you need to trim more, do so carefully.

Step 6: Add texture

Use your scissors to add texture to your bangs. This will create a softer, more wispy look. Hold your scissors at an angle and make small cuts into your bangs.

Step 7: Style your bangs

Once you're happy with the length and texture of your bangs, style them using your fingers or a round brush. You can also use a hair straightener or curling iron to add more volume and texture.

Tips for Cutting Soft Wispy Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own bangs:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use sharp scissors
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time
  • Check the length frequently
  • Add texture to create a softer, more wispy look

FAQ

How do I know where to cut my bangs?

Start from the center of your forehead and comb your hair forward towards your nose. This will give you an idea of where your hair naturally falls and where to start cutting.

How do I add texture to my bangs?

Hold your scissors at an angle and make small cuts into your bangs. This will create a softer, more wispy look.

Can I cut my bangs if my hair is curly?

It's best to cut your bangs when your hair is straight. If you have curly hair, blow-dry it straight before you start cutting.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your soft wispy bangs looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a hair mask once a week
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Trim your bangs every few weeks to maintain the length

Cutting soft wispy bangs at home is easier than you think. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a trendy new hairstyle without leaving your house. Follow the steps and tips in this article to get started!
