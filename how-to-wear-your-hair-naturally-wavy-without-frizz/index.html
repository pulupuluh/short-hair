How To Wear Your Hair Naturally Wavy Without Frizz A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 13, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have naturally wavy hair, you know that keeping it under control can be a challenge. Frizz, flyaways, and tangles are common problems that can make it difficult to achieve the effortlessly chic look you're going for. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll show you how to wear your hair naturally wavy without frizz, and give you plenty of tips and tricks along the way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Your Hair Naturally Wavy Without Frizz
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Naturally Wavy Hair

How to Wear Your Hair Naturally Wavy Without Frizz

The first step in achieving frizz-free waves is to start with a good haircut. Layers can help to add volume and movement to your hair, while also reducing bulk and weight. If you have thick or coarse hair, you may want to consider getting a texturizing treatment to help tame your curls and reduce frizz.

Once you have the right cut, it's time to start styling. Here are some tips to help you get the perfect wavy look:

1. Use the Right Products

Choosing the right products is key to achieving frizz-free waves. Look for products that are specifically designed for wavy hair, such as mousses, gels, or creams. These products can help to define your curls while also reducing frizz and adding shine. Be sure to apply them evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

2. Don't Touch Your Hair Too Much

One of the biggest mistakes people make when styling wavy hair is touching it too much. This can cause your curls to become frizzy and lose their shape. Instead, try to let your hair air dry as much as possible, and avoid brushing or combing it too much.

3. Use a Diffuser

A diffuser is a must-have tool for anyone with wavy hair. This attachment fits onto the end of your hair dryer and helps to distribute heat evenly throughout your hair, while also reducing frizz and enhancing your curls. To use a diffuser, simply attach it to your hair dryer and hold it close to your head, scrunching your hair as you go.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect wavy look:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for wavy hair. Be sure to rinse thoroughly to remove any product buildup.

2. Apply Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and hold it close to your head, scrunching your hair as you go. Be sure to dry your hair on a low heat setting to avoid damage.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish with a light hairspray to help hold your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few more tips to help you achieve the perfect wavy look:

1. Sleep on Silk

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to reduce frizz and tangles, while also keeping your hair looking smooth and shiny.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

A wide-tooth comb is gentler on your hair than a brush, and can help to detangle your curls without causing frizz.

3. Embrace Second-Day Hair

Wavy hair tends to look better on the second day, so don't be afraid to skip a wash and let your hair air dry instead.

FAQ

Q. Can I wear my hair naturally wavy without using any products?

A. While it's possible to wear your hair naturally wavy without using any products, it's not recommended. Products help to define your curls and reduce frizz, making it easier to achieve the perfect look.

Q. How often should I wash my wavy hair?

A. It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but most people with wavy hair can go 2-3 days between washes.

Hair Care for Naturally Wavy Hair

Here are a few more tips to help you keep your naturally wavy hair looking its best:

1. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and cause frizz. Try to avoid using hot tools as much as possible, and let your hair air dry instead.

2. Use a Deep Conditioner

Deep conditioning treatments can help to nourish and hydrate your hair, reducing frizz and improving overall health and vitality.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to keep your hair safe.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to wear your hair naturally wavy without frizz in no time. Remember, it's all about finding the right products and techniques that work for your hair type and lifestyle. So experiment, have fun, and enjoy your beautiful waves!
