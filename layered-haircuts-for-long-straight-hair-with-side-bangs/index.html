Layered Haircuts For Long Straight Hair With Side Bangs The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Mar 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to switch up your long straight hair, adding layers and side bangs can give your hair a fresh and modern look. Layered haircuts for long straight hair with side bangs are perfect for those who want to add volume, texture, and movement to their hair. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about layered haircuts for long straight hair with side bangs, including how to get the look, maintenance tips, and more.

What are Layered Haircuts for Long Straight Hair with Side Bangs?

Layered haircuts for long straight hair with side bangs involve cutting your hair into different lengths to create layers. The layers add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and more dynamic. Side bangs are also added to frame your face and add a touch of elegance to your hairstyle.

How to Get Layered Haircuts for Long Straight Hair with Side Bangs

Getting a layered haircut with side bangs requires some precision, so we recommend going to a professional hairstylist for the best results. Here's what to expect during your appointment:

  1. Consultation: Your hairstylist will talk to you about the look you're going for, your hair texture, and your face shape to determine the best layered haircut with side bangs for you.
  2. Shampoo: Your hairstylist will shampoo your hair to get rid of any dirt and buildup.
  3. Cutting: Your hairstylist will cut your hair into different lengths to create layers. They will also add side bangs to frame your face.
  4. Styling: Your hairstylist will blow-dry and style your hair to finish the look.

Once you have your layered haircut with side bangs, you'll want to maintain it to keep it looking fresh and healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining your layered haircut with side bangs:

Maintenance Tips

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your layered haircut.
  • Use hair serum or oil to add shine and smoothness to your hair.
  • Don't forget to brush your hair regularly to prevent tangles and matting.

FAQ

Still have some questions about layered haircuts for long straight hair with side bangs? Here are some frequently asked questions to help you out:

Q: Will layered haircuts with side bangs work on all face shapes?

A: Yes, layered haircuts with side bangs can work on all face shapes, but the length and placement of the layers and bangs may vary depending on your face shape.

Q: Can I color my hair after getting a layered haircut with side bangs?

A: Yes, you can color your hair after getting a layered haircut with side bangs. However, we recommend waiting a few weeks to let your hair settle before coloring it.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential to maintaining a healthy and beautiful look. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Choose a brush that's gentle on your hair, like a boar bristle brush.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate hair growth and improve circulation.
  • Invest in a good quality hair mask to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a gorgeous, layered haircut with side bangs that will turn heads and make you feel confident and beautiful. So why wait? Book an appointment with your hairstylist today and get ready to rock your new look!
