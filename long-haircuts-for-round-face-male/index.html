Long Haircuts For Round Face Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Aug 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a male with a round face and long hair? Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut that flatters your face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about long haircuts for round face males. From how to choose the right haircut to hair care tips, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Long Haircut Styles for Round Face Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Long Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Long Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long Hair

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When it comes to choosing the right haircut for round face males, the key is to create angles and balance. Long haircuts that frame the face and add height to the top of the head can help elongate the face and create a more balanced look. Avoid haircuts that are too short or too flat, as they can make the face appear rounder.

It's also important to consider your hair texture and personal style when choosing a haircut. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect long haircut for your face shape.

Long Haircut Styles for Round Face Male

There are many long haircut styles that can flatter a round face male. Here are some popular options:

The Layered Look

A layered haircut can help add volume and movement to long hair. Layers can also create angles that help elongate the face. Consider adding layers to the top and sides of the hair for a more balanced look.

The Side Part

A side part can add height to the top of the head, which can help elongate the face. Consider pairing a side part with long, side-swept bangs for a more balanced look.

The Ponytail

A low ponytail can help elongate the face and create a more balanced look. Consider pulling hair back into a low ponytail at the nape of the neck for a sleek and sophisticated look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Long Hair

If you're looking to cut your own long hair, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  3. Starting at the back, use a comb to separate a small section of hair.
  4. Hold the section of hair between two fingers and cut the desired length.
  5. Continue working your way up the back of the head, cutting small sections of hair at a time.
  6. Once the back is complete, move on to the sides and top, cutting small sections of hair at a time.
  7. Once all sections are complete, use a comb to blend the layers together.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair

Maintaining long hair requires some extra care and attention. Here are some tips:

  • Wash hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on hair too often.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep hair hydrated.
  • Trim hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on hair and cause breakage.

FAQs

What if my hair is curly?

Curly hair can be a great asset when it comes to creating angles and volume. Consider a layered haircut that works with your natural texture.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

For long hair, it's recommended to get a trim every 8-12 weeks to prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Long hair requires some extra care and attention to stay healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid brushing hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps, as they can cause breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect long haircut for your round face male and maintain healthy, strong hair. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
