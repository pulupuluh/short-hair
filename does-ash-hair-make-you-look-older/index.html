Does Ash Hair Make You Look Older Debunking The Myths

Written by Oliver Jun 21, 2023 · 7 min read

Ash hair has been a popular trend in recent years. It’s a beautiful shade that can give your hair a cool, silver tone. However, there has been a long-standing myth that ash hair makes you look older. In this blog post, we’re going to debunk this myth and give you tips on how to wear ash hair without looking older.

Description

Ash hair is a shade of blonde that has a cool, silver tone to it. It’s a popular trend because it’s a unique and striking color. However, many people believe that ash hair makes you look older. This is because the color can be harsh on some skin tones and can wash out your complexion.

How to Wear Ash Hair Without Looking Older

The key to wearing ash hair without looking older is to find the right shade for your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, ash hair may not be the best choice for you. Instead, try a warmer shade of blonde or a caramel color. If you have a cool skin tone, ash hair can be a great option.

Another way to wear ash hair without looking older is to add dimension to your hair. This can be done by adding lowlights or highlights to your hair. This will give your hair depth and make it look more natural.

Step by Step

1. Determine your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, consider a warmer shade of blonde. If you have a cool skin tone, ash hair can be a great option.

2. Find the right shade of ash hair for your skin tone. If you have a fair complexion, a lighter shade of ash hair may be better for you. If you have a darker complexion, a darker shade of ash hair may be more suitable.

3. Add dimension to your hair by adding lowlights or highlights. This will give your hair depth and make it look more natural.

Tips

1. Use a purple shampoo to keep your ash hair from turning brassy.

2. Avoid using too much heat on your hair. This can cause damage and make your hair look older.

3. Keep your hair healthy by using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

FAQ

Q: Does ash hair make you look older?

A: No, ash hair does not make you look older. The myth that ash hair makes you look older is simply not true. It’s all about finding the right shade of ash hair for your skin tone and adding dimension to your hair.

Q: Can I wear ash hair if I have a warm skin tone?

A: Yes, you can wear ash hair if you have a warm skin tone. However, it’s important to find the right shade of ash hair for your skin tone.

Q: How can I keep my ash hair from turning brassy?

A: Use a purple shampoo to keep your ash hair from turning brassy. This will help to neutralize any yellow or orange tones in your hair.

Hair Care

When you have ash hair, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make your hair look older. Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.

Overall, ash hair does not make you look older. It’s all about finding the right shade of ash hair for your skin tone and adding dimension to your hair. With the right care and maintenance, ash hair can be a beautiful and striking shade that will make you look and feel amazing.
