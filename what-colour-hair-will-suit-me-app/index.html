What Colour Hair Will Suit Me App The Ultimate Hair Colour Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of endlessly scrolling through hair colour inspiration on Instagram, only to be left unsure of what would actually suit you? Look no further than the "What Colour Hair Will Suit Me" app! This revolutionary app takes the guesswork out of hair colouring and helps you find the perfect shade for your skin tone and features. In this article, we'll walk you through how to use the app, offer some tips for choosing the best hair colour for you, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Use the What Colour Hair Will Suit Me App

Using the app is simple and straightforward. First, download the app from the app store and open it on your phone. You'll be prompted to take a selfie, which the app will analyze to determine your skin tone, eye colour, and facial features. Once the analysis is complete, the app will suggest a range of hair colours that will complement your unique features.

From there, you can browse through different hair colours, save your favourites, and even try them on virtually using the app's augmented reality feature. The app also includes helpful information about each hair colour, such as whether it requires bleaching or how to maintain it.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using the App

1. Download the "What Colour Hair Will Suit Me" app from the app store.

2. Take a selfie and allow the app to analyze your skin tone, eye colour, and facial features.

3. Browse through the suggested hair colours and save your favourites.

4. Use the app's augmented reality feature to try on different hair colours virtually.

5. Read up on each hair colour's characteristics, such as whether it requires bleaching or how to maintain it.

Tips for Choosing the Best Hair Colour

While the "What Colour Hair Will Suit Me" app is a great starting point, there are some additional tips you can keep in mind when choosing a hair colour.

- Consider your skin tone: Cooler skin tones generally look best with cooler hair colours, while warmer skin tones work well with warmer shades.

- Think about your eye colour: Your eye colour can also play a role in determining which hair colour will suit you best. For example, green eyes often look great with red or auburn hair.

- Don't be afraid to go bold: If you're feeling adventurous, try a bold hair colour like purple or blue. Just make sure it complements your skin tone and features.

FAQ

Can the app be used on all hair types and textures?

Yes! The app is designed to work for all hair types and textures.

Will the app suggest hair colours that require a lot of upkeep?

The app includes information about each hair colour's upkeep requirements, so you can choose a shade that fits your lifestyle.

Is the app free to use?

The app is free to download and use, although some features may require in-app purchases.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your new colour:

- Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause the colour to fade.

- Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

- Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Conclusion

The "What Colour Hair Will Suit Me" app is a game-changer for anyone looking to switch up their hair colour. With its advanced analysis and augmented reality features, you can find the perfect shade for your unique features and try it on virtually before committing. And with our tips for choosing a hair colour and maintaining it, you'll be sure to turn heads with your new 'do.
