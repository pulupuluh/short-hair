Hair Color That Makes You Look Younger 2019 Tips How To And Faqs

Are you looking for ways to look younger? Changing your hair color might be the answer. In 2019, hair color trends focused on natural, low-maintenance shades that bring out your best features. Read on to find out how you can achieve a hair color that makes you look younger.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How-To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

As we age, our skin tone changes and our hair starts to turn gray. By choosing the right hair color, you can bring out your best features and look younger. In 2019, the hair color trend focused on natural, low-maintenance shades that enhance your natural beauty.

How-To

The first step in choosing a hair color that makes you look younger is to consider your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, choose a hair color that has warm, golden tones. If you have cool undertones, choose a hair color that has cool, ashy tones. If you're not sure, consult with a professional hairstylist.

Step-by-Step

1. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone.

2. Consider the maintenance level of the hair color.

3. Consult with a professional hairstylist.

4. Prepare your hair for coloring by washing it with a clarifying shampoo.

5. Apply the hair color according to the instructions.

6. Rinse your hair thoroughly and condition it.

Tips

- Choose a hair color that is no more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural hair color.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to protect your hair color.

- Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQs

Q: How often should I color my hair to maintain a youthful appearance?

A: It depends on the maintenance level of the hair color you choose. If you choose a low-maintenance hair color, you can go up to six months between touch-ups.

Q: Can I color my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can color your hair at home, but it's recommended to consult with a professional hairstylist to ensure you choose the right hair color and apply it correctly.

Q: Will coloring my hair damage it?

A: Coloring your hair can cause some damage, but using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and protecting your hair from sun damage can help minimize the damage.

Hair Care

After coloring your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools on your hair. Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray. Visit your hairstylist regularly for touch-ups and trims.

In conclusion, choosing a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural beauty can make you look younger. Consider the maintenance level of the hair color and consult with a professional hairstylist to ensure you choose the right hair color and apply it correctly. Take care of your hair after coloring it to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy.
