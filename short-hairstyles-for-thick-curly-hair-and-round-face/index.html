Short Hairstyles For Thick Curly Hair And Round Face

Written by Oliver May 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick curly hair is a blessing, but it can be a challenge to find the right hairstyle that suits your face shape. If you have a round face, it can be even more difficult. However, with the right short hairstyle, you can accentuate your features and look stunning. In this article, we will explore some of the best short hairstyles for thick curly hair and round face, along with tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Short hairstyles for thick curly hair and round face should aim to create volume at the crown while slimming down the face. The right hairstyle can help to balance out the roundness of the face and highlight the best features. There are many options available, from curly bobs to short pixie cuts, that can suit different face shapes and hair types. Choosing the right hairstyle requires some research and experimentation, but the results can be worth it.

How to

To choose the right short hairstyle for your thick curly hair and round face, you should consider your hair type, face shape, and personal style. Look for inspiration from celebrities or online galleries, and talk to your hairdresser about what would suit you best. Consider factors like texture, length, layers, and highlights, as they can all affect the overall look of your hairstyle.

Step by Step

Here are some popular short hairstyles for thick curly hair and round face:

Curly Bob

A curly bob is a great option for those who want to show off their curls while keeping the hair short. Ask your hairdresser for a chin-length bob with layers to add volume and texture. Use a curling iron to enhance your curls and create a bouncy effect.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and stylish option for those who want to go super short. This style can work well for thick curly hair, as it can create a lot of volume at the crown. Ask your hairdresser for a layered pixie cut that is longer on top and shorter on the sides. Use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls and create a tousled look.

Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut can be a great way to add some edge to your hairstyle while slimming down the face. Ask your hairdresser for a layered cut that is shorter on one side and longer on the other. Use a styling product to create a sleek and polished look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for thick curly hair and round face:

  • Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a hairstyle
  • Choose a style that flatters your face shape
  • Experiment with different lengths and layers to find the best fit
  • Use styling products to enhance your curls and add volume
  • Talk to your hairdresser about maintenance and care for your new hairstyle

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for thick curly hair and round face:

What is the best short hairstyle for round face?

The best short hairstyle for round face depends on the individual's hair type, texture, and personal style. Some popular options include curly bobs, pixie cuts, and asymmetrical cuts.

Can short hair work for thick curly hair?

Yes, short hair can work for thick curly hair. However, it is important to choose a style that takes into account the natural texture and volume of the hair. Talk to your hairdresser about the best options for your hair type.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle for thick curly hair and round face looking its best, it is important to take care of your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that is designed for curly hair. Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet. Use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls and add volume. Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

In conclusion, short hairstyles for thick curly hair and round face can be a great way to show off your natural beauty and enhance your best features. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel confident every day.
