How To Make Your Hair Wavy Mens A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 12, 2023 · 9 min read

Want to add some texture and volume to your hair? Wavy hair is a great way to achieve a relaxed, effortless look. Whether you have straight or curly hair, there are several ways to create waves. In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair wavy mens with a step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Wavy Mens
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Your Hair Wavy
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair

How to Make Your Hair Wavy Mens

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over the different methods you can use to make your hair wavy.

Curling Iron

A curling iron is a classic way to create waves. It's easy to use and gives you control over the size and shape of your waves. This method is best for short to medium hair lengths.

Flat Iron

You can also use a flat iron to create waves. This method is great for longer hair lengths and gives you more natural-looking waves. It takes a bit more practice to master, but the results are worth it.

Braids

Braiding your hair is a heat-free way to create waves. This method is best for longer hair lengths and gives you loose, beachy waves. You can braid your hair before bed and wake up with waves in the morning.

Twist and Pin

The twist and pin method is a quick and easy way to create waves. Simply twist sections of your hair and pin them in place. This method is best for short to medium hair lengths and gives you natural-looking waves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the different methods for making your hair wavy, let's go over a step-by-step guide for each one.

Curling Iron

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small sections.
  4. Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron.
  5. Hold for 10-15 seconds and release.
  6. Repeat until all sections of hair are curled.
  7. Use your fingers to loosen the curls into waves.
  8. Finish with hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Flat Iron

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small sections.
  4. Take one section of hair and twist it away from your face.
  5. Run the flat iron over the twisted section of hair.
  6. Release the twist and repeat until all sections of hair are waved.
  7. Use your fingers to loosen the waves.
  8. Finish with hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Braids

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or sea salt spray, to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small sections.
  4. Braid each section of hair and secure with a hair tie.
  5. Leave the braids in overnight.
  6. Unwrap the braids in the morning and use your fingers to loosen the waves.
  7. Finish with hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Twist and Pin

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or sea salt spray, to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small sections.
  4. Twist each section of hair and pin it in place with a bobby pin.
  5. Leave the twists in for 20-30 minutes.
  6. Remove the bobby pins and use your fingers to loosen the waves.
  7. Finish with hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips for Making Your Hair Wavy

Here are some tips to help you make your hair wavy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
  • For natural-looking waves, vary the size and direction of your curls.
  • Loosen your curls with your fingers for a more relaxed, beachy look.
  • Use sea salt spray or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your waves.
  • Don't overdo it with hairspray. Too much hairspray can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make my hair wavy without heat?

Yes, you can make your hair wavy without heat by braiding your hair or using the twist and pin method.

How long will my waves last?

How long your waves last depends on your hair type, the method you use, and how well you maintain them. Generally, waves will last for a few days with proper care.

Can I make my hair wavy if I have short hair?

Yes, you can make your hair wavy if you have short hair. The curling iron and twist and pin methods work well for short hair lengths.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Wavy hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming dry and frizzy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep them moisturized.
  • Don't wash your hair every day. Wavy hair tends to be dry, so washing it too often can strip away its natural oils.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disturbing your waves.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily make your hair wavy mens. Whether you prefer loose, beachy waves or tighter curls, there's a method that will work for you. Just remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
