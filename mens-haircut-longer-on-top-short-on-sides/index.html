Men s Haircut Longer On Top Short On Sides

Written by Bardi Feb 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to our blog post all about the popular men's haircut that has been taking the world by storm! If you're wondering what the fuss is all about, then let us introduce you to the 'longer on top, short on sides' hairstyle. This haircut has been around for quite some time now, but it's only recently gained popularity. It's a classic and versatile hairstyle that has been rocked by many celebrities and will continue to be popular in the years to come.

Description

The men's haircut longer on top, short on sides is a style where the hair on the top of the head is kept long and the sides and back are cut short. This creates a contrast between the longer hair and the shorter hair, giving a neat and tidy look. This haircut is suitable for all hair types and face shapes, making it a versatile hairstyle that anyone can rock.

How To

If you're looking to get this haircut, then it's important to find a good hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you desire. Here's how to get this haircut:

Step 1: Consultation

The first step is to consult with your hairstylist. This is where you can discuss the length you want on the top and the style you want to achieve. Your hairstylist can also recommend the best products to use to maintain the style.

Step 2: Cutting the Sides

The hairstylist will start by cutting the sides and back of the hair with clippers. The length of the sides will depend on the style you want to achieve. It's important to tell your hairstylist how short you want the sides to be.

Step 3: Cutting the Top

The hairstylist will then cut the hair on the top of the head. The length of the hair on the top will depend on the style you want to achieve. You can ask your hairstylist to cut the hair with scissors or clippers.

Step 4: Styling

Once the haircut is complete, the hairstylist will style the hair using the products they recommend. This will help the hair stay in place and maintain the style.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your longer on top, short on sides haircut:

  • Use the recommended hair products to maintain the style.
  • Get regular trims to keep the hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styles using hair wax or pomade.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut longer on top, short on sides:

Q: Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut is suitable for all hair types.

Q: How often should I get a trim?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Q: Can I style this haircut differently?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different styles using hair wax or pomade.

Hair Care

To maintain the health of your hair, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair as it can damage the hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet to provide the hair with the necessary nutrients.

We hope this post has helped you understand the style better and given you some tips on how to maintain it. If you're looking to get this haircut, then don't hesitate to book an appointment with your hairstylist today.
