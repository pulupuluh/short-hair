Oval Face Short Hair The Ultimate Guide For 2023

If you have an oval face, you are in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile, which means you can rock just about any hairstyle. However, if you are looking for a short hairstyle that will flatter your face shape, keep reading. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on oval face short hair, including how to style it, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  • How to Style Oval Face Short Hair

Description

An oval face is characterized by a slightly wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. This face shape is considered the most balanced and symmetrical, which means you can pull off a variety of hairstyles, including short haircuts. Short haircuts can help accentuate your face shape and bring attention to your best features. However, not all short haircuts are created equal, and some may not flatter your oval face shape. That is why it is important to choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape.

How to Style Oval Face Short Hair

When it comes to styling oval face short hair, there are many options to choose from. You can go for a pixie cut, a bob, a lob, or a shaggy cut. The key is to choose a hairstyle that enhances your best features and flatters your face shape. Here are some tips on how to style oval face short hair:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is cut close to the scalp. This style is perfect for oval faces because it accentuates the cheekbones and highlights the eyes. To style a pixie cut, apply some styling wax or pomade to your hair and use your fingers to create texture and volume. You can also use a flat iron to add some waves or curls.

2. Bob

A bob is a classic short hairstyle that is perfect for oval faces. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit your personal style. To style a bob, use a round brush to blow dry your hair and add some volume. You can also use a flat iron to create sleek and straight hair.

3. Lob

A lob is a longer version of a bob and is perfect for oval faces. This style is chic and sophisticated and can be worn with a side part or a center part. To style a lob, use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls. You can also use a flat iron to create a sleek and straight look.

4. Shaggy Cut

A shaggy cut is a short hairstyle that is layered and choppy. This style is perfect for oval faces because it adds texture and volume to your hair. To style a shaggy cut, use a texturizing spray or pomade to create a messy and undone look. You can also use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style oval face short hair:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.

2. Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Do not rub your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause damage and breakage.

3. Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or a texturizing spray, to your hair. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

4. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to blow dry your hair. Start by blow-drying the roots of your hair and work your way down to the ends. Use the round brush to create volume and shape.

5. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a styling tool, such as a flat iron, curling iron, or texturizing spray, to style your hair. Experiment with different styles until you find a look that you love.

Tips for Oval Face Short Hair

Here are some tips for styling oval face short hair:

  • Choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape.
  • Experiment with different styles until you find a look that you love.
  • Use styling products, such as texturizing sprays and pomades, to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to create different styles, such as sleek and straight or loose waves and curls.

FAQs

1. What short hairstyles are best for oval faces?

Short hairstyles that are best for oval faces include pixie cuts, bobs, lobs, and shaggy cuts.

2. What styling products should I use for oval face short hair?

You should use styling products that add volume and texture to your hair, such as texturizing sprays and pomades.

3. How do I blow dry my hair to create volume and shape?

To blow dry your hair to create volume and shape, use a round brush and start by blow-drying the roots of your hair. Work your way down to the ends of your hair, using the round brush to create volume and shape.

Hair Care Advice for Oval Face Short Hair

Here are some hair care tips for oval face short hair:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and nourished.

Now that you have read our complete guide on oval face short hair, you are ready to rock a short hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Remember to choose a style that complements your best features and experiment with different styles until you find a look that you love. With the right styling tools and products, you can achieve any short hairstyle you desire.
