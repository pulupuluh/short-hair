Good Haircuts For Round Faces Guys

Written by Frank Sep 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a guy with a round face and struggling to find a haircut that suits you? Look no further because we have compiled a list of the best haircuts for round faces guys. With these haircuts, you can enhance your facial features and maintain a stylish look.

Description

Round faces are characterized by soft and curved features. The cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the jawline is round. The objective of a good haircut for a round face is to create an illusion of length and sharpness. The right haircut will balance out the features and make the face appear more oval-shaped.

How to

When selecting a haircut, consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which haircut will suit you best. Bring reference pictures to show them the exact look you want. Here are some haircuts that work well for round faces guys:

1. Short Crop

This haircut is perfect for guys who want a low-maintenance hairstyle. The short crop is a buzz cut that is slightly longer on top. This style creates a textured look that adds height to the face, making it appear longer and slimmer.

2. Side Part

The side part is a classic haircut that works well for round faces. The hair is parted to one side, and the top is styled with some volume. This style creates an elongated look and makes the face appear slimmer.

3. Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a popular haircut that is edgy and stylish. The hair is styled in a way that creates a mohawk-like shape, but with less extreme sides. This style adds height to the face and creates an illusion of length.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve a perfect haircut for round faces guys:

Step 1: Consultation

Discuss your hair type, lifestyle, and personal style with your hairstylist. Show them reference pictures to help them understand the exact look you want.

Step 2: Preparation

Wash your hair and towel dry it. Apply a styling product to add texture and hold to your hair.

Step 3: Styling

Style your hair according to the selected haircut. Use a blow dryer and a brush to add volume and create the desired shape. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Apply a styling product that suits your hair type and desired style.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair as it can damage the hair and make it look unhealthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for round faces guys:

1. Which haircut is best for a round face?

Short crop, side part, and faux hawk are some haircuts that work well for round faces guys.

2. Can guys with round faces have long hair?

Yes, guys with round faces can have long hair, but it should be styled in a way that adds height and creates an elongated look.

3. Should I consult with a hairstylist before getting a haircut?

Yes, it is recommended to consult with a hairstylist to determine which haircut will suit you best and help you achieve your desired look.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair as it can damage the hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes protein and vitamins to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.

With these tips, you can maintain healthy hair and achieve the perfect haircut for your round face. Embrace your facial features and enhance them with the right haircut.
