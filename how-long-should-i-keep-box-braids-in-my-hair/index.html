How Long Should I Keep Box Braids In My Hair

Written by Oliver May 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Box braids are a popular hairstyle that has been around for decades. They are a protective style that involves braiding your natural hair into small sections using synthetic hair extensions. Box braids can last up to eight weeks if properly cared for, but many people wonder how long they should keep them in their hair. In this article, we will explore how long you should keep box braids in your hair and provide tips for maintaining them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Box braids are a type of protective style that can help to protect your natural hair from damage. They are created by braiding your natural hair into small sections and adding synthetic hair extensions. Box braids can be styled in many different ways and are a great option for those looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle.

How To

To create box braids, you will need to start by washing and conditioning your hair. Once your hair is clean and detangled, you can begin sectioning it into small parts. Each section should be about the width of your finger. Next, you will need to add synthetic hair extensions to each section. You can do this by using a crochet needle to loop the hair through your natural hair or by braiding the extensions in with your natural hair.

Step by Step

1. Wash and condition your hair
2. Section your hair into small parts
3. Add synthetic hair extensions
4. Braid your hair
5. Style your braids as desired

Tips

When wearing box braids, it is important to properly care for them to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your box braids:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly
  • Avoid using heavy products on your braids
  • Cover your hair with a silk scarf or bonnet at night
  • Avoid excessive pulling or tugging on your braids
  • Wash your hair every two weeks

FAQ

Q: How long should I keep box braids in my hair?
A: Box braids can last up to eight weeks if properly cared for.

Q: Can I wash my hair with box braids?
A: Yes, you can wash your hair every two weeks while wearing box braids.

Hair Care

When wearing box braids, it is important to properly care for your natural hair as well as your braids. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair while wearing box braids:

  • Moisturize your scalp regularly
  • Avoid using heavy products on your hair
  • Avoid excessive heat styling
  • Trim your ends regularly
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated

Overall, box braids can be a great protective style for those looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle. They can last up to eight weeks if properly cared for, but it is important to maintain healthy hair while wearing them. By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and your box braids looking great!
