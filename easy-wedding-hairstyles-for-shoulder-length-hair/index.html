Easy Wedding Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Oliver Mar 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you attending a wedding soon and looking for easy wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions and tips for achieving beautiful hairstyles that are perfect for any wedding occasion.

Description

Shoulder length hair is the perfect length for wedding hairstyles. It's not too long that it becomes difficult to manage, and it's not too short that it limits your styling options. With the right techniques and tools, you can create stunning hairstyles that will complement your outfit and make you stand out at the wedding.

How to

Here are some easy wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair:

1. Half-Up Half-Down

This is a classic wedding hairstyle that is simple to achieve. Start by parting your hair down the middle and taking a section of hair from each side of your head. Twist each section and bring them to the back of your head, securing them with bobby pins. Leave the rest of your hair down and add some curls or waves for a more romantic look.

2. Braided Updo

Braids are always a great option for weddings, and this braided updo is no exception. Start by parting your hair down the middle and creating two French braids on each side of your head. Then, bring the braids to the back of your head and secure them with bobby pins. Twist the rest of your hair into a bun and secure it with more bobby pins.

3. Low Bun

A low bun is a chic and elegant hairstyle that is perfect for weddings. Start by parting your hair down the middle and pulling it back into a low ponytail. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. You can also add some accessories like flowers or a hairpiece to make your hairstyle more glamorous.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide for achieving the Half-Up Half-Down hairstyle:

Step 1:

Part your hair down the middle using a comb.

Step 2:

Take a section of hair from each side of your head and twist them.

Step 3:

Bring the twisted sections to the back of your head and secure them with bobby pins.

Step 4:

Curl or wave the rest of your hair using a curling iron or wand.

Step 5:

Spray your hair with hairspray to set the curls and keep the hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for achieving the perfect wedding hairstyle:

1. Use the right products

Make sure to use products that are suited for your hair type and the hairstyle you want to achieve. For example, use a heat protectant spray when curling your hair, and use a hairspray that provides a strong hold for updos.

2. Don't forget the accessories

Add some accessories like flowers, hairpins, or a hairpiece to make your hairstyle more glamorous and unique.

3. Practice beforehand

Practice your hairstyle beforehand to make sure you know what you're doing and to avoid any last-minute mishaps on the day of the wedding.

FAQ

Q: What are some easy wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair?

A: Some easy wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair include the Half-Up Half-Down, Braided Updo, and Low Bun.

Q: What products do I need to achieve a wedding hairstyle?

A: You may need products such as heat protectant spray, hairspray, and hairpins to achieve a wedding hairstyle.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair in the weeks leading up to the wedding. This includes regular washing, conditioning, and deep conditioning treatments to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and try to limit the use of styling tools.

In conclusion, there are many easy wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair that you can achieve with the right techniques and tools. Whether you prefer a classic Half-Up Half-Down or a chic Low Bun, remember to practice beforehand and use the right products to achieve the perfect wedding hairstyle.
