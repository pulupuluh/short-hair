How To Curl Hair Outwards With A Straightener A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Feb 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're tired of the same old straight hair, you might want to try curling your hair outwards with a straightener. It may sound counterintuitive, but using a straightener to curl your hair can give you natural-looking, loose waves that are perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of how to curl hair outwards with a straightener so you can achieve the look you've always wanted.

How to Curl Hair Outwards with a Straightener

Before we get started, here are some things you'll need:

  • A straightener with rounded edges
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair clips
  • Hairspray

Now that you have everything you need, let's get started!

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Once your hair is clean, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections

Using hair clips, divide your hair into sections. The size of the sections will depend on how thick your hair is. If you have thick hair, you may need to make smaller sections.

Step 3: Start curling

Take a section of hair and clamp the straightener at the root of your hair. Slowly rotate the straightener outwards while pulling it down. This will give you a natural-looking curl that curls away from your face.

Step 4: Repeat

Repeat step 3 with the remaining sections of your hair until you have curled all of your hair.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Once you have curled all of your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help keep your curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curl:

  • Use a straightener with rounded edges to prevent kinks in your hair
  • Take small sections of hair for tighter curls and larger sections for loose waves
  • Don't hold the straightener in one spot for too long, as this can cause heat damage
  • Always use a heat protectant spray

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling hair outwards with a straightener:

Can I curl my hair with a flat iron?

Yes, you can curl your hair with a flat iron. In fact, a flat iron can give you natural-looking, loose waves that are perfect for any occasion.

Can I curl my hair with a straightener every day?

It's not recommended to curl your hair with a straightener every day, as this can cause heat damage and breakage.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair with a straightener. Additionally, consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to curl your hair outwards with a straightener like a pro. Happy styling!
