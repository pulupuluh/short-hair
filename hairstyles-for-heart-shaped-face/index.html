Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Face The Ultimate Guide

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and a narrow chin, resembling the shape of a heart. This face shape is considered one of the most challenging to style, as it requires a cut and style that balances the proportions of the face. In this article, we will share with you the best hairstyles for heart shaped face, along with tips, step by step tutorials, and hair care to help you achieve a flawless look.


How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Heart Shaped Face

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to choosing the right hairstyle for your heart shaped face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, and comparing them to each other.

Step 2: Identify Your Best Features

Once you have determined your face shape, identify your best features, such as your eyes, lips, or cheekbones. The right hairstyle should highlight your best features and balance the proportions of your face.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your lifestyle and how much time you can dedicate to styling your hair. Choose a cut and style that suits your daily routine and is easy to maintain.


Step by Step Tutorials for Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Face

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for heart shaped faces, as they soften the chin and balance the proportions of the face. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long layers that start at the chin and blend into the rest of the hair.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can also work well for heart shaped faces, especially if you have a petite frame. This cut highlights your best features and draws attention to your eyes and cheekbones. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a short, cropped cut with layers that frame the face.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to balance the proportions of a heart shaped face. They draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones, while softening the forehead and chin. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long, side-swept bangs that start at the temple and blend into the rest of the hair.


Tips for Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Face

Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle for your heart shaped face:

  • Opt for soft, layered cuts that balance the proportions of your face
  • Avoid blunt cuts that accentuate the width of your forehead
  • Experiment with side-swept bangs to soften your forehead and balance your features
  • Consider highlighting your best features, such as your eyes or cheekbones, with your hairstyle
  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain

FAQ about Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Face

1. Can heart shaped faces wear short hair?

Yes, heart shaped faces can wear short hair, such as a pixie cut or a bob. However, it's important to choose a cut that balances the proportions of your face and highlights your best features.

2. What is the best hairstyle for heart shaped faces with curly hair?

For heart shaped faces with curly hair, opt for a layered cut that adds volume and bounce to your curls. Avoid blunt cuts that can make your face appear wider.

3. Can heart shaped faces wear bangs?

Yes, heart shaped faces can wear bangs, especially side-swept bangs that soften the forehead and balance the proportions of the face.


Hair Care for Heart Shaped Face

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends
