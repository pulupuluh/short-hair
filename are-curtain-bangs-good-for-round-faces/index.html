Are Curtain Bangs Good For Round Faces Everything You Need To Know

Description

Curtain bangs have been a trendy hairstyle for a while now, but one question that keeps popping up is whether they are suitable for round faces. The answer is yes! Curtain bangs can look great on round faces when styled correctly.

Not only do curtain bangs add texture and volume to the hair, but they can also help elongate the face and make it appear slimmer. In this article, we will dive into everything you need to know about curtain bangs for round faces.

How to

Before getting curtain bangs, it's important to consider the length and thickness of your hair. Typically, curtain bangs look best on medium to long hair. If you have shorter hair, it may be difficult to achieve the desired look.

When getting your bangs cut, make sure to communicate with your hairstylist about your face shape and what you want to achieve. They can help you determine the best length and style for your face shape.

Step by Step

Once you have your curtain bangs, it's important to style them correctly. Here are the steps:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  4. Using a flat iron or curling iron, curl your bangs away from your face to create a soft, curtain-like effect.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when rocking curtain bangs with a round face:

  • Avoid heavy, blunt bangs as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Opt for a wispy, feathered look to add texture and soften your features.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create dimension and frame your face.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that flatters your face shape the most.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs for round faces:

Q: Can curtain bangs make my face look rounder?

A: Only if they are cut too short or blunt. Wispy, feathered curtain bangs can actually help elongate the face and make it appear slimmer.

Q: What face shapes do curtain bangs look best on?

A: Curtain bangs can look great on most face shapes, but they are particularly flattering on oval and heart-shaped faces.

Q: How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly your bangs start to get in your eyes. Generally, it's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to extend the life of your blowout and reduce oiliness.

Overall, curtain bangs can be a great choice for those with round faces. As long as you get the right cut and style them correctly, you can achieve a flattering and trendy look that complements your face shape.
