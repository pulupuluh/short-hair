How To Do A Fancy High Ponytail

Written by Daniel Jul 01, 2023 ¬∑ 5 min read

Are you looking to add some pizzazz to your everyday ponytail? A fancy high ponytail is the perfect hairstyle to elevate your look for any occasion. In this tutorial, we will provide step-by-step instructions on how to achieve this stylish hairstyle. Read on to learn more!

Table of Contents

  • How to Do a Fancy High Ponytail
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for a Perfect Ponytail
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these simple steps to create a fancy high ponytail:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash your hair with your preferred shampoo and conditioner. Allow your hair to air-dry or use a hairdryer to dry it completely. Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Create a high ponytail

Using a brush, gather all your hair at the crown of your head. Secure the ponytail with a hair elastic. Ensure that the ponytail is tightly secured to avoid any sagging.

Step 3: Add volume

Use a comb to tease the hair at the base of the ponytail. This step will create volume and make your ponytail appear fuller.

Step 4: Wrap a strand of hair around the hair elastic

Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair elastic. Use a bobby pin to secure the strand of hair in place.

Step 5: Accessorize

Add hair accessories such as hairpins, clips, or a headband to enhance the look of your fancy high ponytail.

Tips for a Perfect Ponytail

Here are some tips to create a perfect fancy high ponytail:

  • Use a hair elastic that matches your hair color to avoid any visible hair ties.
  • Tease the hair at the base of your ponytail to create volume.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any flyaways or loose strands.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories to personalize your fancy high ponytail look.

FAQs

Can I do a fancy high ponytail with short hair?

Yes, you can create a fancy high ponytail with short hair by using hair extensions or adding volume to your hair with a hair volumizer.

How long does it take to create a fancy high ponytail?

A fancy high ponytail can take between five to ten minutes to create depending on your hair length and the complexity of the hairstyle.

Can I wear a fancy high ponytail to a formal event?

Yes, you can wear a fancy high ponytail to a formal event by accessorizing it with elegant hairpins or a jeweled headband.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons frequently.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and breakage.
  • Use a hair mask at least once a week to nourish your hair.

Now that you know how to create a fancy high ponytail, you can add some glamour to your everyday look. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair accessories and styles to create a personalized look that suits your taste.
