Low Maintenance Short Haircuts For Round Faces

Written by Oliver Apr 12, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a round face, finding a low maintenance short haircut that flatters your features can be a challenge. Luckily, there are plenty of stylish and easy-to-maintain options available. In this article, we'll explore some of the best short haircuts for round faces that require minimal styling and upkeep.

Description

A round face is typically characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide forehead. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a haircut that elongates your features and creates the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. Short haircuts can be a great option, as they can help to streamline your features and draw attention to your best assets.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, it's important to consider your hair texture, as well as your face shape. Some of the best options for low maintenance short haircuts for round faces include:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped haircut that can be customized to suit a range of face shapes. For round faces, a pixie cut with longer layers on top can help to elongate your features and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. This style requires minimal styling, making it a great option for those who are short on time.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic short haircut that can be tailored to suit a variety of face shapes. For round faces, a bob cut with longer layers on the sides can help to balance out your features and create a more elongated look. This style can be worn straight or with a slight wave, and requires minimal styling to maintain.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is a versatile short haircut that can be customized to suit a range of face shapes. For round faces, a layered cut with longer layers on top can help to elongate your features and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. This style requires minimal styling, making it a great option for those who are short on time.

Step-by-Step

Once you've chosen a low maintenance short haircut for your round face, it's important to know how to style and maintain it. Here are some step-by-step tips for keeping your short haircut looking fresh and stylish:

Step 1: Start with clean, towel-dried hair

Before styling your short haircut, make sure your hair is clean and towel-dried. This will make it easier to work with and help your styling products to adhere better.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Depending on your hair type and the style you're going for, you may want to apply a styling product to your hair. This could be a mousse, a gel, or a styling cream. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and ends.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair

Using a blow dryer and a round brush, blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go. If you have a pixie cut, you may want to use your fingers to create texture and volume. If you have a bob cut, you may want to use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or waves.

Step 4: Finish with a styling spray

Once your hair is dry, finish with a styling spray to lock in your style and add shine. This could be a hairspray, a texturizing spray, or a shine spray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your low maintenance short haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy
  • Invest in a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking its best
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create dimension and depth

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for a round face?

The best short haircut for a round face depends on your hair texture and personal style. Some of the most flattering options include pixie cuts, bob cuts, and layered cuts with longer layers on top.

How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

You should get your short haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

What styling products should I use for my short haircut?

The best styling products for your short haircut will depend on your hair type and style. Some popular options include mousse, gel, styling cream, texturizing spray, and shine spray.

Hair Care

Caring for your short haircut is important for maintaining its health and vitality. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can cause damage to your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF
  • Consider getting a deep conditioning treatment every few months to keep your hair nourished and hydrated

By following these tips and choosing a low maintenance short haircut that flatters your features, you can enjoy a stylish and effortless look that's perfect for any occasion.
