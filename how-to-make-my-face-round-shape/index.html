How To Make My Face Round Shape Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you have a long or square-shaped face and want to make it rounder? You're not alone! Many people prefer a rounder face shape as it gives a more youthful and softer appearance. In this article, we'll discuss how to make my face round shape through various tips and tricks.

Table of Contents

  • Facial Exercises
  • Hairstyles
  • Makeup Techniques
  • Diet and Lifestyle Changes
  • FAQs

Facial Exercises

Facial exercises are a great way to tone and strengthen the muscles in your face, which can help make your face appear rounder. Here are a few exercises to try:

Cheek Puff

Take a deep breath and puff your cheeks out. Hold for a few seconds, then release. Repeat 10 times.

Jaw Release

Open your mouth wide, then move your jaw up and down like you're chewing gum. Repeat 10 times.

Tongue Stretch

Stick your tongue out as far as possible, then hold for a few seconds. Repeat 10 times.

Hairstyles

The right hairstyle can make a big difference in how round your face appears. Here are some tips:

Shorter Hair

Shorter hair tends to make faces look rounder, so consider a shorter cut if you have long hair.

Layered Hair

Layered hair can add volume and texture to your hair, which can help make your face appear rounder.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help frame your face and make it appear rounder.

Makeup Techniques

Makeup can be a powerful tool in creating the illusion of a rounder face. Here are some techniques to try:

Contouring

Contouring involves using makeup to create shadows and highlights on your face, which can help create the illusion of a rounder face. Apply a darker shade of foundation or contour powder to the hollows of your cheeks and blend well.

Blush

Applying blush to the apples of your cheeks can help create a rounder, more youthful appearance.

Diet and Lifestyle Changes

While there's no magic pill to make your face rounder, making certain diet and lifestyle changes can help. Here are some tips:

Drink Plenty of Water

Staying hydrated can help keep your skin looking plump and healthy.

Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and lean protein can help you maintain a healthy weight and keep your skin looking healthy.

FAQs

Q: Can facial exercises really make my face rounder?

A: While facial exercises won't change the bone structure of your face, they can help tone and strengthen the muscles, which can help create a more defined and rounder appearance.

Q: Will contouring make my face look unnatural?

A: Contouring can be tricky, so start with a light hand and build up slowly. Make sure to blend well to avoid any harsh lines.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair, regardless of the style or length. Regular washing, conditioning, and trimming can go a long way in keeping your hair healthy and looking its best.
