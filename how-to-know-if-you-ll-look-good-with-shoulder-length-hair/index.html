How to Know If You'll Look Good with Shoulder Length Hair

If you're considering a change in hairstyle and are wondering if shoulder length hair is the right choice for you, there are a few things to consider. In this article, we'll go over how to know if you'll look good with shoulder length hair and provide you with some tips on how to care for your new style.

Table of Contents

  • Face Shape
  • Hair Type
  • Lifestyle
  • Tips for Shoulder Length Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

Face Shape

One of the most important things to consider when deciding on a new hairstyle is your face shape. Certain hairstyles look better on certain face shapes, so it's important to choose a style that flatters your features. If you have an oval, heart, or square-shaped face, shoulder length hair can be a great choice. If you have a round or oblong-shaped face, however, you may want to consider a different style.

Hair Type

Another factor to consider is your hair type. If you have thick, straight hair, shoulder length hair can create a beautiful, sleek look. If you have curly or wavy hair, shoulder length hair can give you a more relaxed, beachy vibe. If you have fine hair, however, you may want to consider a shorter style that adds volume and dimension.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is also an important consideration when choosing a new hairstyle. If you lead an active lifestyle and don't have a lot of time to spend on your hair, shoulder length hair can be a great choice. It's easy to style and maintain, and it can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Tips for Shoulder Length Hair

If you've decided that shoulder length hair is the right choice for you, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling with heat tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and dimension.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is shoulder length hair easy to style?

Yes, shoulder length hair is generally easy to style and maintain. It can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion, and it's a versatile length that can work for a variety of hair types and face shapes.

What should I consider before getting shoulder length hair?

Before getting shoulder length hair, you should consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. You should also talk to your hairstylist to get their professional opinion and advice.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair

To keep your shoulder length hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.


Now that you know how to know if you'll look good with shoulder length hair, you can make an informed decision about your next hairstyle. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
