Best Short Hair For Narrow Faces Tips And Styles

Written by Daniel Apr 13, 2023 · 7 min read

Having a narrow face can make it challenging to find the right hairstyle. But with the right cut and style, you can accentuate your features and make your face look fuller. In this article, we'll explore the best short haircuts for narrow faces, how to style them, and tips for hair care.

  • Best Short Haircuts for Narrow Faces

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Narrow Faces

Before choosing a short haircut for your narrow face, it's important to determine your face shape. If you have a narrow face, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width. Here are some tips to help you choose the right haircut:

  • Look for haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face, such as layers, waves, and curls.
  • Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look even narrower.
  • Consider a side part or a fringe to create the illusion of width.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Best Short Haircuts for Narrow Faces

Here are some of the best short haircuts for narrow faces:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well for narrow faces. It adds volume to the sides of your face and draws attention to your eyes and cheekbones. You can style it with a side part, a fringe, or messy layers.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular short haircut that flatters narrow faces. A chin-length bob with layers or waves can add volume to your face and make it look wider. You can also try a bob with an asymmetrical or angled cut to create the illusion of width.

Short Shag

The short shag is a trendy and versatile haircut that can add texture and volume to your hair. It works well for narrow faces because it creates a tousled, messy look that adds width to your face. You can style it with a side part, curtain bangs, or choppy layers.

How to Style Short Hair for Narrow Faces

Once you've chosen the right short haircut for your narrow face, it's time to style it. Here are some step-by-step tips:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume.
  • Use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls, depending on your haircut and desired style.
  • Finish with a texturizing spray or pomade to add texture and hold.

Tips for Hair Care

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut in shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage and dry out your strands.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear bangs with a short haircut for narrow faces?

A: Yes, bangs can be a great way to add width to your face and balance out your features. Consider a side-swept fringe or curtain bangs that graze your cheekbones.

Q: What hair color works best for narrow faces?

A: Any hair color can work for narrow faces, but lighter shades can create the illusion of volume and width. Consider highlights, balayage, or ombre to add dimension to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. Use your judgment and listen to your hair's needs.

With the right short haircut and styling techniques, you can enhance your features and make your narrow face look fuller. Experiment with different cuts, styles, and colors to find the one that suits you best. And don't forget to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
