How To Blend Short Sides With Long Top A Complete Guide

Blending short sides with a long top is a popular hairstyle that has been around for a while. It's a great way to have a versatile look that you can dress up or down depending on the occasion. But achieving this look can be a bit tricky if you don't know what you're doing. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps on how to blend short sides with a long top.

How to Blend Short Sides with Long Top

Blending short sides with long top requires some skills and techniques. To achieve a well-blended hairstyle, you need to ensure that the length transition from short to long is seamless. Here are the steps to follow:

1. Decide on the length of the sides and the top

The first step is to decide on the length of the sides and the top. Typically, the sides should be shaved or trimmed short using clippers, while the top is left long. You can decide how short or long you want the sides to be, but we recommend going with a 1 or 2 clipper guard length for a cleaner look.

2. Use a comb to blend the sides and the top

Once you've decided on the length of the sides and the top, it's time to blend them together. Use a comb to create a seamless transition from the sides to the top. Start by combing the hair on the sides upwards and towards the top. Then, use a pair of scissors to trim any stray hairs that are sticking out.

3. Trim the top to your desired length

Now that you've blended the sides and the top, it's time to trim the top to your desired length. Use a pair of scissors to cut the hair evenly across the top. Make sure to leave some length at the front so that you can style it later.

4. Style your hair

Once you've trimmed the top to your desired length, it's time to style your hair. You can use a hair product such as pomade or wax to create a sleek and polished look. Alternatively, you can leave it natural for a more relaxed look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to blend short sides with long top:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt or oil that may be on your hair and scalp.

Step 2: Decide on the length of the sides and the top

Decide on the length of the sides and the top. Use clippers to shave or trim the sides to your desired length. We recommend using a 1 or 2 clipper guard length for a cleaner look.

Step 3: Comb your hair

Use a comb to comb your hair upwards and towards the top. This will help blend the sides with the top.

Step 4: Trim any stray hairs

Use a pair of scissors to trim any stray hairs that are sticking out. This will help create a seamless transition from the sides to the top.

Step 5: Trim the top to your desired length

Use a pair of scissors to cut the hair evenly across the top. Leave some length at the front so that you can style it later.

Step 6: Style your hair

Use a hair product such as pomade or wax to create a sleek and polished look. Alternatively, you can leave it natural for a more relaxed look.

Tips for Blending Short Sides with Long Top

Here are some tips to help you achieve a well-blended hairstyle:

1. Use the right tools

Use clippers and scissors to trim your hair. Make sure that they are sharp and in good condition to prevent any uneven cuts.

2. Start with longer hair

If you're not sure how short you want your hair to be, start with longer hair and gradually trim it down. This will help you avoid any mistakes.

3. Don't rush

Take your time when trimming your hair. Rushing can lead to uneven cuts and an unbalanced hairstyle.

4. Experiment with different styles

Try different hair products and styles to find the one that works best for you. You can also ask your barber or stylist for recommendations.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I trim my hair?

We recommend trimming your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and well-maintained.

2. Can I blend short sides with long top at home?

Yes, you can blend short sides with long top at home. However, if you're not confident in your skills, we recommend going to a barber or stylist for a professional cut.

3. What hair products should I use?

You can use hair products such as pomade or wax to create a sleek and polished look. Alternatively, you can leave it natural for a more relaxed look.

Hair Care for Blending Short Sides with Long Top

To keep your hair looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Use a hair mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.

3. Avoid heat styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

4. Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as it's less damaging than a brush.

Blending short sides with long top is a stylish and versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. By following our guide, you can achieve a well-blended look that will turn heads. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
