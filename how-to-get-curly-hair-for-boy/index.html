How To Get Curly Hair For Boys Tips Steps And Hair Care

Written by Eveline Apr 08, 2023 · 11 min read

Curly hair is becoming more popular for boys, and achieving this look is easier than you think. Whether you have naturally straight hair or wavy hair, you can learn how to get curly hair for boy with the right techniques and products. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide to get curly hair for boy and some essential hair care tips to keep your curls looking healthy and shiny.

  • Steps to Get Curly Hair for Boys
  • Tips to Maintain Curly Hair for Boys
  • FAQ about Curly Hair for Boys
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair Boys

How to Get Curly Hair for Boys

Before we start with the steps, it is essential to understand that not every boy can achieve curly hair. If you have naturally straight hair, it might be challenging to get tight curls. However, if your hair is slightly wavy, you can achieve a curly look with some effort. Here are some ways to get curly hair for boy:

1. Use a Curling Iron

The easiest way to get curly hair for boy is to use a curling iron. This tool can help you achieve tight curls or loose waves, depending on the size of the barrel. Here’s how to use a curling iron:

  1. Wash your hair and let it dry naturally or use a blow dryer to dry it.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and start curling each section from the bottom to the top.
  4. Hold the curling iron for 10-15 seconds, depending on the heat level.
  5. Release the curl and let it cool down before moving to the next section.
  6. Once you’ve curled all your hair, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a natural look.

Remember to set the curling iron to the right temperature to avoid damaging your hair. If you have thin or fine hair, use a lower heat setting, and if you have thick hair, use a higher heat setting.

2. Use a Perm

If you want to get long-lasting curls, you can try a perm. A perm is a chemical treatment that can change the texture of your hair and create curls or waves. However, this process can be damaging to your hair, so it is essential to consult a professional stylist before trying it.

3. Braid Your Hair

You can also get curly hair for boy by braiding your hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and let it dry naturally or use a blow dryer to dry it.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and braid each section tightly.
  3. Leave the braids in for a few hours or overnight.
  4. Undo the braids and use your fingers to loosen the curls.

This method can give you loose waves or natural-looking curls, depending on how tightly you braid your hair.

Steps to Get Curly Hair for Boys

Now that you know how to get curly hair for boy let’s take a look at the step-by-step process:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. These products are specially formulated to enhance your natural curls and add volume and definition to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Curl-Enhancing Product

After washing your hair, apply a curl-enhancing product such as a mousse or gel. These products can help define your curls and keep them in place throughout the day.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and use it to dry your hair. A diffuser helps distribute the heat evenly and can prevent your curls from becoming frizzy. Use a low heat setting and move the diffuser around your hair to dry it completely.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is completely dry, use your fingers to style your curls. You can use a curling iron or a wand to create tight curls or loose waves. Alternatively, you can braid your hair and leave the braids in for a few hours or overnight to get natural-looking curls.

Tips to Maintain Curly Hair for Boys

Getting curly hair for boy is one thing, but maintaining it is another. Here are some tips to keep your curls looking healthy and shiny:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Avoid using a brush on your curly hair as it can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls gently.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every few months to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth. This can help your curls look more defined and bouncy.

3. Deep Condition Your Hair

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy. This can prevent dryness and frizz and make your curls more manageable.

FAQ about Curly Hair for Boys

1. Can boys with straight hair get curly hair?

Yes, boys with straight hair can get curly hair using a curling iron or a perm. However, the results might not be as tight as natural curls.

2. Is it okay to blow dry curly hair?

Yes, you can blow dry your curly hair, but make sure to use a diffuser and a low heat setting to prevent frizz and damage.

3. How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, wash it once or twice a week. If you have oily hair or sweat a lot, you might need to wash it more frequently.

Hair Care for Curly Hair Boys

Here are some hair care tips for curly hair boys:

1. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Limit the use of heat styling tools as they can damage your hair and make your curls frizzy. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize the damage.

2. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Switch to a silk pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage while you sleep. This can help your curls stay in place and prevent frizz.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to protect your curls from the harmful UV rays. This can prevent dryness and damage to your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can get curly hair for boy and keep it looking healthy and shiny. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and styling methods to find what works best for you. Good luck!
