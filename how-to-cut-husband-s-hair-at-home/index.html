How To Cut Your Husband s Hair At Home In 2023

Written by Daniel Mar 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of spending money on your husband's haircuts at the salon? Why not try cutting his hair at home? With a few simple steps, some patience, and the right tools, you can give your husband a stylish haircut without ever leaving your house. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut husband's hair at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Husband's Hair at Home
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for a Successful Haircut

How to Cut Husband's Hair at Home

To cut your husband's hair at home, you will need a few essential tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Cape or towel
  • Mirror

Once you have these tools, you are ready to give your husband a haircut.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Hair

Before you begin, make sure your husband's hair is clean and dry. Wet hair is more challenging to cut, and it will not give you the desired results. After washing, towel-dry his hair and comb it to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clipper Guard

Clipper guards come in different sizes, and each size will give you a different length of hair. Choose the right clipper guard for the length of hair your husband wants. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one if needed.

Step 3: Start at the Nape of the Neck

Begin cutting at the nape of the neck and work your way up. Use the clippers to create an even cut. Make sure to cut against the grain of the hair to get a closer cut. Use the comb to lift the hair and make sure you are cutting the same length all around.

Step 4: Move to the Sides and Top

Once you have finished the back, move to the sides and top of the head. Use the same technique as before, cutting against the grain of the hair. Use the comb to lift the hair and make sure you are cutting the same length all around. Be careful when cutting around the ears.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Use the scissors to blend the hair on the top and sides. Cut any longer hairs that stick out and make sure the hair is even all around. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut along the comb to ensure an even cut.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Use the clippers to clean up the neckline. Create a straight line across the neck, following the natural hairline. Use the scissors to trim any longer hairs that stick out.

Step 7: Trim the Sideburns and Around the Ears

Use the scissors to trim the sideburns to the desired length. Be careful not to cut too much off. Use the clippers to trim around the ears, making sure to follow the natural curve of the ear.

Tips for a Successful Haircut

  • Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down to a shorter one if needed.
  • Don't rush the haircut. Take your time to ensure you are getting an even cut.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and cut along the comb to ensure an even cut.
  • Be careful when cutting around the ears and the neckline.
  • If you are unsure, start with a shorter length and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head and make sure it is even.

FAQs

Can I use regular scissors to cut hair?

No, regular scissors are not sharp enough to cut hair. You need hair-cutting scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair.

How often should I cut my husband's hair?

It depends on how fast his hair grows and the style he wants. On average, every 4-6 weeks is a good time frame to cut hair.

What do I do if I make a mistake while cutting his hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim more hair off to fix it. If it is a significant mistake, you can always take him to a professional to fix it.

Hair Care After the Haircut

After the haircut, make sure to clean up any loose hair around the neck and ears. Wash the hair to remove any loose hair and style as desired. Your husband's hair may be shorter than he is used to, so make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep his hair healthy.

Conclusion

Cutting your husband's hair at home may seem daunting, but with these simple steps, you can give him a stylish haircut without ever leaving your house. Make sure to have the right tools, take your time, and be patient. With practice, you can become an expert at cutting hair at home.
