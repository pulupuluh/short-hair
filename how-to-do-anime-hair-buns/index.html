How To Do Anime Hair Buns A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a fan of anime, then you've probably seen some of your favorite characters sporting cute and stylish hair buns. But have you ever wondered how to do anime hair buns yourself? In this guide, we'll show you how to create these popular hairstyles step-by-step.

Description

Anime hair buns are a popular hairstyle in the world of anime and manga. They are often worn by female characters and are characterized by their cute and playful look. Anime hair buns can be worn in a variety of ways, from two simple buns on top of the head to more elaborate styles with braids and twists.

How to Do Anime Hair Buns: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you begin creating your anime hair buns, you'll need to prepare your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair to make sure it's clean and soft. Then, towel-dry your hair and brush it to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Next, divide your hair into two equal sections. You can do this by parting your hair down the middle or by using hair ties to create two ponytails on top of your head.

Step 3: Create Your Buns

Take one section of hair and twist it into a bun. You can do this by wrapping the hair around itself and securing it with bobby pins. Repeat this process with the other section of hair.

Step 4: Secure Your Buns

Once you've created your buns, make sure to secure them with bobby pins or hair ties to keep them in place. You can also use hairspray or hair gel to help hold your buns in place.

Tips for Creating Anime Hair Buns

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect anime hair buns:

  • Use bobby pins or hair ties to secure your buns.
  • Consider adding braids or twists to your buns for a more elaborate look.
  • Use hairspray or hair gel to help hold your buns in place.
  • Experiment with different styles and sizes of buns to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

What type of hair works best for anime hair buns?

Any type of hair can be used to create anime hair buns. However, longer hair will give you more options for different styles and sizes of buns.

Can I create anime hair buns with short hair?

Yes, you can still create anime hair buns with short hair. You may need to use hair extensions or hairpieces to create the desired look.

How long will it take to create anime hair buns?

The time it takes to create anime hair buns will depend on the complexity of the style and your level of experience. Simple buns can be created in just a few minutes, while more elaborate styles may take upwards of an hour.

Hair Care for Anime Hair Buns

To keep your anime hair buns looking their best, make sure to take good care of your hair. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using heat protectant products when styling your hair, and avoiding excessive heat styling.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create cute and stylish anime hair buns in no time. So go ahead and give it a try!
