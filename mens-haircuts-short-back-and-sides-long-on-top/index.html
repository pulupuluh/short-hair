Mens Haircuts Short Back And Sides Long On Top

Written by Frank Apr 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

In this year 2023, mens haircuts short back and sides long on top still remain popular. This classic style is versatile and can be customized to suit individual preferences. Whether you're looking for a professional look, a casual style, or something in between, this haircut can deliver. In this blog post, we'll cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, from how to get it to how to maintain it.

Description

The mens haircuts short back and sides long on top is a hairstyle where the sides and back are cut short, while the top is left longer. This style is also known as a disconnected undercut, as the top and sides are disconnected. The length of the top can vary depending on personal preference and face shape. This style can be worn with or without a part, and can be styled with a variety of products.

How to

If you're interested in getting a mens haircut short back and sides long on top, the first step is to find a skilled barber or stylist who is experienced in this style. Once you've found someone you trust, follow these steps:

Step 1: Consultation

During your consultation, discuss your desired length and style with your barber or stylist. They can offer suggestions based on your hair type and face shape, and help you choose the best length and style for you.

Step 2: Cutting the Sides and Back

The sides and back are typically cut with clippers, using a guard that corresponds to the desired length. The barber or stylist will start at the bottom and work their way up, blending the hair as they go.

Step 3: Cutting the Top

The top is typically cut with scissors, using the point-cutting technique to add texture and movement. The length of the top can vary depending on personal preference, but it's usually left long enough to be styled in a variety of ways.

Step 4: Styling

After the cut is complete, your barber or stylist will style your hair using a product of your choice. This can be anything from pomade to wax to clay, depending on the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you're considering a mens haircut short back and sides long on top:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape
  • Use high-quality styling products to achieve your desired look
  • Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens haircuts short back and sides long on top:

What is the best product to use for this style?

The best product to use will depend on your hair type and the desired look. Pomade is a popular choice for a slick, polished look, while wax or clay can be used to achieve a more textured, messy look.

Can this style work for all hair types?

Yes, this style can work for all hair types, but the length and styling products used may vary depending on the hair type.

Hair Care

Maintaining a mens haircut short back and sides long on top requires regular trims and proper hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, as they can damage the hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and other elements by wearing a hat or using a hair product with UV protection

With proper care and maintenance, a mens haircut short back and sides long on top can be a stylish and versatile choice for any man.
