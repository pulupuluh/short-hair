Can You Grow A Man Bun With Thick Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jul 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have thick hair and love the man bun style, you might be wondering if it's possible to grow a man bun with your hair type. The answer is yes, you can grow a man bun with thick hair, but there are a few things to keep in mind. In this article, we'll explore how to grow a man bun with thick hair, offer tips and tricks, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

A man bun is a popular hairstyle that involves pulling the hair back into a bun at the top of the head. The style has been around for centuries and has been embraced by men of all ages and cultures. However, growing a man bun with thick hair can be challenging, as the hair is often heavy and difficult to manage. Nevertheless, there are ways to grow a man bun with thick hair and make it look great.

How to Grow a Man Bun with Thick Hair

The first step in growing a man bun with thick hair is to let your hair grow out. This may take several months or even a year, depending on how fast your hair grows. During this time, you'll want to avoid cutting your hair and allow it to grow as long as possible. Once your hair has grown out, you can start styling it into a man bun.

To style your hair into a man bun, you'll need to brush your hair back and gather it into a ponytail at the top of your head. You can then twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with an elastic band or hair tie. You may also want to use hairspray or gel to keep the bun in place.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Let your hair grow out.
  2. Brush your hair back and gather it into a ponytail at the top of your head.
  3. Twist the ponytail into a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with an elastic band or hair tie.
  5. Use hairspray or gel to keep the bun in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for growing a man bun with thick hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling it.
  • Apply a conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep it healthy and strong.
  • Trim the ends of your hair every few months to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair dryer to dry your hair before styling it, as wet hair is more difficult to manage.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories, such as headbands and bandanas, to find a look that suits you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing a man bun with thick hair:

Q: How long does it take to grow a man bun with thick hair?

A: It can take several months to a year to grow a man bun with thick hair, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Q: Is it necessary to use hairspray or gel to keep the man bun in place?

A: It's not necessary to use hairspray or gel, but it can help keep the man bun in place and prevent flyaways.

Q: Can I still use hair products, such as pomade and wax, with a man bun?

A: Yes, you can still use hair products with a man bun. Just be sure to apply them sparingly and avoid applying them to the roots of your hair, as this can weigh down the hair and make the bun look flat.

Hair Care

Growing a man bun with thick hair requires good hair care. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat-styling tools, as they can damage the hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals, such as protein, zinc, and biotin, which can help promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can grow a man bun with thick hair and look great while doing it. So go ahead and embrace the man bun trend!
