How To Make Your Hair Grow Faster And Longer Naturally Home Remedy

Written by Eveline Jun 28, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of having slow hair growth? Do you want to achieve longer and healthier locks without spending too much money on expensive treatments? If yes, then you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll share with you some effective home remedies to make your hair grow faster and longer naturally.

Table of Content

  1. How to Make Your Hair Grow Faster and Longer Naturally Home Remedy
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips to Grow Your Hair Faster
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

Step by Step Guide

Here are some easy and effective home remedies to make your hair grow faster and longer:

1. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp with your fingertips increases blood flow to your hair follicles, which promotes hair growth. You can use coconut oil or olive oil for this massage. Massage your scalp for at least 5 minutes every day.

2. Apply Onion Juice

Onion juice is rich in sulfur, which promotes collagen production and hair growth. Blend an onion and extract its juice. Apply the juice to your scalp and hair. Leave it on for 30 minutes and then rinse it off with a mild shampoo.

3. Use Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera contains proteolytic enzymes that repair dead skin cells on your scalp. This promotes hair growth and prevents dandruff. Apply fresh Aloe Vera gel to your scalp and hair. Leave it on for 30 minutes and then rinse with a mild shampoo.

4. Apply Egg Mask

Eggs are rich in protein, which promotes hair growth. Beat an egg and add a tablespoon of olive oil. Apply the mixture to your hair and scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes and then rinse it off with a mild shampoo.

5. Use Fenugreek Seeds

Fenugreek seeds are rich in protein and nicotinic acid, which promotes hair growth. Soak 2-3 tablespoons of fenugreek seeds in water overnight. In the morning, grind them into a paste and apply it to your scalp and hair. Leave it on for 30 minutes and then rinse it off with a mild shampoo.

Tips to Grow Your Hair Faster

Here are some additional tips to make your hair grow faster:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a healthy and balanced diet is essential for healthy hair growth. Include foods that are rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and slow down hair growth. Avoid using them as much as possible.

3. Don’t Wash Your Hair Too Often

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can slow down hair growth. Try to wash your hair every other day.

FAQs

1. How long does it take for hair to grow?

Hair typically grows about half an inch per month. However, the rate of hair growth can vary depending on factors like genetics, age, and overall health.

2. Can stress affect hair growth?

Yes, stress can affect hair growth. When you’re stressed, your body produces cortisol, which can damage hair follicles and slow down hair growth.

3. Does trimming your hair make it grow faster?

No, trimming your hair doesn’t make it grow faster. However, it can prevent split ends, which can lead to breakage and slower hair growth.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to promote healthy hair growth:

1. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Using a wide-toothed comb can prevent hair breakage and promote healthy hair growth.

2. Protect Your Hair from Sun Damage

Exposure to the sun’s UV rays can damage your hair and slow down hair growth. Protect your hair by wearing a hat or using a protective hair spray.

3. Don’t Brush Your Hair When It’s Wet

Wet hair is more prone to breakage than dry hair. Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet.

By following these home remedies and hair care tips, you can achieve longer and healthier locks in no time. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine. Happy growing!
