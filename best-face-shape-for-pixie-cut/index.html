Best Face Shapes for Pixie Cuts

Pixie cuts are a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle that can complement various face shapes. However, some face shapes are more suited for pixie cuts than others. The best face shapes for pixie cuts are:

  • Oval face shape
  • Heart face shape
  • Square face shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle, including pixie cuts. Heart-shaped faces have a wider forehead and a narrower chin, making a short pixie cut an ideal way to balance out the proportions of the face. Square face shapes benefit from pixie cuts that add volume on top and soften the jawline.

How to Choose the Perfect Pixie Cut Style

Choosing the right pixie cut style for your face shape and hair type is crucial in achieving a flattering and stylish look. Here are some tips on how to choose the perfect pixie cut:

  • Consider your face shape: As mentioned earlier, some face shapes are more suited for pixie cuts than others. Determine your face shape and consult with a hairstylist on the best pixie cut style for you.
  • Take into account your hair type: Pixie cuts require minimal maintenance, but they can look drastically different depending on your hair type. Thick hair can handle more volume and texture, while fine hair benefits from shorter and more layered styles.
  • Think about your lifestyle: Pixie cuts are perfect for those who prefer low-maintenance and easy-to-style hair. However, some styles require more upkeep than others, so consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Pixie Cut

Now that you've decided on the perfect pixie cut style for you, it's time to head to the salon and get your hair transformed! Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a pixie cut:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist on the best pixie cut style for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Wash your hair and arrive at the salon with clean and dry hair.
  3. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  4. Start with the back section and cut the hair to the desired length.
  5. Move on to the sides and cut the hair accordingly.
  6. Finish with the top section and add volume and texture as desired.
  7. Style your hair with a texturizing spray or wax.
  8. Enjoy your new pixie cut!

Pixie Cut Hair Care Tips

Now that you have a new pixie cut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and texture. Here are some hair care tips for pixie cuts:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to add moisture and shine to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Visit the salon every six to eight weeks for a trim to maintain the shape of your pixie cut.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or clips, to add variety to your pixie cut.

FAQs About Pixie Cuts

1. Will a pixie cut suit my face shape?

If you have an oval, heart, or square face shape, a pixie cut will likely suit you. However, it's important to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best pixie cut style for your face shape and hair type.

2. Will a pixie cut make my hair look thinner?

No, a pixie cut can actually make your hair look thicker and fuller, especially if you have fine hair. The short layers and volume on top create the illusion of thicker hair.

3. How often should I get a trim for my pixie cut?

It's recommended to visit the salon every six to eight weeks for a trim to maintain the shape of your pixie cut.

With these tips and tricks, you're now ready to rock a stylish and flattering pixie cut. Remember, the key to a successful pixie cut is choosing the right style for your face shape and hair type and maintaining it with proper hair care. Happy styling!
