Does Long Hair Make You Look Skinnier

Written by William Aug 08, 2023 · 9 min read

Long hair has always been a symbol of beauty and femininity. It can also be a great way to enhance your features and make you stand out from the crowd. But does long hair make you look skinnier? In this blog post, we will answer this question and provide you with some tips on how to make the most of your long locks.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Many people believe that long hair can make you look skinnier, and there may be some truth to this statement. Long hair can elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer silhouette. This is especially true if you have a round or square face shape, as long hair can soften your features and make you look more delicate.

However, it is important to note that the effectiveness of this trick depends on your hair type and the way you style it. If your hair is thin and limp, long hair may not be the best choice for you, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. On the other hand, if you have thick and voluminous hair, long hair can add to the fullness and make you look wider.

How to

If you want to use long hair to make yourself look skinnier, there are a few things you can do to achieve this effect:

1. Keep your hair straight

Straight hair can make your face look slimmer and longer. Use a flat iron or blow dryer to straighten your hair, or visit a salon for a professional straightening treatment.

2. Add layers

Layers can add movement and texture to your hair, making it look lighter and more voluminous. Ask your stylist to add some layers to your hair, especially around your face and neck.

3. Avoid heavy bangs

Bangs can be a great way to frame your face, but heavy bangs can make your face look wider. Opt for wispy or side-swept bangs instead.

4. Use hair extensions

If your hair is too thin or short for the long hair look, you can use hair extensions to add length and volume. Make sure to choose extensions that match your hair color and texture, and follow the instructions carefully to avoid damaging your hair.

Step by step

Here are some steps you can follow to make your long hair work for you:

1. Wash and condition your hair

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to cleanse and moisturize your hair. Rinse thoroughly and squeeze out excess water.

2. Apply a heat protectant

Before using any heating tools, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage and breakage.

3. Straighten your hair

Use a flat iron or blow dryer to straighten your hair, working in small sections. Make sure to apply even pressure and move the tool slowly to avoid creating kinks or waves.

4. Add layers

Ask your stylist to add some layers to your hair, focusing on the areas around your face and neck.

5. Style your hair

You can leave your hair straight or create some waves or curls with a curling iron or hot rollers. Use a hairspray or mousse to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and features.
  • Avoid hairstyles that require a lot of maintenance, as they can be time-consuming and damaging to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

FAQ

Does long hair make you look older?

Long hair can make you look younger or older, depending on how you style it. If you wear your hair in a youthful, trendy way, it can make you look more youthful. On the other hand, if you wear your hair in a dated or matronly style, it can make you look older.

Can long hair make your face look wider?

If you have thick and voluminous hair, long hair can add to the fullness and make you look wider. However, if you have thin and limp hair, long hair can elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer silhouette.

Hair Care

To keep your long hair looking healthy and beautiful, it is important to follow a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking fresh.

In conclusion, long hair can make you look skinnier if styled correctly. Keep your hair straight, add layers, and avoid heavy bangs to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
