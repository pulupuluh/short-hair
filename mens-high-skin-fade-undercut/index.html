Mens High Skin Fade Undercut The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Jun 18, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and modern haircut, then the mens high skin fade undercut might just be what you need. This trendy hairstyle has been popular for a while now and it's not hard to see why. It's versatile, low maintenance, and suitable for all hair types. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about the mens high skin fade undercut, including how to get it, tips for styling, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Mens High Skin Fade Undercut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting a High Skin Fade Undercut
  • Tips for Styling a Mens High Skin Fade Undercut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for a Mens High Skin Fade Undercut

Description

The mens high skin fade undercut is a stylish haircut that involves cutting the sides and back of the head very short or even shaved, while leaving longer hair on top. The hair on top is usually styled in a textured or messy way, and can be swept to the side or slicked back. This haircut is perfect for men who want to look fresh, modern, and edgy.

How to Get a Mens High Skin Fade Undercut

The best way to get a mens high skin fade undercut is to go to a professional barber or hairstylist. They will have the skills and knowledge to give you the perfect haircut. However, if you're feeling brave, you can try cutting it yourself at home. Here's how:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, comb, and a mirror.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want for the hair on top and the skin fade. You can use a number guide to help you with this. A number 1 guard is about 1/8 inch, a number 2 is 1/4 inch, and so on.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Use the clippers with the desired guard to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head. Start from the bottom and work your way up, blending the hair as you go.

Step 4: Work on the Top

Use the scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. You can use a razor or thinning shears to add texture if you want.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Style the hair on top with your preferred product. You can use wax, pomade, or gel to achieve different looks.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting a High Skin Fade Undercut

If you're not confident in cutting your own hair or want to learn how to do it professionally, here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Wash the hair and towel dry it. Make sure it's completely dry before starting the haircut.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Section the hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Start with the sides and back of the head. Use a clipper with the desired guard to cut the hair. Start from the bottom and work your way up, blending the hair as you go.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. You can use a razor or thinning shears to add texture if you want.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top. Use scissors or clippers to blend the hair and create a seamless transition.

Step 6: Style the Hair

Style the hair on top with your preferred product. You can use wax, pomade, or gel to achieve different looks.

Tips for Styling a Mens High Skin Fade Undercut

Styling a mens high skin fade undercut is easy, but there are a few tips that can help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Use a hair dryer to add volume and texture to the hair on top.
  • Apply the product evenly to avoid clumps or flakes.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to create the desired style.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and switch it up every once in a while.

FAQ

What face shapes suit a mens high skin fade undercut?

A mens high skin fade undercut can suit most face shapes. However, it's best for men with oval, square, or diamond-shaped faces.

How often should I get a haircut for a mens high skin fade undercut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep the sides. Generally, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the look.

Can I style a mens high skin fade undercut with curly or wavy hair?

Yes, you can style a mens high skin fade undercut with curly or wavy hair. Just make sure to use a product that works well with your hair type and texture.

Hair Care for a Mens High Skin Fade Undercut

Keeping your hair healthy and clean is important for maintaining a mens high skin fade undercut. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can build up and make your hair look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.
  • Trim the ends of your hair every few weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and health.

Overall, a mens high skin fade undercut is a stylish and modern haircut that can suit any man. Whether you go to a professional barber or do it yourself at home, make sure to follow these tips and take care of your hair to maintain the look.
