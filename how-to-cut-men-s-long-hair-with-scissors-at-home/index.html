How To Cut Men s Long Hair With Scissors At Home

Written by Frank May 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a guy with long hair who's looking to trim your locks at home? With the right tools and techniques, you can give yourself a great haircut without having to go to a salon. In this article, we'll show you how to cut men's long hair with scissors at home.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • How to Cut Men's Long Hair with Scissors at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Men's Long Hair with Scissors at Home
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting Men's Long Hair with Scissors at Home

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather a few tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips or elastics
  • Mirror
  • Spray bottle with water

How to Cut Men's Long Hair with Scissors at Home

The first step is to decide on the length you want your hair to be. If you're not sure, start by trimming a little bit at a time until you reach the desired length.

Once you've decided on the length, follow these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Divide your hair into sections using clips or elastics.
  4. Start with one section and hold it between your fingers.
  5. Using the comb, comb the hair downwards and hold it taut.
  6. Using the scissors, cut the hair in a straight line, following the comb downwards.
  7. Repeat steps 4-6 with each section of hair.
  8. Check your work in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Men's Long Hair with Scissors at Home

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as it can be difficult to fix mistakes.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp, as dull scissors can damage your hair.
  • Use a spray bottle with water to dampen your hair if it's difficult to comb through.
  • Take breaks if you need to, as cutting your own hair can be tiring.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair with regular scissors?

While you can technically cut your hair with regular scissors, it's best to use hair-cutting scissors, as they're designed to cut hair cleanly and evenly.

How often should I cut my long hair?

How often you cut your hair depends on how fast it grows and your personal preferences. Some people prefer to trim their hair every few months, while others may do it more frequently.

Is it better to cut my hair wet or dry?

It's generally easier to cut wet hair, as it's easier to comb through and more uniform in texture. However, some people may prefer to cut their hair dry, as it allows them to see the shape of the cut more clearly.

Hair Care After Cutting Men's Long Hair with Scissors at Home

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to prevent tangles.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Consider using hair products, such as pomade or wax, to style your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great after cutting it yourself at home.
