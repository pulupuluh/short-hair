Popular Men s Short Haircuts 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia May 23, 2023 Â· 13 min read

Short haircuts have always been a popular choice among men, and 2021 is no exception. With the pandemic still affecting our daily lives, many men have opted for low-maintenance hairstyles that are easy to maintain and style at home. In this article, we will discuss the most popular men's short haircuts of 2021, how to style them, and tips for maintaining them.

Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic short haircut that is achieved by using clippers to trim the hair to a uniform length all over the head. It is a low-maintenance haircut that requires little to no styling, making it a popular choice for men who prefer a fuss-free hairstyle. The buzz cut is also versatile and can be styled in different ways depending on the occasion.

To maintain a buzz cut, it is recommended to have it trimmed every 2-4 weeks. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy.

Crew Cut

The crew cut is a short haircut that is longer on top and shorter on the sides and back. It is a versatile haircut that can be styled in different ways, making it a popular choice among men of all ages. The crew cut is easy to maintain and can be styled with a side part or slicked back for a more polished look.

To maintain a crew cut, it is recommended to have it trimmed every 3-4 weeks. Use a good quality pomade or wax to style the hair and keep it in place throughout the day.

Ivy League

The Ivy League haircut is a classic short hairstyle that is longer on top and tapered on the sides and back. It is a popular choice among men who want a professional-looking haircut that is easy to maintain. The Ivy League can be styled in different ways, making it a versatile choice for any occasion.

To maintain an Ivy League haircut, it is recommended to have it trimmed every 4-6 weeks. Use a good quality styling cream or pomade to add texture and volume to the hair.

French Crop

The French Crop is a short haircut that is longer on top and tapered on the sides and back. It is characterized by a short fringe or bangs that are styled forward. The French Crop is a popular choice among men who want a modern and stylish haircut that is easy to maintain.

To maintain a French Crop, it is recommended to have it trimmed every 3-4 weeks. Use a good quality styling cream or pomade to add texture and volume to the hair.

Fade Haircut

The Fade Haircut is a short haircut that is tapered on the sides and back, gradually getting shorter towards the nape of the neck. It is a popular choice among men who want a modern and stylish haircut that is easy to maintain. The Fade Haircut can be styled in different ways, making it a versatile choice for any occasion.

To maintain a Fade Haircut, it is recommended to have it trimmed every 2-3 weeks. Use a good quality styling cream or pomade to add texture and volume to the hair.

Textured Crop

The Textured Crop is a short haircut that is longer on top and tapered on the sides and back. It is characterized by its textured and messy appearance, making it a popular choice among men who want a modern and effortless-looking haircut. The Textured Crop can be styled in different ways, making it a versatile choice for any occasion.

To maintain a Textured Crop, it is recommended to have it trimmed every 4-6 weeks. Use a good quality sea salt spray or styling cream to add texture and volume to the hair.

Slick Back

The Slick Back is a short haircut that is combed back and styled with a pomade or wax. It is a popular choice among men who want a polished and sophisticated look. The Slick Back can be styled with a side part or a taper fade for a more modern look.

To maintain a Slick Back, it is recommended to have it trimmed every 3-4 weeks. Use a good quality pomade or wax to style the hair and keep it in place throughout the day.

Quiff

The Quiff is a short haircut that is longer on top and styled with a volumizing product. It is a popular choice among men who want a modern and stylish haircut that is easy to maintain. The Quiff can be styled with a side part or a taper fade for a more polished look.

To maintain a Quiff, it is recommended to have it trimmed every 4-6 weeks. Use a good quality volumizing product to add texture and volume to the hair.

Side Part

The Side Part is a short haircut that is combed to one side and styled with a pomade or wax. It is a classic and timeless haircut that is popular among men of all ages. The Side Part can be styled with a taper fade or a hard part for a more modern look.

To maintain a Side Part, it is recommended to have it trimmed every 3-4 weeks. Use a good quality pomade or wax to style the hair and keep it in place throughout the day.

Caesar Cut

The Caesar Cut is a short haircut that is characterized by its short fringe or bangs that are styled forward. It is a popular choice among men who want a low-maintenance and easy-to-style haircut. The Caesar Cut can be styled with a taper fade or a textured crop for a more modern look.

To maintain a Caesar Cut, it is recommended to have it trimmed every 2-3 weeks. Use a good quality styling cream or pomade to add texture and volume to the hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining any hairstyle, especially short haircuts. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type to keep it clean and healthy. Avoid using hot water and harsh chemicals that can damage the hair and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Regular trimming is also important for maintaining a short haircut. Visit your barber or hairstylist every 2-6 weeks depending on your haircut and hair growth rate. Use a good quality styling product that is suitable for your hair type and hairstyle to add texture, volume, and hold to your hair.

FAQ

What is the most popular men's short haircut of 2021?

The most popular men's short haircut of 2021 is the Textured Crop, followed by the Fade Haircut and the Quiff.

How often should I trim my short haircut?

It is recommended to trim your short haircut every 2-6 weeks depending on your haircut and hair growth rate.

What styling products should I use for my short haircut?

Use a good quality styling product that is suitable for your hair type and hairstyle, such as pomade, wax, styling cream, or sea salt spray.

How can I maintain the health of my short hair?

Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type, avoid using hot water and harsh chemicals, and visit your barber or hairstylist regularly for trimming and styling.

In conclusion, there are many popular men's short haircuts of 2021 to choose from, each with its own unique style and personality. Whether you prefer a classic and timeless haircut or a modern and trendy one, there is a short haircut that will suit your taste and lifestyle. By following these tips and advice, you can maintain your short haircut and keep it looking stylish and healthy.
