How To Keep Hair Looking Good At The Beach

Written by Frank Jun 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Heading to the beach is a great way to relax and unwind, but the salty sea water and harsh sun can wreak havoc on your hair. If you're wondering how to keep hair looking good at the beach, you're in luck. With a few simple tips and tricks, you can keep your locks looking their best even after a day in the sun and sand. Here's what you need to know:

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in keeping your hair looking good at the beach is to protect it from the sun. The UV rays can damage hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Use a hair sunscreen or wear a hat to protect your hair and scalp. Additionally, try to avoid exposing your hair to the sun during peak hours from 10 am to 4 pm.

Another way to keep your hair looking good at the beach is to keep it moisturized. Salt water can be extremely drying, so use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your locks hydrated. Apply the product to your hair before heading to the beach and reapply as needed throughout the day.

Step by Step

Step 1: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Apply a hair sunscreen or wear a hat to protect your hair from harmful UV rays.

Step 2: Use a Leave-In Conditioner or Hair Oil

Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair before heading to the beach to keep it moisturized.

Step 3: Rinse Your Hair with Fresh Water

Rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming in the ocean to remove salt and sand.

Step 4: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.

Tips

  • Wear a swim cap to protect your hair from salt water and chlorine in the pool.
  • Try braiding your hair to keep it out of your face and reduce tangles.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup from salt water and hair products.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair while at the beach.

FAQ

How often should I apply leave-in conditioner or hair oil?

You should apply leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair before heading to the beach and reapply as needed throughout the day.

Can I use a regular shampoo and conditioner at the beach?

Yes, you can use a regular shampoo and conditioner at the beach, but it's a good idea to use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup from salt water and hair products.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair looking good at the beach is all about proper hair care. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools. Additionally, try to limit your use of hair products, as they can cause buildup and damage your hair. With a little extra care and attention, you can keep your locks looking their best no matter where your beach vacation takes you.
