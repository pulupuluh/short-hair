If you have curly hair, you know that finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the top mens hairstyles for curly hair in 2020 and how to style and care for your curls.

How to Style Curly Hair

Styling curly hair can be tricky, but with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a great look. Here are some tips:

1. Start with clean, conditioned hair

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, and argan oil.

2. Use a leave-in conditioner

A leave-in conditioner will help keep your curls hydrated and defined. Apply it to damp hair and distribute it evenly with your fingers or a wide-tooth comb.

3. Apply a curl defining product

There are many products on the market that are specifically designed to enhance curls. Look for a curl cream, gel, or mousse that works well for your hair type and texture.

4. Use a diffuser

A diffuser attachment for your blow dryer can help create bouncy, defined curls. Use it on a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair while drying.

5. Don't touch your curls too much

Once you've styled your hair, try not to touch it too much. This can cause frizz and disrupt your curl pattern.

Top Mens Hairstyles for Curly Hair 2020

Now that you know how to style your curly hair, let's take a look at some of the top hairstyles for men in 2020:

1. Short and messy

A short, messy style can be a great option for curly hair. Ask your barber for a textured cut with plenty of layers, and use a styling product to add definition.

2. Medium length with a side part

A medium length cut with a side part can be a classic and sophisticated look for curly hair. Use a styling cream or pomade to keep your curls in place.

3. Long and loose

If you have long curly hair, consider embracing your natural texture with a loose, carefree style. Use a leave-in conditioner and a curl defining product to enhance your curls.

Tips for Curly Hair Care

In addition to styling your hair, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

1. Don't wash your hair too often

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness. Aim to wash your hair once or twice a week.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb

A wide-tooth comb can help detangle your hair without causing breakage. Use it to gently comb your hair when it's wet.

3. Sleep on a silk pillowcase

A silk pillowcase can help reduce friction and prevent frizz. It can also help keep your curls looking defined and bouncy.

FAQs

1. Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair if you want a different look. However, it's important to use a heat protectant and to avoid straightening your hair too often, as this can cause damage.

2. How can I prevent frizz?

To prevent frizz, avoid touching your hair too much, use a leave-in conditioner and a curl defining product, and sleep on a silk pillowcase.

3. Should I cut my curly hair when it's wet or dry?

It's generally recommended to cut curly hair when it's dry, as this allows your stylist to see the natural curl pattern and avoid cutting off too much length.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you can rock your curly hair with confidence. Remember to keep your hair hydrated and use the right products for your hair type, and you'll be sure to have great-looking curls all year round.
