How To Cut Men s Curly Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 20, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of shelling out money for haircuts every few weeks? Do you have curly hair that requires a special touch? If so, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll show you how to cut men's curly hair yourself. You don't need to be a professional stylist to achieve a great haircut. With the right tools and techniques, you can get the look you want from the comfort of your own home.

How to Cut Men's Curly Hair Yourself

The first step in cutting men's curly hair yourself is to gather the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. You may also want to invest in a pair of hair clippers to trim the sides and back of your head.

Once you have your tools, it's time to get started. Follow these steps to achieve a great haircut:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. Curly hair is best cut when it's dry, so resist the urge to towel dry or blow dry your hair before cutting.
  2. Use your comb to part your hair where you want your part to be. This will help you determine the length of your hair on each side of your head.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and comb it straight down. Use your scissors to trim the ends, cutting at an angle to create a natural-looking shape. Repeat this process on the other side of your head.
  4. Continue working your way around your head, taking small sections of hair and trimming the ends. Be sure to cut at an angle to avoid creating blunt lines.
  5. Use your clippers to trim the sides and back of your head. Start with the longest guard and work your way down to a shorter one until you achieve the desired length.
  6. Blend the sides and back of your head with the top using scissors. Take small sections of hair and trim the ends, cutting at an angle to create a seamless transition.
  7. Use your comb to check for any uneven spots or long hairs. Trim as necessary until you achieve the desired shape and length.
  8. Finish by styling your hair as desired. You can use a pomade or hair gel to define your curls and add texture.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

Cutting curly hair can be tricky, but with these tips, you'll be a pro in no time:

  • Always cut curly hair when it's dry. Wet hair can shrink up to an inch, making it difficult to achieve the desired length.
  • Cut your hair in a well-lit area so you can see what you're doing.
  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way around your head.
  • Don't be afraid to trim less hair than you think you need to. You can always go back and cut more if necessary.
  • Use your fingers to pull your hair straight before cutting. This will help you see any uneven spots or long hairs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I cut my curly hair with regular scissors?

A: Yes, but it's best to use scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair. These scissors are sharper and will create a cleaner cut.

Q: How often should I cut my curly hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. As a general rule, it's best to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Cutting curly hair is just one step in achieving a great hairstyle. To keep your curls looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair on a daily basis. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair to define your curls and add moisture.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat on a low setting.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to reduce frizz and prevent your curls from getting tangled.

By following these tips and using the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut for your curly hair at home. Say goodbye to expensive haircuts and hello to a new sense of confidence!
