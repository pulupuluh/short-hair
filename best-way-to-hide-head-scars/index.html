Best Way To Hide Head Scars

Written by Daniel May 17, 2023 · 7 min read

Head scars can be quite challenging to hide, especially when they are in prominent areas such as the forehead or crown. However, there are several ways to cover them up and boost your confidence. In this article, we'll share some of the best ways to hide head scars.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Hide Head Scars
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips to Keep in Mind
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Scars

How to Hide Head Scars

Here are some of the best ways to hide head scars:

1. Scarf or Headband

One of the easiest ways to cover up head scars is by wearing a scarf or headband. This not only hides the scar but also adds a stylish touch to your outfit. You can choose from different styles and colors to match your clothing or mood.

2. Hat

A hat is another great option to hide head scars. You can wear a baseball cap, beanie, or fedora to cover up the scar. Hats also protect your head from the sun and keep you warm in cold weather.

3. Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can also help hide head scars. If the scar is on the top of your head, you can try a side part or comb-over. If it's on the back of your head, you can opt for a longer hairstyle that covers the area.

4. Makeup

Makeup can be a lifesaver when it comes to hiding head scars. You can use a concealer that matches your skin tone to cover up the scar. Make sure to blend it well and set it with powder for a natural look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to hide head scars:

Step 1: Cleanse and Moisturize

Start by cleansing and moisturizing your face and scalp. This will help create a smooth canvas for makeup application.

Step 2: Apply Primer

Apply a primer to your scalp to help the makeup adhere better and last longer. You can use a silicone-based primer or a mattifying primer depending on your skin type.

Step 3: Apply Concealer

Use a concealer that matches your skin tone to cover up the scar. Apply it in thin layers and blend it well with a makeup sponge or brush. Make sure to cover the edges of the scar to create a seamless look.

Step 4: Set with Powder

Set the concealer with a translucent powder to prevent it from smudging or creasing. Use a large fluffy brush to dust off any excess powder.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when hiding head scars:

1. Choose the Right Color

Make sure to choose a scarf, headband, or hat that matches your skin tone and clothing. This will create a cohesive and polished look.

2. Practice Makes Perfect

Practice applying makeup and styling your hair to cover up the scar. This will help you find the best technique and products that work for you.

3. Be Confident

Remember that scars are a part of your story and make you who you are. Embrace them and be confident in your own skin.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hiding head scars:

1. Can I use hair extensions to cover up head scars?

Yes, hair extensions can be a great option to cover up head scars. You can choose from clip-in, tape-in, or sew-in extensions depending on your preference.

2. Do I need to use a specific type of makeup for head scars?

No, you can use any type of makeup that matches your skin tone and provides coverage. However, make sure to choose products that are non-comedogenic and won't clog your pores.

Hair Care for Scars

Here are some hair care tips for scars:

1. Keep the Area Clean

Make sure to keep the scar and surrounding area clean and dry. Use a gentle shampoo and avoid harsh chemicals that can irritate the skin.

2. Massage the Area

Massaging the scar can help improve blood flow and promote healing. Use gentle circular motions with your fingertips to massage the area.

3. Protect from the Sun

Scars can be more sensitive to the sun's rays, so make sure to protect the area with a hat or sunscreen. This will prevent further damage and discoloration.

By following these tips and techniques, you can confidently hide head scars and feel great about yourself. Remember to embrace your scars and be proud of your journey.
