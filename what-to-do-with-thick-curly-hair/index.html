What To Do With Thick Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 10, 2023 · 9 min read

Thick curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's full of volume and texture, but on the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. If you're struggling with what to do with your thick curly hair, don't worry – we've got you covered. In this article, we'll provide you with tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions on how to style your hair to perfection.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Thick Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Managing Thick Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Routine for Thick Curly Hair

How to Style Thick Curly Hair

When it comes to styling thick curly hair, the key is to embrace your natural texture. Trying to fight against your curls will only lead to frustration and damage to your hair. Instead, focus on enhancing your curls and making them the star of the show. Here are some tips for styling thick curly hair:

1. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Thick curly hair tends to be dry, so it's important to keep it moisturized. Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and add moisture. Apply the leave-in conditioner to damp hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.

2. Scrunch Your Hair

To enhance your curls, scrunch your hair while it's still damp. This will help to create definition and bring out your natural texture. Avoid towel-drying your hair, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

3. Use a Diffuser

A diffuser attachment on your hairdryer can be a game-changer for curly hair. It helps to distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz. Flip your head over and use the diffuser on a low heat setting to dry your hair.

4. Avoid Brushing Your Hair

Brushing your hair can lead to frizz and breakage, especially with thick curly hair. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. Start from the ends and work your way up to avoid pulling and tugging on your hair.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you know some tips for styling thick curly hair, here are some step-by-step instructions to help you achieve the perfect look:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently squeeze out any excess water.

2. Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair, starting from the ends and working your way up. Use your fingers to detangle your hair and distribute the product evenly.

3. Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to create definition and enhance your curls. Avoid towel-drying your hair, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

4. Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your hairdryer and set it to a low heat setting. Flip your head over and use the diffuser to dry your hair, scrunching your hair as you go.

5. Finish with a Styling Product

Once your hair is dry, use a styling product to help hold your curls in place. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Tips for Managing Thick Curly Hair

Managing thick curly hair can be a challenge, but with these tips, you'll be able to keep your hair looking its best:

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair no more than twice a week.

2. Use a Satin Pillowcase

Sleeping on a satin pillowcase can help to reduce friction and prevent frizz. It also helps to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to trim your hair every 8-12 weeks.

4. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to use them sparingly. When you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

FAQs

1. Can I brush my thick curly hair?

Avoid brushing your thick curly hair, as this can lead to frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

2. How often should I wash my thick curly hair?

Aim to wash your thick curly hair no more than twice a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

3. What's the best way to dry my thick curly hair?

Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your thick curly hair. This will help to distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz.

Hair Care Routine for Thick Curly Hair

Here's a simple hair care routine for thick curly hair:

1. Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair no more than twice a week with a hydrating shampoo and conditioner.

2. Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.

3. Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to create definition and enhance your curls.

4. Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair.

5. Finish with a Styling Product

Use a styling product to help hold your curls in place.

By following these tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions, you'll be able to manage your thick curly hair with ease. Remember to embrace your natural texture and have fun experimenting with different styles!
