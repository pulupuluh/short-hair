Best Face for Short Hair: A Guide to Flattering Your Features

If you're thinking about getting a short haircut, you might be wondering what the best face shape is for this style. While short hair can look great on anyone, there are certain face shapes that are particularly well-suited for this look. In this guide, we'll explore the best face shapes for short hair and offer some styling tips to help you make the most of your new 'do.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best short haircuts for different face shapes, it's important to know how to determine your own face shape. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back from your face.
  2. Examine the shape of your face. Is it long and narrow, or short and wide? Is your forehead or jawline more prominent?
  3. Compare your face to the following descriptions to determine your shape:

Once you know your face shape, you can use the following guide to find the best short haircut for your features.

Best Short Haircuts for Oval Faces

Oval faces are well-suited for almost any short haircut style. This is because oval faces are balanced and symmetrical, with a gently rounded hairline and jawline. Some popular short haircuts for oval faces include:

  • Pixie cuts
  • Bobs
  • Layered crops

These styles emphasize the natural balance of the oval face shape, while adding texture and movement.

Best Short Haircuts for Round Faces

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. The best short haircuts for round faces are those that create the illusion of length and angles. Some popular styles include:

  • Asymmetrical cuts
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Short shags

These styles add height and dimension to the face, creating the illusion of a longer, more angular shape.

Best Short Haircuts for Square Faces

Square faces have a strong, angular jawline and a wide forehead. The best short haircuts for square faces are those that soften the jawline and create balance. Some popular styles include:

  • Soft, layered bobs
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length cuts

These styles add softness and movement to the face, creating a more balanced and flattering look.

Best Short Haircuts for Heart-Shaped Faces

Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a wide forehead and a narrow chin. The best short haircuts for heart-shaped faces are those that add fullness to the chin area and balance the forehead. Some popular styles include:

  • Chin-length bobs
  • Layered pixie cuts
  • Side-swept bangs

These styles add fullness to the chin area, creating a more balanced and flattering look.

Best Short Haircuts for Diamond-Shaped Faces

Diamond-shaped faces have high cheekbones and a narrow forehead and chin. The best short haircuts for diamond-shaped faces are those that add fullness to the forehead and chin area. Some popular styles include:

  • Layered bobs
  • Textured pixie cuts
  • Side-swept bangs

These styles add fullness to the forehead and chin area, creating a more balanced and flattering look.

Styling Tips for Short Hair

Once you've chosen the best short haircut for your face shape, it's important to know how to style it to flatter your features. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing mousse to add texture and fullness to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and waves to your hair.
  • Experiment with different parts to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear short hair if I have a round face?
A: Absolutely! Just choose a short haircut that adds height and angles to your face, like an asymmetrical cut or side-swept bangs.

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look wider?
A: Not necessarily. The right short haircut can actually create the illusion of length and angles, making your face look more balanced and flattering.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires some special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and soft.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and polished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the best short haircut for your face shape and rock it with confidence!
