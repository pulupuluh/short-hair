Is Side Swept Bangs Still In Style Find Out In 2023

Written by William May 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Side swept bangs have been in fashion for decades and have undergone several transformations over the years. It's no surprise that many women still wonder whether side swept bangs are still in style in 2023. In this blog post, we'll answer that question and provide you with tips on how to rock this classic hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Side Swept Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Side Swept Bangs
  • Tips for Maintaining Side Swept Bangs
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Side Swept Bangs

Description

Side swept bangs are a timeless hairstyle that can be worn by women of all ages. This style involves sweeping a section of hair across the forehead, creating an asymmetrical look that frames the face. Side swept bangs can be long or short, and they can be worn with any hair length or texture.

Many celebrities have rocked side swept bangs over the years, including Emma Stone, Taylor Swift, and Jennifer Aniston. These celebrities have shown that side swept bangs can be worn in a variety of ways, from a casual, tousled look to a sleek and polished style.

How to Style Side Swept Bangs

Styling side swept bangs is easy, and there are many ways to wear this classic hairstyle. Here are some popular ways to style side swept bangs:

  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Sweep your bangs to the side using a flat iron for a sleek and polished look.
  • Create loose curls with a curling iron and sweep your bangs to the side for a romantic look.
  • Twist your bangs to the side and secure them with a bobby pin for a casual, effortless look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Side Swept Bangs

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your side swept bangs:

  1. Wash your hair and blow-dry it until it's about 80% dry.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush, sweeping them to the side as you go.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth and straighten your bangs, if desired.
  5. Apply a styling product, such as a serum or hairspray, to keep your bangs in place.

Tips for Maintaining Side Swept Bangs

Here are some tips for maintaining your side swept bangs:

  • Get regular trims to keep your bangs looking fresh and prevent split ends.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your bangs from getting greasy between washes.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them greasy and cause them to lose their shape.
  • Consider using a heat protectant spray before styling your bangs to prevent damage from hot styling tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are side swept bangs still in style in 2023?

A: Yes! Side swept bangs are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of fashion. They can be worn in a variety of ways and can be adapted to suit any face shape or hair type.

Q: What face shape is best suited for side swept bangs?

A: Side swept bangs look great on all face shapes, but they are particularly flattering on oval and heart-shaped faces.

Hair Care Tips for Side Swept Bangs

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your side swept bangs:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your bangs looking fresh and clean.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs, as this can cause damage and split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs, as this can weigh them down and make them look greasy.

So, is side swept bangs still in style in 2023? Absolutely! This classic hairstyle is as popular as ever and can be adapted to suit any hair type or face shape. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or a casual, tousled style, side swept bangs are a great choice for anyone looking to add some flair to their hairstyle.
