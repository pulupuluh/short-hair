How To Control Wavy Thick Hair For Guys Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Mar 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Wavy hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it can look effortlessly cool, it can also be difficult to manage, especially if you have thick hair. Fortunately, there are several ways to control your wavy thick hair and achieve a great hairstyle. In this blog post, we will share some tips and tricks on how to control wavy thick hair for guys.

How to

The following are some steps on how to control wavy thick hair for guys:

Step 1: Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

The first step to controlling your wavy thick hair is to choose the right shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed for wavy or curly hair. These products will help to define your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 2: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and help to control frizz. Apply the conditioner to damp hair and comb it through with your fingers.

Step 3: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When your hair is wet, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

Step 4: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to help define your curls and reduce frizz. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and dry your hair on a low heat setting.

Step 5: Use Hair Products

Use hair products such as hair gel or pomade to help control your wavy thick hair. Apply the product to your hair when it is damp and style your hair as desired.

Tips

The following are some tips to help you control your wavy thick hair:

Tip 1: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Overwashing your hair can strip your hair of its natural oils, causing it to become dry and frizzy. Instead, wash your hair every other day or every three days.

Tip 2: Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help to keep your hair in good condition and prevent split ends. Visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks for a trim.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools such as curling irons or straighteners.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about controlling wavy thick hair for guys:

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every other day or every three days.

What hair products should I use for wavy thick hair?

Look for hair products that are specifically designed for wavy or curly hair. Hair gel or pomade can help to control your hair.

How can I prevent frizz in my hair?

Use a leave-in conditioner, a wide-tooth comb, and a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help prevent frizz.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential for controlling wavy thick hair. Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

Tip 1: Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Tip 2: Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help to stimulate hair growth and improve circulation.

Tip 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help to keep your hair healthy and strong.

By following these tips and tricks, you can control your wavy thick hair and achieve a great hairstyle. Remember to take care of your hair and use the right products to keep it healthy and strong.
