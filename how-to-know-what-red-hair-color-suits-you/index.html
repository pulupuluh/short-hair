How To Know What Red Hair Color Suits You

Written by William Jun 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are thinking of coloring your hair red, but are not sure which shade will suit you best, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you on how to know what red hair color suits you. We will also provide you with some tips on how to take care of your newly colored hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Know What Red Hair Color Suits You
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Red Hair Color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Red Hair

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to determine which red hair color will best suit you:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining the right red hair color for you is to identify your skin tone. There are two main categories of skin tones- cool and warm. Cool skin tones have blue or pink undertones, while warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones.

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool skin tone, and if they appear green, you have a warm skin tone.

Step 2: Identify Which Red Hair Color Suits Your Skin Tone

Once you have identified your skin tone, you can start looking for the right red hair color. Here are some suggestions:

  • For cool skin tones, choose a red hair color with blue undertones such as auburn or burgundy.
  • For warm skin tones, choose a red hair color with golden undertones such as copper or strawberry blonde.

Step 3: Consider Your Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color can also affect which red hair color will suit you best. If you have dark hair, you may want to choose a darker red shade such as burgundy. If you have light hair, you may want to go for a lighter red shade such as strawberry blonde.

Tips for Choosing the Right Red Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right red hair color for you:

  • Consider your lifestyle- if you have a busy schedule or work in a conservative environment, you may want to choose a more subtle red hair color.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual hair color apps to see how different red hair colors will look on you.
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist- they can help you choose the right red hair color and also provide you with hair care advice.

FAQs

Q: Can anyone pull off red hair?

A: Yes, anyone can pull off red hair. The key is to choose the right shade that complements your skin tone and natural hair color.

Q: Will red hair color damage my hair?

A: Dyeing your hair can cause some damage, but using a good quality hair dye and following proper hair care routine can minimize the damage.

Q: How often should I touch up my red hair color?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how much your natural hair color differs from the red hair color. On average, touch-ups are needed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Red Hair

Here are some tips on how to take care of your newly colored red hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often- 2-3 times a week is enough.
  • Protect your hair from UV rays and heat styling tools.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Now that you know how to choose the right red hair color and how to take care of it, you can confidently rock your new look. Happy coloring!
