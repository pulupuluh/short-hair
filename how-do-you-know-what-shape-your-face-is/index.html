How Do You Know What Shape Your Face Is

Written by Robby Sep 01, 2023 · 7 min read

Knowing your face shape is essential when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle, makeup, and even glasses. But how do you determine your face shape? In this article, we’ll show you how to identify your face shape and give you some tips on how to enhance your best features.

Table of Content

  1. How to Determine Your Face Shape
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Enhancing Your Face Shape
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

How to Determine Your Face Shape

There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, you need to look at the proportions of your face and identify the shape that matches the most.

Here are some key features to look for:

  • Oval: forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the face gently tapers towards the jawline.
  • Round: cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the face is almost as wide as it is long.
  • Square: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost the same width, and the angle of the jaw is sharp.
  • Heart: forehead is the widest part of the face, and the face tapers to a point at the chin.
  • Diamond: cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the forehead and jawline are narrower.
  • Oblong: face is longer than it is wide, with a high forehead and a narrow chin.

Step-by-Step Guide

To make it easier to identify your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Take a photo of your face straight on, with your hair pulled back.
  2. Print out the photo or open it on your computer.
  3. Draw a line across your forehead at the widest point.
  4. Draw lines from the widest point of your forehead to the outside of your jawline.
  5. Measure the length of your face from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin.
  6. Compare your measurements and the lines you drew with the descriptions of the six face shapes.

Tips for Enhancing Your Face Shape

Once you’ve identified your face shape, you can use these tips to enhance your best features:

  • Oval: almost any hairstyle and makeup will suit this face shape.
  • Round: add height to your hair and contour your cheeks to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Square: soften your jawline with layers and waves.
  • Heart: draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones with a side-swept fringe and contouring.
  • Diamond: balance your cheekbones with a hairstyle that adds width to the forehead and chin.
  • Oblong: add volume to the sides of your face with a layered haircut.

FAQs

What if my face doesn’t fit any of the six face shapes?

It’s possible that your face has a combination of features from different face shapes. In this case, choose the shape that most closely matches your overall proportions.

Can my face shape change over time?

While the basic shape of your face is determined by your bone structure, your face shape can appear to change due to factors such as weight gain or loss, aging, and certain medical conditions.

Do I have to follow the rules for my face shape?

These tips are only guidelines, and you should always choose a hairstyle and makeup that make you feel confident and comfortable.

Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some ideas:

  • Oval: long layers, side-swept bangs, and a center part.
  • Round: long layers, a side part, and volume at the crown.
  • Square: soft waves, a middle part, and layers around the face.
  • Heart: side-swept bangs, a layered bob, and volume at the crown.
  • Diamond: a long bob, a side part, and layers that frame the face.
  • Oblong: a layered bob, side-swept bangs, and volume at the sides.

Remember, these are just ideas, and you should always choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful. With these tips, you’ll be able to determine your face shape and enhance your best features.
