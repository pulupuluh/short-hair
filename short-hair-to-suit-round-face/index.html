Short Hair To Suit Round Face How To Choose Style And Care For Your New Look

Written by Aprilia Jul 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and want to try something new? Short hair can be a great option, especially if you have a round face. But choosing the right cut and styling it can be tricky. Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll give you tips on how to choose the best short hair to suit your round face, how to style it, and how to take care of your new look.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Choose
  • How to Style
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Choose:

When it comes to short haircuts for round faces, the goal is to elongate your face and create angles. Here are some tips:

1. Consider the Length:

Short haircuts that hit just above or below the chin are great for round faces, as they create the illusion of length. Avoid cuts that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear wider or more circular.

2. Go for Layers:

Layers can add volume and texture to your hair, creating the illusion of a longer and slimmer face. Opt for layers around the crown or near the ends, but avoid layers that start at the chin or cheekbones, as they can accentuate the roundness of your face.

3. Embrace Angles:

Angled cuts, such as bobs or asymmetrical styles, can create the illusion of a longer and more angular face. Consider a bob that is shorter in the back and longer in the front, or a side-swept pixie cut with asymmetrical bangs.

How to Style:

Once you've chosen your short haircut, it's time to style it. Here are some tips:

1. Add Volume:

Volumizing products, such as mousse or root-lifting spray, can add volume to your hair and create the illusion of a longer and slimmer face. Apply these products to your roots and blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume.

2. Use a Round Brush:

When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to create volume and texture. Roll the brush away from your face to create a side-swept look, or roll it towards your face to create a more angular style.

3. Experiment with Bangs:

Bangs can be a great way to add dimension to your face and create the illusion of length. Consider side-swept bangs or asymmetrical bangs that are longer on one side.

Tips:

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

1. Consult with a Stylist:

A professional stylist can help you choose the best short haircut for your face shape and recommend products and styling techniques that work best for you.

2. Experiment with Colors:

Adding highlights or lowlights can add dimension to your hair and complement your new short haircut.

3. Don't Be Afraid to Experiment:

Short hair can be versatile and fun to style. Don't be afraid to experiment with different products, styling techniques, and accessories to find a look that works best for you.

FAQ:

1. Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in a variety of ways, including updos and ponytails. Consider using accessories, such as headbands or clips, to add some flair to your new look.

2. Will a short haircut require more maintenance?

Short haircuts can require more frequent trims to maintain their shape, but they can also be easier to style and maintain on a daily basis.

Hair Care:

Proper hair care is important for any haircut, but especially for short hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo:

Short hair can be more prone to breakage, so it's important to use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat:

When styling your hair with heat tools, such as a blow dryer or flat iron, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

3. Get Regular Trims:

To maintain the shape of your short haircut, it's important to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

With these tips, you can find the perfect short hair to suit your round face and rock your new look with confidence. Happy styling!
