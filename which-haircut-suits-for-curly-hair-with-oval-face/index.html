Which Haircut Suits For Curly Hair With Oval Face

Written by William Jul 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have curly hair and an oval face, finding the right haircut can be challenging. You want a style that will enhance your natural curls and complement your face shape. In this blog post, we'll give you some tips on which haircut suits for curly hair with oval face, and how to take care of your curls.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, with prominent cheekbones. It is considered the most versatile face shape, as almost any haircut can look good on it. However, when it comes to curly hair, you need to be more careful in choosing a hairstyle that will flatter your face shape and enhance your curls.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

Here are some tips on how to choose the right haircut for your curly hair and oval face:

  • Consider the length of your curls. If you have long curls, you can go for a layered haircut that will add movement and volume to your hair. Shorter curls look best with a bob or pixie cut.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that will make your hair look too heavy and flat. Instead, go for soft layers that will add texture and shape to your curls.
  • Choose a haircut that will frame your face and highlight your best features. For example, if you have a strong jawline, a chin-length bob will accentuate it.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut that suits your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect haircut for your curly hair and oval face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for curly hair.
  2. Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. Do not use a brush, as it can damage your curls.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on low heat. This will help enhance your curls' natural shape and prevent frizz.
  4. Section your hair and start cutting it according to the chosen haircut. Use sharp hair scissors and cut your hair in small sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.
  5. Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it using a curl-defining product that suits your hair type. Scrunch your curls gently to enhance their shape and let them air dry.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers when it is wet.
  • Do not towel-dry your hair aggressively. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze the excess water out of your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or a curl-enhancing cream to your hair to keep it moisturized and defined.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for curly hair with oval face:

  • Can I straighten my curly hair if I have an oval face? Yes, you can straighten your hair if you want to. However, make sure to use heat protectant products and avoid using high heat to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Can I dye my curly hair if I have an oval face? Yes, you can dye your hair if you want to. However, make sure to use hair dye that is specifically designed for curly hair and avoid using harsh chemicals that can damage your curls.
  • How often should I wash my curly hair? It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or a swim cap.
  • Get regular deep conditioning treatments to keep your curls soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

With these tips, you can find the perfect haircut that suits your curly hair and oval face. Remember to take good care of your curls, and they will look beautiful and healthy.
