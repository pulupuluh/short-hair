Things To Do With Medium Length Hair For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 28, 2023 · 8 min read

If you’re a guy with medium length hair, you might be wondering what kind of hairstyles you can try out. Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll be discussing everything you need to know about things to do with medium length hair for guys. From how to style your hair to hair care tips, we’ve got it all. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Length Hair for Guys
  3. Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair for Guys
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care Tips for Guys with Medium Length Hair

How to Style Medium Length Hair for Guys

If you’re looking for some inspiration on how to style your medium length hair, here are a few ideas:

1. The Messy Look

The messy look is a great option for guys with medium length hair. To achieve this look, apply some texturizing hair product to your hair and scrunch it up. You can also use your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.

2. The Man Bun

The man bun is a classic hairstyle that works well with medium length hair. To create a man bun, pull your hair back into a ponytail and twist it up into a bun. Secure it with a hair tie or bobby pins.

3. The Slicked Back Look

The slicked back look is a sophisticated and stylish hairstyle that works well with medium length hair. To achieve this look, apply some pomade or gel to your hair and comb it back. You can also use a hair dryer to add some volume.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Medium Length Hair for Guys

If you’re unsure of how to style your medium length hair, here’s a step-by-step guide:

1. Wash your hair

Start off by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to clean your hair and make it easier to style.

2. Towel dry your hair

After washing your hair, towel dry it to remove any excess water. You don’t want your hair to be dripping wet when you start styling it.

3. Apply hair product

Apply your preferred hair product to your hair. This could be a pomade, gel, wax or mousse. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

4. Style your hair

Using your fingers or a comb, style your hair into your desired look. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find one that you like.

5. Finish with hairspray

Once you’ve styled your hair, finish it off with a spritz of hairspray. This will help to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair for Guys

Here are a few tips for maintaining your medium length hair:

1. Get regular trims

Even though you’re growing your hair out, it’s important to get regular trims to keep it looking neat and tidy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

2. Use a good quality hairbrush

Invest in a good quality hairbrush to help detangle your hair and distribute natural oils throughout your hair.

3. Protect your hair from heat damage

If you’re using heat tools to style your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can guys with medium length hair still wear hats?

A: Yes, guys with medium length hair can still wear hats. Opt for a beanie or snapback hat that won’t mess up your hair too much.

Q: How often should I wash my medium length hair?

A: It’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I dye my medium length hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your medium length hair. However, it’s important to use a hair dye that is specifically designed for men’s hair.

Hair Care Tips for Guys with Medium Length Hair

Here are a few hair care tips for guys with medium length hair:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can be harsh on your hair and scalp, so it’s best to use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid any irritation.

2. Apply a hair mask once a week

Applying a hair mask once a week can help to nourish your hair and keep it looking healthy and shiny.

3. Avoid using hot water

Hot water can strip your hair of its natural oils, so it’s best to use lukewarm or cool water when washing your hair.

That’s everything you need to know about things to do with medium length hair for guys. We hope you found this article helpful and that you’re feeling inspired to try out some new hairstyles. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles until you find one that you love. Happy styling!
