Treatment To Prevent Hair Loss During Chemo

Written by Daniel Apr 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you or someone you know is undergoing chemotherapy treatment, you may be concerned about hair loss. Losing hair during chemo is a common side effect that can be emotionally challenging for many patients. Fortunately, there are several treatments available that can help prevent hair loss during chemo. In this article, we will discuss the most effective treatments to prevent hair loss during chemo, how to use them, and tips for hair care during treatment.

What Causes Hair Loss During Chemo?

Hair loss during chemotherapy occurs because the drugs used in treatment target rapidly dividing cells in the body, including hair follicles. The hair loss is usually temporary and may begin within a few weeks of starting treatment. Hair loss can occur on the scalp, eyebrows, eyelashes, and other parts of the body.

How to Prevent Hair Loss During Chemo

Scalp Cooling

Scalp cooling is a treatment that involves wearing a special cap during chemotherapy treatment to reduce the amount of chemotherapy drugs that reach the hair follicles. The cap is chilled to a very low temperature, which constricts the blood vessels in the scalp and reduces blood flow to the hair follicles. This helps to protect the hair follicles from the effects of chemotherapy drugs, which may reduce the amount of hair loss. Scalp cooling is effective, but not all patients are candidates for this treatment.

Minoxidil

Minoxidil is a topical medication that is approved by the FDA to treat hair loss. It is used to promote hair growth and can be used during chemotherapy treatment to prevent hair loss. Minoxidil works by increasing blood flow to the hair follicles, which can help to reduce hair loss. It is important to talk to your doctor before using minoxidil during chemotherapy treatment.

Wigs and Hairpieces

Wigs and hairpieces can be used to cover hair loss during chemotherapy treatment. There are many options available, including synthetic and human hair wigs. Wigs and hairpieces can be customized to fit the patient's head size and hair color. Many patients find that wearing a wig or hairpiece helps to boost their self-confidence during treatment.

FAQ

Is Hair Loss During Chemo Permanent?

Hair loss during chemotherapy treatment is usually temporary. Hair may start to grow back a few weeks after treatment ends, but it may take several months for hair to return to its normal thickness and texture.

Can Scalp Cooling Be Used During All Types of Chemotherapy?

Scalp cooling may not be effective for all types of chemotherapy drugs. Your doctor can help determine if scalp cooling is a viable option for your treatment plan.

Hair Care Tips During Chemotherapy Treatment

During chemotherapy treatment, it is important to take care of your hair and scalp. Here are some tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot styling tools
  • Avoid hair dyes and chemical treatments
  • Protect your scalp from the sun with a hat or scarf

By following these tips and using the appropriate treatments, you can help prevent hair loss during chemotherapy treatment. Remember to talk to your doctor about your treatment options and to take care of yourself during this difficult time.
