How To Cut Men s Long Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a man with long hair, you know how important it is to keep it looking good. But going to the salon can be expensive and time-consuming. If you want to save money and have more control over your hairstyle, you can learn how to cut your own hair with scissors. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step by step and give you some tips to make it easier.

Description

Cutting men's long hair with scissors can be a bit intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it's not as difficult as you might think. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some clips to hold your hair in place. It's also a good idea to have a mirror set up so you can see the back of your head.

How to Cut Men's Long Hair with Scissors

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help you see the natural shape of your hair and make it easier to cut evenly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited for your hair type.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a comb to detangle it. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots. This will help you remove any knots or tangles that might make it difficult to cut your hair evenly.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use clips to divide your hair into sections. Start with the back of your head and work your way forward. This will help you keep track of which sections you've already cut and make it easier to cut your hair evenly.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Starting with the back of your head, take a small section of hair between your fingers and cut it to the desired length. Use your first cut as a guide for the rest of your hair. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you've cut all the sections you've divided.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check the back of your head. Make any adjustments as necessary to ensure your hair is even.

Tips

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair you want to cut and cut it straight across.
  • Don't cut too much at once. You can always go back and cut more if you need to.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair with regular scissors?

While you can use regular scissors to cut your own hair, it's best to use scissors that are designed for cutting hair. Hair scissors are sharper and have a better edge, which makes it easier to cut your hair evenly.

How often should I cut my hair?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to change your hairstyle. On average, most men with long hair need to cut their hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking good.

Hair Care

Cutting your own hair with scissors is just one aspect of hair care. To keep your hair looking healthy, it's important to wash it regularly, use a conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. You should also get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips and techniques, you can learn how to cut your own long hair with scissors and save money on salon visits. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect hairstyle every time.
