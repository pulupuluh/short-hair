How To Put Guards On Andis Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Feb 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Andis clippers have been a trusted brand in the hair care industry for decades. They are known for their durability, precision, and ease of use. One of the most important features of Andis clippers is the ability to attach guards to the blades. Guards are essential for achieving a consistent haircut length and preventing accidents. In this article, we will guide you through the process of putting guards on Andis clippers.

How to Put Guards on Andis Clippers

Before we start, make sure you have the following items:

  • Andis clippers
  • Guard set
  • Cleaning brush
  • Clipper oil

Step 1: Turn off the Clippers

Before you start attaching the guards, make sure your Andis clippers are turned off to avoid any accidents.

Step 2: Clean the Blade

Using the cleaning brush, remove any hair or debris from the blade. This will ensure that the guard fits properly and does not get stuck.

Step 3: Choose the Right Guard

Andis clippers come with a set of guards that vary in length. Choose the guard that corresponds to the desired length of the haircut. The length of the guard is usually indicated on the side of the guard.

Step 4: Attach the Guard

Slide the guard onto the blade of the clipper. Make sure it clicks into place and fits snugly. Check that the guard is aligned with the blade and not crooked.

Step 5: Repeat for Other Guards

If you need to change the guard length, simply remove the current guard and attach the new one. Make sure to clean the blade before attaching a new guard.

Tips for Putting Guards on Andis Clippers

  • Make sure the clipper is turned off before attaching the guard.
  • Choose the right guard for the desired haircut length.
  • Make sure the guard clicks into place and fits snugly.
  • Check that the guard is aligned with the blade and not crooked.
  • Clean the blade before attaching a new guard.
  • Apply clipper oil to the blade after use to maintain its performance.

FAQs

Q: Can I use other brand guards with Andis clippers?

A: It is recommended to use Andis guards with Andis clippers for the best performance and fit. However, some other brand guards may be compatible with Andis clippers.

Q: How often should I clean my Andis clippers?

A: It is recommended to clean your Andis clippers after each use to keep them in good condition and prevent hair buildup.

Hair Care Tips

Using guards with Andis clippers can help prevent accidents and achieve a consistent haircut length. However, it is also important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends.

By following these hair care tips and using guards with your Andis clippers, you can achieve a great haircut and keep your hair healthy.
