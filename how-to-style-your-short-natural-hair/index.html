How To Style Your Short Natural Hair

Written by Christine Aug 12, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have short natural hair, you might be wondering how to style it without spending hours in front of the mirror. Luckily, there are plenty of easy and stylish options available that will help you show off your natural texture and feel confident and beautiful every day. In this post, we’ll share some tips and tricks for styling short natural hair, as well as some hair care advice to keep your locks healthy and strong.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Your Short Natural Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Natural Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Natural Hair

How to Style Your Short Natural Hair

Short natural hair can be styled in a variety of ways depending on your personal preference and hair type. Here are some popular options:

Braid-Out

A braid-out is a great way to add texture and volume to your short natural hair. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner and a styling product to your hair.
  3. Part your hair into small sections and braid each section tightly.
  4. Leave the braids in overnight or until your hair is completely dry.
  5. Take out the braids and use your fingers to separate the curls and add volume.

Twist-Out

A twist-out is another popular option for short natural hair. It’s similar to a braid-out, but with twists instead of braids. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner and a styling product to your hair.
  3. Part your hair into small sections and twist each section tightly.
  4. Leave the twists in overnight or until your hair is completely dry.
  5. Take out the twists and use your fingers to separate the curls and add volume.

Finger Coils

Finger coils are a great option if you want defined curls and coils. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner and a styling product to your hair.
  3. Take small sections of your hair and twist them around your finger, starting at the root and working your way down.
  4. Repeat until all of your hair is twisted into coils.
  5. Allow your hair to air-dry or use a diffuser to dry it.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your short natural hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are designed for your hair type.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to moisturize and protect it.
  3. Apply a styling product to your hair to help define your curls or coils.
  4. Choose a styling method that works for your hair type and personal preference.
  5. Follow the instructions for your chosen styling method.
  6. Use your fingers to separate the curls and add volume.

Tips for Styling Short Natural Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your short natural hair:

  • Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling methods to find what works best for you.
  • Don’t be afraid to use accessories like headbands or scarves to add some extra flair to your style.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping with a satin or silk scarf or pillowcase.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

How often should I wash my short natural hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people find that they need to wash their hair every few days, while others can go a week or longer between washes. Experiment to find what works best for you.

What should I do if my hair is dry and brittle?

Make sure you’re using products that are designed for your hair type and that you’re moisturizing your hair regularly. You can also try using a deep conditioner or hair mask once a week to help restore moisture and repair damage.

Can I color my short natural hair?

Yes, but be aware that coloring your hair can be damaging, especially if you’re using harsh chemicals. If you want to color your hair, consult with a professional stylist and make sure you’re using high-quality products that won’t damage your hair.

Hair Care for Short Natural Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your short natural hair healthy and strong:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat on your hair too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and experimenting with different styling methods, you can find the perfect look for your short natural hair and feel confident and beautiful every day.
