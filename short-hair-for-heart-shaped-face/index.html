Short Hair For Heart Shaped Face A Guide For 2023

Written by Frank Jun 13, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you might be wondering what hairstyles would suit you best. Short hair is a great option for heart-shaped faces, as it can balance out your features and highlight your cheekbones. In this guide, we'll go over how to choose the best short haircuts for heart-shaped faces, as well as tips for styling and caring for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Short Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces
  • Step by Step Guide for Styling Short Hair for Heart Shaped Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Heart Shaped Faces
  • FAQ about Short Hair for Heart Shaped Faces
  • Hair Care for Short Hair for Heart Shaped Faces

How to Choose the Best Short Haircuts for Heart Shaped Faces

When choosing a short haircut for a heart-shaped face, the goal is to balance out the wider forehead and narrow chin. Here are some styles that can achieve this:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut with short layers can balance out the forehead and highlight the cheekbones. This style can be customized with bangs, asymmetrical cuts, or longer layers on top.

Bob Cut

A bob cut can also work well for heart-shaped faces, especially if it's angled or layered. This can create the illusion of a wider chin while still highlighting the cheekbones.

Short Shag

A short shag with choppy layers can create texture and volume around the chin, balancing out the face shape. This style can be customized with bangs or longer layers on top.

Step by Step Guide for Styling Short Hair for Heart Shaped Faces

Once you've chosen a short haircut that suits your face shape, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide for styling short hair for heart-shaped faces:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling. This will help your hair hold its shape and prevent it from becoming too oily or frizzy.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as mousse or gel. This will help your hair hold its shape and add texture.

Step 3: Use a Small Round Brush

Use a small round brush to smooth out your hair and create volume around the chin. Blow dry your hair in sections, starting at the back and working your way to the front.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron or Curling Iron

Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and shape to your hair. This can be done by curling the ends or adding waves to the top layers.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day. Be sure to use a hairspray that won't weigh down your hair or make it look greasy.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Heart Shaped Faces

Short hair can be low-maintenance, but it still requires some care to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair for heart-shaped faces:

Get Regular Trims

Short haircuts need to be trimmed more often than longer styles, as they can lose their shape quickly. Plan to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh.

Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Using heat styling tools can damage your hair, so be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron or curling iron. You can also air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to minimize heat damage.

Use the Right Products

Choose hair products that are designed for your hair type and texture. This can help prevent frizz, breakage, and other damage.

FAQ about Short Hair for Heart Shaped Faces

Q: Can heart-shaped faces pull off a pixie cut?

A: Yes, a pixie cut can work well for heart-shaped faces, especially if it's customized with layers or bangs.

Q: Will short hair make my face look wider?

A: No, short hair can actually help balance out a wider forehead and create the illusion of a wider chin.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if it's short?

A: Yes, there are plenty of ways to style short hair in updos, including braids, twists, and ponytails.

Hair Care for Short Hair for Heart Shaped Faces

Short hair is generally easier to care for than longer styles, but it still requires some attention. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Short hair can become oily more quickly than longer styles, so be sure to wash it every 2-3 days to keep it looking fresh.

Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help keep your hair soft and nourished, especially if you have dry or damaged hair.

Avoid Over-Styling

Using too many styling products or heat styling tools can damage your hair, so be sure to give your hair a break and let it air dry or style it in a low-maintenance way.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can be damaging to your hair, so be sure to wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF if you'll be spending time outside.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock short hair for heart-shaped faces with confidence and style. Whether you choose a pixie cut, bob, or short shag, be sure to maintain your hair with regular trims and proper care.
