How Do I Find The Best Haircut For My Face Shape

Written by William Apr 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of getting a new haircut that just doesn't seem to suit your face shape? You're not alone. Finding the best haircut for your face shape can be a challenge, but it's not impossible. In this article, we'll provide you with some tips and tricks on how to find the perfect haircut for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Best Haircut for Your Face Shape
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut
  • FAQs

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best haircut for your face shape is to determine what your face shape actually is. There are six common face shapes: oval, square, round, heart, diamond, and oblong. Here are some tips on how to determine your face shape:

  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead is slightly wider than your jawline.
  • Square: Your face is roughly equal in length and width, and your jawline is square.
  • Round: Your face is roughly equal in length and width, and your jawline is soft and rounded.
  • Heart: Your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your chin comes to a point.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrower.
  • Oblong: Your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Best Haircut for Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find the best haircut for it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Look for inspiration: Look for pictures of hairstyles that you like and that are suited to your face shape.
  2. Consult a professional: A hairstylist can help you determine the best haircut for your face shape.
  3. Consider your hair texture: Your hair's texture can affect which haircuts will look best on you. For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to avoid haircuts that require a lot of styling.
  4. Think about your lifestyle: If you don't have a lot of time to style your hair, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance haircut.
  5. Try it out: Before committing to a new haircut, try it out with a wig or by using a virtual makeover tool.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right haircut:

  • Consider your age: Certain haircuts may be more flattering at different ages.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment: Trying out a new haircut can be a fun way to change up your look.
  • Keep your face shape in mind: A haircut that looks great on someone else may not be the best choice for your face shape.
  • Ask for advice: Your hairstylist can help you determine which haircuts will look best on you.
  • Take care of your hair: Regular trims and proper hair care can help any haircut look its best.

FAQs

What haircut suits a round face?

Hairstyles that add volume and height to the crown of the head can help elongate a round face shape. Examples include long layers, asymmetrical cuts, and side-swept bangs.

What haircut suits an oval face?

Oval faces are versatile and can pull off a variety of haircuts. Some great options include long layers, blunt bobs, and pixie cuts.

What haircut suits a square face?

Hairstyles that soften the jawline can flatter a square face shape. Examples include long layers, side-swept bangs, and textured waves.

Hair Care for Your Face Shape

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as it can cause breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the best haircut for your face shape and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
