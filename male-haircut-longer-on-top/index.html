Male Haircut Longer On Top A Relaxed Guide

Written by Christine Aug 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, a male haircut longer on top might be the perfect option for you. This versatile hairstyle can be worn in many different ways and is easy to maintain. In this guide, we'll show you how to get the perfect male haircut longer on top, provide tips on how to style it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Get a Male Haircut Longer on Top

A male haircut longer on top is a classic style that's been popular for decades. The key to this style is keeping the hair on top longer than the hair on the sides and back. This creates a contrast that adds depth and texture to the hair. The length of the hair on top can vary depending on your preference, but it's typically around 2-4 inches.

When getting this haircut, it's important to communicate with your barber or stylist about the length you want. You can also bring in pictures of the style you're going for to give them a better idea of what you're looking for. It's also a good idea to ask for a fade on the sides and back to create a seamless transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a male haircut longer on top:

  1. Communicate with your barber or stylist about the length you want for the hair on top.
  2. Ask for a fade on the sides and back.
  3. Style your hair as desired using a matte pomade or wax.

Tips for Styling a Male Haircut Longer on Top

Styling a male haircut longer on top is easy and can be done in many different ways. Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your hairstyle:

  • Use a matte pomade or wax to create texture and hold.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find what works best for you.
  • Try blow-drying your hair in different directions to create volume and texture.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair, depending on the look you're going for.

FAQ

How often should I get my haircut?

You should aim to get your haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your male haircut longer on top.

Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

Absolutely! A male haircut longer on top is versatile and can be styled in many different ways to suit different occasions.

What hair products should I use?

We recommend using a matte pomade or wax to style your hair. These products create texture and hold without making your hair look greasy or shiny.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your male haircut longer on top looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the length and shape of your hair.

That's it! With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a male haircut longer on top with confidence and style.
