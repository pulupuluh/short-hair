How To Make Your Hair Shorter Without Cutting It Troom Troom

Written by Oliver Mar 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your long hair but not ready to commit to a haircut? Well, you don't have to! There are plenty of ways to make your hair shorter without cutting it, and today we're going to show you how to do it Troom Troom style.

Description

Troom Troom is a popular DIY channel on YouTube that specializes in quirky and fun hacks. They've come up with some innovative ways to make your hair shorter without cutting it, and we're going to share some of their best tips with you today.

How to

There are several ways to make your hair shorter without cutting it, and the best part is that they're all easy to do at home. Here are some of our favorite methods:

1. Braids

Braiding your hair is a great way to make it appear shorter. Start by dividing your hair into sections and braiding each section tightly. Once all the sections are braided, wrap them around the back of your head and secure them with bobby pins. Voila! Instant short hair.

2. Pin Curls

Pin curls are a classic way to create shorter-looking hair. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry until it's damp. Then, divide your hair into small sections and twist them tightly. Pin each twist to your head with bobby pins and let them dry completely. Once they're dry, remove the pins and brush out your curls. You'll be left with bouncy, short-looking hair.

3. Half-Up Style

This style is perfect for those with longer hair who want to create the illusion of shorter locks. Start by pulling the top half of your hair back into a ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail tightly and wrap it around the base of the ponytail, securing it with bobby pins. You'll be left with a cute, short-looking style that's perfect for any occasion.

Step by Step

Here are step-by-step instructions for each of the three methods we just mentioned:

Braids

  1. Divide your hair into sections
  2. Braid each section tightly
  3. Wrap the braids around the back of your head
  4. Secure them with bobby pins

Pin Curls

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's damp
  2. Divide your hair into small sections
  3. Twist each section tightly
  4. Pin each twist to your head with bobby pins
  5. Let your hair dry completely
  6. Remove the pins and brush out your curls

Half-Up Style

  1. Pull the top half of your hair back into a ponytail
  2. Twist the ponytail tightly
  3. Wrap it around the base of the ponytail
  4. Secure it with bobby pins

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your hair shorter without cutting it:

  • Use hair accessories like headbands and clips to create shorter-looking styles
  • Experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that works for you
  • Don't be afraid to try something new

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your hair shorter without cutting it:

Can I make my hair shorter without cutting it?

Yes, there are several ways to make your hair appear shorter without actually cutting it.

How long will these styles last?

It depends on your hair type and the method you use. Braids and pin curls can last for several days, while the half-up style may need to be redone daily.

Do I need any special tools to create these styles?

No, all of these styles can be created with basic hair tools like bobby pins and hair ties.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, even when you're not cutting it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools

Now that you know how to make your hair shorter without cutting it Troom Troom style, it's time to get creative and try out some of these fun and easy methods. Who knows, you may just discover a new favorite hairstyle!
