What Is The Best Type Of Human Hair For Wigs

Written by Robby Aug 26, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you considering buying a wig made of human hair? If so, you may be wondering what type of human hair is best for wigs. In this article, we will explore the different types of human hair and help you choose the best one for your needs.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Human Hair for Wigs
  • Step by Step Guide in Choosing the Best Human Hair for Wigs
  • Tips in Maintaining Human Hair Wigs
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Human Hair Wigs

Description

Human hair wigs are made of real human hair, which is why they look and feel more natural compared to synthetic wigs. There are different types of human hair used in making wigs, and each one has its own characteristics that affect its quality and price.

The most common types of human hair used in wigs are Brazilian, Indian, Peruvian, and Chinese. Brazilian and Indian hair are the most popular because they are the easiest to find and have good quality. Peruvian hair is becoming more popular because of its softness and silky texture, while Chinese hair is the most affordable but may not be as soft and manageable as the other types.

How to Choose the Best Human Hair for Wigs

Choosing the best human hair for wigs depends on your budget, desired look, and lifestyle. If you want a more natural and realistic look, Brazilian or Indian hair is a good choice. If you prefer a softer and silkier texture, Peruvian hair is a great option. If you are on a tight budget, Chinese hair is an affordable alternative.

It is also important to consider the quality of the hair. Look for wigs made of 100% human hair and avoid those that are mixed with synthetic fibers. Check the cuticles of the hair to make sure they are aligned in the same direction, which prevents tangling and matting.

Step by Step Guide in Choosing the Best Human Hair for Wigs

1. Determine your budget.

2. Decide on the desired look and texture.

3. Research the different types of human hair used in wigs.

4. Read reviews and check the quality of the hair.

5. Choose a reputable wig supplier.

Tips in Maintaining Human Hair Wigs

Maintaining human hair wigs requires proper care and attention to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wig brush or comb to detangle the hair gently.
  • Wash the wig regularly using a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners as they can damage the hair.
  • Store the wig properly in a wig stand or mannequin head when not in use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does human hair wigs last?

A: With proper care, human hair wigs can last up to a year or more.

Q: Can I color or style my human hair wig?

A: Yes, you can color and style your human hair wig just like your natural hair. However, it is recommended to have it done by a professional stylist to avoid damage.

Hair Care for Human Hair Wigs

Caring for human hair wigs is crucial to maintaining their quality and longevity. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash the wig with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on the wig to prevent damage.
  • Store the wig properly in a cool and dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Use a wig brush or comb to detangle the hair gently.

Overall, choosing the best human hair for wigs depends on your personal preferences and needs. By following these tips and taking proper care of your wig, you can enjoy its natural look and feel for a long time.
