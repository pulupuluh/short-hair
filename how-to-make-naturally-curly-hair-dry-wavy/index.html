How To Make Naturally Curly Hair Dry Wavy

Written by Bardi Mar 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your naturally curly hair and want to switch things up? Maybe you're looking for a new hairstyle but don't want to use heat tools or chemicals. The good news is that you can make your naturally curly hair dry wavy with a few simple steps. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve this look and give you some tips on how to care for your new hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Naturally Curly Hair Dry Wavy
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Step by Step

Follow these steps to make naturally curly hair dry wavy:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's important to use products that will hydrate your hair. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water to help seal the cuticle and reduce frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair while it's still damp. This will help define your curls and make them more manageable. You can use a mousse, gel, or cream depending on your hair type and preference. Work the product through your hair from root to tip.

Step 3: Twist Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections and twist each section. This will help create waves in your hair as it dries. You can twist your hair tightly for more defined waves or loosely for a more natural look. Secure each twist with a clip or hair tie.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Dry

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on low heat if you're in a hurry. Avoid using a hair dryer on high heat as this can damage your hair and cause frizz. Once your hair is dry, remove the clips or hair ties and gently separate the twists to create waves.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect wavy look:

  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel. This will help reduce frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid touching your hair while it's drying as this can cause frizz.
  • Sleep with your hair in a pineapple or loose bun to help preserve your waves overnight.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making naturally curly hair dry wavy:

Can I make my hair wavy without using heat?

Yes, you can make your hair wavy without using heat. Follow the steps outlined in this article to make your naturally curly hair dry wavy.

How long will my waves last?

Your waves should last until your next wash day. To help preserve your waves, use a satin or silk pillowcase and avoid touching your hair too much.

Can I use this method on all hair types?

This method works best on naturally curly or wavy hair. If you have straight hair, you may need to use heat tools to create waves.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and moisturized, it's important to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the steps outlined in this article, you can make your naturally curly hair dry wavy and achieve a new look without damaging your hair. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
