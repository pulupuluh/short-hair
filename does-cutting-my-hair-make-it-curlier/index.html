Does Cutting My Hair Make It Curlier

Written by Bardi Sep 09, 2023 · 7 min read

If you've been searching for ways to add some bounce to your hair, you may have come across the idea that cutting your hair can make it curlier. But is this really true? In this article, we'll explore whether or not cutting your hair can actually make it curlier.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Some people believe that cutting your hair can make it curlier because it removes the weight of the hair, allowing it to bounce up more. However, this is not entirely true.

The truth is that the curliness of your hair is determined by your genetics. If you have naturally curly hair, cutting it may make it appear curlier because the shorter length will enhance the natural curl pattern. However, if you have straight hair, cutting it won't magically make it curly.

That being said, there are some things you can do to enhance your natural curl pattern, even if you don't have naturally curly hair.

How to

If you want to enhance your natural curls, there are a few things you can try:

  • Use a curl-enhancing product, such as a curl cream or gel
  • Diffuse your hair with a blow dryer on a low heat setting
  • Scrunch your hair while it's drying to encourage the curls
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to create waves

Step by Step

Using a Curl-Enhancing Product

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product throughout your hair
  3. Scrunch your hair to encourage the curls
  4. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser on a low heat setting

Diffusing Your Hair

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant product
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer
  4. Flip your head upside down and diffuse your hair on a low heat setting

Sleeping with Your Hair in a Bun or Braid

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Create a loose bun or braid
  3. Secure with a hair tie
  4. Sleep on it
  5. In the morning, take out the bun or braid and scrunch your hair

Tips

  • Experiment with different curl-enhancing products to find one that works best for you
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can create frizz
  • Be gentle with your hair when scrunching or diffusing, as rough handling can cause breakage

FAQ

Can cutting my hair make it curlier?

Cutting your hair won't make it curlier if you don't have naturally curly hair. However, if you do have naturally curly hair, cutting it may enhance the curl pattern.

How can I enhance my natural curls?

You can enhance your natural curls by using a curl-enhancing product, diffusing your hair, scrunching your hair, and sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid.

Hair Care

To keep your curls looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

In conclusion, cutting your hair won't magically make it curlier if you don't have naturally curly hair. However, there are things you can do to enhance your natural curl pattern, such as using a curl-enhancing product, diffusing your hair, scrunching your hair, and sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid. By taking good care of your hair, you can keep your curls looking their best.
