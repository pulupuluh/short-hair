Long Hairstyles For Chubby Faces Male

Written by Bardi Aug 17, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you a male with a chubby face? Do you want to style your long hair but don't know what hairstyle would suit your face shape? Don't worry; we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss some of the best long hairstyles for chubby faces male.

Description

Chubby faces can be a bit challenging to style, but with the right hairstyle, it's easy to enhance your features and make them look more defined. Long hairstyles are perfect for chubby faces as they create an illusion of a slimmer face. With a variety of hairstyles to choose from, it can be overwhelming to pick the right one. However, we have compiled a list of the best long hairstyles for chubby faces male that will make you look your best.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's discuss how to choose the right hairstyle for your face shape. The key is to balance out the roundness of your face by adding texture and volume to the top of your head. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face as it will only make your face look wider.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when choosing a hairstyle for your chubby face:

  • Identify your face shape
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face
  • Add texture and volume to the top of your head
  • Opt for longer hairstyles as they create an illusion of a slimmer face

Top 5 Long Hairstyles for Chubby Faces Male

1. The Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for chubby faces. It adds texture and volume to the top of your head, creating an illusion of a slimmer face. To style this haircut, simply comb your hair to one side and apply some hair wax to keep it in place. This hairstyle looks great with a beard or a clean-shaven face.

2. The Messy Bun

The messy bun is a popular hairstyle that is perfect for long hair. It adds volume to the top of your head while keeping your hair out of your face. To style this haircut, gather your hair into a loose bun and secure it with a hair tie. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

3. The Slick Back

The slick back is a sleek and sophisticated hairstyle that is perfect for formal events. It adds volume to the top of your head while keeping your hair off your face. To style this haircut, apply some hair gel to your hair and comb it back. This hairstyle looks great with a suit and tie.

4. The Ponytail

The ponytail is a simple and easy hairstyle that is perfect for casual occasions. It adds volume to the top of your head while keeping your hair out of your face. To style this haircut, gather your hair into a ponytail and secure it with a hair tie. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

5. The Layered Cut

The layered cut is a popular hairstyle that is perfect for long hair. It adds texture and volume to the top of your head while creating an illusion of a slimmer face. To style this haircut, ask your hairstylist to add layers to your hair. This hairstyle looks great with a beard or a clean-shaven face.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face
  • Add texture and volume to the top of your head
  • Opt for longer hairstyles as they create an illusion of a slimmer face
  • Use hair products such as hair wax or gel to keep your hair in place
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a chubby face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as the bowl cut or the mushroom cut. These hairstyles will only make your face look wider.

What hairstyles are best for chubby faces?

Long hairstyles are perfect for chubby faces as they create an illusion of a slimmer face. Opt for hairstyles that add texture and volume to the top of your head, such as the side part, messy bun, slick back, ponytail, or layered cut.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and shiny look. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons or straighteners daily as it can damage your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using too much hair products as it can weigh down your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth

Now that you know the best long hairstyles for chubby faces male, it's time to try them out. Remember to choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and personal style. Be confident and rock your new hairstyle with pride!
