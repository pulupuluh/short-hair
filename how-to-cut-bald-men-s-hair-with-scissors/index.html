How To Cut Bald Men s Hair With Scissors

Written by Frank Aug 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

For some men, baldness is a choice, while for others, it is a result of genetics or medical conditions. Whatever the reason, cutting bald men's hair with scissors is a skill that every barber should have. In this article, we will discuss how to cut bald men's hair with scissors, step by step.

How to Cut Bald Men's Hair with Scissors

Cutting bald men's hair with scissors is a simple process that requires precision and patience. Here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Use a comb to remove any tangles or knots, and trim any long hairs with clippers or scissors.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Using the comb, divide the hair into sections. Start at the front and work your way back, making sure each section is even and straight.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

Using scissors, cut the hair in each section, starting with the longest hair and gradually moving to the shorter hair. Take your time and cut each section evenly and straight.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

Once you have cut all the sections, use a comb to check for evenness. Trim any uneven areas with scissors.

Step 5: Finish the Cut

After you have checked for evenness, use scissors to blend any rough edges and create a clean, polished look. Finish by styling the hair as desired.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a more detailed step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or towel

Step 2: Prepare the Hair

Wash the hair with shampoo and water, and rinse thoroughly. Dry the hair using a towel or blow dryer. Make sure the hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Using the comb, divide the hair into sections. Start at the front and work your way back, making sure each section is even and straight. Use clips to secure each section as you go.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Using scissors, cut the hair in each section, starting with the longest hair and gradually moving to the shorter hair. Take your time and cut each section evenly and straight.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

Once you have cut all the sections, use a comb to check for evenness. Trim any uneven areas with scissors.

Step 6: Finish the Cut

After you have checked for evenness, use scissors to blend any rough edges and create a clean, polished look. Finish by styling the hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Bald Men's Hair with Scissors

Here are a few tips to help you cut bald men's hair with scissors:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Work in small sections to ensure evenness
  • Take your time and be patient
  • Blend rough edges with scissors
  • Use a comb to check for evenness

FAQ

What type of scissors should I use to cut bald men's hair?

Use sharp, professional-grade scissors to ensure a clean cut.

Do I need to use clippers to cut bald men's hair?

No, clippers are not necessary, but they can be helpful for trimming longer hair before using scissors.

Hair Care for Bald Men

While bald men may not have much hair to care for, it is still important to maintain a healthy scalp. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your scalp regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Moisturize your scalp with a light lotion or oil
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or products on your scalp
  • Protect your scalp from the sun with a hat or sunscreen

Cutting bald men's hair with scissors is a simple process that requires precision and patience. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a clean, polished look that your clients will love.
