How To Hide A Big Forehead With Thin Hair

Written by Christine Jul 25, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a big forehead and thin hair, you might feel self-conscious about your appearance. However, there are several ways to style your hair that can help you hide your forehead and boost your confidence. Here, we’ll share some tips and tricks for how to hide a big forehead with thin hair.

Description

A big forehead can make you feel like your hairline starts too high on your head. This can be especially true if you also have thin hair, as the lack of volume can make your forehead appear even larger. However, there are several hairstyles and haircuts that can help you balance your facial features and create the illusion of a smaller forehead.

How to

There are several ways to style your hair to hide a big forehead with thin hair. Here are some of the most effective techniques:

Try a side-swept fringe

A side-swept fringe can help create the illusion of a smaller forehead. This style can work well with thin hair, as the fringe can be cut to blend seamlessly with the rest of your hair. To style a side-swept fringe, start by parting your hair to one side. Then, use a round brush to blow-dry your fringe, sweeping it to the side as you go.

Experiment with layers

Layered hairstyles can add volume and texture to thin hair, which can help balance out a big forehead. Talk to your hairstylist about adding layers to your haircut, and ask for advice on which length and shape will work best for your face shape.

Try a high ponytail

A high ponytail can help create the illusion of a smaller forehead by drawing attention upward. To style a high ponytail, gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with an elastic band. You can also wrap a small section of hair around the band to conceal it.

Step by Step

If you’re not sure how to create these hairstyles, here are some step-by-step instructions:

Side-swept fringe:

  1. Part your hair to one side.
  2. Use a round brush to blow-dry your fringe, sweeping it to the side as you go.
  3. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold your fringe in place.

Layered haircut:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and shape for your face.
  2. Ask for layers to be added throughout your hair to add volume and texture.
  3. Style your hair as desired, using a volumizing mousse or spray to add extra lift.

High ponytail:

  1. Gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with an elastic band.
  2. Wrap a small section of hair around the band to conceal it.
  3. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold your ponytail in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you hide a big forehead with thin hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Try using hair accessories like headbands or hair clips to add visual interest to your hair.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to see what works best for your face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to hide a big forehead with thin hair:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a big forehead and thin hair?

Avoid hairstyles that involve slicking your hair back or wearing it in a tight bun, as these styles can emphasize your forehead. Instead, opt for styles that add volume and texture to your hair.

Can I still wear my hair in a center part if I have a big forehead?

Yes, but you may want to add some volume at the roots to create the illusion of a smaller forehead. You can also try a deep side part or a side-swept fringe to balance out your facial features.

Hair Care

It’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with hot tools.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to nourish your hair from the inside out.

With these tips and tricks, you can feel confident and stylish no matter what your hair type or facial features.
