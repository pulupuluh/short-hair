How To Pick A Hair Colour That Suits Me Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Apr 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task. With so many shades and hues available, it can be difficult to know where to start. However, the right hair colour can enhance your features, boost your confidence and make you feel like a million dollars. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks on how to pick a hair colour that suits you.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Pick a Hair Colour that Suits Me
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Pick a Hair Colour that Suits Me

Before you start, you need to determine your skin tone. This is important as it will help you identify the colours that will suit you best. Skin tones can be divided into three categories: cool, warm and neutral. To determine your skin tone, you can:

  • Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool undertone. If they appear green, you have a warm undertone.
  • Examine the colour of your eyes. If they are blue or grey, you have a cool undertone. If they are brown or hazel, you have a warm undertone.
  • Consider the colour of your natural hair. If it is black, brown or blonde, you have a cool undertone. If it is red or strawberry blonde, you have a warm undertone.

Once you have identified your skin tone, you can start exploring the colours that will suit you best. The general rule of thumb is:

  • Cool undertones: ash blonde, platinum blonde, cool brown, black
  • Warm undertones: golden blonde, honey blonde, warm brown, auburn
  • Neutral undertones: most colours will suit you

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know your skin tone and the colours that will suit you best, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Consider your lifestyle. Are you a low maintenance person or do you enjoy spending time on your hair? If you are low maintenance, consider a shade that will require minimal upkeep.
  2. Think about your natural hair colour. If you want a subtle change, consider a shade that is a few shades lighter or darker than your natural hair colour.
  3. Look at your wardrobe. Consider the colours that you wear most often. Your hair colour should complement your wardrobe.
  4. Consider your job. If you work in a conservative environment, consider a natural hair colour.
  5. Think about the season. In the summer, you may want a lighter shade, while in the winter, you may want a darker shade.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks:

  • Consider your age. As you age, your skin tone changes. What suited you in your 20s may not suit you in your 40s.
  • Consider your eye colour. Your hair colour should enhance your eye colour.
  • Consider your face shape. The right hair colour can enhance your features and make your face appear more symmetrical.
  • Consider highlights or lowlights. These can add dimension and depth to your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions:

  • Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

    • Yes, but you should choose a dye that is specifically formulated for sensitive skin.

  • Can I dye my hair if I have grey hair?

    • Yes, but you may need to use a dye that is specifically formulated for grey hair.

  • How often should I dye my hair?

    • This depends on the colour and the rate of hair growth. Generally, you should dye your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips

Once you have dyed your hair, it is important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically formulated for coloured hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip the colour.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to the sun for prolonged periods of time, as this can cause the colour to fade.

By following these tips and tricks, you can pick a hair colour that suits you and maintain it for longer. Remember, the right hair colour can make you feel confident and beautiful.
