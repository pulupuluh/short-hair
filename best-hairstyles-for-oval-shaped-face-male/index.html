Best Hairstyles For Oval Shaped Face Male

Written by Petter Jul 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you an oval shaped face male looking for the best hairstyles to flatter your facial structure? Look no further because we have compiled a list of the most suitable hairstyles for your face shape. Whether you have long, short, or medium length hair, there's a perfect hairstyle that will make you look your best.

Description

An oval shaped face is characterized by balanced proportions with a slightly curved jawline. This shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for an oval shaped face is to enhance your features and create balance.

How to Choose a Hairstyle for Oval Shaped Face Male

When selecting a hairstyle, consider the following:

  • Your hair type: Is your hair straight, curly, or wavy?
  • Your lifestyle: Do you have an active lifestyle that requires low maintenance hair?
  • Your personal style: What kind of look are you going for?

Once you have considered these factors, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape.

Best Hairstyles for Oval Shaped Face Male

Short Hairstyles

For oval shaped face male, short hairstyles work well to highlight your facial features. Consider a buzz cut, crew cut, or a textured crop. These styles are easy to maintain and can be styled in different ways.

Medium Length Hairstyles

If you have medium length hair, consider a side-parted comb-over, a messy fringe, or a slicked-back undercut. These styles create balance and add some texture to your hair.

Long Hairstyles

For oval shaped face male with longer hair, consider a layered cut or a man bun. These styles add volume and texture to your hair and create an overall balanced look.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Hairstyle

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean hair. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  2. Blow-dry your hair. Use a hair dryer to dry your hair and add volume.
  3. Apply styling product. Choose a product that's suitable for your hair type and style.
  4. Style your hair. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.
  5. Finish with hairspray. Use hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Get regular haircuts. Regular haircuts will keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use quality hair products. Invest in quality hair products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Protect your hair from heat. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.

FAQ

What hairstyles should oval shaped face male avoid?

Oval shaped face male should avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of the head, such as a high pompadour or a quiff. These styles can make your face look longer and unbalanced.

Can oval shaped face male pull off a fringe?

Yes, oval shaped face male can pull off a fringe. A messy fringe or a side-swept fringe can create balance and highlight your facial features.

What is the best hairstyle for oval shaped face male with curly hair?

The best hairstyle for oval shaped face male with curly hair is a medium length cut with layers. This style adds volume and texture to your hair and creates a balanced look.

Hair Care for Oval Shaped Face Male

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a suitable shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.

With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, oval shaped face male can look their best and enhance their facial features. Try out different styles and experiment to find the perfect look for you.
