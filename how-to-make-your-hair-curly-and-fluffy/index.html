How To Make Your Hair Curly And Fluffy

Written by Bardi Aug 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your straight and boring hair? Do you want to add some life and volume to your hair? Well, you're in luck because in this article, we'll be discussing how to make your hair curly and fluffy!

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the details, let's talk about what you'll need to make your hair curly and fluffy:

  • Curling iron or curlers
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray
  • Detangling brush

Now that you have your tools ready, let's get started!

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt and oil from your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply heat protectant spray

Once your hair is clean and damp, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help to protect your hair from the heat of the curling iron or curlers.

Step 3: Detangle your hair

Use a detangling brush to remove any tangles from your hair. This will make it easier to curl your hair and prevent any breakage.

Step 4: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 5: Curl your hair

Using a curling iron or curlers, curl your hair in small sections. Start at the root of your hair and wrap the hair around the curling iron or curlers. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat until all sections of your hair are curled.

Step 6: Apply hairspray

Once you've curled your hair, apply a light mist of hairspray to set the curls.

Tips

  • For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions.
  • Use a smaller curling iron or curlers for tighter curls.
  • Use a larger curling iron or curlers for looser curls.
  • Don't overuse hairspray as it can weigh down your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my hair curly without heat?

A: Yes, you can make your hair curly without heat by using overnight curlers or braiding your hair and leaving it overnight.

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: Your curls can last up to a few days depending on how well you take care of them.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain your curls. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your curls from drying out.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent your curls from getting tangled.

Now that you know how to make your hair curly and fluffy, it's time to give it a try! Don't be afraid to experiment with different curling techniques and products to find what works best for you.
