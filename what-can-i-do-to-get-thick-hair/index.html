What Can I Do To Get Thick Hair Tips And Tricks For Fuller Healthier Locks

Written by Luffy May 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for ways to get thicker, healthier hair, you're not alone. Many people struggle with thinning hair or hair that lacks volume and body. Fortunately, there are several things you can do to improve the thickness and overall health of your hair. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for achieving fuller, more luscious locks.

Table of Contents:

  • Hair Care
  • Nutrition
  • Supplements
  • Styling
  • FAQ

Hair Care

The foundation of healthy, thick hair is a good hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Use a gentle shampoo

Harsh shampoos can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Look for a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo that will cleanse your hair without damaging it.

Condition regularly

Conditioner helps add moisture and nourishment to your hair, which can help improve its thickness and overall health. Use a conditioner that's designed for your hair type, whether it's dry, oily, or somewhere in between.

Avoid heat styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair, making it more prone to breakage and thinning. If you must use heat tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature as low as possible.

Treat your hair gently

Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, especially when it's wet. Wet hair is more fragile and prone to breakage, so use a wide-tooth comb or a brush designed for wet hair to avoid pulling and tugging.

Nutrition

The food you eat can have a big impact on the health and thickness of your hair. Here are some foods to incorporate into your diet:

Protein

Hair is made up of protein, so it's important to make sure you're getting enough in your diet. Good sources of protein include chicken, fish, eggs, and beans.

Vitamins

Vitamins such as biotin, vitamin D, and vitamin E can help improve the health of your hair. You can find these vitamins in foods like nuts, seeds, and leafy greens, or take a supplement if you're not getting enough in your diet.

Omega-3 fatty acids

Omega-3 fatty acids are important for overall hair health, as they help nourish hair follicles and promote growth. You can find omega-3s in fatty fish like salmon, as well as in nuts and seeds.

Supplements

In addition to getting the nutrients you need from your diet, you can also take supplements to help improve the health of your hair. Here are some supplements to consider:

Biotin

Biotin is a B vitamin that's essential for healthy hair, skin, and nails. Taking a biotin supplement can help improve the thickness and overall health of your hair.

Collagen

Collagen is a protein that's important for healthy skin, hair, and nails. Taking a collagen supplement can help improve the thickness and strength of your hair.

Vitamin D

Vitamin D is important for overall hair health, as it helps regulate hair growth. Taking a vitamin D supplement can help ensure you're getting enough of this important nutrient.

Styling

While excessive heat styling can damage your hair, there are some styling techniques you can use to help improve its thickness and volume:

Add layers

Adding layers to your hair can help create the illusion of thickness and volume. Talk to your stylist about adding layers that will work with your hair type and face shape.

Use volumizing products

There are many volumizing products on the market that can help create the appearance of thicker, fuller hair. Look for products like volumizing mousse or root-lifting spray to add body and volume to your hair.

Try a hair topper

If you're struggling with significant hair loss, a hair topper can be a great way to add volume and thickness to your hair. Hair toppers are designed to blend seamlessly with your natural hair, giving you a natural-looking boost of volume.

FAQ

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss. When you're under stress, your body produces cortisol, a hormone that can cause hair follicles to shrink and stop producing hair.

Q: Are there any natural remedies for thinning hair?

A: There are several natural remedies that can help improve the thickness and health of your hair, including using coconut oil, massaging your scalp, and rinsing your hair with apple cider vinegar.

Q: Can hair products cause hair loss?

A: Some hair products, particularly those that contain harsh chemicals, can damage your hair and contribute to hair loss over time. Look for gentle, natural hair products that won't damage your hair or scalp.

In conclusion, there are many things you can do to improve the thickness and overall health of your hair. By following a good hair care routine, getting the right nutrition, taking supplements, and using styling techniques that promote volume and body, you can achieve the fuller, healthier hair you've always wanted.
