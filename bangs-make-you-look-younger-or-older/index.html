Do Bangs Make You Look Younger Or Older The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Mar 24, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you thinking about getting bangs? One of the most common questions people ask is whether bangs make you look younger or older. The answer is not straightforward, as it depends on various factors, including your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this guide, we'll explore the pros and cons of bangs, how to choose the right type of bangs for your face, and how to take care of them. Let's get started!

Pros and Cons of Bangs

Bangs can be a game-changer for your look. They can frame your face, hide a high forehead, and add a touch of sophistication to your hairstyle. However, they also have some downsides. Here are some pros and cons to consider before getting bangs:

Pros

  • Bangs can make you look younger.
  • Bangs can hide a high forehead or a receding hairline.
  • Bangs can frame your face and highlight your eyes.
  • Bangs can add volume and texture to your hair.

Cons

  • Bangs require regular maintenance and trimming.
  • Bangs can get greasy and oily faster than the rest of your hair.
  • Bangs can be challenging to style if you have curly or frizzy hair.
  • Bangs can make you look older if they're too heavy or blunt.

Overall, the pros and cons of bangs depend on your hair type, lifestyle, and personal preferences. If you're considering bangs, talk to your hairstylist about the best option for you.

Choosing the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape

The right type of bangs can enhance your face shape and bring out your best features. Here are some guidelines to follow:

Round Face

If you have a round face, go for long, wispy bangs that sweep to the side. Avoid blunt or heavy bangs that can make your face look wider.

Square Face

If you have a square face, opt for soft, curved bangs that blend with the rest of your hair. Avoid straight, blunt bangs that can exaggerate your angular features.

Heart-Shaped Face

If you have a heart-shaped face, try side-swept or layered bangs that soften your forehead and cheekbones. Avoid heavy, straight-across bangs that can make your chin look too pointy.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, lucky you! You can pull off almost any type of bangs, from blunt to wispy to side-swept. Just make sure they don't cover your eyes or make your face look too long.

Choosing the Right Bangs for Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also influence the type of bangs that suit you best.

Straight Hair

If you have straight hair, you can rock almost any type of bangs, from blunt to choppy to wispy. Just make sure to blow-dry them with a round brush to avoid flatness.

Wavy Hair

If you have wavy hair, try soft, side-swept bangs that blend with the rest of your hair. Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can make your hair look too voluminous.

Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, opt for long, layered bangs that can be worn curly or straight. Avoid short, wispy bangs that can get frizzy or puffy.

Choosing the Right Bangs for Your Personal Style

Your personal style is also a crucial factor in choosing the right bangs. Do you want to look classic, edgy, or romantic? Here are some ideas:

Classic

If you want a classic look, go for blunt, straight-across bangs that are perfectly trimmed. This style looks great with a sleek bob or a long, layered haircut.

Edgy

If you want an edgier look, try choppy, textured bangs that are unevenly cut. This style looks great with a shaggy haircut or a messy updo.

Romantic

If you want a romantic look, try soft, wispy bangs that are slightly longer on the sides. This style looks great with loose waves or a braided hairstyle.

How to Take Care of Bangs

Once you've chosen the right type of bangs, it's essential to take care of them properly. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to avoid split ends and maintain the shape.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to add volume and prevent flatness.
  • Wash your bangs more often than the rest of your hair to avoid oiliness and greasiness.
  • Avoid touching your bangs with your fingers, as this can transfer oil and dirt to them.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your bangs between washes.

FAQ About Bangs

Do bangs make you look younger or older?

Bangs can make you look younger if they're soft, wispy, and layered. However, heavy, blunt bangs can make you look older.

Can I wear bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can wear bangs if you have curly hair. However, make sure to choose long, layered bangs that can be worn curly or straight.

How often should I trim my bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and prevent split ends.

Can I style my bangs differently every day?

Yes, you can style your bangs differently every day, depending on your mood and outfit. Just make sure to use a heat protectant and avoid over-styling them.

Can I wear bangs if I have a small forehead?

Yes, you can wear bangs if you have a small forehead. Just make sure to choose long, side-swept bangs that don't cover your forehead completely.

In conclusion, bangs can be a great way to enhance your look and add some flair to your hairstyle. Whether you want to look younger, edgier, or more romantic, there's a type of bangs that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Just remember to take care of them properly, trim them regularly, and have fun experimenting with different styles!
