How To Make A Bun With Short Hair Black Girl A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 05, 2023 ¬∑ 6 min read

Are you a black girl with short hair looking for a quick and easy hairstyle? A bun is a classic and stylish option that can be achieved with just a few simple steps. In this article, we will guide you through the process of making a bun with short hair. Whether you have natural or relaxed hair, this hairstyle is perfect for any occasion.

Table of Contents

  • Materials You Will Need
  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Creating the Bun
  • Finishing Touches
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

Materials You Will Need

To make a bun with short hair, you will need the following:

  • Comb or brush
  • Bobby pins
  • Hair tie
  • Hair gel or edge control (optional)

Prepping Your Hair

Start by combing or brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. If you have natural hair, you may want to use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine. If you have relaxed hair, make sure it is freshly washed and conditioned.

Next, apply hair gel or edge control around your edges to smooth them down. This step is optional but can help to create a neater and more polished bun.

Creating the Bun

There are several ways to create a bun with short hair, but we will show you the easiest and most popular method:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Twist the ponytail tightly until it coils around itself.
  3. Tuck the end of the ponytail under the bun and secure it with bobby pins.

If you want a larger bun, you can use a hair donut or bun maker to add more volume. Simply slip the donut over your ponytail and wrap your hair around it, securing it with bobby pins.

Finishing Touches

Once your bun is in place, use bobby pins to secure any loose strands or flyaways. You can also use a hair scarf or headband to accessorize your bun and add some flair.

Tips and Tricks

  • If you have very short hair, you can use a hair sponge to create a textured look before making the bun.
  • If your hair is too short to make a full bun, you can create a half bun by gathering the top half of your hair into a ponytail and twisting it into a bun.
  • If you want a sleeker bun, apply a small amount of oil or serum to your hair before making the bun.

FAQs

Q: Can I make a bun with relaxed hair?

A: Yes, you can make a bun with relaxed hair. Just make sure your hair is freshly washed and conditioned before starting.

Q: How do I prevent my bun from falling out?

A: Use bobby pins to secure the bun and any loose strands. You can also use a hair tie with good grip and hold to secure the ponytail.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine.
  • Sleep with a silk or satin bonnet or pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily make a bun with short hair as a black girl. Whether you are going to work, school, or a special event, this hairstyle is versatile and chic. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
