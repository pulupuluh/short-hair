How To Thin Fringe With Thinning Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 18, 2023 ¬∑ 6 min read

Are you tired of your thick and heavy fringe? Do you want a light and airy look? Thinning scissors can help you achieve the perfect look. In this article, we will show you how to thin fringe with thinning scissors. We will also provide tips on how to use them, and answer some frequently asked questions. Read on to learn more!

How to Thin Fringe with Thinning Scissors

Before we start, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of thinning scissors and a comb. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start with clean and dry hair. This will make it easier for you to work with your hair.

Step 2: Comb Your Fringe

Using a comb, brush your fringe to remove any tangles. Make sure your hair is straight and smooth.

Step 3: Divide Your Fringe

Separate your fringe into sections. You can use clips to hold the sections in place.

Step 4: Cut the Hair with Thinning Scissors

Hold the thinning scissors vertically and cut the hair in a zigzag motion. Make sure you don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to cut a little bit at a time to avoid any mistakes.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you have cut a few sections, check your progress. Make sure your fringe is evenly thinned out. If you notice any unevenness, go back and fix it.

Step 6: Blend Your Fringe

Using your fingers, blend your fringe to make it look natural. This will also help you see if any areas need more trimming.

Step 7: Style Your Fringe

Once you are happy with your fringe, style it as you normally would. You can use a hair straightener or curling iron to add some extra flair.

Tips for Using Thinning Scissors

Here are some tips to help you use thinning scissors:

  • Start with small sections of hair.
  • Use a comb to separate your hair.
  • Hold the scissors vertically.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once.
  • Blend your fringe to make it look natural.

FAQ

How do I choose the right thinning scissors?

Look for thinning scissors that have teeth on both blades. This will give you a more natural look. You should also choose scissors that are comfortable to hold.

Can thinning scissors damage my hair?

Thinning scissors are safe to use if you use them correctly. However, if you cut too much hair at once, you can damage your hair. It's important to start with small sections of hair and cut a little bit at a time.

Hair Care

After thinning your fringe, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair in shape.

Now that you know how to thin your fringe with thinning scissors, you can create the perfect look. Remember to use the right tools, start with small sections of hair, and blend your fringe to make it look natural. With a little practice, you can achieve the perfect look every time.
