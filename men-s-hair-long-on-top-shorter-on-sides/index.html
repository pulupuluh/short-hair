Men s Hair Long On Top Shorter On Sides A Relaxed Style

Written by Bardi Mar 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on the popular men's hairstyle: long on top and shorter on the sides. This style has been around for a while, and it's not hard to see why. It's easy to maintain, can be styled in various ways, and looks great on almost anyone.

Description

The long on top and shorter on the sides style is exactly what it sounds like. The hair on the top of the head is left longer, while the hair on the sides and back is cut shorter. This creates a contrast between the two sections of hair, which can be styled in different ways.

This style is versatile and can be adapted to suit different hair textures and face shapes. It can be slicked back, styled into a quiff, or left messy for a more relaxed look.

How to

The long on top and shorter on the sides style is best achieved with the help of a skilled stylist. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look:

Step 1: Choose your length

The first step is to decide how long you want the hair on top to be. This will depend on your personal style and the shape of your face. It's important to communicate your preferences to your stylist so they can create a cut that suits you.

Step 2: Cut the sides and back

The next step is to cut the hair on the sides and back. This should be done with clippers or scissors, depending on the desired length. The most common lengths for this style are a number 2 or 3 on the sides and back.

Step 3: Blend the hair

Once the sides and back have been cut, the hair on top can be blended into the shorter sections. This creates a seamless transition between the two sections of hair.

Step 4: Style the hair

Now that the hair has been cut, it's time to style it. This can be done with the help of a styling product such as wax, pomade, or gel. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long on top and shorter on the sides hairstyle:

  • Regular trims are essential to keep the hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Invest in a good styling product to help you achieve your desired look.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Consider your face shape when choosing the length of the hair on top.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the long on top and shorter on the sides hairstyle:

Is this style suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this style can be adapted to suit different hair types. However, it's important to communicate your preferences with your stylist so they can create a cut that works for you.

How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the hair looking neat and tidy.

Can I style this hair myself?

Yes, this style is easy to maintain and can be styled at home with the help of a good quality styling product.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for keeping your long on top and shorter on the sides hairstyle looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep the hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage the hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock the long on top and shorter on the sides hairstyle with confidence. Remember to communicate your preferences with your stylist and experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
