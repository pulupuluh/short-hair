Guys Medium Long Hairstyles How To Achieve The Look In 2023

Written by Petter May 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's not too short and not too long, then a medium long hairstyle might be perfect for you. This style is versatile, easy to maintain, and can be worn in a variety of ways. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about guys medium long hairstyles, including how to achieve the look, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Achieve the Look
  2. Step by Step Instructions
  3. Tips to Maintain the Style
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Advice

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the guys medium long hairstyle, you'll need to have hair that's at least shoulder length. This style typically features layers that add volume and movement to the hair. You can also add texture to the hair to give it a more natural look.

When it comes to styling, you can wear your hair down or pull it back into a ponytail or bun. You can also experiment with different parts, such as a side part or center part, depending on your face shape and personal preference.

Step by Step Instructions

To achieve the guys medium long hairstyle, follow these step by step instructions:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or styling cream, to add volume and texture to the hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement.
  4. If desired, use a flat iron to straighten the hair or create loose waves.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the style in place.

Tips to Maintain the Style

Here are some tips to help you maintain your guys medium long hairstyle:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.

FAQs

What face shapes does the guys medium long hairstyle suit?

The guys medium long hairstyle can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. However, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid this style as it can make your face look even rounder.

Can I wear my hair in a bun or ponytail with the guys medium long hairstyle?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a bun or ponytail with the guys medium long hairstyle. This style is versatile and can be worn in a variety of ways.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to avoid pulling and tugging on your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair and always use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect guys medium long hairstyle in 2023. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, this style is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident.
