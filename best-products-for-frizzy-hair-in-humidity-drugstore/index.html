Best Products For Frizzy Hair In Humidity Drugstore

Written by Petter Mar 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Humidity can be a nightmare for those with frizzy hair. It can cause hair to become unruly and unmanageable. Fortunately, there are plenty of products available in drugstores that can help combat frizz and keep your hair looking sleek and smooth. In this article, we will explore the best products for frizzy hair in humidity drugstore.

Description

Frizzy hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, humidity, and damage from heat styling tools. Humidity is one of the most common causes of frizz, as it causes hair to absorb moisture from the air, leading to swelling and frizz. Finding the right products can help keep frizz at bay and leave your hair looking smooth and sleek.

How to Combat Frizz in Humidity

There are several ways to combat frizz in humidity, including:

  • Using a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoiding heat styling tools
  • Using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil
  • Using a frizz-fighting styling product

Best Products for Frizzy Hair in Humidity Drugstore

1. John Frieda Frizz Ease Extra Strength Serum

This serum is a cult favorite for a reason. It works to smooth hair and protect against humidity, leaving hair looking sleek and shiny. Apply a dime-sized amount to damp hair and blow-dry for best results.

2. Tresemme Keratin Smooth Heat Protection Shine Spray

This spray not only protects hair from heat damage, but it also helps to smooth and control frizz. Spray onto damp hair before blow-drying or onto dry hair before using heat styling tools.

3. L'Oreal Paris EverSleek Frizz Finish Oil-in-Serum

This oil-in-serum formula helps to control frizz and add shine to hair. Apply to damp hair before blow-drying or to dry hair for a finishing touch.

4. Pantene Smooth and Sleek Shampoo and Conditioner

This shampoo and conditioner duo works to smooth and control frizz, leaving hair looking sleek and shiny. Use regularly for best results.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to keep frizz at bay:

  1. Wash hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to damp hair.
  3. Avoid using heat styling tools, but if necessary, use a heat protectant spray.
  4. Apply a frizz-fighting styling product, such as a serum or oil-in-serum formula.

Tips for Fighting Frizz in Humidity

Here are some additional tips for fighting frizz in humidity:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to help smooth hair and reduce frizz.

FAQ

Q: Can I use these products on color-treated hair?

A: Yes, these products are safe to use on color-treated hair.

Q: Do I need to use all of these products to combat frizz?

A: No, you can choose the products that work best for your hair type and needs.

Q: How often should I use a frizz-fighting product?

A: It depends on your hair type and the level of frizz you experience. Experiment with different products to find the right frequency for your hair.

Hair Care for Frizzy Hair in Humidity

Fighting frizz in humidity doesn't have to be a daunting task. With the right products and hair care routine, you can keep frizz at bay and leave your hair looking sleek and smooth. Experiment with different products and find what works best for your hair type and needs. Remember to avoid over-washing your hair and limit your use of heat styling tools to prevent further damage.
