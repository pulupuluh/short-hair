What About Mobile Hairdressers In Tier 2 A Guide For 2023

Written by Robby Jul 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're someone who loves getting your hair done regularly or you're a mobile hairdresser yourself, you may be wondering what the current rules are for mobile hairdressing in Tier 2 areas. In this guide, we'll go through everything you need to know about mobile hairdressers in Tier 2, including how to stay safe, what precautions to take, and more.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

In Tier 2 areas, mobile hairdressing is allowed to continue as long as the necessary safety measures are taken. This means that mobile hairdressers are able to travel to their clients' homes and provide haircuts, styling, and other services.

However, it's important to note that mobile hairdressers must follow certain guidelines and precautions in order to prevent the spread of COVID-19. These guidelines include wearing a face mask, washing hands regularly, disinfecting equipment between clients, and more.

How To

If you're a mobile hairdresser in a Tier 2 area, it's important to follow the guidelines set out by the government and health authorities. This includes wearing a face mask, washing your hands regularly, and disinfecting your equipment between clients.

You should also consider limiting the number of clients you see in a day and ensuring that there is enough time between appointments for thorough cleaning and disinfecting. It's also a good idea to communicate with your clients beforehand about any safety measures they should take, such as wearing a mask during the appointment.

Step By Step

Here are the steps you can take as a mobile hairdresser in a Tier 2 area to ensure that you're following the necessary safety measures:

  1. Wear a face mask at all times during the appointment
  2. Wash your hands regularly, especially before and after each appointment
  3. Disinfect your equipment between clients
  4. Limit the number of clients you see in a day
  5. Ensure there is enough time between appointments for thorough cleaning and disinfecting
  6. Communicate with your clients beforehand about any safety measures they should take

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind as a mobile hairdresser in a Tier 2 area:

  • Consider offering virtual consultations to reduce the number of in-person appointments
  • Use contactless payment methods to avoid handling cash or cards
  • Encourage clients to wash their hair before the appointment to reduce the time spent in close proximity
  • Consider investing in disposable capes and towels to reduce the risk of transmission

FAQ

What are the guidelines for mobile hairdressing in Tier 2 areas?

Mobile hairdressing is allowed to continue in Tier 2 areas as long as the necessary safety measures are taken. This includes wearing a face mask, washing hands regularly, disinfecting equipment between clients, and more.

Can mobile hairdressers travel to their clients' homes in Tier 2 areas?

Yes, mobile hairdressers are able to travel to their clients' homes and provide haircuts, styling, and other services in Tier 2 areas.

Hair Care

While it's important to stay safe during these times, it's also important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often as this can strip the natural oils
  • Use a conditioner to keep hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Consider using hair masks or treatments to nourish your hair

By following these tips and staying safe during mobile hairdressing appointments, you can continue to enjoy great hair while also protecting your health and the health of others.
