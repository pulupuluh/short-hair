How To Find A Hair Color That Suits You Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy May 12, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of your current hair color and looking for a change? Choosing a new hair color can be overwhelming, but it's also an exciting opportunity to transform your look. With so many shades and tones to choose from, it can be challenging to find the right one. This guide will help you find a hair color that suits you and complements your skin tone, eye color, and personal style.

How to Find a Hair Color That Suits You

Here are some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect hair color:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is an essential factor in determining which hair color will look best on you. If you have a warm skin tone, golden and reddish shades will complement your skin. For those with cool skin tones, ashy and cool-toned hair colors will suit you best. Neutral skin tones can pull off a wide range of colors, but it's best to stick to shades that are close to your natural hair color.

Determine Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in choosing the right hair color. If you have blue or green eyes, warm hair colors like honey and caramel will make your eyes pop. For those with brown eyes, cool-toned colors like ash blonde and platinum will complement your eyes.

Consider Your Lifestyle

When choosing a new hair color, it's essential to consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance you're willing to commit to. If you're always on the go and don't have time for frequent salon visits, a low-maintenance hair color like balayage or ombre might be the best choice for you.

Look at Your Wardrobe

Take a look at your wardrobe and consider which colors you wear the most. If you tend to wear warm colors like reds and oranges, warm-toned hair colors like copper and auburn will complement your wardrobe. For those who wear cool colors like blues and greens, ashy and cool-toned hair colors will be the best match.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have some tips for finding the right hair color, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve your desired look:

Step 1: Determine Your Natural Hair Color

Start by determining your natural hair color. This will help you choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and eye color. If you're unsure of your natural hair color, consult with your hairstylist.

Step 2: Choose a Hair Color

Based on your skin tone, eye color, and personal style, choose a hair color that suits you. If you're unsure, consult with your hairstylist for recommendations.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Before coloring your hair, it's essential to prepare it by washing and conditioning it thoroughly. This will help the color adhere to your hair and last longer.

Step 4: Apply the Hair Color

Follow the instructions on the hair color box and apply the color to your hair. Make sure to wear gloves and apply the color evenly from root to tip.

Step 5: Rinse and Condition

Once the color has set, rinse your hair thoroughly and apply the conditioner provided in the hair color box. This will help seal in the color and keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Now that you have a new hair color, it's essential to take care of it to ensure it lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair color:

Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Regular shampoo and conditioner can strip your hair of its color, so it's essential to use color-safe products that are specifically designed to protect your hair color.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause your color to fade faster. Try to avoid heat styling as much as possible, or use heat protectant products to minimize damage.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's rays can also cause your hair color to fade, so it's important to protect your hair when you're outside. Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to shield your hair from the sun.

FAQ

What hair color suits me if I have pale skin?

If you have pale skin, cool-toned hair colors like ash blonde and platinum will complement your skin tone best.

How often should I color my hair?

It depends on the hair color and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, it's recommended to color your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired color.

Can I color my hair at home?

Yes, you can color your hair at home, but it's essential to follow the instructions on the hair color box carefully and take all necessary precautions to avoid damaging your hair.

Conclusion

Choosing a new hair color can be a fun and exciting way to change up your look. By considering your skin tone, eye color, and personal style, you can find a hair color that suits you and complements your features. Remember to take care of your hair color by using color-safe products and avoiding heat styling as much as possible. With these tips and tricks, you'll be on your way to a fabulous new hair color in no time!
