How To Cut Female Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jun 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of spending a lot of money at the salon just for a simple haircut? Do you want to learn how to cut female hair with clippers? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut female hair with clippers. We will also give you tips on how to care for your hair after the cut. Let's get started!

How to Cut Female Hair with Clippers

Before we start, make sure that you have the right tools. You will need a good quality clipper, hair scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a cape or towel to cover your clothes. Once you have all the tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before cutting your hair, make sure that it is clean and dry. This will make it easier for you to cut and style. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner, and then dry it using a hair dryer.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, left side, right side, and back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Start with the Back

Start with the back section of your hair. Use the clipper to trim the hair at the nape of your neck. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a comb to guide the clipper. Make sure to go slowly and use a consistent motion.

Step 4: Move to the Sides

Once you have finished the back section, move to the sides. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the same technique as before. Make sure to blend the hair at the back and sides.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Use the scissors to cut the hair on the top of your head. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length. Make sure to blend the hair at the top and sides.

Step 6: Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, use the scissors to trim them to the desired length. Start at the center and work your way outwards, using the comb to guide the scissors.

Step 7: Check for Any Uneven Spots

After you have finished cutting your hair, use the comb to check for any uneven spots. If you find any, trim them with the scissors.

Step 8: Clean Up

Once you have finished cutting your hair, clean up any loose hair using a brush or vacuum cleaner. Remove the hair clips and cape or towel.

Tips for Cutting Female Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips that will help you cut your hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head
  • Go slowly and use a consistent motion
  • Don't cut too much hair at once
  • Use the scissors to blend the hair at the top and sides

FAQs

Can I cut my hair with clippers if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can cut your hair with clippers if you have curly hair. Just make sure to use a longer guard and go slowly to avoid cutting too much hair.

Do I need to oil my clippers?

Yes, it is important to oil your clippers regularly to keep them running smoothly. Follow the manufacturer's instructions on how to oil your clippers.

How often should I cut my hair with clippers?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and the style that you want to maintain. Generally, it is recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair
  • Get regular trims to maintain your hairstyle

Now that you know how to cut female hair with clippers, you can save money and time by doing it yourself. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow these steps. Happy cutting!
