Why Are My Eyebrow Hairs Sticking Up

Written by Frank Jul 22, 2023 · 7 min read
Why Are My Eyebrow Hairs Sticking Up? | Hair Care Blog

Do you wake up every morning to find that your eyebrow hairs are sticking up in different directions? Don't worry, you're not alone. This is a common issue that many people face. In this article, we will discuss the reasons why your eyebrow hairs are sticking up and how to fix them.

Causes of Eyebrow Hairs Sticking Up

There are several reasons why your eyebrow hairs may be sticking up, such as:

  • Dryness: When the eyebrow hairs are dry, they tend to stick up in different directions. This can happen due to various reasons like harsh weather, excessive use of eyebrow products, and not moisturizing the eyebrows regularly.
  • Genetics: If your parents have bushy eyebrows, then there is a chance that you may inherit the same.
  • Age: As you age, your hair tends to become drier and coarser, which results in sticking up.
  • Underlying Medical Conditions: Certain medical conditions like eczema and psoriasis can cause dryness in the eyebrows, leading to hair sticking up.

How to Fix Eyebrow Hairs Sticking Up

The good news is that there are several ways to fix eyebrow hairs sticking up. Here are some of the ways:

  • Use a Brow Gel: Brow gel is an excellent product to keep your eyebrow hairs in place. Apply a small amount of brow gel and brush it through your eyebrows to keep them in place.
  • Moisturize: Regularly moisturizing your eyebrows can prevent them from becoming dry and frizzy. Use a gentle moisturizer or a hair oil and massage it into your eyebrows.
  • Trim Your Eyebrows: If your eyebrows are too bushy, you may want to consider trimming them. Use a small pair of scissors to trim any long hairs that are sticking up.
  • Use a Toothbrush: A clean toothbrush can be used to brush your eyebrows gently. This will help to keep them in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to fix eyebrow hairs sticking up:

  1. Take a small amount of brow gel on a spoolie brush.
  2. Brush the gel through your eyebrows in the direction of hair growth.
  3. Use a clean toothbrush to brush your eyebrows gently.
  4. Trim any long hairs that are sticking up using a small pair of scissors.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair oil or moisturizer to your eyebrows and massage it in.

Tips to Prevent Eyebrow Hairs Sticking Up

Here are some tips to prevent eyebrow hairs from sticking up:

  • Moisturize: Regularly moisturize your eyebrows to keep them hydrated and prevent dryness.
  • Avoid Over-Plucking: Over-plucking your eyebrows can cause them to become sparse and uneven, which can make it difficult to keep them in place.
  • Use Gentle Products: Use gentle products that do not contain harsh chemicals that can dry out your eyebrows.
  • Brush Your Eyebrows: Brush your eyebrows regularly to keep them in place.

FAQs on Eyebrow Hairs Sticking Up

Q. Can Stress Cause Eyebrow Hairs to Stick Up?

A. Yes, stress can cause hair loss or hair thinning, which can make your eyebrow hairs stick up.

Q. Can Using Too Much Eyebrow Product Cause Eyebrow Hairs to Stick Up?

A. Yes, using too much eyebrow product can cause dryness, which can make your eyebrow hairs stick up.

Q. Can Eyebrow Hairs Stick Up Due to Hormonal Changes?

A. Yes, hormonal changes can cause hair growth in unwanted areas, including eyebrows, which can lead to sticking up.

Hair Care for Eyebrow Hairs

Just like the hair on your head, your eyebrow hairs require proper care to stay healthy and in place. Here are some tips to take care of your eyebrow hairs:

  • Moisturize: Regularly moisturize your eyebrows to keep them hydrated and prevent dryness.
  • Avoid Over-Plucking: Over-plucking your eyebrows can cause them to become sparse and uneven, which can make it difficult to keep them in place.
  • Use Gentle Products: Use gentle products that do not contain harsh chemicals that can dry out your eyebrows.
  • Brush Your Eyebrows: Brush your eyebrows regularly to keep them in place.

By following these tips, you can keep your eyebrow hairs healthy, hydrated, and in place.
