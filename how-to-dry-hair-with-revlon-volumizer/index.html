How To Dry Hair With Revlon Volumizer A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 22, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for an easy and effective way to dry your hair, the Revlon Volumizer is the perfect tool for you. This all-in-one styling tool combines a hair dryer and a round brush to give you a salon-quality blowout in the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve a perfect blowout with the Revlon Volumizer.

Description

The Revlon Volumizer is a styling tool that combines a hair dryer and a round brush. It has a unique oval shape that helps to create volume and lift at the roots, while the round edges smooth and straighten your hair. The Volumizer has three heat and speed settings, making it suitable for all hair types. It also has a cool shot button to set your style in place.

How to Use the Revlon Volumizer

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before using the Volumizer, make sure your hair is clean and towel-dried. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from damage. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start with the bottom sections and work your way up.

Step 2: Turn on the Volumizer

Plug in your Volumizer and turn it on. Select the heat and speed setting that is appropriate for your hair type. For fine or thin hair, use the low heat and speed setting. For thick or coarse hair, use the high heat and speed setting.

Step 3: Start with the Roots

Take a section of hair and place the Volumizer at the roots. Hold it there for a few seconds to create volume and lift. Then, slowly glide the Volumizer down the length of your hair, rotating it as you go. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Use the Cool Shot Button

Once you've finished drying each section of hair, use the cool shot button to set your style in place. Hold the button for a few seconds before releasing it.

Tips for Using the Revlon Volumizer

  • Start with clean, towel-dried hair to achieve the best results.
  • Divide your hair into sections for easier styling.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from damage.
  • Don't overload the Volumizer with too much hair at once. This can cause it to tangle or get stuck.
  • Use the cool shot button to set your style in place and add shine.

FAQ

Is the Revlon Volumizer suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the Volumizer has three heat and speed settings, making it suitable for all hair types.

Can I use the Volumizer on wet hair?

No, it is recommended to towel-dry your hair before using the Volumizer.

How often should I clean my Volumizer?

It is recommended to clean your Volumizer after every use. Use a soft, damp cloth to wipe down the brush head and remove any hair or debris.

Hair Care Tips

  • Avoid using the Volumizer on wet hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Don't overload the Volumizer with too much hair at once. This can cause it to tangle or get stuck.
  • Use the cool shot button to add shine and set your style in place.
  • Clean your Volumizer after every use to prolong its lifespan.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a perfect blowout with the Revlon Volumizer. This versatile styling tool is a game-changer for anyone who wants to achieve salon-quality results at home.
