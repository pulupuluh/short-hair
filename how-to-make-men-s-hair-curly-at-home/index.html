How To Make Men s Hair Curly At Home A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 02, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're a man with straight hair and you're looking to switch up your look, making your hair curly can be a great option. However, getting your hair curled at a salon can be expensive and time-consuming. Luckily, there are ways to make men's hair curly at home. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to make men's hair curly at home, including tips, step-by-step instructions, FAQs, and more.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Men's Hair Curly at Home
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Making Men's Hair Curly
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Make Men's Hair Curly at Home

Before we dive into the step-by-step instructions, let's talk about the different methods you can use to make men's hair curly at home:

  • Using a curling iron
  • Using a hair straightener
  • Using rollers
  • Using a perm kit

In this article, we'll focus on using a hair straightener to make men's hair curly, as it's the most effective and versatile method.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these steps to make men's hair curly with a hair straightener:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Apply heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the hair straightener.
  3. Section your hair into small sections using hair clips.
  4. Take one section of hair and clamp the hair straightener near the roots.
  5. Twist the hair straightener away from your face, and slowly pull it down the length of the section of hair.
  6. Repeat steps 4-5 for each section of hair.
  7. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a more natural look.
  8. Apply hairspray or styling cream to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Making Men's Hair Curly

Here are some tips to keep in mind when making men's hair curly:

  • Use a hair straightener with ceramic plates, as they heat up evenly and won't damage your hair as much as other materials.
  • Don't clamp the hair straightener too tightly on your hair, as this can cause creases and damage.
  • Experiment with different sizes of sections to see what works best for your hair.
  • Use a high-quality heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • If you want more defined curls, use a smaller hair straightener.

FAQs

Can men's hair be permed?

Yes, men's hair can be permed using a perm kit. However, this is a more permanent solution and can be damaging to your hair.

How long will the curls last?

The length of time the curls will last depends on your hair type and the method you use to curl your hair. Generally, the curls will last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Can I make my hair curly without heat?

Yes, you can make your hair curly without heat by using rollers or braiding your hair while it's wet and letting it air dry.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep your curly hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently, as this can cause damage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl defining cream to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

Now that you know how to make men's hair curly at home, you can experiment with different styles and find the one that works best for you. Just remember to take care of your hair and use high-quality products to keep it healthy and looking its best.
