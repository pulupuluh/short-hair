How To Style Short Layered Hair With Bangs Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jun 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

In this day and age, short haircuts are becoming more popular, and for good reason. They're easy to maintain, can suit any face shape, and can be styled in a variety of ways. One of the most popular short haircuts is the short layered hair with bangs. In this article, we'll show you how to style your short layered hair with bangs in a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Short Layered Hair with Bangs
  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Short Layered Hair with Bangs

When it comes to styling short layered hair with bangs, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose the right haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture
  • Use the right styling products to add texture and volume
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to style your short layered hair with bangs:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray from roots to tips. This will help add volume and texture to your hair.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush. Focus on lifting the roots and creating volume at the crown of your head.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture and waves to your hair. You can also use a texturizing spray to add more volume and hold.
  5. If you have bangs, use a small round brush to blow dry them. This will help add volume and shape to your bangs.
  6. Finish off your hairstyle with a hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your short layered hair with bangs:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you. You can try a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or a half-up, half-down style.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes. This will help absorb oil and add volume to your hair.
  • Invest in the right styling tools, such as a good quality curling iron or flat iron, to make styling your hair easier and more efficient.
  • Don't be afraid to trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh and neat.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short layered hair with bangs:

  • Can I style my short layered hair with bangs without heat?
    Yes, you can use rollers or braids to create waves in your hair without heat.
  • How often should I wash my short layered hair with bangs?
    It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week.
  • What kind of products should I use on my short layered hair with bangs?
    You should use products that add volume and texture to your hair, such as mousse, texturizing spray, and dry shampoo. Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down.

Hair Care for Short Layered Hair with Bangs

Here are some tips for maintaining your short layered hair with bangs:

  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and healthy.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup from styling products.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your short layered hair with bangs in no time. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you.
