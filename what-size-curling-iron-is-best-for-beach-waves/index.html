What Size Curling Iron Is Best For Beach Waves

Written by Robby Aug 26, 2023 · 6 min read

Beach waves are a timeless hairstyle that can be achieved with the right tools and techniques. One of the most important tools is a curling iron. But what size curling iron is best for beach waves? In this blog post, we will answer that question and give you tips for achieving the perfect beachy waves.

Description

Beach waves are a relaxed, tousled hairstyle that mimics the natural waves and texture of hair after a day spent at the beach. This hairstyle is popular for its effortless and carefree look, and can be achieved with the right curling iron size.

How to Achieve Beach Waves

To achieve beach waves, you will need a curling iron with a barrel size between 1 and 2 inches. You will also need a heat protectant spray, a comb, and a finishing spray. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. This will prevent damage from the heat of the curling iron. Blow-dry your hair until it is completely dry.

Step 2: Section your hair

Using a comb, divide your hair into sections. The size of the sections will depend on the thickness of your hair. Thicker hair will require larger sections.

Step 3: Curl your hair

Take a section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for 10-15 seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process with all sections of hair.

Step 4: Finish your hair

Once all sections of hair are curled, apply a finishing spray to hold the waves in place. You can also use a comb to gently loosen the waves and create a more natural look.

Tips for Achieving Perfect Beach Waves

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect beachy waves:

  • Use a curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline barrel. These materials distribute heat evenly, reducing the risk of damage to your hair.
  • For tighter waves, use a smaller barrel size. For looser waves, use a larger barrel size.
  • Don’t curl your hair all the way to the ends. Leave the ends straight for a more natural look.
  • Use a finishing spray with a matte finish to avoid a shiny, greasy look.

FAQ

What size curling iron should I use for beach waves?

A curling iron with a barrel size between 1 and 2 inches is best for beach waves.

What type of curling iron should I use for beach waves?

A curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline barrel is best for beach waves.

How do I prevent damage to my hair when curling?

Apply a heat protectant spray before curling your hair to prevent damage from the heat of the curling iron.

Hair Care for Beach Waves

To maintain your beachy waves, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a sea salt spray to enhance the texture of your waves. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.

In conclusion, a curling iron with a barrel size between 1 and 2 inches is ideal for achieving perfect beach waves. Follow the steps and tips in this blog post to achieve the perfect beachy look, and remember to take care of your hair to maintain healthy and beautiful waves.
