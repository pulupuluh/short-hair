How To Slick Back Long Hair Without Looking Greasy

Written by Frank Aug 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

In today's world, slicked back hair is a trend that has been around for quite some time. However, when it comes to long hair, it can be quite tricky to achieve this look without looking greasy. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to slick back long hair without looking greasy, as well as some tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Back Long Hair Without Looking Greasy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Back Long Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect slicked back look for your long hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. Make sure that you thoroughly rinse out all of the product to avoid any buildup that could cause your hair to look greasy.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as pomade or gel, to your damp hair. Make sure to use a small amount, as too much product can make your hair look greasy. Work the product through your hair with your fingers, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to comb your hair back, starting from the front and moving towards the back of your head. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction that you want it to stay in place.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while continuing to comb it back. Make sure to use a low heat setting and hold the dryer at a distance to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish the look with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold your hair in place without making it look greasy.

Tips for Slicking Back Long Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back look:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that is designed for your hair type.
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Make sure to comb your hair in the direction that you want it to stay in place.
  • Use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Finish the look with a light mist of hairspray to help hold your hair in place.

FAQs

Can I Slick Back Long Hair Without Using Product?

While it is possible to slick back long hair without using product, it may not stay in place as well. Using a styling product can help to hold your hair in place and prevent it from looking greasy.

What Type of Styling Product Should I Use?

The type of styling product that you should use depends on your hair type and personal preference. Pomade and gel are popular choices for slicked back hair, but you can also use wax or mousse.

How Often Should I Wash My Hair?

You should wash your hair as often as needed to keep it clean and healthy. However, washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can cause it to become dry and brittle.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your long hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and using the step-by-step guide provided in this article, you can achieve the perfect slicked back look for your long hair without looking greasy. Remember to use a high-quality styling product, comb your hair in the direction that you want it to stay in place, and finish the look with a light mist of hairspray. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to rock this trendy hairstyle with confidence!
