What Type Of Haircut Suits My Face

Written by Bardi Apr 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of getting the wrong haircut that doesn't suit your face? Choosing the right haircut can be overwhelming, but it's essential to enhance your facial features. In this article, we will discuss what type of haircut suits your face shape, how to get it, and hair care tips to maintain your new look.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Haircuts for Different Face Shapes
  • How to Get the Right Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs

Determining Your Face Shape

Before selecting the perfect haircut, you need to determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are round, square, oval, heart, and diamond. Here's how to determine your face shape:

  • Round face: Your face is equal in length and width, and your jawline is soft and curved.
  • Square face: Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost equal in width, and your jawline is angular.
  • Oval face: Your face is longer than it is wide, and your jawline is slightly curved.
  • Heart face: Your forehead is wider than your cheekbones, and your face gradually narrows towards your chin.
  • Diamond face: Your cheekbones are wider than your forehead and jawline, and your chin is pointed.

Haircuts for Different Face Shapes

Now that you know your face shape, here are the haircuts that suit different face shapes:

  • Round face: Layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and long bobs.
  • Square face: Soft layers, textured bobs, and side-swept bangs.
  • Oval face: Almost any haircut suits this face shape, but long layers and blunt bobs look great.
  • Heart face: Side-swept bangs, layered pixie cuts, and chin-length bobs.
  • Diamond face: Side-swept bangs, textured layers, and long bobs.

How to Get the Right Haircut

Now that you know the haircut that suits your face shape, it's time to get it right. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Research the haircut you want and show your stylist pictures to ensure they understand.
  2. Discuss your lifestyle, hair type, and maintenance routine with your stylist to determine the best haircut for you.
  3. Ask your stylist to cut your hair dry to ensure they can see the shape better.
  4. Consider getting a trim regularly to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Maintaining your haircut is essential to ensure it lasts longer and looks great. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair with sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and use a heat protectant spray when necessary.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Q. Will a short haircut suit a round face?

A. Yes, a short haircut like a pixie cut or a bob can elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

Q. Can I get bangs with a square face?

A. Yes, side-swept bangs or textured bangs can soften your angular jawline and balance your face.

Q. What haircut should I get if I have an oval face?

A. Almost any haircut suits an oval face, but long layers and blunt bobs look great.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have the perfect haircut, it's essential to take care of it. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and use a heat protectant spray when necessary.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Choosing the right haircut can enhance your facial features and boost your confidence. By following these tips, you can get the perfect haircut for your face shape and maintain it for longer. Don't be afraid to experiment with different haircuts until you find the one that suits you the best.
