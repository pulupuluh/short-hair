How To Get Your Hair Really Curly

Written by Eveline Jun 05, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair and want to add some curls to your locks? Look no further! In this article, we will explore the steps you can take to get your hair really curly. From using the right products to styling techniques, we’ve got you covered. Read on to learn more.

Description

Curly hair is beautiful, and it’s no wonder why so many people want to achieve this look. However, getting your hair to curl can be a challenge, especially if you have straight hair. But don’t worry, with the right tools and techniques, you can get your hair really curly. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect curls.

How to

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to getting your hair really curly is to start with clean hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any buildup from your hair. This will help your curls to form better and last longer.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. You can use a mousse, gel, or curl cream, depending on your hair type and texture. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to work it into each strand.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, starting from the back of your head. Use clips to hold each section in place. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that each strand is curled evenly.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

Using a curling iron, curl each section of your hair. Start at the root and wrap the hair around the barrel of the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process for each section of your hair, working from the back to the front.

Step 5: Apply Hairspray

Once you have curled all of your hair, apply a light hairspray to hold the curls in place. Be sure to hold the can at least 6 inches from your head to avoid making your hair sticky or stiff.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you get your hair really curly:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Experiment with different curling iron sizes to achieve different types of curls.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural curls.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a bun or braid overnight to achieve heatless curls.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a straightener to curl my hair?

A: Yes, you can use a straightener to curl your hair. Simply twist your hair around the straightener and pull it through to create a curl.

Q: How often should I curl my hair?

A: It’s best to limit heat styling to once or twice a week to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: How do I maintain my curls?

A: To maintain your curls, avoid brushing or combing your hair too often. Instead, use your fingers to gently separate the curls. You can also refresh your curls by applying a light mist of water or curl refresher spray.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can get your hair really curly and achieve the perfect curly look. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. With a little practice, you’ll be able to create beautiful curls that will last all day.
