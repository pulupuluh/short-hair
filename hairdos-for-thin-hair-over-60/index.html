Hairdos For Thin Hair Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Mar 13, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairdo for your thin hair over 60? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with some of the best hairdos for thin hair over 60, along with tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look. Let’s dive in!

How to Choose the Right Hairdo

Before we get into the specific hairdos, it’s important to understand how to choose the right one for you. When it comes to thin hair, it’s best to avoid styles that are too long or too short, as they can make your hair look even thinner. Instead, opt for styles that are shoulder-length or slightly shorter.

Layers can also be your best friend when it comes to thin hair. They can create the illusion of volume and texture, making your hair look fuller and thicker.

Hairdos for Thin Hair over 60

1. The Bob

The bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages. For thin hair over 60, opt for a chin-length bob with layers. This will create the illusion of volume and texture, making your hair look fuller and thicker.

2. The Pixie

If you’re feeling brave, the pixie cut is a great option for thin hair over 60. This short, low-maintenance style is perfect for those who want a chic and modern look.

3. The Shag

The shag is another great option for thin hair over 60. This style features layers of varying lengths, creating volume and texture. It’s a great way to add some edge to your look without sacrificing femininity.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Hairdo

Now that you’ve chosen your hairdo, it’s time to learn how to achieve the perfect look. Follow these steps for a flawless finish:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots.

3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, focusing on the roots.

4. If you’re using a straightener or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray first.

5. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairdo

Maintaining your hairdo is just as important as achieving it. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking great:

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add volume and texture to your hair.

2. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it and make it look even thinner.

3. Use a dry shampoo in between washes to help add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I still dye my hair if it’s thin?

Yes, you can still dye your hair if it’s thin. However, it’s important to choose a dye that is gentle on your hair and scalp, as harsh chemicals can damage thin hair even more.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if it’s thin?

It’s best to wash your hair every other day if it’s thin. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it look even thinner.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your hair is crucial for maintaining a healthy and voluminous look. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in tip-top shape:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as it’s less harsh than a brush.

2. Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as it can cause breakage and thinning.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish your hair.

4. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock any hairdo with confidence, no matter how thin your hair may be. Remember, it’s all about choosing the right style and taking care of your hair to achieve the perfect look!
