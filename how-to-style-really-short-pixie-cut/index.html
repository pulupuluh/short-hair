How to Style Really Short Pixie Cut

Styling a really short pixie cut may seem like a daunting task, but it's actually quite simple. The key is to focus on creating texture and volume, which can be achieved with the right products and tools.

1. Start with Clean Hair

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid any buildup, which can weigh down your hair and make it harder to style.

2. Apply a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair to add texture and volume. Use a small amount and work it through your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to lift your hair and create more volume.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using your fingers to lift and tousle your hair. This will help to create more texture and volume. Use a round brush to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

4. Use a Texturizing Spray

Once your hair is dry, use a texturizing spray to add more texture and volume. Spray it onto your hair and use your fingers to tousle your hair and create more movement.

5. Finish with a Hairspray

Finish off the look with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place. Spray it onto your hair and use your fingers to tousle and shape your hair into your desired style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style really short pixie cut:

Step 1: Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid any buildup.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair to add texture and volume.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair while using your fingers to lift and tousle your hair.

Step 4: Use a texturizing spray to add more texture and movement to your hair.

Step 5: Finish off the look with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your really short pixie cut:

1. Use the right products. Look for volumizing mousses, texturizing sprays, and light hold hairsprays to add texture and volume to your hair.

2. Don't be afraid to experiment. Try different styles and techniques to find what works best for you.

3. Use your fingers to tousle and shape your hair. This will help to create more texture and volume.

4. Visit your stylist regularly to maintain the look and keep your hair healthy.

FAQs

Q: Can I style really short pixie cut with curly hair?

A: Yes, you can! Follow the same steps as above, but use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls.

Q: How often should I wash my really short pixie cut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how often you style it. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every two to three days to avoid any buildup.

Hair Care for Really Short Pixie Cut

Maintaining a really short pixie cut requires some extra care to keep your hair healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

1. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid any buildup.

2. Avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.

3. Visit your stylist regularly to maintain the look and keep your hair healthy.

4. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Styling a really short pixie cut can be fun and easy with the right products and techniques. Follow our guide and tips to create the perfect look that suits your style and personality.
