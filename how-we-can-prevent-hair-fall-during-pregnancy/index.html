How To Prevent Hair Fall During Pregnancy

Written by Robby May 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you experiencing hair fall during pregnancy? You are not alone. Many women experience hair fall during pregnancy due to hormonal changes in their body. However, there are ways to prevent hair fall during pregnancy. In this article, we will discuss how to prevent hair fall during pregnancy and share some tips to keep your hair healthy and strong.

How to Prevent Hair Fall During Pregnancy

There are several ways to prevent hair fall during pregnancy. Here are some of the most effective ways:

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet is essential during pregnancy. Your body needs essential nutrients to support the growth of your baby and to keep your hair healthy. Make sure to include plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, and healthy fats in your diet. Avoid processed foods and sugary drinks as they do not provide any nutritional value.

Manage Stress

Stress can cause hair fall. During pregnancy, your body goes through many changes, and it can be stressful. Take some time for yourself to relax and unwind. Practice meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises to manage stress.

Use a Mild Shampoo

Using a mild shampoo can prevent hair fall during pregnancy. Choose a shampoo that is gentle on your hair and scalp. Avoid shampoos that contain harsh chemicals as they can damage your hair.

Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools like flat irons, curling irons, and hair dryers can damage your hair and cause hair fall. Avoid using heat styling tools during pregnancy and let your hair air dry. If you must use heat styling tools, use them sparingly and at a low heat setting.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to prevent hair fall during pregnancy:

  1. Eat a healthy diet
  2. Manage stress
  3. Use a mild shampoo
  4. Avoid heat styling tools
  5. Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends
  6. Massage your scalp to improve blood circulation
  7. Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall During Pregnancy

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall during pregnancy:

  • Take prenatal vitamins to provide your body with essential nutrients
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your body hydrated
  • Avoid smoking and alcohol as they can damage your hair
  • Get enough sleep to reduce stress and promote hair growth
  • Use natural hair masks like coconut oil or aloe vera to nourish your hair

FAQs

Can hair fall during pregnancy be prevented?

Yes, hair fall during pregnancy can be prevented by eating a healthy diet, managing stress, using a mild shampoo, avoiding heat styling tools, and getting regular haircuts.

When does hair fall stop after pregnancy?

Hair fall usually stops three to six months after pregnancy. This is because hormonal changes in your body return to normal after giving birth.

Is it normal to lose hair during pregnancy?

Yes, it is normal to lose some hair during pregnancy due to hormonal changes in your body. However, excessive hair fall during pregnancy should be addressed by a healthcare provider.

Hair Care After Pregnancy

After pregnancy, it is important to continue taking care of your hair to prevent hair fall. Here are some tips:

  • Eat a healthy diet
  • Use a mild shampoo
  • Avoid heat styling tools
  • Get regular haircuts
  • Massage your scalp
  • Avoid tight hairstyles

In conclusion, hair fall during pregnancy is normal, but it can be prevented by following a healthy diet, managing stress, using a mild shampoo, and avoiding heat styling tools. By taking care of your hair during and after pregnancy, you can keep your hair healthy and strong.
