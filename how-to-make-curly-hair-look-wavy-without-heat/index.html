How To Make Curly Hair Look Wavy Without Heat A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to switch up your curly hair routine, trying out a wavy style can be a fun and low-maintenance option. But if you don't want to use heat tools like curling irons or wands, how can you achieve this look? In this article, we'll show you how to make curly hair look wavy without heat.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Make Curly Hair Look Wavy Without Heat

The key to making curly hair look wavy without heat is to use products that will help loosen the curls and create a more relaxed wave pattern. Here are some products you'll need:

  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Sea salt spray
  • Hair oil or serum
  • Hair ties or clips

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make your curly hair look wavy without heat:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle and moisturize your curls.
  2. Section your hair into two or more parts, depending on how thick your hair is. Clip or tie each section out of the way.
  3. Take one section of hair and spray it with sea salt spray, focusing on the mid-lengths to ends. Use your fingers to scrunch up the hair and create a wave pattern.
  4. Repeat the previous step on each section of hair, making sure to apply enough sea salt spray to create a defined wave pattern.
  5. Once you've sprayed and scrunched all of your hair, apply a hair oil or serum to the ends to keep them hydrated and prevent frizz.
  6. Loosely twist each section of hair and clip it up, creating a loose bun or twist. Leave your hair like this for several hours or overnight.
  7. Take out the clips or ties and gently shake out your hair. Use your fingers to separate the waves and create more volume.
  8. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips for Making Curly Hair Look Wavy Without Heat

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect wavy look:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before applying any products.
  • For a more defined wave pattern, twist each section of hair before applying sea salt spray.
  • Experiment with different types of sea salt sprays to find one that works best for your hair type.
  • If you have fine or thin hair, avoid applying too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Don't over-manipulate your hair while it's drying, as this can disrupt the wave pattern.

FAQ

Can I use this method on all types of curly hair?

Yes, this method can work on all types of curly hair, from loose waves to tight coils. However, the end result may vary depending on your hair type and the products you use.

How long will the waves last?

The waves can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on your hair type and the amount of product you use. To prolong the waves, try sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid.

Can I use other styling products, like mousse or gel?

Yes, you can use other styling products in addition to sea salt spray and hair oil. Just make sure to apply them in moderation, as too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

In order to keep your curly hair healthy and beautiful, it's important to follow a few basic hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly, using a hair mask or oil treatment to hydrate your curls.
  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and disrupt the curl pattern.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can make your curly hair look wavy without heat and keep it healthy and beautiful.
