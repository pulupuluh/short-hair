How To Grow Hair After Bariatric Surgery

Written by Christine Mar 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss after undergoing bariatric surgery? Don't worry, you are not alone. Post-bariatric surgery hair loss is a common issue that many people face. In this article, we will discuss how to grow hair after bariatric surgery and provide you with useful tips to help you regain your locks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Grow Hair After Bariatric Surgery
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Promote Hair Growth
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Routine

How to Grow Hair After Bariatric Surgery

It is important to note that hair loss after bariatric surgery is temporary and usually lasts for six to twelve months. The good news is that you can take steps to promote hair growth and prevent further hair loss. Here's how:

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Ensure you are consuming enough protein, vitamins, and minerals. Protein is essential for hair growth, and a lack of it can cause hair loss. Include foods such as eggs, fish, chicken, nuts, and legumes in your diet. Additionally, consume foods rich in iron, zinc, and biotin, such as leafy greens, beans, and nuts.

2. Take Supplements

Supplements can help restore the nutrients lost after bariatric surgery. Consult with your doctor or a nutritionist to determine which supplements suit your needs. Biotin, zinc, and iron supplements are known to promote hair growth.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat tools such as blow dryers, curling irons, and straighteners as they can damage your hair and cause further hair loss. If you must use heat tools, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair.

4. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp increases blood flow to the hair follicles, promoting hair growth. Use your fingertips to massage your scalp gently for a few minutes every day.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to grow hair after bariatric surgery:

Step 1: Consult with Your Doctor

Consult with your doctor to determine if there are any underlying medical conditions causing hair loss. Additionally, they can recommend supplements that can help promote hair growth.

Step 2: Eat a Balanced Diet

Ensure that your diet is rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote hair growth.

Step 3: Take Supplements

Take supplements recommended by your doctor or nutritionist to restore lost nutrients.

Step 4: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat tools or use a heat protectant spray.

Step 5: Massage Your Scalp

Massage your scalp gently every day to promote blood flow to the hair follicles.

Tips to Promote Hair Growth

Here are some additional tips to help you promote hair growth:

1. Use a Mild Shampoo

Use a mild shampoo that does not contain harsh chemicals or sulfates. A mild shampoo will be gentle on your hair and scalp.

2. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause further hair loss.

3. Be Patient

Remember, hair growth is a slow process, and it may take several months for you to see results. Be patient and consistent with your hair care routine.

FAQs

Q: Is hair loss after bariatric surgery permanent?

A: No, hair loss after bariatric surgery is temporary and usually lasts for six to twelve months.

Q: Can supplements help promote hair growth?

A: Yes, supplements such as biotin, zinc, and iron are known to promote hair growth.

Hair Care Routine

Here is a simple hair care routine to help promote hair growth after bariatric surgery:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water as it can damage your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously as it can cause further hair loss.

Step 3: Apply Hair Oil

Apply a few drops of hair oil to your scalp and hair. Massage your scalp gently to promote blood flow to the hair follicles.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Avoid using heat tools to style your hair. Instead, opt for natural hairstyles that do not pull on your hair.

In conclusion, hair loss after bariatric surgery is common, but it is temporary. You can take steps to promote hair growth and prevent further hair loss by following a balanced diet, taking supplements, avoiding heat styling, massaging your scalp, and following a hair care routine. Be patient and consistent, and you will soon regain your locks.
