Will A Perm Make Curly Hair Curlier

Description

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While many people love the natural curls they were born with, others want even curlier hair. If you fall into the latter category, you may be wondering if a perm is the answer to your dreams. So, will a perm make curly hair curlier?

The answer is yes, but it depends on your hair type and the type of perm you get. A perm can make your curls tighter and more defined, which can give the appearance of curlier hair. However, if you already have tight curls, a perm may not make much of a difference.

How to Get Curlier Hair with a Perm

If you want to get curlier hair with a perm, the first step is to find a stylist who is experienced in working with curly hair. They can help you choose the right type of perm for your hair type and desired look.

There are a few different types of perms to choose from, including:

  • Acid perms, which are best for fine or damaged hair
  • Alkaline perms, which are best for thick or coarse hair
  • Exothermic perms, which use heat to activate the solution and are best for resistant hair

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Perm

Once you've chosen the type of perm you want, your stylist will take you through the following steps:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and wrap each section around a perm rod.
  3. Apply the perm solution to your hair and let it sit for the recommended amount of time.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly and apply a neutralizing solution to stop the perming process.
  5. Remove the perm rods and style your hair as desired.

Tips to Keep Your Perm Looking Great

Once you have a perm, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair and undo your perm.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions About Perms

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from a few months to a year, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

Can I color my hair after getting a perm?

You should wait at least a week after getting a perm before coloring your hair. It's best to consult with your stylist to determine the best timing for your specific hair type and perm.

Will a perm damage my hair?

A perm can be damaging to your hair if not done properly or if you don't take good care of your hair after the process. It's important to choose an experienced stylist and follow proper hair care techniques.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Whether you have natural curls or a perm, taking good care of your hair is essential to keeping it looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair and undo your curls.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help enhance your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, a perm can make curly hair curlier, but it depends on your hair type and the type of perm you get. If you're considering getting a perm, be sure to consult with an experienced stylist and take good care of your hair to keep it looking great.
