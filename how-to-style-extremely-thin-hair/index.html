How To Style Extremely Thin Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Christine Aug 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you struggling to style your extremely thin hair? Do you feel like you're limited to the same boring hairstyles every day? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with thin hair face similar challenges when it comes to styling their locks. However, with the right tips, tricks, and care, you can achieve voluminous, gorgeous hair that you'll be proud to show off.

Hair Care

Before we dive into styling, it's essential to understand how to care for thin hair properly. Here are some tips that can help:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid over-drying.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy hair care products like oils and creams that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons that can damage your hair.

By following these hair care tips, you'll create the perfect foundation for styling your thin hair.

How to Style Extremely Thin Hair

Now that you've got the basics down let's talk about how to style extremely thin hair. Here are some styles that you can try:

1. Bob with Bangs

A bob with bangs is a classic hairstyle that can add volume to your hair. The bangs create a frame for your face, while the bob adds body to your hair. If you're worried about your hair looking too flat, try adding some layers to your bob to create movement.

2. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling brave, a pixie cut can be a perfect option for those with extremely thin hair. A short cut can make your hair look fuller and thicker. Plus, it's super easy to style and maintain.

3. Layered Cut

If you're not ready to cut your hair short, adding layers to your hair can create the illusion of thickness. Layers can add volume and texture to your hair. You can also try adding some highlights to your hair to create dimension.

4. Beach Waves

Beach waves are a perfect style for those with thin hair. The waves create movement and texture, making your hair look thicker. To achieve this look, use a curling wand or flat iron to create loose waves. Finish by spritzing some salt spray onto your hair for extra texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to create beach waves:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from damage.
  4. Section your hair into small sections.
  5. Using a curling wand or flat iron, wrap each section around the barrel and hold for a few seconds.
  6. Release the curl and let it cool for a few seconds.
  7. Repeat until all sections are curled.
  8. Spray some salt spray onto your hair and scrunch to create texture.
  9. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your thin hair:

  • Avoid using heavy hair accessories like clips and barrettes that can weigh down your hair.
  • Try using a dry shampoo to add volume to your roots.
  • Opt for a shorter haircut that can make your hair look fuller.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume.
  • Invest in a good quality volumizing mousse or spray.

FAQ

Q: Can I use hair extensions to add volume to my thin hair?

A: Yes, you can use hair extensions to add volume to your hair. However, make sure to choose extensions that are lightweight and comfortable to wear. Also, avoid using clip-in extensions that can pull on your hair, causing damage.

Q: Can I color my thin hair?

A: Yes, you can color your thin hair. However, it's essential to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and looks natural. Also, avoid using harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

Q: Can I style my thin hair without using heat tools?

A: Yes, you can style your thin hair without using heat tools. Try air-drying your hair or using different hair accessories like headbands, scarves, and hairpins to create different looks.

In conclusion, styling extremely thin hair can be challenging, but it's not impossible. By following these tips, tricks, and hair care, you can create voluminous, gorgeous hair that you'll be proud to show off.
