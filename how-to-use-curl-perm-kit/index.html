How To Use Curl Perm Kit A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Mar 08, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Curl perm kits are a great way to add some bounce and volume to your hair. If you're new to using a perm kit or need a refresher, this guide will take you through the process step-by-step. From preparing your hair to applying the perm solution and neutralizer, we've got you covered.

Description: What is a Curl Perm Kit?

A curl perm kit is a hair product that is used to create curls or waves in your hair. It typically comes with a perm solution, a neutralizer, and sometimes a pre-treatment product. The perm solution breaks down the bonds in your hair, allowing it to be reshaped into curls. The neutralizer helps to reform the bonds and set the curls in place.

How to Use Curl Perm Kit: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before applying the perm solution, you need to make sure your hair is clean and dry. Shampoo your hair and avoid using any conditioner or styling products. If your hair is damaged or previously dyed, it's important to wait at least a week before using a perm kit.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four equal parts, using hair clips to keep each section in place. Make sure to use plastic gloves to protect your hands from the chemicals in the perm kit.

Step 3: Apply the Perm Solution

Starting at the nape of your neck, use a perm rod to wrap a small section of hair around it. Apply the perm solution to the wrapped hair, avoiding any contact with your scalp. Repeat this process on each section of your hair, making sure to use the same size perm rod on each section.

Step 4: Wait for the Perm Solution to Set

After applying the perm solution, cover your hair with a plastic cap and wait for the solution to set. The amount of time needed can vary based on the strength of the perm solution and your hair type. Make sure to read the instructions on the kit to determine the appropriate time.

Step 5: Rinse Your Hair

Once the perm solution has set, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water. Make sure to remove all the solution from your hair.

Step 6: Apply the Neutralizer

Apply the neutralizer to your hair, using the same process as the perm solution. Make sure to use the same size perm rod on each section of your hair as before. Cover your hair with a plastic cap and wait for the neutralizer to set.

Step 7: Rinse Your Hair Again

After the neutralizer has set, rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water. Make sure to remove all the neutralizer from your hair.

Step 8: Remove the Perm Rods

Remove the perm rods from your hair, starting at the nape of your neck and working your way up. Make sure to do this gently to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 9: Style Your Hair

Once the perm rods are removed, you can style your hair as desired. Avoid using any heat styling tools for at least 24 hours after using a perm kit to allow your curls to set.

Tips for Using Curl Perm Kits

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using a curl perm kit:

  • Always follow the instructions on the kit carefully.
  • Avoid using a perm kit on damaged or previously dyed hair.
  • Don't wash your hair for at least 24 hours after using a perm kit.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools for at least 24 hours after using a perm kit.
  • Use a deep conditioner regularly to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQs About Curl Perm Kits

Here are some frequently asked questions about curl perm kits:

Do perm kits work on all hair types?

Perms can work on most hair types, but the effectiveness can vary based on your hair's texture and thickness. Talk to a stylist if you're unsure if a perm is right for you.

How long do perms last?

Perms can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of your hair after using a perm kit.

Can you use a perm kit on colored hair?

You should avoid using a perm kit on hair that has been colored in the last week or two. If you have colored hair, talk to a stylist to determine if a perm kit is right for you.

Hair Care After Using a Curl Perm Kit

After using a curl perm kit, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain your curls and keep your hair healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 24 hours after using a perm kit.
  • Use a deep conditioner regularly to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools for at least 24 hours after using a perm kit.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can use a curl perm kit to add some bounce and volume to your hair. Just remember to follow the instructions carefully and take good care of your hair afterward.
