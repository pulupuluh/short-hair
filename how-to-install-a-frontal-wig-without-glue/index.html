How To Install A Frontal Wig Without Glue Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Frontal wigs are a popular alternative to traditional wigs, as they provide a more natural-looking hairline. However, many people are intimidated by the idea of using glue to install them, as it can be messy and time-consuming. The good news is that you can install a frontal wig without glue, using only a few simple tools and techniques. In this guide, we'll show you how to install a frontal wig without glue, step-by-step.

Description

Installing a frontal wig without glue requires a bit of practice and patience, but it is a great alternative for those who are sensitive to adhesives or want a more versatile wig. This method involves using wig clips, combs, and elastic bands to secure the wig onto your head. These tools provide a secure hold, without damaging your natural hair or scalp.

How to Install a Frontal Wig Without Glue: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair and Scalp

Before installing your wig, make sure your hair and scalp are clean and free of any oils or products. Tie your hair back into a low ponytail or braid, and apply a wig cap to keep your hair in place. This will also help to protect your natural hair and scalp from any rubbing or friction.

Step 2: Install the Wig Clips

Take your wig and place it onto your head, making sure the hairline matches up with your natural hairline. Use a comb to create a parting in the wig where you want to place the first wig clip. Attach the wig clip onto the wig, making sure the teeth are facing towards your scalp. Repeat this process for the remaining clips, placing them evenly around the perimeter of the wig.

Step 3: Secure with Elastic Bands

Take two elastic bands and place them around the front and back of your head, securing the wig in place. Make sure the bands are tight enough to hold the wig securely, but not too tight that they cause discomfort or headaches.

Step 4: Style and Blend the Wig

Once your wig is secure, you can style it as desired. Use a flat iron, curling iron, or hair dryer to create your desired look. Use a comb or brush to blend the wig with your natural hair, making sure there are no visible lines or gaps.

Tips

- Use a wig cap to protect your natural hair and scalp
- Apply a small amount of hairspray or mousse to the wig to help it hold its shape
- Take your time when installing the wig, and make sure it is secure before styling
- Experiment with different styles and partings to find the look that suits you best.

FAQ

Can I swim with a frontal wig installed without glue?

It is not recommended to swim with a frontal wig installed without glue, as the water and chlorine can damage the wig and cause it to shift or come loose. If you want to swim with a wig, it is best to use a waterproof adhesive or wear a swimming cap over the wig.

How long will a frontal wig installed without glue last?

A frontal wig installed without glue can last up to 2-4 weeks, depending on how well it is maintained and how often it is worn. It is important to take care of the wig, by washing it regularly and storing it properly when not in use.

Hair Care

To keep your frontal wig looking its best, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

- Wash the wig regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner
- Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle the wig, starting at the ends and working your way up
- Avoid using heat styling tools on the wig, as this can damage the fibers
- Store the wig on a wig stand or mannequin head, to help maintain its shape and prevent tangles.
