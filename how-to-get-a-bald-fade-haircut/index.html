How To Get A Bald Fade Haircut A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old haircut and want to try something new? A bald fade haircut might be just what you need. This trendy haircut has been gaining popularity in recent years and is a favorite among men of all ages. In this article, we will guide you through the process of getting a bald fade haircut, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Description of a Bald Fade Haircut
  • How to Get a Bald Fade Haircut
  • Step by Step Guide to Getting a Bald Fade Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Bald Fade Haircut
  • FAQs About Bald Fade Haircuts
  • Hair Care Tips for Bald Fade Haircuts

Description of a Bald Fade Haircut

A bald fade haircut, also known as a skin fade, is a haircut in which the hair is gradually faded from the bottom up. The hair is cut very short at the bottom and gradually fades into longer hair on top. The hair is usually cut to a length of 1/16th of an inch at the bottom and gradually fades up to a length of 1/2 inch or more on top. The hair is usually styled with a side part or slicked back.

How to Get a Bald Fade Haircut

If you are interested in getting a bald fade haircut, the first step is to find a barber who specializes in this type of haircut. You can ask for recommendations from friends or family members or search for a barber online. Once you have found a barber, book an appointment and prepare for your haircut.

Step by Step Guide to Getting a Bald Fade Haircut

Step 1: Choose the length of your hair on top. This will determine how much hair needs to be cut off.

Step 2: Your barber will start by cutting your hair to the desired length on top. They will then use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head very short.

Step 3: Your barber will then use a razor or clippers to gradually fade the hair from the bottom up. This process will create a smooth transition from short hair to longer hair on top.

Step 4: Your barber will finish the haircut by trimming and shaping the hair on top to your desired style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bald Fade Haircut

Tip 1: Regular touch-ups are important to keep your bald fade haircut looking fresh. Plan to visit your barber every 2-3 weeks.

Tip 2: Use a good quality hair product to style your hair on top. This will help to keep it in place and prevent frizz.

Tip 3: Protect your scalp from sunburn by wearing a hat or applying sunscreen to your scalp.

FAQs About Bald Fade Haircuts

Q: How long does a bald fade haircut take?

A: A bald fade haircut usually takes around 30-45 minutes.

Q: Is a bald fade haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, a bald fade haircut can be done on any hair type.

Hair Care Tips for Bald Fade Haircuts

Tip 1: Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

Tip 2: Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.

Tip 3: Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate hair growth and improve circulation.

In conclusion, a bald fade haircut is a stylish and trendy haircut that can give you a new look. By following the steps and tips outlined in this article, you can get the perfect bald fade haircut and keep it looking fresh for weeks to come.
