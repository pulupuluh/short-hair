Does Dark Hair Make You Look Younger Or Older

Written by Aprilia Jun 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Dark hair has always been a topic of debate when it comes to looking younger or older. While some people believe that dark hair makes you look older, others argue that it can make you look younger. In this article, we will explore whether dark hair makes you look younger or older and provide some tips on how to care for your dark hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to hair color, there is no one-size-fits-all answer to whether dark hair makes you look younger or older. It depends on various factors such as skin tone, face shape, and personal style. However, some studies suggest that dark hair can make you look younger because it creates a contrast between your hair and skin, making your skin appear brighter and more youthful. On the other hand, some people believe that dark hair can make you look older because it can accentuate fine lines and wrinkles.

How to

If you're considering dyeing your hair dark, there are a few things you should consider to ensure that it complements your skin tone and features:

  • Consult a professional hairstylist to determine the best shade of dark hair for your skin tone and face shape.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to break up the solid color and create dimension.
  • Choose a haircare routine that suits your hair type and color to maintain the health and shine of your hair.

Step by Step

If you've decided to dye your hair dark, here's a step-by-step guide to achieve the perfect shade:

  1. Choose the right shade of dark hair dye based on your skin tone and face shape.
  2. Prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and oils.
  3. Apply the hair dye according to the instructions on the package, making sure to cover all strands evenly.
  4. Leave the hair dye on for the recommended time, then rinse it off with cool water to seal the color.
  5. Apply a deep conditioning treatment to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain the health and shine of your dark hair:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair to prevent stripping the natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid fading and drying out your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using a styling tool.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about dark hair:

  • Can dark hair make you look older? It depends on various factors such as skin tone, face shape, and personal style. However, some people believe that dark hair can accentuate fine lines and wrinkles, making you look older.
  • Can dark hair make you look younger? Some studies suggest that dark hair can create a contrast between your hair and skin, making your skin appear brighter and more youthful.
  • How do I choose the right shade of dark hair? Consult a professional hairstylist to determine the best shade for your skin tone and face shape.

Hair Care

Caring for your dark hair is essential to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying it out.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair to prevent stripping the natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat damage by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.

In conclusion, whether dark hair makes you look younger or older depends on various factors such as skin tone, face shape, and personal style. However, by following the tips and hair care routine mentioned in this article, you can ensure that your dark hair complements your features and looks healthy and shiny.
