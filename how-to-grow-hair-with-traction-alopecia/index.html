How To Grow Hair With Traction Alopecia

Written by Bardi May 26, 2023 路 6 min read

Traction alopecia is a type of hair loss that occurs when there is too much tension on the hair follicles. It can be caused by tight hairstyles such as braids, weaves, buns, and ponytails. If left untreated, traction alopecia can lead to permanent hair loss.

Description

If you have traction alopecia, it is important to take steps to promote hair growth and prevent further damage. This blog post will provide you with tips on how to grow hair with traction alopecia.

How to

The first step in growing hair with traction alopecia is to stop wearing tight hairstyles. This will give your hair follicles a chance to recover and regrow.

You can also try massaging your scalp with essential oils such as peppermint, lavender, and rosemary. These oils can help increase blood flow to the hair follicles and promote hair growth.

Another way to promote hair growth is to take supplements such as biotin, vitamin D, and iron. These nutrients are essential for healthy hair growth and can help prevent further hair loss.

Step by Step

Step 1: Stop Wearing Tight Hairstyles

The first step in growing hair with traction alopecia is to stop wearing tight hairstyles. This includes braids, weaves, buns, and ponytails. Instead, wear your hair in loose styles such as a messy bun or a low ponytail.

Step 2: Massage Your Scalp with Essential Oils

Another way to promote hair growth is to massage your scalp with essential oils. Mix a few drops of peppermint, lavender, and rosemary oil with a carrier oil such as coconut or jojoba oil. Massage the mixture into your scalp for 5-10 minutes, then rinse with warm water.

Step 3: Take Supplements

Taking supplements such as biotin, vitamin D, and iron can also help promote hair growth. These nutrients are essential for healthy hair growth and can help prevent further hair loss.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for growing hair with traction alopecia:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals such as relaxers and hair dyes.
  • Wear a satin or silk scarf or bonnet to bed to protect your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can traction alopecia be reversed?

A: Yes, traction alopecia can be reversed if caught early enough. However, if the hair follicles have been damaged for too long, the hair loss may be permanent.

Q: How long does it take for hair to grow back after traction alopecia?

A: It can take several months to a year for hair to grow back after traction alopecia. However, this timeline can vary depending on the severity of the hair loss and the individual's hair growth rate.

Hair Care

In addition to the tips mentioned above, it is important to take good care of your hair if you have traction alopecia. This includes:

  • Washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Conditioning your hair to keep it moisturized
  • Protecting your hair from the sun and harsh weather

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can promote hair growth and prevent further damage from traction alopecia.
