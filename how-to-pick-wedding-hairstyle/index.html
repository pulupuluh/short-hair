How To Pick Your Perfect Wedding Hairstyle

Written by Bardi Sep 13, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you getting married soon and still unsure of what hairstyle to choose? Don't worry, we've got you covered! Your wedding day is one of the most important days of your life, and you want to look your best. In this article, we will guide you on how to pick the perfect wedding hairstyle that will make you feel confident and beautiful on your big day.

How to Pick Your Wedding Hairstyle

Choosing the perfect wedding hairstyle can be overwhelming with so many options available. Here are some tips to help you pick your wedding hairstyle:

Consider Your Dress

Your wedding dress is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing your wedding hairstyle. You want a hairstyle that complements your dress and enhances your overall bridal look. If you have a detailed neckline or back, consider an updo that will showcase your dress's beauty.

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining the right hairstyle for you. Different face shapes look best with different hairstyles. For example, if you have a round face, a low bun or a side-swept hairstyle will look great on you. If you have an oval-shaped face, you can pull off any hairstyle, so you have more options to choose from.

Consider Your Hair Texture and Length

Your hair texture and length will also influence the type of hairstyle that suits you. If you have long hair, you can choose from many updos, braids, or loose curls. If you have short hair, you can opt for a chic bob or a sleek pixie cut.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Your Wedding Hairstyle

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose your wedding hairstyle:

Step 1: Research

Research different wedding hairstyles by browsing bridal magazines, Pinterest, and Instagram. This will give you an idea of what you like and what will look good on you.

Step 2: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Book a consultation with your hairstylist to discuss your options. Bring pictures of your dress and your favorite hairstyles to the consultation, so your hairstylist can help you choose the best hairstyle for you.

Step 3: Schedule a Trial Run

Once you have decided on a hairstyle, schedule a trial run. This will give you an idea of how the hairstyle looks on you and whether it suits you. Make sure to bring your hair accessories to the trial run, so your hairstylist can incorporate them into your hairstyle.

Step 4: Make Adjustments

If you're not happy with the hairstyle after the trial run, don't worry! You can make adjustments or try a different hairstyle until you find the perfect one.

Tips for Choosing Your Wedding Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you choose your wedding hairstyle:

Choose a Style That You're Comfortable With

Choose a hairstyle that you're comfortable with, and that makes you feel confident. Don't choose a hairstyle that is too complicated or requires too much maintenance if you're not used to it.

Consider Your Wedding Theme

Your wedding theme can also influence the type of hairstyle that suits you. If you're having a beach wedding, consider a loose, bohemian hairstyle. If you're having a formal wedding, opt for a sleek updo.

Don't Forget About Accessories

Your hair accessories can enhance your wedding hairstyle and make it more glamorous. Consider a tiara, a flower crown, or a veil to complete your bridal look.

FAQs About Choosing Your Wedding Hairstyle

What if I Can't Decide on a Hairstyle?

If you're having trouble deciding on a hairstyle, consult with your hairstylist or try different hairstyles until you find the perfect one.

When Should I Schedule My Hair Trial Run?

Schedule your hair trial run at least a month before your wedding day to make sure you have enough time to make any adjustments.

How Do I Know if a Hairstyle Suits Me?

A hairstyle that suits you should enhance your facial features and complement your dress. If you're not sure, consult with your hairstylist or try a different hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips for Your Wedding Day

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking great on your wedding day:

Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair the night before your wedding day to give it time to air dry and settle into its natural texture.

Use Heat Protectant

If you're using heat tools to style your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

Avoid Using Too Much Product

Using too much product can make your hair look greasy and weighed down. Use only the necessary amount of product to achieve your desired hairstyle.

Bring Touch-Up Products

Bring touch-up products, such as hairspray, bobby pins, and a comb, to your wedding venue to fix any hair mishaps throughout the day.

In conclusion, choosing the perfect wedding hairstyle is crucial to your bridal look. Consider your dress, face shape, hair texture and length, and wedding theme when choosing your hairstyle. Follow our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care tips to ensure that your hair looks great on your wedding day.
