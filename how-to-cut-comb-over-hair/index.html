How To Cut Comb Over Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank May 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a classic, stylish haircut that can work for any occasion, a comb over is a great option. The comb over is a versatile hairstyle that can be tailored to your individual preferences and hair type. In this article, we'll go over how to cut comb over hair, as well as tips for maintenance and styling.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Cut Comb Over Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintenance and Styling
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Cut Comb Over Hair

Before we get started, it's important to note that cutting hair can be a difficult task. If you're not comfortable cutting your own hair, it's best to visit a professional stylist. However, if you're ready to give it a try, here are the basic steps for cutting comb over hair:

Tools You'll Need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers
  • Clipper guards
  • Haircut cape or towel
  • Two mirrors (one front-facing, one handheld)

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash and dry your hair before you begin cutting. This will make it easier to see the natural texture and shape of your hair, which will help you achieve a more accurate cut.


2. Section your hair.

Using a comb, part your hair on one side where you want your comb over to sit. Then, section off the hair on the shorter side of the part. You can use a clip to hold the hair in place.


3. Determine your desired length.

Using your clippers and the appropriate guard, determine the length you want for your hair. Start with the longest guard and work your way down until you reach your desired length.


4. Begin cutting.

Starting at the nape of your neck, use your clippers to cut the hair in an upward motion. Move the clippers around the sides and back of your head, using the same guard length.


5. Blend the sides and back.

Use the clippers without a guard to blend the hair on the sides and back of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using an upward motion to blend the hair.


6. Cut the top.

Use your scissors to cut the hair on the top of your head. Start at the front and work your way back, using a comb to section off the hair. Cut the hair at an angle, following the natural shape of your head.


7. Blend the top and sides.

Use your scissors to blend the hair on the top of your head with the hair on the sides. Cut the hair at an angle, using a comb to guide you.


8. Style your hair.

Use a comb and hair product to style your hair in your desired comb over style.


Tips for Maintenance and Styling:

  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your comb over hairstyle.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Apply a small amount of hair product to your hair before styling to help hold your comb over in place.
  • If you have thick or curly hair, consider using a hair straightener to help smooth your hair for a sleeker look.

FAQ:

Q: How long should my hair be for a comb over?

A: The length of your hair for a comb over can vary depending on your individual preferences. However, a good rule of thumb is to have at least 2-3 inches of hair on the top of your head.

Q: Can I use a different part for a comb over?

A: Yes! While a side part is traditional for a comb over, you can experiment with a middle part or even a zigzag part for a unique look.

Hair Care:

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, strong hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in great condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and classic comb over haircut. Whether you're looking for a sleek, professional look or a trendy, casual style, a comb over is a versatile option that can work for any occasion.
