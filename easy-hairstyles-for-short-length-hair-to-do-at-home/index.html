Easy Hairstyles For Short Length Hair To Do At Home

Written by Aprilia Jul 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Short hair can be tricky to style, but with the right techniques and tools, you can create beautiful hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion. Whether you're looking for a simple look for a casual day out or an elegant up-do for a formal event, there are plenty of easy hairstyles for short length hair that you can do at home. In this article, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to create some of the most popular short hairstyles, as well as tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

Short hair can be a challenge to style, especially if you're used to longer locks. But with the right tools and techniques, you can create a variety of easy hairstyles that look great and are easy to maintain. From sleek bobs to messy pixie cuts, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this article, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to create some of the most popular short hairstyles, as well as tips on how to care for your hair.

How to

1. Sleek Bob

To create a sleek bob, start by blow-drying your hair using a round brush to add volume. Then, use a straightening iron to straighten your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Finish by spraying your hair with a light-hold hairspray to keep it in place.

2. Messy Pixie Cut

To create a messy pixie cut, start by applying a texturizing spray to your hair to add volume and texture. Then, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, undone look. Finish by spraying your hair with a flexible-hold hairspray to keep it in place.

3. Half-Up Bun

To create a half-up bun, start by dividing your hair into two sections, with the top section being slightly larger than the bottom section. Then, use a hair tie to create a small bun with the top section of your hair, leaving the bottom section loose. Finish by spraying your hair with a light-hold hairspray to keep it in place.

Step by Step

1. Sleek Bob

  1. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  2. Straighten your hair using a straightening iron.
  3. Spray your hair with a light-hold hairspray to keep it in place.

2. Messy Pixie Cut

  1. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair to add volume and texture.
  2. Tousle your hair using your fingers to create a messy, undone look.
  3. Spray your hair with a flexible-hold hairspray to keep it in place.

3. Half-Up Bun

  1. Divide your hair into two sections, with the top section being slightly larger than the bottom section.
  2. Create a small bun with the top section of your hair using a hair tie.
  3. Spray your hair with a light-hold hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your short hair:

  • Invest in quality styling tools, such as a good blow dryer and straightening iron.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the ones that work best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to use accessories, such as hair clips and headbands, to add some extra flair to your hair.

FAQ

Q. What are some other easy hairstyles for short length hair?

A. Other easy hairstyles for short hair include a messy bun, a side braid, and a slicked-back look.

Q. How do I care for my short hair?

A. To care for your short hair, make sure to shampoo and condition regularly, use a heat protectant before using styling tools, and get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to help you care for your short locks:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Use a heat protectant before using styling tools to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

By following these tips and using the right techniques, you can create beautiful hairstyles for short length hair that are easy to do at home. Whether you're looking for a simple look for a casual day out or an elegant up-do for a formal event, there are plenty of options to choose from. So why not try out some of these easy hairstyles for short hair and see how they can transform your look?
