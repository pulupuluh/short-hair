How To Cut Your Own Hair Short Back And Sides With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver May 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to cut your own hair short back and sides with clippers, you're in the right place. This guide will take you through everything you need to know to achieve a great haircut from the comfort of your own home. Whether you're looking to save money, avoid a trip to the barbershop, or simply try something new, this guide has got you covered.

Description

When it comes to cutting your own hair, it's important to have the right tools. In this case, you'll need a set of clippers with different guard sizes, a mirror, and some patience. Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with a bit of practice and the right technique, you'll be able to achieve a great result in no time.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Short Back and Sides with Clippers

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to decide on the length you want to achieve. You can use a picture for reference, or simply decide on a length that feels comfortable for you. Once you've decided on the length, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it thoroughly. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get a more accurate result.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard Size

Choose the guard size that corresponds to the length you want to achieve. Start with the longest guard size and work your way down to shorter lengths if necessary.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start by cutting the sides of your head. Begin at the bottom and work your way up, using the clippers to trim the hair in an upward motion. Be sure to use a mirror to check your progress and make sure everything is even.

Step 4: Move to the Back of Your Head

Once you've finished the sides, move to the back of your head. Again, start at the bottom and work your way up, using the clippers to trim the hair in an upward motion.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Now it's time to trim the top of your head. Use scissors to trim the hair to the desired length, then use the clippers to blend the sides and back into the top.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check your work. Make any necessary adjustments, and be sure to clean up any stray hairs.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve a great result:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to shorter lengths as needed
  • Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure everything is even
  • Be patient and take your time
  • Clean up any stray hairs with a pair of scissors or clippers

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair with regular scissors?

While it's possible to cut your own hair with regular scissors, it's not recommended. Hair cutting scissors are designed to be sharper and more precise, which makes them better for cutting hair.

How often should I cut my own hair?

How often you cut your own hair will depend on how fast it grows and how quickly you want to maintain your desired length. As a general rule, most people cut their hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors

Cutting your own hair can be a great way to save money and experiment with new styles. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a great result from the comfort of your own home. So why not give it a try?
