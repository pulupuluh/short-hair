How To Cut Men s Hair Crew Cut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi May 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking to cut your hair at home and achieve the classic crew cut look? Look no further! In this article, we'll go through a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's hair crew cut style. From the necessary tools to tips for a clean finish, we've got you covered.

Tools You'll Need

Before we begin, it's important to have the right tools on hand. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers with guards in different lengths
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Cape or towel to cover clothing
  • Handheld mirror or second mirror to check the back of your head
  • Hair spray or gel for styling (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it completely. This will make it easier to cut and ensure a more even finish.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

Next, choose the length you want for your crew cut. Typically, a crew cut is shorter on the sides and longer on top. Use the clippers with the appropriate guard to achieve your desired length. If you're not sure what length to start with, it's best to go longer and work your way shorter.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Begin by using the clippers to trim the sides of your hair. Use a lower guard setting for a shorter cut and a higher guard for a longer cut. Start at the bottom of your hairline and work your way up, going against the grain of your hair for a closer cut.

Step 4: Blend the Top and Sides

Once you've finished the sides, blend the top and sides by using a higher guard setting for the area where they meet. Use the clippers to create a gradual fade between the two lengths.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Now it's time to cut the top of your hair. Use scissors to trim any excess length and create the desired shape. You can go for a more textured look or a clean, straight finish.

Step 6: Check the Back

Use a handheld mirror or second mirror to check the back of your head. Make sure the cut is even and adjust as necessary.

Step 7: Clean Up the Edges

Use the clippers without a guard to clean up the edges around your hairline and ears. This will give you a clean, polished finish.

Step 8: Style as Desired

If you want to add some styling product, now is the time to do so. Use a small amount of hair spray or gel to create your desired look.

Tips for a Clean Finish

Here are some tips to ensure a clean and professional-looking crew cut:

  • Take your time and work slowly. It's better to go slow and steady than to rush and make mistakes.
  • Use a comb to ensure an even cut.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.
  • Use the right tools for the job to ensure a clean cut.
  • Remember that hair grows back, so don't stress too much if you make a mistake.

FAQ

How often should I get a crew cut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the look. Generally, a crew cut will need to be maintained every 2-4 weeks.

Can I cut my own hair crew cut style?

Yes, you can! Just make sure you have the right tools and take your time.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't worry too much if you make a mistake. Hair grows back, and you can always go to a professional if you need help fixing it.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to ensure it stays healthy and looks great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your crew cut.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair crew cut style, you can maintain this classic look from the comfort of your own home. Just remember to take your time and use the right tools, and you'll have a clean and professional-looking cut in no time.
