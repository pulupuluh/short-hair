How Long Is A 4 Guard Haircut

Written by Frank Aug 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you planning to get a new haircut and wondering how long is a #4 guard haircut? In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this popular haircut, including how to get it, tips for maintaining it, and more.

Description

A #4 guard haircut is typically achieved using a clipper guard that is 1/2 inch or 12.7mm in length. This means that the hair will be cut to a uniform length of 1/2 inch all around the head, except for the hairline and sideburns, which may be tapered or faded.

How to Get a #4 Guard Haircut

The easiest way to get a #4 guard haircut is to visit a professional barber or hairstylist. Simply tell them that you want a #4 guard haircut and they will know what to do. Alternatively, if you have a clipper at home, you can purchase a #4 guard and cut your hair yourself. However, we recommend leaving it to the professionals if you're not experienced in cutting hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're getting a #4 guard haircut, here's what you can expect:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before your haircut, it's important to wash your hair thoroughly to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup.

Step 2: Consult with Your Barber or Stylist

Discuss your desired haircut with your barber or stylist to ensure that they understand what you want. You can also show them a picture of the haircut you have in mind.

Step 3: Trim Your Hair

Using a #4 guard, your barber or stylist will trim your hair to a uniform length of 1/2 inch. They may use different guards for the hairline and sideburns to create a tapered or faded look.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your haircut is complete, your barber or stylist will style your hair using a comb or brush and hair product if desired.

Tips for Maintaining a #4 Guard Haircut

To maintain your #4 guard haircut, we recommend visiting your barber or stylist every four to eight weeks for a trim. You can also use a clipper at home to touch up your hairline and sideburns between haircuts. Additionally, it's important to use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type and to avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools.

FAQ

What Other Guard Sizes Are Available?

Clipper guards are available in various sizes, with the #1 guard being the shortest and the #8 guard being the longest.

Can I Get a #4 Guard Haircut with Curly Hair?

Yes, you can get a #4 guard haircut with curly hair. However, your barber or stylist may need to use different techniques to ensure that the haircut looks good with your natural hair texture.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, we recommend using a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Additionally, avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, and protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with UV protection.

In conclusion, a #4 guard haircut is a popular choice for men who want a neat and tidy look without going too short. Whether you're getting it done by a professional or doing it yourself at home, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks its best.
