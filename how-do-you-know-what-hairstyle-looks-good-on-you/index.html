How Do You Know What Hairstyle Looks Good On You

Written by Bardi May 24, 2023 · 7 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you’re not sure where to start. With so many options available, it can be hard to know what will look good on you. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks to help you figure out the best hairstyle for your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

Table of Contents:

  • Face Shape
  • Hair Type
  • Personal Style
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Face Shape

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your face shape. The right haircut can enhance your best features and downplay any areas you’re not as fond of. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long.

If you have an oval face shape, you’re in luck! Almost any hairstyle will look good on you. For round faces, try a long layered cut or a side-swept bang to elongate the face. Square faces can benefit from a soft, layered look, while heart-shaped faces look great with a bob or a pixie cut. Diamond-shaped faces can pull off a short, spiky cut or a sleek, straight style. Long faces can be balanced out with a shoulder-length cut or soft waves.

Hair Type

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair type. Whether you have thick, curly hair or fine, straight hair, there’s a style that will work for you. If you have thick hair, try a layered cut to add movement and texture. For fine hair, a blunt cut can add the illusion of thickness. If you have curly hair, embrace your natural texture with a layered cut or a curly bob. For straight hair, try a sleek, straight style or add some waves for a beachy look.

Personal Style

Your personal style is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Do you prefer a classic, polished look or something more edgy and trendy? Are you willing to spend time styling your hair every day, or do you need something low-maintenance?

If you prefer a classic look, a bob or a sleek, straight style may be perfect for you. If you’re more edgy, consider a pixie cut or a bold hair color. If you don’t have a lot of time to style your hair, a low-maintenance cut like a messy bob or a shaggy cut may be the way to go.

FAQ

How do I know if a certain hairstyle will look good on me?

Consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle. It may also be helpful to bring pictures to your stylist to give them an idea of what you’re looking for.

What if I’m not sure what my face shape is?

You can use a measuring tape to measure your face length, width, and jawline to determine your face shape. Alternatively, you can ask your stylist for their opinion.

What if I want to try something new, but I’m not sure if it will look good?

If you’re feeling adventurous, go for it! Hair grows back, and you can always try a different style if you’re not happy with the results.

Hair Care

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it’s important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims can also help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your face shape, hair type, and personal style, you can find a style that looks and feels great. And remember – if you’re not happy with the results, you can always try something new!
