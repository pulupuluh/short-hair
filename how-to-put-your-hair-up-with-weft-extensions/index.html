How To Put Your Hair Up With Weft Extensions A Step By Step Guide

How to Put Your Hair Up with Weft Extensions

Weft extensions are a great way to add length, volume, and thickness to your natural hair. However, putting your hair up with weft extensions can be a bit tricky if you're not sure how to do it. Here are some steps to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Separate a section of hair at the top of your head and clip it out of the way.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the side of your head and tease it with a comb or brush to add volume.
  4. Apply hairspray to the teased section to help hold it in place.
  5. Take a weft extension and clip it onto the teased section of hair.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 on the other side of your head.
  7. Release the top section of hair and tease it to add volume.
  8. Apply hairspray to the teased section and clip a weft extension onto it.
  9. Repeat step 8 if you want to add more weft extensions.
  10. Gather all of your hair into a ponytail, bun, or other desired hairstyle.
  11. Use bobby pins or hair ties to secure your hair in place.
  12. Apply hairspray to set your hairstyle.

Congratulations! You have successfully put your hair up with weft extensions. Now, let's move on to some tips to make the process easier.

Tips:

  • Before putting your hair up, make sure your weft extensions are clean and free of tangles.
  • Tease your hair gently to avoid damaging it.
  • Use a comb or brush to smooth out any bumps or lumps in your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: How long will my weft extensions last?

A: Weft extensions can last up to six months with proper care and maintenance.

Q: Can I swim or shower with weft extensions?

A: It's best to avoid getting your weft extensions wet if possible, as chlorine and salt water can damage them. If you do get them wet, be sure to wash and condition them as soon as possible.

Q: How do I remove my weft extensions?

A: To remove your weft extensions, gently slide them out of your hair using a comb or your fingers.

Hair Care Advice:

It's important to take care of your weft extensions to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your weft extensions regularly using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your weft extensions, as this can damage them.
  • Store your weft extensions in a cool, dry place to avoid tangling and matting.
  • Brush your weft extensions gently to avoid pulling on them.

And there you have it! A step-by-step guide on how to put your hair up with weft extensions. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create a variety of hairstyles that showcase your weft extensions. Happy styling!
