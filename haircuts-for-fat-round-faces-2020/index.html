Haircuts For Fat Round Faces 2020 Tips How To And Hair Care

Are you looking for a new haircut that will flatter your fat round face? Look no further because we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll be sharing some of the best haircuts for fat round faces in 2020. We’ll also provide you with tips on how to style your hair and take care of it. So, let’s get started!

Haircuts for Fat Round Faces

When it comes to haircuts for fat round faces, you want to choose a style that will elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Here are some of the best haircuts for fat round faces in 2020:

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short and sassy haircut that looks great on fat round faces. The key is to keep the hair on top of your head slightly longer than the sides. This will create the illusion of length and make your face appear slimmer.

The Bob

The bob is a classic haircut that never goes out of style. For fat round faces, we recommend a long bob that falls just below the chin. This will elongate your face and create a more flattering look.

The Shag

The shag is a layered haircut that looks great on fat round faces. The layers will add movement and texture to your hair, creating a more dynamic look.

The Side-Swept Bangs

If you want to keep your hair long, consider adding side-swept bangs to your look. This will frame your face and create a more flattering shape.

How to Style Your Hair

Now that you’ve chosen your haircut, it’s time to learn how to style it. Here are some tips on how to style your hair for a fat round face:

Use a Round Brush

When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to create volume and movement. This will help elongate your face and create a more flattering look.

Experiment with Texture

Adding texture to your hair can help create the illusion of length. Try using a texturizing spray or mousse to add some volume and movement to your hair.

Try a Side Part

Instead of parting your hair down the middle, try a side part. This will create a more asymmetrical look and draw attention away from the roundness of your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your hair for a fat round face:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to your roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the roots.
  4. Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture to your hair.
  5. Finish with a texturizing spray to add volume and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair and keeping it looking its best:

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends from forming.

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week will help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make it look dry and frizzy. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools and use a heat protectant spray when you do.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a fat round face?

The best haircut for a fat round face is one that creates the illusion of length. We recommend a long bob or a shag with layers.

How do I style my hair for a fat round face?

To style your hair for a fat round face, use a round brush when blow-drying, experiment with texture, and try a side part.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly will help keep it clean and prevent product buildup.

Use a Silk Pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase will help prevent breakage and keep your hair looking smooth and shiny.

Limit Heat Styling

As mentioned earlier, excessive heat styling can damage your hair. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools and use a heat protectant spray when you do.

In conclusion, choosing the right haircut for a fat round face can make all the difference. We hope this article has provided you with some helpful tips and inspiration for your next haircut. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
