How To Curl Your Hair With A Small Wand A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 04, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for a way to add some bounce and volume to your hair? Small wands are a great tool for curling your hair, but it can be tricky to know where to start. In this article, we’ll give you a step-by-step guide on how to curl your hair with a small wand. We’ll also give you some tips on how to maintain your curls and answer some frequently asked questions about hair care. So, let’s get started!

How to Curl Your Hair with a Small Wand

Curling your hair with a small wand might seem intimidating at first, but don’t worry – it’s easier than it looks. Here’s how to do it:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start curling, you need to prepare your hair. Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat of the wand. You can also apply some mousse or hairspray to your hair to help your curls hold.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections. The size of the sections will depend on the thickness of your hair. If you have thick hair, you’ll need to make smaller sections. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate.

Step 3: Start Curling

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the wand, starting at the base of the hair. Hold the hair around the wand for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process with each section of hair.

Step 4: Finish Your Look

Once you’ve curled all of your hair, it’s time to finish your look. Use a brush to gently brush out your curls. You can also add some hairspray to help your curls hold.

Tips for Curling Your Hair with a Small Wand

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Divide your hair into small sections for the best results.
  • Hold the wand vertically for tighter curls and horizontally for looser curls.
  • Don’t hold the hair around the wand for too long – a few seconds is enough.
  • Use a brush to gently brush out your curls for a natural look.

FAQs About Hair Care

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair care:

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you might need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you might only need to wash it once or twice a week. If you exercise or sweat a lot, you might need to wash your hair more often.

How can I prevent split ends?

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to moisturize your hair and prevent breakage.

How can I make my hair grow faster?

Eating a healthy diet, taking vitamins, and getting regular exercise can all help promote hair growth. You can also try massaging your scalp to stimulate blood flow to the hair follicles.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Don’t brush your hair when it’s wet – use a comb instead.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

Now that you know how to curl your hair with a small wand, you can add some volume and bounce to your hair. Remember to take care of your hair by using a heat protectant spray and following some general hair care tips. Happy curling!
