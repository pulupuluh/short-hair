Best Haircut For Fat Face Boy How To Choose The Perfect Style

Written by Petter Mar 03, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re a boy with a round or chubby face, finding the best haircut can be a challenge. You want to choose a style that will flatter your face shape and enhance your features, while also being easy to maintain and style. In this article, we’ll go over some of the best haircuts for fat face boys, as well as tips for choosing the perfect style and caring for your hair.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Fat Face Boy

Before we dive into specific haircut styles, it’s important to understand some general rules of thumb when it comes to choosing a haircut for a round face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a style that adds height and volume to the top of your head
  • Avoid styles that are too short or flat on top, as these can make your face look wider
  • Consider adding texture or layers to your hair to create movement and definition
  • Avoid styles that are too long, as these can overwhelm your face and make it look even rounder
  • Consider a side part or asymmetrical fringe to create the illusion of a more angular face shape

Best Haircuts for Fat Face Boy

The Buzz Cut

While it may seem counterintuitive to choose a style that is typically associated with a more angular face shape, the buzz cut can actually work well on a round face. The key is to keep the sides and back short and tapered, while leaving some length on top. This will create a more balanced look and draw attention away from your face shape.

The Side Part

A side part can be a great option for a fat face boy, as it creates the illusion of a more angular face shape. To achieve this look, ask your barber to leave some length on top and to cut the sides and back short. Then, use a comb to create a deep side part and style the hair to one side.

The Textured Crop

A textured crop is a great option for a fat face boy who wants a low-maintenance style that still looks stylish. To achieve this look, ask your barber to cut your hair short on the sides and back and to leave some length on top. Then, use a texturizing product to create definition and texture in your hair.

How to Care for Your Hair

Once you’ve chosen the perfect haircut for your fat face, it’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle
  • Use a styling product that works well with your hair type and helps to enhance your style
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy

FAQ

What are some hairstyles to avoid for a fat face boy?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short, flat on top, or too long. These styles can make your face look wider or overwhelm your features.

What should I tell my barber when getting a haircut for a fat face?

Be sure to tell your barber that you have a round or chubby face and ask for a style that will flatter your face shape. You can also bring in a picture of a style you like to give your barber a better idea of what you’re looking for.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It’s a good idea to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Conclusion

Choosing the best haircut for a fat face boy can be challenging, but with the right style and some simple hair care tips, you can look and feel your best. Be sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your features, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and stylish.
