How To Get Slicked Back Long Hair

Written by Robby Mar 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a classic and stylish hairstyle, slicked back long hair might be the perfect choice for you. This look is versatile and can work for both casual and formal events. In this article, we'll show you how to get slicked back long hair and provide some tips for maintaining your new 'do.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Slicked Back Long Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Slicked Back Long Hair
  • FAQs About Slicked Back Long Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Slicked Back Long Hair

How to Get Slicked Back Long Hair

To achieve the slicked back long hair look, you'll need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with clean, towel-dried hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and towel-dry it until it's damp. Avoid using a hair dryer, as this can damage your hair and make it harder to style.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or gel, to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, making sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Using a wide-tooth comb, comb your hair back from your forehead towards the crown of your head. Make sure your hair is evenly distributed and there are no tangles or knots.

Step 4: Smooth out any bumps or flyaways

Use your comb or your fingers to smooth out any bumps or flyaways in your hair. You want your hair to be as sleek and polished as possible.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Once your hair is styled the way you want it, finish with a light mist of hairspray to help hold the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

To recap, here's a step-by-step guide for getting slicked back long hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair
  2. Apply a styling product
  3. Comb your hair back
  4. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways
  5. Finish with hairspray

Tips for Maintaining Slicked Back Long Hair

Once you've achieved the slicked back long hair look, you'll want to maintain it for as long as possible. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking sleek and polished:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can make your hair look greasy
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends

FAQs About Slicked Back Long Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicked back long hair:

Q: Can I achieve this look with shorter hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve a slicked back look with shorter hair. However, you may need to use a different styling product and technique to get the desired effect.

Q: Is this style easy to maintain?

A: Slicked back long hair is relatively easy to maintain, but you will need to use styling products and give your hair a little extra attention to keep it looking its best.

Q: Can I wear this style to work?

A: Yes, slicked back long hair can be a professional and stylish look for the workplace. Just make sure to keep your hair neat and polished, and avoid using too much product.

Hair Care Tips for Slicked Back Long Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your slicked back long hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners, as these can damage your hair over time
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using a brush
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more difficult to style

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and polished look with your long hair. Whether you're heading to the office or a night out on the town, slicked back long hair is a versatile and stylish choice that's sure to turn heads.
