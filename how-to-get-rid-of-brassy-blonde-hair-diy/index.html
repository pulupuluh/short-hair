How To Get Rid Of Brassy Blonde Hair Diy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your blonde hair turning brassy and yellow? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with blonde hair struggle with this issue, but fortunately, there are DIY solutions that can help. In this article, we will go over how to get rid of brassy blonde hair DIY. We'll cover the basics, the step-by-step process, and some tips to help you maintain your blonde hair's color.

Table of Contents

  1. The Basics
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Blonde Hair Color
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Blonde Hair

The Basics

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some basics. When blonde hair turns brassy, it means there is too much warmth in the hair. This can be caused by a variety of factors, including exposure to the sun, chlorine, hard water, and even certain shampoos and conditioners. To get rid of brassy tones in blonde hair, you need to neutralize the warmth. This can be done using a toner or a purple shampoo.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow to get rid of brassy blonde hair DIY:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo. This will help to remove any buildup in your hair and prepare it for the next steps. Rinse thoroughly with warm water.

Step 2: Mix the Toner

If you're using a toner, mix it according to the instructions on the package. If you're using a purple shampoo, skip to the next step.

Step 3: Apply the Toner

Apply the toner to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Make sure to saturate your hair evenly with the toner. Leave the toner on for the recommended amount of time, usually around 20-30 minutes.

Step 4: Rinse and Condition

After the recommended time has passed, rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water. Follow up with a moisturizing conditioner to help restore moisture to your hair.

Step 5: Use a Purple Shampoo

If you're using a purple shampoo, wet your hair and apply the shampoo evenly throughout your hair. Massage the shampoo into your hair and leave it on for the recommended amount of time, usually around 5-10 minutes. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.

Tips for Maintaining Blonde Hair Color

Here are some tips to help you maintain your blonde hair color:

  • Use a purple shampoo once a week to help prevent brassy tones from developing.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and lead to brassy tones.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine and hard water, as these can also cause brassy tones.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions about getting rid of brassy blonde hair:

Q: Can I use a toner and purple shampoo together?

A: Yes, you can use a toner and purple shampoo together. Just make sure to follow the instructions on each product carefully.

Q: How often should I use a purple shampoo?

A: You should use a purple shampoo once a week to help maintain your blonde hair color.

Q: Will using a toner or purple shampoo damage my hair?

A: No, using a toner or purple shampoo should not damage your hair. Just make sure to follow the instructions on each product carefully.

Hair Care for Blonde Hair

Blonde hair requires special care to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for caring for your blonde hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to help nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine and hard water, as these can cause damage and breakage.

With these tips and the DIY solution we provided, you can keep your blonde hair looking beautiful and vibrant. Just remember to follow the instructions carefully and take good care of your hair.
