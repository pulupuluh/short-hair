How To Trim Short Hair To Grow Out A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 09, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of waiting for your short hair to grow out? Trimming your hair can actually help it grow longer and healthier. But how do you do it without ending up with a shorter haircut? In this article, we’ll show you how to trim short hair to grow out. We’ll cover everything from the tools you’ll need to the step-by-step process, as well as tips for taking care of your hair after the trim.

Table of Contents

  1. Tools You’ll Need
  2. How to Trim Short Hair to Grow Out
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Taking Care of Your Hair After the Trim
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Healthy Hair Growth

Tools You’ll Need

Before you start trimming your hair, make sure you have the right tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips or rubber bands
  • Water spray bottle
  • Mirror (preferably a handheld one and a larger one that you can hang up)

How to Trim Short Hair to Grow Out

The key to trimming short hair to grow out is to focus on the ends. You want to trim off any split ends or damaged hair that could be slowing down your hair growth. Here’s how to do it:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow-drying it on a low heat setting.
  2. Part your hair down the middle or to one side, depending on your preference.
  3. Clip up the top layer of your hair and separate the bottom layer into small sections.
  4. Take a section of hair and comb it straight down.
  5. Hold the section of hair between your index and middle fingers, about an inch above the ends.
  6. Use the hair cutting scissors to trim off the ends, making sure to cut straight across.
  7. Move on to the next section of hair and repeat the process, working your way around your head.
  8. Once you’ve trimmed all the sections in the bottom layer, unclip the top layer and repeat the process.
  9. Check your hair in the mirror to make sure it’s even.
  10. If you notice any uneven spots, go back and trim them as needed.

Tips for Taking Care of Your Hair After the Trim

Now that you’ve trimmed your hair, it’s important to take good care of it to promote healthy hair growth. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s free of sulfates and parabens.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, and always use a heat protectant spray if you do use them.
  • Get regular trims every 8-12 weeks to keep your ends healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet that’s rich in vitamins and minerals to support healthy hair growth.
  • Consider taking a hair growth supplement, such as biotin or collagen.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about trimming short hair to grow out:

How often should I trim my hair to promote growth?

You should aim to trim your hair every 8-12 weeks to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.

Will trimming my hair make it grow faster?

Trimming your hair won’t make it grow faster, but it will help prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down the growth process.

Can I trim my hair myself, or should I go to a salon?

You can trim your hair yourself if you feel comfortable doing so, but if you’re not confident in your skills, it’s best to go to a salon.

Hair Care for Healthy Hair Growth

Trimming your hair is just one part of promoting healthy hair growth. Here are some other hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Aim for 2-3 times per week.
  • Avoid using hot water when you wash your hair, as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly into hairstyles, as it can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush with soft bristles to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals, such as alcohol or sulfates.

There you have it – our step-by-step guide to trimming short hair to grow out. Remember, the key is to focus on the ends and to take good care of your hair after the trim. With some patience and care, you’ll have long, healthy hair in no time!
