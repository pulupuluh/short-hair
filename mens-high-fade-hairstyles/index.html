Mens High Fade Hairstyles The Ultimate Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Mar 18, 2023 · 7 min read

If you’re looking for a stylish and modern haircut, you can’t go wrong with a high fade. This popular hairstyle has been around for a while, and it’s not going away anytime soon. In this ultimate guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about mens high fade hairstyles, including how to get one, styling tips, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Get a High Fade
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling a High Fade
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care for High Fades

Description

A high fade is a haircut where the hair on the sides and back of the head is cut very short or shaved, and the hair on top is left longer. The hair gradually fades from long to short, creating a stylish and modern look. High fades can be combined with other hairstyles, such as pompadours, quiffs, and slick backs, to create a variety of different looks.

How to Get a High Fade

If you’re interested in getting a high fade, the first step is to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. It’s important to communicate your preferences clearly, so your stylist knows what you’re looking for. You can bring pictures or examples of high fades you like to help your stylist understand your vision.

Choose the Right Fade

There are several different types of fades you can choose from, including high fades, low fades, skin fades, and bald fades. High fades start higher up on the head, while low fades start lower. Skin fades are very short and gradually fade into the skin, while bald fades are shaved down to the skin.

Select a Length for the Top

Once you’ve chosen the type of fade you want, it’s time to select a length for the hair on top. You can choose anything from short and textured to long and slicked back. The length you choose will depend on your personal style and preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting a high fade:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose the type of fade you want.
  3. Select a length for the hair on top.
  4. Use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head, starting with a longer guard and gradually working your way down.
  5. Blend the hair on the sides and back into the hair on top using scissors or clippers.
  6. Style the hair on top using pomade, gel, or wax.
  7. Use a comb to create your desired style.
  8. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling a High Fade

Here are some tips to help you style your high fade:

  • Use a quality styling product to keep your hair in place all day.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Visit your stylist regularly to maintain your fade and keep it looking fresh.
  • Consider adding some color or highlights to your hair to add dimension and depth to your style.

FAQ

How often do I need to get a high fade?

You should visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your high fade and keep it looking sharp.

Can I style my high fade differently for different occasions?

Absolutely! High fades are versatile and can be styled in a variety of different ways. Experiment with different looks to find the one that works best for you.

What type of styling product should I use for my high fade?

It depends on your hair type and the style you’re going for. Pomade, gel, and wax are all good options for styling a high fade.

Hair Care for High Fades

If you want your high fade to look its best, it’s important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for high fades:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock a stylish high fade that looks great and feels great. So, what are you waiting for? Go out and get yourself a high fade today!
