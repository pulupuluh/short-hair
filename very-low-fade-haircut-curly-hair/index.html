Very Low Fade Haircut For Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Aug 17, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how hard it can be to find the perfect haircut. But, have you ever considered getting a very low fade haircut? This type of haircut is perfect for curly hair because it allows you to keep your curls while still having a stylish and modern look. In this article, we will guide you through everything you need to know about getting a very low fade haircut for curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Very Low Fade Haircut for Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining a Very Low Fade Haircut for Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Description

A very low fade haircut is a type of haircut that fades from the hairline to the nape of the neck. It is a popular haircut for men because it is both stylish and easy to maintain. For curly hair, a very low fade haircut can help to define your curls and make them stand out.

How to Get a Very Low Fade Haircut for Curly Hair

Getting a very low fade haircut for curly hair is not complicated, but it does require some attention to detail. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Choose a Good Barber

The first step to getting a very low fade haircut for curly hair is to find a good barber. Look for someone who has experience cutting curly hair and who can give you the look you want.

Step 2: Decide on the Length of Your Hair

Decide on the length of your hair. Do you want a short, medium, or long haircut? Keep in mind that the longer your hair is, the harder it will be to maintain a low fade.

Step 3: Choose the Type of Fade

Choose the type of fade you want. A very low fade will start at the hairline and gradually fade to the nape of the neck.

Step 4: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you go to the barber, make sure your hair is clean and conditioned. This will make it easier for the barber to work with your hair.

Step 5: Communicate with Your Barber

Communicate with your barber and let them know exactly what you want. Show them pictures if possible, and be clear about the length and style you want.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get a very low fade haircut for curly hair:

Step 1: Trim the Top of Your Hair

The first step is to trim the top of your hair to the desired length. This will create a contrast between the top and the sides of your hair.

Step 2: Create a Guideline

Create a guideline for the fade by using clippers to cut a line around the back and sides of your head.

Step 3: Start Fading

Start fading the sides of your hair by using clippers with a lower guard setting. Gradually move from the bottom to the top, using a higher guard setting for each pass.

Step 4: Blend the Top and Sides

Blend the top and sides of your hair by using scissors to cut any longer hairs that are sticking out.

Step 5: Finish the Fade

Finish the fade by using clippers with a higher guard setting to blend the top and sides even more.

Tips for Maintaining a Very Low Fade Haircut for Curly Hair

Maintaining a very low fade haircut for curly hair requires some effort. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking great:

  • Use a good conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat.
  • Use a comb or brush to detangle your curls.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a very low fade haircut for curly hair?

A very low fade haircut for curly hair is a type of haircut that fades from the hairline to the nape of the neck. It is a popular haircut for men because it is both stylish and easy to maintain. For curly hair, a very low fade haircut can help to define your curls and make them stand out.

How do I maintain a very low fade haircut for curly hair?

Maintaining a very low fade haircut for curly hair requires some effort. Use a good conditioner to keep your curls moisturized, avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, get regular trims, use a comb or brush to detangle your curls, and avoid touching your hair too much.

Can I get a very low fade haircut if I have long curly hair?

Yes, you can get a very low fade haircut if you have long curly hair. However, keep in mind that the longer your hair is, the harder it will be to maintain a low fade.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires some special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to help you take care of your curly hair:

  • Use a good conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Detangle your hair gently with a comb or brush.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat.

Getting a very low fade haircut for curly hair is a great way to stay stylish and modern while keeping your curls. With the right barber and some effort to maintain your hair, you can enjoy a great haircut that will make you look and feel your best.
