How To Know Which Hairstyle Will Suit Me

Introduction

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially when you have so many options to choose from. Whether you’re trying to find a new style or want to update your current one, it’s important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when making your decision. In this article, we’ll provide a step-by-step guide on how to know which hairstyle will suit you, as well as tips and FAQs to help you make the best decision for your hair.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Take a close look at your face and compare it to the following descriptions:

  • Oval: If your face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded jawline and forehead, you have an oval face shape.
  • Round: If your face has a circular shape with a wide hairline and full cheeks, you have a round face shape.
  • Square: If your face has a strong jawline and forehead, with a square or rectangular shape, you have a square face shape.
  • Heart: If your face is wider at the temples and narrows at the jawline, with a pointed chin, you have a heart face shape.
  • Diamond: If your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at the forehead and jawline, with a pointed chin, you have a diamond face shape.
  • Oblong: If your face is longer than it is wide, with a narrow chin and forehead, you have an oblong face shape.

Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features.

Step by Step Guide to Finding the Right Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, it’s time to find the right hairstyle. Follow these steps:

  1. Consider your lifestyle: Are you a busy mom who needs a low-maintenance hairstyle, or do you have the time to style your hair every day?
  2. Think about your hair texture: Do you have curly or straight hair? Thick or thin?
  3. Explore different hairstyles: Look online or in magazines for hairstyles that you like and that will complement your face shape.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist: A professional hairstylist can help you choose the right hairstyle based on your face shape and hair texture.
  5. Try it out: Once you’ve found a hairstyle you like, try it out! You can always make adjustments or try something new if it doesn’t work out.

Remember, finding the right hairstyle may take some trial and error. Be patient and keep an open mind!

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair color: Certain hairstyles may look better with certain hair colors.
  • Think about your face features: If you have a prominent nose, for example, you may want to consider a hairstyle that draws attention away from it.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment: Trying new hairstyles can be fun and can help you discover what looks best on you.
  • Take care of your hair: No matter what hairstyle you choose, it’s important to take care of your hair by washing it regularly, using the right products, and protecting it from heat damage.

FAQs

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It’s not uncommon to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, you can choose a hairstyle that complements the features of both shapes.

Can I still wear a certain hairstyle if it doesn’t match my face shape?

Of course! Ultimately, the decision is up to you. Just keep in mind that certain hairstyles may not be as flattering as others.

How often should I change my hairstyle?

There’s no set rule for how often you should change your hairstyle. It’s up to you to decide when you want a change.

Hair Care Tips

Once you’ve found the right hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these hair care tips and choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape and hair texture, you can achieve a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
