What Hair Colours Suit Me Quiz A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Shade

Written by Christine Sep 01, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for a change in your hair colour but don't know where to start? Taking a "What Hair Colours Suit Me Quiz" can help guide you in finding the perfect shade for you. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to take the quiz, tips on how to choose the right hair colour, and answer some frequently asked questions. Plus, we'll give you some hair care tips to keep your new hair colour looking flawless. Let's get started!

How to take the "What Hair Colours Suit Me Quiz"

Before we dive into the quiz, it's important to note that this quiz is just a guide. It's not a guarantee that the recommended hair colour will suit you perfectly. However, it's a great starting point to help you narrow down your options. Here's how to take the quiz:

  1. Search for "What Hair Colours Suit Me Quiz" on your preferred search engine.
  2. Choose a quiz from a reputable website or brand.
  3. Answer questions about your skin tone, eye colour, current hair colour, and lifestyle.
  4. Based on your answers, the quiz will recommend hair colours that would suit you best.

It's important to answer the questions as honestly as possible to get the most accurate results. Don't worry if you're unsure about some questions – just go with your best guess.

Tips on Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Now that you've taken the quiz, you may have a few hair colours in mind. Here are some tips to help you choose the right shade:

  • Consider your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, opt for warm hair colours like golden blonde or chestnut brown. If you have cool undertones, choose cool colours like ash blonde or jet black.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you're someone who's always on the go, a low-maintenance colour like balayage or highlights may be a better choice than a full head of colour.
  • Don't choose a colour based on trends. While it may be tempting to follow the latest hair colour trend, it's important to choose a colour that suits you and your style.

Remember, your hair colour should complement your natural features, not overpower them.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "What Hair Colours Suit Me Quiz":

Can I trust the results of the quiz?

The quiz is just a guide, so the results may not be 100% accurate. However, it's a great starting point to help you narrow down your options.

How often should I change my hair colour?

It's up to you! Some people change their hair colour every few months, while others stick with the same colour for years. It's important to choose a colour that you're comfortable with and makes you feel confident.

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home. However, it's important to follow the instructions carefully and use high-quality products to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Whether you're changing your hair colour or sticking with your current shade, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Conclusion

Taking a "What Hair Colours Suit Me Quiz" can be a fun and helpful way to find your perfect shade. Remember to consider your skin tone, lifestyle, and personal style when choosing a hair colour. And don't forget to take care of your hair with the right products and hair care routine. With these tips, you'll be rocking your new hair colour in no time!
