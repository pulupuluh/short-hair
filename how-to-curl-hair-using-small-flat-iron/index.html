How To Curl Hair Using Small Flat Iron

Written by Luffy Aug 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you want to achieve bouncy curls without spending too much money on a curling iron? Then, a small flat iron is perfect for you! In this article, we'll teach you how to curl hair using small flat iron. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve salon-quality curls at home.

Description

A small flat iron is a versatile hair tool that can be used for straightening, curling, and creating waves. It has smaller plates than a typical flat iron, making it easier to use on shorter hair or for creating tighter curls. Unlike a curling iron, a small flat iron can also be used to straighten hair or create sleek, smooth styles.

How to

Here are the steps on how to curl hair using small flat iron:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before using a small flat iron, it's important to prep your hair with heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties.

Step 2: Heat Up the Flat Iron

Plug in your flat iron and set it to the desired temperature. For fine hair, use a lower heat setting while thicker hair needs a higher heat setting. Wait for the flat iron to heat up before using it.

Step 3: Start Curling

Take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron down at the root. Twist the flat iron 180 degrees away from your face, then glide it down to the end of the hair section. Release the flat iron and let the curl cool down. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've curled all sections of your hair, apply hairspray to hold the curls in place. Use your fingers to gently separate the curls for a more natural look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve perfect curls using a small flat iron:

  • Use a small flat iron with ceramic plates to prevent damage and ensure even heat distribution.
  • Don't hold the flat iron on your hair for too long as it can cause damage.
  • Twist the flat iron away from your face for a more natural look.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using the flat iron to prevent damage.
  • Start with a lower heat setting and adjust as needed to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling hair using a small flat iron:

Can I use a small flat iron on short hair?

Yes! A small flat iron is perfect for creating curls on short hair. Just make sure to use smaller sections of hair to achieve tighter curls.

Can I use a small flat iron to straighten my hair?

Yes! A small flat iron can be used to straighten hair as well as create curls and waves.

How long do the curls last?

The length of time the curls last depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, the curls can last up to 24 hours with the right products and techniques.

Hair Care

Using a small flat iron to curl your hair can cause damage if not done properly. To keep your hair healthy, use a heat protectant spray before using the flat iron and avoid using it on wet hair. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Now that you know how to curl hair using small flat iron, you can achieve bouncy curls at home without spending too much money. With the right techniques and products, you can create salon-quality curls that will last all day.
