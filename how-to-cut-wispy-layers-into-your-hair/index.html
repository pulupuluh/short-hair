How To Cut Wispy Layers Into Your Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a change in your hairstyle? Wispy layers can give your hair a fresh, new look. However, going to a salon can be expensive, and you might not even get the desired result. So, why not try cutting wispy layers into your hair at home? In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut wispy layers into your hair.

How to Cut Wispy Layers into Your Hair

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It is important to start with clean and dry hair. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and then towel dry it. Avoid using a blow dryer, as it can cause damage to your hair. Instead, let your hair air dry naturally.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Take a comb and divide your hair into sections. Start by parting your hair in the middle and then dividing it into two halves. Then, take each half and divide it into two sections, one on each side of your head.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Take a pair of sharp scissors and start cutting your hair. Start with the bottom layer, and then move on to the next layer. The key to getting wispy layers is to cut at an angle. Hold your hair at a 45-degree angle and cut into it.

Step 4: Move Upward

Once you are done with one section, move on to the next. Start from the bottom and work your way up. Remember to cut at an angle to get those wispy layers.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you have finished cutting all sections, check your progress in the mirror. If you think that some layers are too long or too short, you can always go back and fix them.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting your hair:

  • Use sharp scissors
  • Start with small cuts
  • Don't cut too much at once
  • Take your time
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet
  • Be patient and practice

FAQ

How do I know if wispy layers will suit me?

Wispy layers suit most face shapes and hair types. However, it's always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist to determine what will suit you best.

Can I cut wispy layers into short hair?

Yes, you can cut wispy layers into short hair. Just remember to use small cuts and take your time.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair after cutting it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Apply a hair mask once a week
  • Avoid tying your hair too tightly
  • Get regular trims to maintain your wispy layers

Now that you know how to cut wispy layers into your hair, you can try it out at home. Remember to be patient, take your time, and practice. With a little effort, you can achieve a fresh, new look without breaking the bank.
