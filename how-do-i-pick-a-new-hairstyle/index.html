How Do I Pick A New Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel May 19, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? Picking a new hairstyle can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to pick a new hairstyle that suits you. We will also answer frequently asked questions and provide hair care tips to keep your new hairstyle looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Pick a New Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Picking a New Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Pick a New Hairstyle

Picking a new hairstyle can be overwhelming, especially with so many options available. Here are some tips to help you narrow down your choices:

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which hairstyle will suit you best. For example, if you have a round face, a hairstyle with volume on top and sleek sides will complement your features. On the other hand, if you have a square face, a soft and layered hairstyle will help soften your angles.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also impact your hairstyle choice. If you have a low-maintenance lifestyle, you may want to consider a hairstyle that requires minimal styling, such as a short bob or a pixie cut. If you have an active lifestyle, a hairstyle that can be tied up, such as a ponytail or braids, may be more practical.

Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type can also influence your hairstyle choice. If you have thin hair, a layered cut can create the illusion of volume. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut can help control the volume. If you have curly hair, a layered cut can help enhance your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to pick a new hairstyle:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Take a look at your face in the mirror and determine your face shape. This will help you narrow down your hairstyle choices.

Step 2: Consider Your Lifestyle

Think about your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend on styling your hair. This will help you choose a low-maintenance or high-maintenance hairstyle.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Type

Determine your hair type, such as thin, thick, or curly. This will help you choose a hairstyle that suits your hair type.

Step 4: Research Hairstyle Options

Look for hairstyle options that suit your face shape, lifestyle, and hair type. You can browse online or look through magazines for inspiration.

Step 5: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist and bring pictures of the hairstyles you are considering. Your hairstylist can provide you with advice on which hairstyle will suit you best.

Step 6: Try It Out

If you are unsure about a hairstyle, try it out with a wig or temporary hair dye. This will give you a better idea of how the hairstyle will look on you.

Tips for Picking a New Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to help you pick a new hairstyle:

  • Consider the maintenance required for the hairstyle.
  • Think about how the hairstyle will look with your natural hair color.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personal style.
  • Consider the length of the hairstyle and how it will look with different outfits.
  • Take into account any hair accessories you like to wear, such as headbands or clips.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I change my hairstyle?

There is no set timeframe for changing your hairstyle. Some people may change their hairstyle every few months, while others may keep the same hairstyle for years. It ultimately depends on your personal preference and how often you want a change.

What if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you don't like your new hairstyle, don't panic. Hair grows back, and you can always change it again. Talk to your hairstylist and see if there are any adjustments that can be made to the hairstyle.

How do I maintain my new hairstyle?

Maintaining your new hairstyle will depend on the specific hairstyle you choose. Your hairstylist can provide you with advice on how to maintain your new hairstyle, including product recommendations and styling techniques.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your new hairstyle looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage.

By following these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can pick a new hairstyle that will suit you and keep it looking its best.
