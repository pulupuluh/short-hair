How To Fix Damaged Hair From Bleach At Home

Written by Robby May 31, 2023 · 5 min read

Table of Content:

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Bleaching your hair can be a fun and exciting way to change up your look, but it can also cause damage to your hair. If you've bleached your hair and are now dealing with dryness, breakage, and overall damage, don't worry – there are ways to fix it! In this article, we'll go over how to fix damaged hair from bleach at home, using natural ingredients and simple hair care techniques.

How to

The first step in fixing damaged hair from bleach is to stop using any more harsh chemicals on your hair. This means no more bleaching, no more hair dyes, and no more styling products that contain alcohol or sulfates. Instead, you'll want to focus on nourishing and hydrating your hair with natural ingredients like avocado, honey, and coconut oil.

Step by step

Here are the steps you can take to fix damaged hair from bleach:

  1. Stop using harsh chemicals on your hair
  2. Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to wash your hair
  3. Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week
  4. Use natural oils like coconut oil and avocado oil to nourish your hair
  5. Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons
  6. Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when trying to fix damaged hair from bleach:

  • Be patient – it may take some time for your hair to fully recover
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Consider using a silk pillowcase to reduce friction and minimize breakage while you sleep
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair after bleaching it?

A: It's best to wait until your hair is fully healed before dyeing it again. This will help prevent further damage and ensure that the color looks even and vibrant.

Q: Can I use store-bought hair masks to fix my damaged hair?

A: Yes, but be sure to read the ingredients carefully and avoid any products that contain alcohol or sulfates.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep split ends at bay.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair on a daily basis to prevent further damage and promote healthy growth. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly into a ponytail or bun, as this can cause breakage
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water
  • Get plenty of rest to reduce stress and promote hair growth

By following these tips and techniques, you can fix damaged hair from bleach at home and enjoy healthy, beautiful hair once again!
