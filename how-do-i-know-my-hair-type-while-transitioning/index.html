How Do I Know My Hair Type While Transitioning

Written by Bardi Mar 07, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you transitioning from relaxed to natural hair and unsure of your hair type? Knowing your hair type is important for choosing the right products and styling techniques. In this article, we’ll guide you through how to determine your hair type while transitioning.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Hair Type
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Transitioning Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Determine Hair Type

Hair types are classified into four categories: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Each category has subcategories, which are determined by the shape of the hair follicle and the curl pattern. Here are the subcategories:

  • Straight (Type 1A, 1B, 1C)
  • Wavy (Type 2A, 2B, 2C)
  • Curly (Type 3A, 3B, 3C)
  • Coily (Type 4A, 4B, 4C)

Knowing your hair type helps you understand how to care for it, including what products to use, how to style it, and how to maintain healthy hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

The following steps will help you determine your hair type:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then let it air dry.
  2. Do not brush or comb your hair after washing.
  3. Observe the shape of your hair strands: straight, wavy, curly, or coily.
  4. Check the curl pattern of your hair strands.
  5. Determine the thickness of your hair strands: fine, medium, or coarse.

Once you have determined your hair type, you can look up the best products and styling techniques for your specific hair type.

Tips for Transitioning Hair

Transitioning from relaxed to natural hair can be challenging, but with the right tips, it can be a smooth and successful process. Here are some tips for transitioning hair:

  • Be patient: transitioning takes time and requires patience.
  • Avoid heat: excessive heat can damage your hair and slow down the transitioning process.
  • Moisturize regularly: natural hair needs moisture to prevent breakage and promote healthy growth.
  • Protective styling: protective styles such as braids, twists, and bantu knots help to protect your hair from damage.
  • Trim regularly: trimming your hair regularly helps to get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

What is the best way to transition from relaxed to natural hair?

The best way to transition from relaxed to natural hair is to gradually cut off the relaxed ends while growing out your natural hair. This process is known as the big chop.

How often should I wash my transitioning hair?

It is recommended to wash your transitioning hair once a week or every two weeks using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

Can I still use heat on my transitioning hair?

It is best to avoid heat as much as possible while transitioning to prevent damage to your natural hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for healthy natural hair:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners
  • Deep condition regularly
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf

Now that you know your hair type and have some tips for transitioning hair and hair care, you can take care of your hair properly and achieve healthy, beautiful natural hair.
