Does Short Hair Flatter Round Faces

Description

Short hair has always been a popular hairstyle for women, but it can be difficult to determine if it flatters a round face. A round face is characterized by a fullness in the cheeks and a rounded jawline. The key to finding the perfect short hairstyle for a round face is to choose a cut that will elongate the face and create the illusion of a more angular jawline. In this blog post, we will explore whether short hair flatters round faces and provide tips on finding the perfect cut.

How to

When considering whether short hair flatters round faces, it's important to keep in mind the overall shape of the face. A round face is widest at the cheeks and has a rounded chin. The goal of any hairstyle is to create balance and proportion, so when choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, it's important to choose a cut that will elongate the face and create the illusion of a more angular jawline.

Step by Step

1. Determine the shape of your face by measuring the widest part of your face, which is typically at the cheeks, and the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.

2. Choose a short hairstyle that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a more angular jawline. This can include a pixie cut, a bob, or a lob.

3. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a curly bob or a blunt cut with no layers.

4. Consider adding layers to your short hairstyle, as this can create movement and texture that will help elongate your face.

5. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect cut for your face shape.

Tips

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, consider the following tips:

  • Choose a cut that elongates your face and creates the illusion of a more angular jawline.
  • Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Add layers to your short hairstyle to create movement and texture.
  • Consider a side part or asymmetrical cut to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Use styling products to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: What is the best short hairstyle for a round face?

A: The best short hairstyle for a round face is one that elongates the face and creates the illusion of a more angular jawline. This can include a pixie cut, a bob, or a lob.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

A: Bangs can be flattering for a round face, but it's important to choose a style that will elongate your face. Consider side-swept bangs or asymmetrical bangs that create the illusion of a longer face.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. To maintain your short hairstyle, consider the following tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day, as this can damage your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain your short hairstyle.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

In conclusion, short hair can flatter round faces if you choose the right cut. By following the tips and advice in this blog post, you can find the perfect short hairstyle for your face shape and keep it looking its best with proper hair care.
