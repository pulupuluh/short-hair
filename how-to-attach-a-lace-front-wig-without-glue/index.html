How To Attach A Lace Front Wig Without Glue

Written by Luffy Mar 09, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of using glue to attach your lace front wig? Or are you allergic to wig glue? Whatever your reason may be, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we will show you how to attach a lace front wig without glue.

Table of Contents

  • How to Attach a Lace Front Wig Without Glue
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Attaching a Lace Front Wig Without Glue
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Your Wig

How to Attach a Lace Front Wig Without Glue

There are different ways to attach a lace front wig without glue. One of the most popular methods is using wig tape or wig clips. Both options can provide a secure and comfortable fit.

Option 1: Wig Tape

Wig tape is a double-sided adhesive that is safe for skin and hair. It comes in different shapes and sizes, and you can choose the one that best fits your wig and hairline. Here’s how to use wig tape to attach your lace front wig:

Step 1: Clean your hairline

Before applying the wig tape, it’s important to clean your hairline with rubbing alcohol or witch hazel. This will remove any oils or dirt that may affect the adhesive.

Step 2: Cut the wig tape to size

Measure the length of the wig tape along your hairline and cut it to size. Peel off one side of the tape and apply it to your hairline.

Step 3: Attach the wig

Position your lace front wig on your head and align it with your hairline. Press the wig onto the tape, starting from the front and working your way back. Smooth out any bumps or wrinkles.

Option 2: Wig Clips

Wig clips are small combs that attach to the wig cap and grip onto your natural hair. They are easy to use and can be removed without damaging your wig or hair. Here’s how to attach a lace front wig with wig clips:

Step 1: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections and pin them out of the way. This will make it easier to attach the wig clips.

Step 2: Sew the wig clips onto the wig cap

Position the wig clips on the wig cap where you want them to be. Sew them onto the cap using a needle and thread. Make sure they are secure and won’t come loose.

Step 3: Attach the wig

Put on the wig and align it with your hairline. Snap the wig clips onto your natural hair, starting from the front and working your way back. Adjust the wig as needed.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to attach a lace front wig without glue:

  1. Clean your hairline with rubbing alcohol or witch hazel.
  2. Cut the wig tape to size or sew the wig clips onto the cap.
  3. Attach the wig tape or wig clips to your hairline or natural hair.
  4. Align the wig with your hairline and press it onto the tape or snap the wig clips onto your hair.
  5. Adjust the wig as needed and style it to your liking.

Tips for Attaching a Lace Front Wig Without Glue

Here are some tips to help you attach your lace front wig without glue:

  • Choose the right wig tape or wig clips for your hair type and wig cap.
  • Test the adhesive on a small area of your skin before applying it to your hairline.
  • Use a wig cap to protect your natural hair and make the wig more comfortable to wear.
  • Don’t pull the wig too tight or it may cause tension headaches or hair loss.
  • Protect your wig from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray or avoiding hot styling tools.

FAQs

Q: How long does wig tape last?

A: Wig tape can last for several days or up to two weeks, depending on the strength of the adhesive and how well it’s applied. You may need to replace the tape more frequently if you sweat or exercise.

Q: Can I swim with a wig attached with wig tape or clips?

A: It’s not recommended to swim with a wig attached with wig tape or clips, as the water may loosen the adhesive or cause the wig to shift. If you need to swim, consider using a waterproof wig adhesive or wearing a swim cap over the wig.

Q: Can I sleep with a wig attached with wig tape or clips?

A: It’s not recommended to sleep with a wig attached with wig tape or clips, as it may cause discomfort or damage to the wig or your natural hair. It’s best to remove the wig before going to bed.

Hair Care Tips for Your Wig

Proper hair care is essential to keep your wig looking and feeling its best. Here are some tips to help you care for your wig:

  • Wash your wig regularly with a wig shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to detangle your wig gently.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools or exposing your wig to direct sunlight or heat.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head when not in use.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hair products on your wig.

By following these tips, you can enjoy a beautiful and comfortable lace front wig without glue. Whether you choose wig tape or wig clips, make sure to choose the one that suits your needs and preferences. With a little practice, you can attach your wig like a pro and rock any hairstyle you desire!
