What Hairstyle Best Suits A Heart Shaped Face

Description

Heart shaped faces have a wide forehead and cheekbones that taper down to a narrow chin. Celebrities with heart shaped faces include Reese Witherspoon, Jennifer Love Hewitt, and Scarlett Johansson. When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape to enhance your natural features. In this blog post, we'll discuss what hairstyle best suits a heart shaped face and provide tips on how to maintain it.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle for your heart shaped face is to determine your face shape. Here are the steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Trace the outline of your face onto the mirror with a lipstick or eyeliner.
  3. Compare the shape to the following face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong.

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape has a forehead that's slightly wider than the chin, and the length is about one and a half times the width.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape has a forehead and jawline that are about the same width, and the length is about the same as the width.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong jawline and a forehead that's about the same width as the jawline.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape has a wide forehead and cheekbones that taper down to a narrow chin.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and chin with wide cheekbones.

Oblong Face Shape

An oblong face shape has a long and narrow face with a high forehead and a narrow chin.

Step-by-Step Guide on Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Once you've determined that you have a heart shaped face, you can start choosing the right hairstyle. Here are some guidelines to follow:

  1. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as this will make your forehead look wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the bottom of your hair.
  2. Consider getting bangs to balance out your forehead. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are great options.
  3. Long hairstyles that frame your face and add volume to the bottom are great for heart shaped faces. Consider getting layers or beachy waves.
  4. Avoid hairstyles that are slicked back or tight to your head, as this will emphasize your forehead.
  5. If you want a short hairstyle, consider a pixie cut with long side bangs or a bob that falls just below your chin.

Tips on Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you've chosen the right hairstyle, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to the bottom of your hair.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo on days when you don't wash your hair to add texture and volume.

FAQs

Can heart shaped faces pull off bangs?

Yes, heart shaped faces can pull off bangs. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are great options to balance out your forehead.

What hairstyles should heart shaped faces avoid?

Heart shaped faces should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, as this will make the forehead look wider. They should also avoid hairstyles that are slicked back or tight to the head, as this will emphasize the forehead.

Can heart shaped faces have short hair?

Yes, heart shaped faces can have short hair. A pixie cut with long side bangs or a bob that falls just below the chin are great options.

Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Faces

Here are some additional hair care tips for heart shaped faces:

  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know what hairstyle best suits a heart shaped face, you can confidently choose a hairstyle that enhances your natural features. Remember to follow the steps on how to determine your face shape and the guidelines on choosing the right hairstyle. With the right hairstyle and hair care, you can look and feel your best.
