How To Cut My Bangs Side Swept A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Feb 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of having the same old boring hairstyle? Why not try cutting your bangs side swept? It's a simple and easy way to change up your look without committing to a drastic haircut. In this post, we'll show you how to cut your bangs side swept in a relaxed and easy-to-understand manner.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Cut My Bangs Side Swept
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Your Bangs Side Swept
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Side swept bangs are a popular hairstyle that frames your face and adds dimension to your hair. They can be cut at different lengths depending on your preference and face shape. Side swept bangs work well with both long and short hair and can be styled in various ways.

How to Cut My Bangs Side Swept

Before you start cutting your bangs, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off your hair. You may also want to have a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp while cutting.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Start by sectioning off your bangs. Use a comb to create a triangle shape at the front of your head, with the point of the triangle ending at the center of your forehead.
  2. Clip the rest of your hair back so it's out of the way.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the triangle shape and comb it straight down. This will be your guide for cutting the rest of your bangs.
  4. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Start with small snips and work your way across the section of hair.
  5. Repeat this process with the rest of your bangs, using the first section as a guide.
  6. When you reach the sides of your head, angle the scissors slightly downwards to create the side swept effect. This will help blend your bangs into the rest of your hair.
  7. Check your progress by flipping your bangs back and forth to make sure they're even.
  8. Once you're happy with the length and shape of your bangs, comb them to the side and style them as desired.

Tips for Cutting Your Bangs Side Swept:

  • Start with small snips and work your way across your bangs. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's cut.
  • Keep your hair damp while cutting to help control frizz and make it easier to cut.
  • If you're not confident in your cutting skills, consider going to a salon or asking a friend to help you.
  • Remember that bangs will dry shorter than they appear when wet, so be cautious when cutting.
  • Don't cut too close to your scalp, as this can create a choppy and uneven look.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: Can I cut my bangs side swept myself?
A: Yes, you can cut your bangs side swept yourself, but it's important to follow the proper steps and be cautious.

Q: How often should I trim my side swept bangs?
A: It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Hair Care Tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Cutting your bangs side swept is a simple and easy way to change up your hairstyle. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a stylish and trendy look that frames your face and adds dimension to your hair. Remember to be cautious and take your time when cutting your bangs, and consider seeking professional help if you're not confident in your skills.
