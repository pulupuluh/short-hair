How To Keep Hair Back For Guys A Step By Step Guide In 2023

Written by Frank Aug 01, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're a guy with long hair or a hairstyle that falls into your face, you may be wondering how to keep hair back for guys. It can be frustrating to constantly have to push your hair out of your eyes or deal with it getting in your mouth while you're trying to eat or talk. Fortunately, there are several ways to keep your hair back and out of your face. In this guide, we'll go over some tips and tricks for keeping your hair back and looking great.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Keep Hair Back for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Keeping Hair Back
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Keep Hair Back for Guys

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's take a look at some of the most popular ways to keep hair back for guys:

  • Headbands
  • Pomade or Hair Gel
  • Bobby Pins or Hair Clips
  • Hair Ties or Hair Bands
  • Hats or Beanies

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some of the most popular ways to keep hair back for guys, let's take a closer look at each method:

1. Headbands

To wear a headband, simply place it over your forehead and slide it back to your hairline. Make sure it's snug but not too tight. You can choose from a variety of materials, such as cloth, elastic, or even leather. Headbands are a great option for guys with longer hair or if you're growing out your hair and don't want it falling in your face.

2. Pomade or Hair Gel

If you have shorter hair, pomade or hair gel can help keep your hair back and in place. Simply apply a small amount to your hands and run it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips. This will help keep your hair in place all day and prevent it from falling in your face.

3. Bobby Pins or Hair Clips

Bobby pins or hair clips are another option for guys with shorter hair. Simply section off the hair that falls into your face and use a bobby pin or hair clip to hold it back. This is a great option for guys who want to keep their hair out of their face but don't want to wear a headband or tie it back with a hair band.

4. Hair Ties or Hair Bands

If you have longer hair, hair ties or hair bands can help keep your hair back and out of your face. Simply gather your hair into a ponytail, and secure it with a hair tie or hair band. You can also braid your hair and secure it with a hair tie or hair band for a different look.

5. Hats or Beanies

Hats or beanies are a great option for guys who want to keep their hair back and out of their face while also staying warm. Simply place the hat or beanie on your head, making sure it covers your forehead and hairline. This is a great option for guys with longer hair or if you're growing out your hair and don't want it falling in your face.

Tips for Keeping Hair Back

Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair back:

  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.
  • Try different hairstyles, such as a slicked-back look or a side part.
  • Use a comb or brush to style your hair and keep it in place.
  • Consider getting a haircut or trim to help keep your hair back and out of your face.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping hair back for guys:

What is the best way to keep hair back for guys?

The best way to keep hair back for guys depends on your hair length and personal preference. Some popular options include headbands, pomade or hair gel, bobby pins or hair clips, hair ties or hair bands, and hats or beanies.

How do I keep my hair back without damaging it?

To keep your hair back without damaging it, use a light hold hairspray or styling product, and avoid pulling your hair too tightly. You can also try different hairstyles, such as a slicked-back look or a side part, to keep your hair out of your face.

Can I wear a headband with short hair?

Yes, you can wear a headband with short hair. Simply place the headband over your forehead and slide it back to your hairline. Make sure it's snug but not too tight.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers or straighteners, too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get regular haircuts or trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair back and looking great. Whether you prefer headbands, pomade or hair gel, bobby pins or hair clips, hair ties or hair bands, or hats or beanies, there's a solution that will work for you. Experiment with different styles and products to find the perfect way to keep your hair back for your lifestyle and personal preferences.
