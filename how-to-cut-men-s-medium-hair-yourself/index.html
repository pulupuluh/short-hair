How To Cut Men s Medium Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 14, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts at the salon every month? Well, you don't have to anymore! With this step-by-step guide, you can learn how to cut men's medium hair yourself from the comfort of your own home. Here's everything you need to know:

How to

To get started, gather all the necessary tools: scissors, clippers, a comb, a spray bottle filled with water, and a mirror.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing and drying your hair.

2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

3. Start with the sides. Use clippers with a number 3 or 4 guard to trim the hair around your temples and sides.

4. Move on to the back. Use clippers with a number 4 or 5 guard to trim the hair at the nape of your neck.

5. Next, work on the top section. Use scissors to cut the hair on top to your desired length. Use the comb to lift the hair up and trim it evenly.

6. Blend the sides and back with the top using scissors. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle to blend the sections together.

7. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven spots.

8. Use the clippers without a guard to clean up the hairline around your ears and neck.

9. Finally, use the mirror to check for any spots you may have missed or need to touch up.

Tips

- Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.

- Use caution when using scissors near your face and ears.

- Take breaks if you need to rest your arms or hands. Cutting your hair can be tiring!

FAQ

Q: How long should I wait before cutting my hair again?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Generally, you can wait 4-6 weeks between haircuts.

Q: Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

A: It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can appear longer than it actually is.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often.

Cutting your hair at home can seem intimidating, but with these tips and guidelines, you can achieve a professional-looking cut in no time. Give it a try and see how much money and time you can save!
