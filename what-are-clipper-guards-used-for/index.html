What Are Clipper Guards Used For

Written by Petter May 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Clipper guards are an essential tool when it comes to hair cutting. They are an attachment that fits onto the blades of hair clippers and are used to determine the length of hair that is cut. In this article, we will discuss what clipper guards are used for, how to use them, and some tips on using them effectively.

Description

Clipper guards come in different sizes, ranging from 0.5mm to 25mm. They are usually made of plastic and have a number printed on them that corresponds to the length of hair they will cut. For example, a size 1 clipper guard will cut hair to 1/8 inch in length, while a size 8 guard will cut hair to 1 inch in length. Clipper guards are used by both professionals and non-professionals alike, and they can be purchased at most beauty supply stores.

How to Use Clipper Guards

Using clipper guards is relatively simple. First, attach the guard to the clipper blade. Make sure it is securely attached and won't fall off during use. Next, turn on the clippers and start cutting. It's important to use a slow, steady motion when using clipper guards to ensure an even cut. It's also important to start with a larger guard size and work your way down to a smaller size if you want a shorter cut.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Choose the right guard size

Choose the right guard size depending on the length of hair you want to cut. If you're not sure, start with a larger size and work your way down.

Step 2: Attach the guard to the clipper blade

Attach the guard to the clipper blade and make sure it is securely attached.

Step 3: Turn on the clippers

Turn on the clippers and start cutting. Use a slow, steady motion to ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Work your way down to a smaller size

If you want a shorter cut, work your way down to a smaller guard size.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you use clipper guards effectively:

  • Start with a larger guard size and work your way down to a smaller size if you want a shorter cut.
  • Use a slow, steady motion when using clipper guards to ensure an even cut.
  • Clean your clipper guards regularly to prevent hair buildup and ensure they last longer.
  • Use clipper oil on your clippers and guards to keep them running smoothly.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different guard sizes to find the perfect length for your hair.

A clipper guard determines the length of hair that is cut, while a clipper blade cuts the hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after using clipper guards. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

Clipper guards are an essential tool for anyone who wants to cut their own hair or the hair of others. With the right technique and some practice, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut in the comfort of your own home. Remember to choose the right guard size, use a slow, steady motion, and take care of your hair after using clipper guards.
