How To Get Curly Hair Dry Naturally Without Frizz

Written by Luffy Jun 26, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to dry it without causing frizz. Blow drying or using heat styling tools can damage your hair over time, so it's important to know how to air dry your curls without causing damage.

How to Get Curly Hair Dry Naturally Without Frizz

The key to drying curly hair without frizz is to avoid touching your hair while it's drying. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Squeeze Out Excess Water

After washing your hair, gently squeeze out excess water with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as this can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply Curl Enhancing Products

Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair while it's still wet. This will help define your curls and prevent frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Let your hair air dry without touching it. You can either let it hang loose or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz. If you don't have a diffuser attachment, you can scrunch your curls gently with your hands while drying.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to drying your curly hair without frizz:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Squeeze out excess water with a microfiber towel or old t-shirt.
  3. Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair while it's still wet.
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  5. Let your hair air dry without touching it.
  6. If you have a diffuser attachment, use it to dry your curls without causing frizz.
  7. If you don't have a diffuser attachment, scrunch your curls gently with your hands while drying.
  8. Once your hair is dry, you can use a serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.
  9. Avoid touching your hair throughout the day to prevent frizz.
  10. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid damaging your curls while you sleep.

Tips for Drying Curly Hair Naturally

Here are some additional tips to help you dry your curly hair without frizz:

  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Don't touch your hair while it's drying, as this can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using a regular towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.
  • Apply a curl enhancing product to your hair while it's still wet to define your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz.
  • Scrunch your curls gently with your hands while drying if you don't have a diffuser attachment.
  • Avoid touching your hair throughout the day to prevent frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid damaging your curls while you sleep.

FAQs

Can I use a regular towel to dry my curly hair?

No, using a regular towel to dry your curly hair can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.

What products should I use to dry my curly hair without frizz?

Use a curl enhancing product to define your curls and prevent frizz. Look for products that contain natural ingredients and avoid products that contain sulfates or silicones.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash your hair once a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair every other day. Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of natural oils and cause damage.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair over time.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or headbands, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid damaging your curls while you sleep.

By following these tips and techniques, you can dry your curly hair naturally without causing frizz. Remember to be gentle with your hair and avoid touching it while it's drying. With the right products and care, you can have beautiful, healthy curls.
