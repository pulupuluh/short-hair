How To Wear Medium Length Hair For Guys A Complete Guide

Medium length hair has become increasingly popular among guys in recent years. It's a versatile length that can be styled in a variety of ways to suit different occasions and personal preferences. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to wear medium length hair for guys, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

How to Wear Medium Length Hair for Guys

Medium length hair can be worn in a variety of ways, depending on your personal style and the occasion. Here are some popular ways to wear medium length hair:

1. Slicked Back

The slicked back hairstyle is a classic look that can be achieved with medium length hair. To achieve this style, apply a styling product such as pomade or gel to your hair and comb it back away from your face. This is a great look for formal occasions or for a polished, put-together look.

2. Messy Texture

The messy texture look is a popular style for guys with medium length hair. To achieve this look, apply a texturizing product such as sea salt spray or hair wax to your hair and scrunch it up to create a messy, tousled look. This is a great look for casual occasions or for a relaxed, effortless style.

3. Side Part

The side part is a classic look that can be achieved with medium length hair. To achieve this style, comb your hair to one side and apply a styling product such as pomade or wax to keep it in place. This is a great look for formal occasions or for a polished, sophisticated look.

4. Half-Up, Half-Down

The half-up, half-down hairstyle is a great way to wear medium length hair for a casual, laid-back look. To achieve this style, gather the top half of your hair and tie it back with a hair tie or clip while leaving the bottom half loose. This is a great look for everyday wear or for a relaxed, bohemian style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to wear medium length hair for guys:

1. Choose Your Style

Decide which style you want to wear your medium length hair in, such as slicked back, messy texture, side part, or half-up, half-down.

2. Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type. Towel dry your hair gently.

3. Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product such as pomade, gel, wax, or sea salt spray to your hair to achieve your desired style.

4. Style Your Hair

Use a comb, brush, or your fingers to style your hair into your desired look, such as slicked back, messy texture, side part, or half-up, half-down.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a spritz of hairspray to keep your hair in place and add shine.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Hair

Here are some tips for styling medium length hair:

1. Experiment with Different Products

Try out different styling products such as pomade, gel, wax, or sea salt spray to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.

2. Don't Overdo It

Use a small amount of styling product at a time to avoid weighing down or greasing up your hair.

3. Use the Right Tools

Use a comb, brush, or your fingers to style your hair depending on the desired look.

4. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

FAQs

1. Can guys with curly hair wear medium length hair?

Yes, guys with curly hair can wear medium length hair. They can achieve a variety of styles, such as letting their curls loose, slicking them back, or creating a messy texture.

2. Can medium length hair be worn in a professional setting?

Yes, medium length hair can be worn in a professional setting. Styles such as slicked back or side part can create a polished, professional look.

3. How often should I wash my medium length hair?

It's recommended to wash medium length hair every 2-3 days to maintain its natural oils and prevent dryness.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Hair

Here are some hair care tips for medium length hair:

1. Use a Shampoo and Conditioner Suitable for Your Hair Type

Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type, such as dry, oily, or curly hair.

2. Avoid Overusing Heat Styling Tools

Avoid overusing heat styling tools such as hairdryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Get regular haircuts every 4-6 weeks to maintain the health and shape of your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily wear medium length hair for guys in a variety of styles and occasions. Remember to experiment with different products and tools to find the ones that work best for your hair type and desired style, and to maintain the health of your hair with regular haircuts and proper hair care.
