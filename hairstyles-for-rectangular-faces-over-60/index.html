Hairstyles For Rectangular Faces Over 60

Written by Eveline Sep 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you over 60 and have a rectangular face shape? Do you want to find a flattering hairstyle that suits your face shape and age? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for rectangular faces over 60. So, let's get started.

Description:

The rectangular face shape is characterized by a long face with a strong jawline, wide forehead, and cheekbones. The goal of choosing a hairstyle is to soften the angles of the face and create an illusion of length. It's essential to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you look younger and more vibrant.

How To:

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your rectangular face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring your face's length and width and comparing the measurements. Once you have determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that suit your features.

Step by Step:

Step 1: Determine your face shape
Step 2: Look for hairstyles that flatter your features
Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist
Step 4: Try different hairstyles and see what suits you best
Step 5: Experiment with different hair colors and styles

Tips:

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your rectangular face:

  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight
  • Try hairstyles with layers, waves, and curls
  • Consider getting a fringe or bangs to soften your forehead
  • Experiment with different hair colors to add dimension to your hair

FAQ:

Q: What hairstyles suit rectangular faces over 60?
A: Hairstyles with layers, waves, and curls suit rectangular faces over 60. Fringes or bangs can also soften the forehead and add volume to the sides of the face.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a rectangular face over 60?
A: Avoid hairstyles that are too long and straight as they can make your face look even longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short and choppy as they can accentuate your strong jawline.

Hair Care:

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking fresh
  • Use hair products that suit your hair type

Now that you know some of the best hairstyles for rectangular faces over 60, it's time to experiment and find the one that suits you best. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Good luck!
