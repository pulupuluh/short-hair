How Can I Train My Hair To Be Straight

Written by Luffy Aug 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are someone who has naturally curly or wavy hair, you may have always wanted to have straight hair. While it may seem like an impossible feat, the good news is that you can train your hair to be straight. In this post, we will discuss how you can do that step by step.

Description

Training your hair to be straight involves a combination of techniques and products that help to straighten and smooth out your hair over time. It is important to note that this is not a permanent solution, and you will need to continue using these methods to maintain the straightness of your hair.

How to Train Your Hair to Be Straight

Here are the steps to follow if you want to train your hair to be straight:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

It is important to start with clean hair before you begin the straightening process. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup from your hair, which can prevent the straightening products from working effectively.

Step 2: Apply a Straightening Product

Next, apply a straightening product to your hair. This can be a leave-in conditioner or a styling cream that is specifically designed to smooth out curly or wavy hair. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while brushing it straight. Use a round brush to create tension and pull your hair straight as you dry it. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 4: Use a Straightening Iron

Once your hair is dry, use a straightening iron to further straighten your hair. Use a high-quality iron with ceramic plates to prevent damage to your hair. Take small sections of hair and run the iron through each section, starting at the roots and pulling it down to the ends.

Step 5: Finish with a Serum

Finally, apply a serum to your hair to add shine and reduce frizz. This will help to keep your hair straight and smooth throughout the day.

Tips for Training Your Hair to Be Straight

Here are some tips to keep in mind as you train your hair to be straight:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before you begin the straightening process.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Try not to use a straightening iron every day, as this can cause damage to your hair over time.
  • Invest in a high-quality hair dryer and straightening iron to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment to make your hair smoother and easier to straighten.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about training your hair to be straight:

Can I train my hair to be straight without using a straightening iron?

While a straightening iron is the most effective way to straighten your hair, you can also use a blow dryer and a round brush to create a straighter look.

How long does it take to train your hair to be straight?

It can take several weeks or even months to train your hair to be straight, depending on how curly or wavy your hair is. Be patient and consistent with your straightening routine.

Can I still curl my hair after training it to be straight?

Yes, you can still curl your hair after training it to be straight. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using the straightening iron every day.

Hair Care for Straight Hair

Once you have trained your hair to be straight, it is important to take care of it to maintain its straightness. Here are some tips for caring for straight hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent frizz and maintain shine.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day, as this can cause damage to your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can train your hair to be straight and enjoy the sleek, smooth look you have always wanted.
