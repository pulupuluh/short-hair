Men s Haircut Long On Top Short On Bottom A Stylish And Low Maintenance Look

Written by Eveline May 16, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle, then a men's haircut long on top short on bottom might be the perfect option for you. This hairstyle has gained popularity in recent years, and for good reason. It's a versatile style that can work for different hair types and face shapes. In this post, we'll cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, tips for styling and maintenance, and more.

Description

A men's haircut long on top short on bottom is exactly what it sounds like. The hair on the top of the head is left longer, while the sides and back are cut shorter. This creates a contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the bottom. The length of the hair on top can vary, from a few inches to several inches, depending on your preference.

How to Get a Men's Haircut Long on Top Short on Bottom

The best way to get this hairstyle is to go to a professional stylist or barber. They will be able to give you a precise cut that suits your face shape and hair type. Here are the basic steps involved in getting a men's haircut long on top short on bottom:

Step 1: Consultation

Before your stylist starts cutting your hair, they will have a consultation with you. This is the time to discuss your preferences and style goals. You can show them pictures of the hairstyle you want or ask for their recommendations based on your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Cutting the Sides and Back

The stylist will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair. They will use clippers to create a short and even cut. The length of the hair can vary, but it's usually around 1-2 inches.

Step 3: Cutting the Top

Next, the stylist will move on to the top of your hair. They will use scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. They may also use thinning shears to create texture and blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back.

Step 4: Styling

Once the haircut is done, the stylist will style your hair according to your preferences. You can ask for a textured or slicked-back look, or leave it natural for a more relaxed style.

Tips for Styling and Maintenance

Here are some tips to keep your men's haircut long on top short on bottom looking great:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Apply a styling product, such as wax or pomade, to the top of your hair. This will help to add texture and hold.
  • If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

What face shape is best for a men's haircut long on top short on bottom?

This hairstyle can work for different face shapes, but it looks particularly good on men with oval or square faces.

Can I still get a men's haircut long on top short on bottom if I have thinning hair?

Yes, this hairstyle can work for men with thinning hair. Your stylist can create texture and volume on the top of your hair to make it look fuller.

How often should I get a trim?

You should visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hairdryers and straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Overall, a men's haircut long on top short on bottom is a stylish and low-maintenance look that can work for different hair types and face shapes. With the right styling and maintenance, you can keep your hair looking great and feeling healthy.
