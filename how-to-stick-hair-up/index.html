How To Stick Hair Up A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to style your hair, sticking it up is a great option. Whether you're trying to keep it out of your face or just want a sleek and stylish look, there are plenty of ways to stick your hair up. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process step by step so you can achieve the perfect look.

How to Stick Hair Up

Before we get started, it's important to note that there are many different ways to stick your hair up. The method you choose will depend on your hair type, length, and personal style. Some popular options include:

  • Ponytail
  • Bun
  • Braid
  • Half-up, half-down
  • Top knot

Here's how to stick your hair up using a ponytail:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If you haven't washed it recently, use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil. Brush your hair out to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Using a brush or comb, gather your hair at the top of your head. You can position the ponytail wherever you like, depending on the look you're going for.

Step 3: Secure with an Elastic

Once you've gathered your hair, use an elastic to secure it in place. Make sure it's tight enough to hold your hair up, but not so tight that it's uncomfortable. If you have thick or heavy hair, you may need to use multiple elastics to keep it in place.

Step 4: Smooth and Style

Use a brush or your fingers to smooth out any bumps or flyaways. You can also use a bit of hairspray to keep everything in place. If you want to add some extra style, you can wrap a small section of hair around the elastic and secure it with a bobby pin.

Tips for Sticking Hair Up

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you
  • Use hair accessories like bobby pins or headbands to add some extra flair
  • Try using a texturizing spray or pomade to add some volume and texture
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're having trouble styling the back of your head

FAQ About Sticking Hair Up

Here are some frequently asked questions about sticking hair up:

Is it bad for your hair to stick it up?

Sticking your hair up can be damaging if you do it too often or use tight elastics. Make sure to give your hair a break and use gentle elastics to avoid breakage.

Can I stick my hair up if it's short?

Yes! Even if your hair is short, you can still create a cute and stylish look by sticking it up. Try a half-up, half-down style or a small bun.

Hair Care Tips

Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often
  • Use a heat protectant if you use hot tools like a flat iron or curling iron
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause breakage
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to stick your hair up in no time. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, there's a style that's perfect for you.
