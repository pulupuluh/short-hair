Best Haircut For Long Face Over 60 Tips And Ideas

Written by Bardi Aug 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a long face and are over 60, finding the perfect haircut can be challenging. You want to find a style that flatters your features and complements your age. In this article, we'll show you the best haircut for long face over 60, how to achieve it, and tips for maintaining it.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Best Haircut
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintenance
  5. FAQs

Description

As we age, our hair tends to lose volume and thickness, making it more challenging to find a flattering hairstyle. For women over 60 with long faces, the best haircut is one that adds volume and width to the face. Layers, bangs, and waves are excellent options that can help soften the face and bring attention to your best features.

How to Choose the Best Haircut

When choosing the best haircut for a long face over 60, consider the following factors:

  • Face Shape: If you have a long face, choose a style that adds width and volume to the face. Layers, bangs, and waves are excellent options to soften the face and bring attention to your best features.
  • Hair Texture: Consider your hair's texture when choosing a style. If you have thin hair, layers and waves can add volume and thickness. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut with soft layers can help frame the face.
  • Lifestyle: Choose a style that suits your lifestyle. If you have an active lifestyle, a low-maintenance style like a bob or pixie cut may be more suitable. If you prefer a more polished look, a layered cut with bangs may be a better option.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the best haircut for a long face over 60:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best style for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Choose a style that complements your age and lifestyle.
  3. Ask for layers, bangs, or waves to add volume and width to the face.
  4. Maintain your haircut with regular trims to keep it looking fresh.

Tips for Maintenance

Here are some tips for maintaining your best haircut for a long face over 60:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add thickness and volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage and dry out your locks.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks for regular trims to maintain your style.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for a long face over 60:

  • Q: What is the best haircut for a long face over 60?
  • A: The best haircut for a long face over 60 is one that adds volume and width to the face. Layers, bangs, and waves are excellent options that can help soften the face and bring attention to your best features.
  • Q: Can women over 60 wear bangs?
  • A: Yes, women over 60 can wear bangs. Bangs can help soften the face and bring attention to your best features.
  • Q: How often should I get a haircut?
  • A: It's recommended to get a haircut every six to eight weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as it can damage and dry out your locks.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized.

Now that you know the best haircut for a long face over 60, it's time to schedule a consultation with your hairstylist. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a style, and maintain your haircut with regular trims and proper hair care. With the right haircut, you can look and feel your best at any age.
