How To Get Natural Dreads With Curly Hair

Written by Eveline May 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to get natural dreads with curly hair. Dreads, also known as dreadlocks, have been a popular hairstyle for centuries. They are versatile, low maintenance, and can be customized to fit any personality. If you have curly hair and want to achieve natural-looking dreads, then this guide is for you. Read on to learn how to get natural dreads with curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Natural Dreads with Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get Natural Dreads with Curly Hair

Getting natural dreads with curly hair requires patience and dedication. If you want to achieve natural-looking dreads, then you need to avoid using any chemicals or products that can damage your hair. Here are the steps you need to follow to get natural dreads with curly hair:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before starting the process of getting natural dreads with curly hair, make sure your hair is clean. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or excess oil from your hair. This will help your hair knot and tangle more easily.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small, equal parts. Use a comb to create sections that are the same size. This will help you achieve even and uniform dreads. Use hair clips to keep each section separated.

Step 3: Twist and Knot Hair

Take one section of hair and twist it tightly. Use your fingers to knot the twisted hair together. Continue to twist and knot the hair until it starts to form a dread. Repeat this process with each section of hair.

Step 4: Maintain Your Dreads

Once you have achieved the desired number of dreads, you need to maintain them. This involves washing them regularly with a residue-free shampoo, and separating any knots or tangles that may form. You can also use a natural oil or locking gel to help your dreads stay in place.

Tips for Maintaining Natural Dreads with Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you maintain your natural dreads with curly hair:

  • Use a residue-free shampoo to wash your dreads. This will prevent buildup and keep them clean.
  • Avoid using any products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and cause damage.
  • Use a natural oil or locking gel to help your dreads stay in place.
  • Be patient, as it can take several months for your dreads to fully mature.
  • Separate any knots or tangles that may form, as this will prevent your dreads from merging together.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting natural dreads with curly hair:

Q: How long does it take to get natural dreads with curly hair?

A: It can take several months to a year to fully mature natural dreads with curly hair.

Q: Can I use products to help my dreads form?

A: Yes, you can use natural oils or locking gel to help your dreads form and stay in place.

Q: How often should I wash my dreads?

A: You should wash your dreads every 1-2 weeks with a residue-free shampoo.

Hair Care Tips for Natural Dreads with Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for natural dreads with curly hair:

  • Avoid using any chemical treatments or products that can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before starting the process of getting dreads.
  • Apply a natural oil or conditioner to your hair to keep it soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Be patient and consistent with your hair care routine to achieve natural-looking dreads with curly hair.

That's it for our guide on how to get natural dreads with curly hair. We hope this guide has been helpful in achieving your desired look. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and avoid using any products or treatments that can damage your hair.
