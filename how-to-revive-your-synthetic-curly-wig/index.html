How To Revive Your Synthetic Curly Wig

Written by Petter May 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a fan of wigs, then you know that they can be a great way to switch up your look without committing to a haircut or hair color change. Synthetic wigs, in particular, are a popular choice because they're affordable and easy to maintain. However, over time, your synthetic curly wig can lose its luster and bounce, leaving you with a lackluster hairstyle. That's why we're here to help you revive your synthetic curly wig in just a few easy steps.

Description

Synthetic wigs are a popular choice for those who want to change their hairstyle without damaging their natural hair. Curly wigs, in particular, offer a fun and playful look that can be styled in many ways. However, synthetic wigs tend to lose their bounce and curl over time, leaving you with a flat and lifeless wig. In this article, we'll show you how to revive your synthetic curly wig and bring it back to life.

How to

Here are the steps you need to follow to revive your synthetic curly wig:

Step 1: Detangle

Before you start the revival process, you need to detangle your wig. Use a wide-tooth comb or a wig brush to gently remove any knots or tangles. Be careful not to pull on the hair too hard, as this can damage the fibers.

Step 2: Soak

Fill a basin with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Place your wig in the water and gently swirl it around to distribute the shampoo. Let it soak for a few minutes.

Step 3: Rinse

Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water to remove any shampoo residue.

Step 4: Condition

Apply a small amount of wig conditioner to the hair and work it in gently with your fingers. Let it sit for a few minutes, then rinse it out with cool water.

Step 5: Dry

Gently squeeze out any excess water from your wig and place it on a towel. Roll the towel up and press gently to remove any remaining water. Do not rub the hair or twist it, as this can cause damage.

Once you've removed most of the water, place your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to air dry. Do not use a blow dryer or any heat styling tools, as this can damage the fibers.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you revive your synthetic curly wig:

  • Use a wig stand or mannequin head to help your wig maintain its shape as it dries
  • Avoid using hot water or harsh chemicals on your wig, as this can damage the fibers
  • Don't brush your wig when it's wet, as this can cause damage
  • Use a wig cap to keep your natural hair from getting tangled in the wig
  • Store your wig in a cool, dry place to prevent damage

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about reviving synthetic curly wigs:

Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my wig?

No, regular shampoo and conditioner can be too harsh on synthetic wig fibers. Use a wig-specific shampoo and conditioner to ensure that your wig stays in good condition.

How often should I wash my wig?

It depends on how often you wear it and how much styling product you use. Generally, you should wash your wig every 6-8 wears, or when it starts to look dull or dirty.

Can I use heat styling tools on my synthetic wig?

No, heat styling tools can cause damage to synthetic wig fibers. Stick to cool styling tools, like rollers or flexi rods, if you want to add some curl or volume to your wig.

Hair Care

Taking care of your synthetic curly wig is important if you want it to last. Here are some hair care tips to keep your wig looking its best:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help it maintain its shape
  • Use a wig brush or wide-tooth comb to detangle your wig
  • Avoid using hot water or harsh chemicals on your wig
  • Wash your wig every 6-8 wears, or when it starts to look dull or dirty
  • Air dry your wig, and avoid using heat styling tools

With these tips and tricks, you can easily revive your synthetic curly wig and keep it looking great for longer.
