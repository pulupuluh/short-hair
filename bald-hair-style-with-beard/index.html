Bald Hair Style With Beard A Perfect Look For Men In 2023

If you are looking for a trendy and stylish look for 2023, then bald hair style with beard is the perfect option for you. This style has become popular among men in recent years as it gives a bold and masculine appearance. In this article, we will discuss in detail about this style, how to achieve it, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Content

Description

Bald hair style with beard is a popular look where men shave their head completely bald and keep a beard. This style gives a bold and masculine appearance, and it is suitable for men with a good head shape. It is a low-maintenance style that requires minimal effort to maintain.

How to Achieve Bald Hair Style with Beard

To achieve bald hair style with beard, you need to follow the below steps:

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect bald hair style with beard:

  1. Trim your beard to the desired length.
  2. Shave your head completely bald using a razor or clippers.
  3. Apply shaving cream or gel to your scalp before shaving.
  4. Use a sharp razor to shave your head in the direction of hair growth.
  5. Rinse your scalp with cold water and pat it dry with a towel.
  6. Apply aftershave lotion or balm to soothe your scalp.
  7. Apply beard oil or balm to your beard to keep it soft and shiny.

Tips for Maintaining Bald Hair Style with Beard

Here are some tips to maintain your bald hair style with beard:

  • Use a moisturizer to keep your scalp hydrated.
  • Apply sunscreen to your scalp when going out in the sun.
  • Trim your beard regularly to maintain the desired length.
  • Clean your beard regularly to avoid any infections.
  • Use a good quality razor to shave your head.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about bald hair style with beard:

  • Q. Is bald hair style with beard suitable for all face shapes?
  • A. No, bald hair style with beard is suitable for men with a good head shape.
  • Q. How often do I need to shave my head to maintain this style?
  • A. You need to shave your head every two to three days to maintain this style.
  • Q. Can I use a hair clipper to shave my head?
  • A. Yes, you can use a hair clipper to shave your head, but it may not give you a smooth finish like a razor.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for hair care when you have bald hair style with beard:

  • Apply moisturizer to your scalp to keep it hydrated.
  • Use a mild shampoo to clean your scalp and beard.
  • Apply conditioner to your beard to keep it soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot water on your scalp as it can dry out your skin.
  • Use a soft towel to pat dry your scalp and beard.

So, there you have it, everything you need to know about bald hair style with beard. It is a stylish and easy-to-maintain look that can give you a bold and masculine appearance. Follow the tips and tricks mentioned in this article to achieve the perfect look for 2023.
