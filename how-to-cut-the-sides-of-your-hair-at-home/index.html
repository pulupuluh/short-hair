How To Cut The Sides Of Your Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Feb 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to save some money and time by cutting your own hair at home, you may be wondering how to cut the sides of your hair without making a mess of it. Don't worry, with a few simple tips and tricks, you can achieve a salon-worthy look from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll show you how to cut the sides of your hair at home, step-by-step, along with some helpful tips and FAQs.

How to Cut the Sides of Your Hair at Home

First and foremost, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to help keep your hair damp while you cut it.

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Start by dampening your hair with the spray bottle. This will help make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an even cut.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to part your hair down the middle, dividing it into two sections. Then, comb the hair on one side straight down so that it's hanging over your ear.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Using your scissors, start cutting your hair in a straight line, following the natural curve of your head. Be sure to cut small sections at a time, and check your progress in the mirror frequently.

Repeat this process on the other side, making sure to keep the length and shape consistent with the first side.

Step 4: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting both sides, check your work in the mirror. If you notice any uneven spots, use your scissors to trim them until you're happy with the result.

Tips for Cutting the Sides of Your Hair at Home

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting the sides of your hair at home:

  • Never cut your hair when it's dry, as this can result in an uneven cut.
  • Start by cutting a little bit at a time, as it's always easier to cut more off than to try to fix a mistake.
  • If you have trouble cutting the back of your head, ask a friend or family member to help you.
  • Consider investing in some hair clippers if you plan on cutting your hair at home regularly.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut your curly hair at home, but it's important to be extra careful when doing so. Curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries, so be sure to cut it a little longer than you actually want it to be.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always visit a professional hairdresser to fix it, or wait for it to grow out.

Hair Care

Now that you've cut the sides of your hair at home, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and avoid using too much heat or chemicals on it.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a salon-worthy look from the comfort of your own home. Happy cutting!
