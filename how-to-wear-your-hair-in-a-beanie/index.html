How To Wear Your Hair In A Beanie Keep Your Head Warm And Stylish

Written by Robby May 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Winter is here, and with it comes the need to keep warm. Beanies are a popular accessory to wear during this season, but figuring out how to wear your hair in a beanie can be a bit tricky. Fortunately, we've got you covered with these step-by-step instructions and tips on how to wear your hair in a beanie while still looking stylish.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Your Hair in a Beanie
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Beanie
  • FAQs

How to Wear Your Hair in a Beanie

Before we get into the step-by-step guide, let's go over the different ways you can wear your hair in a beanie.

Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is a classic and simple way to wear your hair in a beanie. Simply gather your hair at the nape of your neck and tie it with an elastic band. Then, put on your beanie, making sure to cover your ears.

Messy Bun

A messy bun is another easy option for wearing your hair in a beanie. To create a messy bun, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it around itself until it forms a bun shape. Secure with bobby pins or another elastic band. Then, put on your beanie.

Braids

If you have long hair, you can also try braiding it and tucking it under your beanie. This will keep your hair out of your face and add some texture to your look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the different ways you can wear your hair in a beanie, let's go over the step-by-step guide.

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

It's important to start with clean, dry hair when wearing a beanie. This will prevent your hair from getting tangled and knotted under the beanie.

Step 2: Choose your hairstyle.

Decide which hairstyle you want to wear under your beanie. You can choose from a low ponytail, messy bun, braids, or any other style that you prefer.

Step 3: Put on your beanie.

Place the beanie on your head, making sure to cover your ears. If you have a slouchy beanie, you can pull it back slightly to create a more relaxed look.

Step 4: Adjust your hair.

Adjust your hair so that it sits comfortably under the beanie. If you're wearing a low ponytail, make sure it's not too high on your head. If you're wearing a messy bun, make sure it's not too tight.

Step 5: Style your bangs.

If you have bangs, you can either leave them out or tuck them under the beanie. Tucking them under the beanie will keep them out of your face and create a more streamlined look.

Tips for Wearing a Beanie

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing a beanie:

  • Choose a beanie that fits your head comfortably. It shouldn't be too tight or too loose.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to see which one you like best under a beanie.
  • Don't be afraid to accessorize your beanie with a scarf or gloves.
  • Wash your beanie regularly to keep it clean and fresh.

FAQs

Can I wear a beanie if I have short hair?

Absolutely! Short hair can look great under a beanie. You can try tucking your hair behind your ears or wearing a headband to keep it in place.

What should I do if my hair gets static under a beanie?

To prevent static, try using a leave-in conditioner or anti-static spray. You can also rub a dryer sheet over your hair to tame any flyaways.

Hair Care Tips

Wearing a beanie can sometimes cause your hair to become dry and brittle. To keep your hair healthy, try these tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Limit your use of hair products like hairspray or gel, as they can dry out your hair.

Now that you know how to wear your hair in a beanie, you can stay warm and stylish all winter long. Whether you prefer a low ponytail or a messy bun, there's a beanie hairstyle that's perfect for you.
