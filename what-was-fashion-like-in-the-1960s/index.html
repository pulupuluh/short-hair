What Was Fashion Like In The 1960S

Written by Daniel Mar 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

The 1960s were a decade of social and cultural revolution, and fashion played a significant role in this cultural shift. Fashion in the 1960s was characterized by bold colors, new fabrics, and daring designs that reflected the changing attitudes of the time. In this blog post, we will explore what fashion was like in the 1960s and how it evolved over the decade.

Description

The 1960s was a time of change and experimentation, and this was reflected in the fashion trends of the time. The decade began with a continuation of the conservative styles of the 1950s, but as the decade progressed, the fashion became more daring and unconventional.

How to

If you want to recreate the fashion of the 1960s, there are a few key elements that you should incorporate into your wardrobe. These include bright colors, bold prints, mini skirts, and go-go boots. You can also experiment with new fabrics like PVC and metallics, which were popular in the 1960s.

Step by Step

1. Start by incorporating bright colors into your wardrobe. The 1960s were all about bold colors like pink, orange, and yellow.
2. Embrace bold prints like polka dots, stripes, and paisley.
3. Invest in a mini skirt. This was one of the defining fashion trends of the 1960s.
4. Don't be afraid to experiment with new fabrics like PVC and metallics.
5. Finish off your look with a pair of go-go boots, which were a staple of 1960s fashion.

Tips

If you're not ready to go all-in on the 1960s fashion trend, you can still incorporate elements of the decade into your wardrobe. For example, you could wear a bright-colored shirt with a pair of jeans, or accessorize with a paisley scarf or headband.

FAQ

What were some popular hairstyles in the 1960s?

Popular hairstyles in the 1960s included the beehive, the bouffant, and the pixie cut. These hairstyles were characterized by volume and height, and were often achieved using hairspray and backcombing.

What were some popular accessories in the 1960s?

Popular accessories in the 1960s included headbands, scarves, and oversized sunglasses. Jewelry was also a big part of 1960s fashion, with statement pieces like large hoop earrings and chunky bracelets being popular.

Hair Care

If you want to recreate a 1960s hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. You can also use a volumizing mousse or hairspray to add volume and hold to your hair.

Conclusion

Fashion in the 1960s was a reflection of the cultural revolution that was taking place at the time. It was bold, daring, and unconventional, and it paved the way for the fashion trends of the decades that followed. If you want to incorporate some 1960s fashion into your wardrobe, start by embracing bright colors, bold prints, and new fabrics, and finish off your look with a pair of go-go boots.
