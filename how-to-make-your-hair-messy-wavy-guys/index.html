How To Make Your Hair Messy Wavy Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Mar 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your hair looking flat and lifeless? Do you want to add some texture and volume to your locks? One of the best ways to achieve this is by creating messy, wavy hair. In this guide, we will show you how to make your hair messy wavy guys in a relaxed and easy-to-follow manner. We will cover everything from the tools you need to the best techniques to use. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Tools You Will Need
  • Preparing Your Hair
  • Wavy Hair Techniques
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Messy Wavy Hair

Tools You Will Need

Before we dive into the techniques for creating messy, wavy hair, let's talk about the tools you will need:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Flat iron or curling wand
  • Hairspray
  • Dry shampoo (optional)

Now that you have your tools ready, let's move on to preparing your hair for styling.

Preparing Your Hair

Before you start using any heat tools on your hair, it's important to protect it from damage. Here's how:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Dry your hair completely.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help prevent damage caused by the heat from your styling tools.
  3. Run a wide-tooth comb through your hair to detangle any knots or tangles.

Now that your hair is prepped and ready, let's move on to the techniques for creating messy, wavy hair.

Wavy Hair Techniques

There are several techniques you can use to create messy, wavy hair. Here are some of the best:

Flat Iron Waves

  1. Take a small section of hair and clamp your flat iron near the roots.
  2. Twist the flat iron away from your face while sliding it down the section of hair.
  3. Repeat this process on the rest of your hair, alternating the direction of the twists.
  4. Run your fingers through your hair to break up the waves and create a messy texture.
  5. Spray hairspray to hold the style.

Curling Wand Waves

  1. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around your curling wand.
  2. Hold the curling wand in place for a few seconds, then release the curl.
  3. Repeat this process on the rest of your hair, alternating the direction of the curls.
  4. Run your fingers through your hair to break up the curls and create a messy texture.
  5. Spray hairspray to hold the style.

Now that you know how to create messy, wavy hair, let's move on to some tips and tricks to help you perfect your style.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect messy, wavy hair:

  • Use a dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match techniques to create a unique style.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Use a hairspray with a flexible hold to keep your waves in place without making your hair feel stiff.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about creating messy, wavy hair:

Can I create messy, wavy hair without using heat tools?

Yes, you can create messy, wavy hair without using heat tools. One way to do this is by braiding your damp hair and leaving it overnight. When you take out the braids in the morning, you'll have natural-looking waves.

How often should I wash my hair if I want to create messy, wavy hair?

It's best to wash your hair every other day if you want to create messy, wavy hair. This will help maintain the natural oils in your hair and prevent it from becoming too dry and brittle.

Hair Care for Messy Wavy Hair

Messy, wavy hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day. This can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create the perfect messy, wavy hair in no time. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
