Ways To Wear Your Short Hair Up

Written by Daniel Aug 20, 2023 · 10 min read

Short hair is trendy and versatile, but sometimes you want to switch things up and wear your hair up. Whether you have a pixie cut or a bob, there are plenty of ways to create chic and effortless updos. In this article, we’ll show you some easy and stylish ways to wear your short hair up.

Table of Contents

  • Messy Bun
  • Twisted Updo
  • French Twist
  • Braided Crown
  • Half-Up Half-Down
  • Top Knot
  • Slicked Back
  • Voluminous Updo
  • Messy Chignon

Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic and effortless style that works great with short hair. To create this look, start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out some strands to create a messy effect. This style is perfect for a casual day out or a lazy day at home.

Twisted Updo

The twisted updo is a chic and elegant style that’s perfect for a formal occasion. To create this look, start by sectioning your hair into two parts. Twist each section towards the back of your head and secure them with bobby pins. Then, gather the remaining hair and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out some strands to create a messy effect. This style is perfect for a wedding, prom, or any other special occasion.

French Twist

The French twist is a timeless and sophisticated style that’s perfect for short hair. To create this look, start by gathering your hair at the nape of your neck. Twist the hair upwards and tuck it into the twist. Secure the twist with bobby pins and pull out some strands to create a messy effect. This style is perfect for a job interview, a business meeting, or any other formal event.

Braided Crown

The braided crown is a romantic and bohemian style that’s perfect for short hair. To create this look, start by parting your hair in the middle. Then, French braid each side towards the back of your head. Secure the braids with bobby pins and tuck the ends under the braids to create a crown. This style is perfect for a music festival, a beach day, or any other outdoor event.

Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down style is a cute and playful look that works great with short hair. To create this look, start by sectioning the top half of your hair. Pull the top section back and secure it with a clip or a hair tie. Leave the bottom half down and add some waves or curls for extra volume. This style is perfect for a date night, a brunch with friends, or any other casual event.

Top Knot

The top knot is a trendy and stylish look that’s perfect for short hair. To create this look, start by pulling your hair up into a high ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a knot. Secure the knot with bobby pins and pull out some strands to create a messy effect. This style is perfect for a night out, a concert, or any other fun event.

Slicked Back

The slicked back style is a sleek and edgy look that’s perfect for short hair. To create this look, start by applying some hair gel or pomade to your hair. Use a comb to slick your hair back and create a neat and polished effect. This style is perfect for a formal event, a night out, or any other occasion where you want to make a statement.

Voluminous Updo

The voluminous updo is a glamorous and elegant look that’s perfect for short hair. To create this look, start by adding some volumizing mousse or spray to your hair. Then, tease the crown of your hair to create some volume. Pull your hair back into a low bun and secure it with bobby pins. This style is perfect for a wedding, a gala, or any other formal event.

Messy Chignon

The messy chignon is a romantic and effortless look that’s perfect for short hair. To create this look, start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail. Divide the ponytail into two sections and twist each section. Then, wrap the twisted sections around the base of the ponytail to create a chignon. Secure the chignon with bobby pins and pull out some strands to create a messy effect. This style is perfect for a date night, a wedding, or any other romantic event.

Hair Care Tips

Wearing your short hair up can be a great way to switch up your look, but it’s important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

FAQ

What if my hair is too short to wear up?

If your hair is too short to wear up, try adding some hair extensions or accessories to create the illusion of longer hair. You can also experiment with different haircuts and styles to find what works best for you.

Can I wear my short hair up every day?

While wearing your hair up occasionally is fine, it’s important to give your hair a break and wear it down sometimes to prevent breakage and damage. You can also experiment with different styles and accessories to switch up your look.

What if my hair is too thick or too thin?

If your hair is too thick, try using a texturizing spray or powder to create some volume and texture. If your hair is too thin, try using a volumizing mousse or spray to add some body and thickness.

Wearing your short hair up can be a fun and versatile way to switch up your look. Whether you prefer a messy bun, a French twist, or a braided crown, there are plenty of styles to choose from. Just remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
