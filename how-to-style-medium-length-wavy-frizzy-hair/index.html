How To Style Medium Length Wavy Frizzy Hair

Written by Aprilia Feb 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Medium length wavy frizzy hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a beautiful, effortless look. In this article, we will be sharing how to style medium length wavy frizzy hair, step-by-step instructions, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

How to Style Medium Length Wavy Frizzy Hair

The first step in styling medium length wavy frizzy hair is to start with clean, conditioned hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help tame frizz and hydrate your hair. After washing, gently towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream.

Next, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and part it in the desired direction. If you have a natural part, use it as a guide. If not, try a side part or a middle part to see what works best for your face shape.

Now it's time to style your hair. There are many options for styling medium length wavy frizzy hair, including:

  • Beachy waves
  • Braids
  • Buns
  • Ponytails
  • Half-up half-down styles

Choose a style that you feel comfortable with and that complements your face shape and outfit. To enhance your natural waves, use a curling iron or a curling wand to create loose, beachy waves. Alternatively, you can braid your hair overnight to achieve a similar effect.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are step-by-step instructions for styling medium length wavy frizzy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Gently towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and part it in the desired direction.
  4. Choose a style that complements your face shape and outfit.
  5. Enhance your natural waves with a curling iron or a curling wand.
  6. Alternatively, braid your hair overnight to achieve loose, beachy waves.
  7. Secure your hair with bobby pins or hair ties if needed.
  8. Finish with a hairspray or a hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Wavy Frizzy Hair

Here are some tips for styling medium length wavy frizzy hair:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to tame frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling medium length wavy frizzy hair:

Q: What products should I use for styling medium length wavy frizzy hair?

A: Use a leave-in conditioner, a styling cream, and a hairspray or a hair oil to add shine and hold.

Q: Can I straighten my medium length wavy frizzy hair?

A: Yes, you can use a flat iron to straighten your hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Q: How often should I wash my medium length wavy frizzy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week.

Hair Care Advice for Medium Length Wavy Frizzy Hair

Here are some hair care tips for medium length wavy frizzy hair:

  • Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to tame frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair to prevent damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful, effortless look with your medium length wavy frizzy hair. Remember to take care of your hair by using quality products and avoiding heat damage, and you'll be on your way to healthy, gorgeous locks.
