How To Do Half Up Half Down Hairstyles With Weave A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 14, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Half up half down hairstyles are a popular choice for those who want to show off their hair while still keeping it out of their face. This style is perfect for any occasion whether it's a wedding, prom, or just a casual day out. If you're looking to add some length or volume to your hair, adding a weave can help achieve the look you desire. In this article, we'll show you how to do half up half down hairstyles with a weave.

Description

A half up half down hairstyle is a style that pulls back the top half of the hair and leaves the bottom half down. This style is perfect for those who want to show off their hair while still keeping it out of their face. Adding a weave can help add length or volume to your hair.

How to

Here's how you can achieve the half up half down hairstyle with a weave:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and free of any tangles. If you're adding a weave, make sure it's clean as well. You can wash your weave with shampoo and conditioner just like you would your natural hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, create a part in your hair from ear to ear. This will create two sections, the top section which will be pulled back and the bottom section which will be left down.

Step 3: Secure the Top Section

Take the top section of your hair and pull it back. Use a hair tie or bobby pins to secure it in place. You can leave some hair out in the front to frame your face.

Step 4: Add the Weave

If you're adding a weave, now is the time to add it. You can sew in the weave or use clip-ins. Make sure the weave blends in with your natural hair.

Step 5: Style the Bottom Section

Now that the top section is secured, you can style the bottom section. You can leave it straight, curl it, or add waves. Use a flat iron or curling iron to achieve the look you desire.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your hairstyle in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help keep any flyaways in place.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to comb through your hair to avoid any tangles.
  • If you're using clip-ins, make sure they're secure so they don't fall out throughout the day.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Be gentle when combing through your hair to avoid any breakage.

FAQ

Can I do a half up half down hairstyle with short hair?

Yes, you can still achieve a half up half down hairstyle with short hair. You may need to use bobby pins to secure the hair in place.

How long does it take to do a half up half down hairstyle with a weave?

It can take anywhere from 20 to 40 minutes depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

How often should I wash my weave?

You should wash your weave every 1-2 weeks to keep it clean and free of any buildup.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your natural hair and any weave you may have. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as it can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Moisturize your hair and scalp regularly to avoid dryness.

With these tips, you can achieve a beautiful half up half down hairstyle with a weave that will turn heads wherever you go!
