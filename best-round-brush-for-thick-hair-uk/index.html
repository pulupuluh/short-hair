Best Round Brush For Thick Hair Uk

Written by William Jul 11, 2023 Â· 9 min read

In this article, we will be discussing the best round brush for thick hair in the UK. We will cover everything from what to look for in a round brush to tips on how to use it. Whether you're a professional hairstylist or someone who loves to style their hair at home, this guide will help you choose the best round brush for your thick hair.

Description

Thick hair can be a challenge to style, especially if you're using the wrong tools. A round brush is an essential styling tool for anyone with thick hair as it can help you achieve a sleek and smooth finish. However, not all round brushes are created equal, and finding the right one for your hair type can be a daunting task. In this guide, we will help you find the best round brush for your thick hair.

How to Choose the Best Round Brush for Thick Hair UK

When choosing a round brush for thick hair, there are a few things to consider:

1. Bristle Type

The bristle type is an essential factor to consider when choosing a round brush for thick hair. For thick hair, a brush with natural boar bristles is the best as it can easily grip the hair and smooth it out. Synthetic bristles can also work, but they may not be as effective as natural bristles.

2. Size

The size of the round brush is also an important factor to consider. For thick hair, a larger brush will work best as it can easily grab larger sections of hair. A small round brush may not be effective as it will take longer to style your hair.

3. Shape

Round brushes come in various shapes, including cylindrical, vented, and concave. For thick hair, a cylindrical brush is the best as it can easily create volume and smooth out the hair. A vented brush can also work, but it may not be as effective as a cylindrical brush.

Step by Step Guide on How to Use a Round Brush for Thick Hair UK

Here's a step by step guide on how to use a round brush for thick hair:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start styling. You can also apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to style your hair.

3. Begin Styling

Start at the roots and wrap a section of hair around the round brush. Use a hair dryer to apply heat to the hair while you're brushing it with the round brush.

4. Move Down the Hair Shaft

Work your way down the hair shaft while still using the round brush and hair dryer. Repeat this process until you've styled all of your hair.

5. Finish with a Blast of Cold Air

Finish off with a blast of cold air from your hair dryer to set the style and add shine to your hair.

Tips for Using a Round Brush for Thick Hair UK

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when using a round brush for thick hair:

1. Use a Heat Protectant Spray

Always use a heat protectant spray before using a hair dryer or any heat styling tool to protect your hair from heat damage.

2. Don't Overheat Your Hair

Avoid overheating your hair as this can cause damage to your hair. Use a low to medium heat setting when using a hair dryer and round brush.

3. Use the Right Products

Use the right hair products to help you achieve the style you want. For example, if you want to add volume to your hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray.

FAQ

What is the Best Round Brush for Thick Hair UK?

The best round brush for thick hair in the UK is one with natural boar bristles, a larger size, and a cylindrical shape.

Can I Use a Round Brush on Wet Hair?

No, it's not recommended to use a round brush on wet hair. Wet hair is more susceptible to damage, and using a round brush on wet hair can cause breakage and split ends.

How Often Should I Clean My Round Brush?

You should clean your round brush at least once a month to remove any product buildup or hair.

Hair Care for Thick Hair UK

Thick hair requires a little extra care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for taking care of your thick hair:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Thick hair tends to be dryer than other hair types, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep it hydrated.

2. Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day or every three days.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.

4. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to protect your hair from damage.

By following these tips and using the best round brush for thick hair, you can achieve a sleek and smooth finish that will leave your hair looking its best.
