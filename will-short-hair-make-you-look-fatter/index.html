Will Short Hair Make You Look Fatter

Written by Daniel Mar 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're considering getting a short haircut, you might be wondering whether it will make you look fatter. The truth is, the answer to this question really depends on a few different factors. In this article, we'll explore those factors and give you some tips on how to make sure your short haircut looks great on you.

Description

Short haircuts have been popular for many years, and for good reason. They can be low-maintenance, stylish, and versatile. However, there's a common misconception that short haircuts will make you look fatter. While this might be true in some cases, it's not a hard and fast rule.

How To

If you're considering a short haircut, there are a few things you can do to make sure it looks great on you. First, consider your face shape. Some short haircuts are more flattering on certain face shapes than others. For example, if you have a round face, a pixie cut might not be the best choice for you. Instead, consider a bob or a lob.

Another important factor to consider is your hair texture. If you have thick hair, a short haircut can actually make you look slimmer. On the other hand, if you have fine hair, a short haircut might make your face look fuller. In this case, adding some layers to your haircut can help create the illusion of volume.

Step By Step

When getting a short haircut, it's important to communicate with your stylist. Let them know your concerns and what you're hoping to achieve with your new haircut. They can help guide you towards a style that will flatter your face shape and hair texture.

Once you have your new haircut, there are a few styling tips you can use to make sure it looks great. For example, adding some volume at the crown of your head can help elongate your face and make you look slimmer. Using a volumizing mousse or spray can help achieve this look.

Tips

If you're still unsure whether a short haircut will make you look fatter, there are a few other things you can do to create a slimming effect. For example, wearing clothing that fits well and has a structured silhouette can help create a streamlined look. Avoiding bulky or oversized clothing can also help.

Finally, it's important to remember that confidence is key. If you feel great about your new haircut, you'll look great too. So embrace your new look and rock it with confidence!

FAQ

Will a short haircut make me look fatter?

It depends on your face shape and hair texture. Some short haircuts can be very slimming, while others might not be as flattering. It's important to consider these factors when choosing a new haircut.

What short haircuts are the most slimming?

Short bobs and lobs can be very slimming, especially when they're styled with volume at the crown of the head. Pixie cuts can also be slimming, but they're not always the most flattering choice for every face shape.

Hair Care

Whatever haircut you choose, it's important to take good care of your hair. This means using high-quality shampoos and conditioners, avoiding heat styling as much as possible, and getting regular trims to keep split ends at bay.

With the right haircut and a little bit of self-confidence, you can rock a short hairstyle and look amazing. So don't be afraid to take the plunge and try something new!
