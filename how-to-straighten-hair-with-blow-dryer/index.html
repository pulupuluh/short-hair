How To Straighten Hair With Blow Dryer A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 09, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of using a flat iron to straighten your hair? Did you know that you can achieve the same results with a blow dryer? Straightening your hair with a blow dryer is a great alternative to using a flat iron, especially if you have thick, curly or frizzy hair. In this article, we will show you how to straighten your hair with a blow dryer in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Straightening Hair with Blow Dryer

How to Straighten Hair with Blow Dryer

Straightening your hair with a blow dryer is a quick and easy process. All you need is a good quality blow dryer, a brush, and some heat protectant spray. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and conditioned. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, and product buildup from your hair, making it easier to straighten.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant Spray

Before you start blow drying your hair, it's important to use a heat protectant spray. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy. Spray the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair, making sure to cover all sections.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to blow dry your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections. Start by dividing your hair into two sections, one at the top and one at the bottom.

Step 4: Start Blow Drying

Start blow drying your hair using a medium heat setting. Hold the blow dryer about 6 inches away from your hair and use a brush to comb through each section. Pull the brush through your hair in a downward motion, following the brush with the blow dryer. This will help to straighten your hair and remove any frizz.

Step 5: Repeat the Process

Repeat the process for each section of your hair, working your way from the bottom to the top. Use the brush to guide your hair in the direction you want it to go, and use the blow dryer to apply heat evenly.

Step 6: Finish with a Cool Shot

Once you have straightened your hair, finish with a cool shot of air from the blow dryer. This will help to set your style and make it last longer.

Tips for Straightening Hair with Blow Dryer

Straightening your hair with a blow dryer can be tricky, especially if you have thick, curly or frizzy hair. Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Use a good quality blow dryer with a concentrator nozzle for more precise styling.
  • Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start straightening.
  • Don't use too much heat, as this can damage your hair. Use a medium heat setting and work slowly to avoid overheating your hair.
  • Use a brush with natural bristles, as this will help to distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz.
  • Apply a smoothing serum to help reduce frizz and add shine to your hair.

FAQs

Can I use a blow dryer to straighten my hair?

Yes, you can use a blow dryer to straighten your hair. It's a great alternative to using a flat iron, especially if you have thick, curly or frizzy hair.

What is the best heat setting to use when straightening hair with a blow dryer?

It's best to use a medium heat setting when straightening your hair with a blow dryer. This will help to avoid overheating your hair and causing damage.

How do I protect my hair from heat damage when straightening with a blow dryer?

Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying your hair. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy.

Hair Care Tips

Straightening your hair with a blow dryer can be damaging, especially if you do it too often. Here are some tips to help keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Air dry your hair whenever possible to avoid using heat styling tools.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, as this will reduce breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep it healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve straight, shiny hair with a blow dryer. Remember to always use a heat protectant spray and work slowly to avoid damaging your hair. With practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect straightened look in no time!
