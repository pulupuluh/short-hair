How To Style Medium Wavy Hair With Bangs

Written by Petter Jun 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Medium wavy hair with bangs is a popular hairstyle that can be styled in many ways to suit different occasions. Whether you have natural waves or have created them with a curling iron, there are many ways to style your medium wavy hair with bangs. In this article, we will guide you on how to style your hair in a relaxed and effortless way.

Description

Medium wavy hair with bangs is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in various ways. The length of the hair falls between the shoulders and the collarbone, and the waves add volume and texture to the hair. The bangs can be blunt or side-swept, and they can be styled to frame the face or left to fall naturally. This hairstyle is suitable for all face shapes and can be styled to suit different occasions.

How to

To style your medium wavy hair with bangs, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray. This will protect your hair from damage caused by heat styling tools.

Step 2: Create waves

If you have naturally wavy hair, you can skip this step. If not, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Divide your hair into sections and wrap each section around the curling iron. Hold for a few seconds and release. Repeat until all sections of your hair are curled.

Step 3: Style your bangs

If you have blunt bangs, blow-dry them with a round brush to create volume. If you have side-swept bangs, use a flat iron to straighten them. You can also add a slight bend to your bangs by using a curling iron.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

Spray your hair with a medium-hold hairspray to set the waves and keep your hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your medium wavy hair with bangs:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Don't use too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add some extra style to your hair.
  • If you have frizzy hair, use a frizz control serum to tame the frizz.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling medium wavy hair with bangs:

Q: Can I style my hair without heat?

A: Yes, you can style your hair without heat by using braids or twists. Simply braid or twist your hair before going to bed and undo the braids or twists in the morning for natural waves.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day to prevent it from getting too dry or oily. If you have very oily hair, you can wash it every day.

Hair Care

To keep your medium wavy hair with bangs looking healthy and shiny, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to deep-condition your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your medium wavy hair with bangs in a relaxed and effortless way. Whether you're going to work or a party, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
