What Size Clipper Guard To Use

Written by Frank Sep 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to cutting your hair with clippers, choosing the right size clipper guard is crucial to achieving the desired haircut. The clipper guard is the attachment that fits over the blades of the clipper and determines the length of the hair that will be cut. In this article, we will discuss what size clipper guard to use for different hair lengths and styles.

How to Choose the Right Size Clipper Guard

Choosing the right size clipper guard depends on the length of the hair you want to cut. The clipper guards are numbered and each number represents the length of the hair in millimeters. For example, a #1 clipper guard will leave the hair 1/8 inch long, while a #8 clipper guard will leave the hair 1 inch long.

The best way to choose the right size clipper guard is to start with a longer guard and work your way down until you reach the desired length. It's always better to cut less hair at first and then go back and cut more if needed.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right size clipper guard:

  1. Decide on the length of the hair you want to cut.
  2. Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down until you reach the desired length.
  3. Cut the hair in the direction of growth, moving the clippers against the grain will leave the hair shorter than intended.
  4. Trim around the ears and neckline with a smaller clipper guard or without a guard to create a clean, finished look.
  5. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that were missed with the clippers.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting hair with clippers:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers in the direction of hair growth.
  • Don't press too hard with the clippers, as this can cause uneven cutting.
  • Use a smaller clipper guard or no guard to trim around the ears and neckline for a clean look.
  • Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that were missed with the clippers.
  • Clean the clippers after each use to prevent bacteria buildup.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right size clipper guard:

What size clipper guard should I use for a buzz cut?

A #1 or #2 clipper guard is typically used for a buzz cut, leaving the hair 1/8 to 1/4 inch long.

What size clipper guard should I use for a crew cut?

A #3 or #4 clipper guard is typically used for a crew cut, leaving the hair 3/8 to 1/2 inch long.

What size clipper guard should I use for a fade?

The size of the clipper guard used for a fade will depend on the length of the hair at the top of the head and the desired length at the sides and back. A #1 or #2 guard is typically used for a low fade, while a #3 or #4 guard is used for a high fade.

Hair Care After Cutting with Clippers

After cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and maintain the style. Here are some tips for hair care after cutting with clippers:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair soft and smooth.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the style and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heating tools such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage the hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Choosing the right size clipper guard is essential to achieving the perfect haircut. By following these tips and using the right size guard, you can create a variety of haircuts and styles at home with ease.
