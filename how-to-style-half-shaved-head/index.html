How To Style Half Shaved Head In Relaxed English Language

Written by Aprilia Aug 09, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you thinking of getting a half shaved head but unsure of how to style it? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to style your half shaved head. We’ll also provide you with some tips and frequently asked questions to ensure that you get the look you’re after.

How to Style Half Shaved Head

Styling a half shaved head can seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a breeze. Here are some styles to consider:

1. Side Part

A side part is a classic and easy way to style a half shaved head. Simply part your hair to the side and brush it over to one side. You can use a hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.

2. Braids

Another option for styling a half shaved head is to braid the longer section of your hair. You can create a French braid or a Dutch braid, or even a simple three-strand braid. This style can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion.

3. Top Knot

If you have longer hair on the non-shaved side, you can create a top knot. Simply gather all of your hair on the non-shaved side and tie it into a knot at the top of your head. You can also leave some hair down for a more relaxed look.

4. Half Up, Half Down

You can also style your half shaved head by pulling the longer section of your hair up into a half up, half down style. You can use a hair tie or clip to secure the hair in place.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Now that you have some ideas for how to style your half shaved head, here are step-by-step instructions to achieve each look:

Side Part

  1. Wash and dry your hair as usual.
  2. Part your hair to the side.
  3. Brush your hair over to one side.
  4. Apply a hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.

Braids

  1. Wash and dry your hair as usual.
  2. Part your hair to the side.
  3. Braid the longer section of your hair. You can create a French braid, Dutch braid, or three-strand braid.
  4. Secure the braid with a hair tie.

Top Knot

  1. Wash and dry your hair as usual.
  2. Gather all of your hair on the non-shaved side.
  3. Tie your hair into a knot at the top of your head.

Half Up, Half Down

  1. Wash and dry your hair as usual.
  2. Gather the longer section of your hair into a half up, half down style.
  3. Secure the hair with a hair tie or clip.

Tips for Styling Half Shaved Head

Here are some tips to help you style your half shaved head:

  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use a hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.
  • Invest in some hair accessories, such as hair ties, clips, and headbands.
  • Consider getting your hair trimmed regularly to maintain your style.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Will a half shaved head work for short hair?

Yes, a half shaved head can work for short hair. Talk to your hairstylist to find a style that works best for you.

2. Can I dye my hair if I have a half shaved head?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have a half shaved head. Just be sure to talk to your hairstylist beforehand to ensure that the dye won’t damage your hair.

3. Do I need to use a specific type of hair gel or pomade?

No, you don’t need to use a specific type of hair gel or pomade. However, it’s important to choose a product that works well with your hair type.

Hair Care Tips for Half Shaved Head

Here are some hair care tips to keep your half shaved head looking its best:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your style.

Now that you know how to style your half shaved head, it’s time to try out some new looks. With these tips and techniques, you’ll be able to rock your half shaved head with confidence.
