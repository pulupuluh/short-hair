Men s Hairstyles Long On Top Short On Sides A Relaxed Guide

Written by Petter Apr 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and trendy hairstyle, then men's hairstyles long on top short on sides may be the perfect look for you. This hairstyle has been around for decades, but it has become increasingly popular in recent years. In this guide, we will give you all the information you need to achieve this stylish look.

Description

Men's hairstyles long on top short on sides are characterized by leaving the hair on the top of the head longer than the hair on the sides and back. This creates a contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides. This style can be achieved with a variety of hair types, from straight to wavy to curly.

How To

To achieve this hairstyle, follow these simple steps:

  1. Decide on the length of the hair on top and the sides. The length of the hair on top can vary from a few inches to shoulder-length, while the hair on the sides can be buzzed, faded, or undercut.
  2. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to the longer hair on top. Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair into the desired shape.
  4. Use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back to the desired length.
  5. Finish by applying a small amount of styling product to the hair on the sides and back to keep it in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect men's hairstyles long on top short on sides:

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length of the hair on top and the sides. This will depend on your personal preferences and your hair type. If you have straight hair, you may want to keep the hair on top longer and the sides shorter. If you have wavy or curly hair, you may want to keep the hair on top shorter and the sides longer.

Step 2: Wash and Towel-Dry Your Hair

Wash your hair with shampoo and condition it. Towel-dry your hair until it is slightly damp. Avoid using a hairdryer, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to the hair on top. Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair into the desired shape. You may want to use a hairdryer to help set the style.

Step 4: Trim the Hair on the Sides

Use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back to the desired length. You may want to start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard for a gradual fade.

Step 5: Apply Styling Product to the Sides and Back

Finish by applying a small amount of styling product to the hair on the sides and back to keep it in place. Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair into the desired shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect men's hairstyles long on top short on sides:

  • Choose a styling product that works well with your hair type.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair on top into the desired shape.
  • Consider a tapered or faded cut for a more modern look.
  • Ask your barber or hairstylist for advice on the best length for your hair type and face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can men's hairstyles long on top short on sides work for all hair types?

A: Yes, men's hairstyles long on top short on sides can work for all hair types, from straight to wavy to curly.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut for this style?

A: This will depend on the length of the hair on top and the sides, but generally, you will need to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Q: What styling products work best for men's hairstyles long on top short on sides?

A: Pomade and wax are great options for this style, as they provide hold and texture without making the hair look greasy or weighed down.

Hair Care

To keep your men's hairstyles long on top short on sides looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you must use hot tools.
  • Visit your barber or hairstylist regularly to maintain the style.

Now that you have all the information you need, it's time to try out men's hairstyles long on top short on sides for yourself. With the right cut and styling products, you can achieve a trendy and versatile look that will suit any occasion.
