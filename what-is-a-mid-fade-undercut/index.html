What Is A Mid Fade Undercut

Written by Petter Mar 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to men's hairstyles, there are a lot of options to choose from. One of the most popular choices in recent years has been the mid fade undercut. This style is characterized by short sides that gradually blend into longer hair on top. The result is a sleek and modern look that can be tailored to suit any man's style.

Description

The mid fade undercut is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in a variety of ways. The key to achieving this look is to have the sides of your hair gradually fade from short to long. The length of the fade can vary depending on your preference, but typically it starts around the temple and fades down to the ear.

The undercut portion of the hairstyle refers to the longer hair on top. This can be styled in many different ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. The length of the hair on top can also vary, with some men opting for shorter hair and others choosing to grow it out longer.

How to Achieve a Mid Fade Undercut

If you're interested in trying out a mid fade undercut for yourself, there are a few steps you'll need to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Length of Your Fade

The first step in achieving a mid fade undercut is to decide how short you want the sides of your hair to be. You can choose a shorter fade for a more dramatic look, or opt for a longer fade for a more subtle effect.

Step 2: Cut the Sides of Your Hair

Once you've decided on the length of your fade, it's time to cut the sides of your hair. This can be done with clippers or scissors, depending on your preference. Be sure to blend the hair as you go to create a smooth transition from short to long.

Step 3: Cut the Top of Your Hair

Next, it's time to cut the hair on top of your head. The length of this hair can vary depending on your preference, but it should be long enough to create a noticeable contrast with the short sides. You can choose to style this hair in any way you like, whether that's slicked back, messy, or textured.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, it's time to style it. This can be done with pomade, wax, or gel, depending on your preference. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.

Tips for Maintaining a Mid Fade Undercut

Once you've achieved the perfect mid fade undercut, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are a few tips to help you keep your hair looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use high-quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

Is the mid fade undercut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the mid fade undercut can be worn by men with all hair types. However, the style may look slightly different depending on your hair's texture and thickness.

How often should I get a trim?

It's recommended that you get a trim every four to six weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Can I style my hair differently each day?

Yes, the mid fade undercut is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different products and styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Hair Care for Mid Fade Undercut

Maintaining a mid fade undercut requires proper hair care. Here are a few tips to help you keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your mid fade undercut looking great for weeks to come!
