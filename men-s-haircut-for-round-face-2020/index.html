Men s Haircut For Round Face 2020 A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Robby Feb 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right haircut can be challenging. But fear not! With the right style, you can accentuate your features and create a more flattering look. In this article, we'll explore the top men's haircuts for round faces in 2020.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Top Haircuts for Round Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Round Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for round faces, it's important to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Trace the outline of your face onto the mirror using a washable marker.
  3. Step back and take a look at the shape you've drawn.

If your face is widest at the cheekbones and has a round chin, you have a round face shape.

Top Haircuts for Round Faces

Now that you've determined your face shape, let's take a look at the best haircuts for round faces:

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic, low-maintenance hairstyle that's perfect for round faces. It's short all around and doesn't add any volume to the sides of your face, which can make your face appear slimmer.

The Side Part

The side part is a timeless style that works well for round faces. It adds height to the top of your head, which elongates your face and makes it appear less round.

The Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a modern take on the classic mohawk. It's shorter on the sides and longer on top, which creates the illusion of height and length in your face.

The Pompadour

The pompadour is a stylish and sophisticated haircut that works well for round faces. It's longer on top and shorter on the sides, which adds height and length to your face.

The Undercut

The undercut is a trendy and edgy haircut that can work well for round faces. It's short on the sides and longer on top, which creates volume and height.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you've chosen a haircut that works well for your round face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Invest in a good hairbrush or comb to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Consider using a hairdryer to add volume and shape to your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's haircuts for round faces:

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face depends on your personal style and hair type. However, some of the most flattering haircuts for round faces include the buzz cut, side part, faux hawk, pompadour, and undercut.

Should I get a short or long haircut for a round face?

A short haircut can help to elongate your face and make it appear less round. However, a longer haircut with layers can also be flattering for round faces. It's best to consult with your barber or stylist to find a style that works best for you.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair straighteners and curling irons, which can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

By following these tips and choosing a flattering haircut, you can create a stylish and confident look that showcases your best features.
