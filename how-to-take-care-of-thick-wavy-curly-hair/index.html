How To Take Care Of Thick Wavy Curly Hair

Written by Eveline Apr 27, 2023 · 6 min read

Thick wavy curly hair can be a challenge to manage, but with the right care and techniques, it can look stunning. In this article, we will discuss how to take care of thick wavy curly hair in relaxed English language. We’ll cover everything from how to wash and style your hair to the best products and tools to use. Follow these tips and tricks to keep your locks looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wash Thick Wavy Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thick Wavy Curly Hair
  • Tips for Managing Thick Wavy Curly Hair

  • Hair Care Products for Thick Wavy Curly Hair

How to Wash Thick Wavy Curly Hair

Washing your hair is the first step in taking care of your curls. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Wet your hair with warm water.
  2. Apply a sulfate-free shampoo to your scalp and massage it in with your fingertips.
  3. Rinse out the shampoo with warm water.
  4. Apply conditioner to the length of your hair, avoiding the scalp.
  5. Comb through your hair with a wide-toothed comb to distribute the conditioner.
  6. Leave the conditioner in for a few minutes, then rinse it out with cool water.
  7. Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water from your hair. Do not rub your hair with a towel, as this can cause frizz.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thick Wavy Curly Hair

Here’s how to style your thick wavy curly hair:

  1. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair to help detangle and moisturize your curls.
  2. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to gently comb through your hair.
  3. Apply a curl-defining cream or gel throughout your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.
  4. Scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp to encourage the curls to form.
  5. Diffuse your hair with a hair dryer on low heat, scrunching your curls upwards as you go.
  6. Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of hair oil to smooth down any frizz or flyaways.

Tips for Managing Thick Wavy Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you manage your thick wavy curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

FAQs

How often should I wash my thick wavy curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and how active you are. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you exercise regularly, you may need to wash it more often to remove sweat and dirt.

What’s the best way to dry my thick wavy curly hair?

The best way to dry your hair is to let it air dry, but if you’re short on time, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. To avoid frizz, don’t rub your hair with a towel.


Hair Care Products for Thick Wavy Curly Hair

Here are some hair care products that are best for thick wavy curly hair:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo
  • Conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Curl-defining cream or gel
  • Hair oil
  • Deep conditioner

By following these tips and using the right products, you can keep your thick wavy curly hair looking healthy and beautiful.
