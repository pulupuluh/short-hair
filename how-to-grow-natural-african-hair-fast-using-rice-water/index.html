How To Grow Natural African Hair Fast Using Rice Water

Written by Frank May 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to grow your natural African hair faster and healthier, then you may want to try using rice water. Rice water has been used for centuries in Asia as a natural hair treatment, and it's recently gained popularity in the West due to its many benefits. In this article, we'll show you how to grow natural African hair fast using rice water.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Rice water is the starchy water that is left over after soaking or boiling rice. It's rich in amino acids, vitamins, and minerals that are essential for healthy hair growth. The amino acids in rice water help to strengthen the hair shaft, while the vitamins and minerals nourish the hair follicles and promote new hair growth.

How to

To use rice water for hair growth, you'll need to follow these simple steps:

Step by Step

  1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and rinse it out thoroughly.
  2. Add 1 cup of rice to 2 cups of water and let it soak for 30 minutes.
  3. After 30 minutes, strain the rice and keep the water in a clean bowl.
  4. Apply the rice water to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Massage your scalp for 5 minutes to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth.
  6. Leave the rice water on your hair for 20-30 minutes.
  7. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  8. You can repeat this process once or twice a week for best results.

Tips

Here are some tips to get the most out of your rice water hair treatment:

  • Use organic rice to avoid any chemicals or preservatives that may be present in commercial rice.
  • Don't leave the rice water on your hair for more than 30 minutes as it may dry out your hair.
  • If you have oily hair, you can add a few drops of lemon juice to the rice water to help balance your scalp's pH levels.
  • If you have dry hair, you can add a few drops of olive oil or coconut oil to the rice water for added moisture.
  • Use a shower cap or wrap your hair in a towel to keep the rice water in place while it's on your hair.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about using rice water for hair growth:

  • Will rice water make my hair grow faster? Rice water contains amino acids, vitamins, and minerals that are essential for healthy hair growth. By using rice water regularly, you can nourish your hair follicles and promote new hair growth.
  • How often should I use rice water on my hair? You can use rice water on your hair once or twice a week for best results.
  • Can I use rice water on color-treated hair? Yes, you can use rice water on color-treated hair as it's a natural treatment that won't strip your hair of its color.

Hair Care

In addition to using rice water for hair growth, it's important to take care of your hair in other ways. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush as it can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to remove split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to nourish your hair from the inside out.

By following these tips and using rice water regularly, you can grow your natural African hair faster and healthier. Give it a try and see the difference it can make!
