Why Do Beards Make You Look Better

Written by Robby Apr 13, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you contemplating growing a beard? Or are you already rocking one but wondering why it makes you look better? Well, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll explore the reasons why beards make you look better and provide some tips on how to maintain them. Let's dive in!

The History of Beards

Beards have been around for centuries, and their popularity has ebbed and flowed over time. In ancient Greece, for example, beards were seen as a sign of wisdom and intellect. In the Middle Ages, beards were associated with knights and chivalry. During the Renaissance, beards were seen as a sign of wealth and power.

However, in the 20th century, beards fell out of fashion, and many men started shaving regularly. But in recent years, beards have made a comeback, and they're now more popular than ever.

Beards and Masculinity

One of the reasons why beards make you look better is that they're associated with masculinity. Men with beards are often seen as more rugged, confident, and powerful. This is because beards are a sign of testosterone, which is the hormone that makes men more masculine.

Studies have also shown that women find men with beards more attractive than men without beards. So if you want to up your attractiveness, growing a beard is a great place to start.

Beards and Personality

Beards can also say a lot about your personality. If you have a well-groomed beard, it can show that you're detail-oriented and take pride in your appearance. On the other hand, if you have a scruffy beard, it can show that you're laid-back and carefree.

Ultimately, your beard can say a lot about you, so it's important to make sure it's sending the right message.

Beards and Attractiveness

We've already mentioned that women find men with beards more attractive, but why is this the case? One theory is that beards make men look more mature and experienced, which is a trait that many women find attractive.

Beards can also make your jawline look more defined, which can make you look more attractive overall. And if you have a well-groomed beard, it can show that you take pride in your appearance, which is always a good thing.

How to Grow and Maintain a Beard

So now that you know why beards make you look better, you might be wondering how to grow and maintain one. Here are some tips:

  1. Start by letting your beard grow for a few weeks without trimming it.
  2. Invest in a good beard trimmer and use it to shape your beard as it grows.
  3. Wash your beard regularly with a beard-specific shampoo.
  4. Use beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and hydrated.

Tips for Maintaining a Beard

Here are some additional tips for maintaining a healthy and attractive beard:

  • Brush your beard regularly to distribute oils and prevent tangles.
  • Trim your beard regularly to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid over-washing your beard, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Be patient – it can take several weeks for your beard to grow in fully.

FAQs about Beards

Here are some frequently asked questions about beards:

Do beards make you look older?

Not necessarily. While beards can make you look more mature, they don't necessarily make you look older. It all depends on how you style and maintain your beard.

Do beards make you look more professional?

It depends on the industry you're in. In some industries, beards are seen as unprofessional, while in others, they're perfectly acceptable. If you're not sure, it's always best to err on the side of caution and keep your beard well-groomed and tidy.

Can anyone grow a beard?

Most men can grow a beard, but the rate of growth and thickness can vary from person to person. If you're having trouble growing a beard, be patient and give it time – it can take several weeks or even months for your beard to grow in fully.

Beard Hair Care

Just like the hair on your head, your beard needs proper care and attention to look its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your beard regularly with a beard-specific shampoo.
  • Use beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and hydrated.
  • Brush your beard regularly to distribute oils and prevent tangles.
  • Avoid over-washing your beard, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Trim your beard regularly to maintain its shape.

So there you have it – the reasons why beards make you look better and some tips on how to grow and maintain one. Whether you're going for a full beard or a shorter, more trimmed look, remember to take care of your beard and make it your own.
