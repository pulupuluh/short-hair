How To Cut Men s Medium Long Hair With Scissors Hair Tutorial

Written by Christine Jul 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to give a haircut to a man with medium to long hair, you're in the right place. Cutting hair with scissors may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done with ease. In this hair tutorial, we'll guide you through the steps of cutting men's medium/long hair with scissors.

Description

This hair tutorial is for anyone who wants to learn how to give a haircut to a man with medium to long hair. We'll cover everything from the tools you'll need to the techniques you'll use to cut the hair. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced hairstylist, this tutorial will give you the confidence you need to cut men's medium/long hair with scissors.

How to

To cut men's medium/long hair with scissors, you'll need the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clipper
  • Haircut cape

Once you have your tools ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you can start cutting the hair, you need to wash and dry it. This will help you see the natural texture of the hair and make it easier to cut. Use a towel to dry the hair, or use a blow dryer if the hair is long and thick.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Using a comb, section the hair into four parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Use clips to secure each section of the hair.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Starting with the sides, use a clipper to trim the hair to the desired length. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or to shape the hair around the ears.

Step 4: Cut the Back

Trim the hair in the back using scissors. Start at the nape of the neck and work your way up, using the comb to guide the hair as you go. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or to shape the hair around the neckline.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Starting at the front of the head, use scissors to trim the hair on top to the desired length. Use the comb to guide the hair as you go. Be sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides of the head.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Use scissors to blend the hair on the sides and the back with the hair on top. This will create a seamless transition between the different sections of the hair.

Step 7: Check for Unevenness

Check the hair for any unevenness. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or to shape the hair around the ears and neckline.

Step 8: Finish the Haircut

Use a clipper to trim any hair around the ears and neckline. Remove the haircut cape and use a blow dryer to style the hair as desired.

Tips

  • Start with longer hair and gradually cut more to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use the comb to guide the hair as you cut to ensure evenness.
  • Take your time and don't rush the haircut.

FAQ

Can I use scissors to cut men's medium/long hair?

Yes, scissors can be used to cut men's medium/long hair. However, it's important to use the right techniques and tools to ensure a successful haircut.

Do I need to use a clipper?

A clipper can be helpful for trimming the sides and back of the hair, but it's not necessary. Scissors can be used to trim the entire head of hair if desired.

Hair Care

After the haircut, it's important to take care of the hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for the hair type to keep it healthy and hydrated. Regular trims every 4-6 weeks can help maintain the shape of the haircut and keep the hair looking fresh.

With the right tools and techniques, cutting men's medium/long hair with scissors can be a simple and rewarding process. Follow these steps and tips, and you'll be able to give a great haircut every time.
