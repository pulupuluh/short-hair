How To Figure Out What Hair Color Is Best For You

Written by Eveline Aug 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering a hair color change but not sure what shade to go for? Choosing the right hair color can be daunting, but with a few tips and tricks, you can find the perfect shade that complements your skin tone and enhances your features. In this article, we will guide you through the process of discovering what hair color is best for you.

  2. How to
  3. Step by step

Description

Choosing the right hair color can be overwhelming, especially with endless options to choose from. However, the best way to figure out what hair color is best for you is by considering your skin tone and eye color. Your natural hair color and lifestyle also play a significant role in determining the perfect shade for you.

How to

The first step in figuring out what hair color is best for you is by determining your skin tone. There are three main categories of skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral. People with cool skin tones tend to have pink, red, or blue undertones. Warm skin tones have yellow, peach, or golden undertones, while neutral skin tones have a mix of both warm and cool undertones.

Once you have determined your skin tone, the next step is to consider your eye color. Your eye color can help you determine which hair color shades will enhance your features. For instance, if you have blue or green eyes, warm shades like honey blonde or copper can make your eyes pop. If you have brown eyes, you can experiment with various shades of brown, red, or blonde.

Step by step

Here are the steps to figure out what hair color is best for you:

  1. Determine your skin tone.
  2. Consider your eye color.
  3. Assess your natural hair color.
  4. Think about your lifestyle and how much maintenance you are willing to do.
  5. Consult with a professional hairstylist.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hair color for you:

  • Consider your skin tone and eye color when choosing a hair color shade.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different shades to find what works for you.
  • Be mindful of your lifestyle and how much time and money you are willing to invest in hair maintenance.
  • Consult with a professional hairstylist for their expert opinion.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about figuring out what hair color is best for you:

Q: Can I dye my hair a completely different color than my natural hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair any color you want, regardless of your natural hair color. However, keep in mind that drastic changes may require more maintenance and care.

Q: How do I know if a hair color will look good on me?

A: You can get an idea of how a hair color will look on you by using virtual try-on tools or trying on wigs in similar shades. However, the best way to determine if a hair color will look good on you is by consulting with a professional hairstylist.

Hair Care

After you have chosen the perfect hair color for you, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its color and health. Use color-safe shampoos and conditioners to prevent fading and dryness. Avoid using hot tools frequently and use a heat protectant spray when necessary. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Figuring out what hair color is best for you can be a fun and exciting process. Use these tips and tricks to find the perfect shade that enhances your features and suits your lifestyle.
