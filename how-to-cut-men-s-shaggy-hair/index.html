How To Cut Men s Shaggy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your shaggy hair and want to give yourself a fresh look? In this guide, we will teach you how to cut men's shaggy hair in a few simple steps. By following these instructions, you can have a sleek and stylish haircut that will make you look like a new man.

  • How to Cut Men's Shaggy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Men's Shaggy Hair

How to Cut Men's Shaggy Hair

Before starting the process, you should have the right tools. You will need a good pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. If you have a clipper, it will be helpful, but it is not necessary.

Now, let's get started with the process of cutting men's shaggy hair.

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. Make sure your hair is damp, but not dripping wet. This will make it easier to cut your hair and give you a more accurate result.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Using a comb, brush your hair to remove any tangles and make it easier to cut. You can also use your fingers to comb your hair if you don't have a comb.

Step 3: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want for your hair. You can use a picture as a reference or simply decide on a length that suits your face shape and lifestyle.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Now, it's time to start cutting. Use the scissors to cut the hair at the back of your head first, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Make sure you cut your hair in small sections, so that you can achieve an even result.

Once you have cut the back of your head, move on to the sides and the top. Be sure to cut your hair in layers to achieve a shaggy look.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After you have finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check your work. Make sure that your hair is evenly cut and that there are no uneven or longer strands left behind.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to cutting men's shaggy hair:

  1. Wet your hair
  2. Comb your hair
  3. Decide on the length
  4. Start cutting from the back of your head
  5. Cut in small sections
  6. Cut in layers to achieve a shaggy look
  7. Check your work using a mirror

Tips for Cutting Men's Shaggy Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting men's shaggy hair:

  • Start with small sections and work your way up
  • Cut in layers to achieve a shaggy look
  • Use a mirror to check your work
  • Don't cut too much hair at once
  • Be patient and take your time

FAQs

How often should I cut my shaggy hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how shaggy you want it to look. Generally, you should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking its best.

Can I use a clipper to cut my shaggy hair?

Yes, you can use a clipper to cut your shaggy hair. However, using scissors will give you a more precise result.

Do I need any special skills to cut my shaggy hair?

No, you don't need any special skills to cut your shaggy hair. Just follow the instructions in this guide, and you will be able to achieve a great result.

Hair Care After Cutting Men's Shaggy Hair

After cutting your shaggy hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. You can also use a hair serum or oil to add shine and moisture to your hair.

By following these tips and instructions, you can cut your shaggy hair and achieve a stylish and trendy look. Remember to be patient and take your time, and you will be able to achieve a great result.
