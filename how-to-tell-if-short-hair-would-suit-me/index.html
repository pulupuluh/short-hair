How to Tell If Short Hair Would Suit Me

Are you considering chopping off your locks and going for a short hairdo? Short hair can be a bold and fun change, but it’s important to know if it will suit your face shape and personal style. In this article, we will go over how to tell if short hair would suit you and provide tips on how to rock a short hairdo.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Consider Your Hair Type
  • Think About Your Lifestyle
  • Reflect on Your Personality
  • Consulting with a Hairstylist
  • Tips for a Successful Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

Determining Your Face Shape

Before deciding on a short haircut, it’s important to determine your face shape. Your face shape will help you choose a haircut that will flatter your features and bring out your best qualities. The four main face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart-shaped

Each face shape has different features that can be highlighted with the right haircut. For example, a round face can be elongated with a short haircut that has volume on top, while a square face can be softened with layers and a side-swept fringe.

Consider Your Hair Type

Another factor to consider when deciding on a short haircut is your hair type. Short haircuts can look drastically different depending on your hair texture and thickness. If you have thick, curly hair, a short haircut can be a great way to manage your hair and bring out your natural texture. However, if you have fine, straight hair, a short haircut can make your hair appear thinner and flatter.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Short hair can be a low-maintenance option for those with busy lifestyles. However, it’s important to consider how a short haircut will fit into your daily routine. If you’re used to tying your hair up in a ponytail, a short haircut may not be as practical. Additionally, if you work in a conservative office environment, you may want to choose a short haircut that can be styled in a professional manner.

Reflect on Your Personality

Your personal style and personality should also be taken into consideration when deciding on a short haircut. Short hair can be a bold and confident statement, but it’s important to choose a haircut that reflects your personal style. If you’re someone who likes to experiment with different styles and colors, a short haircut can be a great canvas for creativity.

Consulting with a Hairstylist

If you’re still unsure if a short haircut would suit you, it’s always best to consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can provide advice on which short haircuts would be most flattering for your face shape, hair type, and personal style. They can also give you an idea of what a short haircut would look like on you with a virtual haircut consultation.

Tips for a Successful Haircut

Once you’ve decided to go for a short haircut, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Bring pictures of short haircuts you like to your hairstylist
  • Be open to suggestions from your hairstylist
  • Choose a haircut that can be styled in different ways
  • Invest in quality hair products to maintain your short haircut

FAQs

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look bigger?

A: Depending on your face shape, a short haircut can actually make your face appear slimmer. A stylist can help you choose a haircut that flatters your features.

Q: Can short hair be styled in different ways?

A: Absolutely! Short hair can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to curly and voluminous.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires less maintenance than longer hair, but it’s still important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Invest in a good styling product, such as a texturizing spray or pomade
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and to maintain your haircut

Now that you know how to tell if short hair would suit you, it’s time to book that haircut appointment! Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, lifestyle, and personal style when choosing a short haircut. With the right haircut and proper hair care, you can rock a short hairdo with confidence.
