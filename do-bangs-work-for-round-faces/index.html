Do Bangs Work For Round Faces

Written by Christine Aug 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, you might be wondering whether bangs are a good choice for you or not. This is a common question that many people ask, and the answer is not always straightforward. In this article, we will explore the question of whether bangs work for round faces, and provide you with some tips and tricks for making the most of your new hairstyle.

Description

Bangs are a popular hairstyle that involves cutting the hair across the forehead. This style can be worn in a variety of ways, from short and choppy to long and side-swept. Bangs can be a great way to change up your look, but if you have a round face, you might be worried about whether they will suit you.

How to

If you're considering getting bangs and you have a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you should consider the shape of your face. Round faces are typically wider in the middle, with fuller cheeks and a softer chin. Bangs can help to balance out this shape by drawing attention to the eyes and forehead.

When choosing a style of bangs, it's important to consider the length and thickness of your hair. If you have thick hair, heavy bangs can be a great choice, as they will help to frame your face and make it appear slimmer. If your hair is fine, you might want to opt for lighter, wispy bangs that won't overwhelm your features.

Another factor to consider is the length of your bangs. Short bangs can be a good choice for round faces, as they will help to elongate the face and make it appear more oval. Longer bangs can also work well, particularly if they are swept to the side, as this will draw attention away from the width of the face.

Step by Step

If you're ready to take the plunge and get bangs, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best style of bangs for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Decide on the length and thickness of your bangs.
  3. Consider whether you want blunt or wispy bangs.
  4. Choose a length that will flatter your features.
  5. Ask your hairstylist to cut your bangs to your desired length and style.
  6. Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your bangs, sweeping them to the side or straight across your forehead.
  7. Use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz.
  8. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for making the most of your bangs:

  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles of bangs to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add body to your hair.
  • Keep your bangs trimmed regularly to avoid split ends and maintain their shape.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs for round faces:

Q: Will bangs make my face look rounder?

A: Bangs can help to balance out the shape of a round face, but it's important to choose the right style. Short, choppy bangs can make the face appear wider, while longer, side-swept bangs can help to elongate the face.

Q: Can I wear bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with curly hair, but it's important to choose a style that will work with your natural texture. Light, wispy bangs are a good choice for curly hair, as they won't weigh down your curls.

Q: How often do I need to trim my bangs?

A: It's a good idea to trim your bangs every four to six weeks to maintain their shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron or curling iron.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage.

In conclusion, bangs can be a great choice for round faces, as long as you choose the right style and take good care of your hair. By following the tips and tricks in this article, you can achieve a fresh, new look that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
