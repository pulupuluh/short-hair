Haircuts For Oval Face Shape Male Tips Styles And How To

Written by Daniel Mar 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of getting the same haircut every time you visit your stylist? Do you want to experiment with different hairstyles but don't know where to start? If you have an oval face shape, then you're in luck because you can pull off almost any haircut. In this article, we'll discuss the best haircuts for oval face shape male, along with tips on how to style and maintain them.

What is an Oval Face Shape?

Before we dive into the haircuts, let's define what an oval face shape is. An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that's slightly wider than the jawline, prominent cheekbones, and a chin that's slightly narrower than the forehead. It's considered the most versatile face shape because it can pull off almost any hairstyle.

How to Determine Your Face Shape?

If you're not sure what your face shape is, here's a simple way to determine it. Stand in front of a mirror and use a washable marker to trace the outline of your face on the mirror. Step back and look at the shape you've traced. If your face is longer than it is wide, with your forehead and jawline being roughly the same width, then you have an oval face shape.

Best Haircuts for Oval Face Shape Male

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are perfect for oval face shape male because they highlight the face's features. A buzz cut, crew cut, or a classic side-parted haircut are some of the best short haircuts for oval face shape male. These haircuts are easy to maintain and style, and they work well for both casual and formal occasions.

Medium Length Haircuts

If you want to keep some length but still want a low-maintenance haircut, then medium length haircuts are a great option. A textured crop, messy quiff, or a slicked back undercut are some of the best medium length haircuts for oval face shape male. These haircuts are versatile and can be styled in different ways to suit your preference.

Long Haircuts

Long haircuts can be tricky for oval face shape male because they can make the face appear longer. However, if you want to grow your hair out, there are still some haircuts that will work for you. A shoulder-length shag, messy bun, or a half-up half-down hairstyle are some of the best long haircuts for oval face shape male. These haircuts add volume to the sides of the face, making it appear less elongated.

How to Style Haircuts for Oval Face Shape Male

Once you've decided on a haircut, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some tips on how to style haircuts for oval face shape male:

  • Use a hair styling product that works for your hair type
  • Don't overuse hair products
  • Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go
  • Use a round brush to add volume to your hair
  • Experiment with different hairstyles

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shape Male

Maintaining healthy hair is just as important as getting a good haircut. Here are some hair care tips for oval face shape male:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape of your hair
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol

Frequently Asked Questions

What face shapes suit a buzz cut?

A buzz cut works well for oval, square, and heart-shaped faces.

Can oval face shape male pull off a fringe?

Yes, oval face shape male can pull off a fringe, but it's important to get the right length and style that suits your face shape.

What is the best hairstyle for oval face shape male with curly hair?

A short curly haircut or a medium-length curly haircut with layers are some of the best hairstyles for oval face shape male with curly hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut for your face shape can make a big difference in how you look and feel. If you have an oval face shape, then you have the freedom to experiment with different haircuts. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there's a haircut that will suit your face shape. Remember to style and maintain your hair properly to get the most out of your haircut.
