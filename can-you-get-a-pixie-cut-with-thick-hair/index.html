Can You Get A Pixie Cut With Thick Hair

Written by Christine May 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your thick hair getting in the way and weighing you down? Do you want a fresh, new look that is low-maintenance and trendy? Consider getting a pixie cut! But can you get a pixie cut with thick hair? The answer is yes! In this article, we will discuss how to achieve the perfect pixie cut with thick hair.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is typically cut above the ears and can be styled in a variety of ways. It is a low-maintenance option that is perfect for those who don't want to spend a lot of time styling their hair. Pixie cuts have been popular for decades and have been worn by celebrities such as Audrey Hepburn, Emma Watson, and Halle Berry.

How to

The first step in getting a pixie cut with thick hair is to find a skilled hairstylist who has experience working with thick hair. They will be able to help you determine the best length and style for your face shape and hair texture. Once you have found a stylist you trust, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Prep Your Hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add body and texture to your thick hair. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant before blow-drying your hair to add volume and reduce frizz.

Step 2: Cut Your Hair

Your hairstylist will cut your hair to the desired length and style. They may use thinning shears to help remove some of the bulk from your thick hair and add texture to the cut. Be sure to communicate with your stylist throughout the cutting process to ensure you are happy with the result.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, your stylist will style it to your liking. Pixie cuts can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, tousled, or with bangs. Your stylist may use a styling product such as pomade or wax to help hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your pixie cut with thick hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add body to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume and reduce frizz.
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

FAQ

Can I get a pixie cut with curly hair?

Yes, you can get a pixie cut with curly hair! However, it may require more maintenance and styling than straight hair. Be sure to find a stylist who has experience working with curly hair and can help you determine the best length and style for your hair texture.

Will a pixie cut make my hair look thinner?

No, a pixie cut will not make your hair look thinner. In fact, it can help add body and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and healthier.

How often do I need to get my pixie cut trimmed?

You should get your pixie cut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care

After getting a pixie cut with thick hair, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

In conclusion, getting a pixie cut with thick hair is definitely possible! Follow these tips and tricks to achieve the perfect look for you. Remember to communicate with your stylist throughout the process to ensure you are happy with the result.
