How To Style Short Bob Cut Hair Tips Tricks And Care

Written by Luffy Aug 19, 2023 · 9 min read

Short bob cut hair is one of the most versatile and timeless hairstyles for women. It’s easy to maintain, suits all face shapes, and can be styled in many different ways depending on your mood and occasion. Whether you’re going for a sleek and sophisticated look or a fun and edgy vibe, there’s a bob cut style that will suit you. In this article, we’ll show you how to style short bob cut hair, including tips, tricks and care.

How to Style Short Bob Cut Hair

There are many ways to style short bob cut hair, but here are some of our favorites:

1. Straight and Sleek

For a chic and sophisticated look, straighten your bob cut hair using a flat iron. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat. Then, section your hair into small parts and run the flat iron through each section, starting from the roots to the tips. Finish off with a shine serum to add gloss and smoothness to your hair.

2. Beachy Waves

If you want to go for a more casual and laid-back vibe, try beachy waves. Start by applying a texturizing spray to your damp hair, then use a curling wand to create loose waves. To make it look more effortless, use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair. Finish off with a sea salt spray to add texture and volume.

3. Messy Bun

For a quick and easy hairstyle, try a messy bun. Start by tying your hair into a low ponytail, then twist and wrap it around the base of the ponytail to create a bun. Secure it with bobby pins and use your fingers to loosen some strands for a messy look. You can also leave some strands out to frame your face.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style short bob cut hair:

1. Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.

2. Towel Dry

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel as this can cause frizz.

3. Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a mousse or gel, to help hold the style in place. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

4. Blow Dry

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape to your bob cut. Start from the roots, lifting your hair up and away from your scalp as you blow dry. Continue until your hair is completely dry.

5. Style

Choose your preferred styling method, such as straightening, curling, or creating a messy bun, and style your hair accordingly. Finish off with a hairspray or setting spray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Short Bob Cut Hair

Here are some tips and tricks for styling short bob cut hair:

1. Use Heat Protectant

When using heat styling tools, always use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Experiment with Products

Don’t be afraid to try different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.

3. Don’t Overdo It

Avoid over-styling your hair as this can cause damage and breakage. Give your hair a break and let it air dry naturally from time to time.

FAQs about Short Bob Cut Hair

Q: Is short bob cut hair suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, short bob cut hair is a versatile hairstyle that suits all face shapes.

Q: How often should I wash my short bob cut hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but generally, washing your hair every 2-3 days is recommended.

Q: Can I color my short bob cut hair?

A: Yes, you can color your short bob cut hair. However, make sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent the color from fading quickly.

Hair Care for Short Bob Cut Hair

Here are some tips on how to take care of your short bob cut hair:

1. Trim Regularly

Regular trims are important to maintain the shape and health of your bob cut hair. Aim to trim it every 6-8 weeks.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage and damage.

3. Protect from Heat

Use heat protectant sprays and avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

With these tips, tricks and care, you can style your short bob cut hair in many different ways and keep it healthy and beautiful. So go ahead and experiment with different styles and find what works best for you!
