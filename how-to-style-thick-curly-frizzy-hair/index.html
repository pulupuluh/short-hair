How To Style Thick Curly Frizzy Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Apr 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick, curly, and frizzy hair can be a challenge to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a beautiful and manageable look. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to style thick curly frizzy hair, as well as tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you maintain healthy and beautiful locks.

How to Style Thick Curly Frizzy Hair

For styling thick curly frizzy hair, you will need the right products and tools. Here are some things you will need:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Frizz control serum or cream
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Curl-defining cream or mousse
  • Hairdryer with diffuser attachment
  • Curling iron or wand

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to style your thick curly frizzy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to hydrate your hair and reduce frizz.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair to help detangle and moisturize your curls.
  3. Apply a frizz control serum or cream to your hair to help tame any flyaways and reduce frizz.
  4. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from damage caused by heat styling tools.
  5. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb through your hair to remove any tangles.
  6. Apply a curl-defining cream or mousse to your hair to help define your curls.
  7. Use a hairdryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair on low heat. Scrunch your hair with the diffuser to help enhance your curls.
  8. Use a curling iron or wand to touch up any areas that need more definition. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron or wand, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  9. Finish with a light hold hairspray to help set your style.

Tips for Styling Thick Curly Frizzy Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your thick curly frizzy hair more easily:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush, which can cause breakage and damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and dryness. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to help reduce frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Try to avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curl pattern.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling thick curly frizzy hair:

Q: How often should I wash my thick curly frizzy hair?

A: It is recommended to wash your thick curly frizzy hair once or twice a week, as washing too frequently can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.

Q: How can I reduce frizz in my thick curly hair?

A: To reduce frizz in your thick curly hair, you should use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, apply a leave-in conditioner, use a frizz control serum or cream, and avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.

Q: Can I straighten my thick curly frizzy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your thick curly frizzy hair, but it is important to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage and dryness.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Curly Frizzy Hair

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy and beautiful thick curly frizzy hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help hydrate and nourish your curls.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing to help detangle and moisturize your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your curls.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and cause frizz.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and manageable style for your thick curly frizzy hair. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and tools, and avoid using too much heat on your hair too often. With a little patience and practice, you can achieve the perfect style for your hair type.
