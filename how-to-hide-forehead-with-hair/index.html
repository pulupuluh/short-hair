How To Hide Forehead With Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Apr 13, 2023 · 6 min read

Do you have a high forehead and feel self-conscious about it? Don't worry, there are several ways to hide forehead with hair. In this blog post, we'll discuss different techniques and styles to make your forehead look smaller and more proportionate to your face. Let's get started!

How to Hide Forehead with Hair – Step by Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in hiding your forehead is choosing the right haircut. A side-swept bang or fringe can do wonders in covering your forehead. If you prefer longer hairstyles, try a layered cut that falls below your chin. Avoid blunt cuts or center parts as they tend to emphasize your forehead.

Step 2: Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories like headbands, scarves, and hats can help hide your forehead. A headband with a bow or knot can add style and cover your forehead at the same time. You can also use a scarf to create a boho-chic look or a winter hat to keep you warm and stylish.

Step 3: Try a High Ponytail

A high ponytail can pull your hair back and create an illusion of a smaller forehead. You can also tease your hair at the crown to add volume and make your face appear more proportionate. Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to avoid drawing attention to your forehead.

Step 4: Experiment with Hair Parting

Changing your hair parting can also make a difference in hiding your forehead. A side parting can create asymmetry and draw attention away from your forehead. You can also try a zigzag parting or a deep side parting to add interest to your hairstyle.

Tips for Hiding Forehead with Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when trying to hide your forehead with hair:

  • Use hair products like mousse or hairspray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that flatten your hair, as it can make your forehead more visible.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair and draw attention away from your forehead.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories until you find what works best for you.

FAQ – How to Hide Forehead with Hair

Q: Can I hide my forehead with a short haircut?

A: Yes, a short haircut can also hide your forehead. Try a pixie cut or a bob with bangs to add volume and cover your forehead.

Q: How long should my bangs be to hide my forehead?

A: Bangs that fall just below your eyebrows are ideal for hiding your forehead. You can also try longer bangs that fall to your cheekbones or jawline.

Q: Can I hide my forehead without bangs?

A: Yes, you can hide your forehead without bangs. Try a hairstyle with layers or volume at the crown to create an illusion of a smaller forehead.

Hair Care – How to Keep Your Hair Healthy

Keeping your hair healthy is essential for achieving the best hairstyle. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to moisturize and detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote hair growth.

Conclusion

There are several ways to hide your forehead with hair, from choosing the right haircut to using hair accessories. Experiment with different styles and techniques until you find what works best for you. Don't forget to keep your hair healthy with proper hair care!
