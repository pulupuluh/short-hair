What Hairstyles Are Trending For 2020

Written by Oliver Aug 09, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle you've been rocking for the past few years? It's time to switch things up! In 2020, there are plenty of hairstyles that are trending and will give you a fresh new look. Whether you have long hair, short hair, curly hair, or straight hair, there's a style out there for you. Let's dive in and explore what hairstyles are trending for 2020.

Description

2020 has been a year of change and transformation in many aspects of life, including hair trends. This year, we're seeing a lot of natural and effortless styles, as well as bold and daring looks. From blunt bobs to messy buns, there's a hairstyle for every occasion and personality. Whether you're looking to chop off your locks or add some color, this year's hair trends have got you covered.

How To

If you're looking to try out a new hairstyle in 2020, here's how to do it:

  1. Research different styles that appeal to you
  2. Consult with a hairstylist to see if the look will work with your hair type and face shape
  3. Book an appointment and get ready for a new look!

Remember, it's important to take care of your hair before and after any drastic changes. Make sure to use the right products and follow a proper hair care routine to keep your locks healthy and shiny.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of the trending hairstyles in 2020:

Blunt Bob

1. Start with clean, dry hair
2. Divide your hair into sections
3. Cut straight across at the desired length
4. Style with a flat iron or curling wand for a sleek or textured look

Messy Bun

1. Gather hair into a high ponytail
2. Twist the hair and wrap it around the base of the ponytail
3. Secure with bobby pins
4. Pull out some strands to create a messy, effortless look

Braids

1. Start with clean, dry hair
2. Decide on the type of braid you want to do (French, Dutch, fishtail, etc.)
3. Section off the hair
4. Begin braiding and secure with an elastic band

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying out a new hairstyle:

  • Consider your face shape and hair type
  • Choose a style that complements your personality and lifestyle
  • Bring pictures to your hairstylist for reference
  • Be open to trying something new

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about 2020 hair trends:

Q: What hair colors are trending in 2020?

A: Natural-looking colors such as honey blonde, caramel brown, and chestnut are trending this year. However, bold colors like blue, pink, and purple are also popular.

Q: Are bangs still in style?

A: Yes, bangs are still in style in 2020. Curtain bangs and choppy bangs are particularly trendy.

Q: What hair accessories are popular this year?

A: Scrunchies, headbands, and hair clips are all popular hair accessories in 2020. The bigger and bolder, the better!

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

With these tips and trends, you're ready to rock a new hairstyle in 2020. Whether you choose a bold new color or a natural-looking cut, make sure to take care of your locks for a healthy and beautiful look.
