The Best Hairstyle For Oval Face Indian Girl A Complete Guide

Are you an Indian girl with an oval face and struggling to find the right hairstyle? The good news is that you have a lot of options to choose from. An oval face is considered to be the most versatile face shape, and you can experiment with different hairstyles to find the best one that suits you. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyle for oval face Indian girl, how to style it, tips, and FAQs. Read on to find out more.

How To Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into finding the best hairstyle for oval face Indian girl, let's first determine if you have an oval face shape. An oval face is characterized by having a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, with prominent cheekbones that curve inward. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror, and pull your hair back. Then, use a washable marker to trace the outline of your face on the mirror. Once you have traced your face, step back, and look at the shape. If your face is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and jawline, then you have an oval face shape.

The Best Hairstyle For Oval Face Indian Girl

Now that you know you have an oval face, let's talk about the best hairstyle for you. An oval face shape can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, the most flattering hairstyles for oval face Indian girl are those that accentuate the face shape's natural balance and symmetry. Here are some of the best hairstyles for oval face Indian girl:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are one of the best hairstyles for oval face Indian girl. The layers frame the face and add volume to the hair, giving it a soft and feminine look.

2. Bob

A bob is another great hairstyle for oval face Indian girl. The length of the bob can vary, but it should always be chin-length or above. A bob frames the face and highlights the cheekbones, giving the face a more defined look.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for oval face Indian girl. They add dimension to the face and break up the forehead's length, giving the face a more balanced look.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and stylish hairstyle for oval face Indian girl. It is a short and cropped hairstyle that highlights the face's natural features, such as the cheekbones and jawline.

5. Beach Waves

Beach waves are a popular hairstyle for oval face Indian girl. It is a low-maintenance hairstyle that gives the hair a natural and effortless look. The waves frame the face and add volume to the hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Style Your Hair

Now that you know the best hairstyle for oval face Indian girl, here is a step-by-step guide to style your hair:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry your hair.

2. Apply a Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant to your hair to protect it from heat damage. Heat protectant will also help your hairstyle last longer.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. This will add volume to your hair and make it easier to style.

4. Style Your Hair

Style your hair according to the best hairstyle for oval face Indian girl that you have chosen. Use a curling iron or straightener to style your hair, depending on the hairstyle you choose.

5. Apply a Hair Spray

Finish off your hairstyle by applying a hair spray to hold it in place.

Tips To Maintain Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to maintain your hairstyle:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant before styling your hair with heat tools.

FAQs

1. Can oval face Indian girl pull off bangs?

Yes, oval face Indian girl can pull off bangs. However, it is important to choose the right type of bangs that suit your face shape. Side-swept bangs are the most flattering for oval face Indian girl.

2. What is the best haircut for oval face Indian girl with curly hair?

The best haircut for oval face Indian girl with curly hair is one that adds volume and texture to the hair. Layers are a great option for curly hair as they add definition to the curls.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

1. Use a Shampoo and Conditioner that Suits Your Hair Type

Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Avoid Using Heat Tools Too Often

Avoid using heat tools too often as they can damage your hair. If you must use heat tools, use a heat protectant to protect your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.

In conclusion, an oval face shape is the most versatile face shape, and oval face Indian girl can pull off almost any hairstyle. The best hairstyle for oval face Indian girl is one that accentuates the face shape's natural balance and symmetry. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best. Follow the step-by-step guide to style your hair, and use the tips to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
