Hairstyles For Oblong Faces Male A Complete Guide

Are you a man with an oblong face shape looking for the perfect hairstyle? Look no further! We have put together a comprehensive guide on the best hairstyles for oblong faces male. From short to long hair, we have got you covered. Read on for tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice to keep your locks looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step

Description

An oblong face shape is longer than it is wide, with a narrow jawline and forehead. The aim of any hairstyle for oblong faces male is to make the face appear shorter and wider. This can be achieved by adding volume to the sides of the head.

How To

When choosing a hairstyle for oblong faces male, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, avoid hairstyles that are too long or flat. Secondly, opt for styles that add volume to the sides of the head. Finally, consider adding a fringe or bangs to break up the length of the face.

Short Hair

For short hair, a textured crop or a side-parted fringe are great options. The textured crop adds volume to the sides of the head, while the side-parted fringe breaks up the length of the face. Both styles are low maintenance and easy to style.

Medium Hair

For medium hair, a layered cut with a side-swept fringe is a great option. The layers add volume to the sides of the head, while the side-swept fringe breaks up the length of the face. This style can be styled with a bit of product for a messy, textured look.

Long Hair

For long hair, a layered cut with a side-swept fringe is also a great option. The layers add volume to the sides of the head, while the side-swept fringe breaks up the length of the face. Long hair can also be styled into a messy bun or ponytail for a casual look.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step guides on how to style a few of our recommended hairstyles for oblong faces male:

Textured Crop

  1. Apply a small amount of styling product to damp hair.
  2. Blow dry hair with a round brush, lifting the hair at the roots to add volume.
  3. Use a texture paste to add definition to the hair.
  4. Finish with a matte hairspray.

Side-Swept Fringe

  1. Apply a small amount of styling product to damp hair.
  2. Blow dry hair with a round brush, sweeping the fringe to one side.
  3. Use a texture paste to add definition to the hair.
  4. Finish with a matte hairspray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Invest in a good quality styling product to help you achieve your desired look.
  • Consider visiting a professional hairstylist for a consultation on the best hairstyle for your face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for oblong faces male:

What is the best hairstyle for an oblong face?

The best hairstyle for an oblong face is one that adds volume to the sides of the head and breaks up the length of the face. Short, textured haircuts and side-swept fringes are great options.

Can I still have long hair with an oblong face?

Yes, you can still have long hair with an oblong face. The key is to add volume to the sides of the head with layers and a side-swept fringe.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is essential for achieving your desired hairstyle. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it over time.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.

By following these tips and using our recommended hairstyles for oblong faces male, you can achieve a stylish and flattering look that complements your face shape. Remember to invest in a good quality styling product and visit a professional hairstylist for a consultation if you are unsure which style to choose.
