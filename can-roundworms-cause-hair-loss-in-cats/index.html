Can Roundworms Cause Hair Loss In Cats

Written by Bardi Aug 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As a cat owner, you want to make sure that your feline friend is healthy and happy. One common concern among cat owners is hair loss, also known as alopecia. There are many reasons why a cat may experience hair loss, and one of them is roundworms. In this article, we will explore whether roundworms can cause hair loss in cats and what you can do to prevent it.

Table of Contents

  • What are roundworms?
  • Can roundworms cause hair loss in cats?
  • Other symptoms of roundworms in cats
  • How to prevent roundworms in cats
  • When to see a veterinarian
  • FAQs
  • Conclusion

What are roundworms?

Roundworms are a type of parasitic worm that can infect cats. They are typically found in the intestines and can grow up to several inches long. Roundworms are commonly spread through contact with infected feces or contaminated soil. Kittens are particularly susceptible to roundworms as they can be passed from their mother's milk.

Can roundworms cause hair loss in cats?

Yes, roundworms can cause hair loss in cats. When a cat is infected with roundworms, the worms can irritate the intestinal lining and cause inflammation. This inflammation can lead to poor absorption of nutrients, including those that are important for healthy hair growth. As a result, a cat may experience thinning or bald patches in their fur.

Other symptoms of roundworms in cats

In addition to hair loss, cats with roundworms may experience other symptoms such as:

  • Vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Weight loss
  • Swollen belly
  • Coughing
  • Lethargy

If you notice any of these symptoms in your cat, it's important to see a veterinarian as soon as possible.

How to prevent roundworms in cats

The best way to prevent roundworms in cats is to keep them on a regular deworming schedule. Your veterinarian can recommend a deworming medication that is safe and effective for your cat. In addition, it's important to practice good hygiene by cleaning your cat's litter box regularly and washing your hands thoroughly after handling your cat or their feces.

When to see a veterinarian

If you suspect that your cat may have roundworms, it's important to see a veterinarian. Your veterinarian can perform a fecal examination to confirm the presence of roundworms and recommend the appropriate treatment. Do not attempt to treat your cat with over-the-counter medications without consulting a veterinarian first.

FAQs

Can roundworms be passed from cats to humans?

Yes, roundworms can be passed from cats to humans. It's important to practice good hygiene, including washing your hands thoroughly after handling your cat or their feces, to reduce the risk of infection.

How often should I deworm my cat?

Your veterinarian can recommend a deworming schedule that is appropriate for your cat's age, lifestyle, and health status. In general, adult cats should be dewormed at least once a year.

Conclusion

Roundworms can be a serious health concern for cats, and can even cause hair loss. By keeping your cat on a regular deworming schedule and practicing good hygiene, you can help prevent the spread of roundworms and keep your feline friend healthy and happy.
