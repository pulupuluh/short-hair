How To Get Fluffy Tiktok Boy Hair In 2023

Written by Christine Aug 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a fan of TikTok, you've probably noticed the popularity of fluffy hair for boys. This style has taken the app by storm, with many users trying to replicate the look. If you're wondering how to get fluffy TikTok boy hair, you're in luck. In this article, we'll give you step-by-step instructions on how to achieve this trendy hairstyle. We'll also provide tips on how to maintain your hair and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

Description

The fluffy TikTok boy hair trend is all about achieving a voluminous, tousled look. This style is characterized by long, layered hair that's styled to add texture and volume. It's often paired with a middle part, and some users opt for subtle highlights or lowlights to add dimension to their hair.

How to Get Fluffy TikTok Boy Hair

Getting fluffy TikTok boy hair requires some effort, but it's not as complicated as you might think. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Grow Your Hair

The first step to achieving fluffy TikTok boy hair is to grow out your hair. You'll need at least 6-8 inches of length to achieve this style. If your hair is already long, you can skip this step.

Step 2: Get a Haircut

Once your hair has grown out, it's time to get a haircut. You'll want to ask your stylist for long layers to add volume to your hair. You can also ask for a feathered cut, which will add texture to your hair.

Step 3: Wash Your Hair

Before styling your hair, you'll need to wash it thoroughly. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. Rinse your hair with cold water to help seal the cuticle and add shine.

Step 4: Apply Styling Products

Next, apply styling products to your hair. Start with a volumizing mousse, which will add body and hold to your hair. You can also use a texturizing spray to add texture and definition to your hair.

Step 5: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help to enhance your natural waves and curls while adding volume to your hair. Use your hands to scrunch your hair as you dry it to create texture.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, it's time to style it. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls. You can also use a straightener to add subtle bends to your hair. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a messy, effortless look.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish your look with a light-hold hairspray. This will help to keep your hair in place while maintaining its natural movement.

Tips for Maintaining Your Fluffy TikTok Boy Hair

Now that you've achieved your desired look, it's important to maintain your hair to keep it healthy and voluminous. Here are some tips:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove product buildup.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

1. Can I achieve fluffy TikTok boy hair with short hair?

Unfortunately, you'll need at least 6-8 inches of length to achieve the fluffy TikTok boy hair look. If your hair is shorter than this, you can still try to add volume and texture to your hair with styling products and a blow dryer.

2. Do I need to use a specific type of shampoo or conditioner?

It's best to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. Look for products that are designed for your hair type, whether it's fine, thick, or curly.

3. How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every other day or every three days, depending on your hair type. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to use products that are tailored to your hair type and concerns. Here are some products that can help you achieve and maintain fluffy TikTok boy hair:

  • Volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Volumizing mousse
  • Texturizing spray
  • Diffuser attachment for your blow dryer
  • Curling iron or wand
  • Straightener
  • Hairspray with a light hold

With these tips and products, you'll be able to achieve fluffy TikTok boy hair in no time. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and voluminous. Happy styling!
