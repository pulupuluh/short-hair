Bangs For Round Faces 2020 A Guide To Flattering Your Face Shape

Written by Christine Aug 29, 2023 Â· 11 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering if bangs are a good option for you. The answer is yes! Bangs can be a great way to balance out your face shape and highlight your best features. In this guide, we'll explore the best bangs for round faces in 2020 and how to style them.

Description: What are Bangs for Round Faces?

Bangs for round faces are a type of fringe that is cut to complement the roundness of your face. These bangs can be short or long and can be styled in a variety of ways. The key is to choose a style that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features. Bangs can be a great way to add dimension and texture to your hair while also framing your face in a flattering way.

How to: Choosing the Right Bangs for Round Faces

Choosing the right bangs for your face shape can be a bit of a challenge, but with a few tips and tricks, you can find the perfect style for you. Here are some things to keep in mind when choosing bangs for round faces:

1. Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can have a big impact on what type of bangs will look best on you. If you have thick, coarse hair, you may want to opt for heavier bangs that will help to balance out your face shape. If you have fine hair, you may want to choose lighter, wispy bangs that will frame your face without overwhelming it.

2. Think About Your Face Shape

While round faces can be beautiful and symmetrical, they can also be a bit tricky to style. When choosing bangs for round faces, it's important to consider your face shape and what features you want to highlight. Soft, side-swept bangs can be a great way to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, while blunt bangs can help to create a more angular look.

3. Experiment with Length

The length of your bangs can also have a big impact on how they look with your face shape. Shorter bangs can be great for emphasizing your forehead and drawing attention to your eyes, while longer bangs can help to create a more dramatic, face-framing effect.

Step by Step: How to Style Bangs for Round Faces

Once you've chosen the perfect bangs for your round face, it's time to style them. Here's a simple step-by-step guide to styling your bangs:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you start styling your bangs, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help to ensure that your bangs stay in place and don't get weighed down by excess oil or product.

2. Use a Round Brush

To create volume and texture in your bangs, use a round brush to blow dry them. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to lift and separate your hair as you go.

3. Add Product for Hold

Once your bangs are dry, you can add a bit of product to help them stay in place. A light-hold hairspray or styling cream can be a great option for keeping your bangs looking fresh and polished all day long.

Tips: How to Care for Bangs for Round Faces

Now that you know how to choose and style bangs for round faces, it's important to take good care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for caring for your bangs:

1. Trim Your Bangs Regularly

To keep your bangs looking neat and polished, it's important to trim them regularly. Depending on the length and style of your bangs, you may need to trim them every few weeks or once a month.

2. Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a quality shampoo and conditioner can help to keep your hair healthy and strong, which is especially important if you have bangs. Look for products that are designed to nourish and protect your hair, without weighing it down or leaving it greasy.

3. Protect Your Bangs from Heat Damage

If you use heat tools to style your hair, it's important to protect your bangs from heat damage. Use a heat protectant spray or cream before using your flat iron or curling iron to help prevent breakage and split ends.

FAQ: Answers to Your Questions About Bangs for Round Faces

Here are some common questions about bangs for round faces:

Q: Can bangs make my face look slimmer?

A: Yes, the right type of bangs can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. Soft, side-swept bangs can be particularly flattering for round faces.

Q: What type of bangs should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for lighter, wispy bangs that will frame your face without overwhelming it.

Q: Can I wear my bangs to the side?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs can be a great option for round faces. Just make sure to keep them trimmed and styled so they don't get in your eyes.

Hair Care: How to Keep Your Bangs Looking Great

Keeping your bangs looking great requires a bit of extra care and attention. Here are some tips for taking care of your bangs:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When brushing your bangs, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle any knots or tangles. Avoid using a regular brush, which can pull and damage your hair.

2. Avoid Touching Your Bangs Too Much

Touching your bangs too much can cause them to become greasy and flat. Try to avoid touching your bangs throughout the day, and use a bit of dry shampoo to keep them looking fresh.

3. Schedule Regular Trims

To keep your bangs looking neat and polished, schedule regular trims with your hairstylist. This will help to prevent split ends and keep your bangs looking healthy and strong.

Now that you know everything there is to know about bangs for round faces in 2020, you're ready to choose the perfect style for you. Whether you go for short, blunt bangs or long, side-swept ones, the key is to choose a style that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features. With a bit of care and attention, your bangs will look great all year long.
