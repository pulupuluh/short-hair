How To Style Male Long Curly Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long curly hair can be a blessing or a curse, depending on how you style it. While some men prefer to keep their curls short, others embrace their natural texture and let their locks grow long. If you fall into the latter category, you may be wondering how to style your mane to look its best. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to perfecting your long curly hair look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Male Long Curly Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Long Curly Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

How to Style Male Long Curly Hair

The first step to styling long curly hair is to find the right cut. If you're growing out your hair, you'll want to visit a stylist who is experienced with curly hair. They can help you choose a cut that will work with your natural texture and face shape.

Once you have the right cut, it's time to start styling. Here are some tips to get you started:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Curly hair tends to be dry, so you'll want to avoid products that strip your hair of its natural oils.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your damp hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly.

Step 3: Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. Avoid using a regular hair dryer, as the heat can cause frizz and damage to your curls.

Step 4: Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to shape your curls. You can also use a pick comb to add volume to your roots.

Step 5: Finish with a light hold hairspray or styling cream to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Styling Long Curly Hair

Tip 1: Use a microfiber towel or cotton t-shirt to dry your hair. Regular towels can cause frizz and breakage.

Tip 2: Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. This can cause your curls to separate and lose their shape.

Tip 3: Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair. Some men prefer a heavier cream, while others prefer a lighter gel.

Tip 4: Don't be afraid to trim your hair regularly. This can help prevent split ends and keep your curls looking healthy.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my long curly hair?

A: While it's possible to straighten curly hair with a flat iron, it's not recommended for long-term use. Heat styling can cause damage to your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

Q: How often should I wash my long curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days, as curly hair tends to dry out more easily than straight hair.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

Tip 1: Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Apply a deep conditioning mask once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

Tip 3: Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling wands, as they can damage your hair.

Tip 4: Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your long curly hair with confidence. Remember to embrace your natural texture and have fun experimenting with different looks!
