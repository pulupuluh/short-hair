Men s Short Fade Haircuts 2020 A Complete Guide

Are you looking for a stylish and trendy haircut that will make you stand out in 2020? Look no further than the short fade haircut for men. This versatile haircut is perfect for any occasion, from casual to formal events. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about men's short fade haircuts in 2020, including how to get one, tips to maintain it, and more.

How to Get a Men's Short Fade Haircut

To get a men's short fade haircut, you'll need to visit a barber or hairstylist who specializes in men's haircuts. They will be able to give you a fade that is tailored to your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Before you go to the salon, it's a good idea to do some research on the different types of fades and decide which one you like best. Some popular options include the low fade, high fade, and skin fade.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Men's Short Fade Haircut

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a men's short fade haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Decide on the type of fade you want (low, high, or skin).
  3. Use clippers to buzz the sides and back of your head, starting with a longer guard and gradually decreasing the length.
  4. Blend the hair on the top of your head into the faded sides using scissors or clippers.
  5. Style your hair as desired using pomade or wax.

Tips for Maintaining a Men's Short Fade Haircut

To keep your men's short fade haircut looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

  • Visit the salon regularly (every 4-6 weeks) for touch-ups.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Style your hair using a pomade or wax that works well with your hair texture.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to keep your look fresh and exciting.

Frequently Asked Questions About Men's Short Fade Haircuts

Q: What is a short fade haircut?

A: A short fade haircut is a men's haircut that involves gradually decreasing the length of hair on the sides and back of the head, while leaving the hair on top longer.

Q: What is the difference between a low fade and a high fade?

A: A low fade starts just above the ears and gradually fades into the skin, while a high fade starts at the temples and fades into the skin, leaving more hair on top.

Q: How long does a men's short fade haircut last?

A: A men's short fade haircut usually lasts 4-6 weeks before it needs to be touched up.

Hair Care Tips for Men's Short Fade Haircuts

To keep your men's short fade haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools (such as straighteners or curling irons) on a daily basis, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to style your hair, as this will help prevent breakage.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and trendy men's short fade haircut in 2020 and beyond.
