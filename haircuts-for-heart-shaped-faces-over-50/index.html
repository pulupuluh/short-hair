Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces Over 50 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Apr 09, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face and are over 50, you might be wondering what hairstyles would suit you best. Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll take you through some of the best haircuts for heart-shaped faces over 50, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Basics

Description

Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. For women over 50 with heart-shaped faces, the best haircuts are ones that balance out the face shape, draw attention to the eyes, and create softness around the jawline. Here are some of the best haircuts for heart-shaped faces over 50:

1. Bob Haircut

A classic bob cut is perfect for heart-shaped faces over 50. The length of the bob can be chin-length or longer, and it should be styled with soft layers that frame the face. This haircut will draw attention to the eyes and create softness around the jawline.

2. Shoulder-Length Haircut

A shoulder-length haircut with layers is another great option for heart-shaped faces over 50. This haircut will add volume to the hair and create a soft, feminine look. It's important to make sure that the layers are soft and not too choppy, as this can create a harsh look.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold choice for heart-shaped faces over 50, but it can also look amazing if done right. The key is to make sure that the cut is soft and not too severe. The hair should be styled with soft layers that frame the face and add volume to the hair.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a heart-shaped face over 50, it's important to consider the length, layers, and texture of the hair. Here are some tips:

1. Length

For heart-shaped faces over 50, it's best to avoid haircuts that are too short, as this can emphasize the narrowness of the chin. Instead, opt for haircuts that are chin-length or longer.

2. Layers

Layers are important for creating volume and softness around the face. However, it's important to make sure that the layers are soft and not too choppy, as this can create a harsh look.

3. Texture

Texture is important for adding volume and movement to the hair. Soft waves or curls can be a great way to add texture to a haircut for a heart-shaped face over 50.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Once you've chosen the right haircut for your heart-shaped face over 50, it's important to know how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for your hair type. For heart-shaped faces over 50, it's best to use products that add volume and softness to the hair.

2. Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product to your hair while it's still damp. This could be a mousse, volumizing spray, or styling cream. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

3. Blow Dry

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and movement. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends of your hair. Make sure to blow dry your hair in the direction that you want it to fall.

4. Style

Once your hair is dry, you can style it using a curling iron or flat iron. Soft waves or curls can be a great way to add texture to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair and keeping it looking healthy and beautiful:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to trim it regularly. This will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

2. Use the Right Products

Use products that are suitable for your hair type and that will help to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or that can damage your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

When using heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage. Avoid using these tools too often, as this can also damage your hair.

FAQs

1. What is the best haircut for a heart-shaped face over 50?

The best haircuts for heart-shaped faces over 50 are ones that balance out the face shape, draw attention to the eyes, and create softness around the jawline. Some great options include a bob haircut, shoulder-length haircut, and pixie cut.

2. How often should I trim my hair?

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to trim it every 6-8 weeks. This will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Hair Care Basics

Here are some basic tips for keeping your hair healthy and beautiful:

1. Wash and Condition

Wash and condition your hair regularly using products that are suitable for your hair type. This will help to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

When spending time in the sun, make sure to wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your hair from damage.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help to keep your hair looking healthy and strong.

With these tips and haircuts for heart-shaped faces over 50, you can feel confident and beautiful no matter your age!
