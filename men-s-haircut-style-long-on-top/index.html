Men s Haircut Style Long On Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and trendy haircut, then the men's haircut style long on top could be the perfect option for you. This style features longer hair on the top of the head, with shorter sides and back.

Description

The men's haircut style long on top is a popular trend that has been around for a few years now. It's a versatile cut that can be styled in many ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured. This style is perfect for men who want to keep their hair long but still want a clean and polished look.

How to Get the Men's Haircut Style Long on Top

Getting the men's haircut style long on top is relatively easy, but it's important to go to a stylist who is experienced in this type of cut. Here are the steps to get this style:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step is to decide how long you want your hair on top. This will depend on your personal preference and face shape. It's best to consult with your stylist to find the perfect length for you.

Step 2: Cut the Sides and Back

The next step is to cut the sides and back of your hair. This can be done with clippers or scissors, depending on the length you want. The sides and back should be shorter than the top to create contrast.

Step 3: Blend the Top and Sides

Once the sides and back are cut, your stylist will blend the top and sides together to create a seamless transition. This is where their expertise comes in handy, as they will know exactly how to blend the hair to create the perfect look.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Now that your haircut is complete, it's time to style your hair. You can use a variety of products, including pomade, wax, or gel, to create your desired look. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Styling Men's Haircut Style Long on Top

Here are some tips for styling your men's haircut style long on top:

  • Use a blow dryer to add volume to your hair
  • Apply product to damp hair for better control
  • Use a comb or your fingers to create texture
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best

FAQ About Men's Haircut Style Long on Top

Q: Can I wear a hat with this style?

A: Yes, you can wear a hat with this style. Just make sure to style your hair in a way that will not be affected by the hat, such as slicking it back.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain your style. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh.

Q: Can I dye my hair with this style?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair with this style. Just make sure to use a gentle dye and follow proper hair care techniques to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Men's Haircut Style Long on Top

Here are some tips for taking care of your men's haircut style long on top:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to prevent frizz
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

Overall, the men's haircut style long on top is a versatile and trendy option that can be styled in many ways. Whether you prefer a polished or messy look, this style can be customized to suit your personal taste.
