How To Cut Curly Hair To Make It Look Fuller

Written by Bardi Jun 20, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can look voluminous and beautiful, it can also be challenging to style and maintain. If you have curly hair and feel like it lacks volume, you may be wondering how to achieve the fuller, more voluminous look you desire. One solution is to cut your hair in a way that enhances its natural texture and shape. In this blog post, we'll share tips and tricks for cutting curly hair to make it look fuller.

Description

Curly hair can be beautiful, but it can also be difficult to style and maintain. If you feel like your curls lack volume, you may be wondering how to achieve a fuller look. Cutting your curly hair in a way that enhances its natural texture and shape can help you achieve the volume you desire.

How to Cut Curly Hair to Make it Look Fuller

Cutting curly hair to make it look fuller requires a bit of skill and technique. Here are some tips for achieving the desired look:

1. Consider layering

Layering is a great technique for adding volume to curly hair. Layers can help to remove weight from the hair, allowing the curls to bounce back and create more volume. When layering curly hair, it's important to work with the natural curl pattern and avoid creating choppy layers that can make the hair look thinner.

2. Opt for longer layers

If you have long curly hair, longer layers can help to add volume without sacrificing length. Longer layers can help to remove weight from the bottom of the hair, allowing the curls to bounce back and create more volume.

3. Try a curly cut

A curly cut is a specialized haircut designed specifically for curly hair. A curly cut can help to enhance the natural curl pattern and create more volume. This type of cut is typically done on dry hair to allow the stylist to see how the curls naturally fall.

4. Consider a DevaCut

A DevaCut is a type of curly cut that is done using a special technique. The stylist cuts the hair curl by curl to create a more defined shape and enhance the natural curl pattern. This type of cut can help to add volume and bounce to curly hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for cutting curly hair to make it look fuller:

Step 1: Start with dry hair

Cutting curly hair when it's wet can result in a different shape than when it's dry. Starting with dry hair allows the stylist to see how the curls naturally fall and create a shape that enhances the natural texture.

Step 2: Section the hair

Section the hair into manageable sections using clips or elastics. This will help you to see each section clearly and ensure that the cut is even.

Step 3: Cut the hair

Using your preferred cutting technique, cut the hair section by section. Work with the natural curl pattern and avoid creating choppy layers that can make the hair look thinner.

Step 4: Finish the cut

Once you've cut all of the sections, go back and make any necessary adjustments to create a more even shape. Finish the cut by trimming any flyaways or split ends.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair to Make it Look Fuller

Here are some additional tips for cutting curly hair to make it look fuller:

1. Work with a professional stylist

Cutting curly hair requires skill and technique. Working with a professional stylist who specializes in curly hair can help you achieve the desired look and avoid any mistakes that could damage your hair.

2. Avoid using thinning shears

Thinning shears can create choppy layers that make the hair look thinner. Instead, opt for a technique that removes weight from the hair without creating choppy layers.

3. Use the right products

Using the right products can help to enhance the natural curl pattern and create more volume. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair and avoid products that contain harsh chemicals that can damage the hair.

FAQ

Can cutting curly hair make it look thinner?

Cutting curly hair can make it look thinner if it's not done correctly. It's important to work with a professional stylist who understands how to cut curly hair in a way that enhances its natural texture and shape.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

The frequency of haircuts depends on the individual and their hair type. However, it's generally recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Maintaining healthy, voluminous curly hair requires proper hair care. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Curly hair is often dry, so it's important to use a gentle shampoo that won't strip the hair of its natural oils. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo that's specifically designed for curly hair.

2. Condition regularly

Conditioning is essential for maintaining healthy, hydrated curly hair. Look for a conditioner that's designed for curly hair and use it regularly to keep your curls looking their best.

3. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage curly hair and make it look thinner. Instead, opt for air-drying or use a diffuser to dry your hair gently.

4. Protect your hair at night

Sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and breakage. You can also wrap your hair in a silk or satin scarf to protect it while you sleep.

By following these tips and tricks, you can cut your curly hair to make it look fuller and achieve the voluminous, beautiful look you desire.
