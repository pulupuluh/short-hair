Are Face Framing Layers Good For Curly Hair

Written by Christine Apr 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be quite challenging to manage, especially when it comes to styling. One of the best ways to add some dimension and shape to your curly locks is by getting face-framing layers. But are face framing layers good for curly hair? In this article, we will explore this question and provide you with some tips and tricks on how to make the most of your curly hair with face framing layers.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to Get Face Framing Layers for Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Curly Hair with Face Framing Layers
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair with Face Framing Layers

Description

Face framing layers are a great way to add some definition and depth to your curly hair. These layers are cut around the face, creating a frame that draws attention to your best features. The layers can be long or short, depending on your preference, and can be styled in a variety of ways. Face framing layers can make your curly hair look more voluminous and add some movement and bounce to your curls.

How to Get Face Framing Layers for Curly Hair

If you're considering getting face framing layers for your curly hair, the first step is to find a hair stylist who specializes in curly hair. Curly hair can be tricky to cut, and you want to make sure that your stylist has experience working with curly hair. Once you've found a stylist, schedule a consultation to discuss the type of layers you want and how they will complement your face shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get face framing layers for your curly hair:

  1. Find a hair stylist who specializes in curly hair.
  2. Schedule a consultation to discuss the type of layers you want and how they will complement your face shape.
  3. Wash and detangle your hair before your appointment.
  4. Communicate with your stylist about the length and style of layers you want.
  5. Ask your stylist to cut the layers while your hair is dry to ensure that they fall correctly.
  6. Style your hair as desired once the cut is complete.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair with Face Framing Layers

Here are some tips for styling curly hair with face framing layers:

  • Use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls and add definition.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the best look for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about face framing layers for curly hair:

  • Will face framing layers work for all face shapes? Face framing layers can work for most face shapes, but it's important to consult with a stylist to ensure that the layers will complement your face shape.
  • How often do I need to get my face framing layers trimmed? It's recommended to get your layers trimmed every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and style.
  • Can I dye my hair with face framing layers? Yes, you can dye your hair with face framing layers, but it's important to use a hair dye that is safe for curly hair and to take extra care when styling your hair after dyeing.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair with Face Framing Layers

Here are some hair care tips for curly hair with face framing layers:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and moisturized.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

Overall, face framing layers can be a great way to enhance your curly hair and add some dimension and shape. With the right styling products and techniques, you can make the most of your curly hair with face framing layers. Remember to consult with a stylist who specializes in curly hair and to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant.
