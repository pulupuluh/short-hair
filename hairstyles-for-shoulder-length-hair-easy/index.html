Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair Easy Create A Stylish Look

Written by Aprilia Sep 15, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have shoulder-length hair, you may find it challenging to style your hair every day. However, there are plenty of hairstyles for shoulder length hair easy to try. In this article, we will explore some of the best and easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair. Whether you prefer a sleek and sophisticated look or a more relaxed and casual style, you are sure to find a hairstyle that suits your taste.

Description

Shoulder-length hair is one of the most versatile hair lengths. With the right style, you can create a sleek and sophisticated look or a more relaxed and casual style. The key is to find a hairstyle that suits your face shape and personal style. In this article, we will explore some of the best and easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair.

How to

Creating a stylish look with shoulder-length hair is easy. Here are some of the best hairstyles for shoulder length hair easy:

1. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a great way to add volume and texture to your shoulder-length hair. To create this look, start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair. Then, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Finish the look by scrunching your hair with your fingers and applying a texturizing spray.

2. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a quick and easy hairstyle that looks great on shoulder-length hair. To create this look, gather your hair into a loose ponytail at the nape of your neck. Then, twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face for a relaxed and casual look.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is a great way to show off your shoulder-length hair while keeping it out of your face. To create this look, start by parting your hair down the middle. Then, gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie. Leave the bottom half of your hair down and add some loose waves for a romantic look.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions for creating the beachy waves hairstyle:

Step 1: Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.

Before using a curling iron, it's essential to protect your hair from heat damage. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.

Step 2: Use a curling iron to create loose waves.

Take small sections of hair and wrap them around a curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 3: Scrunch your hair with your fingers.

Once your hair is curled, use your fingers to scrunch your hair and break up the curls. This will give your hair a more natural and relaxed look.

Step 4: Apply a texturizing spray.

Finish the look by applying a texturizing spray to your hair. This will add volume and texture to your hair and help hold the waves in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling your shoulder-length hair:

1. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage.

Curling irons and straighteners can damage your hair if you don't use a heat protectant spray. Always apply a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.

2. Use bobby pins to secure your hair.

Bobby pins are a great way to secure your hair in place. Use them to create a messy bun or to hold your half-up half-down hairstyle in place.

3. Experiment with different styles.

Don't be afraid to try new hairstyles. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my shoulder-length hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your shoulder-length hair. Use a straightener with a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage.

Q: How often should I wash my shoulder-length hair?

A: It depends on your hair type. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every few days.

Hair Care

Keeping your shoulder-length hair healthy is essential for creating a stylish look. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo.

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

A deep conditioning treatment can help repair damaged hair and keep it healthy. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair looking its best.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Using heat styling tools too often can damage your hair. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools and use a heat protectant spray when you do use them.

With these easy hairstyles for shoulder length hair, you can create a stylish look in no time. Whether you prefer a sleek and sophisticated look or a more relaxed and casual style, there's a hairstyle for you. Experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you best.
