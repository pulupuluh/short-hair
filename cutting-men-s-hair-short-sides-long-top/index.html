Cutting Men s Hair Short Sides Long Top A Relaxed Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, then cutting men's hair short sides long top might be the way to go. This is a versatile haircut that suits all face shapes and hair types. In this guide, we'll show you how to achieve this look, step by step, with tips and FAQs on how to maintain it.

Description

The short sides long top haircut is a modern and stylish haircut that features short hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. This haircut is also known as the "undercut" and can be styled in many ways, such as slicked back, messy, or with a quiff. This look is popular among men of all ages and has been seen on celebrities such as David Beckham, Brad Pitt, and Ryan Gosling.

How to Cut Men's Hair Short Sides Long Top

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting men's hair short sides long top, you'll need to gather your tools. You'll need a pair of barber shears, a comb, hair clippers, and a trimmer. You'll also need a towel to wrap around the client's neck and a spray bottle to dampen the hair.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair. Use your comb to create a part from the crown of the head to the temples. Clip the hair on the top of the head out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Use your clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of the head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard. Blend the hair on the sides into the longer hair on top.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Unclip the hair on top of the head and comb it straight up. Use your shears to cut the hair to the desired length. Remember to cut the hair in small sections and blend it into the hair on the sides.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Use your trimmer to clean up the hairline around the ears and nape of the neck. Use your comb to check for any uneven spots and trim as necessary. Style the hair with your client's preferred product.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair Short Sides Long Top

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short sides long top haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard when cutting the sides.
  • Cut the hair on top in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Blend the hair on the sides into the longer hair on top for a seamless transition.
  • Use your trimmer to clean up the hairline around the ears and nape of the neck.
  • Style the hair with your client's preferred product.

FAQs

How often do I need to get a haircut?

The frequency of haircuts depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain it. On average, most men get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Can I style my short sides long top haircut differently?

Yes, the short sides long top haircut is versatile and can be styled in many ways. You can slick back the hair, create a quiff, or leave it messy.

What products should I use to style my short sides long top haircut?

The products you use depend on your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve. For a slicked-back look, use a pomade or gel. For a messy look, use a texturizing spray or wax.

Hair Care for Short Sides Long Top Haircut

To maintain your short sides long top haircut, you'll need to wash and condition your hair regularly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers and flat irons, as they can damage your hair. Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Cutting men's hair short sides long top is a modern and stylish haircut that is easy to maintain. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a seamless transition from short sides to long top. Remember to take care of your hair and style it with the right products to keep it looking its best.
