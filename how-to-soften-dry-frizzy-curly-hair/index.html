How To Soften Dry Frizzy Curly Hair

Written by Frank Jun 05, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to keep it looking soft and frizz-free. Unfortunately, curly hair is naturally more prone to dryness and frizz due to its unique structure. But don't worry, there are ways to soften dry frizzy curly hair and keep it looking fabulous! In this article, we'll discuss some tips and tricks to help you achieve softer, more manageable curls.

How to Soften Dry Frizzy Curly Hair

The first step to softening dry frizzy curly hair is to identify the cause of the problem. Curly hair is naturally more prone to dryness and frizz due to its unique structure. The hair cuticle of curly hair is not as tightly sealed as that of straight hair, which means that moisture can escape more easily. This can lead to dryness and frizz.

To soften dry frizzy curly hair, you need to focus on two things: hydration and sealing in moisture. Hydration involves adding moisture to the hair, while sealing in moisture involves using products that help to lock in moisture and prevent it from escaping.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to softening dry frizzy curly hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo. Look for shampoos that contain ingredients like glycerin, aloe vera, or honey, which can help to hydrate the hair.
  2. Follow up with a moisturizing conditioner. Look for conditioners that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil, which can help to hydrate and soften the hair.
  3. Once you've rinsed out the conditioner, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair. This will help to provide additional moisture and prevent frizz. Look for leave-in conditioners that contain ingredients like jojoba oil, avocado oil, or silk protein.
  4. Next, apply a styling product to your hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair and contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil. These products will help to seal in moisture and define your curls.
  5. After applying your styling product, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to minimize frizz and define your curls.
  6. Once your hair is dry, you can apply a small amount of oil or serum to the ends of your hair to add additional shine and softness.

Tips for Softening Dry Frizzy Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for softening dry frizzy curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. Heat can strip your hair of moisture and lead to dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid using harsh products that contain sulfates or alcohol. These ingredients can be drying and damaging to curly hair.
  • Try using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help hydrate and soften your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. This can lead to breakage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Consider getting a hair cut to remove any damaged or split ends. This will help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent further damage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about softening dry frizzy curly hair:

Q: Can I use oils to soften my curly hair?

A: Yes, oils can be a great way to soften curly hair. Look for oils like coconut oil, argan oil, or jojoba oil, which are known for their moisturizing properties.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair find that washing once or twice a week is sufficient, while others prefer to wash more frequently. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: Can I use a hair mask to soften my curly hair?

A: Yes, a hair mask can be a great way to hydrate and soften curly hair. Look for masks that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or avocado oil, which can help to nourish and moisturize the hair.

Hair Care for Softening Dry Frizzy Curly Hair

To keep your curly hair soft and frizz-free, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Avoid using hot water when you wash your hair. Hot water can strip your hair of moisture and lead to dryness and frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help hydrate and nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh products that contain sulfates or alcohol. These ingredients can be drying and damaging to curly hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. If you do use them, use a heat protectant spray or serum to minimize damage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can soften dry frizzy curly hair and keep it looking fabulous. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.
