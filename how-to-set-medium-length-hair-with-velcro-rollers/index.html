How To Set Medium Length Hair With Velcro Rollers

Written by Christine Jul 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In this article, we will talk about how to set medium length hair with velcro rollers. Velcro rollers are a great tool for achieving voluminous, bouncy curls without damaging your hair. They are easy to use and can be done at home, making them a cost-effective option. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to set medium length hair with velcro rollers.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Set Medium Length Hair with Velcro Rollers

Before we begin, here are some things you will need:

  • Velcro rollers of different sizes
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Hairspray

Step 1: Wash and Dry your Hair

Start by washing your hair with your regular shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and make sure it is completely dry before using the velcro rollers. Using the rollers on damp hair can cause damage and breakage.

Step 2: Section your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using a comb. Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up. Use hair clips to secure the sections.

Step 3: Roll your Hair

Take a small section of hair and roll it onto the velcro roller, starting from the ends and rolling it towards the roots. Make sure the roller is placed securely against your scalp. Repeat this step for all the sections of hair.

Step 4: Wait and Unroll

Leave the rollers in your hair for 30 minutes to an hour, depending on how tight you want your curls to be. Once the time is up, carefully unroll the rollers from your hair.

Step 5: Style your Hair

Use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create your desired hairstyle. Finish off with some hairspray to set the curls in place.

Tips

  • Use smaller rollers for tighter curls and bigger rollers for looser curls.
  • If you have thin hair, use fewer rollers to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • For best results, use velcro rollers on clean, dry hair.
  • If you want your curls to last longer, wrap your hair in a silk scarf or sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.

FAQ

Q: How long should I keep the rollers in my hair?

A: Keep the rollers in your hair for 30 minutes to an hour, depending on how tight you want your curls to be.

Q: Can I use velcro rollers on wet hair?

A: It is not recommended to use velcro rollers on wet hair as it can cause damage and breakage.

Q: How many rollers should I use?

A: Use as many rollers as you need depending on the thickness and length of your hair. If you have thin hair, use fewer rollers to avoid weighing down your hair.

Hair Care

Using velcro rollers can be a great way to achieve voluminous, bouncy curls without damaging your hair. However, it is important to take care of your hair by using a heat protectant and not using the rollers too often. Overuse of velcro rollers can cause damage and breakage to your hair. Make sure to deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and nourished.

Now that you know how to set medium length hair with velcro rollers, give it a try and enjoy your new bouncy curls!
