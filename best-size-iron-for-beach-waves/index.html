The Best Size Iron For Beach Waves A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking to achieve those perfect beachy waves? Well, the secret is in the iron you use. The size of your curling iron can make all the difference in achieving those effortless, tousled waves. In this post, we'll be discussing the best size iron for beach waves and everything you need to know to achieve the perfect look!

Description

Beach waves are a coveted style that many women aspire to achieve. This effortless, casual look has been popular for years and doesn't seem to be going anywhere anytime soon. But achieving those perfect waves can be tricky, especially if you're not using the right size iron. The size of your iron can determine the size and shape of your waves, so it's important to choose the right one.

How to Achieve Beach Waves

The first step to achieving beach waves is to start with clean, dry hair. Using a heat protectant spray is also important to protect your hair from damage. Next, section your hair into manageable pieces and begin curling. The technique for achieving beach waves involves wrapping your hair around the iron, leaving the ends out and twisting the iron away from your face. Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently tousle the curls and create the perfect beachy wave.

The Best Size Iron for Beach Waves

The best size iron for beach waves is typically a 1-inch or 1.25-inch curling iron. These sizes are perfect for creating loose, tousled waves that mimic the look of a day spent at the beach. A smaller iron, such as a 0.75-inch, can create tighter curls that don't quite achieve the beachy look. A larger iron, such as a 1.5-inch or 2-inch, can create looser waves that don't hold their shape as well.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect beach waves:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash and dry your hair before beginning the styling process. Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from damage.

Step 2: Section your hair.

Section your hair into manageable pieces. The smaller the sections, the tighter the curls will be. The larger the sections, the looser the curls will be.

Step 3: Begin curling.

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, leaving the ends out. Twist the iron away from your face and hold for a few seconds before releasing.

Step 4: Repeat.

Repeat the curling process on all sections of your hair until your entire head is curled.

Step 5: Tousle.

Use your fingers to gently tousle the curls and create the perfect beachy wave.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Beach Waves

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when trying to achieve the perfect beach waves:

  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from damage.
  • Section your hair into manageable pieces for easier styling.
  • Twist the iron away from your face to create a more natural-looking wave.
  • Use your fingers to tousle the curls and create the perfect beachy look.
  • Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

What products should I use to achieve beach waves?

Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from damage. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place. For added texture, you can also use a sea salt spray or texturizing spray.

Hair Care for Beach Waves

It's important to take care of your hair when styling it with heat. Using a heat protectant spray or serum can help protect your hair from damage. Additionally, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

When styling your hair with heat, it's also important to use a high-quality curling iron. Investing in a good iron can help prevent damage and ensure that your waves look perfect every time.

Finally, it's important to remember to give your hair a break from heat styling every once in a while. Try air-drying your hair or opting for a different style to give your hair a break from the heat.
