Best Hair Style For Oval Face Woman

Written by Eveline Jun 05, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of constantly searching for a hairstyle that suits your oval face? Do you want to know how to enhance your natural features with the perfect hairstyle? Look no further! We have compiled a comprehensive guide on the best hair styles for oval face women. Read on to get the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.

Description

An oval face shape is considered the most versatile and balanced shape. It features a gently rounded hairline, wide cheekbones, and a slightly narrower jawline. This face shape can pull off almost any hairstyle, from long, short, curly, or straight. However, certain hairstyles can accentuate your natural features and take your look to the next level.

How to Choose the Right Hair Style for Oval Face Woman

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can be a daunting task. Here are some tips on how to choose the perfect hairstyle:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Look in the mirror and identify the widest part of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the length of your face. Once you have identified your face shape, it will be easier to choose the perfect hairstyle.

2. Consider Your Hair Type

Consider your hair type, whether it is straight, curly, or wavy. This will affect the way your hair falls and how it can be styled.

3. Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also be considered when choosing a hairstyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, a low-maintenance hairstyle may be more suitable for you.

Step by Step Guide for Hair Style for Oval Face Woman

Here are some step by step guides on how to style your hair for an oval face:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for oval face women. The layers add volume and movement to the hair, creating a soft and feminine look. To achieve this style, ask your stylist for long layers that start at your chin and blend into the rest of your hair.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is another great option for oval face women. A chin-length bob with layers around the face can highlight your cheekbones and jawline. This style is perfect for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair.

3. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are perfect for oval face women with a high forehead. The bangs can help to balance the face and create a softer look. The bangs should start from the side of the head and sweep across the forehead.

Tips for Hair Style for Oval Face Woman

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the perfect hairstyle:

1. Avoid Heavy Bangs

Avoid heavy bangs that cover your forehead. They can make your face appear shorter and wider.

2. Add Volume to the Crown

Add volume to the crown of your head to create an illusion of a longer face. This can be achieved by backcombing the hair or using volumizing products.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Embrace your natural texture and work with it. Curly hair can be styled with layers to create volume, while straight hair can be styled with a blunt cut for a sleek look.

FAQ

What is an Oval Face?

Can Oval Face Women Pull off Any Hairstyle?

Yes, oval face women can pull off almost any hairstyle, from long, short, curly, or straight. However, certain hairstyles can accentuate your natural features and take your look to the next level.

Hair Care for Oval Face Woman

Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is suitable for your hair type. This will prevent damage and breakage.

2. Condition Your Hair

Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and moisturized. This will also prevent tangles and breakage.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

In conclusion, choosing the perfect hairstyle for an oval face can be a daunting task. However, with the right tips and guidance, you can enhance your natural features and take your look to the next level. Follow our step by step guide, tips, and hair care advice to get the perfect hairstyle for your oval face.
