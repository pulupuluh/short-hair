How To Cut A Pixie Bob Hairstyle Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 11, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’re looking for a trendy and low-maintenance hairstyle, a pixie bob might just be the perfect fit for you. This short and edgy cut can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. But before you head to the salon, why not try cutting it yourself? In this step-by-step guide, we’ll show you how to cut a pixie bob hairstyle from the comfort of your own home.

Description

The pixie bob is a hybrid between a pixie cut and a bob. It’s typically short in the back and longer in the front, with choppy layers that add texture and movement. This style can be worn straight or with natural curls, and it’s perfect for those who want a bold and daring look.

How to Cut a Pixie Bob Hairstyle: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You’ll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off your hair. If you have curly hair, you may also want to invest in a pair of thinning shears to remove bulk.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: the top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Starting at the nape of your neck, use your scissors to cut the hair in a straight line. Work your way up to the crown of your head, using your fingers to gauge the length.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Next, move on to the sides of your head. Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers, cutting it at an angle to create a tapered effect. Continue working your way around your head, making sure the sides are even.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Now it’s time to tackle the top of your head. Use your comb to lift a section of hair, then cut it at an angle to create layers. This will add volume and texture to your pixie bob.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Using your scissors, blend the layers together to create a seamless transition between the different sections of your hair. This will help your pixie bob look natural and effortless.

Step 7: Check for Evenness

Once you’ve finished cutting, check your hair in the mirror to make sure it’s even. If you notice any stray hairs, trim them with your scissors.

Tips for Cutting a Pixie Bob Hairstyle

  • Start with dry hair to get a more accurate cut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head as you cut.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • If you’re unsure about cutting your own hair, ask a friend to help.

FAQ

Can I cut a pixie bob hairstyle with curly hair?

Yes, you can cut a pixie bob with curly hair. Just make sure to use thinning shears to remove bulk and create a softer, more natural look.

How often do I need to trim my pixie bob?

It’s recommended to trim your pixie bob every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care for Pixie Bob Hairstyle

To keep your pixie bob looking its best, use a styling product to add texture and hold. You can also use a flat iron or curling wand to create different styles. And don’t forget to wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny.

Cutting a pixie bob hairstyle may seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, it’s easier than you think. Follow these step-by-step instructions and you’ll have a trendy and stylish look in no time.
