Does Short Hair Make Your Face Look Skinnier

Written by Petter Jun 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair is a trend that never goes out of style. It is a great way to switch up your look and give yourself a fresh start. Many people choose to cut their hair short for various reasons, but one of the most common questions that people ask is, "Does short hair make your face look skinnier?".

Description

Short hair has been known to have a slimming effect on the face. This is because it exposes more of the neck and jawline, making the face appear longer and slimmer. Short haircuts can also help to draw attention away from rounder features such as cheeks and a full chin.

How To

If you are considering cutting your hair short to achieve a slimmer look, here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Face Shape

It is important to choose a short haircut that complements your face shape. A professional stylist can help you determine the best style for your face shape.

2. Layers

Adding layers to your short haircut can help to add dimension and movement to your hair. This can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

3. Bangs

If you have a rounder face, adding bangs to your short haircut can help to create the illusion of a longer face. However, if you have a longer face, it is best to avoid bangs as they can make your face appear even longer.

Step by Step

If you have decided to cut your hair short to achieve a slimmer look, here are the steps you should follow:

1. Choose a Style

Choose a short haircut that complements your face shape. Consider adding layers or bangs if they will help to achieve a slimmer look.

2. Consult with a Professional stylist

A professional stylist can help you determine the best short haircut for your face shape and provide you with tips on how to style your hair to achieve a slimmer look.

3. Regular Maintenance

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking sharp. Make sure to visit your stylist regularly to keep your hair looking its best.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting your hair short to achieve a slimmer look:

1. Consider Your Lifestyle

Choose a short haircut that fits your lifestyle. If you are constantly on the go, choose a low maintenance haircut that is easy to style.

2. Use Hair Products

Using hair products such as volumizing mousse or texturizing spray can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

3. Be Confident

Short haircuts can take some getting used to, but it is important to be confident in your new look. Embrace your new hairstyle and rock it with confidence!

FAQ

1. Will a short haircut make my face look too masculine?

No, a short haircut can be feminine and stylish. It is all about choosing the right style for your face shape.

2. Can a short haircut make my face look wider?

It is unlikely that a short haircut will make your face look wider. Short haircuts can have a slimming effect on the face.

3. How often should I visit the salon to maintain my short haircut?

Short haircuts require regular maintenance, so it is recommended to visit the salon every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

Hair Care

Short hair requires proper care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type.

2. Protect Your Hair

Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray when styling with heat tools.

3. Use Hair Masks

Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Conclusion

Short hair can have a slimming effect on the face, making it a great option for those looking to achieve a slimmer look. However, it is important to choose a style that complements your face shape and to properly care for your hair to keep it looking its best.
