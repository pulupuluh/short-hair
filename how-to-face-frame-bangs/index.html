How To Face Frame Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 29, 2023 · 6 min read

Face framing bangs are a great way to add some dimension and personality to your hairstyle. Whether you have long or short hair, face framing bangs can be a fun and stylish addition to your look. In this article, we’ll walk you through the steps to achieve the perfect face framing bangs, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Face Frame Bangs
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Perfect Face Framing Bangs
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Advice

How to Face Frame Bangs

Face framing bangs are a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit your individual look. They can be cut to any length, from short and choppy to long and wispy. The key to achieving the perfect face framing bangs is to work with your natural hair texture and face shape.

What You’ll Need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle
  • Flat iron or curling iron (optional)

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect face framing bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Using a comb, part your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a section of hair from the front of your head, above your forehead. This section should be about two inches wide.
  3. Use your comb to comb the section of hair straight down in front of your face.
  4. Hold the section of hair between your index and middle fingers, at the length you want your bangs to be. Remember to leave some room for error, as you can always trim more later.
  5. Using your scissors, cut the section of hair in a straight line. Be sure to cut slowly and carefully, making small snips rather than one big cut.
  6. Repeat steps 2-5 on the other side of your head.
  7. Use your comb to blend your new bangs into the rest of your hair. You can also use a flat iron or curling iron to add some texture or shape to your bangs.

Tips for Perfect Face Framing Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect face framing bangs:

  • Start with longer bangs and gradually work your way shorter. It’s easier to cut more hair off than it is to add it back on.
  • Don’t cut your bangs straight across. Instead, angle your scissors slightly to create a softer, more natural look.
  • If you’re unsure about cutting your own bangs, consider going to a professional stylist for help.

FAQs

Q: How do I know if face framing bangs will look good on me?

A: Face framing bangs can be flattering on many face shapes, but it’s always a good idea to consult with a professional stylist before making any drastic changes to your hairstyle.

Q: How often do I need to trim my face framing bangs?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep your bangs. On average, you should plan to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your face framing bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to help extend the time between washes.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh.

With the right tools and techniques, anyone can achieve the perfect face framing bangs. Whether you’re looking to switch up your hairstyle or just add some dimension to your look, face framing bangs are a fun and easy way to do it. Follow these steps, tips, and hair care advice to get the look you want.
