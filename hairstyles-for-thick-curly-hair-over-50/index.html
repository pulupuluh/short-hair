Hairstyles For Thick Curly Hair Over 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Mar 04, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As we age, our hair undergoes changes that can often leave us feeling frustrated and unsure of how to style it. For women with thick curly hair over 50, finding the right hairstyle can be even more challenging. However, with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve a hairstyle that not only complements your hair but also makes you feel confident and beautiful.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thick Curly Hair

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for thick curly hair over 50, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

Consider Your Face Shape

Choosing a hairstyle that complements your face shape can help accentuate your best features. For example, if you have a round face, a layered bob or long layers can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, soft curls or waves can help soften your features.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Thick curly hair requires a haircut that can manage its volume and texture. A layered haircut can help distribute the weight of your hair, making it easier to style. If you have coarse hair, a shorter haircut can help keep your hair under control.

Consider Your Personal Style

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your personal style. Are you a low-maintenance person who prefers a wash-and-go style? Or do you enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles and products? Choose a hairstyle that fits your lifestyle and makes you feel confident.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thick Curly Hair

Styling thick curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right technique, you can achieve a beautiful, voluminous look. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling thick curly hair:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. This will help hydrate your hair and prevent frizz. Towel dry your hair gently, being careful not to rub your hair too hard with the towel.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair while it's still damp. A curl-enhancing cream or mousse can help define your curls and reduce frizz. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 3: Diffuse Your Hair

Using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, dry your hair on a low or medium heat setting. This will help set your curls and prevent frizz. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp and move it in a circular motion to lift your roots and add volume.

Step 4: Finish with a Hair Oil

Once your hair is dry, apply a hair oil to the ends of your hair. This will help add shine and prevent frizz. Use a small amount of oil and apply it evenly throughout your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Curly Hair

Maintaining thick curly hair requires some extra care and attention. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every 2-3 days, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to refresh your hair.

Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When detangling your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to prevent breakage. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots, being gentle and patient.

Sleep with a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and reduce hair breakage. Silk is gentle on your hair, and it helps your hair retain moisture.

FAQs About Hairstyles for Thick Curly Hair Over 50

Q: What is the best haircut for thick curly hair over 50?

A: A layered haircut is a great option for thick curly hair over 50. It can help distribute the weight of your hair and make it easier to style.

Q: How do I prevent frizz in my thick curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz, use a curl-enhancing product and avoid touching your hair too much. Sleep on a silk pillowcase, and avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Curly Hair Over 50

Invest in a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain moisturizing ingredients like shea butter or coconut oil.

Deep Condition Your Hair Once a Week

Deep conditioning your hair once a week can help keep it hydrated and prevent breakage. Use a deep conditioning treatment that contains ingredients like argan oil or avocado oil.

Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and cause frizz. Try to air dry your hair as much as possible, and if you do use hot tools, use a heat protectant spray first.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a beautiful, voluminous hairstyle that complements your thick curly hair over 50. Remember to choose a hairstyle that fits your personal style and lifestyle, and take care of your hair with a good hair care routine.
