How To Treat Hair Loss Around Dogs Eyes A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Feb 20, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As a pet owner, it can be concerning to notice hair loss around your dog's eyes. This can be a sign of an underlying health issue or simply due to irritation from rubbing or scratching. In this article, we will discuss how to treat hair loss around dogs eyes, including tips and FAQs.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Treat Hair Loss Around Dogs Eyes
  • Tips for Preventing Hair Loss Around Dogs Eyes
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Dogs

Description:

Hair loss around dogs eyes can be caused by a variety of factors, including allergies, eye infections, mites, and even stress. In some cases, it may be a natural part of the aging process. It is important to identify the underlying cause of the hair loss in order to properly treat it.

How to Treat Hair Loss Around Dogs Eyes:

Step 1: Visit a Veterinarian

The first step in treating hair loss around dogs eyes is to visit a veterinarian. They can properly diagnose the underlying cause and recommend the appropriate treatment. This may include medication, topical creams, or changes in diet.

Step 2: Keep the Eye Area Clean and Dry

Keeping the eye area clean and dry can help prevent further irritation and hair loss. Use a clean, damp cloth to gently wipe away any discharge or debris around the eyes. Make sure to dry the area thoroughly after cleaning.

Step 3: Use a Soothing Eye Cream

A soothing eye cream can help reduce inflammation and promote healing. Look for creams that contain natural ingredients like chamomile or aloe vera. Apply the cream gently around the eye area, being careful not to get it in their eyes.

Step 4: Consider Changing Their Diet

In some cases, hair loss around dogs eyes may be caused by a food allergy or sensitivity. Consider switching to a high-quality, hypoallergenic dog food and monitor their symptoms to see if there is any improvement.

Step 5: Address Any Underlying Health Issues

If the hair loss is caused by an underlying health issue, such as an eye infection or mites, it is important to address that issue first. Follow your veterinarian's recommended treatment plan to help resolve the issue and promote healing.

Tips for Preventing Hair Loss Around Dogs Eyes:

  • Keep their face clean and dry
  • Trim any long hair around the eyes
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or shampoos around the eyes
  • Use a gentle, natural pet shampoo
  • Monitor their diet and consider switching to a hypoallergenic food

FAQs:

Q: Is hair loss around dogs eyes a serious issue?

A: Hair loss around dogs eyes can be a sign of an underlying health issue, so it is important to have it properly diagnosed by a veterinarian.

Q: Can I use human eye cream on my dog?

A: No, it is not recommended to use human eye cream on your dog. Look for products specifically designed for pets.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss around dogs eyes?

A: Yes, stress can be a contributing factor to hair loss around dogs eyes. Try to identify and address any sources of stress in your dog's life.

Hair Care for Dogs:

Proper hair care is important for maintaining your dog's overall health and preventing hair loss around their eyes. Here are some tips for keeping your dog's hair healthy:

  • Brush their hair regularly to prevent tangles and matting
  • Use a gentle, natural pet shampoo
  • Trim any long hair around their eyes and ears
  • Monitor their diet and make sure they are getting the proper nutrients

By following these tips and properly treating any underlying health issues, you can help prevent and treat hair loss around dogs eyes. Remember to always consult with a veterinarian before starting any new treatments or medications.
