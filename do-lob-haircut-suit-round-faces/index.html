Do Lob Haircut Suit Round Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face shape, you may be wondering if a lob haircut will suit you. The answer is yes! A lob haircut, which is a long bob, is a versatile and flattering hairstyle that can work for a variety of face shapes, including round faces.

How to Choose the Right Lob for Your Round Face Shape

When choosing a lob haircut for a round face shape, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to choose a length that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. A lob that falls just below the chin or longer can be a great option.

Next, you want to consider the layers in your lob. Layers can add movement and texture to your hair, but too many layers can make your face appear wider. Opt for longer layers that start at or below the chin.

Finally, consider adding side-swept bangs to your lob. Bangs can help to balance out a round face shape and draw attention to your eyes.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Lob Haircut

If you've decided that a lob haircut is right for you, here's a step-by-step guide to getting the look:

  1. Start by finding inspiration photos of lobs that you like. Bring these photos to your hairstylist to help them understand the look you're going for.
  2. Discuss your face shape with your hairstylist to ensure that they choose the right length and layers for your round face.
  3. Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow dry it straight.
  4. Sit in the chair and let your hairstylist work their magic. They will likely cut your hair in sections, starting at the back and working their way forward.
  5. Once your hair is cut, your hairstylist may add layers or texture to your hair using scissors or a razor.
  6. Finally, your hairstylist will style your hair and may add products to help your lob hold its shape.

Tips for Maintaining Your Lob Haircut

Once you've got your lob haircut, you'll want to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your lob:

  • Get regular trims to keep your lob looking fresh.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools to style your hair.
  • Consider using a volumizing product at your roots to help create lift.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as curling or straightening, to create different looks with your lob.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my lob straight or curly?

A: Yes! A lob can be styled straight or curly, depending on your preference. Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

Q: Will a lob make my round face look wider?

A: No. When done correctly, a lob can actually elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape.

Q: Can I add highlights or lowlights to my lob?

A: Absolutely! Adding highlights or lowlights to your lob can add dimension and depth to your hair.

Hair Care for Your Lob

It's important to take care of your hair to keep your lob looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as this can weigh down your hair.

In conclusion, a lob haircut can absolutely suit round faces. By choosing the right length, layers, and bangs, you can create a flattering and stylish look that works for you. With regular trims and proper hair care, your lob will look great and make you feel confident and beautiful.
