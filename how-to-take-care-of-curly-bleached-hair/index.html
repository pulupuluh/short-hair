How To Take Care Of Curly Bleached Hair

Written by Bardi Mar 22, 2023 · 7 min read

Curly bleached hair can be a great way to switch up your look and add some personality to your appearance. However, it’s important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain its health. In this article, we’ll go through some tips and tricks to help you take care of curly bleached hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Take Care of Curly Bleached Hair
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Expert Tips
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Products

How to Take Care of Curly Bleached Hair

The following steps are crucial in taking care of curly bleached hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Curly hair tends to be dry and fragile, and bleaching can make it even more so. It’s important to use a gentle shampoo that won’t strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free and contain moisturizing ingredients like coconut oil or shea butter.

2. Condition regularly

Conditioning your hair is important to replenish the moisture that’s lost during the bleaching process. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

3. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling wands can damage curly bleached hair. If you must use heat, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.

4. Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays from the sun can cause damage to your hair, especially if it’s been bleached. Wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair when you’re outside for extended periods of time.

5. Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims will help prevent split ends and breakage. It’s recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to take care of curly bleached hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo

Use a sulfate-free shampoo that’s gentle on your hair. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and hair, and rinse thoroughly with warm water.

Step 2: Apply a deep conditioning treatment

Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair, focusing on the ends. Leave it on for 10-15 minutes, then rinse with cool water.

Step 3: Towel dry your hair

Gently squeeze the excess water from your hair with a towel. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause damage.

Step 4: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it hydrated and healthy. Comb it through your hair with a wide-tooth comb.

Step 5: Air dry your hair

Air drying is the best way to dry curly bleached hair. If you must use a blow dryer, use a diffuser attachment and keep the temperature low.

Expert Tips

Here are some expert tips to help you take care of curly bleached hair:

1. Use a silk pillowcase

Silk pillowcases are gentler on your hair than cotton ones, and can help prevent breakage and frizz.

2. Apply a hair mask once a week

A hair mask can help moisturize and strengthen your hair. Apply it once a week for best results.

3. Don’t wash your hair every day

Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days instead.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my curly bleached hair?

A: Yes, but it’s important to wait at least a week after bleaching before dyeing your hair. Use a semi-permanent dye, as permanent dyes can be too harsh on your hair.

Q: Can I straighten my curly bleached hair?

A: Yes, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low to prevent damage.

Q: How often should I trim my curly bleached hair?

A: It’s recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Hair Care Products

Here are some recommended hair care products for curly bleached hair:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair mask

By following these tips and using the right products, you can keep your curly bleached hair healthy and beautiful.
