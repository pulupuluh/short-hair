How To Cut Comb Over Fade A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 10, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking for a stylish haircut that is both modern and classic? Look no further than the comb over fade. This popular men's haircut features short sides that gradually blend into longer hair on top, styled into a sleek comb over. With the right tools and techniques, you can easily create this look for your clients. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of how to cut comb over fade, including tips and hair care advice to ensure a flawless finish.

How to Cut Comb Over Fade

Before you begin, make sure you have the necessary tools on hand. You'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clipper guards
  • Hair products (optional)

Step 1: Determine the Length

The first step in cutting a comb over fade is to determine the length of the hair on top. Use scissors to trim the hair to the desired length, leaving enough to create a comb over. Be sure to blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back.

Step 2: Use Clippers to Trim the Sides

Use a clipper with a guard to trim the sides and back of the head. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down to a shorter guard to create a fade effect. Use a comb to blend the hair as you go, making sure there are no harsh lines.

Step 3: Blend the Top and Sides

Once the sides are trimmed, use scissors to blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back. Use a comb to ensure a smooth transition between the two lengths.

Step 4: Style the Hair

Apply a styling product to the hair on top, such as pomade or wax. Use a comb to create a sleek comb over, directing the hair to one side. You can also use a hair dryer to add volume and hold to the style.

Tips for Cutting Comb Over Fade

Here are some additional tips to ensure a successful comb over fade:

  • Take your time and work slowly to avoid mistakes.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head and ensure even trimming.
  • Experiment with different clipper guards and blending techniques to find the perfect fade.
  • Consult with your client to determine their desired length and style.

FAQ: Comb Over Fade

What hair types work best for comb over fade?

Comb over fade works best on straight or wavy hair. However, it can also work on curly hair with the right styling techniques.

How often should I get a comb over fade?

It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and keep the fade looking sharp.

Can I create a comb over fade at home?

While it's possible to create a comb over fade at home, it's recommended to visit a professional barber or stylist to ensure a flawless finish.

Hair Care for Comb Over Fade

To keep your comb over fade looking fresh, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Apply a conditioner to keep hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a styling product to keep the hair in place and maintain the style.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently create a comb over fade for your clients. Remember to take your time, work slowly, and consult with your client to ensure their desired style. With practice, you'll become a pro at cutting this classic and stylish men's haircut.
