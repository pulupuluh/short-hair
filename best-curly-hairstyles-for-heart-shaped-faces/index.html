Best Curly Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Christine Mar 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for the perfect curly hairstyles for your heart shaped face? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best curly hairstyles that will complement your heart shaped face. From bouncy curls to loose waves, we have got you covered.

Description

A heart shaped face has a broad forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow chin. The aim of a hairstyle for a heart shaped face is to draw attention away from the forehead and towards the jawline. Curly hairstyles do just that! They add volume to the lower half of the face, creating balance and symmetry.

How To

To achieve the perfect curly hairstyle for your heart shaped face, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a curl-defining cream or mousse.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or hot rollers to add more defined curls or waves.
  5. Finish off your hairstyle with a hairspray that will hold your curls in place.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to define your curls and add volume.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, towel dry it gently. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel as this can cause frizz.

Step 3: Apply Curl-Defining Cream or Mousse

Apply a curl-defining cream or mousse to your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and keep them in place.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser Attachment on Your Blow Dryer

Divide your hair into sections and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting. This will help to keep your curls intact and prevent frizz.

Step 5: Add More Defined Curls or Waves

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or hot rollers to add more defined curls or waves. This will help to create volume and balance out your heart shaped face.

Step 6: Finish Off with Hairspray

Finish off your hairstyle with a hairspray that will hold your curls in place. This will help to keep your hairstyle looking great all day long.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a curly hairstyle for your heart shaped face:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the lower half of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head.
  • Experiment with different curl sizes to find the perfect look for you.
  • Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to help define your curls.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curly hair as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting.

FAQ

What are the best curly hairstyles for heart shaped faces?

The best curly hairstyles for heart shaped faces are those that add volume to the lower half of the face. Bouncy curls, loose waves, and layered hairstyles are great options.

What should I avoid when choosing a curly hairstyle for my heart shaped face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head. This will only make your forehead look larger and draw attention away from your jawline.

How can I maintain my curly hairstyle?

Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using a brush on your curly hair. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting, and use a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to help define your curls and prevent frizz. Avoid using a brush on your curly hair as this can cause frizz. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting, and use a hairspray to hold your curls in place.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock the perfect curly hairstyle for your heart shaped face! Whether you prefer bouncy curls or loose waves, there is a hairstyle out there that will make you look and feel your best.
