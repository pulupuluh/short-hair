How To Smooth Wavy Frizzy Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Apr 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have wavy frizzy hair, you know how difficult it can be to achieve a smooth, sleek look. No matter how much you straighten or blow-dry your hair, it always seems to revert back to its natural texture. But fear not! In this guide, we will provide you with tips, tricks, and products to help you achieve the smooth, silky hair of your dreams.

Description

Wavy frizzy hair is characterized by uneven curls and a tendency to become frizzy in humid conditions. This hair type can be difficult to manage and style, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a smooth, sleek look.

How to Achieve Smooth Wavy Frizzy Hair

To achieve smooth wavy frizzy hair, you need to take a multi-step approach that involves proper hair care, styling techniques, and the use of smoothing products. Here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step to achieving smooth wavy frizzy hair is to wash and condition your hair with products that are specifically formulated for your hair type. Look for products that contain ingredients like keratin, argan oil, and shea butter, as these will help to smooth and hydrate your hair.

Step 2: Use a Smoothing Serum

After washing and conditioning your hair, apply a smoothing serum to your damp hair. Look for a serum that contains ingredients like silicone and dimethicone, as these will help to smooth and protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Next, blow-dry your hair using a round brush to smooth out your curls. Start at the root and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to pull your hair straight as you go. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron

After blow-drying your hair, use a flat iron to further smooth out your curls. Use a low to medium heat setting and make sure to apply a heat protectant spray before using the flat iron. Section your hair and slowly run the flat iron over each section, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 5: Finish with a Smoothing Cream

Finally, finish off your smooth wavy frizzy hair with a smoothing cream. Look for a cream that contains ingredients like coconut oil and shea butter, as these will help to keep your hair smooth and frizz-free.

Tips for Smoothing Wavy Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips for achieving smooth wavy frizzy hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear my hair curly if I want to achieve smooth wavy frizzy hair?

A: Yes! You can still wear your hair curly if you want to achieve smooth wavy frizzy hair. Simply follow the same steps, but instead of using a flat iron, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls.

Q: How often should I use a smoothing serum?

A: You can use a smoothing serum every time you wash your hair, but be careful not to overuse it, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Hair Care for Smooth Wavy Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for smooth wavy frizzy hair:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos and conditioners that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve smooth wavy frizzy hair that looks and feels amazing. Remember to take good care of your hair and use the right products to keep it healthy and hydrated.
