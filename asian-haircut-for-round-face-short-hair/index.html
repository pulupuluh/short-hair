Asian Haircut For Round Face Short Hair How To Get The Perfect Look

Written by Luffy Sep 06, 2023 · 10 min read

If you have a round face and short hair, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. But don't worry – there are plenty of stylish options for Asian women with round faces and short hair. In this article, we'll show you how to choose the right haircut and style for your face shape, and give you some tips on how to care for your hair. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Styling Your Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs About Asian Haircuts for Round Faces and Short Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a round face and short hair, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. A round face has soft curves and a width that is almost equal to its length. To balance out your face shape, you should look for haircuts that add height and volume to the top of your head.

Some popular haircuts for Asian women with round faces and short hair include:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob cut
  • Layered cut
  • Asymmetrical cut
  • Bowl cut

When choosing a haircut, make sure to consult with your hairstylist and bring pictures of styles that you like. They will be able to recommend a style that suits your face shape and hair texture.

Best Haircuts for Asian Women with Round Faces and Short Hair

Here are some of the best haircuts for Asian women with round faces and short hair:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short, layered haircut that is perfect for women with round faces. This style adds height and volume to the top of your head, which helps to elongate your face. The pixie cut is also very low maintenance and easy to style.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. For women with round faces, a chin-length or asymmetrical bob can help to balance out your face shape. This style also works well with both straight and wavy hair textures.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great option for women with thin or fine hair. This style adds volume and movement to your hair, which can help to make your face appear slimmer. You can also add layers to a pixie or bob cut for added texture and volume.

Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut is a bold and edgy haircut that can help to elongate your face. This style features longer hair on one side of your head and shorter hair on the other side. The asymmetrical cut is great for women with round faces who want to make a statement.

Bowl Cut

The bowl cut is a retro hairstyle that has made a comeback in recent years. This cut features a straight fringe and hair that is cut to the same length all around. For women with round faces, a bowl cut can help to balance out your face shape and add a quirky touch to your style.

Styling Your Haircut

Once you've chosen your perfect haircut, it's important to know how to style it. Here are some tips for styling your Asian haircut for round face short hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add height and volume to the top of your head.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create more volume.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and movement to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and clips, to add interest to your style.

Products for Styling Short Hair

Here are some recommended products for styling short hair:

  • Volumizing mousse or spray
  • Texturizing spray
  • Flat iron or curling iron
  • Hair accessories, such as headbands and clips

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your Asian haircut for round face short hair:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your hairstyle.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent your scalp from overproducing oil.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.

Recommended Hair Care Products for Short Hair

Here are some recommended hair care products for short hair:

  • Moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Dry shampoo
  • Hair oil or serum

FAQs About Asian Haircuts for Round Faces and Short Hair

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a round face and short hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs with a round face and short hair. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape. Side-swept or wispy bangs can help to elongate your face, while blunt bangs can make your face appear rounder.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I still wear my hair wavy or curly with a round face and short hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair wavy or curly with a round face and short hair. In fact, adding texture to your hair can help to elongate your face and create a more flattering look.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for short hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish Asian haircut for round face short hair with confidence. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture. Happy styling!
