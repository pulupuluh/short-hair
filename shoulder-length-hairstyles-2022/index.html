Shoulder Length Hairstyles 2022 Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Apr 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for the perfect hairstyle for 2022, look no further than shoulder length hairstyles. They're versatile, easy to maintain, and can suit every face shape and hair texture. In this guide, we'll take a look at some of the best shoulder length hairstyles for 2022, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.

Description

Shoulder length hairstyles are a great choice for anyone who wants a versatile and low-maintenance look. They can be worn straight, wavy, or curly, and can be styled in a variety of different ways. Whether you have thick, thin, curly, or straight hair, there's a shoulder length hairstyle that will work for you.

How To

One of the great things about shoulder length hairstyles is that they're easy to style. Here's how to achieve some of the most popular looks:

Straight and Sleek

To achieve a straight and sleek look, start by washing your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner. Blow dry your hair straight, using a round brush to smooth out any kinks. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten any remaining waves or curls. Finish with a shine serum to add gloss and smoothness to your hair.

Wavy and Textured

To achieve a wavy and textured look, start by washing your hair with a texturizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair, and apply a texturizing spray or mousse. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands. Once your hair is dry, you can add more texture with a curling iron or flat iron.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a shoulder length hairstyle:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suited to your hair type. Rinse thoroughly, and towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Products

Apply any styling products you're using, such as mousse, gel, or texturizing spray. Use your fingers or a comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Style

Style your hair as desired, using a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron. If you're going for a wavy or curly look, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help define your curls.

Step 4: Finish

Once you've achieved your desired style, finish with a hairspray, shine serum, or other finishing product to help hold your hair in place and add shine.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your shoulder length hairstyle:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's suited to your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum when using hot tools on your hair.
  • Try different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length hairstyles:

What face shape is best suited for a shoulder length hairstyle?

Shoulder length hairstyles can suit any face shape, but they work particularly well for oval, heart-shaped, and square faces.

What hair texture is best suited for a shoulder length hairstyle?

Shoulder length hairstyles can work for any hair texture, but they're particularly well-suited to thick, straight, or wavy hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily it gets. Generally, you should aim to wash your hair 2-3 times per week.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length hairstyle looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, and always use a heat protectant spray or serum.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Try to avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.

With these tips and techniques, you're sure to find the perfect shoulder length hairstyle for 2022. Whether you're going for a sleek and polished look, or a wavy and textured style, there's a shoulder length hairstyle that will work for you. So go ahead and experiment with different styles, and have fun creating a look that's uniquely yours!
