Description

Medium length thick hair with bangs can be challenging to style, but it's a versatile hair type that can suit many different hairstyles. In this blog post, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles for medium length thick hair with bangs, as well as provide tips on how to maintain and care for this hair type.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's important to understand how to prepare your hair for styling. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type. If you have thick hair, a volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help add body and texture.

Next, towel dry your hair and apply a styling product that suits your hair type and the hairstyle you want to achieve. For example, if you want a sleek, straight look, use a heat protectant spray and straightening iron. If you want a more natural, messy look, use a texturizing spray or mousse.

Step by step

1. Wavy Lob with Bangs

Start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush to add volume. Then, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Finish off with a texturizing spray for a messy, beachy look.

2. Layered Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair into a layered bob with side-swept bangs. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and texture. Use a straightening iron to create a sleek, polished look.

3. Half-Up Bun with Wispy Bangs

Gather the top half of your hair and tie it into a bun. Leave some wispy strands of hair around your face. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture.

4. Straight and Sleek with Blunt Bangs

Blow-dry your hair with a flat brush to create a smooth, straight look. Use a straightening iron to add extra sleekness. Finish off with a shine spray.

Tips

  • Invest in a good quality heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as it can strip away natural oils and make your hair more prone to breakage.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to distribute natural oils throughout your hair and add shine.

FAQ

What hairstyles suit medium length thick hair with bangs?

Medium length thick hair with bangs can suit many different hairstyles, including layered bobs, wavy lobs, half-up buns, and straight and sleek styles.

How do I care for medium length thick hair with bangs?

To care for medium length thick hair with bangs, use products that are designed for your hair type, avoid over-washing, and use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Hair Care

Medium length thick hair with bangs requires regular maintenance and care to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair every 3-4 days with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to hydrate and nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
