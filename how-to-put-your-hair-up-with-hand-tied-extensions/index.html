How To Put Your Hair Up With Hand Tied Extensions

Written by Luffy Mar 10, 2023 · 6 min read

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Hand tied extensions are an excellent way to add length, volume, and thickness to your hair. They are also great for creating a variety of hairstyles, including updos. However, putting your hair up with hand tied extensions can be tricky, especially if you're new to extensions. In this blog post, we'll show you how to put your hair up with hand tied extensions, step by step.

How to

Before we get started, here are a few things you'll need:

  • Bobby pins
  • Elastic hair ties
  • Hairspray

Now, let's get into how to put your hair up with hand tied extensions:

Step by step

Step 1: Start by brushing your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Gather the top section of your hair, leaving out a small section of hair in the front, and tie it into a high ponytail using an elastic hair tie. Make sure the ponytail is tight and secure.

Step 3: Take a weft of hand tied extensions and clip it onto the base of the ponytail. Make sure the weft is clipped in securely.

Step 4: Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic hair tie to hide it. Secure the end of the section of hair with a bobby pin.

Step 5: Take another weft of hand tied extensions and clip it onto the base of the ponytail, below the first weft. Again, make sure the weft is clipped in securely.

Step 6: Divide the ponytail into two equal sections and twist each section in opposite directions. Then, wrap the twisted sections around each other to create a twisted bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Step 7: Take another weft of hand tied extensions and clip it onto the base of the bun. Again, make sure the weft is clipped in securely.

Step 8: Take the small section of hair that you left out at the beginning and wrap it around the base of the bun to hide the weft. Secure the end of the section of hair with a bobby pin.

Step 9: Use hairspray to set your updo in place.

Tips

  • Make sure your hand tied extensions are the same length as your natural hair.
  • Use bobby pins that match the color of your hair to hide them better.
  • Experiment with different updo styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I put my hair up with hand tied extensions if I have short hair?

A: It depends on how short your hair is. If your hair is too short, the wefts may not blend in well with your natural hair. We recommend consulting with a hair extension specialist to determine if hand tied extensions are suitable for your hair length.

Q: How do I take care of my hand tied extensions?

A: You should wash your extensions every 3-4 days with sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot tools on your extensions, as this can damage them. Brush your extensions gently to prevent tangling and shedding.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and longevity of your hand tied extensions. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

By following these tips and the steps outlined in this blog post, you can put your hair up with hand tied extensions and create a variety of beautiful updos. Good luck!
