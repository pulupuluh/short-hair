How To Make A Square Face Look Thinner

Written by Luffy Aug 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a square face shape, you may feel self-conscious about the angles and width of your face. However, there are a few simple tricks you can use to make your face look thinner and more balanced. In this article, we'll share some tips and techniques for how to make a square face look thinner.

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a prominent jawline, wide forehead, and angular features. This face shape can be challenging to style, as it is often perceived as strong and masculine. However, with the right techniques, you can soften your features and create a more feminine, balanced look.

How to Make a Square Face Look Thinner

There are several ways to make a square face look thinner:

1. Choose the Right Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can make a big difference in how your face looks. Avoid blunt cuts and straight bangs, as they can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for soft layers and side-swept bangs to create a more balanced look. A long, layered haircut can also help to elongate your face and make it look thinner.

2. Contour Your Face

Contouring can help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and slimmer face. Use a contouring powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone, and apply it along the sides of your face, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline. Blend well for a natural look.

3. Use Highlighter

Highlighter can help to create the illusion of a more lifted, sculpted face. Apply highlighter to the tops of your cheekbones, the bridge of your nose, and your forehead. This will help to draw attention to the center of your face and make it appear slimmer.

4. Choose the Right Accessories

The right accessories can help to balance out your face shape. Opt for earrings that are long and dangling, as they can help to elongate your face. Avoid chokers and wide necklaces, as they can make your face look wider.

5. Wear the Right Makeup

The right makeup can help to create a more balanced, feminine look. Use a light, natural-looking foundation and avoid heavy contouring. Opt for soft, neutral eyeshadow colors and a bright lip color to draw attention to your lips and away from your jawline.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow to make a square face look thinner:

Step 1: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Opt for soft layers and side-swept bangs to create a more balanced look. A long, layered haircut can also help to elongate your face and make it look thinner.

Step 2: Contour Your Face

Use a contouring powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone, and apply it along the sides of your face, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline. Blend well for a natural look.

Step 3: Use Highlighter

Apply highlighter to the tops of your cheekbones, the bridge of your nose, and your forehead.

Step 4: Choose the Right Accessories

Opt for earrings that are long and dangling, as they can help to elongate your face.

Step 5: Wear the Right Makeup

Use a light, natural-looking foundation and avoid heavy contouring. Opt for soft, neutral eyeshadow colors and a bright lip color to draw attention to your lips and away from your jawline.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips for making a square face look thinner:

  • Wear your hair in a side part to create the illusion of a more elongated face
  • Choose sunglasses with rounded frames to soften your features
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too severe or slicked back, as they can make your face look wider
  • Opt for soft, natural-looking blush to create a more youthful, feminine look

FAQ

Can I still wear a bob haircut if I have a square face?

Yes, you can wear a bob haircut if you have a square face. However, opt for a longer bob with soft layers to create a more balanced look.

What kind of contouring brush should I use?

Use a small, angled brush for precise application.

Can I still wear bold lipstick if I have a square face?

Yes, you can still wear bold lipstick if you have a square face. However, opt for a bright color that will draw attention to your lips and away from your jawline.

Hair Care

In addition to styling your hair to flatter your face shape, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you have color-treated hair, use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading. Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make a square face look thinner and more balanced. Remember to choose hairstyles, makeup, and accessories that flatter your features and make you feel confident and beautiful.
