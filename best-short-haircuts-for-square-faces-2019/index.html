Best Short Haircuts For Square Faces 2019 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Aug 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are one of the many women with a square face shape, you might have often wondered what the best haircut is for you. After all, a haircut can make or break one's appearance. In this comprehensive guide, we will explore the best short haircuts for square faces in 2019. From the description of faces to hairstyle tips, we have got you covered.

Description of Square Face Shape

A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, broad forehead, and angular features. The length and width of a square face are proportionate to each other, resulting in a sharp and defined appearance.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

If you are unsure of your face shape, take a picture of yourself facing the camera directly. Then, trace the outline of your face using a pencil or your finger. Compare the shape you traced to the following shapes: round, oval, square, heart, or diamond.

Best Short Haircuts for Square Faces in 2019

1. Pixie Cut - A pixie cut is a popular and trendy haircut that works well with square faces. It frames the face and accentuates the cheekbones.

2. Bob Cut - A bob cut is a classic haircut that flatters square faces. It softens the jawline and slims down the face.

3. Wavy Bob Cut - A wavy bob cut is a modern twist on the classic bob cut. The waves add texture and volume to the hair, which balances the sharp features of a square face.

4. Asymmetrical Cut - An asymmetrical cut is a bold and edgy haircut that suits square faces. The uneven length of the hair distracts from the sharp angles of the face.

Step by Step Guide: How to Get a Short Haircut for Square Faces

1. Research - Look for inspiration pictures of short haircuts that suit square faces.

2. Find a Professional Hairstylist - Look for a hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts and has experience working with square faces.

3. Consultation - Book a consultation with your hairstylist and discuss your desired haircut. They can advise you on the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

4. Maintenance - Short haircuts require frequent maintenance to keep the shape and style intact. Schedule regular appointments with your hairstylist for trims and touch-ups.

Tips for Styling Short Haircuts for Square Faces

1. Use Texturizing Spray - Texturizing spray adds volume and texture to short haircuts, making them look fuller and more voluminous.

2. Avoid Heavy Bangs - Heavy bangs can make a square face look boxy and emphasize the forehead. Opt for side-swept bangs instead.

3. Experiment with Hair Accessories - Hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and scarves can add a pop of color and personality to short haircuts.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Can I get a short haircut if I have a square face?

A: Absolutely! There are many short haircuts that flatter square faces.

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look even more square?

A: No, a well-executed short haircut can balance out the sharp features of a square face.

Q: How do I know if a short haircut is right for me?

A: Consult with a professional hairstylist who can advise you on the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo - Short haircuts need to be washed frequently to maintain their shape and style. Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip the hair of its natural oils.

2. Condition Regularly - Conditioning the hair regularly can prevent breakage and keep it soft and shiny.

3. Protect from Heat Damage - Short haircuts are more prone to heat damage from styling tools such as flat irons and hair dryers. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

4. Get Regular Trims - Regular trims can prevent split ends and keep the hair looking healthy and vibrant.

Conclusion

In conclusion, there are many short haircuts that flatter square faces. From the classic bob cut to the edgy asymmetrical cut, there is a style for everyone. By following our step-by-step guide and tips, you can achieve a chic and stylish short haircut that accentuates your best features.
