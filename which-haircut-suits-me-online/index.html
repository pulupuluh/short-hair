Which Haircut Suits Me Online A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Cut

Written by Christine Jul 19, 2023 Â· 11 min read

Are you tired of the same old haircut and looking for a change? Or, maybe you're unsure which haircut suits your face shape and hair type? Whatever your reason, finding the perfect haircut can be a daunting task. But fear not, with the help of online resources, you can easily find a haircut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. In this article, we'll explore how to determine which haircut suits you online, step by step, with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Determine Which Haircut Suits You Online

The first step in finding your perfect haircut is to determine your face shape. Face shape plays a significant role in determining which haircut will flatter your features. To determine your face shape, you can use online tools such as face shape calculators or take a selfie and analyze your facial features. Once you have determined your face shape, you can then start exploring different haircuts that suit your features.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

As mentioned earlier, determining your face shape is the first step in finding your perfect haircut. There are six main face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has specific features that determine which haircut will flatter your face.

Round Face: A round face has soft curves with a width and height that are almost equal. To elongate the face, opt for haircuts that create height and volume at the crown and avoid styles that add width to the face.

Oval Face: An oval face has balanced proportions, with a slightly narrower chin and forehead. Most haircuts flatter an oval face, but medium to long haircuts that add volume to the sides can enhance your features.

Square Face: A square face has a strong jawline and forehead, with equal width and length. To soften your features, opt for haircuts with soft layers or curls that add volume to the top and sides of the face.

Heart Face: A heart-shaped face has a wide forehead and narrow chin. To balance your features, opt for haircuts that add volume to the chin area and narrow at the forehead.

Diamond Face: A diamond-shaped face has a narrow forehead and chin, with wide cheekbones. To balance your features, opt for haircuts that create volume at the forehead and chin area.

Oblong Face: An oblong face has a long and narrow shape, with a slightly larger forehead and chin. To balance your features, opt for haircuts that create volume at the sides and avoid styles that add height to the top of the head.

Step 2: Determine Your Hair Type

After determining your face shape, the next step is to determine your hair type. Your hair type plays a significant role in determining which haircut will suit you. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Each hair type has specific characteristics that determine which haircut will flatter your hair.

Straight Hair: Straight hair is easy to manage and style, making it perfect for short haircuts that require minimal maintenance.

Wavy Hair: Wavy hair has natural waves that add texture and volume to your hair. Long haircuts that enhance your waves will flatter your features.

Curly Hair: Curly hair has tight curls that require more maintenance and styling. Medium to long haircuts that enhance your curls will flatter your features.

Coily Hair: Coily hair has tight coils that require more moisture and maintenance. Short haircuts that enhance your coils will flatter your features.

Step 3: Explore Different Haircuts

After determining your face shape and hair type, the next step is to explore different haircuts. You can use online resources such as Pinterest or hairstyle websites to explore different haircuts that suit your features. It's essential to consider your lifestyle and maintenance requirements when choosing a haircut. Opt for a haircut that's easy to maintain and suits your everyday routine.

Tips for Finding Your Perfect Haircut

Now that you know how to determine which haircut suits you online let's explore some tips for finding your perfect haircut.

Consider Your Lifestyle: It's essential to consider your lifestyle when choosing a haircut. Opt for haircuts that suit your everyday routine and are easy to maintain.

Consult a Stylist: If you're unsure which haircut suits you, consult a stylist. They can recommend haircuts that flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty.

Experiment with Styles: Don't be afraid to experiment with different haircuts until you find the perfect one. Hair grows back, so if you don't like a particular haircut, you can always try a different style.

Consider Your Hair Texture: Your hair texture plays a significant role in determining which haircut will suit you. Consider your hair texture when choosing a haircut and opt for styles that enhance your natural texture.

FAQs

Q: How do I know which haircut suits me?

A: Determine your face shape and hair type to find haircuts that flatter your features. Consult a stylist for recommendations if you're unsure.

Q: What haircuts suit a round face?

A: Haircuts that add height and volume at the crown flatter a round face. Avoid styles that add width to the face.

Q: What haircuts suit an oval face?

A: Most haircuts flatter an oval face, but medium to long haircuts that add volume to the sides can enhance your features.

Hair Care Advice

After finding your perfect haircut, it's essential to take care of your hair. Follow these hair care tips to maintain healthy and beautiful hair.

Wash Your Hair Regularly: Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil buildup. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.

Use Heat Protection: If you use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Trim Your Hair Regularly: Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

Use a Hair Mask: Use a hair mask once a week to deeply nourish and moisturize your hair.

Now that you know how to determine which haircut suits you online, it's time to experiment with different haircuts and find your perfect style. Remember to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when choosing a haircut and take care of your hair to maintain healthy and beautiful locks.
