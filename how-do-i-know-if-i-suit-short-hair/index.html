How Do I Know If I Suit Short Hair

Written by William Jun 04, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you considering chopping off your locks but not sure if short hair will suit you? Short hair can be a bold and empowering choice, but it's important to make sure it complements your face shape and features. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks to help you determine whether short hair is the right choice for you.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Considering Your Hair Texture
  • Assessing Your Lifestyle
  • Highlighting Your Face Features
  • Trying on Wigs or Virtual Hairstyles

  • FAQ

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in determining whether short hair will suit you is to determine your face shape. Face shapes are generally categorized as oval, round, square, heart, or diamond. Each face shape has certain hairstyles that complement it best.

For example, those with oval faces can pull off almost any hairstyle, while those with round faces may want to avoid hairstyles that add extra volume to the sides of their face. Square faces tend to look best with soft, rounded hairstyles, while heart-shaped faces look great with styles that accentuate the chin area.

If you're unsure about your face shape, there are plenty of online resources that can help you determine it. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking at hairstyles that will complement it.

Considering Your Hair Texture

In addition to your face shape, you'll also want to consider your hair texture when deciding if short hair is right for you. Different hair textures will react differently to short haircuts, so it's important to choose a cut that will work with your natural texture.

If you have fine hair, for example, you may want to avoid very short cuts that can make your hair look flat and lifeless. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, a short cut can help to reduce bulk and make your hair more manageable.

Assessing Your Lifestyle

Another important factor to consider when deciding if short hair is right for you is your lifestyle. Short haircuts can be incredibly low-maintenance, but they may not be practical for everyone.

If you're someone who loves to experiment with different hairstyles and spend time styling your hair every day, a short cut may not be the best choice for you. On the other hand, if you're someone who prefers a more natural and effortless look, a short cut may be the perfect fit.

Highlighting Your Face Features

Short haircuts can be a great way to highlight your facial features, such as your eyes, cheekbones, and lips. If you have striking facial features that you want to show off, a short cut can be a great way to do so.

For example, if you have beautiful eyes, a short cut that frames your face and draws attention to your eyes can be incredibly flattering. If you have high cheekbones, a short cut that's swept back from your face can accentuate them beautifully.

Trying on Wigs or Virtual Hairstyles

If you're still unsure whether short hair is right for you, one great way to test it out is to try on wigs or experiment with virtual hairstyles. There are plenty of websites and apps that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles virtually.

You can also visit a wig store and try on different styles to see how they look on you. This can be a great way to get a feel for what short hair would look like on you before you take the plunge.

Taking Care of Short Hair

If you do decide to go for a short cut, it's important to take care of your hair properly to keep it looking its best. Short haircuts often require more frequent trims to maintain their shape, and you'll need to use the right products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Make sure to invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type, and consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

FAQ

Will short hair suit me if I have a round face?

Short hair can definitely suit those with round faces, but it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape. Look for styles that add height to the top of your head and avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face.

What if I have curly hair?

Short hair can be a great choice for those with curly hair, but it's important to choose a style that works with your natural texture. Look for styles that embrace your curls and avoid styles that require a lot of straightening or heat styling.

Can I still style short hair?

Absolutely! While short hair may be more low-maintenance than longer styles, there are still plenty of ways to style it. Experiment with different products and techniques to find a look that works for you.

Do I need to use different products for short hair?

Short hair may require different products than longer styles, depending on your hair type and texture. Talk to your stylist about the best products for your hair, and make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for short hair.

Now that you know how to determine whether short hair is right for you, you can make an informed decision about whether to take the plunge. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
