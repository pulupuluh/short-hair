Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top A Guide For Men In 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, the hairstyle short sides long top may be the perfect choice for you. This style has been popular for quite some time now, and it's not going away anytime soon. In this guide, we'll show you how to achieve the look, offer some tips and tricks, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve the Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

How to Achieve the Hairstyle Short Sides Long Top

The hairstyle short sides long top is relatively easy to achieve, but it does require some skill and patience. To get the look, you'll need to ask your barber for a tapered or faded cut on the sides and leave the top longer. You can also ask for an undercut, which is a more extreme version of the style that involves shaving the sides and leaving the top long.

Once you have the basic haircut, you can style it in a variety of ways, depending on your preferences. Some popular options include slicking the top back with a pomade, spiking it up with a gel, or leaving it messy and tousled for a more casual look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the hairstyle short sides long top:

  1. Visit a reputable barber or hairstylist and ask for a tapered or faded cut on the sides and leave the top longer.
  2. Choose a styling product that suits your hair type and personal style.
  3. Apply the product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up to the tips.
  4. Style your hair as desired, using a comb, brush, or your fingers.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle short sides long top:

  • Choose a styling product that works well with your hair type and texture.
  • Experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your barber or hairstylist for advice on how to style your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality comb or brush to help you achieve the look you want.
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to your hair to add depth and dimension.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the hairstyle short sides long top:

Q: Is the hairstyle short sides long top suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this style can work well with all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and thick hair.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut to maintain this style?

A: That depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. Generally, you'll need to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the sides short and the top long.

Q: Can I still wear a hat with this hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can still wear a hat with this style. Just make sure you style your hair first and choose a hat that fits well and doesn't flatten your hair too much.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hairstyle short sides long top looking great, you'll need to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it harder to style.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve and maintain the perfect hairstyle short sides long top. Whether you're going for a classic look or something more edgy, this style is a great choice for men of all ages and hair types.
