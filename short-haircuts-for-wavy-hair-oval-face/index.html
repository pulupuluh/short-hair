Short Haircuts For Wavy Hair Oval Face A Complete Guide

Are you tired of dealing with long, unruly hair that just won't cooperate? If you have an oval face shape and wavy hair, you're in luck! There are plenty of short haircuts that can flatter your face shape and enhance your natural texture. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about short haircuts for wavy hair oval face, including how to choose the right cut and style, tips for maintenance, and more. Let's get started!

  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting and Styling Short Hair

How to Choose the Right Cut and Style

The first step in choosing the right short haircut for wavy hair oval face is determining your face shape. An oval face shape is longer than it is wide and has a slightly rounded jawline. If you have an oval face shape, you have a lot of flexibility when it comes to hairstyles. Many short haircuts work well with this face shape, including bobs, pixies, and shags.

When choosing a haircut, it's important to consider your hair texture. Wavy hair can be thick or thin, so you'll need to choose a haircut that works with your natural volume. If you have thick, wavy hair, a layered bob or shag can help to thin out your hair and create a more manageable style. If your hair is thinner, a pixie cut or blunt bob can help to add volume and texture.

Once you've chosen a cut, it's time to think about styling. Wavy hair looks best when it's allowed to air dry, so you may want to consider a wash-and-go hairstyle. If you prefer to style your hair, consider using a curling iron or a texturizing spray to enhance your natural waves.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting and Styling Short Hair

If you're ready to take the plunge and cut your hair short, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. This will help you to see the natural texture of your hair and make it easier to cut.
  2. Choose the right haircut based on your face shape and hair texture.
  3. Section your hair into small sections, starting at the back and working your way forward.
  4. Use a sharp pair of scissors to cut your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  5. Once you've cut your hair to the desired length, use texturizing shears to add texture and movement.
  6. Style your hair using a curling iron or texturizing spray, if desired.
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Wavy Hair Oval Face

Short haircuts for wavy hair oval face require a bit of maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your new hairstyle:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for wavy hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQ About Short Haircuts for Wavy Hair Oval Face

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A: No, a short haircut can actually make your face appear longer and narrower. If you have an oval face shape, you have a lot of flexibility when it comes to hairstyles.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your style. For most people, getting a trim every 6-8 weeks is sufficient.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. Consider using small hair clips or bobby pins to secure your hair in place.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

Whether you have a short haircut or long hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few hair care tips for wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair, as this can weigh down your waves and make them look limp.

With these tips and tricks, you're well on your way to finding the perfect short haircut for wavy hair oval face. Remember to choose a cut that works with your natural texture and face shape, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
