Best Short Cuts For Oval Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Feb 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have an oval face, you are lucky enough to be able to pull off a wide range of hairstyles. However, with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the best short cut for your face shape. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide to the best short cuts for oval faces, including a description, how to style each cut, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline being roughly the same width. This face shape is versatile and can pull off a wide range of hairstyles, including short cuts. Short cuts that work well with oval faces include pixie cuts, bobs, and blunt cuts.

How to Style

The best short cuts for oval faces are those that accentuate your features and complement your face shape. Here are some of the most popular short cuts for oval faces:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped cut that is both chic and edgy. This cut works well with oval faces as it accentuates the cheekbones and draws attention to the eyes. To style a pixie cut, use a small amount of pomade or wax to create texture and definition. Use your fingers to tousle the hair for a messy, effortless look.

Bob

A bob is a classic short cut that is both elegant and versatile. This cut works well with oval faces as it draws attention to the jawline and elongates the neck. To style a bob, use a round brush and blow-dry the hair smooth. Use a flat iron to add additional smoothness and shine. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Blunt Cut

A blunt cut is a short cut that is all one length. This cut works well with oval faces as it accentuates the cheekbones and draws attention to the eyes. To style a blunt cut, use a flat iron to create sleek, straight hair. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are the step-by-step instructions for styling the best short cuts for oval faces:

Pixie Cut

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax to your fingertips.
  3. Rub your fingertips together to distribute the product.
  4. Run your fingers through your hair to create texture and definition.
  5. Tousle your hair with your fingers for a messy, effortless look.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Bob

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush.
  4. Use a flat iron to smooth your hair and add shine.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Blunt Cut

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  3. Use a flat iron to create sleek, straight hair.
  4. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling the best short cuts for oval faces:

  • Choose a cut that complements your face shape and accentuates your features.
  • Use the right products for your hair type.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron and round brush.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the perfect cut for you.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice.

FAQs

What short cuts work best for oval faces?

Short cuts that work well with oval faces include pixie cuts, bobs, and blunt cuts.

What should I consider when choosing a short cut for my oval face?

You should consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a short cut for your oval face.

How do I style a pixie cut?

To style a pixie cut, use a small amount of pomade or wax to create texture and definition. Use your fingers to tousle the hair for a messy, effortless look.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your short cut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of your cut.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Now that you know the best short cuts for oval faces, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist and try out a new look. Remember to choose a cut that complements your face shape and accentuates your features, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you.
