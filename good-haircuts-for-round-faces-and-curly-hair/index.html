Good Haircuts For Round Faces And Curly Hair

Written by Petter Aug 03, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse, especially if you have a round face. Finding the right haircut for your face shape and hair type can be a challenge, but there are plenty of options available that will flatter your features and make your curls look their best. In this article, we'll go over some of the best haircuts for round faces and curly hair, as well as tips for maintaining your curls and keeping them healthy.

  • How to Choose a Haircut for Round Faces and Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Good Haircut

How to Choose a Haircut for Round Faces and Curly Hair

When choosing a haircut for round faces and curly hair, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. Round faces tend to have soft, curved features and a wide forehead and cheekbones. Curly hair can be thick and voluminous, with a tendency to frizz or become dry and damaged.

One of the best haircuts for round faces and curly hair is a long, layered style that falls just below the shoulders. This cut helps to elongate the face and draw attention away from the cheeks. Layers also help to add movement and texture to curly hair, making it more manageable and less prone to frizz.

If you prefer a shorter style, consider a shoulder-length bob with layers around the face. This cut can be flattering for round faces and works well with curly hair. Another option is a short, choppy pixie cut with layers for added texture and volume.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces and Curly Hair:

  • Long, layered style
  • Shoulder-length bob with face-framing layers
  • Short, choppy pixie cut with layers
  • Asymmetrical bob with layers
  • Curtain bangs with layers

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Good Haircut

Getting a good haircut starts with finding a stylist who understands your hair type and face shape. Look for someone who specializes in curly hair and has experience working with round faces. When you book your appointment, be sure to bring pictures of the style you want and communicate your preferences clearly.

During your appointment, your stylist will likely start by washing and conditioning your hair. They may also apply a styling product to enhance your curls and make them easier to work with. Next, they'll begin cutting your hair, taking care to create layers that flatter your face shape and hair texture.

After the cut is complete, your stylist may style your hair using a diffuser and a curl-enhancing product. They'll also provide tips for maintaining your curls at home and keeping your new haircut looking its best.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right products and techniques, you can keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls while they're wet.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair while it's still wet to help define your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause it to lose its natural curl pattern.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage while you sleep.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face and curly hair?

One of the best haircuts for round faces and curly hair is a long, layered style that falls just below the shoulders. This cut helps to elongate the face and draw attention away from the cheeks. Other good options include a shoulder-length bob with layers around the face or a short, choppy pixie cut with layers for added texture and volume.

How often should I get my curly hair cut?

It's recommended that you get your curly hair cut every 8-12 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends. However, the frequency of your haircuts may depend on your hair type and how quickly it grows.

Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair using a flat iron or other heat styling tools. However, frequent heat styling can damage your hair and cause it to lose its natural curl pattern. If you want to straighten your hair, use a heat protectant spray and limit the frequency of your heat styling.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Keeping your curls healthy and beautiful requires a consistent hair care routine. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and frizz.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can cause dryness and frizz. Instead, use lukewarm water.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel to dry it, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, gently squeeze the excess water out of your hair with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt.

By following these tips and choosing a flattering haircut for your face shape and hair type, you can rock your curly hair with confidence and style.
