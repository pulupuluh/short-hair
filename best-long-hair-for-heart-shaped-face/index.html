Best Long Hair For Heart Shaped Face Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Mar 09, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you know how important it is to choose the right hairstyle. The right haircut can accentuate your features and flatter your face shape, while the wrong one can make you look unbalanced. If you have long hair, you have a lot of options to choose from that can complement your heart shaped face. In this article, we will go over the best long hair for heart shaped face, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

  • How to Choose the Best Long Hair for Heart Shaped Face
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Heart Shaped Face
  • Tips for Maintaining Long Hair for Heart Shaped Face
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Long Hair for Heart Shaped Face

Description

Heart shaped faces have a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow, pointy chin. If you have a heart shaped face, you want to balance your features by choosing a hairstyle that adds volume to the chin and the bottom of the face. Long hair is a great option for heart shaped faces because it can help elongate the face and add volume to the bottom. However, you want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, as this can make your forehead appear even wider.

How to Choose the Best Long Hair for Heart Shaped Face

When choosing the best long hair for heart shaped face, you want to consider your hair texture and your personal style. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Layers are your friend: Layers can help add volume to the bottom of the face and soften the cheekbones. Consider adding layers to your long hair to create movement and dimension.
  • Avoid blunt cuts: Blunt cuts can make your chin appear even more narrow. Instead, opt for a cut with soft, face-framing layers.
  • Side-swept bangs: Side-swept bangs can help balance your forehead and add volume to the bottom of the face. Consider getting long, side-swept bangs that blend seamlessly with the rest of your hair.
  • Consider highlights: Highlights can add dimension and depth to your hair, which can be especially flattering for heart shaped faces. However, avoid highlights that are too chunky or placed at the top of the head.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Heart Shaped Face

Styling long hair for heart shaped face can be easy and fun. Here's a step by step guide to help you get started:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Using a curling iron or a straightener, create loose waves or curls in your hair. Make sure to curl the bottom of your hair to add volume and balance to the face.
  4. If you have side-swept bangs, use a round brush and a blow dryer to style them. If you don't have bangs, consider creating a deep side part to add volume to the bottom of the face.
  5. Finish off your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your curls or waves in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair for Heart Shaped Face

Long hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to help you take care of your long hair for heart shaped face:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair. Try air drying your hair or using heatless styling methods instead.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best haircut for heart shaped face?

The best haircut for heart shaped face is one that adds volume to the bottom of the face and balances the forehead. Long hair with face-framing layers and side-swept bangs is a great option for heart shaped faces.

Can heart shaped face have short hair?

Yes, heart shaped faces can have short hair. However, you want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head, as this can make your forehead appear even wider. Consider a short bob with face-framing layers or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs.

What kind of highlights are best for heart shaped face?

The best highlights for heart shaped face are those that add dimension and depth to the hair. Consider subtle highlights that are placed around the face to add volume to the bottom of the face.

Hair Care for Long Hair for Heart Shaped Face

Long hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for long hair for heart shaped face:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair. Try air drying your hair or using heatless styling methods instead.
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

Choosing the best long hair for heart shaped face can seem daunting, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and complements your personal style. Remember to take care of your hair by using high quality products and maintaining a regular hair care routine.
