How To Style Short Pixie Haircut A Complete Guide

If you've recently gotten a short pixie haircut or are considering getting one, you may be wondering how to style it. The good news is that styling a short pixie cut is easy and versatile, allowing you to create a different look every day. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to style your short pixie cut and provide tips and FAQ to help you achieve the perfect look.

How to Style Short Pixie Haircut

Styling a short pixie haircut can be done in a few simple steps:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair or dampen your hair with a spray bottle.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product such as mousse, gel, or pomade to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush or use your fingers to create volume and texture.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls to your hair if desired.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Now that you know the basic steps, let's dive into a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently pat dry with a towel. If you prefer, you can also dampen your hair with a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product such as mousse, gel, or pomade to your hair. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and ends.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush or your fingers, blow-dry your hair while creating volume and texture. If you have a short pixie cut with longer layers, use a round brush to lift the hair at the roots and create volume. For a more textured look, use your fingers to tousle your hair while blow-drying.

Step 4: Add Waves or Curls (Optional)

If you want to add waves or curls to your hair, use a flat iron or curling iron to create the desired look. For loose waves, wrap small sections of hair around the barrel of a curling iron and hold for a few seconds before releasing. For tighter curls, use a smaller barrel curling iron and hold for a longer period of time.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light hairspray to hold your style in place. Spray the hairspray evenly over your hair, holding the can about 10 inches away from your head.

Tips for Styling Short Pixie Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short pixie haircut:

  • Use a volumizing mousse to create volume and texture in your hair.
  • Be gentle when blow-drying your hair to avoid damaging your locks.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron with a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Invest in a good quality hairspray to hold your style in place.

FAQs

Can I style my short pixie haircut without heat?

Yes, you can style your short pixie haircut without heat. You can use styling products such as mousse, gel, or pomade to create volume and texture in your hair.

How often should I wash my short pixie haircut?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may be able to go longer between washes. As a general rule, aim to wash your hair every two to three days.

Hair Care for Short Pixie Haircut

To keep your short pixie haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Apply a weekly hair mask to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons too often to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach or hair dyes that can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to style your short pixie haircut, it's time to experiment with different looks and find the perfect style for you. With the right products and techniques, you can create a variety of styles that will have you looking and feeling your best.
