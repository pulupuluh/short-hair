How To Style Short Grey Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Mar 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're one of the many women who have embraced their natural grey hair, you may be wondering how to style it to make the most of your new look. Styling short grey hair can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can create a chic, modern style that flatters your face and complements your hair color.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Grey Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Grey Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Grey Hair

How to Style Short Grey Hair

Short grey hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and sophisticated to edgy and modern. Here are some popular options to consider:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. For grey hair, consider adding layers and texture to create a more dynamic look.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is another versatile option for short grey hair. You can choose a blunt cut for a more polished look, or go for a layered bob to add movement and volume to your hair.

Short Shag

A short shag is a great choice if you want a low-maintenance style that still looks chic and modern. This style features choppy layers and texture, and can be styled in a variety of ways depending on your mood and outfit.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to style short grey hair in a few simple steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair tends to be greasy or oily, use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil and add volume.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a mousse or hair wax. This will help to add texture and hold your style in place.
  3. Using a comb or your fingers, style your hair into the desired shape. You can use a blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair, or let it air dry for a more natural look.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place and add shine.

Tips for Styling Short Grey Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your short grey hair:

  • Experiment with different styling products until you find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add depth and dimension to your color.
  • Use a round brush to add volume to your hair while blow drying. Start at the roots and work your way to the ends for maximum volume.
  • Accessorize your hair with hairpins, headbands, or scarves to add a pop of color and style to your look.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my grey hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your grey hair if you want to change your hair color. However, it's important to choose a hair dye that is specifically formulated for grey hair, as grey hair can be more resistant to color than other hair types.

Q: What is the best haircut for short grey hair?

A: The best haircut for short grey hair depends on your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Some popular options include the pixie cut, bob cut, and short shag.

Hair Care for Short Grey Hair

Short grey hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to help you care for your short grey hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and polished.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your short grey hair with confidence and flair. Whether you prefer a sleek and sophisticated look or a more edgy and modern style, you can create a chic and flattering look that complements your natural hair color and highlights your unique beauty.
