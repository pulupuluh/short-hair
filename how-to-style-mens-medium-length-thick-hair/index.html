How to Style Men's Medium Length Thick Hair

Styling men's medium length thick hair is all about finding the right products and techniques to tame your locks. Here's how to do it:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo that's designed for thick hair. This will help to remove any excess oil or product buildup and leave your hair feeling clean and refreshed.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair. This will help to moisturize your hair and make it more manageable. Be sure to distribute the conditioner evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair until it's about 80% dry. Use your fingers to tousle your hair as you dry it, which will help to create volume and texture.

4. Apply Styling Product

Once your hair is mostly dry, apply a styling product that's designed for medium length thick hair. You can use a pomade, wax, or gel, depending on the look you're going for.

5. Style Your Hair

Style your hair by using your fingers or a comb to create the desired look. You can create a messy, textured look or a more polished style, depending on your preference.

Tips for Styling Men's Medium Length Thick Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your hair with ease:

1. Experiment with Different Products

Try different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling it.

3. Don't Overdo It

Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

4. Don't Overheat Your Hair

Don't use a blow dryer or styling tools on high heat, as this can damage your hair.


Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

What type of styling product should I use?

It depends on the look you're going for. Pomades are great for a sleek, polished look, while waxes and gels are better for a messy, textured look.

How can I make my hair look thicker?

You can use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow dry your hair with your head upside down, or use a thickening spray or mousse.


Hair Care for Men's Medium Length Thick Hair

To keep your hair looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

1. Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for thick hair.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using high heat on your hair, as this can damage it.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily style your men's medium length thick hair and keep it looking its best!
