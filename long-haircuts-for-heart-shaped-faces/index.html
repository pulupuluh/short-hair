Long Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces Flattering Styles For 2023

Written by William Jul 31, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape while also showing off your long locks. Look no further because we have compiled a list of the best long haircuts for heart-shaped faces. Read on to discover the perfect style for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Long Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Description

Heart-shaped faces have a wide forehead and cheekbones that narrow into a pointed chin. The goal of a long haircut for this face shape is to balance out the forehead and chin while accentuating the cheekbones. Layers are key to achieving this look. They soften the angles of the face and add movement to long hair. Here are our top picks for long haircuts for heart-shaped faces in 2023.

1. Long Layers

A long layer cut is the most versatile and flattering style for heart-shaped faces. It adds volume and texture to the hair, which balances out the face's proportions. The layers should start at the chin and blend downward. This cut is perfect for those who want to keep their hair long and flowing.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

If you want to add a little drama to your long haircut, side-swept bangs are the way to go. They draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones while softening your forehead's width. This style works best for those with long and thick hair.

3. Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers start at the cheekbones and add softness and dimension to the hair. They bring attention to the eyes and cheekbones, making them perfect for heart-shaped faces. This style works on all hair types and lengths.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

Choosing the right haircut for your heart-shaped face can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect style:

  • Consider your hair type and texture. Some styles work better on thick hair while others work better on fine hair.
  • Think about your lifestyle. Are you willing to spend time styling your hair every day or do you prefer a low-maintenance style?
  • Consult with your stylist. They can help you choose a style that flatters your face shape and works with your hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Now that you have chosen the perfect long haircut for your heart-shaped face, it's time to style it. Follow these steps for a flawless look:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the roots.
  4. Use a curling iron to add loose waves to the ends of your hair.
  5. Apply a texturizing spray to add movement and hold to your hair.
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Long Haircut

Maintaining a long haircut requires a little extra effort, but it's worth it. Here are some tips to help you keep your long locks looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs

1. Can heart-shaped faces wear short haircuts?

Yes, heart-shaped faces can wear short haircuts, but they should avoid styles that are too short or blunt. A layered bob or pixie cut with side-swept bangs is the best option.

2. What hair color is best for heart-shaped faces?

Any hair color can work for heart-shaped faces, but warm tones like honey blonde, copper, and chestnut brown are particularly flattering. They add warmth to the face and bring out the cheekbones.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Long hair requires special care to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips to help you take care of your long locks:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote hair growth.

With the right long haircut and hair care routine, heart-shaped faces can rock long locks like no other. Choose the style that best fits your personality and lifestyle, and don't be afraid to experiment with different looks. After all, hair is the ultimate accessory.
