The Best Hair Cream For Thick Curly Hair A 2023 Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 19, 2023 · 7 min read

When it comes to managing thick curly hair, finding the right hair cream can be a game changer. With so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to figure out which one will work best for your hair type. That’s why we’ve put together this guide to help you find the best hair cream for your thick curly hair in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Hair Cream for Thick Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Applying Hair Cream
  • Tips for Keeping Thick Curly Hair Healthy
  • FAQs about Hair Cream for Thick Curly Hair
  • The Importance of Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Description

Hair cream is a versatile product that can help with a variety of hair concerns. It can add moisture, define curls, and tame frizz. When it comes to thick curly hair, a good hair cream can help enhance curls and keep them defined throughout the day.

The best hair cream for thick curly hair will be lightweight and provide enough hold to keep curls in place without weighing them down. It should also be moisturizing to help combat dryness, which is a common concern for curly hair.

How to Choose the Best Hair Cream for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some things to keep in mind when selecting a hair cream for your thick curly hair:

  • Look for a cream that is specifically formulated for curly hair. These products often contain ingredients that help define curls and combat frizz.
  • Choose a cream that is lightweight and won’t weigh down your curls. Heavy creams can cause curls to become limp and lose their shape.
  • Consider the level of hold you need. If you have thick hair, you may need a cream with a stronger hold to keep your curls in place.
  • Check the ingredients list for moisturizing ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil. These ingredients can help combat dryness and keep your curls looking healthy.

Step-by-Step Guide to Applying Hair Cream

Once you’ve chosen the best hair cream for your thick curly hair, it’s important to know how to apply it properly. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. If your hair is too wet, the product won’t distribute evenly. If it’s too dry, the cream won’t be able to penetrate the hair shaft.
  2. Squeeze a quarter-sized amount of product into your palm.
  3. Rub your palms together to distribute the product evenly.
  4. Flip your head upside down and scrunch the product into your hair, focusing on the ends.
  5. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and prevent frizz.

Tips for Keeping Thick Curly Hair Healthy

Here are some tips to help keep your thick curly hair looking healthy:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for curly hair. These products will help keep your curls moisturized and defined.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause breakage.

FAQs about Hair Cream for Thick Curly Hair

Q: Can hair cream be used on wet or dry hair?

A: Hair cream can be used on both wet and dry hair, but it’s best applied to damp hair to help distribute the product evenly.

Q: How often should I use hair cream?

A: This will depend on your hair type and how often you wash your hair. If you have thick curly hair that tends to be dry, you may want to use hair cream every time you wash your hair to help keep it moisturized.

Q: Can hair cream be used with other styling products?

A: Yes, hair cream can be used with other styling products like gel or mousse to help enhance curls and provide additional hold.

The Importance of Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Thick curly hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and defined. Using the right hair cream is just one part of a comprehensive hair care routine. By following the tips outlined in this guide, you can help keep your thick curly hair looking its best in 2023 and beyond.
