How To Wash Curly Lace Front Wig

Written by Robby Jun 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly lace front wigs are a great way to switch up your style and add some volume to your hair. However, caring for your curly lace front wig can be a challenge, especially when it comes to washing. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to wash your curly lace front wig and keep it looking great for longer.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wash Curly Lace Front Wig
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Washing Curly Lace Front Wig
  • Tips for Washing Curly Lace Front Wig
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Lace Front Wig

Step-by-Step Guide to Washing Curly Lace Front Wig

Before washing your curly lace front wig, you'll need to gather some supplies. You'll need a wig stand, a wide-tooth comb, a spray bottle with water, a wig shampoo, and a wig conditioner. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Detangle the Wig

Before washing your wig, you'll need to detangle it. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb out any knots or tangles. Start at the ends of the hair and work your way up to the roots.

Step 2: Wet the Wig

Once your wig is detangled, wet it with a spray bottle filled with water. Be sure to saturate the hair evenly.

Step 3: Apply Shampoo

Apply a small amount of wig shampoo to the hair. Gently massage the shampoo into the hair, working from the roots to the ends. Avoid rubbing or twisting the hair, as this can cause tangling.

Step 4: Rinse the Wig

Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water. Be sure to rinse out all the shampoo to avoid buildup.

Step 5: Apply Conditioner

Apply a small amount of wig conditioner to the hair. Gently work the conditioner through the hair with your fingers, focusing on the ends. Let the conditioner sit for a few minutes, then rinse it out with cool water.

Step 6: Dry the Wig

Once you've rinsed out the conditioner, gently blot the wig with a towel to remove excess water. Place the wig on a wig stand and allow it to air dry. Avoid using a hair dryer, as this can damage the hair.

Tips for Washing Curly Lace Front Wig

Here are some tips to keep in mind when washing your curly lace front wig:

  • Use cool water to wash your wig. Hot water can damage the hair fibers.
  • Be gentle when washing and detangling your wig to avoid tangling and shedding.
  • Avoid using regular shampoo and conditioner on your wig. Use products specifically designed for wigs.
  • Don't twist or rub your wig when washing or drying it. This can cause tangles and damage the hair.
  • Always air dry your wig on a wig stand to maintain its shape and prevent damage.

FAQs

How often should I wash my curly lace front wig?

You should wash your wig every 6-8 wears, or as needed if it becomes dirty or oily.

Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my wig?

No, you should use products specifically designed for wigs to avoid damaging the hair fibers.

Can I blow dry my wig?

No, you should avoid using a hair dryer on your wig to prevent damage to the hair fibers.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Lace Front Wig

In addition to washing your wig, here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head when you're not wearing it to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your wig, as this can damage the hair fibers.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your wig, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to harsh chemicals, such as chlorine or saltwater, as this can damage the hair fibers.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can keep your curly lace front wig looking great for longer.
