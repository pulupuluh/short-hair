What Hairstyle Suits Fat Face

Written by Robby Jul 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Having a fat face is not a problem when it comes to hairstyles. With the right style, you can look absolutely stunning. It's all about finding the right balance between your facial features and your hair. In this article, we will discuss what hairstyle suits fat face, how to achieve it, and some tips and tricks to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Description

A fat face is generally characterized by roundness in the cheeks and jawline. It's important to choose a hairstyle that will accentuate your best features while minimizing the roundness of your face. The right hairstyle can also help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones.

How to

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for a fat face is to determine your face shape. There are four main face shapes: oval, round, square, and heart-shaped. Once you have determined your face shape, you can then choose a hairstyle that will complement it.

If you have a round face, you should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and length. A high ponytail, a messy bun, or a layered bob are all great options. If you prefer longer hair, consider adding layers to create movement and texture.

If you have a square face, you should choose hairstyles that soften your features. Soft waves or curls can help to create a more feminine look. A long bob or a shoulder-length cut with layers can also be flattering. Avoid straight, blunt cuts as they can make your face appear even more square.

If you have a heart-shaped face, you should aim to balance out your features. A side-swept fringe or a layered bob can help to soften your forehead and create a more balanced look. Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape
Step 2: Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape
Step 3: Consider adding layers for movement and texture
Step 4: Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face
Step 5: Soften your features with waves or curls
Step 6: Consider a side-swept fringe or layered bob to balance out your features

Tips

1. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles
2. Consider getting a professional haircut to ensure the best results
3. Use hair products that are designed for your hair type
4. Avoid hairstyles that require a lot of maintenance if you don't have the time
5. Be patient â it may take some time to find the perfect hairstyle for you

FAQ

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have a fat face?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a fat face. However, it's important to choose a style that will complement your face shape. Consider adding layers to create movement and texture.

Q: What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

A: The best hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height and length. A high ponytail, a messy bun, or a layered bob are all great options.

Q: Can I still have bangs if I have a fat face?

A: Yes, you can still have bangs if you have a fat face. However, it's important to choose a style that will complement your face shape. Consider a side-swept fringe to soften your forehead.

Hair Care

It's important to take good care of your hair to ensure it looks its best. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can cause damage. Consider using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can look absolutely stunning with a fat face. Remember to experiment and have fun with your hair â after all, it's one of your best accessories!
