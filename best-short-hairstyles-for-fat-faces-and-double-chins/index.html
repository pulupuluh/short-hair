Best Short Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins

Written by Oliver Mar 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your round or chubby face? If you have a double chin and want to slim down your look, a short hairstyle can be a game-changer. In this article, we will explore the best short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins to help you feel confident and beautiful.

Description

Having a round face or a double chin can make it challenging to find a flattering haircut. However, with the right style and cut, you can accentuate your best features and make your face appear slimmer. Short hairstyles are ideal for women with fat faces and double chins as they create a more defined jawline and draw attention away from the cheeks.

How to

The key to finding the perfect short hairstyle for your face shape is to consider the length, texture, and volume. Short haircuts that are layered, textured, or tapered towards the back of the head can create a slimming effect and add volume to the crown. Bangs can also help to balance out the face and draw attention to the eyes.

Step by Step

1. Start by consulting with your hairstylist and discussing your face shape and features.

2. Choose a short hairstyle that complements your face shape and enhances your best features.

3. Consider adding layers, texture, or volume to create a more defined jawline and slim down your face.

4. Opt for bangs that suit your face shape and draw attention to your eyes.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for fat faces and double chins:

- Avoid hairstyles that are too short or blunt as they can make the face appear wider.

- Opt for a side part to create a more asymmetrical look and draw attention away from the cheeks.

- Consider highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.

FAQ

Q: What is the best short hairstyle for a round face?

A: The best short hairstyle for a round face is one that creates volume at the crown and adds length to the face. Layered haircuts, textured bobs, and asymmetrical pixie cuts are all great options.

Q: Can bangs help to slim down the face?

A: Yes, bangs can help to balance out the face and draw attention to the eyes. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are ideal for women with round faces or double chins.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and vibrant look. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture. Avoid using too much heat or styling products that can damage your hair. Regular trims can also help to keep your short haircut looking fresh and stylish.

Now that you know the best short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new look. With the right style and cut, you can feel confident and beautiful every day.
