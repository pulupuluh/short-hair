What Is An 8 Guard On Clippers

Written by Frank May 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

When it comes to haircuts, the term guard might not be something you're familiar with. But if you've ever been to a barber or hair salon, you've probably seen a set of clippers. Clippers are an essential tool for many haircuts, and the guards that come with them determine the length of the cut. In this article, we'll specifically talk about what an 8 guard on clippers is and how to use it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use an 8 Guard on Clippers
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using an 8 Guard on Clippers
  • FAQs

Description

An 8 guard on clippers is a guard that measures 1 inch or 25 millimeters in length. It's one of the longer guards that you can use, and it's typically used for longer haircuts like buzz cuts or crew cuts. This guard is ideal for people who want a shorter haircut without going too short, or for those who want a more natural look without too much hair being cut off.

The 8 guard is designed to be used with clippers, which are electric tools that have a motor that moves a set of blades back and forth. The blades are covered by the guard, which determines the length of the cut. Guards are numbered, and each number corresponds to a specific length. The higher the number, the longer the cut.

How to Use an 8 Guard on Clippers

Using an 8 guard on clippers is relatively straightforward. You'll need a set of clippers that come with an 8 guard, a comb, and a pair of scissors. Here's how to do it:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by combing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly.
  2. Attach the 8 guard to your clippers. Make sure that it's securely attached, and that the blades are clean and oiled.
  3. Turn on the clippers and start cutting your hair. Begin at the sides and move up to the top of your head. Make sure that you're cutting in the direction of your hair growth.
  4. Use the comb to lift sections of hair that you want to cut. Hold the comb at a 45-degree angle and run the clippers over the comb. This will help to create a more even cut.
  5. Continue cutting your hair until you've achieved the desired length.
  6. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs that the clippers might have missed.

Tips for Using an 8 Guard on Clippers

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results when using an 8 guard on clippers:

  • Make sure that your clippers are clean and oiled before using them. This will help them to cut more smoothly and reduce the risk of pulling or tugging on your hair.
  • Use the comb to lift sections of hair that you want to cut. This will help you to create a more even cut and prevent you from cutting too much hair at once.
  • Don't forget to trim the hair around your ears and the back of your neck. These areas can be easy to overlook, but they can make a big difference in the overall look of your haircut.

FAQs

What other guard lengths are available?

Guards come in a variety of lengths, ranging from 0.5 to 8. The 0.5 guard is the shortest, while the 8 guard is the longest. There are also guards that come in between these lengths, such as the 1, 2, and 3 guards.

Can I use an 8 guard on curly hair?

Yes, you can use an 8 guard on curly hair. However, keep in mind that curly hair tends to look shorter than it actually is. So, if you want a longer haircut, you might need to use a longer guard than you would for straight hair.

Can I use an 8 guard for a bald fade?

No, an 8 guard is too long to use for a bald fade. For a bald fade, you'll need to use a shorter guard, such as a 0.5 or 1 guard.

Hair Care Tips

After you've finished cutting your hair with an 8 guard, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as hair dryers and flat irons.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and using an 8 guard on clippers, you can achieve a great haircut that looks great and is easy to maintain.
