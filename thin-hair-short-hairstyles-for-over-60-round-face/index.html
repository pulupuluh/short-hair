Thin Hair Short Hairstyles For Over 60 Round Face Tips And Ideas

Written by Bardi May 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you over 60 with a round face and thin hair? Do you struggle to find hairstyles that suit your features? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many women over 60 have concerns about their hair, but with the right haircut, you can still look fabulous. In this article, we will explore some of the best thin hair short hairstyles for over 60 round face, along with tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

As we age, our hair becomes thinner, and our face shape changes. If you have a round face, you may want to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and makes it look slimmer. Short haircuts are a great option for women over 60 because they are low maintenance and can make you look younger. However, finding the right short hairstyle for thin hair can be challenging. You want a style that adds volume and texture without weighing down your hair.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

Consider Your Face Shape

If you have a round face, choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the crown of your head. This will elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

Choose a Short Hairstyle

Short haircuts are the best option for thin hair because they add volume and texture. They also require less maintenance than longer hairstyles.

Add Layers

Layers are a great way to add volume and texture to thin hair. They also create movement and make your hair look thicker.

Avoid Heavy Products

Heavy products can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Opt for lightweight products that add volume and texture.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for thin hair. This will help to add volume and texture.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause damage.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product that is designed for thin hair. This will add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and texture. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, style it using a flat iron or curling iron. Add hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Caring for Thin Hair

Here are some tips for caring for thin hair:

Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is designed for thin hair. This will help to prevent damage and breakage.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools on a regular basis, as this can cause damage to your hair. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray.

Be Gentle When Brushing Your Hair

Be gentle when brushing your hair, as rough brushing can cause damage and breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This will also help to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

FAQ

What are the best hairstyles for thin hair?

The best hairstyles for thin hair are short haircuts with layers. These styles add volume and texture to thin hair.

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is a style that adds height and volume to the crown of your head, such as a layered bob or a pixie cut.

How can I make my hair look thicker?

You can make your hair look thicker by adding layers, using volumizing products, and avoiding heavy products that weigh down your hair.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle for thin hair and a round face can be challenging, but with the right haircut and styling products, you can look fabulous at any age. Remember to choose a short hairstyle with layers, avoid heavy products, and take care of your hair with gentle shampoo and regular trims. With these tips, you can rock any thin hair short hairstyles for over 60 round face with confidence.
