How To Get Rid Of Undercut Haircut A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Sep 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your undercut haircut and want to change your look? Don't worry; we've got you covered. In this article, we will guide you through the process of getting rid of an undercut haircut. We will cover everything from how to do it, step by step, to hair care tips to keep your hair healthy after the change. Let's get started.

Description

An undercut haircut is a style where the hair on the sides and back of the head is shaved, leaving a longer layer of hair on the top. This style has been popular among men and women for a while now. However, if you want to switch to a different hairstyle, you need to get rid of your undercut. The good news is that you can do it yourself at home without going to a salon.

How to Get Rid of Undercut Haircut

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get rid of your undercut haircut:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, and a clipper with a guard. Make sure that the guard is not too short. You can use a #2 or #3 guard.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections. Use a comb to create a parting in the middle of your head from the front to the back. Then, divide the hair on the sides into sections and clip them away from the middle parting.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting the hair on the sides with the clipper and the #2 or #3 guard. Cut the hair to the same length as the hair on the top of your head. Make sure that you blend the hair on the sides with the hair on the top.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Cut the hair on the top of your head to the desired length using a pair of scissors. You can leave it long or cut it short, depending on the style you want.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as you normally would. You can use a hair dryer, a straightener, or a curling iron to create your desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting your hair.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want shorter hair.
  • If you are not confident in cutting your hair, ask a friend or family member to help you.

FAQ

1. Can I get rid of my undercut haircut without cutting my hair?

No, you cannot. The only way to get rid of an undercut haircut is to cut your hair.

2. Can I shave my head to get rid of my undercut?

Yes, you can. If you don't mind having a buzz cut or shaved head, you can shave your head to get rid of your undercut.

3. How long will it take for my hair to grow back after getting rid of my undercut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month. So, it may take a few months to grow back to the length you want.

Hair Care

After getting rid of your undercut, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips, you can get rid of your undercut haircut and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
