What Hair Colour Suits Thin Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your thin hair looking dull and lifeless? Do you want to add some dimension to your hair without making it look even thinner? One solution is to try a new hair colour. But, with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this article, we will guide you on what hair colour suits thin hair and how to take care of it.

Description

Thin hair can be tricky to style and colour. It lacks volume, and the wrong hair colour can make it look even thinner. However, with the right hair colour, you can create the illusion of thickness and add some personality to your hair. The key is to choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and natural hair colour.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour for Thin Hair

Choosing the right hair colour for thin hair can be a daunting task. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair colour:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a crucial role in determining which hair colour will suit you. If you have warm undertones, opt for warm colours like golden blonde, copper, and honey. If you have cool undertones, go for cool colours like ash blonde, platinum, and silver.

Consider Your Natural Hair Colour

Your natural hair colour is an excellent starting point when choosing a new hair colour. Stick to colours that are not too far from your natural shade. Going too far can make your hair look unhealthy and thin. For example, if you have light brown hair, go for a darker brown shade.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also influence your hair colour choice. If you have a busy lifestyle and don't have much time for maintenance, opt for a low-maintenance hair colour like balayage or ombre. These colours require less maintenance than full colour.

Step-by-Step Guide on Colouring Thin Hair

Colouring thin hair requires precision and care. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to colour thin hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Colour

Refer to the tips above to choose the right colour for your hair.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Wash your hair and let it dry completely. Comb your hair to get rid of any tangles.

Step 3: Protect Your Skin

Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline to protect your skin from the hair dye.

Step 4: Apply the Hair Dye

Apply the hair dye to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the tips. Use a brush to evenly distribute the dye. Leave the dye on for the recommended time.

Step 5: Rinse and Condition

Rinse your hair thoroughly with water until the water runs clear. Apply conditioner and let it sit for a few minutes before rinsing it off.

Tips to Take Care of Coloured Thin Hair

Coloured thin hair requires extra care to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some tips to take care of your coloured thin hair:

Use a Colour-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specially formulated for coloured hair. These products help maintain the colour and prevent it from fading.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons as they can damage your hair and make it look even thinner.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQs

What hair colour makes thin hair look thicker?

Opt for hair colours that create the illusion of thickness, like balayage or ombre.

Can I colour thin hair at home?

Yes, you can colour thin hair at home. However, it's best to seek the advice of a professional colourist if you're unsure about the process.

How often should I colour my thin hair?

Avoid colouring your hair too frequently as it can damage your hair. Stick to once every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Colour for Thin Hair - In Conclusion

Choosing the right hair colour for thin hair can make a world of difference. Keep in mind your skin tone, natural hair colour, and lifestyle when choosing a hair colour. Follow our step-by-step guide on colouring thin hair and take care of your coloured hair using our tips. With the right hair colour and care, your thin hair can look vibrant and healthy.
