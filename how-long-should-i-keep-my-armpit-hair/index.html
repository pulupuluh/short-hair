How Long Should I Keep My Armpit Hair

Written by Oliver Feb 24, 2023 · 7 min read

When it comes to body hair, there’s no right or wrong answer as to how long you should keep it. However, armpit hair is a topic that many people have different opinions on. Some choose to shave it all off, while others let it grow naturally. So, how long should you keep your armpit hair? Let’s explore this topic in more detail.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Armpit hair is a natural occurrence in both men and women. It’s a type of body hair that grows in the armpit area and serves as a protective layer for the skin. The hair traps moisture and sweat, which can cause bacteria to grow and lead to body odor. However, some people choose to keep their armpit hair long for personal, cultural, or religious reasons. Others prefer to remove it for aesthetic reasons or to reduce body odor.

How to

There are several ways to keep your armpit hair, depending on your personal preference:

Shave it off

Shaving is the most common and quickest way to remove armpit hair. It’s also the most temporary, as hair will grow back quickly. Shaving can be done with a razor, an electric shaver, or a depilatory cream.

Trim it

Trimming is a good option for those who want to keep some hair but don’t want it to be too long. You can use scissors or clippers to trim the hair to your desired length.

Let it grow

If you prefer to keep your armpit hair long, then you don’t have to do anything. Just let it grow and trim it occasionally to prevent it from becoming too long.

Step by step

Shaving

  1. Wet your armpits with warm water to soften the hair and open pores.
  2. Apply shaving cream or gel to the armpit area.
  3. Shave in the direction of hair growth, using a sharp razor.
  4. Rinse the area with cool water and pat dry with a towel.
  5. Apply a soothing lotion or cream to prevent irritation.

Trimming

  1. Use scissors or clippers to trim the hair to your desired length.
  2. Be careful not to cut yourself or trim the hair too short.
  3. Rinse the area with water and pat dry with a towel.
  4. Apply a moisturizer to prevent dryness.

Letting it grow

Simply let your armpit hair grow naturally and trim it occasionally to prevent it from becoming too long.

Tips

  • Wash your armpits daily to prevent bacteria and body odor.
  • Exfoliate your armpits regularly to remove dead skin cells and prevent ingrown hairs.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your armpits, such as bleach or hair dye.
  • If you experience irritation or razor burn from shaving, try using a different shaving cream or razor.
  • Consider using a natural deodorant to reduce body odor without harsh chemicals.

FAQ

Is it unhygienic to keep armpit hair?

No, it’s not unhygienic to keep armpit hair as long as you wash your armpits regularly and practice good hygiene habits.

Does armpit hair cause body odor?

Armpit hair can trap moisture and sweat, which can lead to the growth of bacteria and cause body odor. However, good hygiene habits can help prevent this.

Is it safe to shave my armpits?

Yes, it’s safe to shave your armpits as long as you take precautions to prevent irritation and infection.

Hair Care

If you choose to keep your armpit hair, it’s important to take care of it just like you would with the hair on your head. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep it soft and smooth. You can also use a hair oil or serum to add shine and prevent frizz.

In conclusion, how long you keep your armpit hair is a personal choice. Whether you choose to shave it off, trim it, or let it grow naturally, it’s important to practice good hygiene habits to prevent bacteria and body odor.
