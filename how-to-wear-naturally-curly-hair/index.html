How To Wear Naturally Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jun 21, 2023 · 8 min read

As someone with naturally curly hair, it can be challenging to know how to style and wear your hair in a way that feels effortless and natural. However, with the right tips and tricks, you can embrace your curls and feel confident in your hairstyle. In this article, we’ll go over some ways to wear naturally curly hair that will enhance your natural texture and showcase your unique style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Naturally Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Naturally Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Naturally Curly Hair

How to Wear Naturally Curly Hair

The key to wearing naturally curly hair is to embrace your texture and work with it, rather than fighting against it. Here are some ways to style and wear your curls:

1. Let it air dry

Ditch the blow dryer and let your curls air dry for a more natural look. After washing your hair, gently squeeze out any excess water with a microfiber towel or t-shirt. Then, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream and scrunch your hair upward to encourage your curls to form.

2. Use a diffuser

If you’re short on time, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the drying process. This will help to reduce frizz and enhance your curls. Make sure to use a low or medium heat setting and avoid touching your hair too much while drying.

3. Style with a headband or scarf

Add some flair to your curly hair by styling it with a headband or scarf. This is a great option for days when you don’t have time to style your hair, or when you want to add some extra volume and texture to your curls.

4. Braid or twist your hair

Braids and twists are a great way to change up your hairstyle and add some definition to your curls. Simply braid or twist small sections of your hair and leave them in overnight. In the morning, unravel the braids or twists and gently separate your curls with your fingers.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your naturally curly hair:

1. Wash your hair

Start with clean, freshly washed hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream

Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to wet hair. Make sure to distribute the product evenly through your hair, focusing on the ends.

3. Scrunch your hair upward

Scrunch your hair upward with your hands to encourage your curls to form. This will help to enhance your natural texture and reduce frizz.

4. Let it air dry or use a diffuser

Let your hair air dry for a more natural look, or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to speed up the process.

5. Style with a headband or scarf (optional)

To add some extra flair to your curls, style your hair with a headband or scarf.

6. Braid or twist your hair (optional)

If you want to switch up your hairstyle, try braiding or twisting small sections of your hair and leaving them in overnight.

Tips for Styling Naturally Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling your naturally curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it’s wet.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Don’t be afraid to trim your hair regularly to keep your curls looking healthy and defined.

FAQs

Q. Should I use a sulfate-free shampoo?

A. Yes, using a sulfate-free shampoo can help to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils, which is essential for maintaining healthy, defined curls.

Q. Can I straighten my naturally curly hair?

A. Yes, you can straighten your hair if you want to switch up your look. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Hair Care for Naturally Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your naturally curly hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and cause frizz.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with UV protection.

By following these tips and tricks, you can wear your naturally curly hair with confidence and style. Embrace your unique texture and showcase your individuality with a beautiful, natural hairstyle.
