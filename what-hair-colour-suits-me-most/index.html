What Hair Colour Suits Me Most A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jul 08, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour and looking to switch it up? Choosing a new hair colour can be a daunting task, but with the right information, you can find the perfect shade for you. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about what hair colour suits you most, from how to choose the right shade to hair care tips to maintain your new look. Let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour

Choosing the right hair colour can be challenging, especially if you're not sure where to start. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect shade:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair colour. If you have warm undertones, you'll want to choose warm colours like golden browns or honey blonde. If you have cool undertones, you'll want to choose cool colours like ash blonde or platinum.

Think About Your Natural Hair Colour

If you're not looking for a drastic change, it's important to consider your natural hair colour when choosing a new shade. Going too far from your natural colour can be difficult to maintain and may not suit you as well as a more subtle change.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also be taken into consideration when choosing a new hair colour. If you're constantly on the go and don't have time for regular touch-ups, you may want to choose a low-maintenance colour like balayage or ombre.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing Your New Hair Colour

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a new hair colour, here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Use the veins on your wrist to determine if you have warm or cool undertones. If your veins appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones.

Step 2: Consider Your Natural Hair Colour

Think about how much of a change you're comfortable with. If you're looking for a subtle change, choose a shade that's similar to your natural hair colour. If you want a more drastic change, consider going several shades lighter or darker.

Step 3: Choose a Colour Based on Your Skin Tone

Use your skin tone to guide your choice of hair colour. If you have warm undertones, choose warm colours like golden or caramel. If you have cool undertones, choose cool colours like ash blonde or platinum.

Step 4: Consider Your Lifestyle

Think about how much time you have for regular touch-ups. If you're busy and don't have time for regular trips to the salon, choose a low-maintenance colour like balayage or ombre.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Colour

Once you've chosen your new hair colour, it's essential to take care of it to keep it looking fresh and vibrant. Here are some tips:

Use Colour-Protecting Products

Invest in shampoos, conditioners, and styling products specifically designed for coloured hair. These products will help protect your new colour from fading.

Avoid Hot Tools

Excessive heat can cause your hair colour to fade quickly. Try to avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons as much as possible.

Get Regular Touch-Ups

If you want to maintain your hair colour, it's essential to get regular touch-ups. How often you need to touch up your colour will depend on the type of colour you choose and your hair growth rate.

FAQs About Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Q: How do I know if a hair colour will suit me?

A: Consider your skin tone and natural hair colour when choosing a new hair colour. If you're still unsure, consult with a professional stylist for their expert opinion.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but it's essential to follow the instructions carefully and choose the right shade for your skin tone and natural hair colour.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair colour can be a fun and exciting experience, but it's essential to do your research and take your time to make the right decision. By considering your skin tone, natural hair colour, and lifestyle, you can find the perfect shade for you. Remember to take care of your new hair colour with colour-protecting products, avoid excessive heat, and get regular touch-ups to keep it looking fresh and vibrant.
