What Kind Of Jeans Should A 40 Year Old Man Wear

Written by Oliver Aug 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a 40 year old man, you may be wondering what kind of jeans you should wear. With so many options out there, it can be tough to know where to start. In this article, we'll give you some tips on what kind of jeans you should wear to look stylish and age-appropriate.

Description

Jeans are a staple of any man's wardrobe, no matter what age you are. But as you get older, you may want to reconsider the types of jeans you wear. The right jeans can make you look stylish and put-together, while the wrong ones can make you look like you're trying too hard to look young.

How To

When it comes to choosing jeans, there are a few things to keep in mind:

Fit

The most important thing to consider when choosing jeans is the fit. As you get older, you may want to opt for a more relaxed fit, as tight jeans can look too youthful. Look for jeans that fit well in the waist and hips, but aren't too tight in the thighs or calves.

Wash

The wash of your jeans can also make a big difference in how age-appropriate they look. Light wash jeans can look too casual, while dark wash jeans can look more dressy. A medium wash is a good middle ground.

Style

When it comes to style, stick to classic styles that never go out of fashion. Straight-leg or bootcut jeans are always a safe bet. Avoid trendy styles like skinny jeans or distressed jeans.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to choosing the right jeans:

Step 1: Determine Your Body Type

Before you start shopping for jeans, take a look at your body type. Are you tall and slim, or shorter and more muscular? This will help you choose the right fit and style of jeans.

Step 2: Choose a Relaxed Fit

As you get older, a more relaxed fit is generally more flattering. Look for jeans that fit well in the waist and hips, but aren't too tight in the thighs or calves.

Step 3: Stick to Classic Styles

Avoid trendy styles like skinny jeans or distressed jeans. Stick to classic styles like straight-leg or bootcut jeans.

Step 4: Choose a Medium Wash

A medium wash is a good middle ground between light and dark wash jeans. It's dressy enough for a night out, but casual enough for everyday wear.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind:

Invest in Quality Jeans

A good pair of jeans is worth investing in. Look for high-quality denim that will last for years.

Dress Up Your Jeans

Jeans don't have to be casual. You can dress them up with a blazer and dress shoes for a night out.

Accessorize

Accessorize your jeans with a belt and watch to complete the look.

FAQ

What Kind of Shoes Should I Wear with Jeans?

For a casual look, sneakers or loafers are a good choice. For a dressier look, opt for dress shoes like oxfords or brogues.

Can I Wear Jeans to Work?

It depends on your workplace dress code. If your workplace is casual, jeans may be acceptable. If it's more formal, you may want to stick to dress pants.

Hair Care

While this article is about jeans, it's worth mentioning that taking care of your hair is also important as you get older. Consider getting a shorter haircut or using products to keep your hair looking healthy and styled.

Choosing the right jeans can make a big difference in how you look and feel. By following these tips, you can find the perfect pair of jeans that are both stylish and age-appropriate.
