Pixie Styles For Over 60S A Guide To Perfecting Your Look

Written by Bardi Jun 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're over 60 and looking to change up your hairstyle, consider a pixie cut. Pixie styles for over 60s are trendy, low-maintenance, and can make you look years younger. In this guide, we'll discuss everything you need to know about getting the perfect pixie cut, including how to style it, tips for maintenance, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that typically features short layers on top and longer layers on the sides and back. This style can be cut to fit any face shape and hair texture, making it a versatile option for women of all ages. Pixie styles for over 60s are designed to be easy to manage and require minimal styling, making them a great option for women who want to look good without spending a lot of time on their hair.

How to

The first step in getting a pixie cut is to find a stylist who specializes in this type of hairstyle. Look for someone who has experience working with older clients and who can help you choose a cut that will flatter your face shape and hair texture. Once you've found a stylist you like, schedule a consultation to discuss your options.

During your consultation, your stylist will likely ask you about your hair goals, lifestyle, and maintenance preferences. They may also take measurements of your face and head to ensure the cut is tailored to your features. Once you've agreed on a cut, your stylist will shampoo your hair and begin cutting.

After your hair is cut, your stylist will likely style it using a blow dryer and styling products. They may also show you how to style your hair at home to achieve the same look. Remember, the key to a great pixie cut is to keep it simple and low-maintenance.

Step by Step

1. Find a stylist who specializes in pixie cuts for older women.

2. Schedule a consultation to discuss your options.

3. Agree on a cut that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

4. Shampoo your hair and begin cutting.

5. Style your hair using a blow dryer and styling products.

Tips

1. Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

2. Use a volumizing spray to add lift to your hair.

3. Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.

4. Use a lightweight mousse or gel to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

5. Consider wearing a headband or hair accessory to add interest to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Will a pixie cut make me look older?

A: No, a pixie cut can actually make you look younger by highlighting your facial features and drawing attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Q: How often do I need to get my pixie cut trimmed?

A: It's recommended that you get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and prevent split ends.

Q: What if I don't like my pixie cut?

A: Don't panic! Hair grows back, and you can always try a different style or length next time.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your pixie cut looking its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining your style:

1. Wash your hair every other day to prevent over-drying.

2. Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair soft and manageable.

3. Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.

4. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.

5. Consider using a dry shampoo to extend the life of your style between washes.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to look stylish and confident in your new pixie cut. Remember, the key to any great hairstyle is to choose a cut that flatters your features and makes you feel good. Good luck!
