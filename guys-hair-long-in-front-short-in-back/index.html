Guys Hair Long In Front Short In Back A Bold And Stylish Look

Written by Luffy Apr 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a bold and stylish haircut, guys hair long in front short in back might be the perfect option for you. This haircut, also known as a mullet, has been around for decades and is making a comeback in 2023. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this trendy hairstyle, from how to style it to hair care tips.

Description

The guys hair long in front short in back haircut is characterized by longer hair on the top and front of the head, gradually getting shorter towards the back. The length of the hair can vary, but the key is to have a noticeable contrast between the front and back. This style is often associated with the 80s and 90s, but has recently gained popularity among younger generations.

How to Get the Look

If you're interested in getting the guys hair long in front short in back haircut, the first step is to find a skilled hairstylist. They will be able to give you the perfect cut and help you achieve the desired length and shape. To maintain the look, you'll need to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to get the guys hair long in front short in back haircut:

  1. Choose a skilled hairstylist
  2. Show them a picture of the desired haircut
  3. Determine the length of the hair
  4. Cut the hair shorter towards the back
  5. Blend the hair to create a smooth transition
  6. Style the hair as desired

Tips for Styling

Styling the guys hair long in front short in back haircut can be a bit tricky, but with the right techniques, you can achieve a stylish and modern look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality styling product to keep the hair in place
  • Blow dry the hair on low heat to create volume
  • Use a comb to create a sleek and polished look
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a messy or slicked back look

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the guys hair long in front short in back haircut:

Is the mullet still in style?

Yes, the mullet has made a comeback in recent years and is considered a trendy haircut.

What face shape is best for the mullet?

The mullet can work with any face shape, but it's best for those with a square or oval face.

Can you wear the mullet to a formal event?

While the mullet is a more casual look, you can style it in a way that's appropriate for a formal event.

Hair Care

To keep your guys hair long in front short in back haircut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh environmental factors

With these tips and tricks, you can rock the guys hair long in front short in back haircut with confidence and style. So go ahead and try this bold and trendy look for yourself!
