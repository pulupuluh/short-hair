How To Put Your Hair Up With Tape Extensions

Written by Oliver Feb 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have tape extensions in your hair, putting it up can be a bit tricky. You don't want to damage the extensions or have them show through your hair. In this blog post, we'll go over how to put your hair up with tape extensions and give you some tips on how to care for your extensions.

How to Put Your Hair Up with Tape Extensions

Putting your hair up with tape extensions is actually quite easy once you know how to do it. Follow these steps to put your hair up with tape extensions:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Before you put your hair up, make sure to brush it thoroughly. This will help to detangle any knots and make it easier to work with.

Step 2: Separate Your Hair

Separate your hair into two sections: the top and the bottom. Use a clip or hair tie to hold the top section out of the way.

Step 3: Create a Ponytail

Take the bottom section of your hair and create a ponytail. Make sure to include all of your hair, including the tape extensions. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail in place.

Step 4: Style Your Ponytail

Now that you have your ponytail secured in place, you can style it however you like. You can braid it, twist it, or leave it as is.

Step 5: Add the Top Section

Take the top section of your hair and release it from the clip or hair tie. Comb it out, making sure to include the tape extensions. Then, wrap it around the base of your ponytail, covering the hair tie.

Step 6: Secure Your Hair

Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place. Make sure to hide the bobby pins so they don't show through your hair.

Tips for Putting Your Hair Up with Tape Extensions

Here are some tips to keep in mind when putting your hair up with tape extensions:

Tip 1: Use High-Quality Hair Ties

Use high-quality hair ties that won't damage your hair or your extensions. Avoid using rubber bands or elastic bands, as they can cause breakage.

Tip 2: Don't Pull Too Tight

When creating your ponytail, don't pull too tight. This can cause tension on your hair and your extensions, leading to breakage.

Tip 3: Be Gentle

Be gentle when styling your hair with tape extensions. Use a soft brush and avoid tugging or pulling on your hair.

FAQ

Can I put my hair up with tape extensions?

Yes, you can put your hair up with tape extensions. Just follow the steps outlined in this blog post.

Will putting my hair up damage my tape extensions?

If you put your hair up correctly, it shouldn't damage your tape extensions. Just be gentle and avoid pulling too tight on your hair.

How do I care for my tape extensions?

To care for your tape extensions, avoid using hot tools, use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and brush your hair regularly.

Hair Care

To keep your hair and your tape extensions looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients.

Tip 2: Avoid Hot Tools

Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners on your tape extensions. This can cause damage to both your hair and your extensions.

Tip 3: Brush Your Hair Regularly

Use a soft brush to brush your hair regularly. This will help to detangle any knots and distribute the natural oils throughout your hair.

By following these tips, you can put your hair up with tape extensions without damaging them. Just be gentle, use high-quality products, and take care of your hair.
