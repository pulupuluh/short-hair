Is Thick Hair Good For Short Haircuts

Written by William Aug 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have thick hair and are contemplating a short haircut, you may be wondering if it's a good idea. The answer is yes! Thick hair is perfect for short haircuts, as it can add volume, texture, and dimension to your look. In this article, we'll explore why thick hair is good for short haircuts, how to choose the right style for your hair type, and how to care for your new 'do.

Why is Thick Hair Good for Short Haircuts?

Thick hair has a natural volume that can be enhanced with a short haircut. Short haircuts can add texture and dimension to your hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous. Thick hair also tends to hold a style well, which is perfect for short haircuts that require a lot of styling.

Another advantage of having thick hair is that it can be cut in a variety of styles. From pixie cuts to bobs, there are many short haircuts that can work for thick hair. You can also experiment with different textures and layers to create a unique and personalized look.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Thick Hair

When choosing a short haircut for thick hair, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. For example, a bob or a lob can be flattering for most face shapes, while a pixie cut may not work for everyone.

You should also consider the amount of maintenance you're willing to put into your hair. Short haircuts often require more styling than longer hair, so make sure you're prepared to invest time and effort into your new look.

Additionally, if you have curly or wavy hair, you may want to consider a haircut that embraces your natural texture. A layered bob or a shaggy cut can be perfect for showcasing your curls and waves.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Hair for Thick Hair

If you're considering cutting your thick hair into a short haircut, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Choose your style

Before you start cutting your hair, decide on the style you want. Look at pictures of short haircuts for inspiration and choose the one that best suits your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or elastics. This will help you to cut your hair evenly and make it easier to style.

Step 3: Start cutting

Starting at the back of your head, use scissors to cut your hair to your desired length. Work your way around your head, cutting small sections at a time.

Step 4: Add texture and layers

To add texture and dimension to your hair, use thinning shears or scissors to create layers throughout your hair. This will help to make your hair look fuller and more voluminous.

Step 5: Style your hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it using your favorite products. You may want to use a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron to achieve the look you want.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Thick Hair

Now that you've got your new short haircut, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair for thick hair:

  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs

Q: Is short hair good for thick hair?

A: Yes, short hair is perfect for thick hair. It can add volume, texture, and dimension to your look.

Q: What short haircuts are good for thick hair?

A: There are many short haircuts that are good for thick hair, including bobs, pixie cuts, and layered cuts.

Q: How do I style short hair for thick hair?

A: You can style short hair for thick hair using a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.

Conclusion

If you have thick hair, a short haircut can be a great way to showcase your natural volume and texture. By choosing the right style for your hair type and taking care of your new 'do, you can rock a short haircut with confidence and style.
