How To Do Undercut Haircut At Home A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut? The undercut is a popular hairstyle that's been around for a while. It's a versatile look that can be customized to suit your preferences. The best part is, you can do it at home with the right tools and techniques. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve the perfect undercut haircut at home.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The undercut is a hairstyle where the hair on the sides and back of the head is cut short while the hair on top is left longer. It's a popular hairstyle for men and women and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. The undercut can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. It's a low-maintenance hairstyle that's easy to maintain and looks great on everyone.

How to

To achieve an undercut haircut at home, you'll need the following tools:

  • Clipper
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror

Before you start cutting your hair, it's essential to choose the right undercut style that suits your face shape and hair type. You can search for inspiration online or consult with a hair stylist for recommendations. Once you've decided on the style, follow these steps:

Step by step

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Section off the top of your hair by clipping it up or tying it in a bun.
  3. Using the clipper, start cutting the sides and back of your hair. Start with a higher guard and work your way down slowly. Take your time and be careful not to cut too much hair.
  4. Blend the sides and back with the top by using scissors to cut the hair on the crown of your head. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.
  5. Release the top section of your hair and comb it down. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. You can add texture by point cutting or using thinning scissors.
  6. Blend the top with the sides and back by using scissors to cut the hair at an angle. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length.
  7. Check the length and texture of your hair by using a mirror to see the back and sides. Make any necessary adjustments.
  8. Finish by styling your hair as desired.

Tips

  • Invest in high-quality tools to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Start with a higher guard when cutting your hair and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Take your time and be patient. It's better to cut a little bit of hair at a time than to cut too much.
  • Consult with a hair stylist if you're unsure about the style or technique.

FAQ

Can I do an undercut haircut on my own?

Yes, you can do an undercut haircut at home with the right tools and techniques. It's important to choose the right style and take your time when cutting your hair.

What's the best way to style an undercut haircut?

The best way to style an undercut haircut depends on the style and your hair type. You can experiment with different products, such as pomade, wax, or gel, to achieve the desired look.

How often do I need to cut my undercut haircut?

The frequency of your undercut haircut depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it. It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your undercut haircut looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and protect it from the sun and chlorine. Regular trims will keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect undercut haircut at home. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your new look!
