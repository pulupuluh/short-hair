How To Find Out What Hair Colour Suits You

Written by William Jun 15, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a change in your hair colour, it can be overwhelming to decide what shade to go for. With so many hair colours available, it can be hard to choose the one that suits you best. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will guide you through the process of finding the perfect hair colour for you.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Find Out What Hair Colour Suits You
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to find out what hair colour suits you:

1. Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair colour is to determine your skin tone. There are three main types of skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow, peach, and golden undertones. Cool skin tones have pink, red, and blue undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mix of warm and cool undertones.

2. Consider Your Eye Colour

Eye colour is another important factor to consider when choosing a hair colour. If you have blue or green eyes, cool tones such as ash blonde or platinum blonde will complement your eyes. If you have brown or hazel eyes, warmer tones such as caramel or chestnut will suit you better.

3. Think About Your Natural Hair Colour

Your natural hair colour is also an important factor to consider. If you have naturally dark hair, going too light can be a drastic change. On the other hand, if you have naturally light hair, going too dark can be too harsh. Consider going only a few shades lighter or darker than your natural hair colour.

4. Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is also important when choosing a hair colour. If you have a busy schedule and don't have time to touch up your roots often, consider a low-maintenance hair colour.

5. Consult with a Professional

If you're still unsure of what hair colour suits you, consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you find the perfect shade based on your skin tone, eye colour, and lifestyle.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair colour:

  • Look at pictures online or in magazines for inspiration
  • Consider your wardrobe and the colours that look best on you
  • Choose a hair colour that complements your personality
  • Experiment with temporary hair colour before committing to a permanent change

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have a sensitive scalp?

A: Yes, there are hair dyes available for people with sensitive scalps. Look for hair dyes that are labeled as "gentle" or "sensitive."

Q: Will a drastic hair colour change damage my hair?

A: Any hair colour change can cause damage to your hair, especially if you're going for a drastic change. Be sure to use a good quality hair dye and follow the instructions carefully to minimize damage.

Hair Care Tips

After dyeing your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for coloured hair
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated

Now that you know how to find out what hair colour suits you, it's time to make a change. Whether you choose a subtle change or a drastic one, be sure to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
