What Should I Use To Slick My Hair Back

Written by Eveline May 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a sleek, sophisticated hairstyle that will turn heads? Slicking your hair back is a classic, timeless look that never goes out of style. Whether you're attending a formal event or simply want to look polished and put-together, slicked-back hair is the way to go. But what should you use to achieve this look? In this article, we'll explore the best products to use for slicked-back hair and give you some tips on how to style it.

Description

Slicked-back hair is a hairstyle in which the hair is combed back away from the face and held in place with a hair product. This style is popular among men and women and can be worn in a variety of settings, from the office to a night out on the town. Slicked-back hair can be achieved with a variety of products, including pomade, gel, wax, and hairspray.

How to Slick Your Hair Back

Before we get into the best products to use for slicked-back hair, let's go over the basic steps for achieving this look:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in achieving slicked-back hair is to start with clean, freshly washed hair. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to ensure that your hair is clean, soft, and manageable.

Step 2: Apply Product

Next, apply your chosen hair product to your hair. Depending on the product you choose, you may need to apply it to wet or dry hair. Be sure to read the instructions on the product carefully to ensure that you're using it correctly.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Using a comb, comb your hair back away from your face. Be sure to comb all of your hair, including the sides and the back. If you have longer hair, you may want to use a brush instead of a comb to ensure that your hair is smooth and tangle-free.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you've combed your hair back, use your fingers to style it into the desired shape. You may want to add some volume to the top of your hair or create a side part for a more polished look.

Tips for Slicked-Back Hair

Now that you know the basic steps for achieving slicked-back hair, here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your look:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. You don't want to use too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.
  • Use a hairspray to set your hair in place and prevent flyaways.
  • If you have longer hair, consider tying it back into a low ponytail or bun for a sleek, sophisticated look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicked-back hair:

What is the best product to use for slicked-back hair?

The best product to use for slicked-back hair depends on your hair type and desired look. Pomade, gel, wax, and hairspray are all good options to consider.

Can women wear their hair slicked back?

Absolutely! Slicked-back hair is a versatile style that can be worn by both men and women.

Is slicked-back hair appropriate for formal events?

Yes, slicked-back hair is a sophisticated, elegant look that is perfect for formal events.

Hair Care for Slicked-Back Hair

While slicked-back hair is a stylish and polished look, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it prone to breakage.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that includes plenty of vitamins and nutrients to support strong, healthy hair.

With the right products and styling techniques, you can achieve the perfect slicked-back hair look. Whether you're headed to a formal event or simply want to look polished and put-together, slicked-back hair is a classic, timeless style that never goes out of fashion.
