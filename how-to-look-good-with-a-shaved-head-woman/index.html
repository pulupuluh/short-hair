How To Look Good With A Shaved Head Woman

Written by William Jul 24, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you considering shaving your head? Or have you already taken the plunge and are now wondering how to look your best with a buzzed cut? A shaved head can be a bold and empowering choice for women, but it can also be intimidating to style and maintain. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to look good with a shaved head woman, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Shave Your Head
  • Styling Tips for a Shaved Head
  • Accessorizing with a Shaved Head
  • Makeup Recommendations for a Shaved Head
  • Hair Care for a Shaved Head
  • FAQs

How to Shave Your Head

The first step to rocking a shaved head is, of course, shaving it. Here are some tips on how to do it yourself or with the help of a professional:

DIY Shave

If you want to shave your head at home, follow these steps:

  1. Invest in a good pair of clippers or a razor.
  2. Cut your hair as short as possible with scissors or clippers.
  3. Wet your hair and apply shaving cream or gel.
  4. Shave your head in the direction of hair growth.
  5. Rinse your head with warm water and pat dry with a towel.
  6. Apply moisturizer or aftershave to soothe the scalp.

Professional Shave

If you prefer to have your head shaved by a professional, follow these steps:

  1. Research reputable salons or barbershops that offer head shaving services.
  2. Book an appointment and communicate your desired style with your stylist.
  3. Relax and let the stylist do their job.
  4. Ask for tips on how to maintain your shaved head at home.

Styling Tips for a Shaved Head

Styling a shaved head may seem limited, but there are actually many ways to switch up your look. Here are some styling tips:

  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands, scarves, or hair clips.
  • Try out bold makeup looks, such as a bold lip or smoky eye.
  • Rock statement jewelry to draw attention to your face.
  • Wear clothing that complements your personal style and showcases your confidence.

Accessorizing with a Shaved Head

Accessorizing with a shaved head can be a fun way to add personality to your look. Here are some accessory ideas:

  • Headbands or scarves
  • Bold earrings or statement necklaces
  • Hats or beanies
  • Hair clips or bobby pins

Makeup Recommendations for a Shaved Head

Makeup can be a powerful tool to enhance your features and complement your shaved head. Here are some makeup recommendations:

  • Focus on bold eyebrows to frame your face.
  • Experiment with bright or dark lipsticks to draw attention to your mouth.
  • Try out smoky or colorful eye makeup to make your eyes pop.
  • Use highlighter to bring out your cheekbones.

Hair Care for a Shaved Head

Even though you don't have hair to style, a shaved head still requires some maintenance. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Moisturize your scalp to prevent dryness and flakiness.
  • Apply sunscreen or wear a hat to protect your scalp from UV rays.
  • Wash your scalp regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Massage your scalp to promote circulation and healthy hair growth.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaving your head:

Will my hair grow back the same after shaving it?

Yes, your hair will grow back the same texture and color as before.

Will shaving my head make my hair thicker?

No, shaving your head will not change the thickness of your hair. Hair thickness is determined by genetics.

How often do I need to shave my head?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain a buzzed cut. On average, you may need to shave your head once every 1-2 weeks.

Can I still dye my hair if I have a shaved head?

Yes, you can still dye your hair even if you have a shaved head. Just be sure to follow the dye instructions carefully and protect your scalp from irritation.

Will a shaved head make me look masculine?

No, a shaved head can be a feminine and stylish look for women. It all depends on how you style and accessorize it.

Now that you know how to look good with a shaved head woman, go forth and embrace your bold and beautiful new look!
