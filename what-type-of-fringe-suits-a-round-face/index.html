What Type Of Fringe Suits A Round Face

Written by Christine Mar 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? One way to switch things up is by getting a fringe. However, if you have a round face, not all fringes will suit you. In this article, we will discuss what type of fringe suits a round face and how you can style it to complement your features.

Description

A fringe, also known as bangs, is a section of hair that falls over the forehead. It can be cut in different lengths and styles, depending on your preference. However, if you have a round face, you need to be careful when choosing a fringe style to avoid making your face look wider than it already is.

How to Choose the Right Fringe for a Round Face

When choosing a fringe style for a round face, you need to consider the following factors:

Face Shape

A round face has soft curves and is almost as wide as it is long. To balance the roundness of your face, you need a fringe that creates the illusion of length. So, avoid fringes that are too short and go for ones that are longer and side-swept.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also affect the type of fringe that suits you. If you have fine hair, a blunt fringe can make your hair look flat and lifeless. Instead, opt for a textured fringe that adds volume and movement to your hair. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, a blunt fringe can work well as it will add structure and definition to your hair.

Personal Style

Your personal style is also an important factor when choosing a fringe. If you prefer a classic look, a straight blunt fringe can work well. However, if you like to experiment with your hair, you can try a choppy or asymmetrical fringe.

Step by Step Guide to Styling a Fringe for a Round Face

Once you have chosen the right fringe style for your round face, here is a step-by-step guide on how to style it:

Step 1: Wash and Blow-dry your Hair

Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it straight. Use a round brush to add volume and bounce to your hair.

Step 2: Section your Hair

Section the hair at the crown of your head and clip it away. This will help you focus on styling your fringe.

Step 3: Style your Fringe

Using a straightening iron, style your fringe by brushing it forwards and then downwards. This will create a side-swept effect that will lengthen your face.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off by using a hairspray to hold your fringe in place. You can also use a serum to add shine to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining a Fringe

Here are some tips for maintaining your fringe:

Trim your Fringe Regularly

To keep your fringe looking neat and tidy, it is important to trim it regularly. You can do this yourself or visit a hairdresser every few weeks.

Blow-dry your Fringe

Blow-drying your fringe will help it stay in place and look sleek. Use a round brush to add volume and bounce to your hair.

Use Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo can help refresh your fringe and absorb excess oil. It is also a great way to add volume to your hair.

FAQ

Can I get a fringe if I have a round face?

Yes, you can get a fringe if you have a round face. However, you need to choose the right style that will complement your features.

What type of fringe suits a round face?

A side-swept fringe that is longer than your eyebrows is the best option for a round face.

How do I style my fringe?

You can style your fringe by blow-drying it forwards and then downwards using a straightening iron. Finish off with hairspray to hold it in place.

Hair Care for Fringe

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your fringe:

Wash your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair. If you need to use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize the damage.

Protect your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray. This will prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle.

Conclusion

A fringe can be a great way to switch up your hairstyle and add a new dimension to your look. However, if you have a round face, you need to choose the right style that will complement your features. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you can find a fringe that suits you. Remember to maintain your fringe by trimming it regularly and using the right hair care products to keep it healthy and shiny.
