How To Fix Up Barbie Doll Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Mar 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a parent or collector of Barbie dolls, you know that their hair can get messy and tangled over time. Fortunately, fixing up Barbie doll hair is a relatively easy process that can have your dolls looking as good as new. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to restore your Barbie doll's hair to its former glory.

How to Fix Up Barbie Doll Hair

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

The first step in fixing up Barbie doll hair is to gather your supplies. You'll need:

  • a comb or brush
  • a spray bottle filled with water
  • a small bowl
  • a soft-bristled toothbrush
  • a towel or cloth
  • a small amount of fabric softener

Step 2: Detangle the Hair

Start by gently combing or brushing through the hair to remove any tangles or knots. Be sure to work slowly and carefully to avoid damaging the hair. If the hair is particularly tangled, you may want to use your fingers to separate the strands before using the comb or brush.

Step 3: Wash the Hair

Once the hair is detangled, it's time to wash it. Fill the small bowl with warm water and add a small amount of fabric softener. Dip the hair into the water and gently massage the fabric softener into the strands. Be sure to avoid getting any water on the doll's body or clothing.

Step 4: Rinse and Dry the Hair

After washing the hair, rinse it thoroughly with warm water to remove any soap or fabric softener. Use the towel or cloth to gently blot the hair and remove any excess water. Then, use the soft-bristled toothbrush to gently brush through the hair and remove any remaining tangles.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Now that the hair is clean and detangled, it's time to style it. You can use a comb or brush to create different hairstyles, or you can use your fingers to create a more natural look. If you want to add some extra shine to the hair, you can use a small amount of hair gel or spray.

Tips for Fixing Up Barbie Doll Hair

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when fixing up Barbie doll hair:

  • Work slowly and carefully to avoid damaging the hair
  • Use a soft-bristled brush or toothbrush to detangle the hair
  • Don't use hot water or a blow dryer to dry the hair, as this can damage it
  • Store your Barbie dolls in a dry, cool place to prevent tangling and damage to the hair

FAQ

Can I use regular shampoo to wash Barbie doll hair?

No, it's best to use fabric softener to wash Barbie doll hair. Regular shampoo can be too harsh and can damage the hair.

How often should I wash Barbie doll hair?

You should only need to wash Barbie doll hair when it becomes visibly dirty or tangled. Over-washing can damage the hair.

What should I do if the hair is too damaged to fix?

If the hair is too damaged to fix, you may need to consider replacing it. There are many online retailers that sell replacement Barbie doll hair.

Hair Care for Barbie Dolls

Now that you know how to fix up Barbie doll hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Store your dolls in a cool, dry place
  • Avoid exposing the hair to direct sunlight or heat
  • Use a soft-bristled brush or toothbrush to detangle the hair
  • Don't use hot water or a blow dryer to dry the hair
  • Consider using a small amount of hair gel or spray to add shine

By following these tips and the steps outlined in this guide, you can keep your Barbie dolls looking their best for years to come.
