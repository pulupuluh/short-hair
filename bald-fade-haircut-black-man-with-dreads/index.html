Bald Fade Haircut For Black Men With Dreads A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Apr 20, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're a black man with dreads, you might be wondering how to keep your hair looking neat and groomed. One popular hairstyle that can help you achieve this is the bald fade haircut. In this article, we'll break down everything you need to know about this hairstyle, from what it is to how to maintain it.

What is a Bald Fade Haircut?

A bald fade haircut is a hairstyle where the sides of the head are shaved or cut very short, gradually blending into longer hair on the top of the head. The fade can be high or low, depending on your preference. This hairstyle is popular among black men because it can be customized to suit their individual hair type and style.

How to Get a Bald Fade Haircut with Dreads

Getting a bald fade haircut with dreads can be a bit tricky, but it's definitely doable. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Find a Good Barber

The first step is to find a barber who is experienced with cutting black men's hair. Look for someone who has a good reputation and can show you examples of their work. It's important to find a barber who you trust and who can help you achieve the look you want.

Step 2: Decide on the Type of Fade

Next, decide on the type of fade you want. Do you want a high or low fade? Do you want a skin fade or a taper fade? Consult with your barber to determine the best type of fade for your hair type and style.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Your barber will start by cutting the sides of your head, gradually blending the hair into a longer length on the top. Depending on the type of fade you want, the sides can be cut very short or shaved completely.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Your barber will then cut the hair on the top of your head to the desired length. If you have dreads, they may need to be trimmed or shaped to fit the style of the haircut.

Step 5: Blend the Fade

The final step is to blend the fade, making sure there are no harsh lines or uneven spots. Your barber will use clippers and scissors to blend the hair seamlessly from short to long.

Tips for Maintaining a Bald Fade Haircut with Dreads

Once you have your bald fade haircut with dreads, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Keep Your Hair Clean

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo that is designed for dreadlocks, and be gentle when washing your hair to avoid damaging your dreads.

Tip 2: Moisturize Your Hair

Dreads can be dry and brittle, so it's important to moisturize them regularly. Use a moisturizing oil or cream to keep your hair soft and healthy.

Tip 3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

To maintain the shape of your haircut, it's important to trim your hair regularly. This will help prevent your dreads from becoming too long or unruly.

FAQs

Q: How long does a bald fade haircut with dreads last?

A: A bald fade haircut can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, depending on how quickly your hair grows. It's important to maintain the shape of the haircut by trimming your hair regularly.

Q: Can I get a bald fade haircut with short dreads?

A: Yes, you can get a bald fade haircut with short dreads. Your barber will be able to customize the haircut to suit your individual hair type and style.

Hair Care for Bald Fade Haircut with Dreads

Maintaining a bald fade haircut with dreads requires proper hair care. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Dreadlock Shampoo

Use a shampoo that is designed for dreadlocks to keep your hair clean and healthy.

Tip 2: Moisturize Your Hair

Moisturize your hair regularly to keep it soft and healthy. Use a moisturizing oil or cream to keep your dreads looking neat and tidy.

Tip 3: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your haircut. This will help prevent your dreads from becoming too long or unruly.

Overall, a bald fade haircut with dreads is a great way to keep your hair looking neat and groomed. With the right barber and proper hair care, you can achieve a stylish and trendy look that suits your individual style.
