How To Pick The Right Hair Color For Your Skin Tone Quiz

Written by Aprilia Jun 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're thinking about changing your hair color, it's important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to figure out which hair color will look best on you. That's why we've put together this quiz to help you determine the perfect hair color for your skin tone. Follow these tips and you'll be on your way to a gorgeous new look!

Description

Our quiz will take you through a series of questions to determine your skin tone and the best hair colors for you. You'll be asked about your natural hair color, your eye color, and your skin undertones. Once you've completed the quiz, you'll receive personalized recommendations for hair colors that will flatter your complexion.

How to Take the Quiz

To take the quiz, simply click on the link and answer the questions. Make sure you're in a well-lit area so you can accurately determine your skin undertones. Take your time and answer each question honestly to get the most accurate results.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Click on the link to take the quiz.
Step 2: Answer the questions about your natural hair color, eye color, and skin undertones.
Step 3: Receive personalized recommendations for hair colors that will flatter your complexion.
Step 4: Use your quiz results to choose a hair color that will look great on you.

Tips

- Remember to consider your skin undertones when choosing a hair color.
- If you have warm undertones, choose hair colors with warm shades like golden blonde or auburn.
- If you have cool undertones, choose hair colors with cool shades like ash blonde or platinum.
- If you're unsure about your skin undertones, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones.
- Consider getting a consultation with a professional hairstylist to ensure the best results.

FAQ

What if I don't like the recommended hair colors?

Our quiz is just a guide to help you choose a hair color that will flatter your complexion. Ultimately, the decision is up to you. If you don't like the recommended hair colors, consider talking to a professional hairstylist for more options.

What if I have multiple skin undertones?

If you have multiple skin undertones, choose a hair color that complements the dominant undertone. For example, if you have warm and cool undertones, but your skin appears more warm, choose a hair color with warm shades.

What if I want a dramatic change?

If you want a dramatic change, consider getting a consultation with a professional hairstylist. They can help you choose a hair color that will complement your skin tone and achieve the look you want.

Hair Care

After you've chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading. Avoid exposing your hair to excessive heat and sunlight, which can cause damage and fade your color. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Changing your hair color can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look. With our quiz and these tips, you'll be able to choose a hair color that flatters your complexion and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
