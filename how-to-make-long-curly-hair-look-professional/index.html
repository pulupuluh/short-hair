How To Make Long Curly Hair Look Professional

Written by Aprilia Jun 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Long curly hair is a beautiful asset, but it can be a challenge to style it in a professional way. Whether you're going to work or attending a formal event, you want to look polished and put-together. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for making long curly hair look professional.

Table of Contents

  1. Hair Care
  2. How to Style Long Curly Hair Professionally
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips and Tricks
  5. FAQ

Hair Care

Before we dive into styling tips, it's important to take care of your curly hair. Curly hair is naturally dry and prone to frizz, so it's essential to keep it moisturized. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized throughout the day.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant spray beforehand.

How to Style Long Curly Hair Professionally

Now that your hair is well-nourished and hydrated, it's time to style it in a professional way. Here are some tips:

  • Start by parting your hair where it naturally falls. A deep side part or a center part can look chic and polished.
  • Consider getting layers to add structure and shape to your curls. Ask your stylist for long, face-framing layers.
  • Experiment with different updos, such as a low bun or a twisted chignon. These styles can look elegant and sophisticated.
  • Use hair accessories, such as a headband or a hair clip, to add some flair to your look.
  • If you prefer to wear your hair down, try a half-up, half-down style. This can look feminine and professional.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy. Instead, use a lightweight mousse or a curl-enhancing cream.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling long curly hair professionally:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and condition it with a deep conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  3. Part your hair where it naturally falls.
  4. Apply a lightweight mousse or curl-enhancing cream to your hair.
  5. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  6. If you want to wear your hair down, use a curling iron to define your curls. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  7. If you want to wear your hair up, gather it into a low bun or a twisted chignon. Secure with bobby pins.
  8. Add a hair accessory, such as a headband or a hair clip, for some extra flair.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling long curly hair professionally:

  • Invest in a high-quality diffuser attachment for your blow dryer. This will help enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for you.
  • If you're attending a formal event, consider getting your hair professionally styled.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage.
  • If you're in a rush, try a messy bun or a side braid. These styles can look chic and effortless.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long curly hair professionally:

Can I straighten my curly hair for a professional look?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair if you prefer a sleek, polished look. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid straightening your hair too often, as it can damage your hair.

What kind of hair accessories should I use?

You can use a variety of hair accessories, such as headbands, hair clips, and barrettes. Choose accessories that complement your outfit and personal style.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people with curly hair can go several days without washing, while others may need to wash their hair every other day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

How can I reduce frizz in my curly hair?

Use a sulfate-free shampoo, deep condition your hair regularly, and avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized, and invest in a high-quality diffuser attachment for your blow dryer.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your long curly hair look professional and polished. Remember to take care of your hair, experiment with different styles, and have fun with it!
