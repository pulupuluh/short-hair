The Best Volumizer Spray For Fuller Bouncier Hair

Written by Robby Mar 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have fine, limp hair, you know how frustrating it can be to try to get some volume and body into your locks. Luckily, there is one product that can help: volumizer spray. In this article, we'll go over the best volumizer spray on the market and give you tips on how to use it to get the most out of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use Volumizer Spray
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Volumizer Spray
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Volumized Hair

Description

Volumizer spray is a product designed to give your hair some extra lift and body. It works by coating the hair shaft and adding thickness, making your hair look fuller and bouncier. Volumizer spray can be used on all hair types, but it is especially helpful for those with fine, limp hair who struggle to get any volume at all.

The best volumizer spray will not only give you the volume you crave, but it will also be lightweight and non-greasy, so your hair won't be weighed down or feel sticky.

How to Use Volumizer Spray

Using volumizer spray is simple, but there are a few things to keep in mind to get the best results:

  • Start with clean, towel-dried hair
  • Shake the can well before use
  • Hold the can about 6 inches away from your hair
  • Spray the product onto your roots, focusing on the crown of your head
  • Use your fingers to massage the product into your roots
  • Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to lift the roots as you go
  • Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a more detailed guide to using volumizer spray:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair. If you have just washed your hair, make sure to squeeze out any excess water with a towel.
  2. Shake the can of volumizer spray well to mix up the product.
  3. Hold the can about 6 inches away from your hair and aim the nozzle at your roots. Spray the product onto your roots, focusing on the crown of your head. Be careful not to use too much product or your hair may look greasy.
  4. Use your fingers to massage the product into your roots. This will help distribute the product evenly and create more lift.
  5. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to lift the roots as you go. Aim the blow dryer at your roots, lifting them up with the brush as you dry. This will create even more volume.
  6. When your hair is almost dry, switch to the cool setting on your blow dryer and give your hair a blast of cool air. This will help set the style and make your hair look even fuller.

Tips for Using Volumizer Spray

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your volumizer spray:

  • Don't use too much product. A little goes a long way, and using too much can make your hair look greasy.
  • Focus on the roots. Volumizer spray works best when applied to the roots, so make sure to concentrate the product there.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down. This will help create even more lift and volume.
  • Use a round brush. A round brush will help lift your roots as you blow dry, creating more volume.
  • Use a lightweight hairspray. If you want to make your style last longer, finish with a lightweight hairspray to hold everything in place.

FAQs

Q: Can volumizer spray be used on curly hair?

A: Yes, volumizer spray can be used on curly hair to add body and lift. Just be sure to apply the product to the roots and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your curls without disrupting them too much.

Q: Will volumizer spray make my hair look greasy?

A: Not if you use it correctly. Be sure to apply the product only to your roots and use a light hand. If you do accidentally use too much product, you can always use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil.

Q: How often should I use volumizer spray?

A: You can use volumizer spray as often as you like, but it's best to limit use to once or twice a week to avoid product buildup.

Hair Care Tips for Volumized Hair

Once you've achieved your desired volume with volumizer spray, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and full. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help maintain your style.
  • Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With the right volumizer spray and a few styling tips, you can achieve the full, bouncy hair you've always wanted. Give it a try and see the difference it can make!
