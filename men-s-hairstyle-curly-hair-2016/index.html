Men s Hairstyle Curly Hair 2016 A Guide To The Best Looks

Written by Bardi Jul 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Curly hair has always been a challenge for men when it comes to styling, but in 2016, the curly look was in full swing. Whether you have natural curls or want to create them, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this guide, we'll cover the best men's hairstyle curly hair 2016 looks, how to achieve them, and tips for maintaining your curly hair.

Description

The men's hairstyle curly hair 2016 trend was all about embracing natural curls and texture. This meant many men opted for longer haircuts with layers to enhance their curls. However, shorter haircuts with defined curls were also popular. The key was to keep it low maintenance and effortless.

How to Achieve Men's Hairstyle Curly Hair 2016

If you have natural curls, consider a layered haircut to enhance your texture. Use a curl-enhancing product like mousse or gel to define your curls and keep them in place. For those without natural curls, use a curling iron or perm to achieve your desired curl pattern. Opt for a shorter haircut with defined curls for a more polished look.

Step by Step Guide

For Natural Curls:

  1. Get a layered haircut to enhance your curls.
  2. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing product like mousse or gel to damp hair.
  4. Scrunch your hair with your hands to define your curls.
  5. Air dry or use a diffuser on low heat to dry your hair.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or serum to keep your curls in place.

For Defined Curls:

  1. Get a shorter haircut with layers.
  2. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  3. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  4. Use a curling iron to curl small sections of hair.
  5. Use hairspray or a serum to keep your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Men's Hairstyle Curly Hair 2016

Caring for curly hair requires a little extra effort to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your curly hair:

  • Use products designed for curly hair to avoid frizz and enhance your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry to prevent breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your curls looking fresh.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for curly hair?

A layered haircut is the best option for curly hair as it enhances your natural texture and prevents your curls from looking weighed down.

What products should I use for curly hair?

Use products designed for curly hair, such as mousse, gel, or curl cream, to define your curls and prevent frizz.

Can I straighten curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten curly hair with a flat iron, but it's important to use a heat protectant and to avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy curls. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Wash your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your curls looking fresh.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair as it can cause breakage and damage.

Overall, the men's hairstyle curly hair 2016 trend was all about embracing natural curls and texture. Whether you have natural curls or want to create them, there are plenty of options to choose from. Follow these tips and tricks to achieve the perfect curly look and maintain healthy, bouncy curls.
