How To Cut A Comb Over Fringe A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 31, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that will make you look stylish and trendy? A comb over fringe might just be what you need. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be worn by men and women of all ages. This guide will show you how to cut a comb over fringe in easy-to-follow steps.

Description of a Comb Over Fringe

A comb over fringe is a hairstyle where the hair is combed to one side and cut diagonally across the forehead. This creates a fringe that is longer on one side and shorter on the other. It's a modern take on the classic fringe and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to Cut a Comb Over Fringe

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help you get a better idea of how your hair will fall and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Section your hair

Divide your hair into three sections: one at the crown of your head, one at the back, and one at the front. Clip the back and crown sections out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the fringe

Start by cutting the fringe section. Use a comb to create a diagonal parting across your forehead. The parting should start from one side of your head and end at the other. Use your fingers to hold the hair in place and cut it at an angle. Make sure to cut it longer on one side than the other. Repeat this process until you've cut all the hair in the fringe section.

Step 4: Cut the crown section

Release the crown section and comb it forward. Use the fringe as a guide and cut the hair at an angle, making sure to blend it with the fringe. You can use thinning shears to create a more textured look.

Step 5: Cut the back section

Release the back section and comb it forward. Use the crown section as a guide and cut the hair at an angle, making sure to blend it with the rest of the hair. You can use thinning shears to create a more layered look.

Step 6: Style your hair

Once you've finished cutting, style your hair as desired. You can use a hair dryer and a round brush to create volume, or use a straightener for a sleeker look.

Tips for Cutting a Comb Over Fringe

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Make sure to cut the hair at an angle to create a natural-looking fringe.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to cut gradually and check your progress as you go.
  • If you're not confident in cutting your own hair, consider going to a professional stylist.

FAQs

Can anyone pull off a comb over fringe?

Yes, a comb over fringe can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. However, it's important to consult with a professional stylist to determine the best cut for your individual features.

Can I cut a comb over fringe on curly hair?

Yes, a comb over fringe can work on curly hair. However, it's important to cut the hair when it's dry to ensure the length and shape are accurate.

Hair Care for a Comb Over Fringe

To maintain a comb over fringe, it's important to keep it clean and well-groomed. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of natural oils. Use a hairbrush or comb to detangle the hair and style it as desired. Regular trims are also important to keep the fringe looking fresh and neat.
