How To Cut Your Hair Short In The Front A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're thinking about making a change to your hairstyle, cutting your hair short in the front can be a great way to switch things up. This style works well for people with a variety of face shapes and hair types, and it can be a low-maintenance option for those who want to spend less time styling their hair.

Description

Short hair in the front can be achieved with a variety of cutting techniques, including layering, razor cutting, or blunt cutting. The key is to create a shape that flatters your face and works with your hair texture. You can also add fringe or bangs to your short cut to frame your face and give your style a little more personality.

How to Cut Your Hair Short in the Front

If you're ready to take the plunge and cut your hair short in the front, there are a few things you should keep in mind before you get started. First, make sure you have the right tools on hand, including a pair of sharp scissors and a comb. You may also want to invest in some texturizing shears if you plan on creating a layered look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting your hair short in the front:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the front portion of your hair, leaving the back section loose.
  3. Decide how short you want your hair to be in the front, and use your scissors to make a straight cut across the section of hair.
  4. Blend the cut section with the rest of your hair by cutting at a diagonal angle.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you've achieved your desired length and shape.
  6. If you want to add fringe or bangs, section off a smaller portion of hair and cut it to your desired length.
  7. Blend the fringe or bangs with the rest of your hair, using texturizing shears if desired.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Short in the Front

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting your hair short in the front:

  • Start with a conservative cut and work your way up to a shorter length if you're not sure how short you want to go.
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your short hair.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.
  • If you're not comfortable cutting your own hair, consider visiting a professional stylist for a consultation.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair short in the front if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! In fact, a short cut can be a great option for people with curly hair, as it can help define your curls and make them easier to manage. Just make sure to choose a cutting technique that works well with your hair texture.

Will cutting my hair short in the front make my face look rounder?

Not necessarily. A short cut can actually help to elongate your face if you choose the right shape and length for your face shape.

How often do I need to trim my short hair?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your style. Generally, you'll want to schedule a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your short hair looking fresh and stylish.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Once you've cut your hair short in the front, you'll need to adjust your hair care routine to keep your style looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent your scalp from becoming too oily.
  • Use a lightweight conditioner to keep your hair hydrated without weighing it down.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can make your hair look greasy or stiff.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as a brush can create frizz and damage your hair.

Cutting your hair short in the front can be a fun and stylish way to update your look. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a flattering and low-maintenance style that works for you!
