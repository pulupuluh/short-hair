How To Put Your Hair Up W A Clip Easy Steps For Relaxed Styles

Written by William Apr 13, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to style your hair, putting it up with a clip is a great option. Whether you're running errands, going to work, or simply want to keep your hair out of your face, clips are a versatile and stylish option. In this post, we'll show you how to put your hair up w a clip and offer some tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair with Clips

How to Put Your Hair Up w a Clip

Putting your hair up with a clip is a simple and easy process. Here are the basic steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Choose Your Clip

There are many different types of hair clips available, including barrettes, alligator clips, and claw clips. Choose one that suits your hair type and the style you're going for.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair

Use a brush or comb to remove any tangles and smooth out your hair. This will make it easier to clip your hair up and keep it in place.

Step 3: Gather Your Hair

Use your hands to gather your hair into a ponytail, bun, or other style. Make sure it's secure and positioned where you want it before moving on to the next step.

Step 4: Place the Clip

Take your clip and position it at the base of your ponytail or bun. Snap it shut, making sure it's secure and holding your hair in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're new to using hair clips, here's a more detailed guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Choose Your Clip

As we mentioned earlier, there are many different types of hair clips available. Choose one that suits your hair type and the style you're going for. If you have thick hair, you may need a larger clip or a clip with a stronger grip. If you're going for a more formal look, you may want to choose a barrette or hair pin instead.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and free of tangles. Use a brush or comb to remove any knots and tangles, and smooth out your hair. This will make it easier to clip your hair up and keep it in place.

Step 3: Gather Your Hair

Use your hands to gather your hair into a ponytail, bun, or other style. Make sure it's secure and positioned where you want it before moving on to the next step.

Step 4: Place the Clip

Take your clip and position it at the base of your ponytail or bun. Snap it shut, making sure it's secure and holding your hair in place. If you're using a claw clip or alligator clip, you may need to twist it slightly to get it to grip your hair properly.

Tips for Styling Your Hair with Clips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your hair clips:

Tip 1: Choose the Right Clip

As we mentioned earlier, different types of clips work better for different hair types and styles. Experiment with different clips to see which ones work best for you.

Tip 2: Use Multiple Clips

If you have thick or long hair, you may need to use multiple clips to hold your hair in place. This can also help add volume and texture to your hairstyle.

Tip 3: Add Accessories

Clips can be a great way to add some extra style to your hair. Try adding a decorative barrette or hair pin to your hairstyle for a fun and unique look.

Tip 4: Don't Overdo It

While clips can be a great way to keep your hair in place, using too many can look messy and overwhelming. Stick to one or two clips at most for a sleek and stylish look.

FAQs

Q: How long will my hair stay in place with a clip?

A: This depends on the type of clip you're using and the thickness of your hair. Generally, clips will hold your hair in place for several hours, but you may need to reposition them or add more clips throughout the day.

Q: Can I use a clip on wet hair?

A: It's best to avoid using clips on wet hair, as this can cause breakage and damage. Wait until your hair is dry before using a clip.

Q: How do I remove a clip without damaging my hair?

A: To remove a clip, simply press the release mechanism and gently slide it out of your hair. Avoid pulling or tugging on the clip, as this can cause breakage and damage.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy and beautiful hair:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that's gentle and free of harsh chemicals. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can cause damage to your hair over time. Try to limit your use of these tools, or use them on a lower heat setting.

Tip 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals is essential for healthy hair. Make sure you're getting enough protein, iron, and other nutrients to keep your hair strong and shiny.

Tip 4: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, or as needed.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to put your hair up w a clip and maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Experiment with different styles and clips to find the perfect look for you.
