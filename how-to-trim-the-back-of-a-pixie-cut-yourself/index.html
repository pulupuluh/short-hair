How To Trim The Back Of A Pixie Cut Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 08, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're rocking a pixie cut, you know that keeping it trimmed and styled is essential to maintaining its shape and overall look. While getting regular haircuts from a professional stylist is important, you may not always have the time or budget to do so. Luckily, with a little patience and practice, you can learn how to trim the back of your pixie cut yourself. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps and offer some tips to help you achieve a polished and professional-looking cut.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that typically features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. It is a popular hairstyle for women who want a low-maintenance look that still exudes confidence and style. While the front of the pixie cut can be relatively easy to trim, the back can be more challenging to reach and cut evenly. Learning how to trim the back of your pixie cut yourself can save you time and money, while also giving you greater control over your hairstyle.

How to Trim the Back of a Pixie Cut Yourself

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start trimming, make sure you have all the necessary tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror. You may also want to use hair clips to section off your hair and a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp while you work.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair before you begin trimming. Wash your hair with your favorite shampoo and conditioner, then let it air dry or blow dry it with a hair dryer. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start trimming.

Step 3: Section Off Your Hair

Using hair clips, section off the hair on top of your head and around your face. This will allow you to focus on the back of your head without getting in the way of the longer hair on top. You can also use a comb to help create a clean part in your hair.

Step 4: Start with the Bottom Layer

Starting at the nape of your neck, use the handheld mirror to see the back of your head. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Use your scissors to trim the hair to your desired length, being careful not to cut too much at once. Work your way up through the back of your head, taking small sections of hair and trimming them as you go.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you've trimmed the entire back of your head, use the handheld mirror to check your progress. Make any additional cuts as needed to achieve your desired length and shape.

Step 6: Blend the Sides and Back

To ensure a seamless transition from the sides to the back of your head, use your scissors to blend the hair at the back of your head with the hair on the sides. Take small sections of hair and trim them at an angle, working your way up to the top of your head.

Step 7: Trim the Hairline

Using the handheld mirror, check the hairline at the back of your neck. Use your scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven spots, being careful not to cut too close to your skin.

Step 8: Style as Desired

Once you've finished trimming the back of your pixie cut, style your hair as desired. You may want to use a styling gel or mousse to add texture and hold to your hair.

Tips for Trimming the Back of a Pixie Cut Yourself

  • Take your time and work in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Keep your scissors sharp for a clean cut.
  • Use the handheld mirror to check your progress frequently.
  • Don't be afraid to make small cuts and adjustments as you go.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Can I trim the back of my pixie cut myself?

Yes, with the right tools and a little practice, you can learn how to trim the back of your pixie cut yourself.

What tools do I need to trim the back of my pixie cut?

You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a handheld mirror. You may also want to use hair clips to section off your hair and a spray bottle filled with water to keep your hair damp while you work.

How often should I trim the back of my pixie cut?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the shape of your pixie cut. As a general rule, you should trim the back of your pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and polished.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

Keeping your pixie cut looking its best requires some basic hair care. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, which can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and techniques, you can learn how to trim the back of your pixie cut yourself and keep your hairstyle looking fresh and stylish. Happy trimming!
