What Is A Layered Haircut Called

Written by William Feb 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're someone who loves getting a haircut but doesn't know the technical terms for different styles, then you're in the right place. One of the most popular and versatile haircuts out there is the layered cut. But what is a layered haircut called in relaxed English language? In this guide, we'll explore everything you need to know about layered haircuts, from what they're called to how to get one.

Description

A layered haircut is a style where the hair is cut at different lengths throughout the head, creating layers. This style is perfect for anyone looking to add texture, volume, and movement to their hair. The layers can be subtle or dramatic, depending on the desired look. Layered haircuts work with any hair type, from straight to curly, and can be customized to fit any face shape.

How to Get a Layered Haircut

If you're interested in getting a layered haircut, the first step is to find a quality hairstylist who specializes in this type of cut. Once you've found the right stylist, you'll want to bring in a few pictures of the style you're looking for. This will help your stylist understand your vision and create the perfect cut for you.

Before your stylist starts cutting, they'll likely wash and dry your hair. Then, they'll section off your hair and start cutting each section at a different length. This will create the layers and give your hair the desired texture and movement. Your stylist may also use thinning shears to remove bulk and add even more texture to your hair.

Step by Step

If you're a visual learner, here's a step-by-step guide to getting a layered haircut:

  1. Find a hairstylist who specializes in layered haircuts.
  2. Bring in pictures of the style you want.
  3. Wash and dry your hair.
  4. Section off your hair.
  5. Cut each section at a different length to create layers.
  6. Use thinning shears to remove bulk and add texture.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a layered haircut:

  • Make sure you're comfortable with your stylist and trust their expertise.
  • Be clear about the length and style you want.
  • Ask your stylist for tips on how to style your hair at home.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to enhance the layers.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and texture of your haircut.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about layered haircuts:

What face shapes work best with layered haircuts?

Layered haircuts work with any face shape, but they're particularly flattering on round or oval faces.

Can layered haircuts work with curly hair?

Yes! Layered haircuts can add volume and movement to curly hair.

How often should I get a layered haircut?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of your layered haircut.

Hair Care

One of the best things about layered haircuts is that they're low maintenance. However, it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips for caring for your layered haircut:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular deep conditioning treatments to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

In conclusion, a layered haircut is a versatile and popular style that can work for anyone. Whether you're looking to add volume, texture, or movement to your hair, a layered cut is a great choice. Now that you know what a layered haircut is called in relaxed English language, you can confidently ask your hairstylist for the style you want. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best!
