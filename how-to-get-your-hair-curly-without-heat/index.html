How To Get Your Hair Curly Without Heat A Complete Guide

How to Get Your Hair Curly Without Heat

There are a few different methods for getting curly hair without heat, including:

  • Twist and pin method
  • Braid method
  • Twist out method
  • Curlformers
  • Foam rollers
  • Flexi rods

Each method provides a different type of curl, so it's important to experiment and find what works best for your hair type and desired look.

Twist and Pin Method

The twist and pin method involves twisting small sections of hair and pinning them in place to create curls. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Twist each section of hair tightly.
  4. Pin the twisted section to your scalp with a bobby pin.
  5. Repeat with all sections of hair.
  6. Leave the pins in overnight.
  7. Remove the pins and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

This method works well for creating tight, defined curls.

Braid Method

The braid method involves braiding your hair and leaving it to dry overnight. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Braid each section of hair tightly.
  4. Leave the braids in overnight.
  5. Undo the braids and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

This method works well for creating loose, beachy waves.

Twist Out Method

The twist out method involves twisting your hair and leaving it to dry overnight. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Divide your hair into small sections.
  3. Twist each section of hair tightly.
  4. Leave the twists in overnight.
  5. Undo the twists and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

This method works well for creating loose, defined curls.

Curlformers

Curlformers are a type of hair curler that don't use heat. They work by stretching your hair into a curl shape and holding it in place. Here's how to use them:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Insert the hook into the Curlformer and hook onto a section of hair at the root.
  3. Pull the Curlformer down to the end of your hair.
  4. Repeat with all sections of hair.
  5. Leave the Curlformers in until your hair is completely dry.
  6. Remove the Curlformers and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

This method works well for creating tight, uniform curls.

Foam Rollers

Foam rollers are another type of hair curler that don't use heat. Here's how to use them:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Wrap a section of hair around the foam roller.
  3. Roll the foam roller up to your scalp.
  4. Secure the foam roller in place with a clip.
  5. Repeat with all sections of hair.
  6. Leave the foam rollers in until your hair is completely dry.
  7. Remove the foam rollers and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

This method works well for creating loose, bouncy curls.

Flexi Rods

Flexi rods are a flexible type of hair curler that don't use heat. Here's how to use them:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Wrap a section of hair around the flexi rod.
  3. Bend the flexi rod to hold the hair in place.
  4. Repeat with all sections of hair.
  5. Leave the flexi rods in until your hair is completely dry.
  6. Remove the flexi rods and gently separate the curls with your fingers.

This method works well for creating tight, spiral curls.

Step by Step Instructions

Here are step by step instructions for getting curly hair without heat:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Choose a method for getting curls without heat.
  3. Follow the instructions for your chosen method.
  4. Leave the curls in until your hair is completely dry.
  5. Remove the curls and gently separate them with your fingers.

Repeat this process as often as you like to achieve your desired level of curliness.

Tips for Getting Curly Hair Without Heat

Here are some tips for getting curly hair without heat:

  • Use a hair product designed for curls to help hold the shape.
  • Experiment with different methods to find what works best for your hair and desired look.
  • Don't brush or comb your hair after creating curls without heat, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz and maintain curls overnight.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause curls to fall out or become frizzy.

FAQ

Can you get curly hair without heat?

Yes! There are plenty of ways to get curly hair without heat, including twisting, braiding, and using curlers.

What's the best way to get curly hair without heat?

The best way to get curly hair without heat depends on your hair type and desired look. Experiment with different methods to find what works best for you.

How long do curls without heat last?

Curls without heat can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on your hair type and the method used.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair can be a bit different than caring for straight hair. Here are some tips for keeping your curls looking their best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz.

By following these tips and experimenting with different methods for getting curls without heat, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls without damaging your hair.
