Short Hair Styles That Suit Round Faces

Written by Luffy May 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of constantly styling long hair that doesn't seem to work well with your round face? Short haircuts are perfect for round faces as they can help elongate the face and make it appear more oval-shaped. In this article, we will show you some of the best short hair styles that are perfect for round faces.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Perfect Short Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

How to Choose the Perfect Short Haircut

When it comes to choosing the perfect short haircut for a round face, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • Choose a style with volume at the crown of the head to elongate the face.
  • Avoid styles that are too blunt or straight, as they can make the face appear rounder.
  • Consider styles with layers or side-swept bangs to add dimension to the face.
  • Opt for styles that are shorter in the back and longer in the front to create an elongated effect.

Some of the best short haircuts for round faces include:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well with round faces. It features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top that can be styled in various ways. This haircut adds volume to the crown of the head, which can help elongate the face.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another great option for those with round faces. It features hair that is shorter in the back and longer in the front, which can help create a more oval-shaped face. A bob cut with layers or side-swept bangs can add dimension to the face and make it appear less round.

Asymmetrical Cut

The asymmetrical cut is a bold and edgy option for those with round faces. It features hair that is longer on one side of the head and shorter on the other, which can create an elongated effect. This haircut works well with side-swept bangs or layers.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Styling short hair can be quick and easy with the right tools and techniques. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as mousse or hair gel.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head.
  4. If you have bangs, use a flat iron to straighten them.
  5. Use a curling iron to add curls or waves to your hair, if desired.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Maintaining short hair can be simple with these tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your style between washes.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I still wear a short haircut if I have a double chin?

Yes, you can still wear a short haircut if you have a double chin. Opt for a style with layers or volume at the crown of your head to draw attention away from your chin.

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, you can still wear a short haircut. Consider a style with layers or side-swept bangs to add dimension to your hair. Use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls and prevent frizz.

What if I don't like my short haircut?

If you don't like your short haircut, don't worry. Hair grows back! Talk to your stylist about options for growing out your hair, or try experimenting with different styling techniques to find a look you love.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using brushes that can damage the hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Consider getting a scalp massage to promote hair growth and improve circulation.

Short haircuts can be a great option for those with round faces. With the right style and hair care routine, you can rock a short haircut that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident.
