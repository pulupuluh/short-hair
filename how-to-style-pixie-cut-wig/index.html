How To Style A Pixie Cut Wig Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jul 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that's low maintenance but still stylish? A pixie cut wig might be just what you need. Pixie cut wigs are short and sassy, and they can be styled in a variety of ways. In this article, we'll show you how to style a pixie cut wig and give you some tips and tricks to make it look great.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style a Pixie Cut Wig
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style a Pixie Cut Wig

Styling a pixie cut wig is easy once you know what you're doing. Here are the basic steps:

  • Start with a clean and dry wig. If it's wet, blow dry it first.
  • Apply some wig hairspray or mousse to give it some hold.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to style the wig the way you want it.
  • Finish with some more hairspray to keep it in place.

Of course, there are many variations on this basic technique. You can use different products, tools, and methods to achieve different looks. Keep reading for a more detailed step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed guide to styling a pixie cut wig:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your wig. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and rinse it thoroughly. Let it air dry or blow dry it on a low setting.
  2. Apply some wig hairspray or mousse to give it some hold. You can also use a styling cream or gel if you prefer.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style the wig the way you want it. You can create different looks by using your fingers to tousle the hair or by using a comb to create a more polished look.
  4. If you want to add some volume, use a round brush to lift the hair at the roots while blow drying on a low setting.
  5. To create a sleeker look, use a flat iron to straighten the hair. You can also use a curling iron or hot rollers to add curls or waves.
  6. Finish with some more hairspray to keep it in place. You may also want to use some shine spray or serum to add some shine.

Experiment with different products and techniques to find the look that works best for you. Don't be afraid to try something new!

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your pixie cut wig:

  • Use a wig stand or mannequin head to make it easier to style the wig.
  • Don't be afraid to use heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, but be sure to use them on a low setting to avoid damaging the wig.
  • Use wig hairspray or mousse sparingly to avoid making the hair look stiff or unnatural.
  • Try using bobby pins or hair clips to create different styles, like a side-swept bang or a pinned-back style.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or wig brush to detangle the hair before styling.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create a variety of different looks with your pixie cut wig.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling a pixie cut wig:

How often should I wash my wig?

You should wash your wig after 8-10 wears, or whenever it starts to look dirty or oily.

Can I use regular hair products on my wig?

You can use some regular hair products on your wig, but be sure to avoid products that contain alcohol, which can dry out the hair.

How do I store my wig?

You should store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help it maintain its shape. Keep it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to help keep your pixie cut wig looking great:

  • Use a wig conditioner to keep the hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to heat, such as a hot oven or open flame.
  • Don't brush or comb your wig when it's wet, as this can damage the hair.
  • Store your wig properly when you're not wearing it to help it maintain its shape.

With these hair care tips, you'll be able to keep your pixie cut wig looking great for many wears to come.

Conclusion

Styling a pixie cut wig is easy once you know the basic steps. Whether you want a sleek and polished look or a tousled and textured style, there are many ways to style your wig to suit your mood and personality. By following our tips and tricks, you'll be able to create a variety of different looks with your pixie cut wig.
