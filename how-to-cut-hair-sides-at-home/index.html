How To Cut Hair Sides At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 30, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're looking to save money on haircuts or just want to try your hand at cutting your own hair, cutting the sides can be a great place to start. With a little practice and the right tools, you can achieve a clean, professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to cut hair sides at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Hair Sides at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Successful Cut
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Cut Hair Sides at Home

Cutting hair sides involves trimming the hair on the sides of the head with clippers or scissors. This is a relatively simple process that requires some basic tools and a steady hand. Here are the tools you'll need:

  • Clippers or scissors
  • Comb
  • Clip or hair tie
  • Mirror
  • Towel or cape

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut hair sides at home:

Step 1: Prepare Your Tools and Work Area

Before you begin cutting, make sure you have all the tools you need within reach. Place a towel or cape around your shoulders to catch any loose hair, and set up a mirror so you can see the sides of your head clearly.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to separate the top of your hair from the sides. Clip the top section up or tie it with a hair tie so it's out of the way while you work on the sides.

Step 3: Choose Your Cutting Tool

You can use clippers or scissors to cut hair sides. Clippers are faster and more precise, but scissors allow for more control and can create a softer look. Choose the tool that works best for you.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Begin cutting at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up. Use the clipper or scissors to trim the hair in small sections, moving from one side of the head to the other. Be sure to cut evenly on both sides.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've finished cutting the sides, use the clippers or scissors to blend the hair where it meets the top section. This will create a seamless transition between the sides and the top of your hair.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Use a mirror to check both sides of your head for any uneven spots or missed areas. Make any necessary adjustments before moving on.

Step 7: Clean Up

Use a brush or comb to remove any loose hair from your head and neck. Remove the towel or cape and clean up your work area.

Tips for a Successful Cut

Here are some tips to help you achieve a successful cut:

  • Start with a longer guard on your clippers or longer hair length if using scissors and gradually work your way to a shorter length. This will help you avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair up and away from the scalp as you cut. This will help you achieve an even cut and avoid accidentally cutting too close to the scalp.
  • Take breaks as needed to rest your hands and avoid fatigue. Cutting hair can be tiring work!

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about cutting hair sides at home:

Can I cut my hair sides with scissors?

Yes, you can cut hair sides with scissors. However, it may take longer and require more skill to achieve a clean, even cut.

What kind of clippers should I use?

Look for clippers with adjustable guards that allow you to choose the length of the cut. Cordless clippers can also be convenient for use at home.

How often should I cut my hair sides?

This will depend on your hair type and desired style. As a general rule, most people need to trim their hair sides every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length and shape.

Hair Care After the Cut

After you've finished cutting your hair sides, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair over time.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and improve manageability.
  • Visit a professional stylist for regular trims and to maintain the overall health and shape of your hair.

With these tips and steps in mind, you can confidently cut your hair sides at home for a fresh, new look.
