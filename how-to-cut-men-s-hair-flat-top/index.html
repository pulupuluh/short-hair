How To Cut Men s Hair Flat Top A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 11, 2023 · 7 min read

If you want to achieve a classic, timeless look for men, the flat top haircut is the way to go. This style has been around for decades and continues to be a popular choice among men of all ages. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut men's hair flat top so you can achieve the perfect look every time.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The flat top haircut is a short, military-inspired hairstyle that is cut flat on top and tapered on the sides and back. This style is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance haircut that is easy to style and looks great in any situation.

To achieve the flat top look, you'll need to use clippers and scissors to create a uniform length on top and tapered sides and back. This style works best on men with straight or slightly wavy hair and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style.

How to

Before you start cutting, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a set of clippers with various guards, scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water.

To begin, wet the hair with the spray bottle and comb it straight up from the crown of the head. Use the clippers with the longest guard to cut the hair on top to a uniform length. Work from front to back, cutting the hair in small sections to ensure an even cut.

Next, use the clippers with a shorter guard to taper the sides and back. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to hold the hair away from the scalp as you cut. Blend the top and sides together using scissors, cutting at a slight angle to create a seamless transition.

Step-by-Step

1. Wet the hair with a spray bottle and comb it straight up from the crown of the head.

2. Use the clippers with the longest guard to cut the hair on top to a uniform length.

3. Work from front to back, cutting the hair in small sections to ensure an even cut.

4. Use the clippers with a shorter guard to taper the sides and back.

5. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to hold the hair away from the scalp as you cut.

6. Blend the top and sides together using scissors, cutting at a slight angle to create a seamless transition.

Tips

- Start with a longer guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length.

- Use scissors to blend the top and sides together for a natural look.

- Cut small sections of hair at a time to ensure an even cut.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve the flat top look at home?

A: Yes, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the flat top look at home.

Q: How often should I get a flat top haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a flat top haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Hair Care

To keep your flat top looking sharp, use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and healthy. Style with a light-hold gel or pomade for a sleek, polished look.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair flat top, you can achieve this classic look at home or in the salon. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to create a stylish, low-maintenance haircut that looks great on any man.
