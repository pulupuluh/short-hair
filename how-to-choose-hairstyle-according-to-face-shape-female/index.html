How To Choose The Perfect Hairstyle According To Your Face Shape A Guide For Women

Choosing the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially when you're not sure which styles will suit your face shape. The good news is that there are plenty of hairstyles out there that will complement your unique features and enhance your natural beauty. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to choose the perfect hairstyle according to your face shape as a female.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing a Hairstyle

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back away from your face. Then, use a measuring tape to measure the following:

  • Forehead: Measure the width of your forehead at the widest point, which is usually just above your eyebrows.
  • Cheeks: Measure the width of your cheeks at the widest point, which is usually around the center of your face.
  • Jawline: Measure the width of your jawline at the widest point, which is usually just below your ears.
  • Face Length: Measure the length of your face from the center of your forehead to the bottom of your chin.

Once you have these measurements, you can use them to determine your face shape:

  • Oval: If your face length is approximately one and a half times the width of your forehead, and your forehead, cheeks, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have an oval face shape.
  • Round: If your face length is approximately the same as the width of your face, and your cheeks are the widest part of your face, you have a round face shape.
  • Square: If your forehead, cheeks, and jawline are roughly the same width, and your face length is about the same as the width of your face, you have a square face shape.
  • Heart: If your forehead is wider than your cheeks and jawline, and your chin is pointed, you have a heart-shaped face.
  • Diamond: If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrower, you have a diamond face shape.
  • Oblong: If your face length is longer than your forehead, and your forehead, cheeks, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have an oblong face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing a Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle that will complement your features. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Research hairstyles that are recommended for your face shape. You can use Google or Pinterest to find inspiration.
  2. Consider your hair type and texture. Certain hairstyles work better with straight hair, while others look better with curly or wavy hair.
  3. Think about your lifestyle. Are you someone who likes to spend a lot of time styling your hair, or do you prefer a low-maintenance look?
  4. Consider your personality. Your hairstyle should reflect your personal style and make you feel confident.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you choose a hairstyle that will suit your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your best features. If you have beautiful eyes, choose a hairstyle that will draw attention to them.
  • Avoid hairstyles that emphasize your flaws. For example, if you have a large forehead, avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair. Layers can add dimension and movement to your hair, which can be very flattering.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles. Don't be afraid to try something new and see how it looks on you.

FAQs

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

It's not uncommon to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, choose a hairstyle that flatters the most dominant features of your face.

Can I still wear a certain hairstyle even if it's not recommended for my face shape?

Of course! The most important thing is that you feel confident and comfortable in your hairstyle. However, keep in mind that certain hairstyles may not be as flattering as others.

How often should I get my hair cut?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. On average, most people get their hair cut every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Choosing the perfect hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips and considering your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, you can find a hairstyle that will make you feel confident and beautiful every day.
