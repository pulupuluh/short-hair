How To Wear A Hijab With Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine May 31, 2023 · 9 min read

Wearing a hijab can be a beautiful expression of faith and culture, but it can also be a challenge if you have short hair. Many women worry that their hair will show or that the hijab won't stay in place. But don't worry! With a few simple steps and some helpful tips, you can wear a hijab with confidence and style, no matter how short your hair is.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Hijab with Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Hijab with Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Hijab Wearers

How to Wear a Hijab with Short Hair

Before we get into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about some of the basics of wearing a hijab with short hair. First of all, you don't need to have long hair to wear a hijab. In fact, having short hair can actually make it easier to keep your hijab in place. Additionally, there are many different styles of hijab that can work well with short hair, from simple wrap styles to more elaborate turban styles.

When choosing a hijab, look for one that is made from a lightweight, breathable fabric, such as cotton or jersey. These fabrics are less likely to slip or slide around on your head, which will help keep your hijab in place. You should also choose a style that feels comfortable and secure for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now let's get into the step-by-step guide for wearing a hijab with short hair:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and completely dry. This will help your hijab stay in place and prevent your hair from showing through.

Step 2: Put on an undercap

To help keep your hijab in place, start by putting on an undercap. This can be a simple cap made from cotton or jersey fabric, or you can use a tube-style undercap made specifically for hijab wearers. Make sure the undercap covers your hairline and ears.

Step 3: Fold your hijab

Next, fold your hijab in half diagonally to create a triangle shape. You can also fold it in half again if you prefer a narrower style.

Step 4: Place the hijab on your head

Place the folded edge of the hijab at the back of your head, just above your hairline. Make sure the two ends of the hijab are even.

Step 5: Cross the ends over your chin

Take the two ends of the hijab and cross them over your chin, bringing them up towards your ears.

Step 6: Tie the ends behind your head

Tie the two ends of the hijab behind your head, making sure they are snug but not too tight. You can tie them in a knot or a bow, depending on your preference.

Step 7: Adjust the hijab

Adjust the hijab so that it covers your hairline and ears completely. You may need to tuck in any loose ends or adjust the folds to create a neat, comfortable fit.

Tips for Wearing a Hijab with Short Hair

Now that you know the basics of how to wear a hijab with short hair, here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Use bobby pins or a scarf clip to help keep your hijab in place.
  • Experiment with different styles and fabrics to find what works best for you.
  • Choose a hijab that complements your outfit and personal style.
  • If you're new to wearing a hijab, practice in front of a mirror until you feel comfortable.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a hijab with short hair:

Can I wear a hijab if I have very short hair?

Yes, you can wear a hijab with any length of hair. Just make sure your hair is clean and dry, and use an undercap to help keep your hijab in place.

What styles of hijab work best with short hair?

There are many styles of hijab that work well with short hair, including simple wrap styles, turban styles, and more elaborate styles with draping and folds. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

How can I prevent my hair from showing through my hijab?

Make sure your hair is completely covered by an undercap, and choose a hijab made from a lightweight, breathable fabric that won't slip or slide around on your head.

Hair Care for Hijab Wearers

Wearing a hijab can be a great way to protect your hair from the elements and keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair while wearing a hijab:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair oily and attract dirt and dust.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Take breaks from wearing a hijab to allow your hair and scalp to breathe.

With these tips and tricks, you can wear a hijab with confidence and style, no matter how short your hair is. Remember to experiment with different styles and fabrics to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to ask for help or advice from other hijab wearers. Happy styling!
