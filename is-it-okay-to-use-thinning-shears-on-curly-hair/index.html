Is It Okay To Use Thinning Shears On Curly Hair

Description

Curly hair can be tricky to manage, and sometimes it can be challenging to achieve the desired style. Many people with curly hair turn to thinning shears to thin out their hair and make it more manageable. However, there is a lot of debate about whether it is okay to use thinning shears on curly hair. Some people believe that it can damage the hair, while others swear by the technique. In this article, we will explore the issue and answer the question, is it okay to use thinning shears on curly hair?

How to Use Thinning Shears on Curly Hair

Thinning shears are a type of scissors that are specifically designed to thin out hair. They are used to remove bulk from the hair without significantly altering the length. To use thinning shears on curly hair, you need to follow a specific technique to avoid damaging the hair.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Use Thinning Shears on Curly Hair

Follow these steps to use thinning shears on curly hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb.
  3. Section your hair into small sections using hair clips.
  4. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight.
  5. Hold the thinning shears vertically and cut into the hair, starting from the mid-length of the hair.
  6. Make sure not to cut too much hair at once.
  7. Repeat the process on the other side of the hair.
  8. Continue until you have thinned out all the sections of your hair.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Using Thinning Shears on Curly Hair

When using thinning shears on curly hair, it is essential to keep the following tips in mind:

  • Start by using the thinning shears sparingly. You can always go back and cut more hair if necessary.
  • Make sure to use sharp thinning shears to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Don't use thinning shears on wet or damp hair as it can cause the hair to stretch and break.
  • Always make sure that you are cutting into the hair and not across it.
  • Be careful not to over-thin your hair as it can cause your hair to look thin and frizzy.

Frequently Asked Questions About Using Thinning Shears on Curly Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about using thinning shears on curly hair:

Can thinning shears damage curly hair?

Thinning shears can damage curly hair if they are not used correctly. It is essential to use sharp thinning shears and to cut into the hair rather than across it to avoid damaging the hair.

Will thinning shears make my curly hair frizzy?

Thinning shears can make your hair frizzy if you over-thin your hair. It is essential to use thinning shears sparingly and to avoid over-thinning your hair.

Can I use thinning shears on wet hair?

No, it is not recommended to use thinning shears on wet hair as it can cause the hair to stretch and break.

Hair Care for Curly Hair After Using Thinning Shears

After using thinning shears on curly hair, it is essential to take care of your hair to avoid damage. Here are some tips for hair care for curly hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment regularly to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos and conditioners that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it is wet.

In conclusion, it is okay to use thinning shears on curly hair as long as they are used correctly. It is essential to use sharp thinning shears and to cut into the hair rather than across it. With the right technique and care, thinning shears can be a great tool for managing curly hair.
