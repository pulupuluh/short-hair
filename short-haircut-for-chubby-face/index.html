Short Haircut For Chubby Face A Guide To Flattering Hairstyles

Written by Christine Mar 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a chubby face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. Short haircuts are a great option for those who want to frame their face and accentuate their features. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about short haircuts for chubby faces, including how to choose the right style, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Description

Short haircuts are a stylish and chic way to show off your features, but it can be tricky to find the right style if you have a chubby face. The key is to look for hairstyles that flatter your face shape and highlight your best features. From pixie cuts to bobs, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can work for you.

How to Choose the Right Style

When it comes to choosing the right short haircut for your chubby face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your face shape. If you have a round face, look for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of your head. If you have a square face, opt for hairstyles that soften your jawline.

Next, think about your hair texture. If you have thick, coarse hair, consider a layered bob or a shaggy pixie cut. If you have fine hair, a blunt bob or a sleek pixie may be a better option.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here are some step-by-step instructions for a few popular short haircuts for chubby faces:

Pixie Cut

  1. Start by sectioning your hair into four parts.
  2. Using a pair of scissors, cut the hair at the nape of your neck to your desired length.
  3. Continue cutting the hair in sections, working your way up to the crown of your head.
  4. Use a razor to add texture and layers to the cut.
  5. Style your hair with a texturizing spray or wax for a messy, edgy look.

Bob Cut

  1. Begin by sectioning your hair into four parts.
  2. Using a pair of scissors, cut the hair at the nape of your neck to your desired length.
  3. Continue cutting the hair in sections, working your way up to the crown of your head.
  4. Use a razor to add texture and layers to the cut.
  5. Style your hair with a flat iron or curling iron for a sleek or wavy look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short haircut for a chubby face:

  • Choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture.
  • Ask your stylist for their recommendations.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for you.
  • Accessorize with bold earrings or statement necklaces to draw attention to your face.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for chubby faces:

What hairstyles flatter a round face?

Hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown of your head, such as a layered pixie or a tousled bob, can flatter a round face.

What hairstyles flatter a square face?

Hairstyles that soften your jawline, such as a layered bob or a side-swept pixie, can flatter a square face.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim and to maintain your hairstyle.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools.
  • Experiment with different products, such as texturizing sprays or volumizing mousses, to find what works best for your hair type.

Now that you know everything there is to know about short haircuts for chubby faces, it's time to book your appointment with your stylist and try out a new look. Remember to choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture, and don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles until you find what works best for you.
