Mens Hairstyles Long On Top A Guide To Relaxed And Stylish Hair

Written by William Aug 13, 2023 · 7 min read

Long hair on top is a style that’s been around for a while now and it’s not going anywhere anytime soon. This versatile look is popular among men of all ages and can be customized to suit different face shapes and personal styles. In this article, we’ll discuss how to achieve and maintain mens hairstyles long on top, including tips for styling and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve Mens Hairstyles Long on Top
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining Long Hair on Top
  • FAQs About Mens Hairstyles Long on Top
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair on Top

How to Achieve Mens Hairstyles Long on Top

If you’re starting from short hair, it may take some time to grow your hair out to the desired length. However, if you already have medium to long hair, you can easily achieve this look by getting a trim and keeping the length on top while trimming the sides and back.

When choosing a haircut, consider your face shape and hair texture. If you have a round face, opt for a longer cut on top with shorter sides to create a more balanced look. If you have straight hair, you can achieve a sleek and polished look by keeping your hair longer and using a high-quality styling product.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

To style mens hairstyles long on top, start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product such as gel, pomade, or wax. Use a comb or your fingers to create the desired style, whether it’s a slicked-back look, messy waves, or a quiff.

If you have curly or wavy hair, you can enhance your natural texture by using a curl-defining cream or mousse. For a more polished look, blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair on Top

Maintaining mens hairstyles long on top requires some effort, but it’s worth it for the stylish and versatile look it provides. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep split ends at bay and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner formulated for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner before swimming.

FAQs About Mens Hairstyles Long on Top

What face shape suits mens hairstyles long on top?

Mens hairstyles long on top can suit a variety of face shapes, but it’s especially flattering for those with a square, oval, or heart-shaped face.

How do I style mens hairstyles long on top for a formal event?

To style your hair for a formal event, consider a sleek and polished look such as a slicked-back style or a side part. Use a high-quality styling product and a comb for a clean and refined finish.

Can I still wear a hat with mens hairstyles long on top?

A hat can be a great accessory to complement your long hair on top. Opt for a beanie, fedora, or baseball cap to style your look.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair on Top

Long hair requires special care to keep it healthy and nourished. Here are some tips for maintaining mens hairstyles long on top:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Aim for 2-3 times per week.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve and maintain mens hairstyles long on top for a stylish and relaxed look that’s sure to turn heads.
