How To Train Men s Hair To Slick Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 29, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking to achieve a classic and polished look, slicking back your hair might just be the perfect style for you. However, training your hair to slick back can be a bit challenging. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve the perfect slicked-back look that will last all day. So, let's get started!

Description

The slicked-back hairstyle has been a classic look for men for decades. It is a versatile style that can be dressed up or down, making it perfect for any occasion. The style involves combing the hair back and using a hair product to keep it in place. While it may seem simple, it takes some effort to train your hair to stay slicked back. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and polished look that will turn heads.

How to Train Men's Hair to Slick Back

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to achieving a slicked-back hairstyle is to start with clean hair. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or product buildup. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any leftover residue that can make it difficult to style your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, towel dry it until it's damp. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel as it can cause damage and frizz. Instead, gently pat your hair dry using the towel. Make sure your hair is not too wet as it can dilute the product and make it less effective.

Step 3: Apply Product

Apply a generous amount of styling product to your hair. The type of product you use depends on your hair type and the desired hold. For a strong hold, use a pomade or gel. For a lighter hold, use a hair cream or mousse. Start at the back of your head and work the product through your hair, making sure to evenly distribute it.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a comb, start at the front of your head and comb your hair straight back. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction you want it to stay. If you're looking for a more classic look, comb your hair straight back. If you want a more modern look, try combing your hair diagonally back.

Step 5: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while combing it back. This will help to set the product in your hair and give it a polished look. Use a round brush to add volume to your hair and create a sleeker look.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish it off with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to keep your hair in place all day. Make sure to hold the hairspray bottle at least 6 inches away from your hair to avoid any clumping or buildup.

Tips for Training Men's Hair to Slick Back

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back look:

  • Use a high-quality styling product to avoid any flaking or residue.
  • Comb your hair in the direction you want it to stay.
  • Blow dry your hair while combing it back to set the product in your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

How often should I wash my hair to maintain a slicked-back hairstyle?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to maintain a slicked-back hairstyle. Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, making it difficult to style.

What type of hair products should I use to achieve a slicked-back hairstyle?

The type of hair product you use depends on your hair type and the desired hold. For a strong hold, use a pomade or gel. For a lighter hold, use a hair cream or mousse.

Can I achieve a slicked-back hairstyle with curly hair?

Yes, you can achieve a slicked-back hairstyle with curly hair. You may need to use a stronger hold product and blow dry your hair more to set the product in your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and polished look. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons too frequently as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to avoid split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and polished look that will turn heads. So, go ahead and try out the slicked-back hairstyle and see the difference it can make!
