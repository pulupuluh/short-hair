Best Haircuts For Round Faces In 2023

Best Haircuts for Round Faces in 2023 | Tips, Step by Step & FAQ

Are you tired of trying different haircuts and not finding the right one for your round face? Don't worry; we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be sharing some of the best haircuts that look good on round faces. Whether you have long hair or short hair, curly or straight, we have a haircut for every hair type. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face has full cheeks and a rounded chin, with the width and length of the face almost equal. The goal of a good haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of length and definition. This can be achieved by adding volume to the crown, creating layers, and adding texture. Avoid haircuts that accentuate the roundness of the face, such as blunt bangs, one-length bobs, and center parts.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a round face, consider the following:

  • The length of the hair
  • The texture of the hair
  • The thickness of the hair
  • The shape of the face

These factors will help you determine the best haircut for your round face.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best haircuts for round faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can add volume and texture to the hair, creating the illusion of length. Opt for layers that start at the chin or below, and avoid layers that start at the cheekbones or above, as they can make the face appear wider.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help create the illusion of length and add definition to the face. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can accentuate the roundness of the face.

3. Bob with Volume

A bob with volume at the crown can elongate the face and create the illusion of length. Avoid one-length bobs, as they can accentuate the roundness of the face.

4. Wavy Lob

A wavy lob can add texture and volume to the hair, creating the illusion of length. Avoid straight haircuts, as they can make the face appear wider.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a round face:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height and volume to the crown.
  • Avoid hairstyles that accentuate the roundness of the face.
  • Consider adding layers and texture to the hair to create the illusion of length.
  • Try side-swept bangs to add definition to the face.

FAQ

1. Can I have a short haircut if I have a round face?

Yes, you can have a short haircut if you have a round face. Opt for a short haircut with volume at the crown to elongate the face.

2. Should I avoid center parts if I have a round face?

Yes, you should avoid center parts if you have a round face. Center parts can accentuate the roundness of the face.

3. What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

You should avoid hairstyles that accentuate the roundness of the face, such as blunt bangs, one-length bobs, and center parts.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best haircuts for round faces, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist. Remember to consider the length, texture, and thickness of your hair, as well as the shape of your face, when choosing a haircut. With the right haircut, you can accentuate your best features and create the illusion of length and definition.
