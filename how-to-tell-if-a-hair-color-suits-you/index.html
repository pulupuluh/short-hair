How To Tell If A Hair Color Suits You Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Jul 29, 2023 · 8 min read

Choosing a new hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you’re not sure which shade will suit you best. Whether you’re considering a bold new look or just want to freshen up your current style, it’s essential to choose a color that complements your skin tone and features. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks for how to tell if a hair color suits you, so you can make the right choice for your next salon visit.

How to Tell if a Hair Color Suits You: Step by Step

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing a hair color that suits you is to determine your skin tone. There are three main skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral. Cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones, warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones, and neutral skin tones have a mix of both. You can determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist – if they appear blue, you have a cool undertone, and if they appear green, you have a warm undertone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining which hair color will suit you best. Generally, warm hair colors like gold and honey complement warm eye colors like brown and hazel, while cool hair colors like ash and platinum flatter cool eye colors like blue and green.

Step 3: Look at Your Natural Hair Color

When choosing a new hair color, it’s important to consider your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, you may want to stick to shades that are within a few shades of your natural color. If you have light hair, you may be able to experiment with bolder shades. You can also consider highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair without completely changing the color.

Step 4: Try on Wigs or Use Virtual Try-On Tools

If you’re still not sure which hair color will suit you best, you can try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools to see how different shades will look on you. Many online retailers offer virtual try-on tools that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and see how different hair colors will look on you.

Tips for Choosing a Hair Color That Suits You

Now that you know how to determine your skin tone, consider your eye color, and look at your natural hair color, here are some additional tips for choosing a hair color that suits you:

  • Consider your lifestyle – if you have a low-maintenance lifestyle, choose a color that requires minimal upkeep
  • Think about the season – if it’s summertime, you may want to go for a lighter, brighter shade, while in the winter, a darker, richer color may be more appropriate
  • Consult with a professional – if you’re not sure which hair color will suit you best, schedule a consultation with a professional stylist who can offer advice and recommendations based on your unique features and preferences

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair colors suit cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones tend to look best with hair colors that have cool undertones, such as platinum blonde, ash brown, and cherry red.

What hair colors suit warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones tend to look best with hair colors that have warm undertones, such as honey blonde, caramel brown, and auburn red.

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

If you have sensitive skin, it’s important to choose a hair dye that is gentle and free of harsh chemicals. Look for products that are labeled “hypoallergenic” or “for sensitive skin.” You can also do a patch test before applying the dye to your entire head to ensure that you don’t have an allergic reaction.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Once you’ve chosen a hair color that suits you, it’s important to take care of your hair to ensure that the color lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your new hair color:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip the color
  • Use a color-safe hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as these can also cause the color to fade

By following these tips and tricks, you can choose a hair color that suits you and keep your locks looking vibrant and healthy. Remember to consult with a professional stylist if you’re unsure which color to choose, and don’t be afraid to experiment with different shades and styles until you find the perfect match.
