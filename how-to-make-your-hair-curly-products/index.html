How To Make Your Hair Curly Products A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Apr 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Curly hair is often seen as a symbol of beauty, but not everyone is blessed with naturally curly hair. Fortunately, there are many products available in the market that can help you achieve those luscious curls. However, these products can be quite expensive and may contain harsh chemicals that can damage your hair over time. In this blog post, we will share with you how to make your hair curly products using natural ingredients that are readily available in your kitchen.

What You Will Need

Before we get started, here are the ingredients that you will need to make your hair curly products:

  • Coconut oil
  • Honey
  • Aloe vera gel
  • Castor oil
  • Egg whites
  • Cornstarch

How to Make Your Hair Curly Products

Now that you have all the ingredients, let's get started on making your hair curly products:

Coconut Oil and Honey Hair Mask

Coconut oil is known for its moisturizing properties, while honey is a natural humectant that helps to retain moisture in your hair. Here's how to make this hair mask:

  1. Mix 2 tablespoons of coconut oil and 1 tablespoon of honey in a bowl.
  2. Apply the mixture to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  3. Leave it on for 30 minutes before rinsing it off with lukewarm water.
  4. Style your hair as desired.

Aloe Vera Gel and Castor Oil Hair Gel

Aloe vera gel is a natural hair conditioner that helps to keep your hair moisturized, while castor oil is known for its hair growth properties. Here's how to make this hair gel:

  1. Mix 2 tablespoons of aloe vera gel and 1 tablespoon of castor oil in a bowl.
  2. Apply the mixture to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  3. Style your hair as desired.

Egg Whites and Cornstarch Hair Mousse

Egg whites are a great source of protein that helps to strengthen your hair, while cornstarch helps to add volume to your hair. Here's how to make this hair mousse:

  1. Separate the egg whites from the yolks and whisk them until they are frothy.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of cornstarch to the egg whites and mix well.
  3. Apply the mixture to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  4. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Using Your Hair Curly Products

Here are some tips to help you get the best results from your hair curly products:

  • Apply the products to damp hair for best results.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to distribute the products evenly throughout your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.

FAQs

Can I use these products on chemically treated hair?

Yes, you can use these products on chemically treated hair. However, it is important to do a patch test before using the products to ensure that you are not allergic to any of the ingredients.

How often can I use these products?

You can use these products once a week or as often as needed to achieve the desired results.

Can I store these products?

Yes, you can store these products in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to a week.

Hair Care

While these hair curly products can help you achieve beautiful, luscious curls, it is important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using these natural hair curly products, you can achieve beautiful, healthy curls that will make you feel confident and beautiful.
