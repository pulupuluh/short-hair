How To Make Poofy Curly Hair Flat

Written by Frank Mar 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have poofy curly hair, you know how frustrating it can be to style. It can feel like your hair has a mind of its own, and no matter what you do, it just won't cooperate. However, there are ways to make poofy curly hair flat, so you can achieve the sleek, straight look you desire. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make poofy curly hair flat, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Make Poofy Curly Hair Flat

There are several methods you can try to make poofy curly hair flat. Here are some options:

Blow-Drying

One of the easiest ways to make poofy curly hair flat is by using a blow dryer. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to tame frizz and make your hair easier to style.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the blow dryer.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, pulling the brush through your hair as you go. Make sure to use the cool setting on your blow dryer to set the style.

Flat Ironing

Another option for making poofy curly hair flat is by using a flat iron. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  2. Section your hair into small sections using clips.
  3. Starting at the roots, clamp the flat iron onto a section of hair and slowly glide it down to the ends. Repeat this process on each section of hair.
  4. Use a comb to smooth out any flyaways.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make poofy curly hair flat:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a smoothing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. When you get out of the shower, gently towel dry your hair. Do not rub your hair vigorously with the towel, as this can cause frizz.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or detangling spray to your hair. This will help to keep your hair smooth and soft.
  4. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles.
  5. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.
  6. Choose your preferred method for making your hair flat (blow-drying or flat ironing).
  7. Style your hair as desired.
  8. Finish with a hairspray or other hair styling product to hold your style in place.

Tips for Making Poofy Curly Hair Flat

Here are some tips to help you make poofy curly hair flat:

  • Use a smoothing shampoo and conditioner to help tame frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or detangling spray to your hair to keep it soft and smooth.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.
  • When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to help smooth out your hair.
  • When flat ironing your hair, section it off into small sections to ensure that you get every strand.
  • Use a comb to smooth out any flyaways.
  • Finish with a hairspray or other hair styling product to hold your style in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make poofy curly hair flat without using heat?

Yes, you can make poofy curly hair flat without using heat by using a hair straightening brush or a hair straightening treatment.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

What is the best type of flat iron to use on poofy curly hair?

A ceramic or tourmaline flat iron is best for poofy curly hair, as it helps to distribute heat evenly and prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips for Poofy Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for poofy curly hair:

  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.

By following these tips and using the methods mentioned in this article, you can make poofy curly hair flat and achieve the sleek, straight look you desire.
