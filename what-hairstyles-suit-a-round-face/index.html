What Hairstyles Suit A Round Face

Written by Aprilia Jul 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and enhances your features. In this article, we will go over the hairstyles that suit a round face and provide you with tips on how to style your hair to achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Round Face
  3. Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for a Round Face
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care for Round Face

Description

A round face is characterized by a circular shape with equal length and width. The cheeks are full, and the jawline is soft. When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, the goal is to create the illusion of length and minimize the width of the face. The ideal hairstyle for a round face will elongate the face and provide balance.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Round Face

The right hairstyle can make all the difference in how your face looks. When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, consider the following:

Length

The length of your hair can have a significant impact on how your face looks. Long hair can elongate the face and make it appear slimmer, while short hair can make the face appear wider. If you have a round face, opt for hairstyles that are longer than chin-length.

Layers

Layered hairstyles can provide the illusion of length and add volume to the hair. If you have a round face, consider adding layers to your hair to create the appearance of a slimmer face.

Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to draw attention away from the width of the face. If you have a round face, consider getting side-swept bangs or layered bangs that fall to the side of the face.

Texture

Adding texture to your hair can provide volume and create the illusion of length. Consider adding waves or curls to your hair to create a more elongated look.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair for a Round Face

Once you have chosen the right hairstyle for your face shape, follow these steps to style your hair:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling it. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply a styling product

Apply a styling product, such as mousse or styling cream, to your hair to add volume and texture.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create the illusion of length.

Step 4: Style your hair

Style your hair according to the hairstyle you have chosen. Use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or straighten your hair.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Apply a light coat of hairspray to set your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

Get regular trims

Getting regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Use heat protectant

When using heat styling tools, always use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

Use a deep conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as blunt bangs, bobs, and short hairstyles.

Can I wear a middle part with a round face?

A middle part can make a round face appear wider. Consider a side part or a deep side part instead.

Should I avoid high ponytails?

High ponytails can accentuate the roundness of the face. Opt for lower ponytails or half-up hairstyles instead.

Hair Care for Round Face

Caring for your hair is essential to maintaining a healthy and beautiful hairstyle. Here are some tips for hair care:

Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

Avoid using hot water

Hot water can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Use lukewarm water instead.

Use a clarifying shampoo

Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove any product buildup and keep your hair healthy.

Avoid over-styling your hair

Over-styling your hair can cause damage and breakage. Give your hair a break from styling tools and let it air dry occasionally.

In conclusion, finding the right hairstyle for a round face can be a challenge, but with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering look. Consider the length, layers, bangs, and texture of your hair when choosing a hairstyle, and follow our step-by-step guide to style your hair. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty.
