How To Know If Bangs Would Look Good On You

Written by Luffy Aug 11, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you thinking about getting bangs but unsure if they will suit you? It's a common concern, but don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss how to know if bangs would look good on you, and provide you with helpful tips and advice for maintaining your new hairstyle.

How to Know If Bangs Would Look Good on You

Before deciding to get bangs, there are a few things you should consider. Here are some tips to help you determine if bangs would look good on you:

Face Shape

The first thing to consider is your face shape. Bangs can be flattering on all face shapes, but it's important to find the right length and style that works for you. For example, if you have a round face, longer, side-swept bangs can help elongate your face. On the other hand, if you have a heart-shaped face, a blunt fringe can help balance out your features.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider. If you have fine hair, wispy bangs can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a fuller, blunt fringe can help tame your locks. Curly hair can also benefit from bangs, but it's important to choose a style that works with your natural texture.

Personal Style

Your personal style is also an important factor to consider. Bangs can be versatile and can be styled in many different ways, but it's important to choose a style that fits your personal taste and lifestyle. If you prefer a low-maintenance look, longer, side-swept bangs may be best. If you like to experiment with different hairstyles, a shorter, choppy fringe may be more your style.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you determine if bangs would look good on you:

  1. Consider your face shape.
  2. Think about your hair texture.
  3. Consider your personal style and lifestyle.
  4. Look for inspiration online or in magazines.
  5. Talk to your hairstylist about your options.
  6. Try on a wig or use a virtual makeover tool to get an idea of what you would look like with bangs.
  7. Consider getting a trial fringe – a temporary clip-in or tape-in bang – to see how you like the look before committing to a full cut.
  8. Take the plunge and get your new bangs!

Tips for Getting the Perfect Bangs

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect bangs:

  • Bring pictures of your desired look to your hairstylist.
  • Communicate with your hairstylist about your hair texture and personal style.
  • Consider getting a dry cut – cutting your bangs when they are dry can help you see how they will lay and prevent any surprises when they dry.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron or round brush to style your bangs.
  • Don't be afraid to trim your bangs at home in between salon visits – just be sure to use sharp scissors and go slowly.

FAQs

How often do I need to trim my bangs?

It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long.

What if I don't like my new bangs?

If you don't like your new bangs, don't panic! Bangs can be styled in many different ways, and they will grow out eventually. Talk to your hairstylist about your concerns and they may be able to help you find a new style that works for you.

Can I still wear my hair up with bangs?

Absolutely! Bangs can be styled in many different ways, including pinned back or swept to the side. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your new bangs:

  • Wash your bangs daily or every other day to keep them looking fresh.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to help them lay flat.
  • Use a dry shampoo on your bangs to help absorb oil between washes.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much to prevent them from becoming oily.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to prevent them from becoming too long or getting in your eyes.

Now that you know how to know if bangs would look good on you, it's time to make the decision and take the plunge. Remember, bangs can be a fun and versatile way to switch up your look and express your personal style. Have fun with it and don't be afraid to experiment!
