Short Haircuts for Over 50 with Square Face

If you are over 50 and have a square face, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your best features. In this article, we will discuss some of the best short haircuts for over 50 with square face.

Description

A square face has a strong jawline and a broad forehead. The width of the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost the same. The goal of a short haircut for a square face is to soften the sharp angles of the face and make it appear more oval-shaped.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for a square face, consider a style that adds height to the crown and length to the top of the head. This will elongate the face and make it appear more oval. Avoid styles that accentuate the jawline, such as blunt bobs or straight-across bangs.

Step by Step

1. Consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with square face shapes.
2. Consider a short haircut with layers or textured ends to add movement and soften the angles of your face.
3. Opt for a side-swept or asymmetrical fringe to create the illusion of a longer face.
4. Choose a style that adds volume to the crown and height to the top of the head.
5. Avoid styles that accentuate the jawline, such as blunt bobs or straight-across bangs.

Tips

1. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your short haircut.
2. Use styling products, such as mousse or volumizing spray, to add volume to the crown and create height.
3. Experiment with different parting styles to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have short hair if I have a square face?
A: Yes, you can still have short hair if you have a square face. Just make sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your best features.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your short haircut to keep it looking its best. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type and texture. Regularly trim your hair to maintain the shape and style. Use styling products sparingly to avoid weighing down the hair.

Overall, there are many short haircuts for over 50 with square face that can flatter your face shape and enhance your best features. Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect style for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
