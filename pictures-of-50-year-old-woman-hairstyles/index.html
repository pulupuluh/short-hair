How to Choose the Perfect Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing the perfect hairstyle, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your face shape. Oval faces can pull off most hairstyles, while round faces look best with longer hair and angular cuts. Heart-shaped faces can benefit from shorter styles that accentuate the cheekbones.

Next, think about your hair texture. If you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a layered cut to add movement and volume. If your hair is thin, try a shorter style to create the illusion of thickness. Finally, consider your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, a low-maintenance cut may be the best option.

Stylish Hairstyles for Women Over 50

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hairstyle, let's take a look at some of the most stylish options for women over 50.

Short and Sassy

Short hairstyles can be incredibly flattering for women over 50. A pixie cut, for example, can help highlight your cheekbones and draw attention to your eyes. If you're not ready to go that short, a bob or lob (long bob) can also be a great option.

Long and Layered

Long hair can also be stunning on women over 50, especially when it's layered. Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, creating a youthful and vibrant look. Plus, longer hair gives you more styling options, from braids to updos.

Curly and Playful

If you have naturally curly hair, embrace it! Curly hair can be playful and fun, and there are plenty of styles that can showcase your gorgeous curls. Consider a layered cut or a shaggy bob to add texture and volume to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, it's important to know how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you create the perfect look:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Use a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or gel, to help hold your style.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the roots to create volume. Use a straightening iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  3. If you're curling your hair, use a curling iron to create loose, beachy waves. Hold the iron vertically and wrap small sections of hair around the barrel, then release after a few seconds.
  4. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to help hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Healthy hair is key to any great hairstyle, so here are a few tips to help keep your locks looking their best:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's tailored to your hair type. If you have color-treated hair, look for products that are specifically designed to protect and enhance your color.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can lead to damage and breakage. Instead, try to air-dry your hair as often as possible, and use hot tools sparingly.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and nutrients, as this can help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions women over 50 have about their hair:

Q: Is it true that women over 50 shouldn't wear long hair?

A: Absolutely not! Women over 50 can look gorgeous with long hair, as long as it's well-maintained and styled properly.

Q: What's the best hairstyle for thinning hair?

A: A shorter hairstyle, such as a bob or pixie cut, can help create the illusion of thickness for women with thinning hair.

Q: Can I still color my hair if I'm over 50?

A: Of course! Just be sure to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and natural hair color.

  • Aveda Damage Remedy Daily Hair Repair
  • Nioxin Scalp Therapy Conditioner
  • Oribe Dry Texturizing Spray
  • Bumble and Bumble Hairdresser's Invisible Oil Heat/UV Protective Primer

With these tips, styles, and products, you're sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your 50-year-old self. Whether you opt for a short and sassy cut or long and layered locks, remember to take care of your hair and have fun with your new look!
