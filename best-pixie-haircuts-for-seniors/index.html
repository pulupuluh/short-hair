Best Pixie Haircuts For Seniors

Written by Frank May 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut that's easy to maintain and stylish, a pixie cut could be the perfect choice. Pixie haircuts are short, chic, and versatile, and they can be customized to suit any face shape and hair texture. Plus, they're a great option for seniors who want a low-maintenance style that's still trendy and youthful. In this article, we'll explore some of the best pixie haircuts for seniors and offer tips on how to care for your new style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get a Pixie Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Pixie Cut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Seniors with Pixie Haircuts

How to Get a Pixie Haircut

The first step in getting a pixie haircut is to find a hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts. Look for someone who has experience working with seniors and can help you choose a style that will flatter your face shape and hair texture. When you go to your appointment, bring pictures of pixie haircuts that you like and be prepared to discuss your lifestyle and hair care routine.

Your hairstylist will start by washing and drying your hair, then using scissors or clippers to cut your hair to the desired length. They may also use a razor to add texture and layers to your hair. Once your haircut is complete, your hairstylist will show you how to style your hair and recommend products to help you maintain your new look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Pixie Cut

Styling a pixie haircut is simple and easy, especially if you have the right tools and products. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your new haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume to your hair.
  2. Use a small round brush to lift and style your hair. Blow-dry your hair in sections, starting at the back and working your way forward.
  3. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and volume. You can also use a styling cream or wax to define your hair and add shine.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

One of the best things about a pixie haircut is that it's low-maintenance and easy to care for. However, there are a few tips you should keep in mind to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and texture.

Frequently Asked Questions

What face shapes are best for pixie haircuts?

Pixie haircuts can flatter a variety of face shapes, but they're especially well-suited to oval and heart-shaped faces. If you have a round or square face, you may want to opt for a longer pixie cut that adds height and volume to your hair.

Can seniors with thinning hair wear pixie haircuts?

Yes! Pixie haircuts can actually be a great option for seniors with thinning hair, as they create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair. Your hairstylist can help you choose a style that works best for your hair type and texture.

How often should I wash my pixie haircut?

It's up to you! Pixie haircuts are low-maintenance and easy to care for, so you can wash your hair as often as you like. However, if you have dry or brittle hair, you may want to limit washing to once or twice a week to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care Tips for Seniors with Pixie Haircuts

If you have a pixie haircut, it's important to take care of your hair and scalp to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair and scalp.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote healthy hair growth and circulation.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf when you're outside.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a pixie haircut at any age and feel confident and stylish. So why not take the plunge and try a new look today?
