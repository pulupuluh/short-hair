How To Look Pretty Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia May 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you over 60 and want to look pretty and confident? You are in the right place. This article will provide you with tips and tricks to help you look your best, from your hair to your makeup, and everything in between.

How to

The first step to looking pretty over 60 is to take care of your hair. As we age, our hair becomes thinner and more fragile. To keep your hair healthy, wash it with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too many styling products. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume, and use a hair serum to add shine.

The second step is to take care of your skin. As we age, our skin becomes thinner and less elastic. To keep your skin looking young and healthy, use a moisturizer with SPF every day, and avoid spending too much time in the sun. Use a gentle cleanser to remove makeup and dirt, and use a toner to help balance your skin's pH levels.

The third step is to use makeup to enhance your natural beauty. Choose a foundation that matches your skin tone, and use a concealer to cover any blemishes or dark circles. Use a blush to add color to your cheeks, and a lipstick or lip gloss to add color to your lips.

Step by Step

Step 1: Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume.

Step 3: Use a hair serum to add shine.

Step 4: Use a moisturizer with SPF every day to keep your skin looking young and healthy.

Step 5: Use a gentle cleanser to remove makeup and dirt.

Step 6: Use a toner to help balance your skin's pH levels.

Step 7: Choose a foundation that matches your skin tone.

Step 8: Use a concealer to cover any blemishes or dark circles.

Step 9: Use a blush to add color to your cheeks.

Step 10: Use a lipstick or lip gloss to add color to your lips.

Tips

Tip 1: Get enough sleep. Lack of sleep can make you look tired and older.

Tip 2: Eat a healthy diet. A diet rich in fruits and vegetables can help keep your skin looking young and healthy.

Tip 3: Exercise regularly. Exercise can help keep your body and mind healthy.

Tip 4: Wear clothes that fit well and flatter your body shape.

Tip 5: Wear accessories that complement your outfit and enhance your style.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear makeup if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, you can still wear makeup if you have sensitive skin. Just choose products that are labeled "hypoallergenic" or "non-comedogenic" to avoid irritation.

Q: Can I dye my hair if I am over 60?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if you are over 60. Just choose a color that complements your skin tone and looks natural.

Hair Care

Finally, take care of your hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep it looking healthy.

In conclusion, looking pretty over 60 is all about taking care of yourself and enhancing your natural beauty. Follow these tips and tricks to look and feel your best at any age.
