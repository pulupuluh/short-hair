How To Cut A Little Boy s Hair With Scissors

Written by Petter May 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're a parent, cutting your little boy's hair can be a daunting task. However, with a few tips and tricks, it can be a manageable and even enjoyable experience. In this article, we'll go over how to cut a little boy's hair with scissors step by step, as well as provide some tips and answer some common questions.

How to Cut a Little Boy's Hair with Scissors

The first step to cutting a little boy's hair with scissors is to gather all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, a spray bottle filled with water, and a towel or cape to drape over your little one's shoulders to catch any stray hairs.

Step 1: Prep the Hair

Start by wetting your little boy's hair with the spray bottle. This will make it easier to cut and help prevent any flyaway hairs. Comb through the hair to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Determine the Length

Decide on the length you want to cut your little boy's hair. It's a good idea to start with a longer length and gradually go shorter if needed.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Using the scissors, begin cutting the hair along the desired length. Take small sections at a time and cut straight across. Keep in mind that hair tends to shrink when dry, so it's better to err on the side of caution and cut less than you think you need.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

After you've cut the hair to the desired length, use the scissors to blend any harsh lines. Take small sections of hair and point-cut into them. This will create a more natural, blended look.

Step 5: Finish Up

Once you've finished cutting the hair, comb it out and make any final adjustments. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven sections.

Tips for Cutting a Little Boy's Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips to make cutting your little boy's hair with scissors easier:

  • Use sharp scissors to make clean cuts and avoid damaging the hair.
  • Take breaks if your little one gets antsy or needs a break.
  • Watch some tutorials or ask a professional stylist for tips if you're unsure about cutting hair.

FAQ About Cutting a Little Boy's Hair with Scissors

Is it necessary to use a cape or towel when cutting a little boy's hair?

Yes, using a cape or towel can help catch stray hairs and protect your little one's clothes.

What should I do if I accidentally cut too much hair?

If you accidentally cut too much hair, don't panic. You can always go shorter or wait for it to grow back. Alternatively, you can take your little one to a professional stylist to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care After Cutting a Little Boy's Hair with Scissors

After cutting your little boy's hair with scissors, it's important to take care of their hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash their hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep their hair soft and manageable.
  • Comb their hair regularly to prevent tangles.

Cutting a little boy's hair with scissors can be a fun and rewarding experience. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to give your little one a stylish haircut from the comfort of your own home.
