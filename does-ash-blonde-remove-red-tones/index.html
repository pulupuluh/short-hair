Does Ash Blonde Remove Red Tones

Written by William Apr 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Description | How To | Step By Step | Tips | FAQ | Hair Care

Description

Ash blonde is a popular hair color that many women love. It is a cool-toned blonde that can help to neutralize unwanted warm tones in the hair. But, can ash blonde remove red tones? This is a common question that many women have when considering this hair color. The answer is, yes, ash blonde can help to remove red tones from the hair.

If you have naturally red hair or have dyed your hair red in the past, you may notice that it can be difficult to get rid of the red tones. Ash blonde can help to neutralize these tones and create a more natural-looking blonde color. Keep reading to learn how to achieve ash blonde hair and remove red tones from your locks.

How To Achieve Ash Blonde Hair

If you want to achieve ash blonde hair, there are a few steps you should follow:

Step 1: Lighten Your Hair

In order to achieve ash blonde hair, you will need to lighten your hair to a pale blonde color. This can be done using bleach or a hair lightener. It is important to follow the instructions carefully and to never leave the product on for longer than recommended.

Step 2: Tone Your Hair

Once your hair is lightened, it is important to tone it to achieve the ash blonde color. You can use a toner that is specifically designed for ash blonde hair or mix a toner with purple shampoo. The toner will help to neutralize any remaining warm tones in your hair and create the cool-toned ash blonde color.

Step By Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve ash blonde hair:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and free of any styling products. It is also a good idea to apply a hair mask or deep conditioner to help protect your hair from damage.

Step 2: Lighten Your Hair

Follow the instructions on your hair lightener carefully. Apply the product evenly to your hair and leave it on for the recommended amount of time. Rinse your hair thoroughly and shampoo and condition as usual.

Step 3: Tone Your Hair

Apply the toner to your hair and leave it on for the recommended amount of time. Rinse your hair thoroughly and shampoo and condition as usual. If you want an even more intense ash blonde color, you can mix a toner with purple shampoo and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when achieving ash blonde hair:

  • Always follow the instructions on your hair lightener and toner carefully.
  • Use a purple shampoo to help maintain the ash blonde color and prevent brassiness.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can cause the color to fade more quickly.
  • Protect your hair from heat styling tools by using a heat protectant spray.
  • Consider visiting a professional hairstylist to achieve the perfect ash blonde color.

FAQ

Q: Can ash blonde remove red tones?

A: Yes, ash blonde can help to neutralize red tones in the hair.

Q: Will ash blonde work on all hair types?

A: Ash blonde can work on most hair types, but it may require more lightening for darker hair colors.

Q: How often should I use purple shampoo?

A: You can use purple shampoo once or twice a week to help maintain the ash blonde color.

Hair Care

After achieving ash blonde hair, it is important to take good care of your locks to keep them looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help prevent color fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to help nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

Conclusion

Ash blonde can help to remove red tones from the hair and create a beautiful cool-toned blonde color. Follow the steps and tips outlined in this article to achieve the perfect ash blonde hair and keep your locks looking healthy and vibrant.
