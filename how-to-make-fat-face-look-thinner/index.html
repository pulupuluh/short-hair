How To Make Fat Face Look Thinner Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Mar 20, 2023 · 9 min read

Do you have a round or chubby face? Are you tired of people telling you that you look cute or adorable? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to make a fat face look thinner. We will cover everything from hairstyles to makeup to facial exercises. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • Facial Exercises
  • Makeup
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

Hair Care

The right hairstyle can do wonders for your face shape. If you have a round or chubby face, then you should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that create height and volume on top.

A layered haircut can add texture and movement to your hair, making your face look slimmer. A side-swept bang can also create the illusion of a longer face. Avoid blunt bangs or a center part, as they can make your face look wider.

Another trick is to add highlights or lowlights to your hair. This can create depth and dimension, making your face look more angular. Just make sure to consult with a professional hairstylist to find the right color and style for your face shape.

Facial Exercises

Facial exercises are a great way to tone and strengthen the muscles in your face. This can help to reduce puffiness and double chin, giving your face a more sculpted look.

One simple exercise is to pucker your lips and hold for a few seconds. Repeat this 10 times. Another exercise is to smile as wide as you can and hold for a few seconds. Repeat this 10 times as well.

You can also try the fish face exercise. Suck in your cheeks and lips and hold for a few seconds. Repeat this 10 times. Remember to do these exercises regularly to see results.

Makeup

Makeup can work wonders in creating the illusion of a slimmer face. Here are some tips to try:

  • Contouring: Use a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to contour the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline. This can create the illusion of a more chiseled face.
  • Highlighting: Use a lighter shade of foundation or highlighter on the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and nose. This can create the illusion of a more lifted and defined face.
  • Blush: Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks and blend towards your temples. This can add a pop of color and create a more angular look.
  • Lipstick: Opt for a bold lip color to draw attention away from your face shape.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to make your face look thinner:

  • Drink plenty of water to flush out toxins and reduce puffiness.
  • Avoid salty and processed foods, as they can cause water retention and bloating.
  • Wear statement earrings or necklaces to draw attention away from your face shape.
  • Wear clothes that flatter your body shape and make you feel confident.
  • Practice good posture to elongate your neck and create a more elongated look.

FAQ

Q: Can losing weight make my face look thinner?

A: Yes, losing weight can help to reduce fat in your face and create a more defined look. However, it's important to maintain a healthy weight through a balanced diet and regular exercise.

Q: Can sleeping on my side cause my face to look puffy?

A: Yes, sleeping on your side can cause fluid to build up in your face, leading to puffiness. Try sleeping on your back instead, or prop up your head with an extra pillow to elevate your face.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that I should avoid if I have a round face?

A: Yes, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs, a center part, or a bob that ends at chin level. Instead, opt for hairstyles that create height and volume on top, such as a layered haircut or a side-swept bang.

Q: Can facial exercises help to reduce a double chin?

A: Yes, facial exercises can help to tone and strengthen the muscles in your chin and neck, reducing the appearance of a double chin. Try exercises such as chin lifts or jaw clenches to target this area.

Q: Is contouring difficult to do?

A: Contouring can take some practice to get right, but there are many tutorials and guides available online to help you. Start with a light hand and build up gradually, blending well to avoid harsh lines.

Hair Care

Lastly, taking care of your hair can also make a difference in how your face looks. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make your face look wider. And don't forget to get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and fresh.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your fat face look thinner and more defined. Remember, confidence comes from within, so embrace your unique features and rock your look with pride!
