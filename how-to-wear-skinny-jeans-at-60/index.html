How To Wear Skinny Jeans At 60 A Guide For Women

Written by Aprilia Aug 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're over 60 and you've been hesitant to try out the skinny jeans trend, don't be! Skinny jeans can be a flattering and stylish choice for women of any age, as long as you know how to wear them in a way that works for your body and your personal style. In this guide, we'll give you some tips and tricks for pulling off skinny jeans at 60 with confidence.

How to Wear Skinny Jeans at 60: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose the Right Fit

When it comes to skinny jeans, the fit is key. You want to find a pair that fits comfortably but snugly, without being too tight or too loose. Look for a pair with a high waist to help smooth out any lumps or bumps, and choose a stretchy fabric that will move with you.

Step 2: Pair with the Right Shoes

One of the keys to pulling off skinny jeans is choosing the right shoes. If you're over 60, you might want to steer clear of super-high heels and opt for something more comfortable and practical, like a pair of ankle boots or sneakers.

Step 3: Balance the Proportions

Skinny jeans can be very form-fitting, so it's important to balance out the proportions of your outfit. For example, if you're wearing skinny jeans with a fitted top, consider adding a loose cardigan or a flowy scarf to add some volume to the top half of your body.

Step 4: Accessorize Wisely

Accessories can make or break an outfit, and this is especially true when it comes to skinny jeans. Keep it simple and understated, with a classic watch or a delicate necklace. Avoid anything too chunky or flashy, which can overwhelm the look.

Tips for Wearing Skinny Jeans at 60

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when wearing skinny jeans at 60:

  • Choose a dark wash for a more sophisticated look
  • Avoid distressed or ripped jeans, which can look too youthful
  • Stick to simple, solid-colored tops
  • Experiment with different styles, like high-waisted skinny jeans or cropped skinny jeans
  • Don't be afraid to add a pop of color with a bright scarf or a bold lipstick

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I wear skinny jeans if I'm over 60?

Absolutely! Skinny jeans can be a flattering and stylish choice for women of any age, as long as you know how to wear them in a way that works for your body and your personal style.

What shoes should I wear with skinny jeans at 60?

Ankle boots and sneakers are both great options for pairing with skinny jeans at 60. Avoid super-high heels, which can be uncomfortable and impractical.

How do I balance out the proportions when wearing skinny jeans?

To balance out the proportions when wearing skinny jeans, consider adding volume to the top half of your body with a loose cardigan or a flowy scarf.

Hair Care for Women Over 60

While we're on the topic of fashion for women over 60, let's not forget about hair care! As we age, our hair can become thinner, drier, and more prone to breakage. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair
  • Invest in a good hair oil or serum to add shine and moisture

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock a pair of skinny jeans at 60 and beyond!
