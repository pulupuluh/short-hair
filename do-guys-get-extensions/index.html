Do Guys Get Extensions Everything You Need To Know

Written by Robby Jul 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a guy who's struggling with thinning hair or just wants to add some length and volume to your locks, you might be wondering if extensions are an option for you. The good news is that yes, guys can get extensions! In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about getting extensions as a guy, including how to choose the right type of extensions, how to care for them, and more.

How to Choose the Right Type of Extensions

When it comes to choosing the right type of extensions for guys, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you'll want to consider the texture of your natural hair. If you have curly hair, for example, you'll want to look for extensions that are made from natural hair that has a similar curl pattern.

Another important factor to consider is the length and thickness of the extensions. If you're just looking to add a bit of volume to your hair, you might only need a few wefts or strands of extensions. However, if you're looking to add significant length, you'll need more extensions to achieve the desired look.

Types of Extensions

There are several different types of extensions to choose from, including:

  • Clip-in extensions: These are temporary extensions that can be clipped into your hair and removed whenever you want. They're a great option if you're looking for a low-commitment way to try out extensions.
  • Tape-in extensions: These are semi-permanent extensions that are taped to your natural hair using a special adhesive. They can last for several weeks to a few months, depending on how well you care for them.
  • Sew-in extensions: These are extensions that are sewn into your natural hair using a needle and thread. They can last for several weeks to a few months, and are a popular choice for people with thick or coarse hair.
  • Bonded extensions: These are extensions that are bonded to your natural hair using a keratin glue. They can last for several months, but can be damaging to your natural hair if not applied and removed properly.

How to Care for Extensions

Caring for your extensions is essential if you want them to last as long as possible and look their best. Here are a few tips for caring for your extensions:

  • Brush your extensions gently and regularly to prevent tangling.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your extensions, as this can damage the hair.
  • Wash your extensions with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using products that contain sulfates or alcohol.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair, as this can cause tangling and matting.
  • When you're not wearing your extensions, store them in a cool, dry place to prevent damage.

FAQs

Q: How much do extensions cost?

A: The cost of extensions can vary depending on the type of extensions you choose, the amount of hair you need, and where you live. Generally, you can expect to pay anywhere from $100 to $1,000 or more for extensions.

Q: How long do extensions last?

A: The lifespan of your extensions will depend on the type of extensions you choose, how well you care for them, and how fast your natural hair grows. Generally, you can expect tape-in extensions to last for several weeks to a few months, while sew-in and bonded extensions can last for several months.

Q: Can I dye my extensions?

A: It's possible to dye extensions, but it's important to be careful and use a gentle, non-damaging dye. It's also a good idea to have a professional stylist dye your extensions to ensure the best results.

Hair Care Tips

If you're considering getting extensions, it's important to take good care of your natural hair as well. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your natural hair.
  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

In conclusion, guys can definitely get extensions! Whether you're looking to add length, volume, or just switch up your look, there are plenty of options to choose from. Just be sure to choose the right type of extensions, take good care of them, and keep your natural hair healthy as well.
