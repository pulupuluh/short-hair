How To Style My Short Layered Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jul 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short layered hair has been in trend for a while now, and it's easy to see why. It's a versatile hairstyle that looks great on anyone, regardless of face shape or hair type. However, figuring out how to style short layered hair can be a bit of a challenge. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you style your short layered hair in different ways.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Layered Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Layered Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Layered Hair

How to Style Short Layered Hair

Styling short layered hair can be fun and easy once you know what you're doing. Here are some tips to get you started:

1. Use the Right Products

The key to styling short layered hair is to use the right products. Look for products that provide texture, volume, and hold. Some good options include texturizing sprays, volumizing mousses, and styling gels.

2. Consider Your Face Shape

When styling short layered hair, it's important to consider your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to add volume at the top to elongate your face. If you have a square face, you may want to soften your features with layers.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Short layered hair is versatile, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. You can try a messy, textured look one day and a sleek, polished look the next. Play around with different styles and find what works for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short layered hair:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before you style your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will help the products you use work better and last longer.

2. Apply Product

Apply a small amount of product to your hair, focusing on the roots and ends. You can use a texturizing spray, volumizing mousse, or styling gel.

3. Blow Dry

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture. For a sleeker look, use a flat brush instead.

4. Style

Style your hair using your fingers or a comb. You can create a messy, textured look by tousling your hair with your fingers. For a sleeker look, use a comb to smooth your hair down.

Tips for Styling Short Layered Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling short layered hair:

1. Don't Overdo It

When it comes to styling short layered hair, less is often more. Don't overdo it with the products, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

2. Use the Right Tools

Make sure you have the right tools on hand for styling short layered hair. A round brush, flat brush, and comb are all essential.

3. Have Fun

Styling short layered hair should be fun, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products. Enjoy the process!

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my short layered hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you may be able to go longer between washes.

Q: Can I still style my short layered hair if I have thin hair?

A: Yes! Short layered hair can actually be a great option for those with thin hair, as it can create the illusion of volume and texture.

Hair Care for Short Layered Hair

Proper hair care is important for keeping your short layered hair looking great. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair. If you do use heat, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

3. Trim Regularly

Get regular trims to keep your short layered hair looking fresh and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your short layered hair in no time. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles!
