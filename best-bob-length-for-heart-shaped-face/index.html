Best Bob Length For Heart Shaped Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you might be wondering what the best bob length is for your face shape. Bobs are a versatile and stylish haircut that can look great on anyone, but finding the right length for your face shape is key to making sure you look your best. In this ultimate guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about finding the perfect bob length for your heart shaped face, including how to measure your face shape, what to look for in a bob, and tips for styling and maintaining your new haircut.

How to Measure Your Heart Shaped Face

The first step in finding the best bob length for your heart shaped face is to measure your face shape. To do this, you'll need a flexible tape measure and a mirror.

  1. Measure the width of your forehead at the widest point, usually just above your eyebrows.
  2. Measure the width of your cheekbones at the widest point, usually just below your eyes.
  3. Measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.
  4. Measure the width of your jawline at the widest point, usually just below your ears.

Once you have these measurements, you can determine whether you have a heart shaped face. Heart shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones, a narrow chin, and a pointy jawline.

What to Look for in a Bob

When choosing the best bob length for your heart shaped face, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Length: A bob that hits at or just below the chin is usually the most flattering for heart shaped faces.
  • Layers: Layers can help add volume and movement to your hair, which can balance out a wider forehead and narrower chin.
  • Side-swept bangs: Bangs that are swept to the side can help soften a pointy jawline and draw attention to your eyes.

Step by Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Bob Length

Now that you know what to look for in a bob, here's a step by step guide to finding the perfect length for your heart shaped face:

  1. Start with a chin-length bob: A bob that hits at or just below your chin is usually the most flattering length for heart shaped faces.
  2. Add layers: Ask your stylist to add layers throughout your hair to help add volume and movement.
  3. Consider side-swept bangs: Side-swept bangs can help soften your jawline and draw attention to your eyes.
  4. Try a longer bob: If you're feeling adventurous, you can try a longer bob that hits just above your shoulders.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining Your Bob

Once you've found the perfect bob length for your heart shaped face, here are some tips for styling and maintaining your new haircut:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add volume to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to help add volume and movement.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

What bob length is best for heart shaped faces?

A bob that hits at or just below the chin is usually the most flattering length for heart shaped faces.

What should I look for in a bob if I have a heart shaped face?

You should look for a bob with layers to add volume and movement, and side-swept bangs to help soften your jawline.

How do I style and maintain my bob?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow dry with a round brush, and get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy.

Conclusion

Finding the best bob length for your heart shaped face is all about balance. A bob that hits at or just below your chin, with layers to add volume and movement, and side-swept bangs to soften your jawline, is usually the most flattering. With the right styling and maintenance, your new bob will have you looking and feeling your best.
