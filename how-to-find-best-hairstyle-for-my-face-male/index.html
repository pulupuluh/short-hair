How To Find The Best Hairstyle For Your Face Shape As A Male

Written by Bardi Jun 19, 2023 · 7 min read

As a male, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenging task. With so many options to choose from, it can be difficult to determine which one will suit your face shape the best. In this blog post, we will discuss how to find the best hairstyle for your face shape as a male.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Find the Best Hairstyle for Your Face Shape as a Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Finding the Best Hairstyle for Your Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Before we dive into how to find the best hairstyle for your face shape, let's briefly discuss the importance of choosing the right style. Your hairstyle can significantly impact your overall appearance and even your confidence level. The right hairstyle can accentuate your best features while downplaying any areas you may not want to emphasize. Therefore, it's essential to choose a hairstyle that compliments your face shape.

How to Find the Best Hairstyle for Your Face Shape as a Male

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for your face shape as a male is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Square
  • Round
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Once you've determined your face shape, you can then start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to find the best hairstyle for your face shape as a male:

  1. Determine your face shape by measuring the width and length of your face.
  2. Research hairstyles that will suit your face shape.
  3. Consider your hair type and texture when selecting a hairstyle.
  4. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for your face shape and lifestyle.

Tips for Finding the Best Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

Here are some tips for finding the best hairstyle for your face shape:

  • Consider your hair type and texture when selecting a hairstyle. For example, if you have thick, curly hair, a short, cropped style may not be the best option.
  • Look for hairstyles that balance out your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, look for styles that add height and volume to the top of your head.
  • Consider your lifestyle when selecting a hairstyle. If you have a busy schedule, opt for a low maintenance style that doesn't require a lot of upkeep.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

What face shape do I have?

To determine your face shape, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three are approximately the same width, you have a square face shape. If your forehead and jawline are narrower than your cheekbones, you have a heart-shaped face. If your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oval face shape.

What hairstyle is best for a square face?

Hairstyles that add height and volume to the top of the head, such as a pompadour or quiff, work well for a square face shape. Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear even more angular.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

By following these hair care tips and selecting the best hairstyle for your face shape, you can achieve a stylish and flattering look that will boost your confidence and overall appearance.
