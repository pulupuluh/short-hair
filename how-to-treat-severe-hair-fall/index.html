How To Treat Severe Hair Fall

Written by Christine Jul 01, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're experiencing severe hair loss, you're not alone. Many people suffer from hair fall due to a variety of reasons such as genetics, stress, hormonal changes, and poor hair care habits. But don't worry, there are steps you can take to prevent further hair loss and promote hair growth. Here are some tips on how to treat severe hair fall.

Description

Hair loss is a common problem and can be very distressing for both men and women. Severe hair fall occurs when you lose more hair than what is considered normal. This can be caused by a variety of factors such as genetics, stress, hormonal changes, and poor hair care habits. Hair fall can affect your self-esteem and confidence, but there are steps you can take to prevent further hair loss and promote hair growth.

How to

1. Determine the cause of your hair fall

The first step in treating severe hair fall is to determine the cause of your hair loss. This can be done by consulting a dermatologist or trichologist. They will examine your scalp and hair and may recommend some tests to determine the underlying cause of your hair loss.

2. Improve your diet

A healthy and balanced diet is essential for hair growth. Make sure you are getting enough protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet. Foods rich in protein such as lean meat, fish, and beans are essential for strong and healthy hair. Vitamins such as Biotin, Vitamin D, and Iron are also important for hair growth. You can also take supplements to ensure you are getting enough nutrients.

3. Use hair growth products

There are many hair growth products available in the market that can help treat severe hair fall. Look for products that contain ingredients such as minoxidil, caffeine, and keratin. These ingredients are known to promote hair growth and prevent further hair loss. Use these products as directed by the manufacturer.

4. Manage stress

Stress is a common cause of hair loss. Practice stress-reducing activities such as yoga, meditation, and deep breathing exercises. This will help reduce stress levels and promote hair growth.

5. Avoid harsh hair treatments

Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair such as hair dyes, perms, and relaxers. These treatments can damage your hair and lead to hair fall. Opt for natural hair care products that are gentle on your hair.

6. Get enough sleep

Sleep is essential for hair growth. Make sure you are getting enough sleep every night. Lack of sleep can lead to stress and hormonal imbalances that can cause hair fall.

7. Exercise regularly

Regular exercise can improve blood circulation and promote hair growth. Make sure you are getting enough exercise every week. This can be in the form of walking, jogging, or any other physical activity you enjoy.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine the cause of your hair fall by consulting a dermatologist or trichologist.
Step 2: Improve your diet by eating foods rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.
Step 3: Use hair growth products that contain ingredients such as minoxidil, caffeine, and keratin.
Step 4: Manage stress by practicing stress-reducing activities such as yoga, meditation, and deep breathing exercises.
Step 5: Avoid harsh hair treatments such as hair dyes, perms, and relaxers.
Step 6: Get enough sleep every night.
Step 7: Exercise regularly to improve blood circulation and promote hair growth.

Tips

- Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation.
- Avoid wearing tight hairstyles such as ponytails and braids.
- Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
- Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.
- Do not use hot styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons too often.

FAQ

Q. Can stress cause hair fall?

A. Yes, stress is a common cause of hair loss. It can lead to hormonal imbalances that can cause hair fall.

Q. Can hair growth products cause side effects?

A. Some hair growth products can cause side effects such as scalp irritation and dryness. It is important to use these products as directed by the manufacturer.

Q. How long does it take to see results from hair growth products?

A. It can take several weeks to several months to see results from hair growth products. It is important to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential for promoting hair growth and preventing hair fall. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil buildup. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

2. Use a hair mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. You can use natural ingredients such as coconut oil, honey, and avocado to make a hair mask at home.

3. Trim your hair regularly

Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage. This will also help your hair look healthy and shiny.

4. Protect your hair from heat

Avoid using hot styling tools too often as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

In conclusion, treating severe hair fall requires a holistic approach that involves improving your diet, using hair growth products, managing stress, and taking care of your hair. By following these tips, you can prevent further hair loss and promote hair growth.
