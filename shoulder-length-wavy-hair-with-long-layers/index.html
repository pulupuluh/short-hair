Shoulder Length Wavy Hair With Long Layers The Perfect Look For 2023

Written by Aprilia Aug 01, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Looking for a hairstyle that is both chic and low maintenance? Shoulder length wavy hair with long layers might just be the perfect look for you! This trendy hairstyle is versatile, easy to style, and suits a wide range of face shapes and hair textures. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look, as well as some tips and tricks on how to maintain it.

Description

Shoulder length wavy hair with long layers is a hairstyle where the hair is cut to shoulder length with longer layers towards the ends. This creates a soft, wavy look that is both feminine and natural. The layers add movement and texture to the hair, making it look more voluminous and bouncy. This hairstyle works well on both straight and wavy hair, and can be customized to suit different face shapes and personal preferences.

How to

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve shoulder length wavy hair with long layers:

Step 1: Consult with your hairstylist

Before getting a haircut, it is important to consult with a professional hairstylist. They will be able to assess your hair texture, face shape, and personal preferences to create a customized haircut that suits you best. Make sure to bring some inspiration photos or ideas to the consultation to help communicate your desired look.

Step 2: Shampoo and condition your hair

On the day of the haircut, make sure to shampoo and condition your hair to ensure that it is clean and free of any product buildup. This will also make it easier for the hairstylist to cut and style your hair.

Step 3: Cut your hair to shoulder length

The hairstylist will cut your hair to shoulder length, making sure to create a soft, blended look. They will also add longer layers towards the ends of your hair to create the wavy effect.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once the haircut is done, you can style your hair to create the wavy look. You can use a curling iron, a flat iron, or even just scrunch your hair with some product to create natural-looking waves. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray and a hair oil or serum to protect your hair and add shine.

Tips

Here are some tips and tricks on how to maintain shoulder length wavy hair with long layers:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to brush your hair, as this will minimize breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dry and brittle. Instead, try to wash it every other day or every two days.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it look dull and lifeless. If you must use heat, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and a lower heat setting.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shoulder length wavy hair with long layers:

Q: Will this hairstyle suit my face shape?

A: Shoulder length wavy hair with long layers is a versatile hairstyle that suits a wide range of face shapes, including round, oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. However, it is important to consult with a professional hairstylist to ensure that the haircut is customized to suit your specific face shape and personal preferences.

Q: Can I wear this hairstyle to work?

A: Yes, shoulder length wavy hair with long layers is a chic and professional hairstyle that is suitable for the workplace. However, make sure to style it in a neat and polished way, and avoid using too much product or accessories.

Q: How do I prevent my hair from frizzing?

A: To prevent your hair from frizzing, make sure to use a smoothing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a hair oil or serum to add shine and control. Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can create friction and cause frizz. You can also use a silk or satin pillowcase to minimize friction while you sleep.

Hair Care

Shoulder length wavy hair with long layers requires minimal maintenance, but it is still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can help promote healthy hair growth and prevent damage.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated from the inside out.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve and maintain the perfect shoulder length wavy hair with long layers. Whether you're looking for a casual, beachy vibe or a polished, professional look, this hairstyle has got you covered. So go ahead and give it a try!
