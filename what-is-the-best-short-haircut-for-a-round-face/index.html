What Is The Best Short Haircut For A Round Face

Written by William Jun 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that do not compliment your round face? If so, you are not alone. A round face can be a bit tricky when it comes to choosing the right haircut. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this blog post, we will discuss the best short haircut for a round face.

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a wide forehead. This face shape is generally symmetrical, with the width and length of the face being almost equal. When it comes to choosing a short haircut for a round face, the goal is to create an illusion of length and balance.

How to

The best short haircut for a round face is the pixie cut. This haircut is perfect for creating an illusion of length and balance. The pixie cut is a short haircut that is typically cut close to the head, with layers added to create texture and movement. It is a low-maintenance haircut that is perfect for busy women.

Step by Step

Here is the step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect pixie cut for a round face:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape.
  2. Wash and dry your hair completely.
  3. Section your hair into manageable parts.
  4. Start cutting your hair from the back, gradually working your way to the front.
  5. Add layers to create texture and movement.
  6. Style your hair using a styling product that works for your hair type.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best short haircut for a round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin or cheekbone level, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Opt for a side-swept fringe or bangs to add length to your face.
  • Choose a haircut with layers to create texture and movement.
  • Use a volumizing product to add volume to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best short haircut for a round face:

Q. Can I still have long hair with a round face?

A. Yes, you can still have long hair with a round face. However, it is important to keep in mind that the length of your hair can affect the appearance of your face shape. Opt for long layers to create an illusion of length and balance.

Q. What other haircuts can I try for a round face?

A. Apart from the pixie cut, you can also try a bob, a lob (long bob), or a layered haircut.

Q. How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A. It is recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it is important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

In conclusion, the best short haircut for a round face is the pixie cut. It is a low-maintenance haircut that is perfect for busy women. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape. Follow these tips and hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best.
