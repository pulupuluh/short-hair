How To Make 40S Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to add some vintage glamour to your hairstyle, a 40s-inspired look is the perfect choice. This classic look is characterized by soft waves, voluminous curls, and elegant updos. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve the perfect 40s hairstyle.

How to

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

To get the most out of your 40s hairstyle, you'll want to start with clean, dry hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils, and let your hair air dry or blow dry it on a low heat setting.

Step 2: Create a deep side part

The 40s look typically features a deep side part, so use a comb to create a part that begins at the highest point of your eyebrow and extends to the crown of your head.

Step 3: Curl your hair

Use a curling iron to create soft waves or voluminous curls. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down to the ends of your hair, curling small sections at a time.

Step 4: Pin your hair

Once you've curled your hair, use bobby pins to pin it into place. For an elegant updo, twist your hair into a low bun at the nape of your neck and secure it with bobby pins.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

To ensure your 40s hairstyle stays in place all day, finish it off with a light mist of hairspray.

Tips

- Use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair to avoid damage.

- If you have fine hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and body.

- Experiment with different curling techniques to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

Q: How long will a 40s hairstyle last?

A: With the right products and techniques, a 40s hairstyle can last all day.

Q: Can I achieve a 40s hairstyle with short hair?

A: Yes, you can! Use a curling iron to create soft waves or curls, and pin them into place with bobby pins.

Q: Do I need to use a specific type of hairspray for a 40s hairstyle?

A: No, any light-hold hairspray will work. Just be sure to use it sparingly to avoid a stiff or crunchy look.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, be sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve the perfect 40s hairstyle in no time. Whether you're looking to channel old Hollywood glamour or just want to add a touch of vintage charm to your look, this classic hairstyle is sure to impress.
