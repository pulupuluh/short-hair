What Hairstyle Is Best For Long Thick Hair

Written by Frank May 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Long thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can look stunning, it can also be difficult to manage and style. If you're looking for some inspiration for your long thick locks, read on for our top hairstyle recommendations.

Description

Long thick hair is defined as hair that is longer than shoulder-length and has a large amount of hair strands. This hair type can be challenging to manage, but there are many hairstyles that can help tame it while still looking fabulous.

How-To

The best hairstyle for long thick hair is one that considers the texture and volume of your hair. Here are some of the most popular hairstyles for long thick hair:

1. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut can help thin out your hair and add movement and texture. This style works well for those with thick hair as it can lighten the weight of the hair and make it easier to style. It also adds dimension to your hair and gives it a more voluminous appearance.

2. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are the perfect hairstyle for long thick hair. This hairstyle is effortless and creates a relaxed, carefree look. To achieve this look, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves in your hair. Finish with a texturizing spray for added volume and hold.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for long thick hair as it allows you to show off your length while keeping your hair out of your face. To achieve this look, take the top half of your hair and pull it back, securing it with a hair tie or clip. Leave the bottom half of your hair down for a sleek and polished look.

Step-by-Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving the perfect hairstyle for long thick hair:

1. Layered Haircut

  1. Visit a hair salon and ask for a layered haircut.
  2. Ask your stylist to thin out your hair to remove some of the weight.
  3. Style your hair as desired.

2. Beachy Waves

  1. Wash your hair and apply a heat protectant.
  2. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves in your hair.
  3. Finish with a texturizing spray for added volume and hold.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Take the top half of your hair and pull it back, securing it with a hair tie or clip.
  2. Leave the bottom half of your hair down and style as desired.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling long thick hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush to help distribute your hair's natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for long thick hair:

1. How do I prevent my hair from looking too heavy?

One way to prevent your hair from looking too heavy is to get regular haircuts to thin out your hair. You can also try using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add lift and body to your hair.

2. Can I wear my hair straight if it's long and thick?

Absolutely! Long thick hair looks stunning when worn straight. Just be sure to use a heat protectant and straightening product to prevent damage to your hair.

3. What's the best way to style my hair for a formal event?

For a formal event, you may want to consider an updo or a sleek blowout. Consult with your hairstylist to find a hairstyle that complements your face shape and outfit.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful long thick hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and hairstyle recommendations, you can embrace your long thick hair and look fabulous every day.
