Low Top Taper Fade A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 17, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and versatile haircut, low top taper fade is definitely worth considering. This popular hairstyle features short sides that gradually taper down to the neckline, creating a neat and polished look. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about low top taper fade, from how to get it to how to care for it.

Description

Low top taper fade is a type of haircut that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving the top longer. The hair on the sides and back is then gradually tapered down to the neckline, creating a seamless blend with the longer hair on top. The result is a stylish and modern look that works well for all hair types and face shapes.

How to Get a Low Top Taper Fade

Getting a low top taper fade is relatively easy, but it's important to find a skilled barber who can create the look you want. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a low top taper fade:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step is to decide how short you want your hair to be on the sides and back. This will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. A good rule of thumb is to start with a longer length and gradually go shorter until you achieve the desired look.

Step 2: Create the Fade

Next, your barber will create the fade by using clippers to gradually taper the hair down to the neckline. The key is to blend the shorter hair on the sides and back with the longer hair on top, creating a seamless transition.

Step 3: Trim the Top

Once the fade is complete, your barber will trim the hair on top to the desired length. This can be done with scissors or clippers, depending on the look you're going for.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Finally, it's time to style your hair. Low top taper fade works well with a variety of styles, from messy and textured to slick and polished. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining a Low Top Taper Fade

Once you have your low top taper fade, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Use high-quality styling products to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hairdryers and straighteners, as these can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQ

Is a low top taper fade suitable for all hair types?

Yes, low top taper fade works well for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often should I get my low top taper fade trimmed?

It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Can I style my low top taper fade in different ways?

Yes, low top taper fade is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many different ways, from messy and textured to slick and polished.

Hair Care for Low Top Taper Fade

Keeping your hair healthy and moisturized is key to maintaining a low top taper fade. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hairdryers and straighteners too often, as these can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to support healthy hair growth.
  • Get enough sleep and exercise regularly to reduce stress and promote overall health.

Now that you know everything about low top taper fade, it's time to book an appointment with your barber and try it out for yourself. With the right care and styling, this haircut can help you achieve a polished and stylish look that's sure to turn heads.
