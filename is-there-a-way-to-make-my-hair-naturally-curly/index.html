Is There A Way To Make My Hair Naturally Curly

Written by Daniel Aug 12, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have straight hair and have always dreamed of having bouncy, curly locks, you may be wondering if there is a way to make your hair naturally curly. The good news is that there are several methods you can try to achieve those coveted curls! In this article, we'll explore some of the most popular ways to make your hair naturally curly, as well as provide tips and tricks for maintaining your new look.

Description

Before we dive into the how-to of making your hair curly, let's talk about what we mean by "naturally curly". There are two main types of curly hair: natural curls and styled curls. Natural curls are those that occur naturally due to the shape of the hair follicle. Styled curls are created using heat tools, such as curling irons or hot rollers.

In this article, we'll be focusing on ways to make your hair naturally curly, without the use of heat tools or chemicals. Keep in mind that while these methods can help you achieve curly hair, the results may vary depending on your hair type and texture.

How To: Make Your Hair Naturally Curly

Method 1: Twist-Outs

Twist-outs are a popular method for making straight hair curly. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair.
  2. Section your hair into small sections and twist each section tightly, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  3. Allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until completely dry.
  4. Untwist your hair and fluff to create volume.

Tip: For best results, use a curl cream specifically designed for twist-outs.

Method 2: Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are another popular method for making straight hair curly. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair.
  2. Section your hair into small sections and twist each section tightly, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  3. Wrap each twisted section into a small knot and secure with a bobby pin or hair elastic.
  4. Allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until completely dry.
  5. Untwist your hair and fluff to create volume.

Tip: For best results, wrap the knots tightly and use a curl cream specifically designed for Bantu knots.

Method 3: Flexi Rods

Flexi rods are a heat-free way to create curls in straight hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair.
  2. Section your hair into small sections and wrap each section tightly around a flexi rod.
  3. Allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until completely dry.
  4. Remove the flexi rods and fluff to create volume.

Tip: For best results, use a curl cream specifically designed for use with flexi rods.

Step by Step: How to Make Your Hair Naturally Curly

Now that you know the methods, let's break down the steps:

  1. Start with freshly washed hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair.
  3. Section your hair into small sections.
  4. Choose your method (twist-outs, Bantu knots, or flexi rods) and follow the steps outlined above.
  5. Allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until completely dry.
  6. Remove the twists, knots, or rods and fluff your hair to create volume.

Tip: To maintain your curly hair, use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz. Avoid using heat tools or harsh chemicals, as these can damage your curls.

Tips for Making Your Hair Naturally Curly

Here are some additional tips for making your hair naturally curly:

  • Start with freshly washed hair for best results.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair to help define your curls.
  • Section your hair into small sections to ensure that each curl is well-defined.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Be patient! It may take some trial and error to find the method that works best for your hair type and texture.

FAQs

Q: Will these methods work on all hair types?

A: While these methods can work on most hair types, the results may vary depending on your hair texture and thickness. It may take some trial and error to find the method that works best for you.

Q: How long will the curls last?

A: The longevity of your curls will depend on your hair type and how well you maintain them. On average, the curls can last for a few days to a week.

Q: Can I use heat tools to make my hair curly?

A: While heat tools can create curls, they can also damage your hair. We recommend using heat-free methods to make your hair naturally curly.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair to help define your curls.
  • Avoid using heat tools or harsh chemicals, as these can damage your curls.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your hair naturally curly and keep it healthy and beautiful!
