How To Bend Hair Backwards Tips Tricks And Care

Written by Christine Aug 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

In the world of hair, there are endless styles and trends that come and go. One style that has been around for a while and doesn't seem to be going anywhere anytime soon is the backwards bend. This style involves bending your hair backwards, away from your face, to create a sleek, polished look. In this article, we'll be discussing how to achieve this look with ease, as well as some tips and tricks to keep your hair healthy and glowing.

How to Bend Hair Backwards

The backwards bend is a relatively simple style to achieve, but it does require some practice and patience. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair tends to be oily, you may want to use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil.
  2. Part your hair down the middle or to one side, whichever you prefer.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front and hold it straight up.
  4. Using a flat iron, clamp down on the section of hair and twist the iron away from your face.
  5. Slowly slide the iron down the length of the hair, continuing to twist as you go.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 on the other side of your head.
  7. Finish with a light spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

It's important to note that the backwards bend works best on hair that is shoulder-length or longer. If your hair is shorter, you may need to use smaller sections or a different technique to achieve the look.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin styling your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. If your hair is wet or damp, it will be more difficult to achieve the backwards bend. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair completely, or let it air dry if you have time.

Step 2: Part your hair

Decide where you want your part to be and use a comb to create a clean, straight line. You can part your hair down the middle or to one side, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Take a small section of hair

Starting at the front of your head, take a small section of hair and hold it straight up. This will be the first section you style.

Step 4: Use a flat iron

Using a flat iron, clamp down on the section of hair and twist the iron away from your face. Make sure the iron is positioned at the root of the hair so that the bend starts at the root.

Step 5: Slide the iron down the hair

Slowly slide the iron down the length of the hair, continuing to twist as you go. The slower you go, the more defined the bend will be.

Step 6: Repeat on the other side

Repeat steps 3-5 on the other side of your head to create a balanced look.

Step 7: Finish with hairspray

Once you've styled all of your hair, finish with a light spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place. You can also use a shine spray or serum to add some extra shine.

Tips for Bending Hair Backwards

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect backwards bend:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't use too much hairspray - a light mist is all you need.
  • Experiment with different sizes of sections to find what works best for your hair.
  • Be patient - the backwards bend takes some practice to master.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve the backwards bend on short hair?

A: Yes, but you may need to use smaller sections or a different technique to achieve the look.

Q: Do I need to use a flat iron to achieve the backwards bend?

A: A flat iron is the easiest tool to use, but you can also use a curling iron or hot rollers to achieve the look.

Q: How long will the backwards bend last?

A: The backwards bend should last all day with the help of a light spritz of hairspray.

Hair Care for the Backwards Bend

To keep your hair healthy and glowing, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair - use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature of your styling tools on the lower side.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair - 2-3 times a week is usually enough.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to prevent breakage and damage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect backwards bend with ease. Remember to take care of your hair properly to keep it healthy and glowing.
