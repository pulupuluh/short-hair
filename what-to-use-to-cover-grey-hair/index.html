What To Use To Cover Grey Hair Tips How Tos And Faqs

Written by Christine Feb 20, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Grey hair is a natural occurrence, but that doesn't mean you have to embrace it if you don't want to. Covering up grey hair can be a quick and easy fix to help you look younger and more vibrant. In this blog post, we'll be discussing what to use to cover grey hair, including tips, how-tos, and FAQs.

What to Use to Cover Grey Hair: Description

There are many options for covering grey hair, including hair dyes, root touch-up sprays, and hair mascaras. Hair dye is the most popular option, as it provides long-lasting coverage and comes in a wide range of shades. Root touch-up sprays and hair mascaras are great for temporary coverage, and are ideal for touching up roots in between dye jobs.

How to Cover Grey Hair: Step by Step

Covering grey hair is a simple process that can be done at home or at a salon. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

  1. Choose a hair dye that matches your natural hair color or the color you want to achieve.
  2. Read the instructions on the box carefully before starting.
  3. Wear gloves to protect your hands and apply the dye to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the tips.
  4. Leave the dye on for the recommended time, then rinse it out thoroughly with warm water.
  5. Apply a conditioner to your hair to help it stay soft and shiny.

For root touch-up sprays and hair mascaras, simply apply the product to your roots or grey hairs and blend it in with your fingers or a brush.

Tips for Covering Grey Hair

Here are some tips to help you cover up grey hair effectively:

  • Choose a hair dye that is specifically designed for grey hair coverage.
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help your color last longer.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can cause your color to fade faster.
  • If you're using a root touch-up spray or hair mascara, make sure to blend it in well to avoid any streaks or clumps.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have about covering grey hair:

Q: Can I use regular hair dye to cover grey hair?

A: Yes, you can use regular hair dye to cover grey hair. However, there are hair dyes specifically designed for grey hair coverage that may be more effective.

Q: Will covering my grey hair damage my hair?

A: Covering grey hair with hair dye or other products shouldn't cause any damage to your hair, as long as you follow the instructions carefully and use products that are safe for your hair type.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: This will depend on how fast your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. Some people may need to touch up their roots every 2-3 weeks, while others can go up to 6 weeks without needing a touch-up.

Hair Care for Covering Grey Hair

When covering grey hair, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to give your hair a deep conditioning treatment.

Covering grey hair is a simple and effective way to look younger and more vibrant. Whether you're using hair dye, root touch-up sprays, or hair mascaras, make sure to follow the instructions carefully and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
