Male Haircut Short Back And Sides Long On Top

Written by Daniel Sep 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read
Are you looking for a stylish and trendy haircut that looks good on any occasion? Then, you might want to try the male haircut short back and sides long on top. This hairstyle has been around for a while, but it's still a popular choice among men of all ages. In this blog post, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, tips, and hair care.

Description

The male haircut short back and sides long on top is a versatile haircut that features a short cut on the back and sides of the head, while the top is kept longer. This hairstyle is also known as the "undercut," and it's perfect for men who want a low-maintenance but stylish haircut. This haircut can be customized to suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style.

How to

Getting a male haircut short back and sides long on top is easy if you follow these simple steps: 1. Choose the right length: Determine how long you want the top of your hair to be. You can go for a short or long top, depending on your preference and face shape. 2. Cut the back and sides: Ask your barber or hairstylist to cut the back and sides of your hair short. You can choose the length you want, but usually, it's around 1-2 inches. 3. Style the top: Style the top of your hair as you like. You can slick it back, comb it over, or leave it messy. Use a hair product to keep it in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a male haircut short back and sides long on top: 1. Choose your preferred length for the top of your hair. 2. Show a picture of the hairstyle to your barber or hairstylist. 3. Ask your barber or hairstylist to cut the back and sides of your hair short. 4. Style the top of your hair using a hair product.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a male haircut short back and sides long on top: 1. Choose the right hair product: Use a hair product that suits your hair type and style. If you have thin hair, use a volumizing product. If you have curly hair, use a product that enhances your curls. 2. Maintain the length: Trim the back and sides of your hair regularly to maintain the length. This will help keep your hairstyle clean and polished. 3. Experiment with different styles: Don't be afraid to try different styles with your haircut. You can slick it back, comb it over, or leave it messy. Experimenting with different styles can help you find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Q: Is the male haircut short back and sides long on top suitable for all face shapes?
A: Yes, this haircut can be customized to suit any face shape. Q: How often should I get a trim?
A: It depends on how fast your hair grows. Usually, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks. Q: Can I style the top of my hair differently?
A: Yes, you can style the top of your hair any way you like. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips: 1. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. 2. Avoid using too much heat on your hair. If you use a blow dryer or straightener, use a heat protectant spray. 3. Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length and avoid split ends. 4. Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair. In conclusion, the male haircut short back and sides long on top is a stylish and trendy hairstyle that looks good on anyone. Whether you have a busy lifestyle or you want to look sharp for a special occasion, this haircut is a great choice. Follow the tips and hair care advice we've provided in this blog post to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.
