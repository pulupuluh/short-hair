Mens Haircut Fade Long On Top A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Apr 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and modern haircut for men that is both versatile and low maintenance? If so, then the mens haircut fade long on top may be perfect for you. This popular hairstyle features a gradual taper on the sides and back that blends into longer hair on top, creating a striking contrast that is both trendy and timeless.

Description

The mens haircut fade long on top is a popular variation of the classic fade haircut. It is characterized by a gradual taper on the sides and back that gradually blends into longer hair on top. The longer hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured. This hairstyle is often paired with a beard or stubble to create a rugged and masculine look.

How To

If you are interested in getting a mens haircut fade long on top, you should visit a professional barber or hairstylist. They will be able to assess your hair type and face shape, and recommend a style that will suit you best. Here are the basic steps involved in getting a mens haircut fade long on top:

Step 1: Choose your desired length and style

Before you visit the barber or hairstylist, you should have a clear idea of the length and style that you want. Look for inspiration online or in magazines, and bring photos with you to show your stylist.

Step 2: Determine the fade length

The fade length refers to how short the hair is tapered on the sides and back. You can choose from a high fade, where the hair is tapered very short near the top of the head, or a low fade, where the hair is tapered longer near the ears.

Step 3: Trim the top

The hairstylist will trim the longer hair on top to your desired length and style it according to your preferences.

Step 4: Blend the fade

The barber or hairstylist will use clippers to gradually blend the shorter hair on the sides and back into the longer hair on top. This creates a gradual taper that is seamless and natural-looking.

Step 5: Finish with styling products

Once the haircut is complete, the stylist will apply styling products to help you achieve your desired look. This may include pomade, wax, or gel.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a mens haircut fade long on top:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Bring photos of your desired style to show your stylist.
  • Be open to your stylist's recommendations and advice.
  • Maintain your haircut with regular trims and styling products.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQ

How often do I need to get a mens haircut fade long on top?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep it looking sharp.

Can I style my hair differently with a mens haircut fade long on top?

Yes, you can experiment with different styles and textures on top, from slicked back to messy and textured.

Is the mens haircut fade long on top suitable for all hair types?

The mens haircut fade long on top can be adapted to suit most hair types, from straight to curly and everything in between. Your stylist can recommend a style and products that will work best for your hair type.

Hair Care

To keep your mens haircut fade long on top looking its best, you should use quality hair care products and follow a regular grooming routine. This may include shampooing and conditioning your hair regularly, using styling products to achieve your desired look, and getting regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Overall, the mens haircut fade long on top is a versatile and modern hairstyle that can be adapted to suit a variety of face shapes and hair types. By following these tips and maintaining a regular grooming routine, you can keep your haircut looking sharp and stylish for weeks to come.
