Mid Length Wavy Hair With Layers The Perfect Style For 2023

Written by Daniel Jun 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's easy to maintain and looks great, mid length wavy hair with layers might just be the perfect choice for you. This versatile style can work for any occasion, from a day at the office to a night out with friends. In this guide, we'll show you how to achieve this look and provide tips on how to care for your hair once you've got it.

Description

Mid length wavy hair with layers is a popular hairstyle that's been in fashion for many years. It's a great way to add volume and movement to your hair, while still keeping it manageable. This style works well for all hair types, from fine and straight to thick and curly.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're interested in trying out mid length wavy hair with layers, here's how to achieve the look:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair soft and hydrated, which is important for achieving the perfect waves.
  2. Once you've washed your hair, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage when you're using styling tools.
  3. Next, blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a smooth base for your waves.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves throughout your hair. Start at the bottom of your hair and work your way up, curling small sections of hair at a time.
  5. Once you've curled your entire head, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create a more natural, wavy look.
  6. Finally, finish off your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your waves in place.

Tips

If you want to make the most of your mid length wavy hair with layers, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet. This will help to prevent breakage and damage.
  • Avoid using a lot of heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness. If you do need to use heat, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize the damage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Try to avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mid length wavy hair with layers:

What face shapes work best with this style?

This style works well for most face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days, as this will help to maintain your hair's natural oils and prevent dryness.

What type of products should I use on my hair?

For mid length wavy hair with layers, it's best to use lightweight products that won't weigh your hair down. Look for products that provide moisture and hold without being too heavy, such as mousse or texturizing spray.

Hair Care

To keep your mid length wavy hair with layers looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Try to avoid using heat on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Finally, make sure to get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect mid length wavy hair with layers and keep it looking its best. Whether you're heading to work or out for a night on the town, this versatile style is sure to turn heads.
