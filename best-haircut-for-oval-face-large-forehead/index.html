Best Haircut For Oval Face Large Forehead

Written by Petter Mar 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oval face with a large forehead, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that will flatter your face shape and balance out your features. In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for an oval face with a large forehead, along with some tips and tricks for maintaining your new look.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, and cheekbones that are high and prominent. If you also have a large forehead, you may feel self-conscious about your features. The right haircut can help you feel confident and beautiful.

How To

The best haircut for an oval face with a large forehead is a style that adds volume and texture to the top of the head. This will balance out your features and draw attention away from your forehead. Here are some steps to achieve this look:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

For an oval face with a large forehead, a medium-length haircut is the best choice. This length will add volume to the top of the head and balance out your features. Avoid short haircuts that can make your forehead look even larger.

Step 2: Add Layers

Layers are an excellent way to add volume and texture to your hair. Ask your stylist to add layers to the top of your head, starting at the crown. This will create volume and balance out your features.

Step 3: Consider Bangs

Bangs are an excellent option for an oval face with a large forehead. They can help to cover up your forehead and draw attention to your eyes. Consider side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that blend in with the rest of your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a round brush to create volume and texture.
  • Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for an oval face with a large forehead:

What is the best haircut for a large forehead?

The best haircut for a large forehead is a style that adds volume and texture to the top of the head. Consider a medium-length haircut with layers and side-swept bangs.

What face shape is best for a bob?

A bob is a versatile haircut that can flatter a variety of face shapes. However, it is particularly well-suited to oval and heart-shaped faces.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail?

A ponytail can be a great option for an oval face with a large forehead. However, be sure to add volume to the top of your head to balance out your features.

Hair Care

Finally, it is important to take good care of your hair to maintain your new haircut. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid harsh chemicals and excessive heat styling. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when wet to prevent breakage. With the right care and maintenance, your new haircut will look beautiful and flattering for weeks to come.

Overall, the best haircut for an oval face with a large forehead is a medium-length style with layers and side-swept bangs. This will add volume and texture to the top of your head, balance out your features, and help you feel confident and beautiful. With the right care and maintenance, your new haircut will look great for weeks to come.
