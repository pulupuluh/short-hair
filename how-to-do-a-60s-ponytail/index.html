How To Do A 60S Ponytail A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 06, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking for a classic and chic hairstyle that's easy to do, a 60s ponytail might be just what you need. This iconic style is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a casual day out or a formal event. How to Do a 60s Ponytail

A 60s ponytail is a simple yet elegant hairstyle that can be achieved with just a few steps. Here's what you'll need:

  • Brush
  • Hair elastic
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these easy steps to achieve a 60s ponytail:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. You want your hair to be smooth and free of any bumps.

Step 2: Tie Your Hair

Gather your hair at the crown of your head and tie it with a hair elastic. Make sure the elastic is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not too tight that it hurts your scalp.

Step 3: Tease Your Hair

Take a comb or brush and tease the ponytail to create volume. This step is important to achieve a 60s-inspired look.

Step 4: Hairspray Your Hair

Spray your ponytail with hairspray to keep it in place. You can also spray the rest of your hair to prevent any flyaways.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off the look with a final spritz of hairspray for extra hold and shine.

Tips for a Perfect 60s Ponytail

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect 60s ponytail:

  • Use a boar bristle brush to create a smooth finish
  • Tease your hair in sections for more volume
  • Use a hair elastic that matches your hair color
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil to add shine and prevent frizz

FAQs

What hair length is best for a 60s ponytail?

A 60s ponytail can be achieved with any hair length, but longer hair will give you more styling options.

Can I wear a 60s ponytail to a formal event?

Yes, a 60s ponytail is a versatile hairstyle that can be dressed up or down. You can add accessories such as a headband or hairpins for a more formal look.

How long does a 60s ponytail take to do?

A 60s ponytail is a quick and easy hairstyle that can be done in just a few minutes.

Hair Care for a 60s Ponytail

To keep your 60s ponytail looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, which can cause damage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you're ready to rock a 60s ponytail like a pro. Whether you're headed to a party or just running errands, this timeless hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
