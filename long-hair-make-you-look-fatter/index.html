Does Long Hair Make You Look Fatter The Truth Revealed

Written by Luffy Mar 03, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're someone who likes to keep their hair long, you might have heard this popular myth that long hair makes you look fatter. But is that really true? In this post, we'll explore the truth behind this myth and how you can style your long hair to look your best.

Description

Long hair has always been associated with beauty and femininity. But some people believe that having long hair can make you look heavier than you actually are. This is a common misconception that has been around for years, and it's time to set the record straight.

How to

If you're worried that your long hair is making you look heavier, there are a few things you can do to change that. Here are some tips:

1. Get the Right Cut

The right haircut can make a huge difference in the way your hair frames your face. If you have a round face, for example, you might want to consider getting layers or a side-swept bang to help elongate your face.

2. Add Volume

Adding volume to your hair can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. You can do this by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-drying your hair upside down, or using a hair volumizer.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

There are many different ways to style long hair, and some styles might be more flattering for your face shape than others. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your long hair to look your best:

Step 1: Choose the Right Cut

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to choose the right cut. If you have a round face, for example, you might want to consider getting layers or a side-swept bang to help elongate your face.

Step 2: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add volume to your hair and create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Step 3: Blow-Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow-dry your hair upside down to add even more volume. Use a round brush to help create waves and curls.

Step 4: Experiment with Different Styles

Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. You can try a half-up, half-down style, a messy bun, or loose waves.

Step 5: Use Hair Accessories

Finally, add some hair accessories to complete your look. A headband or hair clip can help add interest to your hairstyle and draw attention away from your face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your long hair and look your best:

1. Avoid Heavy Products

Avoid using heavy products like oils and serums, as these can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.

2. Use a Light-Hold Hairspray

Use a light-hold hairspray to help keep your style in place without weighing your hair down.

3. Consider Getting Highlights

Consider getting highlights to help add dimension to your hair and create the illusion of a slimmer face.

FAQ

Does Long Hair Make You Look Fatter?

No, long hair does not make you look fatter. This is a common misconception that has been around for years. The way you style your hair and the cut you choose can make a difference in the way your face looks, but the length of your hair is not a determining factor.

What Hairstyle is Best for a Round Face?

If you have a round face, you might want to consider getting layers or a side-swept bang to help elongate your face. A long bob can also be a flattering option.

How Can I Make My Hair Look Thicker?

You can make your hair look thicker by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow-drying your hair upside down, and using a hair volumizer. Adding highlights can also help create the illusion of thicker hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair over time.

3. Use a Heat Protectant

If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant to help prevent damage.

4. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and styling your hair in a way that flatters your face shape, you can look and feel your best with long hair.
