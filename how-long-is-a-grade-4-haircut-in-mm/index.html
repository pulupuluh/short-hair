How Long Is A Grade 4 Haircut In Mm A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you wondering how long is a grade 4 haircut in mm? Look no further as we have prepared a comprehensive guide for you! In this article, we will discuss how to achieve a grade 4 haircut, its length in millimeters, and some tips for hair care. Let's dive in!

What is a Grade 4 Haircut?

A grade 4 haircut is a clipper size that cuts the hair to a uniform length of 1/2 inch or 12.7 millimeters. It is a popular choice for men who want a short and neat hairstyle without going completely bald. This haircut is also known as a "number 4" or "4 guard" in some barbershops.

How to Achieve a Grade 4 Haircut?

If you want to achieve a grade 4 haircut, you will need a clipper with a number 4 guard or attachment. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and will prevent the clipper from getting clogged with hair.

Step 2: Attach the Number 4 Guard

Attach the number 4 guard or attachment to your clipper. Make sure it is securely attached so it doesn't come off while you are cutting your hair.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair from the bottom and work your way up. Hold the clipper against your head and move it in an upward motion. Make sure to go over each section of hair several times to ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Trim the Sides and Back

Use a mirror to trim the sides and back of your head. You can use scissors or a smaller clipper guard to achieve a more precise cut.

Step 5: Clean Up

After you are done cutting your hair, use a brush or comb to remove any loose hair. Take a shower or wash your hair to remove any remaining hair.

Tips for Hair Care

Now that you know how to achieve a grade 4 haircut, here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage and dryness.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about a grade 4 haircut:

What other haircuts can I achieve with a clipper and guard?

You can achieve different hair lengths with different clipper guards. For example, a number 1 guard will cut the hair to 1/8 inch or 3 millimeters while a number 8 guard will cut the hair to 1 inch or 25 millimeters.

Can I achieve a grade 4 haircut with scissors?

Yes, you can achieve a grade 4 haircut with scissors but it will be more time-consuming and less precise than using a clipper with a guard.

How often should I get a haircut?

You should get a haircut every 4 to 6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair but it is recommended to have a professional do it for best results.

Conclusion

A grade 4 haircut is a popular choice for men who want a short and neat hairstyle. It is achieved by using a clipper with a number 4 guard or attachment which cuts the hair to a uniform length of 1/2 inch or 12.7 millimeters. Regular hair care and haircuts are necessary to maintain this hairstyle. We hope this guide has answered your questions about how long is a grade 4 haircut in mm and how to achieve it. Happy cutting!
