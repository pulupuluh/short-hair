Where Can I Get An Undercut Near Me

Written by Aprilia Mar 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy haircut that's easy to maintain, an undercut might be just what you need. This style involves shaving the hair on the sides and back of your head, leaving the hair on top longer. But where can you get an undercut near you? In this article, we'll explore your options and give you some tips on finding the right stylist.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get an Undercut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Great Undercut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Undercuts

How to Get an Undercut

The first step in getting an undercut is finding a stylist who is experienced in this type of haircut. You can start by asking friends or family members for recommendations. If you don't know anyone who has an undercut, you can search online for salons in your area that specialize in this style.

Once you've found a stylist you trust, it's important to communicate your vision for the haircut. Bring in pictures or describe the look you're going for. Your stylist can help you decide on the length and shape of the undercut, as well as any additional details like a shaved design.

Before getting started, your stylist will likely wash your hair and prepare it for the cut. They may use clippers or scissors to remove the hair on the sides and back of your head, leaving the hair on top longer. The length of the top hair can vary depending on your preference, but it's typically longer than the sides.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting an undercut:

  1. Research salons in your area that specialize in undercuts
  2. Book an appointment with a stylist who has experience with this style
  3. Communicate your vision for the haircut, including length and shape
  4. Wash your hair before the appointment
  5. Have your stylist remove the hair on the sides and back of your head
  6. Leave the hair on top longer
  7. Ask your stylist for any additional details, like a shaved design
  8. Maintain the haircut by getting regular trims and using styling products

Tips for a Great Undercut

Here are some tips to ensure your undercut looks great:

  • Choose a length and shape that complements your face shape
  • Use styling products like pomade or wax to keep the top hair in place
  • Maintain the haircut by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks
  • Consider adding a shaved design for a unique touch

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about undercuts:

How long does an undercut last?

An undercut can last anywhere from 4-8 weeks depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Can I still tie my hair up with an undercut?

Yes, you can still tie your hair up with an undercut. However, the shaved sides and back may be visible depending on how you style your hair.

Is an undercut suitable for all hair types?

An undercut can work for most hair types, but it may be more difficult to style if you have very thick or curly hair.

Hair Care for Undercuts

To keep your undercut looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Use styling products like pomade or wax to keep the top hair in place
  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of the undercut

Now that you know where to get an undercut near you, it's time to find a stylist and try out this trendy haircut. With the right length and shape, an undercut can be a stylish and low-maintenance option for any hair type. Just be sure to take care of your hair and maintain the cut regularly for the best results.
