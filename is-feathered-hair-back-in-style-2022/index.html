Is Feathered Hair Back In Style 2022

Written by Robby Jun 17, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Feathered hair was a popular hairstyle during the 70s and 80s, and it seems like it's making a comeback in 2022. Many celebrities have been seen sporting feathered hair, and it's been featured in fashion shows and magazines. But the question is, is feathered hair back in style in 2022?

Description

Feathered hair is a hairstyle where the hair is cut into layers, and the ends of the hair are feathered or flipped outwards. This hairstyle was popular in the 70s and 80s and was famously worn by Farrah Fawcett. The hairstyle has made a comeback in recent years, and it's a popular choice among celebrities.

How to Achieve Feathered Hair

If you're interested in achieving feathered hair, here's how you can do it:

  1. Start by washing your hair and blow-drying it.
  2. Section your hair into layers.
  3. Use a round brush to curl your hair outwards.
  4. Finish off with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Alternatively, you can visit a hairstylist to achieve the feathered hair look. They can help you choose the right cut and style for your hair type and face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving feathered hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help give your hair body and lift.

Step 2: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Flip your head upside down to add volume to your roots.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into layers using clips or hair ties. This will help you achieve an even cut.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Use a pair of scissors to cut your hair into layers. Start at the back and work your way forward. Feather the ends of your hair to create the feathered look.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Use a round brush to curl your hair outwards. Finish off with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Feathered Hair

If you want to maintain your feathered hair, here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Use a round brush to curl your hair outwards.
  • Use hairspray to hold the style in place.
  • Visit a hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut.

FAQ

Q: Is feathered hair suitable for all hair types?

A: Feathered hair can work for most hair types, but it's best suited for those with medium to long hair.

Q: Is feathered hair easy to maintain?

A: Feathered hair requires regular maintenance, such as trimming the ends and styling it with a round brush.

Q: Can I achieve feathered hair at home?

A: Yes, you can achieve feathered hair at home with the right tools and techniques. However, it's best to visit a hairstylist if you're unsure about the process.

Hair Care

Feathered hair requires regular hair care to maintain its shape and style. Here are some tips for taking care of feathered hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the ends.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray, as it can weigh down your hair.

Overall, feathered hair is back in style in 2022, and it's a popular choice among celebrities and fashion icons. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the feathered hair look at home or with the help of a hairstylist.
