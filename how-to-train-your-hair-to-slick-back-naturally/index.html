How To Train Your Hair To Slick Back Naturally A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of using hair gel or pomade to slick back your hair every day? Do you want to train your hair to stay in place naturally? If so, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll show you how to train your hair to slick back naturally with a simple step-by-step guide. By the end of this article, you'll be able to achieve a sleek and polished look without any products.

How to Train Your Hair to Slick Back Naturally

Training your hair to slick back naturally requires patience and consistency. It's not something that can be achieved overnight, but with practice, you can train your hair to stay in place without any products. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to training your hair is to start with clean hair. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or excess oil. This will help your hair to stay in place without any resistance.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair Back

While your hair is still wet, use a wide-tooth comb or a boar bristle brush to brush your hair back. Start from the front and work your way to the back. This will help to train your hair to stay in place.

Step 3: Apply Heat

Use a blow dryer to apply heat to your hair while brushing it back. This will help to set the shape and encourage your hair to stay in place. Be sure to use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Use a Hair Tie

If your hair is long enough, use a hair tie to secure it in a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. This will help to train your hair to stay in place and prevent it from falling forward.

Step 5: Repeat Daily

Repeat these steps daily until your hair starts to stay in place naturally. Be patient and consistent, and you'll see results in no time.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're still unsure about how to train your hair to slick back naturally, here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Start with clean hair.
  2. Brush your hair back while it's still wet.
  3. Apply heat with a blow dryer while brushing your hair back.
  4. Use a hair tie to secure your hair in a low ponytail.
  5. Repeat daily until your hair stays in place naturally.

Tips for Training Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you train your hair to slick back naturally:

  • Be patient and consistent.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo to remove product buildup.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a boar bristle brush to brush your hair back.
  • Use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to set the shape.
  • Use a hair tie to secure your hair in a low ponytail to train it to stay in place.

FAQs

How long does it take to train your hair to slick back naturally?

It can take anywhere from a few weeks to a few months to train your hair to slick back naturally. It depends on how often you repeat the process and how well your hair responds to the training.

Can I use hair products while training my hair?

No, it's best to avoid using hair products while training your hair to slick back naturally. Products can make it harder for your hair to stay in place and can undo the progress you've made.

Hair Care Tips

Training your hair to slick back naturally requires proper hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.

By following these tips, you'll not only train your hair to slick back naturally, but you'll also keep it healthy and strong. Happy training!
