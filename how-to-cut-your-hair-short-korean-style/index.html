How To Cut Your Hair Short Korean Style A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Korean hairstyles have been gaining popularity over the years. One of the most sought-after hairstyles is the short Korean style. Not only is it chic and stylish, but it is also easy to maintain. If you're thinking of cutting your hair short Korean style, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of getting the perfect Korean short haircut.

Description

The Korean short style is characterized by its layered cut that adds volume and texture to the hair. It is usually styled with a side-swept fringe, giving it a youthful and trendy look. This style is perfect for those who want to add a touch of sophistication to their appearance.

How to

Before cutting your hair, it is important to have a clear idea of what you want. Look for pictures of Korean short hairstyles that you like and show them to your hairdresser. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will make it easier to cut and style your hair. Use a towel to dry your hair and comb it thoroughly.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to cut. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Start cutting your hair from the back and work your way towards the front. Use sharp scissors to cut small sections of hair at a time. Cut the hair at an angle to create layers. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 4: Trim the Fringe

If you want a side-swept fringe, trim it at an angle. Start from the center and work your way towards the side. Keep trimming until you get the desired length.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you're done cutting your hair, style it the way you want. You can use a hair dryer, hair straightener or curling iron to create different styles. Finish off with some hair spray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind before and after cutting your hair:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape.
  • Always use sharp scissors to cut your hair.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your hair short Korean style:

Q: Will the Korean short style suit me?

A: The Korean short style suits most face shapes. However, it is always best to consult with your hairdresser before making any drastic changes to your hair.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the style.

Q: Can I style my hair differently?

A: Yes, you can style your hair in different ways using a hair dryer, hair straightener or curling iron.

Hair Care

Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut your hair short Korean style, why not give it a try? With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect Korean short haircut.
