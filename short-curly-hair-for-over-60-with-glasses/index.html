Short Curly Hair For Over 60 With Glasses

Written by Bardi Jun 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you over 60 and wear glasses but still want to look stylish with your short curly hair? Look no further as we have compiled a guide for you on how to achieve the perfect short curly hair for over 60 with glasses. With the right cut, style, and hair care, you can rock your short curly hair and glasses confidently.

Description

As we age, our hair may start to thin, become brittle, or lose its natural shine. However, having short curly hair can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and healthier. Adding glasses to the mix can also give you a sophisticated and stylish look.

How to Achieve Short Curly Hair for Over 60 with Glasses

Step 1: Choose the right cut

Choosing the right cut is crucial in achieving the perfect short curly hair for over 60 with glasses. A layered cut can add volume and dimension to your hair, while a pixie cut can give you a chic and edgy look. Consult with your hairstylist on which cut will suit your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Style your hair

There are many ways to style your short curly hair. You can use a diffuser to enhance your natural curls or use a curling iron to create loose waves. Adding some hairspray or mousse can help hold your curls in place and add texture to your hair. Experiment with different styles until you find the one that suits you best.

Step 3: Choose the right glasses

Choosing the right glasses can complement your short curly hair and enhance your overall look. Consider the shape of your face, the color of your hair, and your personal style when choosing glasses. Avoid frames that are too big or too small for your face.

Tips

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, especially when wet.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage your curls.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and depth.

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut my short curly hair?

A: It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I still wear bangs with short curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs with short curly hair. However, it is best to go for side-swept or wispy bangs instead of blunt bangs to complement your curls.

Q: Can I color my short curly hair?

A: Yes, you can color your short curly hair. However, it is best to consult with your hairstylist to choose the right color that will suit your skin tone and hair type.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential in maintaining healthy and beautiful short curly hair. Here are some tips:

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

- Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.

- Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

- Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect short curly hair for over 60 with glasses. Remember to embrace your natural curls and wear your glasses with confidence.
