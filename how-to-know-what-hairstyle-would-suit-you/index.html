How To Know What Hairstyle Would Suit You

Written by Luffy Aug 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially with so many options available. It's important to choose a hairstyle that not only looks great but also suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. In this article, we'll share some tips on how to know what hairstyle would suit you.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Understanding Your Hair Type
  • Considering Your Lifestyle
  • Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQs

Determining Your Face Shape

One of the most important factors in choosing a hairstyle is your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and long. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. Use a measuring tape to measure the widest part of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that will complement your features. For example, if you have a round face, a long, layered cut or a side-swept bang can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a soft, wavy bob or a layered cut with side-swept bangs can soften your features.

Understanding Your Hair Type

Your hair type is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Each hair type has different characteristics and requires different styling techniques.

Straight hair is typically easy to manage and can be styled in a variety of ways. Wavy hair has a slight wave pattern and can be styled with a diffuser to enhance the wave. Curly hair has a more defined curl pattern and requires more moisture and styling products. Coily hair has a tight curl pattern and requires a lot of moisture and styling products to maintain its shape.

Considering Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, a low-maintenance haircut may be best. If you're active and enjoy sports or outdoor activities, a short, easy-to-style haircut may be a better option.

You should also consider your profession when choosing a hairstyle. Some professions may require a more conservative hairstyle, while others may allow for more creativity and experimentation.

Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, it's important to maintain it properly. This includes regular haircuts to keep your style looking fresh, as well as using the right hair care products for your hair type.

It's also important to protect your hair from heat damage caused by styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons. Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using these tools to prevent damage.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your style looking fresh.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a certain style if it doesn't suit my face shape?

A: Of course! Ultimately, the most important thing is that you feel confident and comfortable in your hairstyle.

Q: How do I know which hair care products to use?

A: It's important to choose hair care products that are specifically formulated for your hair type. This can include shampoos, conditioners, and styling products.

In conclusion, choosing a hairstyle that suits you involves considering several factors, including your face shape, hair type, lifestyle, and hair care routine. By taking these factors into account, you can choose a hairstyle that looks great and makes you feel confident.
