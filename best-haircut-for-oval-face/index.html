Description

An oval face is considered to be the ideal face shape. It is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead being slightly wider than the jawline. If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck because almost any haircut can flatter your features. However, there are some hairstyles that can enhance the natural beauty of your face even more.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircut for oval face, let's first determine if you have an oval face shape. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Take a close look at the shape of your face.
  3. Measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.
  4. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  5. Compare the measurements.
  6. If your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead is slightly wider than your jawline, then you have an oval face shape.

Best Haircut for Oval Face

The best haircut for oval face is one that balances the proportions of your face and highlights your best features. Here are some options:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can add volume and movement to your hair, which can make your face look more youthful. They also work well with any hair type, from straight to curly.

2. Bob

A classic bob that hits just below the chin can highlight your jawline and cheekbones. For added volume and texture, you can opt for a layered bob.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold choice, but it can also be incredibly flattering for oval faces. It can highlight your cheekbones and draw attention to your eyes.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can soften your features and add dimension to your hair. They can also draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Get the Best Haircut for Oval Face

Now that you know the best haircut for oval face, here's how to get it:

  1. Find a reputable hairstylist who has experience with oval face shapes.
  2. Show them a picture of the haircut you want.
  3. Ask for their opinion on whether the style will flatter your face shape.
  4. Trust their professional judgment and let them work their magic.
  5. Once you have your new haircut, ask for styling tips on how to maintain it at home.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some general tips for styling your hair, regardless of the haircut:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and comb.
  • Apply hair products evenly throughout your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQs

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair and an oval face shape, you can still rock any of the haircuts mentioned above. Just make sure to find a hairstylist who knows how to cut curly hair.

Can I still wear a middle part?

Yes, you can still wear a middle part. In fact, a middle part can elongate your face and make it look even more oval.

What if I have a round face with an oval forehead?

If you have a round face with an oval forehead, you can still consider yourself to have an oval face shape. You can also follow the tips outlined in this article to find the best haircut for your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face

Here are some additional hair care tips specifically for those with an oval face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head, as this can make your face look longer.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best haircut for oval face, it's time to book your next hair appointment! Remember to communicate with your hairstylist and trust their professional judgment to achieve the best results.
