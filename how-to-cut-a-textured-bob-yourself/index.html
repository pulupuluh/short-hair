How To Cut A Textured Bob Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by William May 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on haircuts? With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a stylish textured bob at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting a textured bob yourself.

How to Cut a Textured Bob Yourself

Before we begin, gather the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Haircutting cape or towel

Once you have all the necessary tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it completely. The hair should be tangle-free and easy to comb.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, part your hair down the middle and divide it into four sections: two at the front and two at the back. Secure each section with a hair clip.

Step 3: Cut the Back Section

Start by unclipping the back section of your hair. Comb it straight down and gather it at the nape of your neck. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut it straight across.

Next, create a guide by taking a small section of hair from the cut section and combing it straight up. Cut the hair at an angle, creating a textured look. Continue this process until you have cut all the hair in the back section.

Step 4: Cut the Front Section

Unclip one of the front sections of your hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle, starting from the cheekbone and ending at the chin.

Repeat this process on the other side, making sure that both sides are even.

Step 5: Texturize Your Hair

To achieve a textured look, take small sections of hair and cut them at different angles. This will create layers and add volume to your hair.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you have finished cutting your hair, check for evenness by comparing both sides. Make any necessary adjustments.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Blow-dry and style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting a Textured Bob Yourself

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take breaks if you feel fatigued.
  • Check for evenness throughout the process.

FAQs

Can I cut a textured bob myself?

Yes, with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a stylish textured bob at home.

What tools do I need to cut a textured bob?

You will need scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a haircutting cape or towel.

How do I achieve a textured look?

To achieve a textured look, take small sections of hair and cut them at different angles. This will create layers and add volume to your hair.

Hair Care After Cutting a Textured Bob Yourself

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly to maintain its texture and style. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your bob.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Cutting a textured bob yourself can be intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, it is possible to achieve a salon-worthy look at home. Follow these steps and tips, and you'll be on your way to a stylish, low-maintenance haircut.
