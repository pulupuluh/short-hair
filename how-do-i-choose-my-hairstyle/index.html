How Do I Choose My Hairstyle Tips Step By Step And Faq

Written by Christine Jun 18, 2023 · 9 min read

Choosing a hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you are unsure of what would suit you. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide which one to go for. However, with a little knowledge and guidance, you can find the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this blog post, we will take you through a step-by-step process on how to choose your hairstyle, along with some tips and frequently asked questions.

How To Choose Your Hairstyle?

Choosing a hairstyle that suits you can be a challenging task, but it does not have to be. Here is how you can choose the perfect hairstyle:

Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which hairstyle would suit you. There are six common face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Here is how you can determine your face shape:

  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded jawline and forehead.
  • Round: Your face is almost as wide as it is long, with a rounded chin and forehead.
  • Square: Your face is almost as wide as it is long, with a square jawline and forehead.
  • Heart: Your face is wider at the forehead and narrow at the jawline.
  • Diamond: Your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrow at the forehead and jawline.
  • Oblong: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a narrow jawline and forehead.

Once you have identified your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Oval: Almost any hairstyle suits an oval face shape, so you can experiment with different styles.
  • Round: A hairstyle that adds height and length to your face would suit you. Avoid styles that add width to your face.
  • Square: Soft hairstyles that add roundness to your face would suit you. Avoid styles that emphasize your square jawline.
  • Heart: A hairstyle that adds volume to your jawline would suit you. Avoid styles that add height to your forehead.
  • Diamond: A hairstyle that adds width to your forehead and chin would suit you. Avoid styles that emphasize your cheekbones.
  • Oblong: A hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face would suit you. Avoid styles that add height to your forehead.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture also plays a significant role in determining which hairstyle would suit you. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Straight Hair: A hairstyle that adds volume and movement would suit you.
  • Wavy Hair: A hairstyle that enhances your natural waves would suit you.
  • Curly Hair: A hairstyle that defines your curls would suit you.
  • Thick Hair: A hairstyle that thins out your hair would suit you. Avoid styles that add volume to your hair.
  • Thin Hair: A hairstyle that adds volume and thickness to your hair would suit you. Avoid styles that flatten your hair.

Step 3: Consider Your Personal Style

Your personal style should also be taken into consideration when choosing a hairstyle. Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and lifestyle. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Casual Style: A hairstyle that is easy to maintain and does not require much styling would suit you.
  • Formal Style: A hairstyle that is polished and elegant would suit you.
  • Edgy Style: A hairstyle that is unconventional and bold would suit you.
  • Natural Style: A hairstyle that enhances your natural features and does not require much styling would suit you.

Tips For Choosing Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing your hairstyle:

  • Research different hairstyles and bring pictures to your hairstylist for reference.
  • Consider the maintenance and upkeep of the hairstyle before choosing it.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and do not be afraid to try something new.
  • Consider the occasion and dress code when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Take into consideration the climate and weather when choosing a hairstyle.

FAQ About Choosing Your Hairstyle

What Should I Do If I Am Unsure Of Which Hairstyle To Choose?

If you are unsure of which hairstyle to choose, consult with your hairstylist. They can suggest a hairstyle that would suit you based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. They can also provide you with styling tips and advice on how to maintain the hairstyle.

Should I Consider My Skin Tone When Choosing A Hairstyle?

Yes, your skin tone should also be taken into consideration when choosing a hairstyle. Certain hair colors and styles complement different skin tones. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which hair color and style would suit your skin tone.

What Should I Do If I Do Not Like The Hairstyle I Chose?

If you do not like the hairstyle you chose, do not panic. Hair grows back, and you can always change your hairstyle. Consult with your hairstylist to determine what changes can be made to the hairstyle to make it more suitable for you.

Hair Care Tips

Once you have chosen your hairstyle, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much hair products, as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Choosing a hairstyle can be a fun and exciting process. By following these tips and guidelines, you can find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features and personal style.
