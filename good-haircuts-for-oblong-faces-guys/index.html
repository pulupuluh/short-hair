Good Haircuts For Oblong Faces Guys

Written by Eveline May 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As a guy with an oblong face shape, it can be challenging to find the perfect haircut that complements your features. However, the good news is that there are a variety of hairstyles that can enhance your appearance and make your face look more proportional. In this article, we will be discussing the best haircuts for oblong face guys, and providing tips on how to maintain and style them.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Before we jump into the haircuts for oblong face guys, let's understand what an oblong face shape is. An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a prominent chin and forehead. This face shape can also have a high forehead and a sharp jawline.

The ideal haircut for guys with oblong face shapes is one that adds width to the face and balances out the proportions. The goal is to avoid hairstyles that make the face look even longer and narrower. Instead, opt for haircuts that add volume and width to the sides of the face.

How To

When choosing a haircut for your oblong face shape, keep in mind the following:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short on the sides
  • Opt for haircuts that add volume and width to the sides of the face
  • Choose a haircut that complements your hair type (e.g. curly, straight, thick, thin)
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair

Step By Step

Here are some of the best haircuts for oblong face guys:

1. The Side Part

The side part is a classic and timeless haircut that works well for guys with oblong face shapes. This haircut adds volume and width to the sides of the face, creating a more balanced look. To style, simply part your hair on one side and comb it over to the other side.

2. The Quiff

The quiff is a popular haircut that adds volume to the hair and creates a more balanced look for guys with oblong face shapes. To style, apply some pomade to your hair and brush it up and back.

3. The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a modern and stylish haircut that adds volume and texture to the hair. This haircut works well for guys with oblong face shapes as it adds width and balances out the proportions. To style, apply some texturizing spray to your hair and mess it up with your fingers.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining and styling your oblong face haircut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you

FAQ

What hairstyles should guys with oblong face shapes avoid?

Guys with oblong face shapes should avoid hairstyles that make their face look even longer and narrower. This includes hairstyles that are too short on the sides, slicked-back hairstyles, and hairstyles with too much volume on top.

Can guys with oblong face shapes have long hair?

Yes, guys with oblong face shapes can have long hair. However, it's important to add layers and texture to the hair to create a more balanced look. Long hair that is flat and straight can make the face look longer and narrower.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. This includes:

  • Washing your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoiding excessive heat styling
  • Trimming your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and healthy
  • Eating a healthy diet and staying hydrated to promote healthy hair growth

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for your oblong face shape, you can enhance your appearance and feel confident in your style.
