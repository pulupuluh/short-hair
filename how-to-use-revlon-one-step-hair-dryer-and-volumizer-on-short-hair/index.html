How To Use Revlon One Step Hair Dryer And Volumizer On Short Hair

Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is a revolutionary hair styling tool that helps to dry, straighten, and volumize hair in just one step. It is a perfect hair styling tool for those who have short hair and want to add volume and shine to their hair. In this article, we will be discussing how to use Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on short hair.

Description

Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is designed to deliver salon-level hair styling at the comfort of your home. It comes with a unique oval brush design that helps to create volume, smoothness, and shine to your hair. The brush consists of nylon pins and tufted bristles that help to detangle and style your hair effectively. The hair dryer and volumizer has three heat/speed settings that allow you to customize the heat and speed according to your hair type.

How to Use Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on Short Hair

Using Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on short hair is easy and straightforward. Here are the steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Pat dry your hair with a towel.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from heat damage.
  3. Plug in the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer and set the heat/speed settings according to your hair type. For short hair, it is recommended to use low to medium heat and speed settings.
  4. Divide your hair into small sections using hair clips.
  5. Take a section of your hair and place the brush near the roots.
  6. Slowly glide the brush downwards towards the ends of your hair. Keep rotating the brush to create volume and bounce.
  7. Repeat the same process for all the sections of your hair.
  8. When you are done, switch off the Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer and let it cool down.
  9. Style your hair as desired using a hair serum or spray.

Tips for Using Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on Short Hair

Here are some tips that you can follow while using Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on short hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Divide your hair into small sections to make it easy to style.
  • Use low to medium heat and speed settings for short hair.
  • Rotate the brush while styling to create volume and bounce.
  • Use a hair serum or spray to style your hair as desired.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about using Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on short hair:

Q. Can I use Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on wet hair?

A. No, it is not recommended to use Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on wet hair. It is recommended to pat dry your hair with a towel before using the hair dryer and volumizer.

Q. How often can I use Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on my hair?

A. It is recommended to use Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer once or twice a week to avoid hair damage.

Q. Can I use Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on colored hair?

A. Yes, you can use Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on colored hair. However, it is recommended to use a heat protectant spray or serum before styling to prevent color fading.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips that you can follow to maintain healthy and shiny hair:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water for hair wash as it can strip away natural oils from your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hair styling tools frequently as it can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals for healthy hair.

Using Revlon One-Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer on short hair is easy and convenient. With the right technique and tips, you can achieve salon-level hair styling at home. Follow the steps and tips mentioned in this article to get voluminous and shiny hair in no time!
