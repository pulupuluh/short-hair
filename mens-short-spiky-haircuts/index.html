Mens Short Spiky Haircuts How To Get The Look In 2023

Written by Frank May 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you want to rock a trendy hairstyle that's both stylish and easy to maintain? Look no further than mens short spiky haircuts! This versatile, edgy style is perfect for any occasion, whether you're going to work or hitting the town. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect spiky look, provide tips for maintaining your hair, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Get the Look

The key to achieving the perfect short spiky haircut is to have the right cut and styling products. Here's how to get the look:

Step 1: Get a Short Cut

The first step to achieving a spiky look is to get a short haircut. You can experiment with different lengths, but generally, a short cut that's about one inch long is ideal. Make sure to communicate with your barber or stylist about the type of cut you want.

Step 2: Use the Right Styling Products

After your haircut, it's time to style your hair. Use a strong-hold hair gel or pomade to create the spikes. Apply the product to your hair while it's still damp, then use your fingers or a comb to create the spikes. You can also use a blow dryer to help set the style.

Step 3: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've created the spikes, finish the look with a light mist of hairspray. This will help keep your hair in place all day long.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Now that you've achieved the perfect spiky look, it's important to maintain your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy

FAQ

What face shapes suit a short spiky haircut?

A short spiky haircut can suit a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and diamond. However, it's best to consult with your barber or stylist to determine the best cut for your face shape.

How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Can I style my hair differently?

Yes! While spiky hair is a popular choice, you can experiment with different styles, such as a messy, textured look or a slicked-back style.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect short spiky haircut and keep your hair looking its best all year round. Whether you're going for a bold, edgy look or a more subtle style, mens short spiky haircuts are a great choice for any man who wants to look his best.
