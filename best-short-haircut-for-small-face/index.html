The Best Short Haircut For Small Face How To Choose And Style

Written by Luffy Mar 07, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have a small face, finding the right haircut can be a bit of a challenge. You want a style that complements your features without overwhelming them. One of the best options for small faces is a short haircut. But with so many styles to choose from, how do you know which one is right for you? In this article, we will discuss the best short haircut for small face, how to choose and style it, and hair care tips to keep your locks looking fabulous.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Small Face

When choosing a short haircut for small face, the goal is to create the illusion of more width and volume in the face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider your face shape: Small faces come in different shapes, so it's important to choose a style that flatters your unique features. If you have a round face, a short bob with layers can help elongate your face. If you have an oval face, a pixie cut can accentuate your cheekbones.
  • Think about your hair texture: Short haircuts can work for all hair types, but it's important to choose a style that works with your natural texture. If you have curly hair, a short bob can help define your curls. If you have straight hair, a pixie cut can give you a sleek, modern look.
  • Consider your lifestyle: Short haircuts require more frequent maintenance, so it's important to choose a style that fits your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, a low-maintenance pixie cut might be a better option than a high-maintenance bob.

Types of Short Haircut for Small Face

Here are some of the best short haircuts for small face:

  • Pixie Cut: This is a short, layered haircut that's perfect for small faces. It accentuates your cheekbones and draws attention to your eyes.
  • Bob: A short bob with layers can help elongate your face and add volume.
  • French Crop: This is a short, textured haircut that's perfect for small faces. It's low-maintenance and looks great on all hair types.
  • Bowl Cut: This is a retro-style haircut that's making a comeback. It's a short, blunt cut that's perfect for small faces.
  • Buzz Cut: If you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut, a buzz cut might be the perfect option. It's a short, all-over cut that's easy to maintain.

How to Style Short Haircut for Small Face

Styling a short haircut for small face is easy and fun. Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Use styling products: To add volume and texture to your hair, use styling products like mousse, pomade, or hairspray.
  • Blow-dry your hair: Use a blow dryer to add volume to your hair. Use a round brush to create a sleek, polished look.
  • Experiment with different styles: Short haircuts are versatile, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. Try adding bangs or playing with the texture of your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircut for Small Face

Maintaining a short haircut for small face requires some effort, but it's worth it. Here are some tips to help you keep your locks looking fabulous:

  • Get regular trims: Short haircuts require more frequent maintenance than longer styles, so make sure to get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and polished.
  • Protect your hair from heat: Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair with heat tools like flat irons or curling irons.
  • Use a deep conditioner: Short haircuts can be prone to dryness, so use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for small faces?

The best haircut for small faces is a short haircut that adds volume and width to the face. Some of the best options include a pixie cut, bob, or French crop.

Can I pull off a pixie cut with a small face?

Yes, a pixie cut is a great option for small faces. It accentuates your cheekbones and draws attention to your eyes.

How often should I get a trim with a short haircut?

Short haircuts require more frequent maintenance than longer styles, so it's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircut for Small Face

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short haircut for small face looking fabulous:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Short haircuts can become greasy faster than longer styles, so make sure to wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Avoid using too much heat: Excessive heat can damage your hair, so avoid using heat tools too often.
  • Use a silk pillowcase: Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and breakage.

Choosing the best short haircut for small face can be a bit of a challenge, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a style that flatters your unique features. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your new look!
