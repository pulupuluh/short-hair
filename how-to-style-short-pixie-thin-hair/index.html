How To Style Short Pixie Thin Hair Tips And Tricks

Short pixie thin hair can be challenging to style, but with the right tips and tricks, you can create a stylish and chic look that suits your personality. This article will guide you on how to style short pixie thin hair, from choosing the right haircut to using the right styling products.

How to Style Short Pixie Thin Hair

If you have short pixie thin hair, the first thing to do is to choose the right haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture. A layered pixie cut can add volume and texture to thin hair, while a side-swept fringe can frame your face and add dimension to your hairstyle.

Once you have the right haircut, you can style your short pixie thin hair in various ways, such as:

  • Adding volume with a volumizing mousse or spray
  • Creating texture with a texturizing spray or wax
  • Adding shine with a hair serum or oil
  • Creating waves with a curling iron or flat iron
  • Adding a pop of color with hair chalk or temporary hair dye

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style short pixie thin hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and fullness to your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently to remove excess water, then apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and mid-lengths.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting your hair at the roots to add volume and texture.
  4. Once your hair is dry, apply a texturizing spray or wax to your fingertips and run them through your hair to create definition and texture.
  5. If you want to add waves or curls, use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves or curls, then tousle them with your fingers for a more natural look.
  6. Finish off by adding a hair serum or oil to add shine and tame any flyaways.

Tips for Styling Short Pixie Thin Hair

Here are some tips to help you style short pixie thin hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or paddle brush to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a messy pixie or a sleek and polished look.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes and add volume.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short pixie thin hair:

  • Q: Can I add extensions to my short pixie thin hair?
  • A: Yes, you can add clip-in extensions or a hairpiece to add length and volume to your hair.
  • Q: How often should I wash my short pixie thin hair?
  • A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it daily, while if you have dry hair, you can wash it every other day.
  • Q: How can I prevent my short pixie thin hair from looking flat?
  • A: Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and add a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and mid-lengths. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots.

Hair Care Tips for Short Pixie Thin Hair

To keep your short pixie thin hair healthy and strong, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for thin hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more brittle.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in protein and vitamins, as this can promote hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
