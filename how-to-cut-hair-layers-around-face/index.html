How To Cut Hair Layers Around Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jun 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you thinking about cutting your own hair layers around the face but don't know where to start? Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with a little bit of knowledge and some practice, you can achieve a salon-worthy look. In this article, we'll show you how to cut hair layers around the face with ease.

How to Cut Hair Layers Around Face

Cutting hair layers around the face is a great way to add volume, texture, and movement to your hair. Here are some steps to help you achieve the perfect haircut:

Step 1: Gather Your Supplies

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp hair cutting shears, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into four sections. Use hair clips to secure each section, leaving the front section of your hair loose.

Step 3: Determine the Length

Decide on the length of your layers. Hold a small section of hair in front of your face and determine the length you want your layers to be.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Using your hair cutting shears, start cutting the hair in front of your face. Make sure to cut the hair at an angle, starting from the shortest length in front and gradually getting longer towards the back.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Once you have finished cutting the front section of your hair, it's time to blend the layers. Take small sections of hair from the back and blend them with the front layers.

Step 6: Repeat

Repeat these steps for each section of your hair until you have achieved the desired look.

Tips for Cutting Hair Layers Around Face

Cutting your own hair can be a little intimidating, but with these tips, you'll be able to achieve a salon-worthy look:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to longer cuts.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go.
  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting hair can be tiring on your arms.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about cutting hair layers around the face:

How often should I cut my hair layers?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your layers and keep your hair healthy.

Can I cut my own hair if I have curly hair?

Cutting curly hair can be a little more challenging than cutting straight hair, but it's definitely doable. Just make sure to cut your hair when it's dry so you can see the natural curl pattern.

Is it better to cut my hair when it's wet or dry?

It's best to cut your hair when it's dry because wet hair can stretch and appear longer than it actually is.

Hair Care after Cutting Hair Layers Around Face

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain a healthy and vibrant look. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to protect your hair color and prevent damage.
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools like a hair dryer or straightener.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

By following these tips, you'll be able to achieve a beautiful haircut and keep your hair healthy and vibrant.

Cutting your own hair can be a little intimidating, but with the right tools and knowledge, you can achieve a salon-worthy look. Follow these steps, tips, and tricks to cut hair layers around the face with ease.
