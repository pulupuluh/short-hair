How To Cut Your Own Curtain Bangs At Home

Written by Christine Apr 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle without breaking the bank or making a trip to the salon, cutting your own curtain bangs at home is a great option. Curtain bangs are a trendy and effortless style that can add a touch of chic to any look. With the right tools and some patience, you can achieve the perfect curtain bangs in the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll go over the steps on how to cut your own curtain bangs, as well as tips and hair care advice to keep them looking fresh.

How to Cut Your Own Curtain Bangs at Home

Before starting, make sure you have the necessary tools: scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It's also important to have a clear idea of how you want your curtain bangs to look. Do you want them to be short or long? How much hair do you want to cut?

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning off the hair you want to cut. Divide your hair in the middle and clip the rest of your hair away from your face. Take the section of hair you want to cut and divide it into two equal parts.

Step 2: Cut the Middle Section

Take the middle section of your hair and cut it straight across. Start with small snips and work your way up to the desired length. Remember, you can always cut more hair off, but you can't put it back on.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Next, take one side of your hair and hold it at a 45-degree angle. Cut diagonally from the middle section to the outer edge of your hair. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Check for Symmetry

Check to make sure your curtain bangs are symmetrical. If one side is longer than the other, make small snips to even them out.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Style your new curtain bangs by blow-drying them with a round brush, or use a straightener for a sleek look. You can also add some texture with a curling iron.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Curtain Bangs

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting your own curtain bangs:

  • Start with dry hair so you can see how your bangs will fall
  • Use sharp scissors to get a clean cut
  • Take your time and make small snips
  • Don't cut too much hair at once
  • Take breaks if your arms get tired

FAQs

Can I cut my own curtain bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still cut your own curtain bangs if you have curly hair. Just make sure to cut them while your hair is dry and styled in its natural state.

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

It's recommended to trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Once you've cut your own curtain bangs, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup
  • Use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools
  • Avoid using too much product on your bangs, as it can weigh them down
  • Trim your bangs regularly to prevent split ends

Now that you know how to cut your own curtain bangs at home, it's time to give it a try. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at cutting your own bangs in no time.
