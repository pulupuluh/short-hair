Does Wearing Your Hair Up Make You Look Older

Written by William Jul 18, 2023 · 6 min read

Table of Contents:

  • How To Wear Your Hair Up Without Looking Older
  • Step-By-Step Guide
  • Tips To Keep Your Hair Looking Young

Introduction

One of the most common beauty myths is that wearing your hair up can make you look older. While this may be true in some cases, it's not a hard and fast rule. In fact, wearing your hair up can be a great way to look stylish and put together, regardless of your age. So, does wearing your hair up make you look older? Let's dive into this topic and find out.

How To Wear Your Hair Up Without Looking Older

The key to wearing your hair up without looking older is to choose the right style. While some updos can be ageing, others can be very flattering. Here are a few tips:

1. Avoid Tight Styles

Tight hairstyles like sleek buns and ponytails can pull your skin tight and accentuate wrinkles. Instead, opt for looser styles that allow your skin to breathe.

2. Add Volume

Adding volume to your hair can help give you a more youthful look. Try adding some texture to your hair using a texturizing spray or by teasing the crown of your head.

3. Choose Wisely

Choose an updo that flatters your face shape and features. A messy bun or a loose braid can be very attractive, while a severe updo can be unflattering.

Step-By-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a youthful updo:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Apply a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and texture.

Step 2: Create a Loose Bun

Gather your hair into a loose bun at the back of your head. Secure it with bobby pins or a hair tie.

Step 3: Pull Out Strands

Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to soften the look.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips To Keep Your Hair Looking Young

Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking youthful:

1. Avoid Overstyling

Overstyling your hair can cause damage and breakage, making it look dull and lifeless. Keep your styling to a minimum and opt for natural looks.

2. Use Heat Protectant

If you do use styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs About Wearing Your Hair Up

Q: Does wearing your hair up cause hair loss?

A: No, wearing your hair up does not cause hair loss. However, pulling your hair too tight can cause traction alopecia, which is a type of hair loss caused by pulling on the hair.

Q: Can wearing your hair up cause headaches?

A: Wearing your hair up too tight can cause tension headaches. To avoid this, choose looser hairstyles and avoid pulling your hair too tight.

Hair Care Tips For Healthy Hair

Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Condition Your Hair

Condition your hair after shampooing to keep it soft and manageable.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals can help keep your hair healthy and strong.

In conclusion, wearing your hair up does not necessarily make you look older. By choosing the right style and following a few simple tips, you can wear your hair up and look stylish and youthful.
