How To Make My Face Look Round Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter May 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a long or angular face, you may be looking for ways to make it appear more round. With a few simple makeup techniques and hair styling tricks, you can achieve a more rounded face shape. In this article, we will discuss how to make your face look round in easy, relaxed English.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Make My Face Look Round
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Round Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Round Face Shape

How to Make My Face Look Round:

There are a few makeup tricks that can help you achieve a more rounded face shape. Here are some tips:

1. Contouring:

Contouring is the process of creating shadows on your face to make it appear more defined. To make your face look rounder, you can use a bronzer or contour powder to create shadows on the sides of your face. Apply the powder along your hairline, temples, and under your cheekbones, blending it in well.

2. Blush:

Blush can add a pop of color to your cheeks and help to round out your face. Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks, blending it upwards towards your temples. Choose a warm, rosy color that complements your complexion.

3. Highlighting:

Highlighting is the process of adding light to your face to make it appear more defined. To make your face look rounder, apply a highlighter to the center of your forehead, down the bridge of your nose, and on your chin.

4. Eye Makeup:

Play up your eyes to draw attention away from angular features. Use eyeliner to create a cat-eye or winged look, and add mascara to make your eyelashes look longer and fuller.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Now that you know some makeup tricks, here is a step-by-step guide to making your face look round:

Step 1: Prep Your Skin

Start by washing your face and applying a moisturizer. Use a primer to help your makeup stay in place.

Step 2: Contour

Use a bronzer or contour powder to create shadows on the sides of your face. Apply the powder along your hairline, temples, and under your cheekbones, blending it in well.

Step 3: Blush

Add a pop of color to your cheeks with blush. Apply it to the apples of your cheeks, blending upwards towards your temples.

Step 4: Highlight

Use a highlighter to add light to your face. Apply it to the center of your forehead, down the bridge of your nose, and on your chin.

Step 5: Eye Makeup

Play up your eyes with eyeliner and mascara.

Tips for Round Face Shape:

Here are some additional tips for making your face look round:

1. Bangs:

Consider getting bangs to help frame your face and make it appear more rounded. Side-swept bangs can also help to soften angular features.

2. Hair Length:

Long hair can elongate your face, so consider a shorter hairstyle if you want to make your face look rounder. A bob or shoulder-length cut can help to frame your face and add volume.

3. Accessories:

Wear accessories that draw attention to your face, such as earrings or a statement necklace. Avoid wearing hats or headbands that can elongate your face.

FAQs:

Q: Can I make my face look round without makeup?

A: Yes, you can make your face look rounder by choosing the right hairstyle and accessories. Bangs, shorter haircuts, and statement accessories can all help to soften angular features.

Q: Can I use a cream contour instead of powder?

A: Yes, a cream contour can work just as well as a powder contour. Just be sure to blend it in well for a natural-looking finish.

Hair Care for Round Face Shape:

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair if you have a round face shape:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo:

Choose a shampoo that is gentle and sulfate-free to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Condition Regularly:

Use a conditioner after shampooing to help keep your hair smooth and hydrated.

3. Protect Your Hair:

Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, which can damage your hair. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

4. Get Regular Trims:

Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your face look rounder and achieve a more balanced, symmetrical appearance. Remember to choose makeup and hairstyles that make you feel confident and comfortable in your own skin.
