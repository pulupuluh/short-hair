Best Hairstyles For Long Thick Straight Hair

Written by William Jun 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you blessed with long, thick, and straight hair? Lucky you! These gorgeous locks of yours can be styled in countless ways, from classic braids to chic updos. However, with so many options available, choosing the perfect hairstyle can be overwhelming. In this blog post, we will explore the best hairstyles for long thick straight hair, including how to achieve them, tips, and hair care. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How To

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for long thick straight hair, let's talk about the basics. To create any of these hairstyles, you'll need the right tools and products. Here's what you'll need:

  • A high-quality hairbrush
  • Heat protectant spray
  • A curling iron or straightener
  • Bobby pins and hair ties
  • Hairspray

Now that you have your tools and products, let's explore the best hairstyles for long thick straight hair.

1. Sleek and Straight

This classic hairstyle is perfect for any occasion. To achieve this look, start by applying heat protectant spray to your hair. Then, use a straightener to straighten your hair in small sections. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your locks in place.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

This hairstyle is both cute and practical. To achieve this look, start by parting your hair in the middle. Then, take the top half of your hair and tie it up with a hair tie. You can leave your hair straight or add some curls or waves to the bottom half for extra texture.

3. Braided Crown

This elegant hairstyle is perfect for weddings or formal events. To achieve this look, start by parting your hair down the middle. Then, braid each side of your hair and wrap the braids around your head like a crown. Secure the braids with bobby pins and finish with hairspray.

4. Low Ponytail

This chic hairstyle is perfect for a day at the office or a night out with friends. To achieve this look, start by brushing your hair back into a low ponytail. You can add some volume to the crown of your head by teasing your hair before tying it back. Finish with a light mist of hairspray.

Step By Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the braided crown hairstyle:

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Braid each side of your hair.
  3. Wrap the braids around your head like a crown.
  4. Secure the braids with bobby pins.
  5. Finish with hairspray.

Tips

  • Use a high-quality hairbrush to prevent breakage.
  • Apply heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Try adding some texture to your hair with waves or curls.

FAQ

What products should I use to style my long thick straight hair?

You should use a high-quality hairbrush, heat protectant spray, a curling iron or straightener, bobby pins and hair ties, and hairspray.

What are the best hairstyles for long thick straight hair?

The best hairstyles for long thick straight hair include sleek and straight, half-up half-down, braided crown, and low ponytail.

Hair Care

Long, thick, and straight hair requires proper care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can rock your long thick straight hair with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair properly to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Happy styling!
