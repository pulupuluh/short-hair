Bob Haircut For Round Face 2020 Tips How To And Hair Care

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will flatter your round face? Look no further than the bob haircut! This classic and versatile hairstyle has been around for decades, and it's perfect for those with round faces. In this article, we'll give you tips on how to choose the best bob haircut for your round face, a step-by-step guide on how to get the perfect bob, and hair care tips to maintain your new hairstyle.

How to Choose the Best Bob Haircut for Round Face 2020

The key to choosing the best bob haircut for your round face is to find a style that will elongate your face and create the illusion of height. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Length

Choose a bob that is either above or below your jawline. Avoid bobs that end right at your jawline, as this will only emphasize the roundness of your face.

2. Layers

Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, but be careful not to add too much volume around your cheeks. Opt for longer layers that start below your chin instead.

3. Bangs

Consider getting bangs to balance out the roundness of your face. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are great options, as they create a diagonal line that elongates your face.

4. Texture

Adding texture to your hair can help create the illusion of height. Consider getting a textured bob with choppy layers or beachy waves.

How to Get the Perfect Bob Haircut for Round Face 2020

Now that you know what to look for in a bob haircut for round face, here's how to get the perfect cut:

1. Choose a Professional Stylist

Make sure you choose a stylist who is experienced with cutting hair for round faces. They will know the best techniques to create a flattering bob for your face shape.

2. Bring Inspiration Photos

Bring in photos of bobs that you like to show your stylist. This will help them understand the exact style you're looking for.

3. Consider Your Hair Type

Keep in mind your hair type when choosing the length and style of your bob. For example, if you have thick hair, you may want to opt for longer layers to avoid adding too much volume around your cheeks.

4. Trust Your Stylist

Finally, trust your stylist's opinion. They are the experts and will know what will look best on you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bob Haircut for Round Face 2020

Now that you have your perfect bob haircut, here are some tips to keep it looking fresh:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture. For example, if you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.

2. Trim Regularly

To maintain the shape of your bob, get regular trims every six to eight weeks.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like a blow dryer or flat iron.

4. Experiment with Styling

Try different styling techniques to keep your bob looking fresh. For example, try curling your hair with a curling iron or flat iron to create different looks.

FAQs About Bob Haircut for Round Face 2020

1. Will a bob haircut make my face look rounder?

No, if you choose the right bob haircut for your round face, it can actually make your face look slimmer and more elongated.

2. Can I still get a bob haircut if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Just make sure you choose a stylist who is experienced with cutting curly hair to avoid any mishaps.

3. Can I style my bob haircut in different ways?

Yes, a bob haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Try experimenting with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Bob Haircut for Round Face 2020

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your bob looking its best:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every two to three days to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and hydrated.

3. Avoid Over-Styling

Avoid over-styling your hair with hot tools, as this can lead to damage and breakage.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a bob haircut for round face 2020 like a pro! Remember to choose a style that flatters your face shape, and maintain it with regular trims and proper hair care.
