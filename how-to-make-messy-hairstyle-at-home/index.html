How To Make Messy Hairstyle At Home

Written by Bardi Jun 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle to try out, why not go for a messy look? Messy hairstyles are a popular choice because they look effortless and can be done at home with minimal effort. In this article, we'll show you how to make a messy hairstyle at home, step by step. We'll also provide some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect messy look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Messy Hairstyle at Home
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step in creating a messy hairstyle is to start with clean hair. Wash and condition your hair as you normally would, then let it air dry or blow dry it. If you have naturally curly hair, you can leave it as is or use a curling iron to create loose waves.

Step 2: Apply Product

Next, apply a small amount of product to your hair. Use a texturizing spray, pomade, or wax to give your hair some grip and hold. Work the product through your hair with your fingers, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

Step 3: Create Texture

To create a messy look, you need to add texture to your hair. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair, twist sections of it, and tease it with a comb or brush. You can also use a flat iron to create waves or curls in your hair.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you've created the desired texture, finish off your messy hairstyle with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold your hair in place and prevent it from falling flat throughout the day.

Tips and Tricks

Tip 1: Don't Overdo It

When creating a messy hairstyle, it's important not to overdo it. You want your hair to look effortless, not like you spent hours trying to make it look messy. Use a light hand when applying products and creating texture, and don't be afraid to leave some sections of your hair untouched.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

To create a messy hairstyle, you need to use the right products. Look for texturizing sprays, pomades, and waxes that are designed to give your hair some grip and hold. Avoid using heavy products that will weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Tip 3: Experiment with Different Styles

There are many different ways to create a messy hairstyle, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. Try adding braids or twists to your hair, or create a messy bun or ponytail. The possibilities are endless!

FAQ

Q: Can I create a messy hairstyle if I have short hair?

A: Yes! Messy hairstyles can be created on any length of hair. If you have short hair, try using a texturizing spray or pomade to create a tousled look.

Q: How do I prevent my hair from looking greasy?

A: Use a small amount of product when creating your messy hairstyle, and avoid using heavy products that will weigh your hair down. Also, be sure to wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup.

Hair Care

Tip 1: Use a Clarifying Shampoo

Regularly using a clarifying shampoo can help remove buildup from your hair and keep it looking healthy. Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to keep your hair looking its best.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from Heat

When using heat tools like curling irons or flat irons, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. Also, avoid using heat tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Tip 3: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

Now that you know how to make a messy hairstyle at home, it's time to give it a try! Remember to use the right products, experiment with different styles, and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
