Best Haircut For Thinning Hair At The Crown

Written by Eveline Apr 30, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're experiencing thinning hair at the crown of your head, you're not alone. It's a common problem that affects both men and women, and it can be frustrating to deal with. Fortunately, there are hairstyles that can help camouflage thinning hair and make it less noticeable. In this article, we'll discuss the best haircut for thinning hair at the crown and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

How to Determine the Best Haircut for Thinning Hair at the Crown

Before you head to the salon, it's important to determine the best haircut for your thinning hair. Here are some tips:

  • Short haircuts tend to work best for thinning hair at the crown. This is because longer hair can weigh down the hair and make it look even thinner.
  • A layered haircut can also help give the illusion of thicker hair. Layers add volume and movement, which can make your hair look fuller.
  • Avoid blunt cuts, as they can accentuate the thinning areas. Instead, opt for a haircut with layers or texture.
  • Consider a side part, as it can help camouflage thinning hair at the crown. A middle part can make the thinning more obvious.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Best Haircut for Thinning Hair at the Crown

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut, here's a step-by-step guide to getting the best haircut for thinning hair at the crown:

  1. Find a stylist who has experience working with thinning hair. They can provide recommendations based on your specific hair type and texture.
  2. Show your stylist pictures of hairstyles that you like. They can help determine which styles will work best for your hair type and face shape.
  3. Ask your stylist to add layers or texture to your haircut to help give the illusion of thicker hair.
  4. Consider a side part, and ask your stylist to create volume at the crown of your head to help camouflage thinning hair.
  5. Ask your stylist for styling tips to help maintain your haircut and make your hair look fuller.

Tips for Styling Thinning Hair at the Crown

Once you have your new haircut, it's important to know how to style it to make your hair look fuller. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid heavy products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look even thinner. Instead, opt for lightweight products like mousse or volumizing spray.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume at the crown.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles like ponytails or braids, as they can accentuate thinning areas.
  • Consider using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help give your hair more body.

FAQs About the Best Haircut for Thinning Hair at the Crown

Q: Will cutting my hair short make it look thinner?

A: No, cutting your hair short can actually help make it look thicker. This is because longer hair can weigh down your hair and make it look even thinner.

Q: What if I don't want to cut my hair short?

A: You don't have to cut your hair short, but you may want to consider adding layers or texture to help give the illusion of thicker hair.

Q: Will styling my hair with heat tools damage it?

A: Yes, using heat tools like flat irons or curling irons can damage your hair if you don't use them properly. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using heat tools on wet hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thinning Hair at the Crown

In addition to getting the right haircut and styling your hair properly, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent further thinning. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid harsh chemicals like bleach or relaxers, as they can damage your hair and make it more prone to thinning.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of protein and vitamins to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull at your hair and cause breakage.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free of sulfates and parabens.

In conclusion, the best haircut for thinning hair at the crown is a short, layered haircut with volume at the crown. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can help make your thinning hair less noticeable and promote healthy hair growth.
