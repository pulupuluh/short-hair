What Hair Color Makes Your Forehead Look Smaller

Written by William Feb 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Do you have a larger forehead that you would like to minimize? Hair color can be a great way to achieve this look without having to resort to more invasive measures. In this article, we will discuss what hair color makes your forehead look smaller and provide tips on how to achieve this look.

Description

A larger forehead can be a source of insecurity for many people. However, by choosing the right hair color, you can create the illusion of a smaller forehead. The key is to choose a hair color that draws attention away from your forehead and instead highlights your facial features.

How to

Here are some tips on how to achieve a hair color that makes your forehead look smaller:

1. Highlights and lowlights

Adding highlights and lowlights to your hair can create depth and dimension, drawing attention away from your forehead. This technique works especially well for those with longer hair.

2. Ombre

The ombre technique involves gradually fading your hair color from dark to light. This technique can make your forehead appear smaller by drawing attention to the ends of your hair rather than the roots.

3. Balayage

Similar to ombre, balayage involves painting the hair to create a gradual, natural-looking fade. This technique can be used to create a more subtle effect than ombre, with the color starting higher up on the hair shaft.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a hair color that makes your forehead look smaller:

1. Choose the right hair color

Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and highlights your facial features. Consider using highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.

2. Consult with a stylist

If you are unsure which hair color will work best for you, consult with a professional stylist. They can provide advice and guidance on which colors will work best for your hair type and face shape.

3. Apply hair color

Apply the hair color according to the instructions on the packaging. Be sure to follow the instructions carefully to avoid damaging your hair.

4. Maintain your hair color

Once you have achieved your desired hair color, it is important to maintain it. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking vibrant and healthy.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color that makes your forehead look smaller:

1. Consider your skin tone

Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, consider warmer hair colors such as golden blonde or copper. If you have a cooler skin tone, consider cooler hair colors such as ash blonde or platinum.

2. Use hair accessories

Consider using hair accessories such as headbands or scarves to draw attention away from your forehead.

FAQ

What hair color makes your forehead look smaller?

Highlights and lowlights, ombre, and balayage are all hair color techniques that can make your forehead appear smaller. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and highlights your facial features.

Can bangs make your forehead look smaller?

Yes, bangs can make your forehead appear smaller. However, it is important to choose the right length and style of bangs for your face shape. Consult with a stylist to determine which type of bangs will work best for you.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair after coloring it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair color from fading. Additionally, consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

In conclusion, choosing the right hair color can make a big difference in how your forehead looks. By using techniques such as highlights and lowlights, ombre, or balayage, you can create the illusion of a smaller forehead. Consult with a stylist to determine which hair color technique will work best for you and remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
