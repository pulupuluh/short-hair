Which Hair Cut Looks Good On Round Face

Written by Aprilia Apr 01, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't seem to flatter your round face? Don't worry; we've got you covered. In this blog post, we'll be discussing the best haircuts that look good on round faces. So, if you're ready to revamp your look, let's get started.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by soft, curved lines with the width and length being almost the same. The key is to choose a haircut that will elongate the face and create an illusion of a slimmer face. The ideal haircuts are those that add volume and height to the crown while avoiding styles that create width around the cheeks and ears.

How to

When it comes to choosing a haircut, there are a few things to consider:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair texture
  • Your lifestyle

It's important to consult with your hairstylist to choose the best haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture. They will also recommend the best hair care products to maintain your new haircut.

Step by step

Here are some of the best haircuts that look good on round faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers help to create the illusion of length, which is perfect for a round face. The layers add volume and movement to the hair, making it look more dynamic. This style works well for those with straight or wavy hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are great for a round face as they help to create the illusion of a longer face. The bangs should start from the side of the forehead and gradually get longer towards the end, blending into the rest of the hair.

3. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a great option for those with round faces. The length should be just above the shoulders, and the ends should be slightly angled. This style helps to elongate the face and create a more defined jawline.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin as they tend to make the face appear wider
  • Avoid heavy, thick bangs that cover the forehead as they tend to make the face appear shorter
  • Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture
  • Use hair care products that are suitable for your hair type to maintain your new haircut

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for round faces:

1. What haircut makes a round face look slimmer?

Long layers, side-swept bangs, and a bob haircut are great options for making a round face look slimmer.

2. Can I wear my hair short if I have a round face?

A short haircut can work for a round face, but it's important to choose the right style. A pixie cut or a short bob with layers and side-swept bangs can be flattering for a round face.

Hair Care

After getting a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and style. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to trim your hair and maintain its shape

Now that you know which haircut looks good on round faces, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and transform your look. Remember to choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture, and don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style.
