Short Hairstyles For Double Chins Photos A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 26, 2023 · 10 min read

If you have a double chin, it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that flatters your face. Fortunately, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can help minimize the appearance of a double chin. In this article, we’ll take a look at some of the best short hairstyles for double chins photos, how to style them, and how to care for your hair.

The Best Short Hairstyles for Double Chins Photos

When it comes to finding the perfect short hairstyle for a double chin, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want a style that draws attention away from your chin and towards your eyes and cheekbones. You also want a style that adds volume to the top of your head, as this can help elongate your face.

Bob with Bangs

The bob with bangs is a classic short hairstyle that works well for those with a double chin. The bangs draw attention to your eyes, while the bob adds volume to the top of your head. To style this look, simply blow-dry your hair straight and use a round brush to curl the ends under.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is another great short hairstyle for those with a double chin. This style is all about volume, so be sure to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner when washing your hair. To style, blow-dry your hair with a round brush and use a bit of pomade to create texture and definition.

Layered Bob

The layered bob is a versatile short hairstyle that can help minimize the appearance of a double chin. The layers add volume and movement to your hair, which can help elongate your face. To style, blow-dry your hair with a round brush and use a bit of hairspray to hold the layers in place.

How to Style Short Hairstyles for Double Chins Photos

Styling short hairstyles for double chins photos is easy once you know the basics. Here are a few tips to help you get started:

Blow-Drying

When blow-drying your hair, be sure to use a round brush to create volume at the roots. This will help elongate your face and draw attention away from your chin.

Product

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. You can also use a bit of pomade or hairspray to create texture and definition.

Cut

When getting your hair cut, be sure to ask your stylist for layers and volume at the top of your head. This will help elongate your face and draw attention away from your chin.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hairstyles for Double Chins Photos

To style your short hairstyle for a double chin, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add volume to your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume at the roots. Be sure to blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to fall.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a bit of pomade or hairspray to create texture and definition. Use your fingers to create volume and movement at the top of your head.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a bit of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Double Chins Photos

Here are a few tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Be sure to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Use a Heat Protectant

When using heat styling tools, such as a blow dryer or flat iron, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

When spending time in the sun, be sure to protect your hair from the harmful UV rays by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQs about Short Hairstyles for Double Chins Photos

Q: Will short hairstyles make my double chin more noticeable?

A: No, short hairstyles can actually help minimize the appearance of a double chin by drawing attention away from your chin and towards your eyes and cheekbones.

Q: What is the best short hairstyle for a double chin?

A: The best short hairstyle for a double chin is one that adds volume to the top of your head and draws attention away from your chin, such as a bob with bangs, a pixie cut, or a layered bob.

Q: Do I need to use special products to style my short hairstyle?

A: No, you don’t need to use special products to style your short hairstyle. However, using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner and a bit of pomade or hairspray can help create texture and definition.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Double Chins Photos

When it comes to caring for your hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. Here are some tips:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Be sure to wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as a blow dryer or flat iron, as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.

Protect Your Hair from the Elements

Protect your hair from the elements, such as wind and sun, by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect short hairstyle for your double chin and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
