How To Cover Bald Spots With Box Braids

Written by Daniel Jun 05, 2023 · 6 min read
How to Cover Bald Spots with Box Braids

Box braids are a great way to protect your hair and give it a new look. They are also a great solution for hiding bald spots. In this article, we will show you how to cover bald spots with box braids.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cover Bald Spots with Box Braids
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Covering Bald Spots with Box Braids
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Box Braids

How to Cover Bald Spots with Box Braids

The first step to covering bald spots with box braids is to choose the right size of braids. If you have small bald spots, you can choose smaller braids. If you have larger bald spots, you can choose larger braids. You can also choose the length of the braids based on your preference and hair type.

Choose the Right Hair Extensions

When choosing hair extensions for your box braids, it is important to choose high-quality hair that matches your hair texture and color. You can ask for recommendations from your stylist or do some research online to find the best hair extensions for your hair type.

Prepare Your Hair

Before getting box braids, make sure your hair is clean and moisturized. This will help protect your hair from damage and make it easier to braid. You can also use a scalp treatment to help promote hair growth and prevent further balding.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cover bald spots with box braids:

  1. Divide your hair into sections.
  2. Take a small section of hair and braid it tightly.
  3. Attach the hair extension to the braid and continue braiding.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until all of your hair is braided.
  5. Style your braids as desired.

Tips for Covering Bald Spots with Box Braids

  • Choose the right size of braids for your bald spots.
  • Choose high-quality hair extensions that match your hair texture and color.
  • Make sure your hair is clean and moisturized before getting box braids.
  • Use a scalp treatment to promote hair growth and prevent further balding.
  • Avoid pulling your braids too tight, as this can cause further damage to your hair.
  • Protect your braids at night by wearing a silk or satin bonnet or scarf.

FAQs

How long can I keep box braids in?

You can keep box braids in for up to 8 weeks, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of your braids.

Will box braids damage my hair?

Box braids can cause damage if they are done too tightly or if you leave them in for too long. It is important to follow proper hair care techniques and not leave your braids in for too long.

Hair Care Tips for Box Braids

  • Wash your braids every 2-3 weeks with a gentle shampoo.
  • Moisturize your scalp and braids with a light oil or leave-in conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy products, as this can weigh down your braids and cause them to unravel.
  • Avoid exposing your braids to heat, such as from a blow dryer or flat iron.
  • Protect your braids at night by wearing a silk or satin bonnet or scarf.

By following these tips, you can cover bald spots with box braids and keep your hair looking great. Remember to take care of your hair and scalp to promote healthy hair growth and prevent further balding.
