How Many Mm Is A No 3 Haircut A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 29, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle but don't know where to start? Have you heard about a "No 3" haircut but don't know how long it is? Look no further! In this article, we will explain what a No 3 haircut is and how many mm it is.

Description

A No 3 haircut is a popular choice for men who want a short, but not buzzed, hairstyle. It is also known as a "crew cut" and is typically cut with clippers using a guard size of 3. The No 3 haircut leaves about 3/8 of an inch or 9.5 mm of hair on the scalp. This length is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance haircut that is easy to style and maintain.

How to

If you are getting a No 3 haircut at a barbershop, simply tell your barber that you want a crew cut with a No 3 guard. They will then use clippers to cut your hair to the desired length. If you are cutting your hair at home, make sure you have a set of clippers with a No 3 guard. Start at the sides and back of your head, and work your way up to the top of your head, making sure to keep the clippers level and even.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Attach a No 3 guard to your clippers.

3. Begin at the sides and back of your head, using the clippers to cut the hair to the desired length.

4. Move to the top of your head, making sure to keep the clippers level and even.

5. Blend the sides and top of your hair together for a seamless look.

6. Use scissors or clippers without a guard to trim any stray hairs.

7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

- If you want a shorter haircut, try a No 2 or No 1 guard.

- If you want a longer haircut, try a No 4 or No 5 guard.

- It's always better to start with a longer guard and work your way down, as you can't add hair back once it's been cut.

- Use a comb to help guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

FAQ

How often should I get a No 3 haircut?

A No 3 haircut typically lasts about 4-6 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Can I style my hair with a No 3 haircut?

Yes, a No 3 haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Use a pomade or wax to create a slicked-back look, or leave it messy and textured for a more casual style.

Is a No 3 haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a No 3 haircut is a classic style that looks good on most face shapes. However, if you have a particularly round or square face, you may want to consider a different haircut that will complement your features.

Hair Care

Now that you know how many mm a No 3 haircut is, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and style. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated, and avoid using too much heat when styling. Regular trims every 4-6 weeks will also help keep your No 3 haircut looking sharp and fresh.

Now that you have all the information you need about a No 3 haircut, why not give it a try? It's a classic style that is easy to maintain and looks good on almost everyone.
