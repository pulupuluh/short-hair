Mens Short Blonde Hair Styles The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Aug 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Blonde hair is not just for women. Men can rock this hair color too! In fact, mens short blonde hair styles have been gaining popularity in recent years. If you're thinking of going blonde, or you're already blonde but looking for a new style, this guide is for you. We'll cover everything from how to achieve the perfect blonde shade to the best short hairstyles for men.

Description

Blonde hair is a hair color characterized by low levels of the dark pigment eumelanin. It can range from almost white platinum blonde to dark blonde. In recent years, more and more men have been experimenting with blonde hair, and it's not just for surfer dudes anymore.

If you're considering going blonde, it's important to choose the right shade for your skin tone. A professional stylist can help you determine the best shade of blonde for you.

How to Achieve the Perfect Blonde Shade

If you're starting with dark hair, achieving the perfect blonde shade can take some time and effort. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Bleach Your Hair

The first step to achieving blonde hair is to bleach your hair. This process can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to have it done by a professional stylist. They will use a lightener to strip your hair of its natural pigment.

Step 2: Tone Your Hair

After bleaching your hair, it's important to tone it to remove any brassy or yellow tones. Your stylist will use a toner to achieve the perfect shade of blonde.

Step 3: Maintain Your Color

Maintaining your blonde hair color is important to keep it looking fresh. Use a purple shampoo to tone your hair between salon visits, and avoid over-washing your hair to prevent it from drying out.

Short Blonde Hair Styles for Men

Now that you've achieved the perfect blonde shade, it's time to choose a hairstyle. Here are some of the best short blonde hair styles for men:

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic short hairstyle for men. It's low maintenance and easy to style. A blonde buzz cut looks great on guys with a square or oval face shape.

The Crew Cut

The crew cut is a slightly longer version of the buzz cut. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. A blonde crew cut looks great on guys with any face shape.

The Textured Crop

The textured crop is a modern take on the classic crop. It's a great option for guys with wavy or curly hair. A blonde textured crop looks great on guys with a round or heart-shaped face.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Blonde Hair

Maintaining your short blonde hair is important to keep it looking fresh. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Purple Shampoo

Use a purple shampoo to tone your hair and remove any brassy or yellow tones.

Tip 2: Don't Over-Wash Your Hair

Over-washing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and dry it out. Aim to wash your hair every other day.

Tip 3: Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQ

Can any guy pull off blonde hair?

Blonde hair looks great on guys with fair skin tones. If you have a darker skin tone, you may want to consider a different hair color.

How often should I get my hair toned?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you wash it. Generally, you should get your hair toned every 4-6 weeks.

Can I dye my hair blonde at home?

We do not recommend dyeing your hair blonde at home. The process can be damaging to your hair, and it's best to have it done by a professional stylist.

Hair Care

Maintaining your short blonde hair requires a little extra care. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 2: Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Tip 3: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

In conclusion, mens short blonde hair styles are a great way to experiment with your hair. With the right shade of blonde and a great hairstyle, you can achieve a fresh and modern look. Remember to maintain your hair with regular salon visits and proper hair care.
