Best Mousse for Curly Hair 2020 - A Guide to Perfect Curls

Curly hair can be beautiful, but it can also be a challenge to style. One of the best ways to get perfect curls is to use the right mousse. In this guide, we will go over the best mousse for curly hair 2020, and give you tips on how to use it for the best results.

Table of Contents

  • How to Use Mousse for Curly Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Mousse for Curly Hair
  • FAQ

Description

Curly hair can be difficult to manage, but adding mousse can make it much easier. Mousse is a lightweight foam that can add volume and definition to your curls without weighing them down. The best mousse for curly hair 2020 will contain ingredients that moisturize your hair and help to control frizz.

When choosing a mousse for curly hair, look for products that contain natural ingredients like shea butter or coconut oil. These ingredients will help to hydrate your hair and keep it healthy. Avoid products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and cause damage.

How to Use Mousse for Curly Hair

Using mousse for curly hair is easy, and can be done in just a few simple steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to hydrate your hair and prepare it for styling.

Step 2: Apply Mousse

Apply a golf ball-sized amount of mousse to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Make sure to distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your curls to form. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Dry

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting. Avoid touching your hair while it is drying, as this can cause frizz.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to using mousse for curly hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to hydrate your hair and prepare it for styling.

Step 2: Apply Mousse

Apply a golf ball-sized amount of mousse to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Make sure to distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. This will help to distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage your curls to form. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Dry

Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting. Avoid touching your hair while it is drying, as this can cause frizz.

Tips for Using Mousse for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for using mousse for curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair.
  • Avoid using too much mousse, as this can weigh down your curls.
  • If your hair is prone to frizz, use a frizz control serum before applying mousse.
  • Try using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls.

FAQ

What is the best mousse for curly hair?

The best mousse for curly hair 2020 is one that contains natural ingredients like shea butter or coconut oil. It should also moisturize your hair and help to control frizz.

How often should I use mousse on my curly hair?

You can use mousse on your curly hair every day, but it's important not to use too much. A golf ball-sized amount should be enough to define your curls without weighing them down.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Try using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

By following these tips and using the best mousse for curly hair 2020, you can achieve perfect curls every time.
