Updo Hairstyles For Medium Length Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of wearing your curly hair down all the time? Do you want to try something new and stylish? If yes, then you are in the right place. In this article, we will discuss some of the best updo hairstyles for medium length curly hair. So, let's get started.

Description

Medium length curly hair can be a bit difficult to manage, especially when you want to create an updo. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a stunning updo hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd. In this article, we will discuss different types of updo hairstyles for medium length curly hair, how to create them, and how to take care of your curly hair.

How to Create Updo Hairstyles for Medium Length Curly Hair

Creating updo hairstyles for medium length curly hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stunning look. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

The first step is to prepare your hair. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Then, apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help detangle it. Next, apply a styling product to your hair to help define your curls and add some hold.

Step 2: Choose Your Updo Style

Next, choose the updo style that you want to create. You can choose from a high bun, low bun, braided updo, twisted updo, or any other style that you like.

Step 3: Create Your Updo

Once you have chosen your updo style, it's time to create it. Start by gathering your hair into a ponytail and securing it with an elastic band. Then, twist or braid your hair and wrap it around the base of the ponytail to create your updo. Secure it with bobby pins or hairpins.

Step 4: Finish Your Updo

Finally, finish your updo by applying a hairspray to hold it in place. You can also add some accessories like flowers, hairpins, or headbands to make it more stylish.

Tips for Creating Updo Hairstyles for Medium Length Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you create beautiful updo hairstyles for medium length curly hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair.
  • Use a styling product to define your curls and add some hold.
  • Choose an updo style that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Use bobby pins or hairpins to secure your updo in place.
  • Apply a hairspray to hold your updo in place.
  • Accessorize your updo with flowers, hairpins, or headbands to make it more stylish.

FAQ

Q. Can I create an updo hairstyle on my own?

A. Yes, you can create an updo hairstyle on your own. However, it may take some practice to get it right.

Q. What products should I use to create an updo hairstyle for medium length curly hair?

A. You should use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, a leave-in conditioner, and a styling product to define your curls and add some hold.

Q. How can I make my updo hairstyle last longer?

A. You can make your updo hairstyle last longer by using a hairspray to hold it in place and by securing it with bobby pins or hairpins.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Curly Hair

In addition to creating updo hairstyles, it's essential to take care of your curly hair. Here are some hair care tips for medium length curly hair:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry.
  • Use a satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips, you can maintain healthy and beautiful medium length curly hair.

Conclusion

Updo hairstyles for medium length curly hair can be stunning and stylish. With the right techniques and products, you can create a beautiful updo that will make you stand out from the crowd. Follow the steps and tips mentioned in this article to create your own updo hairstyle. Don't forget to take care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
