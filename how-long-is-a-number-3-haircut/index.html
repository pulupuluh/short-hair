How Long Is A Number 3 Haircut

Written by Robby Mar 09, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you looking to get a number 3 haircut but not sure how long it is? Let’s break it down! A number 3 haircut is typically 3/8 of an inch or 9.5mm long. It’s a popular choice for men who want a short, low-maintenance haircut that doesn’t require frequent trips to the barbershop.

How To Get a Number 3 Haircut

Getting a number 3 haircut is easy and can be done at home if you have the right tools. Here’s how:

Step 1: Choose Your Clippers

The first step is to choose your clippers. You’ll need a set of clippers with a number 3 guard attachment. If you don’t have clippers, you can purchase them online or at your local beauty supply store.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Washing your hair will remove any dirt or oils that could clog your clippers.

Step 3: Attach the Number 3 Guard

Attach the number 3 guard to your clippers. Make sure it’s securely in place before you start cutting.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start cutting your hair, using the clippers with the number 3 guard. Start at the sides and work your way up to the top of your head. Use short, even strokes to ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Once you’ve finished the sides, trim the top of your hair. Use scissors to trim any longer pieces that were missed by the clippers.

Step 6: Clean Up

Once you’re finished cutting, clean up any loose hairs around your neck and ears with a small brush or cloth. Take a shower to wash away any remaining loose hairs.

Tips for a Great Number 3 Haircut

Here are some tips to ensure a great number 3 haircut:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use even strokes with your clippers
  • Trim any longer pieces with scissors
  • Clean up loose hairs around your neck and ears
  • Take a shower to wash away any remaining loose hairs

FAQ About Number 3 Haircuts

How often should I get a number 3 haircut?

A number 3 haircut is a low-maintenance style, so you can go up to four weeks between haircuts.

What type of hair works best with a number 3 haircut?

A number 3 haircut works well with most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Can I get a number 3 haircut if I have a receding hairline?

A number 3 haircut can help conceal a receding hairline. Talk to your barber about how to best style your hair to minimize the appearance of a receding hairline.

Hair Care After a Number 3 Haircut

After getting a number 3 haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using heavy styling products
  • Use a light conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Visit your barber every four weeks for a touch-up

Now that you know how long a number 3 haircut is, you can confidently ask for this style at the barbershop or try it at home. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and style. Happy cutting!
