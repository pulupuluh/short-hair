Hairstyles For Thin Curly Hair Round Face Tips And Ideas

Written by Luffy Jun 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Having thin curly hair and a round face can be a challenging combination when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle. You want to enhance your natural curls while also slimming down your face shape. But don't worry, there are plenty of hairstyles out there that can flatter your features and boost your confidence. In this article, we'll explore different hairstyles, tips, and care routines for thin curly hair round face.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-step guide to styling thin curly hair round face
  • Tips for maintaining healthy thin curly hair round face
  • FAQ about thin curly hair round face
  • Hair care routine for thin curly hair round face

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Thin Curly Hair Round Face

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle for thin curly hair round face, the goal is to elongate your face and create more volume. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for hairstyles that have layers or textured cuts instead of blunt cuts. This will add movement and dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long. A mid-length cut that falls between your chin and shoulders is ideal.
  • Add volume to your roots by using a volumizing mousse or spray before styling your hair.
  • Consider getting bangs that are side-swept or asymmetrical to create a more angular look.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that flatters your face shape the most.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thin Curly Hair Round Face

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, let's dive into how to style your thin curly hair round face:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner to detangle and moisturize your curls.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing cream or gel to define your curls and add shine.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to gently dry your hair. Scrunch your curls as you go to add volume.
  4. If you want to add more volume, use a round brush to lift your roots as you blow-dry your hair.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to hold your style in place and add extra shine.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Thin Curly Hair Round Face

Thin curly hair round face can be prone to breakage and frizz, so it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to nourish and moisturize your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your curls when they're dry. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

FAQ about Thin Curly Hair Round Face

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have thin curly hair round face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear wider. Blunt cuts can also make your hair look flat and lifeless.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have thin curly hair round face?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail. Just make sure to add some volume to your roots and leave some curls loose around your face to create a more flattering look.

Do I need to use a diffuser attachment on my hair dryer?

Using a diffuser attachment can help to minimize frizz and enhance your natural curls. However, if you prefer to air dry your hair, that's fine too.

Hair Care Routine for Thin Curly Hair Round Face

Here's a simple hair care routine for thin curly hair round face:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to detangle and moisturize your curls.
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing cream or gel to define your curls.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to gently dry your hair.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to hold your style in place and add extra shine.

Remember to deep condition your hair once a week and avoid brushing your curls when they're dry. With the right care and styling, you can rock any hairstyle and feel confident in your thin curly hair round face.
