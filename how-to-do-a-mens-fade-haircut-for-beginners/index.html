How To Do A Men s Fade Haircut For Beginners

Written by Christine Mar 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a beginner looking to learn how to do a men's fade haircut, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll go over the steps you need to take to achieve a great-looking fade haircut. Whether you're looking to do it on yourself or on a friend, these tips will help you get started.

Description

A fade haircut is a style that gradually goes from short to long, usually from the bottom of the hairline to the top of the crown. It's a popular style that can be worn by anyone, regardless of hair type or length. The key to a great fade haircut is blending the different lengths of hair seamlessly, creating a smooth and natural transition.

How to

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, guards, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Determine the Length

Decide on the length you want for your fade. The most common lengths are 1/8 inch, 1/4 inch, and 1/2 inch. Choose the guard that corresponds to your desired length and attach it to your clippers.

Step 2: Create the Base

Start by creating a base for your fade. Use the clippers and the guard you chose to trim the bottom of the hairline, going up about an inch or two. This will create a foundation for the fade.

Step 3: Remove the Guard

Remove the guard from your clippers and start trimming the hair above the base. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length. Make sure to blend the hair as you go, so there are no harsh lines.

Step 4: Switch Guards

Switch to a guard that is one size smaller than the one you used for the base. Repeat the previous step, trimming the hair above the previous line and blending it in as you go.

Step 5: Repeat

Continue switching to smaller guards and trimming the hair above the previous line, until you reach the desired length at the top. Make sure to blend the hair at each step, so there are no harsh lines.

Step 6: Clean Up

Use your scissors to clean up any stray hairs and to blend in any areas that need it. Use your comb to check for any uneven spots and trim them as needed.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve a great-looking fade haircut:

  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Use a mirror to check your work as you go.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to shorter guards.
  • Blend the hair at each step to avoid harsh lines.
  • Clean up any stray hairs with scissors.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing a men's fade haircut:

What is a fade haircut?

A fade haircut is a style that gradually goes from short to long, creating a smooth and natural transition.

What tools do I need to do a fade haircut?

You'll need clippers, guards, scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

What lengths are common for a fade haircut?

The most common lengths are 1/8 inch, 1/4 inch, and 1/2 inch.

Can I do a fade haircut on myself?

Yes, but it may be easier to have someone else do it for you.

Hair Care

After you've done your fade haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

With these tips and steps, you should be able to achieve a great-looking fade haircut. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!
