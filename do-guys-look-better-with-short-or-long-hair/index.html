Do Guys Look Better With Short Or Long Hair

Written by William Mar 25, 2023 · 8 min read

When it comes to men’s hairstyles, there’s always been a debate on whether they look better with short or long hair. While some people believe that short hair is the way to go, others argue that longer hair can look just as good. In this article, we’ll explore both options and try to answer the age-old question: do guys look better with short or long hair?

Table of Content

  • Short Hair
  • Long Hair
  • How to Decide
  • Hair Care
  • FAQ

Short Hair

Short hair is a popular choice for many men. It’s low maintenance, easy to style, and can look very professional. Short haircuts can range from a buzz cut to a crew cut, to a side part. Short hair can also help to make a man’s face look more defined and chiseled.

One of the benefits of short hair is that it’s easy to maintain. It doesn’t require a lot of washing or styling, so it’s perfect for guys who are always on the go. Short hair is also great for warmer climates, as it can help to keep a man’s head cool.

However, short hair may not be the best option for everyone. If a man has a round face or a larger forehead, short hair can accentuate these features. Short hair can also make a man look younger, which may not be desirable in certain situations.

Long Hair

Long hair is another option for men who want to switch up their hairstyle. Long hair can range from shoulder-length to waist-length, and can be styled in a variety of ways. Long hair can make a man look more bohemian or artistic, and can be great for guys who want to stand out.

One of the benefits of long hair is that it can be very versatile. It can be worn straight, curly, or wavy, and can be styled in a variety of ways. Long hair can also help to make a man look more masculine and rugged.

However, long hair can also be high maintenance. It requires regular washing, conditioning, and styling to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Long hair can also be more difficult to manage, especially if a man has thick or curly hair.

How to Decide

So, how do you decide whether short hair or long hair is the right choice for you? Here are a few factors to consider:

  • Face shape: Consider your face shape when deciding on a hairstyle. Short hair can be great for guys with square or oval faces, while long hair may look better on guys with heart-shaped or triangular faces.
  • Personal style: Think about your personal style and the image you want to project. Short hair can be great for guys who want to look professional and put-together, while long hair can be great for guys who want to look more bohemian or artistic.
  • Maintenance: Consider how much time and effort you’re willing to put into maintaining your hairstyle. Short hair is low maintenance, while long hair requires more time and effort to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.

Hair Care

No matter which hairstyle you choose, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash regularly: Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Style carefully: If you use styling products, be sure to use them sparingly and carefully. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular haircuts: Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. This is especially important if you have short hair.

FAQ

Q: Does hair length affect hair loss?

A: No, hair length does not affect hair loss. However, certain hairstyles can cause hair breakage or damage, which can make hair appear thinner.

Q: Can I still style my hair if it’s short?

A: Yes, there are plenty of styling options for short hair. You can use pomade, gel, or wax to create a variety of looks.

Q: Will long hair make me look unprofessional?

A: Not necessarily. Long hair can be styled in a professional manner, and can actually add to a man’s overall appearance and confidence.

So, do guys look better with short or long hair? The answer ultimately depends on your personal style, face shape, and maintenance preferences. Whatever your choice, be sure to take care of your hair and style it with confidence!
