How To Get Short Dreads With Straight Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're someone with straight hair and you've always wanted to try dreadlocks, you might be wondering if it's even possible. The answer is yes, it is! In this guide, we'll show you how to get short dreads with straight hair.

Description of Short Dreads with Straight Hair

Short dreads with straight hair are a style that involves creating small, tightly coiled locks in straight hair. This style can be achieved by using a technique called "twist and rip," which involves twisting sections of hair and then pulling them apart to create knots. The end result is a unique and stylish look that is perfect for those who want to try something new.

How to Get Short Dreads with Straight Hair

Getting short dreads with straight hair requires some preparation and patience, but the end result is definitely worth it. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step is to wash your hair thoroughly with a clarifying shampoo. This will remove any product buildup or oils that might prevent your hair from holding the dreadlocks. Rinse your hair with warm water and towel dry it gently.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, use a comb to section your hair into small, even sections. The size of the sections will depend on how thick you want your dreadlocks to be. Use hair clips to keep the sections in place.

Step 3: Twist and Rip

Take one section of hair and twist it tightly from the roots to the ends. Then, use your fingers to rip the twisted section apart. This will create small knots in your hair, which will eventually turn into dreadlocks. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Maintain Your Dreadlocks

Once you have created your dreadlocks, you will need to maintain them properly. This involves washing them regularly with a residue-free shampoo and using a special dreadlock conditioner. You will also need to retwist and palm roll your dreadlocks regularly to keep them tight and neat.

Tips for Getting Short Dreads with Straight Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with clean, dry hair to ensure that the knots hold properly.
  • Be patient â it can take several months for your dreadlocks to fully mature.
  • Avoid using products that contain heavy oils or waxes, as these can make your hair greasy and weigh down your dreadlocks.
  • Consider consulting with a professional stylist who has experience creating dreadlocks.

FAQ about Short Dreads with Straight Hair

Can I get short dreads with relaxed hair?

Yes, it is possible to get short dreads with relaxed hair. However, the process may be slightly different, and you should consult with a stylist who has experience working with relaxed hair.

How long does it take to get short dreads with straight hair?

It can take several months for your dreadlocks to fully mature, so be patient and consistent with your maintenance routine.

Can I still style my hair with short dreads?

Absolutely! Short dreads can be styled in a variety of ways, from updos to braids to simple ponytails.

Hair Care for Short Dreads with Straight Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful dreadlocks. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Use a special dreadlock conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Retwist and palm roll your dreadlocks regularly to keep them neat and tight.
  • Avoid using hot tools or chemicals on your hair, as these can damage your dreadlocks.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful and stylish short dreads with straight hair. So why not give it a try and see how you like this unique and eye-catching look?
