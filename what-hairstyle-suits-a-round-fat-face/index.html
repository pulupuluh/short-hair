What Hairstyle Suits A Round Fat Face

Written by Daniel Feb 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to find a hairstyle that flatters your round, fat face? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with round faces find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that complements their features. But fear not, as we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing what hairstyle suits a round fat face and how you can achieve the look you desire.

Description

A round face is characterized by soft, curved lines with a similar width and length. People with round faces tend to have fuller cheeks and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that creates the illusion of length and angles, as this will help to balance out your features and give the appearance of a more defined jawline.

How to

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for a round fat face, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, you'll want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will only make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and length to your face. Secondly, choose hairstyles that have layers or angles, as this will help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

Step by step

Step 1:

Start by identifying your face shape. You can do this by pulling your hair back and looking in the mirror. If your face is round, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that adds height and length to your face.

Step 2:

Choose a hairstyle with layers or angles. This will help to create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

Step 3:

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. Opt for hairstyles that add height and length to your face instead.

Step 4:

Consider getting a side-swept fringe or parting. This will help to create the illusion of a more angular face shape.

Step 5:

If you have long hair, try wearing it in a loose, tousled style. This will help to create the illusion of length and angles.

Tips

  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as these can make your face appear wider.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and don't be afraid to try something new.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have a bob haircut if I have a round fat face?

A: Yes, you can still have a bob haircut if you have a round fat face. However, you'll want to make sure that the length of your bob falls below your chin and that it has layers or angles to create the illusion of a more defined jawline.

Q: What are some hairstyles that I should avoid if I have a round fat face?

A: You should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a blunt fringe or a straight bob. You should also avoid hairstyles that are too short, as these can make your face appear wider.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to choose products that are suitable for your hair type. If you have fine or thin hair, look for volumizing products that will add body and texture to your hair. If you have thick or curly hair, look for smoothing products that will help to tame frizz and define your curls.

Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools, such as a hair straightener or curling iron. This will help to prevent damage to your hair and keep it looking healthy and shiny.

Finally, don't forget to get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle for a round fat face can be challenging, but with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve the look you desire. Remember to choose hairstyles that add height and length to your face, opt for layers or angles, and avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. With these tips, you'll be well on your way to finding the perfect hairstyle for your round fat face.
