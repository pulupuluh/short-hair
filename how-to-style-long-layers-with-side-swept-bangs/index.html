How To Style Long Layers With Side Swept Bangs

Written by William Jul 21, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking for a chic and effortless hairstyle, long layers with side swept bangs are the perfect choice. This versatile style can be dressed up or down and suits all face shapes. In this article, we'll show you how to style long layers with side swept bangs in a few easy steps.

Description

Long layers with side swept bangs is a popular hairstyle that involves long layers of hair cut at different lengths to create texture and movement. The side swept bangs are cut diagonally across the forehead and swept to the side for a flattering and feminine look. This hairstyle is perfect for those with long hair who want to add some dimension and style to their locks.

How to Style Long Layers with Side Swept Bangs

Follow these easy steps to style your long layers with side swept bangs:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Before styling, prep your hair by washing and conditioning it with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will give your hair some lift and body. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Blow-dry your hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and smooth out any frizz. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the brush, pulling the brush away from your scalp and blowing hot air onto the hair. Continue until your hair is completely dry.

Step 3: Curl your hair

Using a curling iron, curl small sections of your hair away from your face. This will give your hair some extra volume and add some texture to your layers. Hold the curling iron at a 45-degree angle and wrap the hair around the barrel, holding for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat until all sections of your hair are curled.

Step 4: Style your bangs

To style your side swept bangs, use a round brush and blow-dry them to the side. You can also use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or waves. If you want to add some extra volume, tease your bangs at the roots using a comb.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

To set your style in place, spritz your hair with a light-hold hairspray. This will keep your curls and volume in place all day long.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your long layers with side swept bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage when blow-drying and curling.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • When curling your hair, curl away from your face for a more natural look.
  • Tease your hair at the roots for extra volume.

FAQ

How often should I trim my long layers with side swept bangs?

We recommend getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your layers and bangs looking fresh and healthy.

Can I style my long layers with side swept bangs without heat?

Yes, you can style your hair without heat by using a texturizing spray or mousse and scrunching your hair with your fingers. This will give your hair a natural and effortless look.

Hair Care

To keep your long layers with side swept bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too oily.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray when styling.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style your long layers with side swept bangs and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Whether you're heading to work or out for a night on the town, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
