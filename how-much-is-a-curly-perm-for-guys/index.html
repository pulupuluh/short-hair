How Much Is A Curly Perm For Guys

Written by Robby Feb 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a guy looking to change up your hairstyle, you may be wondering how much a curly perm will cost. This guide will break down everything you need to know about the cost of a curly perm for guys, including what factors can impact the price.

Description

A curly perm is a chemical treatment that adds curl or wave to hair that is normally straight. This treatment is typically done in a salon and can last for several months. While perms are often associated with women, they can also be a great option for men who want to add some texture and volume to their hair.

How to Get a Curly Perm

If you're interested in getting a curly perm, the first step is to find a salon that offers the service. You'll want to make sure you choose a stylist who has experience working with men's hair, as the process can be slightly different than it is for women.

During the appointment, your stylist will apply a chemical solution to your hair that will break down the bonds in your hair. They'll then wrap your hair around rods to create the desired curl pattern. After the solution has set, they'll rinse it out and apply a neutralizer to re-form the hair bonds in their new shape.

Step by Step Guide

If you're interested in getting a curly perm, here's a step-by-step guide to what you can expect:

  1. Find a salon that offers curly perms for men.
  2. Make an appointment with a stylist who has experience working with men's hair.
  3. Discuss the type of curl pattern you want and any concerns you have with your stylist.
  4. Your stylist will apply a chemical solution to your hair to break down the bonds.
  5. Your hair will be wrapped around rods to create the desired curl pattern.
  6. The solution will be rinsed out, and a neutralizer will be applied to re-form the hair bonds.
  7. Your hair will be rinsed and styled to your liking.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Perm

Once you've gotten a curly perm, it's important to take steps to maintain it. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a perm.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Consider using a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.

FAQ

How much does a curly perm for guys cost?

The cost of a curly perm for guys can vary depending on a variety of factors, including the salon you go to, the stylist you choose, and the length and thickness of your hair. On average, you can expect to pay anywhere from $50 to $150 for a curly perm.

How long does a curly perm last?

A curly perm can last for several months, but the exact length of time will depend on a variety of factors, including the type of perm you get, how well you take care of your hair, and your hair's natural growth rate.

Is a curly perm damaging to hair?

A curly perm can be damaging to hair if it's not done properly or if the hair is already damaged. However, if done correctly and on healthy hair, a curly perm can be a safe and effective way to add texture and volume to your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Perms

It's important to take good care of your hair after getting a curly perm to ensure that it stays healthy and looks its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to define your curls.

Conclusion

A curly perm can be a great way for guys to switch up their hairstyle and add some texture and volume to their hair. While the cost can vary depending on a variety of factors, on average, you can expect to pay between $50 and $150 for a curly perm. By taking good care of your hair and following the tips outlined in this guide, you can enjoy your new curls for months to come.
