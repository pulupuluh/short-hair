How To Style Wavy Thick Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Aug 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Wavy thick hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it looks voluminous and stunning, but on the other, it can be difficult to manage and style. If you're struggling with styling your wavy thick hair, don't worry. In this article, we'll show you how to style wavy thick hair in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Wavy Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Wavy Thick Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Wavy Thick Hair

Description

Wavy thick hair is characterized by its natural waves and thick texture. It can be challenging to manage, but with the right products and techniques, you can make it look stunning.

How to Style Wavy Thick Hair

Here are some general tips for styling wavy thick hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to tame frizz and add moisture to your hair
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some general tips for styling wavy thick hair, here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect style:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner to tame frizz and add moisture.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance your natural waves.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and dry your hair on a low heat setting.
  5. Once your hair is dry, apply a light hold styling cream to enhance your waves and add texture.
  6. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.
  7. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold your waves in place.

Tips for Styling Wavy Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling wavy thick hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, which can cause frizz.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends and add shine.
  • Invest in a high-quality diffuser attachment for your blow dryer.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids or messy buns, to add variety to your look.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove any buildup from hair products.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling wavy thick hair:

Q: How often should I wash my wavy thick hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces. As a general rule, you should wash your hair every two to three days.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my wavy thick hair?

A: Use a leave-in conditioner and a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Hair Care for Wavy Thick Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for wavy thick hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time in the sun to prevent sun damage.

In conclusion, styling wavy thick hair may seem daunting, but with the right products and techniques, it can be easy and effortless. Follow our tips and tricks, and you'll have stunning, voluminous waves in no time.
