How To Contour Heart Face Shape Tutorial In Relaxed English Language

Written by Christine Apr 24, 2023 · 6 min read

Do you have a heart-shaped face and want to know how to contour it? Contouring can help enhance your natural features and create a more defined look. In this article, we will go through a step-by-step tutorial on how to contour a heart face shape in relaxed English language.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Contour Heart Face Shape Tutorial
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. Contouring can help balance out these features and create a more symmetrical look. The key is to use darker shades to create shadows and lighter shades to enhance and highlight certain areas of the face.

How to Contour Heart Face Shape Tutorial

Before we begin, make sure you have the following makeup products:

  • Contour powder or cream
  • Highlighter powder or cream
  • Blush
  • Makeup brushes

Step by Step

Follow these steps to contour a heart-shaped face:

  1. Start by applying foundation to create an even base.
  2. Next, use a contour powder or cream to create shadows on the forehead and temples. Apply the contour product in a "C" shape from the temples to the hollows of the cheeks.
  3. Blend the contour product using a makeup brush or sponge to create a natural look.
  4. Apply highlighter powder or cream to the high points of the face, including the cheekbones, bridge of the nose, and cupid's bow.
  5. Blend the highlighter using a makeup brush or sponge.
  6. Use a blush to add color to the cheeks. Apply the blush to the apples of the cheeks and blend towards the temples.
  7. Finish the look with a setting spray to lock in the makeup.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you contour a heart-shaped face:

  • Choose a contour product that is one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone.
  • Use a highlighter that is one or two shades lighter than your natural skin tone.
  • Blend, blend, blend! Blending is key to creating a natural and seamless look.
  • Consider using a matte contour powder for a more natural look.
  • Use a small makeup brush to apply the contour product for more precision.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about contouring a heart-shaped face:

  • Can contouring change the shape of my face? Contouring can help enhance your natural features, but it cannot change the shape of your face.
  • What if I don't have a contour product? You can use a matte bronzer as a substitute for a contour product.
  • Should I contour my chin? It's best to avoid contouring the chin as it can create an unnatural look.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care for a heart-shaped face, consider a hairstyle that adds volume to the chin area. This can help balance out the wider forehead and high cheekbones. Bangs can also be a great option for heart-shaped faces, as they can help create the illusion of a narrower forehead.

Now that you know how to contour a heart-shaped face, give it a try and see how it can enhance your natural features. With these tips and tricks, you'll be a contouring pro in no time!
