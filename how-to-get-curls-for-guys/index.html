How To Get Curls For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Are you a guy who wants to achieve curly hair but doesn't know where to start? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll discuss how to get curls for guys using simple steps and tips.

How to Get Curls for Guys

Before we start, it's important to note that getting curls for guys isn't as straightforward as it is for women. Men generally have shorter hair, which makes it harder to achieve curls. Nevertheless, it's not impossible.

The first thing you need to do is determine your hair type. There are three main types of hair: straight, wavy, and curly. If you have straight hair, you'll need to put in more effort to achieve curls. If you have wavy hair, you're already halfway there. If you have curly hair, you're in luck!

Once you've determined your hair type, you can start thinking about the different methods you can use to get curls. There are two main ways to achieve curls for guys: using heat or using products. Let's take a look at each method.

Step-by-Step Guide

Method 1: Using Heat

Step 1: Wash your hair and towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will prevent your hair from getting damaged by the heat.

Step 3: Divide your hair into sections.

Step 4: Take a curling iron and curl each section of hair. Hold the curling iron for about 10-15 seconds before releasing it.

Step 5: Once you've curled all sections of your hair, use a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Method 2: Using Products

Step 1: Wash your hair and towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. This could be a mousse, gel, or cream. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer and dry your hair on a low heat setting. This will help to enhance the curls.

Step 4: Once your hair is dry, use a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Getting curls for guys is just the first step. Maintaining them is equally important. Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from getting dry.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curly hair. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage your curls.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your curls hydrated.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting curls for guys:

Q. Can guys with short hair get curls?

A. Yes, guys with short hair can get curls. However, it may be harder to achieve as you'll need to use smaller sections of hair to curl.

Q. How long will my curls last?

A. This will depend on your hair type and the method you use to get curls. Heat-based curls will generally last longer than product-based curls.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Instead, wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair as it can dry out your curls. Use lukewarm water instead.
  • Use a microfiber towel or a t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel. This will help to prevent frizz.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated throughout the day.

With these tips, you'll be able to get curls for guys and keep them looking great. Remember, it's all about finding the right method and taking care of your curls. Good luck!
