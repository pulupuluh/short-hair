What Is The Best Face Shape For Curtain Bangs

Written by Robby Aug 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering getting curtain bangs but unsure if they will suit your face shape? Curtain bangs have become a popular hairstyle trend in recent years and can give your hair a fresh and chic look. However, not every face shape is suitable for curtain bangs. In this article, we will discuss the best face shape for curtain bangs and other important information you need to know before getting this style.

What are Curtain Bangs?

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that are parted down the middle, framing the face on both sides. They are called "curtain" bangs because they resemble the drapes that hang on either side of a window. This style can be worn long or short and can be customized to suit each individual's face shape and hair type.

Best Face Shape for Curtain Bangs

The good news is that curtain bangs can suit almost any face shape! However, they are particularly flattering for those with heart-shaped or oval faces. Heart-shaped faces have a wide forehead and a narrow chin, making curtain bangs perfect for balancing out the face. Oval faces have a balanced and symmetrical shape, so curtain bangs can accentuate the cheekbones and complement the face shape.

If you have a round or square face, you can still wear curtain bangs, but it is important to ensure that the bangs are not too short or too heavy. For round faces, longer curtain bangs that graze the cheekbones can create the illusion of a more angular face shape. For square faces, adding layers around the face can soften the features and create a more balanced look.

How to Style Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs are versatile and can be styled in many ways. Here are some popular styling options:

  • Straight and sleek: Use a flat iron to straighten the bangs and create a sleek look.
  • Natural and tousled: Use a curling wand to add loose waves to the bangs for a more relaxed look.
  • Pinned back: Use bobby pins to pin the bangs back for a more polished look.
  • Blowout: Use a round brush and blow dryer to give the bangs volume and bounce.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Curtain Bangs

If you are considering cutting your own curtain bangs at home, here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into bangs.
  3. Comb the hair straight down and determine the desired length of the bangs.
  4. Use hair cutting scissors to cut the bangs in a straight line.
  5. Part the bangs down the middle and use thinning shears to create a wispy, textured look.
  6. Style the bangs as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips to keep your curtain bangs looking fresh and stylish:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them at the desired length.
  • Use dry shampoo to keep your bangs from getting oily and weighed down.
  • Blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall for best results.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down the bangs.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear curtain bangs if I have curly hair?

A: Yes! In fact, curtain bangs can look great on curly hair. Just make sure to cut them a bit longer than you would for straight hair to allow for shrinkage.

Q: Will curtain bangs suit me if I have a small forehead?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can actually make your forehead appear longer and more balanced.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

It is important to take care of your hair to keep your curtain bangs looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out the hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish the hair and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair to prevent breakage.

Now that you know the best face shape for curtain bangs and how to style and maintain them, you can confidently rock this chic and trendy hairstyle!
