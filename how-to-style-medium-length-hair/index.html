How To Style Medium Length Hair Tips Tricks And Techniques

Written by Bardi Mar 09, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairdo? Do you want to switch things up and try something new? If you have medium length hair, you're in luck! There are so many ways to style your hair that are both easy and trendy. In this post, we'll cover everything from braids to waves to updos. Whether you're heading to work or going out for a night on the town, these styles will have you looking and feeling your best. Read on to learn how to style medium length hair!

How to Style Medium Length Hair: Description

Medium length hair is versatile and easy to manage. It's long enough to experiment with different styles, but short enough to keep out of your face. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there are plenty of ways to style it. From beachy waves to sleek updos, medium length hair is perfect for any occasion. In this post, we'll show you how to create a variety of looks that are both stylish and effortless.

How to Style Medium Length Hair: How To

Before we dive into the styling techniques, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Invest in quality hair products. The right shampoo, conditioner, and styling products can make all the difference.
  • Use heat protectant spray. If you're using hot tools like a curling iron or straightener, make sure to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Don't forget to brush your hair. Brushing regularly can help distribute natural oils and prevent tangles.
  • Experiment with different styles. Don't be afraid to try something new!

1. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a classic look that never goes out of style. To achieve this look, start by applying a texturizing spray to damp hair. Then, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Finish off with a spritz of hairspray to keep your waves in place.

2. Braids

Braids are a great way to switch up your hairstyle. There are many different types of braids to choose from, including French braids, fishtail braids, and Dutch braids. To create a simple braid, start by sectioning your hair into three equal parts. Then, cross the right section over the middle section, followed by the left section over the new middle section. Repeat until you reach the end of your hair and secure with an elastic.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down style is a great option for those who want to keep their hair out of their face. To achieve this look, start by sectioning off the top half of your hair. Secure with an elastic or clip. You can leave the bottom half straight, curl it, or create waves. This style is perfect for both casual and formal occasions.

4. Low Bun

A low bun is a chic and sophisticated style that's perfect for work or a night out. Start by pulling your hair back into a low ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun. Secure with bobby pins and a spritz of hairspray.

5. Straight and Sleek

If you have naturally straight hair, embrace it! To achieve a sleek and polished look, start by applying a heat protectant spray. Then, use a straightening iron to straighten your hair in sections. Finish off with a shine serum for added shine.

How to Style Medium Length Hair: Step by Step

Here are step-by-step instructions for creating some of the styles we mentioned above:

Beachy Waves

  1. Apply texturizing spray to damp hair.
  2. Section hair and use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  3. Finish with a spritz of hairspray.

Braids

  1. Section hair and create a simple braid by crossing the right section over the middle section, followed by the left section over the new middle section.
  2. Secure with an elastic.

Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Section off the top half of your hair and secure with an elastic or clip.
  2. Leave the bottom half straight or create waves.

Low Bun

  1. Pull hair back into a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  3. Secure with bobby pins and a spritz of hairspray.

Straight and Sleek

  1. Apply heat protectant spray to hair.
  2. Straighten hair in sections using a straightening iron.
  3. Finish with a shine serum.

How to Style Medium Length Hair: Tips

Here are a few more tips to help you style your medium length hair:

  • Experiment with different parts. A middle part can create a sleek and sophisticated look, while a deep side part can add volume and texture.
  • Add accessories. Headbands, clips, and hair ties can add a pop of color or texture to your hairdo.
  • Use dry shampoo. Dry shampoo can help absorb excess oil and extend the life of your hairstyle.

How to Style Medium Length Hair: FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling medium length hair:

  • Q: How often should I wash my hair?
  • A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out frequently, you may need to wash your hair more often. Otherwise, you can probably get away with washing your hair every other day or every few days.
  • Q: Can I still wear a ponytail with medium length hair?
  • A: Yes! A ponytail is a great option for medium length hair. You can create a high ponytail, a low ponytail, or a side ponytail.
  • Q: How can I add volume to my hair?
  • A: Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow dry your hair upside down, or tease your hair at the roots.

How to Style Medium Length Hair: Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your hair healthy and strong. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated for healthy hair from the inside out.

Now that you know how to style medium length hair, it's time to experiment and find the perfect look for you. Whether you prefer beachy waves or sleek and straight hair, there's a style out there that will make you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to take care of your hair with regular trims and quality hair products, and don't be afraid to try something new!
