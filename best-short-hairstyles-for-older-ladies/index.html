Best Short Hairstyles For Older Ladies A Guide To Looking Fabulous

Written by Oliver Feb 28, 2023 Â· 11 min read

If you're an older lady, you might be wondering what hairstyles would suit you best. You want something that's easy to maintain, looks good, and makes you feel confident. Short hairstyles are a great option for older ladies, as they're low-maintenance and can be styled in a variety of ways. In this post, we'll take a look at some of the best short hairstyles for older ladies and give you some tips on how to style them.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that's perfect for older ladies. It's a low-maintenance cut that can be styled in different ways, depending on the occasion. You can keep it simple and sleek for a professional look, or add some texture for a more casual vibe. The pixie cut is also great for those with thinning hair, as it can create the illusion of volume.

How to Get a Pixie Cut

To get a pixie cut, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back, leaving the top longer. You can choose to have bangs or not, depending on your preference. The stylist will then shape the hair to create the desired look.

Tips for Styling a Pixie Cut

To add texture to a pixie cut, you can use a texturizing spray or wax. Use your fingers to create a messy, tousled look. For a sleeker look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another classic short hairstyle that's perfect for older ladies. It's a versatile cut that can be styled in different ways, depending on the length and texture of your hair. The bob cut is great for those with fine, straight hair, as it can create the illusion of fullness and volume.

How to Get a Bob Cut

To get a bob cut, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair to chin-length or shorter. You can choose to have bangs or not, depending on your preference. The stylist will then shape the hair to create the desired look.

Tips for Styling a Bob Cut

To add volume to a bob cut, use a volumizing mousse or spray. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots as you go. For a sleeker look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair.

Shag Cut

The shag cut is a layered hairstyle that's perfect for older ladies. It's a great way to add volume and texture to your hair, and it can be styled in different ways, depending on the length and texture of your hair. The shag cut is great for those with wavy or curly hair, as it can enhance the natural texture of your hair.

How to Get a Shag Cut

To get a shag cut, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in layers, leaving the top longer. The stylist will then shape the hair to create the desired look.

Tips for Styling a Shag Cut

To enhance the natural texture of a shag cut, use a curl-enhancing mousse or spray. Scrunch your hair with your hands as you blow-dry it. For a sleeker look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair.

Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a shorter, more feminine version of the mohawk. It's a bold and edgy hairstyle that's perfect for older ladies who want to make a statement. The faux hawk is great for those with thick, straight hair, as it can create the illusion of volume and texture.

How to Get a Faux Hawk

To get a faux hawk, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back, leaving the top longer. The stylist will then shape the hair to create the desired look.

Tips for Styling a Faux Hawk

To create a faux hawk, use a texturizing spray or wax. Use your fingers to create a messy, tousled look. For a sleeker look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair.

Cropped Curls

Cropped curls are a fun and flirty hairstyle that's perfect for older ladies. It's a great way to add volume and texture to your hair, and it can be styled in different ways, depending on the length and texture of your hair. Cropped curls are great for those with naturally curly hair, as it can enhance the natural texture of your hair.

How to Get Cropped Curls

To get cropped curls, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair short, leaving some length on top. The stylist will then shape the hair to create the desired look.

Tips for Styling Cropped Curls

To enhance the natural texture of cropped curls, use a curl-enhancing mousse or spray. Scrunch your hair with your hands as you blow-dry it. For a sleeker look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair.

Styling Tips

When styling your short hair, it's important to use the right products and tools. Here are some tips to help you style your short hair:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.
  • Use a texturizing spray or wax to add texture to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and tame frizz.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your hair for a sleek look.
  • Use a curling iron to create curls or waves.

FAQs

What is the best short hairstyle for older ladies?

The best short hairstyle for older ladies depends on your hair type and face shape. Some popular short hairstyles for older ladies include the pixie cut, bob cut, shag cut, faux hawk, and cropped curls.

What should I consider before getting a short hairstyle?

Before getting a short hairstyle, you should consider your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. Short hairstyles are low-maintenance, but they require more frequent trips to the salon for upkeep.

Hair Care Tips

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it's still important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for older ladies with short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing it when it's wet.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can look fabulous at any age. Remember, the best hairstyle is one that makes you feel confident and comfortable.
