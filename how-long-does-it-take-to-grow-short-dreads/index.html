How Long Does It Take To Grow Short Dreads

Written by Robby Sep 08, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you thinking about getting short dreads? If so, one of the questions you may be asking yourself is, "how long does it take to grow short dreads?" The answer to this question is not as straightforward as you may think. There are several factors that can affect how long it takes for your hair to form short dreads, including your hair type, the method you use to create your dreads, and your hair care routine. In this article, we'll explore these factors in more detail and give you some tips on how to grow short dreads quickly.

Description

Dreads are a popular hairstyle that have been around for centuries. They are a great way to express your individuality and can be styled in many different ways. Short dreads are a popular choice, as they are easy to maintain and can be styled in various ways.

How to Grow Short Dreads

There are several methods you can use to grow short dreads, including the twist and rip method, backcombing, and interlocking. The twist and rip method involves twisting small sections of hair and then pulling them apart to create the dread. Backcombing involves teasing the hair and then rolling it into a dread. Interlocking involves using a crochet hook to weave the hair into a dread.

No matter which method you choose, it's important to keep your hair healthy and well-moisturized. This will help your hair to grow faster and prevent breakage. You should also avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair, as these can damage the hair and slow down the growth process.

Step-by-Step Guide to Growing Short Dreads

1. Choose your method: Decide which method you want to use to create your dreads. You can do this on your own or with the help of a professional stylist.

2. Section your hair: Divide your hair into small sections using a comb. The size of the sections will depend on how thick you want your dreads to be.

3. Twist or backcomb your hair: Use your chosen method to twist or backcomb each section of hair. Make sure to work from the roots to the ends of your hair.

4. Secure your dreads: Once you have twisted or backcombed each section, use a rubber band or hair tie to secure the dread in place. You can also use a crochet hook to interlock the hair.

5. Maintain your dreads: To keep your dreads looking their best, you should wash them regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. You should also moisturize your hair with a leave-in conditioner or oil to prevent breakage.

Tips for Growing Short Dreads

1. Be patient: Growing short dreads can take time, so be patient and don't get discouraged if your hair doesn't look exactly how you want it to right away.

2. Keep your hair healthy: Make sure to keep your hair healthy by eating a balanced diet, getting enough sleep, and avoiding stress.

3. Try different styles: Short dreads can be styled in many different ways, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories.

FAQ

Q: How long does it take to grow short dreads?

A: The length of time it takes to grow short dreads depends on several factors, including your hair type, the method you use to create your dreads, and your hair care routine. It can take anywhere from a few months to a year or more to grow short dreads.

Q: Can I get short dreads if my hair is straight?

A: Yes, you can still get short dreads if your hair is straight. However, it may take longer for your hair to form dreads and you may need to use a different method, such as interlocking, to create them.

Hair Care Tips for Short Dreads

1. Wash your hair regularly: To prevent buildup and keep your dreads looking fresh, you should wash your hair at least once a week with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

2. Moisturize your hair: Dreads can be prone to dryness and breakage, so it's important to moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or oil.

3. Protect your hair at night: To prevent frizz and maintain the shape of your dreads, you should protect your hair at night by wearing a silk or satin bonnet or scarf.

Conclusion

In conclusion, growing short dreads can be a fun and rewarding experience. By choosing the right method, maintaining your hair properly, and being patient, you can grow a head full of beautiful and unique short dreads. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find the look that works best for you.
