Short Hair For Round Faces Over 40 A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Mar 21, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you are over 40 and have a round face, then finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. Short hair can be a great option as it can make your face look slimmer and more youthful. In this guide, we will share with you some tips and tricks on how to choose the best short hair for round faces over 40. We'll also provide some step-by-step instructions on how to style your hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.

Description

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. Women with round faces often struggle to find hairstyles that flatter their features. Short hair can be a great option as it can balance out the proportions of your face and make it look slimmer. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and features.

How To Choose The Right Short Haircut

Choosing the right short haircut can make all the difference in how you look and feel. Here are some tips to help you choose the right short haircut for your round face:

Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can play a big role in determining the right haircut for you. If you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a layered cut to help reduce the bulk. If you have fine hair, a blunt cut can help create the illusion of volume.

Think About Your Face Shape

While you have a round face, you may also have other features that can influence the haircut you choose. For example, if you have a short neck or a double chin, you may want to avoid hairstyles that are too short or too tight around the neck.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Short hair can be low-maintenance and easy to style, but it's important to choose a haircut that fits your lifestyle. If you are always on the go, you may want to opt for a wash-and-go style. If you have more time to spend on your hair, you may want to consider a more intricate style.

Step-By-Step Guide To Styling Short Hair

Once you have chosen the right short haircut for your face shape and features, it's time to style it. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you style your short hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it gently.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your damp hair. This can be a mousse, gel, or cream depending on the style you want to achieve.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and direct the airflow downwards.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a styling tool such as a curling iron or straightener to create the desired shape. You can also use a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips For Maintaining Short Hair

Short hair can be low-maintenance, but it still requires some care to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining short hair:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your haircut looking fresh, it's important to get regular trims every 6-8 weeks. This will help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Use A Heat Protectant

If you use a styling tool such as a curling iron or straightener, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Protect Your Hair From The Sun

The sun can be damaging to your hair, so make sure to protect it by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

FAQ

What Short Haircuts Are Best For Round Faces Over 40?

The best short haircuts for round faces over 40 are those that balance out the proportions of your face. A layered bob or pixie cut can be a great option.

Can I Style Short Hair In Different Ways?

Yes, short hair can be styled in many different ways. You can add curls or waves for a more romantic look, or keep it straight and sleek for a more modern look.

How Often Should I Wash My Short Hair?

How often you should wash your short hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently. If you have dry hair, you may be able to wash it less often.

Hair Care Products For Short Hair

Here are some hair care products that can help you maintain and style your short hair:

Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients.

Styling Product

Choose a styling product that is suitable for your hair type and the style you want to achieve. This can be a mousse, gel, cream, or spray.

Hairspray

Use a hairspray to hold your style in place and prevent frizz.

Short hair can be a great option for round faces over 40. By choosing the right haircut and taking care of your hair, you can look and feel your best. We hope this guide has been helpful in giving you some tips and tricks on how to choose the perfect short hairstyle for you.
