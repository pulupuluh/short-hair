Best Hair Length For Round Face Shape Finding The Perfect Balance

Written by Petter Mar 04, 2023 Â· 7 min read

When it comes to finding the best hair length for a round face shape, it's all about creating balance. You want to make sure your hair compliments your face shape, while also highlighting your best features. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect hair length for your round face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hair length for a round face shape, it's important to determine if you actually have a round face shape. Here are the steps:

  1. Pull your hair back into a ponytail or bun.
  2. Stand in front of a mirror and use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you created. Is it circular? Oval? Square? Heart-shaped?

If your face shape is circular, then you have a round face shape. Now, let's move on to finding the best hair length for you.

Best Hair Length for Round Face Shape

When it comes to finding the best hair length for a round face shape, the goal is to elongate the face and create the illusion of length. Here are our top picks:

Long Hair

Long hair is a great option for round face shapes because it helps to lengthen the face. The key is to add some layers to the hair to create movement and texture. This will help to break up the roundness of the face and create a more flattering look.

Shoulder-Length Hair

Shoulder-length hair is another great option for round face shapes. This length hits at the perfect spot to create a slimming effect on the face. To add some dimension, consider getting some face-framing layers or side-swept bangs.

Short Hair

Contrary to popular belief, short hair can actually work well on round face shapes. The key is to keep the hair cut close to the head and add some texture to create the illusion of height. A pixie cut or a bob with choppy layers can work wonders for a round face shape.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've found the best hair length for your round face shape, it's important to style it in a way that flatters your features. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make the face appear shorter.
  • Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or layers around the face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, as this can emphasize the roundness.
  • Instead, try hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and depth.

FAQ

Can I still have a bob if I have a round face shape?

Yes! A bob can actually work well on a round face shape if it's cut at the right length. Aim for a chin-length bob with face-framing layers.

What about a middle part?

A middle part can work on a round face shape, but it's important to add some volume to the crown of the head to avoid making the face appear shorter.

Is it okay to have straight hair?

Absolutely! Straight hair can work well on a round face shape. Just make sure to add some layers to create movement and texture.

Hair Care Tips

No matter what hair length you choose, it's important to take care of your locks. Here are some tips:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Try not to wash your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hair ties that can cause breakage. Opt for scrunchies or hair coils instead.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Finding the best hair length for a round face shape can take some trial and error, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be well on your way to finding the perfect balance.
