How To Do A Slicked Back Low Ponytail A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Apr 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old ponytail? Do you want to try something different and trendy? Look no further than the slicked back low ponytail. This sleek and sophisticated hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, from a day at the office to a night out on the town. Follow this step-by-step guide to achieve the perfect slicked back low ponytail.

Description:

The slicked back low ponytail is a sleek and modern hairstyle that involves pulling the hair back into a low ponytail at the nape of the neck. The hair is then slicked back and smoothed down for a polished look. This style works best on medium to long hair and can be achieved with or without bangs.

How to:

Here's how to achieve the perfect slicked back low ponytail:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair as you normally would. Towel dry your hair and apply a small amount of hair serum or oil to smooth and tame any frizz or flyaways.

Step 2: Part your hair

Using a comb, create a deep side part on the side of your head. This will add some volume and dimension to your hair.

Step 3: Brush your hair back

Using a boar bristle brush, brush your hair back towards the nape of your neck. Make sure to smooth down any bumps or flyaways as you brush.

Step 4: Secure your hair

Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail. Make sure the ponytail is tight and smooth.

Step 5: Add some shine

To add some extra shine and polish to your ponytail, apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to the length of the ponytail. This will give your hair a sleek and glossy finish.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

To keep your hair in place all day or night, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold your hair in place and prevent any flyaways.

Tips:

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing a slicked back low ponytail:

  • Use a boar bristle brush to smooth down any bumps or flyaways.
  • Apply a small amount of hair serum or oil to tame any frizz or flyaways.
  • Make sure the ponytail is tight and smooth for a polished look.
  • Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hair in place.

FAQ:

Q: Can I do a slicked back low ponytail with bangs?

A: Yes, you can do a slicked back low ponytail with or without bangs. If you have bangs, simply leave them down or sweep them to the side for a chic look.

Q: Does my hair need to be straight to do a slicked back low ponytail?

A: No, your hair doesn't need to be straight to do a slicked back low ponytail. This style works well on all hair types, including curly and wavy hair.

Hair Care:

To maintain healthy and shiny hair, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily achieve a slicked back low ponytail for a polished and sophisticated look. Whether you're heading to work or out for a night on the town, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
