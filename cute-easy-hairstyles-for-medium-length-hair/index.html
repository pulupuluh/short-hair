Cute And Easy Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Bardi Jun 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have medium-length hair, you're in luck! There are plenty of cute and easy hairstyles that you can try out. Whether you're going for a casual look or something more formal, there's a hairstyle that will work for you. In this post, we'll go over some of the best cute and easy hairstyles for medium length hair, as well as tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Medium length hair is usually defined as hair that falls between the shoulders and the collarbone. This length is perfect for a variety of hairstyles, from updos to braids to messy buns. The great thing about medium length hair is that it's long enough to be versatile, but not so long that it's difficult to manage. With the right styling techniques, you can create a variety of cute and easy hairstyles that are perfect for any occasion.

How to

The key to creating cute and easy hairstyles for medium length hair is to start with clean, dry hair. You can add some texture and volume by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. From there, you can experiment with different styling techniques, such as braids, twists, and buns. You can also add accessories like headbands, clips, and barrettes to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.

Braids

Braids are a great way to add some interest to your hairstyle. You can do a simple three-strand braid or get more creative with fishtail braids or Dutch braids. Braids are also a great way to keep your hair out of your face on a hot day or during a workout.

Twists

Twists are another easy hairstyle that you can do with medium length hair. You can do a simple twist or get more creative by doing a twist and pin or a twisted ponytail. Twists are a great way to add some texture to your hair without having to use heat styling tools.

Buns

Buns are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. You can do a messy bun or a sleek bun, depending on the occasion. Buns are also a great way to keep your hair out of your face and off your neck on a hot day.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating some cute and easy hairstyles for medium length hair:

Three-Strand Braid

  1. Start by dividing your hair into three equal sections.
  2. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  3. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  4. Repeat steps 2-3 until you reach the end of your hair.
  5. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.

Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out some pieces of hair around your face for a more relaxed look.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  1. Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  2. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  3. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  4. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

What are some cute and easy hairstyles for medium length hair?

Some cute and easy hairstyles for medium length hair include braids, twists, and buns. You can also add accessories like headbands and clips to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.

How can I maintain healthy hair?

You can maintain healthy hair by washing it every 2-3 days, using a heat protectant spray, trimming it every 6-8 weeks, and using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for creating cute and easy hairstyles for medium length hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  1. Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  2. Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with natural bristles to detangle your hair.
  4. Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye.

With these tips and techniques, you can create a variety of cute and easy hairstyles for medium length hair. Whether you're going for a casual look or something more formal, there's a hairstyle that will work for you. So experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
