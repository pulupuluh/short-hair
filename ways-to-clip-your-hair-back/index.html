Ways To Clip Your Hair Back Relaxed And Easy

Written by Daniel Jul 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

There are many ways to clip your hair back that are easy, relaxed, and perfect for any occasion. Whether you're looking for a quick and simple hairstyle for running errands or a more elegant look for a night out, there are plenty of options to choose from. In this article, we'll discuss some of the best ways to clip your hair back, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

Clip Your Hair Back with a Claw Clip

A claw clip is a great way to clip your hair back because it's easy to use and can hold a lot of hair in place. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and make sure it's smooth.
  2. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and pull it back.
  3. Twist the hair and clip it back with the claw clip. You can leave some hair loose at the front to frame your face.
  4. Repeat on the other side if you want a symmetrical look.
  5. You can also use a claw clip to create a messy bun. Just gather your hair in a ponytail, twist it, and clip it back with the claw clip.

Create a Half-Up Hairstyle with a Clip

A half-up hairstyle is a classic look that's perfect for any occasion. Here's how to create it with a clip:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and make sure it's smooth.
  2. Take a section of hair from the top of your head and pull it back.
  3. Twist the hair and clip it back with a clip. You can use a bobby pin or a barrette, depending on your preference.
  4. You can leave the rest of your hair down or create a simple bun or ponytail with the remaining hair.

Use Bobby Pins to Create a Sleek Look

Bobby pins are a great tool for creating a sleek and polished hairstyle. Here's how to use them:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and make sure it's smooth.
  2. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and pull it back.
  3. Secure the hair with a bobby pin by sliding it in vertically and then flipping it horizontally to create an X shape.
  4. Repeat on the other side if you want a symmetrical look.
  5. You can also use bobby pins to create a braided hairstyle. Just braid your hair and secure the ends with bobby pins.

Accessorize Your Hair with a Headband

A headband is a fun and stylish way to clip your hair back. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and make sure it's smooth.
  2. Place the headband on your head, pushing it back to your desired position.
  3. Take a section of hair from the front of your head and tuck it behind the headband.
  4. Repeat on the other side if you want a symmetrical look.
  5. You can also use a headband to create a messy bun or a half-up hairstyle.

FAQ

What type of clip is best for thick hair?

A claw clip or a large barrette is best for thick hair because it can hold a lot of hair in place.

How do I prevent my hair from slipping out of the clip?

Spray a bit of hairspray on the section of hair before clipping it back to help it stay in place.

Can I use a clip on wet hair?

No, it's best to avoid using clips on wet hair because it can cause breakage and damage. Wait until your hair is dry before styling it.

Hair Care Tips

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and nutrients to support healthy hair growth.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these easy and relaxed ways to clip your hair back, you can create a stylish and polished look in no time. Experiment with different clips, pins, and headbands to find the perfect style for you. And don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong!
