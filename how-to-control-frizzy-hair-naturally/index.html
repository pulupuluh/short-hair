How To Control Frizzy Hair Naturally Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Apr 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Frizzy hair can be a nuisance, especially on humid days. It can make your hair look unkempt and can be difficult to manage. While there are many products available in the market that claim to control frizz, they can be expensive and may not always work. In this blog post, we will share some natural ways to control frizzy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Control Frizzy Hair Naturally
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Control Frizzy Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips to Prevent Frizz

How to Control Frizzy Hair Naturally

The following are some natural ways to control frizzy hair:

1. Use Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is a natural conditioner that can help tame frizzy hair. Apply a small amount of coconut oil to your hair and scalp, leave it on for 30 minutes, and then wash it off with a mild shampoo.

2. Apply Aloe Vera Gel

Aloe vera gel can help reduce frizz by moisturizing your hair. Apply a small amount of aloe vera gel to your hair before styling it to keep it smooth and shiny.

3. Use Apple Cider Vinegar

Apple cider vinegar can help balance the pH level of your hair, which can reduce frizz. Mix one part apple cider vinegar with two parts water and use it as a final rinse after shampooing your hair.

4. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush can help reduce frizz. Brushing your hair can create static electricity, which can make your hair frizzy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to controlling frizzy hair naturally:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water, which can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.

Step 2: Apply a Natural Conditioner

Apply a natural conditioner, such as coconut oil or aloe vera gel, to your hair and scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes and then rinse it off with water.

Step 3: Rinse with Apple Cider Vinegar

Mix one part apple cider vinegar with two parts water and use it as a final rinse after shampooing your hair.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb your hair. Avoid using a brush, which can create static electricity and make your hair frizzy.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired using natural styling products, such as aloe vera gel or coconut oil.

Tips to Control Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips to control frizzy hair:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and frizzy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What causes frizzy hair?

Frizzy hair is caused by a lack of moisture in the hair, which can be due to a variety of factors, such as genetics, weather, and styling habits.

Can frizzy hair be cured?

While there is no cure for frizzy hair, it can be managed with proper hair care and styling techniques.

Is it bad to brush frizzy hair?

Brushing frizzy hair can create static electricity, which can make your hair even more frizzy. It is better to use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.

Hair Care Tips to Prevent Frizz

Here are some additional hair care tips to prevent frizz:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent drying out your hair.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as it can cause frizz. Instead, gently pat your hair dry with a microfiber towel.
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal clasps, as they can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain silicone, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

By following these tips and tricks, you can control frizzy hair naturally and keep your hair looking smooth and shiny.
