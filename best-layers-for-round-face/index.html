Best Layers For Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Jul 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. The right cut can enhance your features and make you look stunning. One of the best ways to achieve this is by adding layers to your hair. Layers can add dimension, texture, and movement to your hair, making it look more voluminous and flattering. In this article, we'll discuss the best layers for round face and how to achieve them.

Table of Content

  • How to Get the Best Layers for a Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Adding Layers to Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Layered Haircut
  • FAQs about Layers for Round Face
  • Hair Care Tips for Layered Hair

How to Get the Best Layers for a Round Face

The key to getting the best layers for a round face is to add volume and texture to your hair. This will create an illusion of length and make your face look slimmer. Here are some things to keep in mind when getting layers for a round face:

  • Choose the right length: Layers that are too short can make your face look wider. Opt for layers that start below your chin or at the collarbone.
  • Consider the thickness of your hair: If you have thick hair, you can add more layers to create volume. If your hair is thin, too many layers can make it look limp and lifeless.
  • Get a side-swept fringe: A side-swept fringe can help to elongate your face and create a more flattering shape.
  • Avoid blunt cuts: Blunt cuts can make your face look rounder. Opt for layered cuts with soft, feathered edges.

Step-by-Step Guide to Adding Layers to Your Hair

If you want to add layers to your hair, you can do so at home or at the salon. Here's a step-by-step guide to adding layers to your hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Take a small section of hair and twist it. This will create a guide for your layers.
  4. Using scissors, cut the twisted section of hair at an angle. The shorter you cut, the more layers you'll create.
  5. Continue twisting and cutting sections of hair until you achieve your desired look.
  6. Blend the layers by combing through your hair with a wide-toothed comb.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Layered Haircut

Layered haircuts require some maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your layered haircut:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your layers.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add more body to your hair.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.
  • Consider using hair masks or oils to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQs about Layers for Round Face

1. What type of layers should I get for a round face?

Opt for layers that start below your chin or at the collarbone. Soft, feathered edges are more flattering than blunt cuts.

2. Can I add layers to my short hair?

Yes, you can add layers to short hair. However, keep in mind that layers that are too short can make your face look wider.

3. How do I know if layers will suit my face shape?

Consult with a hairstylist to determine if layers will suit your face shape. They can recommend the best length and style for your features.

Hair Care Tips for Layered Hair

Layered hair requires some extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for layered hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your layers.

Adding layers to your hair is an excellent way to enhance your features and create a more flattering look. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this article, you can achieve the best layers for a round face. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your features, and remember to maintain your layered haircut with regular trims and proper hair care.
