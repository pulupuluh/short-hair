How To Do A Men s High Fade Haircut Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline May 07, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for men? Well, a high fade haircut is the perfect choice for you. The high fade is a popular haircut that blends the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top. In this article, we will guide you on how to do a men's high fade haircut in a step-by-step guide.

Description

A high fade is a type of haircut that has a defined line where the hair starts to fade from the top of the head down to the sides and back. The hair on the sides and back is cut shorter, while the hair on top is left longer. A high fade haircut is a stylish and modern haircut that is perfect for men who want to look sharp and edgy.

How to Do a Men's High Fade Haircut: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather the Required Tools

The first step in doing a men's high fade haircut is to gather the required tools. You will need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Make sure that your clippers have different guard sizes that can be adjusted to different lengths.

Step 2: Trim the Top

The second step is to trim the hair on top of the head. Use your scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Make sure that the hair on top is longer than the hair on the sides and back.

Step 3: Create the Fade

The third step is to create the fade by using your clippers. Start with the longest guard size and work your way down to the shortest. Begin at the temple and work your way around the head. Make sure that you blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top.

Step 4: Clean Up the Edges

The fourth step is to clean up the edges. Use your clippers to trim around the ears and the neckline. Make sure that you create a clean and defined line.

Step 5: Style the Hair

The final step is to style the hair. Use a pomade or a wax to create the desired look. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume to the hair on top.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high fade haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to the shortest.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Clean up the edges to create a defined line.
  • Use a pomade or a wax to style the hair on top.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's high fade haircut:

Q: How long does a high fade haircut last?

A: A high fade haircut can last anywhere from 2 to 4 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Q: Can I do a high fade haircut at home?

A: Yes, you can do a high fade haircut at home if you have the required tools and skills.

Q: What is the difference between a high fade and a low fade?

A: A high fade starts at the top of the head and fades down to the sides and back, while a low fade starts at the bottom and fades up to the sides and back.

Hair Care

After getting a high fade haircut, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its style and health. Here are some hair care tips for men:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and style.

With this step-by-step guide, you can easily do a men's high fade haircut at home. Remember to gather the required tools, follow the steps, and style your hair to achieve the desired look. Don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain its style and health.
