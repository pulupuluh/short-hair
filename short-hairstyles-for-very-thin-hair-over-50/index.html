Short Hairstyles For Very Thin Hair Over 50

Written by Christine Jun 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our hair can become thinner and harder to manage. But don't worry, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can help you look stylish and beautiful. In this article, we'll explore some of the best short hairstyles for very thin hair over 50. Whether you're looking for a pixie cut, a bob, or something in between, we've got you covered.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Very Thin Hair Over 50

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, it's important to understand what to look for when choosing a short hairstyle for very thin hair over 50. First and foremost, you want a style that will add volume and texture to your hair. This can be achieved through layers, curls, or waves. You also want a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural features.

Step by Step: Short Hairstyles for Very Thin Hair Over 50

1. The Pixie Cut:

The pixie cut is a timeless classic that works beautifully for very thin hair. It's short, low maintenance, and adds texture and volume to your hair. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a short cut with lots of layers and texture.

2. The Bob:

A bob is another great option for very thin hair. It's versatile, flattering, and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style. To add volume and texture, opt for a layered bob with choppy ends.

3. The Shag:

The shag is a fun and playful hairstyle that works well for very thin hair. It's a layered cut with lots of texture and movement. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for layers throughout your hair, with shorter layers around your face.

Tips for Styling Short Hair for Very Thin Hair Over 50

1. Use volumizing products:

To add volume and texture to your hair, use volumizing products like mousse or root lifter. Apply these products to your roots, then blow dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.

2. Embrace your natural texture:

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, embrace it! These textures can add volume and movement to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker. Use a curling iron or wand to enhance your natural curls or waves.

3. Experiment with accessories:

Accessories like headbands, scarves, and hair clips can add interest and style to your short hair. Try different accessories to see what works best for you.

FAQ: Short Hairstyles for Very Thin Hair Over 50

Q: Will a short hairstyle make my thin hair look even thinner?

A: Not necessarily. The right short hairstyle can actually make your hair look fuller and thicker. Talk to your stylist about the best options for your hair type and face shape.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on your hair growth and the style you choose. Generally, you should get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care for Very Thin Hair Over 50

1. Use gentle products:

Choose shampoos and conditioners that are gentle and nourishing. Avoid products with harsh chemicals or sulfates, which can damage your hair and make it look even thinner.

2. Protect your hair:

Avoid excessive heat styling, which can damage your hair and make it look thinner. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

3. Eat a healthy diet:

A balanced diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals can help keep your hair healthy and strong. Make sure you're getting enough nutrients to support healthy hair growth.

4. Stay hydrated:

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Aim for at least 8 glasses of water per day.

With these tips and short hairstyles for very thin hair over 50, you can look and feel your best at any age. Remember to choose a style that flatters your face shape, enhances your natural features, and adds volume and texture to your hair.
