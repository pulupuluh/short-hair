Men s Hair Long Top Short Sides A Stylish And Trendy Look

Written by Petter Jul 02, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and trendy hairstyle, then the men's hair long top short sides look may be just what you need. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity over the years, and it's not hard to see why. With a longer top and shorter sides, this hairstyle is the perfect blend of classic and modern style. In this article, we'll take a closer look at this hairstyle, how to achieve it, some tips, and hair care advice to keep your locks looking their best.

Description

The men's hair long top short sides style is characterized by having longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. This look is achieved by cutting the sides and back of the hair short while leaving the top longer. The length of the top can vary depending on personal preference, but it's usually around 4-6 inches long. This hairstyle works well with a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve the men's hair long top short sides look, you'll need to visit a barber or hairstylist. The first step is to decide on the length you want for the top of your hair. You can bring in a picture for reference or ask your stylist for their recommendation. Next, the sides and back of your hair will be trimmed short. The stylist may use clippers or scissors depending on the desired length. Finally, the top of your hair will be styled and trimmed to your desired length and shape.

Step by Step Guide

If you're looking to achieve this hairstyle at home, here's a step by step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner to make sure it's clean and manageable.
  2. Use a comb to part your hair on one side.
  3. Use clippers or scissors to cut the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one until you achieve the desired length.
  4. Blow-dry the top of your hair, using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  5. Use scissors to trim the top of your hair to your desired length and shape. Make sure to blend the top with the sides and back for a natural look.
  6. Apply a small amount of styling product to the top of your hair to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting the men's hair long top short sides look:

  • Bring in a picture for reference to show your stylist what you're looking for.
  • Make sure to communicate with your stylist about your preferences and concerns.
  • Consider your face shape and hair type when deciding on the length and shape of the top of your hair.
  • Use a high-quality styling product to keep your hair in place and maintain its shape.
  • Visit your stylist regularly to maintain the length and shape of your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's hair long top short sides look:

Is this hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle works well with a variety of face shapes, including oval, round, square, and heart-shaped faces. Your stylist can help you determine the best length and shape for your face.

Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

Yes, you can experiment with different styling products and techniques to create different looks for different occasions.

How often should I visit my stylist for maintenance?

You should visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your men's hair long top short sides hairstyle looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like straighteners and curling irons to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a high-quality styling product to keep your hair in place without damaging it.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Overall, the men's hair long top short sides look is a stylish and trendy hairstyle that can work well for a variety of face shapes and hair types. With the right maintenance and care, you can keep your locks looking their best and turn heads wherever you go.
