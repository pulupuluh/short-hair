Do Bobs Suit Square Faces Tips And Tricks For The Perfect Bob

Written by Robby Jul 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you wondering whether bobs suit square faces? If you have a square face shape, you might think that a bob haircut wouldn't work for you. However, that's not necessarily true. With the right cut and styling, you can rock a bob and flatter your features. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose the right bob for a square face shape, how to style it, and how to take care of it. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Choose the Right Bob
  • Style Your Bob
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Choose the Right Bob

The first step in getting a bob that suits a square face shape is choosing the right cut. The most flattering bobs for square faces are those that soften the angles of the jawline and cheekbones. Here are a few options to consider:

Long Bob (Lob)

A long bob, or lob, is a great choice for square faces because it hits just above or below the shoulders, which helps to elongate the face. Additionally, a lob can be styled with waves or curls to add volume and texture, which can also help to soften the angles of the face.

Layered Bob

A layered bob is another good option for square faces because it adds movement and texture to the hair. By layering the hair, the angles of the face are softened, which can make the face appear less harsh. Additionally, a layered bob can be styled with waves or curls to add volume and texture.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob can also be a good choice for square faces because it adds angles and movement to the hair. By having one side longer than the other, the angles of the face are balanced out, which can make the face appear softer. Additionally, an asymmetrical bob can be styled with waves or curls to add volume and texture.

Style Your Bob

Once you've chosen the right bob for your square face shape, it's time to style it. Here are a few tips for styling your bob:

  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and texture.
  • Add waves or curls to your hair to soften the angles of your face.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add movement and texture.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few more tips and tricks for getting the perfect bob for your square face shape:

  • Consider getting a consultation with a stylist to discuss the best bob for your face shape.
  • Bring pictures of bobs that you like to your stylist to give them an idea of what you're looking for.
  • Be open to trying different styles and cuts until you find the perfect bob for you.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about bobs and square face shapes:

Can a bob make a square face look even more square?

If you choose the wrong bob for your face shape, it can make your face look even more square. However, if you choose the right bob for your face shape, it can soften the angles of your face and make it appear less harsh.

What is the best way to style a bob?

The best way to style a bob depends on your hair type and personal preferences. However, using a round brush when blow-drying your hair, adding waves or curls, and using a texturizing spray can all help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your bob looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Here are a few tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your bob looking fresh.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a bob and flatter your square face shape. Remember to choose the right cut, style it properly, and take care of it to keep it looking its best. Happy styling!
