How To Get More Volume In Thin Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have thin hair, you know how frustrating it can be to try and get volume that lasts all day. But don't worry, there are ways to add volume to your hair without damaging it. In this article, we'll be discussing how to get more volume in thin hair, including tips, tricks, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get More Volume in Thin Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Adding Volume to Thin Hair

How to Get More Volume in Thin Hair

There are several ways to add volume to thin hair:

  • Blow-drying
  • Teasing
  • Using volumizing products
  • Getting a haircut
  • Changing your part

Each of these methods can be effective, but it's important to find what works best for your hair type and routine.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with Clean Hair

Before adding volume, make sure your hair is clean and free of any buildup. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any excess oil or product buildup.

2. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for adding volume to thin hair. These products will help create lift and texture.

3. Apply a Volumizing Product

Apply a volumizing product to your roots, such as a mousse or root-lifting spray. These products will help create lift and texture at the root.

4. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair in sections. Lift each section of hair at the root and blow-dry in an upward motion, creating lift and volume.

5. Tease Your Hair

To add even more volume, tease your hair at the crown. Use a fine-tooth comb to backcomb your hair at the root, then smooth the top layer of hair over the teased section.

Tips for Adding Volume to Thin Hair

  • Avoid heavy products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Use dry shampoo to add texture and lift to your hair between washes.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a high ponytail or bun to create volume while you sleep.
  • Consider getting a hair extension to add volume and length to your hair.

FAQs

Can I add volume to my hair without damaging it?

Yes, you can add volume to your hair without damaging it. Use a heat protectant before blow-drying, and avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.

What is the best haircut for thin hair?

A layered haircut is the best option for thin hair, as it adds volume and texture.

Can I use volumizing products on oily hair?

Yes, you can use volumizing products on oily hair. Just make sure to use a clarifying shampoo to remove any excess oil before applying the product.

Hair Care Advice for Thin Hair

  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as it's less damaging than a brush.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles, as they can pull on your hair and cause breakage.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can add volume to your thin hair and achieve a fuller, more voluminous look. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid damaging it with excessive heat or styling.
