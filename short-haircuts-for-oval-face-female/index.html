Short Haircuts For Oval Face Female A Guide To The Best Styles

Written by Frank Sep 12, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have an oval face, congratulations! You have one of the most versatile face shapes when it comes to hairstyles. From pixie cuts to long bobs, there are so many short haircuts for oval face females to choose from. In this guide, we’ll share some of the best styles, as well as tips for maintaining your new ‘do.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Oval Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Short Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut
  • FAQs About Short Haircuts for Oval Face Females
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Oval Face

When it comes to choosing a short haircut for oval face females, there are a few things to keep in mind:

Consider Your Hair Texture

If you have fine hair, a blunt bob may not be the best choice for you. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, a layered pixie cut might be too difficult to maintain. Talk to your stylist about what will work best for your hair texture.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Are you low-maintenance when it comes to styling your hair? Then a wash-and-go pixie cut might be perfect for you. If you enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles, a long bob with layers might be a better fit.

Consider Your Face Shape

While oval face shapes can pull off almost any haircut, there are certain styles that will be especially flattering. For example, a short bob that ends just above your chin will accentuate your jawline and cheekbones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Short Haircut

Once you’ve chosen your perfect short haircut for oval face females, it’s time to learn how to style it. Here’s a simple step-by-step guide:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry it until it’s damp.

2. Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, focusing on the roots. Use a mousse or gel for added volume, or a serum for a sleek look.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, pulling the brush through your hair as you go.

4. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and shape. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Short haircuts for oval face females can be low-maintenance, but they still require some upkeep. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

Get Regular Trims

To keep your haircut looking fresh, get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

If you use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Avoid using hot tools every day, and let your hair air dry whenever possible.

Experiment with Accessories

Short haircuts for oval face females can be accessorized with headbands, scarves, and hair clips. Try different styles to add some variety to your look.

FAQs About Short Haircuts for Oval Face Females

What is the best short haircut for oval face females?

The best short haircut for oval face females depends on your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. Talk to your stylist about what will work best for you.

Can oval face females pull off a pixie cut?

Yes! Oval face shapes can pull off almost any haircut, including a pixie cut. Talk to your stylist about what length and style will be most flattering for your face shape.

How can I add volume to my short haircut?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a mousse or gel to your roots before blow drying. You can also use a round brush to add volume as you style your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require some special care to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that’s specifically designed for your hair type. If you have color-treated hair, use a color-safe shampoo to prevent fading.

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and damage. Try washing your hair every other day, or use a dry shampoo in between washes.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair, causing it to become brittle and prone to breakage. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when you’re spending time outdoors.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect short haircut for oval face females and maintain it with ease. Talk to your stylist about what will work best for you, and enjoy your new look!
