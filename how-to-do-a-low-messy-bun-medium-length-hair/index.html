How To Do A Low Messy Bun For Medium Length Hair

Are you tired of the same old hairstyles and want to try something new? A low messy bun is the perfect hairstyle for any occasion. It's easy to do and looks effortless yet chic. In this article, we will guide you on how to do a low messy bun for medium length hair.

How to Do a Low Messy Bun

A low messy bun is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn on both casual and formal occasions. You can achieve this look with minimal effort and styling products. The key to creating a perfect low messy bun is to keep it loose and natural-looking. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Start with clean and dry hair

Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and let it air dry completely. Avoid using a hair dryer as it can damage your hair and make it frizzy. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you can apply a styling cream or mousse to enhance your curls.

Step 2: Brush your hair

Brush your hair with a wide-tooth comb or a paddle brush to remove any tangles. Make sure that your hair is smooth and free of knots.

Step 3: Create a low ponytail

Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and create a low ponytail. Secure it with a hair elastic. Make sure that the ponytail is loose and not too tight.

Step 4: Twist and wrap the ponytail

Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair elastic. Secure the bun with bobby pins. Leave some strands of hair loose around your face to create a soft and romantic look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to do a low messy bun:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Create a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  4. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the hair elastic.
  5. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  6. Leave some strands of hair loose around your face.
  7. Spray some hairspray to hold the bun in place.

Tips for the Perfect Low Messy Bun

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect low messy bun:

  • Use a hair elastic that matches your hair color.
  • Leave some strands of hair loose around your face for a soft and romantic look.
  • Don't make the bun too tight as it can cause headaches and damage your hair.
  • Pull out a few strands of hair from the bun to create a messy look.
  • Spray some hairspray to hold the bun in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of hair is suitable for a low messy bun?

A low messy bun is suitable for medium length hair. It works best on hair that is not too thick or too thin.

Can I use a hair donut to create a low messy bun?

Yes, you can use a hair donut to create a low messy bun. It will give your bun a fuller and more voluminous look.

How do I keep my low messy bun from falling out?

You can keep your low messy bun from falling out by using bobby pins and hairspray. Make sure that the bobby pins are securely fastened, and spray some hairspray to hold the bun in place.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using a hair dryer as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a paddle brush to remove any tangles.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to get rid of split ends.

Now that you know how to do a low messy bun for medium length hair, you can experiment with different variations and styles. Whether you're going to a wedding or a casual brunch, this hairstyle is perfect for any occasion. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips to achieve the perfect low messy bun.
