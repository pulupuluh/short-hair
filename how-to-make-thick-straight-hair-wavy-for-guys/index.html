How To Make Thick Straight Hair Wavy For Guys

Written by Robby Feb 26, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you have thick straight hair and want to switch things up with some waves? You're in luck because adding waves to your hair is easier than you think. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of making your thick straight hair wavy using simple techniques and products. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Thick Straight Hair Wavy for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Your Hair Wavy
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to make your thick straight hair wavy:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt and oil build-up from your hair, making it easier to work with.


Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to gently dry your hair. Make sure that your hair is damp but not soaking wet.


Step 3: Apply a Wave Spray or Mousse

Apply a wave spray or mousse to your hair. This will help to create texture and hold, making it easier to create waves.


Step 4: Comb Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to comb your hair. This will help to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.


Step 5: Twist Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections and twist each section tightly. Use a hairband to secure the twisted hair in place.


Step 6: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. Hold the diffuser up to your head and move it around in a circular motion to create waves.


Step 7: Release Your Hair

Remove the hairbands and gently shake out your hair. Use your fingers to separate the waves and create a natural look.

Tips for Making Your Hair Wavy

Here are some additional tips to help you make your thick straight hair wavy:

  • Start with clean, damp hair
  • Use a wave spray or mousse to create texture and hold
  • Twist your hair tightly to create defined waves
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to create natural-looking waves
  • Don't overdo it with the product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making thick straight hair wavy:

Can I make my hair wavy without using heat?

Yes, you can make your hair wavy without using heat by braiding your damp hair and leaving it in overnight. When you take out the braids in the morning, you'll have natural-looking waves.


How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or exercise frequently, you may need to wash your hair every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.


Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you take care of your wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and tangle-free
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this will prevent breakage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Now that you know how to make thick straight hair wavy for guys, it's time to try it out for yourself. With the right technique and products, you can create natural-looking waves that will give your hair a fresh new look.
