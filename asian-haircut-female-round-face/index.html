Asian Haircut Female Round Face Tips And Styles

Written by Christine Feb 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a round face shape and you're looking for a new haircut, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll be discussing the best Asian haircuts for females with a round face shape. We'll also be giving you some tips on how to style your hair and take care of it. Let's get started!

Description

A round face shape is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded jawline, and a wide forehead. If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a haircut that elongates your face and makes it appear slimmer. Asian haircuts are a great option for round faces because they often feature layers and angles that create the illusion of length.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a round face, you'll want to look for styles with long layers or angles that create the illusion of length. A long bob or lob is a great option for a round face because it elongates the neck and creates a slimming effect. You can also opt for a side-swept fringe or layers that start at the chin to draw the eye downwards.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting an Asian haircut for a round face:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist and show them pictures of the style you want.
  2. Ask for long layers or angles that create the illusion of length.
  3. Consider adding a side-swept fringe or layers that start at the chin.
  4. Ask your hairstylist to style your hair in a way that elongates your face.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling your Asian haircut:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume at the roots.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume.
  • Use a straightening iron to add texture and waves.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and prevent frizz.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about Asian haircuts for round faces:

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that elongates the face and creates the illusion of length. Long layers, angles, and a side-swept fringe are all great options.

Can I wear a short haircut if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear a short haircut if you have a round face. However, you'll want to look for styles with layers or angles that create the illusion of length. A pixie cut with long layers or an angled bob are both great options.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

You should avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or a bob that ends at your chin. You should also avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous or curly.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent over-drying.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and styles in mind, you'll be sure to find the perfect Asian haircut for your round face shape. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
