Best Round Brush For Thick Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 05, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have thick short hair, finding the right round brush can be a challenge. With so many options available, it can be difficult to know which one is the best for your hair type. In this article, we will explore the best round brush for thick short hair, how to use it, and tips for achieving the perfect hairstyle.

Why Use a Round Brush?

A round brush is an essential tool for anyone who wants to achieve a salon-quality blowout at home. The brush's shape helps to create volume, body, and shape in the hair, while also smoothing out any frizz or flyaways. Using a round brush also helps to distribute heat evenly, reducing the risk of heat damage to your hair.

What to Look for in a Round Brush for Thick Short Hair

When shopping for a round brush for thick short hair, there are a few key features to consider:

Bristles

The bristles on your round brush should be strong enough to grip your hair but gentle enough not to cause damage. Look for a brush with natural boar bristles, which are gentle on the hair and help to distribute natural oils throughout the strands.

Size

The size of the brush you choose will depend on the length of your hair. For short hair, a smaller brush with a diameter of 1-1.5 inches is best.

Shape

The shape of your round brush should be circular or slightly oval, with no flat sides. This will help you to create volume and body in your hair.

How to Use a Round Brush for Thick Short Hair

Using a round brush on thick short hair can be tricky, but with a little practice, you can achieve a beautiful blowout at home. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Blow-dry your hair until it is completely dry. If your hair is prone to frizz, apply a smoothing serum or cream before blow-drying.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using clips. Start with the bottom section and work your way up.

Step 3: Begin Brushing

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the brush, starting at the roots. Hold the brush in place for a few seconds, then slowly pull it down to the ends of the hair while blow-drying with a concentrator nozzle. Repeat until all sections are complete.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have finished blow-drying, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle with your round brush:

Tip 1: Use Heat Protectant

Always use a heat protectant before blow-drying to minimize heat damage to your hair.

Tip 2: Work in Small Sections

Working in small sections will make it easier to achieve a smooth, polished look.

Tip 3: Use the Right Hair Dryer

Invest in a high-quality hair dryer with a concentrator nozzle to help direct the airflow and achieve a smooth finish.

FAQ

What is the best round brush size for short hair?

For short hair, a brush with a diameter of 1-1.5 inches is best.

Can you use a round brush on thick hair?

Yes, a round brush is a great tool for thick hair. Look for a brush with strong bristles to grip your hair and help create volume.

Hair Care for Thick Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep your thick short hair healthy and looking its best:

Tip 1: Regular Trims

Regular trims every 6-8 weeks will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Tip 2: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

Tip 3: Avoid Over-Styling

Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools to prevent damage.

In conclusion, finding the right round brush for thick short hair can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-quality blowout at home. Remember to look for a brush with strong bristles and a circular shape, and always use a heat protectant before blow-drying. With these tips and tricks, your hair will be looking fabulous in no time!
