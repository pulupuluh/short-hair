How To Get Wavy Hair With A Curling Iron

Written by Bardi Aug 20, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to switch things up with some waves? Look no further than a curling iron! In this article, we’ll go over step-by-step instructions on how to get wavy hair with a curling iron, along with some helpful tips and FAQs to ensure your new style looks its best. So, grab your curling iron and let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Wavy Hair with a Curling Iron
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Helpful Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you begin, make sure you have a curling iron with a barrel size of 1-2 inches. This will give you the best results for creating loose waves. Also, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron.

1. Start by dividing your hair into sections. Clip the top half of your hair up and out of the way, leaving the bottom half loose.

2. Take a small section of hair (about 1 inch) and clamp the curling iron at the root. Make sure the clamp is facing downward.

3. Twist the curling iron away from your face and hold for 5-10 seconds. Release the curling iron and gently pull it down to release the curl.

4. Repeat step 3 with the rest of the bottom section of your hair.

5. Release the top half of your hair and repeat steps 2-4 until all of your hair is curled.

6. Once you’ve curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create a more natural, wavy look.

Helpful Tips

- If you want tighter waves, use a smaller barrel size on your curling iron.

- For looser waves, wrap larger sections of hair around the curling iron.

- Don’t hold the curling iron on your hair for too long, as this can cause damage and breakage.

- If you’re having trouble curling the back of your hair, use a handheld mirror to help guide you.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a curling wand instead of a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can use a curling wand to create waves as well. However, be careful not to burn yourself!

Q: Do I need to curl my hair in a certain direction?

A: No, you can curl your hair in whichever direction you prefer. However, for a more natural look, alternate the direction of your curls.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Now that you’ve achieved your desired waves, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.

- Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.

- Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle.

By following these simple steps and tips, you’ll be able to create beautiful, wavy hair with a curling iron. So, go ahead and give it a try – you might just fall in love with your new look!
