How To Get The Haircut You Want Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a guy looking to get a new haircut, you may be wondering how to get the haircut you want male. It can be difficult to communicate your desired style to your barber or stylist, but with the right approach and some effective communication strategies, you can achieve the look you want. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get the haircut you want male.

How to Communicate What You Want

The first step in getting the haircut you want is to communicate what you want to your barber or stylist. Here are some tips on how to effectively communicate your desired style:

Bring a Photo

One of the best ways to show your barber or stylist what you want is to bring a photo of the style you're after. This will help them understand exactly what you're looking for and make it easier for them to achieve the look.

Use Clear and Specific Language

When describing what you want, be as clear and specific as possible. Use descriptive words like "short," "long," "layered," or "textured" to help your barber or stylist understand what you're looking for.

Consider Your Face Shape

Before choosing a specific style, consider your face shape. Certain styles may be more flattering for certain face shapes, so it's important to take this into account when deciding what you want.

The Haircut Process

Once you've communicated what you want, it's time to sit back and let your barber or stylist work their magic. Here's what you can expect during the haircut process:

Washing and Drying

Before your haircut begins, your hair will be washed and dried. This helps to remove any product buildup or dirt and ensures that your hair is clean and ready for cutting.

Cutting

Your barber or stylist will then begin cutting your hair according to the style you've requested. They may use scissors or clippers, depending on the style you want.

Styling

Once your hair is cut, your barber or stylist will then style it. They may use products like gel or pomade to create the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut

After you've gotten your new haircut, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your new style:

Wash and Condition Regularly

Regular washing and conditioning will help keep your hair clean and healthy. Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make all the difference when it comes to styling your hair. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and desired style.

Get Regular Trims

To keep your haircut looking fresh, it's important to get regular trims. This will help prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your cut.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about how to get the haircut you want male:

How much should I tip my barber or stylist?

Tipping practices vary, but it's generally recommended to tip 15-20% of the cost of your haircut.

How do I know if a certain style will work for me?

Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing a style. Your barber or stylist can also offer advice on what styles will work best for you.

How often should I get a haircut?

This depends on your hair type and desired style. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

Conclusion

Getting the haircut you want male is all about effective communication and finding the right stylist. By following the tips in this guide, you can achieve the look you want and keep your hair looking great.
