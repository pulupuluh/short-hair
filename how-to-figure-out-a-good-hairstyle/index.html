How To Figure Out A Good Hairstyle Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby May 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your same old hairstyle? Do you want to switch things up but don't know where to start? Figuring out a good hairstyle can be overwhelming, but don't worry - we've got you covered. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to figure out a good hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Assessing Your Hair Texture
  • Considering Your Personal Style
  • Choosing a Haircut
  • Picking a Hair Color
  • Styling Your Hair
  • Taking Care of Your Hair
  • FAQs

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in figuring out a good hairstyle is determining your face shape. The four main face shapes are round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back into a ponytail and take a good look at your face in the mirror. You can also trace the outline of your face on a piece of paper to make it easier to see.

If you have a round face, go for hairstyles that add height and length to your face. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any hairstyle. If you have a square face, go for hairstyles that soften your jawline. If you have a heart-shaped face, go for hairstyles that add width to your forehead.

Assessing Your Hair Texture

The next step is to assess your hair texture. Is your hair straight, wavy, curly, or kinky? Knowing your hair texture will help you determine what hairstyles will work best for you. For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to go for a hairstyle that allows your curls to shine. If you have straight hair, you may want to go for a hairstyle that adds volume and texture.

Considering Your Personal Style

Your personal style is another important factor to consider when figuring out a good hairstyle. Are you someone who likes to keep things simple and low-maintenance? Or do you like to switch things up and experiment with different styles? Knowing your personal style will help you choose a hairstyle that you'll feel comfortable and confident in.

Choosing a Haircut

Once you've determined your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, it's time to choose a haircut. There are many different haircuts to choose from, including pixie cuts, bobs, shags, layers, and more. If you're unsure of what haircut to choose, consult with your hairstylist. They will be able to recommend a haircut that will work best for you.

Picking a Hair Color

If you're looking to switch up your hair color, there are many different options to choose from. You can go for a subtle change, such as adding highlights or lowlights, or you can go for a dramatic change, such as dyeing your hair a completely different color. When choosing a hair color, consider your skin tone and natural hair color.

Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen a haircut and hair color, it's time to start thinking about how you're going to style your hair. Different hairstyles require different styling techniques and products. If you're not sure how to style your hair, ask your hairstylist for tips and recommendations.

Taking Care of Your Hair

Finally, taking care of your hair is essential for maintaining a good hairstyle. Make sure to use high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type. Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and breakage. And don't forget to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.

FAQs

Q: How do I know if a certain hairstyle will look good on me?

A: The best way to know if a certain hairstyle will look good on you is to consult with your hairstylist. They will be able to recommend a hairstyle that will work best for your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Q: How often should I trim my hair?

A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

Q: What should I do if I don't like my new hairstyle?

A: If you don't like your new hairstyle, talk to your hairstylist. They may be able to make adjustments or suggest a different hairstyle that will work better for you.

Conclusion

Figuring out a good hairstyle can be a daunting task, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and take care of your hair to maintain your new hairstyle.
