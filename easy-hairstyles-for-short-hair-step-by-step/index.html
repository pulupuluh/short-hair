Easy Hairstyles For Short Hair Step By Step

Written by Robby Apr 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short hair can be challenging to style, but it does not have to be. With some creativity and a few hair accessories, you can transform your short hair into a stylish and chic look in minutes. In this article, we will share with you some easy hairstyles for short hair step by step.

Table of Content

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, here are some tips to help you achieve better results:

  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Invest in good hair accessories like bobby pins, clips, and hairbands.
  • Experiment with different hair products like pomade, wax, and hairspray.
  • Don't be afraid to try new things and have fun with your hair.

Step by Step

1. Messy Bun

To achieve a messy bun, follow these steps:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist your hair and wrap it around the base of the ponytail.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.

2. Half-Up Top Knot

To achieve a half-up top knot, follow these steps:

  1. Gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a knot.
  3. Secure the knot with bobby pins.
  4. Let the rest of your hair down.

3. Side Braid

To achieve a side braid, follow these steps:

  1. Part your hair to one side.
  2. Gather a small section of hair from the front and divide it into three sections.
  3. Begin braiding by crossing the right section over the middle section, then the left section over the middle section.
  4. Add more hair to the braid as you go along.
  5. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.

Tips

  • Use a curling iron to add curls or waves to your hair.
  • Add a hair accessory like a hairband or scarf to your hairstyle to add some flair.
  • Use dry shampoo to add volume and refresh your hair.
  • Experiment with different hair parts to create new looks.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or hairstylist.

FAQ

Q: Can I still style my short hair if it's thin?

A: Yes, you can still style your short hair if it's thin. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair, and experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out frequently, you may need to wash your hair more often. If you have dry hair, you may want to wash it less often to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or swim cap when swimming.

With these easy hairstyles for short hair step by step, you can experiment with different looks and find what works best for your hair type and face shape. Remember to have fun with your hair and don't be afraid to try new things.
