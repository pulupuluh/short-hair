How To Decide Your Hairstyle Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Sep 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Choosing a new hairstyle can be daunting, but it doesn't have to be. With the right guidance and a little bit of patience, you can find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape, lifestyle, and personality. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to decide your hairstyle.

How to

There are a few things you should consider when deciding on a new hairstyle:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair texture and thickness
  • Your lifestyle
  • Your personality and personal style

Once you have considered these factors, you can start looking for inspiration. You can browse through magazines, social media, or consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your needs.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to help you decide on the right hairstyle:

  1. Determine your face shape. Look at yourself in the mirror and identify if you have a round, oval, square, or heart-shaped face.
  2. Consider your hair texture and thickness. Do you have fine, thick, curly, or straight hair?
  3. Think about your lifestyle. Do you want a low-maintenance hairstyle or are you willing to spend more time styling your hair?
  4. Consider your personal style. Do you prefer a classic, edgy, or bohemian look?
  5. Look for inspiration. Browse through magazines, social media, or consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your needs.
  6. Try it out. Before committing to a new hairstyle, try it out with a wig or a virtual hairstyle app to see if it suits your face shape and personal style.
  7. Maintain your hairstyle. Once you have found the perfect hairstyle, make sure to maintain it with regular trims and proper hair care.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you decide on the right hairstyle:

  • Consult with your hairstylist. They can give you professional advice on what hairstyle suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Consider your hair color. Certain hairstyles look better with certain hair colors.
  • Be realistic. Make sure the hairstyle you choose is practical for your lifestyle and hair type.
  • Experiment. Don't be afraid to try out new hairstyles and switch things up.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to decide your hairstyle:

What hairstyle suits a round face?

A round face looks best with hairstyles that add height and volume. A long bob, layered haircut, or a side-swept fringe can flatter a round face.

What hairstyle suits an oval face?

An oval face suits most hairstyles, but a middle part or a side-swept fringe can complement an oval face.

What hairstyle suits a square face?

A square face looks best with hairstyles that soften the jawline. A layered haircut, a side-swept fringe, or a bob can flatter a square face.

What hairstyle suits a heart-shaped face?

A heart-shaped face suits hairstyles that balance out the forehead and narrow chin. A layered haircut, a side-swept fringe, or a shoulder-length cut can flatter a heart-shaped face.

Hair Care

Once you have found the perfect hairstyle, make sure to maintain it with proper hair care. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type, and avoid using hot styling tools too often. Regular trims can also help maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair healthy.

Choosing a new hairstyle can be fun and exciting. By considering your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style, you can find the perfect hairstyle that suits your needs. Don't be afraid to experiment and switch things up, and always remember to maintain your hairstyle with proper hair care.
