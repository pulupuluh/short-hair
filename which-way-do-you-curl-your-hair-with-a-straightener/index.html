Which Way Do You Curl Your Hair With A Straightener A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Apr 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to add some volume and texture to your hair, using a straightener to curl your hair is a great option. In this post, we'll cover exactly which way you should curl your hair with a straightener, as well as tips and tricks to ensure your curls last all day long. So, let's get started!

How to Curl Your Hair with a Straightener

Before we dive into the step-by-step process of curling your hair with a straightener, let's go over some basics. First, you'll want to start with clean, dry hair. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may want to straighten it first to ensure your curls are more uniform. Additionally, you'll want to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

The first step to curling your hair with a straightener is to section it off. Depending on the thickness of your hair, you may want to create 3-4 sections. Clip the top layers of your hair up and out of the way.

Step 2: Start with the Bottom Section

Starting with the bottom section of your hair, take a small piece and clamp your straightener around it. Twist the straightener away from your face, then slowly pull it down the length of your hair. The slower you go, the tighter your curl will be. Repeat this process on the rest of the bottom section of your hair.

Step 3: Move on to the Middle Section

Once you've finished the bottom section, unclip the middle section of your hair and repeat the process. Again, take small pieces and twist the straightener away from your face before pulling it down your hair. Make sure to curl each piece in the same direction to ensure a uniform look.

Step 4: Finish with the Top Section

Finally, unclip the top section of your hair and repeat the same process. However, for the pieces closest to your face, you may want to twist the straightener towards your face for a more flattering look.

Tips for Curling Your Hair with a Straightener

To ensure your curls look their best and last all day, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair
  • Start with small sections of hair to achieve tighter curls
  • Twist the straightener away from your face for a more natural look
  • For a more beachy look, alternate the direction of your curls
  • Use a light-hold hairspray to set your curls in place

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it better to curl your hair with a curling iron or a straightener?

Both tools can be used to curl your hair, but a curling iron may be easier to use if you're new to curling your hair. However, using a straightener to curl your hair can create a more natural, beachy look.

How long will my curls last?

The longevity of your curls will depend on several factors, including your hair type, the products you use, and the weather. However, using a heat protectant spray and a light-hold hairspray can help your curls last longer.

How often should I curl my hair?

Curling your hair too often can lead to damage, so it's best to limit it to a few times a week. Additionally, make sure to use a heat protectant spray and deep condition your hair regularly to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy, Beautiful Hair

Curling your hair with a straightener can be a great way to change up your look, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Deep condition your hair regularly
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and using the right techniques, you can achieve beautiful, long-lasting curls with a straightener. Happy styling!
