How To Make Hair Look Greasy A Relaxed Guide

Written by Christine Jun 19, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of having dry, lifeless hair? Do you want to achieve that perfect, just-got-out-of-bed look? Well, you're in luck! In this post, we'll be discussing how to make hair look greasy. From the steps to the tips and even some hair care advice, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips to Make Your Hair Look Greasy

How to Make Hair Look Greasy

Making your hair look greasy may seem counterintuitive, but it's actually a popular trend. The greasy look gives a natural, effortless vibe that many people love. Here are some ways to achieve the greasy look:

  • Use a hair oil or serum
  • Apply dry shampoo
  • Use hairspray
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Avoid using too much conditioner

Step by Step Guide

If you're ready to try this trend, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the greasy look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair. Use your fingers to distribute the product evenly.
  3. Apply dry shampoo to the roots of your hair. Massage the product into your scalp with your fingers.
  4. Spray hairspray all over your hair. This will help hold the style in place and give your hair a greasy texture.
  5. Avoid brushing your hair too much. This can disrupt the greasy texture you've created.
  6. Voila! You now have the greasy hair look.

Tips to Make Your Hair Look Greasy

Now that you know how to achieve the greasy hair look, here are some tips to make it look even better:

  • Use a matte hairspray instead of a shiny one. This will give your hair a more natural, greasy texture.
  • Apply a little bit of petroleum jelly to the ends of your hair for extra shine.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often. This will help maintain the greasy texture.
  • If you have naturally oily hair, embrace it! You may not even need to use any products to achieve the greasy look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the greasy hair trend:

Is the greasy hair trend suitable for all hair types?

Yes, it is! The greasy hair trend can work for all hair types, from straight to curly.

Can I achieve the greasy hair look without using products?

If you have naturally oily hair, you may not need to use any products to achieve the greasy hair look. However, if you have dry hair, you will need to use some products to achieve the greasy texture.

Can I still use heat styling tools with the greasy hair trend?

Yes, you can! You can still use heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons with the greasy hair trend. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Hair Care Advice

While the greasy hair trend may be fun to try, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Don't use too much heat on your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.

There you have it! A complete guide to making your hair look greasy. Whether you're trying the trend for the first time or looking for some tips to up your greasy hair game, we hope this post has been helpful. So, go ahead and rock that greasy hair look with confidence!
