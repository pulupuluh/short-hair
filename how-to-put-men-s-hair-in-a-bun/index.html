How To Put Men s Hair In A Bun A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Apr 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of your long hair getting in the way? Do you want a simple and stylish way to keep your hair out of your face? Look no further than the man bun! This trendy hairstyle is not only practical but can also elevate your look. In this guide, we will show you how to put men's hair in a bun in just a few easy steps.

How to

To start, gather all the necessary tools: a hairbrush, hair ties, and bobby pins. Next, follow these steps:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles and create a smooth base. If you have long hair, you may want to use a detangling spray to make the process easier.

Step 2: Pull Your Hair Back

Gently gather your hair back into a ponytail using your hands. You can choose to position the bun high or low on your head, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Twist Your Hair

Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail until it starts to coil around itself. Keep twisting until you reach the end of your hair.

Step 4: Secure the Bun

Once you have twisted your hair, hold the bun in place with one hand while you use the other to wrap a hair tie around the bun. Make sure the hair tie is tight enough to hold the bun in place.

Step 5: Pin the Bun

If you have any loose strands or shorter hairs that didn't make it into the bun, use bobby pins to secure them in place. You can also use bobby pins to adjust the shape of the bun if needed.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you perfect your man bun:

  • Experiment with different bun styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color for a more natural look.
  • Add a bit of hairspray or gel to keep the bun in place throughout the day.
  • Don't be afraid to accessorize with headbands or hats to elevate your look.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about putting men's hair in a bun:

Is the man bun still in style?

Yes! The man bun has been a popular hairstyle for several years and shows no signs of going out of style.

Can men with short hair put their hair in a bun?

It may be more challenging, but men with shorter hair can still put their hair in a bun. Use bobby pins to secure any loose hairs that don't make it into the bun.

How do I take my hair out of a bun?

Simply remove the hair tie and gently unwind the bun. You may need to use your fingers to loosen any tangles or knots.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you wear it in a bun regularly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any buildup and keep it healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair every few months to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to put your hair in a bun like a pro. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, the man bun is a versatile hairstyle that can elevate any outfit. Give it a try and see how it can transform your look.
