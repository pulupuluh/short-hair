Best Haircut For Oval Face Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 14, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have an oval face with curly hair, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. However, with curly hair, it's important to find a cut that flatters your face shape while also working with your natural texture. In this article, we'll go over the best haircuts for oval face curly hair, including a description, how-to, step-by-step guide, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How-To
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

The best haircut for oval face curly hair is one that enhances your natural curls while also framing your face. Layers are a great option for curly hair as they add volume and movement without sacrificing the shape of your curls. Additionally, a side-swept bang or a middle part can complement your face shape and soften any sharp angles.

When choosing a haircut, it's important to consider the length of your hair. Short haircuts can work well for oval faces, but with curly hair, it's best to keep some length to avoid the dreaded triangle shape. Mid-length haircuts, like a long bob or shoulder-length cut, are great options as they provide enough length to show off your curls while also framing your face.

How-To

When going for a haircut, it's important to communicate with your stylist about your hair type, face shape, and desired look. Bring in pictures of hairstyles you like and discuss any concerns or preferences you have. Your stylist can then recommend a haircut that will work for your hair type and face shape.

When styling your hair at home, start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it's wet, then apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, scrunching your curls upward as you go.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair.
2. Detangle your hair while it's wet using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls.
4. Section your hair and use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, scrunching your curls upward as you go.
5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to touch up any curls that need extra definition.
6. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips

- When choosing a haircut, consider your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle.
- Layers are a great option for curly hair, as they add volume and movement.
- Keep some length to avoid the triangle shape with curly hair.
- Use products designed for curly hair to enhance your natural curls.
- Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.
- Touch up any curls that need extra definition with a curling iron or wand.
- Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for oval face curly hair?

The best haircut for oval face curly hair is one that enhances your natural curls while also framing your face. Layers are a great option for curly hair as they add volume and movement without sacrificing the shape of your curls.

Can I have short curly hair with an oval face?

Short haircuts can work well for oval faces, but with curly hair, it's best to keep some length to avoid the dreaded triangle shape. Consider a long bob or shoulder-length cut instead.

How do I style curly hair?

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. Detangle your hair while it's wet, then apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, scrunching your curls upward as you go. Touch up any curls that need extra definition with a curling iron or wand, then finish with a hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold.

Hair Care Tips

- Use products designed for curly hair to enhance your natural curls.
- Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it's wet.
- Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and recommendations, you can find the best haircut for oval face curly hair that flatters your face shape and works with your natural texture. Remember to communicate with your stylist, use products designed for curly hair, and care for your hair regularly to keep your curls looking their best.
