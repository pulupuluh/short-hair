Mens Comb Back Haircuts A Classic Look For Every Occasion

Written by Petter Jul 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a timeless hairstyle that exudes confidence and sophistication, look no further than the mens comb back haircut. This classic look has been popular for decades, and it's not hard to see why. Whether you're heading to the office or a night out on the town, a comb back haircut is versatile enough to suit any occasion.

Description

The mens comb back haircut is characterized by longer hair on top that is swept back with a comb, while the sides and back are trimmed shorter. The length on top can vary depending on personal preference, but the general rule is that the longer the hair, the more dramatic the look. This style works well for men with straight or wavy hair, although it can be adapted for those with curly hair as well.

How to Get a Mens Comb Back Haircut

If you're interested in getting a mens comb back haircut, the first step is to find a skilled barber who has experience with this style. When you're at the salon, communicate your desired length and volume on top, and how short you want the sides and back to be. Your barber will then use scissors or clippers to create the desired shape and length, and finish off with a comb to sweep the hair back.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to get a mens comb back haircut:

  1. Find a skilled barber who can create this style
  2. Communicate your desired length and volume on top, and how short you want the sides and back to be
  3. Sit back and relax while your barber uses scissors or clippers to create the desired shape and length
  4. Finish off with a comb to sweep the hair back

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Comb Back Haircut

Once you have your mens comb back haircut, there are a few things you can do to keep it looking sharp:

  • Invest in a good quality comb to keep your hair looking neat and tidy
  • Use a styling product like pomade or wax to add volume and hold the hair in place
  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups and trims to keep the style looking fresh

FAQs

1. Who can get a mens comb back haircut?

Anyone with straight, wavy, or curly hair can get a mens comb back haircut. However, it's important to communicate your desired length and volume with your barber to ensure the style suits your face shape and hair type.

2. How long does a mens comb back haircut last?

A mens comb back haircut can last anywhere from 4-6 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. Regular trims and touch-ups can help extend the life of the style.

Hair Care for Mens Comb Back Haircuts

When it comes to hair care for mens comb back haircuts, less is more. Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Instead, aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a styling product like pomade or wax to add volume and hold, but be careful not to overdo it as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock a mens comb back haircut that will never go out of style.
