How To Self Cut Hair Male With Scissors

Written by Bardi Feb 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of shelling out money for a haircut every few weeks? Do you want to try your hand at self-cutting your hair? With the right tools and techniques, it's possible to achieve a great-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll show you how to self cut hair male with scissors.

Description

Self-cutting hair can be a daunting task, especially if you've never tried it before. However, with a little bit of practice and some helpful tips, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut that will save you time and money in the long run. The key is to use the right tools and follow the right techniques.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need a good pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror (ideally, one that you can position to see the back of your head). Here's how to get started:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an even cut.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, comb it straight and remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you get an even cut.

Step 3: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want your hair to be. If you're not sure, start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed.

Step 4: Cut the Sides and Back

Starting at the sides and back, use your scissors to cut small sections of hair at a time. Use the comb to hold the hair in place and cut along the length of the comb, making sure to keep the scissors straight. Repeat this process until you've cut all the hair on the sides and back of your head.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Next, move to the top of your head. Use your scissors to cut small sections of hair at a time, starting at the front and working your way back. Again, use the comb to hold the hair in place and cut along the length of the comb. Make sure to cut the hair evenly and to check your progress in the mirror as you go.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut all the hair to the desired length, use your scissors to blend the hair. This means cutting the hair at an angle to create a smooth transition from one length to the next. Focus on the areas where the hair is longer, such as the top and sides of your head.

Step 7: Check Your Work

Finally, check your work in the mirror. Look for any uneven areas or spots that need to be trimmed. Use your scissors to make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind as you self-cut your hair:

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  • Use the comb to hold the hair in place as you cut.
  • Check your progress in the mirror as you go.
  • Blend the hair for a smooth transition between lengths.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.

FAQ

Can I self-cut my hair with any type of scissors?

No, you need a good pair of hair scissors that are sharp and designed for cutting hair. Using regular scissors or dull scissors can result in a choppy or uneven cut.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim the hair to fix the problem. However, it's best to take your time and cut small sections at a time to avoid making mistakes in the first place.

How often should I self-cut my hair?

This depends on the length and style of your hair. Generally, you should aim to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Hair Care

After you've self-cut your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat-styling tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can self-cut your hair male with scissors and achieve a great-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
