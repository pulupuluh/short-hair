How To Style Curly Hair Out Of Face

Written by Aprilia Sep 07, 2023 · 10 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it looks gorgeous, it can be tough to style, especially when you want to keep it out of your face. Fortunately, there are plenty of ways to style your curly hair away from your face without sacrificing your style.

Description

In this blog post, we’ll show you how to style your curly hair out of your face using a variety of techniques. We’ll cover how to do it step by step, tips for keeping your hair looking great, and some frequently asked questions about curly hair. By the end of this article, you’ll be ready to rock your curls with confidence!

How to Style Curly Hair Out of Face

There are several ways to style curly hair out of your face. The best method for you will depend on the length of your hair, your personal style, and the look you’re going for. Here are some of the most popular techniques:

Pull it Back

The easiest way to keep your curly hair out of your face is to pull it back. You can do this in a variety of ways, from a simple ponytail to a more elaborate updo. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck or higher up, depending on where you want your ponytail to sit.
  3. Tie your hair back with an elastic band or hair tie. You can leave it as a simple ponytail or add twists, braids, or other embellishments.
  4. Secure any loose strands with bobby pins or hair clips.

Another option for pulling your hair back is to create a half-up, half-down style. This is a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still showing off your beautiful curls. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by separating the top section of your hair from the bottom section.
  2. Tie the top section back with an elastic band or hair tie.
  3. Leave the bottom section loose and curly.
  4. Add twists, braids, or other embellishments to the top section for a more elaborate look.
  5. Secure any loose strands with bobby pins or hair clips.

Pin it Up

If you don’t want to pull your hair back completely, you can still keep it out of your face by pinning it up. This is a great option for shorter hair or for adding some texture and interest to your look. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and twist it away from your face.
  3. Secure the twist with a bobby pin or hair clip.
  4. Repeat with another section of hair on the other side of your face.
  5. You can add as many twists as you like, depending on how much hair you want to keep out of your face.

Braid it

Braids are a great way to keep your curly hair out of your face while adding some extra style and texture. There are many different types of braids you can try, from simple three-strand braids to more elaborate fishtail braids. Here’s how to do a basic braid:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into three equal sections.
  3. Take the right section and cross it over the middle section.
  4. Take the left section and cross it over the new middle section.
  5. Continue braiding until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Tie off the end of the braid with an elastic band or hair tie.

Tips for Keeping Your Hair Looking Great

Now that you know how to style your curly hair out of your face, here are some tips for keeping your hair looking great:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help define your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-defining cream to help keep your curls looking their best.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curly hair:

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair find that they need to wash it every day, while others can go several days between washes. Experiment to see what works best for you.

What’s the best way to dry curly hair?

Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, as this can cause frizz. Instead, gently squeeze out excess water with a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.

How can I prevent frizz?

Avoid over-washing your hair, use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair, and avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry. You can also apply a leave-in conditioner or anti-frizz serum to help tame flyaways.

Hair Care

Curly hair can be high maintenance, but with the right care, it can look amazing. Here are some tips for keeping your curly hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner formulated for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can dry out your hair and cause frizz.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hair accessories with metal clasps, as these can damage your hair.
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

Styling curly hair out of your face may take some practice, but with these tips and techniques, you’ll be able to find a style that works for you. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
