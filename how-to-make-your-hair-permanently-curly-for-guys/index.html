How To Make Your Hair Permanently Curly For Guys

Written by Bardi Aug 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of wrestling with your straight hair every day? Do you dream of having luscious curls that never fall flat? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair permanently curly for guys.

Description

Permanently curly hair is a game-changer for many guys who are tired of dealing with straight hair. It's a fantastic way to add volume, texture, and style to your hair without having to use styling tools every day. But how do you make your hair permanently curly? Is it even possible? The answer is yes, and we're going to show you how.

How to Make Your Hair Permanently Curly for Guys

The process of making your hair permanently curly involves chemical treatments that alter the structure of your hair. This process is called a perm, short for "permanent wave."

Step 1: Consult with a Professional

The first step to getting a perm is to consult with a professional hair stylist. They will be able to assess your hair type, texture, and condition to determine if a perm is right for you. They will also be able to recommend the best type of perm for your hair and the look you want to achieve.

Step 2: Prepare your Hair

Before getting a perm, it's essential to prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo. This will remove any product buildup and help the perm solution penetrate your hair more effectively. Your stylist may also trim your hair to ensure that the perm solution is evenly distributed.

Step 3: Apply the Perm Solution

Once your hair is prepared, your stylist will apply the perm solution to your hair. The perm solution contains chemicals that break down the bonds in your hair, allowing it to be reshaped into curls. Your stylist will wrap your hair in rods or rollers, depending on the type of perm you're getting and the look you want to achieve. They will then apply a neutralizer to your hair to stop the chemical process.

Step 4: Rinse and Style

After the neutralizer has been applied, your stylist will rinse your hair thoroughly to remove any remaining chemicals. They will then apply a conditioning treatment to your hair to restore moisture and prevent damage. Once your hair is dry, you can style it as you normally would.

Tips

Getting a perm is a significant decision, and it's essential to take good care of your hair afterward to maintain your curls. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls

FAQ

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from three to six months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

Can I dye my hair after getting a perm?

It's best to wait at least two weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair. This will give your hair time to recover from the chemical process.

Is a perm suitable for all hair types?

No, a perm is not suitable for all hair types. It's best to consult with a professional stylist to determine if a perm is right for you.

Hair Care

It's essential to take good care of your hair after getting a perm to maintain healthy, beautiful curls. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair

And there you have it! With these tips, you can make your hair permanently curly for guys. Just remember to consult with a professional stylist, take good care of your hair, and enjoy your new curls!
