Best Shampoo For Thin Brittle Hair Our Top Picks In 2023

Written by Christine Feb 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of dealing with thin, brittle hair that breaks easily? If so, you’re not alone. Many people struggle with weak hair that lacks strength and volume. One of the best ways to combat this issue is by using the right shampoo. In this post, we’ll share our top picks for the best shampoo for thin brittle hair.

Why Choose a Shampoo for Thin Brittle Hair?

Before we dive into our top picks, let’s discuss why it’s important to choose a shampoo specifically designed for thin, brittle hair. Regular shampoos can be too harsh and strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it even more vulnerable to breakage. Shampoos designed for thin hair, on the other hand, are formulated to be gentle and nourishing, helping to strengthen your hair and promote growth.

How to Choose the Best Shampoo for Thin Brittle Hair

When choosing a shampoo for thin hair, there are a few key things to look for:

1. Ingredients

Look for shampoos that are formulated with nourishing ingredients, such as biotin, keratin, and vitamins. These ingredients can help strengthen and thicken your hair, making it less prone to breakage.

2. pH balance

Choose a shampoo that is pH-balanced, as this can help prevent further damage to your hair. Look for shampoos with a pH between 4.5 and 5.5.

3. Sulfate-free

Sulfates can be harsh on thin, brittle hair, so opt for sulfate-free shampoos instead. These are gentler on your hair and scalp, helping to prevent further damage.

Our Top Picks for the Best Shampoo for Thin Brittle Hair

1. OGX Thick & Full Biotin & Collagen Shampoo

This shampoo is formulated with biotin and collagen, two ingredients that are known to help strengthen and thicken hair. It’s also sulfate-free, making it gentle on thin, brittle hair. Plus, it has a pleasant scent and leaves hair feeling soft and clean.

2. Keranique Scalp Stimulating Shampoo

This shampoo contains keratin, which can help strengthen hair and prevent breakage. It also contains peppermint oil, which can stimulate the scalp and promote hair growth. Overall, this shampoo is a great choice for anyone looking to improve the health and thickness of their hair.

3. Nioxin Cleanser Shampoo

This shampoo is specifically designed for thinning hair, making it a great choice for anyone dealing with hair loss or breakage. It contains ingredients like biotin and niacin, which can help improve the health of your scalp and promote hair growth.

Tips for Using Shampoo on Thin Brittle Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when using shampoo on thin, brittle hair:

1. Don’t overwash

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it more prone to breakage. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every few days, depending on your hair type.

2. Use lukewarm water

Hot water can be damaging to your hair, so use lukewarm water when washing your hair. This can help prevent breakage and keep your hair looking healthy.

3. Be gentle

When washing your hair, be gentle and avoid pulling or tugging on it. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools whenever possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can shampoo really make a difference for thin brittle hair?

Yes! Using a shampoo specifically designed for thin, brittle hair can help strengthen your hair and prevent breakage. Look for shampoos with nourishing and strengthening ingredients, like biotin, keratin, and vitamins.

2. How often should I wash my thin brittle hair?

It’s best to wash your hair every other day or every few days, depending on your hair type. Washing too often can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it more prone to breakage.

3. Can I use conditioner on my thin brittle hair?

Yes! Conditioner can help nourish and strengthen your hair, making it less prone to breakage. Look for a conditioner specifically designed for thin hair, and avoid applying it directly to your roots.

Conclusion

If you’re struggling with thin, brittle hair, choosing the right shampoo can make a big difference. Look for shampoos with nourishing and strengthening ingredients, like biotin and keratin, and avoid harsh sulfates. With the right shampoo and hair care routine, you can improve the health and thickness of your hair.
