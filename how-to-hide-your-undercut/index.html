How To Hide Your Undercut Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Mar 17, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of showing off your undercut every time you tie your hair? Maybe you want to switch up your style, or you want to hide your undercut for a special event. Whatever the reason, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll give you tips and tricks on how to hide your undercut, so you can have the best of both worlds.

How to Hide Your Undercut

There are several ways to hide your undercut. Here are some of our favorite methods:

1. Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that can help you hide your undercut. Simply part your hair to one side, and comb it over your undercut. This will create the illusion of a fuller head of hair, while also covering up your undercut.

2. Low Bun

A low bun is a great way to hide your undercut, especially if you have longer hair. Simply tie your hair into a low bun at the nape of your neck, and tuck any shorter hairs under the bun. This will create a sleek and sophisticated look, while also hiding your undercut.

3. Braids

Braids are a great way to hide your undercut, especially if you have shorter hair. You can braid your hair in a variety of styles, such as a French braid, a fishtail braid, or even a simple braid. This will create a unique and stylish look, while also hiding your undercut.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide on how to hide your undercut using the side part method:

Step 1:

Wet your hair and comb it straight back.

Step 2:

Part your hair to one side, using a comb.

Step 3:

Comb your hair over your undercut, and style it as desired.

That’s it! You’ve successfully hidden your undercut using the side part method.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when hiding your undercut:

1. Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, can help you hide your undercut. Simply clip your hair back, or wear a headband to cover your undercut.

2. Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you. Try out different braids, buns, and updos to see which one hides your undercut the best.

3. Use Hair Products

Hair products, such as pomade or hairspray, can help you style your hair and keep it in place. Use these products to create a sleek and polished look, while also hiding your undercut.

FAQ

Q: Will hiding my undercut damage my hair?

A: No, hiding your undercut will not damage your hair. However, it’s important to use hair products and accessories that are gentle on your hair.

Q: How long does it take to hide my undercut?

A: It depends on the method you choose. Some methods, such as the side part, can be done in a matter of minutes. Others, such as braids, may take longer.

Q: Can I still show off my undercut if I want to?

A: Yes, of course! Hiding your undercut is a great way to switch up your style, but you can still show it off if you want to.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair, especially if you’re hiding your undercut. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won’t strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for shampoos that are free of sulfates and parabens.

2. Condition Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to give your hair a boost.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often. These tools can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily hide your undercut and switch up your style whenever you want. Remember to take care of your hair, and have fun experimenting with different hairstyles!
