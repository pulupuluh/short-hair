Men s Medium Hairstyles Short Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Mar 11, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and trendy hairstyle that is low maintenance and easy to maintain? Look no further than men's medium hairstyles with short sides! This versatile look is perfect for any occasion, whether you're going to work or hitting the town with friends. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about men's medium hairstyles with short sides, including how to style them, maintenance tips, and more.

How to Get Men's Medium Hairstyles Short Sides

The first step in getting men's medium hairstyles with short sides is to decide on the length you want for your hair. Medium length hair is typically defined as hair that falls between the chin and shoulders, so keep that in mind when choosing your cut. Once you've decided on the length, you'll want to find a skilled barber or stylist who can help you achieve your desired look.

When you're at the salon, be sure to communicate clearly with your stylist about the length and style you want. They'll likely use a combination of clippers and scissors to trim the sides of your hair short while leaving the top longer. From there, you can style your hair in a variety of ways to achieve a unique look that suits your personality and style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Men's Medium Hairstyles Short Sides

Styling men's medium hairstyles with short sides is relatively simple and requires minimal effort. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving this look:

Step 1: Clean and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type. Once your hair is clean, gently towel dry it to remove excess water.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Pomade, wax, and clay are all great options for achieving a textured, tousled look. Be sure to work the product evenly throughout your hair, from roots to tips.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is coated in styling product, you can begin to style it. Use your fingers to tousle and shape your hair, focusing on the top and front sections. You can also use a comb or brush to create a more polished look, depending on your personal style preferences.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

To ensure your hairstyle lasts all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to keep your hair in place and prevent frizz and flyaways.

Tips for Maintaining Men's Medium Hairstyles Short Sides

Maintaining men's medium hairstyles with short sides is relatively easy, but there are a few things you can do to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup
  • Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, as this can cause damage
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray

Frequently Asked Questions About Men's Medium Hairstyles Short Sides

What face shape is best for men's medium hairstyles with short sides?

Men's medium hairstyles with short sides can work well for a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. However, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid this style, as it can make your face appear wider.

How often should I wash my hair if I have men's medium hairstyles with short sides?

How often you wash your hair will depend on your hair type and personal preferences. However, most people with men's medium hairstyles with short sides find that washing their hair every 2-3 days is sufficient.

Can I still style my hair if I have thinning hair or a receding hairline?

Absolutely! Men's medium hairstyles with short sides can be a great option for men with thinning hair or a receding hairline, as they can help to create the appearance of fuller, thicker hair. Be sure to work with your stylist to find a style that suits your unique hair type and face shape.

Hair Care for Men's Medium Hairstyles Short Sides

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and stylish men's medium hairstyles with short sides. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, as this can cause damage
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your style
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair

By following these tips and using high-quality hair care products, you can keep your men's medium hairstyles with short sides looking their best.

Conclusion

Men's medium hairstyles with short sides are a stylish and versatile option for men of all ages and hair types. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this guide, you can achieve a trendy and low-maintenance look that is sure to turn heads.
