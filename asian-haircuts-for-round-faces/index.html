Asian Haircuts For Round Faces Flatter Your Features

Written by William Jun 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for the perfect haircut to flatter your round face? Look no further than Asian haircuts! Asian hairstyles are known for their unique cuts and styles that complement round faces perfectly. In this article, we'll go over some of the best Asian haircuts for round faces, along with tips on how to care for your hair and maintain your new look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Asian Haircuts for Round Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Asian Haircut
  • FAQs About Asian Haircuts for Round Faces
  • Hair Care Tips for Asian Hair

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a round face, it's important to consider the shape of your face and the features you want to accentuate. Asian haircuts are perfect for round faces because they tend to have angular cuts and layers that add dimension and shape to the face.

Some of the best Asian haircuts for round faces include:

  • Bob with Side-Swept Bangs
  • Long Layers with Angled Bangs
  • Short Pixie Cut with Choppy Layers
  • Messy Updo with Wispy Bangs

The Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

The bob with side-swept bangs is a classic Asian haircut that flatters round faces. The bob is cut just above the shoulders, with layers that frame the face. The side-swept bangs add dimension and shape to the face.

Long Layers with Angled Bangs

Long layers with angled bangs are perfect for those who want to keep their hair long. The layers add volume and shape to the hair, while the angled bangs accentuate the cheekbones.

Short Pixie Cut with Choppy Layers

The short pixie cut with choppy layers is a bold Asian haircut that flatters round faces. The choppy layers add texture to the hair, while the short length draws attention to the face.

Messy Updo with Wispy Bangs

The messy updo with wispy bangs is a casual Asian haircut that's perfect for everyday wear. The wispy bangs add shape to the face, while the messy updo adds volume and texture to the hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Asian Haircuts for Round Faces

Once you've chosen the perfect Asian haircut for your round face, it's time to head to the salon. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect Asian haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Consult with your stylist about the best way to achieve your desired haircut.
  3. Section your hair and start cutting, making sure to follow the lines of your face and accentuate your features.
  4. Add layers and texture to your hair as desired.
  5. Finish with a blowout or style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Asian Haircut

Once you've gotten your new Asian haircut, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your Asian haircut:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using hair masks or treatments to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

FAQs About Asian Haircuts for Round Faces

Q: Will an Asian haircut work for my round face?

A: Yes! Asian haircuts are perfect for round faces because they tend to have angular cuts and layers that add dimension and shape to the face.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It's recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I dye my hair after getting an Asian haircut?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair after getting an Asian haircut. However, it's important to use a high-quality hair dye and to consult with your stylist beforehand to ensure that the dye won't damage your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Asian Hair

Asian hair can be delicate and prone to breakage, so it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some hair care tips for Asian hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using hair oils or serums to nourish your hair and keep it healthy.

With the right Asian haircut and proper hair care, you can flatter your round face and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. Try out one of the Asian haircuts we've listed here and see the difference it can make!
