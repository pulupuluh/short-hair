Best Swimsuit For Large Bust And Tummy

Written by Frank Aug 06, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of feeling self-conscious about your large bust and tummy while at the beach or pool? Finding the right swimsuit can make all the difference in feeling confident and comfortable. In this article, we will discuss the best swimsuits for large bust and tummy, including how to choose the right one for your body type, tips for styling and care, and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose
  • One-Piece Swimsuits
  • Tankini Swimsuits
  • Bikini Swimsuits
  • Styling Tips

How to Choose

When choosing a swimsuit for a large bust and tummy, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Support: Look for swimsuits with underwire or built-in bras to provide support for your bust.
  • Coverage: One-piece or tankini swimsuits can provide more coverage for your tummy, while high-waisted bikini bottoms can also help to conceal and smooth out your midsection.
  • Adjustable straps: Adjustable straps can help to ensure a better fit and provide additional support for your bust.
  • Material: Look for swimsuits made from thicker, more supportive materials like neoprene or spandex.

One-Piece Swimsuits

One-piece swimsuits are a great option for those who want more coverage and support for their bust and tummy. Look for suits with built-in bras or underwire for added support, and choose styles with ruching or draping to help conceal your midsection. Halter necklines can also help to lift and support your bust.

Tankini Swimsuits

Tankini swimsuits are another great option for those who want more coverage but still want the convenience of a two-piece. Look for tankinis with built-in bras or underwire for added support, and choose styles with longer tops that flow away from the body to conceal your tummy. High-waisted bottoms can also help to smooth and flatter your midsection.

Bikini Swimsuits

If you prefer a bikini, look for styles with underwire or built-in bras to provide support for your bust. High-waisted bottoms can also help to smooth and flatter your tummy, while halter necklines can help to lift and support your bust. Avoid string bikinis or bandeau tops, as they may not provide enough support for a larger bust.

Styling Tips

When styling your swimsuit, consider adding a flowy cover-up or kaftan to provide additional coverage and create a more polished look. Accessorize with sunglasses, a sun hat, and sandals for a complete beach-ready outfit. If you prefer a more casual look, opt for a loose, oversized t-shirt or denim shorts.

Care Tips

To ensure your swimsuit stays in good condition, rinse it with cool water after each use to remove any chlorine or salt. Avoid using harsh detergents or bleach, and never put your swimsuit in the dryer. Instead, lay it flat to dry in a shaded area.

FAQs

Q: How can I tell if a swimsuit provides enough support for my bust?

A: Look for swimsuits with underwire or built-in bras, and adjustable straps. You can also read reviews or consult with a sales associate for recommendations.

Q: Can I wear a bikini if I have a larger bust and tummy?

A: Yes, you can wear a bikini! Look for styles with underwire or built-in bras for added support, and high-waisted bottoms to smooth and flatter your tummy.

Hair Care

Don't forget about your hair while at the beach or pool! To protect your hair from damage, apply a leave-in conditioner or oil before swimming, and wear a swim cap if possible. After swimming, rinse your hair with fresh water and apply a moisturizing conditioner or hair mask.

With these tips in mind, you can find the perfect swimsuit for your body type and feel confident and comfortable while enjoying your time at the beach or pool!
