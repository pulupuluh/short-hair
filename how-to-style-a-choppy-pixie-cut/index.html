How To Style A Choppy Pixie Cut

Written by Oliver Mar 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you thinking of getting a choppy pixie cut? This daring and bold hairstyle can be a great way to change up your look and show off your unique style. But once you have the cut, how do you style it? In this article, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve the perfect choppy pixie cut style.

Description

A choppy pixie cut is a short hairstyle that features layers and choppy, textured ends. This cut is perfect for those who want to make a statement and show off their edgy side. The choppy layers can be styled in many different ways, making it a versatile and fun hairstyle.

How to Style a Choppy Pixie Cut

Styling a choppy pixie cut can seem daunting at first, but with a little practice, you can achieve a variety of looks. Follow these steps to style your choppy pixie cut:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then towel dry it gently. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair and distribute it evenly from root to tip. This will help to add texture and hold to your hair.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a hair dryer, blow dry your hair while using your fingers to create texture and volume. Focus on the roots to add lift and body to your hair. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to create loose waves or curls.

Step 3: Add Volume

To add even more volume and texture, use a volumizing spray or dry shampoo. Apply the product to the roots of your hair and massage it in with your fingertips. This will help to create a tousled, messy look that is perfect for a choppy pixie cut.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

To set your style, spray your hair with a light hold hairspray. This will help to keep your hair in place throughout the day without weighing it down or making it feel crunchy.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your choppy pixie cut:

  • Invest in a good quality flat iron for creating waves and curls.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to add accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to your hair to add some personality to your look.
  • Be sure to get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your choppy pixie cut.

FAQ

How often should I get a trim for my choppy pixie cut?

We recommend getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your choppy pixie cut.

Can I style my choppy pixie cut with a hair straightener?

Yes, a hair straightener can be used to create waves and curls in your choppy pixie cut. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using the straightener to avoid damaging your hair.

What styling products are best for a choppy pixie cut?

Styling products such as mousse, gel, and dry shampoo can help to add texture and hold to your choppy pixie cut. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your choppy pixie cut looking its best, be sure to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and steps, you can style your choppy pixie cut with confidence and show off your unique style.
