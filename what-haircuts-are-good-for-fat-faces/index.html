What Haircuts Are Good For Fat Faces

Written by Eveline Apr 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, it can be challenging to find a haircut that flatters your features. However, with the right cut and styling, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a slimming effect. In this blog post, we will explore what haircuts are good for fat faces and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

A fat face is characterized by a round shape with full cheeks, a soft chin, and a wide forehead. The goal of a good haircut for a fat face is to create the illusion of a longer, more angular face shape. This can be achieved by adding height at the crown, incorporating layers to add movement and texture, and framing the face with angles and asymmetry.

How to

The first step in finding the right haircut for your face shape is to consult with a professional stylist. They can assess your features, hair texture, and personal style to recommend the most flattering options. Here are some haircut ideas to consider:

Layered Lob

A layered lob is a shoulder-length haircut with choppy, textured layers. This cut adds volume and movement to the hair, which can help to balance out a round face shape. Ask your stylist to create layers that start at the jawline and angle downward, creating a slimming effect.

Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, try adding layers to create dimension and shape. Long layers that start at the chin or collarbone can help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the cheeks. Consider adding side-swept bangs to frame the face and create a flattering angle.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob is a short haircut that is longer on one side than the other. This cut adds angles and structure to the face, which can help to create a slimmer appearance. Ask your stylist to create a deep side part and angle the bob so that it is longer on the side with less fullness.

Step by Step

To achieve the best results with your new haircut, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and create movement.
  3. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create waves or curls, depending on your preferred style.
  4. Apply a styling product such as mousse, gel, or hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a fat face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin or jawline, as these can accentuate fullness in the face.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension in the hair.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the most flattering angle for your face.
  • Accessorize with earrings or necklaces to draw attention away from the face.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a fat face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides or top of the head, such as blunt bobs, heavy bangs, or curly hair. Instead, opt for styles that add height and angles to the face.

Can I still have long hair if I have a fat face?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a fat face. Just be sure to add layers and angles to create movement and shape, and consider adding side-swept bangs to frame the face.

How often should I get a haircut?

It is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and beautiful, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling tools and let your hair air dry whenever possible.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.

In conclusion, there are many haircuts that are good for fat faces, from layered lobs to asymmetrical bobs. By consulting with a professional stylist and following these tips, you can find the perfect cut to enhance your natural beauty and create a slimming effect.
