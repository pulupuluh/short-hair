Hairstyles For Very Thin Hair Over 70 Tips Ideas And Care

Written by Eveline Jul 06, 2023 Â· 12 min read

As we age, our hair tends to thin out due to hormonal changes, genetics, and other factors. This can make it challenging to find the right hairstyles that flatter our face shape and match our lifestyle. However, having thin hair doesn't mean you have to sacrifice style and confidence. In this article, we'll share some tips, ideas, and care tips for hairstyles for very thin hair over 70.

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Very Thin Hair Over 70
  • Top Hairstyles for Very Thin Hair Over 70
  • Hair Care Tips for Very Thin Hair Over 70
  • FAQs About Hairstyles for Very Thin Hair Over 70

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Very Thin Hair Over 70

Before we dive into the top hairstyles for very thin hair over 70, let's discuss how to choose the right one for your unique features and preferences.

Consider Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle is to identify your face shape. This will help you determine which styles will highlight your best features and balance out any asymmetries. Some common face shapes include:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart-shaped
  • Diamond-shaped

Once you know your face shape, you can look for hairstyles that complement it. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to opt for styles that add height and volume to elongate your face, such as a pixie cut or a layered bob. If you have a square-shaped face, you may want to go for softer styles that contour your jawline, such as a side-swept bang or a shoulder-length cut.

Consider Your Hair Texture and Type

Another factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair texture and type. If you have very thin hair, you may want to avoid styles that require a lot of volume or layers, as these can make your hair look even thinner. Instead, you may want to opt for styles that add definition and depth, such as a blunt bob or a side-parted pixie cut. If you have fine or straight hair, you may want to consider adding some texture or waves to give your hair more body and movement.

Consider Your Lifestyle and Preferences

Finally, you should also consider your lifestyle and preferences when choosing a hairstyle. Do you prefer low-maintenance styles that require minimal styling and upkeep? Or do you enjoy experimenting with different looks and accessories? Do you need a style that can transition from day to night, or one that's suitable for a specific occasion or event?

By taking these factors into account, you can narrow down your options and find a hairstyle that suits your needs and personality.

Top Hairstyles for Very Thin Hair Over 70

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle, let's explore some of the top options for very thin hair over 70.

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic and versatile style that can work for any age and face shape. It's especially flattering for women with thin hair, as it adds volume and dimension to your hair without weighing it down. You can customize your pixie cut by adding layers, side-swept bangs, or highlights to suit your preferences.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another timeless style that's perfect for thin hair over 70. It's a low-maintenance and practical option that can be worn straight or wavy, depending on your mood. You can opt for a blunt bob for a sleek and modern look, or a layered bob for a softer and more textured look.

3. Shag Cut

The shag cut is a fun and trendy style that can add volume and movement to your thin hair. It's a layered cut that features choppy and textured ends, creating a tousled and effortless look. You can style your shag cut with a center or side part, and add some highlights or lowlights for extra dimension.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

If you prefer to keep your hair longer, you can add some side-swept bangs to frame your face and add some interest to your hair. Side-swept bangs are especially flattering for women with thin hair, as they can create the illusion of fuller and thicker hair. You can pair your bangs with a layered cut or a shoulder-length style for a chic and feminine look.

Hair Care Tips for Very Thin Hair Over 70

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, it's important to take care of your thin hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair:

1. Use Gentle and Nourishing Hair Products

When washing and styling your hair, use gentle and nourishing hair products that won't strip your hair of its natural oils or weigh it down. Look for products that are specifically formulated for thin or fine hair, such as volumizing shampoos and conditioners, lightweight mousses, and texturizing sprays.

2. Avoid Heat Styling and Over-Brushing

Heat styling and over-brushing can damage your hair and make it look even thinner. Try to limit your use of hot tools like blow dryers, curling irons, and straighteners, and air-dry your hair whenever possible. Also, be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, and use a wide-tooth comb or a soft bristle brush to prevent breakage.

3. Eat a Balanced and Nutritious Diet

Your diet can also affect your hair's health and growth. Make sure to eat a balanced and nutritious diet that's rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals, such as leafy greens, whole grains, lean meats, and nuts. You can also take supplements like biotin or omega-3 fatty acids to support your hair's growth and strength.

FAQs About Hairstyles for Very Thin Hair Over 70

Q: Can I still get a perm or a color treatment if I have very thin hair?

A: It depends on the condition of your hair and your hair type. If your hair is already fragile or damaged, it's best to avoid chemical treatments like perms or colors that can further weaken your hair. However, if your hair is healthy and strong enough, you can still get a perm or a color treatment, as long as you follow the proper aftercare and maintenance tips.

Q: What accessories can I use to style my thin hair over 70?

A: You can use various accessories to add some flair and personality to your thin hair. Some popular options include headbands, scarves, hair clips, and barrettes. Just make sure to choose accessories that are lightweight and won't pull or tug on your hair.

Q: Can I still wear long hair if I have very thin hair over 70?

A: Yes, you can still wear long hair if you have very thin hair over 70. However, you may want to opt for styles that add some volume and movement to your hair, such as layered cuts or beachy waves. You can also consider adding some highlights or lowlights to create the illusion of depth and dimension.

In conclusion, having very thin hair over 70 doesn't mean you have to settle for boring or outdated hairstyles. By following these tips, ideas, and care tips, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your features, matches your lifestyle, and boosts your confidence. Remember to embrace your unique beauty and enjoy experimenting with different styles and accessories!
