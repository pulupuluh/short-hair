How To Style Long Thick Hair Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're one of the lucky guys with long, thick hair, you know how much attention it can require. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll show you how to style long thick hair guys with ease. From choosing the right products to tips and tricks, we'll cover everything you need to know to keep your locks looking their best.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Long Thick Hair Guys
  2. Step by Step Guide

How to Style Long Thick Hair Guys

Before we get started, it's important to note that there is no one "right" way to style long thick hair guys. It all depends on your personal preferences, face shape, and hair type. However, there are a few general tips that can help you achieve the look you're going for.

The first step in styling long thick hair is to choose the right products. Look for products that are specifically designed for your hair type, such as thickening shampoos and conditioners. You may also want to invest in a quality styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to help keep your hair in place.

When it comes to styling, there are a few different options to consider. You can wear your hair down, slick it back, or tie it up in a bun or ponytail. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you style your long thick hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as pomade or wax.
  3. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a comb or your fingers to style it into your desired look.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are a few tips and tricks to help you style your long thick hair:

  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place without weighing down your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long thick hair guys:

Q: How often should I wash my long thick hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day to prevent your scalp from producing too much oil.

Q: Can I use a hair mask on my long thick hair?

A: Yes, using a deep conditioning mask once a week can help keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Q: Should I use a brush or a comb to style my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and the style you're going for. A brush can help add volume and lift, while a comb can help create a sleeker look.

Hair Care for Long Thick Hair

In addition to styling, it's important to take care of your long thick hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, as they can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your long thick hair guys with ease. Remember, it's all about finding what works for you and your hair type. So experiment, have fun, and rock that long, thick mane!
