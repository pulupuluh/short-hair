How To Get My Hair Curly Black Male Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine May 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you a black male with straight hair but want to achieve curly locks? You're not alone! Many black men with relaxed hair want to know how to get their hair curly. In this article, we'll discuss some effective tips and tricks to help you achieve curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Get Curly Hair

There are several ways to get curly hair, but not all of them may work for you. Here are some popular methods:

Perm

A perm is a chemical treatment that can make your hair curly. It involves applying a solution to your hair that breaks the bonds that keep it straight. The hair is then wrapped around rods, and another solution is applied to set the curls. The process may take a few hours, and the results can last for several months.

Curling Iron

You can also use a curling iron to create curls. This method is a bit more time-consuming, but it can give you the exact look you want. To use a curling iron, section your hair and wrap each section around the iron. Hold for a few seconds, then release. Repeat until all of your hair is curled.

Twist Out

A twist out is a natural way to create curls. To do a twist out, divide your hair into sections and twist each section. Leave the twists in overnight, then unravel them in the morning. You can use a bit of product to help define the curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you want to try a perm or curling iron, it's best to go to a professional stylist. However, if you want to try a twist out, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash your hair and condition it.
  2. Apply a styling product to your damp hair.
  3. Divide your hair into sections.
  4. Take a section of hair and twist it tightly from the root to the end.
  5. Repeat until all of your hair is twisted.
  6. Leave the twists in overnight or until your hair is completely dry.
  7. Unravel the twists gently.
  8. Use a pick or your fingers to separate the curls.
  9. Apply a bit of product to help define the curls.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Once you have curly hair, you'll need to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help define your curls.
  • Sleep with a satin or silk pillowcase to avoid frizz.

FAQs

Can I get curly hair without using chemicals?

Yes, you can use a twist out or other natural methods to create curls.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last for several months, but the exact length of time will depend on your hair type and how well you take care of it.

Can I still straighten my hair after getting a perm?

It's best to wait at least two weeks before using any heat styling tools after getting a perm. Even then, you'll need to be careful not to damage your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Whether you have curly hair or not, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often.
  • Condition your hair every time you wash it.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can dry out your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve curly hair as a black male with relaxed hair. Whether you opt for a perm, curling iron, or twist out, be sure to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
