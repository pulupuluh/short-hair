How To Dry Very Short Hair Tips Tricks And Techniques

Written by Aprilia Sep 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Drying short hair can be tricky, especially if you're dealing with very short hair. But with the right tools, techniques, and some practice, you can easily dry your hair in a matter of minutes. In this article, we'll show you how to dry very short hair and give you some tips and tricks to make the process easier and more efficient.

How to Dry Very Short Hair

Drying very short hair can be a bit challenging, as there's not much hair to work with. But the good news is that it doesn't take much time or effort to dry your hair if you follow the right steps. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Towel Dry

After shampooing and conditioning your hair, gently pat it dry with a towel. Don't rub your hair vigorously, as this can cause breakage and damage. Instead, use a soft, microfiber towel to absorb excess moisture.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, it's important to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy. Apply the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair, making sure to cover all areas.

Step 3: Use a Blow Dryer

Using a blow dryer is the most efficient way to dry very short hair. Use a small, lightweight blow dryer with a concentrator nozzle to direct the airflow. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, using your fingers to lift and separate your hair as you go.

Step 4: Finish with a Styling Product

Once your hair is dry, you can apply a styling product to add texture and hold. Use a small amount of product and work it through your hair with your fingers. Be careful not to use too much, as this can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.

Tips for Drying Very Short Hair

Here are some tips to help make the process of drying very short hair easier and more efficient:

  • Use a small, lightweight blow dryer with a concentrator nozzle
  • Apply a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools
  • Use your fingers to lift and separate your hair as you dry it
  • Don't use too much styling product, as this can make your hair look greasy or weighed down
  • Be gentle when towel drying your hair, and avoid rubbing it vigorously

FAQ

How long does it take to dry very short hair?

It usually takes about 5-10 minutes to dry very short hair, depending on your hair type and the tools you're using.

Can I air dry very short hair?

Yes, you can air dry very short hair if you prefer not to use a blow dryer. Just be sure to towel dry your hair thoroughly before letting it air dry.

What's the best blow dryer for very short hair?

Look for a small, lightweight blow dryer with a concentrator nozzle. This will help you to direct the airflow and dry your hair quickly and efficiently.

Hair Care for Very Short Hair

Taking care of very short hair is easy if you follow a few simple tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Use a styling product to add texture and hold

With these tips and techniques, you can easily dry very short hair and keep it healthy and looking great. Remember to be gentle with your hair, and avoid using too much heat or styling products. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at drying very short hair in no time!
