Will My Face Shape Look Good With Short Hair

Written by Oliver Jun 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Short hair has been trending for years, and it's not going anywhere. However, before making a big chop, many people ask themselves, "will my face shape look good with short hair?" There are many factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle, and your face shape is one of the most important ones. In this article, we'll discuss how to determine whether short hair will flatter your face shape, how to choose the right short hairstyle, and how to take care of it.

Description

Short hair is a bold and trendy choice, but not all face shapes are created equal. Some short hairstyles may flatter oval faces but not heart-shaped or square faces. That's why it's essential to determine your face shape first before choosing a short hairstyle.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a look at your face's outline.

Oval Face Shape

If your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead is slightly wider than your chin, you have an oval face shape.

Round Face Shape

If your face is as wide as it is long, and you have round cheekbones and a soft jawline, you have a round face shape.

Square Face Shape

If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, and you have a strong jawline, you have a square face shape.

Heart Face Shape

If your forehead is wider than your cheeks and chin, and you have a pointy chin, you have a heart-shaped face.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have high cheekbones, a narrow forehead, and a small chin, you have a diamond face shape.

Oblong Face Shape

If your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have an oblong face shape.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the right short hairstyle. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Research

Research different short hairstyles that flatter your face shape. Look for inspiration in magazines, social media, or ask your hairstylist for recommendations.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Short hairstyles may look different depending on your hair texture. If you have curly or wavy hair, choose a style that enhances your natural texture. If you have straight hair, consider adding layers or bangs to create volume and movement.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

Short hairstyles require more maintenance than long hair. If you're always on the go, choose a low-maintenance style that doesn't require frequent trips to the salon.

Step 4: Try Virtual Makeover Tools

Many websites and apps offer virtual makeover tools that allow you to try on different hairstyles without committing to a cut. Use these tools to get an idea of how different short hairstyles will look on you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a short hairstyle that emphasizes your best features.
  • Consider your skin tone when choosing a hair color.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQs

Q: Will a pixie cut look good on a round face?

A: Yes! A pixie cut can elongate a round face by creating height and volume on top.

Q: Can I pull off a short hairstyle if I have a big forehead?

A: Absolutely! Choose a style with side-swept bangs or layers that frame your face.

Q: Do I need to use different hair products for short hair?

A: Short hair requires different products than long hair. Invest in a good texturizing spray, pomade, or wax to create definition and hold.

Hair Care

Short hair may require less shampoo and conditioner than long hair, but it still needs regular maintenance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day or as needed.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.

Now that you have all the information, it's time to make the big chop! Remember, the most important thing is to choose a short hairstyle that makes you feel confident and stylish.
