Should Oval Faces Have Bangs A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jun 06, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have an oval-shaped face, you might be wondering whether bangs are a good idea. Bangs can be a great way to change up your look and add some interest to your hairstyle. However, not all bangs are created equal, and what works for one face shape might not work for another. In this guide, we’ll explore whether oval faces should have bangs, and if so, what types of bangs work best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Bangs for Oval Faces
  • Tips for Styling Bangs on Oval Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Bangs on Oval Faces

Description

Oval faces are characterized by their balanced proportions and gently rounded shape. Those with oval faces have a forehead that is slightly wider than their chin, and their cheekbones are typically high and well-defined. Bangs can be a great way to add some texture and volume to the hair, but not all bangs will work for oval faces.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in deciding whether or not to get bangs is to determine your face shape. To do this, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back away from your face. Look at the shape of your face and compare it to the following descriptions:

  • Oval: Face length is greater than the width of the cheekbones, and forehead is wider than the jawline.
  • Round: Prominent cheekbones and a rounded chin.
  • Square: Prominent jaw and forehead, with roughly equal width and length.
  • Heart: Wide forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow jawline.
  • Diamond: Narrow forehead and jawline, with wide cheekbones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Bangs for Oval Faces

Now that you’ve determined that you have an oval face, you might be wondering what types of bangs work best. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Consider your hair texture: If you have fine hair, you might want to opt for wispy, feathered bangs. If your hair is thick, blunt bangs might be a better option.
  2. Think about your forehead size: If you have a larger forehead, you might want to go for a thicker, blunt bang. If you have a smaller forehead, wispy bangs might be a better choice.
  3. Consider your face shape: While oval faces can pull off many different types of bangs, it’s important to consider other features of your face. If you have a long face, for example, you might want to avoid super short bangs that will make your face look even longer.
  4. Ask your stylist: Your hairstylist is an expert in all things hair, so don’t be afraid to ask for their opinion. They can help you determine what will work best based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Tips for Styling Bangs on Oval Faces

Once you’ve chosen your bangs, it’s time to style them! Here are some tips:

  • Blow dry: Use a round brush and blow dryer to style your bangs, directing the air downward to avoid frizz.
  • Straighten: If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you might want to straighten your bangs with a flat iron for a sleeker look.
  • Use product: Use a small amount of hair gel or pomade to keep your bangs in place throughout the day.
  • Trim regularly: To keep your bangs looking fresh and neat, trim them every 6-8 weeks.

FAQs

Can oval faces pull off blunt bangs?

Yes, oval faces can definitely pull off blunt bangs! Just make sure to consider your forehead size and hair texture before making the decision.

What types of bangs should oval faces avoid?

Oval faces can pull off many different types of bangs, but they should avoid super short bangs that will make their face look even longer.

Can oval faces have side-swept bangs?

Yes, side-swept bangs can be a great option for oval faces! They can add some interest to the hair without overwhelming the face.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs on Oval Faces

Now that you’ve got your bangs, it’s important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash regularly: Wash your hair and bangs regularly to keep them looking clean and fresh.
  • Use dry shampoo: If you’re in a rush or don’t have time to wash your hair, use dry shampoo to soak up any excess oil.
  • Avoid touching: Try not to touch your bangs too much throughout the day, as this can transfer oil and dirt from your hands to your hair.
  • Protect from heat: Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your bangs.

In conclusion, oval faces can definitely pull off bangs! Just make sure to consider your hair texture, forehead size, and personal style before making the decision. And once you’ve got your bangs, make sure to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Happy hairstyling!
