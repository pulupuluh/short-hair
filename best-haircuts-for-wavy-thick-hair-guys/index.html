Best Haircuts For Wavy Thick Hair Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Apr 14, 2023 · 7 min read

Wavy thick hair can be a blessing and a curse for men. While it provides volume and texture, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Finding the right haircut for wavy thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right approach, you can achieve a stylish and effortless look. In this article, we’ll take a look at the best haircuts for wavy thick hair guys, how to achieve them, and how to maintain them.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut for Wavy Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Wavy Thick Hair

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Wavy Thick Hair

The first step in choosing the right haircut for wavy thick hair is to consider your face shape. Different face shapes require different haircuts to achieve the most flattering look. For example, a square face may benefit from a shorter haircut, while a round face may benefit from a longer style with layers.

Once you’ve determined your face shape, consider the texture and thickness of your hair. Wavy thick hair may be best suited for a layered haircut, which can help to reduce volume and create definition. Other haircuts that work well for wavy thick hair include the undercut, the messy crop, and the quiff.

Best Haircuts for Wavy Thick Hair Guys

Here are some of the best haircuts for wavy thick hair guys:

  • The Undercut
  • The Messy Crop
  • The Quiff
  • The Classic Side Part
  • The Textured Fringe

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Haircut

Now that you’ve chosen the right haircut for your wavy thick hair, it’s time to achieve the perfect look. Follow these steps to get the most out of your haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is designed for wavy hair. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and reduce frizz.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to create texture and hold.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray to add volume and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you’ve achieved the perfect haircut for your wavy thick hair, it’s important to maintain it properly to keep it looking great. Here are some tips to help you maintain your haircut:

  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best haircut for wavy thick hair?

The best haircut for wavy thick hair depends on your face shape and personal style. Some popular haircuts for wavy thick hair include the undercut, the messy crop, and the quiff.

How can I style my wavy thick hair?

You can style your wavy thick hair by using a high-quality styling product, such as a pomade or wax, and blow drying your hair with a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural waves.

How do I maintain my wavy thick hair?

You can maintain your wavy thick hair by visiting your barber or stylist regularly for trims, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and protecting your hair from environmental factors such as the sun.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Thick Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your wavy thick hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with warm water, not hot water, to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos and conditioners that contain sulfates, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush which can cause breakage.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

With the right haircut and proper hair care, wavy thick hair can be a stylish and manageable asset. Follow these tips and you’ll be on your way to achieving the perfect look for your hair type.
