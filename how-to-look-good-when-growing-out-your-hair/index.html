How To Look Good When Growing Out Your Hair

Written by Robby Jun 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are growing out your hair, you might be wondering how to look good during the process. It can be a challenging time, as your hair might not be at the length you want it to be, and it might not be styled the way you want it to be. However, there are ways to make the growing out process easier and more stylish. In this article, we will share some tips on how to look good when growing out your hair.

How to Look Good When Growing Out Your Hair

The first thing to remember when growing out your hair is that patience is key. Growing out your hair takes time, and it might not look the way you want it to right away. However, there are some things you can do to make the growing out process more stylish and manageable.

1. Get Regular Trims

While you are growing out your hair, it's important to get regular trims. This might sound counterintuitive, but regular trims can actually help your hair grow faster and healthier. Trimming your hair every six to eight weeks will help prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down the growth process.

2. Experiment with Hair Accessories

When your hair is in the awkward growing out stage, hair accessories can be your best friend. Try experimenting with headbands, hair clips, and scarves to add some style to your hair. You can also try braiding your hair or putting it up in a bun to add some variety to your look.

3. Try Different Hairstyles

While you are growing out your hair, it's a great time to try out different hairstyles. Experiment with different lengths and styles to see what works best for you. You might find that a shorter haircut or a different style is more flattering during the growing out process.

Step by Step

Step 1: Get Regular Trims

As mentioned earlier, getting regular trims is important when growing out your hair. Make sure to schedule an appointment with your stylist every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Step 2: Experiment with Hair Accessories

Try adding headbands, hair clips, and scarves to your hair to add some style and variety to your look. You can also try braids or buns to switch up your hairstyle.

Step 3: Try Different Hairstyles

Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you. This might mean trying out a shorter haircut or different styles while your hair is growing out.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind while growing out your hair:

  • Be patient - growing out your hair takes time
  • Use styling products to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Consider getting a haircut to remove damaged or split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished
  • Try using a silk pillowcase to prevent breakage and damage to your hair while you sleep

FAQ

Q: How long does it take to grow out hair?

A: The length of time it takes to grow out hair depends on several factors, including genetics, hair type, and how quickly your hair grows. On average, hair grows about half an inch per month.

Q: Can I still style my hair while it's growing out?

A: Yes! While your hair is growing out, you can still experiment with different hairstyles and products to keep your hair looking stylish and healthy.

Hair Care

While you are growing out your hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Make sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Also, consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished and healthy.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can look good and feel confident throughout the growing out process. Remember to be patient and enjoy the journey!
