How To Tell What Haircut Suits You Male A Comprehensive Guide

Are you tired of getting a haircut that doesn't suit you? It's time to change that. If you're wondering how to tell what haircut suits you male, this guide is for you. We'll walk you through the steps to determine your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle to find the perfect haircut for you.

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in determining what haircut suits you male is to determine your face shape. There are four main face shapes: round, square, oval, and heart-shaped. To determine your face shape, look in the mirror and use a tape measure or ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, compare the measurements to the following:

  • Round: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, with a square jawline
  • Oval: Forehead is wider than the cheekbones, with a rounded jawline
  • Heart-shaped: Forehead is wider than the cheekbones, with a pointed chin

Once you determine your face shape, you can then choose a haircut that will complement it.

Step 2: Analyze Your Hair Type

The next step is to analyze your hair type. There are several different hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and kinky. Each hair type requires a different type of cut to look its best. For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to consider a layered cut to help control frizz and enhance your curls. If you have straight hair, a blunt cut may be more suitable.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle also plays a role in determining what haircut suits you male. If you're an active person who spends a lot of time outdoors, you may want a low-maintenance haircut that doesn't require a lot of styling. On the other hand, if you work in a professional setting, you may want a more polished look that requires regular styling.

Step 4: Research Different Haircuts

Once you determine your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, it's time to research different haircuts. Look at pictures of celebrities or models with similar face shapes and hair types to get an idea of what may look good on you. You can also search online for inspiration and bring pictures with you when you go to the salon.

Step 5: Consult with a Professional

Finally, consult with a professional stylist. They can provide you with valuable advice on what haircut will suit you best based on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. They can also recommend products to help you maintain your new haircut.

Tips for Finding the Perfect Haircut

  • Don't be afraid to try something new
  • Bring pictures to the salon to show your stylist what you're looking for
  • Consider your facial hair when choosing a haircut
  • Think about the amount of time you're willing to spend styling your hair each day
  • Be honest with your stylist about your hair concerns

Frequently Asked Questions

What haircut suits a round face?

A round face looks best with haircuts that add height and length to the face. This can be achieved with a layered cut or a side-swept fringe.

What haircut suits a square face?

A square face looks best with haircuts that soften the jawline. This can be achieved with a layered cut or a side part.

What haircut suits an oval face?

An oval face can pull off just about any haircut, but a layered cut or a side-swept fringe can enhance the face's natural shape.

What haircut suits a heart-shaped face?

A heart-shaped face looks best with haircuts that balance the forehead and chin. This can be achieved with a layered cut or a side-swept fringe.

Hair Care Tips

Once you find the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh
  • Use hair products like styling cream or gel to enhance your haircut
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils

Now that you know how to tell what haircut suits you male, it's time to find the perfect haircut for you. Follow these steps, consult with a professional, and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
