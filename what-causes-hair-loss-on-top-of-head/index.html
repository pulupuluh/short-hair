What Causes Hair Loss On Top Of Head

Written by Daniel Aug 25, 2023 Â· 8 min read

When it comes to hair loss, it can be frustrating and overwhelming to figure out what is causing it. Hair loss on the top of the head in particular can be a common problem for many people. In this article, we will explore some of the possible causes of hair loss on top of the head and offer some tips on how to prevent it.

Table of Contents

  • Genetics
  • Aging
  • Hormonal Changes
  • Stress
  • Diet and Nutritional Deficiencies
  • Medications
  • Hairstyles and Hair Treatments

  • Tips for Preventing Hair Loss
  • FAQs

Genetics

One of the most common causes of hair loss on top of the head is genetics. Male pattern baldness, also known as androgenetic alopecia, is an inherited condition that affects many men. Women can also be affected by female pattern hair loss, which is also genetic. These conditions are caused by a combination of genetics and hormones, and they can cause hair to thin and eventually fall out on the top of the head.

Aging

As we age, our bodies go through many changes, and one of those changes can be hair loss. As we get older, our hair follicles can shrink, and our hair can become thinner and weaker. This can lead to hair loss on the top of the head, as well as other areas of the scalp.

Hormonal Changes

Hormonal changes in the body can also contribute to hair loss on top of the head. This can be caused by conditions such as pregnancy, menopause, or thyroid problems. Hormonal imbalances can cause hair to become thin and brittle, and eventually fall out.

Stress

Stress can also be a factor in hair loss. When we are under stress, our bodies can produce hormones that can disrupt the hair growth cycle. This can cause hair to fall out prematurely, and can also cause the hair on the top of the head to become thin and weak.

Diet and Nutritional Deficiencies

What we eat can also have an impact on the health of our hair. A diet that is lacking in certain nutrients, such as iron, protein, and vitamins, can lead to hair loss on the top of the head. Eating a healthy diet that is rich in these nutrients can help to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss.

Medications

Some medications can also contribute to hair loss on the top of the head. This can include medications used to treat cancer, arthritis, and depression, among others. If you are experiencing hair loss and are taking medications, it is important to talk to your doctor to see if they could be causing the problem.

Hairstyles and Hair Treatments

The way we style our hair can also contribute to hair loss on the top of the head. Tight hairstyles, such as braids and ponytails, can cause tension on the hair follicles and lead to hair loss. Chemical treatments, such as perms and hair dyes, can also be damaging to the hair and cause it to become weak and brittle.

Illnesses and Medical Conditions

Finally, some illnesses and medical conditions can also contribute to hair loss on the top of the head. This can include conditions such as alopecia areata, lupus, and diabetes, among others. If you are experiencing hair loss and are also dealing with a medical condition, it is important to talk to your doctor to see if there is a connection.

Tips for Preventing Hair Loss

If you are experiencing hair loss on the top of your head, there are some things you can do to help prevent it:

  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that is rich in nutrients that promote hair growth.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles and hair treatments that can damage the hair.
  • Talk to your doctor about any medications you are taking that could be causing hair loss.
  • Reduce stress in your life through relaxation techniques, exercise, or therapy.
  • Consider using a hair growth shampoo or supplement to help promote hair growth.

FAQs

Q: Is hair loss on the top of the head permanent?

A: Hair loss on the top of the head can be permanent if it is caused by genetic factors. However, if it is caused by other factors, such as stress or nutritional deficiencies, it may be reversible.

Q: Can hair loss on the top of the head be prevented?

A: Yes, there are steps you can take to help prevent hair loss on the top of the head. Eating a healthy diet, avoiding damaging hairstyles and hair treatments, and reducing stress in your life can all be helpful.

Q: Are there any treatments for hair loss on the top of the head?

A: Yes, there are several treatments for hair loss on the top of the head, including medications and hair transplantation surgery. It is important to talk to your doctor to determine the best treatment option for you.
