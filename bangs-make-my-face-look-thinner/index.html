Bangs Make My Face Look Thinner A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Apr 19, 2023 · 7 min read

If you are someone who is looking for a new hairstyle that can make your face look thinner, then you should definitely consider getting bangs. Bangs can be a great way to transform your look and make your face appear slimmer. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about bangs, including how they can make your face look thinner, how to style them, and how to take care of them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Bangs
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling Bangs
  • Tips for Styling Bangs
  • FAQs About Bangs
  • Hair Care for Bangs

Description

Bangs are a great way to frame your face and draw attention to your eyes. They can also make your face look thinner by creating the illusion of a longer face shape. Bangs work particularly well on people with round or square face shapes, as they can help to soften the features and create a more oval face shape.

There are many different types of bangs to choose from, including blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, and curtain bangs. The type of bangs you choose will depend on your face shape, hair type, and personal style preferences.

How to Get Bangs

If you are interested in getting bangs to make your face look thinner, then the first step is to find a hairstyle that you like. You can browse through magazines, Pinterest, or Instagram to find inspiration for your new look.

Once you have found a hairstyle that you like, you should book an appointment with a professional hairstylist. They will be able to advise you on the best type of bangs for your face shape and hair type, and they will also be able to cut your bangs to perfection.

Types of Bangs

There are many different types of bangs to choose from, including:

  • Blunt bangs
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Curtain bangs
  • Micro bangs
  • Wispy bangs

The type of bangs you choose will depend on your face shape, hair type, and personal style preferences. Blunt bangs work well on people with oval or heart-shaped faces, while side-swept bangs work well on people with round or square faces.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Bangs

Styling bangs can be tricky, but with a little bit of practice, you can master the art of bangs styling. Here is a step-by-step guide to styling bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your bangs, such as a styling cream or pomade.
  3. Blow dry your bangs using a round brush, directing the hair downwards towards your face.
  4. If you have side-swept bangs, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips for Styling Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your bangs:

  • Blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to lay.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your bangs.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles of bangs.

FAQs About Bangs

Do bangs make your face look thinner?

Yes, bangs can make your face look thinner by creating the illusion of a longer face shape.

What type of bangs work best for a round face?

Side-swept bangs work well for people with round faces, as they can help to elongate the face shape.

What type of bangs work best for a square face?

Soft, wispy bangs work well for people with square faces, as they can help to soften the features and create a more oval face shape.

Hair Care for Bangs

Here are some tips for taking care of your bangs:

  • Wash your bangs frequently to keep them looking fresh.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to absorb any excess oil.
  • Don't overuse hot tools on your bangs, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Overall, bangs can be a great way to transform your look and make your face appear slimmer. By following these tips and taking good care of your bangs, you can enjoy a stylish and flattering hairstyle for years to come.
