Best Parting For Square Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't flatter your square face shape? It's time to embrace your features and find the best parting for square face shape that suits you. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding the perfect parting for your face shape, along with some tips and tricks to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.

Description

Square face shape is defined by a wide forehead, strong jawline, and angular features. The goal of finding the best parting is to soften the angles and create balance. A well-chosen parting can make your face look longer, slimmer, and more feminine.

How To Find the Best Parting for Square Face Shape

There are three main factors to consider when choosing the best parting:

1. The Angle of Your Jawline

If you have a strong jawline, a center parting can make it look even more prominent. Instead, go for a side parting to soften the angles and draw attention away from the jawline. If your jawline is less pronounced, you can experiment with both center and side partings.

2. The Shape of Your Forehead

If you have a high forehead, a center parting can make it look even higher. Instead, opt for a side parting that will create the illusion of a lower hairline. If your forehead is small, a center parting can make it look more prominent. In this case, a side parting will balance out your features.

3. The Length of Your Hair

The length of your hair can also influence the best parting for you. If you have short hair, a side parting can create the illusion of volume and movement. If you have long hair, a center parting can make your face look longer and slimmer. However, if you have a square face shape, it's best to avoid very long hair as it can make your face appear even wider.

Step by Step Guide to Finding the Best Parting for Square Face Shape

1. Start by determining the shape of your face. Stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner.

2. Identify the widest part of your face. This is usually the area around the cheekbones.

3. Determine the angle of your jawline. Is it square or more rounded?

4. Measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.

5. Analyze your hair texture and length. Is it fine or thick? Short, medium, or long?

6. Experiment with different partings, starting with a side parting. Take a few selfies and compare the results.

7. Try a center parting and see how it looks. Again, take some photos and compare.

8. Don't be afraid to ask for advice from a hairstylist. They can give you personalized recommendations based on your hair type, face shape, and personal style.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair's Health

Now that you've found the best parting for your square face shape, it's important to take care of your hair's health. Here are some tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant first.

- Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

- Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have a square face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs if you have a square face shape. However, it's best to avoid blunt, straight-across bangs that can make your face look boxy. Instead, opt for soft, side-swept bangs that will flatter your features.

Q: Is it necessary to follow the rules of face shape when choosing a parting?

A: No, it's not necessary to follow the rules of face shape when choosing a parting. However, these guidelines can help you find a style that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.

Conclusion

Choosing the best parting for square face shape is all about creating balance and enhancing your natural features. By following the guidelines we've provided, you can find a style that flatters your unique face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
