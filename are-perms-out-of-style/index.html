Are Perms Out Of Style A Comprehensive Guide For 2023

Written by Petter Feb 22, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Perms have been a popular hairstyle for decades, but as fashion and beauty trends continue to shift, many people are wondering if perms are still in style. In this guide, we'll explore the current state of perms and answer some of the most common questions about this iconic hairstyle.

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How to Get a Perm
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Perm
  4. Tips for Maintaining a Perm
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care for Permed Hair

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the structure of the hair to create curls or waves. Perms can be done on any hair type, but they are most commonly associated with thick, straight hair. Perms became popular in the 1980s and 1990s and have since gone through phases of popularity and decline.

Some people love perms for their low-maintenance curls and waves, while others avoid them because of the damage they can cause to hair. So, are perms out of style in 2023? The answer is not straightforward, as it depends on several factors.

How to Get a Perm

If you're considering getting a perm, the first step is to find a reputable hair salon or stylist who specializes in perms. They will be able to assess your hair type and recommend the best type of perm for you.

The next step is to prepare your hair for the perm. This typically involves washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and then conditioning the ends to prevent damage. Your stylist will then wrap sections of your hair around perm rods and apply a chemical solution to break down the hair bonds and create the curls or waves.

After the solution has been applied, your stylist will rinse your hair and apply a neutralizer to set the curls. Finally, they will remove the perm rods and style your hair as desired.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Perm

Getting a perm is a multi-step process that requires careful preparation and execution. Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a perm:

  1. Find a reputable hair salon or stylist who specializes in perms.
  2. Prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo and conditioning the ends.
  3. Section your hair and wrap it around perm rods.
  4. Apply a chemical solution to break down the hair bonds and create the curls or waves.
  5. Rinse your hair and apply a neutralizer to set the curls.
  6. Remove the perm rods and style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining a Perm

Perms require some maintenance to keep the curls or waves looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining a perm:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a perm.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage.
  • Avoid heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage the curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep the ends healthy.

FAQ

Are perms out of style in 2023?

Perms are not necessarily out of style in 2023, but they are not as popular as they were in the past. Many people still enjoy the low-maintenance curls and waves that perms provide, but others prefer to avoid the damage that perms can cause.

What hair types are best suited for perms?

Perms can be done on any hair type, but they are most commonly associated with thick, straight hair. People with thin or damaged hair may want to avoid perms, as they can cause further damage.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from three to six months, depending on the individual's hair type and how well they take care of their hair after the perm.

Hair Care for Permed Hair

Permed hair requires some special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for caring for permed hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage.
  • Avoid heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage the curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep the ends healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or coloring agents on permed hair, as they can cause damage and breakage.

In conclusion, perms are not necessarily out of style in 2023, but they are not as popular as they were in the past. Whether or not to get a perm is a personal choice that depends on factors like hair type, lifestyle, and desired level of maintenance. If you do decide to get a perm, be sure to choose a reputable stylist and follow the tips for maintenance and care to keep your curls looking their best.
