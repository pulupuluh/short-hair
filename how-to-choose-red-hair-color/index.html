How To Choose Red Hair Color A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you thinking of changing your hair color? Red hair color is a bold and beautiful choice that can make you stand out from the crowd. But choosing the right shade of red can be overwhelming. With so many options available, it can be challenging to decide which one would suit you the best. In this article, we will guide you through the process of choosing the perfect red hair color that complements your skin tone and personality.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose Red Hair Color
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips to Consider
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Red Hair

Description

Red hair color is a popular choice among women who want to add a touch of drama to their look. There are different shades of red available, from natural-looking auburn to bold and bright copper. Choosing the right shade of red depends on your skin tone, eye color, and personality. Red hair color can be high maintenance, as it tends to fade faster than other colors. But with the right care and maintenance, it can look stunning and vibrant for a long time.

How to Choose Red Hair Color

Choosing the perfect red hair color can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some tips to help you pick the right shade:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a crucial role in determining which red hair color would suit you the best. If you have a warm undertone, go for shades like auburn, copper, or strawberry blonde. If you have a cool undertone, try shades like cherry red, burgundy, or deep red.

Think About Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also influence which shade of red would look best on you. For example, if you have blue eyes, copper or strawberry blonde can make your eyes pop. If you have brown eyes, deep red or burgundy can create a striking contrast.

Consider Your Natural Hair Color

If you have naturally dark hair, going for a bright red shade can be challenging. In this case, try auburn or burgundy, which will blend well with your natural hair color. If you have light-colored hair, you have more options to experiment with, from copper to bright red.

Think About Your Personality

Choosing a hair color is a personal choice, and it should reflect your personality. If you're bold and daring, go for a bright and vibrant shade of red. If you're more laid-back, try a subtle and natural-looking shade of red.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the perfect red hair color:

  1. Determine your skin tone.
  2. Consider your eye color.
  3. Think about your natural hair color.
  4. Choose a shade of red that complements your skin tone and eye color.
  5. Consider your personality and lifestyle.
  6. Consult with a professional stylist.
  7. Do a patch test before applying the color to your hair.
  8. Follow the instructions on the hair dye box carefully.
  9. Maintain your red hair color with the right care and products.

Tips to Consider

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect red hair color:

  • Experiment with temporary hair color before committing to a permanent one.
  • Take inspiration from celebrities with red hair, but keep in mind that what looks good on them may not look good on you.
  • Consider the season and occasion when choosing your hair color.
  • Choose a hair color that complements your wardrobe.
  • Invest in color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair color.

FAQs

Is red hair color high maintenance?

Yes, red hair color tends to fade faster than other colors, and it requires frequent touch-ups. It's also essential to use color-safe products to maintain the vibrancy of the color.

What is the best red hair color for fair skin?

For fair skin, shades like strawberry blonde, copper, and light auburn can look beautiful.

Can I dye my hair red at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair red at home, but it's recommended to consult with a professional stylist to determine the right shade and to ensure that the color is applied evenly.

Hair Care Tips for Red Hair

Once you've chosen your perfect red hair color, it's essential to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy. Here are some hair care tips for red hair:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip away the color.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage your hair and make the color fade faster.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Choosing the perfect red hair color can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your skin tone, eye color, natural hair color, and personality, you can find a shade that complements your unique features. With the right care and maintenance, your red hair can look stunning and vibrant for a long time.
