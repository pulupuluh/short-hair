How To Style Super Short Pixie Hair

Description

If you have decided to go for the super short pixie hair, congratulations! This is an edgy, trendy and low maintenance look that suits anyone with the right attitude. However, you may wonder how to style such a short cut, considering you have limited length to play with. Fear not, with the right technique and the right products, you can create various looks for any occasion. In this article, we will guide you through the best ways to style super short pixie hair.

How to Style Super Short Pixie Hair

The key to styling super short pixie hair is to add texture and volume to the hair. You can achieve this through a variety of techniques, such as blow-drying, straightening, curling, or using hair products. Depending on your preference, you can create a sleek, messy, or wavy look.

1. Blow-drying

Blow-drying is a great way to add volume to your super short pixie hair. Use a round brush to lift the hair at the roots and blow-dry in an upward direction. You can also use a diffuser attachment to create a more natural, tousled look. For added texture, apply a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying.

2. Straightening

Straightening your super short pixie hair can create a sleek, polished look. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair from the roots to the ends. Apply a heat protectant spray before straightening to prevent damage. You can also add texture by using a texturizing spray or wax.

3. Curling

Curling your super short pixie hair can create a playful, feminine look. Use a curling iron or wand to curl small sections of hair in different directions. For added texture, use a sea salt spray or pomade to define the curls.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style super short pixie hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair.

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair gently.

Step 2: Apply a hair product.

Apply a hair product that suits your hair type and the look you want to achieve. For example, if you want to add volume, use a volumizing mousse or spray. If you want to create a sleek look, use a smoothing serum or cream. If you want to add texture, use a texturizing spray or wax.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair.

Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots and blow-dry in an upward direction. Alternatively, use a diffuser attachment to create a more natural, tousled look.

Step 4: Style your hair.

Use a flat iron, curling iron or wand to create the desired look. Apply a finishing spray or wax to hold the style.

Tips for Styling Super Short Pixie Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your super short pixie hair:

  • Use a high-quality hair product that suits your hair type and the look you want to achieve.
  • Blow-dry your hair in an upward direction to add volume.
  • Straighten your hair for a sleek, polished look.
  • Curl your hair for a playful, feminine look.
  • Use a texturizing spray or wax to add texture.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what suits you best.

FAQs

1. Is super short pixie hair suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, super short pixie hair can suit all face shapes, as long as the cut is tailored to your individual features.

2. Can I style my super short pixie hair with a hairdryer?

Yes, blow-drying your super short pixie hair is a great way to add volume and texture.

3. Can I straighten or curl my super short pixie hair?

Yes, you can straighten or curl your super short pixie hair to create different looks.

Hair Care Tips for Super Short Pixie Hair

To keep your super short pixie hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these styling and hair care tips, you can rock your super short pixie hair with confidence and style. Experiment with different looks and have fun with your new edgy, trendy and low maintenance haircut!
