Does Curtain Bangs Look Good On Thick Hair

Written by Petter Aug 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you considering getting curtain bangs but hesitant because you have thick hair? You're not alone. Many people with thick hair wonder if curtain bangs will suit them. In this article, we'll answer the question: Does curtain bangs look good on thick hair?

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to Get Curtain Bangs
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs on Thick Hair
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Thick Hair with Curtain Bangs

Description

Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle where the bangs are parted in the middle, framing the face on both sides like a curtain. They are low maintenance and versatile, making them suitable for various face shapes and hair types.

Thick hair, on the other hand, can be challenging to style as it tends to be heavy and voluminous. Many people with thick hair worry that curtain bangs will make their hair look even fuller or too poufy. However, with the right cut and styling techniques, curtain bangs can complement thick hair beautifully.

How to Get Curtain Bangs

If you're considering getting curtain bangs, it's essential to choose a stylist who specializes in this type of cut. They will be able to advise you on the best length and shape for your face and hair type. It's also crucial to consider how much maintenance you're willing to commit to, as curtain bangs require regular trimming and styling.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style curtain bangs for thick hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the roots to create volume.
  3. Part your hair in the middle and separate the bangs from the rest of your hair.
  4. Blow-dry the bangs using a small round brush, curling them inward to create a soft, face-framing shape.
  5. Apply a small amount of texturizing spray or pomade to the ends of the bangs for a piecey, tousled look.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs on Thick Hair

Here are some tips to keep your curtain bangs looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep the bangs at the right length and prevent them from becoming too heavy.
  • Use a dry shampoo or volumizing powder to keep the roots lifted and prevent greasiness.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down the hair.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to see what works best for your face shape.

FAQs

Q: Will curtain bangs make my hair look even thicker?

A: It depends on the length and shape of the bangs. Your stylist can recommend a cut that will complement your hair type and face shape.

Q: How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

A: It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them at the right length and prevent them from becoming too heavy.

Q: Can I style curtain bangs without a blow dryer?

A: Yes, you can air-dry your hair and use a flat iron or curling iron to style the bangs. However, using a blow dryer and round brush can create more volume and shape.

Hair Care for Thick Hair with Curtain Bangs

Thick hair requires special care to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair.
  • Brush your hair gently with a wide-tooth comb to prevent tangles and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.

With the right cut and styling techniques, curtain bangs can look great on thick hair. If you're considering this hairstyle, consult with a stylist who specializes in curtain bangs to find the best look for you. With proper care and maintenance, your curtain bangs will look stylish and flattering for months to come.
