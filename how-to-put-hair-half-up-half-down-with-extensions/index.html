How To Put Hair Half Up Half Down With Extensions

Written by Petter Feb 26, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and want to try something new? Do you have short or thin hair and want to add some volume? Look no further, because we have the perfect solution for you! In this blog post, we will be teaching you how to put hair half up half down with extensions.

Description

Half up half down hairstyles are a popular choice because they are both elegant and casual. They are perfect for a night out or a day at the beach. By adding extensions, you can achieve the look of a fuller and longer hairstyle. This can be done by using clip-in or tape-in extensions.

How to Put Hair Half Up Half Down with Extensions

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect half up half down hairstyle with extensions:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the hot tools you will be using. Blow dry your hair and straighten it with a flat iron. This will give your hair a sleek look.

Step 2: Clip in the Extensions

If you are using clip-in extensions, clip them in at the crown of your head. Make sure they are secure and blend in with your natural hair. Use a comb to tease the hair at the crown to give it some volume.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Part your hair down the middle and separate the top section from the bottom section. Use a clip to hold the top section in place.

Step 4: Create the Half Up Half Down Style

Gather the bottom section of your hair and tie it into a ponytail. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band to hide it. Use bobby pins to secure the hair in place.

Release the top section of your hair and comb it back. Use a comb to tease the hair at the crown for more volume. Take a small section of hair from each side of your head and bring it back to the ponytail. Secure the hair with bobby pins.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep it in place. Use a straightener to smooth out any flyaways. You can also add some accessories like a headband or a flower crown to complete the look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect half up half down hairstyle:

  • Choose extensions that match your hair color.
  • Make sure the extensions are clean and free of tangles.
  • Tease your hair at the crown to add volume.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color.
  • Experiment with different accessories to add some personality to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Can I use tape-in extensions for this hairstyle?

Yes, you can use tape-in extensions for this hairstyle. However, make sure they are placed securely so they don't show when you tie your hair up.

Do I need to straighten my hair before putting it half up half down?

No, you don't have to straighten your hair. You can leave it curly or wavy for a more natural look. However, if you want a sleek look, straightening your hair is recommended.

How long does this hairstyle take to create?

This hairstyle can take anywhere from 10 to 30 minutes, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially if you use extensions. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid damaging the extensions.
  • Remove the extensions before going to bed.

Now that you know how to put hair half up half down with extensions, you can try this hairstyle for your next event or just for a casual day out. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
