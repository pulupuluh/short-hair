What Is 12Mm In Clippers Everything You Need To Know

Written by Aprilia Jul 21, 2023 · 6 min read

When it comes to men’s grooming, clippers are an essential tool. They help in trimming the hair to a desired length, giving you the perfect haircut. Clippers come in different sizes, and the 12mm clipper is a popular one. In this blog post, we’ll be discussing what is 12mm in clippers and how you can use it for your grooming needs.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to Use 12mm Clippers
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Using 12mm Clippers
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips

Description

12mm clippers refer to the blade size of the clipper. This means that the blade can cut hair to a length of 12mm. Clippers usually come with multiple blade sizes, and the 12mm blade is ideal for trimming hair to a medium length. It is perfect for creating a classic, neat look or a slightly longer hairstyle.

How to Use 12mm Clippers

Using a 12mm clipper is easy, and you don’t need any special skills to do it. However, it is important to have the right technique to get the desired results. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Before using the clippers, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can make it difficult for the clipper to cut evenly. Use a comb to detangle the hair and remove any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Attach the 12mm Blade

Attach the 12mm blade to the clipper. Make sure it is securely attached to avoid any mishaps during the trimming process.

Step 3: Start Trimming

Start trimming your hair from the sides and work your way towards the top. Use short, even strokes to trim the hair. Keep the clipper flat against the head and move it in the direction of the hair growth.

Step 4: Trim the Back of the Head

Use the same technique to trim the back of the head. Start from the bottom and work your way upwards.

Tips for Using 12mm Clippers

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using 12mm clippers:

  • Start with a longer blade and work your way towards a shorter blade if you want a shorter haircut.
  • Use a comb to guide the hair while trimming.
  • Don’t rush the process. Take your time to get the desired results.
  • Clean the clipper blades regularly to avoid any buildup of hair and debris.

FAQs

Q: Can I use 12mm clippers for a buzz cut?

A: No, 12mm clippers are not suitable for a buzz cut. A buzz cut requires much shorter blades, usually between 1mm and 3mm.

Q: Can I use 12mm clippers for my beard?

A: Yes, 12mm clippers can be used for trimming a beard to a medium length.

Hair Care Tips

Trimming your hair with 12mm clippers is just one part of maintaining healthy hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair in great condition:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free from dirt and oil.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hairdryers and straighteners too often as they can damage the hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

Using 12mm clippers is a great way to achieve a classic, neat look or a slightly longer hairstyle. With the right technique and tips, you can use 12mm clippers to get the perfect haircut. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
