How Will I Look With Short Curly Hair

Written by Daniel Apr 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Short curly hair can be a fun and flirty way to change up your look. It can add volume, texture, and definition to your hair, making it a great option for those who want to try something new. But before you take the plunge, you may be wondering how you will look with short curly hair. In this article, we will explore this question and provide you with tips on how to rock this style.

Description

Short curly hair can come in many shapes and sizes. It can be a tight coil, a loose wave, or anything in between. This style is perfect for those who want to add some texture and volume to their hair, but don't want to commit to long-term treatments like perms or relaxers. Short curly hair can also be a low-maintenance option, as it doesn't require as much styling or upkeep as other styles.

How to Rock Short Curly Hair

If you're considering short curly hair, here are some tips on how to rock this style:

1. Know your face shape

Short curly hair can be flattering on any face shape, but it's important to choose a style that compliments your features. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to opt for a style that adds height to your hair to elongate your face.

2. Choose the right length

Short curly hair can come in a variety of lengths, from a pixie cut to a bob. Choose a length that works well with your face shape and hair texture. If you have thick hair, a shorter length may be better to prevent your hair from looking too bulky.

3. Embrace the texture

One of the best things about short curly hair is the texture it adds to your hair. Don't be afraid to embrace this texture and let your curls do their thing. Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and keep them looking their best.

4. Experiment with color

Short curly hair can be a great canvas for experimenting with color. Try adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

Step by Step Guide

If you're ready to take the plunge and try short curly hair, here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Choose your style

Before you head to the salon, decide on the style you want. Look for inspiration in magazines, online, or on social media. Bring pictures with you to show your stylist.

2. Talk to your stylist

Once you've decided on a style, talk to your stylist about your hair texture, face shape, and lifestyle. They can help you choose a style that works best for you.

3. Cut your hair

Once you and your stylist have decided on a style, it's time to cut your hair. Your stylist will use scissors or clippers to cut your hair to the desired length.

4. Define your curls

After your hair is cut, your stylist may use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls. This will help your curls look their best and last longer.

5. Style your hair

Once your hair is cut and defined, it's time to style it. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disturbing your curls. You can also use a curling iron or wand to touch up any areas that need a little extra definition.

Tips for Maintaining Short Curly Hair

Short curly hair can be easy to maintain, but it's important to take care of your curls to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Use a shampoo that is gentle and sulfate-free to prevent your curls from drying out.

2. Moisturize your hair

Curly hair is naturally dry, so it's important to moisturize your hair regularly. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls hydrated.

3. Don't over-style your hair

Over-styling your hair can cause damage and breakage. Let your curls air dry whenever possible and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

4. Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims can help keep your curls looking their best by preventing split ends and breakage.

FAQ

1. Will short curly hair look good on me?

Short curly hair can look good on anyone, regardless of face shape or hair texture. It's all about finding the right style for you.

2. How do I know what style to choose?

Look for inspiration in magazines, online, or on social media. Bring pictures with you to show your stylist and ask for their advice on what style will work best for you.

3. How do I maintain my short curly hair?

Use a gentle shampoo, moisturize your hair regularly, don't over-style your hair, and trim your hair regularly to maintain your short curly hair.

Hair Care

Short curly hair can be a great way to change up your look and add some texture and volume to your hair. Use the tips and advice in this article to help you rock this style with confidence.
