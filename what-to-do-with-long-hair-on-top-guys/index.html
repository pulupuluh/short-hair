What to Do with Long Hair on Top Guys: Tips, FAQs, and Hair Care in 2023

As a guy with long hair on top, you have a lot of style options. You can rock a man bun, slick back your hair, or let it flow freely. However, maintaining and styling long hair can be a challenge. In this blog post, we'll share tips, step-by-step guides, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you make the most of your long hair. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Long Hair on Top
  2. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair on Top
  3. Tips for Maintaining Long Hair on Top
  4. FAQ About Long Hair on Top
  5. Hair Care for Long Hair on Top

How to Style Long Hair on Top

If you have long hair on top, you have several options for styling it. Here are a few popular styles:

Slicked Back

Slicking back your hair is a classic look that works for both casual and formal occasions. To achieve this style, you'll need a good pomade or gel that can hold your hair in place. Start by applying the product to your hair, then comb it back with a fine-tooth comb. You can also use a hair dryer to set the style in place.

Man Bun

If you want to keep your hair out of your face, a man bun is a great option. To create a man bun, gather your hair at the crown of your head and tie it with a hair tie. You can also use a hair donut to create a more polished look. A man bun works best with longer hair, so make sure your hair is at least shoulder-length before attempting this style.

Long and Loose

If you prefer a more natural look, you can simply let your hair grow long and wear it loose. This works best for guys with wavy or curly hair, but it can also work for straight hair. To keep your hair healthy and shiny, make sure to use a good conditioner and avoid over-washing your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair on Top

Now that you know a few popular styles for long hair on top, here's a step-by-step guide to achieving them:

Slicked Back

  1. Wash your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.
  3. Apply a small amount of pomade or gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up.
  4. Comb your hair back with a fine-tooth comb, making sure to smooth out any bumps or tangles.
  5. Use a hair dryer to set the style in place.

Man Bun

  1. Gather your hair at the crown of your head and tie it with a hair tie.
  2. If you're using a hair donut, slide it onto your ponytail and spread your hair evenly around it.
  3. Wrap your hair around the donut, tucking the ends underneath.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Spray your hair with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Long and Loose

  1. Wash your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel-dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair to keep it soft and manageable.
  4. Let your hair air-dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry it gently.
  5. If your hair is prone to frizz or flyaways, apply a small amount of anti-frizz serum or hair wax to tame it.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair on Top

To keep your long hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner at least twice a week.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a good quality conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

FAQ About Long Hair on Top

1. How long should my hair be to style it on top?

Your hair should be at least shoulder-length to style it on top.

2. What products should I use to style my hair?

You can use a variety of products to style your hair, including pomade, gel, wax, and hairspray. Choose a product that works well with your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

3. How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair at least twice a week, but you can wash it more often if it gets dirty or oily.

4. How can I prevent split ends?

To prevent split ends, trim your hair regularly (every 6-8 weeks) and avoid using heat styling tools too often. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

Hair Care for Long Hair on Top

Long hair requires extra care and attention to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few tips for caring for your long hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a good quality conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein and vitamins to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get enough sleep and exercise to reduce stress, which can cause hair loss and breakage.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can keep your long hair looking healthy, shiny, and stylish. Whether you prefer a slicked-back look, a man bun, or long and loose locks, you can rock a great style with confidence.
