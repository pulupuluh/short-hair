Half Updo Wedding Hairstyles For Long Hair

Written by Bardi Jun 13, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you getting married soon and looking for the perfect hairstyle for your long locks? Half updo wedding hairstyles for long hair are a popular choice for brides who want to show off their length while still having their hair out of their face. In this article, we'll discuss how to achieve this style, tips for maintaining it throughout the day, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

The half updo hairstyle is a classic look that works well for weddings. It involves taking the top section of your hair and pulling it back, leaving the rest of your hair down. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit your individual taste, whether you prefer a sleek and sophisticated look or something more bohemian and romantic.

How To

To achieve a half updo wedding hairstyle for long hair, follow these steps:

  1. Brush out your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tease it to add volume.
  3. Gently pull the teased section back and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Smooth down any bumps or flyaways and add hairspray to hold the style in place.
  5. Leave the rest of your hair down or add curls or waves for a more romantic look.

Step by Step

Step 1: Brush Out Your Hair

Before you begin styling your hair, make sure it's free of any knots or tangles. Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to gently detangle your locks.

Step 2: Tease Your Hair

Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and use a teasing comb or brush to add volume. Tease the hair in small sections, starting at the roots and working your way towards the ends.

Step 3: Secure Your Hair

Take the teased section and gently pull it back, securing it with bobby pins. You can create a small twist or braid at the back of your head for added interest.

Step 4: Smooth Down Flyaways

Use a comb or brush to smooth down any bumps or flyaways around the crown of your head. Apply hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step 5: Add Curls or Waves (optional)

If you want a more romantic look, add curls or waves to the rest of your hair using a curling iron or wand. You can also leave your hair straight for a sleek and sophisticated look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your half updo wedding hairstyle:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • Apply hairspray to your bobby pins before inserting them to help them stay in place.
  • Use a conditioning treatment before the wedding to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Consider adding hair accessories such as a flower crown or hair comb for added interest.

FAQ

Can I do a half updo wedding hairstyle if my hair isn't long?

While a half updo wedding hairstyle is typically done on long hair, it can also work on medium-length hair. You may need to use more bobby pins to secure your hair in place.

What type of hair works best for a half updo wedding hairstyle?

A half updo wedding hairstyle works best on hair that is healthy and shiny. If you're concerned about your hair being too thick or heavy, consider having it thinned out before the wedding.

How can I make my half updo wedding hairstyle last all day?

Apply a strong-hold hairspray to your hair after styling it to help it stay in place. You can also ask your hairstylist to use a setting spray before pinning your hair back.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair leading up to the wedding to ensure it looks its best on the big day. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and steps, you're sure to rock a beautiful half updo wedding hairstyle on your big day. Remember to take care of your hair leading up to the wedding to ensure it looks its best. Congratulations and happy styling!
