Would Circle Glasses Look Good On Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 20, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you considering switching up your eyewear style and wondering if circle glasses would suit you? Look no further! In this article, we will explore the world of circle glasses and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision. From the different types of circle glasses to how to choose the right frame for your face shape, we’ve got you covered. So, let’s dive in!

Description

Circle glasses, also known as round glasses, are a classic style that has been popular for decades. They are characterized by their circular frames and can be made from a variety of materials including metal, plastic, and acetate. Circle glasses are versatile and can be worn by both men and women.

How to

When it comes to choosing the right circle glasses, there are a few things to consider. The first is your face shape. Circle glasses tend to look best on those with square or angular faces as they help to soften the features. If you have a round face, you may want to opt for a slightly more angular frame to balance out your face shape.

The next thing to consider is the size of the glasses. Circle glasses come in a range of sizes, from small and delicate to large and bold. If you have a smaller face, you may want to opt for a smaller frame to avoid overwhelming your features. On the other hand, if you have a larger face, a larger frame may be more flattering.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when choosing circle glasses:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Consider the size of the glasses
  3. Choose a frame material that suits your style and needs
  4. Try on different frames to find the perfect fit
  5. Consider the color of the frames and how they will complement your skin tone and hair color

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when wearing circle glasses:

  • Pair them with a simple outfit to let the glasses be the focal point
  • Experiment with different colors and patterns to add some personality to your look
  • Consider getting prescription circle glasses if you need corrective lenses
  • Invest in high-quality frames to ensure they last for years to come

FAQ

Do circle glasses look good on everyone?

While circle glasses can be flattering on many people, they may not be the best choice for everyone. It ultimately comes down to your face shape and personal style preferences.

How do I know what size circle glasses to get?

You can determine the size of circle glasses you need by measuring the width of your face and choosing a frame that is slightly wider than this measurement.

What is the best material for circle glasses?

The best material for circle glasses depends on your individual needs and preferences. Metal frames are lightweight and durable, while plastic frames come in a range of colors and patterns. Acetate frames are hypoallergenic and can be easily adjusted for a perfect fit.

Hair Care

When wearing circle glasses, it’s important to consider your hairstyle. If you have long hair, consider wearing it up or back to avoid it getting caught in the frames. If you have short hair, experiment with different styles to find one that complements your glasses. Additionally, be sure to clean your glasses regularly to avoid any buildup of hair products on the frames.

Conclusion

In conclusion, circle glasses can be a stylish and timeless addition to any wardrobe. By considering your face shape, size, and personal style preferences, you can find the perfect pair of circle glasses that will enhance your features and make you feel confident and stylish. Remember to take good care of your glasses to ensure they last for years to come!
