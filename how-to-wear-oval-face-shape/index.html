How to Wear Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is characterized by a slightly wider forehead and cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The proportions are well-balanced, and the face has soft curves. Here are some tips on how to wear oval face shape:

  • Keep your hair away from your face to show off your features.
  • Experiment with different hair lengths and styles to find what suits you best.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face look wider.
  • Opt for side-swept bangs or a center part to complement your face shape.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add texture and movement.

Now that you know the basics, let's move on to a step-by-step guide on how to wear oval face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to find the perfect hairstyle for your oval face shape:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before you can start experimenting with hairstyles, you need to determine your face shape. Stand in front of a mirror and use a measuring tape to measure the widest part of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are similar, you have an oval face shape.

Step 2: Consider your hair texture and length

Your hair texture and length will affect the types of hairstyles that will suit you. If you have thick, coarse hair, you may want to consider a shorter cut to make it more manageable. If you have fine, thin hair, you may want to add layers to create the illusion of volume.

Step 3: Choose a hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape and hair texture, it's time to choose a hairstyle. Here are some ideas:

  • Long layers with side-swept bangs
  • A shoulder-length bob with choppy layers
  • A pixie cut with long bangs
  • A messy bun or ponytail

Step 4: Try it out

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, try it out! Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the look that suits you best.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks on how to wear oval face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or sleek, as they can make your face look longer.
  • If you have a long neck, consider wearing your hair up to show it off.
  • If you have a short neck, avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • If you have a prominent forehead, consider adding bangs to balance out your features.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oval face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face look wider. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too flat or sleek, as they can make your face look longer.

Can I wear my hair in a center part if I have an oval face shape?

Yes, a center part is a great option for oval face shapes. It will complement your features and create a balanced look.

What length should my hair be if I have an oval face shape?

You can wear your hair at any length if you have an oval face shape! Experiment with different lengths to find what suits you best.

Hair Care for Oval Face Shape

Now that you know how to wear oval face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it. If you need to use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can wear your oval face shape with confidence and style. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and have fun with your look!
