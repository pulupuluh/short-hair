How To Make Natural Hair Curly And Soft Without Products

Written by Petter May 31, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Having curly and soft hair is a dream for many, but achieving it without using any hair products may seem impossible. However, with the right techniques and a little bit of patience, you can make your natural hair curly and soft without any products. In this article, we will guide you through the process step by step and provide tips for maintaining healthy hair.

  • How to Make Natural Hair Curly and Soft
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Make Natural Hair Curly and Soft

The key to making natural hair curly and soft is to work with your hair's natural texture rather than trying to change it. Here are some techniques you can use:

1. Finger Coiling

Finger coiling is a technique that involves twisting small sections of hair around your finger to create curls. Start by applying a leave-in conditioner to damp hair. Then, take a small section of hair and twist it around your finger, starting from the root and working your way down to the end. Hold the twist in place for a few seconds, then release it. Repeat this process until your entire head is coiled. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser if you're in a hurry.

2. Bantu Knots

Bantu knots are another great way to create curls without using any products. Start by sectioning your hair into small sections. Take one section and twist it tightly, then wrap it around itself to create a small knot. Secure the knot in place by tucking the ends underneath. Repeat this process until your entire head is knotted. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser if you're in a hurry.

3. Twist Outs

Twist outs are a popular technique for creating curls on natural hair. Start by sectioning your hair into small sections. Take one section and divide it into two strands. Twist the two strands around each other, starting from the root and working your way down to the end. Secure the end with a small hair tie or bobby pin. Repeat this process until your entire head is twisted. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser if you're in a hurry. Once your hair is dry, unravel the twists to reveal your curls.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for making natural hair curly and soft without using any products:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent it from drying out.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair. This will help to keep your hair moisturized and make it easier to style.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Choose your technique

Choose the technique that you want to use to create your curls. Finger coiling, Bantu knots, and twist outs are all great options.

Step 5: Start styling

Start styling your hair using your chosen technique. Take your time and be patient.

Step 6: Allow your hair to dry

Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser if you're in a hurry.

Step 7: Unravel your curls

Once your hair is dry, unravel your curls to reveal your new look.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Moisturize regularly

Moisturize your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent breakage.

2. Avoid heat styling

Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible. Heat can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and make it easier to style.

4. Trim your ends regularly

Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQs

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: The longevity of your curls will depend on your hair type and the technique that you used to create them. Generally, finger coiling and Bantu knots will create longer-lasting curls than twist outs.

Q: Can I use this technique on relaxed hair?

A: This technique is best suited for natural hair. If you have relaxed hair, it may not work as well.

Hair Care Routine

In addition to using these techniques to make your hair curly and soft, it's also important to have a good hair care routine. Here are some steps you can take to keep your hair healthy:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

3. Deep condition regularly

Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.

4. Protect your hair at night

Protect your hair at night by sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase, or by wearing a silk or satin bonnet.

By following these tips and techniques, you can make your natural hair curly and soft without using any products. Remember to be patient and gentle with your hair, and always prioritize its health and well-being. Happy styling!
