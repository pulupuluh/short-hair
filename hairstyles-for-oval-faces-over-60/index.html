Hairstyles For Oval Faces Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy May 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are over 60 and have an oval face, it's important to find a hairstyle that flatters your facial features and complements your age. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right hairstyle. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks for finding the perfect hairstyle for your oval face over 60.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 60

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for oval faces over 60, it's important to determine your face shape. The easiest way to do this is to stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back from your face. Look at the outline of your face and determine which shape it most closely resembles.

If your face is oval, it will be longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded hairline and a slightly narrower jawline. An oval face is considered to be the ideal shape, as it is well-balanced and symmetrical.

Hairstyles for Oval Faces Over 60

There are a number of hairstyles that work well for oval faces over 60. Here are some of our top picks:

Short Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut is a great option for oval faces over 60. It's a low-maintenance style that is easy to care for and can be dressed up or down. This style works particularly well if you have thick, straight hair.

Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob is a classic hairstyle that flatters oval faces of all ages. It's a versatile style that can be worn straight or with waves, and it works well for both thin and thick hair.

Layered Lob

A layered lob (long bob) is another great option for oval faces over 60. This style adds volume and movement to your hair, and it works particularly well if you have thin, fine hair. The layers can be styled to frame your face and accentuate your best features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you've chosen your perfect hairstyle, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.
  3. Use a curling iron or straightener to style your hair as desired.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent damage and maintain your style.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep your style looking fresh and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair if I'm over 60?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at any age. However, it's important to choose a dye that is gentle on your hair and scalp, and to follow the instructions carefully to avoid damage.

Q: Should I cut my hair short if I'm over 60?

A: Not necessarily. While short hair can be a great option for some people, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape and complements your age.

Hair Care for Aging Hair

As we age, our hair can become thinner, drier, and more brittle. Here are some tips for caring for aging hair:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best at any age. Use these tips and tricks to find the perfect hairstyle for your oval face over 60.
