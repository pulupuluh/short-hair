What Is The Best Hair Part For Round Faces

Written by Aprilia Apr 19, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hair part for your round face? Look no further! In this article, we’ll discuss the best hair part for round faces and provide you with tips and tricks to make sure you’re rocking your new look.

Description

Round faces are characterized by their soft, curved features, and a lack of defined angles. The goal when choosing a hair part is to create the illusion of length and angles. By doing this, you can balance out the roundness of your face and create a more flattering shape.

How to

The first step in finding the best hair part for your round face is to determine your face shape. The easiest way to do this is to take a selfie and trace the outline of your face with a marker or lipstick. If your face is almost as wide as it is long, you likely have a round face.

Once you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to experiment with different hair parts. The most flattering hair part for round faces is a deep side part. This creates the illusion of length and angles, which helps to balance out the roundness of your face. To create a deep side part, use a comb to create a straight line from the arch of your eyebrow to the crown of your head.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape

2. Experiment with different hair parts

3. Create a deep side part using a comb

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hair part for round faces:

  • Avoid center parts, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • If you prefer a middle part, try a slightly off-center part instead.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create more movement and texture.
  • Don’t be afraid to play with different hair lengths and styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What if I don’t like the deep side part?

If you’re not a fan of the deep side part, try experimenting with a slightly off-center part or a zigzag part. The key is to create the illusion of length and angles, so try different variations until you find what works best for you.

Can I still wear a center part with a round face?

While a center part isn’t the most flattering option for round faces, you can still wear it if you prefer. Just make sure to add some layers or texture to your hair to create more movement and dimension.

What if I have short hair?

The same rules apply, regardless of hair length. Try a deep side part or a slightly off-center part to create the illusion of length and angles.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to ensure it looks its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect hair part for your round face and rock your new look with confidence.
