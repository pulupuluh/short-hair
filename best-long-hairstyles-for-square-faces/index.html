Best Long Hairstyles For Square Faces

Written by Luffy Feb 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for the perfect long hairstyle that will flatter your square face shape? Well, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll be discussing the best long hairstyles for square faces, including how to style them, tips, and hair care. So, grab your hairbrush and let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and a broad forehead. The goal of a long hairstyle for a square face is to soften and balance out these features. The best long hairstyles for square faces are those that create softness and movement around the face. This can be achieved through layers, waves, and curls.

How to

When choosing a long hairstyle for a square face, it's important to consider the length and layers. Long hair that falls below the shoulders can elongate the face and draw attention away from the jawline. Layers around the face can also soften the jawline and create movement. Waves and curls can also add softness and movement.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Part your hair down the middle or to the side.

3. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls.

4. Apply a texturizing spray or mousse to add volume and hold.

5. Use your fingers to separate and tousle the curls for a natural look.

Tips

- Avoid straight, blunt cuts as they can accentuate the squareness of the face.

- Try a side-swept fringe or layers around the face to soften the jawline.

- Experiment with different parting styles to find what suits you best.

- Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail or updo with a square face?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to add some softness and movement around the face with loose strands or curls.

Q: What if I have thick or curly hair?

A: Layers can help to thin out thick hair and add movement to curly hair. Speak to your stylist about the best long hairstyle for your hair type.

Hair Care

To keep your long hairstyle looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes regular trims to keep split ends at bay, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and protecting your hair from heat damage with a heat protectant spray. It's also important to use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Now that you know the best long hairstyles for square faces, it's time to try them out! Remember to experiment with different styles and find what works best for you. With the right hairstyle, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident and beautiful.
