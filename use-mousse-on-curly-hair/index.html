How To Use Mousse On Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jun 02, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to style it. Many people with curly hair struggle to find products that work for them. One product that can be particularly helpful is mousse. Mousse can help define curls and reduce frizz, making it a great addition to any curly hair routine. In this article, we’ll go over how to use mousse on curly hair, as well as tips and tricks for getting the best results.

Table of Contents

  • How to Use Mousse on Curly Hair
  • Tips for Using Mousse on Curly Hair
  • FAQs about Mousse on Curly Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Use Mousse on Curly Hair

Using mousse on curly hair is fairly simple, but there are a few key steps you should follow for the best results. Here’s what to do:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair.

You should begin by washing your hair and allowing it to air dry until it’s damp. Mousse works best on damp hair because it can help hold the curls in place while they dry.

Step 2: Apply a golf ball-sized amount of mousse to your hair.

You don’t need a lot of mousse to get good results. Start with a golf ball-sized amount and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths.

Step 3: Scrunch your curls.

To help define your curls, scrunch your hair from the ends up to the roots. This will help distribute the mousse evenly and encourage your curls to form.

Step 4: Dry your hair.

Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. Avoid touching your hair too much while it dries, as this can cause frizz.

Tips for Using Mousse on Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you get the most out of your mousse:

Tip 1: Experiment with different amounts of mousse.

Some people may find that they need more or less mousse depending on their hair type and the look they’re going for. Don’t be afraid to experiment to find what works best for you.

Tip 2: Use a wide-toothed comb.

After applying mousse, use a wide-toothed comb to gently detangle your hair. This will help distribute the product evenly and reduce any knots or tangles.

Tip 3: Try applying mousse to dry hair.

While mousse works best on damp hair, some people with curly hair have had success applying it to dry hair for a quick refresh. Simply apply a small amount to your hands and scrunch it into your curls.

FAQs about Mousse on Curly Hair

Q: Can mousse make my hair crunchy?

A: It’s possible for mousse to make your hair crunchy if you use too much or don’t scrunch your curls enough. However, if you use the right amount and distribute it evenly, you should be able to avoid crunchiness.

Q: Is mousse good for frizzy hair?

A: Yes! Mousse can help reduce frizz and define curls, making it a great option for those with frizzy hair.

Q: Can I use mousse every day?

A: Yes, you can use mousse every day if you’d like. Just be sure to wash your hair regularly to avoid buildup.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Aside from using mousse, there are a few other things you can do to care for your curly hair:

Tip 1: Use a sulfate-free shampoo.

Sulfates can be harsh on curly hair and strip it of its natural oils. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo that’s gentle on your curls.

Tip 2: Deep condition regularly.

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it’s important to keep it hydrated. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish your curls.

Tip 3: Avoid heat styling.

Heat styling tools can be damaging to curly hair. Try to avoid using them too often, and when you do, use a heat protectant spray.

Using mousse on curly hair can be a game-changer for those with unruly curls. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve defined, frizz-free curls that last all day.
