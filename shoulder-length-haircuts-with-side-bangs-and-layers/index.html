Shoulder Length Haircuts With Side Bangs And Layers

Description

Shoulder length haircuts with side bangs and layers have been a popular trend for years. This style is perfect for those who want to add some texture and volume to their hair, while still maintaining a manageable length. The layers help to add movement and shape to the hair, while the side bangs frame the face and add a touch of femininity. This style is versatile and can be worn straight, wavy, or curly, making it a great choice for any occasion.

How to

If you're thinking about getting a shoulder length haircut with side bangs and layers, it's important to find a stylist who has experience with this specific style. It's also important to bring in photos of what you want your hair to look like, as this will give your stylist a better understanding of your desired outcome.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will give your stylist a clean slate to work with.

2. Section off your hair, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

3. Your stylist will start by cutting the length to your desired shoulder length.

4. Next, they will add layers to your hair to add movement and texture.

5. Finally, they will add side bangs to frame your face.

Tips

1. Always use a heat protectant when styling your hair.

2. Use a round brush when blow drying your hair to add volume and bounce.

3. Use a texturizing spray to add even more movement and texture to your hair.

4. Schedule regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with this style?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with this style. The layers will add some texture and volume, making it a great choice for updos.

Q: Is this style easy to maintain?

A: Yes, this style is easy to maintain. Regular trims will keep the shape and layers looking fresh, and using the right products can help to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length haircut with side bangs and layers looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. This includes:

1. Using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Using a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.

3. Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

4. Avoiding using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can enjoy your shoulder length haircut with side bangs and layers for months to come.
