What Is The Best Hair Type To Buy In 2023

Written by Bardi Jul 20, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking to buy hair extensions, you may be wondering what the best hair type to buy is. With so many options, it can be overwhelming to make a decision. In this article, we'll explore the different hair types available and help you determine which one is best for you.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Best Hair Type to Buy
  3. Step by Step Guide to Buying Hair Extensions
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Extensions
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care for Different Hair Types

Description

There are several hair types available for purchase, including synthetic hair and human hair. Synthetic hair is made from plastic fibers and is typically less expensive than human hair. However, it can be difficult to style and may not look as natural as human hair. Human hair can be purchased in a variety of grades, with higher grades being more expensive. The highest grade of human hair is virgin hair, which has never been chemically treated or processed.

How to Choose the Best Hair Type to Buy

When choosing a hair type to buy, consider your budget, lifestyle, and desired look. If you're on a tight budget, synthetic hair may be a good option. However, if you're looking for a more natural look and have the budget for it, human hair is the way to go.

Consider your lifestyle as well. If you're someone who likes to switch up their look frequently, synthetic hair may be a good option as it is less expensive and can be easily replaced. If you're looking for something more long-term, human hair is a better choice.

Lastly, think about the look you're going for. If you want something that looks natural and can be styled just like your own hair, human hair is the way to go.

Step by Step Guide to Buying Hair Extensions

1. Determine your budget.

2. Decide on the hair type you want (synthetic or human hair).

3. Choose the grade of human hair (if applicable).

4. Decide on the length and texture of the hair.

5. Choose a reputable vendor.

6. Place your order and wait for your hair extensions to arrive.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Extensions

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

2. Brush your hair gently to avoid tangling.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools frequently.

4. Store your hair extensions in a cool, dry place.

5. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent tangling while you sleep.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair extensions?

A: It depends on the type of hair extensions you have. Synthetic hair cannot be dyed, but human hair can be. However, it is important to note that dyeing your hair extensions may damage them and alter the texture.

Q: How long do hair extensions last?

A: It depends on how well you take care of them and the quality of the hair. Synthetic hair typically lasts a few months, while human hair can last up to a year or more with proper care.

Hair Care for Different Hair Types

Synthetic Hair Care

Synthetic hair should be washed with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage the hair. Store your synthetic hair in a cool, dry place.

Human Hair Care

Human hair should be washed with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Use heat styling tools sparingly to avoid damage to the hair. Store your human hair in a cool, dry place.

Ultimately, the best hair type to buy depends on your individual needs and preferences. Consider your budget, lifestyle, and desired look when making your decision. With proper care, both synthetic and human hair extensions can look great and last a long time.
