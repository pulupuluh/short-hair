What To Do With Broken Hair On Top Of Head Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Feb 17, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Broken hair on top of the head can be frustrating and can ruin your hairstyle. It can be caused by various factors such as heat styling, chemical treatments, and even genetics. But don't worry, there are several things you can do to help repair and prevent further damage. In this article, we will discuss how to deal with broken hair on top of the head and provide tips and tricks to keep your hair healthy.

Description

Broken hair on top of the head is a common problem among many people. It can cause your hair to look thin, dull, and lifeless. The hair on top of your head is the most visible and is often subject to damage from heat, chemicals, and other factors. Broken hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including:

  • Heat styling
  • Chemical treatments
  • Over-brushing or combing
  • Poor diet or nutrition
  • Genetics

Whatever the cause may be, it is important to take action to repair and prevent further damage to your hair.

How to Deal with Broken Hair on Top of the Head

If you're dealing with broken hair on top of the head, there are several things you can do to help repair and prevent further damage:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent further damage and breakage. It is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and strong.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling tools such as flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers can cause damage to your hair. Try to avoid using these tools as much as possible or use them on a lower heat setting.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help repair and strengthen your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients such as keratin, argan oil, and shea butter.

4. Be gentle when brushing or combing

When brushing or combing your hair, be gentle and avoid pulling or tugging. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to avoid further damage.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to deal with broken hair on top of the head:

Step 1: Trim your hair

Visit your stylist and get a trim to eliminate split ends and prevent further damage.

Step 2: Avoid heat styling

Avoid using heat styling tools or use them on a lower heat setting to prevent further damage.

Step 3: Deep condition your hair

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to repair and strengthen your hair.

Step 4: Be gentle when brushing or combing

Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles and be gentle when brushing or combing your hair to avoid further damage.

Step 5: Eat a healthy diet

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth.

Tips to Keep Your Hair Healthy

Here are some additional tips to keep your hair healthy:

1. Avoid harsh chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach or relaxers on your hair, as they can cause damage and breakage.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

3. Use a silk pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent breakage and keep your hair healthy.

4. Drink plenty of water

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQ

Q: Can broken hair be repaired?

A: Yes, broken hair can be repaired with the right care and treatment.

Q: How long does it take to repair broken hair?

A: It can take several months to repair broken hair, depending on the severity of the damage.

Q: Can genetics cause broken hair?

A: Yes, genetics can play a role in the health of your hair and can contribute to breakage.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential to keeping it healthy and preventing breakage. Use the tips and tricks in this article to help repair and prevent further damage to your hair. Remember to be gentle when brushing or combing, avoid heat styling, and eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth.
