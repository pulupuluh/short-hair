How To Style Layers On Short Hair

Written by Bardi Jun 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Short hair can be tricky to style, especially when it comes to layers. However, with the right techniques and products, you can create a beautiful and effortless look that suits your personality and lifestyle.

Description

Layers are a great way to add texture, volume, and movement to short hair. They can also help to frame your face and accentuate your best features. However, styling layers on short hair requires some know-how and a few essential tools and products.

How to

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style layers on short hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type. This will help to remove any buildup and make your hair more manageable.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Apply a styling product, such as a mousse, gel, or cream, to your damp hair to add volume and texture. Use a comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, starting from the roots and working your way to the ends. This will help to create volume and lift at the roots and smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

Step 4: Style Your Layers

Once your hair is dry, use a styling tool, such as a flat iron or curling iron, to create waves or curls in your layers. Alternatively, you can use your fingertips to tousle your hair and create a messy, textured look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style layers on short hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and personal style.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice on how to style your layers at home.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling layers on short hair:

Can I still style my hair if I have a pixie cut?

Yes, you can still style your hair if you have a pixie cut. Use a styling product, such as a wax or pomade, to add texture and definition to your hair.

How often should I wash my short hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out frequently, you may need to wash your hair every day or every other day. If you have dry hair or a more sedentary lifestyle, you may be able to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

What kind of styling tool should I use?

It depends on the look you want to achieve. If you want smooth, straight hair, use a flat iron. If you want curls or waves, use a curling iron or wand. If you want a messy, textured look, use your fingers.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by using a conditioning treatment once a week, avoiding heat styling as much as possible, and protecting your hair from the sun and other environmental factors. With the right care and styling techniques, you can rock your layers on short hair with confidence and ease.
