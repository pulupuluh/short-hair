How To Care For Relaxed Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Apr 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of dealing with frizzy and unmanageable hair? Relaxing your hair can be a great solution, but it's important to know how to properly care for it to keep it healthy and looking its best. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to care for relaxed hair.

How to Relax Hair

Before we dive into how to care for relaxed hair, let's first talk about how to properly relax your hair. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup.
  2. Apply a protective base to your scalp and hairline to prevent chemical burns.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and apply the relaxer cream to each section, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Leave the relaxer on for the recommended amount of time, then rinse thoroughly with warm water.
  5. Apply a neutralizing shampoo to stop the chemical process and restore the pH balance of your hair.
  6. Deep condition your hair to restore moisture and prevent breakage.

It's important to note that relaxing your hair can be damaging if not done properly, so it's recommended to have it done by a professional stylist.

How to Care for Relaxed Hair

Now that your hair is relaxed, it's important to take proper care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for relaxed hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent further damage.
  • Deep condition your hair at least once a week to restore moisture.
  • Wrap your hair at night with a silk or satin scarf to prevent breakage.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hair elastics or tight headbands that can cause breakage.

Step-by-Step Guide to Caring for Relaxed Hair

Here's a detailed guide on how to care for your relaxed hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo designed for relaxed hair. Be sure to massage your scalp gently to remove any product buildup.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

Apply a deep conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Leave it on for at least 10 minutes before rinsing with warm water.

Step 3: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to add moisture and prevent breakage. Be sure to apply it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

You can either air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.

Step 5: Protect Your Hair at Night

Wrap your hair at night with a silk or satin scarf to prevent breakage and maintain your hairstyle.

Step 6: Trim Your Ends

Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Relaxed Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep your relaxed hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or hair dyes that can further damage your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of moisture.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.

FAQs About Relaxed Hair

Is it safe to relax my hair?

When done properly, relaxing your hair can be safe. It's important to have it done by a professional stylist to minimize the risk of damage.

How often should I wash my relaxed hair?

It's recommended to wash your hair once or twice a week to prevent stripping it of moisture.

Can I still color my relaxed hair?

Yes, but it's important to use a gentle hair dye specifically designed for relaxed hair to minimize damage.

How can I prevent breakage in my relaxed hair?

Deep conditioning your hair regularly, avoiding heat styling tools, and using a silk or satin scarf to protect your hair at night can all help prevent breakage.

By following these tips and taking proper care of your relaxed hair, you can keep it healthy and looking its best. Remember to be gentle with your hair and avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling tools as much as possible. With a little bit of care, you can enjoy beautiful and manageable relaxed hair.
