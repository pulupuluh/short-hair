How To Thin Out Short Hair With Thinning Scissors

Written by Luffy Aug 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of having thick and bulky hair? Do you want to achieve a more textured and voluminous look? If you have short hair, thinning scissors might just be the tool you need to achieve your desired hairstyle. Thinning scissors are specially designed scissors that have a serrated edge, which helps to remove bulk and weight from the hair. In this article, we will guide you through the process of how to thin out short hair with thinning scissors.

How to:

Before you start thinning your hair, make sure that you have the right tools on hand. You will need a pair of thinning scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. Once you have all your tools ready, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any product buildup and make your hair easier to work with. Once you have washed your hair, dry it with a towel and let it air dry until it is slightly damp.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, divide your hair into sections. Start by parting your hair in the middle and then divide it into four sections, two on each side of your head. Clip each section of hair with a hair clip to keep it out of the way.

Step 3: Start Thinning Your Hair

Take one section of hair and comb it straight. Hold the thinning scissors vertically and start cutting into the hair, starting from the mid-lengths to the ends. Make sure to use a light hand and avoid cutting too much hair at once. Repeat this process on all four sections of your hair.

Step 4: Blend Your Hair

Once you have thinned out your hair, use your regular scissors to blend the layers together. This will help to create a more natural and textured look. You can also use your thinning scissors to create layers and texture throughout your hair.

Tips:

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when using thinning scissors on your short hair:

1. Use a light hand when cutting with thinning scissors to avoid cutting too much hair at once.

2. Start cutting from the mid-lengths to the ends of your hair to achieve a more natural look.

3. Use your regular scissors to blend the layers together to create a more seamless look.

FAQ:

1. Can I use thinning scissors on any hair type?

Yes, you can use thinning scissors on any hair type, but they work best on thick and bulky hair.

2. How often should I thin out my hair?

It depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Generally, it is recommended to thin out your hair every six to eight weeks.

Hair Care:

Thinning out your hair with thinning scissors can help to create a more textured and voluminous look, but it is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vitality. Make sure to use a mild shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat styling tools. Regular trims can also help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Thinning out short hair with thinning scissors might seem daunting at first, but with practice, you can achieve a more textured and voluminous look that suits your style and personality. Follow these simple steps, and you will be on your way to achieving your desired hairstyle.
