Best Beard Styles For Round Faces

Written by Petter Jun 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of constantly searching for the perfect beard style that suits your round face shape? Look no further as we have compiled a list of the best beard styles for round faces. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve your desired look. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces are characterized by a circular shape with a width that is almost equal to the length. The best beard styles for round faces are those that add definition and angles to the face. These styles are meant to elongate the face, making it look more masculine and chiseled. By choosing the right style, you can make your round face appear more angular and give yourself a sharper and more defined look.

How To

Before choosing a beard style, it is important to consider your face shape, hair growth pattern, and personal style preferences. You can consult with a professional barber to help you choose the best style for you.

Factors to consider:

  • Face shape
  • Hair growth pattern
  • Personal style preferences

Step by Step

Here are step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some of the best beard styles for round faces:

The Classic Beard

The classic beard is a timeless style that suits most face shapes. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Let your beard grow out for a few weeks until it reaches the desired length.
  2. Trim the sides and bottom of the beard to create a neat and tidy look.
  3. Keep the mustache trimmed and neat to avoid it covering your lips.
  4. Use a beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and shiny.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and maintaining your beard:

  • Choose a style that suits your face shape and personal style preferences.
  • Maintain your beard regularly by trimming, shaping, and washing it.
  • Use beard oils or balms to keep your beard soft, healthy, and shiny.
  • Avoid over-grooming or trimming your beard too short, as this can make your face appear more round.
  • Experiment with different beard styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about beard styles for round faces:

What beard styles suit round faces?

Beard styles that add definition and angles to the face are best for round faces. These styles include the goatee, the chinstrap, and the Van Dyke.

How do I choose the right beard style for my face shape?

You can consult with a professional barber to help you choose the best style for your face shape and personal style preferences.

How do I maintain my beard?

You can maintain your beard by regularly trimming, shaping, and washing it. You can also use beard oils or balms to keep it soft and healthy.

Hair Care

Here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind when maintaining your beard:

  • Wash your beard regularly with a mild shampoo or beard wash.
  • Use beard oils or balms to keep your beard soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools or harsh chemicals on your beard.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect beard style for your round face shape and maintain it with ease. Remember to choose a style that suits your personal style preferences and maintain it regularly to keep it looking its best.
