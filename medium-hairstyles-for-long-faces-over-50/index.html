Medium Hairstyles For Long Faces Over 50

Written by Daniel Jul 01, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you in your 50s with a long face and looking for a new hairstyle? Choosing the right hairstyle can be tricky, especially when you have a long face. But don’t worry, there are plenty of medium hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and make you look younger and more confident. In this article, we’ll discuss some of the best medium hairstyles for long faces over 50.

Description

A long face is characterized by a forehead, cheekbones, and jawline that are approximately the same width. This face shape can be challenging to style, but it is also versatile. The key is to find a hairstyle that balances out the length of your face and adds volume to the sides.

How to Choose the Right Medium Hairstyle for Long Faces Over 50

When choosing a medium hairstyle for a long face, keep the following tips in mind:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short.
  • Consider adding layers to add dimension and movement to your hair.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your hair texture and color.

Step-by-Step Guide: Medium Hairstyles for Long Faces Over 50

1. Shoulder-Length Bob

The shoulder-length bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It’s a great option for long faces because it adds volume to the sides and frames the face beautifully.

To achieve this look:

  1. Start by washing and blow-drying your hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Use a curling iron to add loose waves to your hair.
  4. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Layered Lob

The layered lob is a trendy and chic hairstyle that looks great on long faces. The layers add volume and movement to the hair, while the length frames the face beautifully.

To achieve this look:

  1. Start by washing and blow-drying your hair.
  2. Part your hair to the side.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair into a lob with layers.
  4. Use a straightener to add subtle waves to your hair.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Medium Hairstyles for Long Faces Over 50

Here are some tips to help you style your medium hairstyle:

  • Invest in quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your hair.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and movement.
  • Consider adding highlights to your hair to add dimension.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as they can make your face look even longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too flat or sleek, as they can accentuate the length of your face.

What hairstyles are best for adding volume to the sides of my face?

Hairstyles with layers, curls, or waves are great for adding volume to the sides of your face. You can also try a side-swept fringe to balance out the length of your face.

How often should I cut my hair?

It’s best to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Hairstyles for Long Faces Over 50

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

By following these tips and trying out some of the medium hairstyles we’ve suggested, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your long face and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
