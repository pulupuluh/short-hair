How To Style Soft Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline May 08, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have soft curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it. Soft curly hair tends to be fine and delicate, which can make it difficult to hold a style. However, with the right products and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, long-lasting curls. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to style soft curly hair, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Tips for Styling Soft Curly Hair
  • How to Style Soft Curly Hair: Step-by-Step
  • FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Tips for Styling Soft Curly Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step instructions, let's go over some tips for styling soft curly hair:

1. Use the right products

Soft curly hair tends to be delicate, so it's important to use products that are gentle and won't weigh your hair down. Look for lightweight mousses, creams, and gels that are specifically designed for curly hair. Avoid heavy oils and serums, as they can make your hair look greasy and flat.

2. Don't touch your hair too much

When you touch your hair too much, you can disrupt the curl pattern and cause frizz. Try to avoid running your fingers through your hair or playing with it too much.

3. Use a diffuser to dry your hair

A diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can help to dry your curls without causing frizz. Use a low heat setting and scrunch your hair with the diffuser to encourage your curls to form.

4. Sleep on a silk pillowcase

A silk pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep. Cotton pillowcases can be rough on your hair, causing it to tangle and break.

How to Style Soft Curly Hair: Step-by-Step

Now that you know some tips for styling soft curly hair, let's go over the step-by-step instructions:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are specifically designed for curly hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it's still wet.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help keep it moisturized and prevent frizz. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Apply a styling product

Apply a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or gel, to your hair. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 4: Use a diffuser to dry your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Use a low heat setting and scrunch your hair with the diffuser to encourage your curls to form.

Step 5: Finish with a hairspray

Finish your style with a lightweight hairspray to help hold your curls in place without weighing your hair down.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair prefer to wash their hair every day, while others only wash it once or twice a week. Experiment to find out what works best for your hair.

Q: Can I brush my curly hair?

A: It's best to avoid brushing your curly hair, as it can disrupt the curl pattern and cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it's still wet.

Q: How can I prevent frizz?

A: To prevent frizz, be gentle with your hair and avoid touching it too much. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair, and sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent breakage.

Hair Care Tips for Soft Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for soft curly hair:

1. Deep condition regularly

Soft curly hair tends to be dry, so it's important to deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized. Look for a deep conditioning treatment that is specifically designed for curly hair.

2. Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your curls look frizzy and dull.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

The sun's rays can damage your hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style your soft curly hair and keep it looking beautiful and healthy. Experiment with different products and techniques to find out what works best for your hair.
