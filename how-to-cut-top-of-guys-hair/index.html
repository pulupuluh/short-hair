How To Cut Top Of Guys Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 04, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Cutting the top of a guy's hair can be challenging, especially if you're not a professional hairstylist. However, with the right tools, techniques, and a bit of practice, you can master the art of cutting the top of the hair. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut top of guys hair in a relaxed English language.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Top of Guys Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Top of Guys Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After Cutting

Description

Cutting the top of guys hair involves trimming the hair on the top of the head, leaving longer hair on top and shorter hair on the sides and back. This style is popular among men of all ages and is easy to maintain.

How to Cut Top of Guys Hair

Before you start cutting the hair, make sure you have the right tools, including hair cutting scissors, clippers, and a comb. You'll also need a mirror, preferably a large one, so you can see the hair from all angles.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these step-by-step instructions to cut the top of a guy's hair:

  1. Start by washing and drying the hair, so it's clean and tangle-free.
  2. Comb the hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  3. Use the clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back, leaving it shorter than the hair on top.
  4. Use the scissors to trim the hair on the top of the head.
  5. Start at the front of the head and cut the hair in small sections, using the scissors to remove any excess length.
  6. Use the comb to lift the hair up and away from the head, so you can see the length you're cutting.
  7. Continue cutting the hair in small sections, working your way toward the back of the head.
  8. Check the hair from all angles in the mirror to make sure it's even.
  9. Use the scissors to blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back.
  10. Finish by trimming any stray hairs and cleaning up the neckline.

Tips for Cutting Top of Guys Hair

Cutting the top of a guy's hair can be tricky, but these tips can help:

  • Start with clean, dry hair, so you can see the natural texture and shape of the hair.
  • Use sharp scissors and clippers to make clean cuts.
  • Cut the hair in small sections, so you can control the length and avoid making mistakes.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair up and away from the head, so you can see the length you're cutting.
  • Check the hair from all angles in the mirror to make sure it's even.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as this can lead to uneven cuts and mistakes.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting the top of guys hair:

  • Q: How often should I cut the top of my hair?
  • A: It depends on your hair growth and style preference, but most men cut their hair every 4-6 weeks.
  • Q: Do I need to use different scissors for cutting hair?
  • A: Yes, hair cutting scissors are designed for cutting hair and are sharper than regular scissors.
  • Q: Should I wet my hair before cutting it?
  • A: It's best to cut clean, dry hair, as wet hair can be harder to cut and style.
  • Q: Can I cut my own hair or should I go to a professional?
  • A: It's possible to cut your own hair, but it's best to go to a professional if you're not confident in your skills.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting the top of guys hair, it's important to take care of the hair to maintain the style and keep it healthy. Here are some tips for hair care after cutting:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on the hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as this can damage the hair.
  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and prevent split ends.
  • Use hair products, such as pomade or hair wax, to style the hair and keep it in place.

Cutting the top of guys hair can be a fun and rewarding experience, especially if you follow these tips and techniques. With practice, you can master the art of cutting hair and create stylish looks that will impress everyone.
