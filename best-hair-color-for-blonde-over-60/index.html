Best Hair Color For Blonde Over 60 A Guide For 2023

Written by Bardi Jul 15, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you a blonde over 60 looking to change up your hair color? Choosing the right hair color can be tricky, especially as we age. However, with the right guidance and knowledge, you can find the perfect hair color that will make you look and feel your best. In this article, we will explore the best hair color options for blonde over 60, as well as tips and tricks for maintaining your new hair color.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Choose the Best Hair Color for Blonde Over 60
  • Step by Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color
  • FAQs About Hair Color for Blonde Over 60
  • Hair Care for Colored Hair

Description

As we age, our hair naturally becomes thinner and loses its pigmentation. This can make it difficult to find the perfect hair color that complements our skin tone and features. However, with the right hair color, you can enhance your natural beauty and also make your hair appear thicker and fuller. The best hair color for blonde over 60 is one that is subtle and natural, while also adding depth and dimension to your hair.

How To Choose the Best Hair Color for Blonde Over 60

The best hair color for blonde over 60 is one that complements your skin tone and eye color, while also adding depth and dimension to your hair. Here are some tips for choosing the best hair color for you:

  • Consider your skin tone: If you have warm, golden undertones in your skin, opt for warm hair colors like golden blonde or honey blonde. If you have cool undertones, ash blonde or platinum blonde may be the best options for you.
  • Consider your eye color: If you have blue or green eyes, cool blonde shades may enhance your eye color. If you have brown eyes, warmer blonde shades may bring out the warmth in your eyes.
  • Consider your natural hair color: If you have naturally blonde hair, consider staying within a few shades of your natural color to avoid harsh contrasts. If you have darker hair, consider going for highlights or balayage to add dimension to your hair.

Step by Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair

So, you've decided on the perfect hair color for blonde over 60. Now, it's time to color your hair. Here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose a high-quality hair dye that is specifically formulated for mature hair.
  2. Do a patch test to make sure you are not allergic to the hair dye.
  3. Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline to prevent the dye from staining your skin.
  4. Follow the instructions on the hair dye package and apply the dye to your hair.
  5. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time.
  6. Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water.
  7. Apply a color-safe conditioner to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Now that you have the perfect hair color, you want it to last as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair color:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent the color from fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as heat can also cause the color to fade.
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater, as they can also cause the color to fade.
  • Consider getting touch-ups every 4-6 weeks to maintain the vibrancy of your hair color.

FAQs About Hair Color for Blonde Over 60

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair color for blonde over 60:

  • What is the best hair color for blonde over 60 with gray hair? If you have gray hair, consider going for a silver or ash blonde hair color to complement your natural gray hair.
  • Can I dye my hair at home? Yes, you can dye your hair at home. However, it's always recommended to consult with a professional hairstylist for advice on which hair color to choose and how to apply it correctly.
  • Will coloring my hair damage it? Coloring your hair can cause some damage, but using high-quality hair dye and following the proper aftercare routine can minimize the damage.

Hair Care for Colored Hair

Colored hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for caring for colored hair:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent the color from fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as heat can also cause the color to fade.
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater, as they can also cause the color to fade.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hair color for blonde over 60 and keep it looking vibrant and healthy for as long as possible. Don't be afraid to experiment with different shades and styles until you find the perfect fit for you.
