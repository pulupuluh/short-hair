Is It Safe To Do Ombre Brows While Pregnant

Written by Petter Jul 03, 2023 · 6 min read

Table of Content:

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Many women wonder if it is safe to do ombre brows while pregnant. Ombre brows are a popular cosmetic procedure that involves creating a gradient effect on the eyebrows by using a special technique to implant pigment into the skin. While this procedure is generally safe, pregnant women should exercise caution before deciding to have it done.

How to

Before booking an appointment for ombre brows, pregnant women should consult with their doctor or midwife to get their opinion on the matter. While there is no evidence to suggest that ombre brows are harmful to pregnant women, it's always better to err on the side of caution.

Step by step

If a pregnant woman decides to go ahead with ombre brows, she should follow these steps:

  1. Choose a reputable and experienced brow artist who uses safe and hygienic practices.
  2. Inform the artist that she is pregnant and ask if they have any special precautions or procedures in place for pregnant clients.
  3. Ensure that the artist uses high-quality pigments that are safe for use during pregnancy.
  4. Follow all aftercare instructions carefully to ensure proper healing and to minimize the risk of infection.

Tips

Here are some tips for pregnant women who want to get ombre brows:

  • Wait until the second trimester before getting any cosmetic procedures done.
  • Avoid getting any cosmetic procedures done if the pregnant woman has any health conditions or complications.
  • Choose a brow artist who is experienced and knowledgeable about working with pregnant clients.
  • Ensure that the artist uses disposable needles and other equipment to minimize the risk of infection.
  • Do a patch test before getting the procedure done to ensure that the pigment doesn't cause an allergic reaction.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting ombre brows while pregnant:

Can getting ombre brows harm the baby?

There is no evidence to suggest that getting ombre brows while pregnant can harm the baby. However, pregnant women should always consult with their doctor or midwife before getting any cosmetic procedures done.

Can the pigment affect the baby?

There is no evidence to suggest that the pigment used in ombre brows can affect the baby. However, pregnant women should choose a brow artist who uses high-quality and safe pigments.

Can pregnant women get numbing cream?

While some numbing creams are safe to use during pregnancy, pregnant women should always consult with their doctor or midwife before using any kind of medication or product.

Hair Care

After getting ombre brows, pregnant women should follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid getting the brows wet for the first 24-48 hours after the procedure.
  • Avoid touching the brows or picking at any scabs that may form.
  • Avoid using any harsh or abrasive products on the brows.
  • Avoid sun exposure or tanning beds for the first week after the procedure.
  • Apply a thin layer of ointment or moisturizer to the brows as directed by the brow artist.

Overall, pregnant women should exercise caution before getting any cosmetic procedures done. While ombre brows are generally safe, it's always better to consult with a doctor or midwife before making any decisions.
