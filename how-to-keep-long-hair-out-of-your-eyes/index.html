How To Keep Long Hair Out Of Your Eyes Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Apr 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Long hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it's beautiful and versatile, it can be a hassle when it keeps getting in your eyes. This problem is especially common for people with bangs or layers. Fortunately, there are many ways to keep your long hair out of your eyes, whether you're at work, school, or just running errands. In this article, we'll share with you some tips and tricks for achieving a hairdo that stays put.

How to Keep Long Hair Out of Your Eyes: The Basics

Before we dive into the specifics, let's go over the basics of keeping long hair out of your eyes. Here are some general tips:

  • Use hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and bobby pins.
  • Opt for hairstyles that don't require much maintenance, such as braids and buns.
  • Try using hair products like hairspray, gel, or mousse to keep your hair in place.
  • Consider getting a haircut that suits your lifestyle and preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Keep Long Hair Out of Your Eyes

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and free of oil and dirt. You can use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair, then towel-dry it gently.

Step 2: Use a Hair Accessory

One of the easiest ways to keep your long hair out of your eyes is to use a hair accessory. Headbands, clips, and bobby pins are all great options. Here's how to use them:

  • Headbands: Simply slide a headband over your hair and position it so that it sits comfortably on your forehead. You can choose from a variety of styles, such as thin bands, wide bands, and embellished bands.
  • Clips: Use a clip to secure a section of hair away from your face. You can clip it to the side or back of your head, depending on your preference.
  • Bobby pins: Twist a section of hair and pin it to the side or back of your head using bobby pins. You can also use multiple pins for a more secure hold.

Step 3: Try a Braid

Braids are a classic hairstyle that can keep your long hair in place all day. Here's how to do a simple braid:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into three sections: left, middle, and right.
  3. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  4. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Tie the end of the braid with an elastic band.

Tips for Keeping Long Hair Out of Your Eyes

Here are some additional tips to help you keep your long hair out of your eyes:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Use hair products sparingly to avoid buildup and greasiness.
  • Invest in high-quality hair accessories that won't break or slip easily.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can loosen your hairstyle and cause it to fall.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: What's the best way to keep long hair out of my face?

A: The best way to keep long hair out of your face is to use hair accessories like headbands, clips, and bobby pins. You can also try braids, buns, and other hairstyles that don't require much maintenance.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If your hair is oily, you may need to wash it more often than if it's dry. Generally, you should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, but you can adjust this based on how your hair feels.

Q: Can I use hairspray to keep my hair in place?

A: Yes, you can use hairspray to keep your hair in place. However, be sure to use it sparingly to avoid buildup and stickiness.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Keeping long hair out of your eyes is just one aspect of hair care. To maintain healthy, shiny, and strong hair, here are some additional tips:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth and health.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and dyes that can strip your hair of moisture.

By following these tips, you can enjoy long, luscious hair that stays out of your eyes and looks great every day.
