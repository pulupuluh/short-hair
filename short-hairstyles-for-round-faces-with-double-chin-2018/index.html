Short Hairstyles For Round Faces With Double Chin 2018

Written by Frank Mar 27, 2023 · 8 min read

If you have a round face and a double chin, it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. However, with the right haircut and styling, you can accentuate your best features and minimize the appearance of your double chin. In this article, we will discuss the top short hairstyles for round faces with double chins in 2018.

Description

A round face with a double chin is a common facial shape, and it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that complements your features. The key to finding the perfect haircut is to choose a style that adds height to your face, elongates your neck, and draws attention away from your double chin.

How to: Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for round faces with double chins, it is essential to consider your facial features and hair type. Here are some tips to help you choose the right haircut:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, it is essential to determine your face shape. If you have a round face, look for hairstyles that add height and volume to your hair to elongate your face shape. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type can also play a significant role in choosing the right hairstyle. If you have thin hair, look for short hairstyles that add volume and texture to your hair. If you have thick hair, opt for a layered haircut that thins out your hair and reduces bulk.

Step by Step: Short Hairstyles for Round Faces with Double Chin 2018

Here are the top short hairstyles for round faces with double chins in 2018:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short and chic hairstyle that is perfect for round faces with double chins. This haircut adds height to your face and draws attention away from your double chin. It also works well with thin or thick hair.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is a classic and versatile hairstyle that is perfect for round faces with double chins. This haircut can be short or long and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair type. It is also easy to maintain and style.

3. Layered Cut

The layered cut is a popular hairstyle for round faces with double chins. This haircut adds volume and texture to your hair and thins out your hair to reduce bulk. It also works well with thin or thick hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your short hair:

- Use volumizing products to add height to your hair - Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face - Use a round brush to style your hair and add volume - Use a hair straightener to create sleek and straight hair - Use a curling iron to create loose and tousled waves

FAQ

What is the best haircut for round faces with double chins?

The best haircut for round faces with double chins is a short hairstyle that adds height to your face and elongates your neck. The pixie cut, bob cut, and layered cut are all great options.

What hair color suits a round face with double chin?

When choosing a hair color, it is essential to consider your skin tone and natural hair color. For round faces with double chins, opt for warm hair colors like golden blonde, honey brown, or copper red. These colors add warmth and dimension to your hair and complement your skin tone.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair looking healthy and shiny, it is essential to maintain a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you care for your short hair:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type - Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair - Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray - Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your hairstyle

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut, you can have a beautiful and flattering hairstyle that complements your features and minimizes the appearance of your double chin. With these short hairstyles for round faces with double chins in 2018, you can look and feel your best.
