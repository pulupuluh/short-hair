How To Make Curly Bangs Straight A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Apr 02, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have curly bangs and want to switch up your look, making them straight can be a great option. While it may seem intimidating at first, with the right tools and techniques, you can get smooth, straight bangs that look great. In this guide, we’ll show you how to make curly bangs straight, including tips, tricks, and hair care advice along the way.

Description

If you have curly bangs, you may be wondering how to straighten them. Whether you want a sleek, polished look, or just want to switch up your style, making curly bangs straight is easier than you might think. By using the right tools and techniques, you can achieve smooth, straight bangs that look great and stay that way all day long.

How to Make Curly Bangs Straight

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you begin straightening your bangs, it’s important to start with clean, conditioned hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any buildup and leave your hair feeling soft and smooth. Rinse thoroughly and gently towel-dry your hair to remove excess moisture.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Once your hair is clean and dry, it’s time to apply a heat protectant. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy. Apply the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair, paying special attention to your bangs. Comb your hair to distribute the product evenly.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, starting with your bangs. Use the brush to pull your bangs straight as you blow dry them, working from root to tip. Repeat this process until your bangs are straight and smooth.

Step 4: Flat Iron Your Bangs

Once your hair is dry, it’s time to flat iron your bangs. Heat up your flat iron and work in small sections, starting at the roots and working your way down. Use the flat iron to straighten your bangs, pulling them taut as you go. Repeat this process until all of your bangs are straight.

Tips

Tip 1: Use the Right Tools

To get the best results when straightening your bangs, it’s important to use the right tools. Invest in a high-quality flat iron and a round brush that’s the right size for your hair.

Tip 2: Be Gentle

When straightening your bangs, be gentle and avoid pulling too hard. This can cause damage and breakage, which can be difficult to repair.

Tip 3: Use Heat Protectant

Always use a heat protectant when straightening your hair, especially your bangs. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

Q: How often should I straighten my bangs?

A: It’s best to limit heat styling as much as possible to prevent damage. Try to straighten your bangs no more than once or twice a week.

Q: Can I straighten my bangs without a flat iron?

A: While a flat iron is the most effective tool for straightening your bangs, you can also try using a hair dryer and a round brush.

Q: How long will my straightened bangs last?

A: The length of time your straightened bangs will last depends on your hair type and the weather. In general, they should stay straight for at least a day or two.

Hair Care

Step 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Step 2: Apply a Hair Mask

Once a week, apply a hair mask to nourish and strengthen your hair. Choose a mask that’s designed for your hair type.

Step 3: Limit Heat Styling

Try to limit heat styling as much as possible to prevent damage. When you do heat style, use a heat protectant and be gentle with your hair.

Step 4: Trim Your Bangs Regularly

Regular trims will keep your bangs looking their best and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Conclusion

With the right tools and techniques, making curly bangs straight is easy and can give you a whole new look. Follow our step-by-step guide, use our tips and hair care advice, and you’ll have smooth, straight bangs in no time.
