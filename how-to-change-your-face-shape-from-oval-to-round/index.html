How To Change Your Face Shape From Oval To Round

Written by Luffy Feb 18, 2023 · 9 min read

Are you tired of having an oval-shaped face and want to try a new look? Changing your face shape from oval to round can be a great way to switch things up and create a more youthful appearance. In this article, we’ll go over everything you need to know about how to change your face shape from oval to round, including tips, steps, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Change Your Face Shape from Oval to Round
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Changing Your Face Shape
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for a Round Face

Description

Your face shape is determined by the bones and muscles in your face, and it can greatly affect how you look. An oval face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. On the other hand, a round face shape is characterized by a wider forehead, fuller cheeks, and a more rounded chin. Changing your face shape from oval to round can be achieved through a combination of makeup techniques, hairstyles, and facial exercises.

How to Change Your Face Shape from Oval to Round

There are several ways to change your face shape from oval to round, including:

1. Use Makeup Techniques

Makeup can be a powerful tool for changing your face shape. To create the illusion of a rounder face, use contouring techniques to create shadows along your cheekbones and jawline, and highlight the center of your face. Use a blush with a round, circular motion on the apples of your cheeks to create a fuller look.

2. Try Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone and strengthen the muscles in your face, which can help create a more rounded appearance. Try exercises like puckering your lips and holding for a few seconds, or smiling while pressing your tongue against the roof of your mouth.

3. Change Your Hairstyle

Your hairstyle can greatly affect the appearance of your face shape. For a rounder look, try hairstyles with volume at the crown of your head, like a bob or layered cut. Avoid hairstyles that emphasize the length of your face, like long, straight hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to change your face shape from oval to round:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in changing your face shape is to determine your current face shape. Use a mirror to look at the proportions of your face and compare it to images of different face shapes.

Step 2: Use Makeup Techniques

Once you’ve determined your face shape, use makeup techniques to create the illusion of a rounder face. Use contouring to create shadows along your cheekbones and jawline, and highlight the center of your face. Use a blush with a round, circular motion on the apples of your cheeks to create a fuller look.

Step 3: Try Facial Exercises

Incorporate facial exercises into your daily routine to help tone and strengthen the muscles in your face. Try exercises like puckering your lips and holding for a few seconds, or smiling while pressing your tongue against the roof of your mouth.

Step 4: Change Your Hairstyle

Choose a hairstyle that creates volume at the crown of your head, like a bob or layered cut. Avoid hairstyles that emphasize the length of your face, like long, straight hair.

Tips for Changing Your Face Shape

Here are some additional tips for changing your face shape:

  • Choose hairstyles and makeup looks that complement your face shape
  • Use facial oils and moisturizers to keep your skin looking plump and youthful
  • Choose clothing styles that flatter your new face shape
  • Practice good posture to help elongate your neck and create a more rounded appearance

FAQs

Can I really change my face shape?

Yes, you can change the appearance of your face shape through a combination of makeup techniques, hairstyles, and facial exercises. While you can’t change the underlying bone structure of your face, you can create the illusion of a different face shape.

How long does it take to change your face shape?

Changing your face shape can take time and patience. You may see results in as little as a few weeks with consistent practice, but it could take several months before you achieve your desired look.

Are there any risks to changing my face shape?

There are no risks associated with changing your face shape through makeup techniques, hairstyles, and facial exercises. However, if you are considering more drastic changes like plastic surgery, it’s important to consult with a qualified professional and weigh the risks and benefits carefully.

Hair Care Tips for a Round Face

Here are some hair care tips to help maintain a round face shape:

  • Choose a hairstyle that creates volume at the crown of your head, like a bob or layered cut
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair
  • Avoid hairstyles that emphasize the length of your face, like long, straight hair
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume at the roots
  • Try using hair accessories like headbands or hair clips to create volume and fullness

Changing your face shape from oval to round can be a fun way to experiment with your look and create a more youthful appearance. By using makeup techniques, facial exercises, and hairstyles that flatter your new face shape, you can achieve a rounder, fuller look in no time.
