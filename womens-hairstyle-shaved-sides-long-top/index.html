Women s Hairstyle Shaved Sides Long Top A Trendy Look In 2023

Written by Robby Apr 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, then the women's hairstyle shaved sides long top might be perfect for you. This hairstyle has been gaining popularity over the years and is still on-trend in 2023. In this blog post, we'll discuss how to achieve this look, some tips and tricks, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips to maintain your shaved sides long top hairstyle.

Table of Content

  • How To Achieve Shaved Sides Long Top Hairstyle
  • Step By Step Guide

How To Achieve Shaved Sides Long Top Hairstyle

The shaved sides long top hairstyle is easy to achieve but requires some time and effort. The first step is to decide how short you want your sides to be. You can choose to have a buzz cut, a fade, or a complete shaved side. Once you're happy with the length of your sides, it's time to focus on the top.

The top of your hair should be longer than the sides, and you can choose to have it styled in any way you want. You can have it straight, wavy, curly, or in a bun or ponytail. The key is to make sure that the top of your hair complements your shaved sides.

Step By Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the shaved sides long top hairstyle:

  1. Decide on the length of your sides
  2. Visit a hairstylist or barber to have your sides shaved or cut
  3. Style the top of your hair in any way you prefer
  4. Apply hair products to keep your hair in place

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you maintain your shaved sides long top hairstyle:

  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type
  • Visit your hairstylist or barber regularly for touch-ups
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the shaved sides long top hairstyle:

Is the shaved sides long top hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle is suitable for all hair types, but you may need to adjust the length of your sides and the style of your top to suit your hair type.

How often should I visit my hairstylist or barber for touch-ups?

You should visit your hairstylist or barber every four to six weeks for touch-ups to maintain your shaved sides long top hairstyle.

Can I style my top in a bun or ponytail?

Yes, you can style your top in a bun or ponytail, but you should make sure that it complements your shaved sides.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your shaved sides long top hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair as it can damage your hair

So, there you have it, a complete guide on how to achieve the women's hairstyle shaved sides long top. With these tips and tricks, you can maintain this trendy hairstyle and rock it with confidence.
