How To Cut A Pixie Haircut With A Razor

Are you tired of the same old hair style and want to try something new and edgy? A pixie haircut might be just what you need! This short and chic cut can be achieved with a razor for a more textured and dynamic look. In this article, we will take you through the steps of how to cut a pixie haircut with a razor and provide some tips and hair care advice along the way.

How to Cut a Pixie Haircut with a Razor

To cut a pixie haircut with a razor, you will need a razor comb, hair clips, and a pair of scissors. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Section the Hair

Start by sectioning the hair into four parts: the top, sides, and back. Clip each section out of the way, leaving the top section loose.

Step 2: Cut the Top Section

Using the razor comb, cut the top section of the hair by holding the hair straight up and cutting at an angle with the razor. This will create a more textured and layered look. Use the scissors to blend any areas that may need it.

Step 3: Cut the Side Sections

Unclip one of the side sections and comb the hair straight down. Use the razor comb to cut the hair at an angle, following the natural shape of the head. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Cut the Back Section

Unclip the back section and comb the hair straight down. Use the razor comb to cut the hair at an angle, again following the natural shape of the head. Blend any areas that may need it with the scissors.

Step 5: Final Touches

Check for any uneven areas and make any final touches with the scissors. Style the hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a pixie haircut with a razor:

  • Start with clean, dry hair to get a more accurate cut.
  • Use a razor comb with a guard to prevent cutting too much hair at once.
  • Always cut at an angle to create a more textured and layered look.
  • Blend any areas that may need it with scissors to avoid choppy lines.
  • Use a light hand when cutting to prevent damaging the hair.

FAQ

Can I cut a pixie haircut with a razor on my own?

While it is possible to cut your own hair with a razor, it is recommended to have a professional do it for you to avoid any mishaps.

How often should I get a pixie haircut with a razor?

It is recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the pixie cut.

Hair Care

After cutting a pixie haircut with a razor, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain the style and health of your locks. Here are some basic hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often to prevent damage to the hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair.
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather with a hat or scarf.

Now that you know how to cut a pixie haircut with a razor, it's time to try it out and rock that edgy new look. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain the style and health of your locks. Happy styling!
