Short Haircuts For Chubby Faces 2021 How To Look Your Best

If you have a round or chubby face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. However, with the right style, you can accentuate your best features and look your best. In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for chubby faces 2021, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Chubby Faces

When choosing a short haircut for a round or chubby face, it is important to take into account the shape of your face and your hair type. The goal is to create an illusion of a longer face and a slimmer jawline. Here are some of the best hairstyles for chubby faces:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a timeless classic that can work well for women with round faces. This haircut is short on the sides and back and longer on top, creating a vertical line that elongates the face. You can also add side-swept bangs to further slim down your face.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular choice for women with round faces. A chin-length bob that grazes the jawline can create the illusion of a slimmer face. You can also add layers to add volume and movement to your hair.

Asymmetrical Cut

An asymmetrical cut can work well for round faces because it creates a diagonal line that slims down the face. You can opt for an asymmetrical bob or pixie cut that is shorter on one side and longer on the other.

Step-by-Step Guide to Style Your Short Haircut

Styling your short haircut can be easy and fun. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you style your hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to use products that are suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously as it can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply styling product

Apply a styling product like a mousse or a styling cream to your hair. This will help add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create movement. Make sure to blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go.

Step 5: Style your hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it using a flat iron or a curling iron. You can also add some hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips to Maintain Your Short Haircut

Maintaining your short haircut can be easy with these tips:

Tip 1: Trim your hair regularly

Short haircuts require regular trimming to maintain their shape and style. Make sure to visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.

Tip 2: Protect your hair from heat

Heat can damage your hair, so make sure to protect it with a heat protectant spray before using a flat iron or a curling iron.

Tip 3: Use the right hair products

Use products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

FAQ About Short Haircuts for Chubby Faces

Q: Can I still wear short hair if I have a chubby face?

A: Yes, you can still wear short hair if you have a chubby face. The key is to choose the right haircut that flatters your face shape and hair type.

Q: Is a pixie cut suitable for a round face?

A: Yes, a pixie cut can work well for a round face. Just make sure to add some side-swept bangs to slim down your face.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require proper care to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips for short haircuts:

Tip 1: Use a clarifying shampoo

Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and keep your hair clean and healthy.

Tip 2: Condition your hair regularly

Condition your hair every time you wash it to keep it soft and hydrated. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a month to nourish your hair.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

In conclusion, finding the right short haircut for a chubby face can be easy with the right style and care. Use these tips and tricks to look your best and feel confident in your new hairstyle.
