What Is Good For Frizzy Curly Hair

Written by Petter Jun 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Frizzy curly hair can be a real pain to manage, but there are some things you can do to tame those wild locks. In this article, we'll discuss some of the best ways to care for frizzy curly hair, including how to prevent and treat frizz, what types of products to use, and other tips and tricks for keeping your hair looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • Preventing Frizz
  • Treating Frizz
  • Best Products for Frizzy Curly Hair
  • Other Tips for Managing Frizzy Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Routine for Frizzy Curly Hair

Preventing Frizz

Preventing frizz is the best way to keep your curly hair looking smooth and sleek. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid heat styling: Heat styling can damage your hair and cause frizz. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser.
  • Use a microfiber towel: Regular towels can cause frizz by roughing up the cuticle. A microfiber towel is gentler on your hair.
  • Don't over-wash: Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to frizz. Try washing your hair every other day or every few days.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb: A wide-toothed comb is gentler on your hair than a brush and can help prevent breakage and frizz.

Treating Frizz

If you already have frizz, don't worry! There are ways to treat it:

  • Use a smoothing serum: A smoothing serum can help tame frizz and add shine to your hair.
  • Try a hair mask: A hair mask can help hydrate your hair and reduce frizz. Look for masks that contain ingredients like argan oil or coconut oil.
  • Avoid touching your hair: Touching your hair can cause frizz, so try to avoid it as much as possible.
  • Use a silk pillowcase: A silk pillowcase is gentler on your hair than a cotton one and can help prevent frizz.

Best Products for Frizzy Curly Hair

When it comes to products for frizzy curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Avoid sulfates: Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to frizz. Look for sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners.
  • Look for moisturizing ingredients: Moisture is key for fighting frizz, so look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or jojoba oil.
  • Consider a leave-in conditioner: A leave-in conditioner can help hydrate your hair and reduce frizz throughout the day.

Other Tips for Managing Frizzy Curly Hair

Here are a few other tips for managing frizzy curly hair:

  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help prevent split ends, which can lead to frizz.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel: Rubbing your hair with a towel can cause frizz, so try gently squeezing out the water instead.
  • Consider a diffuser: A diffuser can help distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz while drying your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a brush on my frizzy curly hair?

A: It's best to avoid using a brush on your curly hair, as it can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my frizzy curly hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day or every few days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: What can I do if my frizz is really bad?

A: If your frizz is really bad, try using a smoothing serum or a hair mask to hydrate your hair and reduce frizz. You can also try wearing your hair in a protective style, like braids or twists, to give your hair a break.

Hair Care Routine for Frizzy Curly Hair

Here's a basic hair care routine for frizzy curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner every other day or every few days.
  2. Gently squeeze out the water from your hair with a microfiber towel.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or smoothing serum to your hair.
  4. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  5. If you're heat styling, use a diffuser and a heat protectant spray.

Remember, managing frizzy curly hair takes time and patience. With the right products and techniques, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
