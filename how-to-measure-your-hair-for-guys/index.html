How To Measure Your Hair For Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Mar 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As a guy, measuring your hair may seem like a task that's reserved for barbershops or hairdressers. However, it's a skill that every man should learn. Knowing your hair length can help you choose the right haircut, track your hair growth progress, and even buy hats that fit comfortably. In this article, we'll show you how to measure your hair length in a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Gather the Right Tools
  • Step 2: Find Your Starting Point
  • Step 3: Measure Your Hair
  • Tips for Measuring Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Men

Step 1: Gather the Right Tools

The first step to measuring your hair is to gather the right tools. You'll need a ruler, measuring tape, or a yardstick to get an accurate measurement. Make sure that the tool you choose is long enough to measure your entire hair length.

Additionally, it's a good idea to have a comb or brush handy to help you straighten your hair for an accurate measurement. If you have curly or wavy hair, try to stretch it out as much as possible before measuring.

Step 2: Find Your Starting Point

Once you have your tools ready, the next step is to find your starting point. This will depend on the type of haircut you have and where you want to measure your hair length from. Here are a few starting points to consider:

  • Crown: Measure from the top of your head, where your hair naturally parts.
  • Forehead: Measure from your hairline at the center of your forehead.
  • Ear: Measure from the top of your ear, where it connects to your head.

Choose the starting point that makes the most sense for your haircut and the measurement you want to take.

Step 3: Measure Your Hair

Now that you have your starting point, it's time to measure your hair length. Here's how:

  1. Use your comb or brush to straighten your hair as much as possible.
  2. Place your ruler, measuring tape, or yardstick at your starting point.
  3. Slowly lower the tool down to the end of your hair, making sure to keep it straight and taut.
  4. Record the measurement in inches or centimeters.

Repeat this process on different sections of your hair if you want to measure different lengths or get a more accurate measurement.

Tips for Measuring Your Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when measuring your hair:

  • Measure your hair when it's dry for a more accurate measurement.
  • Ask someone to help you measure the back of your head if necessary.
  • Take measurements regularly to track your hair growth progress.

FAQs

How often should I measure my hair?

We recommend measuring your hair every 4-6 weeks to track your hair growth progress.

Can I measure my hair if it's curly or wavy?

Yes, you can measure your hair if it's curly or wavy. Just make sure to stretch it out as much as possible before measuring.

What if I don't have a ruler or measuring tape?

You can use a piece of string or a shoelace to measure your hair, then measure the string or shoelace with a ruler or yardstick.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Measuring your hair length is just one part of a good hair care routine. Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as hairdryers or straighteners.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips and measuring your hair length regularly, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Measuring your hair may seem like a small task, but it's an important skill for any guy to learn. By following these simple steps and tips, you can measure your hair length accurately and keep your hair looking its best.
