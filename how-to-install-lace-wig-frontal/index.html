How To Install Lace Wig Frontal Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your everyday hairstyle and looking for something new? Lace wig frontal can be a great option for you to change your look. But, how to install lace wig frontal can be a bit challenging if you're doing it for the first time. In this article, we will guide you step-by-step on how to install lace wig frontal easily and without any hassle.

Description

A lace wig frontal is a type of wig that covers your hairline from ear to ear. It is made of human hair or synthetic hair and can be styled in any way you want. Installing a lace wig frontal can be a great way to change your hairstyle, especially if you're looking for a natural-looking hairline.

How to Install Lace Wig Frontal

Before we start, you will need a few things to install your lace wig frontal:

  • Lace wig frontal
  • Wig cap
  • Scissors
  • Tape or glue
  • Comb and brush

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

The first thing you need to do is prepare your hair. Start by braiding your hair into cornrows and put on a wig cap. This will help keep your hair in place and make it easier to install the lace wig frontal.

Step 2: Cut the Lace

Next, you need to cut the lace on your lace wig frontal. Use scissors to cut the lace as close to the hairline as possible. Be careful not to cut any of the hairs on the lace wig frontal.

Step 3: Apply Glue or Tape

Now, it's time to apply glue or tape to your lace wig frontal. You can use either glue or tape depending on your preference. Apply a thin layer of glue or tape along the hairline of your lace wig frontal.

Step 4: Position the Wig

After you have applied the glue or tape, position the lace wig frontal on your head. Start by placing it on your forehead and adjust it until it fits comfortably. Be sure to line up the hairline of the lace wig frontal with your own hairline.

Step 5: Cut the Lace on the Sides

Once you have positioned your lace wig frontal, it's time to cut the lace on the sides. Use scissors to cut the lace on the sides of the lace wig frontal. Be sure to cut as close to your hairline as possible.

Step 6: Secure the Lace Wig Frontal

After you have cut the lace on the sides, it's time to secure the lace wig frontal. Use a comb to press the lace wig frontal onto your scalp. Be sure to press firmly to ensure it's secure.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Now that you have installed your lace wig frontal, you can style your hair any way you want. You can curl it, straighten it, or even dye it.

Tips for Installing Lace Wig Frontal

  • Always start by preparing your hair. Clean and dry hair will make it easier to install the lace wig frontal.
  • Use a wig cap to keep your hair in place and make it easier to install the lace wig frontal.
  • Be careful when cutting the lace on the lace wig frontal. You don't want to cut any of the hairs on the wig.
  • Use a small amount of glue or tape. Too much can cause damage to your hairline.
  • Be patient. Installing a lace wig frontal can take some time, so don't rush it.

FAQs

How long does a lace wig frontal last?

A lace wig frontal can last up to six weeks, depending on how well you take care of it.

Can I swim with a lace wig frontal?

We do not recommend swimming with a lace wig frontal. Chlorine and saltwater can damage the wig and cause it to loosen.

Can I dye my lace wig frontal?

Yes, you can dye your lace wig frontal. However, we recommend consulting a professional stylist to avoid damaging the wig.

Hair Care Tips for Lace Wig Frontal

  • Always use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your lace wig frontal.
  • Wash your lace wig frontal every 7-10 days using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your lace wig frontal. If you must, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Store your lace wig frontal on a wig stand or mannequin head to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid using oil-based products on your lace wig frontal as they can cause build-up and damage the wig.

Installing a lace wig frontal can seem daunting at first, but with these step-by-step instructions, you can easily do it yourself. Remember to take your time and be patient. With proper care, your lace wig frontal can last for weeks and give you a natural-looking hairstyle.
