How To Frame Your Face With Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby May 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a way to change up your look without making a drastic change, adding bangs can be a great option. Not only do they help frame your face, but they can also add a touch of youthfulness to your appearance. In this guide, we'll show you how to frame your face with bangs in a way that suits your face shape and hair type.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Frame Your Face with Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Bangs

How to Frame Your Face with Bangs

Framing your face with bangs involves selecting the right style based on your face shape and hair type. There are different types of bangs to choose from, including:

  • Straight Bangs
  • Side-Swept Bangs
  • Curly Bangs
  • Textured Bangs

It's important to choose the right type of bangs that will complement your face shape and hair type.

Face Shape:

The first step to framing your face with bangs is to determine your face shape. The most common face shapes are:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

Once you know your face shape, you can choose the right type of bangs that will flatter your features.

Hair Type:

Your hair type is also an important factor to consider when selecting the right type of bangs. If you have naturally curly hair, for example, you may want to opt for textured or curly bangs that will blend in with your natural hair texture.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Follow these steps to frame your face with bangs:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the right type of bangs for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Choose the length of your bangs based on your preference and face shape.
  3. Part your hair to the side or down the middle, depending on the type of bangs you've chosen.
  4. Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  5. Use a straightening iron or curling wand to add texture to your bangs, depending on your hair type and the type of bangs you've chosen.
  6. Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their shape and length.
  7. Use a styling product, such as hairspray or pomade, to keep your bangs in place.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their shape and length.
  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Use a straightening iron or curling wand to add texture to your bangs.
  • Avoid cutting your bangs too short, as they may be difficult to style.
  • Use a styling product, such as hairspray or pomade, to keep your bangs in place.

FAQs:

Q: How do I know if bangs will suit me?

A: Consult with a hairstylist to determine the right type of bangs for your face shape and hair type.

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Q: How do I style my bangs?

A: Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to create volume and shape. Use a straightening iron or curling wand to add texture to your bangs, depending on your hair type and the type of bangs you've chosen.

Hair Care for Bangs:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep your bangs clean and oil-free.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your bangs too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools on your bangs.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their shape and length.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to frame your face with bangs in a way that suits your style and personality. Remember to consult with a hairstylist to determine the right type of bangs for your face shape and hair type, and to maintain your bangs regularly to keep them looking their best.
