How Do I Know If I Look Good With Bangs

Written by Christine May 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you thinking about getting bangs but not sure if they will suit you? Bangs can be a great way to switch up your look and add some dimension to your hairstyle, but they are not for everyone. Here are some tips and tricks to help you determine if bangs are right for you.

Table of Contents

  • Determine Your Face Shape
  • Consider Your Hair Texture
  • Think About Your Lifestyle
  • Try It Out
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQs

Determine Your Face Shape

One of the most important factors to consider when deciding if bangs are right for you is your face shape. Different face shapes suit different types of bangs. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Heart-shaped faces: Side-swept or angled bangs work well to balance out a wider forehead.
  • Oval faces: Almost any type of bangs will suit an oval face shape.
  • Square faces: Soft, wispy bangs can help soften the angles of a square face.
  • Round faces: Long, side-swept bangs can help elongate a round face shape.

Keep in mind that these are just general guidelines and there are always exceptions. It's always best to consult with your hairstylist to determine which type of bangs will suit you best.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in whether or not bangs will suit you. If you have fine or thin hair, blunt bangs may not be the best option as they can make your hair look even thinner. Instead, opt for wispy or side-swept bangs. If you have curly hair, you may want to avoid straight-across bangs as they can be difficult to style.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Another important factor to consider is your lifestyle. Bangs require more upkeep than other hairstyles as they need to be trimmed regularly. If you have a busy schedule and not a lot of time to style your hair, bangs may not be the best option for you. Additionally, if you play sports or work out frequently, bangs may get in the way and be difficult to manage.

Try It Out

The best way to determine if bangs are right for you is to try them out. You can experiment with clip-in bangs or use a virtual hairstyle app to see how different types of bangs will look on you. Keep in mind that it may take some time to get used to having bangs, so give yourself a few weeks before deciding if you like them or not.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've decided to take the plunge and get bangs, it's important to take proper care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their shape.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs as they can weigh them down.
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh in between washes.

FAQs

Q: Can I get bangs if I have a cowlick?

A: Yes, you can still get bangs if you have a cowlick. It's best to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best type of bangs for your hair texture and cowlick.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with bangs?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with bangs. If you have long bangs, you can pin them back or braid them to keep them out of your face.

Q: How often do I need to trim my bangs?

A: It's best to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.

In conclusion, determining if bangs are right for you requires consideration of your face shape, hair texture, lifestyle, and personal preference. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to make an informed decision and rock your new bangs with confidence.
