Bald Fade Haircut With Beard Black Man The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are a black man looking to take your grooming game to the next level, a bald fade haircut with beard is the perfect choice for you. This stylish haircut has been gaining popularity in recent years and it's easy to see why. Not only does it look great, but it's also low maintenance and versatile. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to get the perfect bald fade haircut with beard.

Description

A bald fade haircut with beard is a style where the hair on the sides and back of the head is gradually tapered to a shorter length, while the hair on top remains longer. The hair is shaved down to the skin, creating a smooth, bald look. The beard is then trimmed and shaped to complement the haircut.

How To

The first step in getting a bald fade haircut with beard is to find a skilled barber who has experience with this style. Once you have found a barber, follow these steps to get the perfect haircut:

Step 1: Choose Your Fade

There are different types of fades you can choose from, including low fade, high fade, and mid fade. Choose the one that suits your style and face shape best. A low fade is best for a round face, a high fade for a square face, and a mid fade for an oval face.

Step 2: Choose Your Beard Style

There are different beard styles you can choose from, including full beard, goatee, and stubble. Choose the one that complements your face shape and personal style.

Step 3: Trim Your Beard

Before you get your haircut, trim your beard to the desired length. This will make it easier for your barber to shape your beard and make it look neat and tidy.

Step 4: Start With The Fade

Your barber will start by trimming the hair on the sides and back of your head with clippers. They will gradually trim the hair shorter until it blends in with the hair on top. This will create a smooth, bald look.

Step 5: Shape Your Beard

Your barber will then shape your beard to complement your haircut. They will trim and shape your beard to make it look neat and tidy. They may also use a straight razor to create sharp lines around your beard.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once your haircut is complete, you can style your hair with pomade or gel. Use a comb to create a neat, polished look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a bald fade haircut with beard:

  • Make sure you choose a skilled barber who has experience with this style
  • Bring a photo of the style you want to show to your barber
  • Be clear about the length and style of your beard
  • Maintain your haircut and beard by trimming regularly

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bald fade haircut with beard:

1. How often should I trim my beard?

You should trim your beard every 2-3 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

2. Can I style my beard differently?

Yes, you can experiment with different beard styles to find the one that suits you best.

3. How long does a bald fade haircut last?

A bald fade haircut can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

Hair Care

To keep your bald fade haircut with beard looking its best, you should follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing your hair and beard with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, using a beard oil to keep your beard moisturized, and getting regular trims to maintain your haircut.

Now that you have all the information you need, it's time to book your appointment with a skilled barber and get the perfect bald fade haircut with beard!
