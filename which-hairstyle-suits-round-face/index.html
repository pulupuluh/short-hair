Which Hairstyle Suits Round Face Tips How Tos And Hair Care

Written by Daniel Jul 01, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you one of those people with a round face? If so, it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle to suit your face shape. The good news is, there are plenty of hairstyles that can flatter your features and make you look your best. In this article, we'll be discussing which hairstyle suits round face and providing tips on how to style your hair for your specific face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How-To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces are characterized by a circular shape, with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal of styling your hair when you have a round face is to elongate your face and create the illusion of angles. The key is to avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, which can make your face appear even rounder.

How-To

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by looking in the mirror and measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three are roughly equal, you have a round face.

Next, consider the length of your hair. Short haircuts can be particularly flattering for round faces, as they can create the illusion of height and angles. A pixie cut, for example, can make your face appear longer and leaner. If you prefer longer hair, consider adding layers to create volume and movement.

Step-by-Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for styling your hair when you have a round face:

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair straight, using a round brush to add volume at the crown of your head.
  2. Part your hair to the side, creating an asymmetrical look that will elongate your face.
  3. If you have bangs, consider sweeping them to the side or cutting them just below your eyebrows to create a flattering frame for your face.
  4. Use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls, which can add texture and movement to your hair.
  5. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as bobs that hit at chin length or shorter.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your hair when you have a round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that hit at the chin or above, as they can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for angled cuts that create the illusion of length.
  • If you have curly hair, consider keeping it longer to weigh it down and create a more elongated look.
  • Consider adding highlights to your hair, which can create depth and dimension and draw attention away from the roundness of your face.
  • Avoid center parts, which can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for a side part to create an asymmetrical look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about which hairstyle suits round face:

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail. However, consider pulling it back loosely and leaving some hair around your face to create a more flattering look.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as bobs that hit at chin length or shorter. Also, avoid blunt cuts that hit at the chin or above, as they can make your face appear even rounder.

Hair Care

When you have a round face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, which can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add volume to your roots.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle to suit your round face and keep your hair looking its best.
