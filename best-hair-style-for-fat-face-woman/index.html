Best Hair Style For Fat Face Woman Tips How To And Faq

Written by Robby Jul 30, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have a round or fat face, you might find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that complements your facial features. The key is to find a hairstyle that adds length and dimension to your face. In this article, we’ll share some tips, how-to, and frequently asked questions about the best hair style for fat face woman.

Table of Contents

  • Tips
  • How-To
  • Step by Step
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Tips

Here are some tips to consider when choosing the best hairstyle for a fat face:

  1. Opt for hairstyles that add length and dimension to your face, such as long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts.
  2. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs and chin-length bobs.
  3. Try to add volume to the crown of your head to elongate your face shape.
  4. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.

How-To

Here are some ideas for the best hair style for fat face woman:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for women with fat faces because they add length and volume to the hair. The layers should start at the chin or below and should be soft and subtle. You can also add side-swept bangs to frame your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob is another great option for women with fat faces. The longer side of the bob should be on the opposite side of your face shape to create an illusion of length. You can also add layers and texture to the longer side to add volume and movement.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a classic option for women with fat faces because they draw attention away from the cheeks and create an illusion of length. The bangs should be long and soft, starting from the side of your head and sweeping across your forehead.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for styling your hair:

Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create soft waves.
  4. Use a curling iron to add loose waves to the ends of your hair.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Asymmetrical Bob

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a styling cream to the ends of your hair to create texture.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create soft waves.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten the shorter side of your bob.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair style for fat face woman:

What is the best haircut for a fat face?

The best haircut for a fat face is one that adds length and dimension to the face. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts are great options.

Should I get blunt bangs if I have a fat face?

No, blunt bangs should be avoided if you have a fat face because they add width to your face shape.

How can I add volume to my hair?

You can add volume to your hair by using a volumizing mousse or spray at the roots, blow-drying your hair using a round brush, and using a curling iron to add loose waves to the ends of your hair.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  1. Wash your hair every other day to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and soft.
  3. Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent heat damage.
  4. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  5. Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, the best hair style for fat face woman is one that adds length and dimension to the face. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts are great options to consider. Remember to also take care of your hair by maintaining a healthy hair care routine.
