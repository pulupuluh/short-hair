Is Short Or Long Hair Better For Big Noses

Written by Christine Mar 03, 2023 · 9 min read

Having a big nose can sometimes make people feel self-conscious about their appearance. But did you know that your hairstyle can actually help balance out the size of your nose? In this article, we’ll explore whether short or long hair is better for big noses, and provide tips on how to style your hair to complement your facial features.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Short Hair for Big Noses
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Big Noses
  4. Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle for Your Big Nose
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Big Noses

Description

The answer to whether short or long hair is better for big noses depends on a few factors. Generally speaking, short hair can draw more attention to your facial features, including your nose. On the other hand, long hair can help balance out the proportions of your face and make your nose look smaller.

Ultimately, the best hairstyle for big noses is one that you feel confident and comfortable in. It’s important to choose a style that makes you feel good about yourself, whether that’s short, long, or somewhere in between.

How to Style Short Hair for Big Noses

If you prefer short hair, there are a few things you can do to help balance out the size of your nose:

1. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help create the illusion of a smaller nose by drawing attention to your eyes. Try parting your bangs to one side and sweeping them across your forehead.

2. Layers

Layers can help add volume and dimension to your hair, which can help balance out the proportions of your face. Ask your stylist for choppy layers that frame your face.

3. Short Pixie Cut

If you’re feeling bold, a short pixie cut can help draw attention away from your nose and towards your eyes and cheekbones. This style works best for those with a more angular face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair for Big Noses

If you prefer long hair, there are a few tips and tricks you can use to help balance out the size of your nose:

1. Layers

Long layers can help add volume and movement to your hair, which can help balance out the proportions of your face. Ask your stylist for face-framing layers that start at your cheekbones.

2. Middle Part

A middle part can help create the illusion of a smaller nose by drawing attention to the center of your face. Use a fine-tooth comb to create a straight part down the middle of your head.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves can help soften the angles of your face and create a more balanced look. Use a curling iron or sea salt spray to add texture and volume to your hair.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle for Your Big Nose

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your big nose:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

The shape of your face can also play a role in determining the best hairstyle for your big nose. If you have a more angular face shape, a short haircut may work best. If you have a rounder face shape, longer layers can help elongate your face.

2. Avoid Harsh Lines

Avoid hairstyles that have harsh lines or blunt cuts, as these can draw attention to your nose. Instead, opt for styles that have softer, more natural-looking edges.

3. Experiment with Accessories

Hair accessories like headbands, scarves, and hats can help draw attention away from your nose and towards other parts of your face. Try experimenting with different accessories to see what works best for you.

FAQs

Q. Can highlights or lowlights help balance out the size of my nose?

A. Yes, highlights or lowlights can help add dimension to your hair and create a more balanced look. Talk to your stylist about adding subtle highlights or lowlights to your hair.

Q. Should I avoid wearing my hair in a ponytail if I have a big nose?

A. Not necessarily. Wearing your hair in a ponytail can actually help elongate your face and create a more balanced look. Just be sure to avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can draw attention to your nose.

Hair Care Tips for Big Noses

Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo that won’t strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Condition Regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and hydrated.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too frequently, as they can damage your hair. When you do use them, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.

At the end of the day, the best hairstyle for big noses is one that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Whether you prefer short or long hair, there are plenty of styles and techniques you can use to help balance out the size of your nose and create a more balanced look.
