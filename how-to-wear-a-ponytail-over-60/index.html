How To Wear A Ponytail Over 60 Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jun 11, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’re over 60 and looking for an easy hairstyle that’s both practical and stylish, a ponytail might be just what you need. Not only is it easy to do, but it’s also versatile and can be worn in many different ways. In this article, we’ll show you how to wear a ponytail over 60, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and answers to common questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Ponytail Over 60
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Wearing a Ponytail
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Wear a Ponytail Over 60

First things first, it’s important to choose the right style of ponytail for your face shape and hair texture. Some popular options include:

  • A high ponytail
  • A low ponytail
  • A side ponytail
  • A messy ponytail
  • A sleek, straight ponytail
  • A curly ponytail

Once you’ve chosen your style, you’ll want to make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can add some texture by using a curling iron, straightener, or texturizing spray.

Next, gather your hair at the crown of your head or at the nape of your neck, depending on the style you’ve chosen. Secure it with an elastic band or hair tie.

You can leave some hair loose around your face for a softer, more relaxed look, or slick it back for a more polished appearance.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here’s a breakdown of how to wear a ponytail over 60:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose your ponytail style.
  3. Gather your hair at the crown of your head or at the nape of your neck.
  4. Secure it with an elastic band or hair tie.
  5. Leave some hair loose around your face or slick it back.
  6. Optional: add some texture with a curling iron, straightener, or texturizing spray.

Tips for Wearing a Ponytail

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your ponytail:

  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Use a high-quality elastic band or hair tie to prevent breakage and damage.
  • Don’t pull your hair too tight, as this can cause headaches and discomfort.
  • Add some volume by teasing the hair at the crown of your head.
  • Accessorize with a scarf or headband for a pop of color or texture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some answers to common questions about wearing a ponytail:

  • Can I wear a ponytail if I have short hair?

    • Yes, you can! Just gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with an elastic band or hair tie.

  • Is it okay to wear a ponytail every day?

    • Yes, but be careful not to pull your hair too tight, as this can cause damage over time.

  • Can I wear a ponytail to a formal event?

    • Yes, but you’ll want to make sure it looks polished and put-together. Consider adding some volume or accessorizing with a fancy hair clip or barrette.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and strong, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to wear a ponytail over 60 with confidence and style!
