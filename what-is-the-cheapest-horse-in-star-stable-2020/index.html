Description

The cheapest horse in Star Stable 2020 is the Icelandic Horse. It costs 750 Star Coins and is available for purchase in the game's store. The Icelandic Horse is a small and sturdy horse that is known for its strength and endurance. It has a unique gait called the tölt, which is smooth and comfortable to ride. The Icelandic Horse comes in a variety of colors, including black, chestnut, and gray.

How to Get the Icelandic Horse

To get the Icelandic Horse, you must have at least 750 Star Coins in your account. You can purchase Star Coins with real money or earn them by completing quests and participating in events in the game. Once you have enough Star Coins, go to the game's store and select the Icelandic Horse. Click on the "Buy" button and the horse will be added to your stable.

Stats

The Icelandic Horse has the following stats:

  • Speed: 3/5
  • Acceleration: 2/5
  • Turning: 3/5
  • Stamina: 4/5

While the Icelandic Horse may not be the fastest or most agile horse in the game, it has a high stamina rating, which means it can run for longer periods without getting tired.

Tips

If you're planning on getting the Icelandic Horse, here are some tips to help you take care of it:

  • Feed your horse regularly to keep its energy levels up.
  • Train your horse to improve its stats and make it a better rider.
  • Take your horse on regular rides to keep it in shape and build a bond with it.
  • Customize your horse's appearance with different saddles, bridles, and accessories.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the cheapest horse in Star Stable 2020:

Can I sell my Icelandic Horse?

No, you cannot sell your Icelandic Horse. Once you purchase a horse, it becomes a permanent part of your stable.

Can I breed my Icelandic Horse?

No, you cannot breed your Icelandic Horse. Breeding is not currently available in the game.

What is the lifespan of my Icelandic Horse?

The lifespan of your Icelandic Horse is unlimited, as long as you take care of it properly.

Hair Care

One important aspect of taking care of your Icelandic Horse is maintaining its hair. Here are some tips on how to keep your horse's hair looking its best:

  • Brush your horse's hair regularly to remove tangles and dirt.
  • Use a detangler spray to make brushing easier and prevent breakage.
  • Trim your horse's hair regularly to keep it neat and tidy.
  • Use a conditioning spray to keep your horse's hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your horse's hair, as it can cause damage.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your Icelandic Horse looks and feels its best.

Conclusion

With its sturdy build and high stamina rating, the Icelandic Horse is a reliable and versatile horse that can take you on many adventures in the game. By taking care of your horse's needs and maintaining its hair, you can enjoy your time with your Icelandic Horse to the fullest.
