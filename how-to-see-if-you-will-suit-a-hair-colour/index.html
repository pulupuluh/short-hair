How To See If You Will Suit A Hair Colour

Written by Oliver Jul 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're thinking about changing your hair colour, it's important to choose a shade that will suit you. There are many factors to consider, such as your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. In this article, we'll give you tips on how to see if you will suit a hair colour.

Table of Contents

  • How to See if You Will Suit a Hair Colour
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to See if You Will Suit a Hair Colour

The easiest way to see if you will suit a hair colour is to use a virtual hair colour tool. Many websites and apps offer this service, where you can upload a photo of yourself and try on different hair colours. This will give you an idea of how the hair colour will look on you.

Another way to see if you will suit a hair colour is to look at your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, you will suit warm hair colours such as golden blonde, copper, and auburn. If you have a cool skin tone, you will suit cool hair colours such as ash blonde, silver, and violet. If you have a neutral skin tone, you can choose any hair colour.

Your eye colour can also help you determine which hair colour will suit you. If you have blue or green eyes, you will suit cool hair colours such as ash blonde and silver. If you have brown or hazel eyes, you will suit warm hair colours such as golden blonde and copper.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to see if you will suit a hair colour:

  1. Determine your skin tone - warm, cool, or neutral.
  2. Look at your eye colour - blue or green for cool, brown or hazel for warm.
  3. Use a virtual hair colour tool to try on different hair colours.
  4. Consult with a professional hair stylist for their opinion.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair colour:

  • Consider your natural hair colour - going too far from your natural colour can be damaging and difficult to maintain.
  • Think about your lifestyle - if you have a busy schedule, choose a low maintenance hair colour.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new - hair colour can always be changed if you don't like it.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hair colour:

Q: Will a hair colour look different on me than it does on the model?

A: Yes, hair colour can look different on everyone depending on their natural hair colour, skin tone, and eye colour.

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, but it's important to use a dye specifically formulated for sensitive skin and to do a patch test beforehand.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: It depends on the hair colour and how fast your hair grows, but typically every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the colour and keep your hair healthy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for coloured hair.
  • Avoid hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage the hair and fade the colour.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to see if you will suit a hair colour, you can confidently choose a shade that will flatter you. Remember to consider your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour, and don't be afraid to try something new!
