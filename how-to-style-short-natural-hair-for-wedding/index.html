How To Style Short Natural Hair For Wedding Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Mar 24, 2023 · 8 min read

When it comes to wedding hairstyles, short natural hair can be both chic and stylish. And while some may think that options are limited when it comes to styling short hair, there are actually plenty of ways to make your natural hair look stunning on your big day. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to style short natural hair for a wedding.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Short Natural Hair for Wedding
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Natural Hair for Wedding
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Natural Hair

How to Style Short Natural Hair for Wedding

When it comes to styling short natural hair for a wedding, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to make sure that your hair is healthy and moisturized. This will ensure that your hair looks its best and is easy to style. Second, you want to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and wedding dress. And lastly, you want to make sure that your hairstyle will last all day and look great in photos.

1. Braids and Twists

Braids and twists are a great option for short natural hair. They can add texture and dimension to your hair while also keeping it in place. You can opt for a simple braided crown or twist-out, or you can add some intricate details like cornrows or micro braids.

2. Updos

Updos are another great option for short natural hair. You can create a sleek and sophisticated look with a low bun or opt for a more playful and romantic look with a messy updo. You can also add some accessories like flowers or hairpins to make your updo even more special.

3. Curls and Waves

Short natural hair can also look stunning with some curls or waves. You can use a curling iron or flexi rods to create soft waves or tight curls. You can also add some volume and texture with a diffuser and some mousse or curl cream.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start with Clean Hair

Before styling your short natural hair, make sure it’s clean and moisturized. You can use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to cleanse and hydrate your hair. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to add some extra moisture.

2. Choose Your Style

Once your hair is clean and moisturized, choose the style that you want to create. You can opt for braids, twists, updos, or curls and waves.

3. Prep Your Hair

Depending on the style you choose, you may need to prep your hair. For braids and twists, you can use a styling gel or cream to help keep your hair in place. For updos, you may need to tease your hair or use some bobby pins to secure it. For curls and waves, you can use a curling iron or flexi rods to create the desired texture.

4. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is prepped, start styling. Follow the steps for your chosen style and make sure to secure your hair in place.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is styled, finish with a light hairspray to keep it in place. You can also add some shine spray or oil for extra shine.

Tips for Styling Short Natural Hair for Wedding

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short natural hair for a wedding:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and wedding dress.
  • Make sure your hair is healthy and moisturized.
  • Use a styling gel or cream to help keep your hair in place.
  • Use bobby pins or hairpins to secure your hair in place.
  • Finish with a light hairspray to keep your hair in place.

FAQs

Q: How do I keep my short natural hair from frizzing?

A: To keep your short natural hair from frizzing, make sure to use a leave-in conditioner and a styling cream or gel. You can also use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help reduce frizz.

Q: Can I add extensions to my short natural hair?

A: Yes, you can add extensions to your short natural hair. You can opt for braids or twists with extensions or use clip-in extensions for extra length and volume.

Hair Care for Short Natural Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your short natural hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to cleanse and hydrate your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to add extra moisture to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair at night by using a satin or silk pillowcase or wearing a satin bonnet or scarf.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a stunning hairstyle for your wedding with short natural hair. Whether you opt for braids, updos, or curls, make sure to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful on your big day.
