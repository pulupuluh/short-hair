Hair Styles Over 50 2020 A Guide To Looking Your Best

Written by Daniel Jul 27, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, our hair naturally changes too. It can become thinner, grayer, and lose its luster. However, that doesn't mean we can't still look fabulous! There are plenty of trendy and flattering hair styles for women over 50 in 2020. In this article, we'll explore some of the best options and offer tips on how to achieve them.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose a Flattering Hair Style
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Products to Try

How to Choose a Flattering Hair Style

There are a few key factors to consider when choosing a hair style that will be both trendy and flattering for women over 50. First, consider your face shape. Some styles work better for round faces, while others are better suited for oval or square faces. Second, consider your hair texture. If you have thin hair, you may want to avoid styles that require a lot of volume. Third, think about your lifestyle. Do you have time to style your hair every day, or do you need something low-maintenance?

Some popular hair styles for women over 50 in 2020 include:

1. Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

This classic style can be updated with side-swept bangs, which help to frame the face and add dimension. It works well for women with oval or heart-shaped faces.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, low-maintenance style that can work well for women with thinning hair. It's also a great option for those with square or angular faces.

3. Shaggy Layers

This style involves adding layers to create texture and movement. It's a great option for women with naturally wavy hair or those who don't want to spend a lot of time styling their hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

No matter what hair style you choose, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If you have thin hair, you may want to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair.
  4. If you're using a curling iron or straightener, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and work in small sections.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Healthy hair is key to looking your best, no matter what your age. Here are some tips for keeping your hair in great condition:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: Can I still dye my hair?

A: Yes! There are plenty of hair color options for women over 50. Just be sure to use a dye that is gentle on your hair and scalp.

Hair Care Products to Try

Here are some hair care products that can help keep your hair looking healthy and fabulous:

  • Living Proof Full Dry Volume Blast
  • Oribe Dry Texturizing Spray
  • Bumble and Bumble Thickening Shampoo and Conditioner
  • Redken Extreme Anti-Snap Leave-In Treatment
  • Moroccanoil Treatment

With these tips and hair style options, you can look and feel your best at any age. Don't be afraid to experiment with new styles and products until you find what works best for you!
