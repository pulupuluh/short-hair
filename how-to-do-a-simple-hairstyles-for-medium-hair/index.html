How To Do A Simple Hairstyles For Medium Hair

Written by William Jul 24, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have medium length hair, you may be wondering how to style it in a way that is quick, easy, and looks great. Fortunately, there are several simple hairstyles that you can try at home. In this article, we will show you how to do a simple hairstyles for medium hair, step by step.

Description

Medium length hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. However, sometimes you may not have the time or energy to do a complicated hairstyle. That's where simple hairstyles come in. These styles are quick and easy to do, and can be done with just a few hair tools.

How to

To start, brush your hair to remove any tangles. Then, decide which simple hairstyle you want to try. You can choose from a ponytail, a braid, a bun, or a half-up, half-down style. Each style requires different steps, but all of them are easy to do.

Ponytail

To do a ponytail, gather all of your hair at the back of your head and secure it with a hair tie. You can position the ponytail high or low, depending on your preference. For a sleek look, use a comb to smooth down any flyaways.

Braid

To do a braid, start by dividing your hair into three equal sections. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section. Continue braiding until you reach the end of your hair, then secure with a hair tie.

Bun

To do a bun, gather your hair at the back of your head and twist it into a coil. Then, wrap the coil around itself to form a bun shape. Secure with bobby pins or a hair tie. For a messy bun, pull out a few strands of hair to frame your face.

Half-Up, Half-Down

To do a half-up, half-down style, gather the top section of your hair and secure it with a hair tie. You can position the ponytail high or low, depending on your preference. For a sleek look, use a comb to smooth down any flyaways. Leave the bottom section of your hair down.

Step by Step

Here are the step by step instructions for each simple hairstyle:

Ponytail

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather all of your hair at the back of your head.
  3. Secure the hair with a hair tie.
  4. Smooth down flyaways with a comb.

Braid

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into three equal sections.
  3. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  4. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  5. Continue braiding until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Secure the braid with a hair tie.

Bun

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head.
  3. Twist the hair into a coil.
  4. Wrap the coil around itself to form a bun shape.
  5. Secure the bun with bobby pins or a hair tie.
  6. Pull out a few strands of hair for a messy look.

Half-Up, Half-Down

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather the top section of your hair.
  3. Secure the hair with a hair tie.
  4. Smooth down flyaways with a comb.
  5. Leave the bottom section of your hair down.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect simple hairstyle:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color for a seamless look.
  • Apply a small amount of hairspray to keep your style in place.
  • Experiment with different positions for your ponytail or bun.
  • Add a hair accessory, such as a clip or headband, to dress up your style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about simple hairstyles for medium hair:

What if my hair is too short for these styles?

If your hair is too short for these styles, try using hair extensions or hair accessories to add length and volume.

How can I make my simple hairstyle look more polished?

To make your simple hairstyle look more polished, use a comb to smooth down flyaways and apply a small amount of hairspray to keep your style in place.

What are some other simple hairstyles I can try?

Other simple hairstyles you can try include a top knot, a low bun, a side braid, or a messy updo.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to wash it regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
