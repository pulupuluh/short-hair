Thinning Hair On Top Of Head Male Hairstyles A Guide For 2023

Written by Oliver Apr 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Thinning hair is a common problem that many men face, especially as they age. It can be frustrating and challenging to style your hair when you're dealing with thinning hair on the top of your head. However, there are plenty of hairstyles that can help you look your best while making the most of your hair's texture and thickness. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best hairstyles for men with thinning hair on top of their heads.

Description

Thinning hair on top of the head is a common hair problem for men. As men age, their hair tends to thin out, and this can be especially noticeable on the top of the head. This can make it challenging to find a style that works for you, but there are plenty of options available. From short and spiky to longer and layered, there are many ways to style thinning hair to make it look fuller and thicker.

How to

When it comes to styling thinning hair on top of the head, there are a few things you can do to make the most of your hair. Here are some tips:

1. Keep it short:

Short haircuts can help make thinning hair look fuller and thicker. A buzz cut, crew cut, or short textured cut can be a great option for men with thinning hair on top of their heads.

2. Use volumizing products:

Volumizing products like mousse or volumizing spray can help add volume and fullness to thinning hair. Apply these products to your hair before styling to give your hair a boost.

3. Try a layered cut:

A layered cut can help add texture and volume to thinning hair. Ask your stylist to add layers to your hair to create a fuller, more voluminous look.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling thinning hair on top of the head:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add volume and fullness to your hair.

Step 2: Apply volumizing product

Apply a volumizing product like mousse or spray to your hair. This will help add volume and fullness to your hair.

Step 3: Style your hair

Style your hair using a comb or your fingers. If you have a short haircut, you can use your fingers to create a messy, textured look. If you have longer hair, you can use a comb to create a more polished look.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

Finish your style with a light hold hairspray. This will help keep your hair in place without weighing it down.

Tips

Here are some tips for styling thinning hair on top of the head:

1. Use a blow dryer:

Using a blow dryer can help add volume and fullness to thinning hair. Use a low heat setting and a round brush to create lift at the roots.

2. Avoid heavy products:

Avoid using heavy products like wax or pomade, as these can weigh down thinning hair and make it look even thinner.

3. Consider hair fibers:

Hair fibers can be a great option for men with thinning hair. These fibers attach to your existing hair to create the appearance of thicker, fuller hair.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for thinning hair on top of the head?

A short, textured cut or a layered cut can be a great option for men with thinning hair on top of their heads.

Can volumizing products help with thinning hair?

Yes, volumizing products like mousse or spray can help add volume and fullness to thinning hair.

What should I avoid when styling thinning hair?

Avoid using heavy products like wax or pomade, as these can weigh down thinning hair and make it look even thinner.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for men with thinning hair on top of their heads. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

1. Use a gentle shampoo:

Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging your hair. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients.

2. Avoid heat styling:

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make it look even thinner.

3. Get regular haircuts:

Regular haircuts can help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Thinning hair on top of the head can be a challenge, but with the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look your best. Try out some of these hairstyles and tips to make the most of your thinning hair.
