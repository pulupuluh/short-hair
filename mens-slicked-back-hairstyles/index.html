Mens Slicked Back Hairstyles A Timeless Look For The Modern Man

Written by Christine Sep 11, 2023 · 8 min read

The slicked back hairstyle has been a classic look for men for decades. It exudes a sense of sophistication and confidence, making it a popular choice for both casual and formal occasions. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about mens slicked back hairstyles, including how to achieve the look, step by step instructions, styling tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Look
  • Step by Step Instructions
  • Styling Tips
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

The slicked back hairstyle is characterized by hair that is combed back from the forehead and styled with a slick, shiny finish. It can be achieved with a variety of hair lengths and textures, making it a versatile look for men of all ages and styles. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or a more relaxed and tousled finish, the slicked back hairstyle can be customized to suit your individual preferences.

How to Achieve the Look

Before you begin styling your hair, it's important to choose the right products for your hair type and desired finish. For a sleek and polished look, opt for a high-shine pomade or gel. For a more relaxed and natural finish, choose a matte-finish product or a texturizing spray. Once you have your products ready, follow these steps to achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash and condition your hair as usual, then towel dry until it is slightly damp. Avoid using a blow dryer, as this can make your hair too dry and difficult to style.

Step 2: Apply Product

Scoop a dime-sized amount of product onto your fingertips and rub them together to warm the product. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, starting from the back and working your way forward. Be sure to distribute the product evenly to ensure a consistent finish.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a fine-toothed comb, comb your hair back from your forehead, starting at the crown of your head. Be sure to comb your hair evenly in all directions to avoid any bumps or ridges.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is combed back, use your fingers or a comb to style it into your desired shape. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways, then add additional product as needed to create a sleek, polished finish.

Styling Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle:

  • Experiment with different hair lengths and textures to find the look that suits you best.
  • If you have thick or curly hair, you may need to use additional product to achieve the desired finish.
  • For a more relaxed and natural finish, try using a texturizing spray or a matte-finish product.
  • If you have a receding hairline, avoid slicking your hair back too tightly, as this can accentuate the hair loss.
  • Consider adding some volume to your hair by blow drying it before styling.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the slicked back hairstyle suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the slicked back hairstyle can be customized to suit any face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to add some volume to the top of your hair to balance out your features. If you have a square face, you can slick your hair back tightly to create a more angular look.

What products should I use for a natural finish?

If you prefer a more natural and relaxed finish, try using a texturizing spray or a matte-finish product. These products will give your hair a more tousled and effortless look.

Can I achieve the slicked back hairstyle with short hair?

Yes, the slicked back hairstyle can be achieved with short hair. Simply comb your hair back from your forehead and use a small amount of product to create a sleek finish.

Hair Care Tips

To maintain healthy hair and a polished finish, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too frequently, as they can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a neat appearance.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect slicked back hairstyle and maintain a polished and sophisticated look. Remember to experiment with different products and styles to find the look that suits you best!
