Guy Hair Short Sides Long Top The Trending Hairstyle Of 2023

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile hairstyle that's easy to maintain, then the guy hair short sides long top is the perfect style for you. This hairstyle has been popular among men for years, and it's still going strong in 2023. It's a classic look that never goes out of style and can be adapted to suit any face shape and hair type.

Description

The guy hair short sides long top style is all about contrast. It features short hair on the sides and back of the head, while the top is left longer. This creates an edgy and modern look that's perfect for any occasion. The style is versatile, and you can experiment with different lengths and textures to create a look that's unique to you.

How to Style Guy Hair Short Sides Long Top

The guy hair short sides long top style is easy to style and maintain. You'll need a good quality hair product, such as a pomade or wax, to help you achieve the look. Here's how to style your hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo. This will remove any dirt and oil from your hair and scalp, making it easier to style. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry.

Step 2: Apply Hair Product

Apply a small amount of hair product to your fingertips and rub it together to warm it up. Then, apply it evenly to your hair, starting with the back and sides. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, making sure it's evenly distributed.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape. You can leave the top longer and messy or slick it back for a more formal look. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the guy hair short sides long top style:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

To achieve the guy hair short sides long top look, you'll need to get a haircut that's short on the sides and longer on top. You can ask your barber for a fade, undercut, or tapered cut. Make sure you communicate clearly with your barber to get the look you want.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry.

Step 3: Apply Hair Product

Apply a small amount of hair product to your fingertips and rub it together to warm it up. Then, apply it evenly to your hair, starting with the back and sides. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, making sure it's evenly distributed.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape. You can leave the top longer and messy or slick it back for a more formal look. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

To maintain the guy hair short sides long top style, here are some tips:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep the sides and back short and neat.
  • Use a good quality hair product to help you achieve the desired look.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the guy hair short sides long top style:

Q: Can this style be adapted for curly hair?

A: Yes, this style can be adapted for curly hair. You'll need to work with a barber who has experience cutting curly hair to get the right look.

Q: How long should the top be?

A: The length of the top can vary depending on your personal preference. Some men prefer a longer top, while others prefer a shorter top. Talk to your barber to find the length that suits your face shape and hair type.

Q: What kind of hair product should I use?

A: You can use a pomade, wax, or gel to help you achieve the look. Choose a product that works well with your hair type and provides the hold and shine you want.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

Overall, the guy hair short sides long top style is a classic look that's perfect for any occasion. With a little bit of styling and maintenance, you can achieve the perfect look that suits your personality and style. So, go ahead and try this trendy hairstyle today!
