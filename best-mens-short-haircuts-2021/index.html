Best Mens Short Haircuts 2021

Written by Petter Mar 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, then you'll love these best mens short haircuts 2021. In this article, we'll explore some of the most popular short haircuts for men that are trending in 2021. From classic buzz cuts to modern textured hairstyles, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Short haircuts for men have been popular for decades, and they're not going away anytime soon. In 2021, we're seeing a lot of classic haircuts making a comeback, such as the buzz cut, crew cut, and high and tight. We're also seeing a lot of modern textured hairstyles, such as the spiky hair, messy hair, and slicked-back hair. So whether you're a traditionalist or a trendsetter, there's a short haircut for you in 2021.

How to

Before you get a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to go for a haircut that adds height and volume on top, such as a crew cut or spiky hair. If you have fine hair, you may want to go for a textured haircut that adds thickness and dimension, such as a messy crop or slicked-back hair. Once you've decided on a haircut, you can show a picture to your barber or stylist and they can help you achieve the look you want.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of the most popular short haircuts for men in 2021:

Buzz Cut

  1. Start with a #3 or #4 clipper guard
  2. Cut the hair evenly all over the head
  3. Use a shorter guard for the sides and back
  4. Trim the hairline with clippers or scissors
  5. Apply a small amount of hair product for shine (optional)

Crew Cut

  1. Start with a #4 or #5 clipper guard
  2. Cut the hair shorter on the sides and back
  3. Keep the hair longer on top
  4. Blend the hair with scissors or clippers
  5. Apply a small amount of hair product for texture (optional)

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short haircut:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your face shape
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness
  • Bring a picture to your barber or stylist
  • Communicate clearly what you want
  • Ask for styling tips and product recommendations

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for men:

Q: What is the most popular short haircut for men in 2021?

A: The buzz cut and crew cut are both very popular in 2021.

Q: How often should I get a short haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the length. Generally, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: How do I style a short haircut?

A: Use a small amount of hair product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy and stylish short hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using too much heat styling or chemical treatments
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

In conclusion, there are many great short haircuts for men in 2021. Whether you're looking for a classic or modern look, there's a haircut that will suit your needs. Just remember to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and hair texture, and communicate clearly with your barber or stylist. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can look stylish and confident all year round.
