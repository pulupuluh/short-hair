Best Hair For Round Face Male

Written by Bardi May 12, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. However, with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create a flattering look that complements your features. In this article, we will discuss the best hair for round face male and provide tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face has soft, curved features and a width that is almost equal to its length. Hairstyles that add height and volume on top and length to the sides can help balance out the proportions of a round face. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too flat on top, as they can make the face appear even rounder.

How to

The key to finding the best hair for round face male is to choose a cut that adds height and volume on top and creates the illusion of length on the sides. The following hairstyles are great options for round faces:

1. Side-Parted Undercut

The side-parted undercut is a stylish and versatile haircut that works well for round faces. The longer hair on top adds height and volume, while the shaved sides create the illusion of length. To style, apply a small amount of pomade or wax to damp hair and comb it to one side. Use a blow dryer to add volume, and finish with a light hairspray.

2. Messy Quiff

The messy quiff is a modern and edgy haircut that can flatter round faces. The textured, tousled hair on top adds height and volume, while the shorter sides create a more angular look. To style, apply a texturizing product to damp hair and blow-dry it with your fingers. Use a small amount of pomade or wax to create a messy, undone look.

3. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a low-maintenance and stylish haircut that suits round faces. The short, textured hair on top adds height and volume, while the tapered sides create a more defined jawline. To style, apply a small amount of styling cream to damp hair and comb it forward. Use your fingers to create texture, and finish with a light hairspray.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow to achieve the best hair for round face male:

  1. Choose a haircut that adds height and volume on top and creates the illusion of length on the sides.
  2. Use a texturizing or volumizing product to add volume and texture to your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush or your fingers to create volume.
  4. Use a small amount of pomade or wax to create the desired shape and texture.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best hair for round face male:

  • Choose a haircut that complements your face shape and features.
  • Ask your hairstylist for advice on the best hairstyle for your face shape.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find the perfect look.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dull.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face male?

The best haircut for a round face male is one that adds height and volume on top and creates the illusion of length on the sides. The side-parted undercut, messy quiff, and textured crop are great options.

What should I avoid if I have a round face?

If you have a round face, avoid hairstyles that are too short or too flat on top, as they can make your face appear even rounder. Also, avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as a bowl cut or a fringe.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and healthy.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.

With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create a flattering look that enhances your features and complements your round face. Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find the perfect look for you.
