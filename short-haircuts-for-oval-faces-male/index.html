Short Haircuts For Oval Faces Male A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Sep 02, 2023 · 7 min read

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile, as it can pull off a wide range of hairstyles. However, with so many options available, it can be difficult to decide on the perfect haircut. In this article, we'll guide you through the best short haircuts for oval faces male, including how to achieve them, tips for styling, and hair care advice.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Oval Faces Male

Before choosing a short haircut, it's important to determine your face shape. An oval face is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, and prominent cheekbones. Once you've determined that you have an oval face, the next step is to choose a haircut that complements your features. Here are some tips:

  • Opt for a haircut that is shorter on the sides and longer on top, as this will balance out your face shape.
  • Avoid haircuts that are too short on the sides, as this can make your face look elongated.
  • If you have a receding hairline, consider a buzz cut or a crew cut.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Short Haircuts for Oval Faces Male

Now that you know what to look for in a short haircut, it's time to learn how to achieve it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or product buildup, and leave your hair feeling clean and healthy.
  2. Towel dry your hair, and apply a styling product of your choice. We recommend a pomade or wax for short haircuts, as these products provide hold without weighing your hair down.
  3. Using a comb, create a side part or slick your hair back. If you prefer a messy look, use your fingers to tousle your hair.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Tips for Styling Short Haircuts for Oval Faces Male

Styling short haircuts can be challenging, but with these tips, you'll be able to achieve the perfect look:

  • Invest in high-quality styling products, as these will provide the hold and texture you need without damaging your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles until you find the one that suits you best. Don't be afraid to try something new!
  • If you have thick hair, consider using a thinning scissors to remove bulk and make styling easier.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

FAQs About Short Haircuts for Oval Faces Male

What is the best short haircut for oval faces male?

The best short haircut for oval faces male is one that is shorter on the sides and longer on top, as this will balance out your face shape.

Can oval faces male pull off a buzz cut?

Yes, oval faces male can pull off a buzz cut. However, if you have a receding hairline, a buzz cut may not be the best option.

How often should I visit my barber for a haircut?

We recommend visiting your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts for Oval Faces Male

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or applying a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect short haircut for your oval face shape. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
