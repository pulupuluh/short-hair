What Length Is A No 4 Haircut

Written by Aprilia Jun 12, 2023 · 7 min read

When it comes to getting a haircut, one of the most common questions people ask is, "What length is a No 4 haircut?" The answer is simple: a No 4 haircut is 1/2 inch or 12.7mm long. This length is perfect for those who want a short, but not too short, haircut.

Description

A No 4 haircut is a popular choice for men who want a short, neat and clean look. It is also a great option for those who want a low-maintenance haircut that requires little to no styling. The length of a No 4 haircut is just right – it is not too long, nor is it too short. It is a versatile length that can be styled in various ways.

How to Get a No 4 Haircut

If you want to get a No 4 haircut, you can either visit your local barber or do it yourself at home. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

You will need a good set of clippers to achieve a No 4 haircut. Make sure the clippers have a guard attachment with a length of 1/2 inch or 12.7mm.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and will give you a better result.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Attach the No 4 guard to your clippers and start cutting your hair. Begin at the back of your head and work your way to the front. Make sure to cut your hair evenly to avoid any uneven spots.

Step 4: Clean Up

Once you have finished cutting your hair, use a pair of scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven spots. You can also use a comb to help you see any areas that need trimming.

Tips for Maintaining Your No 4 Haircut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your No 4 haircut:

  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair looking and feeling great.
  • Use a styling product, such as hair gel or pomade, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about No 4 haircuts:

What is a No 4.5 haircut?

A No 4.5 haircut is slightly longer than a No 4 haircut. It is 5/8 inch or 15.875mm long.

What is the difference between a No 3 and No 4 haircut?

A No 3 haircut is 3/8 inch or 9.525mm long, while a No 4 haircut is 1/2 inch or 12.7mm long. The difference between the two haircuts is only 1/8 inch or 3.175mm.

Can women get a No 4 haircut?

Yes, women can get a No 4 haircut. It is a versatile length that can be styled in various ways.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as hair dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get enough sleep to help your body and hair repair itself.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as hair dyes or bleach, as they can damage your hair.

Now that you know what length is a No 4 haircut, you can decide if it is the right haircut for you. Whether you choose to get a No 4 haircut at the barbershop or do it yourself at home, it is a great option for those who want a short, neat and clean look.
