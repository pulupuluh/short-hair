Do Guys Like Big Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Apr 02, 2023 Â· 10 min read

As women, we often wonder if guys prefer long hair, short hair, curly hair, or straight hair. But what about big hair? In this article, we'll explore the question of whether or not guys like big hair and provide some tips on how to achieve this look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve Big Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Big Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Big Hair

Description

Big hair can be defined as voluminous, bouncy, and full of life. It's often associated with the 80s and 90s, but it's making a comeback in today's fashion trends. Some women are hesitant to try big hair because they worry it may come across as too much, but the truth is that big hair can be a real head-turner.

So, do guys like big hair? The answer is yes! Many guys find big hair attractive because it's bold, confident, and eye-catching. It's a statement of individuality and can be a real conversation starter.

How to Achieve Big Hair

Now that we know guys like big hair, let's talk about how to achieve this look. Here are some tips:

1. Start with a good haircut

If you want big hair, you need to start with a good base. A layered haircut can help create volume and movement in your hair. Talk to your hairstylist about what kind of layers would work best for your hair type and face shape.

2. Use the right products

Products can make all the difference when it comes to achieving big hair. Look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners, as well as mousses, sprays, and powders that can add texture and lift. Avoid heavy products like oils and serums, as they can weigh your hair down.

3. Blow-dry your hair upside down

Blow-drying your hair upside down can help add volume and lift at the roots. Use a round brush to smooth and shape your hair as you dry it, and finish off with a blast of cool air to set the style.

4. Tease your hair

Teasing, also known as backcombing, can help create volume and texture in your hair. Use a fine-toothed comb to gently tease the roots of your hair, and then smooth the top layer over the teased section.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving big hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products can help add body and fullness to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse or spray

Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your damp hair. This can help add texture and lift to your hair.

Step 3: Blow-dry your hair upside down

Flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair using a round brush. Focus on the roots of your hair, lifting them up as you dry them.

Step 4: Tease your hair

Take small sections of hair at the crown of your head and tease them with a fine-toothed comb. Gently backcomb the hair at the roots, and then smooth the top layer over the teased section.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish off your look with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Big Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining big hair:

1. Avoid brushing your hair too much

Brushing your hair too much can cause it to lose its volume and become flat. Instead, use your fingers to gently tousle your hair throughout the day.

2. Sleep with your hair in a loose bun

Sleeping with your hair in a loose bun can help maintain the volume and shape of your big hair. Just be sure to use a scrunchie or soft hair tie to avoid damaging your hair.

3. Use dry shampoo

Dry shampoo can help absorb excess oil and add texture to your hair, making it easier to maintain your big hair style.

FAQs

Q: Will big hair work for all hair types?

A: While big hair can work for most hair types, it may be more difficult for those with fine or thin hair to achieve. Adding layers and using volumizing products can help create the illusion of fuller hair.

Q: How long does it take to achieve big hair?

A: The amount of time it takes to achieve big hair will depend on your hair type and the techniques you use. It may take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour to create a full, voluminous look.

Q: Can I achieve big hair without using heat?

A: While blow-drying and teasing can help create big hair, it is possible to achieve a similar look without heat. Try using a volumizing mousse and air-drying your hair, or using rollers to add volume and lift.

Hair Care for Big Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your big hair:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it more difficult to maintain big hair. Look for a sulfate-free shampoo that can cleanse your hair without damaging it.

2. Avoid over-washing your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Try washing your hair every other day, or even every few days, to help maintain its health and volume.

3. Get regular trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look thin and flat.

In conclusion, big hair is definitely a trend that guys like. With the right haircut, products, and techniques, you can achieve a full, voluminous look that is sure to turn heads. Just remember to take care of your hair and avoid damaging it with excessive heat or over-washing.
