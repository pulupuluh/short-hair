Haircut Fade Sides Long Top A Trendy Style In 2023

Written by Eveline Mar 23, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, then the haircut fade sides long top is the perfect choice for you. This haircut is becoming increasingly popular among men of all ages and has been a top choice for many years. In this post, we will discuss everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, step by step, tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The haircut fade sides long top is a popular and trendy style that features short hair on the sides and back of the head that gradually fades into longer hair on top. This style is versatile and can be customized to suit your personal style and preferences. It is perfect for men who want to look stylish and modern without being too flashy.

How to

To get a haircut fade sides long top, you will need to visit your hairstylist or barber. You can bring a picture of the style you want to show them what you are looking for. Your hairstylist will then use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head, gradually fading it into longer hair on top. The length of the hair on top can be customized to your preferences, and you can choose to leave it longer or shorter depending on your style.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting a haircut fade sides long top:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Visit your hairstylist or barber and show them a picture of the style you want.
  3. Your hairstylist will use clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head, gradually fading it into longer hair on top.
  4. The length of the hair on top can be customized to your preferences.
  5. Your hairstylist will then use scissors to trim and shape the hair on top.
  6. The final step is to style your hair using a hair product of your choice.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut fade sides long top:

  • Bring a picture of the style you want to show your hairstylist.
  • Communicate clearly with your hairstylist about the length and style you want.
  • Choose a hair product that works well for your hair type and style.
  • Regularly trim the hair on the sides and back of your head to maintain the fade.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the haircut fade sides long top:

Is the haircut fade sides long top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can be customized to suit any hair type and texture.

How often should I get a haircut fade sides long top?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the fade and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

What hair products should I use to style my hair?

You can use a variety of hair products, including pomade, wax, gel, or hairspray. Choose a product that works well for your hair type and personal style.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it is important to take good care of it. Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to nourish your hair and prevent damage. Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or flat irons too often, as they can damage your hair. Regularly trim the hair on the sides and back of your head to maintain the fade and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

In conclusion, the haircut fade sides long top is a trendy and stylish choice for men of all ages. It is versatile, customizable, and can be tailored to suit your personal style and preferences. With the right care and maintenance, you can keep your hair looking great and feeling healthy for years to come.
