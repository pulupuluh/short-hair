How Do I Know What Hair Colour Suits Me Best

Written by Oliver Apr 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour and want to switch things up? Choosing a new hair colour can be exciting, but also overwhelming. With so many different shades and tones available, it can be hard to decide which one is right for you. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of figuring out what hair colour suits you best.

Table of Contents

  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How To

The first step in figuring out what hair colour suits you best is to consider your skin tone. Your skin tone can be classified as warm, cool, or neutral. Warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones, while cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones. Neutral skin tones have a balance of both warm and cool undertones.

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can start looking at hair colours that will complement it. For warm skin tones, shades of red, copper, and gold work well. Cool skin tones look great with ash blonde, platinum blonde, and cool brown shades. Neutral skin tones can pull off a variety of hair colours, but it's best to stick with shades that aren't too warm or too cool.

Another factor to consider is your eye colour. Certain hair colours can make your eyes pop and enhance their natural beauty. For example, if you have blue eyes, a warm blonde or light brown shade can really make them stand out. If you have green eyes, a rich red or auburn shade can bring out the green tones in your eyes.

Step By Step

Here are the steps to follow when figuring out what hair colour suits you best:

  1. Determine your skin tone (warm, cool, or neutral).
  2. Consider your eye colour.
  3. Look at hair colours that complement your skin tone and eye colour.
  4. Take into account your natural hair colour and how drastic of a change you want to make.
  5. Consult with a professional hair stylist for their expert opinion.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hair colour:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time and money you're willing to invest in maintaining your new hair colour.
  • Think about the season and how your new hair colour will look in different lighting conditions.
  • Take inspiration from celebrities or influencers with similar skin tones and eye colours.
  • Use virtual try-on tools or hair colour apps to get an idea of what different shades will look like on you.

FAQ

What if I have multiple skin tones?

If you have multiple skin tones (for example, warm undertones on your face but cool undertones on your neck), go with the undertone that's most prominent. If you're still unsure, opt for a neutral shade that balances both warm and cool tones.

Can I dye my hair a drastically different colour than my natural hair?

Yes, but keep in mind that a drastic change can require more upkeep and maintenance to keep the colour looking fresh. It's also important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and eye colour to avoid looking washed out or unbalanced.

What if I don't like my new hair colour?

If you don't like your new hair colour, don't panic! Hair colour can always be corrected or changed. Consult with a professional stylist to come up with a plan to get your hair back to a colour you love.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a new hair colour, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking its best. Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading, and avoid excessive heat styling or sun exposure that can damage your hair and cause the colour to fade.

Now that you know how to figure out what hair colour suits you best, it's time to book that appointment and switch up your look! Remember to take your time and choose a shade that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
