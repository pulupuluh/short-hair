Best Hairstyles For Round Face Over 60 How To Choose And Style

Written by Eveline Apr 18, 2023 · 10 min read

Are you in your 60s and looking for the best hairstyles for your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll walk you through how to choose and style the perfect haircut that flatters your features and makes you look younger and more vibrant. Let's dive in!

How to Choose the Best Hairstyles for Round Face over 60

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, let's talk about what to look for when choosing a haircut for round face shapes. The key is to create angles and length in the right places to elongate the face and draw attention away from the fullness of the cheeks.

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Opt for hairstyles with layers and texture to add volume and movement to the hair.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that sit at the jawline, as they can accentuate the roundness of the face.
  • Consider side-swept bangs or a deep side part to create asymmetry and balance out the face shape.
  • Choose hairstyles that frame the face and draw the eye upwards, such as a pixie cut or a chin-length bob.

Short Hairstyles for Round Face over 60

If you're looking for a low-maintenance and youthful haircut, short hairstyles are a great option. They can add volume and texture to the hair and create a more angular appearance.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a classic short haircut that never goes out of style. It's perfect for round faces because it adds height at the crown and creates angles around the cheekbones and jawline. You can opt for a classic pixie with short layers and side-swept bangs or a more modern version with longer layers and choppy texture.

Bob Cut

A chin-length bob is another great option for round faces. It creates a horizontal line that elongates the face and draws attention away from the cheeks. You can choose a blunt cut or add some layers and texture for a more playful look. Consider adding side-swept bangs to create asymmetry and balance out the face shape.

Medium Hairstyles for Round Face over 60

If you prefer a longer hairstyle, medium-length haircuts can be a great option. They offer versatility and can be styled in many different ways to suit your face shape and personal style.

Layered Cut

A layered cut with long, face-framing layers can create a more elongated appearance and add volume and movement to the hair. You can opt for a side-swept bang or a middle part, depending on your preference.

Shoulder-Length Bob

A shoulder-length bob is another great option for round faces. It creates a horizontal line that elongates the face and draws attention away from the cheeks. You can choose a blunt cut or add some layers and texture for a more playful look. Consider adding side-swept bangs to create asymmetry and balance out the face shape.

Long Hairstyles for Round Face over 60

If you're blessed with long hair, there are plenty of hairstyles that can flatter your round face shape. The key is to add layers and texture to create movement and dimension.

Long Layers

Long layers can create a more elongated appearance and add volume and movement to the hair. You can choose a center part or a side part, depending on your preference. Consider adding some waves or curls to create even more texture and dimension.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can create asymmetry and balance out the face shape. They can be paired with any long hairstyle, from loose waves to sleek straight hair. Just make sure to keep the rest of your hair layered and textured to avoid a heavy, weighed-down look.

Hair Care Tips for Round Face over 60

Now that you've chosen the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo that doesn't strip away natural oils.
  • Use a hydrating conditioner to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to deep condition your hair.

FAQs

1. What is the best hairstyle for a round face over 60?

The best hairstyle for a round face over 60 is one that adds length and angles to the face, such as a pixie cut, a chin-length bob, or a layered cut with face-framing layers.

2. Can I still have long hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a round face. Just make sure to add layers and texture to create movement and dimension, and consider adding side-swept bangs to create asymmetry and balance out the face shape.

3. How often should I get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every six to eight weeks to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.

There you have it – our top picks for the best hairstyles for round face over 60. Remember, the key is to create angles and length in the right places to elongate the face and draw attention away from the fullness of the cheeks. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can look younger and more vibrant than ever before!
