How To Style Medium Hair With Layers

Written by William Jul 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Medium length hair with layers is a popular hairstyle that can be worn in various ways. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, adding layers can give your hair more dimension and movement. In this article, we will show you how to style medium hair with layers in relaxed English language.

Description

Medium length hair with layers is a versatile hairstyle that can suit different face shapes and hair types. The layers can be added all over the hair or concentrated around the face to frame it. This hairstyle can be worn in various styles such as straight, wavy, curly, or braided. It can also be accessorized with headbands, clips, or scarves to add more personality to the look.

How to Style Medium Hair with Layers

Here are some steps to follow to style medium hair with layers:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse well and gently towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

If you are going to use heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and smoothness. Start at the roots and work your way down to the tips. Use a nozzle attachment to direct the airflow and avoid frizz.

Step 4: Curl or Straighten Your Hair

If you want to add curls or straighten your hair, use a curling iron or a flat iron. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the iron, holding for a few seconds. Release the hair and repeat until all sections are done.

Step 5: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product such as a hairspray, mousse, or pomade to hold the style in place. Choose a product that suits your hair type and the desired look.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Try different hair accessories such as headbands, clips, or scarves to add more style to your hair.
  • Experiment with different heat styling tools such as curling wands or straightening brushes to achieve different looks.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage. Use the lowest heat setting that works for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: Can I style medium hair with layers without using heat styling tools?

A: Yes, you can style your hair without using heat styling tools. Try air-drying your hair and using a texturizing spray or a sea salt spray to add volume and texture.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more often. If you have dry hair or color-treated hair, you may need to wash your hair less often to avoid stripping the natural oils.

Hair Care

To keep your medium hair with layers healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and the desired look.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of the layers.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach or colorants that can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.

By following these tips and steps, you can style your medium hair with layers in different ways and keep it healthy and beautiful.
