Description

Men's medium short haircuts have been popular for many years and continue to be a popular choice in 2020. These haircuts are perfect for men who want a low-maintenance, yet stylish look. Men's medium short haircuts are versatile and can be styled in many different ways, making them perfect for any occasion.

There are many different types of men's medium short haircuts, including the classic crew cut, the textured crop, and the messy quiff. Each of these haircuts has its own unique style and can be customized to suit your individual preferences.

How To

If you're looking to get a men's medium short haircut, it's important to find a skilled stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. You can start by looking for inspiration online or in magazines and bringing pictures with you to your appointment.

During your appointment, your stylist will work with you to determine the best haircut for your face shape and hair texture. They will also provide advice on how to style and maintain your new haircut.

Step By Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style some of the most popular men's medium short haircuts:

The Crew Cut

  1. Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head to a uniform length.
  3. Use scissors to trim the hair on the top of your head to the desired length.
  4. Style your hair by rubbing a small amount of pomade or wax between your palms and running your hands through your hair.

The Textured Crop

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and texture.
  3. Use scissors to trim the hair on the top of your head to the desired length.
  4. Style your hair by rubbing a small amount of styling cream or wax between your palms and running your hands through your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your men's medium short haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair in good condition and prevent split ends.
  • Use high-quality hair products to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid pulling or breaking it.

FAQ

What is the best men's medium short haircut for me?

The best men's medium short haircut for you will depend on your face shape and hair texture. It's best to consult with a skilled stylist to determine the best haircut for your individual needs.

How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair in good condition and prevent split ends.

What kind of products should I use to style my hair?

You should use high-quality hair products, such as pomade, wax, or styling cream, to style your hair. Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and stylish men's medium short haircut. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can dry it out.
  • Use a hair mask or leave-in conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and prevent damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.

With the right haircut and proper hair care, you can achieve a stylish and low-maintenance men's medium short haircut that will look great all year round.
