Mens Short Haircut Styles 2022 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Aug 24, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're looking for a fresh new look, you might want to consider a short haircut. Men's short haircut styles are versatile, easy to maintain, and can give you a polished, modern look. There are plenty of options to choose from, and we've put together this guide to help you pick the perfect style for you.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Short Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Men's Short Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut
  • FAQs About Men's Short Haircuts
  • Hair Care for Men's Short Haircuts

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut

Before you decide on a short haircut style, you need to consider a few factors:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair texture and thickness
  • Your personal style

You want a style that complements your face shape and hair texture. For example, if you have a round face, you might want to avoid cuts that add volume to the sides. If you have curly hair, you might want a cut that lets your curls shine.

Some popular men's short haircut styles for 2022 include:

The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic short haircut that is easy to maintain. The hair is cut very short all over the head, usually with clippers. This style is great for men who want a low-maintenance look.

The Crew Cut

The crew cut is another classic short haircut that is longer on top and shorter on the sides. This style is versatile and can be styled in different ways. It works well for men with straight or wavy hair.

The Undercut

The undercut is a trendy short haircut that is longer on top and shaved on the sides. This style is great for men who want a bold, edgy look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Men's Short Hair

If you're cutting your hair at home, follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Choose the right clipper guard for the length you want.
  3. Start at the sides and work your way up, using the clipper guard to maintain a consistent length.
  4. Blend the sides into the top using scissors or clippers.
  5. Style the top as desired.

If you're not confident in your haircutting skills, it's best to leave it to a professional.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Here are some tips to keep your short haircut looking great:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Use a styling product to add texture and hold to your hair.

FAQs About Men's Short Haircuts

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you want to maintain your style. Generally, every four to six weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Q: Can I style my short haircut in different ways?

A: Yes! Even a short haircut can be styled in different ways. You can use a styling product to add texture or volume, or slick it back for a more polished look.

Hair Care for Men's Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are generally low-maintenance, but you still need to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized. Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can damage your hair.

Now that you know all about men's short haircut styles for 2022, you can pick the perfect style for you. Whether you choose a classic buzz cut or a trendy undercut, a short haircut can give you a fresh new look that's easy to maintain.
