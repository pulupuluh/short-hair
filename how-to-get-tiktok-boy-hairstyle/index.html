How To Get Tiktok Boy Hairstyle Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are a fan of TikTok, you may have noticed that many boys on the platform have a unique and stylish hairstyle. This haircut is characterized by short sides and a longer top, often styled in a messy or textured way.

If you want to try out the TikTok boy hairstyle for yourself, here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the look.

How to Get TikTok Boy Hairstyle: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Find a Good Barber

The first step to getting the TikTok boy hairstyle is finding a good barber who is experienced in cutting this type of haircut. Look for a barber who has experience with modern styles and who can give you a cut that looks good with your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Choose Your Length

The next step is to decide on the length of your hair. The TikTok boy hairstyle is characterized by short sides and a longer top, so you will need to decide how short you want the sides to be and how long you want the top to be.

Step 3: Get the Cut

Once you have decided on the length of your hair, it is time to get the cut. Your barber will start by cutting the sides of your hair short, leaving the top longer. They may use clippers or scissors to achieve the desired length.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have the cut, it is time to style your hair. To achieve the messy, textured look of the TikTok boy hairstyle, you will need to use a texturizing product. Apply the product to your hair and use your fingers to create a messy, tousled look. You can also use a blow dryer to add volume to the top of your hair.

Tips for Maintaining the TikTok Boy Hairstyle

Now that you have the TikTok boy hairstyle, here are some tips for maintaining the look:

  • Use a texturizing product to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair to add volume to the top.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to create your own unique look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the TikTok boy hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

A: The TikTok boy hairstyle can work for many hair types, but it may not be suitable for very curly or thick hair.

Q: How often should I get a haircut to maintain the TikTok boy hairstyle?

A: You should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh and maintain the shape of the cut.

Q: What products should I use to style my hair for the TikTok boy hairstyle?

A: You should use a texturizing product, such as a sea salt spray or clay, to add texture and hold to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for the TikTok Boy Hairstyle

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily achieve the TikTok boy hairstyle and keep it looking great. Experiment with different styles and products to find the look that works best for you.
