Best Way To Cut Wavy Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for the best way to cut wavy curly hair? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut wavy curly hair, along with tips on how to care for your hair after the cut. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Wavy Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Wavy Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After the Cut

Description

Wavy curly hair can be challenging to cut, but with the right technique, you can achieve a beautiful and polished look. The key is to understand the natural curl pattern of the hair and to cut accordingly. This will help to enhance the curl and reduce frizz.

How to Cut Wavy Curly Hair

Before you start cutting wavy curly hair, it’s important to have the right tools. You’ll need a pair of sharp scissors, a spray bottle filled with water, and a wide-tooth comb. It’s also a good idea to have a mirror and a chair with a backrest.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow when cutting wavy curly hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning the hair.
  2. Detangle the hair using a wide-tooth comb.
  3. Section the hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back.
  4. Begin cutting the hair in small sections, starting from the back.
  5. Take a small section of hair and twist it tightly.
  6. Trim the ends of the twisted section using the scissors.
  7. Release the twist and check the length. Repeat this process until you’ve cut all the sections.
  8. Move to the front sections and repeat the process.
  9. Once you’ve finished cutting all the sections, use the wide-tooth comb to style the hair.
  10. Check the length and make any final adjustments.

Remember to cut less hair than you think you need to, as curly hair tends to shrink when it dries.

Tips for Cutting Wavy Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting wavy curly hair:

  • Start with dry hair if you have tight curls, as wet hair can stretch and lead to uneven cutting.
  • Use a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair if you have looser curls.
  • Don’t cut the hair straight across, as this can lead to a triangular shape. Instead, cut at an angle to create layers.
  • Don’t pull the hair taut when cutting, as this can lead to uneven cutting.
  • Be patient and take your time, as cutting curly hair requires precision and attention to detail.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting wavy curly hair:

How often should I cut my wavy curly hair?

It’s recommended to cut wavy curly hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my wavy curly hair myself?

While it’s possible to cut your own wavy curly hair, it’s recommended to have it done by a professional stylist who has experience with curly hair.

Should I cut my wavy curly hair when it’s wet or dry?

It depends on the tightness of your curls. If you have tight curls, it’s best to cut when the hair is dry. If you have looser curls, it’s best to cut when the hair is damp.

Hair Care After the Cut

After you’ve cut your wavy curly hair, it’s important to take care of it properly to maintain the shape and prevent frizz. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out the hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can lead to frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair moisturized and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage the hair and lead to split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your wavy curly hair looking healthy and beautiful.

That’s it! We hope this guide has been helpful in showing you the best way to cut wavy curly hair. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you’ll achieve a beautiful and polished look.
