Best Short Hair Styles For Over 60S

If you're over 60 and looking for a new short hairstyle, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll go over the best short hair styles for over 60s, including how to achieve them, tips for maintaining them, and answers to some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

How to Choose the Best Short Hair Style for Over 60s

When it comes to choosing a short hair style as you age, there are a few things to consider:

  • Your face shape: Some short hair styles may be more flattering on certain face shapes than others. For example, a bob may look great on someone with a heart-shaped face, while a pixie cut may be better suited for someone with an oval face.
  • Your hair texture: If you have thin or fine hair, you may want to avoid styles that require a lot of volume or texture. On the other hand, if you have thick or curly hair, you may want to choose a style that embraces and enhances your natural texture.
  • Your lifestyle: Do you have a busy schedule? Do you prefer low-maintenance hairstyles? Consider how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling your hair each day.

Some of the best short hair styles for over 60s include:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's low-maintenance, easy to style, and can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. This style is perfect for women who want a chic, modern look.

Bob

The bob is another timeless short hairstyle that can be adapted to suit any face shape and hair texture. Whether you opt for a classic chin-length bob or a longer, angled bob, this style is versatile and flattering.

Layered

A layered short hair style can add volume and texture to thin or fine hair, while enhancing the natural texture of thick or curly hair. This style is perfect for women who want to add some dimension to their hair.

Asymmetrical

An asymmetrical short hair style can be edgy and modern, while also flattering your face shape. This style is perfect for women who want to make a statement with their hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Over 60s

Once you've chosen your short hair style, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair.
  3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, using the brush to create volume and lift at the roots.
  4. If you have a pixie cut, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or flyaways.
  5. If you have a bob or layered cut, use a curling iron to add texture and movement to your hair.
  6. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Remember, less is more when it comes to styling short hair. Don't overload your hair with products or use too much heat, as this can damage your hair and make it look limp and lifeless.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Over 60s

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hair style:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
  • Invest in high-quality styling products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions About Short Hair Styles for Over 60s

Q: Will a short hair style make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. A well-chosen short hair style can actually make you look younger and more vibrant. It's all about choosing a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with a short hair style?

A: Yes, you can. While a short hair style may limit your styling options somewhat, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail or other updo if you choose.

Q: Will a short hair style be too high-maintenance for me?

A: It depends on the style you choose. Some short hair styles are very low-maintenance, while others may require more styling time and effort. Consider your lifestyle and the amount of time you're willing to spend on your hair before choosing a style.

Hair Care Tips for Over 60s with Short Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for over 60s with short hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and flat irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet that includes plenty of protein and vitamins.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

By following these tips and choosing the right short hair style for your face shape and hair texture, you can look and feel your best at any age.
