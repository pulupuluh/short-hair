Short Hairstyles For Thin Curly Hair Round Face

Written by Frank Mar 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have thin curly hair and a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. You want a style that will complement your face shape and accentuate your curls without weighing them down. Luckily, there are plenty of short hairstyles that work well for thin curly hair and a round face. Read on for some ideas and inspiration!

Description

Thin curly hair can be tricky to style, especially if you have a round face shape. You want to create volume and texture without making your face look even rounder. Short hairstyles are a great option for thin curly hair because they add body and movement, while also being easy to manage.

How To

When choosing a short hairstyle for thin curly hair and a round face, consider the length, layers, and texture. A pixie cut, for example, can be a great option if you want to go really short. Opt for layers to add volume and texture, and ask your stylist to create a side-swept fringe to help balance out your face shape.

If you prefer something a bit longer, a bob or lob can work well too. Again, layers are key for adding volume and movement. Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to create dimension and make your curls pop.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. This will help define your curls and make them easier to style.

2. Towel-dry your hair gently, being careful not to rough up your curls too much. Apply a curl-defining mousse or gel to help enhance your curls and add volume.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your curls. This will help reduce frizz and define your curls even further.

4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling wand or curling iron to create some loose, natural-looking curls. This will add even more volume and texture to your hair.

Tips

- Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your curls and make them look flat.

- Consider getting regular trims to help keep your hair looking healthy and voluminous.

- Experiment with different partings to find the one that works best for your face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up if it's short and curly?

A: Absolutely! You can try a half-up, half-down style, or go for a messy bun or topknot.

Q: Will a short hairstyle make my face look even rounder?

A: Not necessarily. With the right cut and styling techniques, a short hairstyle can actually help balance out your face shape and make it look more oval.

Hair Care

To keep your thin curly hair looking its best, it's important to use products that are designed specifically for your hair type. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are formulated to add volume and enhance curls. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools. And don't forget to get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and full of life!

In conclusion, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can work well for thin curly hair and a round face. Experiment with different cuts, lengths, and textures to find the one that works best for you. And don't forget to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
