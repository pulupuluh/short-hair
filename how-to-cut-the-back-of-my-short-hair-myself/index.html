How To Cut The Back Of My Short Hair Myself

Written by Frank Feb 26, 2023 路 6 min read

Are you tired of constantly going to the salon to maintain your short hair? Learning how to cut the back of your short hair yourself can save you time and money. It may seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a fresh, new look that you can be proud of.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Cut the Back of My Short Hair Myself
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Short hair can be a statement look, but it requires regular maintenance to keep it looking sharp. Cutting the back of your hair can be tricky, especially if you don't have experience. However, with practice and patience, you can master this skill and achieve a professional-looking haircut at home.

How to Cut the Back of My Short Hair Myself

Before starting, make sure you have the necessary tools: sharp hair scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a handheld mirror. It's also essential to have a clear idea of the length and style you want to achieve.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair, making sure it's clean and free of tangles. Use a comb to create a clean part down the middle of your head, separating the hair into two sections.

Step 2: Clip the Sides

Clip the sides of your hair to keep them out of the way while you work on the back. Leave enough hair on the sides to blend it with the back later.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Use your handheld mirror to see the back of your head clearly. Take a small section of hair at the nape of your neck and hold it between your index and middle fingers. Cut the hair to your desired length, using your fingers as a guide. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way up to the crown of your head. Make sure to cut evenly on both sides.

Step 4: Blend the Sides

Unclip the sides and blend them with the back using your scissors. Take small sections of hair and cut at an angle, blending the back with the sides. Use your comb to check the length and make sure it's even on both sides.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Use your handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make sure it's even. If you notice any uneven spots, correct them with your scissors.

Tips

  • Start with small sections of hair and cut slowly to avoid mistakes.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to make clean, even cuts.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or frustrated.
  • Watch tutorial videos to get a better idea of the technique.

FAQ

Can I cut my short hair without experience?

Yes, you can learn how to cut the back of your short hair yourself with practice and patience. Start with small sections and take your time to avoid mistakes.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. Try to correct it with your scissors or wait for it to grow out. It's always better to cut less than too much.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, wash it with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat styling, as it can damage your hair. Regular trims will keep your short hair looking fresh and healthy.

Learning how to cut the back of your short hair yourself can be a fun and rewarding experience. With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a stylish look without leaving your home. Remember to take your time, be patient, and practice regularly to perfect your skills.
