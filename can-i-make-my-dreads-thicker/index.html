Can I Make My Dreads Thicker

Written by Daniel Jun 13, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you wondering if you can make your dreads thicker? Well, the answer is yes! In this blog post, we will discuss how to make your dreads thicker, step by step, tips, and hair care. So, let's get started.

Description

Dreads are a popular hairstyle that has been around for centuries. It is a hairstyle that requires time and patience to achieve. Once you have your dreads, you might want to make them thicker. There are several ways to make your dreads thicker, and we will discuss them in this article.

How to Make Your Dreads Thicker

Here are some ways to make your dreads thicker:

Backcombing

Backcombing is a technique that involves teasing your hair in the opposite direction of growth. This technique can help make your dreads thicker. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair into small parts.
  2. Take one section and hold it tightly at the root.
  3. Using a fine-tooth comb, backcomb the hair in the opposite direction of growth.
  4. Repeat this process until you have backcombed all your hair.
  5. Twist each section of hair to form a dreadlock.

Palm Rolling

Palm rolling is a technique that involves rolling your dreads between your palms to make them thicker. Here's how to do it:

  1. Starting at the root, take one dread and hold it between your palms.
  2. Roll the dread back and forth between your palms.
  3. Repeat this process for all your dreads.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make your dreads thicker:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good clarifying shampoo. This will help remove any buildup and prepare your hair for the next steps.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small parts using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work on each section.

Step 3: Backcomb Your Hair

Take one section of hair and hold it tightly at the root. Using a fine-tooth comb, backcomb the hair in the opposite direction of growth. Repeat this process until you have backcombed all your hair.

Step 4: Twist Your Hair

Once you have backcombed all your hair, twist each section of hair to form a dreadlock.

Step 5: Palm Roll Your Hair

Starting at the root, take one dread and hold it between your palms. Roll the dread back and forth between your palms. Repeat this process for all your dreads.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make your dreads thicker:

  • Use a good clarifying shampoo to remove buildup from your hair.
  • Backcomb your hair gently to avoid damaging it.
  • Palm roll your dreads regularly to maintain their thickness.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

FAQ

1. How long does it take to make my dreads thicker?

It depends on how thick you want your dreads to be. It can take several weeks or months to see a noticeable difference.

2. Can I make my dreads thicker without damaging them?

Yes, you can make your dreads thicker without damaging them. Use gentle techniques like backcombing and palm rolling.

3. Will my dreads become too heavy if I make them thicker?

It is possible for your dreads to become heavy if you make them too thick. Avoid using heavy products and be gentle when backcombing.

Hair Care

Maintaining your dreads is important for their health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your dreads regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Moisturize your dreads with a natural oil like coconut or jojoba.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your dreads.
  • Visit a professional stylist for maintenance and repairs.

Conclusion

In conclusion, you can make your dreads thicker using techniques like backcombing and palm rolling. Be gentle with your hair to avoid damage and maintain your dreads with regular hair care. With patience and persistence, you can achieve the thick, beautiful dreads you desire.
