How Can I Make My Long Face Look Shorter

Written by Petter Apr 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Welcome to our blog post about how to make your long face look shorter. Many people with long faces often feel like their features are too stretched out, making their face appear longer than it actually is. However, there are many ways to make your face look shorter and more balanced. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look, as well as tips on hair care and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Long Face Look Shorter
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your New Look
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Long Faces

How to Make Your Long Face Look Shorter

There are several ways to make your long face look shorter:

  • Choosing the right hairstyle
  • Using makeup to contour your face
  • Wearing the right accessories

By following these tips, you can achieve a more balanced and proportionate look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make your long face look shorter:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face. This can include layers, curls, or a side-swept fringe.
  2. Use makeup to contour your face. Apply a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to the sides of your forehead and temples to create the illusion of a shorter face.
  3. Wear accessories such as statement earrings or a scarf to draw attention away from the length of your face.

By following these steps, you can achieve a more balanced and proportionate look.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

Here are some tips for maintaining your new look:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Invest in quality makeup brushes to ensure a flawless application.
  • Choose accessories that complement your facial features.

By following these tips, you can maintain your new look and feel confident every day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to make your long face look shorter:

  • Can I still wear my hair straight? Yes, but make sure to add volume to the sides of your face to balance out the length.
  • Do I have to contour my face every day? No, but it can help to create a more balanced look for special occasions.
  • What kind of accessories should I wear? Choose accessories that draw attention away from the length of your face, such as statement earrings or a bold necklace.

By following these tips, you can achieve a more balanced and proportionate look.

Hair Care for Long Faces

Here are some tips for hair care if you have a long face:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they can make your face look longer.

By following these tips, you can achieve a more balanced and proportionate look.

Thank you for reading our blog post about how to make your long face look shorter. We hope you found these tips helpful!
