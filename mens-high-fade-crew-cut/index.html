Mens High Fade Crew Cut The Ultimate Guide In 2023

Written by Aprilia Jul 18, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, a mens high fade crew cut may be the perfect option for you. This classic hairstyle has been popular for decades, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this guide, we'll go over everything you need to know about getting a mens high fade crew cut, from how to ask for it at the barbershop to how to care for your hair afterwards.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Mens High Fade Crew Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Mens High Fade Crew Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Mens High Fade Crew Cut

Description

A mens high fade crew cut is a short haircut that is tapered on the sides and back, with longer hair on top. The fade refers to the gradual transition of hair length from short to long. The high fade starts near the top of the head and gradually fades down to the skin. The crew cut refers to the longer hair on top, which is usually styled with a side part or slicked back. This haircut is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance look that is also stylish and versatile.

How to Get a Mens High Fade Crew Cut

The first step in getting a mens high fade crew cut is finding a barber who is experienced in this style of haircut. You can ask for recommendations from friends or family members, or do some research online to find a barbershop in your area that specializes in mens haircuts. Once you've found a barber, you'll need to communicate exactly what you want.

Communicating with Your Barber

When you sit down in the barber's chair, it's important to be clear about what you want. You can bring in a picture of the haircut you like, or you can describe it in words. Here are some key terms to know:

  • High fade: This refers to the location of the fade. A high fade starts near the top of the head and gradually fades down to the skin.
  • Crew cut: This refers to the longer hair on top, which is usually styled with a side part or slicked back.
  • Taper: This refers to the gradual reduction of hair length on the sides and back.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a mens high fade crew cut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel drying it.
  2. Sit down in the barber's chair and communicate exactly what you want.
  3. The barber will start by using clippers to create the high fade, starting near the top of the head and gradually fading down to the skin.
  4. Next, the barber will use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length.
  5. The hair on top can be styled with a side part or slicked back, depending on your preference.
  6. The barber will use a razor to clean up any stray hairs around the hairline and neckline.
  7. Once the haircut is finished, the barber may apply some styling product to help hold the hair in place.
  8. Pay the barber and leave a tip if you're satisfied with the haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens High Fade Crew Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens high fade crew cut:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep the fade looking fresh.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Style your hair with a lightweight product that won't weigh it down.
  • Consider using a hair oil or leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQs

How long does a mens high fade crew cut last?

A mens high fade crew cut typically lasts for 2-4 weeks before it starts to grow out and lose its shape. You'll need to get regular haircuts to keep the fade looking fresh.

Is a mens high fade crew cut suitable for all hair types?

A mens high fade crew cut can work for most hair types, but it's particularly well-suited for thick, straight hair. If you have curly or fine hair, you may need to adjust the length and style of the haircut to suit your hair type.

Can I style my hair differently with a mens high fade crew cut?

Yes, one of the great things about a mens high fade crew cut is that it's versatile and can be styled in different ways. You can experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Mens High Fade Crew Cut

Here are some hair care tips for mens high fade crew cut:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a hair oil or leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized.
  • Use a lightweight styling product that won't weigh your hair down.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep the fade looking fresh.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a mens high fade crew cut with confidence and style. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut or a stylish new look, this classic haircut is sure to impress.
