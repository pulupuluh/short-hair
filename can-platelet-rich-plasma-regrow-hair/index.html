Can Platelet Rich Plasma Regrow Hair

Written by Oliver Jul 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you are struggling with hair loss, you may have heard about platelet rich plasma (PRP) therapy. PRP therapy is a non-surgical treatment that uses your own blood to stimulate hair growth. In this blog post, we will explore the question, can platelet rich plasma regrow hair?

What is Platelet Rich Plasma Therapy?

Platelet rich plasma therapy involves taking a small amount of your blood and processing it to concentrate the platelets. Platelets are a type of cell in your blood that are responsible for blood clotting and healing. They also contain growth factors that can stimulate cell growth and repair.

Once the platelets are concentrated, they are injected into your scalp in the areas where you are experiencing hair loss. The growth factors in the platelets can stimulate hair follicles to grow new hair.

How Does PRP Therapy Work?

PRP therapy works by stimulating hair growth at the cellular level. The growth factors in the platelets can activate dormant hair follicles, increase blood flow to the scalp, and promote the production of new hair cells.

PRP therapy can also reduce inflammation in the scalp, which can contribute to hair loss. By reducing inflammation, PRP therapy can create a healthier environment for hair growth.

What are the Steps in PRP Therapy?

The steps involved in PRP therapy are relatively simple:

  1. A small amount of blood is taken from your arm.
  2. The blood is processed to concentrate the platelets.
  3. The platelets are injected into your scalp using a fine needle.
  4. You may need several treatments spaced a few weeks apart to see results.

What are the Benefits of PRP Therapy?

PRP therapy has several benefits, including:

  • Non-surgical: PRP therapy is a non-surgical treatment that does not require incisions or anesthesia.
  • Safe: Since PRP therapy uses your own blood, there is little risk of infection or allergic reaction.
  • Effective: PRP therapy has been shown to stimulate hair growth in both men and women.
  • Natural: PRP therapy uses your body's own healing mechanisms to promote hair growth.

What are the Risks of PRP Therapy?

PRP therapy is generally safe, but like any medical procedure, there are some risks involved. These can include:

  • Pain or discomfort at the injection site
  • Bruising or swelling
  • Infection
  • Allergic reaction

However, these risks are rare and can be minimized by choosing a qualified, experienced provider.

How Can I Take Care of My Hair After PRP Therapy?

After PRP therapy, it is important to take good care of your hair to promote healthy growth. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Get enough sleep to promote overall health and well-being.

FAQs

Who is a good candidate for PRP therapy?

PRP therapy is a good option for anyone experiencing hair loss or thinning, but it may be especially effective for those in the early stages of hair loss.

How long does PRP therapy take?

The entire PRP therapy process takes about an hour, including the blood draw and processing.

How long does it take to see results from PRP therapy?

Results from PRP therapy can vary, but most people start to see new hair growth within three to six months after treatment.

Is PRP therapy covered by insurance?

PRP therapy is considered a cosmetic procedure and is not typically covered by insurance.

Conclusion

So, can platelet rich plasma regrow hair? The answer is yes, PRP therapy can stimulate hair growth and reduce hair loss. If you are struggling with hair loss, PRP therapy may be a safe, effective, and natural solution. Talk to a qualified provider to learn more about this innovative treatment option.
