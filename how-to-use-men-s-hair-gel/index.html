How To Use Men s Hair Gel Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 29, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Men's hair gel is a versatile product that can help you achieve many different hairstyles, from a sleek and polished look to a messy, textured style. But if you've never used hair gel before, it can be intimidating to know where to start. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to using men's hair gel effectively, including tips for applying and styling your hair.

How to Use Men's Hair Gel: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Product

Before you start using hair gel, it's important to choose the right product for your hair type and desired style. Look for a gel that's specifically formulated for men's hair, and consider the hold strength you need. Light hold gels are best for creating a natural look, while strong hold gels are better for more structured styles.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before applying hair gel, it's important to prepare your hair. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then towel dry until it's slightly damp. If you have longer hair, you might want to blow dry it until it's completely dry before using hair gel.

Step 3: Apply the Gel

Start by dispensing a small amount of hair gel onto your fingertips. Rub your hands together to distribute the gel evenly, then work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working towards the ends. Be sure to apply the gel evenly to avoid clumps or uneven texture.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you've applied the gel, you can begin styling your hair. Use a comb or your fingers to create the desired shape and texture, whether that's a slicked-back look or a messy, textured style. Be sure to work quickly, as hair gel dries quickly and can be difficult to restyle once it's set.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To help your hairstyle last all day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help set the style and prevent frizz or flyaways. Hold the can of hairspray about 12 inches from your head, and spray lightly and evenly over your hair.

Tips for Using Men's Hair Gel

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your men's hair gel:

  • Start with a small amount of gel and add more as needed. You can always add more, but it's difficult to remove excess gel once it's applied.
  • Experiment with different styles and textures to find the look that works best for you.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to create texture and volume.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match products, such as using a styling cream or pomade in addition to hair gel.
  • Wash your hair thoroughly at the end of the day to remove any product buildup.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about using men's hair gel:

How much hair gel should I use?

Start with a small amount of hair gel, about the size of a dime, and add more as needed. Remember, it's easier to add more product than to remove excess gel.

How do I avoid clumps or flakes?

To avoid clumps or flakes, be sure to apply the gel evenly and work it through your hair thoroughly. Use a comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly, and avoid applying too much at once.

Can I use hair gel on wet hair?

While it's best to apply hair gel to slightly damp hair, you can also use it on dry hair for a more textured look.

Hair Care with Men's Hair Gel

Using men's hair gel can be a great way to achieve a variety of hairstyles, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Be sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat when styling with tools like blow dryers or flat irons.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to use men's hair gel with confidence and create a variety of stylish looks.
