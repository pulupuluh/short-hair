Short Hairstyles For 60 Year Old Woman With Round Face A Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 09, 2023 · 9 min read

If you're a 60 year old woman with a round face, finding the right short hairstyle can be a challenge. But fear not, because we've got you covered! In this guide, we'll walk you through some of the best short hairstyles for round-faced women in their 60s. From pixie cuts to bobs, we've got plenty of options that will flatter your face shape and make you look and feel fabulous. So let's get started!

Description

A round face is characterized by soft, curved lines and a wide forehead and cheeks. If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a short hairstyle that helps to elongate your face and draw attention away from your cheeks. This can be achieved through a variety of techniques, including layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical styles.

How To

When it comes to choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, there are a few key factors to consider. Here's what you need to keep in mind:

1. Face Shape

The first thing you'll want to do is determine your face shape. If you're not sure whether you have a round face, take a selfie and draw an imaginary line around the edges of your face. If the line forms a perfect circle, you likely have a round face.

2. Hair Texture

The texture of your hair will also play a role in determining the best short hairstyle for you. If you have fine hair, you'll want to choose a style that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you'll want to opt for a style that thins it out a bit.

3. Lifestyle

Your lifestyle will also impact your hairstyle choices. If you're a busy woman who doesn't have a lot of time to style your hair, you'll want to choose a low-maintenance style that requires minimal upkeep.

Step by Step

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a short hairstyle, let's take a look at some of the best options for 60 year old women with round faces:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages. For a round face, you'll want to keep the cut short and textured, with longer layers on top to add height and volume. Side-swept bangs can also help to elongate the face.

2. Bob

A bob is another great option for women with round faces. You'll want to choose a length that falls just below the chin, with layers that frame the face and add volume. Side-swept bangs can also help to create a more elongated shape.

3. Shag

A shag is a layered, choppy hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages. For a round face, you'll want to keep the layers around the crown of the head to add height and volume. Side-swept bangs can also help to create a more elongated shape.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for a round face:

1. Embrace Texture

Adding texture to your hair can help to create a more elongated shape. You can achieve this through layers, choppy cuts, and styling products.

2. Avoid Volume at the Sides

Avoid adding too much volume to the sides of your hair, as this can make your face appear wider. Instead, focus on adding volume to the top of your head.

3. Experiment with Bangs

Side-swept bangs can be a great way to create a more elongated shape, but you can also experiment with other types of bangs, such as blunt or choppy.

FAQ

What Short Hairstyles Should I Avoid if I Have a Round Face?

Avoid short hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your hair, as this can make your face appear wider. You should also avoid hairstyles that are too blunt, as these can make your face look rounder.

How Often Should I Get My Hair Cut?

Short hairstyles require more frequent trims than longer hairstyles, so you'll want to get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

What Products Should I Use to Style My Hair?

For short hairstyles, you'll want to use lightweight styling products that won't weigh down your hair. Look for mousses, gels, and sprays that are formulated for your hair type.

Hair Care

To keep your short hairstyle looking its best, you'll need to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that's formulated for your hair type and is gentle enough to use every day.

2. Condition Regularly

Use a conditioner after every shampoo to keep your hair soft and hydrated.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

If you use hot tools to style your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

With these tips and hairstyles, you're sure to find a short hairstyle that flatters your round face and makes you feel confident and beautiful. So go ahead and try out a new look – you just might love it!
