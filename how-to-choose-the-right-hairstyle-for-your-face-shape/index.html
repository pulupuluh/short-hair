How To Choose The Right Hairstyle For Your Face Shape

Written by Aprilia Jul 23, 2023 · 8 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially when you have no idea which style will suit your face shape. However, with a little bit of knowledge, it's easy to pick a hairstyle that will accentuate your best features and leave you feeling confident and beautiful.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

When choosing the right hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. Your face shape will determine which hairstyles will look best on you and which ones won't. There are six different face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Each face shape has a different set of characteristics that can help you determine which hairstyle will be most flattering.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

The key to choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape is to find a style that will balance out your features. For example, if you have a round face, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. If you have a square face, you'll want to soften your features with a hairstyle that has layers and movement.

Here's a quick breakdown of which hairstyles work best for each face shape:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! Almost any hairstyle will look great on you. However, if you want to showcase your features, try a shoulder-length cut with soft layers.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face. Long, layered hairstyles work well, as do side-swept bangs.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to soften your features with a hairstyle that has layers and movement. A long bob or a shaggy cut with layers will work well.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. A chin-length bob with layers or side-swept bangs will do the trick.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you'll want to create balance by adding volume to the top of your head. A short, layered cut or a pixie cut will work well.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, you'll want to create the illusion of width. A shoulder-length cut with layers or a long bob with side-swept bangs will work well.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right hairstyle for your face shape:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Research which hairstyles work best for your face shape.
  3. Consider your hair texture and lifestyle.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist.
  5. Try out different hairstyles and see which one you like best.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you'll want to avoid heavy, blunt cuts. If you have thick hair, you'll want to avoid styles that require a lot of maintenance.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you're constantly on the go, you'll want a hairstyle that's low maintenance and easy to style.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment. Trying out different hairstyles is the best way to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the right hairstyle:

What if I don't know my face shape?

You can determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have these measurements, you can compare them to the characteristics of each face shape to determine which one you have.

How often should I change my hairstyle?

There's no set rule for how often you should change your hairstyle. It's really up to you and your personal preferences. Some people like to switch up their hairstyle every few months, while others stick with the same style for years.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after every wash to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape doesn't have to be difficult. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a style that flatters your features and leaves you feeling confident and beautiful.
