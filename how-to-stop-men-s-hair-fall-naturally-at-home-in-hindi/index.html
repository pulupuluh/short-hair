How To Stop Men s Hair Fall Naturally At Home In Hindi

Written by Daniel Apr 07, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're experiencing hair fall, you're not alone. Hair fall is a common problem among men and women alike. However, the good news is that there are natural remedies that you can try at home to stop hair fall. In this article, we'll show you how to stop men's hair fall naturally at home in Hindi.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Stop Men's Hair Fall Naturally at Home in Hindi
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Hair fall occurs due to various reasons such as stress, poor diet, hormonal changes, and genetics. However, there are several natural remedies that you can try at home to reduce hair fall. These remedies not only help in stopping hair fall but also promote hair growth.

How to Stop Men's Hair Fall Naturally at Home in Hindi

Here are some natural remedies that you can try at home to stop hair fall:

1. Amla (Indian Gooseberry)

Amla is a rich source of vitamin C, which is essential for hair growth. It also has antioxidant properties that help in preventing hair fall. You can either apply amla juice on your scalp or consume it in the form of amla juice or powder.

2. Onion juice

Onion juice contains sulfur, which helps in promoting hair growth. It also has antibacterial properties that help in preventing scalp infections. You can either apply onion juice directly on your scalp or mix it with honey or coconut oil.

3. Coconut oil

Coconut oil is a natural conditioner that helps in preventing hair breakage. It also has antibacterial properties that help in preventing scalp infections. You can massage your scalp with warm coconut oil and leave it overnight.

4. Aloe vera

Aloe vera is a natural moisturizer that helps in preventing hair breakage. It also has antibacterial properties that help in preventing scalp infections. You can apply aloe vera gel on your scalp and leave it for 30 minutes before washing it off.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use these natural remedies:

1. Amla (Indian Gooseberry)

  1. Take fresh amla and cut it into small pieces.
  2. Blend the amla pieces and strain the juice.
  3. Apply the juice on your scalp and massage it gently.
  4. Leave it for 30 minutes and wash it off with water.

2. Onion juice

  1. Grate an onion and extract the juice.
  2. Mix the onion juice with honey or coconut oil.
  3. Apply the mixture on your scalp and massage it gently.
  4. Leave it for 30 minutes and wash it off with water.

3. Coconut oil

  1. Warm coconut oil in a pan.
  2. Massage the warm oil on your scalp and hair.
  3. Leave it overnight and wash it off with a mild shampoo in the morning.

4. Aloe vera

  1. Take fresh aloe vera leaves and extract the gel.
  2. Apply the gel on your scalp and massage it gently.
  3. Leave it for 30 minutes and wash it off with water.

Tips

Here are some tips that you can follow to prevent hair fall:

  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair such as hair dyes and styling products.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair.
  • Reduce stress through meditation, yoga, or other relaxation techniques.

FAQ

Q. How often should I apply these natural remedies?

A. You can apply these remedies once or twice a week depending on your hair type and the severity of your hair fall.

Q. Can these remedies be used by women as well?

A. Yes, these remedies can be used by both men and women to prevent hair fall.

Hair Care

Here are some general hair care tips that you can follow to maintain healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing to prevent hair breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Avoid combing wet hair as it can cause hair breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these natural remedies and hair care tips, you can prevent hair fall and maintain healthy hair.
