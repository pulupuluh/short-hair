Description

Long rectangular faces are characterized by a long and narrow shape with straight cheekbones and jawline. The best hairstyles for long rectangular faces are those that add width and volume to the face, creating a more balanced look. In this article, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles for long rectangular faces, including short, medium, and long styles.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a long rectangular face, it is important to consider the width and volume of the style. Shorter hairstyles with volume on the sides, such as a bob or pixie cut, can help to add width to the face. Medium-length hairstyles with layers and waves can also help to create a more balanced look. Longer hairstyles with curls or waves can add volume and softness to the face, while straight hairstyles can elongate the face even further.

Step by step

1. Start by determining your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing them.

2. Choose a hairstyle that adds width and volume to the face. Short, medium, and long styles can all work for long rectangular faces, as long as they have volume or waves.

3. Consider adding layers to your hairstyle to create movement and volume. Layers can help to add width and softness to the face.

4. Use styling products, such as mousse or hairspray, to add volume and hold to your hairstyle.

Tips

- Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they can elongate the face even further.

- Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle, as they can help to shorten the face and add balance.

- Experiment with different styles and lengths to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a long rectangular face?

A: Yes, but it is important to add volume and movement to the hair to avoid elongating the face even further.

Q: Should I avoid long hairstyles if I have a long rectangular face?

A: No, but it is important to add volume and softness to the hair to create a more balanced look.

Hair Care

When styling your hair for a long rectangular face, it is important to use products that add volume and hold. Mousse and hairspray can be used to create volume and texture, while a round brush can be used to add movement and body to the hair. It is also important to keep your hair healthy and hydrated with regular conditioning treatments and trims.

Overall, the best hairstyles for long rectangular faces are those that add width and volume to the face, creating a more balanced and flattering look. Short, medium, and long styles can all work, as long as they have volume or waves. Experiment with different styles and lengths to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, and use styling products and hair care techniques to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
