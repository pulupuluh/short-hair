How Can I Cut My Own Hair With Clippers

Written by Oliver Apr 08, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts at the salon or barbershop? Or maybe you just want to try something new with your hair? Either way, cutting your own hair with clippers can be a great option. In this article, we will walk you through the steps to achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.

Description

Cutting your own hair with clippers can seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a simple process. Clippers are electric hair trimmers that come with different attachments or guards to adjust the length of the hair. By using different guards, you can achieve a variety of styles, from a buzz cut to a longer, layered look.

How To

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of clippers with guards, a comb, and a pair of scissors. It's also a good idea to have a mirror or two set up so you can see all angles of your head.

Start by washing and drying your hair. Comb it out so it's tangle-free. Decide on the length you want to cut your hair, and select the appropriate guard for the clippers. Attach the guard to the clippers and turn them on.

Begin cutting your hair at the back of your head, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Use the comb to lift sections of your hair up, and run the clippers over the hair in an upward motion, against the direction of hair growth.

Move the clippers around your head, using the same technique. Be sure to take your time and go slowly, especially around the ears and neckline.

Once you have finished cutting with the clippers, use the scissors to trim any stray hairs or sections that need more attention.

Step by Step

1. Wash and dry your hair and comb it out.

2. Select the appropriate guard for the clippers.

3. Attach the guard to the clippers and turn them on.

4. Begin cutting your hair at the back of your head, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

5. Use the comb to lift sections of your hair up, and run the clippers over the hair in an upward motion, against the direction of hair growth.

6. Move the clippers around your head, using the same technique.

7. Take your time and go slowly, especially around the ears and neckline.

8. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs or sections that need more attention.

9. Repeat the process until you are satisfied with the length and style of your hair.

10. Clean the clippers and store them properly for future use.

Tips

- Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one if necessary.

- Use a spray bottle to mist your hair with water if it's difficult to comb or cut.

- Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head and any other hard-to-see areas.

- If you're unsure about a certain section, don't be afraid to take a break and come back to it later.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair with regular scissors?

You can, but it's not recommended. Regular scissors are not designed for cutting hair and can lead to uneven cuts or split ends.

How often should I cut my hair with clippers?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and the style you want to maintain. Generally, every 4-6 weeks is a good time frame for a trim.

What if I make a mistake?

Don't worry, hair grows back! If you make a mistake, try to blend it in with the rest of your hair or wait for it to grow out. You can always visit a professional stylist to fix any major mistakes.

Hair Care

After you have finished cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using heat tools like hair dryers or straighteners too often. Regular trims can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Cutting your own hair with clippers can be a fun and cost-effective way to experiment with your hairstyle. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home.
