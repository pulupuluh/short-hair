Best Hairstyles For Long Skinny Faces

Written by Bardi Jul 10, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have a long and skinny face, you may find it challenging to find the right hairstyle that complements your features. However, there are plenty of hairstyles that can make your face appear wider and more balanced. In this article, we’ll explore the best hairstyles for long skinny faces, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Long Skinny Faces
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Long Skinny Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for long skinny faces, it’s important to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. Is your face longer than it is wide? Do you have a prominent chin and forehead? If so, you likely have a long and skinny face.

Best Hairstyles for Long Skinny Faces

Now that you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here are some of the best hairstyles for long skinny faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can add volume and movement to your hair, which can balance out a long and skinny face. You can opt for face-framing layers around your chin and cheeks to create the illusion of width.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can create a diagonal line across your forehead, which can shorten the appearance of your face. You can also pair side-swept bangs with a layered haircut to add more volume and dimension.

3. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut can be a great option for long skinny faces, especially if you opt for a chin-length or shoulder-length cut. This can add fullness to the sides of your face and create a more balanced appearance.

4. Wavy Hair

Wavy hair can add texture and dimension to your hair, which can make your face appear wider. You can use a curling iron or texturizing spray to create loose waves that frame your face.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Use volumizing products to add fullness and body to your hair.
  • Avoid slicked-back hairstyles, as they can make your face appear even longer.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering one for your face shape.
  • Avoid straight hairstyles that fall below your shoulders, as they can make your face appear longer.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long skinny face?

Avoid hairstyles that are slicked back or super straight, as they can elongate your face even more. You should also avoid hairstyles that are too short or too voluminous on top, as they can make your face appear even longer.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a long skinny face?

Absolutely! However, you may want to consider a side ponytail or a low ponytail, as these styles can add volume to the sides of your face.

Hair Care for Long Skinny Faces

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you can find the perfect look for your long and skinny face. Remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
