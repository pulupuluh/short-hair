How To Contour Oval Face Shape Tutorial Step By Step Guide

Welcome to our ultimate guide on how to contour oval face shape tutorial. If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky because it is considered the most versatile face shape. However, if you want to enhance your features and define your cheekbones, jawline, and forehead, then contouring is the way to go. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step tutorial on how to contour your oval face shape like a pro. We will also share some tips and answer frequently asked questions to help you achieve the perfect contour.

  • How to Contour Oval Face Shape Tutorial
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Contouring Oval Face Shape
  • FAQs about Contouring Oval Face Shape
  • Hair Care for Oval Face Shape

How to Contour Oval Face Shape Tutorial

Contouring is all about creating shadows and highlights to enhance your best features. For oval face shape, you want to focus on defining your cheekbones, jawline, and forehead. Here are the steps:

Step One: Prep Your Skin

Before contouring, you need to prep your skin by cleansing, toning, and moisturizing. Apply a primer to make sure your makeup stays in place all day.

Step Two: Choose the Right Contour Shade

For oval face shape, you want to choose a contour shade that is one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone. Make sure the shade you choose has cool undertones to create a shadow effect.

Step Three: Apply Contour

Using an angled brush, apply the contour shade along your hairline, temples, and under your cheekbones. Blend the contour shade well to avoid harsh lines.

Step Four: Highlight

Choose a highlight shade that is one or two shades lighter than your natural skin tone. Apply the highlight shade under your eyes, on your forehead, and down the center of your nose. Blend the highlight shade well with a beauty blender or a brush.

Step Five: Finish with Blush

Choose a blush shade that complements your skin tone. Apply the blush to the apples of your cheeks and blend well to create a natural look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Let's dive deeper into each step of the contouring process:

Step One: Prep Your Skin

Before contouring, it is essential to prep your skin by cleansing, toning, and moisturizing. Make sure your skin is clean and free of any makeup residue. Apply a primer to create a smooth canvas for your makeup.

Step Two: Choose the Right Contour Shade

Choosing the right contour shade is crucial for achieving the perfect contour. For oval face shape, you want to choose a contour shade that is one or two shades darker than your natural skin tone. Make sure the shade you choose has cool undertones to create a shadow effect.

Step Three: Apply Contour

Using an angled brush, apply the contour shade along your hairline, temples, and under your cheekbones. Start by applying a small amount of product and build up as needed. Blend the contour shade well to avoid harsh lines.

Pro Tip: To find the perfect placement for your contour, suck in your cheeks and apply the product in the hollows.

Step Four: Highlight

Highlighting is essential to brighten up your face and make your features pop. Choose a highlight shade that is one or two shades lighter than your natural skin tone. Apply the highlight shade under your eyes, on your forehead, down the center of your nose, and on your cupid's bow. Blend the highlight shade well with a beauty blender or a brush.

Pro Tip: To make your highlight pop even more, spray your face with a setting spray before applying the highlight shade.

Step Five: Finish with Blush

Blush adds a natural flush to your cheeks and completes the look. Choose a blush shade that complements your skin tone. Apply the blush to the apples of your cheeks and blend well to create a natural look.

Pro Tip: If you want your blush to last all day, apply a cream blush first and then set it with a powder blush.

Tips for Contouring Oval Face Shape

To achieve the perfect contour for oval face shape, here are some tips:

  • Blend, blend, blend! Make sure you blend your contour shade well to avoid harsh lines.
  • Choose a contour shade that has cool undertones to create a shadow effect.
  • Apply your highlight shade before your contour shade to avoid blending the two together.
  • Don't forget to contour your jawline to define your face shape even more.
  • Apply your blush after your contour and highlight to create a natural flush.

FAQs about Contouring Oval Face Shape

Q: Can I contour with a powder or cream product?

A: Yes, you can use either a powder or cream product to contour your oval face shape. It depends on your preference and skin type. If you have dry skin, a cream product may work better for you. If you have oily skin, a powder product may work better.

Q: What kind of brush should I use to contour?

A: You should use an angled brush to contour your oval face shape. Make sure the brush is soft and dense to blend the product well.

Q: How can I make my contour last all day?

A: To make your contour last all day, use a setting spray after applying your makeup. You can also use a powder product to set your cream contour.

Hair Care for Oval Face Shape

When it comes to hair care for oval face shape, you want to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape. Here are some tips:

  • Opt for a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head to balance out your face shape.
  • A side-swept bang can help soften your face and draw attention to your eyes.
  • A long bob or shoulder-length haircut can also complement your face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short as they can elongate or widen your face.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide on how to contour oval face shape tutorial, you can achieve the perfect contour and enhance your features. Remember to practice, experiment, and have fun with your makeup. Happy contouring!
