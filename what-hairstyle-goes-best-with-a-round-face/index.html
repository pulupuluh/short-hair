What Hairstyle Goes Best With A Round Face

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles but can't seem to find the one that suits your round face? Don't worry because we've got you covered! In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for a round face, including how to achieve them, tips, and hair care.

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by having a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The best hairstyle for a round face is one that adds length and dimension to the face, creating an illusion of a slimmer and longer face shape.

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, it's essential to consider the hair texture, volume, and length. There are various options to choose from, including layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and long bobs.

How to

The first step in choosing the best hairstyle for a round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length of your face from the hairline to the chin and the width of your face at the cheekbones.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start exploring different hairstyles that suit your face shape. Some popular hairstyles for a round face include:

1. Long Layers

Long layers add dimension and movement to the hair, which helps to elongate the face. This hairstyle works best for medium to long hair lengths and can be styled straight or curly.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to break the roundness of the face and create an illusion of length. This hairstyle works well with medium to long hair lengths and can be styled straight or wavy.

3. Bob with Volume

A bob with volume at the crown can help to elongate the face and create a more angular look. This hairstyle works best for short to medium hair lengths and can be styled straight or with loose waves.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some of the best hairstyles for a round face:

1. Long Layers

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and curl at the ends.
  3. Use a curling iron to add loose waves to the hair.
  4. Apply a texturizing spray to add texture and hold.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and curl at the ends.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten the hair.
  4. Create a deep side part and sweep the bangs to the side.
  5. Apply a smoothing serum to add shine and control frizz.

3. Bob with Volume

  1. Wash and condition your hair as usual.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume at the crown.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten the hair.
  4. Apply a volumizing mousse to add hold and texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the sides of the face, as this can make the face appear wider.
  • Consider using hair accessories such as headbands or clips to add texture and dimension to the hair.
  • Experiment with different hair partings to create a more angular look.

FAQ

1. Can I have short hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can have short hair if you have a round face, but it's essential to choose a hairstyle that adds length and dimension to the face. A short bob with volume at the crown or side-swept bangs can work well for a round face.

2. Can I have curly hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can have curly hair if you have a round face, but it's important to choose a hairstyle that adds length and dimension to the face. Long layers or a bob with volume can work well for curly hair with a round face.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for achieving the best hairstyle for a round face. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage the hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair.
  • Trim the hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

Now that you know what hairstyle goes best with a round face, it's time to try out some new styles and experiment with different looks. Remember to choose a hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle, and don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice and recommendations.
