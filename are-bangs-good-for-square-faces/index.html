Are Bangs Good For Square Faces

Written by Petter Jul 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a square face, you may be wondering if bangs are a good option for your hair. Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline and a wide forehead, and many people with this face shape struggle to find a hairstyle that flatters their features. In this article, we'll explore whether bangs are a good choice for square faces and provide some tips for styling them.

Description: What is a Square Face?

A square face is a face shape that is characterized by a strong jawline, a wide forehead, and a square chin. People with square faces often have prominent cheekbones and a symmetrical face shape. This face shape is similar to a round face, but with more angular features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape?

If you're not sure whether you have a square face, there are a few ways to determine your face shape. One method is to stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker. Then, step back and look at the shape you've drawn. If your face is roughly the same width at your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline, you likely have a square face.

Step by Step Guide: Choosing Bangs for Square Faces

Step 1: Consider Your Hair Type

When choosing bangs for a square face, it's important to consider your hair type. If you have curly or thick hair, heavy bangs may be too much and require too much maintenance. If your hair is fine or straight, you may be able to pull off heavier bangs.

Step 2: Think About Your Forehead

Another factor to consider when choosing bangs for a square face is your forehead. If you have a wide forehead, bangs can help to balance out your features. However, if you have a small forehead, bangs may make your face look shorter and wider.

Step 3: Choose the Right Length

The length of your bangs is also important when it comes to flattery. For square faces, it's best to choose bangs that hit just above the eyebrows, as this length will help to soften the strong jawline.

Step 4: Consider the Shape of Your Bangs

Finally, when choosing bangs for a square face, consider the shape of your bangs. Rounded or side-swept bangs can help to soften angular features, while blunt or straight-across bangs can emphasize them.

Tips for Styling Bangs with a Square Face

Once you've chosen the perfect bangs for your square face, it's important to style them properly to maximize their flattery. Here are a few tips:

  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to add volume and create a soft, rounded shape.
  • Use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to smooth down any frizz or flyaways.
  • Consider adding layers to the rest of your hair to balance out your features.
  • Accessorize your bangs with headbands, clips, or barrettes to add interest and texture.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can anyone with a square face wear bangs?

A: Yes, anyone with a square face can wear bangs! It's just a matter of finding the right style and length for your features.

Q: Can bangs make a square face look shorter?

A: Yes, if the wrong length or shape of bangs is chosen, they can make a square face look shorter and wider.

Q: Are there any styles of bangs that should be avoided with a square face?

A: It's best to avoid heavy, blunt bangs that hit at the widest part of the face, as these can emphasize the squareness of the jawline.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs with a Square Face

When it comes to caring for your bangs, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Wash your bangs every 2-3 days to keep them looking fresh.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs, as this can cause damage and split ends.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them at the right length.
  • Consider using dry shampoo or baby powder to absorb excess oil between washes.

In conclusion, bangs can be a great option for square faces, as long as the right style and length is chosen. By considering your hair type, forehead shape, and the shape of your bangs, you can create a flattering and stylish look that highlights your best features.
