How To Make Natural Hair Curly Without Products A Guide For 2023

Written by Eveline Apr 09, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have naturally straight hair, you may be looking for ways to make your hair curly without using any products. While there are many products on the market that claim to give you curls, they can be expensive and may contain harmful chemicals. Fortunately, there are several natural methods you can try to get curly hair without using any products.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make Natural Hair Curly Without Products
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Getting Curly Hair Without Products
  4. FAQs

How to Make Natural Hair Curly Without Products

There are several ways you can make your natural hair curly without using any products:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair
  • Twist your hair into small sections and let it dry naturally
  • Braid your hair before going to bed and take the braids out in the morning
  • Use a curling iron or curling wand
  • Use a straightening iron to create curls
  • Scrunch your hair while it's wet and let it air dry

Some of these methods may work better for you than others, depending on your hair type and personal preferences. Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for making your hair curly without using any products:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blot your hair with a towel to remove excess water.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  4. If you're using a diffuser, attach it to your blow dryer and set it to low heat and low speed. Hold the diffuser close to your head and move it in a circular motion to dry your hair.
  5. If you're twisting your hair, divide it into small sections and twist each section tightly. Let your hair dry naturally or use a hair dryer on low heat to speed up the process.
  6. If you're braiding your hair, divide it into small sections and braid each section tightly. Sleep with your hair in braids and take them out in the morning.
  7. If you're using a curling iron or curling wand, section your hair and wrap each section around the iron or wand. Hold for a few seconds and release.
  8. If you're using a straightening iron, section your hair and clamp the iron near the roots. Twist the iron away from your face and pull it down to the ends of your hair.
  9. If you're scrunching your hair, apply a styling product to your hair while it's wet. Scrunch your hair with your hands and let it air dry.
  10. Finish with a hairspray or other styling product, if desired.

Tips for Getting Curly Hair Without Products

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when making your hair curly without using any products:

  • Start with clean, damp hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Work in small sections for best results.
  • Be patient and allow your hair to dry completely before styling.
  • Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making natural hair curly without using any products:

Can I use a hair dryer without a diffuser to make my hair curly?

Yes, you can use a hair dryer without a diffuser to make your hair curly. However, using a diffuser can help to reduce frizz and create more defined curls.

How long does it take for hair to dry when twisting or braiding?

The time it takes for hair to dry when twisting or braiding depends on your hair type and the thickness of your hair. It can take anywhere from a few hours to overnight for your hair to dry completely.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to help define your curls.

With these tips and techniques, you can make your natural hair curly without using any products. Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for you and enjoy your new curly hairstyle!
