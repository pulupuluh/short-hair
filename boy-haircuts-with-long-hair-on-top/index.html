Boy Haircuts With Long Hair On Top A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Mar 13, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut for your little boy, boy haircuts with long hair on top are a great option. This style has become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. With long hair on top, your boy can experiment with different styles and looks, from messy and textured to slick and polished. In this article, we'll give you a complete guide on boy haircuts with long hair on top, including how to style them, tips for maintenance, and answers to common FAQs.

How to Get a Boy Haircut with Long Hair on Top

The first step in getting a boy haircut with long hair on top is to find a good stylist who is experienced in this type of haircut. Make sure to bring pictures of the style you want so that the stylist can understand your vision. During the haircut, the stylist will typically cut the sides and back short while leaving the hair on top long. The length of the hair on top can vary depending on personal preference, but it's usually around 3-4 inches. The stylist may also add layers or texture to the hair on top to create more volume and movement.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Boy Haircuts with Long Hair on Top

Styling boy haircuts with long hair on top can be simple and easy with the right products and techniques. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling this type of haircut:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, to the hair on top.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style the hair into your desired shape and texture.
  4. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Remember, the key to styling boy haircuts with long hair on top is to keep it looking natural and effortless. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for your boy's hair type and face shape.

Tips for Maintaining Boy Haircuts with Long Hair on Top

Maintaining boy haircuts with long hair on top is important to keep the style looking fresh and polished. Here are some tips to help you maintain this type of haircut:

  • Trim the hair on top every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of the cut.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling, as this can damage the hair.
  • Try different styling products to find what works best for your boy's hair type and texture.

FAQs About Boy Haircuts with Long Hair on Top

What face shape is best for boy haircuts with long hair on top?

Boy haircuts with long hair on top can work well for a variety of face shapes, but they are particularly flattering for boys with oval or round faces. The long hair on top can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

What age is appropriate for boy haircuts with long hair on top?

There is no specific age that is appropriate for boy haircuts with long hair on top. This style can work for boys of all ages, from toddlers to teenagers. However, it's important to consider your boy's lifestyle and preferences when choosing a haircut, as some styles may be more difficult to maintain or may not suit their personality.

Can boy haircuts with long hair on top be worn to school?

Most schools have dress codes that require boys to have a neat and tidy appearance, so it's important to check with your school before choosing a boy haircut with long hair on top. Some schools may allow this type of haircut as long as it's styled in a conservative and professional manner.

Hair Care for Boy Haircuts with Long Hair on Top

Keeping your boy's hair looking healthy and shiny is essential for maintaining boy haircuts with long hair on top. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash the hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water or harsh chemicals on the hair, as this can strip away natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to hydrate and protect the hair.
  • Brush the hair gently with a wide-tooth comb to prevent tangles and breakage.

By following these hair care tips and styling techniques, you can help your boy achieve a trendy and stylish haircut with long hair on top. Remember, the key is to find a style that suits your boy's personality and lifestyle, and to have fun experimenting with different looks and textures.
