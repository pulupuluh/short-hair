How To Find Out Your Hair Type Male A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Knowing your hair type is important in determining the right hair care routine for yourself. However, for men, it can be quite difficult to figure out their hair type since they tend to have shorter hair compared to women. In this article, we will guide you on how to find out your hair type male.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Find Out Your Hair Type Male
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips to Identify Your Hair Type
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips Based on Hair Type

Description

Before we dive into how to find out your hair type male, let's first understand what hair type is. Hair type refers to the texture of your hair, which is determined by the shape of the hair follicle. There are generally four hair types:

  • Straight Hair
  • Wavy Hair
  • Curly Hair
  • Kinky Hair

Each hair type has its own unique characteristics and requires different hair care routines.

How to Find Out Your Hair Type Male

The easiest way to find out your hair type is to look at your hair's natural state. Observe the shape of your hair strands and the way it falls naturally.

Straight Hair: If your hair strands are completely straight and don't have any wave or curl, then you have straight hair.

Wavy Hair: If your hair has a slight curve or wave, then you have wavy hair.

Curly Hair: If your hair has a definite curl or spiral shape, then you have curly hair.

Kinky Hair: If your hair has a very tight curl or coil pattern, then you have kinky hair.

Step by Step Guide

If you're still unsure about your hair type, follow these steps to find out:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Pat your hair dry with a towel, don't rub it.
  3. Let your hair air dry without using any products.
  4. Observe the shape of your hair strands and the way it falls naturally.

Tips to Identify Your Hair Type

Here are some additional tips to help you identify your hair type:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. If your hair easily glides through the comb, then you have straight hair. If it gets a little stuck, you have wavy hair. If it's difficult to comb through, you have curly or kinky hair.
  • Try the strand test: Take a strand of your hair and stretch it gently. If it stretches without breaking and returns to its natural shape, you have healthy hair. If it breaks easily, you have damaged hair.
  • Feel the texture of your hair. If it feels smooth and silky, you have straight or wavy hair. If it feels coarse or rough, you have curly or kinky hair.

FAQs

Q: Can my hair type change over time?

A: Yes, your hair type can change due to hormonal changes, age, and external factors such as heat styling, chemical treatments, and climate.

Q: What hair care products should I use for my hair type?

A: It's important to use hair care products that are specifically designed for your hair type. For example, if you have curly hair, use products that are formulated for curly hair, such as curl defining creams and leave-in conditioners.

Hair Care Tips Based on Hair Type

Now that you know your hair type, here are some general hair care tips:

  • Straight Hair: Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Wavy Hair: Use a curl-enhancing product to define your waves. Avoid using too much product as it can make your hair look greasy.
  • Curly Hair: Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated. Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to prevent frizz.
  • Kinky Hair: Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out. Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

By following these tips and understanding your hair type, you can achieve healthy and beautiful hair.
