How Can A Man Cut His Own Hair With Clippers

Written by Petter Feb 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a man who's looking to save money on haircuts or simply prefer the convenience of cutting your own hair at home, using clippers is a great option. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a clean and professional-looking cut without having to visit a barber. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own hair with clippers.

Description

Before we dive into the details of how to cut your own hair with clippers, it's important to understand what clippers are and how they work. Hair clippers are electric or battery-powered devices that are designed to cut hair. They come with different blade sizes and attachments that allow you to achieve different lengths and styles. Clippers are easy to use and can be a great investment for any man who wants to maintain a well-groomed appearance.

How to

Step 1: Prepare your tools and workspace

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to have all the necessary tools and a clean workspace. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Make sure your clippers are fully charged or plugged in, and clean the blades with a brush or a damp cloth.

Step 2: Choose your desired length and attachment

Most clippers come with different attachments that allow you to choose the length of your hair. Attach the desired length guard to your clippers and start with a higher number, gradually working your way down to a shorter length if necessary. You can always go shorter, but you can't go back once you've cut too much!

Step 3: Start with the sides and back

Using the clippers, start at the bottom of your head and work your way up. Use smooth and steady strokes, moving the clippers against the direction of hair growth. Use the comb to lift the hair and ensure an even cut. Move to the sides and back of your head, making sure to blend the hair as you go.

Step 4: Cut the top

Once you've finished the sides and back, it's time to tackle the top of your head. Use the scissors to trim any longer hair, and then use the clippers to cut the remaining hair to your desired length. Again, use smooth and steady strokes, moving against the direction of hair growth.

Step 5: Trim the neckline and around the ears

Use the clippers without an attachment to trim the hair around your neckline and ears. Use the comb to lift the hair and create a natural-looking transition.

Tips

  • Start with a longer guard attachment and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head and ensure an even cut.
  • Trim your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can be heavier and harder to cut.
  • Clean your clippers after each use to ensure they work properly and last longer.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

You can, but it's not recommended. Hair clippers are designed to cut hair evenly and quickly, while scissors can create uneven cuts and take longer to use.

What if I make a mistake?

If you accidentally cut too much hair, don't panic. You can always go shorter or wait for your hair to grow back. If you're not comfortable cutting your own hair, consider visiting a professional barber.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and desired style. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain a neat and tidy appearance.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type, and avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy appearance.

Cutting your own hair with clippers can be a great way to save money and maintain a well-groomed appearance. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a clean and professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. Follow these steps and tips, and you'll be a pro in no time!
