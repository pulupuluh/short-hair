How To Know If A Hair Color Will Suit Me

How to Know If a Hair Color Will Suit Me

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Choosing a hair color can be overwhelming, especially if you haven’t tried anything new before. You may be wondering if a certain hair color will suit you or not. This article will provide you with the necessary information on how to know if a hair color will suit you or not.

How to

The first thing to consider is your skin tone. Your skin tone plays a crucial role in determining which hair color will suit you. If you have warm undertones, go for warm hair colors such as golden blonde, auburn, or chocolate brown. If you have cool undertones, go for cool hair colors such as ash blonde, platinum blonde, or burgundy.

Another factor to consider is your eye color. If you have blue eyes, go for warm hair colors such as golden blonde or strawberry blonde. If you have green eyes, go for cool hair colors such as ash blonde or platinum blonde. If you have brown eyes, you can go for both warm and cool hair colors.

Your natural hair color also plays a role in determining which hair color will suit you. If you have dark hair, it’s best to stick with darker shades such as espresso brown or black. If you have light hair, you can experiment with lighter shades such as ash blonde or platinum blonde.

Step by Step

To determine which hair color will suit you, follow these steps:

  1. Identify your skin tone
  2. Identify your eye color
  3. Consider your natural hair color
  4. Research hair colors that suit your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color
  5. Consult with a professional hairstylist

Tips

  • Consider the season when choosing a hair color. For example, warm hair colors are perfect for fall and winter, while cool hair colors are perfect for spring and summer.
  • Don’t forget to take into account your personality and lifestyle when choosing a hair color.
  • If you’re unsure about a certain hair color, try a temporary dye or highlights first.

FAQ

Q: Will a drastic hair color change suit me?

A: It depends on your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. It’s best to consult with a professional hairstylist before making a drastic hair color change.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: It depends on the type of hair color you have. Generally, permanent hair color should be touched up every 4-6 weeks, while semi-permanent hair color should be touched up every 2-4 weeks.

Hair Care

After coloring your hair, it’s important to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy and health. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for colored hair. Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often as they can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. Don’t forget to regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends.
