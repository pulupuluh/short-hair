What Should A 60 Year Old Woman Wear To A Beach Wedding

Written by Bardi Feb 20, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As a 60 year old woman attending a beach wedding, you may be wondering what to wear that is appropriate for the occasion while also being comfortable in the warm weather. This guide will provide you with tips on what to wear, how to style your hair, and answer frequently asked questions about beach wedding attire for women over 60.

Table of Contents

  • What to Wear
  • How to Style Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Beach Wedding Attire
  • FAQ

What to Wear

When selecting an outfit for a beach wedding, keep in mind that the dress code is typically more casual than a traditional wedding. That being said, it's still important to dress appropriately for the occasion. Here are some outfit ideas:

Option 1: Maxi Dress

A flowy maxi dress is a great option for a beach wedding. Look for a dress in a lightweight fabric, such as cotton or linen, and in a bright, summery color. Opt for a dress with a v-neckline and empire waist to flatter your figure. Pair with sandals and statement earrings to complete the look.

Option 2: Jumpsuit

If you prefer pants over a dress, a jumpsuit is a stylish and comfortable option. Look for a jumpsuit in a lightweight fabric, such as cotton or rayon, and in a bright, summery color. Opt for a jumpsuit with a v-neckline and wide-leg pants to flatter your figure. Pair with sandals and statement earrings to complete the look.

Option 3: Skirt and Blouse

If you prefer separates over a dress or jumpsuit, a skirt and blouse is a classic and elegant option. Look for a skirt in a lightweight fabric, such as cotton or linen, and in a bright, summery color. Pair with a blouse in a complementary color and statement earrings to complete the look.

How to Style Your Hair

When styling your hair for a beach wedding, keep in mind that the weather may be hot and humid. Opt for hairstyles that are simple and easy to maintain. Here are some hairstyle ideas:

Option 1: Loose Waves

Loose waves are a great option for a beach wedding. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place. If you have short hair, consider adding hair extensions to create more volume and length.

Option 2: Braided Updo

A braided updo is a stylish and elegant option for a beach wedding. Create a loose braid and pin it into a bun at the nape of your neck. Secure with bobby pins and hairspray to hold the style in place.

Option 3: Ponytail

A simple ponytail is a classic and easy option for a beach wedding. Pull your hair back into a high or low ponytail and secure with a hair tie. Use hairspray to tame any flyaways and add shine.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on what to wear to a beach wedding:

  1. Check the dress code on the wedding invitation. If the dress code is not specified, assume it is casual.
  2. Select an outfit that is appropriate for the occasion and comfortable in the warm weather.
  3. Accessorize with statement jewelry and comfortable sandals.
  4. Style your hair in a simple and easy-to-maintain hairstyle.
  5. Apply sunscreen to protect your skin from the sun.
  6. Bring a lightweight shawl or cardigan in case the weather gets cooler in the evening.
  7. Have fun and enjoy the celebration!

Tips for Beach Wedding Attire

Here are some additional tips for selecting beach wedding attire:

  • Opt for lightweight fabrics, such as cotton and linen, to stay cool in the warm weather.
  • Avoid wearing heels, as they may sink into the sand. Opt for comfortable sandals instead.
  • Accessorize with statement jewelry, such as bold earrings or a statement necklace.
  • Bring a lightweight shawl or cardigan in case the weather gets cooler in the evening.
  • Apply sunscreen to protect your skin from the sun.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear white to a beach wedding?

A: It's typically best to avoid wearing white to a wedding, as it is traditionally reserved for the bride. Opt for a bright, summery color instead.

Q: Can I wear shorts to a beach wedding?

A: Shorts are typically too casual for a wedding, even a beach wedding. Opt for a dress, jumpsuit, or skirt and blouse instead.

Q: Can I wear a hat to a beach wedding?

A: A hat can be a stylish and practical accessory for a beach wedding. Opt for a wide-brimmed hat to protect your face from the sun.

Q: Can I wear a swimsuit to a beach wedding?

A: A swimsuit is too casual for a wedding, even a beach wedding. Opt for a dress, jumpsuit, or skirt and blouse instead.

Hair Care

When attending a beach wedding, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage from the sun, salt water, and chlorine. Here are some tips:

  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming in salt water or chlorine.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and flat irons, which can damage your hair in the heat and humidity.
  • Opt for simple and easy-to-maintain hairstyles, such as loose waves or a ponytail.
