How To Tie A Bun With Short Natural Hair

Written by Daniel Sep 14, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have short natural hair, you may think that tying your hair into a bun is impossible. However, with the right techniques and tools, you can create a cute and stylish bun that will make any bad hair day disappear. In this article, we will share with you step-by-step instructions on how to create a bun with short natural hair, along with some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tie a Bun with Short Natural Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Natural Hair

Step-by-Step Instructions

Before we get started, here are some tools and products you will need:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hair ties
  • Hair gel or edge control
  • Wide-tooth comb

Now, let's start creating your bun:

Step 1: Start with clean and detangled hair

Wash and condition your hair, then use a wide-tooth comb to detangle it. This will make it easier to style your hair and prevent breakage.

Step 2: Apply hair gel or edge control

Apply a small amount of hair gel or edge control to your edges and any other areas that need smoothing.

Step 3: Gather your hair into a ponytail

Gather your hair into a ponytail at the top or back of your head. Use a hair tie to secure it in place.

Step 4: Twist and wrap your hair

Take the ponytail and twist it until it starts to coil around the hair tie. Continue twisting and wrapping your hair until you reach the end.

Step 5: Secure the bun with bobby pins

Use bobby pins to secure the ends of your hair in place. If you have any loose strands, tuck them in with bobby pins as well.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect bun:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • If you have short hair, you may need to use more bobby pins to secure your bun in place.
  • If you want to add some volume to your bun, try using a hair donut or sock bun.
  • Experiment with different bun styles, such as a messy bun or braided bun.

FAQs

Can I create a bun with short natural hair?

Yes, you can! Follow the step-by-step instructions above to create a bun with short natural hair.

What if my hair is too short to create a bun?

If your hair is too short to create a bun, try using a hair clip or headband to create a half-up half-down style.

Hair Care for Short Natural Hair

Here are some hair care tips for short natural hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling wands, too often.
  • Protect your hair while sleeping by using a satin or silk bonnet.

Now that you know how to tie a bun with short natural hair, you can rock this style any day of the week. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
