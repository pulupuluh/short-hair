How To Style Hair With Big Forehead Male Tips Tricks And Techniques

If you are a male with a big forehead, you may feel self-conscious about your hairline. But the truth is that there are many ways to style your hair to minimize the appearance of a large forehead. In this article, we will share tips, tricks, and techniques to help you feel confident and stylish no matter what your hair type or length.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Hair with Big Forehead Male
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks

How to Style Hair with Big Forehead Male

The key to styling hair with a big forehead is to draw attention away from your hairline and towards your eyes, cheekbones, or jawline. Here are some ways to do that:

1. Side Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for men with big foreheads because they create a diagonal line that draws the eye down and towards the side of the face. To achieve this look, ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle and style them to the side with some product.

2. Fringe

A fringe is another option for men with big foreheads. This style involves cutting the hair straight across the forehead, creating a horizontal line that balances out the height of the forehead. Fringes can be styled straight down or swept to the side.

3. Short Hair

If you prefer short hair, consider a style that is cropped close to the head. This will minimize the appearance of your forehead and draw attention to your facial features. A short, textured cut with a bit of volume on top can also be flattering.

4. Long Hair

While longer hair can be more challenging to style with a big forehead, it is still possible to make it work. Consider a layered cut that adds volume and movement to the hair. You can also try styling your hair back or to the side with some product.

5. Buzz Cut

If you are looking for a low-maintenance option, a buzz cut can be a great choice. This style involves cutting the hair very short all over the head, which can help minimize the appearance of a large forehead.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your hair with a big forehead:

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type and Length

Before you can choose a hairstyle, you need to know your hair type and length. This will help you determine which styles will work best for you.

Step 2: Choose a Flattering Style

Consider the options listed above and choose a style that will flatter your face shape and minimize the appearance of your forehead.

Step 3: Consult with Your Stylist

Once you have chosen a style, consult with your stylist to determine the best way to achieve it. They can help you select the right products and show you how to style your hair at home.

Step 4: Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make all the difference when it comes to styling your hair. Choose products that work well with your hair type and help you achieve the look you want.

Step 5: Experiment and Have Fun

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products until you find what works best for you. And remember to have fun with your hair!

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling hair with a big forehead:

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Volumizing products can help add body and fullness to your hair, which can make it look thicker and more voluminous. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed to add volume.

2. Try a Texturizing Spray

Texturizing sprays can add texture and definition to your hair, which can help create the illusion of thicker, fuller hair.

3. Use a Blow Dryer

Using a blow dryer can help add volume and body to your hair. Use a round brush to lift the hair at the roots and create volume.

4. Don't Overdo It with Product

While products can help you achieve the look you want, it's important not to overdo it. Too much product can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear a slicked-back style with a big forehead?

A: Yes, you can still wear a slicked-back style with a big forehead. Just be sure to keep the hair on top of your head a bit longer to balance out the height of your forehead.

Q: What is the best way to style curly hair with a big forehead?

A: Curly hair can be more challenging to style with a big forehead, but a textured cut with some volume on top can be flattering. You can also try a side-swept fringe or a messy, tousled look.

Q: Should I use a hair gel or a pomade?

A: It depends on the look you are trying to achieve. Hair gel is best for a slicked-back or wet look, while pomade can help create texture and definition.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help keep it clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Using heat tools like blow dryers and flat irons can damage your hair over time. Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair with heat.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair looking neat and healthy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet can help promote healthy hair growth. Eat a diet rich in vitamins and minerals, and drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.

With these tips, tricks, and techniques, you can style your hair with confidence no matter what your forehead size. Remember to consult with your stylist to find the best style for you and have fun experimenting with different looks!
