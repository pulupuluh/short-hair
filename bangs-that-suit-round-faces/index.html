Bangs That Suit Round Faces

Written by Christine Jun 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, you may have struggled with finding the right bangs to suit your face shape. Bangs can help to frame your face and enhance your features, but it's important to choose the right style to flatter your face shape. In this article, we'll be discussing the best bangs for round faces and how to style them.

Description

Round faces are characterized by a soft, circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal when choosing bangs for round faces is to create the illusion of length and balance out the fullness of the cheeks. The right bangs can help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Round Faces

When choosing bangs for round faces, it's important to consider your hair texture and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the right bangs:

1. Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs

Straight-across bangs can make your face appear even rounder, so it's best to avoid this style. Instead, opt for bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle to create the illusion of length.

2. Choose wispy or side-swept bangs

Wispy or side-swept bangs can help to elongate your face and draw attention to your eyes. These styles are also versatile and can be worn with a variety of hair lengths and textures.

3. Consider your hair texture

If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to avoid heavy bangs that will require a lot of maintenance. Instead, opt for softer, more natural-looking bangs that can be easily styled with a bit of texturizing spray.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Bangs for Round Faces

Once you've chosen the right bangs for your round face shape, it's important to know how to style them to flatter your features. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling bangs for round faces:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling your bangs to ensure that they lay smoothly and don't become greasy or oily.

2. Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs

Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs, brushing them downwards and away from your face. This will help to create volume and lift at the roots.

3. Use a flat iron to smooth your bangs

After blow-drying, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or waves in your bangs. This will help to create a polished, sleek look.

4. Finish with a texturizing spray

To add texture and hold to your bangs, finish with a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. This will help to keep your bangs in place and prevent them from becoming greasy throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs for Round Faces

Here are some tips for maintaining your bangs for round faces:

1. Trim your bangs regularly

To keep your bangs looking fresh and polished, trim them every 4-6 weeks. This will help to prevent split ends and keep your bangs at the right length for your face shape.

2. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfate-free shampoos are gentler on your hair and won't strip away natural oils, which can cause your bangs to become dry and brittle.

3. Avoid touching your bangs too much

Touching your bangs can transfer oils from your hands to your hair, which can cause your bangs to become greasy faster. Try to avoid touching your bangs throughout the day.

FAQ

Q: Can bangs make my face appear thinner?

A: Yes, the right bangs can help to create the illusion of length and balance out the fullness of the cheeks, making your face appear slimmer.

Q: What if I don't like my bangs?

A: If you don't like your bangs, don't worry! Bangs are a temporary hairstyle and can be grown out or trimmed to a different length. Talk to your stylist about your concerns and they can help you find a solution.

Hair Care for Bangs

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful bangs:

1. Use a heat protectant

Before styling your bangs with heat tools, use a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage and breakage.

2. Deep condition regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help to nourish and strengthen your hair, preventing split ends and breakage.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays can cause damage and fade color, so it's important to protect your hair from the sun. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your bangs.

Conclusion

Bangs can be a great way to frame your face and enhance your features, but it's important to choose the right style to flatter your face shape. If you have a round face, opt for wispy or side-swept bangs that can help to elongate your face and create the illusion of length. With the right styling techniques and hair care routine, you can maintain healthy, beautiful bangs that flatter your features.
