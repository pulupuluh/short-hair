How To Make Relaxed Hair Grow Faster And Longer

Written by Daniel Sep 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Growing long, healthy hair can be challenging, especially when it comes to relaxed hair. However, with proper care and attention, it is possible to make relaxed hair grow faster and longer. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this goal through a combination of diet, hair care, and lifestyle changes.

Table of Contents

  1. Understanding Relaxed Hair
  2. How to Make Relaxed Hair Grow Faster and Longer
  3. Tips for Maintaining Long, Healthy Hair
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Final Thoughts

Understanding Relaxed Hair

Relaxed hair is hair that has been chemically treated to break down the natural curl pattern. This process can cause damage to the hair and scalp, making it more difficult to grow long, healthy hair. However, with the right approach, it is possible to promote hair growth and maintain healthy hair.

How to Make Relaxed Hair Grow Faster and Longer

1. Maintain a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet is essential for promoting hair growth. Be sure to include plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals in your daily diet. Foods like salmon, eggs, spinach, and sweet potatoes are excellent choices for promoting hair growth.

2. Keep Your Hair and Scalp Clean

Clean hair and scalp are essential for promoting hair growth. Be sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water and harsh chemicals that can damage your hair and scalp.

3. Moisturize Your Hair

Dry hair is more prone to breakage, which can slow down hair growth. Be sure to moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or oil. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can cause damage to your hair, which can slow down hair growth. Try to avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons. If you must use them, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting low.

5. Limit Chemical Treatments

Chemical treatments like relaxers and dyes can cause damage to your hair and scalp, which can slow down hair growth. Try to limit the number of chemical treatments you use on your hair. If possible, opt for natural hair treatments instead.

Tips for Maintaining Long, Healthy Hair

1. Protect Your Hair at Night

Use a satin or silk scarf or pillowcase to protect your hair while you sleep. This will help to prevent breakage and maintain healthy hair.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and breakage, which can slow down hair growth. Be sure to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

3. Be Gentle with Your Hair

Be gentle when brushing or styling your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb or brush and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How often should I wash my hair?

It is recommended to wash your hair every 7-10 days. However, if you have an oily scalp, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

2. How can I prevent breakage?

To prevent breakage, be sure to moisturize your hair regularly, avoid heat styling, and be gentle when brushing or styling your hair.

3. Can I use hair growth products on relaxed hair?

Yes, there are several hair growth products available that are safe for use on relaxed hair. Be sure to read the product label carefully and follow the instructions.

Final Thoughts

Growing long, healthy hair takes time and effort, but it is possible with the right approach. By maintaining a healthy diet, keeping your hair and scalp clean, moisturizing your hair, and avoiding heat styling and chemical treatments, you can make relaxed hair grow faster and longer.
