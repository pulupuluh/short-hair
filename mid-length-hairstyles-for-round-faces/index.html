Mid Length Hairstyles For Round Faces A Guide To Flattering Cuts

Written by Christine Apr 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you on the hunt for a new hairstyle that will flatter your round face shape? Look no further than mid length hairstyles! This versatile length can work for a variety of hair types and textures, and there are plenty of options that will complement your face shape. Here's everything you need to know about mid length hairstyles for round faces.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Mid Length Hairstyle for Your Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Mid Length Hair for Round Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Mid Length Hairstyle

How to Choose the Right Mid Length Hairstyle for Your Round Face

When it comes to mid length hairstyles for round faces, there are a few key factors to consider. First, you'll want to choose a cut that adds some height to your crown, as this will help elongate your face. You'll also want to avoid any styles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or chin-length bobs.

Some great mid length hairstyles for round faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Shoulder-length waves
  • Textured lob
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical bob

These styles are all great for adding dimension to your hair and drawing attention away from the roundness of your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Mid Length Hair for Round Faces

Once you've chosen your mid length hairstyle, it's time to learn how to style it! Here's a simple step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to protect it from heat damage.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, brushing your hair away from your face to add volume at the crown.
  4. Use a curling iron or wand to add loose waves or curls to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.
  5. Spray your hair with a flexible hold hairspray to help your style last all day.

Remember, this is just a basic guide, and you should adjust your styling routine based on your hair type and the specific cut you've chosen.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mid Length Hairstyle

Once you've got your mid length hairstyle looking just right, you'll want to keep it looking great for as long as possible. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your style:

  • Get regular trims to keep your ends looking healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent heat damage.
  • Try sleeping on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs About Mid Length Hairstyles for Round Faces

What is considered mid length hair?

Mid length hair is typically hair that falls anywhere between the shoulders and the collarbone.

Can I wear bangs with a mid length hairstyle if I have a round face?

Yes, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs. Side-swept bangs or wispy bangs are great options that won't add width to your face.

Can I wear my mid length hair straight if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Just be sure to add volume at the crown to help elongate your face.

Hair Care Tips for Mid Length Hair

Finally, it's important to take good care of your mid length hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray or cream.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a mid length hairstyle that flatters your round face and looks amazing every day!
