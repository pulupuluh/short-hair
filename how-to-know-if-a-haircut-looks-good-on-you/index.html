How To Know If A Haircut Looks Good On You Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Mar 07, 2023 · 9 min read

Getting a haircut can be a major decision, especially if you're trying out a new style or going for a drastic change. But how do you know if a haircut looks good on you? In this article, we'll go through some tips and tricks to help you determine if a new haircut is right for you.

Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in figuring out if a haircut looks good on you is to determine your face shape. This will help you identify which hairstyles will complement your features and which ones might not work as well.

There are six basic face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Rectangle

Once you know your face shape, you can search for hairstyles that flatter your features. For example, if you have a round face, you might want to try a layered haircut or a style with side-swept bangs to add some angles and definition.

Consider Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider when choosing a new haircut is your hair type. Do you have thick, curly hair or fine, straight hair? Different hair types will work better with certain styles.

For example, if you have fine hair, you might want to avoid heavy, blunt cuts that can make your hair look flat and lifeless. Instead, try a layered cut that adds volume and movement.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in determining if a haircut looks good on you. Do you have a job that requires a more conservative look, or are you free to experiment with bold styles?

Consider how much time you're willing to spend on styling your hair each day. If you're low maintenance, you might want to choose a style that doesn't require a lot of upkeep.

Highlight Your Best Features

When choosing a new haircut, it's important to think about which features you want to highlight. Do you have beautiful eyes or high cheekbones? A well-chosen haircut can draw attention to your best features and make you feel more confident.

For example, if you have a long neck, you might want to consider a short, cropped haircut that shows off your elegant neckline.

Explore Different Haircut Types

There are countless haircut types to choose from, so it's important to explore your options before making a decision. Look through magazines and online galleries to find inspiration.

Some popular haircut types include:

  • Pixie
  • Bob
  • Layered
  • Shag
  • Bangs
  • Asymmetrical

Remember, just because a certain cut looks good on someone else doesn't mean it will suit you. Consider your own unique features and hair type when choosing a style.

Consider Styling Options

When choosing a new haircut, it's important to think about how you'll style it on a daily basis. Are you comfortable using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, or do you prefer a more natural look?

Some haircuts require more styling than others. Make sure you choose a style that you're comfortable maintaining.

Factor in Hair Color

Finally, don't forget to factor in your hair color when choosing a new haircut. Some styles work better with certain colors than others.

For example, if you have a bold hair color like red or purple, you might want to choose a haircut that showcases your vibrant hue.

Get a Professional Consultation

If you're still unsure about which haircut is right for you, consider getting a professional consultation. A stylist can help you choose a style that flatters your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

They can also offer advice on how to style and maintain your new cut.

Think About the Commitment

Before you choose a new haircut, make sure you're ready for the commitment. Some styles require frequent trims and styling, while others are more low maintenance.

Make sure you choose a style that fits your lifestyle and time constraints.

FAQ

How do I know if a short haircut will look good on me?

Short haircuts can be a big change, but there are a few ways to determine if they'll suit you. Look at your face shape and hair type to see which short styles might flatter your features. You can also try on a wig or use a virtual makeover tool to get an idea of how you'll look with a short cut.

What should I do if I don't like my new haircut?

If you don't like your new haircut, don't panic. Talk to your stylist and explain what you don't like about the cut. They may be able to make some adjustments to make it more to your liking. If you're still unhappy, you can always grow your hair out or try a new style.

How often should I get a haircut?

The frequency of haircuts depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, people with short haircuts will need to get trims more frequently, while those with longer styles can go longer between cuts. Talk to your stylist about the best schedule for your hair.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling. Regular trims can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Remember, the key to a good haircut is choosing a style that flatters your features and fits your lifestyle. With these tips, you'll be able to find the perfect cut for you.
