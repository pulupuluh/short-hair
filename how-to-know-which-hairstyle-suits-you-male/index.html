How To Know Which Hairstyle Suits You Male

Written by Aprilia Jul 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task for many men. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to find the one that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to know which hairstyle suits you as a male.

  2. Understanding Your Hair Texture

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in finding a hairstyle that suits you is to determine your face shape. Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which hairstyles will complement your features. There are six common face shapes:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Oblong

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Take note of the shape of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Understanding Your Hair Texture

Another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your hair texture. Hair texture refers to the thickness and density of your hair. There are three main hair textures:

  • Fine
  • Medium
  • Coarse

To determine your hair texture, run your fingers through your hair. If you can feel individual strands and they are thin, you likely have fine hair. If your hair feels thicker and more substantial, you may have medium or coarse hair.

Considering Your Personal Style

Your personal style is also an essential factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Your hairstyle should complement your overall style and personality. Do you prefer a classic, clean-cut look, or are you more of a trendsetter?

Popular Hairstyles for Men

There are countless hairstyles to choose from, but some are more popular than others. Here are some of the most popular hairstyles for men:

  • Crew Cut
  • Buzz Cut
  • Side Part
  • Undercut
  • French Crop
  • Quiff
  • Slicked Back
  • Textured Crop

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle that suits you:

  • Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style
  • Bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to your stylist
  • Don't be afraid to try something new
  • Talk to your stylist about maintenance and styling

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and the style you choose. Typically, haircuts should be done every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Q: How do I style my hair?

A: The styling process will depend on the hairstyle you choose. Talk to your stylist about which products and tools are best for your hair type and style.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hairstyle

By following these tips and considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you can find the perfect hairstyle that suits you as a male.
