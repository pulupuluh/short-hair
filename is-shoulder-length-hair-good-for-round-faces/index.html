Is Shoulder Length Hair Good For Round Faces

Written by Luffy May 25, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you wondering if shoulder length hair is good for your round face shape? If you are, then you’ve come to the right place. In this blog post, we’ll answer your questions and give you tips on how to style your shoulder length hair to flatter your round face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Shoulder length hair is a versatile hairstyle that can work for any face shape, including round faces. It can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. However, the key to making shoulder length hair work for round faces is to create volume and texture in the right places. By doing so, you can balance out the roundness of your face and create a more flattering look.

How to

To make shoulder length hair work for your round face, you need to focus on creating volume and texture in the right areas. Here are some tips:

1. Add Layers

Adding layers to your shoulder length hair can help create movement and texture. This, in turn, can help balance out the roundness of your face. Opt for long layers that start at your chin or below to create the illusion of length.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. They can also help balance out the roundness of your face by drawing attention to your eyes and cheekbones. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face appear rounder.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves can help create volume and texture in your hair. This, in turn, can help balance out the roundness of your face. Use a curling iron or a flat iron to create loose waves. To create more volume, use a volumizing mousse or spray.

Step by Step

Here’s how you can style your shoulder length hair for a round face:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash and dry your hair before styling it. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to create volume and texture.

2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray.

Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and lengths. This will help create volume and texture in your hair.

3. Section your hair.

Section your hair into two parts – top and bottom. Use a clip to secure the top section.

4. Curl the bottom section.

Using a curling iron or a flat iron, curl the bottom section of your hair. Create loose waves by wrapping your hair around the iron and holding it for a few seconds. Release the curl and let it cool before moving on to the next section.

5. Curl the top section.

Curl the top section of your hair in the same way you did the bottom section. Make sure to curl away from your face to create volume.

6. Finish with hairspray.

Finish the look with a light hold hairspray. This will help your curls stay in place and create more volume.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your shoulder length hair for a round face:

  • Use a round brush when blow drying your hair to create volume.
  • Avoid straight, blunt cuts. Opt for layers instead.
  • Use a texturizing spray to create more volume and texture in your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to create the illusion of length.

FAQ

Can I wear my shoulder length hair straight if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your shoulder length hair straight if you have a round face. However, it’s important to create volume in your hair to balance out the roundness of your face. Use a volumizing mousse or spray and a round brush when blow drying your hair to create volume.

Can I wear a bob if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear a bob if you have a round face. However, it’s important to create volume and texture in your hair to balance out the roundness of your face. Opt for a layered bob that starts at your chin or below to create the illusion of length.

Hair Care

To keep your shoulder length hair looking healthy and shiny, it’s important to take care of it. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean.
  • Use a nourishing conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, shoulder length hair can work for round faces as long as you create volume and texture in the right areas. Use the tips and techniques outlined in this blog post to style your shoulder length hair for a more flattering look.
