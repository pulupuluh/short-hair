How To Cut Your Own Hair To Shape Your Face

Written by Bardi Sep 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of visiting salons every month to trim your hair? Well, why not try cutting your hair at home. Cutting your hair at home can be a bit intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. In this article, we will discuss how to cut your own hair to shape your face.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Cutting your hair at home can save you a lot of money and time. But before you start cutting your hair, you need to know your face shape. Knowing your face shape is important as it helps you determine which hairstyle will suit you the best.

There are six types of face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and triangle. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your facial features.

How to

The first step in cutting your own hair is to choose the right tools. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, clips, and a mirror. Make sure that the scissors are sharp, and the comb is wide-toothed.

The second step is to wet your hair. Wet hair is more manageable and easier to cut. You can wet your hair by taking a shower or spraying it with water.

Step by step

Now that you have the right tools and wet hair, let's start cutting your hair step by step:

  1. Start by parting your hair in the middle with a comb.
  2. Clip one side of your hair with a clip.
  3. Start cutting your hair from the back, using the comb as a guide.
  4. Trim the ends of your hair, but do not cut too much.
  5. Repeat the same process on the other side of your hair.
  6. Check both sides of your hair in the mirror to ensure that they are even.
  7. Style your hair as you desire.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you cut your hair like a pro:

  • Start by cutting a small amount of hair, and then gradually cut more if necessary.
  • Always use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Do not cut your hair when it is dry, as it can lead to uneven cutting.
  • Trim your hair every 2-3 months to maintain its shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair:

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

No, you need to use scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair. Regular scissors can damage your hair and lead to split ends.

Is it possible to cut my hair without a comb?

No, a comb is necessary to guide your scissors while cutting your hair. Without a comb, you may end up cutting your hair unevenly.

How can I cut my hair in layers?

To cut your hair in layers, you need to section your hair and cut each section at a different length. This will create a layered effect.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 2-3 months to prevent split ends.

Cutting your own hair can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following these tips and techniques, you can cut your hair like a pro and shape it according to your face shape. Happy cutting!
