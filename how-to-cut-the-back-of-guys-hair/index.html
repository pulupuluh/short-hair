How To Cut The Back Of Guys Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to give your guy a haircut, it's essential to know how to cut the back of guys hair. With this guide, you'll learn everything you need to know about cutting the back of guys hair like a pro. From the right tools to use to the proper technique, we'll cover it all. Let's get started!

Description

Whether you're a professional hairstylist or just someone looking to give your guy a haircut, it's important to know how to cut the back of guys hair. The back of the head can be tricky to cut, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a clean and polished look. This guide will walk you through the process step-by-step, so you can feel confident in your ability to give a great haircut.

How to Cut the Back of Guys Hair

Before you get started, it's essential to have the right tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Cape or towel
  • Clip or hair tie

Once you have your tools, follow these steps to cut the back of guys hair:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Make sure your guy's hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be more challenging to cut, and it's easier to see any unevenness in dry hair. If the hair is longer, you may want to wash it the day before to make it easier to handle.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Using your comb, divide the hair into sections. Start by making a part down the middle of the head, from the forehead to the nape of the neck. Then, divide each side into two sections, using a clip or hair tie to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Starting with the bottom section, use your clippers to trim the hair to the desired length. Work your way up the head, using the comb to guide the clippers and create an even cut. Remember to use a comb to lift the hair and avoid cutting too much at once.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut the bottom section, remove the clip or hair tie and blend the hair into the top section. Use your scissors to soften any harsh lines and create a natural-looking transition between the two sections.

Step 5: Repeat on the Other Side

Repeat the same steps on the other side, making sure to match the length and blend the hair evenly.

Tips for Cutting the Back of Guys Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard on your clippers and work your way down to a shorter one if needed.
  • Use a mirror to check your work and make sure the hair is even.
  • Take breaks if needed, so you don't get tired and make mistakes.
  • Be patient and take your time. Rushing can lead to uneven cuts.

FAQ

Q: How often should I cut the back of guys hair?

A: It depends on how fast the hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Generally, guys should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Q: Can I cut the back of guys hair at home?

A: Yes, with the right tools and technique, you can give a great haircut at home. Just make sure to follow the steps carefully and take your time.

Hair Care

After you've cut the hair, it's essential to take care of it to maintain the style. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and moisturized. You can also use styling products like gel or pomade to create a polished look.

Cutting the back of guys hair can be challenging, but with these tips and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!
