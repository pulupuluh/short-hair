Do Round Faces Suit Fringes

Written by Oliver Mar 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering if fringes are a good look for you. The truth is, fringes can work well on round faces, but there are a few things to consider before taking the plunge. In this article, we'll take a look at how to choose the right fringe for your face shape, how to style it, and how to care for your hair once you have your new fringe.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Fringe for Your Face Shape
  • How to Style Your Fringe
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Fringe
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Choose the Right Fringe for Your Face Shape

When it comes to fringes and round faces, the key is to choose a style that will balance out your features. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

Length

If you have a round face, it's generally best to avoid short fringes that hit above the eyebrows. Instead, opt for longer fringes that graze the eyebrows or fall just below them. This will help to elongate your face and make it appear more oval.

Shape

There are a few different fringe shapes that can work well on round faces:

  • Side-swept fringes: These fringes are angled and swept to one side, which helps to create the illusion of length in the face.
  • Curtain fringes: These fringes are parted in the middle and styled to fall on either side of the face. They can be a great option for those who want a more low-maintenance fringe.
  • Textured fringes: These fringes feature choppy, textured layers that can help to add dimension and movement to a round face.

Thickness

If you have a round face, it's generally best to avoid thick, heavy fringes that can overwhelm your features. Instead, opt for a lighter, wispy fringe that will blend in more seamlessly with the rest of your hair.

How to Style Your Fringe

Once you have your fringe, there are a few different ways to style it:

  • Straight: Use a flat iron to straighten your fringe and create a sleek, polished look.
  • Curled: Use a curling iron to add some volume and texture to your fringe.
  • Blow-dried: Use a round brush and a blow dryer to create a smooth, voluminous look.

Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Fringe

Here are a few tips to help you keep your fringe looking its best:

  • Trim regularly: To keep your fringe looking neat and tidy, it's important to trim it every 4-6 weeks.
  • Wash carefully: When washing your hair, be sure to carefully wash your fringe and rinse thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.
  • Blow-dry immediately: After washing your hair, be sure to blow-dry your fringe immediately to keep it looking smooth and polished.
  • Use a small amount of styling product: To keep your fringe in place throughout the day, use a small amount of styling product such as hairspray or wax.

FAQ

Can I still wear a fringe if I have a very round face?

Yes, you can still wear a fringe if you have a very round face. The key is to choose a style that will balance out your features and create the illusion of length in the face.

What fringe length is best for round faces?

If you have a round face, it's generally best to avoid short fringes that hit above the eyebrows. Instead, opt for longer fringes that graze the eyebrows or fall just below them.

What fringe shape is best for round faces?

Side-swept fringes, curtain fringes, and textured fringes can all work well on round faces. Experiment with different shapes to find the one that works best for you.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your hair with a fringe, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Wash regularly: To keep your hair and fringe looking healthy, it's important to wash it regularly.
  • Avoid heat: Excessive heat styling can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools.
  • Use a hair mask: To keep your hair and fringe looking soft and shiny, use a hair mask once a week to deeply moisturize your strands.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: Just like your skin, your hair can be damaged by the sun's UV rays. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your strands.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair and fringe looking healthy and beautiful.
