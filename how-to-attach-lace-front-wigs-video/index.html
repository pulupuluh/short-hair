How To Attach Lace Front Wigs Video A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 17, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of spending hours in front of the mirror trying to perfect your lace front wig install? Look no further than this step-by-step guide on how to attach lace front wigs video. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a flawless install every time.

Description

A lace front wig is a popular choice for those looking to switch up their hairstyle without the commitment of a sew-in or traditional wig. The wig features a sheer lace panel at the front of the wig that mimics a natural hairline, making it a more realistic option than traditional wigs.

This guide will walk you through the process of attaching a lace front wig, from prepping your natural hair to final styling. We’ll also cover some tips and tricks for maintaining your wig and making it last as long as possible.

How to Attach Lace Front Wigs Video: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Prepare your natural hair

Before you begin, it’s important to prep your natural hair for the wig install. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then blow-dry it straight. If you have long hair, consider braiding it or creating a flat twist to help it lay flat under the wig.

Step 2: Apply wig cap

Next, apply a wig cap over your natural hair. This will help keep your hair flat and prevent it from poking through the wig. Make sure the wig cap is snug but not too tight, as this can cause headaches or discomfort.

Step 3: Cut lace

Using a pair of sharp scissors, carefully cut the excess lace from the front of the wig. Be sure to leave a small amount of lace to work with, as this will help you create a more natural-looking hairline.

Step 4: Apply adhesive

Apply a small amount of wig adhesive to your hairline, being careful not to get it on your natural hair. Use a small brush or your finger to spread the adhesive evenly.

Step 5: Place wig

Align the wig with your natural hairline and gently press it down onto the adhesive. Use a comb or your fingers to press the lace into the adhesive, making sure there are no bubbles or gaps.

Step 6: Secure wig

Once the wig is in place, use a scarf or headband to secure it and allow the adhesive to dry for at least 30 minutes.

Step 7: Style wig

Once the adhesive is dry, style your wig as desired. You can use heat tools, like a flat iron or curling iron, to create different looks. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to the wig.

Tips for Maintaining Your Wig

Now that your wig is installed, it’s important to take care of it to make it last as long as possible. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your wig regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or mannequin head to help maintain its shape
  • Avoid exposing your wig to heat sources, like direct sunlight or a hot car
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as these can weigh down the wig and cause it to lose its shape

FAQs

How long will my lace front wig last?

With proper care and maintenance, a lace front wig can last up to a year or more.

Can I swim in my lace front wig?

It’s generally not recommended to swim in a lace front wig, as chlorine and saltwater can damage the wig and cause it to tangle or lose its shape.

Can I wear my lace front wig to sleep?

No, it’s not recommended to wear a lace front wig to sleep. This can cause tangling and damage to the wig.

Hair Care

Remember to take good care of your natural hair while wearing a lace front wig. Be sure to keep your hair and scalp clean and moisturized, and avoid using any harsh chemicals or heat tools that can cause damage.

Now that you know how to attach lace front wigs video, you can enjoy a flawless and natural-looking wig install. With the right techniques and products, you can achieve a beautiful hairstyle that will turn heads wherever you go.
