Best Hair For Round Face 2021

Description

If you have a round face, you know how important it is to find the right hairstyle that flatters your face shape. The right haircut can create the illusion of a longer or slimmer face, which is why it’s essential to choose a hairstyle that complements your round face. In this article, we’ll go through the best hair for round face in 2021 and provide you with some tips on how to style your hair to flatter your face shape.

How to

The first step to figuring out the best hair for round face is to determine your face shape. A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a forehead that is the same width as your cheeks. Once you’ve determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that flatter your features. Here are some tips on how to choose the best hair for round face:

Consider a Long Bob

A long bob is a great haircut for round faces because it can create the illusion of a longer face. The length of the bob should fall just below the chin and should be styled with layers to add volume and texture. This haircut works well with both straight and wavy hair.

Try Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are a great way to create the illusion of a slimmer face. The bangs should be swept to one side and should be longer on one side than the other. This style works well with both long and short hair.

Opt for Layers

Layers can add volume and texture to your hair, which can help to make your face appear slimmer. The layers should be cut around the face and should be longer towards the bottom of your hair. This style works well with both curly and straight hair.

Step by step

Now that you know what to look for in the best hair for round face, here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style your hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a mousse or a volumizing spray. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use the brush to add volume and lift to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a curling iron or a straightener. If you have curly hair, use a diffuser to help define your curls. If you have straight hair, use a flat iron to create sleek, straight locks.

Tips

Here are some additional tips on how to style your hair to flatter your round face:

  • Try a side part to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Consider adding highlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that make your face appear rounder, such as blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, and center parts.

What hairstyles look best on a round face?

The best hairstyles for a round face are those that create the illusion of a longer face, such as long bobs, side swept bangs, and layered haircuts.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.

With the right haircut and styling tips, you can flatter your round face and feel confident in your hairstyle. Experiment with different hairstyles and find what works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
