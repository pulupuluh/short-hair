How To Pick The Right Haircut For Your Face Shape

Written by Eveline Mar 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of getting a haircut that just doesn't suit your face? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle to find the perfect haircut that complements their face shape. In this article, we will guide you on how to pick the right haircut for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

First, let's understand the different face shapes. There are several face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Each face shape has unique features that require specific haircuts to enhance or minimize them.

For example, if you have a round face, you should opt for haircuts that add height and length to your face. On the other hand, if you have a square face, you should choose haircuts that soften your jawline.

How to

The first step in picking the right haircut for your face shape is to identify your face shape. You can do this by looking at yourself in the mirror and measuring the width and length of your face.

Once you have identified your face shape, you can research haircuts that complement your features. You can look for inspiration online or consult with a hairstylist.

Step by step

Here are the steps to pick the right haircut for your face shape:

  1. Identify your face shape
  2. Research haircuts that complement your features
  3. Consult with a hairstylist
  4. Choose a haircut that suits your personality and lifestyle

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while picking the right haircut for your face shape:

  • Consider your hair type and texture
  • Choose a haircut that flatters your best features
  • Avoid haircuts that emphasize your problem areas
  • Experiment with different hairstyles before committing to a haircut
  • Be open to your hairstylist's suggestions

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about picking the right haircut for your face shape:

  • What is the best haircut for a round face? A layered haircut with volume at the crown is the best haircut for a round face.
  • What is the best haircut for an oval face? An oval face can pull off any haircut, but a long bob or shoulder-length cut is particularly flattering.
  • What is the best haircut for a square face? Soft layers and side-swept bangs are the best haircut for a square face.
  • What is the best haircut for a heart-shaped face? A chin-length bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs is the best haircut for a heart-shaped face.
  • What is the best haircut for a diamond face? A layered bob or a side-swept pixie cut is the best haircut for a diamond face.

Hair Care

After getting the right haircut for your face shape, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools frequently
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your haircut
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine

With these tips, you will have not only the perfect haircut but also healthy and beautiful hair. Remember, it's essential to choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Happy hairstyling!
