Oval Face Short Haircuts With Bangs A Trendy Look For 2023

Written by William Feb 18, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your long hair and want to try a new look? If you have an oval face shape, then you are in luck! Oval face short haircuts with bangs are one of the most popular hairstyles for 2023. This trendy look is perfect for those who want to change up their style and show off their beautiful features. In this blog post, we will show you how to achieve this look, step by step, and provide you with tips and hair care advice to maintain your new hairstyle.

Description

Oval face shapes are characterized by a balanced proportion of features, with a slightly wider forehead and a gently tapering jawline. Short haircuts with bangs are a great way to accentuate these features and create a youthful, trendy look. Whether you prefer a sleek bob or a messy pixie cut, there is a style that will suit your face shape and personal style.

How to

If you are looking to achieve an oval face short haircut with bangs, here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Choose your haircut

The first step is to choose the right haircut for your face shape. A bob or a lob (long bob) is a popular option that will frame your face and show off your features. If you prefer a shorter cut, a pixie cut is a great option that will add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Choose your bangs

Once you have chosen your haircut, it's time to choose your bangs. Side-swept bangs are a popular option that will flatter your face shape and add texture to your hair. If you prefer a more dramatic look, blunt bangs are a great option that will make a statement.

Step 3: Get a professional haircut

It's important to get your haircut by a professional hairstylist to ensure that your new look is tailored to your face shape and personal style. Your hairstylist will be able to recommend the right haircut and bangs for your face shape, as well as provide you with tips on how to style and maintain your new hairstyle.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your oval face short haircut with bangs:

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair. - Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add texture and definition to your bangs. - Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair. - Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape. - Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about oval face short haircuts with bangs:

Can I wear a short haircut with bangs if I have a round face?

Short haircuts with bangs can be flattering for round face shapes, but it's important to choose the right haircut and bangs to suit your face shape. Consult with a professional hairstylist to find the right look for you.

How do I style my bangs?

Styling your bangs will depend on the type of bangs you have and your personal style. Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs, or use a flat iron to create a sleek look. Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add texture and definition to your bangs.

How often should I trim my bangs?

It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and prevent them from getting too long.

Hair Care

To keep your oval face short haircut with bangs looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated. - Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. - Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. - Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a trendy oval face short haircut with bangs and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny. So why not take the plunge and try out this popular hairstyle for 2023?
