How To Flip Out Ends Of Short Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline May 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you have short hair and want to add some fun and flirty texture to your locks? Flipping out the ends of your hair is an easy and effective way to do just that. With the right technique, you can create a cute and playful look that adds volume and movement to your hair. In this article, we'll show you how to flip out the ends of short hair in a few simple steps.

How To Flip Out Ends of Short Hair

Follow the steps below to achieve the perfect flipped-out ends on your short hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add body and texture to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to protect it from heat damage. Heat protectant will also help your hair hold its shape and prevent frizz.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair until it's completely dry, using a round brush to add volume to your roots.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, starting at the back of your head.

Step 5: Use a Straightener

Use a straightener to curl the ends of your hair outwards. Clamp the straightener on the ends of your hair, then twist the straightener away from your face. Hold for a few seconds, then release.

Step 6: Repeat

Repeat this process on all sections of your hair until all the ends are flipped out.

Step 7: Add Texture

Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add texture and hold to your hair.

Step 8: Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place all day.

Tips for Flipping Out Ends of Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect flipped-out ends on your short hair:

  • Start with clean, dry hair for best results.
  • Use a high-quality straightener with ceramic plates to minimize heat damage.
  • Section your hair into small sections for more control.
  • Use a light hand when applying heat to avoid burning your hair.
  • Experiment with different angles to find the perfect flip for your face shape.

FAQ About Flipping Out Ends of Short Hair

How long does it take to flip out ends of short hair?

It typically takes about 20-30 minutes to flip out the ends of short hair, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Can you flip out the ends of short hair without a straightener?

Yes, you can achieve a similar look by using a curling iron or hot rollers to curl the ends of your hair outwards.

Will flipping out the ends of my short hair make it look thinner?

No, flipping out the ends of your short hair can actually create the illusion of thicker, more voluminous hair.

Hair Care for Flipped-Out Ends

To keep your flipped-out ends looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your style.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect flipped-out ends on your short hair. Experiment with different angles and products to find the look that works best for you.
