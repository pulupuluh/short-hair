Short Layered Bob With Bangs Over 60 A Flattering Hairstyle For Seniors

Written by Aprilia Aug 18, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're a woman over 60 looking for a chic and stylish haircut that's easy to maintain and makes you look younger, a short layered bob with bangs might be the perfect choice for you. This versatile hairstyle can suit any face shape and hair texture, and it can be customized to your preferences and personality. In this article, we'll show you how to get the most out of your short layered bob with bangs over 60, from choosing the right length and layers to styling and caring for your hair.

Description

A short layered bob with bangs is a haircut that features layers of different lengths that create volume, movement, and texture. The layers can be blended or distinct, depending on the desired effect. The bangs can be blunt, wispy, or side-swept, depending on the face shape and hairline. The overall look is youthful, edgy, and low-maintenance, making it a popular choice among women over 60 who want to refresh their look without sacrificing comfort or elegance.

How to

Step 1: Consult with a hairstylist

Before you decide to get a short layered bob with bangs, it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist who can advise you on the best options for your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle. They can also show you pictures of different bob styles and help you choose the most flattering one for you.

Step 2: Choose the right length and layers

Once you've decided to get a short layered bob with bangs, you need to choose the right length and layers that suit your features and preferences. If you have a round face, you might want to go for a chin-length bob with longer layers that frame your face. If you have a square face, you might want to go for a longer bob with shorter layers that soften your jawline. If you have thin hair, you might want to go for a layered bob with choppy ends that add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you might want to go for a blunt bob with subtle layers that reduce bulk and weight.

Step 3: Style your hair

Styling your short layered bob with bangs is easy and fun. You can blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and curl the ends inward or outward for a retro look. You can use a straightening iron to flatten your hair and add shine for a sleek look. You can apply a styling gel or mousse to your damp hair and scrunch it with your fingers for a messy, beachy look. You can also accessorize your hair with a headband, a scarf, or a clip to add color and texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short layered bob with bangs over 60:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that don't strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it brittle.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your bob.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to detangle your hair without pulling or breaking it.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and pollution by wearing a hat or a scarf.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a short layered bob with bangs over 60 if I have gray hair?

A: Absolutely! Gray hair can look stunning and chic with a short layered bob with bangs. You can enhance the natural texture and shine of your gray hair by using a silver shampoo and conditioner that neutralize yellow tones and add brightness.

Q: Can I wear a short layered bob with bangs over 60 if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can! A short layered bob with bangs can add volume and texture to thin hair, especially if you choose a style with choppy layers and blunt ends. You can also use volumizing products and blow-dry your hair upside down to create lift and fullness.

Q: Can I wear a short layered bob with bangs over 60 if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can! A short layered bob with longer layers that frame your face can elongate your features and create a slimming effect. You can also choose side-swept bangs that draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Hair Care

To keep your short layered bob with bangs over 60 looking healthy and radiant, you need to take good care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Massage your scalp gently with your fingertips to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can dry out your scalp and hair.
  • Rinse your hair with cool water to seal the cuticles and add shine.
  • Apply a hair mask or a deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes that can damage your hair and scalp.

With these tips and tricks, you can enjoy your short layered bob with bangs over 60 and feel confident and stylish every day. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and colors, and embrace your natural beauty and wisdom.
