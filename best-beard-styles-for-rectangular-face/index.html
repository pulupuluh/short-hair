Best Beard Styles For Rectangular Face

Written by Christine Jun 05, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you looking for the best beard styles for a rectangular face? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll be discussing the top beard styles that are perfect for a rectangular face shape. We’ll cover everything from how to choose the right style to how to maintain your beard.

Description

A rectangular face shape is characterized by a long and angular jawline. This face shape is longer than it is wide, and the cheekbones may be less prominent. If you have a rectangular face shape, you want to choose a beard style that will help balance out your features. The right beard style can help add width to your face and create a more balanced look.

How to Choose the Right Style

When choosing a beard style for a rectangular face shape, you want to focus on creating width. This means choosing a style that adds volume to the sides of your face. Here are a few things to keep in mind when choosing a beard style:

  • Choose a style with full sides
  • Avoid styles that are too long or too short
  • Avoid styles that are too narrow or too pointy
  • Consider a style that has a slight curve or roundness to it

Now that you know what to look for, let’s take a look at the top beard styles for rectangular face shapes.

Top Beard Styles for Rectangular Face

The Full Beard

The full beard is a classic style that works well for rectangular face shapes. This style adds volume to the sides of your face and creates a balanced look. To maintain this style, you’ll need to trim your beard regularly and keep it well-groomed.

The Short Boxed Beard

The short boxed beard is another great option for rectangular face shapes. This style is shorter than the full beard but still adds volume to the sides of your face. It’s a low-maintenance style that’s easy to maintain with regular trimming.

The Van Dyke

The Van Dyke is a stylish beard style that works well for rectangular face shapes. This style features a goatee and a mustache, creating a more defined look. It’s important to keep this style well-groomed and trimmed to maintain its shape.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step by step guide to help you achieve the perfect beard style for a rectangular face:

  • Decide on the beard style that you want to achieve
  • Grow your beard out to the desired length
  • Trim your beard regularly to maintain its shape
  • Use beard oil to keep your beard soft and hydrated
  • Use a beard comb to keep your beard well-groomed

By following these steps, you’ll be able to achieve the perfect beard style for your rectangular face shape.

Tips for Maintaining Your Beard

Here are a few tips to help you maintain your beard:

  • Trim your beard regularly
  • Wash your beard with a beard shampoo
  • Use beard oil to keep your beard soft and hydrated
  • Use a beard comb to keep your beard well-groomed
  • Avoid over-trimming your beard

By following these tips, you’ll be able to maintain a healthy and well-groomed beard.

FAQ

Here are answers to some frequently asked questions about beard styles for rectangular face shapes:

What beard styles are best for a rectangular face?

The best beard styles for a rectangular face shape are those that add volume to the sides of your face. This includes styles like the full beard, the short boxed beard, and the Van Dyke.

How do I maintain my beard?

To maintain your beard, you should trim it regularly, wash it with a beard shampoo, use beard oil to keep it soft and hydrated, and use a beard comb to keep it well-groomed.

What should I avoid when choosing a beard style?

You should avoid beard styles that are too long or too short, too narrow or too pointy, or that don’t add volume to the sides of your face.

Hair Care

In addition to maintaining your beard, it’s important to take care of your hair as well. Here are a few tips to help you keep your hair looking healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and health

By following these tips, you’ll be able to maintain healthy hair and a well-groomed beard.

Now that you know the best beard styles for a rectangular face shape, you can choose the perfect style for you. Remember to maintain your beard regularly and take care of your hair to keep your look sharp and stylish.
