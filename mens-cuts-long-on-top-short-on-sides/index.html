Mens Cuts Long On Top Short On Sides How To Get The Perfect Look

Written by Daniel Feb 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, you can't go wrong with a mens cut long on top short on sides. This style has been popular for several years and is still going strong. Whether you're going for a classic or modern look, this hairstyle can suit any face shape and hair type. In this article, we'll show you how to get the perfect style, tips on hair care, and answer some FAQs to help you make the most of your new haircut.

Description

The mens cut long on top short on sides, also known as an undercut, is a hairstyle that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides and back. The hair on the sides and back is usually buzzed or faded, while the length on top can vary from a few inches to several inches. This style can be versatile, allowing you to style it in different ways and experiment with different hair products. It's also a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require too much upkeep or styling.

How To

Getting the perfect mens cut long on top short on sides starts with finding a good barber or stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get the perfect style:

Step 1: Consultation

Before getting the haircut, have a consultation with your barber or stylist to discuss the style you want, your hair type, and face shape. They can offer suggestions and advice on what will work best for you.

Step 2: Clipper Work

The barber will start by using clippers to buzz or fade the hair on the sides and back. The length will depend on the style you want and the shape of your head.

Step 3: Scissor Work

Next, the barber will use scissors to cut and shape the hair on top. They will cut the hair to the desired length and create layers to add texture and volume.

Step 4: Styling

Once the haircut is complete, you can style the hair on top using a hair product of your choice. You can go for a slicked-back look, a messy textured style, or a pompadour.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain and style your mens cut long on top short on sides:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Apply a styling product to damp hair for better hold and control.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and the style you want.
  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

FAQ

1. Will a mens cut long on top short on sides suit me?

Yes, this style can suit any face shape and hair type. However, it's important to have a consultation with your barber or stylist to determine the best length and shape for your hair.

2. How do I style my mens cut long on top short on sides?

You can style your hair using a hair product of your choice, such as pomade, wax, or gel. Apply the product to damp hair and use your fingers or a comb to create the desired style.

3. How long does a mens cut long on top short on sides last?

This style can last for several weeks to a few months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how often you get trims.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and appearance of your hair. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair that can damage the hair shaft and cause breakage.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can rock a mens cut long on top short on sides and look stylish and confident wherever you go.
