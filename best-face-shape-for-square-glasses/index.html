Best Face Shape For Square Glasses

Written by Luffy Apr 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're in the market for a new pair of glasses, you may be wondering what the best face shape is for square glasses. Luckily, there are several face shapes that work well with this style of glasses. In this article, we'll explore the best face shapes for square glasses and provide some tips for how to choose the right pair for your face.

Description

Square glasses are a popular style of eyewear that feature a square-shaped frame. They are often seen as a timeless and classic style, and can be worn by both men and women. Square glasses are a great option for those who want a bold and distinctive look, and they work well with a variety of face shapes.

How to Choose the Right Pair of Square Glasses

When choosing a pair of square glasses, there are several factors to consider. The first is your face shape. As mentioned earlier, there are several face shapes that work well with square glasses. Some of these include:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Diamond
  • Heart

If you have a square face shape, it's best to avoid square glasses, as they can make your face appear even more angular. Instead, opt for round or oval glasses, which will help to soften your features.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Pair of Square Glasses

Step 1: Determine your face shape

The first step in choosing the right pair of square glasses is to determine your face shape. You can do this by looking in the mirror and examining the shape of your face. If you're having trouble determining your face shape, there are several online tools you can use to help.

Step 2: Consider your personal style

When choosing a pair of square glasses, it's important to consider your personal style. Do you prefer a classic or modern look? Are you looking for a bold statement piece or something more subtle? These are all important factors to consider when choosing the right pair of square glasses.

Step 3: Try on several pairs

Once you've determined your face shape and personal style, it's time to start trying on several pairs of square glasses. This will give you an idea of what styles and shapes work best for your face.

Step 4: Consider the fit

When trying on glasses, it's important to consider the fit. The glasses should fit comfortably on your face, without sliding down your nose or feeling too tight. The frames should also sit evenly on your face, without any gaps or unevenness.

Tips for Choosing the Right Pair of Square Glasses

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right pair of square glasses:

  • Choose a frame color that complements your skin tone and hair color
  • Consider the size of the frame in relation to your face
  • Choose a frame material that is durable and long-lasting
  • Consider the shape of the glasses in relation to your eyebrows

FAQ About Best Face Shape for Square Glasses

Q: Can square glasses be worn by both men and women?

A: Yes, square glasses are a unisex style that can be worn by both men and women.

Q: Are square glasses suitable for all face shapes?

A: No, square glasses are best suited for round, oval, diamond, and heart-shaped faces.

Q: How do I determine my face shape?

A: You can determine your face shape by looking in the mirror and examining the shape of your face. There are also several online tools that can help you determine your face shape.

Hair Care and Square Glasses

When wearing square glasses, it's important to consider your hairstyle as well. A shorter hairstyle can help to balance out the boldness of the glasses, while longer hair can help to soften the look. It's also important to keep your hair clean and well-maintained, as this will help to highlight your glasses and overall style.

Conclusion

Square glasses are a classic and timeless style that can be worn by both men and women. When choosing a pair of square glasses, it's important to consider your face shape, personal style, and fit. By following these tips and guidelines, you can find the perfect pair of square glasses that will highlight your features and make you look and feel great.
