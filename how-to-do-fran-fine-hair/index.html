How To Do Fran Fine Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Feb 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you love the iconic look of Fran Fine from the hit TV show "The Nanny," you're not alone. Her bold fashion sense and big hair have become legendary. But if you're wondering how to achieve that voluminous, curly look on your own hair, we've got you covered. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to do Fran Fine hair in a few easy steps.

Description

Fran Fine hair is all about volume and curls. It's a bold look that requires a bit of work, but the results are worth it. The key to achieving this look is to start with clean, dry hair and use the right tools and products.

How to

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step to achieving Fran Fine hair is to start with clean, dry hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair. After washing, dry your hair with a blow dryer until it's completely dry.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will protect your hair from heat damage when you use hot tools. Then, apply a volumizing mousse to your roots and work it through your hair.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Using a 1-inch curling iron, start curling your hair in small sections. Wrap each section around the curling iron, holding it for about 10 seconds before releasing. Continue curling your hair until you've curled all of it.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Once your hair is curled, use a teasing comb to tease the roots of your hair. This will add even more volume to your hair. Be sure to tease gently to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you've teased your hair, use your fingers to style it into place. You can add hairspray to hold your hair in place and to add extra volume.

Tips

- Use a volumizing mousse to add body to your hair
- Use a 1-inch curling iron to create tight curls
- Tease gently to avoid damaging your hair
- Use hairspray to hold your hair in place

FAQ

Q: How long will Fran Fine hair last?

A: Fran Fine hair can last for several days, depending on how well you maintain it. Be sure to use dry shampoo to refresh your hair and hairspray to hold it in place.

Q: Can I achieve Fran Fine hair on short hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve Fran Fine hair on short hair. Use a smaller curling iron and tease your roots to add volume.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, be sure to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and be sure to use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools. Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and avoid split ends.
