How To Straighten Mid Length Hair A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of dealing with frizzy, unruly hair? Straightening your mid length hair is a great way to achieve a sleek and polished look. In this post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to straighten mid length hair.

Table of Contents

How to Straighten Mid Length Hair

Straightening mid length hair can be a daunting task, especially if you have never done it before. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a sleek and polished look.

The first step is to invest in a good quality hair straightener. Look for a straightener that has ceramic plates, as these are less damaging to your hair. You should also choose a straightener with adjustable heat settings, so you can choose the right temperature for your hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to straighten mid length hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before you start straightening your hair, apply a heat protectant spray or serum. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to straighten your hair and ensure that you don't miss any spots.

Step 4: Start Straightening

Start straightening your hair one section at a time. Use the straightener to clamp down on each section of hair and slowly glide it down to the ends. Repeat this process until you have straightened all of your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Serum

Finish off your look with a serum or oil to add shine and tame any flyaways.

Tips for Straightening Mid Length Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when straightening mid length hair:

  • Always use a heat protectant to prevent heat damage
  • Choose the right temperature for your hair type
  • Use a good quality straightener with ceramic plates
  • Section your hair to ensure you don't miss any spots
  • Finish off your look with a serum or oil to add shine and tame flyaways

FAQs

Can I straighten my hair without a straightener?

There are other methods to straighten hair, such as blow drying with a round brush. However, a straightener is the most effective way to achieve a sleek and polished look.

How often should I straighten my hair?

Straightening your hair too often can cause damage. It's best to limit straightening to once or twice a week.

How can I prevent heat damage?

Always use a heat protectant and choose the right temperature for your hair type. You should also avoid going over the same section of hair multiple times.

Hair Care for Straightened Mid Length Hair

After straightening your mid length hair, it's important to take care of it to prevent damage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hair ties that can cause breakage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Now that you know how to straighten mid length hair, you can achieve a sleek and polished look in no time. Remember to always use a heat protectant and choose the right temperature for your hair type to prevent damage.
