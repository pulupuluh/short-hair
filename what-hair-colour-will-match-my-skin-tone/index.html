What Hair Colour Will Match My Skin Tone

Table of Content:

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Choosing the right hair colour that matches your skin tone is essential to look your best. With so many hair colours available, it can be overwhelming to decide which one will suit you. This article will guide you on what hair colour will match your skin tone and how to take care of your hair.

How to

The first step in choosing the right hair colour is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone can be cool, warm or neutral. Cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones, warm skin tones have yellow or golden undertones, and neutral skin tones have a mix of warm and cool undertones.

Once you have determined your skin tone, you can choose the hair colour that will complement it. Cool skin tones look best with hair colours like platinum blonde, ash blonde, ash brown, and black. Warm skin tones look best with hair colours like golden blonde, caramel, honey, and chestnut. Neutral skin tones can pull off any hair colour.

Step by step

To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can't tell, you might have a neutral skin tone.

Next, consider your eye colour. Cool skin tones look best with blue, grey or green eyes. Warm skin tones look best with brown, hazel or amber eyes.

Finally, consider your natural hair colour. If you have cool undertones, your natural hair colour might be black or dark brown. If you have warm undertones, your natural hair colour might be light brown or blonde.

Tips

When choosing a hair colour, it's important to consider your lifestyle and personality. If you work in a conservative environment, you might want to stick to natural hair colours. If you're bold and adventurous, you might want to try a vibrant hair colour like pink or purple.

It's also important to consider your skin's undertones. If you have pink undertones, you might want to avoid hair colours with red undertones, as they can clash with your skin.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose a hair dye that is gentle on the skin. Look for hair dyes that are free of ammonia and peroxide.

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have grey hair?

A: Yes, but you might need to use a stronger hair dye or leave it on for longer to achieve the desired colour.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the colour and prevent damage. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically for coloured hair, and avoid washing your hair too often. Use a heat protectant when using heat styling tools, and avoid exposing your hair to too much sunlight.
