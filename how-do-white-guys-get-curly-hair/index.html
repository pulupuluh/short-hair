How Do White Guys Get Curly Hair

Written by Daniel Mar 07, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're a white guy with straight hair, you might be wondering how to get those luscious curls that seem to come so easily to other guys. Don't worry, you're not alone. With a little bit of effort and some styling techniques, you can achieve beautiful curly hair that will turn heads. In this article, we'll take a look at how to get curly hair for white guys.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get Curly Hair for White Guys

The first thing you need to do is figure out what type of curls you want. Do you want loose, beachy waves, or tight, defined curls? Once you have an idea of what you want, you can start working towards achieving it.

There are a few different methods you can use to get curly hair as a white guy:

  • Heat styling
  • Perm
  • Natural methods

The method you choose will depend on your hair type, desired curl pattern, and personal preference.

Heat Styling

One of the easiest ways to get curly hair is by using heat styling tools like a curling iron or wand. This method works best for guys with medium to long hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it's about 80% dry.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and use a curling iron or wand to curl each section.
  4. Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to separate the curls and add volume.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep the curls in place.

Perm

Another option for getting curly hair is to get a perm. A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the structure of your hair, making it curly. This method is best for guys with short hair. Here's what to expect:

  1. Choose a stylist who specializes in perms and book an appointment.
  2. During the appointment, your stylist will apply a chemical solution to your hair and wrap it in rods.
  3. After a certain amount of time, the rods will be removed and your hair will be rinsed.
  4. You'll need to avoid washing your hair for a few days after the perm to allow the curls to set.

Natural Methods

If you're not a fan of heat styling or chemical treatments, there are natural methods you can use to encourage your hair to curl. Here are a few ideas:

  • Twist your hair into sections while it's damp and let it air dry.
  • Apply a curl-enhancing product like mousse or gel to your hair and scrunch it upwards.
  • Sleep with your hair in braids or twists to create natural curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to get those curls? Here's a step-by-step guide to getting curly hair for white guys:

  1. Choose your desired curl pattern.
  2. Select a method for achieving your curls (heat styling, perm, or natural methods).
  3. Prepare your hair by washing it and applying any necessary products (heat protectant, curl enhancer, etc.).
  4. Follow the steps for your chosen method (heat styling, perm, or natural methods).
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray or other styling product.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Once you've achieved your curly hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can dry out your curls.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to keep your curls defined and moisturized.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can white guys get curly hair?

Yes, absolutely! While it may be more common for guys with certain hair types to have naturally curly hair, anyone can achieve curly hair with the right techniques and products.

2. Will getting a perm damage my hair?

A perm can be damaging to your hair if it's not done correctly or if you don't take care of your hair properly afterwards. However, if you choose a reputable stylist and follow their instructions for care, you should be able to avoid damage.

3. How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's generally recommended to wash curly hair no more than once or twice a week. Washing too often can strip your hair of its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

If you want to keep your curly hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or blow dryers too often, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as this can dry out your curls.
  • Protect your curls from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With a little bit of effort and care, you can achieve beautiful curly hair as a white guy. Whether you choose to use heat styling, get a perm, or go the natural route, the most important thing is to find a method that works for you and your hair type. So go ahead, embrace those curls!
