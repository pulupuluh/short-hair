How To Draw A Girl With Short Hair And Glasses

Written by Frank Jul 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling to draw a girl with short hair and glasses? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this step-by-step guide, we will teach you how to draw a girl with short hair and glasses in a relaxed, easy-to-understand language.

Description

Drawing a girl with short hair and glasses can seem intimidating, but it's actually quite simple. This tutorial will provide you with the necessary steps to create a beautiful and realistic drawing of a girl with short hair and glasses. You can follow these steps to draw a girl with short hair and glasses for your sketchbook, art project, or even as a gift for a friend.

How To

Before we begin, make sure you have the necessary materials. You will need a pencil, eraser, paper, and a reference image of a girl with short hair and glasses.

Step 1: Start by drawing the girl's head. Use a light pencil to create a circle for the head and a line down the middle to mark the center of the face.

Step 2: Next, draw the girl's hair. Use short, quick strokes to create the texture of short hair. Remember to keep the hair close to the head and not too voluminous.

Step 3: Now, draw the girl's glasses. Start by drawing the frame around the eyes, then add the arms that extend behind the ears.

Step 4: Draw the girl's eyes. Follow the shape of the glasses to draw the eyes, and add eyelashes and eyebrows for extra detail.

Step 5: Draw the girl's nose and mouth. Keep it simple and minimalist to complement the short hair and glasses.

Step 6: Finish the drawing by shading in the hair, adding highlights, and adjusting any details as needed.

Step-by-Step

Step 1: Draw a circle for the head and a line down the center to mark the center of the face.

Step 2: Draw the girl's hair using short, quick strokes.

Step 3: Draw the girl's glasses by starting with the frame around the eyes and adding the arms that extend behind the ears.

Step 4: Draw the girl's eyes, following the shape of the glasses. Add eyelashes and eyebrows for extra detail.

Step 5: Draw the girl's nose and mouth, keeping it simple and minimalist.

Step 6: Shade the hair, add highlights, and adjust any details as needed to finish the drawing.

Tips

Tip 1: Use a reference image to guide your drawing.

Tip 2: Keep the hair close to the head and not too voluminous.

Tip 3: Pay attention to the shape of the glasses and use it to guide the eyes.

Tip 4: Keep the nose and mouth simple and minimalist to complement the short hair and glasses.

FAQ

Q: Do I have to use a reference image to draw a girl with short hair and glasses?

A: It's not necessary, but it can be helpful to have a guide to follow.

Q: Can I use a different type of glasses?

A: Of course! You can use any type of glasses you'd like. Just make sure to adjust the shape of the eyes accordingly.

Hair Care

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but it's important to keep it healthy. Regular trims can prevent split ends and breakage, and using a high-quality shampoo and conditioner can keep the hair soft and shiny.

Now that you know how to draw a girl with short hair and glasses, it's time to put your skills to the test. Remember to take your time, use a reference image, and have fun with it. Happy drawing!
