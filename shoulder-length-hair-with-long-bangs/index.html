Shoulder Length Hair With Long Bangs A Trending Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Oliver Sep 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you are looking for a hairstyle that is both chic and versatile, then shoulder length hair with long bangs is an excellent choice. This hairstyle has been trending in 2023, and it is not difficult to see why. The shoulder length cut is low maintenance, and the long bangs can be styled in different ways to suit your preference. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this style, tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

Shoulder length hair with long bangs is a hairstyle that has hair cut to just below the shoulders and long bangs that fall onto the forehead. The bangs can be styled in different ways, such as side-swept or parted in the middle. This hairstyle is suitable for different face shapes, and it can be customized to suit your preference.

How to Achieve Shoulder Length Hair with Long Bangs

To achieve this hairstyle, you need to visit a stylist who is experienced in cutting shoulder length hair with long bangs. Your stylist will consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle to determine the best way to cut your hair. They will use a combination of scissors and razors to create layers and texture in your hair. If you have curly hair, your stylist may use a diffuser to enhance your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving shoulder length hair with long bangs:

  1. Wash your hair and dry it partially.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, bottom, and two sides.
  3. Starting from the back, cut your hair to shoulder length using scissors.
  4. Use a razor to create layers and texture in your hair.
  5. Trim your bangs to the desired length, keeping them longer than your eyebrows.
  6. Style your bangs to suit your preference, such as side-swept or parted in the middle.
  7. Apply hair products, such as mousse or hairspray, to hold the style.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair with Long Bangs

To keep your hair looking healthy and stylish, here are some tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner at least once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly, at least every six weeks, to prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools, such as a flat iron or curling iron.
  • Avoid using too many hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

1. Is shoulder length hair with long bangs suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, shoulder length hair with long bangs is suitable for different face shapes. Your stylist will consider your face shape and cut the hair accordingly to flatter your features.

2. Can I style my long bangs in different ways?

Yes, you can style your long bangs in different ways, such as side-swept, parted in the middle, or pinned back with bobby pins.

3. How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every two to three days, depending on your hair type and lifestyle. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, you need to take good care of it. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair color.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation and promote hair growth.

Shoulder length hair with long bangs is a trendy hairstyle that is easy to maintain and style. With the right cut and care, you can rock this look and make it your own.
