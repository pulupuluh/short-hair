The Best Hot Air Brush Styler For Short Hair Uk Your Ultimate Guide

Written by William Apr 27, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Do you have short hair and are looking for a hot air brush styler to achieve that salon-perfect look? Look no further, as we've rounded up the best hot air brush stylers for short hair in the UK.

Description

A hot air brush styler is a two-in-one hair styling tool that combines a hairbrush and a hair dryer. It is perfect for those who want to achieve a salon-perfect look without spending too much time and money in a hair salon. It can add volume, curl, and straighten your hair, depending on the brush attachment you use.

How to Choose the Best Hot Air Brush Styler for Short Hair UK

When it comes to choosing the best hot air brush styler for short hair in the UK, you need to consider the following factors:

Barrel Size

The barrel size of a hot air brush styler determines the size of the curls or waves it can create. For short hair, a barrel size of 1 inch or less is ideal.

Heat Settings

A hot air brush styler with multiple heat settings is ideal as it allows you to adjust the temperature according to your hair type and styling needs.

Brush Attachments

Choose a hot air brush styler that comes with different brush attachments, such as a round brush or a paddle brush, to achieve different styles and textures.

Cord Length

Make sure to choose a hot air brush styler with a cord length that is long enough to give you flexibility while styling your hair.

Top Hot Air Brush Stylers for Short Hair UK

1. BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium Hot Air Brush Styler

This hot air brush styler from BaBylissPRO features a 1-inch barrel size, multiple heat settings, and a detachable brush head for easy cleaning. It is also lightweight and easy to use, making it perfect for short hair.

2. Remington Volume and Curl Air Styler

The Remington Volume and Curl Air Styler features a 1-inch barrel size, two heat settings, and a cool shot function to set your style in place. It also comes with a 50mm thermal volume brush attachment for added volume and bounce.

3. Philips EssentialCare Hot Air Styler

The Philips EssentialCare Hot Air Styler features a 1-inch barrel size, two heat settings, and a 38mm ceramic thermal brush attachment for even heat distribution. It also has a cool setting to set your style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using a Hot Air Brush Styler for Short Hair UK

Follow these simple steps to achieve a salon-perfect look with a hot air brush styler for short hair in the UK:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and towel-drying your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a hot air brush styler.

Step 2: Apply heat protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from heat damage.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips or elastics. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that all sections are evenly styled.

Step 4: Choose your brush attachment

Choose a brush attachment that is suitable for your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

Step 5: Turn on the hot air brush styler

Turn on the hot air brush styler and select the desired heat setting.

Step 6: Start styling

Start styling your hair by wrapping a section of hair around the brush attachment and holding it in place for a few seconds. Then, release the brush and move on to the next section.

Step 7: Set your style with cool air

Once you have styled all sections, set your style in place by using the cool air setting on the hot air brush styler.

Tips for Using a Hot Air Brush Styler for Short Hair UK

Follow these tips to achieve the best results with your hot air brush styler for short hair in the UK:

Tip 1: Use a heat protectant

Always use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.

Tip 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections to ensure that all sections are evenly styled.

Tip 3: Use the cool air setting

Use the cool air setting to set your style in place and prevent frizz.

FAQ

1. Can I use a hot air brush styler on wet hair?

No, you should always towel-dry your hair before using a hot air brush styler to prevent heat damage.

2. How often should I clean my hot air brush styler?

You should clean your hot air brush styler after every use to prevent product buildup and ensure that it works properly.

3. Can I use a hot air brush styler on all hair types?

Yes, you can use a hot air brush styler on all hair types, but make sure to choose the right heat setting and brush attachment for your hair type.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Follow these hair care tips to keep your short hair healthy and shiny:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and damage to your hair.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.

3. Avoid over-styling

Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools to prevent damage and breakage.

4. Trim your hair regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and the best hot air brush styler for short hair in the UK, you can achieve a salon-perfect look in the comfort of your own home. Happy styling!
