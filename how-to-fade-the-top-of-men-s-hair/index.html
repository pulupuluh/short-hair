How To Fade The Top Of Men s Hair

Written by Christine Jun 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, then a faded hairstyle could be perfect for you. Fading the top of men's hair is a popular hairstyle that gives a clean and sharp look. In this article, we'll show you how to fade the top of men's hair in a step-by-step guide.

Description

Fading the top of men's hair means gradually transitioning the length of the hair from long to short from the top to the sides and back. This hairstyle creates a smooth and seamless look that looks great on many men.

How to Fade the Top of Men's Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to fade the top of men's hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

Before starting, choose the right haircut that will complement your face shape and hair type. Consult with your hairstylist and show them the style you want to achieve.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair thoroughly with shampoo and conditioner. This will help soften the hair and make it easier to cut.

Step 3: Cut the Top Hair

Using scissors or clippers, cut the top hair to the desired length. You can leave it longer or shorter, depending on your preference.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Back

Use a clipper to blend the sides and back with the top hair. Start with a higher clipper guard and gradually decrease the guard size as you move down to the sides and back.

Step 5: Fade the Top Hair

Using a clipper, create a fade by gradually decreasing the guard size as you move up from the sides and back to the top hair. Make sure to blend the fade smoothly for a seamless look.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline and Sideburns

Use a clipper or razor to clean up the neckline and sideburns for a polished look.

Step 7: Style the Hair

Style the hair using a comb, pomade, or wax. You can slick it back, create a messy look, or style it according to your preference.

Tips

- Choose a skilled hairstylist who has experience in fading hair.

- Use a mirror to check the progress of the cut.

- Don't rush the process. Take your time to achieve a seamless fade.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get a fade haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. Generally, it's recommended to get a fade haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I fade my hair at home?

A: Fading hair requires skill and experience. It's recommended to get it done by a professional hairstylist. However, if you have the skills and confidence, you can try fading your hair at home.

Hair Care

To maintain a faded hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and avoid using too many hair products that can damage the hair. Trim the hair regularly to maintain the style and avoid split ends.

Fading the top of men's hair is a stylish and trendy hairstyle that can elevate your look. Follow these steps and tips to achieve a seamless and polished fade. Remember to take care of your hair properly to maintain the style.
