How To Make Short Hair Look Good On Men

Written by Daniel Jun 21, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Short hair is a popular hairstyle for men around the world. It is easy to maintain, versatile, and can be styled in various ways. However, some men struggle with making their short hair look good. If you are one of them, don't worry! In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to make short hair look good on men.

Description

In this article, we will discuss how to make short hair look good on men. We will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and answers to frequently asked questions. We will also cover hair care for short hair.

How to Make Short Hair Look Good on Men

Here are some tips on how to make short hair look good on men:

1. Choose the Right Haircut

The first step to making your short hair look good is choosing the right haircut. Not all haircuts are suitable for every face shape and hair type. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape and hair type. A good haircut can enhance your facial features and make your short hair look good.

2. Maintain Your Hair

Maintaining your short hair is essential to make it look good. Regularly visit your hairstylist to trim your hair and keep it in shape. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy. Avoid using too much hair product as it can make your hair look greasy and flat.

3. Experiment with Styling

Short hair is versatile and can be styled in various ways. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the one that suits you the best. Use a hairdryer to add volume to your hair, use a hair gel to create a sleek look, or use a hair wax to add texture.

4. Pay Attention to Facial Hair

Facial hair can complement your short hair and make it look good. Trim your beard or mustache regularly to keep it in shape. Choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these step-by-step instructions to make your short hair look good:

1. Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Massage your scalp gently to remove dirt and oil from your hair.

2. Towel Dry Your Hair

Towel dry your hair gently to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously as it can cause damage and frizz.

3. Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Use a hair gel, hair wax, or hair pomade depending on the style you want to achieve.

4. Style Your Hair

Style your hair using a hairdryer, comb, or your fingers. Experiment with different styling techniques until you find the one that suits you the best.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your short hair look good:

1. Don't Overuse Hair Product

Using too much hair product can make your hair look greasy and flat. Use a small amount of hair product and add more if needed.

2. Use the Right Hairbrush

Use a hairbrush that suits your hair type and style. A round brush is perfect for creating volume, while a paddle brush is ideal for smoothing your hair.

3. Consider Your Face Shape

Consider your face shape when choosing a haircut and styling your hair. A haircut that suits your face shape can enhance your features and make your short hair look good.

4. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can keep your short hair in shape and make it look good. Visit your hairstylist every four to six weeks for a trim.

FAQ

Q: Can I style my short hair without using hair product?

A: Yes, you can. However, using hair product can help you achieve the style you want and make your hair look good.

Q: How do I know which haircut suits me?

A: Consult with your hairstylist to find the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Q: Can I dye my short hair?

A: Yes, you can. However, it is recommended to consult with a professional hairstylist to avoid damage to your hair.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Here are some tips for hair care for short hair:

1. Use a Good Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and scalp condition. Avoid using too much shampoo as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Avoid Using Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.

4. Avoid Using Too Much Hair Product

Avoid using too much hair product as it can make your hair look greasy and flat. Use a small amount of hair product and add more if needed.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your short hair look good on men. Remember to choose the right haircut, maintain your hair, experiment with styling, and pay attention to facial hair. With a little bit of effort, you can achieve a stylish and attractive look.
