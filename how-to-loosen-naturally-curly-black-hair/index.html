How To Loosen Naturally Curly Black Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jun 09, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your naturally curly black hair being too tight and difficult to manage? Do you want to loosen your curls without permanently damaging your hair? You’ve come to the right place! In this article, we’ll provide you with some tips and tricks on how to loosen naturally curly black hair, so keep on reading!

Table of Contents

  • How to Loosen Naturally Curly Black Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care

How to Loosen Naturally Curly Black Hair

There are several ways to loosen naturally curly black hair:

  1. Blow Drying: Using a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment can help loosen your curls by stretching them out.
  2. Braids: Braiding your hair while it’s wet and leaving it overnight can help stretch out your curls.
  3. Twist Outs: Twist your hair into sections and leave them in overnight. This will help loosen your curls and create a wavy texture.
  4. Hot Oil Treatment: Hot oil treatments can help moisturize your hair and loosen your curls.
  5. Low Manipulation: Avoid styling your hair too often and let it air dry.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to loosen naturally curly black hair:

  1. Start with clean and detangled hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair before blow drying.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and dry your hair on a low heat setting.
  4. For braids, divide your hair into sections and braid each section while it’s wet. Leave the braids in overnight and undo them in the morning.
  5. For twist outs, divide your hair into sections and twist each section while it’s wet. Leave the twists in overnight and undo them in the morning.
  6. For hot oil treatments, heat up your preferred oil and apply it to your hair. Cover your hair with a plastic cap and leave it on for 30 minutes to an hour. Rinse out the oil with warm water.
  7. Avoid styling your hair too often and let it air dry.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks on how to loosen naturally curly black hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before styling.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause heat damage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, as they can permanently damage your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to loosen naturally curly black hair:

  • Will these methods permanently loosen my curls? No, these methods will only temporarily loosen your curls.
  • Can I use these methods if I have relaxed hair? Yes, you can use these methods if you have relaxed hair, but be cautious not to damage your hair further.
  • How often should I do hot oil treatments? It’s recommended to do hot oil treatments once a week or every two weeks.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips on how to take care of your naturally curly black hair:

  • Wash your hair once or twice a week with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how to loosen naturally curly black hair, try out these methods and see what works best for you. Remember to take good care of your hair and embrace your natural curls!
