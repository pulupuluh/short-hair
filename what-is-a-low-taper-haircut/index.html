What Is A Low Taper Haircut

Written by Luffy Feb 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that is both stylish and low maintenance? If so, a low taper haircut may be just what you need! In this blog post, we will explore what a low taper haircut is, how to get one, and some tips for maintaining it.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to Get a Low Taper Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining a Low Taper Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Low Taper Haircut

Description

A low taper haircut is a style that involves short hair on the sides and back, gradually tapering to longer hair on top. The taper is achieved by gradually reducing the length of the hair towards the neckline. This style is versatile, low maintenance, and can be customized to suit individual hair types and face shapes. It is a popular choice for men of all ages and is also suitable for women.

How to Get a Low Taper Haircut

The best way to get a low taper haircut is to visit a professional barber or stylist who has experience with this style. They can help you determine the best taper length and style for your hair type and face shape. However, if you prefer to do it yourself, you can follow the step-by-step guide below.

Step-by-Step Guide:

1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.

2. Use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair to the desired length. The length will depend on how short you want your hair to be. It is recommended to start with a longer guard and gradually decrease the length until you achieve the desired taper.

3. Use scissors to cut the hair on top of your head to the desired length. This length should be longer than the sides and back to create the taper effect.

4. Blend the hair at the transition point from the sides to the top using clippers or scissors. This will create a smooth transition from short to long hair.

5. Clean up the neckline and sideburns using clippers or a razor.

6. Style your hair using a product of your choice.

Tips for Maintaining a Low Taper Haircut

1. Schedule regular haircuts to maintain the taper and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

2. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

3. Use a styling product that works well with your hair type and desired style.

4. Avoid over-styling or using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.

5. If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to maintain your natural curls while still achieving the taper effect.

FAQs

Q: How long does a low taper haircut last?

A: A low taper haircut can last anywhere from 2-4 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Q: Is a low taper haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, a low taper haircut can be customized to suit all hair types and face shapes.

Q: How much does a low taper haircut cost?

A: The cost of a low taper haircut will vary depending on the location and the experience of the barber or stylist. Expect to pay anywhere from $20-$50.

Hair Care for Low Taper Haircut

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a low taper haircut. Here are some tips:

1. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.

3. Use a heat protectant spray if you must use hot tools.

4. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished.

5. Avoid using too much product or over-styling your hair, as this can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a low taper haircut with confidence and style!
