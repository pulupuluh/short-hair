How To Get Rid Of Frizzy Hair On Top

Written by Eveline Jun 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have frizzy hair, you know how frustrating it can be. It can be difficult to style and can make you feel self-conscious. Fortunately, there are steps you can take to get rid of frizzy hair on top. In this article, we will explore some effective tips and techniques to help you achieve smooth, silky hair.

  • How to Get Rid of Frizzy Hair on Top
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get Rid of Frizzy Hair on Top

Frizzy hair occurs when the hair cuticle is raised, causing it to become dry and unruly. There are several ways to get rid of frizzy hair on top:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

One of the most effective ways to combat frizzy hair is to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that contain natural oils, such as argan oil, coconut oil, or jojoba oil, which can help to hydrate and nourish your hair.

2. Apply a Hair Mask

A hair mask is a deep conditioning treatment that can help to restore moisture and shine to your hair. Apply a hair mask once a week to help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

3. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

A leave-in conditioner can help to smooth and tame frizzy hair. Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing and conditioning, focusing on the ends and the top of your hair.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, can cause your hair to become dry and frizzy. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting low.

5. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to reduce frizz and prevent breakage. Silk is gentle on your hair and helps to prevent tangles and knots.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you get rid of frizzy hair on top:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly and gently towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Hair Mask

Apply a hair mask to your hair, focusing on the top section. Leave the mask on for the recommended time and rinse thoroughly.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the top section. Comb through your hair to distribute the product evenly.

Step 4: Dry Your Hair

Gently pat your hair dry with a towel and avoid rubbing or twisting your hair. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush specifically designed for detangling to style your hair. Avoid using heat styling tools, or use them sparingly and with a heat protectant spray.

Tips for Managing Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips for managing frizzy hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause breakage and increase frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or a t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Try using a natural bristle brush, such as a boar bristle brush, to help distribute the natural oils in your hair.

FAQs

1. How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash your hair once a week. Experiment to find what works best for your hair.

2. Can I use a hair mask every day?

No, it is not necessary to use a hair mask every day. Overuse of hair masks can cause your hair to become greasy and weighed down.

3. What is the best way to style frizzy hair?

The best way to style frizzy hair is to embrace your natural texture. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls or waves, or apply a small amount of styling cream or gel to define your curls.

Hair Care for Frizzy Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for frizzy hair:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can get rid of frizzy hair on top and achieve smooth, silky hair. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and don't be afraid to experiment to find what works best for your hair.
