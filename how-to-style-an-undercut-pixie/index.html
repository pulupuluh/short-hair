How To Style An Undercut Pixie A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance, edgy hairstyle that's perfect for the summer, an undercut pixie might be just what you need. This short and sassy haircut is easy to style and looks great on almost everyone. In this post, we'll show you how to style an undercut pixie in just a few simple steps.

Table of Contents:

  • Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair
  • Step 2: Apply Styling Product
  • Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair
  • Step 4: Style Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Undercut Pixie
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Your Undercut Pixie

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step to styling your undercut pixie is to wash and condition your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type. If you have oily hair, look for a clarifying shampoo that will remove excess oil and build-up. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to hydrate and nourish your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Once your hair is clean and towel-dried, apply a styling product to help control your hair and add texture. A texturizing spray or mousse is a great option for an undercut pixie. Apply the product to your hair, focusing on the roots and the ends.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Next, use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Use a round brush to lift and add volume to your hair as you dry it. Focus on the top of your head and the front of your hair, as these are the areas that will be most visible. Use your fingers to style your hair as you dry it, directing it in the direction you want it to go.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, it's time to style it. Depending on the look you're going for, you can use a flat iron to straighten your hair, or a curling iron to add curls or waves. Use a small amount of styling wax or pomade to define your hair and add texture. Use your fingers to create a messy, edgy look or a comb to create a sleeker, more polished look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Undercut Pixie

Here are a few tips for keeping your undercut pixie looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to help control oil and add volume
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type

FAQs

Can anyone wear an undercut pixie?

Yes, an undercut pixie can work for almost any hair type and face shape. Talk to your stylist about the best way to customize the look to suit your features.

How often do I need to get a trim?

You should plan to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Can I still wear my hair up with an undercut pixie?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with an undercut pixie. Use bobby pins or small hair elastics to secure your hair in place.

Hair Care for Your Undercut Pixie

To keep your undercut pixie looking great, it's important to take good care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do. Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy, and experiment with different styling products to find what works best for you.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your undercut pixie like a pro in no time. Whether you're going for a messy, edgy look or a sleek and polished style, this versatile haircut is sure to turn heads.
