Best Hair Color Brand For Over 50 A Guide To Choosing The Right One

Written by Frank Feb 26, 2023 Â· 10 min read

As we age, our hair tends to lose its natural color and become gray or white. While some people embrace their natural hair color, others prefer to dye their hair to maintain a youthful appearance. If you're over 50 and looking to dye your hair, it's important to choose the right hair color brand that suits your needs. In this article, we'll guide you through the best hair color brands for over 50, how to choose the right one, and how to take care of your dyed hair.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color Brand

Choosing the right hair color brand can be overwhelming, especially if you're not familiar with the different types of hair dyes available. Here are some factors to consider when choosing the best hair color brand for over 50:

1. Type of Hair Dye

There are several types of hair dyes available, including permanent, semi-permanent, and demi-permanent. Permanent hair dye lasts the longest but can damage your hair if not applied correctly. Semi-permanent hair dye lasts up to six weeks and is less damaging to your hair. Demi-permanent hair dye is a mix between permanent and semi-permanent and lasts up to 24 washes. Consider your hair type and the level of maintenance you're willing to commit to when choosing the type of hair dye.

2. Hair Color

Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and enhances your natural features. If you're not sure which hair color to choose, consult a hairstylist or use online hair color simulation tools to see how different hair colors would look on you.

3. Safety

Choose a hair color brand that is safe for your hair and scalp. Look for hair dyes that are free of harmful chemicals such as ammonia, PPD, and resorcinol. Read the ingredients carefully and do a patch test before applying the hair dye to your entire head.

Best Hair Color Brands for Over 50

Here are some of the best hair color brands for over 50:

1. Clairol Nice'n Easy

Clairol Nice'n Easy is a popular hair color brand that offers a variety of shades for every skin tone. The hair dye is easy to apply and lasts up to eight weeks. The formula is gentle on your hair and scalp and is free of ammonia.

2. L'OrÃ©al Excellence Age Perfect

L'OrÃ©al Excellence Age Perfect is specifically designed for mature hair and covers gray hair while restoring volume and shine. The hair dye is formulated with Pro-Keratin complex and collagen that strengthen and protect your hair.

3. Garnier Olia

Garnier Olia is an oil-based hair dye that provides vibrant and long-lasting color without damaging your hair. The formula is free of ammonia and contains natural flower oils that nourish and hydrate your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Dyeing Your Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to dyeing your hair:

1. Prepare Your Hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and ensure that the hair dye adheres well to your hair. Dry your hair and brush it thoroughly.

2. Mix the Hair Dye

Follow the instructions on the hair dye package to mix the hair dye. Wear gloves to protect your hands from staining.

3. Apply the Hair Dye

Apply the hair dye to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a comb to distribute the hair dye evenly and avoid any clumps.

4. Wait

Wait for the recommended time according to the hair dye instructions. Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water until the water runs clear.

5. Condition

Apply the conditioner that comes with the hair dye package to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes. Rinse your hair with cool water to seal the color and prevent fading.

Tips for Maintaining Your Dyed Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your dyed hair:

1. Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair color and prevent fading. Avoid using hot water and harsh chemicals that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair serum with UV protection to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays that can fade your hair color.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons that can damage your hair and fade your hair color. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and set the temperature to low.

FAQs

1. Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, you can dye your hair if you have sensitive skin, but it's important to choose a hair dye that is free of harmful chemicals and do a patch test before applying the hair dye to your entire head.

2. How often should I dye my hair?

It's recommended to dye your hair every four to six weeks to maintain the color and cover gray hair.

3. Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but it's important to follow the instructions carefully and choose a hair dye brand that is safe for your hair and scalp.

Hair Care for Over 50

As we age, our hair tends to become thinner, drier, and more brittle. Here are some hair care tips for over 50:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that contains natural oils such as argan oil and coconut oil to hydrate and nourish your hair.

2. Avoid Overwashing

Avoid overwashing your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and breakage. Wash your hair every two to three days and use dry shampoo in between washes.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to maintain the health and shape of your hair. A shorter haircut can make your hair look fuller and thicker.

Choosing the best hair color brand for over 50 can be challenging, but with the right information, you can make an informed decision that suits your needs. Remember to choose a hair dye that is safe for your hair and scalp, and take care of your dyed hair to maintain its color and health.
