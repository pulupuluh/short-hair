Short Haircuts For Fat Faces And Curly Hair Tips And Ideas

Are you tired of dealing with your curly hair and round face? Finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge, especially when you're dealing with two different hair types. However, with the right short haircut, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident. In this article, we'll provide you with tips, step-by-step instructions, and ideas for short haircuts that will flatter your fat face and curly hair.

Introduction

Short haircuts are ideal for those with a round face and curly hair. A short haircut can help to elongate your face and show off your curls. However, finding the right short haircut for your face shape and hair type can be a challenge. In this article, we'll provide you with tips and ideas for short haircuts that will flatter your face and enhance your curls.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a short haircut, it's important to determine your face shape. There are four main face shapes: round, oval, square, and heart. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Use a measuring tape or ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.

If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width and your face is shorter in length, you have a round face shape. If your face is longer than it is wide and your forehead is wider than your jawline, you have an oval face shape. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width and your face is longer in length, you have a square face shape. If your forehead is wider than your cheekbones and jawline and your face is longer in length, you have a heart-shaped face.

Short Haircuts for Fat Faces and Curly Hair

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the right short haircut. Here are some short haircuts that are ideal for those with a round face and curly hair:

Short Bob

The short bob is a classic haircut that's perfect for those with a round face and curly hair. This haircut typically falls just above the chin and frames the face, elongating it. To create a short bob with curly hair, ask your stylist to cut your hair into a bob shape and add layers to enhance your curls.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short and edgy haircut that's perfect for those with a round face and curly hair. This haircut typically features short layers and creates a lot of volume. To create a pixie cut with curly hair, ask your stylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back and leave some length on top to create volume.

Asymmetrical Bob

The asymmetrical bob is a modern and stylish haircut that's perfect for those with a round face and curly hair. This haircut typically features a longer length on one side and a shorter length on the other. To create an asymmetrical bob with curly hair, ask your stylist to cut your hair into a bob shape and leave some length on one side to create an asymmetrical look.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts with Curly Hair

Short haircuts with curly hair require some maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut:

Use the Right Products

Curly hair requires special products to keep it looking healthy and defined. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's specifically designed for curly hair. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair while it's damp to enhance your curls.

Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy. It's best to wash your hair every three to four days to keep it looking healthy.

Get Regular Trims

Short haircuts require regular trims to keep them looking their best. Schedule a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain your haircut.

FAQs

Q: Can I get a short haircut if I have a fat face and curly hair?

A: Yes, short haircuts are ideal for those with a round face and curly hair. A short haircut can help to elongate your face and show off your curls.

Q: What is the best short haircut for a round face and curly hair?

A: The best short haircuts for a round face and curly hair include the short bob, pixie cut, and asymmetrical bob.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and defined. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

Avoid Brushing Your Hair

Brushing your curly hair can cause it to become frizzy and lose its shape. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it's wet.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage your curly hair, causing it to become dry and brittle. It's best to air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls.

Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and maintain your curls. Silk is gentle on your hair and won't cause friction while you sleep.

In conclusion, short haircuts are ideal for those with a round face and curly hair. Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural curls. Use the right hair care products and techniques to maintain your short haircut and keep your curly hair looking healthy and defined.
