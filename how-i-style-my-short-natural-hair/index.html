How I Style My Short Natural Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Frank Jun 18, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Welcome to my guide on how I style my short natural hair! As someone who has been rocking a short cut for years, I've tried a variety of styles and techniques. In this article, I'll share my go-to methods for achieving a variety of looks, from a defined twist out to a sleek bun.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How-To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

If you're anything like me, you love the versatility of short natural hair. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve a variety of styles, from a fluffy 'fro to defined curls. In this article, I'll share my favorite styling methods that have worked for me over the years.

How-To

Before we dive into the specific styles, let's talk about some general tips for styling short natural hair:

  • Start with freshly washed and conditioned hair
  • Use a leave-in conditioner for added moisture
  • Section your hair before styling to ensure even distribution of products
  • Use a styling product that works for your hair type (e.g. gel, cream, mousse)
  • Don't be afraid to experiment!

Step-by-Step

Defined Twist Out

1. Start with freshly washed and conditioned hair.

2. Section your hair into small sections.

3. Apply a twisting cream or gel to each section.

4. Twist each section of hair, making sure to twist tightly for a defined look.

5. Let your hair air dry or sit under a hooded dryer.

6. Once your hair is completely dry, gently unravel each twist.

7. Use an oil or butter to separate the twists for added volume.

Sleek Bun

1. Brush your hair back into a low ponytail.

2. Use a gel to smooth down any flyaways.

3. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure with bobby pins.

4. Use a scarf or headwrap to lay down any remaining flyaways.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling short natural hair:

  • Use a satin scarf or pillowcase to protect your hair at night
  • Don't over-manipulate your hair to prevent damage
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for you
  • Invest in a good leave-in conditioner for added moisture

FAQ

Q: Can I straighten my short natural hair?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant and don't use heat too frequently to prevent damage.

Q: How often should I wash my short natural hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but generally once a week is a good starting point.

Hair Care

In addition to styling, taking care of your hair is crucial for maintaining healthy locks. Here are some tips for keeping your short natural hair in tip-top shape:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Deep condition regularly for added moisture
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent breakage

And there you have it - my guide on how I style my short natural hair! Remember, everyone's hair is different, so don't be afraid to experiment and find what works best for you.
