Good Hairstyles For Long Oval Faces

Written by Aprilia Aug 05, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your long oval face? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing some of the best hairstyles for long oval faces that will help accentuate your features and make you feel confident and beautiful.

Description

Long oval faces are characterized by a longer length than width, with a slightly wider forehead and narrower chin. The key to finding a good hairstyle for this face shape is to balance out the proportions and create the illusion of width.

How to

There are several hairstyles that work well with long oval faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for long oval faces as they create movement and texture, while also adding volume to the sides of the face. This helps to balance out the proportions and create a more rounded appearance.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for long oval faces as they help to break up the length of the face and create the illusion of width. They also draw attention to the eyes, which is a great feature to accentuate.

3. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a classic style that works well with almost any face shape, including long oval faces. Opt for a chin-length bob with layers to add volume and movement to the hair.

4. Pixie Cut

If you're feeling daring, a pixie cut is a great option for long oval faces. This short and edgy style helps to balance out the proportions of the face and draw attention to your best features.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for achieving these hairstyles:

Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Separate your hair into sections.
  3. Cut your hair in long layers, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  4. Use a styling product to add texture and movement to your hair.

Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the front portion of your hair.
  3. Cut your bangs at an angle, starting from the middle of your forehead and working your way out.
  4. Style your bangs to the side using a styling product.

Bob Haircut

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the bottom portion of your hair.
  3. Cut your hair in a straight line at chin-length.
  4. Add layers to your hair for volume and movement.

Pixie Cut

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the top portion of your hair.
  3. Cut your hair in short, choppy layers.
  4. Add texture and volume to your hair using a styling product.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your hair with a long oval face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as these can make your face appear even longer.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the one that suits your face shape best.
  • Add volume to the sides of your face using styling products or by blow-drying your hair in a certain direction.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for long oval faces:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long oval face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short, as these can make your face appear even longer. You should also avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as these can accentuate the length of your face.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a long oval face?

Absolutely! Just make sure to add volume to the sides of your face using styling products or by blow-drying your hair in a certain direction. You can also experiment with different ponytail styles, such as a side ponytail or a high ponytail.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair in order to maintain healthy, shiny locks. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.

With these tips and hairstyles, you're sure to find the perfect look for your long oval face. So go ahead and experiment with different styles, and don't be afraid to try something new!
