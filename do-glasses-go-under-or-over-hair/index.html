Do Glasses Go Under Or Over Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Feb 19, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of constantly adjusting your glasses when they slip down your nose? Are you unsure of whether to wear your glasses under or over your hair? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to wear your glasses comfortably and confidently.

Description

Wearing glasses is not just a necessity, but also a fashion statement. However, the dilemma of whether to wear glasses under or over your hair can be confusing. This article will provide you with a comprehensive guide on the best way to wear your glasses with different hair types and styles.

How to Wear Glasses with Different Hair Types

Straight Hair

If you have straight hair, the best way to wear your glasses is over your hair. This will prevent your hair from getting tangled and keep your glasses in place. To avoid any discomfort, make sure your glasses fit well and do not press against your scalp.

Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, wearing your glasses under your hair can help to reduce friction and prevent your hair from getting tangled. However, make sure your glasses are not pressing against your ears or scalp, as this can cause discomfort.

Braids or Twists

If you have braids or twists, wearing your glasses over your hair can be the best option. This will prevent your hair from getting caught in your glasses and keep your hair out of your face. However, make sure your glasses are not too tight, as this can cause headaches.

Ponytail or Updo

If you have a ponytail or updo, wearing your glasses under your hair can be a good option. This will prevent your glasses from slipping down your nose and keep your hair in place. Make sure your glasses are not pressing against your scalp or ears, as this can cause discomfort.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Adjust Your Glasses

Before wearing your glasses, adjust them to fit comfortably on your face. Make sure the nose pads are not too tight or too loose and the arms of the glasses are not too tight behind your ears.

Step 2: Style Your Hair

Style your hair according to your preference. Whether you have straight hair, curly hair, braids, or a ponytail, make sure your hair is not covering your glasses.

Step 3: Wear Your Glasses

Now it's time to wear your glasses. If you have straight hair or a ponytail, wear your glasses over your hair. If you have curly hair or braids, wear your glasses under your hair.

Step 4: Adjust Your Glasses

Once you have worn your glasses, adjust them to make sure they are not slipping down your nose or pressing against your scalp or ears.

Tips for Wearing Glasses

Here are some tips to help you wear your glasses comfortably:

  • Choose glasses that fit well and are comfortable to wear.
  • Clean your glasses regularly to avoid any discomfort from dirt or smudges.
  • When wearing your glasses over your hair, make sure your hair is not too voluminous or thick, as this can cause discomfort.
  • If your glasses are slipping down your nose, adjust the nose pads or ask your optician to adjust them for you.

FAQs

Should I wear my glasses over or under my hair?

The answer depends on your hair type and style. If you have straight hair or a ponytail, wear your glasses over your hair. If you have curly hair or braids, wear your glasses under your hair.

Why do my glasses keep slipping down my nose?

Your glasses may be too loose or the nose pads may not be adjusted correctly. Adjust the nose pads or ask your optician to adjust them for you.

How do I prevent my glasses from getting tangled in my hair?

Wearing your glasses over your hair can help prevent them from getting tangled. Make sure your hair is not too voluminous or thick, as this can cause discomfort.

Hair Care

Wearing glasses can cause your hair to get tangled or flattened. To avoid this, make sure your hair is well-conditioned and moisturized. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair soft and smooth. Avoid using hair products that can cause buildup or damage to your hair.

In conclusion, wearing glasses can be comfortable and stylish if you wear them correctly. Whether you wear your glasses over or under your hair, make sure they fit well and are comfortable to wear. Follow these tips and tricks to wear your glasses confidently and with ease.
