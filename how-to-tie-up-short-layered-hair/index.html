How To Tie Up Short Layered Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi May 08, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you struggling to keep your short layered hair in check? Do you find it difficult to tie them up in a ponytail or a bun? Fret not, for we have got you covered. In this article, we will guide you on how to tie up short layered hair in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tie Up Short Layered Hair
  • Tips for Tying Up Short Layered Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Layered Hair

How to Tie Up Short Layered Hair

Follow these simple steps to tie up your short layered hair:

Step 1: Brush your hair

Before you tie your hair up, make sure to brush them thoroughly. This will remove any tangles and knots, making it easier to tie them up.

Step 2: Decide on the hairstyle

Decide on the hairstyle you want to create. You can either tie your hair up in a ponytail or a bun.

Step 3: Separate your hair

Separate your hair according to the hairstyle you want to create. For a ponytail, gather all your hair at the back of your head. For a bun, gather your hair at the crown of your head.

Step 4: Tie your hair up

Tie your hair up with a hair tie. Make sure to secure it tightly so that it doesn't come loose.

Step 5: Adjust the hair

Adjust the hair around the hair tie to create the desired look. For a ponytail, you can leave some hair loose at the front to frame your face. For a bun, you can pull out a few strands to create a messy look.

Tips for Tying Up Short Layered Hair

Here are some tips that will help you tie up your short layered hair with ease:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color. This will make it less noticeable.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.
  • Add some volume to your hair before tying it up by using a volumizing spray or mousse.
  • Use a hairband to tie up your hair in a high ponytail. This will make your hair look longer and fuller.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits your face shape.

FAQs

Q. Can I tie up my short layered hair in a messy bun?

A. Yes, you can. A messy bun is a great way to tie up your short layered hair, especially if you want to create a casual look.

Q. Can I tie up my short layered hair in a half-up, half-down hairstyle?

A. Yes, you can. A half-up, half-down hairstyle is perfect for short layered hair as it allows you to show off your layers while keeping your hair out of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Short Layered Hair

Here are some hair care tips that will help you maintain your short layered hair:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet as it can cause breakage.

Now that you know how to tie up your short layered hair, go ahead and experiment with different hairstyles. With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to create the perfect look in no time.
