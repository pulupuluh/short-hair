How To Style Men s Thick Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 09, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're one of those men blessed with thick and wavy hair, congratulations! Your hair type is highly coveted and can be styled in a variety of ways. However, styling thick and wavy hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure where to start. In this article, we'll walk you through how to style men's thick wavy hair in a relaxed and easy-to-follow manner.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Men's Thick Wavy Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Men with Thick Wavy Hair

Description

Thick and wavy hair is a hair type that is characterized by having a lot of volume, texture, and movement. It can be challenging to style, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a variety of looks that suit your personal style.

How to Style Men's Thick Wavy Hair

Before we dive into the styling techniques, it's essential to start with a good foundation. Make sure you're using high-quality hair products that are specifically designed for thick and wavy hair. Here are some basic steps to get you started:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type. This will help to hydrate and nourish your hair, making it easier to style.

  2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet.

  3. Apply a styling product that suits your desired look. We'll go into more detail on this in the next section.

  4. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment on a low heat setting. This will help to enhance your natural waves without creating frizz.

  5. Finish off with a hairspray or styling cream to hold your style in place.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you have the basics down, let's dive into some specific styling techniques for men's thick wavy hair.

1. The Messy Side Part

This is a classic and effortless style that is perfect for a casual day out. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Start by parting your hair to one side.

  2. Apply a small amount of styling cream to your hair, focusing on the ends to create texture.

  3. Use your fingers to tousle your hair until you achieve the desired messy look.

  4. Finish off with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

2. The Classic Pompadour

The pompadour is a timeless hairstyle that is both stylish and sophisticated. Here's how to achieve it:

  1. Start by applying a volumizing mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots.

  2. Blow dry your hair upwards, using a round brush to create volume at the crown.

  3. Apply a small amount of pomade to your hair, focusing on the top and sides.

  4. Use a comb to slick back the sides and create height at the top.

  5. Finish off with a strong hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your thick wavy hair:

  • If you have particularly stubborn hair, try blow drying it with a hair dryer on a high heat setting. This will help to set your style in place.

  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired look.

  • Don't be afraid to play around with your hair and try new styles. After all, hair grows back!

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your hair using a flat iron or straightening brush. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and only straighten your hair occasionally, as frequent use of heat styling tools can damage your hair.

Q: Can I dye my thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair, but it's important to use a high-quality hair dye and follow the instructions carefully. Additionally, be sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your hair color.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Thick Wavy Hair

Finally, here are some hair care tips to keep your thick wavy hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause frizz.

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause damage and breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to style your thick wavy hair with ease and confidence. Remember to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and always prioritize the health of your hair by following good hair care practices.
