How To Make Chubby Cheeks Look Smaller With Makeup

Written by Christine Feb 16, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Do you have chubby cheeks that you want to make look smaller? You're not alone! Many people struggle with this issue. Fortunately, there are some makeup techniques you can use to sculpt your face and create the illusion of slimmer cheeks. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to make chubby cheeks look smaller with makeup.

Description

Chubby cheeks are a common concern for people who want to achieve a more sculpted look. While surgery is an option, it's not always necessary. With the right makeup techniques, you can create the illusion of slimmer cheeks. This technique is called contouring, and it involves using makeup to create shadows and highlights in strategic areas of your face.

How to Make Chubby Cheeks Look Smaller with Makeup

Step 1: Apply Foundation

The first step in any makeup routine is to apply foundation. Choose a shade that matches your skin tone and apply it evenly all over your face. Make sure to blend it in well so that there are no visible lines or streaks.

Step 2: Apply Contour

The next step is to apply contour to your cheeks. Choose a shade that is two or three shades darker than your skin tone. Use a contour brush to apply the product in the hollows of your cheeks. Blend it in well so that there are no harsh lines.

Step 3: Apply Highlight

After you've applied contour, it's time to apply highlight. Choose a shade that is two or three shades lighter than your skin tone. Use a highlight brush to apply the product to the tops of your cheeks, just above where you applied the contour. This will create the illusion of higher cheekbones.

Step 4: Blend

After you've applied both contour and highlight, it's important to blend them together so that there are no harsh lines. Use a blending brush to blend the products together until they look seamless.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your chubby cheeks look smaller with makeup:

  • Use a matte bronzer to contour your cheeks. Shimmery bronzers can actually make your face look rounder.
  • Don't apply too much product. A little goes a long way when it comes to contouring.
  • Make sure to blend well. Blending is key to achieving a natural-looking contour.
  • Use a setting spray to help your makeup stay in place all day.

FAQ

What if I don't have a contour brush?

If you don't have a contour brush, you can use a small, fluffy brush instead. Just make sure to blend well so that there are no harsh lines.

What if I have oily skin?

If you have oily skin, make sure to use a matte foundation and avoid using too much highlight. You can also set your makeup with a mattifying powder to help control oil throughout the day.

Can I use cream contour instead of powder?

Yes, you can use cream contour instead of powder. Just make sure to blend it in well so that it looks natural.

Hair Care

In addition to makeup, your hairstyle can also make a difference in how your face looks. Consider getting a haircut with layers around your face to create the illusion of a slimmer face. You can also try parting your hair in a different place to create a more flattering look.

By following these makeup techniques and hair care tips, you can make your chubby cheeks look smaller and achieve a more sculpted look. Remember, the key is to blend well and use products that complement your skin tone and type.
