How To Style Short Pixie Hair A Step By Step Guide

Do you have short pixie hair and struggle to style it? Look no further than YouTube for inspiration and guidance. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to style short pixie hair and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

Description

A pixie haircut is a short hairstyle that is typically cut above the ears and around the nape of the neck. It's a low maintenance haircut that's perfect for those who want to save time getting ready in the morning. However, styling short pixie hair can be a bit tricky if you're not used to it. That's where YouTube comes in handy. There are plenty of tutorials available that can help you style your short pixie hair in a variety of ways.

How to Style Short Pixie Hair

Before you start styling your short pixie hair, it's important to have the right tools on hand. You'll need a good quality hairbrush, a comb, and some styling products such as hairspray, mousse, or gel. Once you have your tools ready, you can start styling your hair in various ways.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style short pixie hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a comb to part your hair in the way that you prefer.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as hairspray, mousse, or gel.
  4. Use your fingers or a hairbrush to style your hair in the desired shape.
  5. If you want to add volume to your hair, use a hairdryer and a round brush to lift the hair at the roots.
  6. Once your hair is styled the way you want it, add a final spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Short Pixie Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short pixie hair:

  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • If you want to add texture to your hair, try using a salt spray.
  • Don't be afraid to use a little bit of heat to style your hair. A hairdryer and a round brush can work wonders!
  • If you're struggling to style your hair, try watching some YouTube tutorials for inspiration.
  • Remember that short hair is meant to be low maintenance, so don't stress too much about getting it perfect.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can you style short pixie hair without using heat?

A: Yes, you can style short pixie hair without using heat. Try using a texturizing spray or a volumizing mousse to add shape and volume to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my short pixie hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with short hair can go a few days without washing, while others prefer to wash their hair every day. Experiment with different washing schedules to find the one that works best for your hair.

Q: How can I make my short pixie hair grow faster?

A: Unfortunately, there's no magic trick to make short hair grow faster. However, you can promote healthy hair growth by eating a balanced diet, getting enough sleep, and taking care of your hair with regular trims and conditioning treatments.

Hair Care Tips for Short Pixie Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when you have short pixie hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • If you do use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray first.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your short pixie hair with ease. Remember, practice makes perfect, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques. Happy styling!
