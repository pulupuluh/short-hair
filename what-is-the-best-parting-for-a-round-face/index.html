What Is The Best Parting For A Round Face

Written by Aprilia Mar 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect parting for your round face? Well, you're not alone. A lot of people with round faces can find it difficult to choose a hairstyle that flatters their facial shape. The good news is that there are plenty of options available to help you find the perfect parting for your face. In this article, we'll explore the best parting for a round face, how to achieve it, and some tips to keep your hair looking its best.

Table of Contents:

  2. How to Find the Best Parting for a Round Face
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Best Parting for a Round Face
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Parting
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Description

Before we dive into the best parting for a round face, let's take a moment to describe what a round face is. A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The goal of choosing a parting for a round face is to create the illusion of length and balance out the face. This means that you want to avoid partings that accentuate the roundness of your face and instead opt for styles that create a more angular look.

How to Find the Best Parting for a Round Face

The first step in finding the best parting for your round face is to determine your face shape. There are a few different methods for doing this, but one of the easiest is to take a photo of yourself straight on and trace the outline of your face. Once you've determined that you have a round face, you can start to explore different parting options.

One of the best partings for a round face is a deep side part. This creates a diagonal line that helps to elongate the face and draw attention away from the roundness of the cheeks. Another good option is a center part with some volume at the roots. This helps to create the illusion of height and length, which can balance out the width of the face. Avoid partings that are too straight or in the middle of the forehead, as these can make the face look wider.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Best Parting for a Round Face

Now that you know what parting to choose, let's walk through how to achieve it.

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using a tail comb, create your desired parting.
  3. If you're doing a deep side part, use some styling product to hold the hair in place.
  4. If you're doing a center part, use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume to the roots.
  5. Finish with some hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Parting

Once you've achieved the perfect parting, you want to make sure it stays that way. Here are some tips to help:

  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your roots looking fresh between washes.
  • If you have fine hair, try using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some body.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can flatten your parting.
  • If you're using heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a round face?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear bangs with a round face. Opt for bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle, as this can help to create the illusion of length.

Q: Should I avoid wearing my hair up?

A: Not necessarily. Wearing your hair up can actually help to elongate your face, as long as you avoid styles that are too tight or slicked back. Try a messy bun or a loose ponytail instead.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Now that you know the best parting for a round face, let's talk about some general hair care tips that can help keep your hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to avoid breakage and damage.

By following these tips and choosing the right parting for your round face, you can create a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
