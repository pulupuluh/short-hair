Shaved Sides Long Top Haircut Female A Bold And Stylish Choice In 2023

Written by Oliver Sep 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you looking for a daring hairstyle that will make you stand out in the crowd? Look no further than the shaved sides long top haircut for females. This trendy and edgy haircut has become increasingly popular in recent years, and it's easy to see why. It combines the best of both worlds: the boldness of a shaved head with the femininity of long hair on top. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about this hairstyle, from how to get it to how to care for it.

What is a Shaved Sides Long Top Haircut Female?

As the name suggests, a shaved sides long top haircut female involves shaving the sides of your head while leaving the hair on top long. The length of the hair on top can vary from a few inches to shoulder-length, depending on your preference. This haircut is often paired with an undercut, where the hair is shaved even shorter on the sides and back, creating a dramatic contrast with the longer hair on top.

How to Get a Shaved Sides Long Top Haircut Female

If you're interested in getting this haircut, the first step is to find a skilled hairstylist who has experience with this style. It's important to communicate your desired length and style with your stylist to ensure you get the exact look you want. Once you're in the chair, your hairstylist will likely start by shaving the sides of your head, using clippers or a razor to create a clean, even cut. They will then trim and style the hair on top to your desired length and shape. The entire process can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the complexity of the cut.

Step By Step Guide to Shaved Sides Long Top Haircut Female

If you're feeling brave and want to try giving yourself a shaved sides long top haircut female at home, follow these steps:

  1. Start by sectioning off the hair on top of your head using clips or hair ties
  2. Use clippers or a razor to shave the sides of your head, starting at the bottom and working your way up
  3. Be sure to use a mirror to check your progress and ensure that the sides are even
  4. Once you've finished shaving the sides, unclip the hair on top and trim it to your desired length and shape
  5. You can use a straightening iron or curling iron to style the hair on top, depending on your preference

Tips for Maintaining a Shaved Sides Long Top Haircut Female

Like any haircut, a shaved sides long top haircut female requires some maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep the sides trimmed and the hair on top in shape
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Consider using a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to help control and shape the hair on top
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you plan to use hot tools on your hair
  • Protect your scalp from the sun by wearing a hat or using a sunscreen spray

FAQs About Shaved Sides Long Top Haircut Female

1. Will a shaved sides long top haircut female suit me?

This haircut can suit a wide range of face shapes and hair types, but it's important to consult with a hairstylist to determine if it's the right choice for you.

2. How long does a shaved sides long top haircut female last?

The length of time between haircuts will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the cut. Most people need to visit the hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to keep the sides trimmed and the hair on top in shape.

3. Can I still wear my hair up with a shaved sides long top haircut female?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up in a ponytail or bun. Just keep in mind that the sides of your head will be shaved, so the hairstyle may look different than it would with longer hair on the sides.

Hair Care for Shaved Sides Long Top Haircut Female

Caring for a shaved sides long top haircut female is relatively easy. Be sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a styling product if desired. You may also want to consider using a scalp treatment or oil to keep your scalp moisturized and healthy.

Overall, a shaved sides long top haircut female is a bold and stylish choice that can help you stand out from the crowd. If you're considering this hairstyle, be sure to consult with a skilled hairstylist to get the exact look you want, and follow our tips for maintaining and caring for your hair.
