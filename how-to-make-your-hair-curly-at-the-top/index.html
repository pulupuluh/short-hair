How To Make Your Hair Curly At The Top

Written by William May 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

In this article, we will discuss how to make your hair curly at the top. Having curly hair is a great way to switch up your look and add some volume to your hair. However, getting the curls to stay at the top can be challenging. We will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect curly hairstyle.

Description

Curly hair looks gorgeous, and if you have straight hair, you may want to switch it up and create curls. Creating curls at the top of your hair can be challenging, but it's not impossible. In this article, we will guide you on how to make your hair curly at the top.

How to Make Your Hair Curly at the Top

Here are the steps you should follow to make your hair curly at the top:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to creating curly hair is to wash your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. After washing your hair, use a towel to dry it and remove any excess water.

Step 2: Apply Hair Product

Apply a hair product that will help hold the curls in place. You can use mousse, gel, or cream. Apply the product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections. The smaller the section, the tighter the curl. Use clips to hold each section in place.

Step 4: Use a Curling Iron

Use a curling iron to create curls at the top of your hair. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold it in place for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process until you have curled all the sections of your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish the look by applying hairspray. Hairspray will help hold the curls in place and prevent them from falling out.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your hair curly at the top:

  • Use a curling iron with a small barrel for tighter curls.
  • Apply hairspray before and after curling your hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil to your hair to add shine and prevent frizz.

FAQ

Q: How long will the curls last?

The duration of the curls depends on the hair product you use and the method you use to create the curls. If you use a strong hold hairspray, the curls can last for up to 24 hours.

Q: Can I create curls without a curling iron?

Yes, you can create curls without a curling iron. You can use braids, twist outs, or perm rods to create curls.

Hair Care

It's essential to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.

Creating curly hair at the top can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect look. Follow the steps outlined in this article, and you'll be sure to turn heads with your stunning curly hairstyle.
