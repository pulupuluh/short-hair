Best Hairstyles For Guys With Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Christine Mar 05, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of trying to tame your thick, wavy hair? Look no further! In this article, we'll go over the best hairstyles for guys with thick wavy hair. Whether you prefer short or long hair, there's a hairstyle out there for you. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Guys with thick wavy hair have a lot of options when it comes to hairstyles. The key is finding a style that works with your natural texture, rather than against it. Some popular hairstyles for guys with thick wavy hair include the messy textured look, the slicked back style, and the classic undercut. These styles can be adapted to suit your personal taste and lifestyle, whether you're looking for a low-maintenance cut or something more polished.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your thick wavy hair is to find a skilled hairstylist who understands your hair type. They can help you choose a cut that will complement your face shape and work with your natural texture. From there, you can experiment with different styling products and techniques to achieve your desired look.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, damp hair.

2. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to your hair.

3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.

4. Allow your hair to air dry, or use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to speed up the process.

5. Once your hair is dry, you can add additional product or touch up any areas that need it.

Tips

- Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair.

- Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice on styling your hair.

- Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

- Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it frizzy.

FAQ

Q: What is the best haircut for guys with thick wavy hair?

A: The best haircut for guys with thick wavy hair is one that works with your natural texture. Some popular options include the messy textured look, the slicked back style, and the classic undercut.

Q: How do I style my thick wavy hair?

A: Start with clean, damp hair and apply a small amount of styling product. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape, then allow it to air dry or use a blow dryer on a low heat setting.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for guys with thick wavy hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

- Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and nourished.

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.

- Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.

- Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

And there you have it - the best hairstyles for guys with thick wavy hair! With the right cut and styling products, you can rock any of these looks with confidence. Happy styling!
