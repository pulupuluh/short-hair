How To Use A Big Claw Clip A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Apr 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

A big claw clip is the perfect hair accessory for those days when you want to keep your hair out of your face but still look stylish. With its easy-to-use design, it's a versatile accessory that can be used for a variety of hair types and styles. In this article, we will give you a step-by-step guide on how to use a big claw clip and some tips to make the most of this accessory.

How to Use a Big Claw Clip

Using a big claw clip is a simple process that can be done in a matter of seconds. Follow these steps to achieve a quick and easy hairstyle:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before using a big claw clip, make sure that your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can cause the clip to slip out of place or become loose, so it's best to style your hair when it's completely dry.

Step 2: Brush your hair

Use a brush or comb to smooth out any tangles or knots in your hair. This will make it easier to style and ensure that the clip stays in place.

Step 3: Gather your hair

Gather your hair into a ponytail or bun at the back of your head. Make sure that the hair is smooth and free of any bumps or lumps.

Step 4: Position the clip

Open the claw of the clip and position it over the ponytail or bun. The clip should be placed at the base of the ponytail or bun, close to your scalp.

Step 5: Secure the clip

Close the claw of the clip by pressing down on the top. Make sure that the clip is secure and that your hair is held firmly in place.

Step 6: Adjust the clip

If necessary, adjust the position of the clip to create the desired hairstyle. You can move the clip up or down to create more volume or pull hair out to create a messy bun look.

Tips for Using a Big Claw Clip

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your big claw clip:

  • Use a larger clip for thicker hair
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a half-up, half-down look or a messy bun
  • Add accessories, such as a headband or scarf, to create a unique look
  • Use hairspray or styling products to keep your hair in place

FAQ

Q: Can a big claw clip be used on short hair?

A: Yes, a big claw clip can be used on short hair. Simply gather the hair at the back of your head and secure it with the clip.

Q: How long can I wear a big claw clip?

A: A big claw clip can be worn for several hours, depending on the thickness and length of your hair. If you have thick or heavy hair, you may need to readjust the clip every few hours.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair when using a big claw clip. Make sure to avoid using the clip too often or too tightly, as this can cause breakage or damage to your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a variety of hairstyles using a big claw clip. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, this accessory is a great way to keep your hair out of your face and add some style to your outfit.
