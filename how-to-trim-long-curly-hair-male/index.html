How To Trim Long Curly Hair Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline May 28, 2023 · 9 min read

Trimming long curly hair can be a daunting task, especially for men who are new to the process. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can keep your locks looking healthy and well-maintained. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps of how to trim long curly hair male, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  2. Preparation
  3. Sectioning Your Hair
  4. Cutting Your Hair

  8. FAQs
  9. Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

Tools You’ll Need

Before you get started, you’ll need a few tools to make the process easier:

  • Haircutting scissors
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Sectioning clips
  • Mirror
  • Spray bottle (optional)

Preparation

Before you begin trimming your hair, it’s important to prepare your locks for the process. Here’s what you should do:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Detangle your hair using a wide-toothed comb or your fingers.
  3. Blow-dry your hair until it’s about 80% dry.

By following these steps, you’ll make it easier to trim your hair and ensure that you don’t accidentally cut too much off.

Sectioning Your Hair

Once your hair is prepared, it’s time to start sectioning it. This will make it easier to trim your hair evenly and ensure that you don’t miss any sections. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start by dividing your hair down the center, from your forehead to the nape of your neck.
  2. Clip one side up and out of the way.
  3. Divide the other side of your hair into smaller sections, using sectioning clips to hold each section in place.
  4. Continue until you have small, manageable sections that you can easily work with.
  5. Repeat the process on the other side of your head.

By sectioning your hair, you’ll be able to work on one area at a time, which will help you achieve a more even cut.

Cutting Your Hair

Now that your hair is sectioned, it’s time to start cutting it. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start with the bottom section of your hair.
  2. Use your fingers or a comb to hold the section of hair you want to cut.
  3. Trim the ends of your hair, cutting off any split ends or damaged hair.
  4. Continue working your way up your head, section by section, until you’ve trimmed all of your hair.

Remember to take your time and only cut a little bit of hair at a time. You can always go back and trim more if necessary, but it’s much harder to fix a mistake if you’ve cut too much off.

Blending Your Hair

Once you’ve trimmed all of your hair, it’s time to blend the different sections together. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Take a comb and comb through your hair, blending the different sections together.
  2. Use your fingers to gently pull on your hair, blending any uneven sections together.
  3. Check your hair in the mirror to make sure that it looks even and well-blended.

Blending your hair will help ensure that you don’t have any uneven sections or choppy layers.

Cleaning Up

Once you’ve finished trimming and blending your hair, it’s time to clean up any stray hairs. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Use clippers or scissors to trim any stray hairs around your ears and neckline.
  2. Use a comb to brush away any loose hairs.
  3. Shampoo and condition your hair to remove any loose hairs and keep your locks looking healthy.

By cleaning up your hair, you’ll ensure that your haircut looks neat and tidy.

Tips for Trimming Long Curly Hair

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best results when trimming long curly hair:

  • Always cut your hair when it’s dry, as wet hair can look longer than it actually is.
  • Don’t cut too much off at once. It’s better to trim a little bit at a time until you achieve your desired length.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Work in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for help if you’re not sure what you’re doing.

FAQs

How often should I trim my long curly hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I trim my own long curly hair?

Yes, you can trim your own hair, but it’s important to use the right tools and techniques to achieve the best results.

What’s the best way to trim curly hair?

The best way to trim curly hair is to work in small sections, cutting a little bit at a time until you achieve your desired length.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

Now that you’ve trimmed your long curly hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and well-maintained. Here are a few hair care tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.

By following these hair care tips, you’ll be able to keep your long curly hair looking great until it’s time for your next trim.
