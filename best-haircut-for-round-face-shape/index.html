Best Haircut For Round Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your round face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircut options for those with a round face shape. We will also provide tips on how to care for your hair to maintain your new look. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To Find the Best Haircut for Round Face Shape
  3. Step By Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Tips for Round Face Shape

Description

A round face shape is characterized by full cheeks and a rounded jawline. The key to finding the perfect haircut is to create the illusion of length and angles. The right haircut can enhance your features, make your face appear slimmer, and draw attention to your best features.

How To Find the Best Haircut for Round Face Shape

The best way to find the perfect haircut for your round face shape is to consider your hair texture, face shape, and personal style. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose haircuts that add height and volume to the crown of your head.
  • Opt for haircuts that have layers that fall below the chin to create the illusion of length.
  • Avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin or cheekbones, as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Choose hairstyles with side-swept bangs to create angles and draw attention to your eyes.

Step By Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you find the best haircut for your round face shape:

  1. Identify your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Research haircuts that are best suited for your face shape.
  3. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you.
  4. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your appointment.
  5. Ask your stylist for layers that fall below the chin and create angles.
  6. Consider adding side-swept bangs to your hairstyle.
  7. Ask your stylist to add volume to the crown of your head.
  8. Request a style that can be easily maintained at home.
  9. Ask your stylist for recommendations on hair care products to maintain your new look.
  10. Book your next appointment to maintain your new haircut.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your new haircut:

  • Use products that are suited for your hair type.
  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Invest in a quality hairbrush to maintain your hairstyle.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What are the best haircuts for a round face shape?

The best haircuts for a round face shape are those that add height and volume to the crown of your head, have layers that fall below the chin to create the illusion of length, and have side-swept bangs to create angles.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face shape?

Avoid haircuts that are too short or blunt cuts that end at the chin or cheekbones, as they can make your face appear wider and rounder.

How often should I get my hair trimmed?

It is recommended to get your hair trimmed every six to eight weeks to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Round Face Shape

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind if you have a round face shape:

  • Avoid using heavy styling products that weigh down your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage from styling tools.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as it can accentuate the roundness of your face.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut for your round face shape and maintain it with ease. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
