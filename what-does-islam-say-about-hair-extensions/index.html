What Does Islam Say About Hair Extensions

Written by Daniel Sep 07, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you a Muslim woman who wants to know if it is permissible to wear hair extensions? If so, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss in detail what Islam says about hair extensions.

Table of Contents

  • Introduction
  • Hair Extensions in Islam
  • How to Wear Hair Extensions Islamically

Introduction

Hair extensions have become increasingly popular in recent years. Many women use them to add length, volume, or color to their hair. However, for Muslim women, there is a concern about whether wearing hair extensions is permissible in Islam.

Hair Extensions in Islam

Islam encourages modesty in dress and appearance. Muslim women are required to cover their hair in public and dress modestly. While hair extensions are not explicitly forbidden in Islam, they must be worn in a way that is modest and does not attract attention to oneself.

In addition, Muslim women should be careful not to imitate the hairstyles of non-Muslim women. The Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) said, "Whoever imitates a people is one of them." Therefore, if a hairstyle is associated with a non-Muslim culture or religion, it should be avoided.

How to Wear Hair Extensions Islamically

To wear hair extensions Islamically, it is important to choose extensions that are made from natural hair. Synthetic hair extensions should be avoided if possible, as they do not look as natural and can be easily identified as fake.

The color of the extensions should also match the natural hair color as closely as possible. This will ensure that the extensions blend in with the natural hair and do not look fake or unnatural.

Step by Step Guide to Wearing Hair Extensions Islamically

Here is a step by step guide to wearing hair extensions Islamically:

  1. Choose extensions made from natural hair.
  2. Choose extensions that match the natural hair color.
  3. Ensure that the extensions are long enough to cover the entire head.
  4. Part the natural hair in the middle and clip it up.
  5. Place the extensions over the natural hair, making sure that they are centered.
  6. Secure the extensions to the natural hair using clips or bobby pins.
  7. Release the natural hair and blend it with the extensions using a brush or comb.
  8. Style the hair as desired.

Tips for Wearing Hair Extensions Islamically

Here are some tips for wearing hair extensions Islamically:

  • Choose extensions that are not too long or too thick.
  • Wear a hijab or headscarf over the extensions to ensure modesty.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are associated with non-Muslim cultures or religions.
  • Be mindful of the attention that wearing hair extensions can attract.

FAQ

Q. Are hair extensions haram in Islam?

A. Hair extensions are not explicitly forbidden in Islam, but they must be worn in a way that is modest and does not attract attention to oneself.

Q. Can I wear synthetic hair extensions?

A. While synthetic hair extensions are not explicitly forbidden in Islam, it is recommended to wear extensions made from natural hair, as they look more natural and blend in better with the natural hair.

Q. Can I dye my hair extensions?

A. Yes, you can dye your hair extensions as long as the dye is halal and does not contain any haram ingredients.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair extensions to ensure that they last as long as possible. Here are some tips for caring for your hair extensions:

  • Wash your hair extensions regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair extensions.
  • Brush your hair extensions gently to avoid pulling or tugging on the hair.
  • Store your hair extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use.

In conclusion, wearing hair extensions is not haram in Islam, but they must be worn in a way that is modest and does not attract attention to oneself. Muslim women should choose extensions made from natural hair and avoid imitating the hairstyles of non-Muslim women. By following these guidelines, Muslim women can wear hair extensions Islamically and with confidence.
