Men s Medium Length Hairstyles Short Sides The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a versatile hairstyle that can be adapted to any occasion, then men's medium length hairstyles with short sides are the perfect choice. This style is popular among men of all ages, and it's easy to see why. The short sides give the hairstyle a clean and tidy look, while the medium length on top allows for plenty of styling options.

Description

The men's medium length hairstyle with short sides is a classic look that has been popular for decades. The hairstyle is characterized by short hair on the sides and back of the head, usually cut with clippers, and medium-length hair on the top, which can be styled in various ways. The length on top can range from 2-6 inches, depending on personal preference.

How to

To achieve the men's medium length hairstyle with short sides, you will need to visit your barber or hairdresser. Ask for a tapered cut, where the hair gradually gets shorter towards the neck and sides. The length on top should be left longer, so you can style it as desired.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Visit your barber or hairdresser and ask for a tapered cut with short sides and medium-length on top.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and personal style.
  3. Use a comb and some styling product to create your desired look.
  4. Visit your barber or hairdresser regularly for maintenance and touch-ups.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your men's medium length hairstyle with short sides:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that suits your hair type.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that suits you.
  • Visit your barber or hairdresser regularly for maintenance and touch-ups.
  • Consider adding a beard or mustache to complete the look.

FAQ

What face shape suits the men's medium length hairstyle with short sides?

The men's medium length hairstyle with short sides suits most face shapes, especially oval and square-shaped faces. It can also be adapted to suit round and heart-shaped faces.

Can I style my men's medium length hairstyle with short sides for formal occasions?

Yes, you can style your men's medium length hairstyle with short sides for formal occasions. Simply use a comb and some styling product to create a neat and tidy look.

Do I need to use a specific styling product for my men's medium length hairstyle with short sides?

No, you can use any styling product that suits your hair type and desired look. However, it's important to use a high-quality product to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your men's medium length hairstyle with short sides looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers and straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

By following these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the men's medium length hairstyle with short sides with confidence and style. Whether you're headed to the office or a night out on the town, this versatile hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
