Can I Use Clippers Without Guard

Written by William Mar 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

The use of clippers without guard is a common question among people who love to groom their hair at home. Clippers are a great tool to cut hair quickly and efficiently, but without a guard, they can be dangerous. In this article, we will discuss whether you can use clippers without a guard, how to use them, the steps to follow, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care.

Description

Clippers without a guard are used to cut hair very short, almost to the scalp. They are used mainly for buzz cuts, military-style haircuts, or for people who prefer a shorter hairstyle. Clippers without a guard are also used to clean up the neck and around the ears. When using clippers without a guard, it's important to be careful, as it's easy to nick the skin or cut too much hair.

How To Use Clippers Without Guard

Using clippers without a guard requires a steady hand and a lot of patience. Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can clog the clippers and make them less effective. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose The Right Clippers

Choose a clipper that is sharp and has a powerful motor. Clippers with a weak motor will not be able to cut through thick hair. Make sure the blade is oiled and clean before you start.

Step 2: Choose The Right Blade

Choose the right blade for your hair type. If you have coarse hair, use a blade that is designed for coarse hair. If you have fine hair, use a blade that is designed for fine hair. Always make sure the blade is sharp.

Step 3: Hold The Clippers Correctly

Hold the clippers correctly, with the blade facing downwards. Use your dominant hand to hold the clippers and your other hand to guide the clippers over your head. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, using short strokes.

Step 4: Go Slowly

Go slowly and take your time. If you rush, you may end up cutting too much hair or nicking your skin. Make sure you are in a well-lit area, so you can see what you're doing.

Step 5: Clean Up

After you're done, clean up any loose hair around your head and neck. Use a brush or a towel to remove any hair that may have fallen onto your clothes. Clean the clippers and the blade before storing them away.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using clippers without a guard:

  • Always start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter length.
  • Go slowly and be patient.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress.
  • Make sure the blade is sharp and oiled.
  • Use a comb to help guide the clippers over your head.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using clippers without a guard:

Can I use clippers without a guard for a fade?

Yes, you can use clippers without a guard for a fade, but it requires a lot of skill and practice. It's best to start with a longer guard and work your way down slowly.

Can I use clippers without a guard on my beard?

No, it's not recommended to use clippers without a guard on your beard. Use a guard to keep your beard at the desired length.

How often should I clean my clippers?

You should clean your clippers after every use. Use a brush to remove any hair and oil the blade regularly to keep it sharp.

Hair Care

After using clippers without a guard, it's important to take care of your hair and scalp. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type. If you have a sensitive scalp, use a gentle shampoo. Avoid using hot water, as it can dry out your scalp. Use a moisturizer to keep your scalp healthy and hydrated.

Conclusion

Clippers without a guard can be used to create a variety of hairstyles, but they require skill and patience. Always be careful when using clippers without a guard, as it's easy to nick the skin or cut too much hair. Follow the steps and tips outlined in this article, and you'll be able to use clippers without a guard safely and effectively.
