How To Cut A Mullet At Home With Clippers

Written by Eveline Feb 20, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and edgy hairstyle, the mullet might be just what you're looking for. The mullet has made a comeback in recent years, with many celebrities sporting the look. If you're interested in cutting a mullet at home with clippers, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know to achieve the perfect mullet.

Description

The mullet is a hairstyle that is shorter on the top and sides and longer in the back. The hair is typically cut in layers, with the top and sides being shorter and the back being longer. The mullet became popular in the 1980s and has recently made a comeback. It's a trendy and edgy look that can be customized to suit your personal style.

How to Cut a Mullet at Home with Clippers

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. You may also want to have a friend or family member help you, especially if you're new to cutting hair.

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that the cut is even. Wash and dry your hair before you begin.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use the comb to section your hair into three parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Clip the top and sides out of the way so you can focus on the back.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up. Use the clippers to create a gradual taper, going shorter as you get closer to the top. Be sure to blend the hair so there are no harsh lines.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Once you've finished the back, unclip the sides and begin cutting. Use the clippers to create a gradual taper, going shorter as you get closer to the top. Again, be sure to blend the hair so there are no harsh lines.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, unclip the top and use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. The top should be shorter than the back, but longer than the sides. You can add layers for added texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect mullet:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way shorter. You can always go shorter, but you can't go longer.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Be sure to blend the hair so there are no harsh lines.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a mullet:

Can I cut a mullet at home?

Yes, you can cut a mullet at home with the right tools and techniques.

What tools do I need to cut a mullet?

You'll need clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

How do I know what length to cut my hair?

Start with a longer length and work your way shorter. You can always go shorter, but you can't go longer.

Hair Care

Now that you've cut your mullet, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your mullet.

Cutting a mullet at home with clippers is a great way to achieve a trendy and edgy look. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect mullet. Remember to take your time, blend the hair, and use a mirror to check your progress. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at cutting your own hair.
