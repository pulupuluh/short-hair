Best Product To Slick Back Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Are you tired of your thick hair getting in the way of achieving a sleek and polished look? Look no further than the best product to slick back thick hair. In this guide, we will cover everything you need to know about achieving a slicked-back hairstyle, from product recommendations to styling tips and hair care.

Table of Contents

Description

Slicked-back hairstyles have been popular for years, and for good reason. This classic style is perfect for any occasion, from a night out to a professional setting. However, achieving this look can be a challenge for those with thick hair. Luckily, there are products specifically designed to help slick back thick hair with ease.

How to Slick Back Thick Hair

Slicking back thick hair may seem daunting, but with the right product and technique, it can be achieved effortlessly. The key is to find a product that provides hold and control without weighing down your hair. Here are some recommended products:

Hair Gel

Hair gel is a classic product for slicked-back hairstyles. Look for a strong-hold gel that won't flake or leave residue. Apply a dime-sized amount to damp hair and comb through.

Pomade

Pomade is another popular product for slicked-back hairstyles. It provides a medium hold and a shiny finish. Apply a small amount to dry hair and use a comb to style.

Clay

Clay is a great option for those with thick hair because it provides a strong hold without weighing down the hair. Apply a small amount to dry hair and use your fingers to style.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have your product of choice, follow these steps to achieve a slicked-back hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Take a small amount of product and rub it between your palms.
  3. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the front and sides.
  4. Use a comb to slick back your hair, starting at the hairline and working your way back.
  5. Smooth down any flyaways or stray hairs with your hands.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil for added shine and hold.

Tips for Slicking Back Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Start with a small amount of product and build up as needed.
  • Use a comb with fine teeth for a sleeker look.
  • For a more natural look, use your fingers to style instead of a comb.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired hold.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about slicking back thick hair:

What if I have curly or wavy hair?

Curly or wavy hair can still be slicked back with the right product and technique. Use a gel or pomade and a wide-tooth comb to evenly distribute the product and smooth down any curls or waves.

Can I slick back my hair if it's short?

Yes, slicked-back hairstyles can be achieved with short hair. Use a small amount of product and a fine-tooth comb to create a sleek look.

How do I prevent my hair from looking greasy?

Use a small amount of product and avoid applying too much near the roots. Additionally, choose a product that won't leave residue or flake.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Maintaining healthy hair is crucial for achieving a sleek and polished look. Here are some tips for caring for thick hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep hair hydrated.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With the right product and care, slicking back thick hair can be a breeze. Try out different products and techniques to find the perfect look for you.
