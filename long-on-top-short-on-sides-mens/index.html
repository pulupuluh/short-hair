How to Achieve Long on Top Short on Sides Mens

The long on top short on sides mens haircut is all about creating contrast between the top and sides of the head. The top of the hair is left longer and styled in a textured, messy look, while the sides and back are cut short. To achieve this look, you will need to visit a professional barber or hair stylist who specializes in men's haircuts.

What to Tell Your Barber

When you visit your barber, be sure to tell them that you want a long on top short on sides mens haircut. Show them photos of the style you like, and be specific about how short you want the sides and back to be. You can also ask your barber to add texture to the top of your hair by using a razor or scissors to create layers.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the long on top short on sides mens haircut:

  1. Wash and towel-dry your hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to the top of your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume on top.
  4. Use your fingers to style the top of your hair in a messy, textured look.
  5. Apply a small amount of styling product to the sides and back of your hair.
  6. Use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair to your desired length.
  7. Blend the sides and back into the top of your hair using scissors or a razor.
  8. Finish by using a little bit of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

To keep your long on top short on sides mens haircut looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep the sides and back short.
  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent oil buildup.
  • Use a quality styling product to keep the top of your hair looking textured and messy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • If you have a receding hairline, consider adding a fringe to the front of your hair to create the illusion of fullness.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the long on top short on sides mens haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the long on top short on sides mens haircut can work for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. However, it may require more styling for those with curly hair.

Can I style my hair differently with the long on top short on sides mens haircut?

Yes, the long on top short on sides mens haircut is versatile and can be styled in many ways, such as slicked back or parted to the side. Experiment with different looks to find the one that suits you best.

How can I make my long on top short on sides mens haircut last longer?

Regular trims and proper hair care can help your long on top short on sides mens haircut last longer. Be sure to use a quality styling product and avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Long on Top Short on Sides Mens

To keep your long on top short on sides mens haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a mild shampoo to prevent oil buildup.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a quality conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Long on top short on sides mens is a classic and stylish haircut that can work for almost any man. By following these tips and taking proper care of your hair, you can keep your haircut looking its best for weeks to come.
