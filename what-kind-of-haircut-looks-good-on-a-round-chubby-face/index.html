What Kind Of Haircut Looks Good On A Round Chubby Face

Written by William Apr 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a round chubby face, you may be unsure about what kind of haircut would look good on you. Having a round face shape means that the width and length of your face are almost the same, which can make your face appear fuller. But don't worry, there are plenty of hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and highlight your best features. In this article, we'll explore some hairstyles that look great on round chubby faces.

Description

The key to finding a great haircut for a round chubby face is to choose a style that creates the illusion of length and angles. This means that you should look for hairstyles that add height to the top of your head and create volume at the crown. You can also opt for hairstyles with face-framing layers that help to elongate your face shape.

How To

When choosing a haircut for a round chubby face, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, you should avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or straight, as these can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for hairstyles with layers or waves that add texture and movement to your hair.

Another thing to consider is the length of your hair. If you have a round face shape, it's generally best to avoid haircuts that are too short, as these can make your face appear wider. Instead, choose a haircut that falls at or below your chin, as this length will help to elongate your face shape.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for getting a great haircut for a round chubby face:

  1. Start by finding a hairstylist who has experience working with round face shapes.
  2. Discuss your face shape and your hair goals with your hairstylist.
  3. Choose a haircut that adds height to the top of your head and creates volume at the crown.
  4. Opt for face-framing layers that help to elongate your face shape.
  5. Avoid haircuts that are too blunt or straight.
  6. Choose a haircut that falls at or below your chin.
  7. Consider adding texture and movement to your hair with waves or layers.
  8. Ask your hairstylist for styling tips to maintain your new haircut.
  9. Enjoy your new look!

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a round chubby face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as these can make your face appear wider.
  • Opt for side-swept bangs that help to create angles and lengthen your face shape.
  • Avoid center parts, as these can make your face appear even rounder.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that you love!

FAQ

What are the best haircuts for a round chubby face?

The best haircuts for a round chubby face are those that add height to the top of your head and create volume at the crown. You should also look for face-framing layers that help to elongate your face shape.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round chubby face?

If you have a round chubby face, you should avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or straight, as these can make your face appear even rounder. You should also avoid haircuts that are too short, as these can make your face appear wider.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a great haircut for your round chubby face, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful, and show off your new haircut with confidence!
