Male Haircuts Short On Sides Long On Top A Guide To The Latest Trend

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is trendy, versatile, and easy to maintain? Look no further than the male haircut short on sides long on top. This style has been gaining popularity in recent years, and for good reason. It offers the perfect balance between a clean, professional look and a stylish, edgy vibe. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this hairstyle, from how to get it to how to care for it.

How to Get the Male Haircut Short on Sides Long on Top

The male haircut short on sides long on top involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving the hair on top long. This style is also known as the undercut or the disconnected haircut. To get this style, you will need to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who is experienced in this type of cut.

When you go to the salon, be sure to bring a picture of the style you want. This will help your stylist understand your vision and give you the haircut you desire. You can also discuss with your stylist how short you want the sides and back and how long you want the hair on top. This will help them create a customized look that is perfect for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Male Haircut Short on Sides Long on Top

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting the male haircut short on sides long on top:

  1. Wash your hair and towel-dry it.
  2. Sit in the chair and let your stylist section your hair into the sides and top.
  3. Using clippers or scissors, your stylist will cut the hair on the sides and back short.
  4. They will then blend the hair on top with the sides, creating a seamless transition.
  5. Your stylist will then style the hair on top using a comb or their fingers, depending on the look you want.
  6. They may also use a blow dryer or styling product to help set the style.

Tips for Styling the Male Haircut Short on Sides Long on Top

Once you have your male haircut short on sides long on top, you may wonder how to style it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a lightweight styling product, such as a wax or pomade, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the one that suits you best.
  • Consider adding a fade or a taper to the sides and back for a more polished look.
  • Don't be afraid to play with the length on top. You can leave it longer for a more dramatic effect or trim it shorter for a more subtle look.

FAQs About the Male Haircut Short on Sides Long on Top

Q: Is the male haircut short on sides long on top suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this style works well for most hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How often should I get a trim?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you want to maintain the style. Generally, you should go for a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Q: Can I still style my hair if I have a receding hairline?

A: Yes, the male haircut short on sides long on top can be adapted to work with a receding hairline. Your stylist can create a customized look that flatters your face shape and hairline.

Hair Care Tips for the Male Haircut Short on Sides Long on Top

Finally, here are a few tips to help you care for your male haircut short on sides long on top:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock the male haircut short on sides long on top with ease. Whether you prefer a sleek, sophisticated look or a more edgy, punk-inspired vibe, this style is perfect for any occasion.
