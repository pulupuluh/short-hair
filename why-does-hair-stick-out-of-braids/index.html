Why Does Hair Stick Out Of Braids Understanding The Reason Behind This Common Problem

Written by Aprilia Aug 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of braiding your hair only to have some strands sticking out and ruining the look? You're not alone. This is a common problem that many people face, and it can be frustrating to deal with. In this article, we'll explore the reasons why hair sticks out of braids and what you can do to prevent it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the reasons why hair sticks out of braids, let's take a moment to describe what we mean. When you braid your hair, you want the braids to be smooth and uniform. However, sometimes you may notice that some strands of hair are sticking out, ruining the look. This can happen regardless of the type of braid you're doing, whether it's a French braid, cornrows, or any other style.

How to

The first step in preventing hair from sticking out of braids is to make sure your hair is properly detangled. Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to remove any knots or tangles before you start braiding. This will ensure that your hair is smooth and will help prevent any strands from sticking out.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions to help you prevent hair from sticking out of your braids:

  1. Start with detangled hair.
  2. Use a small amount of hair gel or styling cream to smooth down any flyaways.
  3. Take small sections of hair and braid tightly.
  4. Use a small elastic band to secure the end of the braid.
  5. If any strands are sticking out, use a small amount of gel or styling cream to smooth them down.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help prevent hair from sticking out of braids:

  • Use a rat tail comb to help create clean parts when braiding.
  • Avoid braiding your hair too tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Try using a hair oil or serum to help keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.
  • Consider using a silk or satin scarf or pillowcase to help prevent friction and breakage while you sleep.

FAQ

Q: Why does hair stick out of braids?

A: There are several reasons why hair may stick out of braids, including improper detangling, not braiding tightly enough, and using the wrong products or tools.

Q: Can I prevent hair from sticking out of braids?

A: Yes, you can prevent hair from sticking out of braids by properly detangling your hair, braiding tightly, and using the right products and tools.

Q: What products should I use to prevent hair from sticking out of braids?

A: You can use a hair gel or styling cream to help smooth down any flyaways and keep your braids looking neat.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, whether you're wearing braids or not. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to help keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can help prevent hair from sticking out of braids and maintain healthy, beautiful hair.
