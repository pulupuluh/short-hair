How To Get Tape In Extensions To Stay In

Written by Oliver Jun 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to add length and volume to your hair, tape-in extensions are a great option. However, one of the biggest challenges with tape-in extensions is getting them to stay in place. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you get your tape-in extensions to stay in all day long.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Install Tape-In Extensions
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Keeping Tape-In Extensions in Place
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Tape-In Extensions

How to Install Tape-In Extensions

Before we get into how to get tape-in extensions to stay in, let's talk about how to install them properly. Here are the basic steps:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Section your hair into small, even sections.
  3. Remove the adhesive backing from the tape-in extensions.
  4. Place the extensions underneath the section of hair, close to the roots.
  5. Press the extensions firmly into place, sandwiching your real hair between the two tape strips.
  6. Repeat this process until all the extensions are installed.

It's important to note that tape-in extensions should only be installed by a professional stylist. Attempting to install them yourself can result in damage to your hair or the extensions themselves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that your tape-in extensions are installed, it's time to make sure they stay put. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Wait at least 24 hours after installation before washing your hair. This will give the adhesive time to set.
  2. When washing your hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using any products that contain alcohol, as this can weaken the adhesive.
  3. Avoid excessively touching or playing with your extensions. The more you manipulate them, the more likely they are to come loose.
  4. When blow-drying your hair, use a low heat setting and avoid pointing the dryer directly at the extensions. Excessive heat can also weaken the adhesive.
  5. Avoid swimming or exposing your hair to excessive moisture. If you do get your hair wet, gently pat it dry with a towel and avoid rubbing or pulling on the extensions.

Tips for Keeping Tape-In Extensions in Place

Here are some additional tips to help you keep your tape-in extensions in place:

  • Avoid using any hair care products that contain silicone, as this can cause the extensions to slip.
  • Invest in a good quality brush or comb and gently brush your hair from the roots to the ends. Avoid brushing too aggressively or tugging on the extensions.
  • If you notice any extensions starting to come loose, contact your stylist immediately. They can reapply the extensions and ensure they stay in place.

FAQs

Q: How often do I need to get my tape-in extensions reinstalled?

A: This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you take care of your extensions. On average, you'll need to get them reinstalled every 6-8 weeks.

Q: Can I style my hair as usual with tape-in extensions?

A: Yes, you can style your hair as usual with tape-in extensions. Just be mindful of the heat and avoid using excessive force when brushing or styling your hair.

Hair Care for Tape-In Extensions

Tape-in extensions require a bit of extra care to ensure they stay in place and look their best. Here are some hair care tips for tape-in extensions:

  • Avoid using any hair care products that contain alcohol, silicone, or sulfates.
  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid excessive heat and be mindful of the temperature when blow-drying, straightening, or curling your hair.
  • Brush your hair gently, starting at the roots and working your way down.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair, as this can cause tangling and matting.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to excessive moisture, such as swimming or hot tubs.

By following these tips and taking good care of your tape-in extensions, you can ensure they stay in place and look beautiful for weeks to come.
