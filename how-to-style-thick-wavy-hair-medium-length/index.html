How To Style Thick Wavy Hair Medium Length

Written by Daniel Apr 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling to style your thick wavy hair at a medium length? Look no further because we have got you covered with some easy and quick styling tips that will help you achieve a perfect look. In this article, we will guide you on how to style your hair, step by step, along with some tips and tricks to maintain your hair's health and beauty.

Description

Thick wavy hair is one of the most challenging hair types to style, especially if you have medium length hair. It can be challenging to manage and style, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a perfect look in no time.

How to Style Thick Wavy Hair Medium Length

Step 1: Shampoo and Conditioner

The first step in styling your thick wavy hair is to wash it with a good shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to choose a sulfate-free and hydrating shampoo and conditioner that will help tame frizz and enhance your hair's texture.

Step 2: Towel Dry

Gently towel dry your hair and avoid rubbing it vigorously with a towel. This will prevent damage and frizz in your hair.

Step 3: Apply Hair Oil or Serum

Apply a hair oil or serum to your hair while it's still damp. This will help to detangle your hair and add moisture to it. It will also give your hair a natural shine.

Step 4: Blow Dry

Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to keep the curls intact. Use a low heat setting to prevent damage to your hair. You can also scrunch your hair while blow-drying to create more volume and definition.

Step 5: Style with a Curling Iron

If you want to add more definition to your hair, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron to protect your hair from heat damage.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you style your thick wavy hair at medium length:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid using a brush that can create frizz.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Try different hairstyles like braids, buns, or ponytails to keep your hair out of your face and add some variety to your style.

FAQ

How can I maintain the health of my thick wavy hair?

You can maintain the health of your hair by using sulfate-free and hydrating hair products, avoiding too much heat, and using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

Can I straighten my thick wavy hair?

Yes, you can straighten your hair, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using too much heat to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Care

Maintaining the health of your hair is essential, especially if you have thick wavy hair. Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free and hydrating shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes foods rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth and health.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your thick wavy hair at medium length effortlessly. Remember to take care of your hair's health to maintain its beauty and shine.
