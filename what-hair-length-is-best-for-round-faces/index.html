What Hair Length Is Best For Round Faces

Written by William Sep 06, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you wondering what hair length is best for round faces? If you have a round face, it's important to choose a hairstyle that will flatter your features and complement your face shape. In this article, we'll discuss the best hair length and styles for round faces, along with some tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Hair Length for Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Round Faces

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. The best hairstyles for round faces are those that help to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. The right hairstyle can also highlight your best features and create balance in your overall look.

How to Choose the Best Hair Length for Round Faces

The best hair length for round faces depends on a few factors, including your hair texture, face shape, and personal style. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

Short Hair

Short hair can be a great choice for round faces, especially if you have fine hair. A pixie cut or a bob that falls just above the chin can help to create the illusion of a longer face. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as these can make your face look rounder.

Medium Length Hair

Medium length hair can also be a good option for round faces. A shoulder-length cut with layers can help to frame your face and add movement to your hair. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as these can make your face look wider.

Long Hair

If you have thick or curly hair, long hair can be a great option for round faces. Long layers and soft waves can help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look. Avoid straight, blunt cuts, as these can make your face look wider.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle for your round face can be a process of trial and error. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the right hairstyle, you need to determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If these measurements are roughly the same, you have a round face.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in choosing the right hairstyle. Fine hair may look best with a shorter cut, while thick or curly hair may look better with longer layers.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Look for inspiration in magazines or online. Save pictures of hairstyles that you like and bring them to your stylist for guidance.

Step 4: Consult with Your Stylist

Your stylist can help you choose the right hairstyle based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. They can also offer tips on how to maintain your new hairstyle.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your round face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as these can make your face look wider. Also avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a bouffant or a curly afro.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a round face. However, it's best to create some volume on top of your head and avoid pulling your hair back too tightly.

What hair color is best for round faces?

The best hair color for round faces depends on your skin tone and personal style. However, highlights or lowlights can help to add dimension to your hair and create a more balanced look.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Caring for your hair is important, regardless of your face shape. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and healthy.

In conclusion, choosing the right hair length for a round face can help to create balance and highlight your best features. Follow these tips and consult with your stylist to find the perfect hairstyle for you.
