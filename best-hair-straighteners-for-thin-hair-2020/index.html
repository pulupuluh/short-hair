Best Hair Straighteners For Thin Hair 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Aug 08, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you have thin hair, finding the right hair straightener can be a challenge. You want a tool that can straighten your hair without damaging it or causing breakage. In this article, we'll give you a comprehensive guide to the best hair straighteners for thin hair in 2020.

Description

Thin hair can be difficult to manage, especially when it comes to styling. You need to be careful not to damage your hair while trying to achieve the perfect look. That's why it's important to choose the right hair straightener. With so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one. In this article, we'll give you an overview of the best hair straighteners for thin hair in 2020.

How to Choose the Best Hair Straightener for Thin Hair

When choosing a hair straightener for thin hair, there are a few things to consider:

Plate Material

The plate material can affect how your hair reacts to the straightener. Ceramic plates are a popular choice because they distribute heat evenly and don't damage hair. Tourmaline plates are also a good option because they emit negative ions that help reduce frizz.

Plate Width

The width of the plates can affect how well the straightener works on thin hair. Narrow plates are better for short hair or for creating curls, while wider plates are better for longer hair or for straightening larger sections of hair at a time.

Temperature Settings

Thin hair is more delicate than thick hair, so it's important to choose a straightener with adjustable temperature settings. Lower temperatures are better for thin hair because they cause less damage.

Best Hair Straighteners for Thin Hair 2020

After considering the factors above, we've compiled a list of the best hair straighteners for thin hair in 2020:

1. BaBylissPRO Nano Titanium-Plated Ultra-Thin Straightening Iron

This straightener has ultra-thin plates that are ideal for thin hair. The titanium plates heat up quickly and evenly, and the temperature is adjustable up to 450 degrees Fahrenheit. The straightener also has a long cord for easy use.

2. GHD Platinum+ Professional Performance Styler

This straightener uses predictive technology to monitor the temperature and adjust accordingly, so it doesn't damage thin hair. The plates are also ceramic and have a rounded design for creating curls as well as straightening.

3. HSI Professional Glider

This straightener has ceramic plates that distribute heat evenly and prevent damage to thin hair. The temperature is adjustable up to 450 degrees Fahrenheit, and the straightener also comes with a heat-resistant glove for easy use.

4. Remington S5500 Digital Anti-Static Ceramic Hair Straightener

This straightener has ceramic plates that prevent static and frizz, making it ideal for thin hair. The temperature is adjustable up to 410 degrees Fahrenheit, and the straightener also has a digital display for easy use.

Step-by-Step Guide to Straightening Thin Hair

Now that you've chosen the right straightener for your thin hair, here's a step-by-step guide to using it:

1. Wash and Dry Your Hair

Make sure your hair is clean and completely dry before using a straightener. Wet hair can be damaged by heat.

2. Apply Heat Protectant

Spray a heat protectant on your hair to prevent damage from the straightener.

3. Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections so you can straighten each section individually.

4. Straighten Your Hair

Starting at the roots, clamp the straightener onto a small section of hair and slowly glide it down to the ends. Repeat until all sections are straightened.

Tips for Straightening Thin Hair

Here are some tips for straightening thin hair:

1. Use Low Heat

Thin hair is more delicate than thick hair, so it's important to use low heat to prevent damage.

2. Don't Straighten Wet Hair

Straightening wet hair can cause damage and breakage. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using a straightener.

3. Use Heat Protectant

Using a heat protectant can help prevent damage from the straightener.

4. Don't Straighten Every Day

Straightening your hair every day can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit straightening to a few times a week.

FAQs

1. Can I use a straightener on thin hair?

Yes, you can use a straightener on thin hair. Just make sure to choose a straightener with adjustable temperature settings and use low heat to prevent damage.

2. What's the best plate material for thin hair?

Ceramic plates are a good option for thin hair because they distribute heat evenly and don't damage hair.

3. How often should I straighten my thin hair?

Try to limit straightening to a few times a week to prevent damage and breakage.

Hair Care for Thin Hair

Thin hair requires special care to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't weigh down your hair or strip it of natural oils.

2. Don't Overbrush

Brushing your hair too much can cause breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead.

3. Use a Lightweight Conditioner

Use a lightweight conditioner that won't weigh down your hair.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling your hair too often to prevent damage and breakage.

Overall, choosing the right hair straightener and using it properly can help you achieve a sleek, straight look without damaging your thin hair. With the tips and recommendations in this guide, you'll be on your way to healthy, beautiful hair.
