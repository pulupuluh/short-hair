Is Losing 60 Hairs A Day Normal

Written by William Jul 27, 2023 · 7 min read

Table of Content:

  • Tips to Prevent Hair Loss

Description

Hair loss is a common phenomenon and can be caused by various factors such as genetics, age, hormones, and lifestyle. Losing hair is a natural process, and it is normal to lose about 50 to 100 hairs a day. However, many people are concerned about losing 60 hairs a day and wonder if it is normal or not. In this article, we will discuss the causes of hair loss, how to determine if losing 60 hairs a day is normal or not, and provide tips to prevent hair loss.

How to Determine Hair Loss

To determine if losing 60 hairs a day is normal or not, you need to count the number of hairs you are losing. You can do this by following these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair using a mild shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any loose hairs from your scalp, making it easier to count the hairs that fall out during the day.

Step 2: Let Your Hair Dry Naturally

Let your hair dry naturally without using a hairdryer or towel. Using a towel or hairdryer can cause hair breakage and make it difficult to count the hairs that fall out.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Comb your hair gently using a wide-tooth comb. This will help remove any tangles and make it easier to count the hairs that fall out.

Step 4: Count the Hairs

Count the number of hairs on the comb or brush after combing your hair. Repeat this process for several days and calculate the average number of hairs you are losing per day. If you are losing more than 100 hairs per day, it is recommended to consult a dermatologist.

Tips to Prevent Hair Loss

There are several tips you can follow to prevent hair loss:

Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote hair growth and prevent hair loss.

Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals such as hair dyes, bleach, and hair straighteners, as they can cause hair breakage and damage the hair follicles.

Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help improve blood circulation and promote hair growth. Use natural oils such as coconut or olive oil to massage your scalp.

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles such as braids, ponytails, and buns, as they can cause hair breakage and damage the hair follicles.

FAQs

Q: Is losing 60 hairs a day normal?

A: Yes, losing 60 hairs a day is normal and nothing to worry about.

Q: When should I be concerned about hair loss?

A: If you are losing more than 100 hairs per day, it is recommended to consult a dermatologist.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss. Try to manage your stress levels by practicing relaxation techniques such as yoga or meditation.

Hair Care Tips

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can lead to hair loss.

Use a Mild Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type to avoid damaging your hair.

Avoid Overstyling Your Hair

Avoid overstyling your hair with heat tools such as hair straighteners and curling irons, as they can cause hair breakage and damage the hair follicles.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

In conclusion, losing 60 hairs a day is normal and nothing to be concerned about. However, if you are losing more than 100 hairs per day, it is recommended to consult a dermatologist. Follow the tips mentioned in this article to prevent hair loss and promote hair growth.
