How To Cut Cute Side Bangs A Step By Step Guide

In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting cute side bangs in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language. So, let's get started!

How to Cut Cute Side Bangs

Before we begin, it's important to note that cutting your hair can be tricky, especially if you are not a professional hairstylist. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve cute side bangs in the comfort of your own home.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to cut side bangs:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut. Use a comb to create a triangle-shaped section at the front of your hairline.
  3. Clip the rest of your hair out of the way. This will prevent you from accidentally cutting the wrong section of hair.
  4. Comb your bangs straight down and hold them between your fingers where you want to make the cut.
  5. Use a sharp pair of scissors to cut the hair. Remember to cut slowly and in small sections to avoid any mistakes.
  6. Continue to cut until you reach your desired length. Keep in mind that your hair will bounce up once it is dry, so don't cut too much at once.
  7. Style your new side bangs by blow-drying them with a round brush. You can also use a straightener or curling iron to add some texture.

Congratulations, you now have cute side bangs!

Tips for Cutting Side Bangs

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect side bangs:

  • Always use sharp scissors to avoid split ends and uneven cuts.
  • Make sure your hair is dry before cutting to avoid any surprises once it dries.
  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to your desired length.
  • If you're not sure how short to cut, always err on the side of caution and cut less rather than more.
  • Don't forget to angle your scissors for a natural look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting side bangs:

How long should I cut my side bangs?

The length of your side bangs will depend on your personal preference and face shape. Some people prefer longer side bangs that blend into the rest of their hair, while others opt for shorter and more dramatic side bangs.

Can I cut my own side bangs?

Yes, you can cut your own side bangs, but it's important to be careful and take your time. Consider watching online tutorials or practicing on a mannequin head before attempting to cut your own hair.

How often should I trim my side bangs?

It's recommended to trim your side bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have cute side bangs, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.

Now you know how to cut cute side bangs and take care of them. Go ahead and give it a try, and don't forget to share your new look with us!
