Best Haircut For Face Shape Male Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Mar 26, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task for many men, especially if you're not sure about your face shape. The right haircut can enhance your facial features, make you look more attractive, and boost your confidence. In this guide, we will explore the best haircuts for different face shapes, including round, square, oval, and heart-shaped faces. We'll also provide tips on how to maintain and style your new haircut.

Understanding Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for different face shapes, it's essential to understand your face shape. The four main face shapes for men are:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Oval
  • Heart-shaped

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick. Alternatively, you can take a photo of yourself and trace the outline on paper. Once you have the outline, compare it to the four face shapes mentioned above.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces

Men with round faces should opt for haircuts that add definition and length to their face. Shorter haircuts, such as buzz cuts, can make your face appear rounder, so it's best to avoid them. Instead, consider these haircuts:

  • Side-parted undercut
  • Textured quiff
  • Faux hawk
  • Side-swept fringe

These haircuts create the illusion of a longer face and add definition to your jawline.

Best Haircuts for Square Faces

Men with square faces have a strong jawline and angular features. The best haircuts for this face shape should soften the angles and create a more balanced look. Consider these haircuts:

  • Textured crop
  • Side-swept fringe
  • Classic side part
  • Messy top with short sides

These haircuts add volume to the top of your head and create a more balanced look.

Best Haircuts for Oval Faces

Oval faces are considered the most versatile face shape, as they can pull off a wide range of haircuts. If you have an oval face, try these haircuts:

  • Classic crew cut
  • Buzz cut
  • Slicked-back undercut
  • Textured side part

These haircuts accentuate your facial features and create a balanced look.

Best Haircuts for Heart-Shaped Faces

Men with heart-shaped faces have a wider forehead and a narrower chin. The best haircuts for this face shape should balance out the proportions and create a more symmetrical look. Consider these haircuts:

  • Textured fringe
  • Side-swept fringe
  • Short crop
  • Classic side part

These haircuts create the illusion of a wider chin and balance out the proportions of your face.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut

Once you've chosen the best haircut for your face shape, it's essential to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Invest in quality hair products, such as shampoo, conditioner, and styling products.
  • Learn how to style your new haircut properly.
  • Consider your lifestyle and choose a low-maintenance haircut if you're always on the go.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

It's common for men to have a combination of face shapes, such as a round face with a square jawline. In this case, choose a haircut that flatters the dominant face shape.

Can I pull off a buzz cut?

If you have a round face, it's best to avoid a buzz cut, as it can make your face appear rounder. However, if you have an oval or square face, a buzz cut can look great.

Should I consider my hair type when choosing a haircut?

Yes, your hair type is also an important factor to consider when choosing a haircut. For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to opt for longer haircuts that allow your curls to shine. If you have thick hair, you may want to choose a haircut that thins out the hair to make it more manageable.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair and a great haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a quality shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after shampooing to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Invest in quality styling products, such as pomade or wax.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy hair and a great haircut for years to come.

Conclusion

Choosing the best haircut for your face shape can be a game-changer. It can enhance your facial features, create a more balanced look, and boost your confidence. Use this guide to find the best haircut for your face shape, and remember to maintain it properly with quality hair products and regular trims.
