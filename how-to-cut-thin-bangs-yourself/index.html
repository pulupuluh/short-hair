How To Cut Thin Bangs Yourself A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle without breaking the bank, cutting your own bangs is a great option. However, cutting thin bangs can be a bit tricky. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut thin bangs yourself in just a few simple steps.

How to Cut Thin Bangs Yourself

Cutting thin bangs is a great way to add a fresh look to your hairstyle. Here are some things you'll need to get started:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • A mirror

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have everything you need, let's get started:

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Begin by wetting your bangs with the spray bottle. This will make them easier to cut and ensure that they're all the same length.

Step 2: Comb Your Bangs

Use a comb to separate your bangs from the rest of your hair. Comb your bangs to the side and make sure they're straight.

Step 3: Determine Your Desired Length

Decide how long you want your bangs to be. It's always better to start with longer bangs and gradually cut them shorter, so don't be too aggressive with your first cut.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Using your sharp scissors, make a small diagonal cut into your bangs. This will be your guide for the rest of your cuts. From there, slowly cut your bangs in small increments, checking the length after each cut. Remember, it's always better to cut less than more.

Step 5: Check the Length

Take a step back and check the length of your bangs. If they're still too long, repeat step 4 until you've achieved your desired length.

Tips for Cutting Thin Bangs

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect thin bangs:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Cut your bangs when they're dry rather than wet if you have curly hair.
  • Don't cut your bangs straight across. Instead, cut them at an angle for a more natural look.
  • Start with longer bangs and gradually cut them shorter.
  • Don't be too aggressive with your cuts. It's always better to cut less than more.

FAQs

How often should I cut my bangs?

It's recommended that you cut your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy.

Can I cut my own bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still cut your own bangs if you have curly hair. However, it's recommended that you cut them when they're dry rather than wet.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! It's just hair and it will grow back. However, it's always better to cut less than more, so be conservative with your cuts.

Hair Care After Cutting Thin Bangs

Now that you've cut your thin bangs, it's important to take care of them properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a small amount of hair oil to keep your bangs looking shiny and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • If you have curly hair, consider using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help define your curls.
