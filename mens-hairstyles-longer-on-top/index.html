Mens Hairstyles Longer On Top A Guide To Perfecting The Look

Written by Frank Aug 31, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile hairstyle, then you might want to try a mens hairstyle longer on top. This haircut has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's easy to see why. It offers a modern and edgy look while still being professional and stylish. In this article, we'll give you a comprehensive guide on how to achieve this hairstyle, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  • How to Get Mens Hairstyles Longer on Top

How to Get Mens Hairstyles Longer on Top

Before you start cutting your hair, it's essential to know what style you're going for. There are various mens hairstyles longer on top, and each one requires a specific technique to achieve. Here are some popular options:

  • The Quiff
  • The Undercut
  • The Pompadour
  • The Comb-Over
  • The Slick Back
  • The Side Part
  • The Messy Top

Once you've decided on the style you want, it's time to head to your hairdresser or barber. They have the expertise to create the perfect mens hairstyle longer on top that suits your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Be sure to communicate your preferences and ask for their advice on how to maintain your haircut.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're feeling adventurous and want to cut your hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a mens hairstyle longer on top:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into three parts: top, sides, and back.
  3. Using clippers or scissors, cut the sides and back short. The length depends on your preference, but a fade or undercut style is popular.
  4. Blend the top section with the sides and back. The top should be longer than the sides and back, but the transition should be seamless.
  5. Use a comb and scissors to trim the top section to the desired length. You can leave it messy or styled, depending on the look you want to achieve.
  6. Style your hair with a pomade or wax. Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type and style.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Hairstyles Longer on Top

Mens hairstyles longer on top require some effort to maintain their shape and style. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking fresh:

  • Regularly trim the sides and back to avoid hair growing out of shape.
  • Style your hair daily to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to distribute the product evenly and achieve the desired look.
  • Wash your hair twice a week and use a conditioner to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs About Mens Hairstyles Longer on Top

Is a mens hairstyle longer on top suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, but it's essential to choose a style that complements your face shape. For example, a quiff or pompadour is ideal for round faces, while a slick back or side part is better for a square face.

Can I achieve a mens hairstyle longer on top with curly hair?

Absolutely. In fact, curly hair can add texture and volume to the top section, making it easier to style. However, it's essential to keep the sides and back shorter to avoid a triangular shape.

How often should I get a haircut to maintain my mens hairstyle longer on top?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, a trim every four to six weeks is recommended to keep your hair looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Hairstyles Longer on Top

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and appearance of your mens hairstyle longer on top. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a blow dryer to avoid heat damage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for a gentle and sulfate-free shampoo instead.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Now that you have a comprehensive guide on mens hairstyles longer on top, go ahead and try out this trendy and versatile look. Remember to communicate your preferences with your barber or hairdresser and maintain your hair with proper care and styling.
