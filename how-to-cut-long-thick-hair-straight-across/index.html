How To Cut Long Thick Hair Straight Across A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Mar 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have long, thick hair, you know how difficult it can be to maintain. Split ends, tangles, and overall damage can make it feel impossible to keep your locks looking healthy and fresh. One solution to this problem is to cut your hair straight across. This will not only give your hair a neat and polished look, but it can also help to reduce damage and make your hair easier to manage. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut long thick hair straight across.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut Long Thick Hair Straight Across
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Long Thick Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care After Cutting

How to Cut Long Thick Hair Straight Across

Cutting long thick hair straight across can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and technique, you can do it yourself at home. Here is what you will need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A mirror
  • Hair clips
  • A spray bottle filled with water

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your long thick hair straight across:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will help to remove any tangles and make it easier to comb through.
  2. Comb your hair straight back and divide it into two even sections. Secure each section with a hair clip.
  3. Working with one section at a time, remove the hair clip and comb your hair straight down towards your chest.
  4. Hold the hair between your fingers and use your scissors to cut straight across. Make sure to cut the hair evenly and at the desired length.
  5. Repeat this process with the other section of hair.
  6. Once both sections are cut, check to make sure they are even. If needed, make small adjustments to even out any uneven areas.
  7. Comb your hair straight down and check to make sure it is even and straight across.
  8. Trim any stray hairs or split ends as needed.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Long Thick Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting long thick hair straight across:

  • Start by cutting a little bit at a time. You can always go back and cut more if needed.
  • Make sure to use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Use a spray bottle to keep your hair damp while cutting. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that the hair is cut evenly.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting long thick hair can be tiring, so make sure to take breaks as needed.
  • Consider getting a friend or family member to help you. It can be difficult to cut the back of your hair on your own.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long thick hair straight across:

How often should I cut my long thick hair?

You should aim to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and damage.

Can I cut my hair straight across with a razor?

We do not recommend using a razor to cut your hair straight across, as this can cause damage and split ends. Stick to using sharp scissors.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your long thick hair straight across, it is important to take care of it properly to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as these can cause damage and split ends.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant product.

By following these tips, you can maintain your long thick hair and keep it looking healthy and polished.

Cutting long thick hair straight across may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, it can be done at home. By following our step-by-step guide and using our tips, you can achieve a neat and polished look for your locks. Remember to take care of your hair properly after cutting to maintain its health and appearance.
