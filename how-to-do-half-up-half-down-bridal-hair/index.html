How To Do Half Up Half Down Bridal Hair

Written by William Mar 21, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Half up half down bridal hair is a popular wedding hairstyle that combines the elegance of an updo with the romance of loose waves. It's perfect for brides who want a hairstyle that's both chic and relaxed. In this article, we'll show you how to do half up half down bridal hair step-by-step.

Description

Half up half down bridal hair is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit any wedding theme or personal style. It's a great option for brides who want to show off their long locks while keeping their hair out of their face. This hairstyle can be achieved with any hair type and length, whether it's straight, wavy, or curly.

How To

Step 1: Prep your hair
Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage. If you have fine hair, consider using a volumizing mousse to add body to your locks.

Step 2: Curl your hair
Use a curling iron or a wand to create loose waves in your hair. Take small sections of hair and wrap them loosely around the iron. Hold for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat until all your hair is curled.

Step 3: Tease the crown
Take a section of hair at the crown of your head and tease it with a fine-tooth comb. This will add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Section your hair
Take a section of hair from each side of your head and pull them back towards the crown. Use bobby pins to secure them in place. Leave the rest of your hair loose.

Step 5: Pin the hair
Take a few sections of hair from the sides and back of your head and twist them together. Pin them in place just above the bobby pins you used in step 4. This will create a subtle bump in your hair and add some dimension to your hairstyle.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray
Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep it in place all day. You can also add some hair accessories like a flower crown or a veil to complete your bridal look.

Step by Step

Step 1: Prep your hair.
Step 2: Curl your hair.
Step 3: Tease the crown.
Step 4: Section your hair.
Step 5: Pin the hair.
Step 6: Finish with hairspray.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage.
  • If you have fine hair, use a volumizing mousse to add body to your locks.
  • Tease the crown of your head to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Twist and pin small sections of hair to create dimension and a subtle bump in your hairstyle.
  • Add hair accessories like a flower crown or a veil to complete your bridal look.

FAQ

Q: Can I do half up half down bridal hair with short hair?
A: Yes, you can still do half up half down bridal hair with short hair. Use bobby pins to secure the sections of hair in place and add hair extensions if you want more length.

Q: How long does it take to do half up half down bridal hair?
A: It usually takes about 30 minutes to do half up half down bridal hair, depending on your hair length and texture.

Q: Do I need to wash my hair before doing half up half down bridal hair?
A: Yes, it's best to start with clean, dry hair to achieve the best results.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair before and after styling it. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to do half up half down bridal hair, you can create a beautiful and romantic hairstyle for your wedding day. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hair accessories and styles to find the perfect look for you.
