How to Decide What Hair Color Suits You

Before selecting a hair color, it is essential to consider your skin tone, natural hair color, and eye color. These factors play a crucial role in determining which hair color will suit you. Generally, there are four skin tones â fair, medium, olive, and dark.

Fair skin tones look best with cool tones such as platinum blonde, ash blonde, and light brown. Medium skin tones look great with warm colors such as caramel, honey, and golden brown. Olive skin tones suit rich colors such as chocolate brown, dark auburn, and deep burgundy. Dark skin tones look best with bold colors such as jet black, espresso brown, and dark red.

Another factor to consider is your natural hair color. If you have warm undertones such as red or orange, you should choose warm colors such as golden blonde or copper. If you have cool undertones such as blue or green, you should go for cool colors such as ash blonde or platinum blonde.

Lastly, consider your eye color. If you have blue or green eyes, you should go for warm colors such as honey blonde or caramel. If you have brown or hazel eyes, you can opt for either warm or cool colors.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to decide what hair color suits you:

  1. Determine your skin tone â fair, medium, olive, or dark.
  2. Identify your natural hair color â warm or cool undertones.
  3. Consider your eye color â blue or green, brown or hazel.
  4. Research different hair colors that suit your skin tone, natural hair color, and eye color.
  5. Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best hair color for you.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you decide what hair color suits you:

  • Try on a wig or use a virtual hair color app to see what different colors look like on you.
  • Consider your lifestyle and maintenance when selecting a hair color â lighter colors require more upkeep.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with hair colors â it's only hair and can be changed if you don't like it.
  • Take care of your hair by using quality hair care products and minimizing heat styling.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to decide what hair color suits you:

What hair color suits fair skin?

Fair skin tones look best with cool tones such as platinum blonde, ash blonde, and light brown.

What hair color suits medium skin?

Medium skin tones look great with warm colors such as caramel, honey, and golden brown.

What hair color suits olive skin?

Olive skin tones suit rich colors such as chocolate brown, dark auburn, and deep burgundy.

What hair color suits dark skin?

Dark skin tones look best with bold colors such as jet black, espresso brown, and dark red.

Hair Care

After coloring your hair, it is essential to take care of it to maintain the color and avoid damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for color-treated hair.
  • Avoid frequent washing, as this can strip the color.
  • Minimize heat styling and use heat protectant products.
  • Deep condition regularly to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently decide what hair color suits you best. Remember to consider your skin tone, natural hair color, and eye color before making a decision. And most importantly, have fun experimenting with different hair colors!
