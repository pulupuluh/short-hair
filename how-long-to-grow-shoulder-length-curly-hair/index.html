How Long Does It Take To Grow Shoulder Length Curly Hair

Written by Robby Jul 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Curly hair can be a challenge to grow out, especially if you're hoping to achieve shoulder length. But with patience and proper hair care, it is possible to grow your curly locks to this length.

Description

Shoulder length curly hair is typically about 12-14 inches long, depending on the individual's height and hair type. Hair grows an average of 0.5 inches per month, so it can take anywhere from 2-3 years to achieve this length.

However, there are ways to speed up the growth process and maintain healthy hair along the way.

How to Grow Shoulder Length Curly Hair

The key to growing shoulder length curly hair is consistency. It's important to maintain a healthy hair care routine and avoid damaging your hair with harsh chemicals or heat styling.

Here are some tips for growing your curly locks to shoulder length:

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Start with a healthy scalp. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp clean and moisturized.
  2. Trim your hair regularly. This may seem counterintuitive, but regular trims can actually promote hair growth by preventing split ends and breakage.
  3. Avoid heat styling. Heat can damage your hair and slow down the growth process. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant and keep the temperature low.
  4. Protect your hair while sleeping. Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage while you sleep.
  5. Stay hydrated. Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair and scalp healthy.
  6. Eat a balanced diet. Nutrients like protein, iron, and vitamins A and C are essential for healthy hair growth.

Tips for Growing Shoulder Length Curly Hair

In addition to the steps above, there are some other tips that can help speed up the growth process:

  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood flow and stimulate hair growth.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, as this can lead to breakage.
  • Consider using hair growth products like biotin or castor oil.

FAQs

Q: How long does it take to grow shoulder length curly hair?

A: It can take anywhere from 2-3 years to grow shoulder length curly hair, depending on your hair type and growth rate.

Q: Can I speed up the growth process?

A: While you can't make your hair grow faster, you can promote healthy growth by maintaining a healthy hair care routine, staying hydrated, and eating a balanced diet.

Q: Should I use hair growth products?

A: Hair growth products like biotin or castor oil can help promote healthy hair growth, but it's important to talk to your doctor or a hair care professional before starting any new products.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair can be delicate and prone to dryness, so it's important to use products that are specifically formulated for curly hair.

Here are some tips for caring for your curly locks:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can lead to breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With patience and proper care, you can grow your curly hair to shoulder length and beyond. Remember to be gentle with your hair and avoid damaging it with harsh chemicals or heat styling, and you'll be on your way to luscious, healthy curls in no time!
