How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers And Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to give a haircut to a man, you may be wondering how to cut men's hair with clippers and scissors. While it may seem intimidating, cutting men's hair can be a simple and rewarding experience, especially if you follow the right steps. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of cutting men's hair with clippers and scissors, including tips and tricks on how to get the best results.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers and Scissors

Step 1: Gather the Right Tools

The first step in cutting men's hair is to gather the right tools. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. Make sure your clippers are sharp and have a variety of guards to choose from.

Step 2: Prepare the Hair

Before you start cutting, it's important to prepare the hair. Wet the hair with the spray bottle and comb it through to remove any tangles. This will make it easier to cut and ensure a more even result.

Step 3: Start with the Clippers

Begin by using the clippers to trim the sides and back of the head. Choose a guard size that matches the length you want and run the clippers in an upward motion against the hair growth. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to lift the hair and make sure you're cutting evenly.

Step 4: Blend with Scissors

Once you've finished with the clippers, it's time to blend the hair with scissors. Use scissors to trim the top of the head, cutting against the hair growth to create texture and movement. Blend the top with the sides and back by cutting diagonally across the hair, using the comb to lift and guide the hair.

Step 5: Clean up the Edges

Once you've finished cutting the hair, it's time to clean up the edges. Use the clippers or scissors to trim around the ears and the neckline. You can also use scissors to trim any stray hairs that are sticking out.

Tips for Cutting Men's Hair with Clippers and Scissors

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results when cutting men's hair with clippers and scissors:

  • Choose the right guard size for the length you want
  • Work slowly and carefully, taking your time to ensure an even result
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and create a guide for your scissors or clippers
  • Blend the top with the sides and back for a seamless result
  • Use scissors to create texture and movement in the hair
  • Always clean up the edges to ensure a polished finish

FAQ about Cutting Men's Hair with Clippers and Scissors

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair with clippers and scissors:

Can I cut men's hair with just clippers?

Yes, you can cut men's hair with just clippers, but using scissors to blend and texture the hair will give you a better result.

What guard size should I use for a buzz cut?

For a buzz cut, start with a #1 guard size and go shorter if desired.

How often should I cut men's hair?

It depends on the length and style of the hair, but generally every 4-6 weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Hair Care Tips for Men

After you've finished cutting men's hair with clippers and scissors, it's important to take care of the hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few hair care tips for men:

  • Wash hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition hair to keep it soft and manageable
  • Avoid using too much product, which can weigh down the hair
  • Get regular trims to maintain the style and keep split ends at bay

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to confidently cut men's hair with clippers and scissors, creating a polished and professional result every time. Happy cutting!
