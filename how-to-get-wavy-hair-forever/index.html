How To Get Wavy Hair Forever A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Jul 09, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're someone who loves wavy hair, you may be wondering if it's possible to get those effortless, beachy waves permanently. The good news is, it is! In this article, we'll walk you through how to get wavy hair forever, including tips, tricks, and hair care advice. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Wavy Hair Forever
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Wavy Hair

How to Get Wavy Hair Forever

First things first, it's important to understand that getting wavy hair permanently requires some effort and commitment. You may need to change your hair care routine, try new products, and even invest in some tools. Here are some steps you can take to get wavy hair forever:

1. Get a Perm

The most popular and effective way to get wavy hair permanently is by getting a perm. A perm is a chemical treatment that uses heat to break and reform the bonds in your hair, creating a new texture. There are different types of perms available, so make sure to talk to your stylist about the best option for you.

2. Use a Curling Iron

If you don't want to commit to a perm, you can still achieve wavy hair permanently by using a curling iron. Invest in a high-quality curling iron with a ceramic or tourmaline barrel, which will help to reduce heat damage. Use the curling iron to create loose, beachy waves, and then set the style with a strong hold hairspray.

3. Try a Beach Wave Perm

A beach wave perm is a type of perm that creates loose, natural-looking waves, similar to what you would get from spending a day at the beach. This type of perm is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, effortless style. Talk to your stylist about getting a beach wave perm.

Step-by-Step Guide

To help you get wavy hair forever, here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue. This will help the perm or curling iron to work better.

2. Apply a Protective Product

Apply a heat protectant or perm protector to your hair to protect it from damage.

3. Get a Perm or Use a Curling Iron

Either get a perm or use a curling iron to create loose waves.

4. Set the Style

Set the style with a strong hold hairspray or a setting lotion.

Tips for Maintaining Wavy Hair

Once you have wavy hair, you'll want to take care of it to make sure it lasts. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

FAQs

Can I get wavy hair permanently?

Yes, you can get wavy hair permanently by getting a perm or using a curling iron.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 3-6 months, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of it.

Can I still straighten my hair if I have a perm?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair if you have a perm, but it's important to use a heat protectant and avoid doing it too often.

Hair Care for Wavy Hair

Finally, here are some tips for taking care of wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too often, as this can cause frizz.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

We hope this guide has helped you learn how to get wavy hair forever. Remember, it's important to take care of your hair to make sure your waves stay beautiful and healthy. Good luck!
