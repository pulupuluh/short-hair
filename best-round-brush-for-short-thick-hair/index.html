Best Round Brush For Short Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 10, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have short, thick hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it. But with the right tools, you can achieve any look you want. One of the most essential tools for styling short, thick hair is a round brush. In this guide, we'll explore the best round brushes for short, thick hair, and how to use them to achieve the perfect hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Round Brush for Short Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Using a Round Brush on Short Thick Hair
  • Tips for Using a Round Brush on Short Thick Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Thick Hair

Description

A round brush is a must-have tool for anyone who wants to create a variety of hairstyles. It's perfect for adding volume, creating curls or waves, and smoothing out frizz. But not all round brushes are created equal, especially when it comes to short, thick hair. You need a brush that can grip the hair firmly and evenly distribute heat, without causing damage or breakage.

How to Choose the Best Round Brush for Short Thick Hair

When choosing a round brush for short, thick hair, there are a few key factors to consider:

Size

Choose a brush that's the right size for your hair length. For short hair, a small to medium-sized brush is ideal, as it will give you more control over your styling.

Bristles

Look for a brush with natural bristles, such as boar or horsehair. These bristles are gentler on the hair and help to distribute natural oils, leaving your hair looking shiny and healthy. Avoid synthetic bristles, as they can cause damage and breakage.

Shape

A round brush with a slightly curved shape is best for short, thick hair. This will help you create volume and movement, without making your hair look too stiff or unnatural.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using a Round Brush on Short Thick Hair

Here's how to use a round brush on short, thick hair:

Step 1: Wash and condition your hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not soaking wet.

Step 2: Apply heat protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from heat damage.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into manageable sections, using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work with.

Step 4: Start at the roots

Take a section of hair and place the brush at the roots. Roll the brush away from your scalp, using a gentle pulling motion. Keep rolling the brush until you reach the ends of your hair.

Step 5: Apply heat

Use a hair dryer on a medium heat setting to dry your hair as you roll the brush. Focus the heat on the brush, not the hair, to avoid overheating and damaging your hair.

Step 6: Repeat

Repeat this process on all sections of your hair, until you've achieved the desired style.

Tips for Using a Round Brush on Short Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your round brush:

  • Always use a heat protectant to prevent heat damage.
  • Don't use too much tension on your hair, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a hair dryer with a nozzle attachment to direct the heat onto the brush.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time to ensure that each section is properly dried and styled.
  • Experiment with different brush sizes and shapes to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a round brush on wet hair?

A: It's best to use a round brush on damp hair, as wet hair is more prone to breakage.

Q: How often should I clean my round brush?

A: You should clean your round brush at least once a week, using a mild shampoo or soap.

Q: Can I use a round brush on short hair?

A: Yes, a round brush is perfect for styling short hair, as it allows you to create volume and movement.

Hair Care Tips for Short Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep your short, thick hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With the right round brush and a few simple styling techniques, you can achieve any hairstyle you want, even with short, thick hair. Follow these tips and tricks to get the most out of your round brush and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
