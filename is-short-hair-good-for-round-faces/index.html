Is Short Hair Good For Round Faces

Written by Aprilia Aug 27, 2023 Â· 8 min read

When it comes to haircuts, there are so many styles to choose from. However, if you have a round face, you may be wondering if short hair is a good option for you. The good news is that short hair can be extremely flattering on round faces. In this article, we will explore the reasons why short hair is a great choice for those with round faces, as well as some tips on how to style and care for short hair.

Table of Contents

  • Why Short Hair is Good for Round Faces
  • How to Style Short Hair for Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide for Short Haircuts for Round Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair on Round Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair on Round Faces

Why Short Hair is Good for Round Faces

Short hair is a great option for those with round faces because it can help to elongate the face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. Additionally, short hair can draw attention to other features, such as the eyes or cheekbones, rather than the roundness of the face.

Another benefit of short hair for round faces is that it can be low-maintenance and easy to style. Short haircuts require less product and styling time, which can be a plus for those with busy lifestyles.

How to Style Short Hair for Round Faces

When it comes to styling short hair for round faces, there are a few tips to keep in mind. Firstly, consider adding volume at the crown of the head to create the illusion of a longer face. This can be achieved through blow drying or using volumizing products.

Another styling tip for short hair on round faces is to avoid blunt cuts. Instead, opt for layers and texture to add dimension and movement to the hair. This can help to create a more balanced and flattering look.

Step-by-Step Guide for Short Haircuts for Round Faces

If you are considering a short haircut for your round face, here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the look:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best short haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Wash and condition your hair with products that are appropriate for your hair type.
  3. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant product.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume at the crown of the head.
  5. Use a styling product, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to add definition and texture to the hair.
  6. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair on Round Faces

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep the style looking fresh and polished. Here are a few tips for maintaining short hair on round faces:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain the shape of the haircut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage the hair and cause split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant product.

FAQs

Q: Can short hair make a round face look even rounder?

A: While it is possible for a poorly executed short haircut to make a round face look even rounder, a properly styled short haircut can actually help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

Q: What is the best short haircut for a round face?

A: The best short haircut for a round face will depend on factors such as hair type, personal style, and the individual's facial features. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best short haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care for Short Hair on Round Faces

While short hair can be low-maintenance, it still requires proper care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips for caring for short hair on round faces:

  • Use products that are appropriate for your hair type, such as a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner for dry hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently, as this can damage the hair and cause split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant product.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or other moisturizing product to keep the hair healthy and hydrated.

Overall, short hair can be a great option for those with round faces. With the right styling and maintenance, a short haircut can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced and flattering look. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best short haircut for your face shape and hair type, and be sure to follow proper hair care practices to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
