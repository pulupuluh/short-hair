Does Short Hair Look Good On Small Faces

Written by Frank Apr 15, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Short hair has been a trend for years now, and it's not showing any signs of slowing down. But for those with small faces, there's always a lingering question: does short hair look good on them? The answer is yes, short hair can look amazing on small faces! In this article, we'll explore how to choose the right short hairstyle for your small face, and how to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Small Face

When it comes to choosing the right short hairstyle for a small face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to choose a hairstyle that will complement your face shape. Second, you want to consider your hair texture and thickness. Here are some tips to help you choose the right short hairstyle for your small face:

Consider Your Face Shape

Small faces can come in different shapes, including round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. When choosing a short hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to go for a hairstyle that adds height and length to your face, such as a pixie cut or a short bob with volume on top. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any short hairstyle, so feel free to experiment!

Think About Your Hair Texture and Thickness

Another important factor to consider when choosing a short hairstyle is your hair texture and thickness. If you have thin hair, you may want to go for a hairstyle that adds volume and texture, such as a layered bob or a short shag. If you have thick hair, you can go for a more structured, angular hairstyle, such as an A-line bob or a blunt cut.

Step by Step Guide: How to Style Short Hair for Small Faces

Now that you've chosen the right short hairstyle for your small face, it's time to learn how to style it! Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to styling short hair is to wash it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type and texture. Rinse thoroughly and towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or a styling cream, to your hair while it's still damp. This will help to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to blow dry your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use the brush to add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a styling tool, such as a flat iron or a curling iron, to create your desired style. You can go for a sleek and straight look, or add curls or waves for a more textured look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your hairstyle with a light hairspray to hold it in place. Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair on Small Faces

Short hair on small faces can look amazing, but it does require some maintenance to keep it looking great. Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hair:

Get Regular Trims

Short hair tends to grow out quickly, so it's important to get regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Use the Right Products

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type and texture. Avoid using products that are too heavy, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Protect Your Hair from Heat

When using styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

FAQs

Q: Can short hair make small faces look even smaller?

A: No, short hair can actually help to elongate small faces and make them appear more proportional.

Q: What short hairstyle is best for a small face?

A: The best short hairstyle for a small face depends on your face shape and hair texture. Consult with your hairstylist to find the right hairstyle for you.

Q: Is it easy to style short hair?

A: Yes, short hair is generally easier to style than long hair. It requires less time and effort to wash, dry, and style.

Hair Care for Short Hair on Small Faces

Short hair on small faces can look amazing, but it does require some maintenance to keep it looking great. Use the right products for your hair type and texture, get regular trims, and protect your hair from heat damage. With the right care, you can keep your short hair looking stylish and healthy!
