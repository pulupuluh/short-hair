How To Cut Short Curly Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a fresh, new look, a short curly bob might be just what you need. Not only is it stylish, but it's also low-maintenance and perfect for any season. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut a short curly bob in relaxed English language. You'll learn everything from the tools you'll need to the proper technique to achieve a beautiful, bouncy bob.

What You'll Need

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Blow dryer

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then, use a blow dryer to dry it completely. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair into four equal parts: two at the front and two at the back. Clip each section to keep them separate.

Step 3: Cut the Back Section

Start with the back section. Use your comb to pull a small section of hair straight down, and then cut it off at the desired length. Repeat this process, working your way up the back of your head.

Step 4: Cut the Side Sections

Once you've finished the back, move on to the side sections. Take a small section of hair from the front of the section, and cut it off at the desired length. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way towards the back.

Step 5: Cut the Front Sections

Finally, move on to the front sections. Use your comb to pull a small section of hair straight down, and then cut it off at the desired length. Repeat this process for the other front section.

Step 6: Blend and Style

Once you've finished cutting, use your comb to blend the layers together. Then, use your blow dryer to style your hair as desired.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed
  • Use your fingers to twist and curl sections of hair before cutting for a more natural look
  • Don't cut too much at once - you can always go back and trim more if needed
  • Consider visiting a professional stylist if you're unsure about cutting your own hair

FAQ

Can I cut a short curly bob on my own?

Yes, you can cut a short curly bob on your own. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the proper technique.

What's the best length for a short curly bob?

The best length for a short curly bob depends on your face shape and personal preference. Generally, a bob that falls just below the chin is a good starting point.

How often should I trim my short curly bob?

You should trim your short curly bob every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your short curly bob looking its best, make sure you use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools. Finally, consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your curls healthy and strong.
