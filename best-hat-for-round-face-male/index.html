Best Hat For Round Face Male Top Picks And Tips

Written by Eveline Apr 03, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hat for your round face? Look no further - we've got you covered! In this post, we'll explore the best hat styles for round-faced men and provide tips on how to choose the perfect one. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Hat for Round Face Male
  • Top Picks for Best Hat for Round Face Male
  • Tips on Wearing Hats for Round-Faced Men
  • FAQs About Hats for Round-Faced Men

How to Choose the Best Hat for Round Face Male

Choosing the right hat for your face shape can be a daunting task, especially if you have a round face. But don't worry - it's not as difficult as it seems. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hat for your round face:

1. Consider the Shape of the Hat

The shape of the hat is crucial when it comes to choosing the right one for a round face. Look for hats that have a structured or angular shape, such as fedoras, newsboy caps, or bucket hats. These styles will help create the illusion of a longer, more angular face.

2. Pay Attention to the Brim

The brim of the hat can also make a big difference in how it looks on a round face. Look for hats with a wider brim, as they will help balance out the proportions of your face. Avoid hats with a narrow brim, as they can make your face look rounder.

3. Don't Be Afraid to Try Different Styles

Just because a certain hat style is recommended for round-faced men, it doesn't mean you can't try other styles. Experiment with different shapes, sizes, and colors to find the hat that suits you best. You never know - you might find a style that you never thought would look good on you!

Top Picks for Best Hat for Round Face Male

Now that you know what to look for when choosing a hat for your round face, let's take a look at some of the best styles:

1. Fedora

A fedora is a classic hat style that looks great on round-faced men. Its structured shape and wide brim help create the illusion of a longer face. Plus, it's versatile enough to be dressed up or down.

2. Newsboy Cap

A newsboy cap is another great option for round-faced men. Its structured shape and short brim help balance out the proportions of your face. Plus, it adds a touch of sophistication to any outfit.

3. Bucket Hat

If you're looking for a more casual option, a bucket hat is a great choice. Its wide brim and relaxed shape make it perfect for outdoor activities or a day at the beach.

Tips on Wearing Hats for Round-Faced Men

Now that you know which hat styles work best for round-faced men, here are some tips on how to wear them:

1. Wear Your Hat at an Angle

Wearing your hat at a slight angle can help create the illusion of a longer face. Experiment with different angles to find the one that works best for you.

2. Avoid Pulling Your Hat Too Far Down

Pulling your hat too far down can make your face look rounder. Instead, wear your hat slightly higher up on your head.

3. Keep the Rest of Your Outfit Simple

When wearing a hat, it's best to keep the rest of your outfit simple. Let the hat be the statement piece and avoid wearing too many accessories or bold patterns.

FAQs About Hats for Round-Faced Men

Q: Can round-faced men wear baseball caps?

A: Yes, but it's best to choose a baseball cap with a structured shape and a slightly curved brim. Avoid wearing the cap backwards, as this can make your face look rounder.

Q: Can round-faced men wear beanie hats?

A: Yes, but it's best to choose a beanie hat that sits slightly higher on your head. Avoid wearing it too far down, as this can make your face look rounder.

Hair Care for Round-Faced Men

Finally, it's important to consider your hair when choosing a hat. Here are some tips on how to style your hair to complement your round face:

1. Keep Your Hair Short

Short haircuts tend to work best for round-faced men, as they help create the illusion of a longer face. Opt for a style with some texture or volume on top to add height.

2. Avoid Center Parts

Avoid center parts as they can make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for a side part or a messy, textured style.

3. Consider Facial Hair

If you're a fan of facial hair, consider growing a beard or goatee. This can help add some definition to your face and balance out the proportions.

And there you have it - our top picks and tips for the best hat for round face male. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hat that makes you feel confident and comfortable. Happy hat shopping!
