How To Make Men s Hair Really Curly A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Mar 02, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair is a popular trend for men, but not everyone is blessed with natural curls. Fortunately, there are ways to create curly hair without resorting to perms or chemical treatments. In this article, we'll walk you through how to make men's hair really curly.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Men's Hair Really Curly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Men's Hair Really Curly
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Men's Hair Really Curly

If you want to make your hair really curly, there are several methods you can try. The most popular methods include using a curling iron, rollers, or braiding your hair.

However, if you want to avoid using heat or damaging your hair, there are natural methods you can try as well. These methods involve using hair products and techniques to enhance your hair's natural texture and create curls.

Products for Making Men's Hair Really Curly

Here are some products that can help you create curly hair:

  • Curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner
  • Curl-defining cream or mousse
  • Sea salt spray
  • Styling gel

These products work by adding moisture, defining curls, and providing hold to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how you can make your hair really curly:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to your hair.
  3. Towel-dry your hair, but do not rub it aggressively.
  4. Apply a curl-defining cream or mousse to your hair.
  5. Divide your hair into sections and twist each section into a small bun.
  6. Secure the buns with bobby pins or hair clips.
  7. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.
  8. Once your hair is dry, release the buns and use your fingers to tousle your curls.
  9. Apply a sea salt spray or styling gel to hold your curls in place.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

This method works best for medium to long hair.

Tips for Making Men's Hair Really Curly

Here are some additional tips to help you make your hair really curly:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Avoid brushing your hair once it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to enhance your hair's natural texture.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or hair dryer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about making men's hair really curly:

Can I make my hair really curly without using heat?

Yes, you can use natural methods such as braiding or twisting your hair to create curls. You can also use curl-enhancing products to enhance your hair's natural texture.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying out your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

To keep your curly hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to add moisture.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons or curling irons too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Making men's hair really curly is possible with the right products and techniques. Whether you prefer natural methods or using heat, there are many ways to create curls that look great and last all day. With these tips, you'll be rocking curly hair in no time!
