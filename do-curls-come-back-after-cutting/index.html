Do Curls Come Back After Cutting

Written by Eveline Aug 23, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Cutting your hair can be a big decision, especially if you have curly hair. You may be wondering if your curls will come back after cutting. In this article, we will discuss whether or not curls come back after cutting and provide tips on how to care for your curls post-haircut.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curly hair can be unpredictable, and it can be hard to know how your curls will react to a haircut. In general, curls will always grow back, but they may not come back in the same way as before.

The type of haircut you get can also affect how your curls grow back. If you get a short haircut, your curls may appear tighter and more defined. If you get a longer haircut, your curls may appear looser and less defined.

How to

If you want to ensure that your curls come back after cutting, there are a few things you can do.

  • Choose a stylist who is experienced with curly hair.
  • Communicate clearly with your stylist about the type of haircut you want and how much length you want to retain.
  • Ask your stylist to cut your hair while it's dry to ensure that your curls are properly shaped.

Step by step

Here are the steps to take to ensure that your curls come back after cutting:

  1. Research stylists in your area who specialize in curly hair.
  2. Book a consultation with your chosen stylist to discuss your haircut goals.
  3. Bring photos of hairstyles you like and photos of your hair when it was at its best.
  4. Communicate clearly with your stylist about the type of haircut you want and how much length you want to retain.
  5. Ask your stylist to cut your hair while it's dry to ensure that your curls are properly shaped.

Tips

Here are some tips to care for your curls post-haircut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can create frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when they're wet.
  • Apply a curl-defining product to damp hair to help enhance your curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about whether or not curls come back after cutting:

Will my curls come back if I get a short haircut?

Yes, your curls will come back after a short haircut, but they may appear tighter and more defined than before.

Will my curls come back if I get a long haircut?

Yes, your curls will come back after a long haircut, but they may appear looser and less defined than before.

How long does it take for curls to grow back after cutting?

The rate at which your hair grows back will depend on your genetics and overall health. On average, hair grows about 0.5 inches per month.

Hair Care

Caring for your curls post-haircut is important to ensure that they grow back healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Protect your curls while you sleep by using a silk or satin pillowcase.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, curls will always grow back after cutting, but they may not come back in the same way as before. By choosing an experienced stylist, communicating clearly about your haircut goals, and following proper hair care techniques, you can ensure that your curls come back healthy and strong.
