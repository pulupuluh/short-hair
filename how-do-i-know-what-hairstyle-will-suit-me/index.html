How Do I Know What Hairstyle Will Suit Me

Written by Daniel Jun 19, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? Choosing a new hairstyle can be overwhelming, but with a few tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair type, and personal style. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding a hairstyle that will flatter your features and boost your confidence.

Table of Content

  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How To

Before you start browsing through magazines or Pinterest for hairstyle inspiration, it's important to determine your face shape. Your face shape will play a crucial role in finding a hairstyle that flatters your features.

There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and stand in front of a mirror. Use a lipstick or eyeliner to trace the outline of your face. Once you've traced your face, step back and look at the shape you've drawn. This will give you a better idea of your face shape.

Face Shapes and Hairstyles

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to find a hairstyle that complements your features. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Oval: Oval faces are well-balanced, so you can experiment with different hairstyles. Long layers, curly hair, and a side-swept bang will look great on you.
  • Round: Round faces should aim for hairstyles that elongate the face. Long layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and waves will flatter your features.
  • Square: Square faces have strong jawlines, so soft layers and waves will soften your features. A side part and a long bob will also look great on you.
  • Heart: Heart-shaped faces have a wider forehead and a narrow chin. A chin-length bob, side-swept bangs, and layers around the face will complement your features.
  • Diamond: Diamond faces have a narrow forehead and chin and wide cheekbones. Short hairstyles, pixie cuts, and bobs will look great on you.
  • Oblong: Oblong faces are longer than they are wide, so you should aim for hairstyles that add width to your face. A shoulder-length bob, bangs, and curls will look great on you.

Step by Step

Now that you know your face shape and the general guidelines for choosing a hairstyle, it's time to narrow down your options. Here are some steps you can follow:

  1. Consider your hair type: Is your hair straight, curly, or wavy? Is it thick or thin? Knowing your hair type will help you find a hairstyle that works with your natural texture.
  2. Think about your lifestyle: Are you a busy mom who doesn't have much time to style your hair? Or do you work in a corporate environment where you need to look polished? Consider your lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle that fits your daily routine.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist: If you're still unsure about which hairstyle to choose, schedule a consultation with a hairstylist. They can give you personalized recommendations based on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.
  4. Try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools: If you're not ready to commit to a new hairstyle, try on wigs or use virtual try-on tools. This will give you a better idea of how you'll look with a certain hairstyle without making a permanent change.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a new hairstyle:

  • Don't be afraid to try something new, but also consider your personal style.
  • Consider the maintenance of the hairstyle. Will you need to style it every day, or can you let it air dry?
  • Take into consideration the length of the hairstyle. Short hair requires more frequent trims, while long hair requires more maintenance.
  • Accessorize your hairstyle with headbands, clips, or hats to add some variety.

FAQ

What is the most flattering hairstyle?

The most flattering hairstyle depends on your face shape, hair type, and personal style. However, a long layered cut with side-swept bangs is a universally flattering hairstyle that works for most face shapes.

How can I make my hair look thicker?

To make your hair look thicker, try adding layers or opting for a shorter hairstyle. You can also use volumizing products and blow-dry your hair upside down to create more volume.

How often should I get a haircut?

How often you should get a haircut depends on the length of your hair and how fast it grows. As a general rule, short hair should be trimmed every 4-6 weeks, while longer hair can go 6-8 weeks between trims.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a fun and exciting experience. By considering your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident. Remember to consult with a hairstylist if you're unsure, and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
