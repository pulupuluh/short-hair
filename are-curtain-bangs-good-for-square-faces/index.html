Are Curtain Bangs Good For Square Faces

Written by Aprilia Aug 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a square face shape, you may be wondering if curtain bangs are a good option for you. Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that can be flattering on many different face shapes, but what about square faces? In this article, we'll explore whether or not curtain bangs are a good choice for those with square face shapes.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Curtain Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs on a Square Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curtain Bangs on a Square Face

Description

Curtain bangs are a style of bangs that are parted in the middle and swept to the sides, framing the face. They're called "curtain" bangs because they resemble the way curtains hang on a window. This style of bangs can be flattering on many different face shapes, but some people may wonder if they're a good choice for those with square faces.

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline and a forehead that is roughly the same width as the jawline. If you have a square face shape, you may want to choose a hairstyle that softens these features and adds some balance to your face.

How to Get Curtain Bangs

If you're interested in trying out curtain bangs, the first step is to find a stylist who is experienced in creating this style. You'll want to bring in some pictures of the type of curtain bangs you're looking for, as well as any other hair inspiration you have.

Your stylist will likely start by sectioning off the hair at the front of your head and cutting the bangs to the desired length. They may use texturizing techniques to add movement and texture to the bangs. Once the bangs are cut, your stylist will likely style them by blow-drying them with a round brush and using a flat iron to add some bend to the ends.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're interested in trying out curtain bangs at home, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Section off the hair at the front of your head that you want to turn into bangs.
  2. Cut the bangs to the desired length, starting longer and trimming as needed.
  3. Use texturizing scissors or a razor to add movement and texture to the bangs.
  4. Blow-dry the bangs with a round brush to add volume and smoothness.
  5. Use a flat iron to add a slight bend to the ends of the bangs.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs on a Square Face

If you have a square face shape, there are a few things to keep in mind when styling curtain bangs:

  • Make sure the bangs are long enough to soften the jawline. If the bangs are too short, they may emphasize the squareness of the face.
  • Consider adding some layers to the rest of the hair to create more movement and balance.
  • When styling the bangs, try to avoid creating a harsh center part. Instead, part the bangs slightly off-center to create a more flattering look.

FAQs

Can curtain bangs make a square face look softer?

Yes, curtain bangs can help soften the features of a square face shape. The bangs can create a more rounded appearance and add some balance to the face.

Are curtain bangs high maintenance?

Curtain bangs may require more maintenance than other styles of bangs. They may need to be trimmed more frequently to maintain their shape, and they may require styling with a round brush and flat iron to look their best.

Can curtain bangs work with any hair type?

Curtain bangs can work with many different hair types, but they may require different styling techniques. Those with curly or thick hair may need to use a diffuser when blow-drying their bangs, while those with fine hair may need to use a volumizing product to add some body.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs on a Square Face

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage the hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep the bangs looking their best.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add shine and hydration to the hair.

In conclusion, curtain bangs can be a good choice for those with square face shapes. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look with your curtain bangs.
