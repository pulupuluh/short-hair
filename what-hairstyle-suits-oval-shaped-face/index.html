What Hairstyle Suits Oval Shaped Face A Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 14, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have an oval shaped face, then you are quite lucky as you can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task as there are so many options available. In this article, we will guide you through what hairstyle suits oval shaped face and provide you with some tips on how to style it. Let's begin!

Description

An oval shaped face is considered to be the most versatile face shape when it comes to hairstyles. It is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, with prominent cheekbones. This face shape can pull off almost any hairstyle, from long to short, straight to curly.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Oval Shaped Face

When choosing a hairstyle for an oval shaped face, consider the following:

  • Length: Oval faces can pull off any length, but the key is to find the perfect length that flatters your features.
  • Texture: Oval faces can handle any texture, from straight to curly, so choose the one that suits your personality and style.
  • Layers: Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, so consider adding layers to your hairstyle.
  • Parting: Oval faces can pull off any parting, so experiment with different partings to find the one that suits you best.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here is a step by step guide to styling your hair for an oval shaped face:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement to your hair.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or straightener to add texture to your hair.
  5. Finish off your hairstyle with a hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair in between washes.

FAQs

1. Can oval faces pull off bangs?

Yes, oval faces can pull off bangs, but it is important to choose the right type of bangs that flatter your features. Consider getting side-swept bangs or curtain bangs.

2. What is the best hairstyle for oval faces?

The best hairstyle for oval faces is a hairstyle that flatters your features and suits your personality and style. Consider trying out different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

3. Can oval faces pull off short hair?

Yes, oval faces can pull off short hair, but it is important to choose the right length and style that flatters your features.

Hair Care for Oval Shaped Face

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of your hair.

Now that you know what hairstyle suits oval shaped face, go ahead and experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best. With the right hairstyle, you can enhance your features and look your best!
