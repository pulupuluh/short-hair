How To Know What Bangs Look Good On You

Description

Bangs are a great way to change up your hairstyle without doing anything drastic. However, not all bangs are created equal. Depending on your face shape, hair type, and personal style, certain types of bangs may look better on you than others. In this article, we'll go over how to know what bangs look good on you so you can rock the perfect fringe.

How to

Knowing what bangs look good on you requires taking into account several factors. Here are some things to consider:

  • Your face shape: Different face shapes suit different types of bangs. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid blunt, straight-across bangs and opt for side-swept or wispy bangs instead.
  • Your hair type: Bangs can require a lot of upkeep, so you'll want to choose a style that works well with your hair type. If you have curly hair, for example, you may want to avoid bangs that need to be straightened every day.
  • Your personal style: Bangs can be a great way to express your personal style, so think about what type of look you're going for. If you want a more edgy look, you may want to consider choppy or asymmetrical bangs.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to figuring out what bangs look good on you:

  1. Determine your face shape: Look in the mirror and determine whether your face is round, oval, square, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped.
  2. Research bangs styles: Look online or in magazines for bangs styles that suit your face shape and hair type. Make note of the ones you like.
  3. Consult with a stylist: Bring your list of preferred bang styles to a stylist and ask for their opinion. They can help you choose a style that will work well with your face shape and hair type.
  4. Consider your lifestyle: Think about how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling your bangs. Choose a style that you can realistically maintain.
  5. Try it out: Once you've chosen a style, get your bangs cut and see how they look. If you're not happy with the result, don't be afraid to go back to the stylist and make adjustments.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for figuring out what bangs look good on you:

  • Consider your forehead size: If you have a large forehead, you may want to choose bangs that help to minimize it, such as side-swept bangs.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment: Hair grows back, so if you're not happy with your bangs, you can always try a different style.
  • Use Pinterest for inspiration: Pinterest is a great resource for finding bangs styles that suit your face shape and hair type. Create a board of your favorite styles to show your stylist.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about figuring out what bangs look good on you:

Q: Can anyone pull off bangs?

A: While not everyone may be able to pull off all types of bangs, there is usually a style that will work for most people. It's all about finding the right fit for your face shape and hair type.

Q: Do bangs require a lot of upkeep?

A: Depending on the style, bangs can require a lot of upkeep. If you're considering getting bangs, make sure you're willing to put in the time and effort to style them every day.

Hair Care

Once you've figured out what bangs look good on you, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking great. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your bangs regularly: Bangs can get oily quickly, so make sure to wash them every few days.
  • Blow-dry your bangs: Use a round brush and blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to go.
  • Use dry shampoo: If your bangs start to look oily between washes, use a dry shampoo to freshen them up.
  • Trim your bangs regularly: To keep your bangs looking neat and tidy, get them trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the perfect set of bangs that suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style.
