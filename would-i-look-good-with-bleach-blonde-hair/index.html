Would I Look Good With Bleach Blonde Hair

Written by Oliver Jun 27, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're considering changing your hair color to bleach blonde, you might be wondering if it will suit you. It's a bold and dramatic change that requires careful consideration. In this article, we'll answer some common questions and provide tips on how to achieve and maintain bleach blonde hair.

Description

Bleach blonde hair is a light, almost white shade of blonde that is achieved by using bleach or a high-lift hair color. It can be a striking look that complements fair skin tones and light eyes. However, it can also be a challenging color to maintain and can cause damage to your hair if not done correctly.

How to Achieve Bleach Blonde Hair

If you're committed to going bleach blonde, we recommend visiting a professional hairstylist. Bleaching is a complex chemical process that can cause damage to your hair if not done correctly. A professional will be able to assess your hair's condition and advise you on the best approach.

Here are the general steps for achieving bleach blonde hair:

Step 1: Consultation

Your hairstylist will assess the condition of your hair and discuss your desired outcome. They will also advise you on the best approach for your hair type.

Step 2: Bleaching

The hairstylist will apply bleach to your hair, starting at the ends and working their way up to the roots. They will monitor the bleach's progress and rinse it off when your desired level of lightness is achieved.

Step 3: Toning

After bleaching, your hair may have a yellow or brassy tone. The hairstylist will apply toner to neutralize these tones and achieve the desired shade of blonde.

Step 4: Conditioning

Bleaching can be damaging to your hair, so it's essential to condition it thoroughly. Your hairstylist will apply a deep conditioning treatment to restore moisture and prevent breakage.

Tips for Maintaining Bleach Blonde Hair

Bleach blonde hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use Sulfate-Free Shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Bleached hair is more vulnerable to heat damage. Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons as much as possible. If you must use them, protect your hair with a heat protectant spray.

Tip 3: Regular Trims

Bleached hair is prone to split ends and breakage. Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent damage from spreading.

FAQs

Q: Will bleach blonde hair suit me?

A: It depends on your skin tone and eye color. Bleach blonde hair tends to suit fair skin tones and light eyes. However, it's always best to consult with a hairstylist before making a drastic change.

Q: How often should I touch up my bleach blonde hair?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how noticeable the roots are. On average, you should touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can bleach blonde hair damage my hair?

A: Yes, bleach can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly. It's essential to visit a professional hairstylist and use conditioning treatments to minimize damage.

Hair Care for Bleach Blonde Hair

Bleach blonde hair requires extra care to keep it looking and feeling healthy. Here are some hair care tips:

Tip 1: Use a Weekly Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a weekly deep conditioning treatment to restore moisture and prevent breakage.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to fade. Wear a hat or use a UV-protecting hair product when spending time in the sun.

Tip 3: Avoid Chlorine

Chlorine can cause bleach blonde hair to turn green. If you're swimming in a pool, wear a swim cap or rinse your hair thoroughly after swimming.

Conclusion

If you're considering going bleach blonde, it's essential to take the time to consider if it will suit you and how to maintain it. Consult with a professional hairstylist and follow our tips to keep your bleach blonde hair looking healthy and vibrant.
