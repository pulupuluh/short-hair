Description

A rectangular face shape is characterized by a long and narrow appearance. Men with this face shape have a prominent forehead, a strong jawline, and a square chin. Finding the right haircut can be challenging for men with rectangular faces as it's important to find a style that balances the proportions and creates a more rounded appearance.

In this guide, we'll explore the best men's haircut for rectangular faces and provide step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the look. We'll also share tips on how to choose the right haircut and answer some frequently asked questions about men's haircuts.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for rectangular faces, it's important to determine your face shape. Here are the steps:

  1. Use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Write down the measurements.
  3. Compare the measurements to determine which is the widest.

Step-by-Step Guide

The best men's haircut for rectangular faces is one that adds volume to the sides and top of the head while keeping the sides short. This creates a more rounded appearance and balances the proportions of the face. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the look:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's damp.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots and lengths of your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the top and sides.
  4. Use a comb to create a side part, and comb the hair on top of your head towards the opposite side of the part.
  5. Use scissors or clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. Keep the length short, but not too short, to create a more rounded appearance.
  6. Trim the hair on top of your head to the desired length. Keep the length longer than the sides to add volume and create a more rounded appearance.
  7. Use a styling wax or pomade to add texture and hold to your hair. Apply the product to the roots and lengths of your hair, and use your fingers to style it into place.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the best men's haircut for rectangular faces:

  • Avoid haircuts that are too short on the sides and back as they can emphasize the rectangular shape of your face.
  • Choose haircuts that add volume to the sides and top of the head to create a more rounded appearance.
  • Consider adding bangs or a fringe to soften the forehead and create a more balanced appearance.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long and flat as they can make your face look even longer.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a rectangular face?

The best haircut for a rectangular face is one that adds volume to the sides and top of the head while keeping the sides short. This creates a more rounded appearance and balances the proportions of the face.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a rectangular face?

Avoid haircuts that are too short on the sides and back as they can emphasize the rectangular shape of your face. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too long and flat as they can make your face look even longer.

Should I add bangs to my haircut if I have a rectangular face?

Adding bangs or a fringe can soften the forehead and create a more balanced appearance for men with rectangular faces. However, it's important to choose the right length and style to avoid making your face look even longer.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Maintaining healthy hair is essential for achieving the best men's haircut for rectangular faces. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking and feeling its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these tips and choosing the right men's haircut for your rectangular face, you can achieve a balanced and stylish look that complements your features.
