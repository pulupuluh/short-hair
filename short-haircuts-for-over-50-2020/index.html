Short Haircuts For Over 50 In 2020 A Guide To Looking Fabulous

If you're over 50 and looking for a new hairstyle, there are plenty of options available to you. Short haircuts are a great choice for women over 50, as they can be low-maintenance and stylish at the same time. In this post, we'll be exploring some of the best short haircuts for over 50 in 2020, along with tips on how to style and care for your new do.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for You

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. A good stylist can help you find a cut that flatters your features and suits your lifestyle. Here are some popular short haircuts for over 50 in 2020:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well for women of all ages. This style is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. It's a great choice for women with thick hair, as it can help to lighten up the weight of the hair. Pixie cuts are also low-maintenance and easy to style.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular short haircut for women over 50. This style is typically chin-length or shorter, with a straight or slightly angled cut. Bobs can be worn with or without bangs and can be styled straight or wavy. They're a great choice for women with fine hair, as they can create the illusion of volume.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great way to add movement and texture to short hair. This style features layers of varying lengths, which can be styled in a variety of ways. Layered cuts work well for women with thin or fine hair, as they can help to create the illusion of fullness.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Styling short hair can be quick and easy, as long as you have the right tools and products. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your new short haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair.
  3. Using a flat iron or curling iron, style your hair as desired.
  4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray or hair oil to add shine and hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Short haircuts are low-maintenance, but they still require some upkeep to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your new do:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and style.

FAQ about Short Haircuts for Over 50

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. A well-chosen short haircut can actually make you look younger and more vibrant.

Q: Can I wear a short haircut if I have curly hair?

A: Absolutely! Short haircuts can work well for women with curly hair, as long as they're cut to suit your hair texture and face shape.

Q: How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?

A: It's a good idea to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs some TLC to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With the right short haircut and some basic hair care, you can look fabulous and feel confident at any age. So go ahead and take the plunge â you won't regret it!
