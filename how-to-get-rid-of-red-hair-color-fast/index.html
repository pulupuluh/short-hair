How To Get Rid Of Red Hair Color Fast Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 22, 2023 · 9 min read

Red hair color is a beautiful and bold choice, but it can be difficult to maintain. If you're ready for a change, you might be wondering how to get rid of red hair color fast. Fortunately, there are several methods you can use to fade or remove red hair color quickly and safely. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of getting rid of red hair color, step by step.

How to Get Rid of Red Hair Color Fast: Description

Red hair color is one of the most difficult colors to remove from your hair. This is because red hair dye molecules are larger and more complex than other colors, making them more difficult to break down. However, there are several methods you can use to fade or remove red hair color quickly and safely. The best method for you will depend on how much color you want to remove, how long you've had the color, and the condition of your hair.

How to Get Rid of Red Hair Color Fast: How To

There are several methods you can use to get rid of red hair color fast. Here are some of the most effective:

1. Use a Clarifying Shampoo

Clarifying shampoo can help to strip away some of the red hair color from your hair. Look for a clarifying shampoo that contains sulfates, which can help to remove color. Wash your hair with the clarifying shampoo several times, leaving it in for 5-10 minutes each time. Rinse thoroughly with warm water.

2. Use Baking Soda and Shampoo

Baking soda is a natural clarifying agent that can help to remove red hair color. Mix equal parts baking soda and shampoo, and apply the mixture to your hair. Leave it on for 5-10 minutes, then rinse thoroughly with warm water.

3. Use Vitamin C

Vitamin C can help to fade red hair color. Crush up several vitamin C tablets and mix them with a small amount of water to form a paste. Apply the paste to your hair, covering all areas with red hair color. Leave it on for 30 minutes, then rinse thoroughly with warm water.

How to Get Rid of Red Hair Color Fast: Step-By-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting rid of red hair color:

Step 1: Choose a Method

Decide which method you want to use to get rid of your red hair color. Consider the condition of your hair, how much color you want to remove, and how long you've had the color.

Step 2: Gather Your Supplies

Gather all of the supplies you'll need for the method you've chosen. This may include clarifying shampoo, baking soda, shampoo, vitamin C tablets, and water.

Step 3: Prepare Your Hair

Before you begin, make sure your hair is dry and tangle-free. Comb through your hair to ensure there are no knots or tangles.

Step 4: Apply the Method

Apply the method you've chosen to your hair. Follow the instructions carefully, and make sure to cover all areas with red hair color.

Step 5: Rinse Thoroughly

Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water. Make sure to remove all of the product from your hair.

Step 6: Condition Your Hair

After removing the red hair color, your hair may be dry and damaged. Use a deep conditioning treatment to restore moisture and nourishment to your hair.

How to Get Rid of Red Hair Color Fast: Tips

Here are some tips to help you get rid of red hair color fast:

  • Choose a method that is appropriate for your hair type and condition.
  • Be patient – it may take several treatments to completely remove red hair color.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment after removing the red hair color to restore moisture and nourishment to your hair.

How to Get Rid of Red Hair Color Fast: FAQ

Can I use bleach to remove red hair color?

Bleach can be used to remove red hair color, but it can be very damaging to your hair. We recommend using a gentler method, such as clarifying shampoo, baking soda, or vitamin C.

How long will it take to remove red hair color?

The length of time it takes to remove red hair color will depend on several factors, including the method you use, the condition of your hair, and how much color you want to remove. It may take several treatments to completely remove red hair color.

How to Get Rid of Red Hair Color Fast: Hair Care

After removing the red hair color from your hair, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent further damage. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid heat styling tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, as much as possible.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use heat styling tools.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals, such as hair dye and bleach.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to restore moisture and nourishment to your hair.

With these tips and methods, you can safely and effectively get rid of red hair color fast. Remember to be patient and take care of your hair to prevent further damage. Good luck!
