What Is A Medium Fade Haircut

Written by Bardi Apr 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, you may want to consider a medium fade haircut. This particular hairstyle has been gaining popularity over the years and can give you a fresh and modern look. But what exactly is a medium fade haircut? In this blog post, we'll explain everything you need to know about this hairstyle, from its definition to how to maintain it.

Description

A medium fade haircut is a hairstyle that features a gradual fade from the sides to the top of the head. The hair on the sides and back of the head is trimmed short, while the hair on the top of the head is left longer. The transition from the short sides to the longer top is gradual, creating a faded effect. The length of the hair on the top can vary, but typically, it's around 2-3 inches long. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, textured, or messy.

How to Get a Medium Fade Haircut

If you're interested in getting a medium fade haircut, you'll need to visit a barber or hairstylist who specializes in men's haircuts. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get a medium fade haircut:

Step 1: Choose the Right Barber

Look for a barber or hairstylist who has experience in cutting men's hair and specifically, fades. You want someone who can give you a clean and precise cut.

Step 2: Decide on the Length of the Hair on Top

You'll need to decide on the length of the hair on top of your head. If you're not sure, your barber can help you choose a length that suits your face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Decide on the Type of Fade

There are different types of fades, including low, medium, and high fades. For a medium fade haircut, you'll want to ask for a fade that starts around the temples and gradually blends into the longer hair on top.

Step 4: Let the Barber Do Their Magic

Once you've made your decisions, sit back and let your barber do their magic. They'll use clippers and scissors to create the fade and trim the hair on top. They may also use a razor to create a clean and precise hairline.

Tips for Maintaining a Medium Fade Haircut

Once you've got your medium fade haircut, you'll want to maintain it to keep it looking fresh. Here are some tips:

  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups. Depending on how fast your hair grows, you may need to get it trimmed every 2-4 weeks.
  • Use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and clean.
  • Style your hair with a pomade or wax to create the desired look.
  • Brush or comb your hair regularly to prevent tangles and maintain its shape.

FAQs

1. Is a medium fade haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a medium fade haircut can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. Your barber can adjust the length and style of the hair on top to suit your hair type and texture.

2. Can I style my medium fade haircut in different ways?

Yes, a medium fade haircut is versatile and can be styled in various ways, depending on your preference. You can slick it back, create a side part, or add texture and volume.

3. Will a medium fade haircut require a lot of maintenance?

Like any haircut, a medium fade haircut will require some maintenance to keep it looking fresh. You'll need to visit your barber regularly for touch-ups and use good-quality hair products to style and maintain your hair.

Hair Care for a Medium Fade Haircut

A medium fade haircut doesn't require any specific hair care routine, but it's important to keep your hair healthy to maintain its shape and style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Trim any split ends regularly to prevent damage to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock your medium fade haircut with confidence and style!
