How To Style Hair For Round Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Mar 12, 2023 · 8 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect hairstyle that flatters your round face? Look no further! We have put together a comprehensive guide on how to style hair for round faces. From choosing the right haircut to styling tips, we’ve got you covered. Read on to learn more.

Table of Contents

  • What is a Round Face?
  • Best Haircuts for Round Faces
  • Hairstyling Tips for Round Faces
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

What is a Round Face?

A round face is characterized by having a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The width and length of the face are approximately the same, with a soft jawline. Celebrities with round faces include Chrissy Teigen, Emma Stone, and Mila Kunis.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces

When it comes to haircuts for round faces, the key is to create angles and length. Here are some of the best haircuts for round faces:

Long Layers

Long layers that frame the face can elongate a round face shape. The layers should start near the cheekbones and continue down to the ends of the hair. This cut works well for both straight and wavy hair.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to balance out a round face shape. The bangs should be longer on one side, and should start at the middle of the forehead. This cut works well for both short and long hair.

Bob with Face-Framing Layers

A bob with face-framing layers can create the illusion of a longer face shape. The layers should be longer in the front and shorter in the back. This cut works well for straight and wavy hair.

Asymmetrical Pixie

An asymmetrical pixie can create angles and length in a round face shape. The longer side of the pixie should be on the opposite side of the face from the fuller cheek. This cut works well for straight and wavy hair.

Hairstyling Tips for Round Faces

Now that you have the perfect haircut, it’s time to style your hair to flatter your round face. Here are some hairstyling tips for round faces:

Add Volume on Top

Adding volume on top of the head can elongate a round face shape. Use a volumizing mousse or root lifter to add volume to the crown of the head.

Use a Deep Side Part

A deep side part can create the illusion of a longer face shape. Use a comb to create a deep side part, and tuck the hair behind the ear on the smaller side of the part.

Avoid Center Parts

Avoid center parts, as they can make a round face shape look wider. Instead, opt for a side part.

Try Beachy Waves

Beachy waves can add texture and movement to the hair, and can also create the illusion of a longer face shape. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as blunt bobs and blunt bangs. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make the face appear wider.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a round face. However, avoid pulling the hair back too tightly, as this can accentuate the roundness of the face. Instead, opt for a loose, messy ponytail.

Should I color my hair if I have a round face?

Coloring your hair can add dimension and create angles in a round face shape. However, avoid coloring your hair a solid, dark color, as this can make the face appear wider. Instead, opt for highlights or lowlights to add dimension.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair and a flattering hairstyle. Here are some hair care tips for round faces:

Use a Heat Protectant

Using a heat protectant before using heat styling tools can prevent damage to the hair and maintain healthy hair.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair every 6-8 weeks can prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

Avoid Overwashing

Washing your hair too often can strip the hair of its natural oils and lead to dry, damaged hair. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Now that you have all the tips and tricks on how to style hair for round faces, go ahead and experiment with different haircuts and hairstyles to find the perfect look for you. Remember, the most important thing is to feel confident and beautiful in your own skin.
