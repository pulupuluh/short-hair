How To Determine What Hairstyle Suits You A Complete Guide

Are you tired of constantly trying new hairstyles only to be disappointed with the results? Determining what hairstyle suits you can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this article, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to determine what hairstyle suits you best.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in determining what hairstyle suits you best is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, square, round, heart, diamond, and oblong. Knowing your face shape will help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.

To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Pull your hair back and take a close-up photo of your face.
  2. Print the photo or upload it to your computer.
  3. Use a ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  4. Compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is longer than it is wide, with a slightly rounded jawline and forehead. This face shape is considered the most versatile, as it can pull off a variety of hairstyles.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong, angular jawline and a forehead that is roughly the same width as the jawline. The cheekbones are also prominent in this face shape.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape is characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. The width and length of the face are roughly the same.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape has a wider forehead and cheekbones, with a narrower jawline and chin.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and chin, with wide cheekbones.

Oblong Face Shape

An oblong face shape is longer than it is wide, with a narrow forehead and jawline. The cheekbones are not as prominent in this face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Determine Your Ideal Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to determine your ideal hairstyle. Follow these steps:

  1. Consider your hair type. Is it straight, wavy, curly, or coily?
  2. Think about your lifestyle. Do you have time to style your hair every day, or do you need something low-maintenance?
  3. Consider your personality. Are you trendy and edgy, or classic and conservative?
  4. Look for inspiration. Browse magazines, social media, and celebrity hairstyles for ideas.
  5. Consult with a hairstylist. A professional stylist can help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and suits your lifestyle.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can be overwhelming, but these tips can help:

  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape.
  • Consider your hair type and texture.
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you have to style your hair.
  • Choose a hairstyle that fits your personality and personal style.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new and different.

FAQs about Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Q: Can I pull off a short hairstyle?

A: Short hairstyles can be flattering on any face shape, but it's important to choose a style that flatters your features and suits your lifestyle.

Q: Can I change my hairstyle if I don't like it?

A: Of course! Hair grows back, so don't be afraid to experiment with different styles.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on your hair type and style. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle

Once you've chosen your ideal hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain healthy ends.
  • Use hair products, such as hairspray and mousse, to enhance your hairstyle and hold it in place.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips and guidelines, you can determine what hairstyle suits you best and maintain healthy, beautiful hair. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you!
