Best Haircuts For Thick Coarse Straight Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick, coarse, and straight hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it can be easier to manage and style than other hair types. On the other hand, it can be difficult to find the right haircut that flatters your face shape and tames your unruly locks. In this article, we'll explore the best haircuts for thick coarse straight hair, along with tips on hair care and styling. So, let's get started!

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Your Hair Type

When it comes to choosing the best haircut for your hair type, there are a few things to consider. First and foremost, you'll want to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to opt for a longer haircut that frames your face and elongates your features. If you have a square face, a layered haircut with soft edges can help soften your features.

Another important factor to consider is your hair texture. Thick, coarse, and straight hair can be difficult to manage, so you'll want to choose a haircut that works with your natural texture. For example, a blunt bob may not work well with thick hair, as it can make your hair look even thicker and bulkier. Instead, you may want to opt for a layered haircut that thins out your hair and adds movement.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a haircut, let's go over some steps to ensure you get the perfect cut:

  1. Research haircuts that flatter your face shape and work with your hair texture.
  2. Bring pictures of haircuts you like to your hair appointment.
  3. Communicate clearly with your stylist about what you want and don't want.
  4. Ask for layers to thin out your hair and add movement.
  5. Consider getting a side-swept fringe to frame your face.
  6. Ask your stylist to remove bulk from your hair to prevent it from looking too heavy.

Tips for Styling Thick Coarse Straight Hair

Once you have the perfect haircut, you'll want to know how to style it to make the most of your thick, coarse, and straight hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a round brush to smooth out your hair and add a gentle curl at the ends.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair, such as oils and serums.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQs About Haircuts for Thick Coarse Straight Hair

What is the best haircut for thick coarse straight hair?

The best haircut for thick coarse straight hair depends on your face shape and personal style. However, some popular haircuts for this hair type include layered haircuts, long bobs, and shag haircuts.

What should I avoid when getting a haircut for thick coarse straight hair?

Avoid getting a blunt haircut, as it can make your hair look even thicker and bulkier. You should also avoid getting a haircut that is too short, as it can make your hair look too heavy and difficult to manage.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Coarse Straight Hair

Thick, coarse, and straight hair can be prone to dryness and damage, so it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can lead to breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut for your thick, coarse, and straight hair. Remember to take good care of your hair and style it to suit your face shape and personal style. Happy styling!
