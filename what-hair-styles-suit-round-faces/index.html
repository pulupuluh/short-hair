What Hair Styles Suit Round Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 18, 2023 · 9 min read

When it comes to hair styling, one of the most important factors to consider is the shape of your face. Round faces, in particular, require a bit of extra attention, as certain styles can either accentuate or minimize the roundness. In this article, we’ll provide a comprehensive guide on what hair styles suit round faces, complete with how-to instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How-To: Finding the Perfect Hair Style for Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step: How to Style Hair for Round Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Hair Styles for Round Faces
  • FAQs About Hair Styles for Round Faces
  • Hair Care Advice for Round Faces

Description

Round faces are typically characterized by their soft, curved lines and lack of sharp angles. The goal of hair styling for round faces is to create the illusion of length and angles to balance out the natural roundness. This can be achieved through a variety of techniques, including layering, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts.

How-To: Finding the Perfect Hair Style for Round Faces

The first step in finding the perfect hair style for a round face is to determine your face shape. This can be done by measuring the width and length of your face and comparing the two. If your face is roughly the same length as it is wide, you likely have a round face.

Once you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to start exploring hair styles that will complement it. Some popular choices for round faces include:

  • Long, layered cuts
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical cuts
  • Angular bobs
  • High ponytails

While these styles are generally flattering for round faces, it’s important to keep in mind that everyone’s hair texture and personal style is unique. What works for one person may not work for another, so don’t be afraid to experiment with different cuts and styles until you find the perfect match.

Step-by-Step: How to Style Hair for Round Faces

The key to styling hair for round faces is to create the illusion of length and angles. Here’s a step-by-step guide for achieving the perfect look:

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair with a round brush to add volume and create a smooth base.
  2. If you have bangs, consider sweeping them to the side to create a diagonal line that elongates the face.
  3. If you’re going for a long, layered look, ask your stylist to add layers that start below the chin to create the illusion of length.
  4. If you prefer a shorter cut, consider an angular bob with longer layers in the front to create a slimming effect.
  5. When styling your hair, avoid adding volume to the sides of your head, as this can accentuate roundness. Instead, focus on adding volume to the top of your head to create height and balance.
  6. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension, which can also help to elongate the face.
  7. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place without weighing it down.

Tips for Maintaining Hair Styles for Round Faces

Once you’ve found the perfect hair style for your round face, it’s important to maintain it with regular trims and styling. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Invest in high-quality hair care products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage hair and lead to breakage.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • If you have color-treated hair, use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

FAQs About Hair Styles for Round Faces

Here are a few frequently asked questions about hair styles for round faces:

Q: Can short hair work for round faces?

A: Yes! Short hair can be a great option for round faces, as long as it’s cut in a way that creates length and angles. Consider an angular bob or a pixie cut with longer layers in the front to elongate the face.

Q: What type of bangs are best for round faces?

A: Side-swept bangs are generally the most flattering for round faces, as they create a diagonal line that elongates the face. Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, as these can accentuate roundness.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Absolutely! High ponytails can be a great option for round faces, as they create the illusion of height and elongation. Just be sure to avoid pulling the hair too tight on the sides, as this can accentuate roundness.

Hair Care Advice for Round Faces

When it comes to hair care for round faces, the same rules apply as for any other face shape. Here are a few tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip away natural oils and lead to dryness.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently remove tangles and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray first.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth and prevent breakage.

Overall, finding the perfect hair style for a round face requires a bit of experimentation and willingness to try new things. However, with the right cut, styling, and hair care routine, anyone can achieve a look that flatters their unique features and brings out their natural beauty.
