How To Thin Thick Short Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Aug 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While thick hair can be voluminous and luxurious, it can also be difficult to manage and style. If you have thick short hair, you may find that it can be overwhelming and hard to style. In this article, we will show you how to thin thick short hair to make it more manageable and easier to style.

Description

Thick short hair can be difficult to manage and style because it can be heavy and overwhelming. Thinning your hair can help to reduce the weight and volume of your hair, making it more manageable and easier to style. Thinning your hair can also give it a more textured and layered look, which can be perfect for short hairstyles.

How to Thin Thick Short Hair

There are several ways to thin thick short hair. You can use thinning scissors, razor cutting, or texturizing shears to thin your hair. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue. This will make it easier to cut and thin your hair.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Dry your hair completely before you begin cutting. This will help you to see the natural texture and shape of your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to cut and thin your hair evenly.

Step 4: Thin Your Hair

Use thinning scissors, razor cutting, or texturizing shears to thin your hair. Start at the top of your head and work your way down to the ends. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Step 5: Blend Your Hair

Blend your hair by cutting the ends of your hair in a zigzag motion. This will give your hair a more textured and layered look.

Tips for Thinning Thick Short Hair

Thinning your hair can be tricky, so here are some tips to help you:

  • Use thinning scissors, razor cutting, or texturizing shears
  • Start at the top of your head and work your way down to the ends
  • Be careful not to cut too much hair at once
  • Blend your hair by cutting the ends in a zigzag motion
  • Take your time and be patient

FAQ

1. Can I thin my hair at home?

Yes, you can thin your hair at home using thinning scissors, razor cutting, or texturizing shears. However, it is important to be careful and patient, as thinning your hair can be tricky.

2. How often should I thin my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should thin your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and texture.

3. Can thinning my hair damage it?

Thinning your hair can damage it if you cut too much hair at once or use the wrong tools. It is important to use thinning scissors, razor cutting, or texturizing shears and to be careful and patient when thinning your hair.

Hair Care for Thinned Hair

After thinning your hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to maintain your hair's shape and texture
  • Use hair masks and treatments to nourish your hair

Thinning your thick short hair can be a great way to make it more manageable and easier to style. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a more textured and layered look that is perfect for short hairstyles. Remember to be patient, take your time, and take care of your hair properly to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
