How To Turn Up A Dress Without Sewing A Beginner s Guide

Written by Aprilia Mar 03, 2023 · 6 min read

If you’ve ever bought a dress that’s too long for you, you know how frustrating it can be. You can’t wear it without tripping over the hem, but you don’t want to go through the hassle of taking it to a tailor or sewing it yourself. Luckily, there’s a simple solution: turning up the dress without sewing. In this article, we’ll show you how to do it step by step, along with some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

How to Turn Up a Dress Without Sewing

Turning up a dress without sewing is easy and quick. Here’s how to do it:

Step 1: Gather Your Materials

You’ll need the following materials:

  • A dress that needs to be shortened
  • Fabric glue or hemming tape
  • Scissors
  • Measuring tape

Step 2: Measure the Length

Put on the dress and use the measuring tape to determine how much you want to shorten it. Mark the spot with a pin or a chalk.

Step 3: Cut the Excess Fabric

Take off the dress and use the scissors to cut the excess fabric. Be careful not to cut too much.

Step 4: Apply the Glue or Hemming Tape

Apply fabric glue or hemming tape along the hemline. Make sure to apply the glue or tape evenly.

Step 5: Fold the Hem Up

Fold the hem up along the hemline, using the pin or chalk mark as a guide. Press the hem down firmly to make sure it sticks to the glue or tape.

Step 6: Let It Dry

Let the dress dry for at least an hour before wearing it. This will ensure that the glue or tape sets properly.

Tips for Turning Up a Dress Without Sewing

Here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Make sure to measure the length of the dress accurately. You don’t want it to be too short or too long.
  • Use a ruler or a straight edge to make sure the hem is even.
  • Be careful not to get glue or tape on the front of the dress.
  • If you’re using hemming tape, make sure to follow the instructions on the package.

FAQ

Can you turn up any dress without sewing?

Most dresses can be turned up without sewing, as long as they have a hemline that can be folded up. However, dresses made of delicate fabrics such as silk or chiffon may require sewing to avoid damaging the fabric.

How long does the glue or tape last?

The glue or tape should last for several washes. However, it’s always a good idea to check the hem after each wash to make sure it hasn’t come undone.

Can you turn up a dress that has a lining?

Yes, you can turn up a dress that has a lining. However, you’ll need to make sure that the lining is cut to the same length as the dress.

Hair Care

After turning up a dress without sewing, you might want to style your hair to complete your look. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or applying a hair sunscreen.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach or hair dyes, as they can damage your hair.

With these tips, you’ll be able to turn up a dress without sewing and look great while doing it. It’s a quick and easy way to make an old dress feel like new again, without having to spend a lot of money or time.
