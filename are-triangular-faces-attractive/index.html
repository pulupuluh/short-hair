Are Triangular Faces Attractive

Written by Frank Aug 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Triangular faces have a unique shape that can be both attractive and challenging when it comes to styling your hair and makeup. In this article, we will explore the question "Are triangular faces attractive?" and provide tips on how to accentuate your features to look your best.

Description

A triangular face is characterized by a narrow forehead that widens at the cheeks and jawline, creating a triangular shape. This face shape is also known as a heart-shaped face. Many celebrities, including Reese Witherspoon, Jennifer Aniston, and Scarlett Johansson, have triangular faces.

How to Accentuate Your Features

If you have a triangular face, there are several ways to accentuate your features and look your best. Here are some tips:

1. Hairstyles

Choose hairstyles that add width to your forehead and temples, such as side-swept bangs or layered haircuts. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the lower half of your face, such as blunt cuts or bobs that end at your jawline.

2. Makeup

Use makeup to balance your features. Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks to add fullness to your cheekbones. Use contouring to create the illusion of a more defined jawline. Avoid heavy eye makeup that makes your eyes appear smaller.

3. Accessories

Choose accessories that draw attention to your upper half, such as statement earrings or necklaces. Avoid chokers or short necklaces that accentuate your jawline.

Step by Step Guide to Accentuate Your Features

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a tape measure or ruler to measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If your forehead is narrower than your cheeks and jawline, you have a triangular face.

Step 2: Choose Hairstyles that Add Width to Your Forehead

Choose hairstyles that add volume to your forehead and temples, such as side-swept bangs or layered haircuts. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your jawline.

Step 3: Use Makeup to Balance Your Features

Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks to add fullness to your cheekbones. Use contouring to create the illusion of a more defined jawline. Avoid heavy eye makeup that makes your eyes appear smaller.

Step 4: Choose Accessories that Draw Attention to Your Upper Half

Choose statement earrings or necklaces that draw attention to your upper half. Avoid chokers or short necklaces that accentuate your jawline.

Tips for Triangular Faces

Here are some additional tips for triangular faces:

1. Wear V-Neck Tops

V-neck tops draw attention to your upper half and create a more balanced look.

2. Avoid Heavy Bangs

Heavy bangs can make your forehead appear smaller and accentuate your jawline. Opt for side-swept bangs instead.

3. Use Highlighter

Apply highlighter to your cheekbones and temples to create the illusion of a wider forehead.

FAQs

Q: Are triangular faces attractive?

A: Yes, triangular faces can be attractive. Many celebrities, including Reese Witherspoon, Jennifer Aniston, and Scarlett Johansson, have triangular faces.

Q: What hairstyles are best for triangular faces?

A: Hairstyles that add width to your forehead and temples, such as side-swept bangs or layered haircuts, are best for triangular faces.

Q: What makeup should I use for a triangular face?

A: Use makeup to balance your features. Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks to add fullness to your cheekbones. Use contouring to create the illusion of a more defined jawline. Avoid heavy eye makeup that makes your eyes appear smaller.

Hair Care for Triangular Faces

When caring for your hair as a triangular faced person, it is essential to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny. This can be achieved by using a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using too many styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it appear flat.

Regular trims are essential to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut. If you have curly hair, use a diffuser when blow-drying to enhance your curls and add volume to your hair.

Conclusion

In conclusion, triangular faces can be attractive when styled correctly. Choose hairstyles that add width to your forehead, use makeup to balance your features, and choose accessories that draw attention to your upper half. With these tips and tricks, you can accentuate your features and look your best.
