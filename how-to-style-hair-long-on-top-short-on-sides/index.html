How To Style Hair Long On Top Short On Sides A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 14, 2023 · 7 min read

Long on top and short on sides haircuts have been a popular trend for men in recent years, and for good reason. This versatile hairstyle allows you to keep some length on top, while keeping the sides short and neat. In this guide, we’ll show you how to style your hair long on top and short on sides with ease.

Description: What is a Long on Top Short on Sides Haircut?

A long on top short on sides haircut is a men’s hairstyle that features longer hair on the top of the head and shorter hair on the sides. This style is also known as an undercut or a disconnected haircut, and it has been a popular trend for men in recent years.

How to Style Hair Long on Top Short on Sides

Styling hair long on top and short on sides is a simple process that can be achieved in a few easy steps. Follow these steps to get the perfect look:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or buildup. Make sure to towel dry your hair before you begin styling it.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Use a hair gel, pomade, or wax to create the desired look. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Using a comb or your fingers, style your hair as desired. If you have longer hair on top, you can experiment with different styles such as slicked back, messy, or a quiff. For shorter sides, use a trimmer or razor to keep them neat and tidy.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray to keep your hair in place. This will ensure that your style lasts all day long.

Tips for Styling Long on Top Short on Sides Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect long on top short on sides hairstyle:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape.
  • Use a high-quality styling product that works well with your hair type.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the perfect look.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain your haircut.
  • Use a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I style my long on top short on sides haircut without using any product?

A: While it’s possible to style your hair without using any product, it’s recommended that you use a small amount of styling product to achieve the desired look.

Q: How often should I visit my barber for a trim?

A: It’s recommended that you visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Q: Can I style my long on top short on sides haircut with curly hair?

A: Yes, you can style your hair long on top short on sides with curly hair. However, you may need to use additional product and styling techniques to achieve the desired look.

Hair Care for Long on Top Short on Sides Haircuts

Keeping your hair healthy is essential for maintaining a long on top short on sides haircut. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach or hair dyes.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and stylish, while maintaining your long on top short on sides haircut.

Now that you know how to style hair long on top short on sides, you can experiment with different styles and find the perfect look for you. Remember to use high-quality products and visit your barber regularly to maintain your haircut. With a little bit of effort, you can achieve a stylish and trendy long on top short on sides hairstyle.
