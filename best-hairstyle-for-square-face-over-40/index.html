Best Hairstyle For Square Face Over 40 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter May 28, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a square face and are over 40, you might be wondering what hairstyle would suit you best. A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a square chin. It can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features and complements your face shape. In this guide, we will walk you through the best hairstyle for square face over 40, including how to style it, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

When it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle for a square face over 40, you want to choose a style that softens your features and highlights your best assets. The ideal hairstyle should have layers, add volume to the crown, and elongate your face shape. The goal is to create a balance between the width of your jaw and the length of your face.

How To

The best hairstyle for a square face over 40 is a long bob or a shoulder-length haircut with layers. You can also add side-swept bangs to soften your forehead and elongate your face shape. Waves or loose curls add volume and create movement that can complement your features. Avoid straight hairstyles that can make your face appear wider and harsh.

Step By Step

To achieve the best hairstyle for a square face over 40, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a long bob or shoulder-length haircut with layers.
  2. Add side-swept bangs to soften your forehead and elongate your face shape.
  3. Create waves or loose curls to add volume and create movement.
  4. Avoid straight hairstyles that can make your face appear wider and harsh.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to avoid breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as it can strip it of its natural oils.

FAQ

What if I donât want to cut my hair?

If you donât want to cut your hair, you can still achieve a flattering hairstyle by adding layers or highlights. Layers can add volume to your hair and create movement, while highlights can add dimension and depth. Speak to your hairstylist about what options are available to you.

Can I wear my hair straight?

Avoid wearing your hair straight as it can make your face appear wider and harsh. If you prefer straight hair, add some loose waves or curls to soften your features.

What if I have thin hair?

If you have thin hair, you can still achieve a flattering hairstyle by adding layers and volume. Use volumizing products and a round brush to create volume at the crown. Avoid heavy products that can weigh your hair down.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy is essential to achieving the best hairstyle for a square face over 40. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day as it can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair stays healthy and looks great.

Conclusion

Finding the best hairstyle for a square face over 40 can be a challenge, but with the right style and hair care routine, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look. Remember to choose a long bob or shoulder-length haircut with layers, add side-swept bangs, and create waves or loose curls to add volume and movement. Follow our hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great.
