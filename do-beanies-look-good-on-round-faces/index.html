Do Beanies Look Good On Round Faces

Written by Bardi Jul 09, 2023 · 7 min read

Beanies are a must-have accessory for the winter season. They keep you warm and cozy, but they can also add style to your outfit. However, if you have a round face, you might be hesitant to wear a beanie. The question is, do beanies look good on round faces? In this article, we will explore this topic and provide you with tips on how to wear a beanie with a round face.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Wear a Beanie with a Round Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Beanie with a Round Face
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you might be worried that a beanie will make your face appear even rounder. However, with the right beanie and styling, you can rock this accessory without any worries.

How to Wear a Beanie with a Round Face

The key to wearing a beanie with a round face is to choose the right style. Avoid beanies that are too tight or too loose, as they can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for a beanie that fits snugly around your head, with a bit of slouch at the top. This will elongate your face and balance out the width.

Another important factor to consider is the shape of the beanie. A beanie with a wide cuff or fold can add height to your head, making your face appear longer. On the other hand, a beanie with a narrow cuff or no fold can make your face look wider. So, choose a beanie with a wide cuff or fold if you have a round face.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to wear a beanie with a round face:

  1. Choose a beanie that fits snugly around your head, with a bit of slouch at the top.
  2. Opt for a beanie with a wide cuff or fold to add height to your head.
  3. Wear the beanie slightly back on your head to elongate your face.
  4. Avoid pulling the beanie too far down over your forehead, as this can make your face look wider.
  5. Style your hair to add volume at the crown of your head, which will balance out the width of your face.

Tips for Wearing a Beanie with a Round Face

Here are some additional tips for wearing a beanie with a round face:

  • Choose a beanie in a neutral color, such as black, gray, or navy, as these colors are slimming and elongating.
  • Avoid beanies with patterns or prints, as they can make your face look wider.
  • If you want to add some color to your outfit, opt for a beanie with a pom-pom or tassel. This will draw the eye upwards, elongating your face.
  • Pair your beanie with a scarf or jacket that has a V-neckline, as this will also elongate your face.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a beanie with bangs if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a beanie with bangs if you have a round face. However, make sure to wear the beanie slightly back on your head to avoid making your forehead appear wider.

Q: Can I wear a slouchy beanie if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a slouchy beanie if you have a round face. Just make sure that the beanie fits snugly around your head, with a bit of slouch at the top.

Hair Care Tips

To complement your beanie and enhance your overall look, here are some hair care tips:

  • Style your hair with volume at the crown of your head to balance out the width of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the sides, as this can make your face look wider.
  • Consider getting layers or a side-swept fringe to frame your face and add dimension.
  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add volume to your hair.

In conclusion, beanies can look good on round faces if you choose the right style and wear it correctly. Follow the tips and guidelines provided in this article, and you'll be sure to rock your beanie this winter season.
