How To Buzz Cut Men s Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 02, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts or waiting in long lines at the barbershop? With the right tools and technique, you can easily buzz cut your own hair at home. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to achieve a professional-looking buzz cut in the comfort of your own home.

Table of Contents

  • Tools Needed
  • Preparation
  • Buzzing Your Hair
  • Finishing Touches
  • Tips for a Great Buzz Cut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After Your Buzz Cut

Tools Needed

Before you get started, you'll need to gather the following tools:

  • Clippers with guard attachments in various lengths (we recommend starting with a #4 or #5 guard)
  • A comb
  • Scissors
  • A handheld mirror or a second mirror to see the back of your head
  • A towel or cape to catch hair clippings
  • A vacuum or broom to clean up any hair clippings

Preparation

Before you begin buzzing your hair, it's important to properly prepare:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, it will be harder to cut and may result in an uneven buzz cut.
  2. Choose the right clipper guard length. If you're not sure which length to use, start with a longer guard and work your way down as needed.
  3. Set up your workspace. Find a well-lit area with a mirror and lay down a towel or cape to catch hair clippings.
  4. Put on a shirt you don't mind getting hair clippings on.

Buzzing Your Hair

Now it's time to start buzzing your hair:

  1. Attach the appropriate guard to your clippers.
  2. Start at the front of your head and work your way to the back, using the clippers to buzz your hair against the direction of growth.
  3. Move the clippers in an upward motion, going against the grain of your hair. This will ensure an even cut.
  4. Work in small sections, moving from one side of your head to the other.
  5. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers may have missed.

Finishing Touches

Now that you've buzzed your hair, it's time to finish up:

  1. Remove the guard from your clippers and carefully trim around your hairline and sideburns.
  2. Take a handheld mirror or ask someone to help you check the back of your head for any missed spots.
  3. Shower and wash your hair to remove any loose hair clippings.

Tips for a Great Buzz Cut

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great buzz cut:

  • Don't rush. Take your time and work in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down as needed.
  • Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers may have missed.
  • Check the back of your head with a handheld mirror or ask someone to help you.
  • Experiment with different guard lengths to find the perfect buzz cut for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about buzzing men's hair:

  • Can I buzz my own hair if it's curly? Yes, but be sure to use a guard that's longer than you think you'll need, as curly hair will appear shorter when it's buzzed.
  • Can I buzz my own hair if it's thinning? Yes, but be gentle and use a longer guard to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • What if I make a mistake? Don't worry, hair grows back! If you make a mistake, wait a few days and try again.

Hair Care After Your Buzz Cut

Now that you've buzzed your hair, it's important to take care of it:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Apply a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Visit a professional barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your buzz cut.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to buzz cut your own hair at home like a pro. Happy buzzing!
