How Many Millimetres Is A Number 3 Haircut

Written by Bardi Jun 09, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you planning to get a number 3 haircut but unsure about the length in millimetres? Well, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about a number 3 haircut, including its length in millimetres, how to get it, and hair care tips to maintain the style.

Description

A number 3 haircut is one of the most popular haircuts for men. It is a medium-length haircut that leaves approximately 3/8 inch or 10 millimetres of hair on the scalp. The number 3 refers to the guard size used on the clippers to achieve this length. It is shorter than a number 4 but longer than a number 2 haircut.

How to Get a Number 3 Haircut

If you want to get a number 3 haircut, you can either go to a professional barber or do it yourself at home with the help of clippers. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before starting the haircut, make sure to wash and dry your hair to remove any dirt or oil. This will also make it easier to cut the hair evenly.

Step 2: Choose the Right Guard Size

Attach the number 3 guard to your clippers. If you don't have a guard, you can use scissors to trim the hair to the desired length.

Step 3: Start from the Sides

Start cutting the hair from the sides, moving the clippers upward to create an even length. Make sure to maintain a consistent pressure and angle to avoid uneven cuts. Repeat this process on both sides.

Step 4: Move to the Back and Top

Once you have finished the sides, move to the back and top of the head. Use the same technique to trim the hair to the desired length.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

To achieve a natural look, blend the hair on the sides and back by gradually decreasing the guard size towards the neckline. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs.

Tips for Maintaining a Number 3 Haircut

Now that you have achieved the desired length, it's essential to maintain the style to keep it looking fresh. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Trim the hair every 3-4 weeks to prevent it from becoming too long.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much styling product as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Brush or comb the hair regularly to prevent tangles and knots.

FAQs

1. Is a number 3 haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a number 3 haircut is versatile and can be tailored to suit different face shapes. However, it's best to consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the most flattering style for your face shape.

2. Can I style a number 3 haircut differently?

Yes, a number 3 haircut can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, side-parted, or messy. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits your personality and lifestyle.

3. How long does it take to grow out a number 3 haircut?

It usually takes 4-6 weeks for a number 3 haircut to grow out to a medium length. However, the growth rate may vary depending on individual hair type and growth patterns.

Hair Care

A number 3 haircut is easy to maintain and requires minimal styling. However, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo to remove dirt and oil.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish and moisturize the hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently, as they can damage the hair and cause split ends.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.

Now that you know how many millimetres is a number 3 haircut, you can confidently ask your barber for the style or do it yourself at home. Follow the steps and tips mentioned in this article to achieve a stylish and low-maintenance look that suits your personality and lifestyle.
