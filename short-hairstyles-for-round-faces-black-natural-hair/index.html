Short Hairstyles For Round Faces Black Natural Hair

Are you looking for the perfect short hairstyle for your round face and natural black hair? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with tips, how-to guides, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect short hair look that flatters your round face and showcases your natural hair beauty.

Description

Short hairstyles are perfect for round faces with black natural hair. They can help to accentuate your natural beauty and make you look younger and more vibrant. However, finding the perfect short hairstyle for your round face and natural black hair can be challenging. You have to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. In this article, we will help you choose the right short hairstyle for your round face and natural black hair.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Round Faces Black Natural Hair

Choosing the right short hairstyle for your round face and natural black hair involves considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the right short hairstyle for your round face and natural black hair:

  • Consider your face shape: Round faces have full cheeks and a rounded chin. To balance out your face shape, choose a short hairstyle that adds height and volume to the crown of your head.
  • Consider your hair texture: Natural black hair comes in different textures, from coily to straight. Choose a short hairstyle that flatters your hair texture.
  • Consider your personal style: Choose a short hairstyle that reflects your personal style and complements your wardrobe.

Step by Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Short Hairstyle for Round Faces Black Natural Hair

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect short hairstyle for your round face and natural black hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup from your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to your hair to help detangle it and protect it from heat damage.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head.
  5. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage.
  6. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or a curling iron to add waves or curls to your hair.
  7. Apply a styling gel or mousse to your hair to help hold the style in place.
  8. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  9. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle for Round Faces Black Natural Hair

Maintaining your short hairstyle for round faces black natural hair requires some effort. Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hairstyle:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as relaxers and hair dyes.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for round faces black natural hair:

  • Q: What is the best short hairstyle for round faces black natural hair?
  • A: The best short hairstyle for round faces black natural hair is a pixie cut or a short bob.
  • Q: Can I color my natural black hair if I have a short hairstyle?
  • A: Yes, you can color your natural black hair if you have a short hairstyle. However, it is important to use a gentle hair dye that does not contain harsh chemicals.
  • Q: Can I style my short hair in different ways?
  • A: Yes, you can style your short hair in different ways, such as adding waves, curls, or braids.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Round Faces Black Natural Hair

Short hairstyles for round faces black natural hair require proper hair care to maintain their shape and style. Here are some hair care tips for short hairstyles for round faces black natural hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to help detangle your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the crown of your head.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as relaxers and hair dyes.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips, how-to guides, and hair care advice, you can achieve the perfect short hairstyle for your round face and natural black hair. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing the right short hairstyle for you. With proper hair care, your short hairstyle can last for weeks and showcase your natural beauty.
