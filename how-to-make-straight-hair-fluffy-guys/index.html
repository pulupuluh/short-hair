How To Make Straight Hair Fluffy Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're a guy with straight hair, you may feel like your hair is lacking in volume and texture. Luckily, there are several techniques you can use to make your straight hair look fluffy and full. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to make straight hair fluffy guys, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • Tips for Making Straight Hair Fluffy Guys
  • How to Make Straight Hair Fluffy Guys: Step-by-Step
  • FAQs About Making Straight Hair Fluffy Guys
  • Hair Care Tips for Fluffy Straight Hair

Tips for Making Straight Hair Fluffy Guys

Before we dive into the step-by-step instructions, here are a few tips to keep in mind when trying to make straight hair look fluffy and full:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy styling products, which can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a round brush to lift and shape your hair while blow drying.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to add movement and texture to your hair.

How to Make Straight Hair Fluffy Guys: Step-by-Step

Now that you know the basics, let's get into the step-by-step process of making straight hair look fluffy and full:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, then towel dry until your hair is damp but not soaking wet.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse

Apply a golf ball-sized amount of volumizing mousse to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Blow dry upside down

Flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair using a round brush to lift and shape your hair. Focus on the roots to add volume, and work your way down to the ends. Once your hair is dry, flip your head back up and style as desired.

Step 4: Use a texturizing spray

Finish off your look by using a texturizing spray to add even more volume and texture to your hair. Spray the product throughout your hair, then use your fingers to tousle and shape your hair.

FAQs About Making Straight Hair Fluffy Guys

Can I make my hair look fluffy without using heat?

Yes! You can use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, then let your hair air dry for a more natural, fluffy look. You can also try using a texturizing spray or powder to add volume and texture without heat.

What kind of haircut should I get to add volume to my straight hair?

A layered haircut is a great option for adding movement and texture to straight hair. Talk to your stylist about getting layers that start at the chin or below to add volume and fullness.

How often should I wash my hair to keep it looking fluffy?

It's best to avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look flat. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, and use dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Fluffy Straight Hair

Now that you know how to make straight hair fluffy guys, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too frequently, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to help minimize damage.
  • Invest in a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's specifically formulated for your hair type.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, which can cause breakage and make your hair look flat.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to make straight hair look fluffy and full in no time. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
