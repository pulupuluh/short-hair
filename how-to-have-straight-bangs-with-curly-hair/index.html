How To Have Straight Bangs With Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby May 13, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have curly hair, you may think that straight bangs are out of the question. But with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the straight bangs of your dreams. In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to have straight bangs with curly hair, and provide tips for maintaining them.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Have Straight Bangs with Curly Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Straight Bangs
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Have Straight Bangs with Curly Hair

The key to achieving straight bangs with curly hair is to start with a good haircut. Make sure your stylist cuts your bangs to the length you want and leaves them slightly longer than you think you need. When your hair is curly, it tends to shrink up, so you want to account for that when cutting your bangs.

Once you have a good haircut, you’ll need to invest in some tools to help you straighten your bangs. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • A flat iron
  • A heat protectant spray
  • A comb
  • Clips or hair ties to section off your hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to straighten your curly bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow dry it before straightening.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your bangs to prevent damage.
  3. Section off your hair to make it easier to straighten. Clip or tie back the rest of your hair so it’s out of the way.
  4. Starting at the roots, slowly run the flat iron down your bangs. Use a comb to help guide your hair through the flat iron.
  5. Repeat until your bangs are straight.
  6. Release the rest of your hair and style as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Straight Bangs

Here are some tips for keeping your straight bangs looking great:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Use a dry shampoo or spray to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day, as this can cause them to become oily or frizzy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Q: Can I use a hair straightener to straighten my bangs?

A: Yes, a hair straightener (or flat iron) is the best tool for straightening curly bangs.

Q: How often should I straighten my bangs?

A: It’s best to avoid straightening your bangs every day, as this can damage your hair. Aim to straighten them 2-3 times per week.

Q: What should I do if my bangs get wet?

A: If your bangs get wet, blow dry them or use a flat iron to straighten them again.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it’s important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

By following these tips and using the right tools, you can have straight bangs with curly hair. Just remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
