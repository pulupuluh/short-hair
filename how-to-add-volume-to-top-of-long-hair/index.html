How To Add Volume To Top Of Long Hair

Written by Christine Aug 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have long hair, you know the struggle of trying to add volume to the top of your hair. It can be difficult to achieve that voluminous look, especially if your hair is naturally flat or thin. But don't worry, we have some tips and tricks to help you add volume to the top of your long hair.

Description

Adding volume to the top of your long hair can make a big difference in the overall look of your hairstyle. It can make your hair look fuller, thicker, and more voluminous. Achieving this look can be challenging, but with the right technique and products, it's definitely possible.

How to Add Volume to Top of Long Hair

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step to adding volume to your hair is to start with clean hair. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup from your hair. This will help your hair look and feel lighter, which will make it easier to add volume.

Step 2: Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Next, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products are designed to add volume to your hair, so they're essential for achieving the look you want. Apply the shampoo and conditioner to your hair, focusing on the roots.

Step 3: Apply a Volumizing Mousse

After you've washed your hair, apply a volumizing mousse to your roots. This will help lift your hair and add volume. Apply the mousse evenly throughout your hair, and use a comb to distribute it evenly.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

To add even more volume to your hair, blow dry it upside down. This will help lift your roots and add volume. Use a round brush to help smooth out your hair as you blow dry it. Make sure to focus on the roots, as this is where you want the most volume.

Step 5: Tease Your Hair

To add even more volume to your hair, tease it. Take small sections of hair at the crown of your head and backcomb them, starting at the roots and working your way down. This will create a lot of volume at the top of your hair. Be careful not to tease your hair too much, as this can damage your hair.

Tips

  • Use a volumizing spray to add even more volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as these can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make it look flat.

FAQ

What products should I use to add volume to my hair?

Use a clarifying shampoo, a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and a volumizing mousse to add volume to your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every two to three days to keep it clean and healthy.

Can I use hot tools to add volume to my hair?

Avoid using hot tools to add volume to your hair, as these can damage your hair and make it look flat.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. This includes using the right products, washing your hair regularly, and avoiding heat damage. With the right care and attention, you can have voluminous, healthy hair.
