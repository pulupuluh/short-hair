Will A Perm Bring Back My Curls

Written by Eveline May 18, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and missing your curly locks? Have you been wondering if a perm can bring back your curls? Well, you've come to the right place. In this article, we will explore the answer to the question, "will a perm bring back my curls?" We will also provide you with some tips on how to care for your permed hair and keep your curls looking amazing. So, let's get started!

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that alters the structure of your hair. It can be used to create curls, waves, and texture. The process involves applying a chemical solution to your hair that breaks down the bonds that hold your hair in its natural shape. Once the bonds are broken, your hair is wrapped around rods, and a neutralizer is applied to reform the bonds in a new shape. The result is a permanent change in the structure of your hair.

How to

If you are considering getting a perm to bring back your curls, here are the steps you can expect:

Step 1: Consultation

Before you get a perm, you should have a consultation with your stylist. They will assess the condition of your hair, determine if a perm is the right choice for you, and advise you on the best type of perm for your hair type and desired outcome.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before the perm solution is applied, your hair will be washed and dried. Your stylist will also apply a protective cream to your scalp and hairline to prevent irritation from the chemicals.

Step 3: Apply the Perm Solution

Next, the perm solution will be applied to your hair, and it will be wrapped around rods. The size of the rods will determine the size of your curls.

Step 4: Neutralize

Once your hair has been wrapped around the rods, a neutralizer will be applied to reform the bonds in a new shape. This step is crucial to set the curls.

Step 5: Rinse and Style

After the neutralizer has been applied, your hair will be rinsed and styled. Your stylist may use a diffuser to help enhance your curls and create volume.

Tips

Here are some tips to care for your permed hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet
  • Invest in a good hair oil to keep your curls shiny and healthy

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a perm to bring back your curls:

Q: How long do perms last?

A: Perms typically last between 4 and 6 months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you care for your hair.

Q: Can I color my hair after getting a perm?

A: It's best to wait at least a week after getting a perm before coloring your hair to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: Can I get a perm if my hair is damaged?

A: It's not recommended to get a perm if your hair is severely damaged as the chemicals can further damage your hair.

Hair Care

Caring for your permed hair is essential to keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized and avoid using heat styling tools. Brush your hair gently using a wide-tooth comb, and avoid brushing your hair when it's wet. Invest in a good hair oil to keep your curls shiny and healthy.

So, there you have it â the answer to the question, "will a perm bring back my curls?" Remember, getting a perm is a chemical process that can alter the structure of your hair. It's essential to take care of your permed hair to keep your curls looking amazing. Follow our tips, and you'll be on your way to beautiful, bouncy curls in no time!
