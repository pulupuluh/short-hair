What Does Purple Hair Say About A Person

Written by Robby Mar 07, 2023 · 6 min read

Table of Content:

  • How to Get Purple Hair
  • Step by Step Guide

  • FAQ About Purple Hair

Description

Having purple hair can say a lot about a person. It's a bold statement that shows they're willing to take risks and try new things. Purple hair can also be seen as a symbol of creativity and individuality. It's a way to stand out from the crowd and express oneself through their appearance.

Having purple hair can also show a person's confidence and self-assuredness. It takes a certain level of confidence to pull off such a bold, non-traditional hair color. It can also show a person's rebellious side, as purple hair is not a natural hair color and can be seen as going against societal norms.

How to Get Purple Hair

Getting purple hair can be done at home or at a salon. If you're doing it at home, it's important to choose a high-quality hair dye specifically made for coloring hair purple. It's also important to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test before applying the dye to your entire head.

For best results, it's recommended to go to a salon and have a professional colorist do your hair. They will have the knowledge and experience to create the exact shade of purple you're looking for, and can also ensure that the dye is applied evenly and doesn't damage your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get purple hair:

  1. Choose a high-quality hair dye specifically made for coloring hair purple.
  2. Do a patch test before applying the dye to your entire head.
  3. Follow the instructions carefully and apply the dye evenly to your hair.
  4. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time, then rinse it out thoroughly.
  5. Condition your hair to keep it soft and healthy.

Tips for Maintaining Purple Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your purple hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent the color from fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as heat can also cause the color to fade.
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater, as they can also cause the color to fade.
  • Touch up your roots as needed to maintain the color.

FAQ About Purple Hair

Is it safe to dye your hair purple?

Yes, it's safe to dye your hair purple as long as you use a high-quality hair dye made specifically for coloring hair. It's also important to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test before applying the dye to your entire head.

Can I dye my hair purple at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair purple at home. However, for best results, it's recommended to go to a salon and have a professional colorist do your hair.

How long does purple hair last?

Purple hair can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, depending on how well it's maintained and how often it's touched up.

Hair Care for Purple Hair

When it comes to hair care for purple hair, it's important to use products specifically designed for colored hair. This includes sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, as well as leave-in treatments and hair masks to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

It's also important to avoid using hot tools on your hair, as heat can cause the color to fade. If you do use hot tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Finally, be sure to touch up your roots as needed to maintain the color and keep your hair looking fresh and vibrant.
