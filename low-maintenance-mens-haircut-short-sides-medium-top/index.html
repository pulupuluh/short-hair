Low Maintenance Men s Haircut Short Sides Medium Top

Written by Bardi Aug 16, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you're a man who wants to look stylish without spending too much time and effort on your hair, then the low maintenance men's haircut with short sides and a medium top is the perfect option for you. This hairstyle is easy to maintain, versatile, and looks great on everyone. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get It
  • How to Style It
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Get It

The low maintenance men's haircut with short sides and a medium top is a classic style that has been around for decades. To get this look, you'll need to visit a barber or hair stylist who is experienced in cutting men's hair. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Start by telling your barber or stylist that you want a low maintenance haircut with short sides and a medium top.
  2. Ask them to use clippers to cut the sides of your hair short, leaving about an inch or less of hair on the top.
  3. Ask them to blend the sides and top together seamlessly, so there's no harsh line between them.
  4. Finally, ask them to trim the top of your hair to your desired length and style it as you like.

Once you've got your new haircut, you'll be amazed at how easy it is to maintain. All you need to do is wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a little bit of styling product to keep it looking neat and tidy.

How to Style It

The low maintenance men's haircut with short sides and a medium top is incredibly versatile, and can be styled in a variety of ways depending on your personal preferences. Here are some ideas:

  • For a classic look, comb your hair straight back using a small amount of pomade or gel.
  • For a more modern look, style your hair into a messy quiff using a texturizing spray or paste.
  • If you have curly hair, embrace your natural texture and use a leave-in conditioner to define your curls.

Remember, the key to this hairstyle is to keep it looking neat and tidy, so use as much or as little product as you need to achieve the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your low maintenance men's haircut with short sides and a medium top:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a little bit of styling product to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the low maintenance men's haircut with short sides and a medium top:

Is this haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut can be adapted to suit all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily hair.

How often do I need to get this haircut?

You should visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

What styling products should I use?

It depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Experiment with different products, such as pomade, gel, texturizing spray, and paste, to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

To keep your low maintenance men's haircut with short sides and a medium top looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly using high-quality products.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

By following these tips, you can keep your low maintenance men's haircut with short sides and a medium top looking great for weeks on end.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the low maintenance men's haircut with short sides and a medium top, why not give it a try? This versatile and stylish haircut is sure to make you look and feel your best, without having to spend hours in front of the mirror every morning.
