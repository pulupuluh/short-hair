Best Round Brush For Drying Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Apr 14, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you have short hair, you know how difficult it can be to find the right round brush for drying your hair. The wrong brush can make your hair frizzy, tangled, and unmanageable. But with so many options available, how do you know which round brush is the best for your hair type? In this article, we'll take a look at the best round brushes for drying short hair, and provide you with tips on how to use them to achieve the perfect blowout.

  • How To Choose the Best Round Brush for Drying Short Hair

Description

A round brush is an essential tool for anyone who wants to achieve a salon-quality blowout at home. It can help you create volume, smoothness, and shine in your hair, and is particularly useful for those with short hair. The right round brush can help you achieve the perfect blowout in no time, leaving your hair looking sleek, shiny, and healthy.

How to Choose the Best Round Brush for Drying Short Hair

When choosing a round brush for drying short hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Size: The size of the brush will depend on the length of your hair. For short hair, a smaller brush is usually better, as it will allow you to get closer to the roots and create more lift and volume.
  • Bristles: Look for a brush with natural boar bristles, which are gentle on the hair and help distribute natural oils, or a combination of boar and nylon bristles, which are good for detangling and adding shine.
  • Shape: A round brush with a slight curve will help you create a more natural-looking wave or curl, while a completely round brush is better for creating volume and lift.

Recommended Round Brushes for Drying Short Hair

Here are some of the best round brushes for drying short hair:

  • Denman Small Styling Brush: This brush has natural boar bristles and a ceramic-coated barrel for even heat distribution. It's perfect for creating volume and lift in short hair.
  • Olivia Garden Ceramic + Ion Thermal Brush: This brush has a ceramic-coated barrel and ionic bristles that help reduce frizz and static. It's great for creating smooth, shiny styles in short hair.
  • Moroccanoil Ceramic Round Brush: This brush has natural boar bristles and a ceramic-coated barrel infused with argan oil. It's perfect for adding shine and reducing frizz in short hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using a Round Brush for Drying Short Hair

Here's how to use a round brush to achieve the perfect blowout:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  2. Section your hair into small sections, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  3. Take one section of hair and place the round brush at the roots, pointing the nozzle of your hair dryer at the brush.
  4. Slowly pull the brush through your hair, following the brush with the dryer. Use the brush to create tension and smoothness in your hair.
  5. Repeat this process on each section of hair, working your way up to the top.
  6. Once you've dried all of your hair, use the cool shot button on your hair dryer to set the style.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Blowout with a Round Brush

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect blowout with a round brush:

  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Work in small sections, focusing on one section at a time.
  • Use the brush to create tension and smoothness in your hair, pulling the hair taut as you dry.
  • Use the cool shot button on your hair dryer to set the style and add shine.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

FAQs

Can I use a round brush on wet hair?

No, it's not recommended to use a round brush on wet hair. Wet hair is more susceptible to damage, and using a round brush on wet hair can cause breakage and split ends. Always make sure your hair is at least 80% dry before using a round brush.

What's the difference between a ceramic and metal round brush?

Ceramic round brushes are better for reducing frizz and adding shine, as the ceramic material helps distribute heat evenly. Metal round brushes are better for creating volume and lift, but can be harsher on the hair.

How often should I clean my round brush?

You should clean your round brush at least once a month, or more often if you use a lot of styling products. To clean your brush, remove any hair from the bristles and soak the brush in warm water and shampoo for 10-15 minutes. Rinse the brush thoroughly and let it air dry.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

To keep your short hair looking healthy and beautiful, here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every other day, or as needed. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and free from split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage.

With the right round brush and a little bit of practice, you can achieve the perfect blowout on your short hair. Follow these tips and tricks to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful, and enjoy the benefits of a salon-quality blowout from the comfort of your own home.
