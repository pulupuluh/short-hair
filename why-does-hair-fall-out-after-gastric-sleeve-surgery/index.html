Why Does Hair Fall Out After Gastric Sleeve Surgery

Written by Oliver Jul 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss after undergoing gastric sleeve surgery? This is a common side effect of the procedure, but why does it happen?

What is Gastric Sleeve Surgery?

Gastric sleeve surgery, also known as sleeve gastrectomy, is a weight loss surgery that involves the removal of a large portion of the stomach, leaving a narrow sleeve-shaped stomach. This reduces the amount of food a person can eat and helps them lose weight.

Why Does Hair Fall Out After Gastric Sleeve Surgery?

Hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is caused by a combination of factors, including:

  • Lack of Nutrients - After surgery, the body may not absorb nutrients as effectively, leading to deficiencies in important vitamins and minerals that are essential for healthy hair growth.
  • Rapid Weight Loss - Losing weight quickly can put stress on the body, causing hair to enter a resting phase and fall out.
  • Hormonal Changes - Hormonal changes can also contribute to hair loss after surgery.

How to Minimize Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve Surgery

While hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery is common, there are steps you can take to minimize it:

  • Take Vitamins - Talk to your doctor about taking vitamins and supplements to ensure you are getting the nutrients you need for healthy hair growth.
  • Eat a Balanced Diet - Eating a balanced diet that includes protein, fruits and vegetables can also help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid Heat Styling - Heat styling tools can damage hair and make hair loss worse, so try to avoid using them as much as possible.
  • Be Gentle - Be gentle when brushing and styling your hair to avoid pulling out hair that is already weak.

Step by Step Guide to Hair Care After Gastric Sleeve Surgery

Follow these steps to take care of your hair after gastric sleeve surgery:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep your scalp clean and healthy.

Step 2: Use Conditioner

Use a conditioner to help moisturize your hair and prevent breakage.

Step 3: Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that can put stress on your hair and scalp, such as tight braids, ponytails, and buns.

Step 4: Air Dry Your Hair

Avoid using heat styling tools and let your hair air dry to prevent further damage.

FAQ About Hair Loss After Gastric Sleeve Surgery

Q: Will my hair grow back after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: Yes, your hair will typically grow back within a few months to a year after surgery as long as you are getting the nutrients you need and taking care of your hair.

Q: Can I color my hair after gastric sleeve surgery?

A: Yes, you can color your hair after surgery as long as you are careful and avoid using harsh chemicals that can further damage your hair.

Q: How long does hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery last?

A: Hair loss typically lasts for a few months to a year after surgery and then begins to grow back.

Conclusion

Hair loss after gastric sleeve surgery can be a frustrating side effect, but it is usually temporary. By taking care of your hair and getting the nutrients you need, you can help minimize hair loss and promote healthy hair growth.
