How To Cut Shoulder Length Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Mar 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, especially if you have shoulder length curly hair. However, with the right tools, techniques, and a little patience, you can achieve a salon-worthy haircut at home. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of cutting shoulder length curly hair, including how to prepare, what tools you'll need, and step-by-step instructions for achieving the perfect cut.

Table of Contents

  1. Prepare Your Hair
  2. Gather Your Tools
  3. Section Your Hair
  4. Cut Your Hair

Prepare Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to prepare it properly. Start by washing your hair and conditioning it with a moisturizing conditioner. Towel-dry your hair gently, so as not to cause any frizz. Then, comb your hair with a wide-tooth comb to detangle it.

Gather Your Tools

Next, you'll need to gather the right tools for cutting your hair. Here's what you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clips to section your hair
  • A comb
  • A mirror

Section Your Hair

Sectioning your hair is important, especially if you're cutting it for the first time. Divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Use clips to secure each section.

Cut Your Hair

Now it's time to cut your hair. Start by cutting the back of your hair first, as it's the most difficult part to reach. Take a small section of hair from the bottom of the back section and hold it between your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair evenly. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you've cut the entire back section.

Once you've cut the back section, move on to the front sections. Take a small section of hair from the front and hold it between your fingers. Cut the hair evenly with your scissors. Continue cutting small sections of hair until you've cut both front sections.

Add Layers (Optional)

If you want to add some layers to your hair, now is the time to do it. Take small sections of hair and cut them at an angle to create layers. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Cut Your Bangs (Optional)

If you have bangs, you can cut them now. Start by sectioning your bangs and combing them straight down. Then, hold the hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.

Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, you can style it as desired. You can blow-dry your hair straight, or let it air dry for natural curls. You can also use a curling iron or flat iron to style your hair.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

  • Never cut your hair when it's wet, as it will appear shorter when it dries.
  • Always use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.
  • Be patient and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Don't be too hard on yourself if your hair doesn't turn out perfectly. It will grow back!

FAQs

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended to cut your curly hair every 3-4 months to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Should I cut my curly hair myself?

If you're comfortable with cutting your own hair, and have the right tools and techniques, it's possible to achieve a great haircut at home. However, if you're unsure or have never cut your hair before, it's best to visit a professional stylist.

Can I cut my curly hair while it's dry?

Yes, it's possible to cut your curly hair while it's dry. Dry cutting can be especially helpful for curly hair, as it allows you to see the shape of your hair better.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat tools too often. You can also use hair masks and oils to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

Now that you know how to cut shoulder length curly hair, you can achieve a beautiful haircut at home. Remember to take your time, be patient, and don't be too hard on yourself if your hair doesn't turn out perfectly. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at cutting your own hair in no time!
