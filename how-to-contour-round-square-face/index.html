How To Contour Round Square Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Contouring is a makeup technique that can help you define and enhance your facial features. However, if you have a round square face, it can be challenging to contour it effectively. In this article, we'll show you how to contour a round square face step-by-step. We'll also provide some tips and answer frequently asked questions about contouring.

Table of Contents

  • How to Contour Round Square Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Contouring
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Round Square Face

How to Contour Round Square Face

Before we start contouring, we need to understand the shape of a round square face. A round square face has a square jawline, but the rest of the face is round. So, we need to contour the jawline to make it look more angular and define the cheekbones.

Here are the basic steps for contouring a round square face:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep your skin with a moisturizer and a primer. This will help your makeup go on smoothly and last longer.

Step 2: Apply a foundation that matches your skin tone. Blend it evenly using a brush or a sponge.

Step 3: Use a contour stick or powder that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone. Apply it along the jawline, starting from the ear to the chin. Blend it well using a brush or a sponge.

Step 4: Apply the same contour shade on the temples of your forehead, blending it towards your hairline.

Step 5: Apply a highlighter shade that is one or two shades lighter than your skin tone. Apply it on the high points of your face, such as the cheekbones, the bridge of your nose, and the cupid's bow. Blend it well using a brush or a sponge.

Step 6: Apply a blush shade that complements your skin tone. Apply it on the apples of your cheeks and blend it towards your hairline.

Tips for Contouring

Here are some additional tips that can help you contour your round square face more effectively:

  • Use a matte contour shade instead of a shimmer one. Shimmer can make your face look rounder.
  • Blend well to avoid harsh lines.
  • Use a small brush to contour the nose.
  • Apply the highlighter shade sparingly to avoid a shiny, oily look.
  • Use a setting spray to lock in your makeup.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about contouring a round square face:

Q: Can I contour my face without foundation?

A: Yes, you can, but foundation helps to create a smooth base for your contouring.

Q: How often should I contour my face?

A: It depends on your preference. You can contour your face every day or only for special occasions.

Hair Care Tips for Round Square Face

Aside from makeup, you can also style your hair to flatter your round square face. Here are some tips:

  • Try a long, layered haircut to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • A side-swept fringe can help to balance out your face shape.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your jawline, as they can make your face look wider.
  • Experiment with different hair lengths and styles to find what suits you best.

With these tips and techniques, you can contour your round square face like a pro. Remember to practice and experiment to find what works best for you. With a little bit of effort, you can enhance your natural beauty and boost your self-confidence.
