How To Give Yourself A Pixie Haircut Female A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Mar 08, 2023 · 6 min read

Pixie haircuts are a timeless and classic look that can give you a bold and edgy style. However, getting a pixie haircut from a salon can be quite expensive. By following this step-by-step guide, you can learn how to give yourself a pixie haircut female from the comfort of your own home.

How to Give Yourself a Pixie Haircut Female

Giving yourself a pixie haircut may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a breeze. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Hair cutting scissors
  • Hair clippers
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Hair ties

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to give yourself a pixie haircut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair as you normally would. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you begin cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use the comb to section your hair into four parts: top, back, and two sides. Use hair ties to secure each section.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin cutting the back section of your hair. Use the hair clippers with the longest guard to trim the hair to about 1 inch in length.

Next, move on to the sides of your hair. Use the clippers to trim the hair up to the temple area. Switch to the scissors to cut the hair around the ears and hairline.

Lastly, move on to the top section of your hair. Use the scissors to cut the hair to your desired length.

Step 4: Finishing Touches

Once you have cut your hair, use the clippers without a guard to trim the hair around the nape of your neck and ears. Use the scissors to make any final adjustments.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when giving yourself a pixie haircut:

  • Start with longer hair and gradually cut it shorter.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Take breaks to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use hair clips to keep hair out of the way while you’re cutting.
  • Be patient and take your time.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about giving yourself a pixie haircut:

Q: Can I give myself a pixie haircut if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to cut your hair when it’s dry and cut it in small sections to avoid any mistakes.

Q: How short should I cut my hair for a pixie cut?

A: The length of your hair depends on your personal preference, but a typical pixie cut is about 1-2 inches in length.

Q: What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

A: Don’t panic! Just take a break and assess the situation. If it’s a small mistake, you can try to fix it with the scissors. If it’s a bigger mistake, consider going to a professional stylist for help.

Hair Care

After giving yourself a pixie haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. You can also use a styling product to add texture and volume to your hair.

Congratulations, you’ve now learned how to give yourself a pixie haircut female! With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a bold and stylish look from the comfort of your own home.
