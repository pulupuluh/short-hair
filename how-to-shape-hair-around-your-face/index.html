How To Shape Hair Around Your Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jun 30, 2023 · 6 min read
How to Shape Hair Around Your Face - Tips and Tricks | Haircare Blog

Are you struggling with shaping your hair around your face? It can be tricky to get the perfect look, but with a few tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle. In this blog post, we will discuss how to shape hair around your face.

  1. How to Shape Hair Around Your Face
  2. Step by Step Guide

How to Shape Hair Around Your Face

The first step to shaping your hair around your face is to determine your face shape. There are generally six face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Once you determine your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that complements your features.

For example, if you have a round face, you may want to opt for a layered hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head and elongates your face. On the other hand, if you have a square face, you may want to choose a style that softens your features, such as a side-swept bang or a layered bob.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to shape hair around your face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Determine your face shape.
  3. Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape.
  4. Section your hair into manageable parts.
  5. Use a comb and scissors to trim your hair to the desired length.
  6. Blend the layers to create a seamless look.
  7. Style your hair using a blow dryer, curling iron, or straightener.
  8. Finish with hairspray or styling products to hold your look in place.

Tips for Shaping Hair

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when shaping your hair:

  • Always start with clean, dry hair.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Blend the layers to create a seamless look.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles.
  • Invest in quality styling products to hold your look in place.
  • Consider consulting a hairstylist for professional advice.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaping hair around your face:

  • How often should I trim my hair? It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • What if I don't like my new hairstyle? Don't worry, hair grows back! You can always try a new style or consult with a hairstylist for advice.
  • Can I shape my hair at home? Yes, you can shape your hair at home, but it's recommended to consult with a hairstylist for professional advice.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use conditioner to moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

By following these tips and tricks, you can shape your hair around your face and achieve the perfect look. Remember to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and consult with a hairstylist for professional advice if needed.
