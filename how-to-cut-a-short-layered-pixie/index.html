How To Cut A Short Layered Pixie A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 15, 2023 Â· 9 min read
If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that's easy to style and looks great, a short layered pixie might be just what you need. This trendy cut has been popular for years, and it's not going away anytime soon. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut a short layered pixie in relaxed English and provide tips to help you maintain your new look.

Description

A short layered pixie is a hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. The top layers are typically cut at an angle to create volume and texture. This cut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that's easy to style and can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to Cut a Short Layered Pixie

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to section off your hair. Here's how to cut a short layered pixie: 1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help you see the natural texture and movement of your hair. 2. Section off the top layer of your hair and clip it out of the way. 3. Take a section of hair at the back of your head and cut it to your desired length. Use this as a guide for the rest of your hair. 4. Work your way up the back of your head, cutting small sections of hair at a time. Use your first cut as a guide to make sure everything is even. 5. Once you've finished the back, move on to the sides. Use the same technique, cutting small sections of hair at a time and using the back as a guide. 6. Finally, unclip the top layer of your hair and cut it at an angle to create layers and volume. Use your scissors to point cut, which means cutting into the hair at an angle to create texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Wash and dry your hair. 2. Section off the top layer of your hair and clip it out of the way. 3. Take a section of hair at the back of your head and cut it to your desired length. 4. Work your way up the back of your head, cutting small sections of hair at a time. 5. Move on to the sides, using the back as a guide. 6. Unclip the top layer of your hair and cut it at an angle to create layers and volume. 7. Point cut the top layer to create texture. 8. Use your scissors to blend any harsh lines or uneven sections. 9. Style your hair as desired. 10. Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and hold to your new cut.

Tips

- Start with a longer length and work your way shorter. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's gone. - Use your fingers to lift and cut the hair, rather than combing it straight down. This will help create a more natural, textured look. - If you're not confident in your cutting skills, consider going to a professional stylist for your first cut. - Use products specifically designed for short hair to maintain your new cut.

FAQ

Q: How often do I need to get my short layered pixie cut? A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your length. Most people get a trim every 4-6 weeks. Q: Is a short layered pixie suitable for all face shapes? A: Yes! This cut can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types. Ask your stylist for advice on how to customize the cut to suit your features. Q: How do I style my short layered pixie? A: Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and hold to your hair. You can also play around with different styling techniques, like tousling your hair with your fingers or using a flat iron to create waves.

Hair Care

To keep your short layered pixie looking great, make sure you're using the right hair care products. Look for products specifically designed for short hair, like texturizing sprays, pomades, and dry shampoos. Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Cutting a short layered pixie is a great way to change up your look and try something new. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a low-maintenance, stylish cut that looks great on everyone. Just remember to maintain your new cut with regular trims and the right hair care products, and you'll be rocking your short layered pixie for years to come.
