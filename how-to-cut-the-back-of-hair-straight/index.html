How To Cut The Back Of Hair Straight A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 24, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're looking to cut the back of your hair straight, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll go over the steps you need to take to achieve a straight and even cut. We'll also provide some tips and tricks to make the process easier and answer some frequently asked questions about cutting hair at home. Let's get started!

How to Cut the Back of Hair Straight

To cut the back of your hair straight, you'll need a few things:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A comb
  • A handheld mirror (or a friend to help you)

Once you have these things, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. This will make it easier to see the natural shape and texture of your hair, which will help you achieve a straight cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use your comb to section your hair into two parts: the top and the bottom. Clip the top section of your hair on top of your head so that it's out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Section

Starting at the nape of your neck, use your comb to separate a small section of hair. Hold the section of hair between your index and middle fingers, and use your scissors to cut it straight across.

Repeat this process, working your way up the back of your head in small sections. Be sure to keep your cuts straight and even.

Step 4: Blend the Top Section

Once you've finished cutting the bottom section of your hair, unclip the top section and let it fall down. Use your comb to blend the top section with the bottom section, cutting any stray hairs that don't match the length of the rest of your hair.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, use your handheld mirror to check the back of your head. Make sure your cut is straight and even. If you notice any unevenness, go back and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting the Back of Hair Straight

Cutting the back of your hair straight can be tricky, but these tips can help:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Cut your hair in small sections to ensure evenness
  • Take breaks if you need to rest your arms or hands
  • Use a handheld mirror to check your work as you go
  • Don't rush the process – take your time to ensure a straight cut

FAQ: Cutting the Back of Hair Straight

Can I cut the back of my hair straight by myself?

Yes, you can cut the back of your hair straight by yourself. However, it can be helpful to have a friend or family member assist you.

What should I do if I accidentally cut my hair too short?

If you accidentally cut your hair too short, don't panic. You can try using hair extensions to add length or style your hair in a way that hides the shorter pieces.

How often should I cut the back of my hair?

How often you cut the back of your hair depends on how fast your hair grows and the length of your hair. As a general rule, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care After Cutting the Back of Hair Straight

After cutting the back of your hair straight, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Cutting the back of your hair straight can be a bit intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a salon-worthy cut at home. Remember to take your time, use sharp scissors, and check your work as you go. Happy cutting!
