How Can A Man Get Curly Hair

Written by Eveline May 05, 2023 · 6 min read

If you're a man who wants to add some texture and volume to your hair, you may be wondering how to get curly hair. While some men are born with naturally curly hair, others may need to use styling techniques or products to achieve the look they want.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Getting Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Get Curly Hair

There are several ways that men can get curly hair:

  • Using a curling iron or curling wand
  • Getting a perm
  • Using hair products designed for curly hair

Each of these methods has its pros and cons, and the best option for you will depend on your hair type, lifestyle, and personal preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you want to try getting curly hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Shampoo and condition your hair as usual, and then towel dry it gently.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. Look for a product that's specifically designed for curly hair, such as a curl-defining cream or gel.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment. This will help to create volume and define your curls.
  4. Use a curling iron or curling wand to define your curls. Take small sections of hair and wrap them around the barrel of the curling iron or wand, holding for a few seconds before releasing.
  5. Set your curls with a hairspray or other styling product. This will help to keep your curls in place throughout the day.

Tips for Getting Curly Hair

If you're new to styling your hair, here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. You don't want to weigh down your hair or make it look greasy.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or wand. This will help to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques to find the one that works best for you. Some men prefer to wrap their hair around the barrel of the curling iron, while others prefer to twist their hair before curling.
  • Don't overdo it with the hairspray. Too much hairspray can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

FAQs

Can men get perms?

Yes, men can get perms. A perm is a chemical treatment that's designed to create permanent curls or waves in your hair.

How long does a perm last?

A perm typically lasts for three to six months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

What products should I use for curly hair?

You should look for products that are designed specifically for curly hair, such as curl-defining creams, gels, and mousses. These products will help to enhance your curls and keep them looking great all day.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet, as this will help to prevent breakage.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

By following these tips and using the right products and styling techniques, you can get the curly hair you've always wanted. Whether you're looking for tight, defined curls or loose, beachy waves, there's a styling method that's right for you.
