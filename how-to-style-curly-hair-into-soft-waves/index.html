How To Style Curly Hair Into Soft Waves A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia May 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it has a natural bounce and texture that many people envy. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. If you're looking for a way to take your curly hair up a notch and create soft, romantic waves, then you're in luck. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve this look.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Curly Hair into Soft Waves

Soft waves are a great way to add some romance and texture to your curly hair. Here's how to achieve the look:

1. Start with clean, damp hair.

Before you begin, make sure your hair is clean and free of any product buildup. Shampoo and condition your hair as you normally would, then gently towel dry it so that it's damp but not dripping wet.

2. Apply a styling product.

Apply a styling product to your hair that's designed to enhance curls and reduce frizz. This could be a curl cream, mousse, or gel. Use a small amount and distribute it evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

3. Section your hair.

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you get an even look. Start with the bottom section and work your way up.

4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and set it to low or medium heat. Use it to dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you go, which will help to enhance your natural curl pattern.

5. Create waves with a curling iron.

If you want to enhance your waves even further, use a curling iron to create defined curls. Choose a barrel size that's similar to the size of your natural curls. Wrap small sections of hair around the barrel of the curling iron, holding it for a few seconds before releasing. Continue this process until you've curled all of your hair.

6. Finish with a texturizing spray.

Once you've created your waves, finish off with a texturizing spray. This will add some extra hold and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and more voluminous.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're more of a visual learner, here's a step-by-step guide to styling curly hair into soft waves:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a styling product.
  3. Section your hair.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.
  5. Create waves with a curling iron.
  6. Finish with a texturizing spray.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair

Styling curly hair can be tricky, but these tips can help:

  • Don't brush curly hair when it's dry. This can cause frizz and break up your natural curl pattern.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol, which can dry out your hair.
  • Try sleeping on a satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Can I achieve soft waves without using heat?

Yes, you can achieve soft waves without using heat. One way to do this is by braiding your hair when it's damp and leaving it overnight. When you take out the braids in the morning, you should have soft waves.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces. Some people with curly hair can go several days between washes, while others need to wash their hair every other day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

To keep your curly hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the steps outlined in this guide, you can achieve soft, romantic waves that will make you feel confident and beautiful.
