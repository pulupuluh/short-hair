How To Do Black Men s Hair A Guide To Relaxed Hair

Written by Bardi May 11, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you looking for tips on how to do black men's hair? Whether you're a black man looking to take care of your own hair or a stylist hoping to gain more knowledge on how to properly style black men's hair, this guide is for you. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to relax black men's hair to styling tips and hair care advice.

Description

Black men's hair can be tricky to style and maintain, especially if it's relaxed. Relaxing your hair can make it easier to manage and style, but it also requires extra care and attention to keep it healthy. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to properly relax black men's hair and provide tips on how to style and care for it.

How to Relax Black Men's Hair

Relaxing black men's hair is a process that should be done by a professional stylist. However, if you're looking to do it yourself, here are the steps:

  1. Choose the right relaxer. There are different types of relaxers for different hair types, so make sure to choose one that's designed for black men's hair.
  2. Prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo and towel-drying it. Don't use any styling products.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and apply the relaxer to each section, starting at the back and working your way to the front. Make sure to apply the relaxer evenly and avoid getting it on your scalp.
  4. Leave the relaxer on for the recommended time (usually 10-20 minutes) and then rinse it out thoroughly with warm water.
  5. Apply a neutralizing shampoo to your hair to stop the relaxing process and rinse it out thoroughly.
  6. Towel-dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner to moisturize it.

It's important to note that relaxing your hair can cause damage if not done properly, so it's best to leave it to a professional stylist if you're not experienced with the process.

Styling Tips for Relaxed Black Men's Hair

Once your hair is relaxed, there are several styling options to choose from. Here are some tips:

  • Keep your hair moisturized by using a leave-in conditioner daily.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and blow dryers, as they can cause damage to relaxed hair.
  • Try different hairstyles like fades, waves, and twists to find a look that suits you.
  • Use a soft-bristled brush to style your hair and avoid using a comb, which can cause breakage.
  • If you're not sure how to style your hair, consult a professional stylist for advice.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my relaxed hair?

A: You should wash your relaxed hair once a week to keep it clean and moisturized.

Q: Can I color my relaxed hair?

A: Yes, but it's best to consult a professional stylist to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: How often should I get my hair relaxed?

A: You should get your hair relaxed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care for Relaxed Black Men's Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, relaxed hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner designed for relaxed hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Conclusion

Relaxed black men's hair requires special care and attention to keep it healthy and stylish. By following the tips and techniques in this guide, you can achieve the look you want while keeping your hair in great condition. Remember to consult a professional stylist for any major hair changes or if you're unsure about how to care for your hair.
