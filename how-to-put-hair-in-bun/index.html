How To Put Hair In Bun A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to style your hair, a bun is a great option. Not only is it practical, but it can also be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. In this guide, we'll show you how to put hair in bun in a few simple steps.

Description

A bun is a hairstyle that involves twisting the hair into a coil and wrapping it around itself. It can be positioned anywhere on the head and can be secured with hairpins, clips, or a hair tie. The bun is a timeless style that has been popular for centuries and continues to be a go-to for many women today.

How To Put Hair in Bun

Follow these steps to create a simple bun:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to work with and create a smoother finish.

Step 2: Tie Your Hair into a Ponytail

Gather your hair into a high or low ponytail, depending on where you want to position the bun. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or lumps in the ponytail.

Step 3: Twist Your Hair

Take your ponytail and twist it around itself until it begins to coil. Keep twisting until you reach the end of your hair.

Step 4: Wrap Your Hair Around the Base of Your Ponytail

Once you have twisted your hair into a coil, wrap it around the base of your ponytail. Tuck the ends of your hair under the bun to secure it.

Step 5: Secure the Bun with Hairpins or a Hair Tie

Use hairpins or a hair tie to secure the bun in place. If you're using hairpins, insert them into the bun at an angle, making sure to catch the hair underneath to keep it secure.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you create the perfect bun:

  • If you want a fuller bun, tease your hair before creating the ponytail.
  • If you're using a hair tie, choose one that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • If you have shorter hair, use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.
  • If you want a more textured look, use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo before creating the bun.

FAQ

Can I create a bun with wet hair?

It's best to create a bun with dry hair to prevent breakage and damage. If you must create a bun with wet hair, make sure to use a hair tie that won't damage your hair and allow your hair to dry completely before taking down the bun.

How long will a bun stay in place?

A bun can stay in place for several hours, depending on how it's secured and the texture of your hair. If you have fine or slippery hair, you may need to use more hairpins or a stronger hair tie to keep it in place.

Hair Care

Creating a bun is a great way to keep your hair out of your face and protect it from damage. However, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent breakage and promote healthy growth. Make sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling tools, and protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

Now that you know how to put hair in bun, you can create this classic style anytime you want. Whether you're heading to work or a special event, a bun is a versatile and chic hairstyle that will never go out of style.
