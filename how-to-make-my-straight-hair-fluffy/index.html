How To Make My Straight Hair Fluffy Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jul 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have naturally straight hair, you may be wondering how to add some volume and fluffiness to your hair. Fortunately, there are several techniques and products that can help you achieve this look. In this article, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQs on how to make your straight hair fluffy.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make My Straight Hair Fluffy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Fluffy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Fluffy Hair

How to Make My Straight Hair Fluffy

The first step to achieving fluffy hair is to choose the right products. Look for volumizing shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed to add thickness and body to your hair. You can also use a leave-in conditioner that contains keratin or biotin, as these ingredients can help strengthen your hair and prevent breakage.

Another way to make your hair fluffy is to use a volumizing mousse or spray. Apply a small amount of product to your roots and work it through your hair. Be careful not to use too much, as this can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.

If you want to add even more volume, consider using a round brush when blow-drying your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, lifting your hair as you go. Once your hair is dry, flip your head upside down and shake it out to add even more volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your straight hair fluffy:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner that contains keratin or biotin.
  3. Use a volumizing mousse or spray, applying it to your roots and working it through your hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Flip your head upside down and shake it out to add even more volume.

Tips for Fluffy Hair

Here are some additional tips for making your straight hair fluffy:

  • Avoid using heavy products like hair wax or pomade, as these can weigh your hair down.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a bun or loose braid to add volume overnight.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to distribute natural oils throughout your hair, which can add shine and volume.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners, as these can damage your hair and make it look flat.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about making straight hair fluffy:

Can I use dry shampoo to add volume to my hair?

Yes, dry shampoo can help add volume to your hair. Apply it to your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers.

How often should I wash my hair?

This depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people may need to wash their hair every day, while others can go several days without washing. Experiment to find a routine that works for you.

Are there any vitamins or supplements that can help make my hair fluffy?

Yes, biotin and keratin are two supplements that can help strengthen your hair and prevent breakage, which can lead to thicker, fluffier hair.

Hair Care for Fluffy Hair

Once you've achieved fluffy hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain the look. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach or hair dye, as these can damage your hair and make it look flat.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your straight hair fluffy and voluminous. Remember to choose the right products, use a round brush when blow-drying, and avoid heavy styling products that can weigh your hair down. With a little bit of effort, you can achieve the perfect fluffy hair look.
