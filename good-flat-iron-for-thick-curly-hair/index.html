Best Flat Iron For Thick Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver May 24, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you struggling with finding the perfect flat iron for your thick curly hair? Look no further! We have compiled a complete guide to help you find the best flat iron for your hair type.

Description

Thick curly hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right flat iron, it can be tamed into smooth and sleek locks. A good flat iron should not only straighten your hair, but also protect it from damage caused by heat.

How to Choose the Best Flat Iron for Thick Curly Hair

When choosing a flat iron for your hair, consider the following factors:

  • Plate material: Titanium plates are best for thick curly hair as they heat up quickly and evenly, reducing heat damage.
  • Plate size: Opt for wider plates as they can cover more hair and reduce the time spent straightening.
  • Temperature control: Look for a flat iron with adjustable temperature settings as high heat can damage your hair.
  • Additional features: Features such as auto shut-off, swivel cord, and heat-resistant glove can make your styling experience more convenient and safe.

Step by Step Guide to Straightening Thick Curly Hair

Follow these steps to straighten your thick curly hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to minimize damage caused by heat.
  3. Blow-dry your hair until it is about 80% dry.
  4. Section your hair into small sections using hair clips.
  5. Start straightening from the bottom section, working your way up. Use the flat iron slowly and carefully, ensuring that you do not overheat any section.
  6. After straightening, apply a serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Straightening Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you straighten your thick curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before straightening.
  • Do not use the flat iron on wet hair as it can cause damage.
  • Divide your hair into small sections to ensure that every section is straightened properly.
  • Use a heat-resistant glove to protect your fingers from burns.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about straightening thick curly hair:

Can I straighten my hair every day?

No, it is not recommended to straighten your hair every day as it can cause heat damage and make your hair brittle.

What is the best temperature to straighten thick curly hair?

The best temperature to straighten thick curly hair is between 350Â°F to 400Â°F.

How can I reduce frizz when straightening my hair?

You can reduce frizz by applying a serum or oil to your hair after straightening. You can also use a boar bristle brush to distribute the natural oils in your hair.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some tips to keep your thick curly hair healthy:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet as it can cause damage.

With the right flat iron and proper hair care, you can achieve sleek and smooth locks that will make heads turn. Remember to always prioritize the health of your hair and avoid over-heating and over-styling.
