Best Hairstyle For Over 50 With Round Face

Written by Christine Jun 28, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you over 50 with a round face and wondering what hairstyle would suit you best? Look no further, as we have got you covered. A round face has a circular shape, with the width and length being almost equal. The best hairstyle for this face shape would be one that elongates the face, adds volume to the top, and creates an illusion of a more defined jawline. In this article, we will be discussing some of the best hairstyles for over 50 women with round faces, along with tips and FAQs.

Description

As you age, your hair tends to lose its natural bounce and luster. Therefore, it's essential to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape, enhances your features, and makes you feel confident. A round face has soft features, and the right hairstyle can help create a more defined look. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, there are many options available to suit your needs.

How To

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the length and width of your face, and if they are almost equal, then you have a round face. Once you know your face shape, you can start exploring different hairstyles that complement it. Here are some tips to help you get started:

Short Hair

If you prefer short hair, then a pixie cut or a bob would be the perfect choice. A pixie cut with side-swept bangs can create an illusion of a longer face, whereas a bob with layers can add volume to the top and create a more defined jawline. Avoid blunt cuts and too much volume at the sides as they can make your face appear wider.

Medium Hair

If you have medium-length hair, then a layered cut or a shoulder-length cut would suit you best. Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, whereas a shoulder-length cut with side-swept bangs can elongate your face. Avoid blunt cuts and center parts as they can make your face appear rounder.

Long Hair

If you have long hair, then a layered cut with face-framing layers would be the perfect choice. Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, whereas face-framing layers can create an illusion of a more defined jawline. Avoid blunt cuts and too much volume at the sides as they can make your face appear wider.

Step By Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape
Step 2: Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape
Step 3: Consult with your hairstylist
Step 4: Maintain your hair with proper care and styling

Tips

- Avoid blunt cuts and too much volume at the sides
- Experiment with layers and face-framing layers
- Consider side-swept bangs to elongate your face
- Consult with your hairstylist for personalized recommendations
- Use hair products that suit your hair type and texture

FAQ

Q: Can I still have long hair with a round face?
A: Yes, you can have long hair with a round face as long as you choose a layered cut with face-framing layers to create an illusion of a more defined jawline.

Q: Can I have bangs with a round face?
A: Yes, you can have bangs with a round face as long as they are side-swept to elongate your face.

Q: Do I need to consider my hair texture when choosing a hairstyle?
A: Yes, you should consider your hair texture when choosing a hairstyle as certain styles may not suit your hair type.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
- Avoid over-washing your hair
- Use a heat protectant before styling with hot tools
- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
- Use hair masks and treatments to nourish your hair

Now that you know the best hairstyle for over 50 with a round face, you can confidently choose a style that suits you best. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal preferences when choosing a hairstyle. Consult with your hairstylist for personalized recommendations, and maintain your hair with proper care and styling.
