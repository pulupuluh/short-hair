What Color Should I Dye My Hair Buzzfeed

Written by Luffy Mar 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for some hair inspiration, Buzzfeed is a great place to start. With their quizzes and articles, you can find the perfect color to dye your hair. But with so many options, it can be overwhelming to choose just one. So, what color should you dye your hair according to Buzzfeed? Keep reading to find out!

Description

Buzzfeed is known for its fun quizzes and articles, including ones that help you choose the perfect hair color. Whether you're looking for a subtle change or a bold new look, Buzzfeed has got you covered. They offer a wide range of hair color options to choose from, so you're sure to find something that suits your style.

How To

To find your perfect hair color according to Buzzfeed, simply take one of their hair color quizzes. These quizzes will ask you a series of questions about your hair type, skin tone, and personal style. Based on your answers, they will recommend a hair color that they think would look best on you.

Step by Step

1. Visit the Buzzfeed website

2. Search for "hair color quiz"

3. Choose one of the hair color quizzes

4. Answer the questions honestly

5. Get your recommended hair color

Tips

When taking a hair color quiz on Buzzfeed, it's important to answer each question honestly. This will ensure that you get the most accurate recommendation possible. It's also a good idea to take multiple quizzes to see if you consistently get the same hair color recommendation.

FAQ

What if I don't like the hair color recommended by Buzzfeed?

That's okay! The hair color recommended by Buzzfeed is just a suggestion. Ultimately, you should choose a hair color that you feel comfortable with and that suits your personal style.

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home. However, it's important to follow the instructions carefully and to do a patch test before applying the dye to your entire head of hair.

How often should I dye my hair?

This depends on the type of hair dye you use and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, permanent hair dye should be touched up every 4-6 weeks, while semi-permanent hair dye can last up to 8 weeks.

Hair Care

After dyeing your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain the color and keep your hair healthy. This includes using a color-safe shampoo and conditioner, avoiding hot tools as much as possible, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

So, what color should you dye your hair according to Buzzfeed? Take one of their quizzes to find out! And remember, the most important thing is to choose a hair color that you feel confident and comfortable with.
