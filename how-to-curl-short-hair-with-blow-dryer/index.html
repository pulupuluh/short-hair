How To Curl Short Hair With Blow Dryer A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 13, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have short hair, you might think that curling your hair is impossible without a curling iron. However, you can use a blow dryer to achieve beautiful curls. In this article, we will show you how to curl short hair with a blow dryer.

Description

You might think that curling short hair with a blow dryer is difficult, but it is actually quite simple. You can achieve beautiful curls in just a few steps. This method is perfect for those who don't want to use a curling iron, or for those who want to add some volume and texture to their short hair.

How to Curl Short Hair with Blow Dryer

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, you need to prepare it. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. Then, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from the blow dryer.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on how thick your hair is. If you have thin hair, you can divide it into two sections. If you have thicker hair, you can divide it into more sections.

Step 3: Start Curling

Take a section of hair and wrap it around your fingers. Then, hold the hair in place and use the blow dryer to apply heat to the hair. Make sure to hold the dryer at a distance to avoid burning your hair.

After a few seconds, release the hair from your fingers and let it cool. This will help the curl to set. Repeat this process with the other sections of hair.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have curled all of your hair, finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curls:

  • Use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Hold the blow dryer at a distance to avoid burning your hair.
  • Wrap the hair tightly around your fingers to achieve tighter curls.
  • For looser curls, wrap the hair around a larger barrel brush.
  • Use a light mist of hairspray to hold the curls in place.

FAQ

Can you curl short hair with a blow dryer?

Yes, you can curl short hair with a blow dryer. It is a simple and easy method to achieve beautiful curls.

What is the best way to curl short hair with a blow dryer?

The best way to curl short hair with a blow dryer is to divide your hair into sections, wrap each section around your fingers, and use the blow dryer to apply heat to the hair.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant to prevent damage from the blow dryer.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips, you can achieve beautiful curls with a blow dryer. Give it a try and see how easy it is to curl your short hair!
