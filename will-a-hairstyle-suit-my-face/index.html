Will A Hairstyle Suit My Face How To Choose The Perfect Hairstyle For Your Face Shape

Written by Daniel Apr 30, 2023 Â· 11 min read

Choosing the right hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure whether it will suit your face shape. The wrong haircut can make your face look wider or longer than it is, while the right one can bring out your best features and enhance your natural beauty. In this blog post, we'll give you tips on how to choose a hairstyle that will flatter your face shape and make you look and feel your best.

Description: Understanding Face Shapes

The first step in choosing the right hairstyle is to understand your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and long. Each face shape has its own unique characteristics and requires a different approach when it comes to choosing a hairstyle.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The best hairstyles for round faces are those that add height to the top of the head and create the illusion of length. Long, layered hairstyles, asymmetrical bobs, and side-swept bangs are all great options for round faces.

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile face shape. It is characterized by balanced proportions, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin. Almost any hairstyle will suit an oval face shape, but long, layered hairstyles with a side part are particularly flattering.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a wide forehead, and a square chin. The best hairstyles for square faces are those that soften the angles of the face. Soft, layered hairstyles, wavy bobs, and side-swept bangs are all great options for square faces.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape is characterized by a wide forehead, a narrow chin, and high cheekbones. The best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces are those that draw attention away from the forehead and create balance between the upper and lower halves of the face. Long, layered hairstyles, side-swept bangs, and chin-length bobs are all great options for heart-shaped faces.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape is characterized by a narrow forehead, wide cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The best hairstyles for diamond faces are those that create the illusion of width at the forehead and jawline. Short, layered hairstyles, chin-length bobs, and side-swept bangs are all great options for diamond faces.

Long Face Shape

A long face shape is characterized by a long forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The best hairstyles for long faces are those that create the illusion of width and volume. Layered hairstyles, wavy bobs, and side-swept bangs are all great options for long faces.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

Now that you understand your face shape, it's time to choose the right hairstyle. Here are some tips to help you make the right choice:

Tip #1: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in choosing the right hairstyle. If you have fine hair, a short, layered hairstyle can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a longer hairstyle can help to weigh it down and prevent it from looking too bulky.

Tip #2: Think About Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule and don't have a lot of time to spend on your hair, a low-maintenance style like a bob or a pixie cut might be a better option than a high-maintenance style like long, curly hair.

Tip #3: Consider Your Face Proportions

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face proportions. If you have a small face, a large hairstyle can overwhelm your features. If you have a large face, a small hairstyle can make your features look unbalanced.

Tip #4: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Your hairstylist is an expert when it comes to choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape. They can give you advice on which styles will work best with your hair texture, lifestyle, and face shape.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will a short hairstyle suit my face?

A: Short hairstyles can work for any face shape, but it's important to choose a style that complements your features and proportions. Your hairstylist can help you choose the right short hairstyle for your face shape.

Q: Can I wear bangs with a round face shape?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs. Side-swept bangs or long, layered bangs are best for round faces, as they create the illusion of length and draw attention away from the cheeks.

Q: Can I wear a center part with a heart-shaped face?

A: Yes, but it's important to create balance between the upper and lower halves of the face. A center part can work for heart-shaped faces, but it's best to pair it with long, layered hair that adds volume at the bottom of the face.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Hairstyle

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your new hairstyle:

Tip #1: Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can help to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Choose products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture, and avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Tip #2: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help to keep it clean and healthy. Use lukewarm water and a gentle shampoo, and avoid using hot water or harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

Tip #3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair over time. To protect your hair from heat damage, use a heat protectant spray before using these tools, and avoid using them on wet hair.

Tip #4: Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Your hairstylist can recommend how often you should get a haircut based on your hair type and texture.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can be a challenge, but it's worth it to find a style that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful. By following these tips and consulting with your hairstylist, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and maintain it with ease.
