What Does Purple Hair Meaning A Guide To Understanding The Significance Of Purple Hair

Written by Aprilia Mar 16, 2023 Â· 9 min read

Have you ever wondered what does purple hair meaning? If you're considering dyeing your hair purple or have already done so, it's important to understand the symbolism behind the color. In this article, we'll explore the meaning of purple hair and what it can represent.

What Does Purple Hair Meaning?

Purple hair is a bold and unconventional color choice that can represent various things depending on the individual. Historically, the color purple has been associated with royalty, luxury, and wealth. This is because during ancient times, purple dye was extremely rare and expensive to produce, and only royalty and wealthy individuals could afford it.

Today, purple hair can represent a sense of individuality, creativity, and rebellion. It is often chosen by those who want to stand out and make a statement. It can also represent a sense of mystery and spirituality, as the color purple is often associated with the crown chakra and the third eye.

How to Dye Your Hair Purple

If you're interested in dyeing your hair purple, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, it's important to choose the right shade of purple for your skin tone. Cooler shades of purple, such as lavender and lilac, work best on those with cool undertones, while warmer shades, such as plum and magenta, work best on those with warm undertones.

Next, you'll need to bleach your hair to a light blonde in order for the purple dye to show up vibrantly. This can be done at home or at a salon, but it's important to follow the instructions carefully and to use a good quality bleach and developer.

Once your hair is prepped and ready, it's time to apply the purple dye. You can choose from a variety of shades and brands, but it's important to choose a high-quality dye that will last and not damage your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Dyeing Your Hair Purple

Here's a step-by-step guide to dyeing your hair purple:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup and oils. Dry your hair completely.

Step 2: Protect Your Skin

Apply petroleum jelly or a thick conditioner around your hairline, ears, and neck to protect your skin from staining.

Step 3: Mix the Dye

Follow the instructions on the dye packet to mix the dye and developer together.

Step 4: Apply the Dye

Starting at the roots, apply the dye to your hair in sections. Use a comb to evenly distribute the dye and ensure full coverage.

Step 5: Let the Dye Develop

Follow the instructions on the dye packet to determine how long to leave the dye on your hair. Cover your hair with a shower cap or plastic wrap to keep the heat in and help the dye develop.

Step 6: Rinse and Condition

Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water until the water runs clear. Apply a deep conditioner to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Now that you know how to dye your hair purple, let's take a look at some tips for maintaining your new color.

Tips for Maintaining Purple Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your purple hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can cause the color to fade faster.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as these can also cause the color to fade.
  • Use a color-depositing conditioner or hair mask to refresh the color in between dye jobs.
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater, as these can also cause the color to fade.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about purple hair:

Is purple hair hard to maintain?

Purple hair can be harder to maintain than other hair colors, as it tends to fade faster. However, with the right products and care, you can keep your purple hair looking vibrant for as long as possible.

Can I dye my hair purple at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair purple at home. However, it's important to follow the instructions carefully and to use high-quality products to avoid damaging your hair.

What is the best shade of purple for my skin tone?

Cooler shades of purple, such as lavender and lilac, work best on those with cool undertones, while warmer shades, such as plum and magenta, work best on those with warm undertones.

Hair Care for Purple Hair

It's important to take good care of your hair after dyeing it purple. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can cause the color to fade faster.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, as these can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools.

Now that you know what does purple hair meaning and how to dye and care for it, you're ready to rock your new bold and beautiful look!
