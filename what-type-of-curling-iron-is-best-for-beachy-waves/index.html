What Type Of Curling Iron Is Best For Beachy Waves

Written by Oliver Feb 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're looking to achieve those effortless, beachy waves, one of the most important things to consider is the type of curling iron you use. With so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one for your hair type and desired style. In this article, we'll break down the best curling irons for beachy waves and offer some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

Beachy waves are a popular hairstyle that gives off a relaxed, carefree vibe. This style is achieved by creating loose, tousled curls that mimic the look of natural waves. While it may seem like an easy look to achieve, it can be difficult to master without the right tools. That's why choosing the best curling iron for beachy waves is so important.

How to

Step 1: Choose the right curling iron

When it comes to achieving beachy waves, the type of curling iron you use is crucial. The best option is a curling wand with a tapered barrel. This type of wand allows you to create curls of varying sizes, which is key to achieving the natural, tousled look of beachy waves. Look for a wand with a barrel that's at least one inch in diameter at the base and tapers down to 0.5 inches.

Step 2: Prep your hair

Before you begin curling, it's important to prep your hair with a heat protectant spray. This will help prevent damage to your hair from the heat of the curling iron. You should also consider using a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to give your hair some extra texture and hold.

Step 3: Curl your hair

To achieve beachy waves, start by taking small sections of hair and wrapping them around the barrel of the curling wand. Hold the hair in place for a few seconds, then release. Continue curling small sections of hair until your entire head is curled.

Step 4: Finger comb your curls

Once you've finished curling your hair, use your fingers to gently comb through the curls. This will help loosen them up and create a more natural, tousled look.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve the perfect beachy waves:

  • Don't curl your hair too tightly. Loose, relaxed curls are key to achieving the beachy wave look.
  • Alternate the direction of your curls. This will give your hair more texture and a more natural look.
  • Don't forget to use a hairspray to set your curls and keep them in place.

FAQ

What type of curling iron is best for fine hair?

If you have fine hair, you should look for a curling iron with a smaller barrel. This will help create tighter curls that will hold their shape better. Avoid using high heat settings, as this can damage your hair.

Can I achieve beachy waves with a straightening iron?

While it's possible to achieve beachy waves with a straightening iron, it can be more difficult and time-consuming than using a curling wand. If you want to try using a straightening iron, look for one with rounded edges and use it to create loose waves by twisting your hair as you run the iron down the length of your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your beachy waves looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy. And don't forget to schedule regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.

Now that you know what type of curling iron is best for beachy waves, it's time to start experimenting with different styles and techniques. With a little practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect beachy waves every time.
