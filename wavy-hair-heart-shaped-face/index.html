Wavy Hair Heart Shaped Face A Guide To Flattering Hairstyles

Written by Oliver Apr 08, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face and wavy hair, you've hit the genetic jackpot! However, finding the right hairstyle that complements your unique features can be a bit daunting. Don't worry, we've got you covered with this comprehensive guide on wavy hair heart shaped face hairstyles.

Description

A heart-shaped face has a wider forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrower chin. Wavy hair adds volume and movement to this already beautiful face shape. With the right hairstyle, you can accentuate your best features and balance out your face shape.

How to

The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for your wavy hair heart shaped face is to balance out your features. You want to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones while minimizing the width of your forehead and the pointiness of your chin. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Step by Step

  1. Start by determining your face shape. If you're not sure, try the old trick of tracing your face in the mirror with lipstick or eyeliner.
  2. Identify your best features. Do you have striking eyes? A great smile? High cheekbones? You want to accentuate these features with your hairstyle.
  3. Consider the length of your hair. Heart-shaped faces look great with long hair, as it balances out the width of the forehead. However, shorter hairstyles can also work if they add volume to the crown of your head.
  4. Think about layers. Layers can add movement and volume to wavy hair, but you want to avoid too much volume around your chin.
  5. Choose the right bangs. Side-swept bangs can soften a heart-shaped face, while blunt bangs can make your forehead look even wider.
  6. Consider highlights or lowlights. Adding some color to your hair can help accentuate your best features and add depth to your hairstyle.
  7. Consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you determine the best hairstyle for your wavy hair heart shaped face and give you tips on how to style it at home.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your wavy hair heart shaped face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume around your chin, as this can make your face look too pointy.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and haircuts until you find the perfect one.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to keep your wavy hair looking its best.
  • Use a styling product, such as mousse or hairspray, to help define your waves and keep them in place.
  • Consider using a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural waves.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wavy hair heart shaped face hairstyles:

What is the best hairstyle for a heart-shaped face with wavy hair?

The best hairstyle for a heart-shaped face with wavy hair is one that balances out your features. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and loose waves are all great options.

Can heart-shaped faces pull off short haircuts?

Yes! Heart-shaped faces can pull off short haircuts, as long as they add volume to the crown of your head and balance out the width of your forehead.

What should I avoid when choosing a hairstyle for my wavy hair heart shaped face?

Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume around your chin, as this can make your face look too pointy. Also, avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your forehead look even wider.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keeping your wavy hair heart shaped face looking its best. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner before swimming.
  • Trim your hair regularly to remove split ends and keep it looking healthy and vibrant.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock your wavy hair heart shaped face with confidence and style!
