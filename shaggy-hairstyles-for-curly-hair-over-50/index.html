Shaggy Hairstyles For Curly Hair Over 50 A Guide

Written by Robby Jun 10, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, our hair changes in texture and volume. For those with curly hair over 50, it can be challenging to maintain a hairstyle that looks stylish and chic. However, shaggy hairstyles are an excellent option for those who want to keep their curls while still looking trendy. In this guide, we will discuss how to achieve shaggy hairstyles for curly hair over 50, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve Shaggy Hairstyles for Curly Hair Over 50
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining Shaggy Hairstyles for Curly Hair Over 50
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice for Curly Hair Over 50

Description

Shaggy hairstyles are characterized by their textured layers and choppy ends. This style is ideal for those with curly hair over 50 because it adds volume and movement to the hair, making it look more youthful and vibrant. Shaggy hairstyles can vary in length, from short and sassy to long and flowy, depending on your preference.

How to Achieve Shaggy Hairstyles for Curly Hair Over 50

The first step in achieving shaggy hairstyles for curly hair over 50 is to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair. A good stylist will be able to create layers that enhance your curls and flatter your face shape. When discussing your desired style with your stylist, be sure to mention that you want a shaggy look with textured layers and choppy ends.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair to define and enhance your curls.

3. Using a pair of sharp scissors, your stylist will begin to create layers in your hair, starting from the bottom and working their way up.

4. Once the layers are in place, your stylist will create choppy ends by cutting into the hair at an angle.

5. To finish the look, your stylist may use a razor to further texturize your hair and give it a more lived-in look.

Tips for Maintaining Shaggy Hairstyles for Curly Hair Over 50

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and prevent frizz.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without disrupting their natural shape.

4. Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Will shaggy hairstyles work for all face shapes?

A: Yes, shaggy hairstyles can be adapted to flatter all face shapes. Your stylist will be able to customize the layers to suit your features.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed to maintain a shaggy style?

A: It is recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of your shaggy hairstyle.

Hair Care Advice for Curly Hair Over 50

1. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.

2. Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.

3. Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

4. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

By following these tips and step-by-step instructions, you can achieve a shaggy hairstyle that flatters your curls and makes you look and feel younger. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and getting regular trims to maintain your style.
