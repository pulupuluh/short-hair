Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Faces Over 60

Written by Eveline Mar 12, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle that flatters your heart-shaped face? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with tips, how-tos, and hairstyle ideas that will make you look and feel your best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Heart-shaped faces are defined by a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. If you have this face shape and are over 60, it's important to choose a hairstyle that balances out your features and doesn't make your face look too top-heavy.

When choosing a hairstyle, focus on styles that add volume and width to the lower part of your face. This will create balance and soften your features. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head or are too short, as they will only emphasize your forehead.

How To

The first step in choosing a hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Take a look at your face in the mirror and assess your features. If you have a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin, you most likely have a heart-shaped face.

Next, consider your hair texture and personal style. Do you prefer short or long hair? Do you want a low-maintenance style or are you willing to put in some effort to style your hair every day?

Once you have an idea of what you're looking for, consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with heart-shaped faces. They can provide you with personalized recommendations and help you choose a style that flatters your features.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating a few different hairstyles that are perfect for heart-shaped faces over 60:

Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Create a center part.
  3. Taking small sections, curl your hair away from your face using a curling iron.
  4. Once all of your hair is curled, brush through the curls to create loose waves.
  5. Add a small amount of texturizing spray to create volume and hold.

Short Bob

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair to the side.
  3. Cut your hair into a chin-length bob, with the longest pieces at the front.
  4. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, adding volume at the crown.
  5. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a heart-shaped face over 60:

  • Consider getting bangs to help balance out your forehead.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they will emphasize your forehead.
  • Volume at the chin or below will create balance and soften your features.
  • Consider adding layers to create movement and volume.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new and experiment with different styles until you find one that works for you.

FAQ

What is a heart-shaped face?

A heart-shaped face is defined by a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin.

What hairstyles are best for heart-shaped faces over 60?

Hairstyles that add volume and width to the lower part of the face are best for heart-shaped faces over 60. This can include long layers, bobs, and hairstyles with volume at the chin or below.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you're sure to find a look that flatters your heart-shaped face and makes you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to consult with a hairstylist and experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you.
