How To Naturally Make Your Hair Curlier For Guys

Written by Luffy Mar 04, 2023 · 8 min read

If you're a guy with straight hair, you might be looking for ways to add some curls to your locks. While there are plenty of products out there that can help you achieve curly hair, you might be looking for a more natural solution. Luckily, there are plenty of things you can do to encourage your hair to curl naturally. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for making your hair curlier without using any products or heat tools.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Natural Curl Enhancement
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make Your Hair Curlier Naturally

Before we dive into the specifics, it's important to understand how hair gets its natural curl. The shape of your hair follicle and the way the protein keratin is arranged in your hair shaft both play a role in determining how curly your hair is. While you can't change the shape of your follicles or the structure of your hair, you can encourage curls to form by following these tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Less Often

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it harder for curls to form. Try washing your hair every other day or every three days instead of every day. When you do wash your hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help keep your hair moisturized.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When you're combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush. This will help prevent breakage and keep your curls intact.

3. Try the Pineapple Method

The pineapple method is a technique for preserving curls overnight. Before you go to bed, gather your hair into a loose ponytail on top of your head. This will help prevent your curls from getting flattened while you sleep.

4. Use a Diffuser

If you do use a hair dryer, use a diffuser attachment to help encourage curls. A diffuser will help distribute heat more evenly and prevent frizz.

5. Try a Hair Mask

Using a hair mask once a week can help keep your hair healthy and moisturized, which can make it easier for curls to form. Look for a hair mask that contains ingredients like coconut oil, honey, or avocado oil.

6. Use a Curl Enhancing Spray

You can make your own curl enhancing spray by mixing water, salt, and aloe vera gel in a spray bottle. Spritz your hair with the spray and scrunch your curls to encourage them to form.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for making your hair curlier naturally:

  1. Wash your hair less often.
  2. Use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush.
  3. Try the pineapple method to preserve curls overnight.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer.
  5. Use a hair mask once a week.
  6. Make your own curl enhancing spray using water, salt, and aloe vera gel.

Tips for Natural Curl Enhancement

Here are some additional tips for enhancing your natural curls:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair; instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to blot your hair dry.

FAQ

Can I make my hair curlier if it's naturally straight?

While you can't change the shape of your hair follicles, you can encourage curls to form by following the tips in this article.

Will using a hair dryer damage my hair?

Using a hair dryer can be damaging to your hair if you use it too often or on high heat. However, using a diffuser attachment and keeping the dryer on a low or medium heat setting can help minimize damage.

Can I use a hair mask if my hair is oily?

Yes, you can still use a hair mask even if your hair is oily. Just be sure to rinse it out thoroughly to avoid any buildup.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Once you've achieved your desired level of curliness, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for hair care for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair when it's dry.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.

With these tips and tricks, you can make your hair curlier naturally without using any products or heat tools. Remember to be patient, as it may take some time for your curls to fully form. But with a little effort, you can achieve the curly locks you've always wanted.
