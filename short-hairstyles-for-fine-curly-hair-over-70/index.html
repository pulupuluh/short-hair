Short Hairstyles For Fine Curly Hair Over 70 Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jul 31, 2023 · 7 min read

If you're over 70 and have fine, curly hair, you might be wondering what hairstyle would best suit you. There are numerous short hairstyles that can work well with fine curly hair, and in this post, we'll discuss some of the best options for you to consider.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Fine Curly Hair over 70

When choosing a short hairstyle for fine curly hair over 70, it's important to consider a few things:

  • Your face shape – certain hairstyles will look better with certain face shapes
  • Your hair texture – fine curly hair can be difficult to style, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that works well with your natural texture
  • Your lifestyle – consider how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling your hair on a daily basis

Some of the best short hairstyles for fine curly hair over 70 include:

Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that can work well with fine curly hair. It's a low-maintenance style that can be worn straight or curly, and it can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture.

Pixie

A pixie cut is a short, layered hairstyle that can work well with fine curly hair. It's a bold and edgy look that requires minimal styling, making it a great option for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great way to add volume and texture to fine curly hair. It can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture, and it can be worn straight or curly.

Step-by-step Guide to Styling Short Hairstyles for Fine Curly Hair over 70

Styling short hairstyles for fine curly hair over 70 can be a bit tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair
  2. Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or curl cream, to help define your curls
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls
  4. Use a curling iron to define any loose curls or waves
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Fine Curly Hair over 70

Maintaining short hairstyles for fine curly hair over 70 can be a bit challenging, but with the following tips and tricks, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair

FAQs about Short Hairstyles for Fine Curly Hair over 70

Q: Are there any short hairstyles that are easy to style?

A: Yes, a bob or a pixie cut are both low-maintenance hairstyles that are easy to style.

Q: Can I wear a layered cut with fine curly hair?

A: Yes, a layered cut is a great way to add volume and texture to fine curly hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every two to three days to avoid drying it out.

Hair Care Tips for Fine Curly Hair over 70

When it comes to caring for fine curly hair over 70, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your fine curly hair over 70 looking healthy and beautiful, no matter what short hairstyle you choose.
