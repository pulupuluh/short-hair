Best Hairstyle For Long Face Girl

Written by Oliver Mar 19, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Having a long face can be both a blessing and a curse. It can make you look elegant and sophisticated, but it can also make you look older than your age. Choosing the right hairstyle can help you accentuate your features and balance out your face shape. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyle for long face girl.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Long Face Girl
  3. Step by Step Guide on How to Style
  4. Tips on Maintaining the Hairstyle
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips for Long Face Girl

Description

Long face shape is characterized by a long forehead and a narrow chin. The best hairstyle for long face girl is the one that adds width to the face and balances out the features. This can be achieved by adding volume on the sides, creating layers, and adding bangs. Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of the head or create a vertical line, as this will only elongate the face shape even more.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Long Face Girl

When choosing the best hairstyle for long face girl, consider the following factors:

  • Face Shape: Determine your face shape first before choosing a hairstyle. Long face shape can be combined with oval, heart, or square face shape.
  • Hair Type: Consider your hair type and texture when choosing a hairstyle. Some hairstyles may work better on fine hair, while others may work better on thick hair.
  • Lifestyle: Consider your lifestyle and daily activities. Choose a low-maintenance hairstyle if you don't have much time to style your hair every day.
  • Personal Style: Choose a hairstyle that reflects your personality and personal style. Don't be afraid to experiment, but make sure it still complements your face shape.

Step by Step Guide on How to Style

Here are some step by step guide on how to style the best hairstyle for long face girl:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray on the roots to add volume.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume on the sides.
  4. Create layers to add texture and movement to the hair.
  5. Add side-swept bangs to balance out the face shape.
  6. Use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves or curls.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips on Maintaining the Hairstyle

Here are some tips on maintaining the best hairstyle for long face girl:

  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair fresh and voluminous.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hairstyle for long face girl:

  • Can I wear my hair in a ponytail? Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail, but make sure to add volume on the sides and avoid pulling your hair too tight.
  • What if I have curly hair? You can still achieve the best hairstyle for long face girl by adding layers and volume on the sides. Use a curling cream or mousse to enhance your curls.
  • Can I have short hair? Yes, you can have short hair, but make sure to add volume and texture to balance out the face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Long Face Girl

Here are some hair care tips for long face girl:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

In conclusion, the best hairstyle for long face girl is the one that adds width to the face and balances out the features. Consider your face shape, hair type, lifestyle, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle. Use the step by step guide and tips to style and maintain your hair. Lastly, take care of your hair by using the right products and techniques.
