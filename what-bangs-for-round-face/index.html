What Bangs For Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Jun 22, 2023 Â· 10 min read

Are you tired of always having the same hairstyle and looking for something new? Bangs can be a great way to switch things up, but choosing the right style can be a challenge, especially if you have a round face. In this article, we will explore what bangs for round face are the most flattering and provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the perfect look.

Description

Before we dive into what bangs for round face are best, let's first define what a round face is. A round face is characterized by a soft, circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal when choosing bangs for a round face is to create an illusion of length and slimness. Choosing the right style can help elongate your face and balance out your features.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Round Face

When it comes to choosing the right bangs for round face, there are several factors to consider, including your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect bangs:

Consider Your Face Shape

If you have a round face, the goal is to create angles and length. Opt for bangs that are longer on the sides and tapered towards the center. This will help elongate your face and create the illusion of length. Avoid blunt-cut bangs that sit straight across your forehead, as they can make your face appear even rounder.

Take Your Hair Type into Account

When choosing bangs for round face, you should also consider your hair type. If you have thin hair, go for wispy, textured bangs that add volume and movement. If your hair is thick and heavy, opt for heavier, blunt-cut bangs that will balance out your hair texture.

Consider Your Personal Style

Your personal style should also play a role in choosing the right bangs for round face. If you prefer a more classic look, go for long, side-swept bangs. If you're feeling bold, try a choppy, textured fringe. Ultimately, the key is to choose a style that you feel confident and comfortable in.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Bangs for Round Face

Now that you know what bangs for round face are best, it's time to create the perfect look. Follow these step-by-step instructions to achieve the perfect bangs:

Step 1: Choose the Right Bangs

Start by choosing the right bangs for your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Consider consulting with a hairstylist for professional advice and guidance.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Wash and blow-dry your hair to ensure it's clean and free of tangles. Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage and prepare it for styling.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section off the front portion of your hair that will become your bangs. Be sure to choose a section that is wide enough to cover your forehead but not too wide that it overwhelms your face.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Using sharp scissors, cut your bangs at an angle, creating longer pieces on the sides and tapering towards the center. Take your time and cut small sections at a time to ensure precision.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Using a round brush, blow-dry your bangs in the direction you want them to fall. Use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to add texture and hold your bangs in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs for Round Face

Now that you have the perfect bangs, it's important to maintain them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for maintaining your bangs for round face:

Trim Regularly

To keep your bangs looking fresh and well-maintained, trim them every 4-6 weeks. This will help prevent split ends and keep your bangs looking neat and tidy.

Blow-Dry Your Bangs

Blow-drying your bangs every day will help keep them in place and prevent them from becoming greasy or flat. Use a round brush to add volume and create a sleek, polished look.

Use a Dry Shampoo

If your bangs start to look greasy between washes, use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and refresh your hair. This will help extend the life of your bangs and keep them looking their best.

FAQ

What are the Best Bangs for Round Face?

How Do I Choose the Right Bangs for My Face Shape?

How Do I Style My Bangs?

To style your bangs, blow-dry them in the direction you want them to fall and use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to add texture and hold your bangs in place.

Hair Care for Bangs for Round Face

Keeping your hair healthy and well-maintained is essential when it comes to bangs for round face. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and free of dirt and oil. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type.

Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. When you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims every 6-8 weeks will help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and well-maintained.

Conclusion

Choosing the right bangs for round face can be a challenge, but with the right guidance and styling tips, you can achieve the perfect look. Remember to take your face shape, hair type, and personal style into account when choosing bangs and to maintain them regularly to keep them looking their best.
