How To Style Hair For Square Face Shapes

Written by Bardi Apr 13, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your square face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to style your hair to complement your square face shape. Follow these steps and you will be on your way to a hairstyle that will have you feeling confident and beautiful.

Description

Square face shapes have a strong jawline and forehead, which can make finding a flattering hairstyle a challenge. The key is to soften these features with the right haircut and styling techniques. By choosing the right hairstyle, you can enhance your best features and create balance in your overall appearance.

How to Style Hair for Square Face Shapes

Here are some steps to follow when styling hair for square face shapes:

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Before you start styling your hair, itâs important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose the most flattering hairstyles and techniques for your features.

Step 2: Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape

The right haircut can make all the difference when it comes to styling hair for square face shapes. Consider a layered, textured haircut that softens your strong jawline and forehead. A long bob, shaggy layers, or soft waves are great options for square face shapes.

Step 3: Add volume to the crown of your head

Adding volume to the crown of your head can help elongate your face and balance out your strong jawline. Use a volumizing mousse or spray and blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and lift.

Step 4: Side-swept bangs

Side-swept bangs can soften your forehead and create a more balanced appearance. Consider a long, side-swept fringe that blends into the rest of your hair for a natural look.

Step 5: Embrace curls and waves

Curls and waves can add softness and femininity to a square face shape. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose curls or beachy waves. Avoid tight, ringlet curls that can make your face appear more angular.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling hair for square face shapes:

  • Try a middle part to create balance in your overall appearance.
  • Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs that can make your forehead appear wider.
  • Consider a side part to soften your forehead and create a more balanced look.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add movement and dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling hair for square face shapes:

What hairstyles are best for square face shapes?

Layered, textured haircuts that soften your strong jawline and forehead are great options for square face shapes. Consider a long bob, shaggy layers, or soft waves.

Should I avoid straight-across bangs if I have a square face shape?

Yes, blunt, straight-across bangs can make your forehead appear wider and accentuate your strong jawline. Consider side-swept bangs or a long, side-swept fringe instead.

What styling products should I use for square face shapes?

Use volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the crown of your head and create balance in your overall appearance. Texturizing spray can also add movement and dimension to your hair.

Hair Care

Itâs important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and beautiful appearance. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using hot tools on high heat, which can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can style your hair to flatter your square face shape and enhance your best features. Donât be afraid to experiment with different haircuts and styling techniques to find the perfect look for you!
