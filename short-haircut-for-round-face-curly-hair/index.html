Short Haircut For Round Face Curly Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Apr 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of dealing with long, unruly curly hair that seems to take forever to style every day? Are you also struggling to find a hairstyle that complements your round face shape? Look no further than a short haircut for round face curly hair! In this guide, we'll provide you with all the information you need to know to rock a short, curly hairstyle that flatters your round face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A short haircut for round face curly hair is a great way to showcase your curls while also flattering your face shape. A short hairstyle can help to elongate your face and draw attention to your best features. Plus, it's a low-maintenance option that's perfect for busy mornings. Whether you're looking for a bold pixie cut, a trendy bob, or something in between, there's a short haircut for round face curly hair that will work for you.

How to

Before you head to the salon, it's important to do your research and find a short haircut that will work for your face shape and curl pattern. Look for inspiration online or in magazines, and bring pictures to your stylist to ensure you're on the same page.

Consider your face shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to look for a short haircut that helps to elongate your face. This can be achieved with a longer pixie cut or a bob that hits just below the chin. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as this can make your face appear wider.

Think about your curl pattern

Curly hair comes in many different patterns, from loose waves to tight coils. It's important to choose a short haircut that works with your natural curl pattern. A layered cut can help to define your curls and add volume, while a blunt cut can make your curls appear more weighed down.

Step by step

Once you've found a short haircut that works for your face shape and curl pattern, it's time to head to the salon. Follow these steps to ensure you get the perfect cut:

  1. Wash and style your hair as you normally would, so your stylist can see your natural curl pattern.
  2. Bring pictures of the short haircut you want, so your stylist knows exactly what you're looking for.
  3. Communicate with your stylist about your hair goals and any concerns you have.
  4. Let your stylist work their magic!
  5. After the cut, ask your stylist for styling tips and product recommendations to help maintain your new short haircut.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you rock your new short haircut for round face curly hair:

  • Invest in a high-quality diffuser to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your short haircut.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for round face curly hair?

The best short haircut for round face curly hair is one that helps to elongate your face and works with your natural curl pattern. A longer pixie cut or a bob that hits just below the chin are both great options.

How do I style my short curly hair?

Styling short curly hair is all about embracing your natural curl pattern. Use a diffuser to help define your curls and reduce frizz, and avoid brushing your curls as this can cause breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle, and use a curl-enhancing product to add definition and hold.

How often should I get my short curly hair cut?

Short curly hair should be trimmed every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy, hydrated curls is key to rocking a short haircut for round face curly hair. Here are some tips for keeping your curls in top shape:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as this can cause damage and frizz.
  • Invest in a high-quality diffuser to help define your curls and reduce frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking a short haircut for round face curly hair in no time!
