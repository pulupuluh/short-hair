Hairstyles For Long Hair With Bangs A Complete Guide

If you have long hair with bangs, you are in luck. This versatile hairstyle offers a wide range of styling options that can help you switch up your look effortlessly. Whether you want to go for a chic and sleek look or a bold and edgy one, there is a hairstyle that will suit your style and personality.

How to Style Long Hair with Bangs

The key to styling long hair with bangs is to find a look that complements your face shape and hair texture. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, there is a hairstyle that will work for you.

Before you start styling your hair, make sure your bangs are well-trimmed and maintained. You can trim them yourself or go to a salon to get them professionally done. Once your bangs are in good shape, you can start experimenting with different hairstyles.

1. Straight and Sleek

If you want a chic and polished look, you can straighten your hair and bangs using a flat iron. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using the flat iron to prevent damage. Once your hair is straightened, use a serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

2. Loose Waves

For a more relaxed and effortless look, you can create loose waves using a curling iron or a straightening iron. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before using the iron, and wrap small sections of hair around the barrel. Once you have created the waves, use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold to your hair.

3. Half Up, Half Down

If you want to keep your hair out of your face, you can try a half up, half down hairstyle. Pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a hair tie or pins. Leave your bangs and the rest of your hair loose to create a relaxed and casual look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Hairstyles for Long Hair with Bangs

Here is a step-by-step guide to some popular hairstyles for long hair with bangs:

1. Braided Crown

  1. Part your hair in the middle and create two braids on each side of your head, starting from the front of your hairline.
  2. Cross the braids over the top of your head, tucking the ends of the braids under each other to create a crown-like effect.
  3. Secure the braids with bobby pins and finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Messy Bun

  1. Pull your hair back into a high ponytail and secure it with a hair tie.
  2. Twist the ponytail around the hair tie to create a bun, leaving the ends of your hair out.
  3. Pin the bun in place with bobby pins and pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

3. Side Braid

  1. Part your hair on the side and create a loose braid on the opposite side of your part.
  2. Pull the rest of your hair to one side and pin the braid in place behind your ear.
  3. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Long Hair with Bangs

Here are some tips to help you style your long hair with bangs:

  • Invest in a good quality flat iron, curling iron, and hair dryer to achieve the best results.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair when using heat styling tools.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold to your hair.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and well-maintained.

Frequently Asked Questions

What type of bangs are best for long hair?

Side-swept bangs and curtain bangs are popular styles for long hair as they can help frame your face and add dimension to your hair.

How often should I trim my bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking neat and well-maintained.

Can I style my hair with bangs if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! There are many hairstyles that work well with curly hair and bangs, such as loose waves and messy buns.

Hair Care for Long Hair with Bangs

To keep your long hair and bangs looking healthy and shiny, it is important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often to prevent heat damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and strength.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can rock your long hair with bangs and feel confident and stylish every day!
