Description

Slicked back hair has been a popular hairstyle for decades and continues to be a trendy look in 2023. It's a classic style that's versatile and suitable for any occasion, whether it's a formal event or a casual day out. To achieve the perfect slick back hairstyle, you need the right product that can keep your hair in place without feeling heavy or sticky. In this guide, we'll explore the best product to slick back hair UK and provide you with tips on how to achieve the perfect look.

How to Slick Back Hair

Slicking back your hair can be achieved in several ways, depending on the length and texture of your hair. The most common method involves using a styling product to keep your hair in place. You can also slick back your hair using a comb if you don't have a product on hand.

Method 1: Using a Styling Product

To achieve the perfect slick back look, you need a styling product that can hold your hair in place without feeling heavy or sticky. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. If your hair is wet, use a blow dryer to dry it completely.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your palm, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.
  3. Spread the product on your palms and fingers and work it into your hair, starting from the front and working your way to the back.
  4. Use a comb to slick back your hair, starting from the front and working your way to the back. Make sure to comb your hair in the same direction to create a uniform look.
  5. Once you've achieved your desired look, use a hairspray to set your hair in place.

Method 2: Using a Comb

If you don't have a styling product on hand, you can also slick back your hair using a comb. Here's how:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. If your hair is wet, use a blow dryer to dry it completely.
  2. Apply a small amount of water to your hair to make it more manageable.
  3. Use a comb to slick back your hair, starting from the front and working your way to the back. Make sure to comb your hair in the same direction to create a uniform look.
  4. Once you've achieved your desired look, use a hairspray to set your hair in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect slick back look:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair. If your hair is wet, use a blow dryer to dry it completely.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your palm, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.
  3. Spread the product on your palms and fingers and work it into your hair, starting from the front and working your way to the back.
  4. Use a comb to slick back your hair, starting from the front and working your way to the back. Make sure to comb your hair in the same direction to create a uniform look.
  5. Once you've achieved your desired look, use a hairspray to set your hair in place.

Tips for Slicking Back Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slick back hairstyle:

  • Start with clean and dry hair to achieve the best results.
  • Use a small amount of product to avoid a heavy or sticky feel.
  • Work the product into your hair thoroughly to ensure that each strand is coated.
  • Use a comb to create a uniform look.
  • Use a hairspray to set your hair in place and prevent flyaways.

FAQs

1. Can I slick back my hair if it's short?

Yes, you can still slick back short hair. However, you may need to use less product and a smaller comb to achieve the desired look.

2. Can I slick back my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can slick back curly hair. However, you may need to use more product and a stronger hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

3. How do I choose the right product for my hair type?

Choose a product that's suitable for your hair type, whether it's thick, thin, curly, or straight. Look for products that offer a strong hold, but are also lightweight and non-greasy.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

It's important to take care of your hair when you're slicking it back regularly. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any product buildup.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and cause damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.

Conclusion

Slicking back your hair is a classic hairstyle that's versatile and suitable for any occasion. With the right product and technique, you can achieve the perfect slick back look. Remember to choose a product that's suitable for your hair type and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
