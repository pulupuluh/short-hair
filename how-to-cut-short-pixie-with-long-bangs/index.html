How To Cut Short Pixie With Long Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jun 23, 2023 Â· 8 min read

A short pixie cut with long bangs is a trendy and edgy hairstyle that is perfect for those who want to make a statement. It is a low-maintenance haircut that is easy to style and maintain, making it a popular choice among women of all ages. In this article, we will show you how to cut a short pixie with long bangs in relaxed English language. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Short Pixie with Long Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Short Pixie with Long Bangs
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Short Pixie with Long Bangs

Description

A short pixie with long bangs is a haircut that features short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on top and in the front. The longer bangs can be swept to the side or worn straight down, depending on your personal style. This haircut is perfect for those who want a bold and daring look, and it can be customized to suit any face shape and hair type.

How to Cut Short Pixie with Long Bangs

Cutting a short pixie with long bangs can be intimidating, especially if you have never done it before. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the look you want. Here are some steps to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by sectioning off the hair on top of the head. Use a comb to create a triangle shape from the hairline down to the crown of the head. Clip the hair on the sides and back out of the way.

Step 2: Use scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. Start at the back of the head and work your way forward, using your fingers to hold the hair up as you cut. Make sure to cut in small sections and check the length as you go.

Step 3: Once you have cut the top section, blend it into the sides and back. Use scissors or clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back to the desired length. Make sure to use a comb to keep the hair even and to blend the top and sides together.

Step 4: Now it's time to cut the bangs. Start by combing the hair straight down over the forehead. Use scissors to cut the bangs to the desired length, making sure to cut in small sections and check the length as you go. If you want a more textured look, use thinning shears to add some texture to the ends of the bangs.

Step 5: Once you have finished cutting the bangs, style the hair as desired. You can use a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume and texture, or you can let the hair air dry for a more natural look.

Tips for Cutting Short Pixie with Long Bangs

Cutting a short pixie with long bangs can be challenging, but with these tips, you can achieve the look you want:

  • Start with clean, dry hair to make it easier to cut and style.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean and even cut.
  • Cut in small sections to ensure evenness and accuracy.
  • Check the length as you go to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use thinning shears to add texture to the ends of the bangs.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I cut a short pixie with long bangs myself?

A: While it is possible to cut your own hair, we recommend going to a professional stylist to ensure the best results.

Q: Will a short pixie with long bangs suit my face shape?

A: Yes, a short pixie with long bangs can be customized to suit any face shape. Talk to your stylist to find the best style for you.

Hair Care for Short Pixie with Long Bangs

Once you have cut your short pixie with long bangs, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and style.

Cutting a short pixie with long bangs can be a fun and exciting way to switch up your look. With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and trendy haircut that suits your personal style and preferences. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful!
