Is Long Hair Okay For Over 60

Written by Daniel Jul 20, 2023 Â· 8 min read

As we age, it's common to start questioning whether certain hairstyles are still age-appropriate. One question that often comes up is whether long hair is okay for women over 60. The short answer is yes! Long hair can look beautiful and chic at any age. In this article, we'll explore the topic of long hair for older women, including how to wear it, care for it, and style it.

How to Wear Long Hair Over 60

The key to wearing long hair over 60 is to make sure it's styled in a way that's flattering and appropriate for your face shape and lifestyle. Here are some tips:

1. Consider Face Shape

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to avoid long, straight hair that can make your face look wider. Instead, try a layered style with soft waves or curls. If you have a square face, on the other hand, you may want to avoid blunt, straight bangs that can make your face appear more angular. Instead, try side-swept bangs or a layered cut.

2. Keep it Healthy

Long hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy and looking its best. Make sure to get regular trims to prevent split ends and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and shiny.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

Long hair can be high-maintenance, so it's important to consider your lifestyle when deciding whether it's right for you. If you're active and outdoorsy, for example, you may find that a shorter, more practical style is a better fit.

How to Style Long Hair Over 60

Long hair offers a wide range of styling options, from sleek and sophisticated to loose and beachy. Here are some ideas to get you started:

1. Sleek and Straight

A sleek, straight style can be elegant and timeless. Use a straightening iron to smooth out your hair and add shine, and finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep everything in place.

2. Soft Waves

Soft waves can add texture and movement to long hair. Use a curling iron to create loose, beachy waves, and finish with a texturizing spray for a relaxed, tousled look.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down style can be a great option for long hair. Pull the top half of your hair into a ponytail or bun, leaving the rest loose, for a chic and effortless look.

Tips for Long Hair Care Over 60

In addition to regular trims and deep conditioning treatments, there are some other things you can do to keep your long hair looking its best:

1. Use Heat Protectant

If you're using heat styling tools like a straightening iron or curling wand, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to protect it when you're spending time outdoors. Wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to shield your hair from harmful UV rays.

3. Don't Overwash

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair no more than every other day, and use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.

FAQs

Q: Will long hair make me look older?

A: Not necessarily! Long hair can be flattering at any age, as long as it's styled in a way that's appropriate for your face shape and lifestyle.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail or bun?

A: Absolutely! A ponytail or bun can be a great option for long hair, especially on hot days or when you're in a rush.

Q: Will long hair be too high-maintenance for me?

A: It depends on your lifestyle and preferences. Long hair can be high-maintenance, but it can also be easy to style and care for if you choose the right cut and products.

Final Thoughts

Long hair can be a beautiful and stylish choice for women over 60. By choosing the right cut and styling it in a way that's flattering and appropriate for your face shape and lifestyle, you can enjoy the benefits of long hair at any age. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you!
