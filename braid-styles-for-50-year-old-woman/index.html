Braid Styles For 50 Year Old Woman A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Mar 12, 2023 Â· 10 min read

If you're a 50 year old woman looking for a new and trendy hairstyle, then braids may be the perfect choice for you. Braids are a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle that can be worn in various styles and can add a touch of elegance to your overall look. In this article, we will guide you through some of the best braid styles for 50 year old women and provide you with tips on how to achieve each look.

Description of Braid Styles for 50 Year Old Woman

There are many different braid styles that can complement a 50 year old woman's features. Some of the most popular braid styles for this age group include:

  • French Braids
  • Dutch Braids
  • Box Braids
  • Cornrows
  • Fishtail Braids

Each of these styles has its unique look and requires different techniques to achieve. French braids and Dutch braids are perfect for those who want an elegant and polished look. Box braids and cornrows are excellent protective styles that can be worn for weeks at a time. Fishtail braids are perfect for those who want a bohemian or beachy look.

How to Achieve Braid Styles for 50 Year Old Woman

Before you start braiding your hair, make sure that it's clean and free of tangles. Use a detangling spray or conditioner to make the process easier. Once your hair is ready, you can start braiding by following these steps:

  1. Part your hair into sections using a comb or your fingers. The size of the sections will depend on the braid style that you want to achieve.
  2. Start with three strands of hair and cross the right strand over the middle strand.
  3. Next, cross the left strand over the middle strand.
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 until you reach the end of the section.
  5. Secure the braid with an elastic band or a hairpin.

Repeat these steps for each section of hair until you've achieved your desired look. You can also experiment with different braiding techniques, such as twisting or knotting, to achieve a unique look.

Step by Step Guide for Braid Styles for 50 Year Old Woman

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve some of the most popular braid styles for 50 year old women:

French Braids

  1. Begin by separating a section of hair from the top of your head and divide it into three equal parts.
  2. Start braiding by crossing the right strand over the middle strand, then the left strand over the middle strand.
  3. As you continue braiding, add small sections of hair from the sides to the braid.
  4. Continue braiding until you reach the nape of your neck, then secure the braid with an elastic band.
  5. Repeat on the other side.
  6. For a sleeker look, you can use gel to smooth down any flyaways or frizz.

Dutch Braids

  1. Start by dividing your hair into two equal parts.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the top of the left side and divide it into three equal parts.
  3. Start braiding by crossing the right strand under the middle strand, then the left strand under the middle strand.
  4. As you continue braiding, add small sections of hair from the sides to the braid.
  5. Continue braiding until you reach the nape of your neck, then secure the braid with an elastic band.
  6. Repeat on the other side.
  7. For a polished look, you can use hairspray to keep the braids in place.

Box Braids

  1. Start by sectioning your hair and dividing it into smaller sections.
  2. Take one section of hair and divide it into three equal parts.
  3. Start braiding by crossing the right strand over the middle strand, then the left strand over the middle strand.
  4. As you continue braiding, add extensions to each section to create longer braids.
  5. Continue braiding until you reach the end of the section, then secure the braid with an elastic band.
  6. Repeat on each section until you've braided your entire head.
  7. For a sleeker look, you can use gel to smooth down any flyaways or frizz.

Tips for Braid Styles for 50 Year Old Woman

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing and styling braid styles for 50 year old women:

  • Choose a braid style that complements your face shape and features.
  • Use a detangling spray or conditioner to make the braiding process easier.
  • Experiment with different braiding techniques to achieve a unique look.
  • Use hairspray or gel to keep the braids in place and prevent frizz.
  • For a sleeker look, you can use a hair straightener to smooth down any frizz or flyaways.

FAQ About Braid Styles for 50 Year Old Woman

Q. Are braids suitable for thinning hair?

A. Yes, braids can be a great hairstyle for women with thinning hair. They can add volume and texture to your hair and also protect it from damage.

Q. How long do braids last?

A. It depends on the braid style and the type of hair extensions used. Some braids can last up to six weeks with proper maintenance.

Q. Can I wash my hair with braids?

A. Yes, you can wash your hair with braids. Use a mild shampoo and avoid scrubbing your scalp too hard to prevent frizz and damage to the braids.

Hair Care for Braid Styles for 50 Year Old Woman

Proper hair care is essential when wearing braids. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down your hair and cause buildup.
  • Protect your hair at night by wearing a satin bonnet or using a satin pillowcase.
  • Moisturize your hair and scalp with natural oils, such as coconut oil or olive oil.
  • Take breaks between braiding to give your hair a chance to rest and recover.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair stays healthy and beautiful while wearing braids.

Conclusion

Braid styles for 50 year old women are a great way to add elegance and sophistication to your overall look. With the right techniques and care, you can achieve beautiful and trendy braid styles that will make you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to choose a braid style that complements your face shape and features and experiment with different techniques to achieve a unique look. With proper hair care, you can enjoy beautiful and healthy hair while wearing braids.
