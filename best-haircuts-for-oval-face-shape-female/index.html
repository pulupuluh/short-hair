Best Haircuts For Oval Face Shape Female A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 29, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you one of the lucky ones with an oval face shape? If so, you have a versatile canvas to work with when it comes to your hair. However, with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect haircut that flatters your face shape the most. In this article, we’ll guide you through the best haircuts for oval face shape female that you can try out in 2023.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Haircuts for Oval Face Shape Female
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shape Female

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we jump into the best haircuts for oval face shape female, let’s first determine if you have an oval face shape. Here are the characteristics of an oval face shape:

  • Forehead is slightly wider than the chin
  • Cheekbones are high, and the face tapers to a narrow oval chin
  • Face length is about one and a half times the width

If your face fits these characteristics, then you have an oval face shape.

Best Haircuts for Oval Face Shape Female

An oval face shape is considered the ideal shape because it can pull off any haircut. Here are some of the best haircuts for oval face shape female that you can try out:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for oval face shape females because they enhance your cheekbones and make your face appear more symmetrical. Plus, you can style your hair in many ways with long layers, from beachy waves to straight and sleek.

2. Bob

A classic bob is another great haircut for oval face shape female. This haircut frames your face beautifully and accentuates your jawline. You can choose from a chin-length bob or a longer bob, depending on your preference.

3. Pixie Cut

If you’re feeling adventurous, a pixie cut is a great haircut for oval face shape female. This bold and edgy haircut accentuates your facial features, making your eyes and cheekbones pop.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great addition to any of the haircuts mentioned above for oval face shape females. They add dimension to your face and draw attention to your eyes.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Now that you know the best haircuts for oval face shape female, here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Use a round brush to add volume to your hair
  • Blow dry your hair in the opposite direction of your natural part to add volume
  • Use a straightener to create sleek and straight hair
  • Add curls to your hair to create a romantic look
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and hairpins

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have a different face shape?

If you have a different face shape, don’t worry! There are haircuts that flatter every face shape. Check out our other articles for haircuts that flatter round, square, and heart-shaped faces.

How often should I get a haircut?

It’s recommended that you get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut’s shape and style.

What if I don’t want to cut my hair?

If you don’t want to cut your hair, you can always experiment with different hairstyles. Try different partings or add some layers to your hair to create a new look.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shape Female

Lastly, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair

Now that you know the best haircuts for oval face shape female, it’s time to book your next haircut appointment and try out a new style! Remember to consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal preferences when choosing a haircut to ensure that it reflects your unique style.
