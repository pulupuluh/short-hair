How To Cut The Sides Of A Mullet

Written by Petter May 25, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of your regular haircut and want to try something new? A mullet hairstyle can be a great option for those looking for a bold and edgy look. However, to achieve the perfect mullet, you need to know how to cut the sides properly. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut the sides of a mullet.

Description

A mullet is a hairstyle that has short hair on the top and sides with long hair at the back. It was popular in the 80s and early 90s, but it has made a comeback in recent years. The mullet is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in various ways, making it a favorite among those who want a unique look.

How to Cut the Sides of a Mullet

Before you start cutting the sides of your hair, you need to understand the basics of mullet cutting. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, and a razor. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash your hair thoroughly and dry it completely before you start cutting. Wet hair can be tricky to cut, and you may end up with uneven sides. Make sure your hair is dry and tangle-free before you begin.

Step 2: Section your hair

Use the comb to section your hair by parting it in the middle. You need to create two sections â one for the top and one for the bottom. Clip the top section of your hair to keep it out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the sides

Start with one side of your head and comb the hair straight down. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Repeat the process on the other side. You can use a razor to create a more textured look.

Step 4: Blend the sides with the back

Once you have cut the sides, you need to blend them with the back. Use the scissors or razor to create a seamless transition from the sides to the back. This will give your mullet a more polished look.

Step 5: Style your hair

Now that you have cut the sides of your mullet, you can style it as you like. You can use a hair gel or wax to create a messy, textured look, or you can comb it back for a sleek and sophisticated style.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect mullet:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Use a mirror to check the length and ensure that both sides are even.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a friend or a professional hairstylist.

FAQ

Q. Is the mullet hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

A. The mullet can work for most hair types, but it may not be suitable for very fine or thin hair.

Q. Can I cut my own mullet?

A. Yes, you can cut your own mullet, but it's best to get help from someone else, especially if you're not experienced in cutting hair.

Q. How often do I need to cut my mullet?

A. It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it. You may need to trim the sides every few weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Hair Care

After cutting your mullet, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and protect it from the sun and other environmental factors. Regular trims can also help keep your mullet looking fresh and stylish.

Now that you know how to cut the sides of a mullet, why not give it a try? With a little practice and patience, you can achieve the perfect mullet hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd.
