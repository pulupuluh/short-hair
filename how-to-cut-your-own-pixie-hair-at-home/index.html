How To Cut Your Own Pixie Hair At Home

Description

Pixie haircuts are a great way to add style and personality to your look. They are short, easy to maintain, and can be customized to suit your face shape and features. While it is always best to have a professional stylist cut your hair, cutting your own pixie hair at home can save you time and money. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own pixie hair at home.

How to Cut Your Own Pixie Hair at Home

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done. Here are the steps to follow when cutting your own pixie hair at home:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to see the shape of your hair and where to cut.
  2. Section your hair. Divide your hair into sections using clips or elastics. Start by sectioning the top of your hair from the bottom.
  3. Start cutting. Use hair cutting scissors to trim the ends of your hair. Cut a small amount at a time, and work your way around your head.
  4. Trim the sides. Use a comb to help guide your scissors as you cut the sides of your hair. Start with the longer side, and work your way to the shorter side.
  5. Cut the back. Use a mirror to help you see the back of your head. Cut a small amount at a time, and make sure both sides are even.
  6. Check the length. After you have finished cutting your hair, check the length to ensure it is even.
  7. Style your hair. Use styling products to create the desired look. You can use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and volume.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Pixie Hair at Home

Here are some tips to help you cut your own pixie hair at home:

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. Using regular scissors can result in a jagged cut.
  • Start with small cuts. You can always cut more, but you can't add hair back once it's been cut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your hair. It can be difficult to see the back of your head, so use a mirror to make sure both sides are even.
  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to take your time and cut small amounts at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own pixie hair at home:

  • Can I cut my own pixie hair if I have never cut hair before? It is always best to have a professional stylist cut your hair, but if you are confident and have the right tools, you can cut your own hair.
  • What if I make a mistake? If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a stylist to fix it.
  • How often should I cut my pixie hair? Pixie haircuts require regular maintenance, so you should plan on cutting your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Pixie Hair at Home

After cutting your own pixie hair at home, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style.

By following these tips, you can maintain your pixie haircut and keep it looking great.
