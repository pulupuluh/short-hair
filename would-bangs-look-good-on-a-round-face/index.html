Would Bangs Look Good On A Round Face

Written by Oliver Sep 11, 2023 Â· 8 min read

If you have a round face and are considering getting bangs, you may be wondering if they will look good on you. Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with round faces struggle to find the perfect hairstyles that flatter their face shape. In this article, we'll explore whether or not bangs will look good on a round face, along with some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

Before we dive into whether or not bangs will look good on a round face, let's start by understanding what a round face actually is. A round face is characterized by soft, curved lines and a lack of prominent angles. It's typically widest at the cheeks and narrower at the forehead and jawline.

How to

If you have a round face and are considering getting bangs, there are a few things to keep in mind. Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

1. Consider the Length

The length of your bangs can make a big difference when it comes to flattering a round face. Shorter bangs can accentuate the roundness of your face, while longer bangs can help elongate it. Consider opting for longer, side-swept bangs that hit just below your eyebrows.

2. Opt for Soft, Rounded Edges

Sharp, angular bangs can make a round face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for soft, rounded edges that blend seamlessly into the rest of your hair. This will help create a more balanced overall look.

3. Avoid Heavy, Blunt Bangs

Heavy, blunt bangs can be too harsh on a round face. Instead, opt for wispy, textured bangs that have movement and flow. This will help soften the look of your face and create a more natural, effortless style.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect bangs for your round face:

Step 1: Consider Your Face Shape

Before you make any decisions about your bangs, take a good look at your face shape. If you have a round face, you'll want to opt for bangs that help elongate your face and create more angles.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

As we mentioned earlier, the length of your bangs can make a big difference. Opt for longer, side-swept bangs that hit just below your eyebrows.

Step 3: Ask Your Stylist for Soft, Rounded Edges

When you visit your stylist, ask them to give you soft, rounded edges that blend seamlessly into the rest of your hair. This will help create a more balanced overall look.

Step 4: Opt for Wispy, Textured Bangs

To soften the look of your face, opt for wispy, textured bangs that have movement and flow. This will help create a more natural, effortless style.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect bangs for your round face:

1. Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can also impact how your bangs look on your face. If you have thick, coarse hair, you may need to thin out your bangs to avoid them looking too heavy.

2. Use Styling Products

Styling products can help you achieve the perfect look for your bangs. Use a small amount of mousse or texturizing spray to add volume and texture.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the perfect look for you. Try different lengths, textures, and styles until you find the perfect fit.

FAQ

Will bangs make my round face look even rounder?

Short, blunt bangs can make a round face appear even rounder. Opt for longer, side-swept bangs that hit just below your eyebrows to help elongate your face.

What type of bangs should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid heavy, blunt bangs that are too harsh on a round face. Opt for wispy, textured bangs that have movement and flow to soften the look of your face.

What hair texture works best with bangs on a round face?

The best hair texture for bangs on a round face is fine to medium thickness hair. Thicker hair may need to be thinned out to avoid looking too heavy.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Be sure to use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type. Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Don't forget to schedule regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.

In conclusion, bangs can look great on a round face if you choose the right length, edges, and texture. Consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing your bangs, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the perfect fit. With proper hair care, you can achieve a beautiful, flattering look that highlights your best features.
