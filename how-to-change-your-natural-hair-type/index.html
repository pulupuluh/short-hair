How To Change Your Natural Hair Type

Written by Robby Aug 13, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you tired of your natural hair type and want to change it? You're not alone. Many people want to change their hair type for various reasons, but it's important to understand that changing your hair type is not an easy process. In this article, we will discuss how to change your natural hair type, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the process of changing your natural hair type, it's important to understand what your natural hair type is. Your hair type is determined by the shape of your hair follicles. There are four main hair types: straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Each type has its own unique characteristics, such as texture, density, and porosity.

If you want to change your natural hair type, you will need to use different techniques and products depending on your current hair type and the desired hair type. It's important to note that changing your hair type is not a permanent solution, and you will need to continue using the techniques and products to maintain your new hair type.

How to

The process of changing your natural hair type involves a few different steps. The first step is to determine your current hair type and the desired hair type. Once you have identified your current hair type and the desired hair type, you can start using techniques and products to achieve the desired hair type.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to change your natural hair type:

  1. Determine your current hair type and the desired hair type.
  2. Research techniques and products that are suitable for your hair type and the desired hair type.
  3. Start using the techniques and products to achieve the desired hair type.
  4. Maintain the new hair type by continuing to use the techniques and products.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you change your natural hair type:

  • Be patient. Changing your natural hair type takes time and effort.
  • Experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Consult with a hair stylist or professional if you are unsure about what techniques and products to use.
  • Maintain a healthy hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and strong.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your natural hair type:

Can I change my natural hair type permanently?

No, changing your natural hair type is not a permanent solution. You will need to continue using the techniques and products to maintain your new hair type.

How long does it take to change my natural hair type?

It depends on the current hair type and the desired hair type. It can take anywhere from a few weeks to a few months to see results.

What techniques and products should I use to change my natural hair type?

The techniques and products you should use depend on your current hair type and the desired hair type. Research different techniques and products and consult with a hair stylist or professional if you are unsure.

Hair Care

Maintaining a healthy hair care routine is important for keeping your hair healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining a healthy hair care routine:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Changing your natural hair type is not an easy process, but with patience and the right techniques and products, it is possible. Remember to maintain a healthy hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and strong.
