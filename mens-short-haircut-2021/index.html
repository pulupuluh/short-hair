Mens Short Haircut 2021 A Guide To The Latest Trends

Written by Daniel Apr 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Description

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle this year, why not try a mens short haircut? Short hair is low maintenance, stylish, and perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we'll take a look at the latest trends for mens short haircuts in 2021, and give you some tips on how to care for your new cut.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get a Mens Short Haircut
  • Step by Step Guide to Cutting Mens Short Hair
  • Tips for Styling Mens Short Hair
  • FAQ About Mens Short Haircuts

How to Get a Mens Short Haircut

Getting a mens short haircut is easy. You can either go to a barber or stylist and ask for a short cut, or you can do it yourself at home. If you're going to a professional, bring a picture of the style you want so they can give you the right cut. If you're doing it yourself, invest in a good pair of clippers and follow our step-by-step guide below.

Step by Step Guide to Cutting Mens Short Hair

1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's damp. 2. Use a comb to part your hair where you want it. 3. Use clippers with a guard to trim the sides and back of your head. 4. Use scissors to trim the top of your hair to the desired length. 5. Blend the sides and top using clippers or scissors. 6. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs. 7. Style your hair using your fingers or a comb.

Tips for Styling Mens Short Hair

Styling mens short hair is easy. Here are some tips to keep in mind: - Use a matte pomade or wax for a natural, textured look. - Use a comb to create a side part or slicked-back style. - Use your fingers to create a messy, tousled look. - Use a blow dryer to add volume to your hair.

FAQ About Mens Short Haircuts

Q: What are the best short haircuts for men in 2021? A: The buzz cut, crew cut, and textured crop are all popular mens short haircuts in 2021. Q: How often should I get my mens short haircut trimmed? A: Short hair grows quickly, so it's best to get it trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style. Q: Can I style my mens short haircut differently for different occasions? A: Yes, mens short haircuts are versatile and can be styled differently for formal or casual events.

Hair Care Tips for Mens Short Haircuts

Maintaining your mens short haircut is easy with these tips: - Shampoo and condition your hair regularly. - Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized. - Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy. - Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or sunscreen.

Conclusion

Mens short haircuts are a great choice for any man looking for a low-maintenance, stylish haircut in 2021. With the right tools and styling tips, you can create a look that's perfect for any occasion. Remember to take care of your hair with regular washing, conditioning, and styling to keep it looking its best.
