Mens Short Haircut Styles For Thick Hair How To Get A Stylish Look

Written by Bardi Apr 15, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing, but it can also be a challenge when it comes to finding the right haircut. If you're looking for mens short haircut styles for thick hair, you're in luck! In this article, we'll discuss some popular short haircuts for men with thick hair, how to get the look, tips for styling, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get the Look
  • Popular Short Haircuts for Thick Hair
  • Tips for Styling
  • Hair Care Advice
  • FAQ

How to Get the Look

Before you head to the barbershop, it's important to have a clear idea of the haircut you want. Look for inspiration online or in magazines, and bring a photo with you to show your stylist. Be sure to communicate any concerns or preferences you have, such as how short you want the cut or whether you want any texture or layers.

When it comes to styling, mens short haircut styles for thick hair can be low-maintenance and easy to maintain. A simple, classic style like the crew cut or buzz cut can look great on thick hair and requires minimal styling. For a more textured look, consider a choppy, layered cut or a messy fringe.

Popular Short Haircuts for Thick Hair

Here are some popular mens short haircut styles for thick hair:

Crew Cut

The crew cut is a classic short hairstyle that's easy to maintain and looks great on men with thick hair. The hair on the top of the head is cut short and tapered, while the sides and back are buzzed.

Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a very short hairstyle that's perfect for men who want a low-maintenance, no-fuss look. The hair is cut very short all over the head, and there are no distinct layers or textures.

Choppy Layers

If you want a more textured look, consider a choppy, layered cut. This style involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create a messy, tousled look. It's a great option for men with thick hair who want a more relaxed, casual style.

Messy Fringe

The messy fringe is a popular style for men with thick hair who want a more stylish look. This style involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while leaving the hair on top longer and styled into a messy fringe.

Tips for Styling

Once you've got your new haircut, you'll want to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for styling mens short haircut styles for thick hair:

  • Use a quality styling product to keep your hair in place, such as a wax, pomade, or clay.
  • Use a blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair, if desired.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as slicking your hair back or creating a messy, textured look.

Hair Care Advice

Thick hair can be prone to dryness and damage, so it's important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips for men with thick hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often, as these can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for thick hair?

There are many great short haircuts for men with thick hair, including the crew cut, buzz cut, choppy layers, and messy fringe. The best haircut for you will depend on your personal style and preferences.

How do I style my thick hair?

To style mens short haircut styles for thick hair, use a quality styling product and experiment with different techniques, such as slicking your hair back or creating a messy, textured look.

How do I take care of my thick hair?

To take care of thick hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoid using hot tools too often, and get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

With these tips and ideas for mens short haircut styles for thick hair, you'll be able to find a look that's stylish and easy to maintain. Whether you opt for a classic crew cut or a trendy messy fringe, your thick hair will look great with the right haircut and styling techniques.
