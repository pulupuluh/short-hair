How To Style Edges With Edge Control Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jun 16, 2023 · 8 min read

Styling your edges can be a daunting task. With so many products and techniques available, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. But fear not, we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll take you through step-by-step on how to style your edges with edge control. Whether you’re a beginner or a pro, we’re confident that you’ll find these tips and tricks helpful.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Edges with Edge Control
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Edges
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style Edges with Edge Control

Edge control is a styling product that helps to lay down and tame the hair around your hairline. It’s available in different formulas, including gel, cream, and wax. The purpose of edge control is to create a sleek, polished look that enhances your hairstyle.

Choose the Right Edge Control Product

Choosing the right edge control product is crucial for achieving the look you want. Look for a product that is lightweight, non-greasy, and has a strong hold. If you have natural hair, consider a product that is formulated for your hair type.

Prepare Your Hair

Before you start styling your edges, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can use a hairbrush or comb to detangle your hair and remove any knots. Apply a small amount of leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to add moisture and prevent breakage.

Apply Edge Control

Take a small amount of edge control and apply it to your edges using your fingertips or a brush. Start from the front and work your way back, making sure to distribute the product evenly. Use a brush or comb to smooth down your edges and create your desired shape.

Set Your Edges

Once you’ve styled your edges, it’s important to set them in place. You can use a scarf or headwrap to hold your edges down while the product dries. Leave the scarf on for at least 10-15 minutes, or until the product is completely dry.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics of styling your edges with edge control, let’s go through a step-by-step guide.

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. This will remove any buildup and add moisture to your hair.

Step 2: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair. This will make it easier to style your edges.

Step 3: Apply Edge Control

Take a small amount of edge control and apply it to your edges using your fingertips or a brush. Make sure to distribute the product evenly.

Step 4: Style Your Edges

Use a brush or comb to smooth down your edges and create your desired shape.

Step 5: Set Your Edges

Wrap a scarf or headwrap around your hairline to hold your edges down while the product dries. Leave the scarf on for at least 10-15 minutes, or until the product is completely dry.

Tips for Styling Edges

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect edge control style:

  • Use a small amount of edge control to avoid buildup and flaking
  • Don’t apply edge control to wet hair, as this can dilute the product and reduce its effectiveness
  • Use a satin scarf or pillowcase to protect your edges while you sleep
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly or using too much tension when styling your edges, as this can cause breakage

FAQs

How often should I style my edges?

You can style your edges as often as you like, but it’s important to give your hair a break from time to time. Too much styling can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

Can edge control be used on natural hair?

Yes, edge control can be used on natural hair. Look for a product that is formulated for your hair type.

How do I prevent my edges from frizzing?

Use a small amount of edge control to smooth down your edges and prevent frizz. You can also wrap a scarf or headwrap around your hairline to hold your edges down while the product dries.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage

Styling your edges with edge control is a great way to enhance your hairstyle and create a sleek, polished look. With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to achieve the perfect edge control style in no time.
