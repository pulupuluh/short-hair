Can You Cut Curly Hair Short Tips And Tricks For The Perfect Haircut

Written by Petter Feb 23, 2023 · 7 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it’s beautiful and unique, it can also be difficult to manage and style. One of the biggest questions that people with curly hair have is whether or not they can cut their hair short. The answer is yes, you can cut curly hair short! In this blog post, we’ll go over some tips and tricks for the perfect haircut.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Curly Hair Short
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Curly Hair Short
  • Tips for Cutting Curly Hair Short
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Description

Curly hair can be cut short, but it requires a different approach than cutting straight hair. Curly hair tends to shrink up more than straight hair, so it’s important to take that into account when cutting. Additionally, curly hair can be cut in a variety of ways, from a pixie cut to a bob to a shoulder-length cut. The key is to find a cut that works for your face shape and hair type.

How to Cut Curly Hair Short

Cutting curly hair short requires a different technique than cutting straight hair. Here are some general tips:

  • Start with dry hair: Curly hair looks longer when it’s wet, so it’s important to start with dry hair to get an accurate idea of how short you want to go.
  • Use the right tools: Use sharp shears and a comb to cut curly hair. Don’t use thinning shears, as they can create frizz.
  • Go slow: Cut a little bit at a time, as curly hair tends to shrink up more than straight hair.
  • Consider the shape: Curly hair can be cut in a variety of shapes, from a round shape to a square shape to a triangle shape. Consider your face shape and hair type when deciding on a shape.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for help: Cutting curly hair can be tricky, so don’t be afraid to ask a professional for help.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Curly Hair Short

Here’s a step-by-step guide to cutting curly hair short:

  1. Start with dry hair.
  2. Section off your hair into manageable sections.
  3. Comb through each section to get rid of any knots or tangles.
  4. Take a small section of hair and cut a little bit at a time, going slow.
  5. Repeat with each section of hair.
  6. Check the length as you go to make sure it’s even.
  7. Once you’ve cut all the sections of hair, go back through and make any necessary adjustments.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair Short

Here are some additional tips for cutting curly hair short:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying to enhance your natural curls.
  • Avoid using a brush when blow-drying, as it can create frizz.
  • Consider getting your hair cut when it’s in its natural state, as opposed to straightening it first.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and reduce frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

How short can you cut curly hair?

You can cut curly hair as short as you want, but keep in mind that it will shrink up more than straight hair. It’s important to start with dry hair and cut a little bit at a time to get an accurate idea of how short you want to go.

Can you cut curly hair with layers?

Yes, you can cut curly hair with layers. Layers can help add volume and shape to curly hair.

What is the best haircut for curly hair?

The best haircut for curly hair depends on your face shape and hair type. A shoulder-length cut, a bob, or a pixie cut can all work for curly hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as it can create frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.

In conclusion, cutting curly hair short is possible, but it requires a different approach than cutting straight hair. Follow these tips and tricks, and don’t be afraid to ask for help from a professional. With the right cut and care, your curly hair can look beautiful and unique.
