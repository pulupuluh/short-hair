Why Does My Hair Fall Out After Coloring

Written by Luffy Sep 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you noticing more hair falling out after coloring it? This can be a common concern for those who dye their hair. In this article, we will discuss why this happens and what you can do to prevent it.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When you color your hair, the chemicals in the dye can cause damage to your hair. This damage can weaken your hair and cause it to fall out. Additionally, if you have relaxed hair, the chemicals in the relaxer can also contribute to hair loss.

It is important to note that hair loss after coloring can happen to anyone, but it is more common in those who color their hair frequently or use harsh chemical dyes.

How To Prevent Hair Loss After Coloring

The best way to prevent hair loss after coloring is to take good care of your hair before and after coloring. Here are some tips:

1. Choose a gentle hair dye

When choosing a hair dye, look for one that is gentle and does not contain harsh chemicals. A natural or organic hair dye may be a good option.

2. Strengthen your hair before coloring

Use a deep conditioning treatment on your hair before coloring to help strengthen it. This will help to minimize damage from the chemicals in the dye.

3. Follow the instructions carefully

Be sure to follow the instructions on the hair dye carefully. Leaving the dye on for too long or not rinsing it out properly can cause damage to your hair.

4. Avoid heat styling

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair after coloring. The heat can cause further damage to your hair and increase the risk of hair loss.

Step By Step Guide

Step 1:

Choose a gentle hair dye that does not contain harsh chemicals.

Step 2:

Deep condition your hair before coloring to help strengthen it.

Step 3:

Follow the instructions on the hair dye carefully.

Step 4:

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair after coloring.

Tips

  • Avoid coloring your hair too frequently.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Can hair loss after coloring be reversed?

Hair loss after coloring can be reversed by taking good care of your hair and avoiding harsh chemicals. Use gentle hair products, avoid heat styling, and be sure to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

How long does it take for hair to grow back after hair loss from coloring?

Hair typically grows back at a rate of about 1/2 inch per month. However, the time it takes for hair to grow back after hair loss from coloring can vary depending on the individual and the extent of the damage.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and prevent hair loss after coloring, it is important to take good care of your hair. Use gentle hair products, avoid heat styling, and get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can prevent hair loss after coloring and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
