Can You Save Your Hair During Chemotherapy

Written by Frank Apr 10, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you or someone you know is undergoing chemotherapy, one of the biggest concerns may be hair loss. Losing your hair can be a difficult experience, but the good news is that there are steps you can take to help save your hair during chemotherapy. In this article, we will explore how to save your hair during chemotherapy, step-by-step, with tips and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Save Your Hair During Chemotherapy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Saving Your Hair During Chemotherapy
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care During Chemotherapy

How to Save Your Hair During Chemotherapy

The first step in saving your hair during chemotherapy is to talk to your doctor about your options. There are drugs that can help reduce hair loss during chemotherapy, and your doctor may recommend one of these medications.

Another option is to use a scalp cooling system during chemotherapy treatment. This system works by lowering the temperature of the scalp, which can reduce the amount of chemotherapy that reaches the hair follicles. This can help reduce hair loss during treatment.

It's important to note that not all chemotherapy drugs are compatible with scalp cooling systems. Your doctor can help determine if this is a viable option for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you decide to use a scalp cooling system, here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose a scalp cooling system that is compatible with your chemotherapy treatment.
  2. Read the instructions carefully and follow them closely.
  3. Before treatment, wet your hair with cool water.
  4. Apply the scalp cooling system according to the instructions.
  5. Keep the scalp cooling system on for the recommended amount of time.
  6. After treatment, gently wash your hair with a mild shampoo.

It's important to note that scalp cooling systems may not be covered by insurance, so be sure to check with your provider before making a purchase.

Tips for Saving Your Hair During Chemotherapy

In addition to using a scalp cooling system, here are some tips for saving your hair during chemotherapy:

  • Gently brush your hair with a soft-bristled brush.
  • Avoid using heating tools like hair dryers and curling irons.
  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and avoid harsh chemicals.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in protein and vitamins.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about saving your hair during chemotherapy:

Will I definitely lose my hair during chemotherapy?

Not everyone who undergoes chemotherapy will lose their hair, but it is a common side effect. Talk to your doctor about your options for reducing hair loss.

Is a scalp cooling system painful?

Scalp cooling systems can be uncomfortable, but they should not be painful. If you experience significant discomfort, talk to your doctor.

Can I dye my hair during chemotherapy?

It's generally recommended to avoid hair dye during chemotherapy, as the chemicals can be harsh on your scalp and cause irritation. If you must dye your hair, talk to your doctor first.

Hair Care During Chemotherapy

Even if you do experience hair loss during chemotherapy, there are steps you can take to care for your scalp and promote healthy hair growth after treatment. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid harsh chemicals and hot styling tools.
  • Wear a hat or scarf to protect your scalp from the sun.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in protein and vitamins.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair.
  • Consider using a wig or hairpiece if you are self-conscious about hair loss.

Remember that hair loss is a common side effect of chemotherapy, but it is temporary. With the right care and treatment, your hair can grow back healthy and strong after treatment.

If you have any questions or concerns about saving your hair during chemotherapy, be sure to talk to your doctor or a qualified healthcare professional.
