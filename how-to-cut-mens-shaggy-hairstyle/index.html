How To Cut Men s Shaggy Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy May 12, 2023 Â· 9 min read

If you're looking for a versatile and low-maintenance haircut, then the shaggy hairstyle might be just what you need. This hairstyle is popular among men of all ages and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. In this article, we'll show you how to cut men's shaggy hairstyle in simple and easy-to-follow steps. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced barber, this guide will help you create a stylish and effortless look for your clients.

Description

The shaggy hairstyle is a layered haircut that features textured ends and a messy, undone look. It's characterized by its choppy layers and relaxed vibe, making it a popular choice for men who want a casual and effortless style. The shaggy hairstyle can be worn with short, medium, or long hair and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. Whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair, the shaggy hairstyle can work for you.

How to Cut Men's Shaggy Hairstyle

Before you start cutting, make sure that your client's hair is clean and dry. You'll also need the right tools, including scissors, a comb, and a clipper (if necessary). Follow these steps to cut men's shaggy hairstyle:

Step 1: Start with the crown

Begin by sectioning off the hair at the crown of the head. Use your comb to create a horizontal parting, then clip the hair above it. This will help you create a guide for the rest of the haircut.

Step 2: Cut the crown

Hold the hair at the crown between your fingers and point-cut it to create texture and movement. Use a vertical cutting angle and work your way down to the desired length. Don't cut too much hair at once, as this can create a choppy and uneven look.

Step 3: Move to the sides

Release the hair above the ears and comb it down. Use your scissors to point-cut the ends, creating choppy layers. Work your way down to the nape of the neck, cutting the hair in small sections. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Cut the back

Release the hair at the back of the head and comb it down. Use your scissors to point-cut the ends, creating choppy layers. Work your way up to the crown, cutting the hair in small sections. This will help you blend the layers from the sides and the back.

Step 5: Blend the layers

Use your scissors to blend the layers from the crown to the sides and the back. Make sure that the layers are evenly distributed and there are no visible lines or gaps. Use your comb to check for any unevenness and make adjustments as needed.

Step 6: Add texture

If necessary, use your clipper to add texture to the hair. This will help you create a more relaxed and messy look. Use a guard that's appropriate for your client's hair type and start at the nape of the neck, working your way up to the crown.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut men's shaggy hairstyle:

  • Use point-cutting to create texture and movement.
  • Work in small sections to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Blend the layers from the crown to the sides and the back.
  • Use the right tools, including scissors, a comb, and a clipper (if necessary).
  • Communicate with your client and ask for feedback throughout the haircut.

FAQ

Q: Who can wear a shaggy hairstyle?

A: The shaggy hairstyle can work for men of all ages and hair types. It's a versatile and low-maintenance haircut that can be customized to suit different face shapes and personal styles.

Q: How often should I cut my shaggy hairstyle?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have fast-growing hair, you may need to get a trim every 4-6 weeks. If you prefer a longer and more relaxed look, you can wait up to 8-12 weeks between haircuts.

Q: Can I style my shaggy hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can style your shaggy hairstyle in different ways, depending on your personal style and the occasion. You can use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to create a more defined and polished look. Or you can leave it messy and undone for a more casual and effortless vibe.

Hair Care

To maintain your shaggy hairstyle, you should follow a simple and consistent hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and maintain the shape of your shaggy hairstyle.

Cutting men's shaggy hairstyle is a fun and creative way to help your clients look and feel their best. By following these simple steps and tips, you can create a stylish and effortless look that suits their personal style and hair type. Remember to communicate with your client throughout the haircut and ask for feedback to ensure their satisfaction. With practice and patience, you can become a pro at cutting men's shaggy hairstyle.
