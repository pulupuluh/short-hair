How To Hide Extra Eyebrow Hair With Makeup

Written by William May 06, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you struggling with extra eyebrow hair that just won't stay in place? Don't worry, there's a solution that doesn't involve painful plucking or expensive salon treatments. With the right makeup techniques, you can easily hide those stray eyebrow hairs and achieve a flawless, polished look. In this article, we'll show you how to hide extra eyebrow hair with makeup step by step, including tips and tricks for maintaining your beautiful brows.

How to Hide Extra Eyebrow Hair with Makeup

The first step in hiding extra eyebrow hair is to choose the right makeup products. You'll need an eyebrow pencil or powder in a shade that matches your natural eyebrow color, a spoolie brush, and a clear brow gel or wax. Here's how to hide extra eyebrow hair with makeup:

Step 1: Brush Your Brows

Use a spoolie brush to comb your eyebrow hairs upward and outward. This will help you see any stray hairs that need to be hidden.

Step 2: Fill in Your Brows

Using an eyebrow pencil or powder, fill in any gaps or sparse areas in your eyebrows. This will create a fuller, more defined look and make it easier to hide stray hairs.

Step 3: Conceal Stray Hairs

Use a small, angled brush and some concealer that matches your skin tone to carefully cover any stray eyebrow hairs. This will help to blend them in with the rest of your eyebrow and create a clean, polished look.

Step 4: Set Your Brows

Finally, use a clear brow gel or wax to set your brows in place. This will help to keep any stray hairs from popping up throughout the day.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide for hiding extra eyebrow hair with makeup:

Step 1: Prep Your Brows

Before you start applying makeup, make sure your eyebrows are clean and dry. Use a spoolie brush to comb your eyebrows upward and outward.

Step 2: Choose Your Products

Choose an eyebrow pencil or powder that matches your natural eyebrow color. You'll also need a small, angled brush, some concealer that matches your skin tone, and a clear brow gel or wax.

Step 3: Fill in Your Brows

Using your eyebrow pencil or powder, fill in any gaps or sparse areas in your eyebrows. Be sure to use light, feathery strokes to create a natural-looking effect.

Step 4: Conceal Stray Hairs

Use your small, angled brush and some concealer to carefully cover any stray eyebrow hairs. Make sure to blend the concealer in well so that it looks natural.

Step 5: Set Your Brows

Finally, use a clear brow gel or wax to set your brows in place. This will help to keep them looking neat and tidy throughout the day.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for hiding extra eyebrow hair with makeup:

  • Use a light hand when applying makeup to your eyebrows. You don't want to create a harsh, unnatural look.
  • Make sure your eyebrow pencil or powder is sharpened to a fine point for precise application.
  • If you're using a brow gel or wax, be sure to apply it in the direction of hair growth to avoid clumping.
  • Don't forget to clean your makeup tools regularly to avoid bacteria buildup.

FAQs

Can I use regular concealer to hide eyebrow hairs?

Yes, you can use regular concealer to hide eyebrow hairs. Just make sure to choose a shade that matches your skin tone and blend it in well for a natural look.

Do I need to use a brow gel or wax?

No, you don't need to use a brow gel or wax, but it can help to keep your eyebrow hairs in place and prevent them from straying throughout the day.

How often should I fill in my eyebrows?

This will depend on how quickly your eyebrow hairs grow and how defined you want your brows to look. Generally, filling in your eyebrows once a day should be sufficient.

Eyebrow Hair Care

It's important to take care of your eyebrow hairs to keep them looking healthy and strong. Here are some tips for eyebrow hair care:

  • Avoid over-plucking or waxing your eyebrows, as this can damage the hair follicles.
  • Use a gentle cleanser to wash your face and eyebrows, and avoid using harsh chemicals or scrubs on your eyebrows.
  • Apply a nourishing eyebrow serum or oil to help promote hair growth and keep your eyebrow hairs healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily hide extra eyebrow hair with makeup and achieve a flawless, polished look. Remember to take care of your eyebrow hairs to keep them looking healthy and beautiful.
