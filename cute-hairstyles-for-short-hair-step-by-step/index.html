Cute Hairstyles For Short Hair Step By Step

Written by Robby Jul 16, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for cute and easy hairstyles for your short hair? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to create some of the most adorable hairstyles for short hair. These hairstyles are perfect for any occasion, whether it be a casual day out or a fancy event. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Bob Cut
  2. Pixie Cut
  3. Braids
  4. Top Knot
  5. Side Part

Bob Cut

The bob cut is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's easy to maintain and perfect for those who are always on the go. Here's how to create a cute bob cut:

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  2. Using a straightening iron, straighten your hair to create a sleek look.
  3. Create a deep side part and tuck one side behind your ear.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to keep everything in place.

This cute bob cut is perfect for any occasion and can be dressed up or down depending on your outfit.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a bold and daring hairstyle that is perfect for those who want to make a statement. Here's how to create a cute pixie cut:

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  2. Using a straightening iron, straighten your hair to create a sleek look.
  3. Create a deep side part and tuck one side behind your ear.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to keep everything in place.

This cute pixie cut is perfect for any occasion and can be dressed up or down depending on your outfit.

Braids

Braids are a cute and easy hairstyle that can be done in minutes. Here's how to create a cute braided hairstyle for short hair:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Create two braids on either side of your head, starting at the top and braiding all the way down to the ends.
  3. Secure each braid with a small elastic band.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to keep everything in place.

This cute braided hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out or even a fancy event.

Top Knot

The top knot is a cute and trendy hairstyle that can be done in minutes. Here's how to create a cute top knot for short hair:

  1. Gather all of your hair at the top of your head.
  2. Twist your hair around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to keep everything in place.

This cute top knot hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out or even a fancy event.

Side Part

The side part is a classic and elegant hairstyle that can be done in minutes. Here's how to create a cute side part for short hair:

  1. Start by blow-drying your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  2. Create a deep side part on one side of your head.
  3. Using a straightening iron, straighten your hair to create a sleek look.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to keep everything in place.

This cute side part hairstyle is perfect for a fancy event or even a day at the office.

Hair Care Tips

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks and feels healthy. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

FAQs

Q: Can these hairstyles be done on all types of short hair?

A: Yes, these hairstyles can be done on all types of short hair.

Q: Do I need any special tools to create these hairstyles?

A: No, you don't need any special tools to create these hairstyles. All you need is a blow dryer, straightening iron, and some elastic bands.

Q: How long does it take to create these hairstyles?

A: It takes about 10-15 minutes to create these hairstyles.

That wraps up our article on cute hairstyles for short hair step by step. We hope you found these hairstyles helpful and easy to recreate. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Happy styling!
