How To Find Out Which Hairstyle Suits My Face

Are you tired of constantly trying out different hairstyles that just don't seem to work for you? It's time to learn how to find out which hairstyle suits your face. In this article, we'll give you tips and tricks to figure out the perfect hairstyle for your face shape. Let's get started!

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your face is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Rectangle

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a close look at your face in the mirror. Use a measuring tape to measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

For a round face, your cheekbones and face length are roughly the same size. For an oval face, your face length is longer than your cheekbones, and your forehead is slightly wider than your jawline. For a square face, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same size. For a heart-shaped face, your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your chin is pointed. For a diamond face shape, your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are narrower. For a rectangular face shape, your face length is the longest measurement, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same size.

Step 2: Look for Hairstyles That Complement Your Face Shape

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to look for hairstyles that complement your features. Here are some general guidelines:

  • For a round face, choose hairstyles that add height and length, such as a high ponytail or long layers.
  • For an oval face, most hairstyles will work, but avoid styles that add too much volume on top.
  • For a square face, choose hairstyles with soft layers or waves to soften your features.
  • For a heart-shaped face, choose hairstyles that add volume at the bottom, such as a bob or a pixie cut.
  • For a diamond face shape, choose hairstyles that add width at the forehead and jawline, such as a side part or layers that start at the cheekbones.
  • For a rectangular face shape, choose hairstyles with volume on the sides to add width, such as waves or a layered bob.

Step 3: Consult with a Professional

If you're still unsure which hairstyle suits your face, it's always a good idea to consult with a professional hair stylist. They can give you personalized recommendations based on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair.
  • Try out different hairstyles with virtual makeover tools or by trying out wigs.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with color and highlights to enhance your features.
  • Remember that your hairstyle should make you feel confident and comfortable.

FAQs About Choosing the Right Hairstyle

What if I have multiple face shapes?

It's common to have a combination of face shapes, so focus on the areas that you want to enhance or soften.

Can I still wear a hairstyle that doesn't complement my face shape?

Of course! Ultimately, it's up to you to decide what hairstyle you feel most comfortable in.

How often should I change my hairstyle?

It's up to you! Some people like to change their hairstyle every few months, while others prefer to stick with a signature style for years.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

  • Invest in quality hair care products that are tailored to your hair type.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Get regular deep conditioning treatments to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Now that you know how to find out which hairstyle suits your face, it's time to try out some new looks! Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you.
