What Colours Suit You Test Find Your Best Colours

Written by Bardi Jun 22, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you tired of wearing the wrong colours that make you look dull and tired? Do you want to know which colours will make you look your best? Take the "What Colours Suit You Test" and find out which colours suit your skin tone, hair colour, and eye colour. In this article, we will guide you on how to take the test, provide tips on what to do after the test, and answer some frequently asked questions.

What is the "What Colours Suit You Test"?

The "What Colours Suit You Test" is a test that helps determine which colours will complement your skin tone, hair colour, and eye colour. It is a simple test that involves answering a few questions about your physical features and preferences. The results will provide you with a list of colours that will make you look your best.

How to Take the Test

To take the test, you can search online for "what colours suit you test" or use a colour analysis app. Some popular apps include ColorMe, Stylebook, and Cladwell. Once you find a test or app, follow the instructions and answer the questions truthfully. The questions may include:

  • What is your skin tone?
  • What is your hair colour?
  • What is your eye colour?
  • Which colours do you prefer to wear?
  • Which colours do you think look best on you?

After you answer the questions, the test will generate a list of colours that will make you look your best. You can use this list as a guide when shopping for clothes or makeup.

Tips for Using the Results

Once you have your list of colours, you can start incorporating them into your wardrobe. Here are some tips:

  • Start with small items like accessories or makeup before buying a whole outfit in a new colour.
  • Experiment with different shades of the same colour to find the ones that suit you best.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match colours from your list.
  • Remember that the list is a guide, not a rule. You can still wear colours that are not on your list if you love them.

FAQ About the "What Colours Suit You Test"

What if I don't like any of the colours on my list?

It's okay to not like some of the colours on your list. The list is a guide, not a rule. You can still wear colours that are not on your list if you love them. However, keep in mind that the colours on your list will complement your features and make you look your best.

What if I have multiple skin tones or hair colours?

If you have multiple skin tones or hair colours, choose the one that is most dominant or the one you want to enhance. For example, if you have a warm undertone in your skin but cool-toned hair, you can choose warm colours that complement your skin tone.

Do I have to follow the list every time I shop for clothes?

No, you don't have to follow the list every time you shop for clothes. The list is a guide to help you choose colours that will make you look your best. You can still wear colours that are not on your list if you love them.

Hair Care Tips to Complement Your Colours

Wearing the right colours can enhance your features and make you look your best. However, it's also important to take care of your hair to complement your colours. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and colour.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with SPF.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain healthy hair.

By taking the "What Colours Suit You Test" and following these tips, you can enhance your features and feel confident in your appearance.
