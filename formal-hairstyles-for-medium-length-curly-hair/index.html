Formal Hairstyles For Medium Length Curly Hair Tips And Ideas

Introduction

When it comes to formal events, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you have medium length curly hair. However, with the right styling tips and ideas, it's possible to create a stunning and elegant look that will leave everyone in awe.

How to Choose the Perfect Formal Hairstyle for Medium Length Curly Hair

Before choosing a formal hairstyle for your medium length curly hair, it's important to consider a few factors:

  • Face Shape: The shape of your face can influence the style of your hair. For example, if you have an oval face, you can opt for any hairstyle. However, if you have a round face, you may want to go for a style that adds height and lengthens your face.
  • Dress: The style and neckline of your dress can also influence your hairstyle. For example, if you have a strapless dress, an updo can be a great choice to show off your neckline.
  • Comfort: It's important to choose a hairstyle that you feel comfortable in, as you'll be wearing it for several hours.

Top Formal Hairstyles for Medium Length Curly Hair

Here are some of the best formal hairstyles for medium length curly hair:

1. Updo

An updo is a classic and elegant hairstyle that works well for formal events. It's also a great option for medium length curly hair. You can opt for a messy bun or a sleek chignon, depending on your personal style.

2. Half-Up, Half-Down

If you want to show off your curls, a half-up, half-down hairstyle is a great option. You can add some braids or twists to make it more interesting.

3. Braids

Braids can add texture and volume to your hair, and they're a great option for medium length curly hair. You can opt for a simple braid or a more elaborate fishtail or Dutch braid.

4. Side Swept Curls

If you want to keep your hair down, you can opt for side swept curls. This style is simple yet elegant, and it works well for medium length curly hair.

Step-by-Step Tutorial for a Formal Updo

Here's a step-by-step tutorial for creating a formal updo for your medium length curly hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Apply a leave-in conditioner to protect your hair from heat damage.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.
  3. Section your hair into three parts: the top, middle, and bottom.
  4. Twist the bottom section of your hair into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  5. Twist the middle section of your hair and pin it around the bun.
  6. Twist the top section of your hair and pin it around the bun.
  7. Use hairspray to set your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium length curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz.

FAQs

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair for a formal event?
A: Yes, you can straighten your hair if you prefer a sleeker look. Just make sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Q: Can I add hair accessories to my formal hairstyle?
A: Yes, hair accessories can add some extra glamour to your formal hairstyle. You can opt for a jeweled headband, a flower crown, or some sparkly hairpins.

Conclusion

There are plenty of formal hairstyles for medium length curly hair that can make you look stunning and elegant. Whether you opt for an updo, braids, or side swept curls, make sure to choose a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable.
