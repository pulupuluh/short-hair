How To Figure Out The Best Hairstyle For Your Face

Written by Aprilia Mar 17, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking to switch things up? The key to finding a new hairstyle that looks great on you is to consider your face shape. By understanding your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that accentuates your best features and flatters your overall look. In this article, we'll show you how to figure out the best hairstyle for your face, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type
  • Step 3: Look for Inspiration
  • Tips for Choosing the Best Hairstyle for Your Face

  • Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for your face is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To figure out your face shape, you can do the following:

  • Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back away from your face.
  • Trace the outline of your face onto the mirror using a washable marker or lipstick.
  • Step back and look at the shape you traced.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can move on to the next step.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to consider your hair type. Your hair type can influence which hairstyles will work best for you. Consider the following:

  • Is your hair straight, wavy, or curly?
  • Is your hair thick or thin?
  • Do you have any hair texture issues, such as frizz or dryness?

Knowing your hair type will help you choose a hairstyle that works with your natural hair texture and thickness.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Now that you know your face shape and hair type, it's time to look for inspiration. Look online for hairstyle ideas that work well with your face shape and hair type. You can also consult with a hairstylist for professional advice. Consider the following:

  • Look for celebrities with similar face shapes and hair types as yours.
  • Consider the length and style of the hairstyles you like.
  • Think about how much time and effort you are willing to put into styling your hair.

Once you've found some hairstyle ideas that you like, bring them to your hairstylist for their input and advice.

Tips for Choosing the Best Hairstyle for Your Face

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the best hairstyle for your face:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you can devote to styling your hair.
  • Be realistic about your hair type and how it will interact with your chosen hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and lengths to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to consult with a hairstylist for professional advice.

FAQs about Choosing the Best Hairstyle for Your Face

Q: Can I pull off any hairstyle, regardless of my face shape?

A: While you can certainly experiment with different hairstyles, certain styles may not be as flattering on your face shape. By choosing a hairstyle that works well with your face shape, you can accentuate your best features and flatter your overall look.

Q: Is it better to choose a hairstyle based on current trends or what looks best on me?

A: While it's fine to consider current trends, it's ultimately more important to choose a hairstyle that looks best on you. Trends come and go, but a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and hair type will always look great.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hairstyle

Once you've chosen a new hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its appearance. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Use hair products, such as styling gel or hairspray, to help hold your hairstyle in place.

By following these tips, you can keep your new hairstyle looking great for weeks to come.

Choosing the best hairstyle for your face can be a fun and exciting process. By taking into account your face shape, hair type, and personal style, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your overall look and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
