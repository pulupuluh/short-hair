How To Cut Men s Curly Hair With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

If you're a man with curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to find the right haircut. But with the right tools and a little know-how, you can give yourself a great haircut at home. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut men's curly hair with clippers.

How to Cut Men's Curly Hair with Clippers

Cutting men's curly hair with clippers can be a little tricky, but it's definitely doable. Here's what you'll need:

  • A good pair of clippers
  • A comb
  • A mirror
  • A towel or cape to catch hair

Once you have all your tools, you're ready to get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut men's curly hair with clippers:

  1. Wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier to cut.
  2. Decide on the length you want. Use the adjustable clippers to choose the right guard size for your desired length.
  3. Start at the sides. Use the clippers to trim the hair on the sides of your head, working from the bottom up. Use a comb to guide the clippers and make sure you're cutting the hair evenly.
  4. Move to the back. Trim the hair on the back of your head in the same way, working from the bottom up.
  5. Trim the top. Use the clippers to trim the hair on the top of your head, working from front to back. Use a comb to guide the clippers and make sure you're cutting the hair evenly.
  6. Blend the sides and top. Use the clippers to blend the sides and top together, working from the bottom up.
  7. Trim the neckline. Use the clippers to trim the hair on your neckline, working from the bottom up.
  8. Check for any missed spots. Use a mirror to check for any spots you may have missed.
  9. Clean up. Use a towel or cape to catch any loose hair and clean up your work area.

Tips for Cutting Men's Curly Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting men's curly hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Don't cut too much off at once. It's better to take off a little at a time than to cut too much and have to start over.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and make sure you're cutting the hair evenly.
  • Trim the hair on the sides and back before moving to the top.
  • Blend the sides and top together for a more natural look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's curly hair with clippers:

Can you cut curly hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut curly hair with clippers. However, it can be a little more challenging than cutting straight hair.

What guard size should I use to cut curly hair?

The guard size you use will depend on the length you want. Start with a longer guard size and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.

Can I cut my own curly hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own curly hair with clippers. Just make sure you have the right tools and follow the steps we outlined above.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair can be a little different than caring for straight hair. Here are some tips to help you keep your curly hair looking its best:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Don't wash your hair too often. Curly hair can be prone to dryness, so washing it too often can make it even drier.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage curly hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to help define your curls.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can give yourself a great haircut at home. Happy clipping!
