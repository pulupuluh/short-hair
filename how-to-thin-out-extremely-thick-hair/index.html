How To Thin Out Extremely Thick Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 06, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing, but it can also be a curse. If you have extremely thick hair, you might find that it's difficult to manage or style. Thinning out your hair can make it more manageable and give it a lighter, more airy feel. In this article, we'll show you how to thin out extremely thick hair.

How to Thin Out Extremely Thick Hair

Thinning out extremely thick hair can be done in several ways. Here are some of the most common methods:

  • Layering
  • Razor Cutting
  • Thinning Shears
  • Texturizing

Each method has its own pros and cons, and the best method for you will depend on your hair type and personal preferences. In the next section, we'll go over each method in more detail.

Step-by-Step Guide

Layering

Layering is a great way to thin out extremely thick hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Start at the back of your head and work your way up.
  4. Take a small section of hair and hold it straight up.
  5. Cut the ends of the hair at an angle.
  6. Repeat on all sections of hair.

Layering will create movement and texture in your hair, making it look less heavy and more manageable.

Razor Cutting

Razor cutting is another popular method for thinning out extremely thick hair. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Start at the back of your head and work your way up.
  4. Take a small section of hair and hold it straight up.
  5. Using a razor comb, cut the hair at an angle.
  6. Repeat on all sections of hair.

Razor cutting will create a softer, more natural look and is great for adding texture to your hair.

Thinning Shears

Thinning shears are a specialized type of scissors that are designed to thin out hair. Here's how to use them:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Start at the back of your head and work your way up.
  4. Take a small section of hair and hold it straight up.
  5. Using the thinning shears, cut into the hair at an angle.
  6. Repeat on all sections of hair.

Thinning shears are great for removing bulk from your hair while maintaining length and shape.

Texturizing

Texturizing is a method that involves cutting small sections of hair at random to create a more natural, textured look. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Start at the back of your head and work your way up.
  4. Take a small section of hair and hold it straight up.
  5. Using the scissors, cut into the hair at random angles.
  6. Repeat on all sections of hair.

Texturizing will create a more relaxed, natural look and is great for adding volume to your hair.

Tips for Thinning Out Extremely Thick Hair

Thinning out extremely thick hair can be challenging, but here are some tips to make the process easier:

  • Start with dry hair.
  • Use sharp scissors or thinning shears.
  • Take small sections of hair to avoid over-thinning.
  • Don't thin out too much hair at once.
  • Work slowly and carefully.

FAQs

Is it possible to thin out extremely thick hair without cutting it?

No, cutting is the only way to thin out extremely thick hair. However, you can use styling products and techniques to make your hair look less heavy.

Can thinning out my hair cause damage?

Thinning out your hair can cause damage if you overdo it or use the wrong technique. Make sure to work slowly and carefully and take breaks if you need to.

How often should I thin out my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, you should aim to thin out your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

If you have thick hair, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.

By following these tips and using the right techniques to thin out your hair, you can have beautiful, healthy, and manageable hair.
