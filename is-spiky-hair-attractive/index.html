Is Spiky Hair Attractive

Written by Frank May 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're wondering whether spiky hair is attractive or not, you're not alone. Many people have different opinions about this hairstyle. Some think it's cool and edgy, while others think it's outdated and too punk. However, the truth is that spiky hair can be attractive, depending on how it's styled and who's wearing it. In this article, we'll explore the different aspects of spiky hair and how to make it look its best.

Description

Spiky hair is a hairstyle where the hair is styled upwards and outwards, creating a spiky or pointy look. This hairstyle has been popular for decades, especially among men. However, women have also started to embrace this style in recent years. Spiky hair can be achieved with different hair lengths, from short to long. It's usually styled using hair gel or wax to hold the spikes in place.

How to

If you want to try spiky hair, here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply hair gel or wax to your hair, focusing on the roots and tips.
  3. Use your fingers or a comb to style your hair upwards and outwards.
  4. Create spikes by twisting and pulling sections of hair upwards.
  5. Use more hair gel or wax to hold the spikes in place.
  6. Finish with hairspray to keep the spikes in place all day.

Remember that spiky hair requires some maintenance. You'll need to wash your hair regularly and reapply hair gel or wax every day to keep the spikes in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style spiky hair:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner and towel dry it.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to your fingertips.
  3. Starting at the back of your head, use your fingers to work the hair gel or wax into your hair.
  4. Use your fingers to style your hair upwards and outwards, creating spikes.
  5. Use a comb to create more defined spikes.
  6. Repeat steps 3-5 until you've styled all of your hair.
  7. Finish with hairspray to keep the spikes in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your spiky hair look its best:

  • Choose the right hair gel or wax for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different hair lengths and styles to find what looks best on you.
  • Don't overdo it with the hair gel or wax. Too much can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut to achieve the perfect spiky look.
  • Match your spiky hair with a cool outfit to complete the edgy look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about spiky hair:

Is spiky hair only for men?

No, spiky hair can be worn by anyone, regardless of gender. Women can also rock this edgy look.

Is spiky hair suitable for all face shapes?

Spiky hair can work for most face shapes, but it's best suited for oval or square faces. If you have a round face, you might want to avoid spiky hair as it can accentuate the roundness.

What hair products should I use for spiky hair?

You should use hair gel or wax to achieve the spiky look. Look for products that offer a strong hold and don't weigh down your hair.

Hair Care

If you're going to rock spiky hair, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and pollution by wearing a hat or using a hair spray with UV protection.

With proper care and styling, spiky hair can be a cool and attractive hairstyle. So, go ahead and give it a try!
