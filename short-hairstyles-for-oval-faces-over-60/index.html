Short Hairstyles For Oval Faces Over 60 A Guide To Looking Fabulous

Written by Aprilia Apr 30, 2023 Â· 7 min read

If you're over 60 and have an oval face, you might be wondering what hairstyles would suit you best. Fortunately, there are plenty of short hairstyles that would look great on your face shape. In this article, we'll discuss some of the most popular short hairstyles for oval faces over 60. We'll also provide tips on how to style your hair and keep it looking healthy.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  2. Popular Short Hairstyles for Oval Faces over 60
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Short Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

Before we dive into the different hairstyles you can try, it's important to know what to look for when choosing a hairstyle. When you have an oval face, you have the advantage of being able to pull off most hairstyles. However, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume on top of your head, as this can make your face appear longer.
  • Side-swept bangs can help shorten your face and draw attention to your eyes.
  • Short hairstyles that hit at or above your chin will complement your face shape.

Popular Short Hairstyles for Oval Faces over 60

Now that you know what to look for, let's explore some of the most popular short hairstyles for oval faces over 60:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that looks great on oval faces. It's a low-maintenance style that's perfect for women over 60 who want to keep their hair short and easy to manage. You can add side-swept bangs to give your face some dimension.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another timeless hairstyle that's great for oval faces over 60. It's a versatile style that can be customized to suit your preferences. You can opt for a chin-length bob or a longer one that hits at your shoulders.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is perfect if you want to add some volume to your hair. Layers can create the illusion of thicker hair and add texture to your look. You can opt for a short layered cut or a longer one that hits at your collarbone.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Short Hair

Styling short hair can be easy and fun. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you style your short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling wand to add some texture to your hair.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but there are a few things you can do to keep it looking healthy:

  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for oval faces over 60:

Q: Can I wear bangs with short hair?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with short hair. Side-swept bangs are a popular choice for oval faces over 60.

Q: Will a pixie cut make me look older?

A: No, a pixie cut won't necessarily make you look older. It's all about how you style it and what you wear with it.

Q: Can I color my short hair?

A: Yes, you can color your short hair. Just make sure to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the vibrancy of your color.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can create buildup on your scalp.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you'll be able to find a short hairstyle that complements your oval face and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
