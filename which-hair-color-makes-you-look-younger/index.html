Which Hair Color Makes You Look Younger

Written by Frank Mar 21, 2023 Â· 9 min read

As we age, our hair loses its natural pigment and starts to turn gray or white. While some people embrace their gray hair, others prefer to dye their locks to look younger. But with so many hair colors available, which one is the best for making you look younger? In this article, we'll explore the answer to that question and provide tips on how to maintain your hair color.

Table of Contents

  2. How to Choose the Right Hair Color
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

  6. Hair Care Tips for Color-Treated Hair

Description

Many factors determine which hair color will make you look younger, including your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. Generally, warm hair colors like golden blonde, honey blonde, and caramel brown flatter most skin tones and make people look younger. These colors add warmth and depth to the face, which can reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. On the other hand, cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, and jet black can wash out the skin and make people look older.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

Choosing the right hair color can be challenging, especially if you've never dyed your hair before. Here are some tips to help you pick the perfect shade:

  • Consider your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, choose golden or honey shades. If you have cool undertones, choose ashy or cool shades.
  • Think about your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, it's best to stick to shades that are no more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural color. If you have light hair, you can experiment with more dramatic colors.
  • Look at your eye color. Certain shades, like copper and auburn, can bring out the color in your eyes.
  • Consult with a professional stylist. They can help you choose a color that complements your skin tone and features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Coloring Your Hair

If you've decided to dye your hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect color:

  1. Choose a high-quality hair dye that's specifically designed for your hair type and color.
  2. Do a strand test to make sure you're not allergic to the dye and to see how long it takes to achieve the desired color.
  3. Apply petroleum jelly around your hairline to prevent the dye from staining your skin.
  4. Follow the instructions on the hair dye box to mix the dye and apply it evenly to your hair.
  5. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time and then rinse it out with warm water.
  6. Apply a color-safe conditioner to your hair to lock in the color and keep your hair healthy.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Now that you've dyed your hair, it's important to maintain the color to keep it looking fresh and vibrant. Here are some tips to help you do that:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading and keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage the hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine and saltwater, as they can strip the color from your hair.
  • Get regular touch-ups to keep your roots from showing.

FAQs

Q: Can hair color really make you look younger?

A: Yes, hair color can make you look younger. The right hair color can add warmth and depth to your face, which can reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

Q: What hair colors make you look older?

A: Cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, and jet black can wash out the skin and make people look older.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but it's important to follow the instructions carefully and do a strand test beforehand to avoid any allergic reactions or unexpected results.

Hair Care Tips for Color-Treated Hair

Color-treated hair requires special care to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to help you take care of your color-treated hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading and keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as it can strip the color and natural oils from your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as they can damage the hair and cause the color to fade.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine and saltwater, as they can strip the color from your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, choosing the right hair color can make a big difference in how young you look. Warm hair colors like golden blonde, honey blonde, and caramel brown are generally the best for making you look younger, while cool colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, and jet black can wash out the skin and make you look older. If you're unsure which color to choose, consult with a professional stylist who can help you find the perfect shade.
