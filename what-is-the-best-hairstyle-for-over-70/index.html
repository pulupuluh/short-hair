What Is The Best Hairstyle For Over 70

Written by Aprilia Sep 03, 2023 Â· 7 min read

As we age, our hair tends to get thinner, drier, and more brittle. This can make finding the right hairstyle a bit of a challenge, especially if you're over 70. But don't worry, there are plenty of great hairstyles that can make you look and feel fabulous at any age. In this article, we'll discuss what is the best hairstyle for over 70, and provide some tips and tricks for caring for your hair as you age.

Description

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for over 70, there are a few things to keep in mind. First and foremost, you want to choose a style that is easy to maintain and doesn't require a lot of fuss. You also want to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture.

Some of the best hairstyles for over 70 include short pixie cuts, chin-length bobs, and shoulder-length layers. These styles are easy to maintain and can help to add volume and texture to your hair.

How To

When it comes to styling your hair, there are a few tips and tricks that can help you achieve the perfect look. First, make sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for mature hair. This can help to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Next, consider using a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume to your hair. This can help to make your hair look fuller and more youthful.

When styling your hair, consider using a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots. You can also use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture and shape to your hair.

Step by Step

If you're looking for a simple and easy-to-maintain hairstyle, a short pixie cut may be the perfect option for you. Here's how to achieve this look:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, focusing on the roots to create volume and lift.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  5. Finish off the look with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

When choosing a hairstyle for over 70, it's important to keep in mind that less is often more. Stick with simple and easy-to-maintain styles that complement your face shape and hair texture.

Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add depth and dimension. This can help to make your hair look fuller and more youthful.

FAQ

Q: What is the best haircut for fine hair over 70?

A: The best haircut for fine hair over 70 is often a short pixie cut or chin-length bob. These styles can help to add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and more youthful.

Q: How can I make my hair look fuller and thicker?

A: To make your hair look fuller and thicker, consider using a volumizing mousse or spray. You can also try blow drying your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots.

Q: What is the best way to care for my hair as I age?

A: To care for your hair as you age, make sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for mature hair. You can also consider using a hydrating hair mask once a week to help keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Hair Care

As we age, our hair can become more brittle and prone to breakage. To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips for caring for your hair as you age:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for mature hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and flat irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hydrating hair mask once a week to help keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly into ponytails or braids, as this can cause breakage.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can look and feel fabulous at any age!
