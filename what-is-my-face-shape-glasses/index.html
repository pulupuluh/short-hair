What Is My Face Shape Glasses

Written by Robby Aug 01, 2023 Â· 8 min read

Are you wondering which glasses will suit your face shape? Choosing the right frame is as important as choosing the right hairstyle. It can enhance your facial features and make you look more attractive. But, how do you figure out what is my face shape glasses? In this article, we will explore the different face shapes and how to choose the best glasses for your face.

What is My Face Shape?

Before we talk about glasses, it is essential to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and triangle. To find out your face shape, you need to measure your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and face length.

How to Measure Your Face?

Here are the steps to measure your face:

  1. Forehead: Measure the widest part of your forehead with a tape measure.
  2. Cheekbones: Measure the widest part of your cheekbones, just below your eyes.
  3. Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the bottom of your earlobe.
  4. Face Length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Once you have measured your face, compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

What Glasses Suit My Face Shape?

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose the right glasses. Here are some tips:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! Almost any style of glasses will suit you. However, try to avoid frames that are too big or too small for your face.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, choose glasses that are angular or rectangular. This will help to elongate your face and make it look slimmer. Avoid round frames as they will only accentuate the roundness of your face.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, choose glasses that are round or oval. This will help to soften the angles of your face. Avoid square frames as they will only make your face look boxy.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, choose glasses that are wider at the bottom than the top. This will balance out the width of your forehead and narrow chin. Avoid glasses that are too narrow as they will make your forehead look wider.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, choose glasses that are wider than your cheekbones. This will help to highlight your cheekbones and balance out your face. Avoid glasses that are too narrow or too wide.

Triangle Face Shape

If you have a triangle face shape, choose glasses that are wider at the top than the bottom. This will balance out the width of your forehead and narrow chin. Avoid glasses that are too narrow as they will make your forehead look wider.

Tips for Choosing Glasses

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing glasses:

  • Choose glasses that match your personality and style.
  • Choose glasses that complement your skin tone and hair color.
  • Choose glasses that fit well and feel comfortable.
  • Choose glasses that are durable and made of high-quality materials.

FAQ

Can I wear glasses if I have a small face?

Yes, you can wear glasses if you have a small face. Look for glasses that are proportionate to your face and don't overwhelm your features.

Can I wear glasses if I have a big nose?

Yes, you can wear glasses if you have a big nose. Look for glasses that have a nose pad that can support the weight of the glasses.

Can I wear glasses if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can wear glasses if you have curly hair. Look for glasses that complement your hair texture and highlight your facial features.

Hair Care

Choosing the right glasses can enhance your hairstyle. If you have bangs, choose glasses that have a thinner frame to avoid overpowering your bangs. If you have short hair, choose glasses that have a bold frame to add some style. If you have long hair, choose glasses that have a subtle frame to avoid looking too busy.

In conclusion, choosing the right glasses for your face shape is essential to enhance your facial features and make you look more attractive. By following these tips, you can find the perfect glasses that match your personality, style, and face shape.
